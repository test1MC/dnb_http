RELX N.V. (formerly Reed Elsevier NV) traces its roots to the late 19th century, when a number of Dutch publishers joined forces, but today the company operates through 50%-owned RELX Group plc . The international information and publishing giant serves the business, legal, and scientific communities with a variety of digital and print magazines and information products, through units such as Reed Business Information and LexisNexis . UK-based RELX PLC owns the other half of RELX Group. RELX N.V. also owns 61% of Elsevier Reed Finance, the financing arm for RELX Group plc. North America is the company's largest market, contributing about half of its total revenue.
