Conrad Fafard is pretty sure it has mulch "in the bag." The company produces peat moss, soilless plant mixes, top soil, peat humus, potting soils, and composted manures for both retailers and growers. Soilless plant mixes are the fastest-growing segment of its business. Conrad Fafard distributes its products to independent garden centers throughout North America. The company operates production centers in Florida, Minnesota, South Carolina, and Texas, as well as a facility and peat bogs in Canada. Established by Conrad and Annette Fafard years earlier, the family-run company was incorporated in 1945. Conrad Fafard is owned by the US subsidiary of Switzerland's Syngenta .
