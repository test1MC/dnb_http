Cigarette? Miquel y Costas & Miquel manufactures cigarette papers (from 100% wood pulp to 100% flax or hemp), printing papers (high quality fine and opaque), rolling papers, specialty lightweight papers, and textile pulps. The company's products are made at six facilities in Spain and one in Argentina. The history of the company can be traced back to 1725, when the Miquel family began producing paper by hand; the company Miquel y Costas & Miquel actually began in 1879 with the commencement of its first facility in Latin America. Countries outside of Spain represent about 80% of the company's sales.

The company derives 78% of its revenue from the tobacco industry, 13% from industrial products, and the remainder from other sales (printing papers).
