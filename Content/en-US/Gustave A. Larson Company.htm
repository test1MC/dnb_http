Gustave A. Larson keeps the West cool and the Midwest warm. Through some 53 locations in about 20 US states from Utah to Indiana, the company distributes heating, ventilation, air-conditioning, and refrigeration equipment and accessories to retailers and contractors. Gustave A. Larson carries more than 15,000 products from more than 450 manufacturers including American Standard , Honeywell , Trane , Johnson Controls , and Modine . Services include HVAC system design, marketing support, installation, and training programs. Its Larson Environmental unit offers eco-friendly products and services. The founding Larson family continues to own the company.

Gustave A. Larson has 53 locations serving customers in Arizona, Colorado, Idaho, Illinois, Indiana, Iowa, Kansas, Kentucky, Michigan, Minnesota, Missouri, Nebraska, Nevada, New Mexico, North Dakota, Ohio, South Dakota, Utah, Wyoming, and Wisconsin.

The company offers various top-tiered residential equipment manufacturers' products throughout its territory. It offers a full line of equipment, controls, supplies and replacement parts. (The company has more 75,000 stock keeping units in stock).

Gustave A. Larson represents products from more than 450 different leading industry manufacturers in the areas of commercial refrigeration, residential and commercial HVAC, food service, and pressure/temperature controls. Some of the selected clients are Kappler Mechanical, Warren Service & Supply, Advanced Refrigeration, Young's Facility Service, Lockett Refrigeration, and Hurtis Heating.

The company sells its products via sales consultants and online.

The company's growth strategy includes building partnerships, such as with Luxaire and Make-A-Wish Foundation in Chicago.

Gustave A. Larson expanded its geographic reach in 2009 when it bought Indiana-based HVAC distributor Indiana Supply. The deal added three branches in South Bend, Fort Wayne, and Indianapolis, Indiana. The acquisition was in line with Larson's long-term growth strategy.

The company was founded in 1936 by Gustave Larson, a Swedish immigrant.
