Put on your hard hat. Allied Construction Products offers machinery and other products to companies in the construction and mining industries. It has more than 200 distributors located throughout the US and Canada which provide equipment such as excavators, loader/backhoes, skid-steers and mini-excavator mounted attachments, stationary breaking and material handling setups, and underground piercing tools. The company also offers reconditioned equipment and support services including product manuals, a certified rebuild program and a technical service group to answer questions.
