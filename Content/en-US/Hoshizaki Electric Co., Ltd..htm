Hoshizaki Electric likes to keep things cool. Producer of Japan's first ice maker, it makes foodservice equipment, such as ice makers, dishwashers, refrigerators, freezers, and beverage dispensers. The company has production plants in Asia (about a dozen in China), Asia/Pacific, North America, and Europe, selling products in 60-plus countries worldwide. Formed in 1947, Hoshizaki Electric boasts 450 sales branches across Japan. Lancer Corp , a maker of beverage dispensing machines, is Hoshizaki Electric's American subsidiary, which expanded in 2013 by buying Brazilian foodservice equipment maker Aços Macom Indústria E Comércio. Other subsidiaries operate in Europe, Singapore, and China.
