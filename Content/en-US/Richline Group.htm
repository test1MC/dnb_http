The Richline Group has a future cast in gold as a top maker of gold jewelry. The company, established in 2007 and owned by Berkshire Hathaway , manufactures and distributes necklaces, bracelets, earrings, charms, pendants, and rings in gold, ranging from 10 to 18 karats. It also makes freshwater cultured pearls in the US under the Honora brand. The company's brands include AuraGem (precious and semi-precious gemstone jewelry) and Leach & Garner (offering nickel-free hypo-allergenic pieces). Its other brand names include Charmed Memories, EternaGold, and Marie Claire through a license. Products are sold through thousands of jewelry retailers, including Hannoush , Misiak Jewelers, and Touch of Gold.

Richline operates in the US, Italy, Bolivia, the Dominican Republic, South Africa, and the Far East.

Richline was formed in 2007 through the merger of Bel-Oro International and Aurafin. Richline Group operates through four independent strategic business units: Leach & Garner, Richline Brands, Rio Grande, and Inverness Corporation. Consumer brands include Honora, Charmed Memories, EternaGold, and others.

The company markets and distributes its products to key retailers through multiple global channels, such as National Retail Jewelers, Guild Jewelers, department stores, television and electronic shopping networks, mass merchandisers, and wholesalers. Previously named Finecraft, Richline Canada makes and distributes precious metal jewelry to major retailers throughout Canada while Richline South Africa serves the European community.

Richline is extending its reach through licensing agreements. In 2013 it inked a deal with PrivateCollection & Co. to make, market, and distribute jewelry in the US and Canada under the Marie Claire brand, which has a strong presence in both Asia and Australia in fashion, beauty, and home decor (generating some $350 million in sales annually).

It's also expanding geographically by acquiring South African Manufacturing Jewelers in 2012 and renaming it Richline SA (Pty) Ltd. It entered an agreement to purchase US Cookson Material Products Group, based in the New England area.

To expand its portfolio of products, Richline in 2013 bought freshwater cultured pearl brand Honora in the US, as well as the Rio Grande jewelry brand. Boosting its presence in Europe, Richline acquired the assets of Italian jeweler Rosato in 2011.
