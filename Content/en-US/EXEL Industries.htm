EXEL Industries cautions you to say it and spray it. EXEL, formerly known as Groupe Exel, manufactures and distributes an array of sprayers used to protect plants in commercial farming and consumer gardening, as well as materials in industrial processing. The company's sprayers range from handheld tools to ones mounted on wheels with a compressor powered by a 2.5 horsepower Honda motor! Among EXEL's multiple brands are Tecnoma, Hardi, and Berthoud. The company also carries Kremlin-branded spray guns for applying paint, adhesives, and other surface finishes. EXEL subsidiaries dot more than 20 countries, driving revenue directly and through a sales network. Top customers include Renault , Caterpillar , IKEA , Valeo .
