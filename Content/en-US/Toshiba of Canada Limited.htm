Toshiba of Canada provides a wide array of electronic products. The subsidiary of Toshiba operates four product groups: Consumer Electronics (TVs, VCRs, DVD players and recorders, digital audio players), Information Systems (handheld, notebook, and tablet computers), Office Products (copiers, fax machines, projectors, business telephone systems), and Medical Systems (CT scanners, MRI systems, X-ray systems). Toshiba of Canada, which sells to customers in the consumer, education, enterprise, government, and health care markets, maintains sales offices throughout Canada. The company's Toshiba Business Solutions subsidiary sells and services a variety of office products, directly and through independent dealers.

In 2016, Canada Cartage, an asset-based fleet outsourcing provider, agreed to use Toshiba's Digital Office Solutions products throughout the company. In 2015, Toshiba partnered with Sleeman Breweries to help streamline the logistics planning process with its Digital Signage product.

Toshiba of Canada has regional offices in Vancouver, Calgary, Edmonton, Regina, Saskatoon, Ottawa, Quebec City, and Montreal.
