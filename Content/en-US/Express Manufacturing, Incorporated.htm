Express Manufacturing (EMI) provides contract electronics manufacturing services, including printed circuit board (PCB) design, production, and prototyping. The company also offers value-added services, such as materials procurement, inventory management, test engineering, and quality inspection. Customers have included Kingston Technology , Lantronix , Silicon Film Technologies, and Xerox . EMI serves industrial markets, including medical, telecom, aerospace, semiconductor, irrigation, and commercial gaming, to name a few. EMI also operates a factory in China through its EMI Asia Limited. Express Manufacturing was established in 1983 and is owned by members of the Chin family, its founders.

EMI offers turnkey assignments in which it handles a manufacturing job from start to finish as well as consignments, in which it takes care of specific manufacturing or testing tasks for clients. The company provides specialized Engineering Services (such as printed circuit board layout, proto-typing and test development) to Supply Chain Management, Manufacturing, Fulfillment Programs, and Service and Refurbishment Depot Services. The company's customers are in telecommunications, networking, medical, industrial, gambling, irrigation, and radio-frequency identification.

Headquartered in California, EMI has an office in Hong Kong and a manufacturing plant in Dongguan City, China. EMI has customers in Australia, New Zealand, and Israel, as well as regions including North and South America, Europe, and Asia.

The company follows its "strategic alliance program" to find suitable partners. The company is partnered with Beyonics Technology and Corelis (analysis and embedded test tools).

In 2014, EMI won AS9100C 2009 certification, which meets quality standards of the aerospace industry.

The company has also expanded its Dongguan City plant, adding a Chip Mounter CP-65, IC Mounter XP243 from Fuji and Lead Tracer System LTR1000 from RMD Instruments to support customer demand. EMI operates 9 Fuji surface mount technology lines in its China facility.
