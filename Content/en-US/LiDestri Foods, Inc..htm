Ladle it on, LiDestri Foods says. The company manufactures Italian pasta sauces under the Francesco Rinaldi label. They include tomato sauces (such as traditional marinara, meat-flavored, and chunky garden-style), Alfredo sauces (traditional, vodka, and sundried tomato), and ToBe Healthy sauces with Omega 3's. The company also produces olive oils and cooking wines. Products under the Francesco Rinaldi name are sold in US retail food outlets and online. LiDestri Foods also offers its customers private-label manufacturing services. The company, which is today owned and operated by the LiDestri family, has ties to the Ragu packing company founded in 1937.

Headquartered in the Rochester suburb of Fairport, New York, LiDestri Foods operates manufacturing facilities in New York, California, and New Jersey.

LiDestri Foods is one of the largest co-packers of pasta sauce in America. Specializing in sauces, dips, and salsas, LiDestri Foods boasts multiple manufacturing locations nationwide that combined produce more than 2 million jars of sauces and dips daily.

While it makes the Newman's Own line under private label, LiDestri Foods' own brands include Francesco Rinaldi and Santa Fe Chili Co. Additionally, it's a contract manufacturer for Frito-Lay , Barilla , and Nestle , the largest food company in the world, as well as for supermarket chain stores Wegman's , Safeway , and Albertsons .

For growth, LiDestri Foods is looking at libations to fuel its bottom line. It operates a spirits business and moved into Eastman Kodak 's former film-finishing building to make spaghetti sauce on one side and alcoholic beverages on the other. In mid-2012 the food company was granted a trademark for LiDestri Spirits. One of its agreements involves bottling Hard Rock vodka, distributed by Southern Glazer's Wine and Spirits .

LiDestri Foods has since invested in expansion plans at the former Eastman Kodak building. In 2013 it spent $1.4 million for more land in the Eastman Business Park, closing on eight properties at the park that total 180 acres. Already at the park, LiDestri operates a research and development-focused Innovation Center, a production line for smaller run sizes, and a line of high-proof spirits.

It has worked to expand its relationship with Campus Solutions by offering Spikes Salsa to college campuses across the Northeast. College students are one of its target demographics, particularly for its salsas.

Founded as Cantisano Foods by Ralph Cantisano and John LiDestri in 1970 when Cantisano sold Ragu, the LiDestri family took over ownership and management in 1998. The company officially changed its name to LiDestri Foods in 2002.
