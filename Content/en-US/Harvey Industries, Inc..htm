At Harvey Industries the window of opportunity is always open. The company, also known as Harvey Building Products, designs and manufactures its own line of vinyl and wood windows, as well as storm windows, doors, and patio enclosures. It also distributes brand-name exterior building products (including vinyl siding and roofing materials) from manufacturers the likes of CertainTeed , Alcoa Home Exteriors , and James Hardie . Harvey Industries operates nearly 15 showrooms and more than 30 warehouses in the Northeast that mainly serve professional contractors and builders. Co-founders Fred Bigony and Bob Morrison control the company, which was incorporated in 1961.

Harvey has manufacturing facilities in Massachusetts and New Hampshire. Its more than 30 warehouses, which include 14 showrooms, are located in half a dozen New England states, as well as in New Jersey, New York, and Pennsylvania.

The company serves professional contractors and builders.

Rather than retailing items directly to consumers, Harvey distributes a full line of wholesale building products to contractors and builders through its branch locations. It distributes products from partner companies and products that it makes itself. Partner suppliers include CertainTeed , Arconic , James Hardie , Tamko , GAF/Elk, Therma-Tru , Andersen, Velux , and AZEK, among others.

In recent years the company has augmented its product sales with marketing support to help its customers sell better.
