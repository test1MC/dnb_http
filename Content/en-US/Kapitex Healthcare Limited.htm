You may not give much thought to your throat, but some patients dealing with various forms of head or neck cancer have to. Kapitex Healthcare produces medical devices that are designed to help such cancer patients with airway protection and the management of laryngectomy and tracheostomy procedures. The company also sells tracheostomy tubes and equipment, specialized products for those suffering from dysphagia (difficulty in swallowing), as well as communication aid devices. the company's products are marketed under about ten brand names, including Amplicord, Trachi-Naze, and Voiceline.

Kapitex Healthcare's products are sold across Africa, Australia and New Zealand, Europe, North and South America, Russia, Asia and the Middle East.

The company is specialist in laryngectomy, tracheostomy and airway management, dysphagia, and oral motor rehabilitation.

Kapitex Healthcare concentrates on the design, marketing, and distribution of its products. In 2014 the company was stocking Tracoe Liquid Paraffin lubricating fluid for Tracoe Comfort Inner tubes.

Kapitex Healthcare was founded in 1991.
