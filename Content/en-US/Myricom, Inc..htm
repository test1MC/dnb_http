Myricom provides interfaces, switches, and components that are used to interconnect clusters of workstations, PCs, and servers. The company's Myrinet products are used to enable high-performance computing clusters intended to bolster the processing power of networked computers. Myricom supplies Myrinet cluster products to a number of computer manufacturers such as Dell , Hewlett-Packard , IBM , and Sun Microsystems . The company also develops and supplies other standard computer networking products. Myricom was established in 1994. Robert Noland, the company's chairman, is the majority shareholder in Myricom.
