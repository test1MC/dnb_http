For everyone who wants to try it before they buy it, Flexpaq Corporation offers a solution. The company manufactures samples for cosmetic, fragrance, and personal care products. Services include compatibility testing, package design, printing, and assembly. In addition to samples, Flexpaq also offers contract filling services for bottles, jars, tubes, and vials. The company has production plants in the US, Poland, China, and France. A subsidiary of the French company Ileos Group, Flexpaq has many ties to Europe, including a partnership with Seufert in Germany, which makes transparent packaging. Its printed packaging is handled by New Jersey affiliate Flexprint.
