Viridas gave up its textile business in order to help stop global warming. Helping to replace fossil fuels, the company specializes in supplying Crude Jatropha Oil to the biodiesel industry, focusing on the South American, European, and US markets. Its Viridas Brazil Agronegocios subsidiary has developed a trial plantation for producing the oil in Belo Horizonte, and is working on implementing an industrial-scale plantation in Brazil's northeastern region. Other subsidiaries include Viridas Trading Ltd. and Viridas Enterprises Brazil Ltd., both in the UK, and Viridas GmbH, in Germany.  The company also has strategic partnerships with INEOS Enterprise and Cazenave y Asociados SA.
