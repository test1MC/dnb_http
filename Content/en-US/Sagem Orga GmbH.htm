Sagem Orga develops smart cards and terminals used by banks, health care providers, telecommunications companies, and other organizations requiring secure identification systems. The company provides hardware, software, support, and consulting services to help clients implement biometric and electronic identification, electronic ID cards, health cards, drivers' licenses, and cashless payment systems. The company took its current form when Sagem Securité, a subsidiary of SAFRAN , acquired ORGA Kartensysteme and merged it with its own smart card operations. Sagem Orga employs more than 2,000 people in 17 countries.
