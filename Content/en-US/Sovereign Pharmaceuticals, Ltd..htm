Sovereign Pharmaceuticals is holding its own in the drugmaker wars. The company arms its customers with made-to-order OTC and prescription medications that combat ailments such as gastrointestinal, respiratory, and cold symptoms. Its manufacturing plant has the capacity to make more than 3 million pints of liquid and more than 650 million tablets a year. Other capabilities include the manufacture of creams, ointments, and suspensions. In addition, the company offers custom packaging and research and development services. Sovereign Pharmaceuticals was founded in 1990.
