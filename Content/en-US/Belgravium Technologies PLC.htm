With an Old World-sounding name, Belgravium Technologies provides futuristic technology. The company specializes in mobile computing and radio frequency data communications (RF/DC) technology. Belgravium Technologies operates through two subsidiaries, Belgravium Limited and Touchstar Technologies, to design and manufacture mobile computing and radio frequency data communications (RF/DC) technology. Products include barcode printers and scanners and vehicle tracking and routing systems. Customers include BP , Ceva Logistics , Tata Steel , and TOTAL . Founded in 1982 as an engineering company, Belgravium began developing radio data communications systems in 1987.
