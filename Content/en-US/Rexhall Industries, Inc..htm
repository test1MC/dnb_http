Rexhall Industries might not be the king of the recreational vehicle industry, but the company does its best to make its customers feel like royalty. The company makes gasoline and diesel Class A motor homes branded Aerbus, RexAir, and RoseAir. All models include kitchen, bathroom, dining, and bedroom areas, as well as TVs, VCRs, microwave ovens, and stereos; some are equipped with the company's patented TREX slide rooms. Various models offer storage compartments for motorcycles, back-up cameras with monitors, and fireplaces. Rexhall sells its motor homes through dealers in the US, Canada, and Europe. The company filed for Chapter 11 protection from creditors in March 2009.
