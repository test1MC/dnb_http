Dunfermline Press Group is holding a firm line as an independent regional newspaper publisher. The company runs about 30 newspapers in Scotland and Ireland, most of which boast paid circulation. The company's flagship newspaper is Scottish weekly The Dunfermline Press . It is also the parent company of Clyde & Forth Press . Headed by chief executive Deidre Romanes, Dunfermline Press Group has an aggressive acquisition strategy; most notable were its 2007 purchase of Berkshire Regional Newspapers from Trinity Mirror , which added 14 new titles to the publishing house, and its 2005 acquisition of Your Radio FM, the group's first move beyond newspapers. The Romanes family founded the company in 1859.
