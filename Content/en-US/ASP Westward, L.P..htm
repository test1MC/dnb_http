ASP Westward publishes more than 60 community newspapers in Texas and Colorado. Its Houston Community Newspapers offers two dailies and 32 weeklies, including the Greater Houston Weekly , the Pearland Journal , and the Katy Sun, distributed to towns in the metropolitan area. Colorado Community Newspapers publishes a dozen weekly papers in the Denver and Colorado Springs area, and East Texas Community Newspapers offers 15 weekly and twice-weekly papers for a rural area. ASP Westward also maintains Web sites for many of its newspapers. Investment firm American Securities Capital Partners owns the company.
