Coastline International provides contract manufacturing services for customers seeking to cut costs. The company operates two manufacturing facilities in Tijuana, Mexico, with a combined 35,000 sq. ft. of space. Goods are distributed through warehousing facilities located just across the border in San Diego. In addition to overseeing the distribution to customers, Coastline International handles all raw material transportation. The company, founded in 1981, provides services for customers in the electronics, medical, and textile industries. Larry Angione, Coastline International's founder and president, owns the company.
