If you're looking for one of Japan's largest automotive manufacturers, please see Nissan Motor ; for ramen noodles, refer to Nissin Food Products . Nissin Electric makes electrical power equipment, including gas circuit breakers, gas-insulated switchgear, power distribution equipment, and power transformers. The company, established in 1910, also makes photovoltaic arrays and power converters for solar power installations. While power systems account for most of sales, Nissin is also a leader in ion implantation equipment for manufacturing semiconductors and LCDs. Most of the company's sales are in Japan, although Nissin has been growing its exports.
