The good burghers of Britain looking for a cozy cup of java can turn to the Coburg Group. The group operates two coffee firms in the UK: Coburg Coffee Company and CK Coffee (dba Cafe d'Or Coffee Services). Coburg Coffee has been roasting beans in London for more than four decades, producing fresh batches of private-label coffee to businesses throughout the UK and abroad. It also roasts the Cafe d'Or, Rizzi, and Langdons blends for sister firm Cafe d'Or, which supplies coffee, espresso, tea, and related beverage products. Cafe d'Or's customers include restaurants, cafes, hotels, and other foodservice providers, as well as offices.
