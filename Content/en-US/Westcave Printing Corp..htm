Westcave Printing, doing business as Horizon Printing, provides commercial printing services in the central Texas region. The company meets full-service printing needs specializing in graphic design, electronic prepress, bindery services, and mailing and fulfillment. Clients include associations, publishing companies, manufacturing and service firms, as well as agencies and print resellers. The printing company was founded in 1991 and in that time has grown from eight employees to over 40.
