Mikuni American manufactures a wide range of products -- from carburetors to electrolyzed vitamin C sprayers. The US arm of Tokyo-based Mikuni Corp., the company makes performance carburetors for motorcycles, snowmobiles, and watercraft. Other combustion engine parts include intake manifolds, oil pumps, fuel pumps, and throttle bodies. Among other products, Mikuni American makes humidifiers, negative ion air generators, and other sprayers, and sells clay made from silver and gold that can be used to make jewelry. Working with its parent company, Mikuni American also distributes US-made components to the Japanese aerospace industry.

Mikuni American is based in Northridge, California, and has other offices in Chicago; and McAllen, Texas.

The company's service parts are supplied by dealers which include Branch O'Keefe, Custom Chrome, Drag Specialties, Parts Unlimited, and Hot Products. Mikuni American sells through US and Canadian distributors spanning North America.
