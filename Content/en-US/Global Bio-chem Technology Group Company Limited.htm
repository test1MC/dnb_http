Global Bio-chem Technology believes in the power of the corn kernel. The company makes corn-refined and corn-based biochemical products. It has three business units: Amino acids (including lysine, threonine, and tryptophan); Polyol chemicals (including polyol chemicals, hydrogen); and Corn sweeteners (including glucose, maltose and dextrin). Global Bio-chem is the largest producer of lysine (in the fermentation industry) as well as the largest corn refiner and leading corn sweetener manufacturer in China. Its products are primarily used in food and beverages, pharmaceutical products, cosmetics, textiles, and paper. To boost sales outside of China, the firm has a partnership deal with Archer Daniels Midland .
