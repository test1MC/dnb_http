Toagosei Co. is active in both organic and inorganic chemicals. Its largest business segments produce acrylic acids and esters (used in fibers, rubber, and paper processors) and chloralkali chemicals, including caustic soda and chlorine. Toagosei is a major producer of plastic products, such as PVC pipe and couplings and other inorganic chemicals the likes of sulfuric acid and industrial gases for medical use. With a presence in Japan, Singapore, China, Taiwan, South Korea, and the US, Toagosei produces instant adhesive Aron Alpha -- better known in the US as Crazy Glue. Toagosei operates a joint venture with Mitsui Chemicals to produce ethylene carbonates for lithium ion batteries.
