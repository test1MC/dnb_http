Icom America, Japan-based Icom Incorporated's largest subsidiary and distributor, manufactures and markets a wide variety of radios and related equipment for amateur, aviation, land mobile, and marine markets. Its land mobile products are designed specifically for industry and business use, as well as for the government. The US Army has tapped Icom for its Soldier Intercom System. Products include ham radios, air band and marine transceivers, hand-held and vehicle-mounted two-way radios, base station equipment, marine radar, digital and analog radios, and communications receivers. Icom America was founded in 1979.
