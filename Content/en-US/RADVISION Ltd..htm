Radvision sees Internet protocol (IP) as a radical improvement. The company's networking unit makes gateway devices that interface between modern packet and traditional circuit-based networks, gateways that manage communications on packet networks, and network bridges that enable voice or multimedia conferencing. Its technology segment designs software development kits for other communication products vendors. Radvision sells to systems integrators, resellers, and manufacturers, such as Cisco (39% of sales) and Sony . The company gets the majority of its sales in the Americas. Radvision is part of Israel's RAD Group of networking and telecommunications businesses. The company was acquired by Avaya in 2012.
