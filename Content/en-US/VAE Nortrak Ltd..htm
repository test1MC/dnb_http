Voestalpine Nortrak, a subsidiary of Austrian railway manufacturer VAE AG , tries to keep North American trains on track. The company makes railroad trackwork and supplies. Trackwork covers devices that railroads use to transport trains from one track to another. Nortrak's products, many under the Racor brand name, include rails, anchors, guard rails, joint bars, and switches for railroads and transit rail and streetcar systems. It operates through locations in Canada, Mexico, and the US. The company was founded in 1981.
