Was that Barbie and her new beau taking in the sights around London? Chances are, it was. Just ask Mattel UK, which is the British marketing and distribution arm of global toy maker, Mattel . The company's brands include Barbie, of course, Hot Wheels and Matchbox cars, Cabbage Patch Kids, and Fisher-Price toys. Mattel also makes a variety of playthings based on licensed characters such as Harry Potter and Yu-Gi-Oh! . For years, Barbie was queen of the UK, however, her popularity had been waning. To this end, Mattel has tapped the collectibles market to breathe new life into Barbie brand with the addition of a Lady Gaga lookalike, among others.

The company operates in the UK.

Europe accounted for 22% of parent Mattel 's total revenues in 2015.
