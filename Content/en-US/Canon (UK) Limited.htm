Canon UK provides sales and marketing functions for its Japanese parent in the UK and Ireland. The company's operations are divided into two main units: business solutions provides printing and imaging equipment, supplies, and software for the office and professional markets; consumer imaging supplies digital still and video cameras, fax machines, printers, projectors, and scanners. As an offshoot of Canon UK's main businesses, the broadcast division supplys lenses and related equipment for the television broadcasting industry. Canon UK also provides IT consulting and process services. The company has offices in England, Scotland, and Northern Ireland. Canon established its UK operations in 1976.
