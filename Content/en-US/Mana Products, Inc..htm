The chemists at Mana Products work to help you put your best face forward. The company is a leading contract and private-label maker of cosmetics, skin care, and hair care products, serving both the prestige and mass markets. Mana develops, manufactures, and packages these items for the nation's top beauty brands. As part of its business it provides forecasting and consulting services to clients to develop products for the fickle, trend-driven beauty industry. In addition to its private-label business, Mana makes hair care products under the Profound Beauty name. Founded in 1975 by president Nikos Mouyiaris, Mana manufactures its products at two plants in the US and with partners in Asia, including China.

Based in New York, Mana enjoys a global reach.

Mana maintains a pair of manufacturing plants in the US; through partnerships in Asia, the cosmetics company boasts production capabilities abroad as well.

Primarily, Mana is a contract manufacturer for other top beauty companies. It makes its own Profound Beauty-branded hair care items, which are marketed through its own distributed network.

As part of its business, Mana sells its cosmetics, skin care, and hair care items through multiple channels, such as prestige and mass markets. It also produces cosmeceutical, spa, and OTC products.
