Got the flue? Selkirk does. The company sells chimney, venting, and fireplace products such as gas vents, pellet vents, smoke pipe, and flexible chimney liners. Selkirk has manufacturing facilities in the US, Canada, and Mexico. It  sells its products under the brand names AirMate, MetalBest, Max! Fireplaces, SuperPro, and  SuperVent. Customers include commercial and residential heating, ventilation, and air conditioning distributors and retail outlets. British manufacturer Tomkins owns Selkirk. Declining sales drove Tomkins to consolidate Selkirk with sister company Hart & Cooley in 2008.
