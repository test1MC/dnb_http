Chinese cobbler Yue Yuen Industrial has some mighty big shoe orders to fill. The world's largest manufacturer of branded footwear turns out shoes (313.4 million pairs per year) for the biggest names in sports: adidas , ASICS , New Balance , Nike , Puma , Reebok , and Timberland . Its manufacturing centers are located in China, Indonesia, and Vietnam. In addition to these, the company operates about 3,665 athletic footwear and apparel stores directly or through subsidiaries, as well more than 2,260 stores under joint ventures. Founded in 1988, Yue Yuen is majority owned by Taiwanese athletic material maker Pou Chen . The firm spun off its retail unit, Pou Sheng International (Holdings) in a 2008 IPO.
