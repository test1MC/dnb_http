As a major manufacturer of private-label disposable diapers in the UK and the US, Arquest's bottom line is nicely covered. In addition to diapers, Arquest makes training pants, swim pants for infants, and other specialty products. Its retail customers include mass merchandisers such as Wal-Mart and J Sainsbury . The company, founded in 1991 as a spinoff from Johnson & Johnson , is among the top private labelers of diapers in the world and one of the largest suppliers of diapers under private label in North America. Arquest conducts its own R&D (including market and industry research) and boasts a licensing partnership with Scholastic Entertainment , which owns the Clifford the Big Red Dog license.
