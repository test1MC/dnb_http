No ulterior motives here -- Ultera Systems designs and manufactures data storage array controllers. The company sells its products to computer hardware vendors, resellers, and systems integrators. Its products are targeted at enterprises needing more, easier-to-access storage to comply with the requirements of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act and other federal laws and regulations. Ultera sells its products under the trade names such as Striper, LibraryMaster, and Imager. The company was established in 1994 by Mo Nourmohamadian and Cindy Karch.
