Intense is far from lackadaisical about its work. The company designs and manufactures laser diodes for use in a variety of applications, including military hardware, commercial printing equipment, bar coding, and TV displays. Intense uses a technique called quantum well intermixing to combine various optical functions onto a single chip. The company, which was founded in 2000, makes lasers in infra-red, visible, bar and stacked arrays, and individually addressable arrays. It fabricates its chips from compound materials, such as gallium arsenide and indium phosphide. Intense also provides design services in electronics, lasers, optics, and packaging. The company is backed by ORIX USA 's Venture Finance team.
