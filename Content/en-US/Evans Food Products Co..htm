Evans Food Products has something to oink about. The company manufactures pork rinds and pork cracklings under the Macs, La Tonita, Bills, and Porkie brand names. Its products are available at retailers worldwide. The company also provides private-label production and packing, and supplies pork pellets in bulk to companies wishing to pop and package their own pork rinds and pork cracklings. Evans Food's US operations are located in California, Illinois, Ohio, and Texas; overseas, it also has a plant in Mexico. The company markets its products primarily throughout North, Central, and South America.
