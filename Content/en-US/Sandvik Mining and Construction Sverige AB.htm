If you've got to move a mountain, Sandvik Mining and Construction's the one you need to call. The company is one of three business segments of Swedish industrial heavy Sandvik AB ; it generates about 45% of its parent's annual revenues. The unit manufactures, distributes, and services all manner of mining and construction equipment used for mineral and rock exploration and processing. Products include equipment for drilling and mechanical mining, as well as loading and transporting, crushing/sorting, demolition, and recycling. The unit is comprised of three segments: Underground Mining, Surface Mining, and Construction. The US, Finland, Sweden, and Austria are the segment's largest development units.
