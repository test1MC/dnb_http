Hewlett-Packard Singapore is the headquarters for the Asia/Pacific business of US-based HP Inc . The subsidiary is home to HP's head sales office in the region and it coordinates logistics for other HP businesses in Asia. HP Singapore also oversees research and development for HP's mobile and inkjet printer product lines. Its other operations include manufacturing core HP products including PCs and imaging and printing products. The company has counted Singapore's Ministry of Education among its customers. HP Singapore became part of HP Inc. when Hewlett-Packard split into two companies in 2015. Hewlett-Packard Enterprise is the other company.
