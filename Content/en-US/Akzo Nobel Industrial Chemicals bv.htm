A subsidiary of Akzo Nobel , the world's largest paint manufacturer, Akzo Nobel Industrial Chemicals manufactures and supplies chlor-alkali products (including chlorine), salt, and derivatives such as monochloroacetic acid to the glass, detergent, construction, plastic, and pulp and paper industries. It also produces water treatment chemicals. The company operates joint ventures with energy companies, including cogeneration facilities. Industrial Chemicals operates facilities in Asia, Europe, and North America, which manufacture vacuum salt (mining salt through vacuum extraction), chlorine, caustic lye, hydrochloric acid, and metal salts.
