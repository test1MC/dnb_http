Fletcher Building is a supplier to many a Kiwi home. The company makes and distributes concrete, steel, fiberglass insulation, roofing, cranes, and panel products for the residential and commercial construction markets worldwide. It runs more than 45 businesses through five divisions: building products, distribution, infrastructure, laminates and panels, and construction. Included in its network are AHI Group (the world's largest maker of stone chip-coated metal roof tiles), The Laminex Group (top producer of decorative surfaces in Australia and New Zealand), and Formica Corp. (maker of the eponymous high-pressure laminate products). Fletcher's operations span North America, Europe, and the Asia/Pacific region.
