Icefresh Foods offers really cool products. The company supplies private-label frozen foods mainly in two categories -- frozen desserts and ice cream. It works with a number of mostly Belgium, French, German, Dutch, and Finnish food manufacturers, which it refers to as Principals, who produce its products. These manufacturers include Belgian Icecream, Glacio, Quality Pastries, and Poppies International. Icefresh's customers include retail grocery, foodservice, and food manufacturing companies in the UK. Icefresh Foods was founded in 1983 to import frozen vegetables from Spain and ice lollies from the Netherlands.

Icefresh sells its products to the retail and foodservice industry, to instore bakery sectors, and to sales personnel. It markets, sells, and distributes its own food products and currently develops and supplies over 250,000 units every single week.

The company continuously invests in market data and regularly conducts retail field visits to help anticipate future trends to ensure it remains ahead of the competition.
