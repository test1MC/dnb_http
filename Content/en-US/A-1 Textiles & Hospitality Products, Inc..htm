Given its line of work, the last thing A-1 Textiles needs is similarly named A1 steak sauce mussing up its clean linens. A-1 Textiles & Hospitality Products, a linen distribution company, provides hotels, motels, and athletic clubs with towels, bath robes, rugs, bed linens, blankets, and pillows. Founded in the 1970s, the company boasts a vast products portfolio and distributes its products catalog nationwide. A-1 Textiles operates a handful of regional warehouses located in Los Angeles, Seattle, Dallas, Fort Wayne, and Charlotte, North Carolina.
