Small chips are a safe bet for Chipita America. Formerly Nonni's Food Company, it makes and markets Italian-style (savory) panetini and risotto chips, New York Style-brand bagel crisps and pita chips, Melba toast, and flatbreads under the London labels, as well as sweet-filled croissants under the 7Days label. They're distributed nationwide in such major grocery, office supply, and warehouse club stores as Albertsons , Kroger , Staples , Sam's Club , and Costco . Curbing its sweet tooth, the company sold Nonni's Biscotti unit to Wind Point Partners in 2011 and it's selling its New York Style and Old London brands to B&G Foods in 2012. Chipita America is part of Vivartia , one of Greece's top food companies.
