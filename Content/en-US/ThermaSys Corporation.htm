ThermaSys exchanges hot for cold and vice versa. The company makes seam-welded aluminum and copper/brass tubing and manifolds, and other heat exchanger products, such as radiators, oil coolers, and condensers, for the automotive, construction machinery, industrial equipment, and power generation industries. Divisions include Thermal Transfer Products, ThermaSys Heat Exchangers, and General ThermoDynamics for heat exchangers, and ThermaSys Tubing and ARUP for tubing products. Founded in 1971, ThermaSys is a portfolio company of private investment firm Wellspring Capital Management . It works in tandem with API Heat Transfer, another firm Wellspring owns that makes heat-transfer technologies.
