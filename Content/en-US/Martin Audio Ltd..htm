Martin Audio makes professional touring audio speaker systems, catering to customers ranging from rock 'n' roll supergroups to rockers who want to be heard by thousands of people. Martin Audio also installs sound systems for use in hotels, places of worship, pubs, restaurants, retail outlets, sporting arenas, and theme parks, among other locations. It has also installed cinema sound, providing surround-sound amplification for movie theaters. In addition, Martin Audio offers individual electronic components for audiophiles who want to pump up the volume at home. The company sells its products across Europe, the US, Australia, and the Far East through a network of 40 distribution partners.
