The voestalpine Austria Draht is the largest producer of wire rod in Austria, with a tradition dating back to 1892. voestalpine Austria Draht manufactures steel wire rods that it uses to make weld fillers, chains, steel cord, springs, pre-stressing steel, and concrete steel products. The company also provides treatments in pickling and annealing. voestalpine Austria Draht is a part of the voestalpine group. It gets its raw material from the group’s integrated steelworks. The company manages a rolling mill in Donawitz as well as treatment plants. The company’s latest, fully computer-aided wire mill, began operation in 1979. The plant has produced 10 million metric tons of wire rod since it started production.
