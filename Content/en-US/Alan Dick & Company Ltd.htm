Alan Dick & Company (AlanDick) designs, manufactures, and installs wireless telecommunications network towers and other communications infrastructure equipment. The company's products include cellular antennas, equipment shelters, radar systems, and signal amplifiers and repeaters. AlanDick also provides consulting, engineering, installation, network planning, and testing services. Aside from the telecom industry, the company's clients come a range of disciplines including broadcasting, energy, and transportation. Government agencies also call on AlanDick to supply systems for military, security, and weather applications. The company was established in 1971 and operates from offiices on five continents.
