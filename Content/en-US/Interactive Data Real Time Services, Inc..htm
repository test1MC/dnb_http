Interactive Data Real Time Services, formerly ComStock, provides real-time financial market data to more than 1,200 banks, brokerages, hedge funds, and money management firms. Its PlusFeed data service combines news and information from more than 450 worldwide sources. The company's Managed Solutions business is a provider of customized financial information systems. It builds and manages Web-based financial market data portals and terminals for clients, and includes interactive features such as charts, search functions, and decision-support tools and analytics. Interactive Data Real Time Services is a subsidiary of financial information provider Interactive Data Corporation .
