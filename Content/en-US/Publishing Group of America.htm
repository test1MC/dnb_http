Hometown life is the bread and butter for Publisher's Group of America (PGA). The company publishes American Profile , a weekly supplement magazine covering "hometown American life" that is distributed in newspapers across the country, with a circulation of about 10 million readers. In addition, PGA's Hometown Content service provides syndicated content such as crossword puzzles, weather forecasts, and movie reviews to community publishers. Other PGA products include newspaper insert Relish , which covers food-related topics (circulation 15 million), and health and wellness magazine Spry (9 million). All total, the company serves more than 1,600 community newspapers across the country.
