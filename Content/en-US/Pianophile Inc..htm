Pianophile is wholly dedicated to a double-decade-long love affair with all things piano and their players. The company manufactures and distributes products that keep pianists tickling the ivories: bass strings, instrument parts, tools, supplies, and accessories. Customers include pianos and their technicians, repairers, dealers, and general afficianados. Products include piano covers, refinishing supplies, piano lights, custom made strings, soundboard materials, felts, dampers, pedals, casters, benches, and decals. Distribution is via the company's web site.
