Germans who want the whole Web in their hands need to look a little further than ComBOTS AG (formerly known as WEB.DE). The company previously operated a German Internet portal that featured content such as news, sports, weather, and entertainment listings, though that part of the business was sold off in 2005. ComBOTS subsequently switched focus to its digital communications offering, which combined e-mail, chat, and Internet telephony services. However, that business produced disappointing results and was discontinued in late 2007. The company is undergoing a restructuring, and is evaluating possible new online services.
