The Warrior Group has its work cut out for it, literally. A modular construction and construction management services company, Warrior Group builds permanent modular buildings -- including military dormitories, student housing, and office buildings -- from prefabricated wood and metal components. Its construction management offerings include planning, design, purchasing, engineering, and post-construction services. In recent years, the company has worked on projects for the Veterans Administration in Marion, Illinois; built barracks at Fort Bliss and other military installations; and managed a construction project at the University of North Texas . Warrior Group was founded in 1997 by CEO Gail Warrior-Lawrence.
