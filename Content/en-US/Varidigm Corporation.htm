The paradigm for Varidigm has to do with controlling heat both quietly and efficiently. The specialty manufacturer makes variable speed motor controllers and variable firing rate combustion controls for gas and electric furnaces. Its products includes fan speed, burner, and combustion controllers. Matching heat output to demand enables quieter, more efficient, more closely controlled operation of boilers, fan coil units, fan filter boxes, furnaces, and water heaters. Varidigm's products are used in commercial, industrial, and residential HVAC applications.
