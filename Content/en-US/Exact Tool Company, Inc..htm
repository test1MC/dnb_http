Exact Tool exacts a fair price to get its precision products exactly right to meet its customers' requirements. The company is a contract manufacturer of plastic injection molds, stamping dies, precision stampings, and zinc die castings molds. It produces conventional and four-slide molds and castings alike. Although Exact Tool primarily serves the lock industry, it also produces parts for office products, mailboxes, intercom communications, electrical contacts, and other applications. The company, founded in 1969, stocks more than 30 million stamping parts to provide customers with just-in-time delivery.
