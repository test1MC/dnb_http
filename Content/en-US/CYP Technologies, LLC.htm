CYP Technologies, a joint venture of Davis & Davis Rugs Inc. and Burtco, Inc., was formed in late 2001 when it purchased the patents, trademarks, trade names, and intellectual property belonging to Tapistron International Inc. (which had gone into Chapter 11 protection from creditors for the second time) for the purpose of making computerized yarn-placement machinery. CYP Technologies primarily sells to customers in Japan and stateside in Dalton, Georgia.
