'Tis a sweet company. Donegal Creameries is one of Ireland's biggest food and agriculture businesses. The company is involved in the production of  milk and milk products. In the ag sector, it grows mushrooms and provides animal foods, sells seed potatoes and mushroom compost. The company and its subsidiaries have operations in the Republic of Ireland, Northern Ireland, England, and Scotland. It also owns 76% interest in Irish candy company, Oatfield Confectionery, as well as properties, including The Grianan Estate, one of the largest farms in Ireland.
