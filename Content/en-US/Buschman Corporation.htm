Buschman makes wire-wound rods used in coating applications by the paper and converting industries. The company controls all stages of manufacturing for its products, which include wire-wound metering rods, custom-engineered grooved metering and sizing rods, roll-formed rods, smoothing rods, holders, headbox sheets, and tapes for paper machinery and coating applications, as well as customized rods for specific machines. It also provides spare parts and accessories. Customers include 3M , Avery Dennison , Cascades , Hewlett Packard , RockTenn CP , and Temple Inland , among others. The company operates through representatives and distributors located worldwide.
