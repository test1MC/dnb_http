When it comes to ovens, why not Wyott? APW Wyott Foodservice Equipment manufactures an array of foodservice equipment for grab-and-go chains, convenience stores, small eateries, and concession stands. The APW Wyott name brands eight types of equipment: counter top warming and merchandising equipment; char broilers and grills for cooking, conveyors and specialty grills for toasting, foodwells for heating and holding, overhead warmers and holding drawers for space constrained-kitchens, and plate dispensers. Among its customers is major franchise restaurant company Yum! Brands . APW Wyott operates within Standex International Corporation 's largest business segment, Food Service Equipment.
