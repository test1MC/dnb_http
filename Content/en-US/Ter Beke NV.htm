This company has a big slice of the European meat market. Ter Beke is the leading producer of processed meat and fresh prepared meals in Belgium supplying deli meats and other meat products to grocery stores, delicatessens, and butcher shops under the Daniel Coopman and L'Ardennaise brands. Its ready meals operation markets Mediterranean-themed foods such as lasagna, moussaka, and pasta under the Come a casa and Vamos banners, and it produces pizzas under the Pronto brand. Ter Beke has seven processing plants in Belgium, the Netherlands, and France; it sells its products throughout much of Europe. The family of founder Frans Coopman owns about 35% of the company.
