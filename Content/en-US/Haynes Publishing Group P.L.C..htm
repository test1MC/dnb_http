You might not know your intake manifold from your spark plug, but Haynes does. Haynes Publishing Group is best known for its car and motorcycle maintenance manuals (familiarly called "Haynes Manuals") targeting the do-it-yourselfer. For each Haynes Manual, authors strip down and rebuild the subject vehicle to assure the manual's accuracy and ease of use. The company also offers books and DVDs spanning other fields, such as commercial vehicles, tractors, cycling, camping, and aviation. Haynes Publishing has two editorial offices, one in Somerset, England and one near Los Angeles. The company was founded in 1960 by John Haynes. Since its founding, about 150 million Haynes Manuals have sold throughout the world.
