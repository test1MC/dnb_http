The preferred choice of both the nouveau riche and old money aristocracy, many of the most exclusive automobiles in the world are made by Bentley. Bentley's Continental GT costs about $150,000 -- a fair price when one considers the steering wheel's leather stitching takes as many man hours to complete as the building of an entire Ford sedan. Other Bentley models include the Mulsanne, Brooklands, and Continental Flying Spur. The company ceased production of its Azure model in 2010. Bentley has about 170 dealerships worldwide, some 50 of which are in the US. Bentley is owned by Volkswagen .
