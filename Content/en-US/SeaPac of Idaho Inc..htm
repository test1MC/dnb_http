It's not magic beans, it's magic fish. Located in the Magic Valley region of the Gem State, Seapac of Idaho processes seafood into value-added fish products. The company processes farm-raised fish into smoked rainbow trout, trout and salmon jerky, and salmon sausages. It packages and sells its products under the IdaSea brand. Seapac of Idaho's seafood items are available through food distribution companies and at retail grocery stores across the US.
