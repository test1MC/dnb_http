Schnadig Corporation digs customers who desire a house full of furniture. It makes furniture for the living room, dining room, and bedroom, and sells its products through US furniture retailers and internationally. Based in Illinois, Schnadig operates manufacturing plants in California and Mississippi and also runs logistics and warehousing services in Asia through a subsidiary. The family-owned company was founded by namesake Lawrence K. Schnadig in 1952 and is headed by Mr. Schnadig's son-in-law, Donald Belgrad. Schnadig Corporation is owned by investment firm Benefit Capital Cos, which bought the company in 2008. It was announced in January 2009 that Markor International Furniture Co. will acquire Schnadig.
