What do you call a Mexican company that makes ceramic wall and floor tiles, bathroom fixtures, and ceramic adhesives used in the construction industry? Se llama Grupo Lamosa. The company is one of the largest ceramic tile makers with brands including Porcelanite, Lamosa, Firenze, and Itálica. Its bathroom fixtures lines include Ambiance, Lamosa and Vortens. The group distributes its products throughout Mexico and in the US, Central America, and South America. The company is also involved in commercial and residential real estate development in metropolitan Monterrey. Grupo Lamosa was founded as a brickmaker in 2004.
