Ever carried around one of those orange plastic-capped locker keys at the theme park? That's American Locker Group. The company sells and rents coin-, key-, and electronically controlled lockers used by health clubs, amusement parks, ski resorts, bus stations, and employee locker rooms. Customers include SeaWorld , Vail Resorts , Walt Disney World , The UPS Store , and the University of Colorado . Postal mailboxes, such as those used by apartment complexes, make up about a third of sales. Besides the US, American Locker Group serves customers in Canada, Chile, Greece, India, Mexico, and the UK (less than 20% of sales in 2009). The company was founded in 1958.
