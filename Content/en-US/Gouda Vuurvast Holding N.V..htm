This company is hot . Well, at least its products can withstand intense temperatures. Gouda Vuurvast Holding creates, manufactures, and sells refractory materials (bricks and monolithics) used to line ovens, furnaces, kilns, and incinerators. Its construction and services department installs the company's refractory materials around the world. Targeted industries include aluminum, cement, petrochemical, power generation, steel, and waste incineration. Gouda operates foreign subsidiaries in Australia, Belgium, Germany, and the UK. Its sales network spans Africa, Asia, Australia, the Middle East, and North and South America.
