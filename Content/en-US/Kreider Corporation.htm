Metal stampings manufacturer Kreider doesn't crimp on quality. Its processing capabilities include 26 punch presses (which range in capacity from 1.5 tons to 280 tons), two plastisol machines, and other equipment used in making stampings, wire forms, and sub-assemblies. The company's 60,000 sq. ft. manufacturing plant, located on seven acres in Springfield, Ohio. It houses a state-of-the-art tool room which enables the company to also build four-slide tooling, progressive and single action dies, gages, fixtures, and production prototypes. The family-owned company was founded in 1952.
