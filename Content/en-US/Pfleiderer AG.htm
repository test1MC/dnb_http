You may think that Mother Nature engineers wood, but Pfleiderer begs to differ. The German company makes engineered wood products including particle board, medium- and high-density fiberboard, wood panels, and laminate flooring. Pfleiderer sells its products to furniture makers and building supply stores in 80 countries. Germany accounts for about a third of sales. The company makes its wood products through some 20 plants in North America and Europe. In order to restructure and streamline its operations, Pfleiderer sold its former Uniboard subsidiary to Kaycan in 2012 and its former Pergo operations to Mohawk Industries in early 2013. Gustov Pfleiderer founded the enterprise as a timber merchant in 1894.
