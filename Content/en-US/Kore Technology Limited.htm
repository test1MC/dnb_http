At the core of your mass spectrometer, another Kore could be lurking. Kore Technology designs and manufactures custom mass spectrometers and other instrumentation based on its time-of-flight mass spectrometry and ultra-high vacuum devices. The company uses existing modules from previous instruments to design new instruments for specific client applications. Its standard product lines include portable and stationary mass spectrometers and vacuum analysis/processing systems. Formed as independent company in 1992, Kore is controlled by Beijing SDL Technology Company Ltd.

Beijing SDL Technology Company Ltd., a supplier of environmental monitoring and analysis products in China, bought a majority stake in Kore in 2015. SDL sells and supports Kore's mass spectrometer products in China along with SDL's line of environmental and process analyzers.

The key to Kore is its deep bench of designs that it can adapt to the new uses of customers. This reduces research and development as well as manufacturing costs while customizing instruments to the customer's specifications. Kore operates ultra-high vacuum test chambers and a workshop for making metal parts. It also has clean air facilities for assembly and basic equipment for electronic assembly and test, as well as extensive design and mathematical modelling software facilities.

The biggest customers for Kore are research laboratories with specific, specialized needs in their spectrometers. Customers are in health and safety, atmospheric research, forensics and security, materials R&D, and food and beverage.

Kore has reported $2 million in annual revenue.
