Grupo Minsa is the second-largest corn flour producer in Mexico (after Grupo Industrial Maseca ). The company makes four different types of corn flour (or corn masa) for industrial, commercial, and home use -- chip maroon green, organic blue, taco blue, tortilla con aditivo (violet), tortilla sin aditivo under the minsa label (lower-case "m"). Its customers include food retailers, markets, distributors, and wholesalers, as well as supermarket chains and foodservice companies in Mexico and the western and southwestern US. It also supplies the Mexican government with flour for its foodservice operations. Minsa owns and operates four plants in Mexico and two in the US. In 2016 Bunge North America acquired a majority interest in the company.
