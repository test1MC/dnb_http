Carmakers seeking a cure for clunkers call the chain-master, BorgWarner Morse TEC. The BorgWarner subsidiary sets the global standard in making automotive chain systems for integrated engine timing, power transmission, and torque dynamics. Its short list of products includes roller, bushing and silent chains, sprockets, tensioners, and guides. The company supplies oil pumps, too, from gear-type and vane-type pumps for engine and transmission timing. State-of-the-art products, such as its variable cam timing (VCT) tout fuel efficiency, lower emissions, and performance. Customers are a Who's Who of worldwide OEMs -- General Motors , Ford Motor , Chrysler , VW , Honda , as well as automotive aftermarket businesses.
