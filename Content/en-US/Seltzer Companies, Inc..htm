Glanbia Nutritionals (formerly Seltzer Companies) makes the stuff that's in the stuff. It provides specialty chemicals and other ingredients used in nutritional supplements, food, sports beverages, and cosmetics. It sells a full line of amino acids, vitamins, natural and artificial colors, and other specialty ingredients including glucosamine, caffeine powder, and inosine. Glanbia Nutritionals also sells dairy, milk, whey, and flaxseed products and its functional ingredient systems and micronutrient premixes allow customers to include whole sets of ingredients at once. It has plants in the US, Asia, South America, and Europe and customers globally. The company is a subsidiary of Irish food group Glanbia .
