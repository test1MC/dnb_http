Many college students are familiar with the primary pasta products produced by Nissin Foods (USA). The company sells a variety of ramen noodles items under the Top Ramen and Choice Ramen brands. The popular plastic-wrapped, little bundles of pre-fried pasta are known by consumers for their convenience, multiple flavors, and cost-conscious price. Within the company's portfolio of products, Nissin Foods (USA) also offers ready-to-prepare chow mein and cup soups that carry the Souper Meal, Bowl Noodles, Spoon It, Chow Noodles, Big Cup Noodles, and Cup Noodles brand names. Chicken Ramen, the company's first instant ramen noodle product, debuted in 1958. Nissin Foods (USA) was founded by Momofuko Ando in 1948.

California-based Nissin Foods (USA) makes its pasta products from facilities in California and Pennsylvania. The company sells its products worldwide.

Nissin Foods (USA) aims to boost its exports to Canada.

Nissin Foods (USA) is working to make its business operation less susceptible to price competition by refreshing its product line and rolling out new flavors. In 2012 the company launched Top Ramen Cucharealo, a product with Fideo-type noodles available in homestyle flavors. Its Top Ramen Short Cuts launched in 2012, as well. The pre-seasoned, spoonable noodles come in a multi-serving format that allows (for the first time) Nissin Foods (USA) consumers to choose how much of the product to prepare.

Its original and most popular product, Top Ramen, is available in half a dozen varieties. Keeping up with changing food tastes, the company has added to its Oriental-flavored ramen by offering ramen in picante and spicy chili flavors. Originally designed to be prepared on the stove-top, Nissin's foods are now microwave friendly.
