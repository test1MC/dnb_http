Cimetrics likes the symmetry of innovative communication applications and a good return on its investments. The company specializes in tapping into building facilities to retrieve data from operational systems, such as HVAC, lighting, security, and communications, to be transmitted to Cimetrics for analysis. The company bases its communication technology on BACnet (Building Automation and Control networks) standards for automation to enable simple, centralized control (via LAN, WAN, or the Internet) of building systems, with easy access to comprehensive information about every aspect of the systems' operation. Universities and municipalities are major customers.
