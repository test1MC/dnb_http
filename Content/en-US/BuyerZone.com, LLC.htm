Buyers and sellers of business products and services can connect virtually in the BuyerZone. The BuyerZone.com online marketplace makes business transactions easier by providing free multiple competitive price quotes on products and services across more than 150 categories, including construction, financial services, telecom equipment, and human resources. The company's lead generation program refers buyers to suppliers through a network of some 8,500 local and national firms. BuyerZone also offers unbiased, expert purchasing information. The company was founded in 1992 by its president, Mie-Yun Lee. It is a division of information provider Reed Business Information US .
