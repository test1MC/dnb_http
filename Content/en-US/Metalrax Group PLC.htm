Its a case of out of the pan and into the fire for kitchenware company Metalrax Group, who were bought out of administration in 2014, only for the acquiring company to themselves enter administration. The company manufactures, designs, and markets metal and plastic products through two divisions: consumer durables (kitchenware) and specialist engineering (specialty steel products). Consumer durables makes commercial and retail bakeware as well as microwave cookware, wine racks and wooden and plastic housewares. Specialist engineering provides copper coated coil, storage racks and tubing, body hardware, and stairways, as well as specialty steel products for the automotive and off-highway and medical industries. It is currently in liquidation.
