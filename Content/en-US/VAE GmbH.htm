VAE GmbH vies for its share of the global railway market. The company manufactures railway switches and related equipment such as electronic systems, intelligent turnout systems, and special trackwork products. Its products can be found all kinds of rail systems, including high-speed, heavy haul, and commuter trains. The company was founded in 1851 by Earl Hugo Henckel of Donnersmarck. It now has about 35 production sites spanning 15 countries around the world. It is a subsidiary of the voestalpine Bahnsysteme division of Austrian steel company voestalpine .
