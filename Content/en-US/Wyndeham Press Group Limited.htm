From simple leaflets to national magazines, Wyndeham Press Group (WPG) has a handle on all your printing needs. It offers a variety of commercial printing services through about a dozen manufacturing operations in the UK. WPG covers creative design, printing, packaging, finishing, and mailing. The group prints more than 700 magazine titles per month, including Harpers Bazaar , Esquire , and Men's Health . WPG serves media clients such as Financial Times , Bauer Consumer Media , and The Economist Group . It also makes brochures, mail-order catalogs, financial reports, and in-store retail displays. WPG was founded in 1991 and is owned by venture capital firm Walstead Investments.
