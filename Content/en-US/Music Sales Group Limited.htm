You can't stop the music, but you sure can sell it! Music Sales Group, a family of music-related companies, is the largest printed music publisher in Europe. The Group owns, manages, and exploits more than 200,000 music copyrights for use in advertising, film, and TV. It also designs, publishes, and distributes more than 600 sheet music products and song books each year. Music Sales also operates a chain of some 20 retail stores in the UK, offering printed music and musical instruments. The company's principal book publishing imprint, Omnibus Press, was established in 1976.

In addition to the UK, Music Sales distributes its products elsewhere throughout Europe, as well as in the US, Australia, and Japan.

The company publishes songbooks of artists and groups such as Bob Dylan, Jimi Hendrix, Eric Clapton, U2, AC/DC, Coldplay, Oasis, and ABBA.

Like the rest of the publishing world, Music Sales Group has been focusing on its digital operations. The company also about 250,000 music-related products, including sheet music, DVDs, instruments, books about music, lessons, and accessories through its online retail outlet at musicroom.com. In addition, it has a digital publishing unit devoted to iPad apps, e-books, multimedia lessons, and a print on-demand sheet music service (Sheetmusicdirect.com).
