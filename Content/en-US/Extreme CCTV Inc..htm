Extreme CCTV isn't afraid of the dark. The company manufactures security video surveillance units equipped with night vision and infrared technology. It also makes invisible lasers used to read license plates and identify people from long distances. Extreme markets its products to commercial, governmental (including the US Department of Homeland Security ), institutional, and residential customers located in all seven continents. Its night vision cameras have even assisted scientists in Antarctica. Extreme's products can be found in celebrity homes, royal palaces, military bases, chemical plants, oil companies, international airports, and Egypt's pyramids. Robert Bosch GmbH bought Extreme CCTV in 2008.
