Aalberts Industries seeks to serve and control. The company operates through two main units, Flow Control and Industrial Services. The Flow Control operation manufactures and distributes flow control systems that range from beer taps to HVAC components. Its customers include building contractors, industrial manufacturers, energy utilities, and scientific laboratories. The Industrial Services unit makes industrial components used in automobiles, aircraft, electrical goods, telecommunications gear, and military equipment. Aalberts employs a decentralized holding structure in which each of its many subsidiaries operates with a high degree of autonomy.
