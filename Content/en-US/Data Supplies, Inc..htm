Data Supplies is the "go to" company for all things printed. The company prints a range of business materials, including forms, direct-mail pieces, point-of-purchase materials, and envelopes. On the digital front, it offers custom Web templates addressing the needs for ordering business cards, stationery, brochures, flyers, note cards, and other imprinted materials. Its digital asset management operations provide a Web-based repository for managing the control, access, and updating of all customer products. Other online features include search functionality, user authorization, and uploading and downloading capabilities. Founded in 1966, Data Supplies is headquartered in Duluth, Georgia.
