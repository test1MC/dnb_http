If you need someone to "put a lid on it" it could be that Tecnocap is the company you are looking for. The manufacturer produces metal caps and closures, such as aluminum continuous thread closures, child-resistant closures, metal continuous thread caps, and uni-shell closure works. Tecnocap's products are used by the cosmetic, pharmaceutical, and specialty food industries. Its closures are available in a range of sizes, colors and finishes along with various liners including heat induction, paper lined, tamper evident, plastisol, and safety seals. The company is a major subsidiary of Italian company Tecnocap S.p.a.
