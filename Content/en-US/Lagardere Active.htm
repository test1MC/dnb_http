Being among the world's leading magazine publishers is just one of the things that makes Lagardère Active quite fashionable. The firm produces the French edition of the popular fashion title Elle and Parisian what's-hot-and-what's-not journal Paris Match . It also has divisions devoted to radio stations, TV broadcasting and production, digital media, and ad sales. Formerly a global magazine publisher, Lagardère Active is re-focusing on its French assets after the 2011 sale of some 100 international magazine titles to Hearst . French conglomerate Lagardère owns the company.
