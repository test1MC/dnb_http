SprayCool could just as well be called StayCool. The company develops products that prevent overheating of electronic systems used in harsh environments. Its enclosures and rack systems use a liquid cooling technology that sprays a mist of liquid which evaporates and cools electronics. SprayCool systems are used in military and industrial applications-- including customers in the oil and gas exploration and telecommunications industries -- and can be installed on unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), fixed-wing aircrafts, and other vehicles. Clients have included the Department of Defense , the Department of Energy , Lockheed Martin , and Raytheon . SprayCool was founded in 1988.
