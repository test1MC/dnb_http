Bio-Botanica can help extract all sorts of goodness out of life. The company develops and manufactures a variety of botanical extracts used by customers throughout the US and internationally, in the nutraceutical, food and beverage, and cosmetics industries. Bio-Botanica's more than 500 extracts come in solid, semi-solid, powdered, and liquid forms and are used as both active ingredients and preservatives in products ranging from teas to soaps and homeopathic remedies. The company also produces its own line of consumer products, including extracts and blended nutritional supplements, which are sold through retailers under the Nature's Answer brand. The family-owned Bio-Botanica was founded in 1972 by CEO Frank D'Amelio.

Bio-Botanica's product list is expansive and includes plants, herbs, and flowers that range from artichoke extract to licorice root and yehimbe bark.

The company has been advancing its pharmaceutical efforts by testing the potential anti-cancer action of Pueraria mirifica (PM), a root found in Thailand, for use in the management of breast cancer. Bio-Botanica has developed Puresterol, an extract of PM that has been shown to promote healthy skin, and that the company is now studying as a natural alternative for the management of breast cancer.

The company's largest distribution networks are in the US and Canada, but it also sells its wares in the Asia/Pacific region and Europe.

Bio-Botanica sells its products to the cosmetic and personal care, food and beverage, and pharmaceutical and nutraceutical markets.
