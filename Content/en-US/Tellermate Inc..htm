Sometimes the name says it all. Tellermate exists to help tellers and others whose business is to count money. In particular, Tellermate Inc., the US subsidiary of Tellermate Plc , makes cash money counting machines used by retailers, food service businesses, and financial institutions. The company also makes automated cash drawers that verify the contents of cash registers each time the drawer is closed. Customers include Burger King and Comerica .
