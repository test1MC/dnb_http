A beautiful table is the goal of Guy (rhymes with "key") Degrenne -- a table set with its crystal, porcelain dinnerware, and stainless steel flatware and holloware. Originally formed to make knives and stainless steel products, the company is still a major producer of 18/8 stainless steel cutlery and cookware. Subsidiary Guy Degrenne-Industrie makes household appliances and other stainless steel items ranging from pumps and catering supplies to paper dispensers and railway lavatory bowls. Guy Degrenne sells its products through more than 110 boutiques, department stores, and specialty shops in 40-plus countries. Brand names include Guy Degrenne, Tables & Couleurs, and Létang Remy.

The company has 110 shops and locations in department stores in France and Belgium, and more than 700 outlets abroad. It has five production sites -- three in France and one in both Hungary and Thailand. It makes sales in over 40 countries in five continents, and 25% of revenue comes from outside France.
