CEDROM-SNi keeps a sharp eye on les nouvelles . The company monitors, culls, distributes, and archives news articles, broadcasts, and other information for its Canadian and French clients, distributing the information through its online services and via CD-ROM. The company's Newscan.com site serves English Canada, while its Eureka and Europresse online services target French Canada and French-speaking Europe, respectively. Clients include businesses, government entities, academia, and libraries; they use the information for competitive and other types of research. Founded in 1989, the company maintains a partnership with printing company Transcontinental , and has four offices in Canada and one in France.
