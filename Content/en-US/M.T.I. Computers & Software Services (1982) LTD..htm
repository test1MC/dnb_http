We can't tell you what MTI Computers & Software Services does because it's top secret. Just kidding! The group develops miniature wireless devices and other technology for the intelligence industry, but its products also have applications in the civilian market. MTI operates under three subsidiaries: MTI Engineering , MTI Wireless Edge , and MTeye Security. MTeye Security develops and manufactures miniature video surveillance devices (such as matchbox-sized cameras) for the security, cellular telecommunications, and aerospace industries. MTI Computers & Software Services is controlled by the Borovich family.
