The business decision was to go for precision. Precision Cable Assemblies makes heavy gauge wire assemblies, ground straps, battery cables, lead wire assemblies, and cable and wire harnesses, primarily for OEMs. The company's original 53,000 sq. ft. manufacturing plant (in Brookfield, Wisconsin) handles the majority of its heavy gauge cable assemblies, wire harnesses and multi-conductor assemblies. Precision Cable Assemblies also operates a 70,000 sq. ft. facility in Qingdao, China, which produces larger, more labor intensive wire harnesses. Markets served include automotive, heavy duty truck chassis, lawn and garden, marine, power distribution, semi-trailer, snow removal equipment, and utilities.
