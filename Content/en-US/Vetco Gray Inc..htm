Turning onshore and offshore oil and gas activities into onshore profits is the business of Vetco Gray. A unit of General Electric , the company (which does business as VetcoGray) provides upstream production facilities, process systems, technology, services, and products. It maintains, modifies, and manages technologies for offshore oil and gas production plants. Its products include connectors, controls, and valves able to withstand harsh environments. Vetco Gray's products and systems can handle a wide range of production challenges, from a single well drilling project onshore to multiple well deepwater drilling systems and complex subsea production systems.

Vetco Gray is a market leader in the supply of drilling, completion, and production equipment for onshore and subsea applications in oil and gas fields.

It is organized into six segments: capital drilling equipment, floating production systems, mudline equipment, surface drilling and completion systems, subsea drilling systems, and subsea production systems. It also provides a broad range of aftermarket services to the the upstream oil and gas industry.

The company is a pioneer in the production of tension leg platforms, and is engaged in the design and development of floating production systems. VetcoGray is also one of the world's leading suppliers of subsea wellheads, supplying almost half of the world's demand.

The company is a part of the oil and gas segment of its parent company. This segment contributed about 12% of the parent's revenues in 2013 (up 11% from 2012 thanks to higher prices and increased volumes).

The company is an indirect subsidiary of General Electric and a subsidiary of GE Oil & Gas .

Expanding its subsea offerings, in 2010 the company launched the VetcoGray DHXT deepwater horizontal tree and integral control system, which is designed to operate in 10,000 feet of water and under great pressure.

Founded in 1986, in 2007 Vetco Gray was acquired by General Electric for $1.9 billion. The deal boosted GE Oil & Gas' range of oilfield services, products, and technologies.
