USHIO, which began making industrial light sources in 1964, is a supplier of lamps and lighting products used in making semiconductors, LCDs, high-precision optics, office copiers and printers, projectors, and professional lighting, among other products. The company's light sources go into its own products, into systems made by its joint ventures, or into the products of third-party manufacturers. USHIO line of projectors include digital cinema projectors, which the company is strategically focusing on given the rise in popularity of 3-D movies around the world. It is also looking to bolster its product lines that incorporate solid-state light sources such as LEDs and lasers.
