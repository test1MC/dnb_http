Improving upon the food pyramid, R&R Ice Cream counts ice cream as its own food group. The company is Europe's largest private label ice cream manufacturer and distributor. In addition, R&R makes and markets popsicles under brands Fab and Roundtree's, and ice cream tubs under Kelly's Cornish. It also holds the UK license to produce and sell Nestl√© ice cream treats, such as Skinny Cow. R&R customers include retailers such as Tesco and Carrefour and foodservice operators. Beyond the UK, R&R sells its products mainly in France, Germany, and Poland, supported by local facilities. The company, formerly Richmond Ice Cream, is owned by Oaktree Capital , which remade the business by merging it with Germany's Roncadin.
