Ark Therapeutics is loading its boat with therapies that target cancer and vascular disease. The biotech company has preclinical and clinical-stage biotherapeutic candidates that aim to treat different types of cancerous tumors, as well as vascular disorders such as angina (chest pain) and peripheral vascular disease (narrow blood vessels). The firm hopes to eventually commercialize its products in key markets, such as Europe and the US, both directly and through partnerships with other drugmakers. It also seeks to enter licensing and contract manufacturing agreements to provide additional funding for its core R&D programs. Ark Therapeutics divested its commercial line of wound care products in 2011.
