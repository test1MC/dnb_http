Lasko Products makes household items that keep the air -- cool, hot, humid, or otherwise -- moving for its customers. Founded in 1906, Lasko Products is a top portable fan maker that operates several manufacturing plants in the US and distributes its wares internationally. The company's products include indoor and outdoor electric fans, humidifiers, space heaters, utility boxes, and even Christmas tree stands. Through the company's Canadian subsidiary Air King , it also makes ventilating products such as bath fans, kitchen range hoods, and humidifiers that are sold throughout North America. The family-run firm is led by CEO Oscar Lasko and his son William Lasko as president.

Based in Pennsylvania, Lasko Products boasts locations throughout the US and Canada. Its Tennessee and Texas manufacturing plants churn out both indoor and outdoor fans and heaters. The company supplies its products to customers internationally through a primary distribution center located in Columbia, Pennsylvania.

Lasko Products sells its products through retailers and online.

The company has been working to expand its portfolio of products. In 2012 Lasko Products rolled out a Misto Outdoor Misting Fan to serve the growing number of consumers who entertain outdoors.
