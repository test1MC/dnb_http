Talk about retro-fitting: Bills Khakis fit the bill when you're looking for trousers like Grandpa brought back from the war. The company, headquartered in a refurbished Rust Belt factory, makes all of its premium khaki pants, five-pocket jeans, corduroys, seersucker pants, shorts, shirts, headwear, and other apparel in the US.
