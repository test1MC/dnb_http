Vic Firth wants its customers to stick around by making products that keep drummer boys and girls tapping. The company manufactures drumsticks and mallets, as well as bags, headphones, and other accessories for drums and keyboard instruments. It sells its products in music shops through dealers and offers point-of-purchase displays, sell sheets, and specialty items such as name-brand lanyards. Vic Firth has made a name for itself as a sponsor of seminars and clinics for aspiring drummers in the US and internationally. Timpanist and founder Vic Firth still serves as CEO of the Massachusetts-based operation, which has been in business for more than 40 years.
