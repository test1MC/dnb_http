Tongue tied by the alphabet soup of electronics? Tatung Company of America untangles the LCDs (liquid crystal displays) from the LEDs (light-emitting diodes). It offers an array of high-tech goods and manufacturing services for PC and electronics OEMs. Its digital line ranges from signage and security surveillance tools, like cameras and monitors, to computer monitors for PCs, point-of-sale terminals, and touch screens. The company also sells home appliances, such as air purifiers and rice cookers, as well as hospitality conveniences, like microwaves and coffee makers. The company is the US arm of Taiwan's Tatung Company .

Based in Long Beach, California, Tatung Company of America distributes technology products for PC and electronics original equipment manufacturers in the US and internationally.

The company offers a broad range of electronic equipment products including mini dome cameras, digital video recording systems, hand-held test monitors, LCD and LED displays, consumer houseware, (air conditioners, coolers, purifiers, hot water dispensers, pressure cookers, refrigerators and rice cookers), digital signage, cabinets, microwaves, LCD monitors, and PC monitors. It also offers manufacturing services, logistical support, and warranty and repair services.

Tatung Company of America was founded in 1972.
