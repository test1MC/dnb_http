Global Railway Industries is signalling a plan to keep it on track. The company operates through subsidiaries such as CAD Railway Services (locomotive and railcar repair) and Prime Railway Services (rail car doors and replacement parts). It sold its Bach-Simpson unit (vehicle monitoring systems and event recorders) to Westinghouse Air Brake Technologies (Wabtec) for a reported $48 million in 2010. Global Railway is looking to sell other subsidiaries as part of an agreement with lenders. Customers include Bombardier , GE , and ALSTOM . Global Railway Industries was founded in 1997.
