Connectors make Hirose Electric's world go 'round. The electronic components manufacturer develops and makes more than 50,000 products, including circular, rectangular, and fiber-optic connectors, coaxial switches, integrated circuit sockets, and memory cards. Its products are used in the automotive, broadcasting and communications, consumer electronics, and medical industries. Hirose Electric has sales dealerships in about 23 countries throughout Asia, Europe, and the US. It has production plants in China, Indonesia, Malaysia, and the US, with plans to establish new production bases in Europe. Hirose Electric was founded in 1937.
