ViewSonic Europe, a subsidiary of ViewSonic Corporation , is a provider of desktop monitors and flat-panel displays for the consumer, education, enterprise, and government markets. The company's display products utilize both liquid-crystal display (LCD) and light-emitting diode (LED) technologies. Viewsonic Europe also sells other products including projectors, tablets, and digital photo frames. In addition to brick-and-mortar retailers such as Argos , the company sells through online and value-added resellers, and distributors that include Avnet Technology Solutions , Ingram Micro , Northamber , and Premier Distribution. Projectors are sold via a separate reseller network.

ViewSonic Europe's products are available from online merchants including BT Business Direct, Scan Computers, Dabs.com, Misco, Insight Direct, Just Projectors, Amazon.com , and Ebuyer.
