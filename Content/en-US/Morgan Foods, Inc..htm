Morgan Foods aims to warm up tummies with a hot bowl of soup. The company makes private-label condensed and ready-to-serve soups and broths, baked and refried beans, and chilies, as well as a line of gravies and sauces for retail food outlets. It offers such services as consumer-preference testing, regulatory auditing, competitor and sensory and product-assortment analysis, promotional and marketing plans, and label development. The company was founded in 1899 by Joseph Morgan and his partners; the firm is in its fourth generation of Morgan-family ownership and management.
