SCOLR Pharma is a control freak. The company is developing its Controlled Delivery Technology (CDT) to improve the rate of release of a drug into the body. The CDT platform is designed for use with oral solid doses and regulates the drug's release for periods of up to 24 hours. SCOLR's CDT technology is used in private-label nutritional supplements through its partnership with Perrigo . The company is also researching CDT's usefulness for cough and cold remedies, analgesics, cardiovascular treatments, and other medications. SCOLR is seeking additional collaborative partnerships to utilize the CDT system; it has established relationships with BioCryst and Dr. Reddy's .
