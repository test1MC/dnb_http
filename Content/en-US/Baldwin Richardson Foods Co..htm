Baldwin Richardson is sweet on foods. The company started out in in 1916 making dessert toppings. Today, it makes many kinds of liquid products for the food industry, including condiments, sauces, syrups, and fruit fillings. Baldwin Richardson Foods sells its products, such as Mrs. Richardson's toppings and Nance's condiments, to retail food customers. Its consumer offerings also include Baldwin ice cream, Mrs. Richardson's ice cream toppings, and Nance's mustards and condiments. In addition the company has customers in the food manufacturing and foodservice sectors. Baldwin Richardson offers private-label, co-packing, and contract-manufacturing services.
