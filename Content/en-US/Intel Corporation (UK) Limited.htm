Intel UK serves as the European headquarters for the world's top semiconductor maker. The company manufactures microprocessors, used in desktop and notebook PCs, servers, and workstations. Other products include embedded semiconductors used in communications, industrial equipment, and point-of-sale applications. The company also provides professional services, such as application integration and platform migration. Intel's largest customers are computing leaders Dell and Hewlett-Packard .

Intel's UK operation is based in Swindon, Wiltshire England with other locations in Almondsbury, Borehamwood, Egham, Lincoln, and Winnersh Triangle.
