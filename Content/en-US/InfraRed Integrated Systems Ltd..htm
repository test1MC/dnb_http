InfraRed Integrated Systems, dba Irisys, makes electronic thermal imaging products that incorporate high-resolution infrared imagers and detectors. Its products are used by banks, retailers, transportation facilities, and others in such applications as industrial process monitoring, people counting, security, and retail checkout management. Different models of Irisys' counting, thermal imaging, and radiometric products are configured as stand-alone, handheld, or customized units. The company sells through a global network of distributors, resellers, and systems integrators.

While based in the UK, about 80% if the company's products are used in other markets. It has offices in the US and Germany.

Irisys sells through authorized distributors, resellers, and systems integrators. It counts the Dubai Airports, Network Rail , Harley-Davidson , McArthurGlen, and Calzedonia Group as customers.

Strategy

Irisys partners with other companies to integrate products. In 2015, VoloForce agreed to integrate Irisys technology into products for the retail market.
