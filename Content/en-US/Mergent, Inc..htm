Mergent can help you merge all sorts of useful data into your business operations and daily activities. The company provides business and financial data on a multitude of publicly-traded companies, stock exchanges, and indices. Mergent's offerings include Mergent Online (Web-based business information), EventsData (Web-based reporting of corporate news), Mergent Manuals (print editions of corporate information), and Mergent Active (analytical research tool).
