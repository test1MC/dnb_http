Advanced Medical Solutions (AMS) closes the gap on gaping wounds. The company makes and sells a wide range of professional wound care and wound closure products to hospitals, nursing homes, and other health care providers. Its ActivHeal professional wound care products include gauzes, dressings, foams, and gels used for ulcers and pressure sores. AMS' LiquiBand offerings include suture-alternative wound closure products (adhesives, sealants, and films) for use in emergency and operating rooms. The firm's products are sold both directly and through distributors, primarily in Continental Europe, the UK, and the US. AMS also manufactures private label products for other medical products makers and distributors.
