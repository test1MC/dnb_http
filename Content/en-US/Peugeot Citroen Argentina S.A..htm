Peugeot Citroën Argentina (PCA) brings a petite quantité of France to South America. The company, a subsidiary of Peugeot S.A. , manufactures and imports Peugeot and Citroën vehicles. PCA manufactures Peugeot passenger cars, as well as the Partner compact SUV and the Boxer commercial van. It also makes Ion and C-ZERO electric cars. Citroën branded vehicles include the Berlingo, Nemo, Picasso, Xsara, and the C-series. To cater to the Latin American market, Peugeot released its Hoggar pickup, and Citroën its C3 Aircross. Peugeot S.A. is investing $182 million in its Argentinean operations in 2010. PCA was formed through the 1980 merger of the Argentinean operations of Fiat and Peugeot.
