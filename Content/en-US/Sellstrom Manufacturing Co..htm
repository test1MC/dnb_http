Sellstrom Manufacturing hopes to protect you against all sorts of potentially injurious conditions. The company provides protective wear and related products to do-it-yourselfers, as well as corporations, schools, and hospitals. Sellstrom's product line includes eye, face, knee, and head protection (including goggles, face shields, knee pads, and hard hats), welding protection (such as filters and helmets), fall protection (including harnesses, lanyards, and lifeline systems) and emergency eye washers and showers. The business was founded in 1923 by G.E. Sellstrom and his wife, Ellen, as Excell Sales Company.
