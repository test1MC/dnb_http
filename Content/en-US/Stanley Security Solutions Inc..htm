Stanley Security Solutions (SSS), a subsidiary of Stanley Black & Decker , has got your back, and your front. The company provides a swath of security entry products and services to residences, institutions, and governments. Its lineup includes mechanical access products, entry locking mechanisms, as well as electronic security systems and software. (SSS software products are combined with existing security systems or custom designed.) Other offerings range from automatic doors to patient/resident safety products and services, such as wireless nurse calling, magnetic door locks, and key entry systems. Products are sold under brand names Best, Safemasters, Sargent and Greenleaf, and K2.

SSS is stationed in Indianapolis and operates nearly 20 facilities throughout the Americas, Middle East, and Australia.

The company sells its products to more than 500,000 customers primarily through direct sales and independent distribution channels. It serves retailers, educational, financial and healthcare institutions, as well as commercial, governmental, and industrial customers.

SSS operates under the Security Solutions segment of its parent company. This segment generated 22% of its parent's total revenue during 2013.

SSS intends to make good on its growth strategy by targeting opportunities in the security industry through three core portfolios: Mechanical Access, Convergent Security, and Stanley Healthcare (which operates as part of Convergent Security). Each manages an evolving lineup of premium brands, focused largely in the education, healthcare, government, retail, and industrial manufacturing markets.

In 2012 Stanley Healthcare acquired AeroScout, a maker of real-time location systems for the health care and industrial sectors, for around $239 million.
