PJH Group is one of the UK's leading wholesale distributors of kitchen, bedroom, and bathroom furniture. The company's brands include K Kitchens, Ram Kitchens, Moods Bathrooms, and Prima (kitchen and appliances). The company serves retailers and home builders and counts Electrolux , Formica , and Whirlpool as major clients. PJH operates six trading centers throughout the UK. It was bought out by management in 2005 but sold again in 2007 to the Taiwan-based Globe Union Company, a manufacturer of kitchen and bathroom faucets and fixtures. The company, formerly named Peter J. Hopkinson, was founded in 1972.

With more than 150,000 store and 3,000 home deliveries a week, PJH Group is one of the UK's largest manufacturers by volume. It specializes in making whirlpool bath systems. Distributed brands include Abode, AEG, April, Bosch, Franke, Gorenje, and Haro.

PJH's customers include more than 4,000 retailers, building supply stores, home builders, and developers.
