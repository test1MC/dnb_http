A sucker rod is born every day at C-Tech Oilwell Technologies. The company is a top supplier of coiled sucker rods for oil and gas producers. Through subsidiaries C-Tech provides customers with oil and gas field equipment and services. Its Pro-Rod subsidiary manufactures coiled rods, rod handling systems, and tubing. Its C-Tech Design and Manufacturing unit makes coiled tubing units and fabricates steel products, including shipping saddles, oil field boilers, and drilling rig modules. C-Tech also provides rig rebuilding, fabrication, and engineering services. Dover Corporation acquired C-Tech in 2005.
