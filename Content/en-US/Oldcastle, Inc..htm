Oldcastle has many of the materials needed to build your modern castle. As the North American arm of CRH , the company makes architectural and construction building products in the US and Canada, and is organized into three groups. The company's Oldcastle Materials business produces and sells aggregates, asphalt, and ready-mix concrete and provides paving services. Precast, lawn, garden, and concrete masonry products are made by Oldcastle Building Products. Oldcastle Distribution, (DBA Allied Building Products ), delivers roofing, siding, window, and interior products to specialty contractors.

Oldcastle Building Products division organizes its products into three groups: Architectural Products (concrete masonry, packaged cement mixes, packaged lawn and garden products); Precast (utility and structural precast, construction accessories, and composite utility enclosures); and Oldcastle BuildingEnvelope, which provides curtain walls, architectural windows and glass, entrance doors, storefronts, and skylights.

Oldcastle's divisions often work hand-in-hand in offering customers diverse building materials and services for one project. Building Cowboys Stadium in Dallas showcased this benefit, as each of Oldcastle's product groups provided materials (from asphalt in the parking lot to glass for windows) for the project.

The company operates from more than 2,000 locations in 50 states and about a handful of Canadian provinces.

Oldcastle Distribution delivers its interior products to specialty contractors in the construction and remodeling industries.

Some of Oldcastle's clients have included: Target , Best Buy , Lowes , and Costco .
