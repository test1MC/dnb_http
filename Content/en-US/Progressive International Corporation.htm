Progressive International designs and manufactures kitchen tools and gadgets under its namesake brand Progressive, Mr. Dudley (mills for pepper, salt, and spices), and MiracleWare (cookware for the microwave). Founded by entrepreneur Kevin Wold as a kitchenware import company in 1973, Progressive peddles its products in the US and Canada, as well as in Europe, South America, and other international markets. The company sells its items through specialty gourmet shops (The Peppermill, Sur La Table ), department and discount stores ( Bed Bath & Beyond ), boutique coffee shops, and other venues.
