Action Asia is all about talk, all about action, and all about viewing pleasure -- wherever you are. The company makes audio and video equipment for car and RV systems and home entertainment. Action Asia's 6.8- to 15-inch LCD video screens are used mostly in automobiles and public transportation vehicles, while its 5.6- to 20-inch screens can be found in hospitals, homes, and outdoor locations. Its factories in Malaysia and China make products bound for US car and accessory makers and supermarkets. Action Asia plans to open an R&D firm and to pursue the Chinese market. Customers include Audiovox and Thomson Inc. The company, a subsidiary of Action Electronics, was formed in 1987.
