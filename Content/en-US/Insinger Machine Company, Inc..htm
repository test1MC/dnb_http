For galley workers, Insinger Machine Company is a life preserver. The company makes a variety of commercial dishwashers, with the U.S. Navy being a primary customer. Its other products include bakery washers, food waste disposers, pot washers, tray washers and dryers, and vegetable peelers. Insinger Machine's commercial dishwashers are built with the same practical philosophy that have made the company's military equipment a success -- durable, easy to clean, and simple to service. The fourth generation family business was instrumental in developing the first stainless steel dishwashers for shipboard use.
