BOXX Technologies believes the bigger the box, the better. The company supplies high-performance workstation PCs, servers, and related software for professionals involved in film and movie production, game development, industrial design, and other data-intensive applications. Customers have included animation houses Greystone, Janimation, Summer Breeze, and UVPHACTORY; graphics designers RainMaker and Simply Stahlberg; and video producers ETLT Productions, Schnuffie Productions, and Twinkle. BOXX Technologies was incorporated in 1998.
