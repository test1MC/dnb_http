Elec & Eltek International (E&E) makes double-sided and multilayer printed circuit boards (PCBs), LCDs, and magnetic component modules. The company also offers services, including customer relationship management, enterprise resource planning, and supply chain management, to customers in China. Clients come from the computers and peripherals, communications and networking, consumer electronics, and automotive industries. E&E, with manufacturing facilities in China, Hong Kong, and Thailand, is a subsidiary of fellow PCB manufacturer Kingboard Chemical Holdings .
