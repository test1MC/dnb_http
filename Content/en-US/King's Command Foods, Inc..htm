Thanks to this company, you don't need a royal decree to get processed meats. King's Command Foods is a leading supplier of processed beef and pork products focused on the public school market. It offers low-cost food items including hamburgers, chicken patties, and taco meat filling to its education customers by using donated commodity meat. King's Command also makes beef, pork, and chicken products for other foodservice operators. In addition, the company markets fully-cooked burgers, meatballs, and other products to consumers under the Just-A-Minute brand. Harvey Baer started the family-owned business as King's Command Meats in 1966.
