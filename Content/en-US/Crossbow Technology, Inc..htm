Crossbow Technology can help you target the physical world from a digital perspective. The company manufactures sensors used in navigation and guidance systems for aerospace, defense, and transportation applications. Its MEMS (micro-electro-mechanical systems)-based products are used in unmanned aerial vehicles and military ground vehicles, as well as handheld asset-tracking devices. Crossbow's customers include military subcontractors such as Lockheed Martin , Airbus , Raytheon , and Israel Aerospace Industries . The company was founded in 1995 by the late Richard Newton and CEO Mike Horton. In 2011 Moog Inc. acquired Crossbow for about $32 million in cash.
