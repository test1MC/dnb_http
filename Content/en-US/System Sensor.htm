This company keeps your hard work from going up in smoke. System Sensor, a division of aerospace and industrial manufacturer Honeywell International , designs, manufactures, and markets fire detection and notification systems, including sprinkler systems, smoke detection equipment and accessories, and HVAC monitoring devices used in commercial, industrial, and institutional facilities worldwide. The company also makes audible and visible alert devices, including chimes, horns, speakers, and strobes. The company was founded in 1984. It operates as part of Honeywell's Automation and Control Solutions segment.
