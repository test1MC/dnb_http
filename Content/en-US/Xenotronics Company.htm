Xenotronics designs and installs electronic security equipment for commercial and residential customers in the Midwest. The company's products include card access, closed-circuit video, fire protection, and security systems. Customers have included agricultural facilities, banks, hospitals, hotels, insurance companies, and utilities providers. Xenotronics was founded in 1986.
