For piano-maker Young Chang, the color of money is black and white, no matter what currency is tendered for its pianos. Young Chang's pianos -- styles from full-concert grand pianos to Kurzweil-brand player-pianos for barstool Beethovens -- are sold in more than 45 countries. The company's products portfolio boasts several brand names, including Bergmann, Young Chang, and the Pramberger Signature Series. In 2990, Young Chang acquired Kurzweil Music Systems, a manufacturer of synthesizers, player pianos, and digital pianos. Still family-owned and -operated, Young Chang was founded in 1956 by three brothers: Jae-Kim, Jae-Young, and Jae-Sup Kim.
