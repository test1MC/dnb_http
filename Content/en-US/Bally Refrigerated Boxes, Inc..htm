If you are an inhabitant of a mortuary cooler at the city morgue, you probably won't be checking the brand name on the door, but there's a good possibility it reads "Bally." Bally Refrigerated Boxes makes a wide range of refrigerated equipment (blast chillers, modular structures, mortuary coolers, refrigerated buildings, and walk-in coolers and freezers). The company's insulation panels (urethane sandwiched between metal skins) are light, easy to assemble, and insulate very efficiently. Bally Refrigerated Boxes' equipment is used in environmental and scientific applications, kitchens, large refrigerated warehouses, small floral storage units, and (to go back to the beginning, or the end) morgues and mortuaries.
