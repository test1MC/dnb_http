If every chicken tells a story, then JFC has plenty of fine-feathered tales to tell. Founded as a mail-order hatchery for other producers, the family-owned company has grown into a fully integrated poultry processor. Its Just BARE line for upscale and foodservice clients, features anitbiotic-free chickens that are fed a vegetarian diet. Its broader Gold'n Plump brand consists of fresh, frozen, and value-added chicken products, such as rotisserie chicken in "grab and go" packages and bake-it-in-the-package entrees. JFC's poultry products are sold to retail food and foodservice customers nationwide. The company's primary product is sourced through some 350 poultry farmers located in Minnesota and Wisconsin.

Based in Minnesota, JFC operates production plants in Wisconsin and Minnesota, distributing its poultry products nationwide.

Nationwide, JFC provides premium branded and custom chicken products to retail, deli, and foodservice customers. The company uses tongue-in-cheek parachuting chickens as part of its popular Gold'n Plump advertising campaigns.

The company boasts operations in Cold Spring, Luverne, and St. Cloud, Minnesota, as well as in Arcadia and Independence, Wisconsin.

Along with regular chicken products, JFC offers a line of chickens that are raised using no hormones under the brand name All Natural Raised.

Founded in 1926, the company was originally known as St. Cloud Hatcheries. It later became Jack Frost Hatchery and then Jack Frost Layer Farms, before changing its name to Jack Frost, Inc., in 1968 and finally to JFC in 1980.
