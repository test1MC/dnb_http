RKI didn't invent the work truck, but for some 100 years the company's products have made it more useful. RKI manufactures commercial truck bodies, truck-mounted cranes and winches, and truck accessories. To suit a variety of industrial and commercial customers, the company's truck bodies are available with a wide range of shelf and storage options. Its cranes range in capacity from 2,000 to 6,400 pounds. Truck accessories include toolboxes, storage chests, and window grilles and racks. RKI also makes commercial van accessories such as ladder racks. Its lineup is marketed largely by truck parts and supply distributors in the US. RKI, formerly known as Rawson-Koenig, Inc., is owned and run by the Rawson family.
