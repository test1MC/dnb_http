No, Pruce Newman isn't a classic Hollywood actor. Instead, Pruce Newman Pipework fabricates and installs pipework made of black, galvanized, and coated mild steel, ductile iron, stainless and alloy steel, and thermo and thermosetting plastics and plastic-lined mild steel materials. Founded in 1970, the company manages installation projects and provides construction design management, costing feasibility and budgeting, planning, site management, and pipework system and support design services. Its prefabrication operations are maintained at its 9,000 sq. ft. facility.
