MAX International Converters is a global proselytizer of the belief that everyone should have access to ATM machines, and that each of these machines should have paper in them to print receipts, in addition to dispensing cash. MAX International Converters manufactures paper rolls for ATMs and other machines that print receipts, such as gas pumps, food stamp terminals, on-demand coupon dispensers, and cash registers. In addition to producing a wide range of paper tolls the company also provides its customers with copier toner, fax paper, ribbons, ink jet cartridges and printheads, forms, and other supplies.
