While Paiste's product line includes hi-hats, splashes, and swishes, to the untrained eye, they all look like cymbals. The company also makes drums, gongs, and percussion accessories for professional musicians. Its products are distributed worldwide to artists ranging from symphony percussionists to rock and jazz drummers and high school marching bands. Based in Switzerland, Paiste also maintains operations in Estonia, Germany, Spain, and the US. Pronounced "pie-stee," the family-owned company has been making cymbals since 1917.
