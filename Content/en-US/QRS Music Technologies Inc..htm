Tired ivory ticklers could probably use some Pianomation from QRS Music Technologies. The company manufactures and distributes Story & Clark pianos and Pianomation MIDI systems (digital playback technology for acoustic and digital pianos), which are sold through about 500 independent retailers across the country. It also produces CDs and DVDs that can be synchronized with player pianos, and the company's NetPiano subscription service lets customers download music to their Pianomation systems. QRS Music was founded by Melville Clark in 1900. Chairman Richard Dolan owns about 60% of the company.
