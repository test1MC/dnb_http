Norse Dairy Systems (NDS) leaves its customers cold, and that's the way they like it. The company (a division of Interbake Foods ) caters to ice cream novelty manufacturers by providing manufacturing equipment that produces sandwiches, cups, cones, and tubes. NDS makes an assortment of ice cream products including sandwich wafers, cones, crunch and cookie pieces, and other baked ingredients. Its research and development team works with customers to create custom products. The company also manufactures packaging for sleeves, novelty cups, and tube treats. NDS invented the ice cream tube treat in 1960.
