China Intelligent Lighting and Electronics knows how to light up a room. A designer and manufacturer of lighting products, the company makes more than 1000 products used in homes, businesses, warehouses, and outdoor settings. Its core products include residential LED and florescent kitchen and bathroom ceiling lights, as well as wall lamps and recessed, landscape, and spot lighting. The company, which does most of its business in China, sells its products through more than 60 original equipment manufacturers (OEMs); it also distributes select products under its Hyundai lighting brand (not affiliated to the car company). Established in 2005, China Intelligent Lighting went public in 2010.
