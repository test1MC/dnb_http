Curtis Industries makes electrical and electronic components, such as terminal blocks, relay sockets, radio-frequency interference (RFI) power line filters, liquid level controls, and related accessories. The company serves clients in industries such as industrial manufacturing, telecom, health care, and aerospace, as well as military and government sectors. It operates manufacturing and inventory facilities in Wisconsin, Mexico, and China. Curtis Industries is a division of Powers Holdings, a Milwaukee-based company.
