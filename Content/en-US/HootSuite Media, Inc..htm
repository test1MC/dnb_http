You may not give a hoot about Facebook , Twitter , or LinkedIn , but your company probably does. That's where HootSuite comes in. The company's social media management tools are designed to help businesses and other organizations spread the corporate word across multiple social media platforms in one swoop. HootSuite's dashboard offers scheduling and assignments, allows collaborators to join in, and produces social analytics reports. Its interface works with the social media tools listed above, as well and YouTube , Flickr , Tumblr , Digg , Google+ , and others. It also offers a partner program for ad agencies and consultants that want to generate income offering HootSuite to their clients.  HootSuite launched in 2008.
