C&H Sugar's products go hand-in-hand with baked sweets and candy treats. The company processes about 700,000 tons of cane sugar per year, turning the canes into both sugar and molasses. It makes more than 70 types, grades, and package sizes of sugar, including white granulated, baker's, brown, powdered, and superfine. C&H Sugar serves retail food, foodservice, and industrial customers. Its retail brand is C&H; its Baker's Sugar brand is designed for professional bakers. A subsidiary of Florida' agricultural giant, Flo-Sun , C&H's products are manufactured at its refinery in Crockett, California.
