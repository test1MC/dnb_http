Product development takes a circuitous path at Areias Systems. The company designs and manufactures electromechanical parts and prototypes for small- and mid-sized companies in the semiconductor, hard drive, medical device, and solar industries. Using software by Autodesk and SolidWorks , Areias Systems designs circuit cards, control systems, power distribution systems, cables, and wiring harnesses. The company offers its product development and engineering services to startups and established firms. Areias Systems was founded in 2000 by CEO Clemm Noernberg from his garage. It operates out of a 15,000-sq.-ft. production facility.
