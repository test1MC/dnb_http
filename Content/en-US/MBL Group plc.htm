MBL Group helps music and films get from the creator to the consumer. The firm is a UK distributor and wholesaler of home entertainment products, such as music, movies, and games, via DVD and CD. Wholesale activities are conducted through subsidiary ESD Wholesale. The company entered the distribution business in a big way with its acquisition of Redworth, which it purchased for its Music Box Leisure (MBL) distribution operation. It changed its name from Air Music & Media to MBL Group in 2008. The UK supermarket sector accounts for approximately 80% of sales.

The company's revenue increased by 10% in 2014 due to increases in its home entertainment and its garden and leisure segment.

MBL Group targets the "non-traditional" sector, which includes supermarkets, discount retailers, and service stations (rather than conventional CD and DVD retailers).
