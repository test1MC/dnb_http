The tale of the tape is that Main Tape's customers stick with the company because its tapes don't stick to the customers' goods. The company sells pressure-sensitive adhesive protective tapes (surface protection film and paper products) suitable in manufacturing or for protecting a myriad of surfaces; plastics to fiberglass, fabric, wood, marble, and steel. Water-based adhesives are its specialty, along with multiple substrates (polyethylene, polypropylene, polyester, and paper), and light to heavyweight film coatings. Among many, Main Tape serves operations in aerospace, automotive, construction, consumer electronics, furniture, and graphics arts. Calvert Street Capital Partners owns a majority of the company.
