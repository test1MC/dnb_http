Xela Pack excels at packing a promotional punch. The company supplies sample and trial packaging in a variety of sizes and shapes for liquid, cream, and gel-type cosmetics, as well as pharmaceutical powders and pills, and viscous food and household products. Its primary packaging product, the Xela Pack, is a sample-sized plastic- or foil-lined paper container. Xela Pack also makes coupon packs, product information cards, mailers, dispensers, and point-of-purchase displays. The company, which touts its products' low impact on the environment, provides contract packaging and private-label services to top manufacturers Nutraceutical , Bert's Bees , and Watkins . Xela Pack was founded in 1986 by president Al Gentile.
