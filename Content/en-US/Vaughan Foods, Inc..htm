Vaughan Foods is a leading supplier of prepared vegetable salads and salad mixes, as well as soups and sauces, cole slaw, fruit products, dips, pasta, and potato salads. It distributes products under the Allison's and Fresh Cut brand names to restaurants, delis, and other foodservice operators, as well as to retail food outlets in the US's Plains states, along with the southwestern, southeastern, and northeastern regions. The company operates a fleet of about 30 trucks and 45 refrigerated trailers. In addition, Vaughan distributes fresh produce from suppliers located in Arizona, California, Colorado, Florida, and Mexico. In late 2011 the company was acquired by Reser's Fine Foods for more than $18 million.
