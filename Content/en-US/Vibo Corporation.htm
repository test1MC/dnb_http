Vibo is on a roll. Its sole holding is leading tobacco manufacturer General Tobacco, which specializes in making and marketing discount cigarette and pipe tobacco brands, such as GT One, Bronco, Silver, and 32 Degrees. It also makes filtered cigars under the Vaquero and Wrangler brands. All of General Tobacco's products are made in the US, and they are distributed through major retailers in North America and internationally. Vibo was founded by nonsmoking businessman Vidal Suriel in 1997, and its first cigarette brand, GT One, rolled off the line in 2000.
