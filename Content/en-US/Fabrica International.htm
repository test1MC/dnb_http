Choosing a perfect luxury carpet or rug for your home, motor coach. . .or yacht!? You're in luck. Fabrica International, a subsidiary of The Dixie Group , manufactures and markets high-end residential carpets and custom rugs. Products range from broadloom carpet products, carpet underlay, and handcrafted area rugs. Fabrica International offers a wide range of border rugs and design rugs for residential, motor coaches, and yachts. Its customers (including interior decorators, selected retailers and furniture stores, and luxury home builders) are found throughout Asia, Europe, and North America. Fabrica International was founded in 1974. It was acquired by carpet manufacturer and marketer The Dixie Group in 2000.
