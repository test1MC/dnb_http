Who knows how many secrets have stayed secret thanks to ASSA ABLOY, the world's largest lockmaker? Operating in 50 countries, it has installed more than one in ten of all locks and security devices worldwide. ASSA ABLOY produces mechanical and electronic security products, and security doors, for institutional and commercial buildings (hotels, offices, hospitals, schools) and, less so, residences. Products are sold under brands ABLOY, Norton, VingCard, Yale, and more. It provides security planning, maintenance, and consulting services for customers, too. The group was formed in 1994 following the spinoff of Assa AB from security group Securitas . Assa subsequently acquired lockmaster Abloy from Wärtsilä .
