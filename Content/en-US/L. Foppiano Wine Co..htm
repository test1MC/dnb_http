Established in 1896, Foppiano Vineyards is run by the Foppiano family, now in its fifth generation in the winemaking business. The winery, known primarily for its Petite Sirah, is based on a 200-acre estate in the Russian River Valley in Sonoma County. Other Foppiano brands include Riverside Vineyards and Fox Mountain Wines (white wines). Foppiano wines are available through the company's Web site in some states.
