Guard dogs, housesitters, and personal care attendants, beware! VOXCOM wants your job. Founded in 1989, the company is a leading provider of security systems and monitoring services to residential and commercial customers in Canada. Its security systems include intrusion alarms, fire and gas detection, flood sensors, access control units, and video surveillance. VOXCOM also offers a personal emergency response system called LifeCall used by the elderly and physically challenged. The company's equipment is sold through authorized dealers. VOXCOM is controlled by New York-based private equity firm Alinda Capital Partners.
