There's more to moving merchandise than just having a pretty package, but Smurfit Kappa UK knows that packaging can make or (literally) break a product. Smurfit Kappa UK is the holding company for Ireland-based Smurfit Kappa Group 's operations in the UK. The company, which has numerous subsidiaries throughout England and Scotland, focuses primarily on corrugated packaging products, but also makes containerboard and operates printing and recycling facilities. Smurfit Kappa UK additionally offers packaging machinery, such as bag-in-box and folding carton machines. Markets served include food and drink, consumer goods, industrial production, and paper and packaging.

Smufit Kappa UK operates within the UK.

Smurfit Kappa accounts for about 10% of the parent company's net sales.

In 2016, Smurfit Kappa invested in its national depot network of its recycling operation. The investment program was intended to improve operational efficiency, worker safety and customer experience. The work has included development of a new depot in Snodland, and the installation of new balers at the company's Snodland, Blackburn and Glasgow depots.
