Acorn Media Group has grown into its video distribution niche. The company distributes home video in the North American, UK, and Australian markets. Acorn's  business includes hundreds of titles in the drama, mystery, comedy, and science fiction genres, but the company is known for its focus on bringing British TV to DVD. Holdings include the Agatha Christie literary estate, consisting of nearly 40 TV movies, 20 plays, and more than 80 novels.  Its direct to consumer outlet, Acorn Direct, distributes gifts and video content through its Acorn online catalog and Acacia catalog. In 2012 Black Entertainment Television founder Robert Johnson announced plans to buy Acorn Media and combine it with Image Entertainment .
