Need a little magic to sort through SEC filings? Having access to a wizard certainly helps. 10-K Wizard Technology, through its Morningstar Document Research product, provides subscription-based online access to SEC filings, as well as searching and parsing tools to sort through the information. Users can search by company name, ticker, SIC code, industry type, or keyword. The company's services are powered by its data mining technology, which can be applied to complex information such as news feeds. The company also delivers personal custom e-mail alerts of new filings, and uses its technology to power the SEC portions of Web sites. 10-K Wizard is a subsidiary of global investment research firm Morningstar .
