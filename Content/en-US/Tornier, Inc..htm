This Tornier is the US subsidiary of Dutch orthopedic medical device manufacturer Tornier B.V. Tornier markets and sells these devices mainly to health care institutions and distributors in the US and Canada. Products targeted at specialist surgeons include joint replacement prosthetic implants for ankles, elbows, hands, shoulders, and other small bones in the upper and lower extremities. Products targeted at the sports medicine market include high-strength sutures, fixation anchors, and a tunneling device used to place multiple sutures without anchors. Tornier's North American headquarters were established in 2006 to help expand its parent's global footprint. Tornier B.V. filed to go public in mid-2010.
