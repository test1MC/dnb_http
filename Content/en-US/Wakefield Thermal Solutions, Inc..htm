Taking some heat? Heat dissipation is Wakefield Thermal Solutions' solution. The company designs, manufactures, and distributes thermal products and aluminum extrusions for removing heat generated by electronic equipment. The oldest and largest supplier of thermal management equipment in the US, its standard line includes extruded heat sinks, clamp systems, high-density extrusions, stack fins, and related devices. Wakefield caters to aerospace and defense, automotive, computer, construction, industrial control, telecommunications, and transportation applications. In mid-2012 investment firm The Heico Companies LLC acquired Wakefield and announced it's changing Wakefield's name to Wakefield-Vette.
