Varitronix International manufactures LCDs for a broad range of applications, including automotive, commercial, consumer, industrial, military, and telecommunications uses. Its products range from simple LCD displays to complete assemblies for OEMs, and include reflective liquid crystal on silicon microdisplays (LCOSes), organic light-emitting diodes (OLEDs), and bistable cholesteric displays (BCDs). The company has manufacturing facilities in China and Hong Kong, with sales offices around the world. Varitronix was founded in 1978.
