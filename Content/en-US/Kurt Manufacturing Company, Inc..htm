Kurt Manufacturing helps its customers provide decisions on precision when it comes to the manufacturing process. It makes precision-machined parts and components on a contract basis for the aerospace, automotive, oil, and defense markets. Kurt operates through divisions including Hydraulics, Engineered Systems, Kinetic, and Workholding. These divisions make gauging systems, hydraulic coupling and hoses, vises and other workholding tools to a list of clients that have included such big names as ATK , Deere & Co. , General Dynamics , General Electric , General Motors , Honeywell , IBM , and Lockheed Martin . Kurt is an employee owned company.

It Product Solutions segment includes Kurt Hydraulics (hydraulic hose products); Kurt Engineered Systems (precision gauge systems); Kurt Kinetic ( bicycle trainers); and Kurt Workholding (innovative vise products). Its Contract Solutions segment includes Kurt Machining Division Pueblo, Kurt Die Casting Division, Kurt Machining Division Minneapolis, Kurt Gear Division, and Kurt Screw Machining Division.

Kurt claims to hold 50% of the market share on workholding vises, as well as distinct segments in precision gauging and healthcare products.

In 2013 Kurt announced plans to expand its operations in Pueblo, Colorado, by adding equipment to meet capacity for its growing Hydraulics Division.

Supporting its growing vise production line, in 2012 Kurt opened sales and service facilities in China (Kurt Manufacturing Shanghai) to provide onsite technical service and marketing for AngLock vises and accessories across China.

Kurt was founded by Kurt Kuban in 1946.
