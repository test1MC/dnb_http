BakeMark USA is the go-to business for busy bakers. The company makes a variety of ingredients and bakery goods, such as cake and cookie mixes, pie and pastry fillings, various flours and yeast, as well as pre-baked goods and paper supplies. Its lineup is sold under several brand names: Produits Marguerite (nut pastes, mousses, custards for French pastries); Trigal Dorado (mixes and fillings for Mexican pastries); and Westco (baked good mixes and dough). Its primary customers include US retail, wholesale, and in-store bakeries and bakery chains. Along with sister subsidiary BakeMark Canada, BakeMark USA is part of Dutch ingredient maker Corbion 's Bakery Supplies North America business.

From its headquarters in Poco Rivera, California, BakeMark USA provides the nation's retail, wholesale, and bakery customers with its products.

As part of its business, BakeMark USA maintains about 25 branches nationwide to ensure timely delivery of its products to clients.

Corbion's Bakery Supplies North America business generates about half of its sales, so BakeMark USA's parent relies on the company to maintain its momentum year after year.
