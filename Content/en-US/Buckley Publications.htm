Buckley Publications' Business Eye keeps its finger on the pulse of business in Northern Ireland while tickling the ribs of its readers. The company publishes both print and online versions of its monthly magazine, Business Eye , which specializes in providing business news with a humorous point of view. The title has about 7,600 subscribers. The company also produces the UTV Business Eye Awards, an annual event that honors Northern Ireland's leading companies. Buckley Publications first published Business Eye in 1999. Former Ulster Business editor Richard Buckley and his wife Brenda Buckley control the company.
