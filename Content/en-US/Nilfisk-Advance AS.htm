Turning dirt into gold -- it's one of the oldest professions at Nilfisk-Advance. Tracing its roots to the early 1900s, the company has grown to rival the world's top manufacturers of professional cleaning equipment. Nilfisk-Advance makes and sells a slew of vacuums, including backpack vacuums branded Backuum, scrubber-dryers, ride-on sweepers and burnishers, and carpet extractors. Nilfisk offers financing and purchase programs to contract cleaners and after-sales services. Through a network of dealers and branches, Nilfisk-Advance equipment is marketed worldwide to contract cleaners, institutions, building and construction business, and private households. The company is part of the NKT group of companies.
