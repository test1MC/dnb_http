Metalúrgica Gerdau is the holding company for Gerdau SA and its subsidiaries across North and South America. A member of the Gerdau Group, Metalúrgica Gerdau, through its control of Gerdau SA, has grown from a maker of nails to become Brazil's #1 long-rolled steel producer. The group produces about 18 million tons of specialty steel for use in automotive, construction, agricultural, and industrial markets. Its other products include billets, wire rods, bars, and ingots. With a combined installed capacity of more than 25 million tons of steel a year, Metalúrgica Gerdau operates in 14 countries throughout the Americas. The founding Gerdau Johannpeter family controls Metalúrgica Gerdau.

The company operates in Argentina, Brazil, Canada, Chile, Colombia, Spain, the US, Guatemala, India, Mexico, Peru, the Dominican Republic, Uruguay, and Venezuela.

Metalúrgica Gerdau's segments are:

Brazil (operations in Brazil, except Special Steels, and the operation of metallurgical coal and coke in Colombia):

Iron Ore (operations in Brazil);

North America (except those of Mexico and Special Steels);

Latin America (all operations in Latin America, except Brazil and the operation of metallurgical coal and coke in Colombia).

Special Steel (special steel operations in Brazil, Spain, the US and India).

Metalúrgica Gerdau sells through independent distributors, direct sales, from the mills and its retail network.

Industries served include such as construction, manufacturing, and agriculture.

The company's net revenues have risen over last five years. In 2014 Metalúrgica Gerdau's net revenue increased by 7% in local currency due to higher North American revenues (as the result of currency exchange variation and, to a lesser extent, higher net sales per ton sold).

The company's net income has been decreasing over a period of years (2010-2014), except a slight increase in 2013. In 2014 Metalúrgica Gerdau's net income decreased by 12%, primarily due to asset impairments.

Operating cash inflow decreased by 37% due to lower net income and changes in working capital.

Metalúrgica Gerdau mainly focuses on the acquisition or construction of steel mills located close to its customers and sources of the raw materials required for steel production, such as scrap metal, pig iron and iron ore.

In 2013 Metalúrgica Gerdau invested R$ 2.6 billion in fixed assets, mainly for projects already underway.
