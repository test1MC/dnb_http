Horstman Defence Systems wants your ride to be smooth whether its a battle tank or something lighter. The company designs and builds military vehicle suspension and running gear systems for ultra lightweight machines up to the heaviest battle tanks. It also offers machining, assembly, and test services, with expertise in materials such as titanium, ferralium, and nimonics. Other services include program management and logistic support. Clients come from around the world and include government entities, such as the UK Ministry of Defence and Canada's National Defence Forces, and military contractors, such as BAE Systems , Doosan Mottrol, L-3 , and Rheinmetall . Horstman traces its roots to 1913 when Sidney Horstmann began making racing cars.

Horstman Defence makes products for a range of vehicles, from ultra lightweight-wheeled vehicles, C130 air portable vehicles, through infantry and engineer vehicles to heavy battle tanks. Its suspension products include Hydrogas Systems, Hydrostrut Systems, InArm Systems, Integral Rotary Dampers, Lever Arm Rotary Dampers, and Running Gear and Track Tensioners.

From its base in Bath in the UK, Horstman fields other operations in the US (Michigan) and Canada (Toronto).

Horstman's customers include some of the biggest military contractors such as BAE Systems which uses Horstman products in its Warrior family of vehicles and General Dynamics .
