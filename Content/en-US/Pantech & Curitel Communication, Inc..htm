Pantech & Curitel is a master of mobile technology. One of the leading global makers of cellular telephone handsets, the company makes a variety of mobile products including handsets tailored for use in most major global cell phone markets, as well as fixed wireless terminals which enable the connection of traditional phones, fax machines, and other devices to wireless networks. Pantech & Curitel operates worldwide from about 20 regional sales offices. Outside of Korea, the company's key areas of operation include the Americas, China, India, Israel, and Japan. A member of the Pantech Group of wireless products manufacturing and distribution companies, its affiliates include Pantech Co. and Pantech C&I.
