Load King Manufacturing can handle a monarch-sized load. The company makes and installs equipment and fixtures (metal and wood fabrication) for supermarkets, restaurants, and retail stores. Products include counters, shelving systems, sinks, and displays, and its LK Industries division provides turnkey store packages, including designs, equipment, fixtures, millwork, and other services. In addition to installing fixtures and equipment, Load King's specialists and project managers help clients to design, build, and open new stores and help identify ways to cut costs and save money. The company, which is increasingly doing business as LK Industries, can also design artwork for signage, menu boards, and logos.
