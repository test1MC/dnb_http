Unilever UK is one of the numerous companies that comprise the Unilever Global Empire. The UK unit makes and markets consumer products organized along its foods, home, and personal care subsidiaries. To this end, Unilever UK manages a vast products portfolio of noteworthy names. Its foods business includes such brands as Bertolli, Cornetto, Marmite, Slim-Fast, and PG tips. The company also boasts some 40 laundry, household cleaning, and health and beauty brands, which are sold under the labels Cif, Domestos, Dove, Ponds, Sun, and Surf among others. The company operates its UK subsidiaries from its Leatherhead headquarters.

Unilever UK is based in Leatherhead, Surrey, and has manufacturing sites at Crumlin in Gwent, Burton-on-Trent in Staffordshire, Trafford Park in Manchester, and Purfleet in Essex.

The company has five business divisions. Its brands include Knorr Savoury, Beverages, Dressings and Local Jewels (Hellmann's and Marmite), Spreads and Caterplan (food service labels).
