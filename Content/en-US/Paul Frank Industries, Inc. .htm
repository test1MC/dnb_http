Mix a little cartoon monkey with Naugahyde and you get Paul Frank Industries (PFI). The firm has been capitalizing on co-founder Paul Frank's cartoon Julius monkey icon -- and the cute-appeal wave he's been riding -- since the company started in 1997. It also boasts characters Skurvy, Clancy, Sheree, and Worry Bear. Its product lines have expanded from plastic wallets to a themed wardrobe for men and women, children's clothes, bedding, kids furniture, and accessories ranging from eyewear and watches to print towels. PFI also does product collaborations with other pop-icon owners. The company was acquired by an affiliate of the Los Angeles-based investment firm Saban Capital Group (SCG) in 2010.
