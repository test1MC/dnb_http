Breakfast is served by Wells Bloomfield. The company makes and markets products such as coffee grinders, coffee and tea brewers, and hot water dispensers. It also manufactures small kitchen appliances, including waffle makers, griddles, and toasters, as well as large appliances the likes of convection ovens and fryers. Wells Bloomfield, which caters to foodservice and beverage systems businesses, boasts a network of 1,200 distributors worldwide. Founded by Samuel Bloomfield in 1933, the firm was bought by Beatrice in 1964 and became part of UTC in 2000 through its Carrier Commercial Refrigeration . The Middleby Corporation eventually bought Wells Bloomfield in August 2007.
