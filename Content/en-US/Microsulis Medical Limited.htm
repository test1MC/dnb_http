Microsulis Medical is a UK-based company that uses microwave technology to create therapeutic medical equipment. It has designed products with a variety of applications, including oncology, gynecology, and vascular surgery, but its flagship product is the Acculis MTA (microwave tissue ablation) system, a soft tissue ablation device used by clinicians primarily for cancerous tumor removal. The company is part of AngioDynamics ' oncology and vascular surgery segments, which contribute some 15% and 30% of the parent's total revenue, respectively.
