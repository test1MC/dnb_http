ASKO Appliances designs and manufactures high-end ASKO and Eurotech brand washers, dryers, and dishwashers. Its energy- and water-efficient products are scaled to fit homeowners' needs, with units ranging in size from small to large. The Swedish-designed ASKO and Italian-designed Eurotech appliances are made in the US, and they are distributed throughout the US, Canada, and Mexico. Formerly named AM Appliance Group, ASKO Appliances is the North American division of the Antonio Merloni Group of Italy.
