Dryvit Systems manufactures exterior insulation and finish systems (EIFS) for residential and commercial projects. The company's products include acrylic-based exterior finishes and coatings that are resistant to dirt and mildew and resemble brick, tile, stone, slate, adobe, and other materials. The company also makes adhesives and base coats, primers and sealers, glass fiber reinforcing mesh, air barriers, and drainage accessories. It operates four manufacturing plants in the US and has production facilities and offices in Canada, Poland, and China. The company claims its finish systems have been used on more than 400,000 projects worldwide. RPM International owns Dryvit Systems.
