Amway International made its name getting people to sell vitamins and other products to friends and relatives. Imagine the potential in a nation as populous as China. That's just what Amway founder Richard DeVos did after visiting Beijing in the early '90s. The result of his musings is Amway (China), founded in 1992. The firm operates more than 220 outlets in 180 cities across the country, offering some 200 products in Amway's cosmetics, personal care, household, and nutrition and wellness categories.
