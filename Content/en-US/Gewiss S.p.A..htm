Gee whiz, Gewiss makes a lot of electrical products, more than 18,000! The company manufactures home automation (domotics) electrical parts and low-voltage equipment for residential and commercial electrical, appliance, and industrial markets worldwide. Its eight facilities produce fuse boxes to outlets, and switches. Lighting, such as indoor and outdoor floodlights, and road lighting is offered. Moreover, Gewiss touts lamps created by Italian design firm Pininfarina . Gewiss also makes industrial socket outlets resistant to harsh UV rays, salt water, chemicals, and grease. Customers are commercial resellers, builders, and automation service providers. Chairman Domenico Bosatelli owns more than 75% of Gewiss.
