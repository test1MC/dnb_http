In a unique arrangement of business services, Oakridge Holdings can offer customers support and service, whether they are flying high or lying low, above the ground or below it. Through two subsidiaries, (Oakridge Cemetery and Glen Oak Cemetery), it buries human remains, and through another (Stinar Corporation), it makes aviation ground support equipment (lifts, truck mounted stairways, water and catering trucks). Oakridge provides its customers internment, burial plots, crypts, and cremation, as well as stairways for loading aircraft, and lavatory, water, cabin cleaning, and catering trucks for servicing airplanes.
