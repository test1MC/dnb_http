Halex Corporation wouldn't mind if you walk all over them. In fact, you may be stepping all over the flooring products it makes and not even know it. Halex manufactures and distributes products used in flooring installation, such as carpet tack strips, seam tape, flooring nails, plywood underlayment, and height adjustment spacers. The company's Southern California headquarters location houses a technical lab that conducts stability and resilience testing on items such as plywood and nails; from that facility it also distributes products to North and South America, Europe, and Japan. Halex operates a factory complex in Suqian, Jiangsu, China, to meet customer demand in the Asia/Pacific region.
