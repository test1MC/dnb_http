Office drones and couch potatoes unite in their delight when projections glint off their Da-Lite screens. The Da-Lite Screen Company manufactures electrically operated, wall-mounted, and portable projection screens, which are primarily used for professional presentations and home theaters. The firm also engineers rear projection systems and offers related accessories, such as A/V equipment carts, projector mounts, easels, lecterns, and computer tables. Da-Lite has manufacturing facilities in the US and Europe and sells its products through a distributor network that spans more than 100 countries. Audiovisual accessories and equipment supplier Milestone AV Technologies acquired Da-Lite for $202 million in 2011.
