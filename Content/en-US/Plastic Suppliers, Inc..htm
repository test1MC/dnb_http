Plastic Suppliers is in the film industry, but you probably won't find a wanna-be Cecil B. DeMille or Robert Rodriguez there. The company manufactures and distributes plastic (polystyrene, biopolymer, and other poly substrate-based) films, for such applications as flexible packaging, floral sleeves, labels, lamination, shrink films, and tamper evident seals. Plastic Suppliers capabilities include standard master rolls, as well as custom-width roll manufacture, and slitting and sheeting. The company has its manufacturing operations in Ohio; it also owns regional distribution facilities throughout the US, and in Europe. Its specialty films subsidiary, Sidaplax, is established in Belgium.

Plastic Suppliers has locations in Los Angeles; Chicago; Dallas; Columbus, Ohio; and Ghent Belgium.

Plastic Suppliers is growing its share of the plastic films market, as well as countering volatile resin and oil prices, by expanding its lineup with an eco-friendly (fully compostable) biopolymer film. Made from corn, a renewable resource, and polylactide polymer called Ingeo by NatureWorks , the film is marketed under the EarthFirst brand name. Among several applications, it is being used as a liner for pet food packaging, and sleeve label for tangerine juice.

The company has distribution centers across the US -- from Ohio to California -- and across the world in Belgium. The company's distribution partners include Bio Packaging Films, OU Pont, Granwell, And Inteplast Group .

Plastic Suppliers was founded in New Jersey in 1949. It opened a converting facility in Dallas in 1959. In 1985 the company purchased Sidaplax VOF in Ghent, Belgium.
