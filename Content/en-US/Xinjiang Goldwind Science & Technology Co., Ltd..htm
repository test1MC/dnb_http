Xinjiang Goldwind Science & Technology Co. (doing business as Goldwind) hopes its wind turbines make money fall from the sky. The company is China's second-largest turbine manufacturer (behind Sinovel ) and one of the top five manufacturers in the world. It makes 750 kW, 1.5 MW, and 2.5 MW turbine generators for onshore use; a 6 MW model is in development. It has manufacturing plants in China and Germany through subsidiary VENSYS Energy AG. The company also invests and develops wind farms and has installed more than 8,200 turbines across China. Goldwind has some 60 subsidiaries and international offices in Australia, Germany, South Africa, and the US. China Three Gorges Corporation owns 26% of the company.
