Red Smith Foods packs a plethora of puckery products. The company makes pickled snack foods, including sausages; eggs; and various pig parts, including feet, neck bones, and hocks (the little porker's leg from about halfway down to but not including the hoof). The firm distributes its Red Smith and Big John's branded items to wholesalers, distribution companies, convenience and sport supply stores, and supermarkets in the southeastern US. The company also has an online retail subsidiary, Pickle-it.com, which sells the above-mentioned pickled snacks, along with pickle-themed clothing and other merchandise, directly to consumers. Located in Davie, Florida, Red Smith Foods is family owned and operated.
