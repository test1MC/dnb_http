Oeneo is there when wine goes from vat to barrel and bottle to glass. The company produces natural and synthetic corks and screw tops along with aging barrels for the wine industry. The company's cork operations (run through key subsidiary Oeneo Bouchage) extend from the selection of cork oak trees through final production. Its products include natural cork stoppers as well as those made partly from synthetic materials. Oeneo conducts operations in 22 countries worldwide. The company's US presence, Diam Closures USA , is managed by G3 Enterprises (a Gallo business). Oeneo continues to acquire cork and barrel producing companies as it expands internationally.
