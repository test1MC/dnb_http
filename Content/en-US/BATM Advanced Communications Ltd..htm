BATM Advanced Communications manufactures voice and data networking and connectivity hardware. The company's products include optical communications devices ranging from photonic switches, hubs, routers, and media converters to high-speed digital subscriber line (DSL) modems, port modules, and network interface cards. BATM distributes its products in North America through US-based subsidiary Telco Systems . The company also has distribution agreements with Sprint North Supply and PC Connection . CEO and director Zvi Marom owns about a quarter of BATM.
