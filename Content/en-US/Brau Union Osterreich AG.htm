As they join together as members of the European Union, Austrians, Bulgarians, Croatians, Czechs, Hungarians, Macedonians, Poles, Romanians, and Slovakians ( Whew, what a crowd! ) are all welcome to drink beer in the g astgarten (guest garden) of Brau Union Österreich. The Austrian brewer, created from Heineken 's purchase of Österreichische Brau-Beteiligungs-AG , sells the well-known Heineken, Guinness, Edelweiss, Gösser, and Zipfer brands, as well as other popular regional brands. Considered the region's largest brewer, Brau Union has a 52% market share of the Austrian beer market and dominates rest of the Central European beer market as well. The brewer's major shareholder is Heineken International B.V.
