Jada Toys will bring out the little boy in any man. The company makes die-cast collectible cars and model cars, as well as remote controlled vehicles, based on customized, modified street vehicles such as low riders, monster trucks, and sports cars. Jada Toys' brands, well known to collectors, include DUB City, Big Time Muscle, Option D, High Profile, and Chub City. The company also holds the toy licenses for several popular brands including Guitar Hero and Speed Racer.
