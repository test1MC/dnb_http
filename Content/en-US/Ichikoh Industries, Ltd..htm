While magicians are prone to create smoke and mirrors, Ichikoh Industries wants to clear the smoke from your mirrors. The auto parts company is a leader in the Japanese market for rearview mirrors, car lamps, and wipers intended to help driving visibility. It also makes wheel covers, radiator grilles, switches, and reflectors. Major customers include Honda , Mazda , Mitsubishi , Nissan , Toyota , and Fuji Heavy Industries (which owns Subaru ). Its products are used in passenger cars as well as racing cars, cargo trucks, and the Japanese bullet train. Ichikoh Industries has sales offices and plants located in China, Indonesia, Japan, Malaysia, Taiwan, and the US.
