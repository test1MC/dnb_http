A Chicago fixture since 1912, Chicago Hardware & Fixture makes wire rope, chain fittings, and other industrial and marine hardware, including turnbuckles, clips, bolts, hoist rings, swivels, rods, nuts, and pins. It also manufactures custom parts. The company serves customers in agriculture, aerospace, shipping, and mining, among other industries. Chicago Hardware & Fixture operates three plants in the Chicago Metropolitan Area and has warehouse stocking facilities in California, Georgia, Illinois, and Texas. Founded by John A. Herbstritt, as the Chicago Warehouse and Silo Fixture Co., the business adopted its current name in 1926.
