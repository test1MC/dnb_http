Swann-Morton is on the cutting edge of cutting edges. The company is a leading global manufacturer of surgical blades, scalpels, and handles. It offers a range of products that include about 70 individual blade shapes and 30 handles for use by health care professionals in a variety of medical and dental procedures, as well as for arts and crafts in which precision cutting is necessary. Swann-Morton sells its instruments in more than 100 countries through its extensive network of overseas distributors. About two-thirds of its sales originate outside the UK.

The company has a presence in Africa, Australasia, Caribbean, Central America, Europe & Scandinavia, Far East & Asia, Middle East, North America and South America.

The company's daily production of surgical and craft blades runs at about 1.5 million. Swann-Morton also operates a state-of-the-art irradiation center and microbiological laboratory, which it contracts out to other health care manufacturers through Swann-Morton (Microbiological Laboratory Services) Ltd and Swann-Morton (Services) Ltd.

Swann-Morton (Microbiological Laboratory Services) Ltd uses a purpose built microbiology laboratory providing an expert service for monitoring and identifying microbiological contamination, both environmental and product borne.

Swann-Morton (Services) Ltd uses gamma radiation from a Cobalt 60 source to irradiate products in a state of the art computer controlled continuous irradiation facility. The high quality service is available to other producers and suppliers.

Swann-Morton (Services), in conjunction with Swann-Morton (Microbiological Laboratory Services), can provide a dose setting and dose auditing service to determine the required absorbed dose to provide a sterile medical device.

The company offers its products to healthcare professionals such as GPs, nurses, and paramedics as well as practitioners in dentistry, podiatry, and veterinary surgery. Swann-Morton distributors in the US include Cincinnati Surgical, DeRoyal Industries (Surgical Care unit), and Propper Manufacturing Co.

The company was founded as W. R. Swann & Co. in 1932, a razor blade manufacturer, by Walter Swann and Alfred Morton.
