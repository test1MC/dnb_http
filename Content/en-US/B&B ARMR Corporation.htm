B&B ARMR knows the business of traffic control products. It makes crash barriers and security gates, as well as railroad crossing safety barriers, anti-terrorist barricades, and warning gates. The company's barriers are used at schools, power plants, and at government and commercial facilities, including the Pentagon, the San Diego Naval Station, and a number of overseas embassies. Its sells its products through distributors, installers, and system-integrators. In early 2011, B&B ARMR's parent company, Integrated Security Systems, sold the perimeter security business to investors and members of Texas-based Strait Lane Capital Partners for a reported $6 million. B&B ARMR traces its roots back to 1925.
