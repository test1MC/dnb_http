Bluesocket develops software and hardware that allows secure access to wireless networks in airports, hotels, offices, and schools. Its BlueSecure products -- which are used with laptops and wireless communications devices -- incorporate signal encryption technology for security, guarding against computer viruses, spam e-mail, and hacker attacks. The company's BlueView Management System software enables the configuration, monitoring, and management of multiple WLANs. Customers have included Harvard , Honeywell , and Toyota . Established in 1999, Bluesocket received private equity funding from investors including Ironside Ventures, Menlo Ventures , and Ridgewood Capital prior to its acquisition by ADTRAN in 2011.
