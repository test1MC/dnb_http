If a company wants to ship bulk liquids, it needs containers for its products; that's why Hoover Materials Handling Group exists. The company, founded in 1911, manufactures reusable intermediate bulk containers (IBCs) for liquids (Liquitote) and provides a tracking service to monitor the whereabouts of the containers (iTRAM). Hoover Materials Handling Group also has a container rental program and allows customers to trade old equipment in when purchasing new products. The company operates from its headquarters/manufacturing plant in Houston, Texas; it also has regional offices in Louisiana and Illinois.
