Wi-Fi TV (formerly Kanakaris Wireless) asks why wait when you can see it now? The company provides live TV channels and other video based content through its Wi-FiTV.com Web site. It offers access to more than 450 TV stations from some 50 countries. Wi-Fi TV also offers interactive features that let registered members post personal profile pages, create their own Wi-Fi TV Video Stations, and save their favorite places on their Wi-Fi TV personal page. The company also sells Wi-Fi TV stations to individuals, businesses, and social and civic organizations, and other groups for $25,000. Former chairman Alex Kanakaris founded Wi-Fi TV in 1995.
