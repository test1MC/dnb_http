Minnesota? No. Mining? No. Manufacturing? Yes. 3M United Kingdom (3M UK) is the British arm of US-based global diversified chemical and consumer products firm 3M Company . Among 3M's well known product lines are Scotch-brand tape and Post-it Notes. 3M Health Care Ltd. , whose business makes up a large component of 3M UK, makes medical equipment and supplies like respirators and drug delivery systems. The company also manufactures coated abrasives, adhesives, paints, print ink, mastics, high-performance coatings, secure documents, and passport scanners.

3M UK, in tandem with 3M Ireland, is among its parent company's largest regional operations.

The company serves a range of industries, including automotive, commercial solutions, communications, consumer, design and construction, electronics, energy, health care, manufacturing, mining, oil and gas, safety, and transportation. It sells products via distributors and online.

The company is looking to expand primarily through organic growth and selective complementary acquisitions.

3M has been working in the UK since 1929, and 3M UK was its parent company's first expansion outside of the US. The unit was formally established in as 3M Manufacturing Limited in 1951. It became 3M UK in 1989.

The company broadened its Occupational Health and Environmental Safety Division product line in 2010 by acquiring UK-based Dailys Ltd., which supplies chemical coveralls, lab coats, accessories, and other disposable protective clothing.

In 2012 the company acquired assets from the Federal Signal technologies group, including the trade and assets of PiPs Technology Limited and Diamond Consulting Services Limited. In 2012 and 2013 it also acquired fellow subsidiary, 3M HealthCare Ltd. as part of the parent's group restructuring plan.
