When its time to shore-up your understanding of publishing markets call Shore Communications. Shore Communications provides research and advisory services for the information content industry. The company helps clients understand their customers, define their strategy, and implement product and marketing plans through a research products and consulting services. Offerings include trend analysis, goal and strategy setting, vendor analysis and selection, content design, training, usability analysis, and product rollout support. Shore Communications also provides marketplace information through news headlines, blogs, news analysis, newsletters, and private advisory services.
