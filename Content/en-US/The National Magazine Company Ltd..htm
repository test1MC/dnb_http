This publisher knows the UK can be just as cosmopolitan as any other nation. The National Magazine Company, doing business as Hearst Magazines UK, publishes about 25 print magazine brands and produces some 20 digital assets in the UK, including Cosmopolitan, Harper's Bazaar, Esquire , and Good Housekeeping . Websites include women's site handbag.com and consumer health site netdoctor.co.uk. The company also has magazine distribution operations. It is a subsidiary of diversified media company The Hearst Corporation . William Randolph Hearst established a UK presence in 1910 with the purchase of Nash's , a British literary magazine that published fictional works from Rudyard Kipling and George Bernard Shaw.
