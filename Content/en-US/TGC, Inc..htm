This network covers the action from tee to green. TGC owns and operates The Golf Channel featuring 24-hour coverage of all things golf, including PGA TOUR , LPGA , and Champions Tour tournaments. Reaching about 120 million homes worldwide, the sports network offers news and talk shows, instructional programs, and entertainment programming. TGC also operates a popular website offering golfing news, online videos, and other features. Media entrepreneur Joseph Gibbs and golf legend Arnold Palmer launched The Golf Channel in 1995. TGC is part of the portfolio of networks owned by NBCUniversal , one of the world's largest media and entertainment companies.

The Golf Channel is available in more than 60 countries around the world.

Like other television sports networks, The Golf Channel depends on live event coverage to draw in viewers. It kicked off a 10-year broadcasting deal with the LPGA Tour in 2010 that gives the channel rights to air exclusive three- and four-round coverage of domestic events, including one championship. The channel also serves as the LPGA's US broadcasting outlet during international events. In addition, The Golf Channel has a 15-year broadcasting package with the PGA TOUR that started in 2007.

The Golf Channel is embracing the online gaming community hoping to give its audience the ability to virtually play top golf courses.
