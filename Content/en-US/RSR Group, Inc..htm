RSR Group has its sights fixed on the shooting sports industry. The company distributes firearms, optical accessories, gun cases, cleaning equipment and tools, and related gear to retailers throughout the US. It also offers knives and safety products, such as pepper spray and batons. RSR carries more than 12,000 products from 200 manufacturers, including Remington , Smith & Wesson , Mace Security International , and Pelican . It operates five sales centers across the US and a distribution center in Texas. The company was founded in 1977. In 2005 the RSR family of companies merged into the RSR Group, its parent company.
