Walsin Lihwa produces a variety of wire and cable products for use in the power, telecommunications, and transportation industries. Bare copper wire, power and communication cables, fiber optics, steel wires, and optical cables are among its main products, which are sold in Taiwan and China and distributed worldwide. Walsin Lihwa's specialty steel division manufactures billets and slabs, wire rods, straight bars, and stainless steel products. The company additionally invests in high-tech companies such as HannStar Board, HannStar Display , Passive System Alliance, and Winbond Electronics .
