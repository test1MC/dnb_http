AEM Holdings is taking a big bite out of the chip industry. Through numerous subsidiaries, the company designs and makes semiconductor manufacturing equipment, precision engineering products, electroplating chemicals, and organic substrates (raw materials used to produce semiconductor chips). It caters to the global electronics industry, and specifically to companies in semiconductor assembly, testing, and finishing. AEM operates manufacturing plants in China, Malaysia, and Singapore, and markets its products through a network of associates and distributors around the world. The company was established in 2000 when AEM-Tech International and Ever Technologies merged. To capitalize on the growing global demand for substrates, AEM-Evertech acquired Malaysia's Qualitek Electronics (M) in early 2007. AEM-Evertech plans to upgrade the company's existing Malacca manufacturing plant (which is strategically located near several of AEM's existing customers) for substrates production.
