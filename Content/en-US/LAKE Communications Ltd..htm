LAKE Communications is a manufacturer of telecommunications networking hardware and software. The company's products include wired (Centrex) and wireless (Airway) telephone systems, wireless data network connection points (HomeLink, NetLink, and OfficeLink), and associated networking software. LAKE supplies its products to other manufacturers of communications products. The company's products are intended for use primarily in residential applications as well as in small businesses and corporate branch offices.
