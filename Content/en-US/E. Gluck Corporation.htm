E. Gluck wonders, "Do you have the time?" The company makes quartz watches that bear monikers such as Anne Klein, Nine West , and its own Armitron. Its timepieces also feature characters from Disney , Mattel , and Warner Bros , as well as the designer labels of Lucky Brand and JLO by Jennifer Lopez. E. Gluck uses edgy styling, microfiber bands, and digital colors to attract younger customers, as well as interchangeable bracelets and bezels (and even diamonds) for the more sophisticated set. Its watches, which carry a lifetime guarantee on working parts, are sold through jewelers, department stores, and specialty retailers. Owner and CEO Eugen Gluck founded the company in 1955.
