No, it's not a magazine about hair bands. Metal Bulletin publishes periodicals on the international iron, steel, and non-ferrous metal markets. In total, Metal Bulletin publishes pricing information for more than 900 metals and provides paid subscribers with a variety of print and online publications, including Metal Bulletin Weekly. It also publishes industry-specific books, CD-ROMs, directories, e-newsletters, reports, and analyses. Tracing its historical roots back to 1913, Metal Bulletin is a subsidiary of Euromoney Institutional Investor , an international finance publisher owned by Daily Mail and General Trust .

Metal Bulletin has a network of correspondents, analysts, and researchers around the globe. In addition to its editorial headquarters located in London, the company's staff is located in Detroit, Los Angeles, New York, Pittsburgh, Philadelphia, and Washington, DC. It also has staff placed in Mangalore and Mumbai, India; Shanghai, Singapore; Sao Paolo; Santiago; Toronto; and Tehran, Iran.
