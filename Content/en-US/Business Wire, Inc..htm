Some company news is none of your business and some is everyone's business -- that's where Business Wire comes in. The firm electronically distributes company press releases and other information to news media, online services, and databases worldwide. Business Wire disseminates full-text news releases in more than 200 industry segments. It has partnerships with major news organizations such as Agence France-Presse , Associated Press , Bloomberg , Dow Jones , and Thomson Reuters , as well as regional and local news agencies. Lorry Lokey founded Business Wire in 1961. It is a subsidiary of Warren Buffett's Berkshire Hathaway .

Business Wire has more than 30 offices and 20 newsrooms located throughout North America, Europe (Frankfurt, London, And Paris) and Asia-Pacific (Sydney And Tokyo).

The company delivers news directly into editorial systems and leading online news sources, while working to boost its clients SEO and social media engagement.

Business Wire has been busy expanding its offerings to keep up with technological advancements in the media world. The company has enhanced its mobile, distribution, and social services for press releases, giving clients the ability to reach to mobile users worldwide through a variety of apps, increased sharing opportunities via social networks, and new measurement tools.

In 2013 Business Wire partnered with social media visualization company NUVITM to provide companies and organizations with social media monitoring. NUVITM's state-of-the-art platform (NUVI) offers actionable intelligence based on social data because it allows Business Wire's clients to easily track, respond, engage, and influence social conversations.

The company is constantly working to expand its reach through partnerships and distribution agreements.
