Liquid Controls, a subsidiary of IDEX , makes flow meters and electronic registers. The liquid measurement technology specialist provides high-quality flow meters and accessories for accurate measurement of liquids in custody-transfer and process-control applications throughout the world. The company started out serving the military aircraft fueling industry; it has expanded its reach by serving a broad spectrum of industries, from petroleum production, chemical processing, liquefied gases, and hydrocarbon processing to asphalt, adhesives, pulp and paper, cosmetics, and more. Liquid Controls was established in the 1950s and acquired by IDEX in 2001, becoming part of the Fluid & Metering Technologies segment.
