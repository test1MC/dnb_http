Keepsake Quilting publishes books about quilts. Titles include Quilting Basics , Quilts With a Spin, Sew Simple Applique , and Growing Up With Quilts . The company also operates a quilt retail shop in New Hampshire that sells handmade American quilts, as well as quilting supplies such as cotton fabric, quilting kits, and books. In addition, it hosts events such as workshops and classes, and its Web site offers information and resources such as kits, patterns, sales, and a classified section for quilts and quilting-related services.
