This Web animation land is JibJab's land.  JibJab Media offers Web sufers the ability to personalize and send digital animated cards (Sendable eCards) and video downloads (JibJab Starring You videos) via its Web site. Many cards and videos are free; a subscription service is necessary for others. The company launched its JibJab Sendables in 2007, and by 2008 more than 170 comedians, writers, artists, musicians, and performers had created over 1,200 titles in the JibJab Sendables library. JibJab was founded by brothers Gregg and Evan Spiridellis in 1999. Investors include Polaris Venture Partners, Overbrook Entertainment, and Sony Pictures Entertainment.
