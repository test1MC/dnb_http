Fighting terrorism is the very serious business focus of Sotera Defense Solutions. The company operates through an array of subsidiaries. It provides software, system engineering, and technology development to help the Department of Defense , CIA , Homeland Security , and other government agencies fight terrorism and enforce national security. Sotera's services include design of mobile computers (called Force Mobility and Modernization Systems) for military clients. Its intelligence business focuses on counter-terrorism and communications systems; a cyber operation collects and analyzes information in cyberspace. The company is owned by an affiliate of investment firm Ares Management .

The company has locations in about half a dozen US states.

Sotera is a provider of mission-critical, technology-based products and services for the Department of Defense , the Department of Homeland Security , the intelligence community, and federal law enforcement agencies.

Sotera delivers systems and services in support of the critical missions and programs of civilian agencies and federal government agencies charged with ensuring the safety and security of the nation.

The company plans to meet evolving national security needs by providing expert analysis, agile and innovative technology, powerful systems, and secure operational support programs tailored to meet its customers' requirements.

In 2014 Sotera was awarded a $60 million contract for the FBI 's Sentinel Engineering and Sustainment Support Program.
