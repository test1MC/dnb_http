With GPS satellites, there's no excuse for getting lost. OSI Geospacial (formerly Offshore Systems International) uses GPS technology to develop electronic chart and situational awareness systems for maritime military and commercial efforts. Its Electronic Chart Precise Integrated Navigation System (ECPINS) measures a ship's heading, speed, and position, displaying information in real time on an electronic chart. The company also offers warship ID systems and nautical mapping services on a contract basis. In 2012 OSI went private under a plan of arrangement with the Business Corporations Act in British Columbia.
