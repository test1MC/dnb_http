New Orleans Coffee Co. (NOCC) makes and sells instant coffee concentrate made with Arabica coffee beans and chicory root under the CoolBrew brand name throughout the US. The company's customers include grocery stores and other food retailers, as well as foodservice operators and other food manufacturers who use it as an ingredient. The company sells CoolBrew in original, and decaf versions. The coffee concentrate also comes in flavored varieties ,such as French roast, vanilla, hazelnut, mocha, and toasted almond. The family-owned company was founded in 1989 by its president Philip McCrory, a pharmacist.
