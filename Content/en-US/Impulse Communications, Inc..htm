Impulse Communications owns and operates thousands of Web sites. Its e-commerce sites offer a variety of consumer products such as flowers, sporting goods, vitamins, and art supplies. Properties include Cheapflowers.com and FindMoney.com. The company also operates content-based sites that generate ad revenue, such as Bored.com, and sites that generate pay-per-click revenue, such as FindJobs.com and GetWealthy.com. Impulse Communications also sells domain names. The company was founded in 1990 as a computer consulting firm.
