Oskaloosa Food Products doesn't mind having egg on its face. The company produces a wide variety of egg products, including liquid, frozen, dried, and custom-blended items, for retail and wholesale customers. It operates an egg-laying facility, a processing plant, and a storage warehouse, and it maintains its own distribution fleet. Oskaloosa Food serves customers throughout the US, as well as overseas.
