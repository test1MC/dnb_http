Nakajima USA designs and markets collectible, plush, and seasonal toys. An agreement with Sanrio (maker of Hello Kitty brand products) puts the company in charge of Sanrio's US wholesale gift and licensed store businesses, as well as the running of corporate-owned Sanrio retail stores. Nakajima USA's additional licensing agreements have included Warner Bros. Entertainment and Disney . Baby CoCo and Pickles make up its original line of plush characters. The company's products are sold in Target , Toys "R" Us , Claire's , and Spencer Gifts . Founded in 2000, Nakajima USA's products are sold through retailers in the US, South America, and Asia. The company is part of Nakajima Corporation .
