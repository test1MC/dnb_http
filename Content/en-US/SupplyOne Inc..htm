A "one source" supply matrix, SupplyOne sells packaging and packaging products through a network of eight specialty packaging manufacturers. The company provides outer and interior packaging, including labels, packaging supplies, and marking systems, to a range of clients in the automotive, consumer products, electronics, food, healthcare, manufacturing and retail industries. SupplyOne has nearly 15 manufacturing facilities, mainly located in the Southwest and East Coast. It also has a manufacturing facility located in northern Mexico. The company maintains an equal number of distributions centers, but they are concentrated on the West Coast and the South.

The company operates 9 distribution centers and 21 locations across Arizona, Florida, New Jersey, New Mexico, New York, North Carolina, Ohio, Oklahoma, Pennsylvania, South Carolina, Texas, Virginia, and Wisconsin.

SupplyOne serves across a range of industries, including automotive, consumer products, electronics, food and beverage, manufacturing, healthcare, medical, moving and Storage, and retail services.

The company grows organically and via acquisitions.

Expanding SupplyOne's presence in South Carolina with the addition of corrugated manufacturing to its established distribution facility, in 2014 SupplyOne acquired the Spartanburg division of TimBar Packaging and Display, a full service provider of corrugated and custom packaging based in Roebuck, South Carolina.

In 2013 the company acquired Budget Box & Bag, a distributor of corrugated boxes and packaging supplies with locations in Oklahoma City and Norman, Oklahoma.

Founded in 1998, the company set out to expand through acquisitions. It acquired Oklahoma-based CPD Tech Pack, in 2001.

In 2010 SupplyOne acquired Specialty Container, a commercial and industrial corrugated packaging company based in Dallas, Texas. Specialty Container brought its own package of offerings, including design, manufacturing, printing, storage, distribution, and fulfillment, as well as new items such as labels and plastic thermoforming, that will complemented, as well as expanded, SupplyOne's existing portfolio of products and services.
