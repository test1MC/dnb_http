Carmel Container Systems has nothing to do with sugary-sticky cellophane. The company is the largest packager in Israel, operating plants that produce corrugated cardboard containers, board sheets, triple-wall containers, and wooden pallets for the agricultural, food and beverage, industrial, and consumer goods industries. The company's C.D. Packaging Systems makes consumer packaging from micro-fine corrugated board sheets. Carmel Container Systems is owned by Hadera Paper . The company, which had been traded on the American Stock Exchange , became private in 2005.
