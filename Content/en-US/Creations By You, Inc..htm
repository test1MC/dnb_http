Creations By You sells ideas, and leaves the rest to your imagination. It manufactures art kits to make personalized books, calendars, clocks, watches, mugs, and plates. The art kits supply the raw materials (paper, markers, frames, clockworks), so customers can write, draw, or paste words and pictures to create one-of-a-kind mementos. Creations By You also allows customers to craft products electronically using online applications.
