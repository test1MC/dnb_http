Hold the door! Ensor Holdings, through its subsidiary companies, distributes doors and a whole lot more. Its primary segments manufacture and install residential and industrial doors. Encor Building Products distributes roofing and drainage products, Technocover Limited manufactures and installs high-security steel access products for the utilities market. Wood's Packaging and SRC Limited makes rubber surfacing material, while Ensor Xiamen provides logistical support in China. Its largest segment provides building and security products, representing 90% of its net revenue.

Ensor operates through three segments: building and security products (90% of net sales) manufactures, markets, supplies, and distributes building materials, security access products, and access control equipment; the packaging segment (9%) markets and distributes packaging materials; and the "other" segment (1%) operates waste recycling.

Ensor experienced a 18% surge in revenue from 2014 to 2015. The growth was fueled by a 21% spike in packaging sales and a 20% burst of sales from building and security products.
