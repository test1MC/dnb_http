The food and drink industry is served by William Reed Business Media (WRBM). The company publishes business-to-business magazines such as The Grocer , Convenience Store , and Restaurant Magazine , as well as other industry-specific titles. WRMB also publishes directories and organizes exhibitions, conferences, and other events serving the UK food and drink industry. It supplies database management services through its Knowledge Store unit, and operates industry websites such as thegrocer.co.uk and BigHospitality.co.uk. In recent years WRBM has expanded coverage beyond food and drink into areas such as pharmaceuticals and cosmetics. The firm traces its roots to 1862, when founder William Reed launched The Grocer .
