It is thanks to the hard-working folks at Martini & Rossi that Secret Agent 007 gets to sound oh-so-smartly stylish when ordering his favorite drink. The company, from which the quintessentially "cool" mixture of gin and vermouth takes its name, was founded in Italy in 1863 as Martini, Sola & C. It is, appropriately, a leading producer of vermouth worldwide. Martini & Rossi's beverages include its flagship product, Martini Bianco (vermouth), along with Martini Asit (sparking wines). It is a subsidiary of the Bermuda-based wine and spirits giant, Barcardi & Company .
