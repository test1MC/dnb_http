To be safe and compliant, or to not be safe and compliant, that is the question. And once you've figured out the answer, Environmental, Safety & Health, Inc. (ES&H) will follow through. Serving US companies and government agencies, ES&H provides training and services for safety, health, environmental, and regulatory concerns. The company also offers general contractor services for construction, demolition, and remediation projects. Clients include corporations like Schering-Plough and Arconic , as well as districts or regional divisions of government agencies like the FAA and US Army Corps of Engineers . Founded in 1997, ES&H is based in Tennessee and operates offices in Delaware and Louisiana. It filed for Chapter 7 bankruptcy protection in 2015.
