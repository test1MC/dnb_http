Hawaiian Springs wants consumers to say aloha to agua . The company sells Hawaiian Springs brand bottled water that is sourced from an aquifer on the Big Island of Hawaii, at the base of Mauna Loa (the world's largest volcano). The water, filtered naturally through 13,000 feet of porous lava rock, has been found by the US Geological Survey to be some of the purest in the world. Using little mechanical processing, Hawaiian Springs water is free of heavy metals, chemicals, and excessive mineral content. It is available to consumers, retailers, wholesalers, and hotels and restaurants in Hawaii, the US mainland, and in select international markets.
