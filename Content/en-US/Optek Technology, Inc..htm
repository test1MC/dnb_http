Optek Technology senses its customers needs. A manufacturer of optoelectronic components, Optek makes infrared, magnetic, and fiber-optic sensors that detect motion, position, and ultraviolet and infrared light, among other things. Manufacturers use its sensors in office and industrial equipment, cars, medical devices, and point-of-sale equipment. The company also makes LED assemblies, as well as fiber-optic network transmitters and receivers for telecom networks. It provides such services as wafer fabrication, mold tooling, plastic manufacturing, final testing, and assembly. Optek sells worldwide through an extensive network of distributors and resellers. The company is a subsidiary of UK-based TT electronics .

Based in Carrollton, Texas, north of Dallas, Optek has additional offices overseas in Germany, the UK, and China.

The company was founded in 1979 as Crown Semiconductor and changed its name to Optek in 1984. Electronic components and sensor manufacturer TT electronics plc has owned Optek since 2003, when it acquired the company from Dyson-Kissner-Moran .
