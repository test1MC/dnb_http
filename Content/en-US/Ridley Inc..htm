Ridley manufactures and markets animal nutrition and health products and services to egg, meat, and milk producers, as well as horse and other animal owners, throughout North America. The company's products include feed and premixes, meal, pellets, and blocks sold in bulk and various other sizes. Ridley's services include nutrition and sales support for retailers. Other customers include dealers and mass merchandisers. Operating through four main divisions, Ridley's brands include Hubbard Feeds, Stockade Brands, and McCauley Bros. Former controlling owner Fairfax Financial Holdings sold Ridley to US-based animal health biotech firm Alltech in mid-2015.

In 2012 Ridley merged its Canadian commercial feed business, Feed-Rite, with Ag Processing subsidiary Masterfeeds to form the second-largest feed provider in Canada, Masterfeeds LP. The new entity operates 22 manufacturing plants across the Quebec, Ontario, and Prairie provinces. Ridley and Ag Processing each own relative shares in Masterfeeds LP.

The company's block operations division acquired privately held Kansas company Stockade Brands in late 2012. The maker of blocks, loose minerals, and dried molasses products serves the livestock and equine markets. The deal gives Ridley a deeper products menu and access to new marketing channels in the region.
