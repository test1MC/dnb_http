Environmental Recycling Technologies is molding its future. It develops plastic molding processes for the automotive and other industries. The holding company operates through subsidiaries. A leader in plastics recycling technology, the group focuses on developing building products using powder impression molding (PIM) technology. PIM is used to produce a strengthened plastic composite comparable to metal, which is used for products in the construction, automotive, marine, aviation, and other markets. The company licenses its PIM system for both specific products and to geographical areas of the globe to generate fees and royalties. Chairman Ken Brooks and director Bill Widger started the company in 1997.
