Abbeycrest's UK jewelry production is officially past its peak. Abbeycrest, founded in 1979, has spent the past couple years restructuring its business and transitioning all production to Thailand (named Abbeycrest Thailand Limited or ATL) to focus its UK operations on distribution and other functions. One of the UK's leading jewelry designers and manufacturers, the company's products are sold through retailers, such as discount giant ASDA . In addition to gemsets (gold jewelry inset with gemstones, such as rings, necklaces, and pendants), Abbeycrest makes jewelry parts, including clasps and chains. The company boasts manufacturing facilities in Bangkok, Thailand, and the UK.
