These papers cover more than international news. The UK newspaper subsidiary of US-based News Corp. , News International publishes leading national daily The Times (with a circulation of about 500,000) and its weekend companion The Sunday Times , along with London tabloid The Sun . News International also publishes online editions of its flagship publications. In addition to its UK publications, News Corporation also has papers in Australia and the US, including The Australian , the Wall Street Journal (owned through Dow Jones ) and the New York Post .

The company has a global portfolio of newspapers.

News Corporation has focused on boosting the number paid subscribers reading its papers while cutting costs. To that end, Rupert Murdoch has announced plans to make online editions of his newspapers available only to paying customers and blocking access to the papers' content by news aggregation and indexing services, such as Google 's Google News.
