Corks are popping at The Wine Enthusiast Companies. The company publishes Wine Enthusiast Magazine and operates a catalog business featuring wine-related products such as wine racks, cabinets, decanters, glassware, and corkscrews. Wine Enthusiast markets its products via direct mail and the Internet, and through its business-to-business division. In addition, the company publishes wine reviews, as well as information on wine storage. It also provides wine-related education, sponsors events such as consumer and trade wine tastings, and offers wine-cellar design services.
