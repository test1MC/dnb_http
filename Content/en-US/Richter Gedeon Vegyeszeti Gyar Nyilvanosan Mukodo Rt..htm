Gedeon Richter is less interested with Earth's tremors than it is with rumblings caused by human pain and ailments. Hungary's largest producer of pharmaceuticals, the company makes female hormones, ACE inhibitors, anti-anxiety drugs and OTC pain relievers, anti-inflammatories, and vitamin supplements. It specializes in gynecological, cardiovascular, and central nervous system treatments. Brands include Normodipine, Diroton, Panangin, Cavinton, and Mydeton. Gedeon Richter has a presence in 30 countries; its products are distributed worldwide through its subsidiaries. The company was founded by namesake Gedeon Richter in 1901.
