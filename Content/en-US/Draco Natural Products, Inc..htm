Draco Natural Products is nothing if it's not natural. The company wholesales more than 400 powdered herbal extracts for personal care products, nutritional supplements, nutraceuticals, and over-the-counter pharmaceuticals. Draco manufactures the Vital Treasures Botanical Ingredients, which consists of herbal extract ingredients designed to heal, nourish, and tone the skin. It was developed for cosmetic and personal care applications. The company offers its products for the nutritional supplements, functional foods, OTC, and personal care markets. Draco Natural Products was founded in 1995.
