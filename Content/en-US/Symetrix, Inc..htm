Above all, Symetrix wants you to sound good. The company makes professional audio equipment and digital audio workstation technology. Its products include amplifiers, equalizers, mixers, voice processors, and related DSP (digital signal processor) audio equipment, sold under the brand names AirTools, Lucid, Symetrix, and SymNet. Its corporate website, as well as its brand sites, are available in English, Mandarin, and Spanish effective late 2007. The company was founded in 1976 by president and CEO Dane Butcher.
