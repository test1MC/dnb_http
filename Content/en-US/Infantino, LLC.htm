Bambinos have grown attached to Infantino's products. The company makes infant carriers, bedding, mobiles, activity sets, soft toys, and puzzles. Infantino, founded in 1978, also manufactures a line of shopping-cart covers to keep babies germ-free and amused during trips to the grocery store and other venues with shopping carts. The company's products can be found nationwide in retail stores, such as Babies R' Us , Baby Depot , JC Penney , Kmart , and Sears . Infantino's management purchased the company from Dorel Industries in 2000. The Step2 Company owns Infantino.
