Whether you want decks, wheels, trucks, or threads, Gravity Boarding Company, aka Gravity Skateboards, is stoked to be your source. The designer, manufacturer, wholesaler, and retailer offers long board skateboards in a variety of cool looks and ranging in length from 33 to 55 inches with names like Arrogant Bastard, Spoon Nose, and Hyper-Carve. Some models are also named after the riders who design them. In addition to selling complete boards, the company also offers decks (board only), wheels, Randal and Tracker trucks (attach the wheels to the board), and clothing and other accessories with the company's signature graphics. Gravity goes big in the skate scene by sponsoring a variety of skaters and surfers.
