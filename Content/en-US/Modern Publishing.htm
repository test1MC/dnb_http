Kids always need something contemporary to read. Modern Publishing is a children's book publisher specializing in picture books, workbooks, color and activity books, and novelty books. Its titles are geared for children from infancy through age 10. Many Modern Publishing books are based on popular licensed brands, and include titles such as Amazing Pokemon Guide Book and Sesame Street Cookie Monster Bedtime Bath Book . Modern Publishing publishes more than 250 titles per year; it distributes its books to mass market retail outlets and specialty stores in the US and Canada, including Toys R Us and Amazon.com .
