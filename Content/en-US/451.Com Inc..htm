451.Com (doing business as The 451 Group) wants to keep executives up-to-date on white-hot technologies. Named for the temperature at which paper burns, The 451 Group analyzes news and trends in the information technology industry. The company offers subscription-based services featuring news analysis, industry updates, and research reports on trends in various IT sectors. It also provides analysis on mergers and acquisitions and offers counsel to clients, including executives at IT companies and the financial service firms and PR agencies that work with them. The 451 Group additionally produces industry summits and investor conferences covering topics on emerging IT markets.
