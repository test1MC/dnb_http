STT Enviro Corp. wants to clear the air -- literally. The company (formerly Semcan) designs, engineers, assembles, and installs bulk chemical handling equipment used to clean water and wastewater treatment chemicals. The equipment is also used to control air emissions from storage silos, conveying systems, and electronic controls within the oil and gas, utilities, and power sectors. The former Semcan changed its name to STT Enviro Corp. in early 2013 to emphasize its expertise in water remediation and emission control systems.
