Turning carrots into capsules is one way to make sure people get the recommended servings of fruits and veggies each day. NSA is out to keep busy people in good health with its Juice Plus+ nutritional supplement products. The company sells through a direct sales network it supports online through its "Virtual Franchise" system. The company has a manufacturing agreement with Natural Alternatives International to produce the Juice Plus+ product line. Juice Plus+ products are made from fruits and vegetables, and include chewables, capsules, and powdered drink mixes. The Juice Plus+ Children's Foundation provides philanthropic support to research, health care, and volunteer organizations.
