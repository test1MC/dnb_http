Montupet knows the key to today's fuel-efficient cars is lightweight components. The company, one of Europe's leading independent aluminum-casting companies supplying the automotive sector, makes such parts as cylinder heads, structural components, intake manifolds, brake parts, engine blocks, and rims. Through subsidiary Montupet UK, it also produces thousands of cylinder heads each day for carmakers including Ford , General Motors , Peugeot , and Renault . Montupet has operations in Europe and North America. The company traces its roots back to the early 1900s. In early 2016 it was acquired by Canada's Linamar .
