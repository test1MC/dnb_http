The mantra of this company might be better living through health foods. Heathrow Natural Food & Beverage markets and distributes botanically based health foods. The company's products include a line of functional chewing gum. Currently Heathrow makes two gums -- anti-oxidant ACAI Plus Chewing Gum and anti-aging Resveratrol Plus Chewing Gum. It also offers Super Food Bar and Super Food Snack Chips. The company's products are sold at retail outlets under the Heathrow Natural Super Food brand. In 2011 the company announced plans to spin-off all of its food and beverage operations to form Heathrow Enterprises, LLC. Concurrently, it will acquire Aurum Guaranty, a financial services firm, and adopt the Aurum name.
