Neutral Posture makes workplace comfort the focus of its business. The company designs and makes ergonomic office chairs under the AbChair, Balance, Embrace, and N-tune brands. It also sells keyboard trays and related workstation accessories under the elementAl and StandUp brands. Customers include major corporations and government agencies; US General Services Administration is one of its largest customers. Neutral Posture has operations in Texas and Canada. Founded in 1989, its chairs were designed by Texas A&M professor Jerome Congleton, whose research was derived from NASA data on the body's reaction to weightlessness.

Neutral Posture often grows its product line by purchasing other brands from its rivals. In 2014 it acquired the Equity product line from Knoll . The deal expanded its reach in the commercial furniture sector.
