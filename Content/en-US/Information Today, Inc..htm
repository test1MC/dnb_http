When it comes to information, one company focuses on the here and now. Information Today publishes books, magazines, Web content, newsletters, and directories for the library, information, and knowledge management markets. The company publishes the eponymous Information Today , as well as newsletter The Information Advisor . Other publications include Computers in Libraries and EContent . Information Today also stages conferences and trade shows such as Enterprise Search Summit. The company operates subsidiaries and divisions devoted to topics such as streaming media, electronic research, customer relationship management, IT and communications information services, speech technology, and market research.
