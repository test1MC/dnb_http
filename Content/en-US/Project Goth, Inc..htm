For those who desire social networking capabilities on the go, Project Goth may have the Internet service for you. Doing business as mig33, the company offers downloadable Internet-based mobile phone applications such as chat and instant messaging, personal profile and photo sharing, e-mail, VoIP calls (Voice over Internet Protocol), and other social networking services. It offers both free and paid options, and boasts more than 18 million registered users in about 200 countries. Founded in 2005 by Steven Goh and Mei Lin Ng, mig33 is owned by venture capital firms Accel Partners , DCM, and Redpoint Ventures , as well as private investors.
