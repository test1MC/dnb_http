Peace River Citrus Products is a leading producer of bulk juice concentrates, oils, pulp cells, and other byproducts from oranges and white and red grapefruits sourced from Florida growers. The company supplies food and beverage manufacturers from its processing plants in Arcadia and Bartow, Florida. Peace River has diversified its product mix by adding to its not from concentrate storage capabilities at both its Arcadia and Bartow facilities. The company has more than 20 million gallons of not-from-concentrate juice storage to complement its concentrate storage capacity of 21 million gallons. Peace River was founded in 1994 by president Bill Becker.
