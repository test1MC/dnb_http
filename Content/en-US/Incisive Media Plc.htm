Incisive Media cuts straight to the chase with its business-oriented publications. The company provides business information related to a variety of markets, including financial services, legal and accounting services, marketing, technology, and risk management. Incisive Media publishes magazines, websites, newsletters, and related databases. It also organizes conferences and exhibitions. Publications include Accountancy Age , Investment Week , Legal Week , Post Magazine , and Risk . CEO Tim Weller, a former executive at Reuters , along with Incisive Media executives James Hanbury and Nick Rapley, founded the company in 1994. Apax Partners and company management took Incisive Media private in 2006.
