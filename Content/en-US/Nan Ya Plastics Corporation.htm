Nan Ya may be plastic, but it's the real deal. Part of the Formosa Group 's collection of chemical and plastics companies, Nan Ya Plastics primarily manufactures plastics, fibers, and electronic materials. Its primary products are polyester and its derivatives, including polyester staple fiber, polyester chips, PET, and polyester film. Taiwan's largest plastics maker, the company also operates in the US through subsidiary Nan Ya Plastics USA as well as in China and Indonesia. Nan Ya Plastics was founded in 1958 by industrialist Yung-Ching Wang, whose family still controls the Formosa Group companies.
