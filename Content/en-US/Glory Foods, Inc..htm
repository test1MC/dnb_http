Ever think they'd put your grandma's food in a can? Glory Foods reckons it can. The company cans, freezes, and bags the staple foods of old-fashioned Southern-style cooking and African-American dishes. Its more than 80 products include canned seasoned okra, yams, sweet potatoes, collard greens, fried apples, and pinto, string, and pole beans, along with side dishes, sauces, condiments, and bases. It also makes cornbread and muffin mixes. The company sells its food products in US retail food outlets, as well as online through its website. Glory Foods was sold to Southern specialty food maker McCall Farms in 2009 but remains a separate minority-controlled and -managed company.
