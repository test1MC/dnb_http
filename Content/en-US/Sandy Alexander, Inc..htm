Sandy Alexander is handy when it comes to graphics. The company offers a variety of graphics, printing, and marketing services. In addition to its web and sheet-fed printing capabilities, the company provides prepress and finishing services, as well as digital marketing services that include producing digital annual reports, laptop sales presentations, and interactive trade show kiosks. Its customers include a wide range of companies from small design studios to Fortune 500 firms in the cosmetic, pharmaceutical, automotive, and advertising industries.

Sandy Alexander has the bulk of its operations in Florida, New Jersey, New York, and Hong Kong. The company also has a handful of satellite offices to ensure that it's close to its customers.

As part of its focus on the environment, Sandy Alexander uses wind-generated electricity to power its manufacturing facilities.

In 2016 Sandy Alexander announced that Salt Studios and MCT/Liquid joined forces. Salt Studios is Sandy Alexander's visual media production boutique located in New York City. The company now has full-service studios located in New York and Hong Kong.
