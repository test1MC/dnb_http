A & B Security Group helps keep what happens in Las Vegas stay safe and secure in Las Vegas. The company installs and maintains closed-circuit television (CCTV) and electronic-access control systems for customers in the nation's gambling capital and elsewhere in southern Nevada. Its access control systems include bio-metric readers for fingerprint, eye-scan, and voice recognition. The company also offers alarm systems, safes, and locksmith services. Clients include commercial and government entities. A&B Security Group was founded in 1987.
