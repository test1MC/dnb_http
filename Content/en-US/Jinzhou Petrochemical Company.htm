Jinzhou Petrochemical is a producer and distributor of petrochemicals and organic chemicals in China. The company's primary products are gasoline and diesel oil. Other products include dimethyl carbinol, isopropanol, and sulfur oxide. Jinzhou also makes fine chemical products such as solvents. The company operates a joint venture with oil giant Exxon Mobil and petroleum additive maker Infineum International dedicated to the manufacture of additives for industrial lubricants. It sells its products throughout the Asia/Pacific region as well as in Europe and the US. Jinzhou is a subsidiary of China's largest oil and gas company, PetroChina .
