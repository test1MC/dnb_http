Technimark makes its mark by manufacturing plastics products for customers, including members of the automotive, consumer products, electronics, medical equipment, packaging, and power tools industries. It operates nine facilities in Mexico and the US. Its operations are segmented into three divisions. The largest is Diversified Products, which provides custom molding. The Specialty Products division is focused on niche markets, while the Packaging Products unit provides a variety of packaging products. The company is a part of the Klaussner Group of companies.

The company's facilities are located in North America, Europe and Asia. Its full service injection molding, assembly, decoration, and distribution facilities are located in Asheboro, North Carolina; Reynosa, Bajio and Mexicali, Mexico; Aachen, Germany; and Suzhou, China.

Technimark serves the consumer packaging, healthcare and industrial markets.

The company is looking to grow in emerging markets, especially in Latin America. To support this push, in 2014 Technimark opened a new 93,000-square-foot facility in Silao, Mexico, with an initial investment of more than $5 million.

It is also expanding its operations in Germany, at its headquarters in North Carolina and in Suzhou, China.

Technimark was founded in 1983 and became a part of the Klaussner Group of companies in 1990.
