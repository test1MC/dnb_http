Protide Pharmaceuticals is pro-cell. The firm focuses on developing new technologies for cellular and transfusion therapies, including gene therapies, to treat cancer and genetic disorders and to improve cell transplantation and engineering. To fund its work, Protide sells nearly 20 products under the Celox Laboratories brand to its academic, drug, and diagnostic research clients around the world. These products include serum-free growth mediums and supplements, as well as solutions used to cryopreserve cells and tissues. ICN Pharmaceuticals has a non-exclusive global marketing agreement for certain products. Protide also has a private-label manufacturing deal with a division of Sigma-Aldrich .The company is developing ViaStem to expand its product line. ViaStem could be used to preserve stem and dendritic cells, as well as cord blood and platelets.
