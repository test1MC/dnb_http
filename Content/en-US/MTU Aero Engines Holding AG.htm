MTU wants its customers to be propelled to great heights. The company is one of the largest aircraft engine component manufacturers in the world. MTU has three operating units: commercial engines, which involves the production of low-pressure turbines, high pressure turbine components, high pressure compressors, and spare parts; military projects, which are developed through alliances with OEMs and are custom-developed by contract; and commercial MRO (maintenance, repair, and overhaul) services for both commercial and military jet engines. The company began as a subsidiary of Daimler AG , but it was acquired by a private equity firm in 2003 and taken public two years later.
