Cantine Giorgio Lungarotti is always "wining." With its 700-acre estate in Torgiano, the company is a leading Italian vintner. More than half of its wines, which range from aperitif to dessert and sparkling to fortified, are exported to 35 countries across the globe. The company, founded in 1962 by Giorgio Lungarotti, is owned and operated by Teresa Severini and Chiara Lungarotti, daughters of the founder and the first women in Italy to control a major winery. Lungarotti also produces olive oil and operates a hotel/restaurant (Le Tre Vaselle), a vacation rental property (Poggio alle Vigne), the Lungarotti Wine Museum, and an Olive and Olive Oil Museum.
