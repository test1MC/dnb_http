As a seasoned developer of agriculture in Malaysia, Narborough Plantations produces and sells oil palm products there. With its current holdings now mature, the company boasts nearly all of the hectares (1,371 out of 1,393 acres) under cultivation. In late 2010 Narborough Plantations kick-started replanting efforts on 10 hectares of its oil palms field that were some 30 years old. It's working to boost future production. The group produced 12,300 tonnes of oil palm in 2010, down from 12,800 in 2009. The company trades on the London Stock Exchange . Riverview Rubber Estates Berhad owns 49.8% of Narborough Plantations; Hamidah binti Abdul Rahman owns 26%.
