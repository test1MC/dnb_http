From nappies to napkins, Kimberly-Clark Europe keeps its European clients stocked. The subsidiary is part of Kimberly-Clark Corporation , the world's top maker of personal paper products. Kimberly-Clark Europe provides some of the most recognized household brands in Europe including Andrex, Kleenex, and Page bathroom tissue; Kleenex and Scottex facial tissue; Huggies infant and child care products; Kotex and Camelia feminine care products; and Poise adult care items. The personal care and tissue markets in Europe are highly competitive. This European arm of Kimberly-Clark spans Northern, Southern, Central, and Eastern Europe, as well as the Gulf States, Israel, and South Africa.

A core geographic segment for parent Kimberly-Clark , the company's European operations include 13 production facilities.
