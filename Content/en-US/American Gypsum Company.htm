American Gypsum Company, a subsidiary of Eagle Materials , manufactures, sells, and distributes gypsum drywall (also known as wallboard) products. One of the largest producers of gypsum wallboard in the US, the company annually produces nearly 4 billion sq. ft. of the stuff from its plants in Albuquerque and Bernalillo, New Mexico; Gypsum, Colorado; Duke, Oklahoma; and its newest construction facility in Georgetown, South Carolina (opened 2007). It also has reload centers in Arizona, California, and Illinois, and operates gypsum mines near its production facilities.
