KCC Corporation makes a variety of products for the construction industry. The company and its subsidiaries manufacture interior ceiling roofing, insulation, PVC windows and floors, architectural float glass, automotive laminated glass, and paints. It also produces protective coatings for the marine industry, as well as glass fiber, epoxy mold compound, and sealants. KCC operates a network of offices and manufacturing facilities in South Korea, China, Japan, Malaysia, Vietnam, Singapore, Germany, Indonesia, India, Turkey, the UAE, and the UK. The company was created out of the 2000 merger between Kumgang and Korea Chemical Co.
