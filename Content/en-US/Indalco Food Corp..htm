Indalco Food really pours it on. The company manufactures a milk and caramel spread called dulce de leche (literally "milk candy") that is a familiar product in Central and South America. Indalco has brought this traditional food product to the US. The spread can be used in a variety of ways, such as a dessert topping or filling, or in baked goods such as cheesecake. The company's customers include bakeries, donut shops ( Dunkin' Donuts ), restaurants ( McDonald's ), ice cream makers ( Häagen-Dazs ), and confectioners ( Mars ), as well as retail food stores. Indalco's dulce de leche is sold under the Del Campo label.
