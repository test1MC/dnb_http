Whether race car or dump truck, Carlisle Brake & Friction (CBF) enables smoother starts and stops. The company provides high-performance brake, clutch, and transmission products to the off-highway aerospace, agricultural, construction, industrial, military, and motorsports markets. Geared at OEMs and the aftermarket, products include parking brakes, wind turbine brakes, brake pads, transmission discs, insulators, and carbon-based friction materials.
