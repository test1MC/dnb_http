Amtek Engineering designs and manufactures precision toolings, as well as metal stampings of parts and casings for appliances, automotive parts, computers, consumer electronics, mass storage, and telecommunications equipment. The company's other services include industrial design, decorative metal finishing, and plastic and rubber injection molding. China and Singapore account for approximately 75% of sales. Amtek's primary subsidiaries are located in China, Hong Kong, Malaysia, and Singapore. Amtek Engineering has manufacturing facilities in China, the Czech Republic, France, Hungary, Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand, and Vietnam.
