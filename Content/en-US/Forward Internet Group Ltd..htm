Forward Internet Group aims to help consumers and companies in the UK get ahead on the Web. The company operates a handful of businesses, including Forward3D, a digital marketing firm that helps corporate customers increase traffic to their websites, and InvisibleHand, a real-time price comparison tool. Forward Internet's uSwitch lets household consumers compare the prices of various types of service providers -- including electricity, gas, broadband, and personal finance -- and then switch providers. Other holdings include Omio (price-comparison for cell phone plans) and Locaris (multilingual media and marketing services). The company, formerly called TrafficBroker, was founded in 2004 by Neil Hutchinson.
