Contessa may sound like landed gentry but in this case, the wealth comes from the sea. Contessa Premium Foods, a frozen food manufacturer, offers a line of bagged, frozen, prepared shrimp, scallop, and calamari dishes to restaurants and hotels. Its specialties include coconut shrimp, shrimp Marsala, and Dragon Tail shrimp along with unprepared shrimp. Other products include stovetop or mircowave meals ranging from beef stew to paella. Busy home cooks can find Contessa seafood and convenience meals in supermarkets and warehouse stores in North America, Europe, and Asia. Formed in 1984, Contessa filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection in 2011 and was subsequently purchased by investment firm Sun Capital.
