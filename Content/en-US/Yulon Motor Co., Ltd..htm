Yulon Motor makes products with drive. Yulon, an affiliate of Japan's Nissan Motor , ranks as one of Taiwan's largest carmakers (along with company affiliate China Motor , which manufactures Mitsubishi models). Yulon assembles and sells Nissan sedans and compacts, such as the Verita, Cefiro, and Sentra, as well as SUVs like the QX4. It distributes GM Buick, Cadillac, and Opel models in Taiwan through its Yulon GM subsidiary. In 2008 the company established Luxgen Motor Co. to market its own brand of cars, with plans to export to China and Russia.
