TELIRIS is a provider of videoconferencing systems. The company, which markets its products and services under brands such as VirtuaLive, StartPoint, and InterACT, helps companies set up and run multimedia conferencing systems. Its products combine audio, video, and other data transmissions to enable remote meetings, presentations, and training. Its services include consulting, metrics analysis, network management, project management, and training. TELIRIS serves such clients as GlaxoSmithKline , Lazard , Royal Bank of Scotland , and Vodafone . The company has locations in southern Africa, Asia, Europe, and North and South America. Columbia Capital and Fidelity Ventures are among the investors in TELIRIS.
