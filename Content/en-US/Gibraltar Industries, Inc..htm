What rocks Gibraltar's world? A good strong structure. The company makes and distributes products for residential and commercial construction, including metal roofing, vents, rain gutters, steel framing, and mailboxes. Gibraltar is a leading producer of metal roofing and accessories products as well as metal structural connectors including foundation anchors, anchor bolts, and foundation straps. The company operates about 45 facilities in the US, Canada, and Europe. Its building products are sold to builders, architects, and homeowners. Building materials retailer Home Depot is the company's largest customer, accounting for 12% of overall sales.

Gibraltar is stationed in Buffalo, New York, and operates in all 50 US states and throughout the world. It has 42 facilities in 22 states, Canada, Germany, and the UK, which includes 32 manufacturing facilities and 8 distribution centers. It serves customers primarily throughout North America and Europe, and, to a lesser extent, in Asia, Africa, Australia, and Central and South America.

The company operates in two operating segments: Residential Products; and Industrial and Infrastructure Products. Each one represents 50% of its net sales.

Despite experiencing a 4% bump in revenue from 2013 to 2014, Gibraltar experienced a net loss of $82 million during 2014. The loss was primarily due to intangible asset impairment charges of $108 million it paid due to lower estimated fair values of certain operating units in 2014.

The revenue growth for 2014 was fueled by a 10% bump in Residential Products sales. This was fueled by a spike in volume, an increase in pricing to customers, and additional sales generated by an acquisition completed in late 2013.

A key component of Gibraltar's growth strategy involves the use of acquisitions. In 2015 it purchased RBI Solar, Inc. and Rough Brothers Manufacturing Inc. (collectively RBI) for $130 million. The transaction will enable Gibraltar to leverage its expertise in structural metals manufacturing and materials sourcing to meet the fast-growing global demand for solar racking products. RBI is a North American provider of photovoltaic (PV) solar racking components and products.
