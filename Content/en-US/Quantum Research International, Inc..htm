Focusing on R&D, Quantum Research (QRI) shows no limits. The company offers technical consulting, business support, strategic planning assistance, and IT products and services. Customers are US armed forces, in addition to federal, state, and local agencies, allied governments, including the UK Ministry of Defense, and companies, such as BAE and Leidos . QRI's R&D slate ranges from force modernization to C4ISR (Command, Control, Communications, Computers, Intelligence, Surveillance & Reconnaissance), simulation, logistics, missile defense, and survivability analysis. QRI offices neighbor US defense facilities; personnel are also planted on-site with customers in the US and abroad. Founder D. Frank Pitts leads QRI.
