The subsidiary of South Korean electronics giant LG Electronics (the world's largest maker of plasma displays), LG Electronics UK markets and sells a range of electronic consumer conveniences, including TVs and home entertainment systems (Blu-Ray/DVD players), computer products (monitors and related accessories), and mobile devices (smartphones). The company also offers home appliances such as refrigerators, vacuum cleaners, washer and dryers, and microwave ovens. LG Electronics UK operates as part of its parent's European activities, which generate almost 10% of all sales each year.
