This ain't no token operation. Sunshine Minting is a private minter and the primary supplier of silver blanks to the US Mint . The company manufactures and sells precious metal products, such as medallions, medals, tokens, and bullion (bulk precious metal) to the minting and coin marketing industry. Sunshine Minting also has a storage vault on-site for customers who own at least 10 ounces of gold or 500 ounces of silver, and invest in precious metal commodities. The company is located in Coeur d'Alene, Idaho, long known as a mining town rich in silver.
