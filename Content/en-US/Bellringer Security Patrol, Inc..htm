Bellringer Security Patrol designs, installs, and maintains security systems for residential and commercial customers, primarily in Nassau and Suffolk counties in New York state, a service area that includes the upscale Hamptons. The company's offerings include alarms, telephone-activated, hydraulic gate-access systems, and video-surveillance devices. Bellringer Security also provides security-patrol and  live-operator telephone-answering services.
