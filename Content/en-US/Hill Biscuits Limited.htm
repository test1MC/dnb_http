Hill Biscuits knows that sometimes you just want a little something nice . Founded in 1855, Hill Biscuits bakes up all things biscuit (the UK meaning of biscuit, aka cookie), from sweet and semi-sweet to plain and cream-filled, including its nice and simple Nice biscuits. Other selections include custard, bourbon, orange, coconut, and strawberry creams; ginger fingers; and fruit shorties. In addition to its Hill brand, the company makes private-label biscuits and offers contract-manufacture of biscuits and ingredients. Hill's bakery products are available at food retailers, discount stores, and wholesale outlets throughout the UK. It also exports around the world.
