Forget that dusty ol' erector set. K'NEX takes constructive building to an entirely new level with its color-coded building systems. Connectors, cranks, pulleys, wheels, and gear systems allow kids to create a variety of objects including roller coasters, lunar landers, race cars, Ferris wheels, and helicopters. Motorized kits and chrome parts and wheels help bring the creations to life. Products are sold through retailers and the company's online store, as well as being distributed internationally by TOMY . K'NEX is Hasbro 's US licensee for the classic Lincoln Logs building set and also distributes BRIO toys and trains. The company was founded in 1992.
