ProMOS Technologies promotes itself as a leading manufacturer of semiconductor memory products. More specifically, ProMOS specializes in DRAM technology, including stack DRAM and trench DRAM, and also makes flash memory, discrete charge-trapping memory, and phase-change memory. The company operates two 300mm wafer fabrication plants, in Central Taiwan Science Park and in Hsinchu Science Park, Taiwan. ProMOS was established in 1996 and exports its products to countries throughout Asia, the Americas, and Europe. The company has sales offices in Taiwan and the US.
