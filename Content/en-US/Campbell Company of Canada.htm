The first foreign division of US parent Campbell Soup , Campbell Canada began operations in 1930 and has grown to be one of the country's major food manufacturers. Its products include canned and shelf-stable soups and broths, bottled sauces, and canned and bottled juices. It also manufactures value-added frozen food products for the foodservice industry.
