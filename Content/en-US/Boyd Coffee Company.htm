Boyd Coffee Company, founded in 1900, sells coffees, teas, and other beverage products, such as flavoring syrups and drink mixes. It markets them under the brand names Boyds Coffee, Italia D'Oro, Coffee House Roasters, Coffee House Freezers, and Island Mist. The company also sells brewing equipment through its Techni-Brew International division and offers Today Foods-branded soups and soup bases. Boyd Coffee Company supplies retail food outlets and restaurants, hotels, hospitals, and other food service venues in about 25 states, as well as US military commissaries worldwide. Products are also available to consumers through Boyd's online store and via its three coffee shops in the Portland, Oregon, area.

Portland-based Boyd Coffee Company serves customers in more than 25 states and worldwide through US military commissaries. Its namesake coffee is sold in select stores across Alaska, California, Idaho, Illinois, Michigan, Missouri, Montana, Nevada, Oregon, and Washington.

The company's products portfolio includes several brands, such as Boyds Coffee, Italia D'Oro, Coffee House Roasters, Coffee House Freezers, Island Mist, and Today Foods.

Aside from selling coffees, teas, and other beverage items, Boyds Coffee Company peddles brewing equipment through its international division, Techni-Brew.

It offers custom dry blending, roastery direct service, and beverage programs, catering to a variety of customers, such as casinos and Indian gaming venues, coffee shops, convenience stores, distributors, grocery stores, deli shops, hotels, offices, restaurants, and universities and colleges.

The company supplies its products to food retailers, restaurants, hotels, hospitals, and the US military. It also sells its products online through its e-commerce site and through several of its own stores in Oregon. It works to promote itself as an environmentally-friendly coffee company, partnering with organizations that support high-quality, sustainable coffee and the coffee-growing communities. It also seeks to minimize packaging and waste.

In 2013, after 113 years in business, Boyd Coffee Company rolled out its new strategic rebranding, which included a new brand identity, retail coffee packaging, and food service merchandising targeted to convenience stores, restaurants, hotels, hospitals, and other venues. Having partnered with agency Commonwealth, the effort centers on Boyd's rich heritage as the longest-operating coffee roaster in the Pacific Northwest.

In recent years, Boyd Coffee Company has developed new flavors and products under the Coffee House Freezers and Island Mist brands. Coffee House Freezers has an assortment of frozen drink mixes, including Grape Fusion and Root Beer Float. Island Mist offers a range of flavored iced teas, such as Organic Green Tea with Mango and Strawberry Peach Black Tea. Also, in 2014 the coffee company launched a new generation of single-cup coffees for brewers that accept K-Cup portion packs.

Boyd Coffee Company extends the reach of its business through national, state, and local food service and hospitality organizations. They include the National Restaurant Association, National Association of Convenience Stores, Oregon Restaurant & Lodging Association, Washington Restaurant Association, California Restaurant Association, National Association for the Specialty Food Trade, Western Petroleum Marketers Association, Society of Independent Gasoline Marketers, and International Housewares Association.

The family-owned company traces its roots to a horse-drawn operation that began selling tea to Portland residents in 1900.
