The Deflecto Corporation doesn't blow hot air; it disperses the stuff. The company manufactures and distributes air distribution and dryer venting products, including air deflectors, ducts and vents, specialty hoods, chimney liners, exhaust fittings, and other sheet metal and aluminum products. It also makes office products and literature display pieces, such as business card holders, chair mats, floor racks, storage bins, and chart and folder holders. Customers include mass merchandisers, major retailers, and wholesalers. The company is a wholly owned subsidiary of private firm Jordan Industries .
