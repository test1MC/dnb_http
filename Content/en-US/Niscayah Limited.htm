Niscayah Limited was the UK operations of Swedish-headquartered Niscayah Group AB . The subsidiary designed and installed security and fire safety systems for more than 1,700 public and commercial customers in the UK. Customers included The Royal Bank of Scotland Group , Reuters , and the London Stock Exchange . Among its offerings were access control units, bank-note staining, biometrics devices, card readers, CCTV surveillance equipment, electronic locking systems, fire protection, and intruder alarms. The company also provided monitoring services, systems design and integration, training, and warranties. In 2011 its parent was acquired by Stanley Black & Decker .
