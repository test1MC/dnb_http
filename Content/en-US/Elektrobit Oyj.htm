Elektrobit (EB) designs and tests electronic products, especially wireless technology and automotive electronics. Operating in more than 10 countries, Elektrobit primarily serves the automotive and telecommunications industries. The company provides development services for wireless products, including mobile phones and navigation systems. It sells radio channel emulators for testing wireless products. EB also offers software for designing and implementing infotainment systems for the automotive market. The company gets more than half of its sales in Europe. In 2015, its entire automotive operations were purchased by Continental AG .
