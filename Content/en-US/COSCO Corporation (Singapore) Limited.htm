A major provider of ship repair, shipbuilding, and offshore marine engineering services, COSCO Corporation (Singapore) is part of the vast network of companies associated with Chinese state-owned marine transportation giant China Ocean Shipping (Group) Company, known as COSCO Group . The company's holdings include a majority interest in one of China's leading shipyards, COSCO Shipyard Group (which operates 6 major shipyards in China, with 1.73 million dwt docking capacity). COSCO (Singapore) also has a fleet of a dozen dry bulk carriers, which are used to transport commodities such as coal, grain, and iron ore. The company was spun off in 1993 from COSCO Group, which retains a control COSCO (Singapore).
