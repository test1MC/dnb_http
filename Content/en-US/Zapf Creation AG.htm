Zapf Creation doesn't just play around in the doll business. The company is Europe's largest maker of play and functional dolls. Some of the dolls just sit there, while others suck their bottles, wet their diapers, cry, giggle with joy, and even take a dip in the pool. Zapf Creation also makes accessories for the dolls (including special food, potty chairs, and clothing), and collectible designer dolls.
