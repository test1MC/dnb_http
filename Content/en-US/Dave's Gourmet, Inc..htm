Dave's Gourmet believes that no pain is no gain. The specialty foods and condiment manufacturer and wholesaler makes Dave's Insanity Sauce, Bloody Mary and margarita mixes, pasta sauces, mustards. and spicy snacks. The firm's Insanity Sauce is reportedly 40 times hotter than McIlhenny's Tabasco, the industry leader. The sauce contains capsaicin, or mace, an active ingredient in pepper spray. Dave's Gourmet also offers a hotter sauce -- "Ghost Pepper" Jolokia Private Reserve -- which is three times hotter than its Insanity brand, produced in limited quantities, and sold in miniature coffins wrapped in "Caution" tape. Products are sold through its website and in grocery stores and specialty gourmet shops.
