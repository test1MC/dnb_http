Nufarm UK, the Great Britain-based subsidiary of Australia-based crop protection manufacturer Nufarm Limited , is one of the leading suppliers of agrochemical products in the country. It specializes in producing herbicides. In addition to a broad array of phenoxyacetic and phenoxy propionic herbicides, Nufarm UK makes other crop protection and related agricultural chemicals, including fungicides, pesticides, adjuvant oils, and growth regulators. The company's key brand names include Butoxone, Herboxone, and Maton. The company serves the UK and Irish markets.

Nufarm UK supplies phenoxy herbicides to a number of other global crop protection companies. It is also a big producer of glyphosate, a systemic herbicide used to kill weeds, and the most common herbicide in the US (originally sold under the brand name Roundup).

The company is a part of UK operations of Nufarm Limited and part of the parent company's European market segment. In 2014, Europe accounted for 21% of the Nufarm Limited's revenues.

The company's addition of A H Marks in 2009 greatly added to its manufacturing capabilities in the UK., and the company moved its headquarters to that location.

Now known as Nufarm UK at Wyke, the unit produces phenoxy herbicides, glyphosate, intermediates, and inhibitors. Phenoxy herbicides, one of the world's largest herbicide product segments, make up a global market exceeding $550 million, and are used for cereal crops, pasture, and several turf applications.
