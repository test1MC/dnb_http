Service Point Solutions wants to direct customers to its outsourcing business. The company provides businesses across a broad array of industries with information management, communication, and physical and digital print services. Specific offerings include document, print, and mail management, as well as printing and creative services. Service Point's global presence extends throughout nearly a dozen mostly European countries (including Belgium, France, Norway, Russia, Spain, and the UK); its network includes some 130 service centers and nearly 500 managed service programs. The company's customers have included such notable names as Balfour Beatty , Exxon Mobil , and Jaguar .
