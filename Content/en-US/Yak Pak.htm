Yak Pak hopes that making its bottom line is in the bag. The company manufactures utilitarian yet trendy bags, backpacks, messenger bags, totes, and related accessories. Its products are sold through specialty retailers nationwide (including Pacific Sunwear and Urban Outfitters ), as well as online and through a retail store located in San Franciso. Founded in 1989 by Stephen Holt; Yak Pak's first major product, the D-Jay bag, was made specifically for DJs to hold their music. Handbag and accessories firm Accessory Network Group (ANG) acquired Yak Pak in 2011.
