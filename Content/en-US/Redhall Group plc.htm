Worried about rapid unscheduled dis-assemblies -- AKA explosions? Just call Redhall Group. The company manufactures heavy gauge metal doors which resist damage from fire and explosions. Redhall also designs other structural components including blast walls, escape tunnels, firewalls, and windows. The company serves customers in such industries as aviation, defense, industrial, marine, offshore oil, and security. Clients have included such noteworthy names as Bristol-Myers Squibb , ConocoPhillips , British Airways , and Pfizer . The UK is its largest market, representing around 90% of its total net sales.

Redhall's operations consist of two segments: Manufacturing (which encompasses Booth Industries, Jordan Manufacturing, and R Blackett Charlton) and Specialist Services.

Manufacturing encompasses the design, manufacture and installation of bespoke specialist plants and equipment typically within the nuclear, defense, oil and gas, petrochemical, chemical, pharmaceutical, and food sectors. The division has particular expertise in the design and manufacture of high integrity fire and blast resistant doors, window, and wall systems.

Specialist Services has expertise in the installation and maintenance of telecommunications network infrastructures and the installation of process lines in the food and pharmaceutical markets. It also supplies specialist surface finishings to Astute class submarines.

From 2014 to 2015, Redhall's revenues declined due to decreased sales from both its Manufacturing and Specialist Services segments. Its net loss also increased during 2015 due to decreased sales and losses stemming from discontinued operations.
