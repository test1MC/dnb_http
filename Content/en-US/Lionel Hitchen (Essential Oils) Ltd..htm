Lionel Hitchen (Essential Oils) knows a properly squeezed orange can yield more than just juice. The company manufactures natural concentrated citrus oils from oranges, lemons, limes, and grapefruits. Beyond those basics, the company extracts oils from plant parts, including leaves, buds, roots, seeds, and twigs. Lionel Hitchen uses these extracts to make steam distilled oils, alcoholic extracts, oleoresins, liquid seasonings, and dry herbs and spices. Lionel Hitchen is a major supplier to the UK beverage, cosmetic, food, and toiletry industries. The company is owned and operated by founder Lionel Hitchen and his family.
