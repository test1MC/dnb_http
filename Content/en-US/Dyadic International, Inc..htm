Dyadic International hopes to unlock biotechnology dynasties using its C1 technology. The company uses its C1 Expression System to develop biological and chemical substances for a variety of life sciences and industrial applications. The firm's enzyme business makes enzymes and other products for commercial and industrial uses employing C1 know-how. Industries served include textiles, agriculture, and paper mills. Dyadic's biopharma division uses the expression system to make therapeutic proteins for drugmakers. The company is also developing its enzymes for bioenergy applications including biofuels.
