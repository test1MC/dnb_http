International Dehydrated Foods, also known as IDF, makes meat and poultry ingredients for the food-manufacturing and foodservice industries in the US and in 30 other countries worldwide. The company's products include chicken-, turkey-, and meat-derived ingredients, including frozen and shelf-stable chicken broths; shelf-stable chicken fat; and liquid poultry fats. It also makes spray-dried dehydrated products such as powdered turkey, chicken, beef, and pork broths, powdered chicken and beef fats, and powdered cooked chicken, turkey, and beef. Its sister company, American Dehydrated Foods , manufactures animal-feed ingredients. International Dehydrated Foods was founded in 1982 and is owned by the Darr family.
