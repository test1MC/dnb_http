You need at least five of them a day for good health, and Bonduelle needs to sell them for its bottom line. We're talking vegetables -- and Bonduelle, which is one of Europe's largest processed and fresh vegetable businesses -- has sales in more than 100 countries. The company produces canned and frozen vegetables, as well as ready-made salads and raw vegetables, at its nearly 60 processing facilities. Bonduelle sells its products to customers in the retail food, food service, and food-manufacturing industries. In early 2017, Bonduelle bolstered its product portfolio through the purchase of Ready Pac Foods, a maker of private-label salad products sold to retailers.
