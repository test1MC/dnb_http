You'll need more than shiny tokens to access the cornucopia of tasty treats at this Arcade. Arcade Industries (doing business as Arcade Snacks) produces roasted nuts (cashews, pistachios, peanuts, almonds, walnuts), dried fruits (pineapple, cranberries, banana chips), candy (mints, gummies, licorice, malted milk balls and lost more) and snack and trail mixes (including Mother's Mystery Mix and Salsa Sizzler varieties) for retail and wholesale distribution. In business for more than 20 years, the company's customers include bakeries, confectioners, hotels, and restaurants nationwide. Arcade also sells its snack offerings through its website.
