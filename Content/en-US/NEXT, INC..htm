NEXT designs and markets licensed, branded, and private line promotional items and sportswear. The company's licensing agreements have included automakers Chevy , Dodge , and Ford , as well as Chuck E. Cheese and GRITS (Girls Raised In The South). NEXT distributes its products through mass merchandisers, warehouse clubs, department stores, sporting goods chains, college bookshops, and automotive dealers.
