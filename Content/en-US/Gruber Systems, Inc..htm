Gruber Systems makes molds and supplies for manufacturers of cast polymer products, such as bathtubs, showers, and countertops. Gruber's Granatex is a granite-like filler material used in bathroom, kitchen, and architectural products. The company also makes composite processing equipment and offers factory design services. Other products include adhesives, whirlpool parts, and related supplies and materials. Additionally, it provides finished products for the home, such as bathroom sinks and tubs, kitchen sinks, fireplace surrounds, and vanities, under the Solure brand. Gruber has facilities in California, Florida, and Texas. It was founded in 1958 as Gruber Man Made Marble.
