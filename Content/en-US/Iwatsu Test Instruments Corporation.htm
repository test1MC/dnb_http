Spun off from parent company Iwatsu Electric Co. in 2002, Iwatsu Test Instruments Corporation, not surprisingly, specializes in the development and manufacturing of electronic measurement systems and related accessories. Its primary product line is comprised of digital and analog oscilloscopes, digital multimeters, measurement technology used in communications, signal generators, aerospace device systems, gauges, calculation software, automatic measurement equipment, and other related types of networking hardware. The company caters mainly to the Japanese market but has ties with distributors in China/Hong Kong, India, Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore, South Africa, South Korea, Taiwan, Thailand, and Vietnam.
