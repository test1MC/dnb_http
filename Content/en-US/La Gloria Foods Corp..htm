La Gloria Foods revels in the glory of the US' Hispanic-foods market. The company makes corn and flour tortillas, as well as taco and tostada shells, tortilla chips, sopes (a small taco with raised edges that hold toppings). regular and premixed masa (for preparing tamales), as well as other Mexican food products. La Gloria's products are sold to wholesale and retail customers, including restaurants, supermarkets, foodservice suppliers, and food distributors. The company also provides custom manufacturing and private-label packaging services. Its products are made using traditional grinding and slow-cooking techniques. Based in Los Angeles, La Gloria Foods was founded by Manuel Behar in 1954.
