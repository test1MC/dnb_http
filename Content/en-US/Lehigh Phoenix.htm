Lehigh Phoenix (formerly Phoenix Color) likes to do book work. As one of the largest book component printers in North America, the company produces multicolored book covers, jackets, overhead transparencies, inserts, and more for publishers. It also prints specialty products, such as collectible cards, posters, game boards, catalogs, and brochures. The firm has manufacturing plants in Maryland, New Jersey, and Wisconsin. Clients include leading global publishing companies such as HarperCollins , Pearson Publishing, Simon & Schuster , Random House , Holtzbrinck Publishers , and McGraw-Hill Education , among others. Founded in 1979, Phoenix Color was acquired by privately held Visant Corporation in April 2008.
