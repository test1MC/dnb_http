Powerflute makes fluting but not of the woodwind variety. The Nordic company's Savon Sellu paper mill in Finland produces about 300,000 tonnes of semi-chemical fluting per year used to make corrugated board. Sold under the Powerflute brand, the product is made from birch and, due to its strength and moisture resistance, is often used in packaging for the transportation of fresh and frozen produce and various industrial goods. The company's Papierfabrik Scheufelen subsidiary in Germany operates another paper mill that produces roughly the same amount of coated woodfree paper for the printing of promotional materials, such as brochures and leaflets.
