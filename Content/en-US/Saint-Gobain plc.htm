One of the largest building materials companies in the UK, Saint-Gobain plc is the UK arm of Paris-based giant Compagnie de Saint-Gobain . The firm controls some 90 British and Irish subsidiaries that make and distribute building materials and industrial products including flat glass, insulation, ceramics, pipeline, timber, power tools, and plumbing supplies. Its holdings include Saint-Gobain Glass UK, reinforcement products manufacturer Saint-Gobain Technical Fabrics, and building supply company Simmons Building Merchants. Saint-Gobain plc accounts for about a quarter of its parent company's sales.
