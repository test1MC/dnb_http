Jobson Healthcare Information gives medical professionals the information they need -- stat! The company publishes periodicals and other media geared to healthcare professionals through its Information Services group, which includes titles focusing on eye care ( 20/20 Magazine , Review of Optometry ) and the pharmacy industry ( U.S. Pharmacist ). Its Education Services group offers continuing medical education in live, print, multimedia, and online formats to doctors, nurses, and pharmacists. In addition to medical publishing and education, the company also offers marketing services. Jobson is part of The Wicks Group of Companies, a private equity firm focused on the communications, information, and media industries.
