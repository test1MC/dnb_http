Who can take your potpourri, add some essential oils, mix in decorative botanicals, and add an herb or two? Lebermuth can. The company sells botanical fragrances, flavors, oils, herbs, spices, crystals, and decorative botanicals to make the world smell and look better. Its clinical staff includes a chief perfumer, evaluators, and analytical and cosmetic chemists. Lebermuth makes everything they produce for customers in the Craft business ( Michaels and Hobby Lobby ) and for other industries (personal care, fragrance, and aromatherapy). The company is owned by the founding Brown family.

Headquartered in Indiana, Lebermuth operates in 30 countries.

Lebermuth supplies its raw materials to the home fragrance, personal care, and industrial markets.

The company was founded in 1908 by Russian immigrant Morris Brown, as M. Brown & Sons, to sell essential oils. Lebermuth was set up as a separate firm in 1974 to sell potpourri. They subsequently merged under the Lebermuth name.
