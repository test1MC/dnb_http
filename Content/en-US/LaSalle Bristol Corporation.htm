LaSalle Bristol Corporation is a leading manufacturer and full-service supplier of home furnishing products for the manufactured housing, modular home, and recreational vehicle industries. Its key products include ventilation systems, ceiling fans, and lighting components. The company also carries a range of building, decorative, and plumbing products from a variety of vendors, including Moen , Delta Faucet , Ashley Furniture , and ClosetMaid . LaSalle Bristol, a subsidiary of the UK's Arran Isle Ltd., distributes its products through a network of about 20 branch locations and affiliates across the US.
