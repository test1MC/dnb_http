Fujitsu Technology Solutions carries the flag of its parent, Fujitsu Limited, in Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and India. The subsidiary sells IT infrastructure products and services such as desktop and notebook PCs, as well as high-end servers and mainframes, storage devices, and peripherals. It also offers consulting and support services, financing, and software licensing. The company sells to consumer, corporate and public customers. The company has partnerships with other technology vendors such as Microsoft to flesh out its product portfolio. It also resells storage systems from EMC .

Among its other services, Fujitsu Technology Solutions assembles and customizes PCs, primarily for the European market.

In 2013, restructuring at Fujitsu resulted in the reduction of 1,500 jobs in Central Europe and Western Europe, the Middle East, India and Africa. With that process complete, Fujitsu Technology Solutions looks for growth in the region. In Scandinavia, it seeks to tap demand for outsourcing and managed services businesses, especially from large multinational companies based in the region. Fujitsu has global delivery centers in India, Poland, and Portugal and it continues to build out facilities and delivery capabilities.
