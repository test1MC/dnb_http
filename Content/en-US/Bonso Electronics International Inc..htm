Bonso Electronics International's products are weigh cool. The company designs and manufactures scales and balances, ranging from simple bathroom scales to precise electronic models for laboratory and postal applications. Bonso also makes telecommunications products, such as cordless telephones and two-way radios. The company sells its products primarily to OEMs that market Bonso products under their own brand names. Customers include Global Link, Sunbeam , and TTI Tech, as well as Pitney Bowes and Salter Housewares. Bonso manufactures products in China, primarily for customers located in North America and Europe.
