VarTech Systems thinks tough. Its rugged computer monitors and LCD-based displays work in harsh environments and military field conditions, as well as in factories and control rooms. VarTech also produces industrial computers, including panel PCs, intended to save space on the factory floor by packing a fully functional computer into the back of a display. The company manufactures monitors and displays (including CRT-based displays), resells these products from other makers, and offers repair services. Founded in 1988 and going public soon after that, VarTech Systems withdrew its stock listing in 2003.
