CRC Industries is well equipped for tune-ups of all kinds. The Berwind company manufactures more than 1,300 items and develops specialized formulas for a range of markets, including the automotive, industrial, electrical, and marine markets. The company makes lubricants, corrosion inhibitors, and cleaners for maintenance and repair applications. It operates 12 facilities that produce more than 70 million cans of aerosol products per year. Among the company's brand names are CRC, K&W, Marykate, and Sta-Lube. Based in Pennsylvania, the company has major operating groups headquartered in Australia, Belgium, New Zealand, and the US.

CRC has major offices Australia, Canada, Belgium, Chile, China, the Czech Republic, Finland, France, Germany, Hungary, India, Malaysia, Mexico, New Zealand, Poland, South Africa, Spain, Sweden, the UK, and the US. It has manufacturing plants in Australia, Belgium, New Zealand, the UK, and the US.

Its automotive product offerings include specialty chemicals for  brakes, electrical system, fuel system, engine, cooling system, transmission and drive train, and power steering system. CRC also supplies appearance products, cleaners and degreasers, corrosion protectants, general purpose lubricants and penetrants, and tools and shop supplies.

Its other major division (electrical/industrial) offers a broad range of maintenance, repair, and operations products in more than 20 categories, including precision cleaners, heavy duty degreasers, lubricants, corrosion inhibitors, and sealants/adhesives for industrial use, as well as chemical products for the electrical market.

CRC serves several markets including automotive, aviation, electrical, marine, hardware, heavy trucking, and industrial. It has salespeople, customer service and marketing staff knowledgeable in specific market areas to respond to specific customers needs.

The company brings new products online to grow market share. In 2014 CRC announced plans to launch its widely-accepted cooling system additive, K&W FIBERLOCK Head Gasket & Block Repair (a pour-in repair additive that can permanently seal cracks and leaks). In 2012 it introduced CRC Top Engine Cleaner, an aftermarket top end cleaner available for do-it-yourselfers and professional automotive technicians.

In 2010 CRC acquired the Salt Terminator product line of professional grade cleaners and corrosion inhibitors from Kwik Tek, to enhance its current line of products.

Investment management fund Berwind has owned CRC since 1981.

CRC was founded in 1958 as Corrosion Reaction Consultants and made a single product.
