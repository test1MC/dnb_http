From Blueberry Burst to Mango Sunrise, YoCream International can provide a different flavor of frozen yogurt for almost every week of the year. The company sells its 40 different flavors of co-branded frozen yogurt, along with ice cream, fruit and dairy smoothies, and frozen custard mixes to foodservice customers. Outlets for YoCream's products range from small yogurt shops to club store giants. The company's other products include a line of fruit smoothies and sorbet, as well as YoCream frozen custard and ice cream. It also offers private-label and co-packing services. Brothers John, David, and James Hanna founded YoCream in 1977. YoCream International is owned by French dairy giant Groupe Danone .
