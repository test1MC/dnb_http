Country roads, take me home, to a place I belong -- West Virginia. Toyota Motor Manufacturing West Virginia (TMMWV) certainly belongs to the country roads, as it is a major component manufacturer for Toyota Motor's North American assembly operations. The company's chief products are four-cylinder and V6 engines, as well as automatic transmissions for its operations in Canada and Indiana. TMMWV has an annual capacity of over 600,000 engines and over 600,000 automatic transmissions per year. The company's products end up in several Toyota models assembled in North America including the Avalon, Lexus RX350, Matrix, RAV4, Sequoia, and Solara.
