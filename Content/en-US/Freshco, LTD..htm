Freshco makes pasteurized grapefruit and orange juice, which it sells under the Indian River Select label -- so named because the company processes its juice from fruit grown in the Indian River citrus district of Florida. The company also carries a line of grapefruit juice with added fiber and vitamins and a grapefruit juice combined with pomegranate juice. Indian River Select juices are sold in several southeastern states and in Minnesota.  Among the company's customers are Publix , Winn Dixie , Wal-Mart , Target , Kroger , Albertsons , and Whole Foods . Freshco was established in 1999 by Florida real estate developer Clifford Burg.
