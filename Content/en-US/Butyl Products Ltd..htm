Whether you're looking to ensure that your tank equipment is watertight, or transform a puddle into a pond, Butyl Products has a product for you. The company makes and installs geomembrane containment and storage products. Its list of products includes pond and lake liners, valves, pumps and related accessories, water purification and testing kits, and water storage and treatment tanks. Founded in 1962, the company also distributes plastic-welding equipment, designs geomembrane-based lining systems, and provides specialist lining supply and installation contracting services. Additionally, Butyl Products manufactures emergency water supply equipment used in refugee camps and disaster sites.
