Interstate Envelope Manufacturing produces more than 8 million envelopes per day and ships them to customers worldwide. The manufacturer offers more than 1,000 envelope and window dies in all styles, shapes, and sizes. Interstate Envelope Manufacturing has expertise in all types of paper-based envelopes, including plain and printed, single and multiple window, and coated and uncoated stock. Led by the Wilkowski family, the company is a third-generation family-owned and -operated business. Interstate Envelope Manufacturing was founded in 1966. It is certified as a Minority and Women Owned Business Enterprise by both New York State and New York City agencies.
