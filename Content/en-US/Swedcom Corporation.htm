Swedcom may offer a smorgasbord of telecommunications products but the company is not Swedish. Originally an importer of Swedish telecommunications products, Swedcom Corporation now charges into the telecom marketplace armed with its own line of wireless equipment. The company designs and manufactures such products as base station antennas, multiplexers, channel banks, filters, and related items for cellular, PCS, and wireless broadband networks. The company also provides training services and technical support for its current products for a select number of discontinued antennas. Swedcom was founded in 1987.
