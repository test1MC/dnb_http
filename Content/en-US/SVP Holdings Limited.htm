Along with their battering rams, the Vikings came to the new world with their sewing machines. SVP Holdings comprises the operations of three sewing icons -- Singer, Husqvarna Viking, and Pfaff -- owned by Kohlberg & Company . Kohlberg & Company purchased the sewing business of Singer N.V. in late 2004. To expand that acquisition, the buyout firm bought VSM Group from European investment firm Industri Kapital in December 2005. SVP Holdings  designs, manufactures, and distributes sewing machines that embroider and overstitch, as well as download designs and patterns from a computer. Its machines, accessories, sewing room furniture, and sewing software are sold at specialty and fabric stores nationwide.
