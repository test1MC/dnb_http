Kennametal Stellite manufactures high-alloy wear-resistant components and consumables. The company specializes in making components (centrifugal castings, investment castings, resin shell castings), consumables (cobalt- and nickel-based rod, wire, powder and wrought products), and small, regularly shaped components that use formed metallic powders. Kennametal Stellite also provides services such as coating and machining. Customers have included General Electric , Rolls Royce , Siemens , and Westinghouse . The company is owned by metal-cutting tool supplier giant Kennametal .

The company's operations include a large research and development facility and more than a dozen manufacturing plants in some 10 countries such as the US, UK, Italy, India, and China. Industries served include oil and gas, mining, petrochemicals, power generation, steel, and aerospace.
