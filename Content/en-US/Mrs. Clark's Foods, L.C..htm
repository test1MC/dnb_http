Busy Mrs. Clark's apron is always tied around her waist. Mrs. Clark's Foods produces salad dressings, sauces, and juices under the Mrs. Clark's brand name, as well as under private-labels for its foodservice customers. The company is a subsidiary of grain cooperative AGRI Industries . The company's dressings, sauces, and mayonnaise come in porable or spoonable varieties. Its fruit and vegetable juices and juice blends come bottled or in shelf-stable packaging. Sizes vary from individual-serving cups to 2,000-pound totes. Mrs. Clark's also offers co-packing services.
