University Directories publishes campus telephone directories for the higher education market. The company also produces other advertising-supported publications to reach its target audience of college students, such as planners, handbooks, course catalogs, and career guides. All total, University Directories publishes some 300 publications on more than 230 campuses in 42 states, with a combined circulation of more than 3 million. University Directories was established in 1974.
