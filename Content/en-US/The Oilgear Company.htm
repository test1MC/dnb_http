A global, fluid powerhouse, The Oilgear Company makes hydraulic pumps and other fluid power components (motors, cylinders, valves, and fluid meters), electronic controls, and systems. The company integrates hydraulic components and electronic controls to ensure they are matched to the appropriate process. Oilgear products are used in the metals, machine tool, automobile, petroleum, aerospace, chemical, plastics, lumber, rubber, and defense industries. The company has manufacturing and service facilities in about 15 countries around the globe. It was established in 1921.

Headquartered in Milwaukee, Oilgear has facilities in Brazil, China, France, Germany, India, Italy, Japan, Korea, Mexico, Spain, the UK, the United Arab Emirates, and the US. It also has sales engineering support in more than 50 countries.

Oilgear's products are used in different types of applications including in mobile, civil, forging, SMC, aerospace, extrusion, wood product, and oil field uses. It serves the oil and gas, military, construction, civil, marine, and mining sectors, and offers its products through its distributors throughout the globe.

Some of its US distributors include Advanced Fluid Systems and AFP Industries; International distributors include Aratron AS, Tube Control AB, and Control Flow Hydraulics.

When the company was founded in 1921, it operated a single hydraulic press line. By the 2000s it was operating an extensive range of hydraulic products for fluid power pumps of all configurations.
