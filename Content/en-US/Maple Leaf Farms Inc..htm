This farm raises a lot of quackers and cluckers. Maple Leaf Farms is a leading poultry producer known mostly for its duck and chicken products. As part of its business, the company processes some 16 million ducks a year sourced from 140 partner farms. The firm's products include duck legs, duck breasts, and whole duck, as well as a variety of value-added items. Maple Leaf Farms sells chicken products under the Milford Valley Farms and Mighty Bites brand names. In addition to consumer products, the company supplies both retailers and foodservice operators with its products. The family-owned business was founded in 1958 by the late Donald Wentzel. It's run by Wentzel's son-in-law Terry Tucker.
