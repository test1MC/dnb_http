If your home needs to be cooled off or heated up, Backwell's has your back. Backwell IXL and its IXL Appliances division manufacture home appliances. Its inventory includes such products as evaporative coolers, fans, portable heaters, and ventilation systems. The Australia-based company also supplies pressed metal products and metal castings to the automotive industry through its foundry IXL Metal Castings. Its appliances are distributed through department stores as well as specialty appliance and lighting stores throughout Australia and New Zealand. Ebenezer Backwell founded the company as a blacksmith's shop in the mid-1850's. Today it is still owned by the fifth generation of the Backwell family.
