Educational Insights wants children to have fun while learning. The company makes hands-on educational products (including the GeoSafari and Smart Games lines), targeting nearly every school subject for students in kindergarten through eighth grade. It also designs games, science activity kits, and books for home or school use. Educational Insights markets its goods through school supply stores, toy dealers, and learning products retailers. Its offerings are available in the US, as well as Canada, Puerto Rico, the UK, and the Virgin Islands. Founded in 1962, the company is a subsidiary of educational toy supplier Learning Resources .
