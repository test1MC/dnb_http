Whether above or below ground, Hunter Plastics provides the answers to your drainage problems. Hunter Plastics manufactures PVC drainage products, including domestic gutters, high-capacity rainwater pipes, soil and waste systems, plumbing accessories, and underground drainage systems. Its grease management products are used in larger commercial and public kitchens. The company sells its products and services through a network of distributors located in more than 30 countries around the globe.

Hunter Plastics has an established network of stockists and distributors in over 30 countries worldwide, supported by a sales force.

Hunter Plastics was founded in the 1950s by Ronald Edmonton; in 1967 the company launched its own line of PVC rainwater, soil, and waste products. Bought by global building products firm ETEX in 1991, Hunter Plastics today functions as part of the Aliaxis Group.
