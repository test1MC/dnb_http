When it comes to making chemicals for wastewater treatment and manufacturing, PVS Chemicals is in its element. The company's product list includes sulfuric and hydrochloric acids, liquid caustic soda, ferric chloride, and ammonium thiosulfate. These chemicals are used in applications such as water treatment (wastewater, process, and municipal), electronics manufacture (including semiconductor etching), gold and copper mining, and food and aluminum production. PVS Chemicals' subsidiaries include PVS Technologies (water treatment), Dynecol (transportation, analysis, treatment, and recycling of chemicals), and PVS Nolwood (chemical distribution).

The company has 13 locations in the US.

The company offer products made by Oxy , Olin , Dow , Rhodia , Stepan , BASF , PPG , ICL , DeForest, and Clariant , along with many other US and international suppliers.

PVS Technologies makes iron salts. Its liquid ferric chloride and liquid ferrous chloride are NSF-approved for use in potable water treatment. Ferric chloride is the chemical of choice for water and wastewater treatment for removal of turbidity, color, DBP precursors, solids, phosphorus, and sulfide odors. The unit also manufactures etchant grade ferric chloride products specifically engineered to customer-specific etchant needs.

PVS Food Chemistry is a provider of food plant sanitation programs. It offers a complete line of products includes cleaners, sanitizers, conveyor lubricants, water treatment products, and some food ingredients.

PVS Chemicals has a fleet of more than 65 tractors and 90 dedicated chemical tankers (stainless, steel, fiberglass, rubber-lined, vac and bulk) from 4,500 to 10,000 gallon capacity.

PVS-Nolwood Chemicals is one of the chemical distributors in the Midwest with a primary focus on Michigan, Ohio, and Indiana. PVS Chloralkali is a marketer of hydrochloric acid.

The company serves a range of industry segments, including Household, Industrial and Institutional Cleaning, Food Additives and Processing Aids, Electroplating, Fertilizer and Pesticide, Biofuels, Environmental Remediation, Chemical Compounding and Blending, Wastewater Treatment, Paints and Coatings, and Paper and Adhesives.

With an overall emphasis on safety and superior service, PVS Chemicals has grown as a hydrochloric acid marketer by strengthening its customer and supplier relationships.

PVS Food Chemistry system is working to provide a method for food processors to take more control their plant sanitation processes resulting in higher quality finished products at lower costs.
