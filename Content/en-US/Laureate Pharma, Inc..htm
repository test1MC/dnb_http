This Pantheon's temple transforms the way pharmaceuticals are made with a simplified, end-to-end supply chain solution for pharmaceutical and biopharmaceutical companies of all sizes. Pantheon Biologics is engaged in drug substances and drug products, including the development and manufacturing of small and large molecules, and sterile, oral solid, and softgel dosage forms. Pantheon Biologics claims to offer a comprehensive range of services that is wider and deeper than that of any other contract development and manufacturing organization. In 2013 Gallus Biopharmaceuticals acquired Laureate Pharma. Pantheon Biologics' parent Pantheon U.S. Holdings Inc. acquired Gallus in 2014.
