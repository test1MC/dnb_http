United Air Specialists has ESP, and the company knows just when to cause static. The company's industrial air pollution control unit makes electrostatic precipitators (ESP) that electrify airborne particles and remove them from the air. United Air Specialists also makes commercial air-cleaning systems, including kitchen emissions systems. In addition, the company makes electrostatic fluid control and high-precision spraying equipment. The company's products are used in offices, hospitals, bars, schools, and industrial settings. United Air Specialists is part of the industrial environmental filtration business unit of manufacturing group CLARCOR .

United Air Specialists has manufacturing plants in China, Germany, the UK, and the US.

The company's four major product lines are Industrial Dust Collectors, Industrial Mist Collectors, Replacement Filters, and Kitchen Emission Systems. Its top businesses lines include KLEENTEK Electrostatic Varnish Removal Systems, which allow electrostatic cleaning of liquids such as lubricating oils and hydraulic fluids, and TotalStat Electrostatic Spraying Sytems which use electrostatic principles to simultaneously charge and propel liquids such as oils, release agents, flavorings, lubricants, and coatings. United Air Specialists also makes commercial air cleaners such as the Smokeeter a two-stage ESP, Crystal-Aire, and the Smog-Hog industrial ESP line.

The company operates as a part of the industrial environmental filtration segment of CLARCOR .

It sells products through a global representative and dealer network to a wide range of markets including Abrasive Blasting, CNC Machining, Cold Heading, Food Processing, Metalworking, Pharmaceutical, Plasma and Laser Cutting, Plastics Processing, Thermal Metal Spraying, and Welding.

The industrial environmental filtration segment accounted for 49% of CLARCOR's revenues in 2013 (2% up on 2012). Dust collection system sales declined (Dust Hog weld fume collection systems and Smog Hog industrial mist collection systems) as the result of lower general industrial activity in Europe.

Product launches are key to the company's growth strategy. In 2012 United Air Specialists launched a new series of shaker dust collection systems used to remove harmful pollutants resulting from common manufacturing processes. The new design enhances the ability of these shaker-style systems to collect airborne pollutants captured at the source.

United Air Specialists was founded in 1966.
