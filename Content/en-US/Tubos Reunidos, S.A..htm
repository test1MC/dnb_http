Tubos Reunidos (Gathered Tube) has been producing seamless products since its founding in 1892. The Spanish company operates two mills in Araba/Alava and Vizcaya that produce steel slab from which it makes seamless pipes and other steel products, including boilers, high or low pressure service pipes, hollows for cold-drawn tubes, line pipes, mechanical tubes, and tubing and casing. Tubos Reunidos serves the automobile, chemical, construction, oil and gas, and petrochemical industries. It distributes and sells its products through a network of 10 offices and has a presence in more than 100 countries on five continents. In the US, Tubos Reunidos America is located in Houston, Texas.
