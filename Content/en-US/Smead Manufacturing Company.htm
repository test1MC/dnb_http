Smead Manufacturing has kept its customers' offices organized for more than a century. The company manufactures and distributes more than 2,000 paper filing products in the US that are sold in office supply stores nationwide (including Office Depot ) and authorized resellers (such as Amazon.com ). Smead's product assortment includes folders (hanging, tabbed, and expandable), labels, fasteners, and binder and report covers, as well as open and secure shelving systems. The company was founded in 1906 by Charles Smead and later sold to the Hoffman family. Smead is owned and run by the third generation of the Hoffman family.

Smead's S&W Manufacturing division supplies custom filing and organizational products to authorized dealers. The company's portfolio of products is packed with top tab file folders, hanging file folders, expanding files, end tab filing products, presentation products, file pockets and jackets, labeling and software products, shelving products, file folder accessories, and custom-made products.

Minnesota-based Smead sells its products throughout the US and Canada. It has offices in Ohio, South Carolina, Georgia, Texas, and Utah.

Despite its vast portfolio, Smead does not sell its products directly to consumers. Across the US and Canada, products are available through independent office products dealers, as well as through office products superstores, authorized resellers, other retailers, and online.
