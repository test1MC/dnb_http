There's clean, and then there's surgical-room clean -- and Getinge USA will help get you there. The company manufactures and distributes medical sterilization equipment under the Getinge Infection Control brand. Its products include autoclaves, disinfecting machines, detergents, cleaners, scrub sinks, and sterile consumables. The company also offers surgical tables and lighting systems under the Maquet Surgical Workplaces brand. Its clients reside in the health care and life science industries. The company also provides consulting, training, installation, tracking, and maintenance services. Getinge USA is a subsidiary of Sweden-based Getinge AB .

Getinge USA primarily conducts business classified under the global Getinge Infection Control segment, including washer-disinfector machines, sterilizers, consumables, and an inventory tracking system (T-Doc). It also sells select Maquet Surgical Workplace equipment made by Getinge's Maquet division; such products include ceiling units, tables, and lighting systems used in surgical suites.

Other global Getinge products -- such as ventilators and heart/lung machines made by Maquet and patient beds and mobility systems made by the ArjoHuntleigh division -- are sold through US affiliates Arjo Inc. and Maquet Inc., which are classified under Getinge's Extended Care and Medical Systems segments.

Getinge USA's customers include including hospitals and medical practices, as well as pharmaceutical and institutional research centers. The company uses direct sales agents and distributors to market its offerings.

While Europe remains Getinge AB's largest market, the company has steadily built up its US presence through acquisitions and research and development efforts. For instance, to offset the ups and downs in demand for big ticket medical products, the company is expanding its line of sterile disposable products used in acute-care situations. It also forms collaborative development partnerships; Getinge USA teamed up with agricultural equipment manufacturer Detach AB in 2011 to develop sterile cleaning solutions for the North American poultry production industry.
