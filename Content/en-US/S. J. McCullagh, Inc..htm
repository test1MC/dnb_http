S. J. McCullagh (dba McCullagh Coffee Roasters) wakes up and smells the coffee every day. It imports, roasts, and wholesales coffee and related products in the US and Canada. S. J. McCullagh sells Arabica, Columbian and other coffee types at different grinds, grades, and roasts (including cold brew coffees and specialty coffees). The company also markets tea, cocoa, sugar and sweeteners, creamers, and flavor syrups, as well as plates and cutlery, cups and lids, and coffee-making machines. S. J. McCullagh traces its roots to 1867 when Samuel McCullagh and his brother founded the New York Tea Company.
