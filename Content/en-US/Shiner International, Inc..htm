Shiner International knows how to put a shiny finish on its customers' products. Through its operating subsidiaries, the company manufactures plastic-based packaging such as coating film, shrink-wrap, anti-counterfeiting laser holographic film, and tobacco film. These film products are used as packaging for food products, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, CDs, cigarettes, and other consumer goods. In addition, the company also offers color printing services for packaged products. Most of Shiner International's customers are located in China; its other customers are located in Europe, North America, and Southeast Asia. The company was established in 2003.
