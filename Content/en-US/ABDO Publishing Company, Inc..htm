ABDO Publishing specializes in non-fiction books for the school library market. The company's six imprints include Sandcastle (for beginning readers), Buddy Books (non-fiction for grades 2-4), Checkerboard (curriculum-based non-fiction for grades 3-5), ABDO & Daughters (non-fiction for grades 4-8.), Super Sandcastle (larger-sized books for beginning readers), and Essential Library (for middle and high school readers). Titles include War on Terrorism Series , Lewis and Clark , and Checkerboard Animal Library . Joseph M. Abdo, together with daughters, Jill (Abdo) Hansen and Melody (Abdo) Borth, founded ABDO Publishing in 1985. Seven Abdo siblings co-own the company.
