The livestock is always just right at Niman Ranch. The company contracts with some 650 independent ranchers who raise animals to Niman's specifications, which include practicing humane animal husbandry, using all-vegetarian feed, and having traceability accountability. Niman Ranch processes and ships the naturally raised fresh beef, pork, poultry, and lamb to supermarkets, specialty retailers, foodservice distributors, and restaurants. The company's meat is featured at the Chipotle Mexican Grill restaurants. Niman Ranch is owned by Chicago-based Natural Food Holdings.
