In Camaco's opinion, where you sit is much more important than where you stand -- the company specializes in manufacturing stamped metal and wire frame seat structures for the automotive industry. In addition, Camaco makes other automotive interior components such as headrest and armrest structures and instrument panel and console substrates. The company's Camaco Freight Services unit delivers automotive parts to manufacturing plants in Canada and the US. Camaco, which was founded in 1996, operates from facilities in Arkansas, Michigan, Nebraska, and Ohio. Customers have included Ford , General Motors , Faurecia , Johnson Controls , and Lear .
