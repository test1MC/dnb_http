Edsal Manufacturing makes and distributes storage systems and industrial furniture through plants and distribution centers located in the Chicago area. Edsal Manufacturing's products include lateral files, lockers, service carts, shop desks, stools, storage cabinets, and work benches. The storage system provider boasts brand names such as Cabinette (compact storage cabinets), Citadel (school, hospital, industrial, and club lockers), HEVILOAD PLUS II (steel shelving), StorMaster Pallet Rack Storage Systems, and UltraCap (steel shelving). The company was established in 1952 by Ed Saltzberg.

Based in Illinois, Edsal Manufacturing specializes in making and marketing quality storage systems and industrial furniture to customers nationwide.

As part of its business, Edsal Manufacturing maintains seven plants and distribution centers in the Chicago area that altogether span more than 1.4 million sq. ft.

The company sells its products through a network of dealers.

Having earned market share in every region in the country, Edsal Manufacturing has worked to maintain its "made in the USA" business philosophy by not sending jobs overseas to reduce overhead. The company continues to employ more than 500 unionized workers and has been able to hire others who have worked for competitors that no longer have local production.

In addition to its emphasis on US-based operations, the company has noted that its build quality and environmental stewardship exceed the efforts of its overseas rivals. Indeed, the company typically makes its products using more than 50% recycled materials. To boot, Edsal says the prices of its products are comparable, and sometimes lower, than those made abroad.
