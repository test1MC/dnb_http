Nisshin Seifun Group is led by Japan's top flour producer, Nisshin Flour Milling. The company divides its operations into flour milling (wheat, germ, bran, and rye; 40% of revenue), food products (deli foods, baking ingredients, frozen food, processed meat products, materials for confectionery and bread, health foods, and pharmaceuticals; 50%), and other (manufacturing plant design and construction, screen-printing products, sports facilities, and pet foods; 10%). The company offers its products and services to customers in retail, manufacturing, and industrial sectors. Founded in 1900, Nisshin Seifun has operations worldwide and is buying four US flour mills.
