Signature Eyewear views prescription eyeglasses and sunglasses as the ultimate fashion statement and an integral part of its business. Laura Ashley , Nicole Miller, and bebe licensed eyewear accounts for about three-fourths of the firm's sales. Signature Eyewear also licenses frames by Michael Stars, Dakota Smith, and Hart Schaffner Marx. Its own Signature line of frames is produced by contract manufacturers in Hong Kong, China, Japan, and Italy, and markets them to optical retailers in more than 20 countries. The company markets footwear and accessories through its Signature Fashion Group division, which was formed in 2010.
