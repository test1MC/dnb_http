Shanghai Hewlett-Packard helps keep North Asia stocked with high-tech products. A regional unit of technology powerhouse Hewlett-Packard (HP), the company manufactures PCs, computer servers, and other products sold in Hong Kong, mainland China, and South Korea. HP's operations in Shanghai also include a product test center and software development operations, as well as a joint venture with microchip giant Intel . In addition to Shanghai Hewlett-Packard, HP's operations in China include Beijing-based China Hewlett-Packard Company ; Hewlett-Packard Technology (Shanghai) Co., Ltd.; and Hewlett-Packard Trading (Shanghai) Co. Ltd.
