Acacia Research Group (aka Acacia Technologies) offers shade to more than 70patent portfolios. The company (which is the primary subsidiary of Acacia Research ) acquires and licenses patent rights to various technologies related to digital audio-on-demand and video-on-demand transmission. It markets many of these patents to makers of electronics gear, who incorporate the technologies into applications for cable, satellite, and Internet distribution of digital content.

Acacia hold patents on a range of technologies for communications, consumer electronics, database, digital media, displays, eCommerce, energy, financial services, graphics, Internet, manufacturing process, medical, memory, printers, security, semiconductors, software, storage, and wireless and mobile technologies.

In 2015, Acacia Technologies settled a number of lawsuits and licensed patented technologies to a variety of companies including BMW , Leica , Conformis, TomTom , Garmin , and Panasonic .
