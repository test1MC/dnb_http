Hamon & Compagnie International knows that business runs hot and cold. It makes draft and wet-dry cooling systems, heat exchangers, air pollution control systems, heat recovery products, industrial chimneys, water treatment and retention systems, and refractory linings. Its customers include companies in agriculture, glass, petrochemicals, power, and steel. Hamon operates mainly through subsidiaries it has acquired over the years. About half of sales come from North America. The Hamon Group is diversifying into alternative energy through acquisitions. The company was first established in France in 1904.
