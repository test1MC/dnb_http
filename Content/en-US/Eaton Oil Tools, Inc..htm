Eaton Oil Tools has made a successful business out of bits and pieces. The company supplies drill bits and other pieces of downhole equipment to oil and gas customers operating in the Gulf Coast region of the US. Its equipment includes milling tools, fishing equipment, hole openers, stabilizers, whipstocks, and joints. Eaton Oil Tools also provides well abandonment services. Operating from its primary supply facility in Broussard, Louisiana, the company provides a range of other services, including equipment design, manufacturing, and technical support.
