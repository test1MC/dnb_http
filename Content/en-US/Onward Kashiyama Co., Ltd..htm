Onward Kashiyama is plodding along into the future. Onward manufactures and distributes men's and women's apparel under its own brands, as well as for design firms Sonia Rykiel, Calvin Klein , Gianfranco Ferré , and Paul Smith , among others. Products are sold through its own stores and distributed worldwide to department stores by its own distribution subsidiary. The company also has subsidiaries involved a range of other industries, including art (Onward Gallery), boat sales (Onward Marine), dancewear (Chacott Co.), golf (Onward Talofofo Golf Club), resorts (Onward Beach Resort), and travel (Onward Travel). Amid the global economic downturn, Onward Kashiyama is fine-tuning its business operations.
