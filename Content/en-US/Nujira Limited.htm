Nujira develops power amplifier technology for the wireless communications industry. It is a leader in envelope tracking technology, which dynamically adjusts the supply voltage to the power amplifier, through its Coolteq power modulators. The company's module and integrated circuit products are used in smartphones and tablets, cellular terminals and infrastructure, and digital broadcast systems. Nujira has raised more than $60 million from investors such as Amadeus Capital Partners and SAM Private Equity. Four former Tality employees, including managing director Tim Haynes, founded the company in 2002.
