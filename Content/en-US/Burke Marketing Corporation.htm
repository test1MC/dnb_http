The secret to better pizza is its topping, according to Burke Marketing. The company manufactures and markets cooked meats used as ingredients in a variety of dishes made by other food manufacturers, foodservice operators, and restaurants in the US, Canada, and beyond. Burke's lines include toppings (beef and pork crumbles and pepperoni), breakfast patties, chicken strips and pieces, shredded beef, meatballs, and Mexican-style meat fillings. The company offers private label, bulk packaging, and custom recipe development; its own brands include Premoro (Italian meats), Tezzata (Mexican meats), and MagniFoods (specialty). Burke is an affiliated business unit of Hormel Foods , within its refrigerated foods segment.
