Miniaturization is key to Minarik, which manufactures and distributes motion control and automation products to customers in 30 states through 16 branch locations. The company manufactures low-horsepower AC, DC, and brushless drives. Its distributed products include connectors, counters/meters, gearing products, safety devices, sensors, and switches, as well as a wide range of motion control devices. The company distributes equipment made by many of the top manufacturers in the industry, including Applied Motion Products, Danaher , Honeywell , Sumitomo , Superior Electric, and Yaskawa . Minarik was acquired by Kaman Industrial Technologies (KIT), a subsidiary of Kaman , in 2010.
