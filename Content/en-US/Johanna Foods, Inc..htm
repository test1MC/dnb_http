The juices are flowing at Johanna Foods. The company has built a business manufacturing and distributing chilled and aseptic fruit juices, nectars, and drinks, as well as yogurt. The company's brands include La Yogurt, Sabor Latino, Ssips, Earth Wise, and Tree Ripe. Additionally, Johanna Foods offers its customers branded, private-label, and contract services. The firm boasts several customer types, including supermarkets, warehouse clubs, convenience stores, food wholesalers, dairy distributors, and food service operators. Johanna Foods' organic and regular food products are distributed throughout the Northeastern, Southeastern, and Midwestern US; its primary market is on the East Coast.

New Jersey-based Johanna Foods distributes its products primarily along the East Coast -- from New England to Florida -- as well as nationwide and internationally.

Johanna Foods is one of the nation's largest makers and distributors of fruit juices, drinks, and yogurt.

The food firm enjoys leading market shares across its product categories and, specifically, in the New York metropolitan market. Customers include supermarkets, warehouse club stores, convenience stores, wholesalers, dairy distributors, independent grocery stores, and food service organizations.

Johanna Foods expanded its yogurt manufacturing plant in Flemington, New Jersey, by 65,000 sq. ft. in 2013 to accommodate future growth among this part of its business. Doubling its capacity, the addition included 19,000 sq. ft. of cold storage and 46,000 sq. ft. of dry goods warehouse and yogurt processing. While boosting the company's production capacity, adding onto the manufacturing plant enables Johanna Foods to more easily introduce new products to meet demand and consumer preferences.
