HCL Infosystems handles IT from A to Z. The company manufactures and distributes a wide array of technology products, including PCs, servers, data storage and networking equipment, printing and imaging systems, and software. It sells both its own lines and third-party products. HCL also provides services that include system integration, facilities management, and managed services. It markets to consumers in more than 15,000 towns across 664 districts in India. The company sells to enterprise clients in such industries as government, financial services, and education. HCL Infosystems' sister company, HCL Technologies , provides offshore IT services.
