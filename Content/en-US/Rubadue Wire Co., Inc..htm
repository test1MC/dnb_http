When it comes to knowing the wily ways of the wiring world, Rubadue Wire Company is no rube. The family-owned and run company makes tacketed and shielded wire for the appliance wiring, automotive, computers, lighting ballasts, medical equipment and devices, power supplies, security systems, transformers, and telecommunications industries. Its insulation materials include Teflon, Tefzel, PVC, Medical PVC, PPL, PE, Foam PE, XL PE, Kynar, and Nylon. The company also makes custom cables for specific applications. Rubadue Wire operates plants in California and Colorado and warehouses its inventory in Hong Kong, Taiwan, the UK, and the US.
