Perhaps unlike you, a hill of beans is worth a lot to Grupo Britt. The company processes and sells premium coffees from Costa Rica, Mexico, and Peru -- light, medium, dark, and espresso roasts as well as decaffeinated and certified organic beans -- under the Cafe Britt brand. Grupo Britt also markets chocolates, candies, cookies, nuts, and gift boxes. Its goods are sold online and at more than 75 duty-free Britt Shops (both kiosks and retail stores) in airports and hotels in about 10 countries, including the US, Peru, Chile, and Curaçao. Chairman Steven Aronson founded the company in 1985.
