Siltronic knows a thing or two about producing silicon for microelectronics. The company makes ultrapure silicon wafers used as substrates in semiconductor manufacturing. Siltronic provides a variety of products, from nonpolished wafers to wafers with an epitaxial layer and silicon-on-insulator wafers. It supplies wafers to most of the top chip makers in the world, including Infineon and Texas Instruments . Siltronic is an affiliate of Wacker Chemie .
