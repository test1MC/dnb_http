D.E.G. Music Products will give you a horn to play and a place to keep it. The company manufactures and distributes marching and orchestral musical instruments, cases, and accessories for wind and percussion instruments, such as finger rests, lubricants, mouthpieces, and tuba rests. Its instruments are manufactured through its Dynasty USA division and include such brand names as Dynasty (marching brasswind and percussion instruments), Schafer (band instruments), and Willson (brass instruments). The company's musical products are distributed to music stores worldwide.
