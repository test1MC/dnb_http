While televisions come in a variety of sizes, Telegent Systems favors those of the portable variety. A fabless semiconductor company, Telegent develops and markets radio-frequency (RF) and mixed-signal chips that enable analog and digital live TV broadcasts on mobile devices. The company's products, designed for installation in mobile phones, laptops, and portable media players, are sold to OEMs, mobile handset manufacturers, and other mobile device makers located in China and Taiwan. Telegent operates primarily through offices in Sunnyvale, California, Shanghai, China, and Taipei, Taiwan. It was founded in 2004 and bought by China's Spreadtrum Communications in 2011.
