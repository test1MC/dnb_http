Valve making is an open and shut case at Velan. The company makes an array of industrial valves including forged steel globe, gate, check, butterfly, ball, metal-seated, and power ball. Other products include steam traps and gear actuators. The company sells its products under the Velan brand name as well as Securaseal, Torqseal, Adareg, and Proquip. Velan's valve products are used in a variety of industries including nuclear power and power generation, chemical and petrochemical manufacturing, oil and gas, pulp and paper, mining, and shipbuilding. The company caters to customers through worldwide service and sales offices and distribution partnerships. About 60% of sales are made outside of North America.
