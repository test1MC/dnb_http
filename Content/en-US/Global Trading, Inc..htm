Global Trading engages in the packing and marketing of processed and frozen fruit. The company operates processing and packaging plants in Mexico; its customers include bakers, ice-cream makers, and flavoring and preserve companies located mostly in the US and Asia. Global Trading's fruit products include whole, sliced, and diced bananas, blackberries, cantaloupe, honeydew melons, lemons, limes, mangoes, oranges, papayas, peaches, pineapples, raspberries, strawberries, and watermelon. The fruits are either IQF (induvidually quick frozen) or hot packed. The company was founded in the 1940s and is owned by president Arthur Price and his family.
