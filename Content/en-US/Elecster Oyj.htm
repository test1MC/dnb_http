Elecster's doesn't milk the cow but they do everything else related to dairies. The company constructs ultra high temperature (UHT) dairy plants and makes the equipment to go in them, including aseptic and pasteurized filling machines and ice-cream freezers. UHT processed milk doesn't need refrigeration. Elecster also makes milk-packaging film as well as polymer packaging materials for use by agro and food businesses. The company's extrusion and printing division provides blown film lines, flexo printing machines, and pre-sterilized films. It also offers turn-key dairy management services. Foreign customers account for about 90% of Elecster's revenue. The company ships its products to more than 60 countries.
