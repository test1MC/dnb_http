Sangoma Technologies can help you get the most out of your technological investments. The company manufactures PC-based hardware and related software that links computers with public telephone networks to enable telephony functions such as managing PBXs, incorporating interactive voice response applications, and routing data and faxes. Sangoma also does business in external gateways that work outside a server and are good for virtualized and cloud services. Customers have included Oracle , Ericsson , Orange , and Cisco Systems . Sangoma Technologies was founded in 1984.

In 2015 (ended June), net revenue was $16.32 million (Canadian) an 18% increase from 2014, aided by contributions from acquisitions. Sangoma posted a $250,000 (Canadian) profit in 2015, 66% less than the 2014 profit. The company blamed the costs of integrating its acquisitions for the reduced profit.

In 2015 Sangoma acquired Schmooze Communications Inc., for $4 million in cash. Schmooze has been the primary developer of open source technology for PBX systems and manager of the project. FreePBX is one of the mostly widely used Internet Protocol-PBXs, which millions of installations around the world.

Also in 2015 Sangoma bought RockBochs, which has offered fax-over-IP monthly service.
