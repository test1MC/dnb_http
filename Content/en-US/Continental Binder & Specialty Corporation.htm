The ties that bind Continental Binder & Specialty's customers to the company are the trust of and familiarity with the company's products. With operations in California and Colorado, the specialty manufacturer turns out packaging products and machines, including vinyl notebook binders, polypropylene boxes, binding machines, collators, and adhesive products and other accessories. Continental Binder & Specialty also makes leather bound notebooks and folders and provides custom printing services. The company also sells used equipment, including bindery machines, foil stampers and embossers, joggers, laminators, and sealers.
