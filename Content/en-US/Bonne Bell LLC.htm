Bonne Bell and the teenage game of spin the bottle go hand in hand. The company's Lip Smackers lip gloss (introduced in 1973) is geared to 'tweens who want their lips to taste like other things, like strawberries, buttered popcorn, or Coca-Cola. In addition to lipsticks and glosses, the company makes Bonne Bell brand eye makeup (such as eye shadows and mascaras), as well as face and body products (bronzing and self-tanning items). Other brand names include LipLITES, lipLUSHous, VitaGloss, and Eye Style. Bonne Bell sells its products through supermarkets, drugstores, beauty retailers, and specialty shops, as well as online. Health and beauty maker Markwins International is buying Bonne Bell from Aspire Brands.
