Wine Communications Group (formerly Wine Business Communications) provides news and information for wine industry professionals. The company publishes trade journals for the wine industry, including Wine Business Monthly, Wine Business Insider, and Wines & Vines Magazine . The company also operates an online job board, winejobs.com, and distributes the Wine Business Daily News e-mail, which features hand-picked articles that pertain to the wine industry. Wine Communications Group also offers its Wines & Vines marketing software package, which allows wineries and growers to generate mailing labels and export data for their direct marketing efforts.
