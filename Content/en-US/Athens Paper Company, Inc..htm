Athens Paper keeps a torch burning in its heart for all sorts of paper products. The company is an independent distributor of paper and paper products, with operations focused on three primary markets: commercial printers; publishing papers; and industrial paper products. Athens Paper distributes products from paper mills such as Georgia Pacific , International Paper , Appleton Papers , Boise Cascade , and Fraser Papers . Athens Paper was founded in 1952 and is owned by co-founder W. Donald Jenkins, Jr. and the heirs of late co-founder M. H. Chamberlain, Jr.
