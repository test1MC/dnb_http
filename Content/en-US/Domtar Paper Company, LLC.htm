Domtar Paper Company is the US subsidiary of Canadian paper manufacturer Domtar Corporation . The parent company is North America's leading producer of uncoated freesheet paper, which is used by retailers, printers, and publishers, among other customers. It generates about 85% of sales from pulp and paper products, including communication papers and packaging papers. In addition, Domtar makes absorbent hygiene products such as infant diapers and Attends adult disposable briefs, pads, underpads, and tissues. The US market represents about three-fourths of its total revenues.

As per its product line, Domtar Paper belongs to the Pulp and Paper segment of the parent company. The segment accounted for 83% of Domtar 's net revenues in 2015.

Parent Domtar's ten largest customers represented 42% of its Pulp and Paper segment sales in 2015 (35% of total sales). One of these, Staples , represented 10% of its total sales. The majority of its customers purchase products through individual purchase orders. In 2015, 76% of Pulp and Paper segment sales were to US customers, 11% were in Canada, and 13% were in other countries.
