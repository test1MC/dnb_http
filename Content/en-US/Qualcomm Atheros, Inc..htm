Qualcomm Atheros (formerly Atheros Communications) builds high-speed connections right through the ether. The fables chip company provides wired and wireless technologies for the computing, consumer electronics, mobile, and networking markets. Its portfolio includes Wi-Fi, Powerline, and Ethernet products, as well as WLAN, Bluetooth, and GNSS technologies. Qualcomm Atheros generates most of its sales in Asia; customers have included some of the biggest names in computer hardware and consumer electronics. A subsidiary of QUALCOMM , the company was started by faculty members from Stanford and Berkeley .

The company has sales offices in the US, as well as in China, Germany, Japan, Macao, South Korea, and Taiwan.

As mobile devices become more ubiquitous, Qualcomm Atheros develops and introduces products to capitalize on the fast-growing trend.
