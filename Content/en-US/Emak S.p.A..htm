The company (actually a group of companies) is a leading manufacturer of portable gardening and forestry maintenance machinery in Italy. Its products include chain saws, lawn mowers, and hedge trimmers, sold under the Efco and Oleo-Mac brand names. Brushcutters and chainsaws are the company's most popular products. Emak distributes its products through subsidiaries in its major markets, which include Belgium, France, Germany, Italy, and the UK. Holding company Yama S.p.A. controls Emak, which was originally created from the 1992 merger of the Efco and Oleo-Mac companies.
