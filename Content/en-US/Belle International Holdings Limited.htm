Belle International stands on its own two feet as a Chinese shoe company that's not a contract manufacturer for Western brands. It manufactures and sells footwear under its own brands -- Basto, Belle, Jipi Japa, Joy & Peace, Mirabell, Millie's, Senda, Staccato, Tata, and Teenmix. The company operates more than 18,000 retail stores in mainland China and about 150 in Hong Kong and Macao, where it distributes shoes by Bata , BCBG , Caterpillar , Clarks , Geox , and Merrell , as well as sportswear by Adidas , Converse , and Nike , among others. The company's five manufacturing plants have an annual production capacity of 40 million pairs of shoes. Belle International was founded in 1978.
