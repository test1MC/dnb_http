Despite the name, this company probably won't be confused with a certain soccer club . Man Yue Technology Holdings makes and markets electronic components sold under the SAMXON and X-Con brands. The company primarily produces aluminum electrolytic capacitors used in the manufacture of automobile electronics, computers, consumer electronics, industrial machinery, and telecommunications equipment. Man Yue Technology distributes its products mostly in Asia from offices in China, Hong Kong, Malaysia, Taiwan, and the US. The company was established in 1979 strictly as a trading concern and began manufacturing its own products more than a decade later.
