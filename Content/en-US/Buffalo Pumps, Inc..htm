Buffalo Pumps has been pumping out industrial pumps for more than a century. The company -- located yes, just north of Buffalo, New York -- manufactures mainly centrifugal pumps used in refrigeration, power generation, and marine defense industries. Commercial pumps are the Buffalo Pumps' key product, which it supplies to OEMs in the commercial lube oil, paper, and refrigeration markets. The company also offers overhaul services, and is able to reproduce parts originally fabricated over 50 years ago. Major customers have included Carrier , General Electric , Solar Turbines , and the US Navy . Buffalo Pumps is a subsidiary of Pennsylvania-based manufacturer Ampco-Pittsburgh Corporation .

Buffalo Pumps is stationed in New York and has operations throughout Canada and the US.

Buffalo Pumps operates as one of the three divisions comprising the Air and Liquid Processing segment of Ampco-Pittsburgh Corporation. This segment generated 33% of its parent company's sales in fiscal 2013. This segment saw an overall 9% decrease in sales for 2013 due to reduced demand for custom air handling equipment by universities and research facilities.

While the defense market represents the company's largest client base, Buffalo Pumps also serves major OEMs in the power generation, marine, refrigeration, chemical, general service centrifugal pumps, mining, navy, marine, and pulp and paper markets. It sells its products through its sales offices to the sophisticated commercial and industrial users.
