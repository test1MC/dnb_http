Maxell Europe is making sure the information age is recorded and powered up. A subsidiary of storage media company Hitachi Maxell , Maxell Europe manufactures a range of storage media and accessories for consumer and professional applications. Its products include computer and professional broadcast tapes; optical discs; headphones; and alkaline, micro, industrial, consumer audio, and lithium-ion rechargeable batteries. Maxell Europe began with the opening of a German sales office in 1976; the company now has six sales offices across the continent and a plastic injection molding factory in the UK.

The company's European headquarters is based in Maidenhead, UK, supporting all branches and distributors throughout Europe. Its sales offices reside in the UK, Germany, France (and Belgium/Luxembourg territories), Italy, Hungary, (and CEE territories), and Russia.

Maxell Europe also makes industrial products, including functional materials (harnessing tapes used in the automotive industry), silver oxide coin cell (mercury-free watch battery cells), heat-resistant battery, and lithium ion rechargeable batteries for portable electronic devices.
