Looking to go some place other than here? Away.com is a website for travel information and vacation planning. The site operates as part of The Away Network, which also includes Trip.com (flights, hotels, and car rental seach), GORP.com (National Park information and recommendations), and OutsideOnline.com (outdoor adventure-related content). In addition, Away.com offers a searchable database of vacation packages and a travel-booking center. Investors include TEGNA , New World Ventures, Potomac Ventures, Circle T Partners, and National Leisure Group. The company is supported by advertising and was founded in 1999.
