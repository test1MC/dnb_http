Integrated Paving Concepts (IPC) has street smarts. The company's main product is StreetPrint -- decorative asphalt used as crosswalks and sidewalks, traffic medians, driveways, and parking lots. Other products include DuraTherm, LogoTherm, Imprint, and Ride-A-Way, all decorative surface treatments and materials designed to last longer than traditional stone or gravel applications. IPC's products are installed in about 40 countries by a licensed network of around 750 contractors for such end-users as government projects, commercial and residential developers, and homeowners. The company was founded in 1992 and is majority owned by management firm Trimin.
