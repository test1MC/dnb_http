Heat Controller keeps things warm and cozy or cool and breezy -- whatever you're in the mood for. The company makes and distributes the Comfort-Aire and Century brands of HVAC equipment for residential and light commercial use. Its products include central air conditioning units, gas and oil furnaces, portable units, and dehumidifiers; the company also makes air coils, heat pumps, and other unit elements. Heat Controller distributes its products in the US, while subsidiary Aitons' Equipment is active in Canada. Heat Controller was founded in Ohio in 1933 and moved its operations to Michigan in 1955.
