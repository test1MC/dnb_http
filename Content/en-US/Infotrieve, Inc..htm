Infotrieve takes playing "fetch" to a whole new level. The company provides scientific, medical, and technical on-demand information as well as numerous information management services. Infotrieve delivers more than a million articles and reports annually from professional and scholarly journals, conferences, and other sources. It also provides library services such as contract staffing, management, and a variety of software applications. In addition, Infotrieve assists with copyright permissions and clearance. Clients include corporations, professionals, colleges, and universities. Outside of the US, the company has offices in Australia, Canada, Germany, and the Netherlands. It was founded in 1988.
