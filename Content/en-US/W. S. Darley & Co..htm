If the burning ring of fire is for real, and not just love, you'll welcome W. S. Darley. The company makes firefighting equipment, including pump and foam systems and fire trucks. Its extensive range of products includes emergency lights and sirens, fire protection nozzles and other gear, forest fire drip torches, hazmat equipment, rescue gear, and traffic cones. W. S. Darley, which has sizeable export and US Department of Defense (DoD) contracts. Businesses include Odin Foam (compressed air foam systems R&D), PolyBilt (truck bodies), and Ohler Pumps (centrifugal pumps). It ships its products to over 70 countries. Since 1908 four generations of the Darley family have owned and operated the company.
