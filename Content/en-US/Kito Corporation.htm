If your work requires a lift, Kito offers material-handling equipment that can give it a proper boost. The company makes and sells chain hoists and cranes in both standard and customized versions. It holds more than half of the Japanese market share in manual and electric chain hoists and lever blocks, while its rope hoists are popular in the Chinese market. A majority of sales are made from these products, while a smaller percentage is made from aftersales service on equipment and parts. Kito has manufacturing and sales operations in Asia, Europe, and North America. The company targets customers in the chemical, construction, manufacturing, and transportation industries, among others.
