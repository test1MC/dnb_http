Professional Tool Manufacturing does business bit by bit. The company makes drill bit sharpeners, accessories, and replacement parts. It has sold more than 2.5 million Drill Doctor products since its debut in 1997. They're sold through such retailers as Sears , Home Depot , and Lowe's throughout North America and overseas. The Drill Doctor is available in four models. Smaller models include the DD350X, designed for homeowners and weekend craftsmen, and the XP Series, for the serious do-it-yourselfers. The DD500X and the DD750X are both shop models made for heavy-duty use. Parts made by the company include chucks and sharpening wheels.
