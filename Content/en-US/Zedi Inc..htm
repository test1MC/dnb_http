Unlike a Jedi, Zedi doesn't need to use the force to know what is happening. The company has developed equipment for that. Zedi manufactures products and services to support the operation and management of oil and gas wells. Its primary product is Smart-Alek, a wireless meter designed for the remote monitoring of natural gas production and to increase operator efficiencies. Other brands include PetroNet (production accounting) and Roughneck (operations management). In 2011 the company acquired service and supply firm Southern Flow Companies from PowerSecure International for $16.5 million. Zedi was founded in 1987; its name comes from the Japanese word for "the final frontier."
