Never mind that the word "galaxy" is derived from the Latin word for "milk," Galaxy Nutritional Foods operates in an alternate dairy universe. The company's soy-based dairy alternatives include sliced cheese, butter, milk, and yogurt. They're sold in retail food outlets in the US, Canada, and about 10 other countries. Its other brands include Rice, Rice Vegan, Vegan, Veggie, Veggy, and dairy products such as Wholesome Valley Organic Cheese. Wisconsin-based Schreiber Foods makes, supplies, and delivers Galaxy's products. The company was taken private in 2009 when its shares were acquired by majority owner Galaxy Partners and private investment company Mill Road Capital, through affiliate Andromeda Acquisition.

Galaxy had an agreement with Fromageries Bel , initiated in 2003, to distribute its products in the European Union States and more than 20 other European countries and territories. However, the companies terminated the agreement in 2005.

Galaxy sold its supplies and equipment to Schreiber Foods in 2005 and in 2006 signed a renewable five-year manufacturing and supply agreement with Schreiber.
