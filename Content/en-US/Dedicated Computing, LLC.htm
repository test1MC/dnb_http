Dedicated Computing is the name of the business, as well as a product description. Founded in 1978, the company designs, builds, validates, and integrates compute engines (purpose-specific computers) for equipment manufacturers and software developers. Used by clients in communications, digital security, manufacturing automation, medical equipment, defense, and network appliances industries, its products are typically embedded in another piece of digital equipment or are used to host a dedicated software application to improve performance and stability. Products include PC motherboards, servers, single-board computer systems, low power processors, and storage systems.
