VIRA Insight (formerly Vira Manufacturing) helps stores stay tidy and well-organized. The company manufactures merchandising systems and fixtures for the retail industry, including kiosks, display cases, cabinets, dressing rooms, garment racks, and wall systems. It also offers such services as fixture design, prototyping, installation, and spatial analysis. VIRA's customers have included electronics, fashion, and home goods retailers, as well as banking institutions. Vira Manufacturing, which was founded in 1991, merged with Insight Merchandising in 2015 to form VIRA Insight.
