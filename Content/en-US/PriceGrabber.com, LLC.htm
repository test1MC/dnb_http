Sometimes being grabby is encouraged. PriceGrabber.com is an online price comparison service. The website provides information on millions of products through some 25 channels, including computers, clothing, jewelry and watches, movies, music, books and magazines, cameras, and toys. The site also allows users to compare merchants by providing price data and shopper reviews. Advertisers pay PriceGrabber.com a fee for each click it receives through PriceGrabber.com. The firm also offers comparison shopping for Portuguese and US Spanish speakers (PrecioMania.com), as well as through international sites in Canada (French and English), Mexico, and the UK. PriceGrabber.com is part of credit-reporting firm Experian .
