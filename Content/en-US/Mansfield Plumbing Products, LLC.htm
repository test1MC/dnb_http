Mansfield Plumbing Products manufactures plumbing fixtures and fittings for use in residential, commercial, and institutional markets. The company sells its lavatories, bathtubs, toilets, bidets, whirlpools, shower bases, sinks, and brass and plastic plumbing fittings and parts through more than 1,000 wholesale plumbing distributors in North America. Mansfield also sells through retail home-improvement centers and more than 400 kitchen and bath showrooms. Mansfield Plumbing Products, founded in 1929, is controlled by privately owned Organización Corona, a top Colombia-based sanitary ware maker. Mansfield has two production facilities in Ohio and one in Texas.
