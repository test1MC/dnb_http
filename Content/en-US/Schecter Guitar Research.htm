What Schecter Guitar Research doesn't know about guitars could fit on the edge of a pick. The company makes and markets production and custom-built basses, guitars, and seven-string electric guitars. Bands the likes of The Cure and Papa Roach have been know to use its products. Schecter Guitar Research's instruments and strings are sold across the country and internationally at specialty music retail stores, such as Guitar Center , Hermes Music , Best Buy , Music Mart, Music Centre Canada, and Steve's Music Store. Owner and president Michael Ciravolo's wife Trish spun off Daisy Rock Guitars, which makes guitars in whimsical designs (such as daisies and hearts), in 2003.
