Window-smashing hooligans create a lot of inconvenience, but they also create a lot of business for this company. A processor and installer of flat glass in the UK, Glassolutions Saint-Gobain Limited makes tempered glass, thermal insulating glass, silvered glass, laminated glass, and mirrors. It has expertise in external facade and interior glazing design and installation and designing structural glass systems, double-glazed glass, and processed curved glass. It also offers window and frame replacement and repair services. Glassolutions is owned by Compagnie de Saint-Gobain unit Saint-Gobain plc .
