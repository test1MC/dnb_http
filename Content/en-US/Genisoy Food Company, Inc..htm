Use enough MLO and you may not need your HMO. MLO is one of Genisoy Food Company's sports-nutrition products. The company manufactures soy-based, non-genetically-modified powdered health supplements, trail mixes, crisps (the company calls 'em soytatoes), protein shakes and powders, snack foods, and energy bars. Its name-sake product, Genisoy, is a line of snacks and energy bars containing soy protein. The company's goods are available in grocery stores throughout the country; Genisoy also sells certain products through its online retail store. Genisoy Food Company was founded in 1964.
