Rosti is putting it all on plastic. The company develops, manufactures, and sells plastic products for use in the health care and packaging sectors. The company also provides precision plastic component design and manufacturing for consumer electronics, business machines, and automotive products. Specializing in blow molding, the company churns out plastic goods from production sites across Europe, North America, and Asia. Rosti was founded in 1944 as a wholesaler before moving into component manufacturing then taking up the plastics trade. Rosti has been part of A.P. Møller - Mærsk since 1971.
