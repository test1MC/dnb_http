Telsis Group provides infrastructure and SMS services for wireline and wireless operators worldwide. Telsis's network technology includes SMS Routing, application servers, and the SMS Smart Care channel for customer self-service. User services include SMS Smart Services (phone number blocking, cloud archiving); SMS Instant Noticeboards (Just Dot Me -- messages activated by the mere posting of a period/full stop); and SMS SafeGuard (protection from spam and other attacks at the transport, content, and personal levels).
