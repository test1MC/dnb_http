COFIDE - Compagnia Finanziaria De Benedetti (COFIDE, for short) isn't short on activities; the holding company sports businesses as diverse as Italy's regional cuisines. Controlled by Italian businessman Carlo De Benedetti, it operates subsidiaries active in media ( Editoriale L'Espresso group); utilities (Energia); and automotive components (the SOGEFI group). COFIDE additionally includes healthcare (The HSS Group and The Kos Group) and financial services (Jupiter Finance and The Oakwood Group) operations. COFIDE has operations primarily in Europe and the Americas. Its main subsidiary is 48%-owned CIR , which is a holding company for most of these businesses. The De Benedetti family owns some 46% of COFIDE.
