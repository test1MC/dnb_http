Anglia Circuits finds that making many types of one product works well. The company manufactures a variety of printed circuit boards (PCBs), including multilayer, plated through hole, and conventional types; it offers them in various finishes, such as electroplated tin, electroless nickel, and even sterling silver. Anglia Circuits has won awards for its environmental practices, which stress recycling, training, and waste reduction. The company also teams up with Far East PCB suppliers to offer its customers a wider range of price options. Chairman Nigel Kember founded Anglia Circuits in 1969, when he started making blank PCBs -- at the time an emerging technology -- in his garage.

Besides the PCB business, Anglia owns and operates a marina and portfolio of commercial properties in Woodbridge Suffolk, UK.

Anglia is based in the UK and works with PCB suppliers to offer a wider range of pricing options.
