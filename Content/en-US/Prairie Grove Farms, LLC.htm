For those who like their pork without any added au jus of the chemical kind, there is Prairie Grove Farms. The Illinois company contracts with Midwestern farmers to raise hogs without using antibiotics or growth hormones and are vegetarian fed. After the pigs are raised, Prairie Grove takes over, processing, marketing, and distributing fresh and processed value-added natural pork products to both foodservice operators and food retailers in the US. (The Prairie Grove brand was marketed and distributed by Cargill until 2008.)
