eLOT (formerly EXECUTONE Information Systems) has cast its chips into the online gaming arena. The company sold its computer telephony and health-care communications businesses in 2000 to focus on its eLottery subsidiary, which offers online sales of state lottery tickets. The unit operates Web sales of lottery tickets in the US and Jamaica and its eLottoNet unit keeps players informed of lottery results with a free e-mail notification service. The company has targeted its services to provide administration of online sales of lottery tickets for various states. eLOT and its eLottery subsidiary filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection in 2001 emerging in 2002 as a private company.
