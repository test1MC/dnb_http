LOGIC Devices doesn't produce philosophical machines. Rather, LOGIC specializes in high-end digital signal processor (DSP) chips used in applications including medical imaging, instrumentation, telecommunications, and military weapons systems. The company outsources production of its chips to Asian foundries, primarily Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing . LOGIC works with sales representatives and international distributors, and also sells directly to OEMs, including Lockheed Martin , QUALCOMM , Raytheon , Sony , Teradyne , and Texas Instruments .
