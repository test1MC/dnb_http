ElringKlinger knows how to handle pressure. As a manufacturer of automobile parts, the company specializes in cylinder head gaskets for passenger vehicle engines, as well as specialty gaskets for powertrains and exhaust systems. It also operates a division that makes spare engine parts, such as drain plugs, metal rings, and heavy-duty plastic sealing rings and one for lithium-ion batteries. Supplying parts to OEMs in about 140 countries, ElringKlinger's customers include almost every major car manufacturer around the globe, including Ford , Hyundai , Tata Motors , Toyota , and Volkswagen . The company operates through more than 40 facilities located in the US and more than 20 countries in Africa, Asia, and Europe.
