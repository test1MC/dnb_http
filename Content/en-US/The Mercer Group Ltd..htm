The Mercer Group, doing business as Baby Blanket Suncare, wants to make sure that the only time your baby turns red is when the little darling is bawling for a diaper change or feeding. The company markets sunscreen products for babies and adults with sensitive skin. Its line includes sunblock lotions, sticks, sprays, and towelettes, all with SPF ratings of 30 or higher. Baby Blanket Suncare also offers water sandals and sunglasses for babies. The company's products are sold via its website and other online outlets, including drugstore.com . In addition, the firm works with distributors in Mexico, South Africa, and the Asia/Pacific region. Baby Blanket Suncare was founded in 1986 and is owned by its officers.
