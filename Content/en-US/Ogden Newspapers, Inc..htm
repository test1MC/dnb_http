Despite its name, newspapers isn't the only game for this company. Ogden Newspapers is a leading publisher with more than 40 daily papers serving mostly small markets in about 15 states. Its portfolio includes The Messenger (Fort Dodge, Iowa), The News-Sentinel (Fort Wayne, Indiana), and the Wheeling News-Register (West Virginia). Ogden Newspapers also publishes several weekly papers and free shoppers; sister company Ogden Publications produces regional and niche interest magazines such as Mother Earth News . Other operations include commercial printing and phone directories. H.C. Ogden founded the Wheeling News in 1890. His descendents, the Nutting family, continue to control the business.
