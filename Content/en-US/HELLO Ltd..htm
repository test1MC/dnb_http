HELLO! is always ready to heartily greet readers with a little dish on the hottest celebrities and the most newsworthy royals. HELLO! Ltd. publishes British celebrity news magazine HELLO! The company also operates a companion website with celebrity content, including blogs and photos, as well as a digital edition of the print magazine that is available by a paid annual subscription or payment per weekly issue. Its website also offers downloadable desktop tools for those who just can't wait for their celebrity news. The company additionally publishes global sites for gossip lovers in Argentina, Brazil, Canada, Greece, Mexico, Russia, and Spain. HELLO! was launched in 1988.
