Grand Hall Enterprises sounds like a piano manufacturer, but the company actually makes high-end gas barbecue grills. In fact, its X-Series line of grills was designed by Porsche . Grand Hall grills are sold in the US through Frontgate catalogs and Barbecues Galore , but the company also does a lot of business in Europe (where it has a showroom in Paris), Asia, and Australia (where grills are sold at Harvey Norman , among other retailers). The company's more affordable Member's Mark brand is sold only in the US at Sam's Club . Grand Hall also makes an energy-efficient water heater called Eternal Hybrid. The company was founded in 1976.
