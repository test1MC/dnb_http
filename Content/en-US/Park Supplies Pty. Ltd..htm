Park Supplies has all the provisions for fun on the playground. Doing business as Miracle Recreation Equipment, the company designs and manufactures colorful free-standing and combination structures that include swings, slides, carousels, monkey bars, ladders, and water cannons. It also produces recreation area furnishings, such as benches, drinking fountains, shade structures, barbecues, and trash bins. Park Supplies primarily serves schools, water parks, and local government agencies in Australia. It has been distributing its products throughout the country and abroad for more than 25 years.
