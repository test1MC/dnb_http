Aurora Computer Services never seems to have a problem putting a name to a face. The company develops and markets facial recognition technology for such functions as access control, employee attendance, student registration, and passenger boarding. It also provides monitoring software and software development kits. Customers have included government agencies, airports (such as London's Heathrow ), police forces, schools, and construction companies in the UK and the Middle East. Founded in 1998, Aurora Computer Services has deployed its technology at more than 1,000 sites.

Aurora Computer Services operates through three divisions. Aurora Creative provides facial recognition and image recognition products and services for customer's advertising and branding purposes, while Aurora Security provides facial recognition technology for time, attendance, and security applications. In addition, the Aurora Cloud is a developer's tool specifically made for technology enthusiasts.

Performing with over 93% accuracy, the company's face recognition products log over 15 million transactions a year. They utilize infrared technology that is operable in any shade of light, from bright sunlight to darkness. Setting up an ID in the system can be finished in less than five seconds, and the actual identity check can be completed in a second or less.
