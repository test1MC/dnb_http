Vocality offers communications networking equipment and services that allow mobile and remote users to connect to their networks via satellite or other wireless means. Through such brands as BASICS and ELITE, the company provides routers, multiplexers, satellite link simulators, and integrated products. It also provides related software and services, including maintenance and repair, training, configuration, and legacy product support. Vocality serves the military market, as well as businesses in the energy, broadcasting, and maritime industries, among others. The company markets its products through systems integrators, distributors, and resellers.
