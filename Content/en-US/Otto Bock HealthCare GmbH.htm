Otto Bock HealthCare offers mobility options for disabled patients. The company makes and distributes prosthetic and orthotic equipment, including leg braces, electronic hands and shoulders, and cosmetic false limbs, as well as implantable neurostimulation devices. Otto Bock HealthCare also manufactures mobility and seating devices for disabled adults and children, including wheelchairs, positioning supports, and standing devices. Its products are marketed worldwide through internal and external sales representatives. The company is controlled by descendants of founder Otto Bock, who launched the business in 1919.
