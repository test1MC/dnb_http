This magazine publisher is no dummy when it comes to financial matters. SmartMoney publishes SmartMoney and the SmartMoney.com website, offering insight into investing, the economy, personal finance, and business news and information. The monthly title also provides general interest coverage of travel, fashion, and culture, while the website offers daily business news headlines, stock trading information, and interactive financial tools. The magazine debuted in 1992 and the website was launched in 1997. SmartMoney is part of The Wall Street Journal Digital Network . Formerly a joint venture between Dow Jones (owner of The Wall Street Journal ) and Hearst , Dow Jones acquired Hearst's half of SmartMoney in 2010.

The company also owns and operates SmartMoney Custom Solutions, a custom publishing division. Its SmartMoney.com features more than 50 worksheets, a portfolio management system, and its signature Map of the Market product, which allows users to visualize the wide-ranging market in a single view.

Dow Jones and Hearst had shared ownership of the company since its founding; Dow Jones bought the remaining 50% stake in SmartMoney in order to expand its portfolio of financial media brands. It is specifically using SmartMoney -- whose editorial team has won multiple National Magazine Awards and Pulitzer Prizes -- to bolster content at The Wall Street Journal .
