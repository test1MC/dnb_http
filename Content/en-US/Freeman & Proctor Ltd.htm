Freeman & Proctor is a contract manufacturer of fabricated tube and pipe assemblies used in the the aerospace and automotive industries. Services include tube manipulation, fabrication, machining, tool making, and prototype design and manufacturing. It offers welding and brazing of most ferrous and non-ferrous materials, particularly titanium and stainless steel. Its pipe assemblies are used in air, hydraulic, and fuel systems in aerospace engines, while its suspension parts and roll cages are used in high-performance race cars. The company uses computer-aided design to develop new products in its UK facility. Freeman & Proctor was founded in 1949 and is owned by Universal Steels & Aluminum Group.
