M H C S (dba Veuve Clicquot Ponsardin) has made ultra-premium champagnes and wines since the late 1700s. Its portfolio includes vintages from France (where it was founded), Australia, New Zealand, Italy, and the US. Brand names in France include its popular Champagne Veuve Clicquot, as well as Demi-Sec, GlobalLight, La Grand Dame, Vintage Rich, Vinage Rosé, and Yellow Label. In Australia and New Zealand, it produces wines at Cape Mentelle Vineyards and Cloudy Bay Vineyards. Italy boasts Ceretto, Vistorta, and other names. In the US, Clicquot makes wines at Newton Vineyard . The firm was established by Philippe Clicquot in 1772. Veuve Clicquot is part of the Moët Hennessey USA family.

M H C S is headquartered in Reims, France, and has stores in Reims, London, Hamburg, Stockholm, Moscow and Nigeria.

The company sells its products through wine stores.

In order to make its products more recyclable, the company introduced 100% recyclable packaging for its champagne made from grapes.
