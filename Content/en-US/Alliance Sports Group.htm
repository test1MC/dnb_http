Alliance Sports Group (ASG) has shifted its focus outdoors. The firm, once known principally for its fitness accessory products (abdominal exercisers, aerobic steps, barbells and dumbbells, boxing accessories, weightlifting belts and gloves), now sells a line of outdoor and camping products under brand names such as WeatherRite (camping and backyard recreation), Quarrow (fishing rods), Triax (paintball equipment), Nebo (sleeping bags and camping gear), and Lure-Eyes (sport sunglasses). ASG also makes Nebo brand tools, including flashlights, lanterns, cutting tools, and socket wrenches. Bell Sports , a maker of bicycle helmets and accessories, bought the company's fitness accessory line in 2002.
