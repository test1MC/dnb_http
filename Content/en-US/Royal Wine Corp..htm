The libations of the Royal Wine Corp. are never passed over. The company produces, imports, and distributes kosher wine, grape juice, and spirits. It owns and operates two wineries, both of which are located in the US -- the Herzog Wine Cellars, in Oxnard, California, and the Kedem Winery, in Marlboro, New York. In addition to making its own wines, Royal imports both wine and spirits from 18 other countries. Wine is the company's strong suit, however -- it sells more than 1 million cases of wine and grape juice annually. Royal Wine is owned and operated by the Herzog family, which traces its roots back to Phillip Herzog, royal winemaker to Emperor Franz Joseph, leader of the Austro-Hungarian empire.
