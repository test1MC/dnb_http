Johnson Matthey Battery Systems Engineering (JMBSE; formerly Axeon) is a supplier of lithium-ion batteries to the automotive and electronics markets in Europe, processing more than 70 million cells a year. Its products are designed to power electric and hybrid-electric vehicles, power tools, consumer electronics, and medical equipment, among other products. The company's clients have included UK electric vehicle suppliers Allied Vehicles and Modec, as well as power tool maker Bosch . JMBSE has international operations in Germany, Italy, Poland, and Switzerland. It is owned by Johnson Matthey PLC .

Johnson Matthey PLC operates through five divisions: Emission Control Technologies, Process Technologies, Precious Metal Products, Fine Chemicals, and New Businesses. Part of the New Businesses division, Battery Technologies comprises Johnson Matthey's R&D programs in advanced battery materials and JMBSE.
