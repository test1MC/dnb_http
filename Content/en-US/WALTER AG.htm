WALTER always did have a fascination with building things out of metal when it was a babe; now it's a metalworking giant. The company manufactures and sells industrial machining systems and metal-cutting tools. Its tool division makes carbide tools for boring, cutting, milling, and turning. WALTER also produces a line of tool data management software to aid in project planning and production. Founded in 1919, WALTER has operations worldwide. Sandvik AB owns about 96% of the company and groups WALTER under its Sandvik Tooling segment. In the interest of joint global marketing and sales, WALTER bundles Sandvik's PROTOTYP and TITEX brands under its corporate banner.
