Yo, Semiconductor-san -- SANYO Semiconductor has got your chips right here! The ON Semiconductor subsidiary makes a variety of semiconductors, including amorphous silicon solar cells, application-specific integrated circuits, audio and video chips, discrete devices, EEPROMs, flash memory devices, hybrid integrated circuits, and microcontrollers. The company's electronic components are used in automobiles, DVD players and recorders, home appliances, inkjet printers, LCD TVs, PC CD-ROM drives, optical disk drives, and portable electronics. In early 2011 SANYO Electric sold the company to ON Semiconductor in a deal valued at around $520 million.
