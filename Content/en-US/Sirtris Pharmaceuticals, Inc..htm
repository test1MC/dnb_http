Sirtris can help keep you healthy in your golden years. The biopharmaceutical company focuses on developing drugs for treating ailments of aging. Its primary candidate is designed to treat Type 2 Diabetes, which is associated with obesity, by providing health benefits similar to calorie restriction without a change in diet. Other candidates include drugs to treat cancer, cardiovascular, and other diseases often linked to obesity. Sirtris owns or licenses several patent applications for sirtuins, a class of enzymes believed to be associated with the aging process. The activation of sirtuins helps reduce weight gain and improve glucose levels and insulin sensitivity. Sirtris is a subsidiary of GlaxoSmithKline .
