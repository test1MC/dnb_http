Fabbrica D'Armi Pietro Beretta, the world's oldest gunsmith, has seen nearly 500 years of war and peace. Founded in 1526, the Beretta family company has manufactured arms for more than 15 generations. Products include rifles, shotguns, pistols, and knives. The firm has made weaponry for the French, Italian, and US militaries; police forces; and past Olympic medalists. Sporting guns account for nearly all sales. Additionally, Beretta sells apparel and accessories online, through catalogs, in more than 70 Beretta Shop in Shops, and Beretta Gallery stores in Buenos Aires, Dallas, London, Milan, New York City, and Paris. Beretta USA is the company's North American manufacturing and marketing arm.
