The money's in the mattress at VI-Spring. The UK manufacturer builds made-to-order, hand-crafted mattresses and divans. VI-Spring has made its mark by offering a number of industry firsts, including a mattress warranty, mattresses designed with a double-pocketed coil system, and an all-wool mattress. Its collections also boast all-natural materials. VI-Spring sells in the UK, through upscale retailers Harrods and Selfridges and in specialty bedding stores, as well as fine furniture outlets in Europe, Asia, and North America. Its beds, found in many luxury hotels, have also furnished ocean liners the Titanic and Queen Mary. Founded in 1901, the company is part of Spanish mattress company Flex Equipos de Descanso.
