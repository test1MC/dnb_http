Haskel International certainly feels the heat when maintaining its reputation as a leader in high-pressure technology. Haskel manufactures equipment to generate, store, and control high pressure gases and liquids. Its products include hydraulic and pneumatic high-pressure pumps, gas boosters, seal kits, and air-pressure amplifiers. Haskel also provides customer service for its products. A wide range of industries use the company's products, including automotive, defense, aerospace and aviation, and mining, among others. Founded in 1946, Haskel International is a subsidiary of Accudyne Industries.
