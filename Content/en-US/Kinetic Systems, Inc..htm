No vibrations are good vibrations for Kinetic Systems, Inc. (KSI), which makes vibration isolation products, such as tabletops, platforms, workstations, mounts, and mounting plates. KSI's products are used in academic, industrial, and government research; among the markets it serves are aerospace, biosciences, lasers and optics, and semiconductors. In addition to its vibration isolation and damping products the company also makes custom products and offers vibration surveys: often necessary to ensure precision equipment operates correctly. The company was founded in 1968.
