Intercap eCommerce wants to provide a window (and door) to the world. Though it is headquartered in Alberta, Canada, Intercap eCommerce operates mainly through an 83%-ownership in China's Shanghai AL Door & Window Manufacture Co., which designs, makes, and installs aluminum alloy and PVC windows and doors. The manufacturer makes its products in a Shanghai factory and sells to the residential and commercial construction industries worldwide. At one point in the recent past, Intercap eCommerce lived up to its name -- it dabbled in eCommerce and operated a Web site designed to facilitate business ties between China and Canada.
