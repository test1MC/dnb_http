It's a small world after all, but InterMetro Industries means to make the most efficient use of it. The company, part of Emerson Electric 's tools and storage business, makes freestanding, fixed, and mobile storage products, from plastic shelving to medical carts, and workstations to heated kitchen cabinets, used in residential, commercial, foodservice, and healthcare markets. Its Flo Healthcare, Lionville, and MedDispense brands offer storage and medication management equipment. InterMetro also provides services, such as space audits and custom product design.

Company manufacturing and distribution facilities dot the US, with sales mainly in the US and, less so, Europe, Asia, the Middle East, and Latin America.

The competitive global marketplace of recent years forced the company to move some of its manufacturing operations in Pennsylvania and Ohio to a new facility in Mexico.

Emerson Electric purchased InterMetro in 1991. Known as Metro, the company was founded in 1929 as Metropolitan Wire Goods.
