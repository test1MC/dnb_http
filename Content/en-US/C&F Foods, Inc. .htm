C&F Foods serves up fodder for side dishes and snacks. Founded in 1975, the family-owned and -operated company grows, packs, and distributes dried beans (including pinto, kidney, and lima), peas, rice, and popcorn to grocers, foodservice providers, and food manufacturers in the US. It also has international export operations. The company's brands include C&F, C&F Organic, El Orgullo de mi Tierra, Kanga, Premier Fields, and Royal Peacock, among others. C&F Foods ships more than 350 million pounds of agricultural products per year from its plants in California, Idaho, Minnesota, North Carolina, North Dakota, Texas, and Wisconsin.
