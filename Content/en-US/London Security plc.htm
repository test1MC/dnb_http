London Security, through its subsidiaries, provides fire security products and services to more than 160,000 customers in Austria, Belgium, Holland, and the UK. The company's products, which include fire extinguishers, smoke detectors, sprinkler systems, and fire and security alarms, are marketed under brand names Ansul, Master, Nu-Swift, Premier, and Total, among others. Additional offerings include monitoring and servicing fire extinguishers and installing and modifying alarm systems. Because the company has grown through acquisitions, its operations are highly decentralized. London Security was founded in 1897 as an engineering firm.
