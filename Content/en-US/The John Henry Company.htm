The John Henry of folklore may have been a steel-driving man, but The John Henry Company has a more delicate touch. It makes, prints, and sells specialty packaging to the floral industry (baskets, ceramic mugs, glassware, and wreaths), as well as personal note cards, envelopes, and plant tags. The company manufactures custom-designed blister packs, product labels and inserts, and cartons, coupled with a range of printing services, primarily for mid-size pharmaceutical and healthcare companies, and, to a lesser extent, food and beverage markets. The John Henry Company is a part of the Multi Packaging Solutions group of companies.

The company sells its products through wholesale florists in Canada, Latin America, and the US. It also sells through online catalogs.

The John Henry Company traces its historical roots back to 1912.
