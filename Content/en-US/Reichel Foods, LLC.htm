Reichel Foods has the right idea. The company produces value-added branded and private-label ready-to-eat convenience foods such as sliced apples and caramel dip all packaged together for customer convenience.  Just open and eat. Its flagship Dippin' Stix products come in single-serve portions. Several choices of "Stix" are offered, including apples, carrots, pickles, pita chips and come with either caramel, ranch-dressing, yogurt, peanut-butter, or hummus dips. The company also offers party-tray and bulk-pack variations of Dippin' Stix. All are available at food retailers throughout the US. Reichel Foods was founded by president, Craig Reichel, in 1997.
