Men don't mind hopping down the bunny trail paved by Playboy Entertainment Group. Accounting for the lion's share of revenue for Playboy Enterprises , the division oversees pay-television, filmed entertainment, and online content operations for the adult entertainment giant. Its businesses include satellite and cable channels Playboy TV and Spice Networks. Online, Playboy Entertainment offers adult content through its flagship destination Playboy.com , while Club Jenna offers branded content for fans of adult film star Jenna Jameson.
