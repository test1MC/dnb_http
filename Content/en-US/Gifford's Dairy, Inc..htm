Maine-based Gifford's Dairy manufactures Gifford's Famous Ice Cream, which is sold in supermarkets and ice cream shops in New England. The company also markets Gifford's Famous Lowfat Frozen Yogurt, Maine Landmark Ice Cream, and Denali Alaskan Classics products, as well as other labels. Gifford's unusual flavors, which include Cake Batter, Sticky Buns, and Maine Wildberry, are fashioned to bring ice cream stand-style flavors to the retail grocery market. The company also operates five ice cream shops in central Maine. The founding Gifford family owns and manages the company, which was founded in 1974.
