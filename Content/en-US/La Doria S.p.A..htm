You say "tuh- may -toe," and I say "tuh- mah -toe," but La Doria says "cha- ching ." The company is an Italian processor of tomato-based products -- its red line offers canned tomatoes and sauces. (In 2008 the company processed some 5 million tonnes of tomatoes.) La Doria's fruit lines -- juice and canned -- are also important to La Doria's income; its other lines include canned beans, vegetables, and pasta products. Most of La Doria's food is sold under the private labels of Europe's top food retailers, including Tesco , J Sainsbury and Wal-Mart ; but it also offers La Doria and other brands. In 2008 some 90% of its sales were generated in Europe; Australia, New Zealand, and Asia accounted for the rest.
