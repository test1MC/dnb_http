Mondi wraps it up and prints it out. A global paper and packaging company, Mondi grows the wood and manufactures (or recycles) the pulp and paper used in its products. Mondi's offerings include packaging paper and a slew of converted packaging products (containerboard and corrugated boxes, as well as kraft paper for industrial and consumer bags, and extrusion coating products for consumer packaging), uncoated fine paper (office and printing paper), and newsprint. Mondi owns about 80 operating sites in some 30 countries. Its strategic interests dot Europe, Russia, and South Africa, where favorable conditions have led to the company's improved performance.
