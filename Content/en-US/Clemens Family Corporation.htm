The Clemens family helps its customers bring home the bacon -- and other products, too. The Clemens Family Corporation (CFC) operates primarily through the Clemens Food Group (CFG). CFG includes pork product producers Hatfield Quality Meats (bacon, scrapple, deli ham, pork tenderloin) and Nick's Sausage Company (breakfast and specialty sausage, sausage crumbles), as well as logistics and transportation firms (PV Transport and CFC Logistics) and a hog procurement and production company (Country View Family Farms). Its products are sold via grocers in the northeastern and mid-Atlantic regions of the US. Additionally, family-owned CFC operates Clemens Development, which develops real estate owned by the company.

Pennsylvania-based Clemens sells pork products to food service operators and retailers through stores in New England, New York, Pennsylvania, New Jersey, Delaware, Virginia, and Maryland. The company has operations in Lancaster and Quakertown, Pennsylvania, and a pork sausage manufacturing plant in Maryland.

The company's Country View Family Farms business in Lancaster, Pennsylvania is a hog procurement and production company that manages more than 100 family farms that raise hogs under contract. Clemens Development in Hatfield, Pennsylvania manages properties and real estate owned by the Clemens Family Corp.
