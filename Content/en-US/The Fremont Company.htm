Studies concluding that sauerkraut has healing properties are great news for the folks who tot up the sales at The Fremont Company. Fremont makes canned, jarred, and bagged sauerkraut, as well as sauerkraut juice and various glazes, sauces, and vinaigrettes for retail and foodservice and meat-packing customers. The company distributes its sauerkraut under the Frank's Quality Kraut and Snowfloss brands, as well as tomato ketchup and sauces under the Mississippi BBQ and Frank's brands throughout the US. In addition, Fremont provides private-label packaging services for tomato-based products. Fremont is a fourth-generation, family-owned company founded in 1905 by Allen Slessman.
