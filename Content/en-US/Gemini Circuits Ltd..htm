This Gemini has a mission to provide complete contract manufacturing services for electronics companies. Gemini Tec (G-Tec) is a printed circuit board (PCB) manufacturer specializing in low- to medium-volume, rapid turnaround batches. Vendors of electronic goods turn to UK-based G-Tec to take on manufacturing chores more cheaply than they could do themselves. The company also offers services such as product assembly and test, prototyping, artwork scanning, and design layout. Owner and managing director David Harsant founded Gemini Circuits in 1974.

Gemini Tec (G-Tec) deploys processes that makes it no twin of other contract manufacturers in the UK. The company offers solder jet printing across the surface mount technology. The company is privately owned and family operated.

G-Tec is based in Aldershot, Hampshire in the UK and that's where its assembly operations are conducted.

G-Tec customers are in businesses involving transportation, embedded computing and the internet of things, instrumentation, communications, medical, and oil and gas. Among its customers are Hitex Development Ltd., CCWW Ltd., Sangoma Inc., Curtiss-Wright Inc. , and NTronix Ltd.

G-Tec competes in the UK with smaller contract manufacturing houses like itself such as Anglia Circuits as well as the big players that include Flextronics . G-Tec tries to stay ahead of the competition by investing in advanced manufacturing equipment and promising quick turnaround on projects.
