NGK Spark Plugs (U.S.A.) may have a hand in getting your car started. The company is the American manufacturing and distribution arm of Japan-based spark plug manufacturer NGK SPARK PLUG . It has manufacturing and distribution facilities in California, Illinois, and West Virgina. NGK Spark Plugs (U.S.A.) makes not only its namesake product, but also oxygen sensors that control an automobile's fuel and ignition systems for lower emissions and better fuel economy, as well as spark plug wire sets. The subsidiary traces its historical roots back to 1966.

The company operates manufacturing and distribution facilities in Irvine, California; Sissonville, West Virginia; and Chicago.

NGK Spark Plugs (U.S.A.) products are used in cars, trucks, SUVs, ATVs, watercrafts, snowmobiles, lawnmowers, chainsaws, generators, power equipment, outdoor power equipment, and resistors covers.

Like most companies in the manufacturing sector, NGK Spark Plugs (U.S.A.) grows its business by adding additional manufacturing capacity to its operations. In 2012 it announced a 8,000 sq. ft. expansion of its Sissonville, West Virginia facility dedicated to making its line of more technologically advanced oxygen sensors.
