Lexmark International's UK unit helps the company live up to its name. The regional subsidiary provides printing and imaging equipment to corporate, government, and consumer customers. Its products include laser and inkjet printers, multi-function devices, ink cartridges, and other supplies The company also offers services such as consulting, managed printing, paper-to-digital migration, installation, and technical support. Lexmark International markets its products though distributors and resellers, as well as retail outlets such as DSG International 's Dixons consumer electronics chain.
