Shine on, you crazy provider of benefit plan data. Judy Diamond Associates publishes compensation, benefits, insurance, and pension and retirement plan data for the financial services and corporate markets. The company publishes data in book, CD-ROM, and online formats (such as its FreeERISA.com site). Its source material includes form 5500 filings from the US Department of Labor 's Employee Retirement Income Security Act.  The company additionally provides research services, and its data is customizable. Judy Diamond Associates is owned by business information provider Summit Business Media; it operates as part of its parent company's Data Division.
