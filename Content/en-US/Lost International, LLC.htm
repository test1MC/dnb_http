Lost International manufactures colorful surfboards (featuring images such as flaming skulls and voluptuous ladies) as well as surf apparel (T-shirts, board shorts) for men and women. Clothing offerings from the company, which does business as Lost Enterprises, are sold through its Web site as well as at specialty and major department stores ( Macy's , Pacific Sunwear ). Its boards are sold at surf shops nationwide, including Dive N' Surf , Ron Jon Surf Shop, and Becker Surfboards. Lost International also has licensees and distributors in several countries, including Brazil, Canada, South Africa, and Japan. The company, whose roots date back to the '90s, is led by founding partners Matt Biolos and Mike Reola.
