International Game Technology, Inc., is the US subsidiary of London-based gaming behemoth International Game Technology PLC . The parent company is the world's largest end-to-end gaming company, offering lotteries and gaming machines, as well as interactive and social gaming. The parent was created in mid-2015 with the merger of the former International Game Technology, the top maker of gaming machines, and global lottery operator GTECH.
