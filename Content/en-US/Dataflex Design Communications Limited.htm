Dataflex offers an all-access pass to multiple communications protocols. It designs and distributes equipment used by communications service providers worldwide to enable VoIP computer telephony and other networking functions. Its line of ViNE-branded products combine DSL modem, VoIP gateway, router, firewall, ethernet, and other functions into a single device to enable the transmission of voice, data, and video over the Internet, computer networks, and telecom networks. Dataflex also makes the Hera line of machine-to-machine (M2M) products that enables wireless connectivity between and remote control of facilities monitoring systems, vending machines, CCTV networks, and other systems.

The company is a subsidiary of Eyese, which provides connections via cellular networks to the Internet of Things.

Based in the UK, the company operates an engineering facility in India.
