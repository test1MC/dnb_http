Allen Gearing Solutions has a need for speed. More commonly known as Allen Gears, the company makes gearboxes for industrial and marine high-speed rotating equipment. Its short list of products includes building epicyclic gearboxes (used in gas turbine generator drives) and parallel shaft gearboxes (found in high-power, low-ratio equipment). It also provides an array of aftermarket services, including 24-hour access to engineering advice, repair, spare parts, and scheduled inspections. The company's clients hail from the power generation, hydro power, oil and gas, and marine propulsion markets. Allen Gears is owned by GE Oil & Gas .

Headquartered in Worcestershire, UK, the company has regional offices in Europe (Croatia, Slovenia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Czech and Slovak Republics, and Hungary), Asia (India, Japan, Korea, and Malaysia), and Africa (South Africa, Namibia, Swaziland, Lesotho, Mozambique Zimbabwe, Zambia, Malawi, Tanzania, Kenya, and Uganda).

The company targets the key markets of power (main grid and site-power), oil and gas, and marine. Allen Gears has worked with such noteworthy clients as Alstom Hydro, Alstom Power, BP, Centrax, Clyde Pumps, Doosan, Dresser-Rand, and GE.
