Quilmes Industrial (or Quinsa) quenches thirsts across the southern half of South America with its beers, sodas, and water. The brewer dominates the beer market in Argentina, Bolivia, Paraguay, and Uruguay. Although not the dominant brand there, the company also sells its beers in Chile. It is also the exclusive bottler and distributor of PepsiCo products in Argentina and Uruguay. Through its joint venture with Nestlé Waters , Quinsa sells water in Argentina. Brazilian brewer Companhia de Bebidas das Américas (or AmBev) owns 99% of the company.
