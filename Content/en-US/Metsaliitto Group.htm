Metsäliitto Group supplies and produces an array of wood and wood-based products from Nordic wood. The company operates in five segments: tissue and cooking paper, paperboard, pulp, wood products, and wood supply and forest services. An affiliate, Vapo also supplies bio fuels. The company does business in some 30 countries; its sales force serves industrial construction, fine paper and box board manufacturing, a range of consumer packaging, as well as household to institutional end uses. The group is held by Metsäliitto Cooperative, a union of 120,000 members who own about half of the private forests in Finland.

Metsäliitto Group does business in some 30 countries and has production locations in eight. The company's main market is Europe, but the company is also seeking growth in North America and Asia. Finland accounted for 19% of its net sales in 2014. Other major markets include Germany (13%) and the UK (10%).

The company operates through five primary segments: Wood Supply and Forest Services (23% of net revenue), Pulp Industry (19%), Tissue and Cooking Papers (15%), Wood Products Industry (13%), and Paperboard and Paper Industry (10%).

Like most companies within the manufacturing sector, Metsäliitto Group grows by augmenting its manufacturing capacity. In 2015 Metsä Fibre, part of Metsä Group, started construction of a bioproduct mill in Äänekoski, Finland, in the area of an existing pulp mill. The mill is scheduled to be completed during the third quarter of 2017.
