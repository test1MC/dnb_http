In a warehouse, Dematic makes the machinery products that will ensure you handle with care . The company designs and manufactures an array of logistics and automated material handling equipment, including sorting and conveyor systems, warehouse control software, and direct order fulfillment and picking systems. Dematic caters to a host of industries, from beverages (Suncoast Beverage, an Anheuser-Busch distributor) to apparel ( Pacific Sunwear ), retailers ( JCPenney and The Children's Place ), and wholesalers ( JTM ). The company has installed more than 4,000 material handling systems around the world.

The company has a North American presence through 10 regional offices located across the US. It also operates an office in Canada, three manufacturing facilities (in Michigan, Utah, and North Carolina), and nearly 15 field offices, all of which are based in major cities. Its newest engineering offices reside in Detroit, Los Angeles, and Seattle.

The company serves a range of markets including pharmaceutical, food and beverage, retail, electronics, third-party logistics, apparel, manufacturing, and distribution.

The company's growth plan involves increasing its market share in North America. In 2015 the company indirectly acquired a majority stake in SDI Group Limited, a UK-based supplier of garment on hanger and flat sorter technology in Europe. The purchase will expand Dematic's global order fulfillment offerings in the apparel, retail, and e-commerce markets.

In the fall of 2013, the company obtained Montana-based Upturn Solutions, a provider of computerized maintenance management systems (CMMS) designed for distribution, manufacturing, and warehouse environments. Upturn enhanced Dematic's capability into distribution center workflow, asset and inventory management, and preventative maintenance. The deal also enhanced its ability to deliver robust order fulfillment products for diversified markets such as e-commerce, retail, apparel, health care, 3PL, food, beverage, parcel, and manufacturing.
