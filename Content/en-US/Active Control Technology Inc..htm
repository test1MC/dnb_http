Active Control Technology makes wireless radio frequency (RF) devices which are used for access control, safety, and security applications. Its products, which are marketed to customers in North America, include antennas, wireless transmitters, and wireless receivers used to control alarm systems, personal safety alarms, and parking gates. Its ActiveMine is a wireless communications and locating system for mines designed to track miners underground in the event of a collapse. Other brands include ActiveSecure (extends networks for building security, parking management, and gated communities) and ActiveMesh (wireless network control technology for offices, warehouses, campuses, and high security complexes).
