Got milk? And sodas, too, thanks to Royal Vendors, which designs and manufactures cold-drink automatic vending machines. Its soft drink and soda dispenser brands include EZ Vender, Merlin IV , GIII, Royal Vision Vender, 20-Plus Triple-Depth Venders, and the Econo-cool energy efficient vender. Its Milk Merchandiser unit makes a vending machine with a capacity of over 300 one-pint milk cartons. The company's vending machines are sturdy and vandal-resistant, and designed for use in indoor and outdoor locations. Royal Vendors operates a manufacturing plant in Kearneysville, West Virginia. Its products are distributed globally.
