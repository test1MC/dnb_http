What is plastic, encoded, and printed all over? Answer: Card Personalization Solutions Co. (CPS) finished products. CPS offers plastic card and key tag printing services for membership organizations and companies in the gift card, advertising, and healthcare industries. CPS printing services include embossing, magnetic stripe encoding, and thermal personalization (used for adding images, barcodes, and text); the company also offers custom card packaging and mailing services on behalf of its customers. The company is based in Allentown, Pennsylvania but also operates a facility in Addison, Illinois. Founded in 1998, CPS is headed by president Tom Knaus.
