Jindal Stainless is the flagship company of the Jindal Organization, which is among India's largest conglomerates. Founded in 1970, the company produces more than 6 million tons per year, including products such as stainless steel slabs, coin blanks, precision strips, blade steel for razor and surgical blades, and hot rolled and cold rolled coils. Jindal Stainless maintains manufacturing plants in India -- in the states of Andhra Pradesh and Haryana -- as well as in Indonesia. It's also constructing another facility in the Indian state of Orissa.
