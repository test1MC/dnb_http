RTC Industries manufactures and maintains point-of-purchase product displays (such as floor stands and wall systems), shelving, and other store fixtures for major retail chains and consumer products manufacturers worldwide. Leveraging its expertise in retail concepts, RTC also custom-designs interactive kiosks and product displays that integrate touch-screen technology. Its customers have included Coca Cola , Columbia Sportswear , FedEx , Nike , Target , Walgreen , and Wal-Mart . RTC's operations are located in Brazil, China, Costa Rica, the Czech Republic, Hong Kong, Mexico, Peru, Russia, the UK, and the US.

In addition to manufacturing, RTC Industries is engaged in program management and global logistics operations.

RTC Industries is always on the lookout for new and innovative ideas. For example, it introduced a new type of restocking shelving display that allows store employees to load cases of cans, without having to replenish the shelves as customers grab items. The company has approximately 100 patents to its name.

The company often brings on temporary staff for several months a year to help handle labor-intensive projects. For example, RTC changes out the cosmetic display fixtures at Walgreen stores twice each year, and depending on the demands of the project, RTC will hire up to 300 temporary workers.
