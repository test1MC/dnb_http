No matter how hot the weather gets in Thailand, Carrier ensures the indoors stay cool and comfortable. The company, a subsidiary of UTC Climate Controls & Security , manufactures commercial and residential air-conditioning systems and air purifiers. Carrier products are found in homes, offices, hotels, retail stores, hospitals, and factories throughout the country. Carrier mainly trades in air and water cooling systems ranging in size from one to 80 tons. The company traces its Thai roots to 1930 and operates some 10 domestic branches in addition to its Bangkok headquarters.
