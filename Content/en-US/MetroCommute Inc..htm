MetroCommute distributes traffic information directly to consumers, media outlets, and corporate clients through its Web site, and via e-mail and text alerts to subscribers' desktops, pagers, and mobile phones. Users can view traffic reports by roads and regions and can customize their reports. MetroCommute was founded in 1994 by company executives Daniel Broe and Evan Lemonides.
