Although it doesn't serve the elfen world, ELF Corporation caters to those who serve the world of little creatures. The company is a wholesale distributor of pet food and supplies to independent pet retailers. Its brands include KAYTEE bird food, Durvet flea and tick shampoo, and other items. Focusing on the Southeastern US, ELF was founded in 1990 by company president Ernesto Alvarez. Through its trio of warehouses in Florida, ELF Corporation supplies retailers in Florida and Georgia and exporters to the Caribbean with its pet products.
