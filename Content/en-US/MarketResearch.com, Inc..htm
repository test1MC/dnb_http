MarketResearch.com distributes business information. The company operates through an online database of research from more than 700 global publishers (including consulting and advisory firms) such as Datamonitor , Frost & Sullivan , and Plunkett Research . With more than 300,000 market research reports, the company offers content covering global industries, companies, products, and trends. Clients use MarketResearch.com for implementing business plans, marketing campaigns, sales presentations, product development strategies, and annual budgets.
