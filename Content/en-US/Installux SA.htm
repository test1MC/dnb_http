Installux provides the framework that allows it to be the window to your world. The French manufacturer operates several divisions (Installux Aluminum, Stores Roche, Tiaso, and Sofadi), each of which offers different aluminum frame and fitting products used in residential and commercial construction. Its products are used in door, window, awning, and wall partition installation. Installux has operations in Europe and Asia, as well as in the Middle East, where it is actively expanding. It has supplied products to such projects as the Gajria building in Bahrain and the Beverly Hills tower in Qatar.
