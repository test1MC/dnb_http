Putzmeister isn't putzing around; it aims to reach the tops of tall buildings in a single...pump. The world's largest producer of concrete pumps, the company also makes pipe-conveying and concrete placing machines as well as high-pressure washing systems. Products generally shoot concrete over long distances from mixers to the tops of large structures. Putzmeister has operations across Europe, Asia, the Middle East, and the Americas. In the US, the company's business is handled by Putzmeister America . Products are made in varying locations in order to be able to suit local demand. Putzmeister was founded in 1958 by Karl Schlecht, whose family foundation holds 90% voting control, but owns just 1% of shares.
