Metal-finishing company Lorin Industries anodizes aluminum coils and sheets and provides value-added services such as slitting and shearing. (Anodizing provides aluminum with a protective coating that controls oxidation.) In addition, it offers lighting sheet products and stainless aluminum products used for applications in appliance panels and trims, back splashes, ceiling panels, column covers, decorative trims and edges, exterior wall panels, foodservice equipment, interior wall panels, and signage products. Founded in 1943 by Herb Kersman, Lorin Industries is owned by the Kersman family.
