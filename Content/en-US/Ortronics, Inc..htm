Ortronics wants to be your outlet for connectivity. The company, a US-based subsidiary of French electrical fittings maker Legrand Holding , provides cabling systems and other networking equipment used by telecommunication service providers. Key products and brands include the company's line of Clarity cross-connect systems, Mighty Mo cable management systems (named for company founder Maurice "Mo" Orlando), and other patch panels and cords. The company also designs and sells its LabelMo software used to label patch panels and workstation outlets. Founded in 1966, Ortronics has distributors in the Asia/Pacific region, Europe, Latin America, North America, and the Middle East.
