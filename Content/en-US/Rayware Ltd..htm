Rayware wants its customers to feel more at home in the kitchen. Rayware Ltd. is an international supplier of traditionally-styled and contemporary housewares. The company's own-brand product line includes cookware, cutlery, giftware, glassware, kitchen tools and textiles, and ovenware. Its Ravenhead brand was founded in 1892 and has a reputation for durable glassware. Its Dema Designs reflects the more trendy aspects of homeware design, while 250-year-old Arthur Wood maintains classic design in its dishes. The Price & Kensington brand is known for its traditional Stoke teapots and accessories. Other brands include Kilner jars and Mason Cash cookware.

Rayware is headquartered in the UK and has warehouses in the US and in Europe and Asia.
