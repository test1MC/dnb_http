Nexgen Pharma takes drug and supplement production to the next level. The company is a contract and private label manufacturer of nutritional supplements and prescription and over-the-counter pharmaceuticals. Its dietary supplements line includes vitamins, minerals, herbs, and other nutraceuticals, while its drugs come in a variety of forms, including immediate-release and controlled-release tablets, chewable tablets, effervescent tablets, liquids, and powder-filled capsules. Nexgen Pharma helps clients with their drug development efforts through service offerings including formulation development, validation analysis, product testing, and regulatory filing assistance. It also supplies pet health products.

The company specializes in the development and manufacture of solid dose, powder, and liquid Rx drugs, Medical Foods, OTC drugs, and dietary supplements. The start-to-finish manufacturing process includes raw materials sourcing, quality testing, blending, drying, encapsulating, and pill coating and finishing. In addition to developing and manufacturing medicines for its clients, Nexgen Pharma provides warehousing and packaging services as well as FDA submission services.

In addition to contract manufacturing, the company makes private label nutritional supplement lines through its Vitamer Laboratories and VitaCeutical Laboratories division. The subsidiaries' offerings are sold through health food stores and grocery stores under the retailer's label of choice. Products include vitamins, amino acids, enzymes, antioxidants, and other health supplements in a variety of formulations.

Nexgen Pharma has a five facilities in California and Colorado, including 325,000 sq. ft. of manufacturing, laboratory and warehouse/distribution space.

Expanding its distribution network and entering the animal health business, in 2013 Nexgen Pharma acquired Nebraska-based KV Vet Supply and renamed it KV Supply.

The family owned company was founded by two brothers in 1924 as Anabolic Food Products, an early nutritional supplement maker. The Vitamer Laboratories and VitaCeutical Laboratories divisions were formed in 1975 and 1996, respectively.
