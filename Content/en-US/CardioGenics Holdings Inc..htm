CardioGenics is a Canadian biochemical firm in the business of developing products for the in vitro diagnostic testing market. CardioGenics is working to develop and commercialize its QL Care Analyzer, a point of care immuno-analyzer designed to run a variety of diagnostic tests to identify cardiovascular problems. It is also working to gain approval for a series of immunoassay tests to identify cardiac markers at the time of heart attack, as well as a line of magnetic beads used in analyzers. CardioGenics targets physicians' offices, hospital emergency rooms, and other locations looking to perform these types of tests onsite. The company is led by CEO Yahia Gawad, who has about a 35% share in CardioGenics.
