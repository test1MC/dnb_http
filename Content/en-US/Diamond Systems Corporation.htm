Diamond Systems finds opportunity embedded everywhere in the electronics markets. The company makes embedded computer systems and accessories, including CPUs, analog and digital input/output boards, serial ports, vehicle power supplies, and enclosures. Its clients come from industries such as aerospace, transportation, industrial automation, and medical instrumentation, and include such agencies and firms as NASA , Lockheed Martin , Northrop Grumman , and Boeing .
