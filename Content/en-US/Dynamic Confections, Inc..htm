Dynamic Confections is a downright delicious dynamo. The candy company specializes in European molded chocolates and top quality Belgian chocolates. It operates through four business units -- Dynamic Chocolates, Bogdon's, Maxfield's, and Kencraft -- and sells premium chocolate under such names as Botticelli and Dolce D'or. Dynamic Confections also is a top seller of the Hedgehog chocolate bon-bon. Besides chocolates, the company includes a variety of Kencraft-brand non-chocolate products in its pantry, including confections such as lollipops, bubble gum, candy canes, and panoramic sugar eggs. Located in Salt Lake City, Utah, Dynamic's goodies are sold primarily in the US and Canada.
