Provimi North America keeps farm animals well fed. The company provides blending, manufacturing, distribution, and nutritional services to the feed, dairy, meat and egg industries throughout North America. The company provides diet formulation, feeding-program recommendation, and product-development services. It also makes feed-grade drugs, vitamin and mineral products, and specialty feed mixes. In addition to dairy and beef cattle, calves, swine, and poultry, its Akey unit makes products for sheep, goats, lamb, horses, and camelids (camels, llamas, alpacas). The company is owned by US-agribusiness-giant Cargill .

From is headquarters in Brookville, Ohio Provimi North America supplies animal feeds in the US, Mexico, and parts of Canada.

Provimi North America operates four business units: Akey; Vigortone; Sunglo Feeds; and Progressive Nutrition.
