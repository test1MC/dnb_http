Coloma Frozen Foods is all juiced up. The company supplies fresh and frozen fruits and vegetables, along with fruit juice concentrates under the Nature Blessed label. It specializes in cherry juice concentrate, which it packages in quart, half-gallon, and gallon bottles. Coloma also packages an assortment of fresh vegetables, like asparagus and celery, and fresh fruit, such as cherries, blueberries, rhubarb, and apples. Its customers include US foodservice operators and food manufacturers and wholesalers. In addition, Coloma offers custom repacking services. Headquartered in Coloma, Michigan, the company operates a distribution facility and a warehouse in Benton Harbor, Michigan.
