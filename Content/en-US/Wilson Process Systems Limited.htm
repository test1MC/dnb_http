Wilson Process Systems (WPS) is an contract electronics manufacturer operating in the UK. The company makes printed circuit boards (PCBs) and electromechanical assemblies products. Its hand assembly, surface mount, and automated through-hole (including LED assembly) methods allow it to handle anything from one-offs to high volume batches. WPS also makes rack wiring and other products to Ministry of Defence standards. It offers project support services including prototyping, parts procurement, complete system (box build) assembly, testing, and packaging.

WPS has made several investments to increase its manufacturing capacity in the last few years. The company started with moving its manufacturing operations in the UK. In 2014 it added a second Universal Instruments Fuzion line. The XC2-60 can make 65,000 placements in an hour, boosting overall capability to 200,000 placements an hour.

Companies from industries such as automotive, industrial, medical, mobile, and LED lighting contract with WPS. Customers have included Microtronix, Burton Industries, National Instruments , and Beghelli , Omron , Rockwell Automation , and Harman Music Group .
