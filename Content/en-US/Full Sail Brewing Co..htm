Beer is in session at Full Sail Brewing Co. Maker of Session brand premium lagers, which account for about one-third of the brewing company's production, Full Sail also produces a core line of amber, IPA, and pale ales under the Full Sail name, as well as the Wassail winter ale during the holiday season and a rotating selection of Brewmaster Reserve specialty beers. The brewery produces about 2.8 million gallons of beer per year and is the third largest in Oregon after Widmer Brothers and Deschutes . Its online store sells branded t-shirts, hats, pint glasses, and other accessories. Established in 1987, Full Sail Brewing has been an employee-owned company for more than a decade.
