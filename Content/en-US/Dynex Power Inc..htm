Dynex Power seeks to play a dynamic role in power semiconductors. Through operating subsidiary Dynex Semiconductor, the company manufactures a variety of discrete and integrated power semiconductor devices, including diodes, thyristors, transistors, power assemblies, and related modules. The company specializes in high-power bipolar components; manufacturers use Dynex's products in a variety of industrial applications including automation and control equipment, aircraft subsystems, satellites, renewable and distributed power, and electric transmission and distribution. Customers outside the UK account for more than three-quarters of sales. China-based CSR Times Electric holds a 75% stake in Dynex Power.
