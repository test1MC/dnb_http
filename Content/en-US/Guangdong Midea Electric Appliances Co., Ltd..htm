Guangdong Midea Electric Appliances, known as Midea, makes dishwashers, fans, microwaves, refrigerators, residential and commercial air conditioners, washing machines, water heaters, and a long list of other household appliances. Midea also manufactures and sells compressors, motors, and transformers needed to make and maintain those appliances. In addition to more than a dozen production plants in China, the company has built joint-venture production plants in Argentina, Belarus, Brazil, Egypt, India, and Vietnam. Guangdong Midea is part of China's Midea Group.
