Heidelberg Graphic Equipment contributes to the world's overall betterment by helping to provide the tools for reading -- well, at least the machines that make the tools for reading. The company, operating under the name Heidelberg UK, provides printing and publishing companies with sheetfed and web-based printing presses, as well as related components and software. Heidelberg also has a public consumables division, which offers wholesale plates, inks, proofing materials, and plates to printing companies. The company has been operating in and addressing the UK for more than 150 years and is the UK division of Heidelberger Druckmaschinen , a global printing machinery firm based in Germany.
