Nutreco feeds the animals that feed us. The company's Animal Nutrition business makes and sells premixes (under the Selko brand), farm minerals, concentrates, compound feed, young animal feeds, and animal health products and feed additives. Its Skretting fish feed business offers feed for some 60 species of farmed fish, including salmon. Nutreco Iberia makes products for pigs, poultry and ruminants in Spain and Portugal. Founded in 1994, the Netherlands-based company has grown on a steady diet of acquisitions in Europe and abroad. With operations in 35 countries and sales in more than 80 others, Norway is Nutreco's largest market, followed by Canada. Nutreco is owned by SHV Holdings.

The company operates three business segments: Animal Nutrition, Aquafeed and Nutreco Iberia.

The company has some 100 production plants in 35 countries, and sells in over 80. Nutreco operates on four continents.

Nutreco has strong regional brands in Spain, Portugal and Canada. It maintain significant animal nutrition and fish feed businesses in Brazil, Canada, China, Ecuador, Mexico and Vietnam.

In 2015, sales increased 8% on prior year to €5.6 billion amid an increase across all segments attributable to a favorable product mix for the Selko feed additives brand, and in Nanta and Sada. Acquisitions in Brazil also helped push revenue higher.

Nutreco is expanding its feed additive portfolios in various regions across the globe through acquisitions and investment. In 2016, the company acquired Indianapolis-based Micronutrients USA, a leading hydroxy trace minerals company. This acquisition expands the company's specific feed additive products portfolio as well as supports premix value proposition. And in 2015, Nutreco invested €15 million in an animal nutrition plant in East Java, Indonesia with investment targeting production capacity of premixes, farm minerals and young animal feed. Trouw Nutrition, the company's animal nutriotion business in the region, will be able to strengthen market share. In the same year, Nutreco opened a premix facility in Ho Chi Minh, Vietnam, which produces both premixes and farm minerals.

In 2014, Nutreco acquired two animal nutrition companies in Brazil, Fatec and BRNova. The two acquisitions expand the company's presence in Brazil, and enhances its portfolio with the addition of premixes, animal health products and feed specialties, including probiotics.

In mid-2010 the company changed its name from Nutreco Holding N.V. to Nutreco N.V. The company derives its name from the combination of Nutrition, Ecology, and Economy.
