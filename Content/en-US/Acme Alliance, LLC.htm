The epitome of international know-how and industry experience, Acme Alliance specializes in die casting parts (aluminum, magnesium, and zinc). The company touts its capabilities as a one-stop-shop for die casting, finishing, and mechanical assembly, as well as logistics and resource services. With facilities in Brazil, China, Portugal, and the US, Acme Alliance claims a competitive edge in streamlining all aspects of component development and production. The company was established by Lovejoy Industries in 1982 to provide raw basic industrial services to technology companies and power tool industries.
