Just say, "cheese" and you'll get any one of Tetra Pak Cheese and Powder Systems' (CPS) employees to smile. Formerly Carlisle Process Systems, the company manufactures equipment for liquid processing including evaporators and flash dryers for dairy industries, cheese and dairy automated systems, and parts for food and beverage processors. It works in more sectors than its name suggests, tapping into nutrition, pharmaceutical, and healthcare. Customers include Uniq , Reflex Nutrition, Duchy College, Laurens Patisseries, and Nestlé . A subsidiary of Carlisle Companies , Tetra Pak CPS was sold to food processing and packaging giant Tetra Pak in 2006.
