AMTROL works to keep its customers from sweating the small stuff. The manufacturer specializes in pre-pressurized diaphragm expansion tanks and water flow products used in HVAC and hydronic systems. The company's lineup includes well water accumulators, hot-water expansion controls, auto gas cylinders, and refrigerant and chemical cylinders. Its portfolio also boasts shock and surge suppressors and indirect-fired water heaters, as well as fire-control sprinkler systems for residential, commercial, and industrial applications. AMTROL markets its products under such brands as EXTROL (launched in 1954), Diatrol, Therm-X-Trol, Water Worker, and Well-X-Trol.

The company operates manufacturing facilities in Rhode Island, Maryland, Kentucky, and internationally in Portugal and Poland. Its sales and distribution centers are located in Canada, Europe, and Latin America.

Since 2005 AMTROL has sold more than 1 million composite metal LP gas tanks worldwide.

AMTROL's Portugal-based subsidiary, Amtrol Alfa, manufactures refrigerant and heating gas cylinders. Its Poland subsidiary, Amtrol Poland, specializes in refurbishing LPG (liquefied petroleum gas) cylinders. AMTROL's international footprint capitalizes on local market demand.

The company keeps its competitive edge by investing in research and development to improve the technology and design of its products. Notable advances include its products' ergonomic design, stack and storage ability, and impact and corrosion resistance. AMTROL's expansion tanks are designed to reduce water volume and pressure within a water heater due to thermal expansion.

AMTROL sells its products via sales office locations and through sales representatives and distributors the likes of Roth & Company, Coleman-Russell & Associates, Canadian Aqualine, Agence Jacques Desjardins, and Delta-Q. WaterHeaterStore is one of AMTROL's largest customers for expansion tanks.

In addition to its distribution and sales centers, the company's products reach customers through plumbing, heating, and pump specialty wholesalers.
