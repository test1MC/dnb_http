Den-Mat wants to make your pearly whites truly luminous. The company manufactures and markets cosmetic dentistry products to dentists around the globe. Its premier product line, LUMINEERS, is a series of thin porcelain veneers that are placed non-invasively over existing teeth to hide stained, chipped, discolored, and misaligned teeth. The LUMINEERS can be applied without tooth removal or restructuring and can last up to 20 years. Den-Mat also manufactures lights, pastes, cements, and other tools and accessories to help dentists and hygienists place veneers, bleach teeth, and cure and bond restorations. Den-Mat was acquired by investment firms Centre Partners and Mill Street Partners in 2011.
