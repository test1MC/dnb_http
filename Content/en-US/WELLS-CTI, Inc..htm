The products of WELLS-CTI (formerly known as Antares Advanced Test Technologies) are put to the test every day -- except they are on the testing end of the equation. Specializing in semiconductor test products, WELLS-CTI designs and manufactures sockets and connectors serving semiconductor manufacturers: products include burn-in sockets, high-performance test sockets, thermal products, high-performance boards, and docking systems. The company's test products help semiconductor manufacturers test the quality of their finished microchips focusing on the high-sensitivity areas of yield and integration of test disciplines.
