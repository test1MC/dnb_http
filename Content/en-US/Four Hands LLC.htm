Imports by Four Hands had the foresight that American consumers would be really keen to buy antique and handmade household items from India, the Pacific Rim, and Asia. The company wholesales its one-of-a-kind pieces through various high-end retailers such as Crate & Barrel , Z Gallerie , Sundance Catalogue, and Gumps By Mail. Imports by Four Hands has showrooms in Austin, Texas; Atlanta; Dallas; Las Vegas; and High Point, North Carolina. The company also boasts an office in Brussels to promote its growth in Europe. Imports by Four Hands was founded in 1996 by its chairman Brett Hatton.
