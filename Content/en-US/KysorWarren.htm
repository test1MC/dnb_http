Single men and those who dine on Weight Watchers take note: Kysor//Warren puts the chill in frozen entrées. The company builds refrigeration equipment and display merchandisers that supermarkets use to keep meat, dairy, and other perishables an ideal temperature. Its sales network helps retailers design floor plans to give refrigerated goods prominence. Gaining sustainability, product lines are revamping for enhanced energy and visual flexibility. The company has formed partnerships with GreenChill and Ineos Fluor , BASF , Emerson 's Climate Technologies businesses, and Copeland . Lennox acquired Kysor//Warren from Manitowoc in early 2011.
