Spectra doesn't want to put a stop to the transportation industry -- just allow the transportation industry to come to a stop. The company makes brake inspection systems for commercial vehicles in North America. Products include Brake Inspector (in-cab display for use with air brakes), Brake Safe (visual brake stroke indicator). Spectra has also has products that put the brakes on rust. Its Termin-8r lubricant provides a fluid, thin-film coating that removes and prevents rust on vehicles. Termin-8r may also be used on electrical connections and equipment, such as power sources and antenna bases, to prevent moisture contamination.
