The heat is on at BBT Thermotechnik. The company is a leading supplier of heating and water heating equipment, with a 21% market share in Europe. Heating products include floor-standing and wall-hung boilers, heating elements, heating control systems, solar components, heat dissipators, hot water storage tanks, and accessories. The company has 18 production facilities in nine countries, with more than 150 sales offices and subsidiaries worldwide. BBT Thermotechnik was formed in 2004 by the merger of heating technology specialists Bosch Thermotechnik and Buderus, a company founded in 1731. BBT Thermotechnik is a subsidiary of privately owned auto technology group Robert Bosch .
