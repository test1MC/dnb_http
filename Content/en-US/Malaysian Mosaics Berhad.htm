Malaysian Mosaics Berhad manufactures a wide range of premium ceramic and porcelain tile products for customers across the globe. Headquartered in Malaysia, the company's MML-branded tiling products are sold in more than 50 countries and can be found in such projects as the Sydney Olympics Stadium and the Wellington International Airport. Malaysian Mosaics Berhad is also expanding its product base, adding additional lines of decorative glazed porcelain and ceramic tiles to its customer offerings. The company owns three production facilities in the Johor state of Malaysia.
