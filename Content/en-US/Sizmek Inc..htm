Sizmek lets advertisers truly size up the impact of their messages. The company's multiscreen services offer agencies and advertisers a variety of solutions to navigate the complexities of cross-platform advertising. Sizmek provides advertisers with start-to-finish creative services, ad delivery, marketing campaign management, and accurate analytics across various channels such as mobile, rich media, video, social, and search. It works to optimize an ad in the first place, optimize the context in which an ad appears, and build out an effective creative message. The goal for all Sizmek ads is for the right audience to see the ad at exactly the right time and place. The company was acquired by affiliates of Vector Capital in late 2016.

Sizmek's customers include some 3,700 agencies and with representatives in more than 70 countries. The company has a presence throughout North America, EMEA (Europe, Middle East, and Africa), Latin America, and Asia. The US accounted for 55% of revenue in fiscal 2015. EMEA and Asia/Pacific accounted for 26% and 14%, respectively.

Sizmek sells its offerings primarily through a direct sales force or through third-party selling agents. The company's marketing efforts include public relations, industry events, road shows, conferences, advertising, social media, and its website, blog, and research publications.

The company has enjoyed strong year-over-year revenue growth during recent fiscal periods. Its revenue was $172.7 million in fiscal 2015, which was an increase of $1.9 million (or 1%) compared to the prior fiscal period.

Despite the increased revenue, the company suffered a net loss of $136.9 million in fiscal 2015. In addition, Sizmek's cash flow from operations decreased from $13.8 million in fiscal 2014 down to $2.9 by the end of fiscal 2015.

The company's key strategic initiatives include driving new mobile, video, programmatic, and data business with a particular emphasis on omni-channel delivery and measurement.

In 2015 Sizmek acquired PointRoll for $20 million. Point Roll provides rich media and dynamic creative ad serving technologies. Also that year Sizmek acquired privately held StrikeAd and its affiliates for $9.9 million. StrikeAd operates a mobile demand side platform based in the UK.
