From mouse pads to phone cards, Performance Companies can perform. The specialty printer provides point-of-purchase, promotional marketing materials, and custom data card manufacturing from the simple to the sublime. Performance Companies fills the need for such items as shelf danglers, counter mats, and pressure-sensitive decals, offering traditional and sophisticated printing services that enable printing on plastics. The firm's Optigraphics division provides lenticular printing (creating 3-D or animated images) for even more memorable collateral and it supplies custom data cards (gift cards, loyalty cards, and phone cards) through its Performance Card Solutions division.
