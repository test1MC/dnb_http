It may sound unfamiliar, but odds are Keeco is hiding away somewhere in your house. Keeco designs, distributes, and markets textile products for major retail clients (such as Bed Bath & Beyond , J. C. Penney , Sears , Target , and Wal-Mart ) in the US and Canada. The company's products, which are primarily manufactured in China, include bedding sets, comforters, decorative pillows, duvet covers, pillowcases, quilts, shams, sheets, table linens, and window treatments. Keeco operates three showrooms in New York, Dallas, and Bentonville, Arkansas, as well as a linen outlet in California. It was founded in 1979 by chairman Leonard Kee.
