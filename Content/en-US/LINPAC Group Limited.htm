Pack up your fresh fruit, kitty litter, and industrial parts: LINPAC has boxes and more, making thousands of different plastic packages and specialty items. Its primary LINPAC Packaging subsidiary supplies food containers for packaging meat, fruit and vegetables, to-go items, and baked goods. The company works with retailers, brand owners and packers to develop a range of packaging products and services used for protecting, preserving, and presenting food products in the dairy, meat, fish, poultry, bakery, fresh produce and foodservice sectors. LINPAC has a network of 16 plants and 26 sales offices spanning Europe.
