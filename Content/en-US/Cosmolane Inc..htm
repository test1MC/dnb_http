Cosmolane wants women to feel as youthful as it does. Cosmolane is a cosmetics and skin care wholesaler and retailer. Initially solely a distributor of Orlane Paris, the company later added Annayake and Rubis brands to those it distributes online and in Canadian retail shops. Means of distribution include specialty cosmetic stores, as well as such large department stores as Sears Canada and Hudson's Bay , drug stores such as Shoppers Drug Mart and Pharmaprix, and specialty spas. Cosmolane developed brand Signs & Nature for smaller beauty houses to enter the department-store niche. The company has been owned since its 1998 inception by president and CEO Maud Julier van Badenthal.
