This company is big into the oil business. Macro Enterprises, based in Canada, provides pipeline construction, facilities maintenance, custom fabrication of oil field equipment, and insulating services to the oil and gas industry. Customers include large oil and gas producers in Northeastern British Columbia and Northwestern Alberta. Macro Enterprises was created in 2006 to oversee its subsidiary companies including Macro Industries (its flagship company) which provides oil and gas pipeline construction, Omineca Oil Services (oil and gas installation maintenance), and Tracer Industries (oil and gas installation construction).
