The answer to a stale menu may lie with Chef Solutions' side dishes and salads. The company supplies an array of fresh-prepared foods to customers in the retail food and foodservice industries. Its primary operating subsidiary, Orval Kent, offers fresh, refrigerated foods, such as deli-style salads, salad kits, mashed potatoes, as well as fresh-cut fruit, dips, and dressings. Burned by rising commodity prices and a tough recession, Chef Solutions in fall 2011 filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy relief, which opened up an auction. Reser's Fine Foods and Mistral Equity Partners won a $69.2 million bid to buy nearly all of Chef Solution's assets. The company's stakeholders had included Mistral and Questor Management .
