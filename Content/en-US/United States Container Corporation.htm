United States Container  is a leading distributor of industrial containers in the western US. It offers bottles and jars, closures, drums, pails, tin cans, and other miscellaneous packaging ranging in size from a half ounce to 330 gallons; the containers are made from fiber, glass, plastic, steel, and tin. U.S. Container serves the paint, chemical, ink, janitorial, food, and other related industries in more than a half-dozen western states. It has four physical locations in Hayward, Rancho Cucamonga, and Vernon, California, as well as in Phoenix, Arizona. Family-run U.S. Container was founded by CEO Jeff Levine's father, Max, in 1956.
