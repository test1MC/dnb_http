Prima Limited has ground its way to the top. Beginning as a flour miller in Singapore in 1961, the company has grown into a diversified group of companies with worldwide operations. While flour is still the mainstay of Prima, its other businesses include feed milling, food manufacturing (including bakery products and ready meals), foodservice operations, restaurants (noodle shops). Not neglecting to keep up with the fast-paced, modern world, it manufactures supply-chain and logistics computer software. And, after all that, Pima has time left to do property management.
