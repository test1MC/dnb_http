Banner Smoked Fish raises a signal for hungry smoked-fish connoisseurs. The company processes and sells smoked fish. Its primary product is smoked salmon (sometimes referred to as lox). Banner purchases frozen products from global seafood companies; processes, cures, smokes, slices, and packages it; and sells it to specialty food stores, supermarkets, delicatessens, bagel shops, and restaurants. With five wood-fired ovens, the company processes some 50,000 pounds of fish every week. Founded in 1989, Banner Smoked Fish is owned by president Avi Attias.
