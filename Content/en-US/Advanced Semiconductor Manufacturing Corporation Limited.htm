Advanced Semiconductor Manufacturing Corporation (ASMC) operates as an independent silicon foundry, manufacturing analog semiconductors and higher bipolar content-based mixed-signal semiconductors on a contract basis for other chip companies. Its customers include both fabless semiconductor companies and integrated device manufacturers; the latter have their own factories, but often contract out work. NXP owns nearly 27% of the company; Shanghai Chemical Industrial Park Development owns another quarter of ASMC. Established in 1988, the company operates two wafer fabrication plants, or fabs, located in Shanghai.
