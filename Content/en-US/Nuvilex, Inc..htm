Nuvilex is out to fill niche markets with its products. The company makes and sells a handful of items that can be classified as a couple of parts nutraceutical, a part dermatological, and a little bit environmental. Nuvilex's products are sold worldwide and include nutritional supplements Cinnergen and Cinnechol, tattoo ink Infinitink, and scar cream Talysn. Nuvilex has also developed an environmentally safe germicidal topical spray that kills some of the most frequent bacterial pathogens. The company is also developing a sporicidal to kill anthrax, and has formulated Citroxin, a product designed to knock out avian flu viruses.
