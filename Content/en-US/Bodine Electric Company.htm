Bodine Electric knows the drill ... and the lathe, and the conveyor system, and so forth. The venerable manufacturer of electric motors has more than 1,200 standard motors and thousands of custom gearmotors, motion controls, and motors available. The founding Bodine family owned company makes fractional-horsepower electric motors, gear motors, and controls for commercial, industrial, and OEM applications. Products include AC, DC, and brushless DC motors, as well as electronic motion controls. The company also makes fine-pitch gears for metalworking and specialty machinery, and custom builds motors.

Bodine Electric operates manufacturing facilities in Chicago and in Peosta, Iowa. The company has authorized service centers and distributors around the world.

The company offers 1,200 products directly to OEMs. It also sells through and global distributor network and via its online store. Distributors include Motion Industries, Minarik Automation & Control , Miller Bearings, Alstron Engineering, Maco Corporation (India), and Multitech Company.

The company, established by Swedish immigrants Carl and Paul Bodine, made its first electric motor in 1905, and the first electric motor for a dental drill in 1907.
