Bangkok Cable manufactures electric wire and cable. Founded in 1964, the company produces aluminum and copper cable products, including building wires and cables, power and control cables, and flexible wires and cables. Bangkok Cable also makes special flame-retardant, fire-resistant, and vermin-proof cables. The company has expanded to include commercial and governmental clientele among its predominantly Thai customers, and has begun marketing its products to Australia, the Philippines, Bangladesh, and India. Bangkok Cable has two subsidiaries, one of which makes silicon solar modules; it also produces a variety of products through joint venture companies.
