Dorland Healthcare Information publishes business information for the health and managed care markets. The company's offerings include directories, databases, mailing lists, and market research reports. It publishes products such as the Directory of Physician Groups & Networks , the Medical & Healthcare Marketplace Guide , the Case Management Resource Guide , and Dorland's Medical Directory . Dorland Healthcare Information sells subscriptions and advertising space. The company will become a subsidiary of Contexo Media in 2008.
