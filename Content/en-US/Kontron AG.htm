When it comes to embedded computing equipment, Kontron is king. The company supplies manufacturers, systems integrators, and end users with computer boards and integrated systems, including rack-mounted computers and monitors and ruggedized portable computers. Kontron computers control automated manufacturing systems, medical equipment, casino slot machines, military technology, and telecommunications networks; other applications include interactive kiosks, transaction terminals, and Internet transaction systems. Kontron counts Bombardier , Juniper Networks , and Ericsson among its customers. The company has sales offices in 20 countries; the Americas is its largest market, with 35% of sales.
