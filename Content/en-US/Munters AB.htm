If "climate" makes you think of outdoors, Munters would like you to bring your thinking inside. The climate control company sells systems to cleanse, heat, cool, humidify, and dehumidify indoor air. Its four business units -- Air Treatment, Ag/Hort, Spot Climate Control, and Mist Elimination -- serve  pharmaceutical, food, electronics, agriculture, and industrial companies in more than 30 countries worldwide. Munters, which focuses on energy efficiency, has designed special systems for a wide range of applications from museums to dairy farms, tomato green houses to chemical plants; it also creates custom systems for clients. The company was taken private by Nordic Capital affiliate Cidron Intressenter in 2010.
