Set on the Willamette River, the West Linn Paper mill has been independently churning out paper goods for more than 115 years. The oldest active paper mill in Western US, West Linn Paper runs three machines, producing coated free sheet paper -- up to 700 tons daily -- for use on web presses. Its paper lineup is chiefly used for high-impact printing products, such as in advertising, and in publishing books, catalogs, direct mail pieces, and magazines. Brands Capistrano, Sonoma, and Forest Stewardship Council (FSC)-certified grades Nature Web and Nature Plus, are sold direct through the company's customer service, primarily to West Coast businesses. The company is held by Belgravia Investments Ltd.
