Rush-hour traffic might make you hot under the collar, but C P Auto Products can help your car keep its cool. Doing business as Derale Cooling Products, C P Auto manufactures automotive fans, including electric cooling fans, fan clutch fans, and fan-mounted coolers, as well as coolers for engine oil, power steering systems, and transmissions. Other products include oil filtration accessories, as well as oil, power steering, and transmission coolers.
