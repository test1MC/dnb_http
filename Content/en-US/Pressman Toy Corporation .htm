If its customers can avoid being naughty all year long, a Pressman toy might just find its way into your stocking come time for the winter holidays. Pressman Toy makes classic games, such as Mastermind, Rummikub, and Tri-Ominos. The company specializes in licensed games and puzzles based on movies, such as Spider-Man and Spider-Man 2, and television game shows like Wheel of Fortune , Jeopardy , Double Dare , and Who Wants To Be a Millionaire . Jack Pressman founded the company in 1922. Its first smash hit was Chinese Checkers (1928), a game that is still featured in the company's products lineup. President and CEO Jim Pressman, son of the founder, was appointed president of Pressman Toy in 1977.
