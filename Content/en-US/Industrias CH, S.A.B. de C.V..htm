Mexico's Industrias CH has become quite the industrious steel producer. With 22 manufacturing plants in the US, Canada, and Mexico, CH is a leading manufacturer of steel products, which range from specialty steel and welded pipe to structural steel and rebar. It also has four scrap metal processors in Mexico. Its primary product, though, is Special Bar Quality (SBQ) steel. The automotive, oil, and construction sectors are among its main customers. Acquisitions -- including of Simec , Grupo Sidenor, and Republic Steel -- have boosted CH's production rate to more than 6 million tons a year from 70,000 tons in 1991. About 35% of sales come from outside Mexico, mostly in the US. The company was established in 1934.
