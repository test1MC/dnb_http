ULVAC makes vacuum products, but it doesn't compete with Hoover or Electrolux . The company specializes in equipment with vacuum-based processing chambers, used in making semiconductors, flat-panel displays, photovoltaic solar cells, and other precision components. ULVAC also supplies related instruments and parts, such as pumps and leak detectors, and fabricates microelectromechanical system (MEMS) devices on a contract basis. The company serves the alternative energy, automotive, biotechnology, chemical, electronics, food, industrial machinery, metals, pharmaceutical, and semiconductor industries. Established in 1952, ULVAC gets its name from the phrase "the ultimate in vacuum technology."
