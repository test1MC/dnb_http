The Wyedean Weaving Company wants its customers to get into the fray with them. The company makes textile accoutrements for military and occupational uniforms, including berets, aiguilettes, cummerbunds, epaulettes, tassels, sashes, badges, sword belts, and fine-detail gold lace. Wydean Weaving's products have been used for the uniforms of the Beefeaters, American Airlines , and P&O, among others. The company has also provided uniform accoutrements for royalty (Prince Andrew and Princess Anne), the costumes of film actors, including Russell Crowe and Tom Hanks, and the King of Pop Michael Jackson.

Wyedean Weaving is an expert in contract and supply chain management and has a worldwide network of ISO-certified suppliers. The company offers uniform/accessories expertise ,with stringent quality control measures in place and an experienced team of staff, all experts in their respective fields.

The company's customers include the UK Ministry of Defence, (London) Metropolitan Police, London Fire Brigade, Savile Row tailors, and UAE Defence and Security.

Wydean Weaving has been run by the same family for more than four decades. Among its many offerings, the company has even produced false eyelashes for camels and mummy binding. It outfitted actors with military uniforms in the 2011 film Gulliver's Travels , as well as Saving Private Ryan, Indiana Jones: Raiders of the Lost Ark, and The Last Samurai.

The company was founded in the mid-1800s as ribbon weavers.
