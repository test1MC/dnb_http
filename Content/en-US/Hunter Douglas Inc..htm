Don't move, Hunter Douglas has got you covered -- well, at least it has your windows covered. Hunter Douglas, the North American subsidiary of Netherlands-based Hunter Douglas N.V. , makes a variety of blinds, shades, and shutters. The company markets its window coverings under such brand names as Country Woods and Chalet Woods (wood blinds), Silhouette (shades), Palm Beach (custom shutters), Vignette (Roman shades), Luminette (privacy sheers), and Duette (honeycomb shades). In addition to its own sales outlets and specialty blind and home decor stores, Hunter Douglas sells its window products through independent dealers in the US and Canada. It accounts for about 40% of its parent company's sales.

Hunter Douglas boasts about 35 production facilities across North America.

In 2012 Hunter Douglas' sales exceeded $1 billion, a 6% gain over 2011 on an increase in volume.

Stung by deferred home construction and renovation projects during the recession, Hunter Douglas pared down its workforce by about 20% and consolidated its production operations in an effort to get its business back on track. Now with the economy on the mend and the outlook for housing improving the company's sales and profits are improving.

In 2013 Hunter Douglas acquired Gulf Coast Window Covering of Houston, a longtime manufacturer and distributor of Hunter Douglas products.
