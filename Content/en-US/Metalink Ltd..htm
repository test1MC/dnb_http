Wireline or wireless, Metalink's got your communications covered. The fabless semiconductor's DSL-based chipsets help resolve bandwidth bottlenecks on copper phone lines, thereby speeding digital voice and data transmission. Products include transceivers, framers, and mappers, all of which accommodate a variety of DSL technologies. In 2010 Metalink sold its WLAN business to Lantiq, a fabless semiconductor formed by Golden Gate Capital . Metalink nets nearly 80% of its sales outside of the US.
