Those who lack business knowledge may want to consult Alacra. The company gathers business and financial information from about 175 sources (such as Thomson Reuters , Dow Jones , and LexisNexis ) and feeds it to some 300,000 end users at 200 knowledge-hungry business clients, including investment and commercial banks, management consulting firms, law firms, and other corporations. The company's Alacra Book searches for and collects company and industry information relevant to the customer's query, and publishes it for the customer as a single PDF or Word file. The firm was founded in 1996 by former Knight-Ridder execs Steven Goldstein (chairman and CEO) and Michael Angle (president and COO).
