Inova Technology has innovative ways of keeping track of things. The company provides radio frequency identification (RFID) scanners and tags through its RighTag subsidiary. Its Trakkers subsidiary offers a tracking solution that allows trade show exhibitors to scan badges of attendees to capture contact information, then store the data in a specially designated Web site for access from any location. Inova Technology also offers IT consulting and computer network services through its Desert Communications subsidiary. Chairman and CEO Adam Radly controls more than half of the company.
