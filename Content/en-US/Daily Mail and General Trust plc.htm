If not the Daily Mail, who can you trust? The Daily Mail and General Trust (DMGT) is one of the UK's largest media holding companies. Its Associated Newspapers group is made up of its century-old flagship paper, the Daily Mail (with a circulation of about 2 million) and The Mail on Sunday . The company publishes more than 100 local papers across the UK. DMGT's majority-owned Euromoney Institutional Investor publishes some 100 business and finance magazines. In addition, the company owns a 20% stake in Independent Television News , a venture controlled by TV broadcaster ITV .

Subsidiaries include DMG Information (business information) and dmg world media (trade shows).

Within its newspaper operations, DMGT is focused primarily on cutting costs in response to slow but sure declines in readership and advertising revenue that have affected most of the industry. Part of that effort has included staff reductions at many of its papers. The company has also invested in its television and digital media properties as well.
