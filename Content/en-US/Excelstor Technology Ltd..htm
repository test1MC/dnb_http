Excelstor Technology has much in stor- age for its customers. Excelstor makes portable storage devices, internal and external storage for desktop computers, and hard drives for consumer electronics. The company also manufactures security-related hardware such as firewall and system recovery devices. Its other major operations include contract design and manufacturing services for OEMs (original equipment manufacturers). Selling its products through third-party distributors, Excelstor serves customers in Asia, Europe, and South America. Founded in 2000, the company is a subsidiary of computer products manufacturer Great Wall Technology Company .
