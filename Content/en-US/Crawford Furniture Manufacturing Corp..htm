After more than a century in business, Crawford Furniture Manufacturing is still building products to last a lifetime. The company manufactures solid wood beds, dressers, entertainment centers, dining room tables and other furniture at its facility in upstate New York. All of Crawford's pieces are constructed using locally grown ash, black cherry, maple, and oak lumber. They are also hand fitted and finished in a 15-step process. The company was founded by two Swedish craftsmen in 1883. It was purchased in 1941 by Clyne Crawford, who changed the company's name, and since then has been family owned and operated.
