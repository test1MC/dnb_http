Dynasty Fine Wines manufactures and sells wine in China -- and a lot of it! The company produces more than 30,000 tons of wine a year. The winemaker offers 50 types of wine products in four main categories -- red wines, white wines, sparkling wines, and brandy. It also produces other gift wines and alcoholic beverages. All products are sold under the "Dynasty" brand name. Dynasty Fine Wines was established as a Sino-French joint venture between Tianjin City Grape Garden and Rémy Martin in 1980. It was one of the earliest Sino-foreign joint ventures in China. Dynasty is owned by Tianjin Development Holdings Limited and Rémy Cointreau Group .
