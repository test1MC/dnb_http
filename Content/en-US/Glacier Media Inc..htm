Not necessarily slow, but definitely large, Glacier Media is a specialty publisher. Its Business and Professional Group publishes more than 120 technical manuals, medical education materials, real estate reports, and investment information in print, online, and on CD, while the Newspaper and Trade Group includes Canada's largest trade and online magazine publisher (43 trade magazines, 28 directories, and 35 sites). The company's Glacier Newspaper Group owns some 150 community newspapers throughout Canada, as well as a host of agricultural, business, reference, and trade publications. The Newspaper and Trade Group also includes the Business In Vancouver Media Group offering magazines, papers, and directories.
