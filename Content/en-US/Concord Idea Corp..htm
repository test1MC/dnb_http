Concord Idea operates in concord around a simple idea: make memory modules. The company designs and manufactures various kinds of PC memory modules -- including SDRAM, DDR DRAM, and Rambus -based variants -- which it markets under the SyncMAX and SyncPRO brands. In addition, Concord Idea offers engineering services, through which it designs printed circuit board (PCB) layouts and integrates memory components into larger systems. Elpida Memory , one of the world's largest manufacturers of memory devices, is a supplier to the company. Concord Idea was established in 1987 as a distributor of electronic components, and later branched out into the memory module business.
