Click it or ticket; Tokai Rika's products join the campaign to enforce seatbelt use. The company is a maker of car and driver interface components. Its line for driver safety, security, and comfort includes seatbelts as well as child safety seats, airbags, door mirrors, power window and steering switches, smart keys, and wheel covers. Non-automotive operations -- electric components and technology -- focus on sensors and switches for agricultural machinery, residential security systems, microelectronic devices, and ergonomic task measurement. Founded in 1948, Tokai Rika operates worldwide through 28 subsidiaries and 6 affiliates. Toyota Motor , one of the company's largest customers, owns over 30% of Tokai Rika.
