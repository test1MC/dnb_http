Forum Systems makes network security devices used to enable data privacy and encyption for Web-based communications services. The company's Sentry-branded gateway equipment lets customers authenticate their network users and securely manage digital resources specifically by encrypting XML (Extensible Markup Language) data and tags. It markets to financial services companies, Internet retailers, and government agencies among others. Customers have included Amazon.com , Gartner , and The Phoenix Companies . Forum Systems was acquired in 2009 by Massachusetts-based Web services testing and simulation tool specialist Crosscheck Networks.
