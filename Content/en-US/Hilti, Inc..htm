Operating as a subsidiary of Liechtenstein-based Hilti Corporation , Hilti manufactures and markets tools and related items. Its portfolio of products includes adhesives, blades, compressors, drills, fastening systems, foam, screws, and studs for the construction and building-maintenance industries. For contractors who frequent big-box retailers, Hilti runs some 300 Pro Shops inside Home Depot stores. The company's Hilti Tool Fleet Management provides maintenance, repair, and replacement services for the construction industry. Tulsa-based Hilti operates throughout North America, including a facility in Mississauga, Ontario.

Leveraging a global sales force, Hilti also sells its products through its online store. To better serve its US and Canadian customers, the tools maker rolled out a new website that showcases the entire Hilti product line, as well as the company's extensive technical and design resources.

As its parent invests in R&D, Hilti has been rolling out new products. It launched the Impact Socket to help users improve productivity. The sockets have two beveled detent holes to boost speed and accuracy.
