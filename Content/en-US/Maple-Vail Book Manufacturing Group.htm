Maple-Vail Book Manufacturing Group is sweet on books. The book manufacturer produces more than 45 million hardcover and paperback books annually from facilities in Pennsylvania and New York. Services include prepress, offset press (web and sheetfed printing), and bindery services. It also offers distribution and mailing services. The company can handle print runs ranging from 250 copies to more than 500,000. Maple Press was founded in 1901 by Harry A. Wisotzkey.
