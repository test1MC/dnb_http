When a construction company builds a high-rise from the ground up, how is the concrete transported to the top floor? Putzmeister fills this very specific need with its long-distance concrete squirter. Putzmeister America, the US-based subsidiary of Germany's Putzmeister Concrete Pumps , manufactures stationary and truck-mounted pumping equipment and truck-mounted belt conveyors used by construction companies in their building material and concrete handling activities. The company has several stateside locations from which it builds the machinery, enabling efficient, economical transport of the heavy lineup. Customers are concentrated in the North American housing construction industry.
