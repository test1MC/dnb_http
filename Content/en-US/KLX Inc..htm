KLX wants to be the three letters you think about when it comes to aerospace and energy services. The company's KLX Aerospace Solutions segment offers aerospace fasteners, logistic services, and consumables to the global commercial, business jet, and military sectors. KLX Energy Services offers oilfield services and provides rental equipment to oil and gas exploration and production companies. The company has two primary offices in Wellington and Miami, Florida; and one in Houston. KLX was formed in late 2014 as a spin-off from B/E Aerospace , a manufacturer of aircraft cabin interior products.
