At Van's International Foods, waffle is still a noun, not a verb. The company makes and markets specialty frozen waffles, including Belgian, mini, organic, and wheat-free varieties. It also makes frozen French toast sticks, pancakes, panini, and "breakfast in a pocket" wraps. Van's International sells its traditional, vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free products nationally through natural foods retailers and grocery stores. Van's products are sold throughout the US at food retailers such as Whole Foods , Trader Joe's , and Costco . The company is owned by Connecticut-based investment firm, Catterton Partners.
