Centrepoint Technologies targets companies that require communications upgrades in order to hit their fiscal bullseyes. The company is a manufacturer of desktop phone systems and switchboard (PBX) equipment for small businesses. Centrepoint's TalkSwitch devices are designed to connect traditional telephone networks to the Internet (voice over IP) so that businesses can route voice transmissions over data networks. The company's products also allow customers to add voicemail, automated attendants, and extra phone lines to their communications networks. Centrepoint Technologies was founded in 1990.
