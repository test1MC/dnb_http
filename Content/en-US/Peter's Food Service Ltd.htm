Unlike the hero of nursery rhymes and tongue twisters, this Peter picks a peck of pies, rolls, slices, and 900 other products. Peter Food Service produces more than three million pastries weekly, mainly baked meat pies and sausage rolls. But it also supplies an array of chilled and frozen foods, including sandwich fillings, chips and cheese, and cookies. The company's lineup is distributed under its namesake Peter's and Peter's Premier brands to supermarkets, fast food restaurants and fish bars, and institutions, such as hospitals and hotels and schools, across the UK. Mike Grimwood, a former Northern Foods executive, holds control of the company, formerly owned by the John Griffiths family.

Peter's Food Service is located in Caerphilly, UK. Its 10 distribution centers are located at Birmingham, Brighton, Bristol, Cardiff, Durham, High Wycombe, London, Swansea, Wakefield, and Warrington.

The company grows by expanding its product line. In 2014 it added to its portfolio of products sold in retail stores with the launch of a frozen sausages line. The sausages are available in Tesco stores in Wales.
