Hayco Manufacturing has a sweeping line of products. The company makes cleaning tools for the home, car, and industrial uses. Although its primary offerings are brooms and brushes, Hayco Manufacturing also produces electrical floor sweepers, toothbrushes, and scrub brushes for washing dishes and cleaning vegetables. Besides brush products, the company makes fabric covers for outdoor furniture and gardening tools. The firm operates four manufacturing plants in China. It ships products to Australia, Europe, and North America; Wal-Mart is a major client of the company. Hayco was founded in 1983 by Donald Hay, grandson of the founder of SABCO, an Australian brush manufacturer that is more than a century old.
