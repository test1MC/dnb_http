You might say the news gets celebrity treatment from this company. Northern and Shell is a leading UK newspaper publisher with its flagship papers the Daily Express (with a circulation of about 660,000) and Sunday Express . Other newspaper holdings include the Daily Star and the Sunday Star . In addition to mainstream news, the company publishes OK! , a top celebrity and gossip tabloid, as well as magazines New! and Star . Northern and Shell also has subscription and pay TV operations including adult entertainment channel Television X and pay-per-view service Red Hot, and it operates a television production studio in London. Founder and chairman Richard Desmond owns the company.
