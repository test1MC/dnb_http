E.J. Brooks, doing business as TydenBrooks Security Products, is not to be tampered with. The specialty manufacturer makes cross-border metal and plastic high-security seals, tamper-evident adhesive labels, and locking devices. Its lineup is used in a myriad of applications, such as RFID retail distribution, asset tracking, and food and pharmaceutical transportation -- on both airline and ocean carriers. TydenBrooks supplies diverse markets, such as transportation, retail, money handling, and pharmaceutical, as well as US Customs . Its operations dot the US, Asia, Europe, and Latin America. E.J. Brooks was acquired by Tyden Group Holdings in 2009 and integrated under the Tyden aegis.
