School children around the world cringe at the thought of this company's products. Sanofi Pasteur makes vaccines for 20 infectious viral and bacterial diseases, such as diptheria, hepatitis A and B, meningitis, polio, tetanus, and whooping cough. It's also the world's largest maker of the dreaded flu shot, with a 40% market share. The company has about 20 other vaccines in development, including an HIV vaccine and a combination booster shot to protect against six childhood diseases. Sanofi Pasteur has manufacturing facilities across France and the US , as well as in Argentina, Canada, China, and Thailand. The company is a subsidiary of pharmaceutical giant Sanofi .
