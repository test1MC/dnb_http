When it's compared to its sister companies, Anam Electronics is probably more entertaining. The company, a unit of semiconductor giant Anam Group, specializes in making audio/visual and medical electronics products that are sold in more than 30 countries. It makes TVs, VCRs, DVD players, and related home electronics. Its products are sold under the Anam brand in Korea and worldwide. Founded in 1973, Anam Electronics is related to other Anam businesses that produce wristwatches and digital clocks, cable decoders, and semiconductor equipment. As part of the group's effort to spin off Anam Electronics, Anam Semiconductor sold its stake in the company. Seoul Guarantee Insurance owns about 21% of Anam Electronics.
