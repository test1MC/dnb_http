Artaflex (formerly Adeptron Technologies) is adept at adapting to its customers' electronic manufacturing needs. The company is a contract manufacturer of surface-mount, through-hole, and mixed-technology printed circuit boards (PCBs) for customers in North America, mostly in Canada. PCBs of many shapes and sizes are used in almost all types of modern electronic equipment; large electronics makers farm out PCB manufacturing to cut costs and improve efficiency. Artaflex also offers prototyping, box build (complete system) assembly, and systems integration, along with materials management and order fulfillment services.
