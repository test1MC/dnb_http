Con-Space Communications designs and manufactures radio communications equipment for people working in confined spaces and for public safety personnel. The company's products include miniature two-way radios with throat microphone attachments for use inside face masks and other protective clothing. Con-Space serves clients including municipal fire and law enforcement departments and the military. The company operates from offices in the UK and the US. Turret Oy AB, the company's majority shareholder, bought out Con-Space's remaining shares in 2009, taking the company private. Con-Space operates as part of the SAVOX Communications Group, which includes affiliated companies in Finland and the UK.
