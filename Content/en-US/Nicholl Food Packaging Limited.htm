No fooling, Advanta (formerly Nicholl Food Packaging) loves to foil its customers. Advanta manufactures aluminum foil containers primarily for bakery wholesalers, catering distributors, cash-and-carry outlets, and the food-processing industry. Products include foil containers (compartmental, patty pans and cups, medium rounds, oblongs and ovals), aluminum lids (foil board laminate, microwavable boards, heat seal film, and transparent domes), smoothwalls (dessert range and rectangular and round), and trivets. The company also manufactures hand-closing and dispensing machinery. Advanta is owned by UK-based Bawtry Investments.

The company owns production facilities in UK, along with manufacturing facilities in France and Germany. It also has a Northern European warehousing and distribution hub in Belgium.

Advanta has moved beyond aluminum packaging products and its product lines now include compostable vacuum pouches and fiber pulp trays.

To provide its customers with a wide choice of container products, the company has established trading links with other manufacturers in Europe, the US, and Australasia.

The company is focused on product and market development, along with maintaining global alliances and securing strategic acquisitions.Product launches in 2014 included a new foil lid, 81-2000, which fits all foils with the dimensions of 227x177; and a Convi Tray.

That year the company changed its name to Advanta to create a single strong identity.

Bawtry Investments acquired the group in 2012 from an investment group that included management and Barclays Ventures.

Advanta's (Nicholl's) acquisitions have included Scottish foil tray manufacturer Watson & Watson in 2004 and German aluminum container manufacturer Bachman Aluminium in 2007.

The company was founded in 1989.
