Shin-Etsu Handotai is one of the world's leading makers of silicon wafers and ingots used in semiconductor manufacturing. Handotai means "semiconductor" in Japanese. It also makes light-emitting element materials -- gallium phosphide, gallium arsenide phosphide, and aluminum gallium indium phosphide -- used in LEDs and other optoelectronic devices. In addition to manufacturing, the company provides engineering and support services. Customers including Intel , TSMC , and Texas Instruments use materials produced by Shin-Etsu to make chips for computers, consumer electronics, and telecommunications devices. The company, a part of the ShinEtsu Group, has facilities in Malaysia, Taiwan, the UK, and the US.
