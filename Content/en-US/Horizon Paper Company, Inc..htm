With paper orders that stretch to the horizon, Horizon Paper Company is a supplier of paper and board in North America, as well as international markets. The company represents manufacturers of a wide range of boards and graphic papers, including coated and uncoated board, freesheet (paper containing less than 10% groundwood pulp), and groundwood/mechanical pulp (inexpensive grade of paper); newsprint; and supercalendered (smooth and glossy) papers. The company's mill clients have included such big names as Abitibi , Bowater , Domtar , Fraser , International Paper , and Weyerhaeuser .
