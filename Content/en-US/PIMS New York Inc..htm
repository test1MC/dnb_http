Doing business as PIMS, PIMS New York is a commercial printing firm that specializes in serving marketing and public relations agencies, along with clients in such industries as fashion and pharmaceuticals. It offers both digital and offset printing services, as well as fulfillment and inventory management services. In addition, PIMS provides direct mail, promotional printing, and digital media replication services, plus online marketing services including search engine optimization (SEO). The company has offices in Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, New York City, and Washington, DC.
