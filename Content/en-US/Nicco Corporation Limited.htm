Nicco Corporation has ties to many industries. The company provides cable manufacturing and project and engineering services in India. Its Nicco Cables division makes cables used in aircraft, elevators, railways, oil and gas, and mining equipment. Customers include ports, railways, and the armed forces in India. Nicco's project division offers construction, engineering, and procurement contracting to clients in the refining, gas handling and processing, and petrochemical processing industries in India. It has also worked in Australia, Africa, Iraq, and Russia. Nicco Corporation is the flagship company of Nicco Group, which also includes Nicco Engineering (maintenance services) and Nicco Parks (amusement parks).
