AccuWeather wants to blow away the image of the guessing weatherman. The company provides local weather forecasts for all of the US and about 3 million international locations via the Internet, mobile devices, IPTV, and newspapers. AccuWeather's website offers weather-related blogs, headlines, and travel information. The company serves consumers and more than 175,000 clients, including hundreds of TV and radio stations worldwide. It provides content to more than 180,000 third party websites, such as those owned by CNN , ABC , and The Washington Post . The company employs more than 100 meteorologists. AccuWeather was founded in 1962 by company chairman and president Joel Myers, a meteorologist from Penn State University .

AccuWeather is available on 200 TV stations, in 600 newspapers, on 800 radio stations, and through apps on about 85% of mobile devices. It also provides weather-risk management consulting and forecasting to enterprises in the transportation, manufacturing, educational, commodities, government, and manufacturing sectors throughout North America. Key enterprise customers include Disney , Lowe's , Ford , BNSF Railway , Boeing , and Louis Dreyfus Commodities .

AccuWeather is headquartered in Pennsylvania with more than 21,000 outlets worldwide.

In addition to TV, newspaper, radio, website, and mobile customers, AccuWeather also provides enterprise weather-risk management services to transportation, manufacturing, government, and media customers. Key enterprise customers include Disney , Lowe's , Ford , BNSF Railway , Boeing , and Louis Dreyfus Commodities .

In 2013 the company purchased Canada's Sky Motion Research, an ultra-short-term, localized weather forecast developer. AccuWeather then began offering its MinuteCast and Minute by Minute Forecast.
