Customers who want door-to-door, one-stop shopping the American Way turn to Amway Corp. Doing business as Amway Global, the North American affiliate of Amway International , part of Alticor , is an online retailer and direct seller of personal care products, health supplements, and household cleaners. Its offerings include cosmetics, skin care and hair care products, vitamins, as well as air and water purifiers, cleaners for laundry and surfaces, and cookware. The lineup is sold under the name brands Artistry, Satinique, Nutrilite, eSpring, Legacy of Clean, and iCook, among others. Once known as Quixtar, the company in 2009 adopted the Amway Global name to align with the international status of the Amway brand.

The North American arm of Amway International , the company serves independent business owners (IBOs) in the US, Canada, Puerto Rico, and various territories and independent island nations in the Pacific and Atlantic Oceans and the Caribbean Sea. Amyway Corp's IBOs are part of a force of some 3 million worldwide.

Amway is a well-known multi-level marketer and direct seller of household, personal care, nutrition, and cleaning items. The company also peddles the products of other companies worldwide in more than 80 markets.
