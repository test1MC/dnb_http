Canyon Creek Food Company manufactures and sells a variety of prepared-foods to grocery, food service, and institutional customers throughout Canada. Through its Canyon Creek Soup subsidiary, Canyon Creek initially focused on fresh soups, gravies, and sauces, but the company has since added a variety of sides, salads, and entrées to its menu. Its line of Big Joe entrees includes chili, lasagna, and macaroni and cheese. The company is also certified to produce organic soups and entrees. Based in Calgary, Canyon Creek Food Company manufactures its foods from a plant in Edmonton, Alberta. The firm is controlled by its chairman Brian Halina who holds about 59% of its shares.
