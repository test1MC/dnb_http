Gund doesn't beat around the bush; they'll tell you straight up that you gotta getta Gund . The company makes teddy bears and other items for infants, including rugs, play mats, and stuffed animals made of corduroy and plush fabric. In addition to its nursery offerings, Gund designs giftware (water globes, bells, picture frames) for weddings and anniversaries. The company distributes its products throughout the US, Canada, Europe, and Japan. Founded in 1898, Gund is one of the oldest plush toy makers in the US. In 2008 the family-run company sold out to Enesco , a global distributor of giftware and home decor, whose brands include Beatrice Potter Classics, Cherished Teddies, and Pooh & Friends.
