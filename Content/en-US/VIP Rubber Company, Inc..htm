VIP Rubber serves some Very Important Persons -- its customers. The family-owned company has four manufacturing units: rubber extrusion, plastic extrusion, rubber and plastic molding, and calender sheet. VIP Rubber extrudes all organic polymers, including hypalon, natural rubber, nitrile, and neoprene. It also extrudes silicone and fluorocarbons. The company's extruded products are used in bumpers, pumps, seals, tubes, and other products. VIP Rubber molds rubber and plastic products, using injection machines, transfer equipment, and compression presses.
