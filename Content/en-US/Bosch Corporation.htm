Bosch Corporation means German engineering for Japanese automakers. The company, a wholly owned subsidiary of Robert Bosch GmbH , caters to the Japanese automotive industry by developing and manufacturing systems and components for diesel and gasoline engines; brake, skid-prevention, and transmission systems; as well as drives for electric vehicles. Other products include automotive electronics, airbags, and sensors. Bosch Corporation imports/exports aftermarket parts and provides vehicle servicing. Besides automotive parts, the company also makes professional power tools and workshop testing equipment. Customers have included Toyota , Nissan , Honda , Mitsubishi Motors , and Fuji Heavy Industries .
