Some like it hot, others, cold. When it comes to precision forgings, Colfor Manufacturing makes them hot, warm, or cold. Colfor produces auto driveline components, from transmission shafts, transfer case shafts, axle shafts, and transmission gears to wheel spindles. Its shafting and gears power cars, sport utility vehicles, and light trucks. Colfor is the sole supplier for many of General Motors ' needs. Most parts are made for the life of GM vehicle programs under a Lifetime Program Contract. Parts are also made for Chrysler and Ford , and suppliers Timken and Hino . Colfor also makes mining bits. The company is part of American Axle & Manufacturing , a global supplier of chassis and drivetrain systems and parts.
