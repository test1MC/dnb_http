Need sheet metal fabrication services? Here comes Aircom. Contract manufacturer Aircom Manufacturing has a diverse customer base in the automotive, consumer, food service, industrial, medical, and telecommunications markets. The company's manufacturing services include injection molding, sheet metal fabrication, welding, wiring harnesses, precision stamping, tool making, engineering services, assembly, and decorating. Additionally, it makes intricate magnets and sub-assemblies for individual applications, as well as offers supply chain management and distribution. In 2016 Aircom Manufacturing was acquired by Kimball Electronics .

Aircom's subsidiary Medivative Technologies, which began as a manufacturing venture with Theron, is a medical device contract manufacturing company that helps life science companies get their products to market by providing supply chain management, assembly, packaging, and distribution. The companies' coordinated efforts result in reduced time to market, as well as improved cost efficiencies.

For the telecommunications industry, Aircom makes fiber optic connectors, technician handsets, power distribution equipment, and connectivity enclosures for the telecommunications industry. It has counted AT&T among its customers in this segment.

Its automotive and heavy machinery sub-assemblies include suspension and steering system components, as well as decorative components. Its industrial segment encompasses a wide range of products, which include timing and detection systems, precision printer and copier chassis, wiring harnesses, and machined parts.

The company serves the medical, animal health, telecommunications, food and beverage, automotive and heavy machinery, and industrial markets. Some of its customers include AT&T and Western Electric.

The company was founded in 1960 by Robert Lyon as Aircom Metal Products, a two-machine manufacturing shop. The business took off in 1961 when the firm secured Western Electric as its first major client in the telecommunications industry. The company is under the leadership of the Lyon family.
