APM holds the keys to the car for many manufacturers. The company makes original equipment and replacement components for customers in more than 40 countries across the globe. Divisions include suspension (parabolic and air springs, coil springs, shock absorbers, and u-bolts), interior and plastic (seats, interior trims, and side moldings), and heat exchange and electrical (air conditioning systems, radiators, and electrical parts). APM operates from eight plants in its home country of Malaysia. It also owns Radiators Australia (2000) Pty Ltd., APM Springs (Vietnam) Co. Ltd., three joint ventures in Indonesia with Johnson Controls , and two joint ventures with Malaysia and Thailand with IAC Group.
