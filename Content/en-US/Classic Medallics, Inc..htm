For many, memorable days are marked by Classic. Classic Medallics (also known as the Singer Company) makes awards and recognition products, such as trophies, medals, plaques, ribbons, jewelry, pen sets, and clocks. Most of the firm's sales are generated during the holiday and school seasons. Classic Medallics uses brass, bronze, copper, aluminum, zinc, pewter, silver, and gold; wood products are made from walnut, oak, ash, rosewood, and simulated wood finishes. The company is run by president Gerald Singer, whose grandfather founded the firm in 1940, and his cousin and partner Mario Singer.
