Rose Hills arose -- literally -- from the hills of Southern California to become the largest operation of its kind in the US, as it boasts expansive, combined funeral and cemetery options. The company operates the Rose Hills Mortuary and Memorial Park, which spans some 1,400 acres outside Los Angeles. It's considered a full-service funeral provider -- with its funeral homes, funeral and cemetery combinations, and a cemetery. As part of funeral home and cemetery behemoth Service Corporation International (SCI), Rose Hills performs more than 5,000 funeral services and 8,500 interments each year. Rose Hills touts the feng shui of its cemetery, as well as several unusual options.
