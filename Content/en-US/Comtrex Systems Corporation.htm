Comtrex Systems helps restaurateurs keep track of the count. The company's point-of-sale (POS) terminals and software combine traditional fast-food and fine-dining restaurant cash register functions with computer control and data collecting capabilities. The systems track labor, accounting, and inventory, and replace the company's legacy Sprint and SuperSprint terminals. The Comtrex Virtual Office utilizes the Internet to allow users to manage multiple outlets remotely. Comtrex sells directly and through authorized dealers in Canada, the Caribbean, Europe, and the US. The company was established in 1981. Comtrex Systems, once publicly held, is owned by its executives and other shareholders.
