Le Bleu Corporation manufactures bottled water and water-purification systems, using distillation to cleanse its water it of impurities. It also operates a home and office water delivery service in North and South Carolina. Other products sold by the company include fruit, sport, and soft drinks, laundry and dish detergents, sport drinks, and ground coffee especially made for use with Le Bleu purified water. It also makes an automated bottled water system, laundry and dish detergents, hand soap and shampoo, and a chlorine-removing shower filter. Le Bleu's products are distributed throughout the US and overseas in Japan and about a dozen Caribbean countries.
