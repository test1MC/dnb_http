Kodak Limited is anything but a flash in the pan. Part of parent Eastman Kodak 's European, African, and Middle Eastern division, Kodak Limited is the photographic products provider's oldest overseas subsidiary. It operates manufacturing facilities in England that make photographic and synthetic chemicals, as well as photographic films and papers. The company markets Kodak's full range of offerings, including consumer and professional photographic supplies, imaging products for health and graphic applications, audiovisual systems, and copier products. Kodak Limited's roots reach back to 1885, when Eastman Kodak set up its first British office.
