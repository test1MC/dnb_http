MagnaChip is a pretty big chip off the block. The company was formed to acquire the nonmemory operations of Hynix Semiconductor , one of the world's largest producers of memory devices. MagnaChip primarily offers flat-panel display drivers, used in high-definition televisions, LCD TVs, mobile phones, notebook PCs, and PC monitors. It also does a large business in silicon foundry (contract semiconductor manufacturing) services. Companies of the LG Group , primarily LG Display , are MagnaChip's biggest customers. Most of the company's sales are in Asia. After going through bankruptcy reorganization in 2009 and an abandoned IPO attempt in 2010 due to a weak market, MagnaChip completed a public offering of stock in 2011.
