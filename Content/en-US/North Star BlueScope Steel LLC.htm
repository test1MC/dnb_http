North Star BlueScope Steel is a high roller in flat-rolled steel. The joint venture, which is 50-50 owned by Cargill and BlueScope Steel and whose facility is in Ohio, produces hot-rolled steel coils using scrap metal. Its flat-rolling minimill maintains an annual production capacity of nearly 2 million tons. The company's main customers are coil processors, cold rolled strip producers, OEMs, and steel service centers.
