Royal Boon Edam has an open-door policy -- especially if you're in the market for a securely closed door. The company has been making revolving doors and security barriers for more than 100 years. It offers a wide range of customizable products including manual and automated revolving doors, specialty slide doors, and high-capacity turnstiles. Boon Edam also provides handicap access gates and high-impact-resistant vehicle barriers. The company offers installation and maintenance services for all of its products. Boon Edam operates globally, distributing its products in more than 50 countries throughout the world. The company expanded its offerings in 2005 with its acquisition of TOMSED Corporation, a maker of security revolving doors and vehicle barriers. Boon Edam merged TOMSED into its own US operations to form Boon Edam Tomsed.
