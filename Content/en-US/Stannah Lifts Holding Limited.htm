At Stannah Lifts they don't climb the corporate ladder, they just take the lift. The company's products include elevators, stair lifts, access platform lifts, and goods lifts. Stannah is the world's largest supplier of stair lifts and has sold more than 500,000 worldwide. The company is also a leading maker of elevators in the UK. Stannah's services include repair and maintenance. Family-owned, the company was originally founded as a crane and hoist manufacturer by Joseph Stannah in the 1860s. Stannah Lifts, which has manufacturing facilities in Andover and Blaydon, UK, serves customers around the world.
