Berkshire Investments (dba Chicago Extruded Metals Company, CXM) extrudes confidence. The company manufacturers brass and brass alloy (including Naval) bar, coil, rod, wire, and other extruded shapes, using recycled and virgin metals. CXM's brass products are used in automotive parts, electrical components, office furniture, industrial valves/fittings, HVAC parts, musical instrument parts, and medical equipment. In addition to its extrusion capabilities, the company offers pickling, drawing, annealing, and saw-to-length services. Established in 1923, CXM's fully integrated brass mill in Cicero, Illinois, features horizontal continuous casting, indirect extrusion, and multiple finishing lines.
