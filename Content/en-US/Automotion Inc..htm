Automotion helps products dance down the production line. The company designs and manufactures hardware for package conveyor systems and sorters for distribution centers, warehousing facilities, and manufacturing plants. Products include belt, live roller, and sortation conveyors, as well as direct-drive gearmotors. Automotion's partners supply a number of services and products including systems controls, pallet conveyors, steel fabrication, installation, and lifetime support. Automotion distributes its products throughout North and South America. Customers include Crate & Barrel, General Medical, S&P Global , and Pac Sun . The company is a subsidiary of Wynright Corp., which was acquired by Daifuku Webb in 2013.
