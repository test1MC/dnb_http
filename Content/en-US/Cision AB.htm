Cision AB is a market monitoring, analysis, and research firm that informs clients on how they are covered by the media. The company monitors some 50,000 news and information sources, such as newspapers and magazines, web publications, broadcasts, news services, press releases, and databases. Cision covers the Nordic and Baltic, rest of Europe, and North America (where it operates as Cision US ) regions, serving information, communications, investor relations, and marketing departments of companies, as well as public relations firms and public-sector agencies. The company has expanded through acquisitions and in 2015 Cision agreed to acquire PR Newswire .
