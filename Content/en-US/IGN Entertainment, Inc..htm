IGN Entertainment may not stand for International Geek Network, but it does court some serious video gamers. The company operates a network of video game and entertainment-related websites that target males aged 18-34. Its flagship gaming site IGN.com (along with GameSpy, FilePlanet, and TeamXbox) is the US's leading gaming network; IGN also operates lifestyle site AskMen.com. Together, its IGN sites attract more than 40 million unique monthly visitors globally (in the US, Australia, and Germany). IGN also includes GameSpy Technology group, which provides technology for online game play. IGN is a subsidiary of Ziff Davis .
