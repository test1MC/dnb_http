Business success hangs by a thread at Pasupati Spinning and Weaving. India's second largest maker of polyester thread, Pasupati Spinning whirls and weaves cotton and nylon strands into a lineup of cotton yarn, cotton and nylon sewing thread, knitted grey hosier fabric, and two-ply yarn. More than 15,000 tons of yarn and threads each year are produced by this master of spin! Its yarn and thread are used by a diversity of industries, from carpet to terry towel, weaving, and knitwears. The company distributes its sewing threads, branded Bull & Ivory, across India, as well as exports its goods throughout Asia, the Middle East, and Europe. Indian textile firm Pasupati Conglomerate owns Pasupati Spinning and Weaving.
