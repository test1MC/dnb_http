Buzzi Unicem USA has a concrete goal -- to secure a top market spot US cement manufacturing. The company produces portland and masonry cement and ready-mix concrete used in highway and airport paving and concrete block manufacturing. It runs a handful of cement plants with an annual production capacity of about 9.8 million metric tons. It distributes its products throughout the Midwest, Northeast, and Southeast through some 30 distribution terminals in more than 20 states. Formed out of the merger of RC Cement and Dyckerhoff 's Lone Star Industries, Buzzi Unicem USA is a subsidiary of Italy-based BUZZI UNICEM .

Buzzi Unicem USA operates plants in Pennsylvania, Missouri, Indiana, Illinois, Oklahoma, Texas, and Tennessee. The company operates Memphis Ready Mix, which gives it a significant presence in Tennessee.

The company maintains seven cement manufacturing plants and 30 distribution terminals nationwide.

Buzzi Unicem USA supplies cement and masonry cement to more than 3,800 ready-mix concrete, highway and airport-paving firms, concrete block companies, and concrete product firms.

Having produced 9.8 million tons of cement in fiscal 2013, Buzzi Unicem USA contributed more than 25% of its parent's overall revenue.

Buzzi Unicem USA aims to modernize and expand its production facilities. To this end, in 2013 the company is working to double the production capacity at its Maryneal, Texas, plant and reduce the facility's environmental footprint while it serves rising demand for cement in the region from the construction and oil and gas industries.
