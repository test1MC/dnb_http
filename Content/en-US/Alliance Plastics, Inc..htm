Essentra Plastics (formerly Alliance Plastics) can get a grip, put a cap on it, and wrap things up. The company manufactures and distributes more than 6,000 injection and vinyl dip plastic protection and finishing components, from grips to caps and plugs, hose wraps, flange protectors, and netting, for a range of industries and heavy-duty applications. Its growth is based on emerging technologies, driving new products like high-temperature vinyl masking caps that offer chemical protection and heat resistance of up to 425Â°. It is a niche business of UK-based specialty plastic and bonded fiber products supplier Essentra (formerly Filtrona and an arm of Bunzl plc) .

In 2013 Alliance Plastic was rebranded to better align with its parent Essentra and became Essentra Plastics. As part of the move, the parent consolidated its Richco Inc, and Reid Supply subsidiaries into the unit.

The company has distribution locations in 27 countries in North America, South America, Europe, Africa, and Asia/Pacific. Most are in the UK and the US.

Essentra Plastics operates as part of the Component & Protection Solutions segment of its parent company.

It makes and distributes products like access hardware, casters, leveling mounts, bumpers and feet, tube insert glides and caps, wire management, cable ties, and circuit board hardware.

The Component & Protection Solutions segment accounted for 29% of Essentra's revenues in 2014. The segment's revenue was up by 8.9% due to new business wins and the roll out of distribution sites.

In 2015 the parent company planned to open new sites in Dubai, Korea, and Ireland; continue to expand its range in springs and magnets, and globalize product offering; add new eCommerce platforms; and upgrade its CRM systems.

Moving into Australia, in 2014 the company acquired Kelvindale, a distributor of plastic protection and finishing products.

Founded in 1968, the plastic product manufacturer became a niche business of Essentra (formerly Filtrona and an arm of Bunzl plc ) in 1987.
