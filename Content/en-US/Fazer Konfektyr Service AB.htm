How sweet it is for Fazer Konfektyr. (If you only speak English, that's Fazer Confectionery). The company is one of the Nordic region's largest confectioners -- offering both chocolate and non-chocolate candy. Formerly Cloetta Fazer, it manufactures such top-selling brands as Dumle chocolate-covered toffee, Karl Fazer milk chocolate bars, Fazermint chocolate-covered mints, and Tyrkisk Peber liquorice. Fazer's two top markets are Finland (where it has a 42% market share) and Sweden, followed by Norway and Denmark; it also has a strong presence in the Baltic region. The company is part of the Fazer Group.
