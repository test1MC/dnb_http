Sinovel is looking to blow away the competition. China's largest wind turbine manufacturer is also the third largest in the world, behind Vestas and GE Energy . Sinovel makes 1.5 MW turbines for land use and 3 MW and 5 MW turbines for offshore wind farms in China, and has a total production capacity of 3.5 GW. The company sold about 2,300 1.5 MW and 3 MW turbines in 2009; it launched its 5 MW offshore turbine in October 2010. Its turbines, parts, and components are sold under the brands Huarui Fengdian, Huarui Wind, Sinovel, Sinovel Windtec, and Sinowind. Formerly a state-owned company, Sinovel went public on the Shanghai Stock Exchange in January 2011, raising $1.4 billion in its initial public offering.
