Alinabal Holdings operates through its individual business units that serve the automotive, aerospace, and printing industries. Alinabal Motion Transfer Devices manufactures spherical bearings, stamped molded bearing and linkage assemblies, and standard/custom rod ends. Alinabal Engineered Products produces complex mechanical assemblies and precision stampings. Its Alinabal Practical Automation unit provides printers (for use in ticket kiosks and ATMs), and the company also makes aircraft instrumentation (desiccators and dehumidifiers) and optical shutters (aka "beam blockers") through its DACO Instrument subsidiary. Alinabal was founded in 1913.

The company serves a range of agriculture, construction, aerospace, distribution, industrial equipment, lawn and garden, mass transit, medical equipment, racing, recreational vehicles, and commercial trucking OEM markets. It offers its products through distributors and dealers.

The company is diversified, which makes it more stable in the event that any one particular market undergoes an economic downturn. Customers have included the US military, which uses Alinabal's components in smart bombs and mortars, as well as Disney , which uses the company's products in its animatronic characters.
