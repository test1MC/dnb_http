Got Milk? If so, odds are Walker Transport had something to do with bringing it to you. The company makes aluminum, steel, and stainless steel food grade tank trailers used to transport liquids. In addition to hauling milk and other dairy products, Walker Transport's trailers serve other segments of the food and beverage industry, as well as the pharmaceutical and petrochemical industries. Walker Transport is one of four major business areas of Walker Stainless Equipment, an operating company of Walker Group Holdings, which is owned by private investment firm Insight Equity.
