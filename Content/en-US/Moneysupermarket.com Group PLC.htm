Moneysupermarket.com offers price comparison data for products and services across Money, Insurance, Home Services, and Travel vertical markets in the UK. The company's websites helps consumers and businesses comparison-shop for credit cards, insurance policies, mortgages, and a variety of banking products. The sites are free for consumers; the majority of revenues come from fees collected from the sale of loans and credit cards. It also earns money through advertising. Moneysupermarket.com works with providers such as British Airways , RAC , and American Express . The company was founded in 1999.
