Ball Packaging Europe maintains a can-do attitude, manufacturing aluminum beverage cans for beer, carbonated soft drinks, fruit juices, and other consumer uses. The German company produces beverage cans from 375 milliliters (about 12 oz.) to 5-liter novelty containers and controls about 30% of the European market. It makes widget technology (provides foam via releasing nitrogen) cans and full-aperture end products, as well. The company offers container printing (in-can, under the tab), embossing (branding), and luminescent inks for decoration. It operates 12 production plants in Europe, the Netherlands, Poland, and Serbia. Major customers include Coca-Cola , Coors , Heineken , and Guinness .
