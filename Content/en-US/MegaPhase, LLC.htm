If you are feeling testy about coaxial cable, don't worry, it may just be a megaphase you are going through. MegaPhase designs, develops, and makes broadband and high-frequency coaxial cable assemblies, as well as test cables, test adapters, coaxial connectors, and other products used to connect components inside a system. Applications have included testing cell phones ( Sprint Nextel ), handheld analyzers ( Agilent ), and antenna farms for anti-artillery and communications ( US Marine Corps ). Its custom-engineered cable assemblies are used in the US Army 's Hunter tactical unmanned aerial vehicle program. MegaPhase products are also used in the broadband data, instrumentation, and semiconductor industries.
