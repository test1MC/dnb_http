Identive Group grants secure access to the digital world. The company makes hardware and software for securely accessing digital content and services. Its products include smart card readers for electronic IDs and driver's licenses, as well as health care, computer network, and facility access cards. Among other purposes, Identive's digital media readers are used in digital photo kiosks to transfer data to and from flash media. The company sells to computer makers, government contractors, systems integrators, financial institutions, and photo processing equipment makers. Identive has international facilities in Australia, Canada, Germany, Hong Kong, India, Japan, the Netherlands, Singapore, and Switzerland.
