Linden Hose & Adapter is a believer in the power of liquid. The company makes and sells industrial hydraulic systems and components, including hoses, fittings, valves, hydraulic cylinders, and hydraulic adapters. Linden also offers a range of services includes the design and installation of custom hydraulic systems, diagnosis of hydraulic problems, and servicing and repair of hydraulic systems. The company distributes hose and fitting products made by Manuli Hydraulics and Uniflex hose assembly machinery such as workshop and production presses, testing equipment, and cleaning equipment. The company was acquired by the Hydraquip Group in 2013.

Linden Hose & Adaptors is based in northeastern England and operates two fluid power branches in Hartlepool and Newton Aycliffe.

From agriculture and food processing to automotive, aviation and renewable energies, Linden Hose & Adaptors supplies both products and engineering products across the world. The company sales over 55,000 products online.
