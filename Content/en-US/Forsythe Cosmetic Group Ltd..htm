Forsythe Cosmetic Group had the foresight to get its hands in the nail care market. It manufactures and distributes nail care products for beauty retailers, salons, and spas. Forsythe also offers private labeling for distributors and salons. Its products include nail care items, as well as nail polish, lipstick, and makeup available in more than 150 shades. Specializing in professional nail lacquer, Forsythe's brand names include Silicone Shield, Color Club, Art Club, and Funky Fingers. Founded in 1979 by Harriet and Buddy Rose, the company also sells boxed collections and nail polish displays. The Rose's son, Michael, joined the company in 2005.
