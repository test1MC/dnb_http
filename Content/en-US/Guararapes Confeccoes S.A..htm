Brazilians may have done their part to popularize the G-string, but Guararapes Confecções knows that isn't all they wear. The company manufactures and sells a wide range of apparel and accessories for men, women, and children. Its handful of factories produce about 120,000 items every day that it sells through retail subsidiary Lojas Riachuelo under such brand names as Body Work and YC Basic. With some 100 locations, Riachuelo is among the largest clothing retailers in the country and it sells about half of Guararapes output. The rest is divided between several other retail stores. Guararapes also owns the Midway Mall shopping center located in its hometown of Natal.
