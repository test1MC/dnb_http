Singulus Technologies has a reputation for replication. The company makes equipment used to master, mold, and replicate CDs, DVDs, and Blu-ray discs. Its product lines include Skyline CD-audio and CD-ROM replication products, Streamline replication products for recordable CDs, and Bluline replication for Blu-ray discs. The company's machines have been used to produce CDs with holographic images for Microsoft products. Looking to expand into the solar market, Singulus bought a majority interest in STANGL, a German firm that makes wet processing systems used in the production of silicon solar and thin-film solar cells. Customers in Europe and the Americas together account for a majority of sales.
