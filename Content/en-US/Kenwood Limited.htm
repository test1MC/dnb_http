How many appliances would Ken buy if Ken would buy appliances? Just about any number of small kitchen and household appliances you can name! Kenwood designs and manufactures a slew of cooking and food prep tools, including mixers, food processors, the Cooking Chef (an all-in-one machine that mixes and cooks), multi-task attachments, kettles, and coffee makers. Kenwood also makes microwave ovens and vacuum cleaners. Its appliances are sold under the Kenwood brand in supermarkets, department stores, and specialty shops across the UK. The lineup is also available from online retailers. Founded in the late 1940s by Kenneth Wood, Kenwood has operated as a subsidiary of Italian appliance maker De'Longhi since 2001.
