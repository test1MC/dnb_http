Safety Syringes wants to ensure that when you're getting needled, it's with deliberate intention. The company manufactures devices designed to prevent accidental needle pricks. Its products include UltraSafe Needle Guards, which attach to prefilled glass syringes, and UltraSafe Passive Needle Guards, in which a safety guard locks into place upon completion of shot delivery. The firm develops products used for medical and dental drug delivery, vaccines, blood collection, and intravenous catheters. Customers for pre-filled syringes include Amgen , Merck , Roche , and Pfizer . Founded in 1990, the firm primarily operates in North America, Europe, and Asia. Safety Syringes was acquired by Becton, Dickinson in 2012.
