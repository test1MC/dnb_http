Witham Contract Manufacturing (formerly William Ransom & Son) is doing its best to help people grow old naturally and healthily. As one of the oldest pharmaceutical companies in the UK, it aims to lead the natural-product health care industry. Its nutritional supplements and body care products are sold under such brands as Allergenics, AloeDent, Jointflex, Kordel's, and Ransom Naturals. The company's consumer products are distributed through both independent health foods retailers and mass market grocery chains. Witham also manufactures botanical extracts for use as active pharmaceutical ingredients (API's) as well as additives for food and beverages.

Witham's range of products includes active healthcare ingredients (APIs), excipients, over-the-counter medicines and products used to deliver flavor and functionality in food and drinks.

The company is a developer and manufacturer of natural products and extracts to the healthcare, cosmetic and food and drinks industries.
