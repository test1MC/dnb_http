Von Roll has rolled out a new business segment -- Von Roll Elco Transformers -- and added it to its existing portfolio which includes Von Roll Insulation and Von Roll Composites. Its insulation products are used in generators, motors, switchgears, and transformers. Its composite materials are used in industries ranging from paper production to household appliance manufacturing, as well as ballistic protection, electricity distribution, cable protection, and transportation. The company has supplied its wares to customers including ABB , Alstom , Emerson Electric , General Electric , and Toshiba . Von Roll is represented in 19 countries, and staffs over 3,000 employees at 32 sites.
