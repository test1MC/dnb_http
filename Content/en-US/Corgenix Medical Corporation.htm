Corgenix Medical wants to take a peek inside you. The company makes in vitro diagnostics to detect autoimmune, liver, and vascular diseases. Its line of more than 50 diagnostics are used by reference labs, hospitals and clinics, researchers, and other medical facilities around the world. It sells directly to customers in the US and the UK and through independent distributors elsewhere. To expand its product line, Corgenix Medical has released an aspirin resistance diagnostic, and it is developing new tests to diagnose fibromyalgia (pain disorder) and cardiovascular disease. It is also developing tests that detect potential bioterrorism agents using grant funding from the National Institutes of Health .
