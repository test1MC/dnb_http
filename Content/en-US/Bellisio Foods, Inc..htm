Bellisio Foods believes that microwave ovens are beautiful inventions. It's a leading maker of more than 200 frozen and ready-to-heat meals sold mostly under the Michelina's and Boston Market brands but also Lean Gourmet and Budget Gourmet. Products include single-serving pasta-and-sauce dishes and other premium Italian items, as well as Asian and Mexican food varieties. Frozen foods are sold through supermarkets and other retail grocers in North America, as well as in nearly a dozen international markets. Bellisio Foods makes items under the Arden Kitchens, Fusion Culinary Center, and Howlin' Coyote labels for foodservice customers. The company was founded in 1990 by Jeno Paulucci. In late 2016, Bangkok-based food conglomerate Charoen Pokphand Foods acquired Bellisio from Centre Partners for $1 billion.

Already established in Canada and Australia, Minnesota-based Bellisio Foods distributes food products to nearly a dozen international markets across China, India, and Russia.

Bellisio Foods makes its frozen and ready-to-heat meals to sell through supermarkets across North America and in select international markets. As part of its business, it produces food items under private labels for retailers, consumer packaged goods companies, and foodservice customers.

Customers of its Overhill Farms unit include Jenny Craig , Safeway , Panda , Pinnacle Foods , and American Airlines .

The food firm operates three Minnesota facilities in the cities of Minneapolis (where it's headquartered), Lakeville, and Duluth. Bellisio Foods also maintains a primary manufacturing plant in Jackson, Ohio.

Its foodservice divisions supply convenience food products to such restaurant chains as Applebee's and Boston Market . On average Bellisio Foods makes 2 million meals at its primary plant and 100 tons of pizza rolls per day.

In recent years Bellisio Foods has expanded its business abroad and rolled out a new brand of frozen foods. It launched a new line of frozen foods under the name Joy of Cooking, licensed from the family of famed cookbook author Irma S. Rombauer.

To significantly boost its portfolio of brands and gain a West Coast manufacturing presence, Bellisio Foods in 2013 acquired fellow frozen foods maker Overhill Farms for $80.9 million. Overhill enjoys lucrative partnerships with Panda Restaurant Group , Boston Market Corporation , Safeway , Jenny Craig , Target Corporation , Pinnacle Foods Group , and American Airlines . As part of the purchase, Bellisio Foods owns the exclusive license to make the Boston Market brand of frozen food products. Also in 2013, Bellisio Foods took advantage of the opportunity to acquire Austin, Texas-based Austin Packaging Co. in a deal valued at more than $6 million.

The company is named after Paulucci's familial home in Bellisio Solfare, Italy. Paulucci has long been associated with convenience foods: He originally invented Jeno's pizza rolls, a brand now owned by General Mills . The company changed its name from Michelina's in 2007; the rebranding resulted from the growth of its product lines and its expansion into serving foodservice customers.
