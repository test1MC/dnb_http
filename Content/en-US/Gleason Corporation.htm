Gleason Corporation designs, manufactures, and sells cutting tools and metalworking machines that produce gears used in drive shafts. Gleason sells to makers of air and marine craft, buses, cars, RVs, SUVs, and trucks, as well as agricultural and construction machinery and roller coasters. The company also provides replacement parts for the aftermarket, engineering support, and inspection software and training programs for its customers. Gleason has manufacturing facilities spread throughout Europe, the Asia/Pacific, and the US. It is privately owned by members of the Gleason family and the Gleason Foundation.

Gleason's products include machinery for the production, finishing and testing of gears. Its services include a worldwide support system which provides cutting tools, workholding, replacement parts, field service, application development services, gear design and inspection software, training programs, engineering support, and machine rebuild and upgrade services.

Gleason has manufacturing facilities in Brazil, China, Germany, India, Japan, Switzerland, and the US. It also has technical centers and sales and service offices around the world and has sales and service offices throughout North and South America, Europe and in the Asia-Pacific region.

Gleason caters principally to the automotive sector but also serves the aviation, farm machinery production, aerospace, energy, mining, wind power, construction, electric vehicle, power tool, and shipbuilding industries.

To attract additional customers, Gleason launched its mobile app in early 2016. The new service app allows customers to submit service contact forms, access 24/7 service support contact information, and browse its complete product portfolio, among other functions.
