If your afternoon snack attack is acting up and you're looking for something a little different than the same old same old the company vending machines have to offer, turn to AMES International. The company, also known as Emily's Chocolates, manufactures and distributes gourmet chocolates, cookies, nuts, trail mixes, and other snack foods. Founded in 1987, Emily's products are distributed throughout the US in club, grocery, drug and other retail outlets, including Whole Foods , Kroger , Walgreen's , Macy's , Wal-Mart , and World Market , as well as through the company's Web site.
