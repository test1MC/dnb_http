maxingvest ag holds coffee roasting, consumer merchandise, and skin-care products close to its chest. Through its Tchibo operations, the company sells coffee in bakeries and food stores in Europe. The name Tchibo is derived from a combination of the name of co-founder Carl Tchiling and the word 'bohne', German for 'bean'). Tchibo also operates coffee shops that sell related accessories, housewares, clothing, and furnishings. maxingvest additionally owns a majority (slightly more than 50%) of Beiersdorf , maker of NIVEA, Eucerin, and La Prairie-branded skin-care products. The company was founded in 1949 and remains family-owned. The maxingvest name is a reference to co-founding couple Max and Ingeburg Herz.
