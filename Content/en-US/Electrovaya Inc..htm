Electrovaya manufactures and distributes power-related products, including rechargeable lithium-ion batteries, battery systems, and battery-related products for the clean transportation, consumer, health care, mobile computing, and smart grid power markets. It partners with companies around the world on product development. The company also designs and manufactures power sources for laptop computers (PowerPad) and tablet PCs (Scribbler), along with docking stations, keyboards, and adapters. Electrovaya sells its products in North America through resellers ( CDW and Tiger Direct ) and distributors ( Ingram Micro ). It gets more than half of its sales outside North America. The company was founded in 1996.
