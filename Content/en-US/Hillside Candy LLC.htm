Guilt or guilt free. Whatever way you choose your sweets, this company has the answer. Hillside Candy makes sugar-free and organic candy under the Golightly and GoNatural brand names. Its regular candy is made and sold under the Hillside Sweets name. The company's more than 100 sugar-free and regular confections (in more than 30 flavors) include hard candies, creme crunchies, chewies, mints, toffees, chocolates, and cough suppressants and are available some 20 countries worldwide. Hillside Candy also does contract and private-label manufacturing.
