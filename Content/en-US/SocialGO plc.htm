There's no stopping this British social networking service provider. SocialGO develops software that helps businesses, entrepreneurs, and groups create customized, professional-grade social networks. The service primarily targets offline networks (including religious, political, business, and social organizations) who can benefit from taking their members online in order to communicate, coordinate activities, and share information. The SocialGO platform hosts more than 200,000 sites serving a total of 3.5 million users per month. Clients include Levi's , Hilton , and Wal-Mart . SocialGO has offices in London and San Francisco. The company was founded by Alex Halliday, Steve Hardman, and Dominic Wheatley in 2007.
