Fair Oaks Farms produces a variety of fresh, ready-to-cook, and fully cooked beef, pork, and poultry products. The company distributes to restaurants and foodservice operators. Custom specialties include bacon bits, chicken strips, fully-cooked beef patties, hot dogs, meat loaf, pork ribettes, Salisbury steak, and smoked sausage. Along with its own 55,000-sq.-ft. plant in Pleasant Prairie, Wisconsin, Fair Oaks has access to a worldwide network of more than 70 manufacturing facilities through its partnership with custom food giant OSI . Fair Oaks was founded in 1985.
