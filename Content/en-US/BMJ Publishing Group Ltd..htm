Even after physicians survive the rigors of medical school, they can't necessarily put out the midnight oil. BMJ Publishing Group helps doctors and medical students keep up with the latest medical research through its international medical journals. Its flagship title, BMJ (formerly known as the British Medical Journal ), was founded in 1840 and is one of the top medical journals in the UK. BMJ publishes original scientific studies, as well as other articles, papers, and reviews. It targets general practicioners, clinical researchers, and international readers. The journal also publishes an online edition, with some information available for subscribers only. BMJ is a part of the British Medical Association.
