Gaspar's Sausage Company goes far (for its recipes). The company manufactures and sells Portuguese-style sausage in the US. Specifically, the company sells chourico -- a hot spiced sausage and linguica -- a mild smoked sausage. Linguica is made from pork or turkey. Chourico is made only from pork. Both varieties are made into sausages, franks, or patties. The company's products are found in supermarket chains throughout New England and are available to the rest of the US through mail-order and over the Internet. Gaspar's also operates a retail store at its corporate headquarters.
