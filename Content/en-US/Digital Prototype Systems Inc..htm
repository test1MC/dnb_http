Digital Prototype Systems can help you keep an eye on all of your assets. The company, doing business as DPS Telecom, designs and manufactures network monitoring and security devices for the cable television, telecommunications, and gas/electric utility industries, along with transit agencies and manufacturers. Its products also include alarm systems that can be deployed across a variety of network assets. The company counts AT&T , Qwest Communications , Sprint Nextel , and Verizon among its customers. DPS Telecom was established in 1986.

DPS offers combinations of master alarm management system and remote telemetry units (RTUs) that provide end-to-end, single-vendor network monitoring. It specializes in custom supervisory control and data acquisition.

The company's customers are in the telecommunications, manufacturing, railroads, and utilities markets. Customers include Adelphia Communications , Caprock Communications, Consolidated Communications , and Verizon .
