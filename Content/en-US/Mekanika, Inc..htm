Mekanika specializes in making devices for the treatment of spinal instability. The company's restablilization system is used for treating degenerative joint disease, as well as trauma to the spine joint. Mekanika was founded by Chairman and Chief Science Officer David Holmes. The company has an alliance with the University of Miami School of Medicine.
