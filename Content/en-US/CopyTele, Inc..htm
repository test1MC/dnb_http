CopyTele has an original take on display and communications technology. The company licenses technology used in thin, low-voltage phosphor displays. A licensing agreement with Videocon Industries allows the India-based consumer electronics company to develop televisions utilizing CopyTele's technology. CopyTele also provides secure communications products. Its stand-alone devices provide encryption for secure voice, fax, and data communication. Boeing , the exclusive distributor for many of the company's security products, uses CopyTele's encryption products on the Thuraya satellite communications network, which is employed by the US military.
