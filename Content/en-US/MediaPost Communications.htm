This company's got something to post about media. MediaPost Communications provides online resources for media, marketing and advertising professionals. Its Mediapost.com site offers free tools, news, electronic newsletters, and directories to help companies plan and buy both traditional and online advertising. Its Media Post Directories include more than 150,000 media and agency contacts, as well as resource links, event listings, and news and classifieds. MediaPost also produces events ( Agency of the Year Awards ) and publishes industry magazines ( MEDIA Magazine and OMMA Magazine ) and newsletters ( MediaDailyNews ). The company was founded in 1996 by Kenneth Fadner, creator of AdWeek magazine.
