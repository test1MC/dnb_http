Etrema Products Inc. (EPI) is into heavy metal and good vibrations. The company's products (including actuators, audio products, and ultrasonic systems) are based around its Terfenol-D metal. EPI's commercial product, Whispering Windows, is a small disc that uses a glass surface to act as a speaker. For the US Department of the Navy , EPI has begun to develop Galfenol, a new smart-materials technology that the firm boasts has the potential ability to generate electricity through the vibrations of a ship's hull during normal operations. The company, a subsidiary of Edge Technologies, was created in 1987 for the purpose of commercializing the Terfenol-D metal.
