Tokyo-based Meidensha, established in 1897, is the industrial arm of Sumitomo Group . Through key operating subsidiaries, Meidensha does business in Japan, China, Hong Kong, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, the US, and the UK. The company groups its products into several categories including Energy, Environment, Construction, Total Engineering, Information and Communication, Maintenance, and Industrial Systems. The company's devices and systems primarily address the needs of the utility, construction, metal, and transportation industries worldwide. Meidensha operates through five business segments: Social Infrastructure Systems, Industrial Systems, Engineering Systems, Real Estate, and Other.
