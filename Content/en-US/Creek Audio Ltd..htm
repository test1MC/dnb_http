Creek Audio helps make the music flow smoothly -- and at whatever volume you choose. Creek Audio designs and markets high-end stereo equipment, including CD players, integrated amplifiers, and tuners. It also supplies phono preamps, headphone amplifiers, and remote control handsets. The company sells its products in more than 50 countries through a network of distributors, as well as traditional and online retailers.
