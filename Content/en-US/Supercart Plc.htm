If a supermarket cart dings your car, it's not a Supercart! The company designs and markets full- and mid-size shopping carts (or trolleys) with all-plastic construction, as well as all-metal and hybrid (plastic and metal) versions. The all-plastic and hybrid products reportedly reduce damage to store fixtures and vehicles. Its full lineup is recyclable and easy to repair and use. Supercart also makes hand-held shopping baskets. In addition to grocers, customers include such retailers as Toys R Us . Supercart operates in Europe, North America, and South Africa, where it was founded in 1993 by Mike Wolfe. In 2009 Supercart expanded its US arm Plasti-Cart by acquiring the assets of cart-maker Rehrig International.
