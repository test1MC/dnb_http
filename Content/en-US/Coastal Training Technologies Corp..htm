The name of this company's game is training employees from coast to coast. Coastal Training Technologies is a publisher of employee training products. The company publishes materials covering more than 300 training topics in the fields of healthcare, human resources, industrial skills, and safety and environmental issues. Formats include videotape, handbooks, posters, leader guides, CD-ROMs, DVDs, Web-based e-learning, digital video files, and instructor-led courses. Coastal also provides school safety training programs for grades K-12, as well as training programs for US government employees. A global operation, Coastal has production and sales offices in Canada, Mexico, Brazil, Europe, India, and Australia.
