Cantor Entertainment Technology is willing to bet that high-rollers wouldn't mind replacing that slot machine with a tablet computer. Cantor Gaming supplies a handful of Las Vegas resorts with handheld touchscreen computers that offer race and sports books and other mobile casino games; players rent the computers from the casino. In addition, it launched a sports wagering app for Android phones that can be used anywhere in Nevada and is developing apps for Apple and Windows phones.
