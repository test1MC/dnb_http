Suominen works behind the scenes to keep things neat and tidy. The company is the #1 producer of commercial nonwoven wiping fabrics. Its Wipes unit includes wet wipes (Codi Wipes) sheets sold to major consumer products firms and nonwoven fabric rolls used by makers of hygiene, health care, and cleaning products. Flexibles produces plastic film packaging for food, hygiene, retail, and security items including tamper-proof bags for money and documents. Suominen has factories in Brazil, Italy, Spain, and the US and counts Europe and North America as key markets. Paper product maker Ahlstrom owns about a third of Suominen after selling its wipes fabrics business to its fellow Finn in 2011.
