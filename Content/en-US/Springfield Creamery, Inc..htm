If you fancy Nancy's, then chances are that Springfield Creamery has a hold on you. The company makes and markets organic cultured dairy and soy products made with fruit from local farms and natural sweeteners, under the Nancy's brand name. (Springfield Creamery's first bookkeeper, Nancy Van Brasch Hamren, knew how to make yogurt, hence the Nancy's moniker). The company sells its natural yogurt, cottage cheese, kefir, sour cream, cream cheese, and other products through natural foods retailers such as Whole Foods and Wild Oats nationwide. President Chuck Kesey (brother of counterculture icon and author Ken) and his wife Sue started the family-run company in 1960.
