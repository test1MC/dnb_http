Design Studio has designs on globalization. The company is a furniture and fixture manufacturer for the retail, commercial, and home markets, and primarily produces paneling products (cabinets, doors, wardrobes) and kitchen fixtures (sinks, counters, lighting). Expansion sits firmly on Design Studio's horizon, with investments plotted in plants and new technology as well as an international growth scheme. The company plans to focus on increasing the export of its PANELZ brand of panelling products overseas and distribute a wider range of high-end furniture brands.
