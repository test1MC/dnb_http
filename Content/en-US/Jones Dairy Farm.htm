The links on this company's Web site are of the edible variety. Jones Dairy Farm produces breakfast sausages and patties, bacon, ham, and sliced deli meats. The company makes both cooked and uncooked meat products for retail food outlets under the Jones Dairy Farm brand name. For the foodservice trade, Jones Dairy Farm provides bacon, breakfast sandwiches, sausages, and smoked meats. The company also operates a retail meat outlet in its hometown of Fort Atkinson, Wisconsin. In addition it exports its meat products to Canada, Mexico, the Caribbean, Japan, Hong Kong, and Korea. Founded in 1889, the company is still family owned and operated.
