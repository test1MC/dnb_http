SIN Holdings owns and operates Senior-Inet.com (The Senior Information Network), a Web portal for senior citizens throughout the US seeking information about support services for senior citizens in ten cities in Colorado, as well as for the City of Houston. Information categories include health, housing, senior centers, travel services, rehabilitation, Alzheimer's, hospice and adult day care, and funeral services. James Vandeberg, an attorney from Seattle, acquired controlling interest in SIN Holdings from founder and former company president Steve Sinohui.
