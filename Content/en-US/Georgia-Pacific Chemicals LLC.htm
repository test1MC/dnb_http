From one corner of the US all the way to the other, Georgia-Pacific Chemicals manufactures adhesives resins and wood chemicals for its corporate parent, paper and building products giant Georgia-Pacific . The wholly owned subsidiary functions as the chemical division of the parent, manufacturing adhesives, thermoset resins, and wood and paper chemicals; it also manufactures formaldehyde. Georgia-Pacific maintains about about locations, primarily manufacturing facilities, worldwide. It ranks, along with Hexion , among the world's largest thermoset and forest product resins makers.
