China Shoto keeps the power on. The company makes rechargable lead-acid backup batteries that are sold primarily to telecommunications service providers in China -- China Mobile , China Telecom , and China Unicom are all customers -- and to telecom equipment makers including Huawei Technology and ZTE . Its backup batteries are also used in electric power, energy storage, medical equipment, railway, and shipping applications. Most of China Shoto's products are VRLA (valve regulated lead-acid) batteries, which are touted as being virtually maintenance free. While backup batteries make up about 90% of China Shoto's sales, the company also makes power-type batteries for use in electric bicycles.
