Telestone Technologies lays the groundwork for a pathway to effective wireless communications. The company provides integrated wireless network services including project design and management, research and development (R&D), and wireless communications equipment such as antennas, repeaters, and other peripherals. Telestone was established in 1997 as the primary infrastructure provider to China Unicom and China Mobile , which collectively account for more than 70% of Telestone's sales. Chairman Han Daqing controls more than one third of the company's voting stock.
