William A. Lewis Engineering helps customers handle their loads. The company manufactures multi-lift vehicle load-handling systems, diesel engine components and assemblies, and military vehicle sub-assemblies and components. Using such fabrication equipment as CNC press brakes, fly presses, guillotines, saws, and welding plants, the company makes parts from armor plate, plastic, rubber, steel, and wood. It also has laser cutting, painting, and finishing capabilities. William A. Lewis' customers have included such heavy hitters as BAE Systems , Caterpillar , General Dynamics United Kingdom , and Perkins Engines. The company was founded in 1944 as an automotive body panel manufacturer.

The company operates through its manufacturing headquarters in Shrewsbury, UK, which spreads over five acres and acts as a 40,000 sq. ft. factory.

William A. Lewis specializes in the following areas and industry categories: offshore, onshore, specialist vehicle launch and recovery systems, components for buoyancy systems, general purpose and specialist machinery, transport and marine, renewable energy (wave and tidal).

To make its products and parts, the company utilizes a wide array of processes including fabrication, machining, inspecting, painting and finishing, laser cutting, and water jet cutting.
