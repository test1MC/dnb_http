A quarter-pound hamburger might fill you up at lunchtime, but it's just a drop in the bucket for Meat Commodities -- the company trades more than 4 million pounds of beef and pork in an average week. Meat Commodities also serves as a representative for North Carolina-based slaughterhouse Martins Abbatoir and Wholesale Meat. In addition to its trading and brokerage operations, Meats Commodities offers transportation services to its customers and sells meat products under the Haywood & Yancey brand. Company president Alan Silverberg and VP Anthony Adams own Meat Commodities, which was founded in 1974.
