Moreover Technologies puts millions of news articles and social media posts at your fingertips each day -- literally. The company's service gathers data from a wide range of leading news, corporate, and industry sources and delivers real-time headlines to Web sites, search engines, corporate intranets, and enterprise portals. Headlines, which are updated in near real-time, link to full-text articles on the source sites. Moreover indexes articles from over 150 countries across more than 75 languages. The company generates revenue through enterprise sales and licensing its technology.
