NTN Bearing Corporation of America lets the good times roll in the U.S. of A. The company, a subsidiary of Japan's NTN Corp. , manufactures bearings and constant-velocity joints used in an array of general industrial machinery and automotive applications. It has about a dozen plants in the US and Canada. Among thousands of types, NTN products include angular contact ball bearings, disc bearings, clutch products, radial ball bearings, and mounted or adapted bearings. NTN Bearing is part of NTN Corp.'s Americas business segment. Established in 1963, the firm has counted BAE Systems Land & Armaments , Hitachi Automotive Products (USA) , Honda Motor 's Formula One racing team, and John Deere as customers.

NTN America has about a dozen plants in the US and Canada. More than 80% of NTN's US sales are from domestically made products.

NTN America targets customers in the wind energy, construction, agriculture, automotive, heavy duty truck, aerospace, mining, metal manufacturing, machine tool, rail and transit, and food processing sectors. Its parent company has 6,000 NTN authorized dealers spanning the Americas.

Going forward, as the automotive sector slowly gets back to normal, the Americas business segment is banking on sustained demand for its constant-velocity joints and anti-friction bearings. Over the years NTN has signaled a ramp up its production capabilities in the US to meet automotive demand for hub bearings.
