For beer lovers, nothing may taste sweeter than victory. Its brewmasters having trained in Germany, Victory Brewing Company makes European-style craft beers. The company's colorfully named brands -- among them Hop Ranch, Golden Monkey, Moonglow Weizenbock, Mad King's Weiss, and Old Horizontal -- are also sold in taverns and retail outlets in its home state, Pennsylvania, as well as in about a 34 other states, and exported to international markets. It also operates three brewpubs that serve up sandwiches, salads, pizza -- and Victory's beers. Boyhood friends Bill Covaleski and Ron Barchet founded the company in 1996.
