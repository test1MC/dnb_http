When golf gear is trendy, Sport-Haley makes the green. The company designs classically styled, premium-priced women's and men's golf apparel and outerwear under the Sport-Haley label (for women) and Ben Hogan brand name (for men). (The firm distributes Ben Hogan apparel under a license from Callaway Golf .) Garments include jackets, pants, shirts, sweaters, and the Elements line of outerwear. Sport-Haley products are mostly sold at pro shops, country clubs, department stores, and resorts in the US, and often are custom-embroidered for tournaments and other firms. The company also sells apparel online.
