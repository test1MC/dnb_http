Alemite Corporation manufactures lubricating and fluid pumping systems for industrial, automotive, and aerospace and defense (A&D) use. Its products include lubrication fittings, diaphram pumps, hose reels, compressed air components, control valves, grease flow meters, battery-powered grease guns, automatic lubrication systems, and related accessories. The company also offers used fluid handling equipment for sale, and it provides installation and technical support services. Alemite Corporation, which was established in 1918, sells its products directly and through an international network of distributors. Early in 2006 Sentinel Capital Partners sold Alemite to Harbour Group .
