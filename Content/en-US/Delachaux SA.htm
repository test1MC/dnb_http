Delachaux has more than a one-track mind; its Railtech International Group manufactures track laying equipment and elastic fastener and electrification systems. Its Conductic division makes production equipment for cable and fiber-optic sectors, and conductor bars, reeling drums, and slip-rings for the transportation industry. Its Metal division produces pure chromium metal to make super-performance alloys. Its Special Steel unit manufactures rough-cast pieces and machine-finished parts. Financière Danube Sàrl, a company controlled by CVC Capital , and a Delachaux family member acquired a majority stake (more than 63%) in Delachaux in late 2011 and took it private.
