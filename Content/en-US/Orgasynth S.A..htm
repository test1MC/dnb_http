Chemicals maker Orgasynth proves the organic and the synthetic don't have to be mutually exclusive. The company specializes in the synthesis of organic molecular chemicals. Its products include specialty chemicals like flavorings, fragrances, and essential oils. Orgasynth sells its products primarily to European clients in a range of industries, including pharmaceuticals, electronics, food, and animal nutrition. In 2007 it sold its fine chemicals and dyes unit to private investment group Argos Soditic, which set it up as a separate company called Axyntis. Chairman and CEO Emmanuel Alves, a former executive of Rhône-Poulenc (now Sanofi-Aventis after successive mergers), and his family control 74% of Orgasynth.
