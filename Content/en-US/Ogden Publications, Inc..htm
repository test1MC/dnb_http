Ogden Publications caters to a farming crowd. The company publishes magazines and books for readers interested in topics such as sustainability, self sufficiency, and farm memorabilia. Titles include Mother Earth News , Farm Collector , and Herb Companion . It also publishes the Utne Reader, a magazine that compiles articles from non-mainstream media sources and offers original writing. In addition, the company provides custom pushing services through its Ogden Custom Media & Fulfillment Group. Ogden Publications is controlled by the Nutting family, which also owns Ogden Newspapers , a publisher with more than 40 daily papers in about a dozen states.
