Metals parts, assemblies, tools, and dies anchor Anchor Manufacturing Group's bottom line. The company manufactures metal stampings, assemblies, and steering column products (primarily for the auto industry). Anchor Manufacturing's in-house capabilities include manufacturing services such as welding and assembly, laser cutting, heat treating, sawing, shearing, metal stamping (up to 2,000 tons), tool and die (and repair), powder coating, machining, and metal fabricating. Customers have included such noteworthy car makers as Daimler , Chrysler , and Honda .

Anchor Manufacturing operates 340,000 sq. ft. of manufacturing space on 20 acres near the Cleveland Hopkins Airport.

The company's management system is certified to ISO/TS 16949:2002 standards, which specifies the quality system requirements for the design/development, production, installation and servicing of automotive related products. The manufacturer and supplier has been given quality performance awards by both DaimlerChrysler and Honda for quality and cost-saving proposals.

Anchor Manufacturing was founded in 1970 by Edward Pfaff.
