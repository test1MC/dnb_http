You might say this company has added a little Seoul to the Internet. Daum Communications is the leading operator of Korean-language websites, including flagship news and information portal Daum.net. It also offers a plethora of Internet services including search (Daum Search, Social Pick, Twitter Search), maps and traffic reporting (Daum Map), community (Daum Cafe), e-mail and messaging (Daum Mail, My People), news updates (Media Daum), and games (Daum Games). Its mobile platform (Mobile Daum) offers some 40 services optimized for the mobile experience. Launched in 1995, Daum is Korean for both "next" and "variety of sounds."
