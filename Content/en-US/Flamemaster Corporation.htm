Chemicals are a hot topic at Flamemaster, maker of flame-retardant and high heat-resistant coatings and sealants, mostly for the aerospace and defense industries. The firm's fire-retardant coatings prevent the spread of fire along electrical cables, while its heat-resistant coatings are used to protect surfaces near missile launches. Its sealants are used in the seams of aircraft fuel tanks.
