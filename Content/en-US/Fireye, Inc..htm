Fireye keeps an eye out for fire hazards. The company manufactures flame safeguard and combustion controls systems for commercial and industrial applications, including heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC); power generation; petrochemical refining; pulp and paper; and the industrial and process heating industries. Its products are used in schools, factories, petrochemical plants, power utilities, and other large buildings. The company operates about 20 regional sales offices worldwide. Fireye is a division of UTC Climate Controls & Security , a global provider of fire safety and security products and services.
