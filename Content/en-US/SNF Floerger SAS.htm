SNF Floerger is the world's leading manufacturer of synthetic flocculants, laying claim to 38% of the global market. Synthetic flocculants are used in water treatment -- they clarify water by combining with suspended solids. SNF Floerger also makes other products, including superabsorbants and thickeners. The company has 37 subsidiaries and operations in more than 130 countries around the world, including SNF Inc. in the US, France, South Korea, and China. It has 22 plants worldwide that serve some 30,000 customers, who in turn serve more than 250,000 end users.

SNF Floerger has sales offices in Europe, Africa and the Middle East, Asia, North and South America, and Oceania.

The company supports its products though 37 subsidiaries that provide local service and technical assistance.

SNF Floerger serves a range of industries, including Water Treatment, Oil and Gas, Paper, Mining, Agriculture, Textile, Cosmetics, and Construction.

The company follows a long-term organic development strategy based on international expansion by working with companies with large regional manufacturing units; a local service approach (directly or via partners); sustained investment in new product development; employee training; and the continued improvement of SNF Floerger's environmental footprint regarding its products, applications, and production processes.
