Amkor Pacific is counting on its customers smelling and looking nice. The company operates Ravello Beverly Hills, which designs and sells a line of gold and sterling silver fine and fashion jewelry. Its early jewelry, labeled fabulous faux, featured 14k gold and manmade diamonds, emeralds, rubies and sapphires. To extend its reach, Ravello has since expanded its business to include jewelry with natural gemstones. Its products portfolio now also includes its Living Form fragrance and fashion accessories such as belts, hair clips, and purses. Amkor Pacific's products are sold online and in department stores and boutiques. The company was founded in 1989.
