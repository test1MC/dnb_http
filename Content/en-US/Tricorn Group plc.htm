Tricorn Group helps its customers put the pieces together. The company designs, manufactures, and distributes pipe and tubing products. Its Transportation division makes tubular products used in braking systems and engines within construction vehicles and trucks. Malvern Tubular Components makes tubular components for diesel engines and radiator sets within the energy sector. Tricorn supplies customers worldwide in the manufacturing, power generation, and utilities industries, among others. The UK accounts for about 50% of the company's net sales each year.

Tricorn has experienced fluctuating revenues over the last last five years. In 2015 (ended March) its revenues decreased by 28% primarily due to the selling of its aerospace business and to a decline in Transportation segment sales resulting from lower demand in the UK. The decline in 2015 was partially offset by a spike in Energy division sales from the US. In addition, Tricorn has posted straight years with a net loss due to declines from discontinued operations and additional restructuring and China start-up costs.

Throughout 2014 and 2015, Tricorn sold off its Redman Fittings (pipe jointing systems) and RMDG Aerospace (aerospace fittings) operations in order to focus solely on its Transportation and Energy divisions.
