Digital Voice Systems specializes in speech compression technology. The company manufactures electronic components, devices, and software designed to record and process digital audio for storage and transmission. The company's products include vocoder chips and interface boards as well as network devices that convert analog audio to digital formats. Digital Voice's customers use its products to enable such communications applications as digital mobile radio, satellite communications, inflight telephony and aircraft communications, and digital voice recorders. Digital Voice was founded in 1988.
