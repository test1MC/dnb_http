When the candy bell goes off in your brain, reach for Annabelle. Annabelle Candy makes such sweet treats as Rocky Road, Big Hunk, Abba-Zaba, Look!, and U-NO candy bars, all of which are available at retail outlets, primarily in the western US, and through the company's Web site. Its Rocky Road (chocolate, marshmallow, and cashews) and Big Hunk (nougat and peanuts) bars make up more than half of sales. Annabelle Candy was formed in 1950 by Russian immigrant, Sam Altshuler (and named for his daughter); the company is owned and operated by his descendants.
