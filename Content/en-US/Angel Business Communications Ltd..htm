Angel is heaven-sent for customers wanting to low-down on the semiconductor industry. Angel Business Communications publishes print and online trade magazines with a focus on industry sectors such as semiconductors and information technology. Specific topics include data centers, cloud computing, virtualization, and storage networking. Among its portfolio of titles are EuroAsia Semiconductor and DataCentre Solutions (DCS). Angel Business Services also organizes exhibitions, conferences, seminars, awards, and other corporate events. Angel Business Communications was founded as Beere Hobson Associates in 1971.

Publications include Datacentre Solutions Europe, Datacentre Solutions UK, Silicon Semiconductor, Solar + Power Management , and others.

Angel Events, the company's events division, organizes exhibitions, conferences, and seminars. Angel Online is the digital division.

Angel's software products include Angelmail, an email marketing campaign system, Angel Surveys, Angel Webinar, and PublishingNinja.

Angel is based in the UK, and has offices in Coventry and Watford.

Angel serves various types of organizations across a range of sectors, including companies, charities, not-for-profits, and individuals.

It provides marketing solutions from mobile apps and technology, to online, digital and traditional print media.
