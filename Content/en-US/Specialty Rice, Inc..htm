Specialty Rice goes beyond the mainstream in the rice industry. The company grows, processes, packages, and sells white and brown basmati, arborio (for making risotto), jasmine, and koshihikari (sushi) rice. Its products are sold under the Della Gourmet Rice brand name in the US and under the Delrose label in Canada. Specialty Rice operates 6,000 acres of farmland in Arkansas. To promote the use of its exotic rice products, the company is offering cooking courses at select supermarkets throughout the US. Specialty Rice is owned and operated by the Ajmera family.
