Some media are accused of tilting to the left or to the right, but some like to stick strictly to the middle, the middle of the UK, that is. The Midland News Association (MNA) is a regional newspaper publisher. Its titles include the Express & Star (Wolverhampton), and the Shropshire Star evening newspapers, as well as weekly papers (free and paid) in the Herefordshire, mid-Wales, Shropshire, and West Midlands regions. MNA, which is known for its newspaper survey covering the Midlands, has also branched out into radio and online (including mobile) media.

The company's portfolio includes print, online (including mobile), and on air media. It provides two daily newspapers, the E xpress & Star and the Shropshire Star , 15 weekly newspapers, two magazines, two websites, and MNA Digital (a mobile update service). The Express & Star sells more than 51,000 copies per night.

The Express & Star was founded in 1874 and currently boasts the largest regional evening sale in the UK. The Shropshire Star was launched in 1964 and has become one of the UK's most successful newspapers.
