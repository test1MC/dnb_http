Internet software and telecom solutions firm hereUare (formerly PeopleNet International Corporation) offers a suite of Web services that include searching, online classified ads, group and e-mail messaging software, voice over IP (VoIP) telephony, and social-networking portals. Throughout 2008, hereUare was primarily developing products and was not generating revenues. The company's search engine and classified advertising products remain in the Beta testing phase, while hereUare is marketing its e-messaging and groupware products to small and medium-sized businesses. It has an engineering and operations center in Vietnam.
