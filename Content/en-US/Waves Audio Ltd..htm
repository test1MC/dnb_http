Waves customers are adept at catching waves-- sound waves, that is. Waves Audio makes digital signal processing (DSP) systems and software for professional audio processing; its products are used by professional sound mixers and masterers, as well as OEMs. Waves also produces limiters, equalizers, compressors, effects, and noise reduction tools for use in music, movie soundtracks, and computer sound simulation. It also creates special technological processors that allow users to hear sounds otherwise out of the range of human perception of sound. The company was founded in 1992.
