Fordyce Picture Frame Company manufactures private-label and custom wooden frames for photos, photo collages, and certificates. Its frames are made from several species of wood, including basswood, red oak, southern yellow pine, tulipwood poplar, white ash, and white oak. The company also offers framed art, and it makes molding and millwork for stairways. Founded in 1963 by Dewey Barnes, the company is still owned and operated by the Barnes family.
