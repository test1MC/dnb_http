Even if you've got an old ranch, Nufarm would like to help you kill some weeds in the name of protecting your crops. Nufarm manufactures herbicides and pesticides and controls the marketing rights to Monsanto 's Roundup brand of herbicides for Australia and New Zealand. The company also has a growing specialist seeds subsidiary called Nuseed. While the Asia/Pacific region is Nufarm's biggest market, it sells products in more than 100 countries worldwide and continues to expand internationally. Since 2007 the company has made significant acquisitions in Brazil, the UK, and the US. In 2010 Japanese chemical giant Sumitomo bought a 20% stake in Nufarm. The company is reorganizing its business in Australia.
