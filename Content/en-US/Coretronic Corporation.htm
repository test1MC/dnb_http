Coretronic's core business lies in displays and projectors. The Taiwanese company is a leading manufacturer of DLP (digital light processing) projectors and liquid crystal display (LCD) backlight modules. Coretronic also makes LCD monitors for televisions and computers, and video products such as DLP rear-projection display TVs and plasma display panel TVs. Founded in 1992, the company boasts manufacturing facilities throughout Taiwan as well as in China. It has begun shifting more of its research and development activities to Taiwan while maintaining manufacturing in China.
