In the age of electricity deregulation and increasing competition, Aclara wants to be the link between the utility and the user. The company's electronic wireless metering systems (STAR Network) and software are tailored to suit energy companies around the world. Its products enable utilities providers to automate service delivery, metering, marketing, and customer communications functions. Aclara's tools also allow utility customers to access their accounts. The company works with electric and gas companies, wholesalers, distribution operators, and others in the energy business. Clients have included Pacific Gas & Electric . Founded in 1997, Aclara was acquired by diversified manufacturer ESCO Technologies in 2006.
