American Eagle Wheel takes flight when the rubber meets the road. The company makes aluminum aftermarket car and truck wheels and products under the BOSS, Eagle, MSR, Rhino, USA Forged, and VooDoo brands. The company helped develop counter-pressure casting, and it uses precision CNC (computed numerically controlled) machine tools for making its wheels. It also has its own coating facilities to create finishes. Operating a factory in California and another in South Carolina, the company additionally makes wheel accessories, such as caps and lugs. American Eagle Wheel distributes its products through wholesale outlets in the US, Canada, Mexico, and the Caribbean. American Eagle Wheel is a family-owned company.
