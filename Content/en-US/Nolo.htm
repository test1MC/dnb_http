Nolo helps people take the law into their own hands. The company publishes books, software, and online content providing self-help legal information for consumers and small businesses outside of the legal profession. It aims to help people handle their own everyday legal matters -- or learn enough about them to make working with a lawyer a better experience. Nolo also targets small law firms looking to build their practice. The company covers areas of law such as business and human resources; patents, copyright, and art; wills and estate planning; property and money; family law and immigration; and rights and disputes. Website owner Internet Brands acquired Nolo in 2011.
