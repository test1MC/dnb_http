Stephens Media is betting on a need for news. The diversified media company is the publisher of the Las Vegas Review-Journal , boasting a circulation of more than 165,000 in and around Sin City, as well as several neighborhood and specialty papers serving the Vegas market. In total, Stephens Media publishes about a dozen daily and more than 60 weekly newspapers in 10 states and operates three news bureaus. The company also runs websites for many of its publications, as well as portal sites such as LasVegas.com, Hawaii.com, and CasinoGaming.com. Founded by the late Donald Reynolds in 1940, Stephens Media Group is owned by the Stephens Group, an Arkansas-based investment firm.

The company's Review-Journal shares the Vegas market with the smaller Las Vegas Sun , published through a joint operating agreement with Las Vegas Sun, Inc. The Sun is included as an insert in the daily edition of the Review-Journal , which handles advertising and circulation for both papers.

Stephens Media's newspapers are published in Arkansas, Hawaii, Iowa, Missouri, Nevada, North Carolina, Oklahoma, Tennessee, Texas, and Washington. It has news bureaus in Carson City, Nevada; Little Rock, Arkansas; and Washington, DC.

The company has expanded through acquisitions. In late 2012 it purchased the Story City Herald , which serves Story City, Iowa.
