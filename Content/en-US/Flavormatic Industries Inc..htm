Flavormatic Industries has more than flavor in mind when it turns on its mixing machines. The company makes and distributes flavorings, juice blends, essential oils, and fragrances. Its dry and liquid flavorings are used by the food, beverage, and pharmaceutical industries. Flavormatic's essential oils and fragrances are used in cosmetics and cleaners. The firm also makes custom flavored-drink bases for the beverage and dairy sectors. Its custom-formulated products are sold through sales agents in the UK, France, US, Puerto Rico, Japan, and Canada. Flavormatic Industries is a family-owned firm founded in the 1960s.
