You might say this company has a sense of local involvement in the news. American Community Newspapers II (ACN II) is a leading publisher of weekly and niche papers with more than 85 titles reaching audiences in states such as Minnesota, Ohio, Texas, and Virginia. The company's papers serve small communities and suburban areas surrounding such markets as Dallas, Minneapolis, and Washington, DC. Its portfolio of niche publications includes The Other Paper (Columbus, Ohio) and Loudoun Magazine (Virginia). ACN II was formed in 2009 to acquire the assets of its predecessor American Community Newspapers.
