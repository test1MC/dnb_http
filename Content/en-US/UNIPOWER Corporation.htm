Feel the power! Safely grounded, of course. Unipower manufactures switching power supplies. Unipower's converters, inverters, wireless power supplies, and switchers are used in computer hardware, instrumentation devices, medical equipment, and telecom systems. Founded in 1988, Unipower is headquartered in Coral Springs, Florida,with operations in Dunlap, Tennessee; the UK; and China. It caters to companies spanning North America, South and Central America, Europe, Africa and Asia within the data communications, telecommunications, medical, industrial controls, industrial power, and military sectors.

Unipower often uses acquisitions as a means for augmenting its product line and geographical scope. In 2015 it acquired C&D's Integrated Power Systems business (IPS), located in Dunlap, Tennessee. IPS specializes in DC power systems for telecoms and utilities. The acquisition reinforced Unipower's drive into the DC power systems market and significantly expanded the company's product line, customer base, and systems capabilities.

In 2015 Unipower acquired the Network Power Systems division (NPS) of Bel Fuse . NPS specializes in high quality, advanced technology DC power systems for use in networking, telecommunications, and high-speed data transmission. The acquisition expanded the company's global footprint.
