Replogle Globes isn't going anywhere anytime soon, but it's all about going everywhere (visually, at least). The company, a leading manufacturer of geographic globes, produces more than 100 globe models for schools and institutions, as well as for individual homes. The globes are distributed through a variety of sales channels in the US (including Discovery Stores , Pier 1 Imports , Toys 'R' Us , and Wal-Mart ) internationally, and online. In the 1960s Replogle Globes co-founded Scanglobe, best known for producing illuminated globes. Founded in 1930 by Luther Replogle and owned by the William C. Nickels family since 1973, Replogle was acquired by educational supplier Herff Jones in 2011.
