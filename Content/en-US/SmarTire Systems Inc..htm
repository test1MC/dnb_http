SmarTire Systems makes tire pressure monitoring systems (TPMS) that let drivers see, via a display module, the status of the air pressure and temperature in their tires. The systems are designed for use with both conventional and zero-pressure or "run-flat" tires. The systems' sensors provide data to prevent driving too long on a run-flat tire, which is unsafe and can also ruin the tire. SmarTire Systems sells through auto parts retailers and to OEMs such as Ford Motor .
