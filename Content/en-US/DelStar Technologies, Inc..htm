DelStar Technologies touts itself as the world's largest manufacturer of thermoplastic nets, nonwovens, laminates, and extruded components. It makes nonwoven plastics with applications in a range of industries including automobiles, electronics, food, healthcare, and textiles. Its netting products come in a variety of mesh sizes and are used as filtration devices and as fence materials. Established in 2001, DelStar Technologies is a subsidiary of Schweitzer-Mauduit International (SWM), one of the world's leading suppliers of fine papers to the tobacco industry.
