If you are a customer of Sanden International (USA), you are definitely a cool customer. A subsidiary of Tokyo-based Sanden Corporation , Sanden International (USA) manufactures automobile air-conditioning compressors sold in North, Central, and South America. The company's products include piston, scroll, and electric compressors for nearly any automotive application; it also sells service kits for repairing its compressors. Sanden International (USA) has plants in Texas, Michigan, and Mexico that make clutch assemblies. The company traces its roots back to 1974.

Sanden is headquartered in Wylie, Texas. It has additional locations in Detroit; and Saltillo, Mexico. Sanden's compressor manufacturing operations take place at its headquarters, while clutch components are manufactured at its plant in Saltillo, Mexico.

Sanden's products are used in light vehicles, heavy trucks, agricultural equipment, construction vehicles, buses, and other vehicles. Its products are sold through authorized distributors. Distributors in North America include Meridian Auto Parts, JBAR, OMEGA, Environmental Technologies, MEI Corporation, A/C Parts Warehouse, Vintage Air, and DTAC. South American distributors include Italytec Imex Industria e Comercio Ltda and Autofrio Importaciones S.A.S.

The company operates as a part of the Automotive Systems Business Group of its parent company. The Automotive Systems Business Group contributed about 66% of the parent company's total sales in 2014. The group also increased its sales for 2014 due to the recovery of many important world markets and the introduction of new products which featured environmental technologies.

With the whole world going green, the company has been developing electric compressors for electric and hybrid cars. It is also working on more environmentally friendly AC system coolants.
