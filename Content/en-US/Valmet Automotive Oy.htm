Finland might be too cold a climate for convertible cars, but that hasn't stopped Valmet Automotive from making them. The company specializes in manufacturing convertible roof systems for automakers such as Bentley , BMW and MINI, Daimler , and Renault . Valmet also acted as a contract manufacturer for Porsche , making the Boxster and Cayman models in Finland, but that relationship ended in 2012; now it makes another luxury sports car, the Fisker Karma, as well as the Garia luxury golf cart. Valmet was founded in 1968 as Saab-Valmet, a joint venture with Saab Automobile AB . The company is now majority-owed by Metso Corporation ; two Finnish investment companies hold minority stakes.
