Evans Consoles designs and manufactures computer and communications consoles for highly technical environments, such as air traffic control towers, power generation, emergency response, and financial trading centers. In addition to pre-built modular consoles, the company designs and builds systems that combine custom fabricated components and third party equipment. Evans Consoles' range of services includes consulting, project management, and systems integration. The company's customers have included Boeing , Deutsche Telekom , Microsoft , and NASA . Evans is owned by private equity investor CM Equity.
