Bekaert Corporation goes right down to the wire on a daily basis. The company, a subsidiary of Belgium-based NV Bekaert SA , manufactures a variety of wire, and wire and film coatings. For automobiles, Bekaert makes tire cord, control cables, spring wire, and clips. It also produces high carbon, low carbon, and stainless steel wire for industrial applications. Other Bekaert products include telecom and power cables, agricultural fencing material, and sawing cable. Bekaert operates through North America from around 10 manufacturing plants and sales offices.

Bekaert is headquartered at Fairlawn, Ohio, and operates 10 plants and sales offices in Canada and the US.

The company serves OEMs and customers in the automotive, construction, energy, utilities, consumer goods, and agriculture industries. It sells its products through its distributors throughout the North America.
