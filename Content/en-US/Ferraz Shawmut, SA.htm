Ferraz Shawmut, a subsidiary of Paris-based carbon and graphite products supplier Mersen , designs, manufactures, and distributes circuit protection products including industrial fuses, switches, and power management devices. Its wide array of products includes fuse holders; fuses for power distribution blocks; and general purpose, semiconductor, medium-voltage, and special uses. Its power interconnection business makes switchgear and magnetic relays; it also produces custom-made contactors and systems through its Lenoir Elec brand. Ferraz Shawmut also provides thermal management systems, which include air and liquid cooling, to the transportation, power quality, and signal processing markets.
