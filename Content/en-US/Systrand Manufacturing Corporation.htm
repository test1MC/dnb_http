Move over Rosie the Riveter, a new woman is doing her part to keep American manufacturing humming. Led by Sharon Cannarsa, Systrand is a maker of machined, forged, cast, and powdered-metal automotive components. Operations include high-volume production of engine and transmission parts such as axle shafts, cylinder heads, differential cases, engine blocks, gear housings, hubs and input shafts, manifolds, planetary gears, and power steering housings. With production-run capabilities ranging from a few prototypes to hundreds of thousands of parts, Systrand can supply components ranging in size from under a pound to several hundred pounds. Customers have included Chrysler , General Motors , Ford , and Volkswagen .
