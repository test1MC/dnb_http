Jerky Snack Brands eliminates the need to ask "where's the beef?" The company makes and markets beef jerky and other dried meat snacks. Its products are available in pouches, pre-packed counter and floor displays, individually wrapped, or unwrapped in jars. They are sold under the Pioneer, Double B, and Old Settler's brand names. Jerky Snack Brands wholesales its products to retailers, including convenience stores, supermarkets, mass merchandisers, drug stores, and duty-free shops. The company was formed by CEO Jay Link in 1986. Acquisitions have included Double B Foods , Pioneer Snacks, and Old Settler's Beef Jerky.
