Paragon Films' products may never win any Oscars, but if you've got a whole pallet full of the golden statuettes -- say you're Jack Nicholson, Meryl Streep, or the Motion Picture Academy -- well the company's got that all wrapped up. Paragon manufactures stretch film products for packaging and bundling under brands like Global Wrap and Edge Stretch Film. Its Ultimate Force brand film stretches more than 300%. The company also makes stretch film to custom lengths, widths, and colors. With corporate headquarters in Oklahoma and other locations in North Carolina and Washington, Paragon has a distribution network that spans the US.

Paragon has plants Oklahoma, North Carolina, and Washington. It serves customers in all 50 US states, as well as Canada, Mexico, South America and a number of other regions.

The company is a leading manufacturer of premium quality stretch film products. It has combined cutting-edge technology with a highly skilled work force to produce a superior line of stretch film.

Paragon's growth strategy includes reinvesting company profits in technology and delivering innovative new products. In 2013 it opened its third plant, in Union Gap, Washington.
