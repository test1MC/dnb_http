The Chilcote Company is in the preservation business. The firm makes and markets presentation and promotional products targeted to professional photographers, scrapbookers, and the hospitality industry. It operates Crown Photo Products, D. Davis Kenny Company (custom presentation and packaging products, Topflight-brand wedding albums), Wooden Nickel Albums (professional photo albums), and Winthrop-Atkins (promotional products). Other units include A La Carte (custom presentations for the hospitality industry), Complements (scrapbooks), Flora Albums (professional photo albums), Graphic Imaginations (promotional advertising, custom products, packaging), and Taprell Loomis (professional photo packaging).
