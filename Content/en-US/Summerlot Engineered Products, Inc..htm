Summerlot's lot is to manufacture a lot of conveyor equipment and replacement parts. Summerlot Engineered Products provides repair work, general machining, fabrications, conveyor inspection, and design troubleshooting. The company produces custom screw conveyor and screw feeders, as well as agitators, blenders, mixers, and pug mills, and belt conveyors and bucket elevators. It also makes tanks and hoppers. Summerlot Engineered Products also offers conveyor application design, CAD design services, and machine and structural design. The Summerlot family owns the company.
