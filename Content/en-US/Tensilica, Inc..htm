Next time you jam to your favorite tune or watch a DVD, you may have Tensilica to thank. The company, a unit of Cadence Design Systems , offers low-power processors used for embedded control and digital signal processing. Both are key to the operation of mobile phones, portable music players, and digital TVs. The company has two lines of processors: the Diamond standard and the configurable Xtensa processors. Tensilica also offers custom tools to simplify the configuration of its Xtensa line. Tensilica was founded in 1997. Cadence Design Systems acquired Tensilica in 2013 for $380 million.
