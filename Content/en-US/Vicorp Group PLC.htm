The power of the voice is paramount for Vicorp. The company provides software for use with interactive voice response technologies, allowing clients to provide their customers with efficient and secure phone-based self service options. Its primary offerings include the xMP suite (voice automation and self-service applications) and the xSB suite (voice signature and authentication applications).  Vicorp products are used by firms such as Barclays Bank , lastminute.com , and CEZ across the telecommunications, financial services, retail, travel, and broadcasting industries, among others.

Vicorp markets its products directly, as well as through a host of partners, including system integrators (Softel, Fujitsu Siemens), hardware/software vendors ( HP , IBM , Nuance ), and value-added resellers ( Cable & Wireless , Nokia Siemens ).
