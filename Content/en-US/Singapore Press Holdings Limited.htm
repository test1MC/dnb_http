Singapore Press Holdings (SPH) is a leading news and media conglomerate, encompassing newspapers, magazines, and digital content. Its portfolio of nearly 20 daily and weekly papers is anchored by The Straits Times and The Business Times , while its magazine division produces more than 100 titles. SPH also distributes content online through Web sites and mobile offerings like iPhone and iPad apps. Other operations include outdoor advertising, TV broadcasting (through 20%-owned MediaCorp TV), radio broadcasting (80%-owned SPH UnionWorks), and real estate. SPH owns and operates Paragon, a retail and office complex in Singapore. A second mall, The Clementi Mall, opened in 2011, and a third facility opens in 2014.
