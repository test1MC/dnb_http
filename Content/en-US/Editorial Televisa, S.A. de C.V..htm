Editorial Televisa is a leading publisher of Spanish-language magazines with more than 90 titles distributed in more than 20 countries throughout Latin America, as well as in Mexico and the US. It's portfolio includes music magazine Furia Musical , entertainment magazine TV y Novelas , and leading women's magazine Vanidades . Editorial Televisa also produces Spanish-language versions of other titles, including Cosmopolitan, Harper's Bazaar (from Hearst ) and Men's Health ( Rodale ). The company is a subsidiary of Spanish media giant Grupo Televisa .
