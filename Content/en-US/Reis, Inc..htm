Reis knows how to get below the surface of real estate. The company provides commercial real estate market information through online databases containing information on apartment, retail, office, and industrial properties in several US metropolitan markets. Its flagship product, Reis SE, offers trend and forecast analysis, as well as information on rent, vacancy rates, lease terms, sale prices, and new construction listings. Reis also furnishes data to small businesses through its ReisReports product. Its databases are used by real estate investors, lenders, and brokers to make buying, selling, and financing decisions. Customers access Reis' data through subscription or by purchasing reports individually.
