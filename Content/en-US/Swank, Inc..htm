No guy's outfit would be complete without some swank. Swank makes and distributes men's fashion accessories, including leather goods (belts, wallets) and jewelry (cuff links, chains). The items are sold under brand names, such as its namesake Swank, Claiborne , Kenneth Cole , Tommy Hilfiger , Guess? , Nautica , and Donald Trump, among others. Swank also distributes products under the Pierre Cardin name and under its clients' private labels. Customers are mainly US national department stores, specialty stores, mass merchandisers, catalog retailers, and some US military retail exchanges. Swank, founded in 1897 in New York, is owned by Randa Accessories , a maker and marketer of consumer fashion must-haves.

Randa Accessories bought Swank in 2012 for about $57 million.

Accessories manufacturer Swank enjoys a global reach. It produces its products through Randa's offices in the US, Canada, Mexico, the UK, and China.

The company distributes its products to retailers nationwide, as well as in Canada, Mexico, the UK, and China. As part of its operations, the manufacturer sells excess and out-of-line merchandise through a pair of company-owned factory outlet stores located in Pennsylvania and Massachusetts.

Swank's customers include department stores, national chain stores, specialty stores, mass merchants, catalog retailers, and military retail exchanges.

Swank's direct-to-consumer sales channel, consisting of a couple factory outlet stores, trumps rivals.

Making the company an attractive acquisition target, Swank had been posting sales increases in recent years despite the economic downturn. The accessories maker attributes the rise to larger orders of men's belts and personal leather accessories made by club stores and department stores. Many of these retailers are altering their merchandise programs to target the increasing demand for affordable small luxuries.

Swank has pursued more licensing agreements, particularly for private-label products. Sales of merchandise under its dozen licensed labels, however, expose Swank to extended payment terms.
