SNF sniffs out chemical solutions. SNF manufactures water soluble polymers such as synthetic flocculants, coagulants, and dispersants used for municipal and industrial water and wastewater treatment. Other industries served by SNF include paper processing, textile printing, oil production, agriculture, and personal-care products manufacturing. The American arm of French polymer maker SNF Floerger (owned by SPCM SA) the company has about a dozen polymer production facilities around the US. Business units include Chemtall, Floquip (polymer preparation equipment), Polychemie, and Polydyne.

The company produces a broad wide range of polymers and other chemicals, including flocculants, dispersants, polyacrylamides, quaternized cationic monomers, polymer/inorganic blends, and custom polymers and blends. It brings to market more than 1,000 products, such as activated carbon, odor control products, and defoamers.

SNF serves companies in a wide range of industries, including agriculture, cosmetics, detergents, dredging, personal care, municipal water treatment, oil and gas, pulp and paper, and sand and gravel.

The Americas contributed about 50% of the total revenues of French parent SNF Floerger in 2012.

The company invests heavily in research and development. In addition to operating facilities for analyzing and developing new products, in an effort to develop new molecules derived from research and to provide effective technical back-up for SNF's customers, the company has expanded to include pilot laboratories for each different application.
