At birth you're only issued two knees, two hips, two shoulders, and one spine, but Stryker UK can help out with replacement parts later on. The company is a part of the worldwide orthopedic medical device firm, Stryker Corporation . Stryker UK offers joint replacements, spinal discs and rods, bone trauma fixation, orthobiologics, and other implant devices. It also makes powered surgical instruments including navigation systems and endoscopic devices, as well as emergency medical transport and patient handling equipment. In addition, Stryker UK distributes medical devices on behalf of other manufacturers.

Globally, the Stryker organization serves customers in more than 100 countries.

Stryker UK is part of the Stryker Europe division. Despite its broad presence, Stryker Corporation gets about two-thirds of annual revenues from its domestic US market. It gets 43% of its revenues from the orthopedic segment.

The Stryker UK operations include a manufacturing plant in Newbury, which makes reconstructive devices and surgery systems, as well as administrative and sales locations.

Its products include implants used in joint replacement, trauma, craniomaxillofacial and spinal surgeries; biologics; surgical, neurologic, ear, nose and throat and interventional pain equipment; endoscopic, neurovascular, surgical navigation, communications and digital imaging systems; as well as patient handling and emergency medical equipment.

Stryker UK markets its products to hospitals, doctors, and health care facilities through sales representatives and distributors.

The global Stryker organization expands through acquisitions and internal product development efforts. Focus areas of growth include biologic orthopedic implants, improved joint replacement systems, and minimally invasive surgical devices and equipment.

Stryker UK was founded in 1984.
