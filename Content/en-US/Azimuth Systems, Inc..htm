Azimuth Systems is ready to take a bearing for you. It makes hardware and software used to test wireless broadband devices and equipment. The company's W-Series test platform is used for system-level testing of LTE (Long Term Evolution), Wi-Fi, and WiMAX wireless data and voice communications networks. Its DIRECTOR Test Executive software automates the tasks associated with testing, while STUDIO is a database management and reporting tool. Customers include Broadcom , Cisco Systems , Samsung Electronics , and T-Mobile . Azimuth Systems was established in 2002 by former CTO Fanny Mlinarsky.
