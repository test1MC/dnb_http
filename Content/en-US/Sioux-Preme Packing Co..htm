Sioux-Preme Packing is a midsized pork processor that specializes in custom cuts for niche markets. It operates two plants offering cuts of naturally raised pork to grocery retailers and food manufacturers; Sioux-Preme Packing also supplies antibiotic free and organic pork products, as well as porcine products intended for medical use (heart valves, etc.) The company was started in 1969.
