Looks are everything for Plastic Omnium, maker of auto exterior panels, exterior trim, bumpers, and fuel systems. Its automotive parts account for the majority of sales, with major customers including GM and Nissan . Part of the group's growing urban systems segment, subsidiary Plastic Omnium Environment manufactures waste collection and recycling containers; it is also directly involved in recycling management. Its Inergy Automotive Systems subsidiary produces auto fuel systems and has production facilities in Michigan, South Carolina, and Ramos, Mexico. About 70% of the company's sales come from outside France. Burelle controls 57% of the company.
