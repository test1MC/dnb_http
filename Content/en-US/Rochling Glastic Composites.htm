Röchling Glastic Composites can help you compose some finely engineered products. The company, a unit of Röchling-Gruppe, provides engineered composite products such as stock products, sheets, shapes, and materials; offerings include insulation, electrical standoff insulators, laminates, angle iron, metal rods, and solder pallet materials. Röchling Glastic serves markets that demand products with a variety of dielectric, thermal, corrosion, abrasion, and structural properties. The company sells its goods worldwide through a network of distributors and fabricators. Röchling Glastic has facilities in the UK and the US.

The company distributes its products in the US, Australia, Canada, Europe, and Asia.

Röchling Glastic makes wide range of fiber reinforced plastic composite materials and shapes, both as standard products and custom-ordered.

In early 2007 Cignet LLC sold the company to German industrial concern Röchling-Gruppe.
