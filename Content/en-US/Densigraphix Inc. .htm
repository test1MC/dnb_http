Densigraphix doesn't want to see any misplaced blotches of printer ink messing up your final report. The company distributes the parts and supplies needed to keep copiers and printers working. Founded in 1979 as a copier paper supplier to copier dealers, Densigraphix has expanded its product offering to include toner and replacement parts (drums, blades, fuser rollers). Its primary customers are copier dealers and copier remanufacturers that recycle laser toner cartridges. Densigraphix acquired copier part dealer Sel Drum Corporation in 1999.
