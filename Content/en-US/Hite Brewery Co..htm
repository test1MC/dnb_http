Standing tall, the Hite Brewery has brewed its way to the top of the ranks. Hite is the #1 the brewer in South Korea by market share. Its flagship beverage Hite is made with mineral water. The company's other brands include Prime, Exfeel, Hite Saeng, Hite Pitcher, Prime Pitcher, and Carlsberg (licensed). Hite was established in 1933, making it Korea's first brewery as well as its largest. Previously known as Chosun Brewery, the company took the Hite name (Humanity, Innovation, Trust, and Excellence) in 1998.
