iQ Power takes the intelligent approach to battery manufacturing. The company makes diagnostic battery systems, primarily for the OEM automotive market. iQ Power also makes diagnostic systems used to check battery power and hardware and software systems for energy management. The company offers engineering and design services and licenses its technology through iQ Power Licensing. While iQ Power operates primarily in Germany, the company has a joint venture -- iQ Power Asia -- with its partner KG Power in South Korea. Daewoo International , which distributes the company's batteries, also owns part of iQ Power Asia. The company entered insolvency in early 2009 and emerged from the proceedings three months later.
