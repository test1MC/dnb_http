CV Industries is an expert in the area of upscale home furnishings. Its Century Furniture subsidiary makes furniture pieces for every room in the house, as well as outdoor spaces. The unit produces wood and upholstered collections, armoires, desks, and leisure lines branded by such designers as Juan Montoya and Oscar de la Renta . Its furniture is available at retailers across North America and in countries such as Australia, China, and Russia. CV Industries' Valdese Weavers business markets fabrics to furniture manufacturers and distributors. It also provides contract manufacturing for the hospitality and corporate décor industries. The employee-owned company was founded by Harley Shuford, Sr., in 1947.
