Make no bones about it -- paintball can be serious business. The Kingman Group Corporation (formerly Kingman International) makes Spyder and Java brand paintball equipment. It also manufactures protective paintball accessories (gloves, goggles) and tournament-level equipment. Its product lines are sold globally through leading retailers, sporting good chains, and specialty paintball stores. The company also sponsors regional and national paintball  tournaments and competitions (The Spyder Cup). CEO Arthur Chang founded the company in 1992.
