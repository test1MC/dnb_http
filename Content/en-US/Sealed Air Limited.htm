Sealed Air Limited serves as the UK arm of its parent company, Sealed Air Corporation , which produces food packaging, protective packaging, and other products. Its packaging products protect items from damage from hazards such as shock, vibration, and abrasion during the shipping cycle. Sealed Air Limited is one of about 20 other entities parent Sealed Air operates in the UK, such as Chemical Methods (Europe) and Dolphin Packaging. The UK is among the more than 30 countries that compose Sealed Air Corporation's Europe, Middle East, and Africa (EMEA) operations.
