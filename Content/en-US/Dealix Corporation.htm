Dealix Corporation drives information to potential car-buying consumers. Through the company's Web site, InvoiceDealers.com, consumers can research cars and be connected with local car dealers. Dealix provides Internet sales leads to its partners, primarily car dealers, dealer groups, and auto manufacturers. The company provides market and industry data through its Dealix Business Intelligence product, and Internet sales leads are processed through its Dealix Lead Trading Platform, a suite of proprietary technology products and business solutions. Dealix is a subsidiary of ADP .
