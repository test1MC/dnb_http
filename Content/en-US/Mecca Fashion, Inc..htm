Mecca Fashion wants to be the center of all that is cool. The fashion company designs, markets, and peddles street style men and children's clothing and footwear. It has also branched out into women's clothing with its urban-inspired Mecca Femme line. Primarily, the company caters to the 15- to 35-year-old male customer. Mecca's footwear includes boot and sneaker-type shoes. The company's clothing is available in retail stores, such as select Lady Foot Locker stores, The Body Store, and specialty shops the likes of Apparel Connecktions and Urban Flava.
