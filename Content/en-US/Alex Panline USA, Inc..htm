ALEX toys make you never "wanna grow up." Alex Panline USA produces children's art and craft supplies (various writing instruments, easels and chalkboards, paints and brushes, craft kits), creative play and developmental toys and games (doll furniture and dollhouses, puppets and theaters, prism bricks), bath toys (art kits, toothpaste sets, soap dispensers), fashion and jewelry (bead kits, fashion paints, watches), and children's furniture (art tables and desks, stools). Its collection contains more than 650 items that are sold (under the ALEX name) through specialty retail stores, catalogs, and Web sites in more than 25 countries. Founded in 1986 by Nurit and Rick Amdur, the company was named for their son.
