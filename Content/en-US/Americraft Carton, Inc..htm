It's not just the packaging -- it's the craft that goes into it. Americraft Carton manufactures a variety of printed and un-printed folding cartons, as well as custom designed paperboard packaging materials. Its folding carton lineup includes beer trays and display tops with auto bottom trays. The company also produces blister cards, display cards, portfolios, and sleeves. Americraft Carton caters directly to a spectrum of industry packaging needs, from apparel to automotive, candy, dry foods, hardware and household products, sports equipment, and toys. The company's equipment supports low and high volume multicolor printing, along with computerized graphic design, collating, die cutting, high speed gluing.

Americraft is headquartered in Prairie Village, Kansas, and operates seven manufacturing sites as well as many sales and customer service locations throughout the country in Minnesota, Michigan, Ohio, Massachusetts, North Carolina, Tennessee, and Georgia.

Americraft provides folding cartons and other products for a diverse array of industries, including food service, medical and pharmaceutical, cosmetics, candy and confectionery, tobacco, automotive products, household products, toys, clothing and apparel, sports equipment, and ammunition.
