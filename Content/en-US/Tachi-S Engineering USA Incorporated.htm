A unit of Japan-based automotive seat manufacturer Tachi-S, Tachi-S Engineering USA provides engineering, prototyping, and manufacturing services. The company works mainly with Japanese automakers that have manufacturing operations in North America and it also helps its parent company develop relationships with US and European manufacturers. It has several joint ventures with automotive seating and interior electronics company Lear Corporation and makes seats for Nissan cars in several locations throughout North America. Tachi-S Engineering USA was founded in 1986.

Tachi-S works with major automakers, delivering 3.8 complete seats and over 4 million in total seat components to the global market each year.

The company has its headquarters in Farmington Hills, Michigan. It also has presence in Ohio, Tennessee, Kentucky, and Mexico.
