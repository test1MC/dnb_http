Customers turn to Elliott Group for its rotating equipment. Commonly known as Elliott Group and formerly known as Elliott Turbomachinery, the Ebara subsidiary designs, manufactures, and services turbomachinery for the petrochemical, refining, oil and gas, power, and process industries around the world. Its primary products are centrifugal and axial compressors, steam turbines, and lubrication systems for rotating equipment. Elliott Group also provides repair services, spare parts supply, turbine remanufacturing, and upgrades through a global network of service facilities.

Elliott Group operates 40 locations worldwide. Its main manufacturing facilities are located in the US (Jeannette, Pennsylvania) and in Japan (Sodegaura, Chiba).

The company operates 18 service centers in China, Taiwan, Japan, Europe, the Middle East, and North and South America, along with parts-depots in Japan, the UK, and the US; with its field service crews operating worldwide, supported by regional engineering offices in Singapore, Switzerland, and the US, and by major manufacturing facilities in Japan and the US.

Elliott Group operates in three segments: Engineered Products - large, customized turbines and compressors (components are usually custom made and ordered in smaller lot sizes); Industrial Products - industrial steam turbines (components are generally ordered in larger lot sizes); and Service Parts, which provides components to Elliott Group service shops and end users around the world (orders are often for small lot sizes with short lead times).

The company has installed more than 70,000 units of equipment worldwide.

Elliott Group serves a range of industries which include oil and gas,r efining, LNG, boil off gas, offshore compression, petrochemical, ethylene, plant oils, and power generation.

The company operates under the Fluid Machinery & Systems segment of the parent company (71.8% of net sales in 2014).

Strategically, Elliott Group is looking toward emerging markets for growth. Over the past few years, Elliott Group has been making inroads into high-growth markets in eastern Europe, the Middle East, Africa, South American, and the Asia/Pacific region, winning projects and opening offices in Russia, the United Arab Emirates, and Thailand, among others.

In 2015 the company opened a new, 5,780-square-meter turbomachinery service center in Tianjin, China to repair and maintain rotating equipment for customers in the oil and gas, chemical, petrochemical, refining, and coal industries.

In 2014 Elliott Group supplied the main compression equipment for Dow Chemical 's new, world-scale ethylene cracker in Freeport, Texas.

In 2012 it opened a regional office in Abu Dhabi.

The company, a subsidiary of industrial conglomerate Ebara , was founded in 2000.
