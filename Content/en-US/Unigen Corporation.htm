Unigen has a high-tech knack for low-profile designs. The company produces memory modules -- chips connected to small, plug-in boards -- along with flash memory, and wireless communication products. It touts manufacture of complex standard and custom modules. Unigen ties up with processor maker Sandforce to build NAND Flash-based Enterprise Solid State Drives (SSDs) used in data enterprise computing applications. Unigen sells memory modules to OEMs and resellers in the imaging, medical, military, aerospace, networking, PC, and telecommunications industries. It also offers assembly, contract manufacturing, prototyping, and testing services. President and CEO Paul Heng founded Unigen in 1991 and owns the company.
