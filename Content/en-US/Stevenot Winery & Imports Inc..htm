There's wine in them thar hills! Stevenot Winery & Imports produces and imports wines and spirits at a site in the foothills of the Sierra Nevadas that was once mined for gold. Varieties of wine include Chardonnay, Cabernet Sauvignon, Syrah, and Merlot. The company, established in 1973, also makes wine from the Spanish Tempranillo grape. As part of its operations, the company manages 84 acres of vineyards, which produces about 20,000 cases of wine each year. Young's Market Company serves as the Stevenot's distributor.
