Genmab analyzes genomes in its lab. The biotech company creates and develops human antibodies to attack cancer, autoimmune, and inflammatory diseases. It holds a portfolio of candidates in stages of development ranging from pre-clinical to late-stage trials. Its first marketed product is Ofatumumab, for the treatment of a type of chronic lymphocytic leukemia. Genmab is also working on Daratumumab, a monoclonal antibody that holds the potential to kill multiple myeloma cells. The company has licensing and development partnerships with several international pharmaceutical and biotech companies. Genmab was founded in 1999.
