China Finance Online -- the name says it all. The company provides financial information on the Web to individual investors as well as financial professionals such as investment bankers, stock analysts, and financial reporters in China. Its content and services are offered at www.jrj.com.cn and www.jrj.com ("JRJ" is the abbreviation of "Jin Rong Jie," which means "financial industry" in Chinese), where users can obtain financial news, research reports, real-time stock quotes, and historical financial information for China's listed company stocks, bonds, and mutual funds. The company launched its first site in 2000.
