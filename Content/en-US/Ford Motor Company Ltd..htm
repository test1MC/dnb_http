Ford Motor Company Ltd. gives car buyers in the United Kingdom what they want -- compact to midsize passenger cars. The UK-based company sells the popular Ford Focus and Fiesta, as well as cars targeted primarily to the European market that include the midsize Mondeo, the compact 4x4 Kuga, and the sporty compact C-MAX. Ford Motor Company Ltd. also builds and sells the Ford Transit commercial van. The company operates an assembly plant in Southampton, builds engines and transmissions at plants in Bridgend and Halewood, and also runs a product development facility in Dagenham. With roots dating back to the early 1900s, Ford Motor Company Ltd. is part of the European arm of parent company Ford Motor Company .

The company makes Ford cars and commercial vehicles, from the compact Fiesta -- its best selling car -- to the Ford GT supercar, as well as vans and transit vehicles. In addition to individual vehicles, the company offers fleet services, insurance and financing.

The company's cars are sold through a dealership network. It has over 80 branches in the UK.

The UK arm of the Ford Motor Company contributes around 9% to total group sales.
