StoreNext Retail Technologies provides point-of-sale (POS) hardware and software for grocery stores. The company sells POS equipment, self-checkout systems, electronic shelf labels, and wireless handheld devices. Its software includes POS systems, as well as modular, Web-based tools for managing stores and back-office functions. It also offers a POS system extension that provides fuel management capability. The company markets exclusively to independent grocers and small chains, selling through authorized dealers. Initially a joint venture between Fujitsu Transaction Solutions and Retalix , StoreNext became a fully owned subsidiary of Retalix in 2008.
