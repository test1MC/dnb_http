Nothing fickle about First International Computer (FIC). The company gladly designs and manufactures computer products and electronic components for other manufacturers worldwide. Its products include motherboards, embedded computing systems, graphics cards, PCs, and notebook computers. FIC also provides logistics services. The company works closely with such technology partners as AMD , Intel , NVIDIA , and VIA Technologies . FIC provides aftersales repair and parts supply through its Comserve Network locations in the Netherlands and Germany. First International Computer was founded in 1980 by chairman Ming Chien. The company has branch locations in China, Hong Kong, Japan, and the US.
