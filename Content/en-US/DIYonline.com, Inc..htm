From front door to back porch Do-It-Yourself-ers can find a little help at DIYOnline.com. The company provides user generated content in addition to it's how-to guides, helpful articles, and design tools. Its Designer Series Software provides a personalized Specification Kit that includes an installation guide, a suggested materials list, fulfillment services, contractor locators and referrals, and financing sources.
