Nanjing Textiles, or NANTEX, has the fabric to make a lot of different products. The company imports and exports cotton and polyester fabrics for use in clothing and other items. It creates apparel, luggage, and toys for sale in more than 120 countries throughout the world from offices in China, the US, and Singapore. Customers include Wal-Mart , H&M , Carrefour , Sears , and Billabong . In addition to its namesake textiles, the company also makes TVs, DVD players, and other appliances, along with medical equipment, hardware, motorcycles, and tools. Its Pharmaceutical Division exports raw and processed pharmaceutical chemicals. The Chinese government owns about 47% of NANTEX, which was formed in 1978.
