This Peter Pan has much bigger fish to fry than dealing with imaginary snarling pirates and snapping crocodiles. Peter Pan Seafoods, whose Never-Never Land is Alaska, harvests, processes, freezes, and markets wild fish and shellfish pulled from icy waters surrounding the state. The company offers cod, crab, halibut, pollock, tuna, mahi, swordfish and salmon to the US retail and foodservice industries. Its brands include Demings and Double Q. Peter Pan also also makes canned salmon and surimi (crab-flavored processed fish). The company's products are distributed through food retailers and restaurant suppliers in the US, Europe, and Asia.
