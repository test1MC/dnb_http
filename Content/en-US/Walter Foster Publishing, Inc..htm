Are you a budding visual artist looking for guidance? Walter Foster can help you hone your left-brain talents. A subsidiary of The Quarto Group , Walter Foster Publishing offers art instruction books and activity kits designed to instruct readers, both young and old, on drawing and painting techniques. Walter Foster Publishing's books are sold in arts and crafts stores, as well as via the company's Web site. Some titles feature licensed products, such as series on how to draw characters from Disney , Pixar , Nickelodeon , and Warner Bros . The company was founded by artist Walter Foster.
