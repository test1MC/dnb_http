Ogio and its products like to be on the go. The company's a bag maker for sports gear; its unique niche is focusing on bag design for functionality. Ogio manufactures about 400 different types of golf club bags, backpacks, duffel bags, and day packs under the Ogio brand name. The firm makes a line of duffel-type computer bags, corporate travel gear, gym bags, school backpacks, motocross bags, and other accessories. Ogio's products are sold nationwide through retailers such as Best Buy , Dick's Sporting Goods , eBags , and Zones . Ogio also manufactures brands for other companies. The company was founded in 1987 by Mike Pratt after he designed the LockerBag gym bag. In 2017 Callaway Golf Co. acquired Ogio for $75 million.
