Some people are so committed to environmentalism they choose to tread lightly upon the earth even when they're six feet under. J C Atkinson and Son specializes in manufacturing caskets and coffins that are environmentally friendly. The company uses solid or veneer timber from renewable forests, recycled wood, and water-based polishes. It also manufactures cremation urns from natural materials (bark, sawdust, starch) that will fully decompose when interred. J C Atkinson and Son is controlled by managing director Julius Atkinson, a descendant of the founding family. J C Atkinson and Son products have made appearances on several popular TV shows, including EastEnders .

The company sells its caskets and coffins throughout the UK and Ireland via a network of companies, including funeral homes. Altogether, J C Atkinson and Son produces some 60,000 coffins each year. While the company has built a business manufacturing solid oak or mahogany caskets, in recent years it has focused on supplying consumers with more eco-friendly options. Marketed under the Natural Legacy by Hainsworth brand name, J C Atkinson and Son makes woolen coffins, featured by Prince Charles at a festival promoting sustainable products.

Its Heartwood Range encompasses the company's traditional range of products, including its range of UK manufactured coffins, caskets, ash urns and coffin furniture. Its Heartwood Range of products includes its traditional range of coffins, caskets, ash urns and coffin furniture as well as its selection of Italian and Paulownia coffins and caskets and its range of pet caskets.

Its Country Range of alternative country style products is designed to offer customers a comprehensive range of coffins that are manufactured from natural sustainable sources which are made to the best environmental standards, including a range of Fair Trade Coffins. This range of alternative coffins and includes wool, bamboo, seagrass, and wicker and woodland coffins.

Its Reflections Picture Coffin range covers more than 30 catalog designs as well as an unlimited number of personalized custom-built picture coffin designs.

Greener Goodbyes offers a complete greener funeral package that links together an environmentally friendly coffin, tree planting and carbon rebalancing options.

J C Atkinson and Son's Ecollect unit is a clinical waste collection service and offers customers 20% in cost savings on clinical waste collection costs as well as reducing the carbon footprint of the waste collection service.

The company owns and operates its own fleet of delivery vehicles.

J C Atkinson and Son was founded in 1936.
