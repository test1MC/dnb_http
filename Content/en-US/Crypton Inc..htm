How does Superman keep those tights looking so sharp? Well, Crypton would have you believe that they're made from its Crypton fabric (which unlike Kryptonite does him no harm at all). So named because it's a super fabric, Crypton's products are made using a patented formula that resists stains, odors, moisture, and bacteria and stands up to everyday wear and tear. The company markets its fabrics to the healthcare and hospitality industries, as well as government facilities and schools. In the past few years Crypton has ramped up its sales to residential customers (children's furniture, pet beds, mattresses, and carpets). It also sells an upholstery cleaner under the Crypton Care name.
