The British Isles are a lot cleaner and have fewer pesky bugs, thanks to Ecolab Limited. The UK subsidiary of American specialty chemicals company Ecolab, Inc. , it has bought the UK businesses of makers of pest elimination, hospital hygiene, and contamination control products in this decade. The acquisitions have greatly increased the scope of the company's business in Great Britain, which previously had been concentrated in the food and beverage industry as a provider of cleaning and disinfecting systems and services. Ecolab Limited operates from two locations in England.

The company serves a range of markets, including Agriculture, Beverage Production and Brewing, Building Service Contractors, Commercial & Industrial Facilities, Contract Catering, and others. For Agiculture markets, Ecolab offers its services through an Online Shop.

Among the companies Ecolab has acquired are the British businesses of Terminix and Adams Healthcare, contamination control product maker Shield Medicare, and Powles Hunt & Sons in 2006. Shield sells its products to pharmaceutical, medical device, and hospital clean rooms. Powles produces professional laundry products in the UK.

Parent Ecolab was founded in 1923.
