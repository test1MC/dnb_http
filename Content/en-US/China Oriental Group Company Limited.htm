China Oriental produces steel billet and strips for Chinese steel manufacturers, who then process the billet and strips into downstream products such as angles, rebar, wire rods, and pipe. China Oriental's production facilities are located in the provinces of Hebei and Guangdong, the country's largest iron ore producing regions. Its steel production facilities consist of an iron smelter and steel making and rolling plants. The company produces about 9 million tons of steel products per year, making China Oriental one of the largest suppliers in China. The company's founders own 45% of China Oriental, while ArcelorMittal also owns 30%.
