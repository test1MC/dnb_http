Florida Key West makes the key ingredient for key lime pies and Margaritas. The company is a leading producer of fruit extracts specializing in key lime juice and lemon juice. Its products are sold mostly through retail grocery stores; its also distributes juice through food wholesalers. In addition to its brand-name products, Florida Key West offers private-label services for large-volume customers. President Earl Tanner founded the company in 1986.
