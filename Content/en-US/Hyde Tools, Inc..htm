Hyde Group (dba as Hyde Tools) makes surface preparation hand tools for drywall, painting, and plumbing professionals. The niche manufacturing company makes about 2,000 products, including industrial knives and surface preparation tools (scrapers, trowels), are sold worldwide through retailers, such as McCoy Corp. and Sherwin-Williams and online. Hyde Manufacturing early investor Myron Clemence's son and grandson ran the company (which became Hyde Tools) during the 1970s. Some of his descendants are still involved in the business today, including CEO Rick Clemence.

The brand leader in surface preparation and finishing, Hyde Tools is looking to growing business for those who sell to professional contractors and do-it-yourself 'pros' at home. Its full category portfolio and high-impact assortment of products offer competitively priced programs that are well supported and based on a brand that users know and trust.

Some of the companies online retail partners include amazon.com , all-wall, Do it Best, W.W. Grainger , and Direct.

In 1875 Isaac P. Hyde founded Hyde Manufacturing, then specializing in mill blades, in his garage. Hyde eventually sold the majority of his stock to Joseph Lacroix and Myron Clemence.
