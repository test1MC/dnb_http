Magazine publisher Bonnier Corporation serves a wide range of audiences, from those who are packing Salade Ni√ßoise for an afternoon cruise in their yacht to those huddled in a deer stand at the crack of dawn. The company's titles include Saveur (gourmet foods), Cruising World , and Boating Life , as well as Popular Science, Field & Stream , and Outdoor Life . Its magazines are devoted to lifestyle, travel, luxury, and parenting. All total, Bonnier Corporation publishes about 50 special-interest magazines, and produces related multimedia projects and events. The company is owned by 200-year-old Swedish media firm Bonnier AB .

In addition the Lifestyle Group, another key area of the business is the Parenting Group, which includes Working Mother magazine, as well as Babytalk, Conceive, Parenting , and Parenting.com.

Bonnier Corporation was founded in 2007 when parent Bonnier AB purchased 18 magazines from Time Inc . and combined those assets with its World Publications' 20 special interest magazines. (Bonnier AB acquired World Publications, publisher of Garden Design, Saveur , and Sailing World , in 2006.) Bonnier Corporation expanded significantly in 2009 when it acquired five magazines from the struggling Hachette Filipacchi Media U.S., adding niche titles Popular Photography, Flying, Boating, Sound & Vision , and American Photo to its portfolio.
