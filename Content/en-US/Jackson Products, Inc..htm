Sparks might fly around Jackson Products, but the company's customers won't see them. Doing business as Jackson Safety, the firm makes and sells head, eye, face, and hearing protection equipment, such as safety goggles, hard hats, helmets, and ear phones. The company has units devoted to welding safety, personal protective equipment, and traffic safety. Jackson Safety sells through a distributor network and markets its products in North America, South America, and Europe. Personal safety equipment brands include Silencio, Wilson, Smith and Wesson Safety Products, and others. Traffic safety equipment includes barricades, cones, and signs. Jackson Safety is a subsidiary of Kimberly-Clark Corporation .
