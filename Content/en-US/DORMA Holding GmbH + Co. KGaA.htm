When opportunity knocks, the folks at Familie Mankel (aka DORMA) hope it's their products being used. The company is a global producer and supplier of door hardware, door security systems, automatic and revolving doors, glass fittings and decorative and structural glass systems, and partitions and movable doors. The company's divisions include door control hardware; automatic; glass furniture and fittings; security, time, and access; and movable doors.
