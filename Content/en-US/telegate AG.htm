telegate is heeding the call for information. By dialing 11-88-0, the company's customers can access a variety of information services over the telephone, including directory assistance, weather and traffic reports, and movie show times. telegate charges a small fee for each inquiry. In addition, the company's 1880.com and klickTel.de sites allow directory searches online. telegate also provides services to corporate clients, including call handling and creating mailing lists. The company has operations in Austria, Germany, Italy, and Spain. Italian business directory publisher SEAT Pagine Gialle owns about 77% of the company.
