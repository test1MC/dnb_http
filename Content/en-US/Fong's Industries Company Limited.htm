CHTC Fong's Industries Company is behind the machinery that keeps China's textile industry moving. Founded in 1963, the company makes and sells dyeing and finishing machines through its various subsidiaries, including Fong's National Engineering Company, Monforts Fong's Textile Machinery Company, and Fong's Europe. Through Fong's Steels, the company also distributes stainless steel products including sheets, coils, plates, tubes, and flats in China and Hong Kong. Fong's Tycon Alloy Industries subsidiary specializes in the production of metal castings and related technical services. CHTC Fong's is controlled by China Hi-Tech Group (CHTC), a state-owned holding company.
