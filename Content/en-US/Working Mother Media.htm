Working Mother Media addresses the challenges of juggling work and family. The company publishes Working Mother magazine, devoted to issues specific to working moms, and is known for its annual list of 100 Best Companies for Working Mothers. It also operates the National Association for Female Executives (NAFE), which organizes conferences and publishes a magazine specifically for women in business ( NAFE Magazine ). The company also puts on a number of conferences each year, including the Best Companies for Women of Color Conference.
