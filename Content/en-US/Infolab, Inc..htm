Infolab is doing its part to ensure laboratories' success in discovery. The company provides biomedical and surgical supplies, labware, chemicals, and lab equipment to clinical laboratories, testing centers, blood banks, and hospitals. Products include diagnostic test kits, automated analyzers, reagents, glassware, plastic ware, apparel, lab furniture, and other medical and surgical goods. Through six distribution centers across the South, Infolab distributes products nationwide from almost 500 medical product manufacturers. The company was established in 1967 and is one of the oldest independent laboratory equipment providers in the country.
