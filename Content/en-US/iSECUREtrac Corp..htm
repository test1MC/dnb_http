Correctional authorities that need to account for the whereabouts of parolees and probationers can look to iSECUREtrac for answers. In the company's system, offenders wear devices that use GPS (global positioning system) technology to determine their locations; data is transmitted to a central monitoring station. iSECUREtrac also offers systems that enforce house-arrest orders by verifying an offender's presence within a building. The company leases its monitoring equipment to federal, state, and local criminal justice agencies.
