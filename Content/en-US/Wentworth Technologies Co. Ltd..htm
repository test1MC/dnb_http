Wentworth Technologies is molding the future -- the future of plastic products that is. The company manufactures industrial machinery including injection molds and metal stamping tools. It is also one of the world's largest independent makers of PET and extrusion blow molds. Wentworth Technologies' Plastics Processing group makes a variety of disposable thermoformed plastic containers and lids. Wentworth serves customers in such industries as beverage bottlers and packaging companies. The company has more than a dozed manufacturing facilities in Asia, Europe, and North America.
