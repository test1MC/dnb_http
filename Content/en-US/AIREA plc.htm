AIREA is a manufacturer for all areas, producing and selling carpet tiles and rugs. Its offerings are sold under brand names Burmatex and Ryalux. The Burmatex line, made to contract specification, includes fiber bonded and tufted as well as barrier and entrance carpet and tile. Customers are architects and contractors of institutional (health care, education), commercial, and public facilities. AIREA's Ryalux portfolio comprises custom and standard tufted carpet for residential markets, distributed via retail outlets. The UK is its largest market, representing around 80% of net sales.

AIREA divides its operations across two primarily segments: contract floor coverings (70% of net sales) and domestic floor coverings (30%).

From 2013 to 2014, AIREA saw its revenues decrease due to decreased sales from both the contract floor coverings and domestic floor coverings market sectors.
