Many companies elect Elecsys to make their electronics. Elecsys is a contract manufacturer of electronic assemblies and displays. The company makes custom electronic assemblies -- including printed circuit boards, electronic modules, LCDs, light-emitting diodes (LEDs), and wireless communication interface modules -- for OEMs in the aerospace, industrial communications, safety, transportation, military, and other industries. Elecsys also makes ruggedized handheld computers and printers, as well as remote monitoring equipment for customers in the oil and gas exploration and production industries. In 2015 the company was acquired by irrigation equipment maker Lindsay Corporation .
