You've got to hand it to them -- Handgards helps foodservice providers get a grip on hygiene and sanitation issues. The largest manufacturer of plastic gloves in the US, Handgards produces an array of safety and handling products, including disposable gloves, adult and children's bibs, along with high density polyethylene (HDPE) aprons, and food storage and preparation bags and covers. Its 200 different products are sold through independent representatives across the US. About 2,000 North American food service providers, and other businesses and institutions are catered to, from McDonald's to Darden Restaurants ' Red Lobster, Doctor's Associates ' Subway Sandwiches, and SYSCO .
