Universal Audio manufactures the sound we like to hear. The company makes professional audio processing equipment and software for recording artists and studios. Some of its products are reproductions of classic analog equipment. Universal Audio makes a portfolio of plug-ins, co-developed with some of the most prominent brands in audio, including Studer, Ampex, Lexicon, Neve, Manley, Marshall, SSL, and more products. The company is headquartered in Scotts Valley, California, and has international offices within Germany, Austria, Switzerland, France, and Benelux. It was founded by Bill Putnam Jr. and his brother Jim in 1999.
