Summit Foods is the acme, the apex, the peak, the tops. The UK company manufactures frozen, microwaveable snacks, sausages, burgers and ready meals and sells them through retail outlets and food service operators such as Tesco and Wm Morrison . The company produces burgers with beef, chicken, or lamb; doner (lamb) kebabs, chicken kebabs, gamon (ham) sandwiches, and meat-filled pita breads. It also makes packaged frozen meals such as a cheeseburger with fries. Summit's products are available under its own Snacksters brand as well as under private labels. Its Snacksters extended-life sandwiches, subs, and wraps are marketed to customers on the go.
