Stock up the liquor cabinet with bottles from Stock Spirits Group. The company sells around 50 brands of vodka, brandy, and liqueur in Central and Eastern Europe and the US. Vodka brands include Czysta de Luxe, Kegelvich, Orzel, and Poland's most popular brand, Wódka Zoladkowa Gorzka. Brandy and vermouth are sold under the Stock brand, while Limoncè limoncello and the orange liqueur GranGala are popular liqueurs. Stock Spirits Group has distilleries in the Czech Republic, Italy, and Poland, and distributes its brands in Bosnia and Herzegovina, Croatia, Slovakia, Slovenia, and the UK. In the US it does business as Stock Spirits Group USA. Stock Spirits Group is owned by Oaktree Capital Management .
