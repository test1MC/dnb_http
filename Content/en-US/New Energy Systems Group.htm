At New Energy Systems, batteries are included! The company makes batteries for cell phones, digital cameras and camcorders, and laptops under its primary Anytone brand. Other product lines include Anytone solar powered batteries and Kim Fai solar panels used in street lights, traffic lights, and solar power equipment. Anytone batteries are sold to end users in China and via OEMs elsewhere while Kim Fai sells to solar construction and installation companies. New Energy, which was formed in 2001 as Jasmine's Garden and took its current name in 2009, sold its E'Jenie (lithium ion batteries, shells, and caps) and NewPower (battery cells) subsidiaries in 2011 citing declining revenues in both segments.
