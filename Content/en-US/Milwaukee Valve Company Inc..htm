Milwaukee Valve Company, the valve that made Milwaukee famous ... at least to other valve makers around the county. The company makes a variety of flow control products such as actuators, controls, and valves (including ball, butterfly, check, and gate valves). Its 4,000 manual and actuated valves find use in a wide range of industries, including chemical processing, fire protection, iron and steel production, petroleum production and refining, pulp and paper, and water and sewage. Milwaukee Valve's recently developed bronze ball valve is the first of its kind to be clean water certified.
