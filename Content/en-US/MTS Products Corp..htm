MTS Products (stands for Modular Technical Services) is doing its best to keep things contained. The company makes protective cases for musical instruments and other equipment, including tools, cameras, and computers. Products are available in leather, nylon, plastic, polypropylene, vinyl, and wood. The company's manufacturing and warehousing operations are both located at its 100,000 sq. ft. facility in Indiana. MTS Products was founded in 1971.
