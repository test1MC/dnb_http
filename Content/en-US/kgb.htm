You don't need Cold War spies to find that phone number, thanks to this kgb. The company offers traditional directory assistance via mobile and landline phones, SMS, and the Internet. It also offers information services such as movie listings, train schedules, and price comparisons. In addition, it operates a question-and-answer service. kgb does business in the US through kgb USA , while subsidiary The Number UK offers information services in the UK, primarily through The Number 118 118 (akin to 411 in the US), as well as through its 118.com website. Chairman and CEO Robert Pines co-founded kgb in 1992.

The company operates out of about 25 offices located in major cities across the US and a few offices located in Europe (France, Italy, and the UK).

kgb has forged partnerships with local website and industry giants such as Google and Facebook to offer daily deals and expand its reach.

In 2011 kgbdeals acquired TheDealist, New York City's top independent daily deal website. The acquisition expanded kgbdeals' presence in New York City, which was its first market to launch in 2010.
