Elwood International makes condiments for consumers who need diabetic, diet-friendly, and sugar-free products. The company's offerings, which include salad dressings, jellies, sauces, syrups, ketchup, and mayonnaise, come in individual-serving packets. Elwood was formed in the 1960s to serve the health care industry. The food packaging company is owned by president Stuart Roll.
