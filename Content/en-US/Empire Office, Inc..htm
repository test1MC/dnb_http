Offices around the Empire State Building just might be filled with furniture provided by Empire Office. A leading distributor of office furniture nationwide, the company's products -- which come from some 300 manufacturers -- range from cubicle workstations to products for the executive offices, including storage units, lighting, seating, case goods, and accessories. Empire Office also provides installation. The company is one of the leading Steelcase furniture dealers in the US. Empire Office is owned by the family of Abraham Gaslow, who founded it in 1946.

The company serves in New York as well as four Florida cities and an established network of national installers and warehouses across the country.

The company also offers project management services, which include project scheduling, furniture inventory, furniture research and development, bid preparation and execution, procurement and order tracking, and refurbishing management offerings.

Empire Office caters to banks, insurance companies, education institutions, media and advertising agencies, and health care companies.
