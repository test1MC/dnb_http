Although this company produces manga , it's still not in the food business. Panini S.p.A. (known as the Panini Group) is a major publisher and distributor of trading cards, stickers, collectibles, comic books, anime, manga, children's magazines, activity books, and animated DVDs in Europe and Latin America. The group publishes more than 4,000 titles under its Panini Comics imprint and distributes about 1 billion packs of stickers and DVDs every year through PAN Distribuzione. It offers web content through Panini Digital and Panini Interactive and publishes Japanese-style comics through Planet Manga. The group entered the US market in 2009 when it purchased Donruss Playoff and renamed it Panini America .
