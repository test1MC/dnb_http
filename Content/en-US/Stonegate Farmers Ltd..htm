Stonegate has plenty of reasons to look serene on its nest. The company is a major UK egg producer and a world producer of free-range and organic eggs. It oversees egg production from hatching to packing and supplies both branded and own-label eggs to big UK grocers such as ALDI , Asda , Sainsbury , and Waitrose . Beyond large white eggs, shoppers can choose Stonegate eggs based on the breed and diet of hens that laid them. Stationed in Wiltshire, the company operates in the UK and has egg packing machines around England and Wales. Stonegate has been privately owned by the Corbett family since mid-2008.
