BizR makes novelty outdoor toys such as the Xoooomer interlocking ball. Products are sold through the company's Web site and at specialty toy stores such as Toys "R" Us . The company manufactures products through partner Determined Productions, and advertising and marketing firm Mosaic provides brand identity services. BizR was founded by chairman Rick Camp and president Andy Iheanacho.
