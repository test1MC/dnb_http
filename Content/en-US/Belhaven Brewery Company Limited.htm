Belhaven offer thirsty beer drinkers a beer and a safe haven to enjoy it. Belhaven Brewing Company owns and operates breweries, as well as a string of pubs. The company brews several beers including Belhaven Best, 80 Shilling, Twisted Thistle, and St. Andrews Ale. The company touts its flagship Belhaven Best as the top-selling draft ale in Scotland. Belhaven also exports its Scottish Ale and St. Andrews Ale to the European, North American, and Australasian and Asian& markets. Belhaven is a subsidiary of UK brewer and pub operator, Greene King .
