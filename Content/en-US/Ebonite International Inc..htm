Fifteen pound replicas of Jupiter? No, they're bowling balls. Ebonite International makes bowling balls for professionals and style-conscious bowlers. Each type of ball comes in a variety of exotic swirls of color, from peacock to candy; Ebonite even produces balls featuring the popular Angry Birds brand. The company also manufactures ball bags, gloves, wrist supports, bowling coaching software, and other accessories. Products are sold under the Ebonite, Columbia 300, Track, Powerhouse, Hammer, and Robby's brand names. The company traces its historical roots all the way back to 1907.
