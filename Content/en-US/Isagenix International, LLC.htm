Isagenix promotes the belief that a "clean" body is a healthy body. The company develops and manufactures a range of health and wellness products from nutritional and dietary supplements. Its offerings include weight loss solutions, energy and performance products, healthy aging products, shakes, soups, vitamins, nutritional supplements, healthy snacks, sports drinks, and personal care items. Isagenix's line of "Nutritional Cleansing and Replenishing" products aim to improve health by accelerating the removal of impurities from the body and replacing them with nutrients to revive health. The company also offers dietary supplements to help customers lose weight and increase energy. Isagenix was founded in 2002.

While the US accounts for a majority of sales, Isagenix also has a presence in key international markets including Australia, Canada, China, Colombia, Hong Kong, Indonesia, Malaysia, Mexico, New Zealand, Puerto Rico, Singapore, and Taiwan.

A network marketing enterprise, Isagenix provides products and sales support services to an international network of associates, which in turn sell its products and recruit new associates. The company has successfully expanded its sales network over the years to include more than 500,000 home-based associates. Many of the company's associates are health care professionals who recommend Isagenix products to patients.

Having a pipeline of new offerings to deliver to the growing anti-aging products market is key to the Isagenix's growth. Its Rejuvity Skincare System, for instance, helps people achieve restful sleep while supporting healthy brain function. In late 2015, the company introduced a new line of premium, organic slow-roasted coffee.

The company opened the doors to a new state-of-the-art headquarters in the greater Phoenix area in early 2016. The 150,000-sq.-ft. facility includes an auditorium, development laboratories, a museum, and a cafeteria.

Isagenix is also expanding geographically.

Isagenix was founded in 2002 by John Anderson (master formulator), Jim Coover (chairman and president), and Kathy Coover (EVP). Anderson provided expertise in weight-loss products and nutritional supplements while the Coovers (husband and wife) provided years of experience in the network marketing industry.
