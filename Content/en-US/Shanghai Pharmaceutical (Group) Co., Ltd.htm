Shanghai Pharmaceutical Group, or Shanghai Pharma, knows that Big Pharma is big business. One of China's largest pharmaceutical companies, Shanghai Pharma makes prescription drugs, over-the-counter (OTC) medicines, active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs), biopharmaceuticals, and traditional Chinese medicine sold under various brands, including SINE, Leishi, Dragon & Tiger, Cangsong, Guofeng, and Shengxiang. The vertically integrated company distributes its wares and owns almost 2,000 drug stores, including Huashi Pharmacy. Its R&D facility, Central Research Institute, operates 10 centers across China. SPGC formed in 1996 from a merger of several smaller companies.
