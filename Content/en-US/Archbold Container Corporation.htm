Because impulse buying is possible at every turn these days, Archbold Container Corporation (ACC) feeds the need by building the displays. ACC makes corrugated packaging and block and shape molded expandable polystyrene (EPS) foam. One of the biggest sheet plants in the Midwest, ACC also offers digital production (in-store signage, point-of-purchase displays), permanent displays (freestanding, pallet-size, counter, kiosks), and fulfillment services (contract packaging, shrink wrapping, clamshell packaging). ACC also works with customers to design custom displays for a variety of products. Green Bay Packaging bought ACC from Sauder Woodworking Co. in 2005.
