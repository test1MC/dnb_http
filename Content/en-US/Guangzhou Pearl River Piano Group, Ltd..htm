Guangzhou Pearl River Piano Group (mostly known as Pearl River Piano) is one of the world's leading manufacturers of pianos. The company also makes violins, electric guitars, and drums, as well as a variety of ethnic Chinese instruments. Pearl River Piano boasts a handful of manufacturing bases from which it makes vertical pianos, triangle pianos, guitars, and fiddles; it also processes its own wood. The instruments firm produces thousands of pianos, fiddles, and guitars each year. Pearl River Piano has secured 60% of the Chinese piano market. It exports musical instruments to more than 80 countries and regions. The company was established in 1956.
