VION sees not just the pig but the pig in all its potential. The company has three divisions that divvy up animals into parts and go from there. The VION Fresh Meat division, which specializes in pork, also offers fresh beef, lamb, veal, and chicken for sale to the retail and foodservice sectors; the VION Convenience division makes value-added meat products and frozen fruit and vegetables; and VION Ingredients takes the less-than-edible bits and pieces left over from meat production and turns them into ingredients for use in the food, pet-food, pharmaceutical, photographic, and other non-food industries. VION sells its products in the Netherlands, Germany, Poland, Russia, the UK, Thailand, and Australia.
