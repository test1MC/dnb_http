If you're looking for a torrent of tongue-tantalizing tastes, seek out R. Torre's products. The company blends some 85 flavored syrups, which it sells to food and specialty retailers under the Torani brand name. Its products, which include regular and sugar-free syrups in flavors such as amaretto, butterscotch, cheesecake, chocolate, French vanilla, strawberry, and peppermint, are used to flavor coffees, sodas, and teas. The company also produces dessert sauces, and smoothie and flavored-coffee bases. Its products are available throughout the US. The R. Torre & Company was founded in 1925 by Rinaldo and Ezilda Torre and is still owned and operated by the Torre family.
