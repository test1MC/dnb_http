PANDORA has filled its box with shiny things. The vertically integrated company designs, makes, and sells jewelry worldwide. Its rings, bracelets, necklaces, earrings, and watches, most with interchangeable parts, are available at about 420 PANDORA stores and (mostly) independent jewelry stores in more than 55 countries. PANDORA'S patented Moments line of charm bracelets and necklaces have graced celebrities from Catherine Zeta-Jones to Donna Karan to Christiane Amanpour . The company, which keeps its image, distribution, and everything PANDORA on a short chain, makes its wares at four company-owned manufacturing facilities in Thailand. Formed in 1982, the company began trading on NASDAQ OMX in 2010.
