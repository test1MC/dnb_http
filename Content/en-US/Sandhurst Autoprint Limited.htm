Sandhurst Autoprint manufactures, supplies, and distributes thermal number-plate printers and related equipment to the motor vehicle industry. In addition to making number plates, the company makes and sells custom show plates (personalized with logos or other graphics) and offers related accessories such as ribbons, reflective vinyl, and backing plates. The company also sells a parking sensor (a device that attaches to the license plate) to help drivers determine how much room they have when pulling into tight spots. Sandhurst Autoprint was founded in 1997 by license plate salesman Stephen Dear and one of his former clients, Tim Sanders.
