This company satisfies the Asian food cravings of Canadians. Ginger Beef Corporation is a leading manufacturer and distributor of frozen and packaged Asian meals in Canada. Through its Ginger Beef Choice subsidiary, the company markets ready-to-eat and frozen meals to a number of national grocery store chains, including Loblaws , Sobeys , and Canada Safeway . It also supplies such foodservice operators as Bridge Brand (a unit of Gordon Food Service ) and SYSCO . In addition, its Ginger Beef Express subsidiary franchises a handful of full-service and take-out Chinese restaurants in Calgary through its Ginger Beef Express subsidiary. President and CEO Stanley Leung holds nearly 50% of the company.
