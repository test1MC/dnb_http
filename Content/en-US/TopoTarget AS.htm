TopoTarget has put cancer in its sights. The company develops and markets anti-cancer drugs, as well as therapies for cancer-related conditions. Its portfolio includes preclinical candidates and drugs in clinical development. Founded in 2000, TopoTarget works with academic and commercial development partners in the US and Europe. Its biopharmaceutical candidates aim to fight cancers such as lymphoma, melanoma, and ovarian, breast, and liver cancers. The company restructured in 2011, cutting its staff and selling its US subsidiary Topotarget USA to San Diego-based Apricus Biosciences .
