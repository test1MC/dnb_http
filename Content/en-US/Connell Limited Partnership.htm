Limited partnership, unlimited appetite for growth; Connell Limited Partnership (Connell) looks to serve manufacturing companies around the world. The family-owned company keeps megafunds available for investment in and operation of manufacturing companies serving customers in the automotive, energy, mining, construction and agriculture industries. Connell's primary business -- Anchor Danly (die sets and die makers' supplies) -- serves customers in these areas. Connell has more of $500 million of funds available for investment and is continually evaluating new opportunities.

Connell operates through 32 locations and global distribution network. Its operating companies serve more than 20,000 customers in 70 countries worldwide.

Its Anchor Danly business includes Danly IEM and Anchor Lamina , with more than 10 metalworking centers and sales branches in the US and Canada. Additionally, Anchor Danly has operations in China and Germany. Its other portfolio companies include Danly Die Set, Danly Machine, Luria Brothers, Mayville Metal Products, Yuba Heat Transfer, and Wabash Alloys.

Connell principally serves manufacturing customers in the agriculture, automotive, construction, energy, mining, and construction industries.

The company buys, develops, and then sells manufacturing companies to increase its shareholders' returns and make funds available for new investment opportunities. In 2015 it acquired SencorpWhite, a global leader in customized, end-to-end automated products and services for the packaging industry through its Sencorp, Ceratek and Cerapak product lines. SencorpWhite's products and services serve businesses in the healthcare/life sciences, fresh food, medical products, consumer packaging and industrial warehouse/distribution industries.
