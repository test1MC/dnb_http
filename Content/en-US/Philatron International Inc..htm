When the cable guy says, "Git-r-done," Philatron (PI) comes up with a truckload of cable, hoses, and electrical wires. The bulk supplier, also known as Philatron Wire and Cable, manufactures an array of hook-ups, from festoon cable used in cranes and hoists, to speaker wires, jumper cables, and spark plug wires. PI also plies coiled power cords and cables; its Stallion brand coiled cables move the heavy-duty trucking market. Other brands include Bronco, Philaflex, and Philafin. The company's custom cable division works directly with OEMs, commercial contractors, and the Defense Department to make standard and custom cables for a diversity of applications. PI is led by its founder and CEO Phillip M. Ramos, Jr.
