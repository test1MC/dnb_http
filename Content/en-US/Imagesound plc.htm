Whether it's cool jazz or blaring bump 'n grind, shoppers are soaking up the background sounds provided by companies such as Imagesound. Formerly Advance Capital Invest, Imagesound is the largest provider of instore music, radio, and TV to retail and leisure companies in the UK. The company also installs the audio-visual equipment through which its music plays. Imagesound has about 60 major customers, including such brands as adidas , Halifax , Hyatt , McDonald's , PizzaExpress , and Superdrug . The company was formed by a reverse-merger of Imagesound Limited, TTL Music Limited, and Rolec Limited. Chairman Derek Mapp holds a 17% interest in the company.

With offices in Barcelona, Berlin, Budapest, Florida, Hong Kong and Dubai Imagesound now manages music for retailer locations in over 75 countries.
