It's delightful. It's delovely. It's Denali Flavors -- maker of more than more than 40 flavors for the dairy industry under the Alaskan Classics brand name, including its trademark Moose Tracks vanilla flavor. The company's other Alaskan-themed flavors include Bear Foot Browner, Caramel Caribou, Mother Lode, and Otter Paws. Ice creams using Denali flavors are produced by 85 regional and local dairies throughout the US and are sold at major US food retailers, including Wal-Mart , Walgreens , Aldi , Sav-A-Lot , Food 4 Less , Giant , Hannaford , Meijer , Safeway , and Winn Dixie . Denali Flavors is owned by founder and CEO Wally Blume and his wife, June.
