Tipper Tie's products can keep the cows in their field, breakfast sausage in its wrapper, and smoked hams in their carry-home container. The company manufactures an array of netting, clippers, clips, and wires. Its lineup is used to package everything from fresh produce to Christmas trees, as well as to bind and close a variety of packaging, and, even to fabricate fencing. Working outside the box, Tipper Tie also manufactures automated equipment for handling and securing foods with the packaging and clips it makes. Customers are courted through a global network of distributors. Tipper Tie is part of a group of three Dover companies, including Tipper Tie Technopack (Germany), and Tipper Tie Alpina (Switzerland).
