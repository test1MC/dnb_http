Gather operates Gather.com, an online community where members connect and converse, sharing perspectives on topics such as politics, parenting, and poetry. Gather has half a million members, and the site averages more than one million unique visitors per month. The company earns revenues through advertising. Gather was founded by chairman Bill Kling, CEO Tom Gerace, and former director Jon McTaggart. Senator Bill Bradley sits on the board of directors. Gather's long list of investors includes Jim Manzi, former CEO of Lotus Development Corporation; Southern California Public Radio; Hearst ; and S&P Global .
