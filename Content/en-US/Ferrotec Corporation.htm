Ferrotec develops magnetic fluid technology used in vacuum seals and computer seals. Other applications for which these magnetic fluids are used include semiconductor components, flat panel displays, and hard disk drive manufacturing. The company also manufactures thermoelectric modules, which are used in medical devices, automotive equipment, telecommunications equipment, and home electronics. Founded in 1980, Ferrotec has operations in the US, through Ferrotec (USA) Corporation , as well as elsewhere in Asia and Europe. The company has shifted much of its production to China in order to cut costs on its electronics products.
