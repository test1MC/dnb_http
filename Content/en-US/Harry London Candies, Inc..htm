Harry London Candies makes chocolate confections and other candies, including toffee, mints, and caramels. The company's products are sold in select retail stores and through its Web site, as well as to wholesale accounts. Harry London also offers private-label products and corporate-gift packaging, and it facilitates fundraising programs. The business was founded in 1922 by Harry London in Canton, Ohio, where it still maintains a flagship retail store. It filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy in 2003 and exited restructuring when it was acquired by Alpine Confections later that year; previously it had been managed by descendents of Harry London. The company was acquired by 1-800-FLOWERS.COM in 2006.
