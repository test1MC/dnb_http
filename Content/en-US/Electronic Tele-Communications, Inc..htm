So you've dialed the wrong number and it looks like it might rain. At least products from Electronic Tele-Communications (ETC) will give you the bad news in a pleasant voice. ETC's interactive voice information and call processing systems let service providers provide enhanced services such as changed-number announcements, wake-up calls, call return, and time and temperature information over phone networks. For corporate customers, it offers professional voice recording services used to create outgoing voicemail and on-hold messages. The company was founded in 1980 by George Danner, father of president and CEO Dean Danner.
