Indoff has been distributing office furniture and supplies since 1971. Through its material handling division, the company distributes products, such as industrial steel shelving and storage lockers. Its office interiors division offers a range of work furnishings, including tables, chairs, and executive credenzas. Indoff's office supplies division carries basic office products, computer supplies, and business machines. As part of its business, Indoff sells corporate promotional products the likes of caps, shirts, and magnets, as well as commercial printing products. Founded by its owners, John Ross and John Temple, the company maintains a network of about 400 sales partners in the US, Canada, and Mexico.

Based in Missouri, Indoff serves most of North America.

The company operates a handful of divisions, including Material Handling, Commercial Interiors, Business Products, Promotional Products, Commercial Printing, and Appliances and Energy Solutions.

Indoff annually logs revenues of more than $140 million.

Indoff relies on hundreds of sales partners located in the US and Mexico to peddle its products. The company's partners are given the elbow room to make local decisions regarding pricing, service levels, and product selection, as well as manage their own business and customer base.

Indoff's customers span several industries, such as distribution, healthcare, legal, education, engineering, and manufacturing, among others.

To expand its business, Indoff in late 2013 acquired Missouri-based Allied Appliance Sales Company, which serves customers nationwide.
