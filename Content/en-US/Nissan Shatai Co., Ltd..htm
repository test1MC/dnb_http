Nissan Shatai is always thinking about being of sound mind and body. Through its relationship with Nissan Motor , Nissan Shatai makes bodies for more than a dozen Nissan vehicles, including trucks, SUVs, and vans. Other vehicles include a light commercial bus dubbed the Microbus. It also makes conversion vehicles including ambulances and vans with lifts. The company's Kanagawa Prefecture plant makes the Serena minivan and the Infiniti FX 45 luxury car for the North American market. Nissan Shatai, which dates back to 1949 as a railroad car manufacturer, has been an affiliate of Nissan Motor since 1951.
