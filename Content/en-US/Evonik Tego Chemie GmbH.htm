Evonik Tego Chemie makes your paint appear brighter, stick longer, and resist water damage. The company manufactures chemicals used in paints and printing inks. Its products include additives, heat-resistant silicone binders, and hydrophobing agents. Founded in 1984 Tego is a unit of Evonik Degussa . It markets its products to more than 40 countries with production and/or technical centers in Brazil, China, Germany, Italy, Japan., Korea, the UK, and the US. Tego has formed raw-materials supply partnerships with, among others, Kyoeisha and DuPont .
