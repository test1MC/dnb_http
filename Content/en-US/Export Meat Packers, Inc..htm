This Jersey slugger packs some meat into its punch. Export Meat Packers is a Garden State meat processing company. Located in Bayonne, New Jersey, the company prepares, packages, and markets bulk meat products. Its customers include US and international meat wholesalers and retail food customers. Export Meat Packers operates along the eastern coast of the US and in the Caribbean. Company president William Minder founded the company in the 1980s.
