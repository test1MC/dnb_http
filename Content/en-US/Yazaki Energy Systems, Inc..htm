Yazaki Energy Systems is a maker of air-chilled commercial heating, ventilation, and air-conditioning systems that do not use ozone-depleting CFCs, or chlorofluorocarbons. The company makes climate-controlling products for use in schools, offices, hospitals, hotels, and industrial facilities. Yazaki Energy Systems is a subsidiary of Japanese manufacturing firm Yazaki Corporation , which also owns Yazaki North America . The company, which sells its products throughout the Americas and Europe through its network of sales representatives, also offers remote monitoring systems. Sadami Yazaki founded the company and created its red arrowhead logo in 1951.
