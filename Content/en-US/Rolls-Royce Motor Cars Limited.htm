The Spirit of Ecstasy is neither a ghost nor a phantom; it is the iconic mascot of Rolls-Royce Motor Cars. The company is known for its luxurious design, engineering, and price, and especially for its iconic female grill ornament -- the Spirit of Ecstasy. Its model line-up leads with the Phantom, the Phantom  Coupé, and Phantom Drophead. The new Rolls-Royce Ghost, launched in 2011, boasts a single-shell chassis and a V12 engine. In 2003 BMW bought control of the name and the manufacturing of Rolls-Royce cars from Volkswagen AG . The company has been in operation since 1911.
