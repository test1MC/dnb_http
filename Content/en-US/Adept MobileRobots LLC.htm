Adept MobileRobots develops and manufactures mobile robots and robotics software. Its products are aimed at advertising, health care, home entertainment, industrial manufacturing, security, and other applications. Customers include Fujitsu , Hewlett-Packard , Honda , Intel , MIT , Microsoft , Leidos , Siemens , the US Army , and the US Navy . The company sells worldwide through dealers, OEMs, and VARs. Adept MobileRobots has sold thousands of robots and robot platforms in its history. It was established as MobileRobots in 1995 by CEO Jeanne Dietsch and COO William Kennedy. Adept Technology acquired the company in 2010 for about $5 million, including around $1 million in cash and stock, and added its name to the company's brand.
