Searching for that perfect guitar pick? Give D'Andrea a try. The company makes 12 varieties of guitar picks, with varying sizes, grips, shapes, and colors, as well as custom imprinted picks for private labels. It also sells guitar straps and accessories, and musical instrument care products. Still family-owned and -operated, the company was founded in 1922 by Luigi D'Andrea.
