It's safety first for MSDSonline. The company runs the largest online database of material safety data sheets (MSDS), with more than 3.5 million documents containing environmental health and safety information that helps businesses of all sizes comply with workplace regulations set by the likes of the EPA , OSHA and other agencies. Technical product sheets include safety data on substances such as bleach, ethanol, and oil, while company-specific sheets contain data on products made by various manufacturers, including 3M and Chevron . MSDSonline helps companies build and manage their own MSDS libraries and also offers Web tools for injury recordkeeping and workplace safety training. ICG Group owns MSDSonline.
