Panasas provides high-performance parallel storage for high-performance computing (HPC). The company designs storage systems for use with clustered, Linux-based servers. The company's products include storage hardware and its PanFS storage operating system. Customers include energy exploration companies, scientific researchers, film animators, and other commercial enterprises that use its systems to store and analyze massive amounts of data. Los Alamos National Labs and the National Institutes of Health are clients. The company counts Cisco , Extreme Networks , GlassHouse , Intel , Microsoft , and CommVault among its technology partners. Investors in Panasas include Intel Capital and Mohr Davidow Ventures .
