Basic Fun knows what toys are made for. The company makes a variety of small toys, including action figure key chains, miniature arcade and board games, handheld electronics, wind-up toys, and bath time accessories. It also makes Fisher-Price classic toy replicas and other products through more than 80 licensing agreements with such firms as Hasbro , Lucasfilm , Nintendo , and Nickelodeon . Basic Fun toys are sold online and in retail stores, including Claire's , Spencer Gifts , Target , Toys "R" Us , and Wal-Mart . Founded in 1991, Basic Fun is owned by The Good Stuff Company.
