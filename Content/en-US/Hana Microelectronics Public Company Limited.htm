Hana Microelectronics is the story of a teenaged girl who has a secret double life as a pop music star. No, wait, that's Hannah Montana . Hana Microelectronics is the parent company of various high-technology firms, including businesses that offer contract electronics manufacturing and provide integrated circuit assembly and testing services. Its product line features light-emitting diodes (LEDs), optoelectronic and hybrid devices, printed circuit board assembly, and liquid crystal-on-silicon microdisplays. The company was established in 1978 to assemble LED watch modules. While Thailand accounts for more than three-quarters of sales, the company also operates in China, Hong Kong, and the US.
