Lotte Chemical (formerly Honam Petrochemical) manufactures a variety of petrochemical products from two facilities in South Korea. Its primary product is ethylene, and Lotte Chemical ranks among the country's leading producers of polyolefins like high density polyethylene (HDPE) and polypropylene as well as other downstream chemicals like ethylene glycol. The integrated company also operates a naphtha cracker, which supplies the raw materials for its other products. Lotte Chemical is a part of Japanese conglomerate Lotte 's Korean network. While it is publicly traded, various Lotte companies own a majority share in Lotte Chemical. The company took its current name in 2013.

The company has operations in Korea, China, Malaysia, Indonesia, Pakistan, Poland, the UK, and the US.

Lotte Chemical operates in three segments: Monomer, Polymer, and Primary Oil Content.

Monomer supplies ethylene glycol, styrene monomer, butadiene, ethylene oxide adduct, methyl methacrylate, purified terephthalic acid, and purified isophthalic acid.

Polymer provides high-density polyethylene, low-density polyethylene, linear low-density polyethylene, polypropylene, polyethylene terephthalate, and polycarbonate.

Primary Oil Content supplies ethylene, propylene, benzene, toluene, xylene, paraxylene, and oxoxylene.

In 2014 Lotte Chemical's net revenues decreased by 10% due to lower sales in all of the company's reportable segments.

Net income declined by 49% in 2014 due to higher selling and administrative expenses, an increase in finance costs, and higher income tax expenses.

Cash from operating activities decreased by 14% in 2014.

Lotte Chemical is accelerating its efforts to strengthen its global competitiveness by establishing overseas branches in Hong Kong, Russia, and USA, along with a sales corporation in China.

In 2013 parent Lotte merged Honam Petrochemical with one of the largest South Korean polyethylene terephthalate and purified terephthalic acid producers, KP Chemical, and renamed the enlarged company Lotte Chemical.

In 2010 Honam Petrochemical acquired Malaysia's Titan Chemicals for $1.27 billion.

Lotte Chemical (as Honam Petrochemical) was established in 1976.
