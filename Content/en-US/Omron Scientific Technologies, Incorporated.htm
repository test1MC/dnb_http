Omron Scientific Technologies, Inc. (Omron STI) makes a science of safety. The company supplies safety light guards, safety interlocks and relays, safety mats, and controllers used to protect personnel in hazardous manufacturing environments. Omron STI serves such industries as automotive, cosmetics, food and beverage, pharmaceutical, semiconductor, and electronic machinery. The company is a North American subsidiary of Japan-based OMRON Corporation , a maker of electronic controls components. Omron STI is part of OMRON's Industrial Automation segment, which provides safety system consulting, in addition to providing equipment.

The Americas contributed about 13% of the Industrial Automation segment's revenue in 2013.

Omron STI's products are supported worldwide by distributors and OMRON offices. Its primary market is North America.

The Industrial Automation segment contributed about 40% of its parent company's revenue in 2013.
