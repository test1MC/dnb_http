Talk about carbon-based life forms -- Tokai Carbon lives on the stuff. The company's carbon products include carbon black, a reinforcement and coloring material used in car tires, industrial rubber products, paints, and inks. Its artificial-graphite electrodes are used in electric-arc steelmaking furnaces. Tokai also makes specialty carbon products, such as glassy carbon and carbon brushes, as well as friction materials used in clutches and brakes. It's also involved in real estate rental and cargo-handing. Based in Japan, Tokai Carbon operates five plants in three countries (Japan, China, and Thailand) with a combined annual capacity over 500,000 metric tons.
