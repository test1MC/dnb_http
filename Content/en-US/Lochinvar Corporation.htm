Lochinvar knows that being in hot water doesn't always mean trouble. The company makes commercial and residential hot water generators, pool heaters, water heaters, storage tanks, boilers, and related parts and accessories. Other Lochinvar-branded products include commercial solar systems. It also offers high-efficiency products such as boilers, gas water heaters, and electric water heaters. Lochinvar distributes its products throughout the US and Canada, and exports to Europe and other international locations. Lochinvar is a subsidiary of A. O. Smith .
