Bordering Monterrey, DeAcero is the largest steel wire manufacturer in Mexico. The company offers a slew of products for agriculture to building, cable, and industrial applications. Its catalog of fence and mesh items feature reinforced and ornamental hardware cloths, chain links, and sheep and hog floors. A list of wire products ranges from electric, fence and barbed wire, to tire wires, and its nail products include masonry, pallet, and fruit nails. DeAcero also produces long steel products, including rods and bars. The company exports to the US, Europe, Central and South America, and the Caribbean. Building and construction is DeAcero's core market, followed by agricultural, home, and industrial customers.
