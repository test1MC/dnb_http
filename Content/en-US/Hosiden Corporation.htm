Hosiden manufactures a variety of electronic and electromechanical components, such as acoustic devices, actuators, circuit breakers, connectors, DC motors, passive-matrix LCD modules, power plugs and jacks, and switches. The company serves manufacturers in the automotive, cable, computer, consumer electronics, medical, and telecommunications industries. Its products are used to build car navigation systems, cell phones, digital still cameras, laptop computers, and set-top cable boxes. And its corporate Web site has the cutest ladybugs crawling across the pages. Hosiden was founded in 1947 and has manufacturing and sales operations in Asia, Europe, and North America.
