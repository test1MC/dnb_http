No, Traffic.com does not provide information about Steve Winwood. Instead, the leading commercial supplier of digitally gathered traffic data offers real-time data about specific speeds, travel times, and delay times generated by roadside sensors placed on monitored routes. The company covers more than 50 market areas that encompass some of the most traffic congested cities, including Houston and New York. Traffic.com delivers traffic content to clients and consumers on terrestrial and satellite radio, broadcast and cable TV, and via the Internet. It also delivers traffic information by text to wireless devices. Traffic.com is a subsidiary of digital maps provider NAVTEQ .
