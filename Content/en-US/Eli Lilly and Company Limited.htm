Eli Lilly's been standing with feet on both sides of the Atlantic for about 80 years. Eli Lilly and Company Limited (Lilly UK), the UK arm of American drugmaker Eli Lilly and Company , manufactures and markets medicines that treat ailments including cancer, diabetes, heart disease, urinary and erectile dysfunctions, and depression in Europe. The company also conducts research and development operations for its parent, with a focus on neuroscience; research scientists in the UK discovered schizophrenia treatment Zyprexa. Lilly UK also provides consulting and management services for health providers tailored to the needs of the UK National Health Service .

Lilly UK focuses its internal research efforts on three core therapeutic areas: bio-medicines, diabetes, and oncology.

Headquartered in Basingstoke, the company also operates Erl Wood Research Center in Windlesham, Speke Manufacturing Operations in Liverpool, and Bio-Tech Manufacturing in Dundee.
