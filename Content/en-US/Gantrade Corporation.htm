Gantrade gallivants about the globe trading chemical commodities. The company is a petrochemicals firm and producer of vinyl acetate monomer (VAM), as well as carbon black and ethylene and propylene derivatives. Gantrade serves a range of markets, incuding Adhesives and Sealants, Paints and Coatings, Construction, Elastomers and Rubber (Industrial and Tire), Polymers and Resins, Plastics and Plastic Additives, and Polyurethane Intermediates. The company has marketing and storage operations in the US and throughout Europe. The Parekh family owns Gantrade and has interests in various other chemical companies through a venture with Court Square Capital Partners .

The company is based in Montvale, New Jersey, and other locations across the US, Europe, and Asia, including Houston, Chicago, Los Angeles, Charlotte, and Dothan, (Alabama); Antwerp; Felixstowe (UK); Oslo; Genoa; and Shanghai.

Gantrade engages in distributing petrochemical-based products such as acrylonitrile, butyl acrylate, butyl methacrylate.
