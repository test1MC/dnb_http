Organics Corporation of America's main division, Ambix Laboratories, is interested in liquid assets. It's a manufacturer of cosmetics and nutritional products for humans and pets. The company supplies private-label personal care products (including prescription and over-the-counter drugs, such as creams, ear drops, nasal sprays, ointments, and syrups) to drugstore chains and wholesale distributors nationwide. It also makes shampoo and nutritional supplements for pets.
