When French carmaker Renault decided to take a road trip across India, it asked Indian SUV maker Mahindra & Mahindra (M&M) to come along for company. Mahindra Renault builds on Mahindra's distribution in the country and desire to move from SUVs to cars. The company has produced the Logan, a budget-priced subcompact sedan that Renault also sells in seven other emerging markets. Mahindra Renault's Nashik factory produces about 50,000 of the vehicles annually. Renault and M&M embarked on their journey together in 2005; in 2010 M&M bought out Renault's 49% stake in the venture.
