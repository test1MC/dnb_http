American Defense Systems, Inc. (ASDI) does a bang-up job of supporting our troops. The company makes custom bulletproof steel plates and glass panes that provide protection to US military vehicles in combat. It also manufactures bullet- and blast-resistant transparent armor, walls, and doors to protect buildings, as well as barriers, steel gates, and bollards to keep vehicles from crashing into them. ASDI earns all of its revenues from the Departments of Defense and Homeland Security and branches of the US military. In addition, the company runs the American Institute for Defense and Tactical Studies, which offers tactical training courses. CEO Tony Piscitelli owns almost three-quarters of the company's stock.
