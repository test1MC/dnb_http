Ever-more-crowded skies demand efficient and reliable communications; just ask Park Air Systems. A subsidiary of Northrop Grumman , Park Air Systems makes communication, navigation, and traffic control systems for aerospace applications. Its communication systems include VHF and UHF ground-to-air transmitters, receivers, and control equipment; navigation systems include aircraft instrument landing systems and satellite landing systems. The company's air traffic control systems integrate telemetry and radar data, thus allowing air traffic controllers to safely guide multiple planes through public airspace. It has added VoIP technology to its product range.

Based in Peterborough, Canada, Park Air Systems has facilities in the US, Europe, and Asia. In 2016, it expanded communications capabilities of the air traffic control systems at Dubai International Airport and the Dubai World Central airport.
