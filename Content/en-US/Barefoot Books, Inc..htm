Barefoot Books wants the kids to kick of their shoes and read. The company publishes children's books, including titles such as The Animal Boogie , Catch that Goat! , and The Story Tree . Barefoot Books also sells other kid-friendly items such as puppets, puzzles, games, and CDs. The company has offices in the US and the UK. It operates a retail store in Cambridge, Massachusetts, and sells its books through its network of home-based distributors (or Barefoot Books Ambassadors). Nancy Traversy and Tessa Strickland co-founded Barefoot Books in 1993 with the goal of introducing children to the stories and art of other cultures.
