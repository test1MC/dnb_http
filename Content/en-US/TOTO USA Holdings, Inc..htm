There's toilet humor and then there's TOTO USA Holdings, for whom toilets are no laughing matter. Operating through subsidiary TOTO USA, Inc., the company is one of the world's largest plumbing products manufacturers. It makes and distributes a full line of toilets, urinals, bath tubs, faucets, flush valves, and other commercial and decorative plumbing fixtures and fittings. Brand names include Aimes, Neorest, and Washlet. At its Georgia manufacturing facility, many of TOTO USA's products are designed and produced with water and energy conservation in mind. It sells through dealers in the US and Canada. The holding company is a subsidiary of Japanese plumbing giant TOTO, which entered the US market in 1989.
