A household name in Singapore and Malaysia, Yeo Hiap Seng (YHS) is a leading producer of carbonated and non-carbonated beverages, including teas, juices, and flavored waters. Its drinks are marketed under such brands as YEO's, Y-Generation, and JUSTEA. It also bottles Evian , Pepsi products and Red Bull under license. In addition, YHS makes packaged food, including canned curry, instant ramen noodles, and a plethora of sauces. YHS also owns investment properties, including condominiums, offices, warehouses, and farmland. Billionaire Ng Teng Fong, who's holdings include the Sino Land Company and Sino Hotels , controls the company though his Far East Organization.
