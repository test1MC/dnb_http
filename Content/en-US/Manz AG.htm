Machine power is electric at Manz (formerly Manz Automation). Through its subsidiaries, the company makes automation systems for the photovoltaic (solar cells that convert sunlight into electricity) and LCD manufacturing industries. Its technologies, some of which are marketed under its AICO brand, include machine control systems, robotics, laser technology, and related software used for processes like assembling crystalline silicon solar cells for solar panels or producing glass substrates used in LCD flat screens. The company also makes automation systems for other industrial processes, as well as for the life sciences sector. CEO Dieter Manz founded Manz AG in 1987.
