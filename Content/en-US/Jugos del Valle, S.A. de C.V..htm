This company processes enough apples each day to keep the doctor away for a long time -- and in case your MD still makes house calls, Jugos Del Valle processes oranges, mangos, and tomatoes too. Be it osteopath, witch doctor, or great-grandma with a mustard plaster, it sells shelf-stable and frozen juice and nectar all over the world. Operating four Mexican production plants, Jugos sends its products to food retailers (mainly supermarkets) through a network of distribution centers and warehouses. Its main brands include Del Valle, Del Valle Light, Del Valle Soya, and Frutsi. The Coca-Cola Company and Mexican bottler, Coca-Cola FEMSA , jointly bought the company in 2007 for some $370 million in cash.
