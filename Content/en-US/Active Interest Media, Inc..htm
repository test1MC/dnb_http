Active Interest Media is a leading publisher of enthusiast periodicals covering a range of special interest topics; it also produces related websites, books, and events. Publications include Better Nutrition , Log Home Living , and Vegetarian Times . Its Show Management unit produces boat shows in Florida, including the Fort Lauderdale International Boat Show. In addition, Active Interest sells martial arts videos and books, including several by the legendary Bruce Lee. CEO and president Efrem Zimbalist, a former CEO of Times Mirror , started Active Interest Media with private-equity firm Wind Point Partners in 2003.
