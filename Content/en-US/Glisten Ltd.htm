Glisten doesn't need to sugarcoat the truth as most people are already sweet on its offerings. The company makes confectionary, specializing in chocolate- and sugar-coated products. Starting with a lineup of chocolate-coated raisins, peanuts, and Brazil nuts, Glisten has expanded through acquisitions. The company now offers a wider range of sweets including jelly beans, toffees, foil-wrapped chocolate balls, fudge, and novelties. Glisten also makes edible decorations and specialty ingredients for food manufacturers, as well as nutritional snacks. Boasting about 10 manufacturing sites throughout the UK, the company is owned by Finnish food company Raisio .
