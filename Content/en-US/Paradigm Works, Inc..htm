Paradigm Works provides technical consulting services to help semiconductor companies and other parties design new application-specific integrated circuit (ASIC) and field-programmable gate array (FPGA) devices for use in CPU, LAN, SOC, analog, testing, storage, graphics, and telecommunications applications. The company also offers semiconductor intellectual property (IP) cores, chip-design elements that can be added piecemeal and integrated into a new device, protocol verification, chip construction, and foundry support. Branded software products include SystemVerilog FrameWorks (released in 2009), ReleaseWorks, and RegWorks Spec2Reg. Employee-owned Paradigm was founded in 2000 and has offices in the US.
