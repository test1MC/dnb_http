Vericor can really make your boat motor! Vericor Power Systems manufactures, sells, and services power generating equipment, from gas turbines for marine propulsion to marine power gensets, gensets for cogeneration and power generation, and packages for mechanical drive. In addition, Vericor Power Systems offers a slate of site support, technical training, and specialty equipment services for its products. Its roster of customers includes, by and large, the US Navy , shipbuilders ( ThyssenKrupp Marine Systems ), building materials manufacturers ( Sierra Pacific ), and pulp and paper producers ( Quebecor ). Vericor operates as an affiliate of German aerospace gas turbine engine maker MTU-Aero Engines.
