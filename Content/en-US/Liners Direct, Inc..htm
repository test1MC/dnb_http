Liners Direct wants a direct line to your shower stall, but it's no Norman Bates. The company manufactures custom acrylic liners for refurbishing bathtubs, showers, and bathroom walls. It also makes bathtubs, shower bases, and bathroom accessories such as shower seats, safety grab bars, soap dishes, caddies, and shelves. Although Liners Direct does not sell its products directly to the public, it does have a referral system to put homeowners in touch with dealers and contractors. President Jeff Conner and VP Mark Smith own the company. Mark and Sandi Smith founded Liners Direct in 1997.
