WorldTech Devices is a manufacturer of specialty computer keyboards. The company makes keyboards designed to increase productivity by incorporating application specific shortcuts on its products.  Products include keyboards for Apple computers (aiding photo and video editing), and PC keyboards specifically crafted for computer-aided design, photo and video editing, 3-D graphics, and Web design applications. WorldTech's keyboards support software packages from such companies as Adobe , Autodesk , Alibre , Avid Technology , Canopus , Pinnacle Systems , and UGS . The company was established in 2003. CEO Howard Chan is the majority shareholder of WorldTech.
