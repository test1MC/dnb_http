Coates International Ltd. (CIL) may be sparking the next industrial revolution. CEO George J. Coates founded CIL to develop his many patents, the most noteworthy being the Coates Spherical Rotary Valve (CSRV). The CSRV is designed to replace the century-old technology of the internal combustion engine's camshaft and poppet valve system. An engine equipped with the CSRV can run on different fuels while reducing emissions and increasing efficiency; the need for maintenance is also reduced. CIL licenses its CSRV engine technology to makers of heavy-duty vehicles, automobiles, and industrial engines. Major customer Almont Energy (Canada) took first delivery of CSRV engines in 2010.
