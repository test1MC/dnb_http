Oncolytics Biotech is bringing in viral mercenaries to fight in the battle against cancer. The development-stage company's REOLYSIN therapy, which utilizes a Respiratory Enteric Orphan virus (or reovirus) to treat a variety of advanced cancer tumors, is in late-stage clinical testing in the US for the treatment of head and neck cancers. Oncolytics is also conducting clinical trials in Canada and the UK. REOLYSIN is manufactured via a commercial supply agreement with Sigma-Aldrich 's SAFC division, which will manufacture the product throughout the clinical trials as well as once the drug receives regulatory approval. Oncolytics' technologies are based on discoveries made at the University of Calgary .
