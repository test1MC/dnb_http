It's no big deal if your sandwich baggie or lunchtime potato chips aren't perfectly sealed, but it's definitely a big deal if the instruments and supplies your surgeon uses aren't well-sealed and protected. That's where Perfecseal comes in. The company, a subsidiary of Bemis , makes a wide range of packaging material for the health care and pharmaceutical industries, including barrier films (Perfecflex), top web materials, pouches, bags, trays, lids, and printed materials (labels and foil laminations). Medi-Plus, a unit of Perfecseal, also makes sterilization and infection control packaging and accessories.
