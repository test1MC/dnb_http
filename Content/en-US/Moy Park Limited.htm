While many vie for pecking order, Moy Park stands at the top. The company is Northern Ireland's largest food processing company and a major provider of poultry products in the UK. It markets private-label fresh, locally farmed and processed chicken under brand names Moy Park, Kitchen Range Foods, and Albert Van Zoonen. Moy Park supplies retail chains and foodservice distributors from more than a dozen UK sites and in France and Holland. It owns O'Kane Poultry, which sells fresh, processed, and organic chicken and turkey under the O'Kane label. Moy Park was part of Brazil-based Marfrig , before Mafrig sold its European poultry unit to JBS SA , the world's #1 meat packager, in 2015 for $1.5 billion.

The company has manufacturing units in the UK (Northern Ireland and England), France, the Netherlands and the Republic of Ireland.

Moy Park has the capacity to process 240 million birds annually, in addition to producing around 200,000 tons of food per year. Its product portfolio is comprised of a variety of fresh meats, ready-to-eat meals, pre-cooked meals, breaded and frozen foods, vegetarian foods and desserts, supplied to major restaurant chains and retailers in the UK and Continental Europe. It has 35 million chickens at any given time and about 590 broiler farms.

Moy Park Limited is part of JBS EUROPE Division managed by Moy Park Holdings Europe Ltd. It operates in the segments of fresh meat and value-added products, such as ready-to-eat meals, pre-cooked meals, breaded and frozen meats, as well as vegetarian dishes and desserts.

The company's products are supplied to major restaurant chains and retailers in the United Kingdom and Continental Europe.

JBS Europe's net revenues in 2015 were up 2%, reflecting a 10% increase in sales volume in the domestic market, partially offset by the strengthening of the pound against the euro that year.

Under Marfrig's direction, Moy Park focuses on continuing to efficiently serve a growing number of large retailers. Its capacity is ample; Moy Park processes more than 200 million chickens and two million turkeys a year at its primary sites in Northern Ireland and England. As a result, it has managed to build a customer base that includes such major players as Tesco , Sainsbury's , Carrefour , ASDA , and Morrison Supermarkets .
