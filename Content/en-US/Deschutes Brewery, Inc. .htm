Named for a nearby river, Deschutes Brewery goes with the flow of its craft beer. Among its stable of beers are the Black Butte Porter, Obsidian Stout, and Big Rig brands. Headquartered in Bend, Oregon, the company's beers are distributed to beverage retailers throughout the US's Pacific Northwest, as well as in California, Alaska, and Nevada. The brewery also operates two brew pubs -- one in Bend, another in Portland. They serve up the usual pub grub -- appetizers, soups, salads, and a bevy of burgers. And, of course, its craft beer is on tap. Deschutes Brewery, which was founded by company president Gary Fish in 1988, is also targeting expansion to the East Coast (Roanoke, Virginia).
