Arcontech Group knows that real time is money. The company provides real-time market data to the financial community through its CityVision offering. CityVision collects data from multiple sources (exchanges and brokers in the investment banking community, as well as information providers such as Bloomberg and Thomson Reuters ) and distributes it to multiple destinations (Web, mobile, and corporate databases). The firm also offers online trading through its AXE product. In addition to real-time trade, order, and account management services, AXE offers integration for risk, pricing, CRM, and reporting, among other features. Arcontech's revenues primarily come from subscriptions, advertising, and sponsorships.
