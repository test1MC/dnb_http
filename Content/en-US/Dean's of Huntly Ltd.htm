Teatime is Dean's time. Dean's of Huntly is a leading producer of Scottish shortbread, those buttery, baked biscuits (as well as cheese oat nibbles, homestyle biscuits, and traditional oatcakes) that make a cup of Darjeeling complete. The company's range of traditional Scottish shortbread and biscuits features all the classic favorites from rounds, fingers, and petticoat tails to bitesize petit fours. Its customer base has grown to include such major food retailers as ASDA , Coop , Morrisons , Sainsbury , and Tesco . Gift baskets with its shortbread products are available in shops throughout the UK. Dean's shortbread is also distributed in Australia, Canada, China, France, Hong Kong, Japan, Russia, and the US.

Based in Aberdeenshire Scotland, Dean's of Huntly operates across UK, and has a presence in the US, Continental Europe, and Asia.

Dean's of Huntly exports it products to 30 countries, including India, China the US, and several European countries. Its sells its products via online and stores.

The company seeks to be universally recognized as the leading premium manufacturer of the best shortbread, biscuits, and other related hand-baked products.

The company was started in 1975 in Helen Dean's kitchen (to help raise funds for the local Huntly pipe band) and is still owned and operated by the Dean family.
