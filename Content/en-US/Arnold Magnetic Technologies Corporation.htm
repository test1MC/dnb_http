For some it's attractive and for others it's repellant, but that's the way it goes when you're magnetic. Comprising five strategic businesses, Arnold Magnetic manufactures and sells a variety of permanent and soft magnetic products. It also offers precision magnetic and non-magnetic assemblies. The company caters to more than 4,000 customers in the automotive, military and aerospace, medical, industrial, security, and telecommunications industries. It developed the first magnetic brush for Xerox Corp. , one of its largest customers. In 1997 the company became a subsidiary of SPS Technologies (which later became a subsidiary of Precision Castparts ), but was sold to private equity firm Audax Group in early 2005.
