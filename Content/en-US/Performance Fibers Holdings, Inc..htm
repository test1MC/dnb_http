Fibers must be stiff and strong to measure up to Performance Fibers' standards. The global supplier makes industrial-strength polyester fibers, fabrics, and adhesive finishes used in the automotive, military, and manufacturing industries to reinforce such products as tires, hoses, ropes, and seat belts. It manufactures fibers from both natural and synthetic products, including industrial specialty yarns (Nylon 6) and single end cords (PenTec fiber). The company has manufacturing, sales, marketing, and technical operations in North America, Europe, and Asia. Performance Fibers is owned by private investment firm Sun Capital Partners , which acquired the company from Honeywell International in 2004.
