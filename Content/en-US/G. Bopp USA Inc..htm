Kind of the US-based Little Bopper son of the Big Bopper in Europe, G. Bopp USA is the US subsidiary of G. Bopp + Co. AG, a Swiss manufacturer of wire mesh products used in a variety of manufacturing processes. The company's product line includes stainless steel wire cloth used for screen-printing circuit boards, wire cloth for sieving and sizing, steel mesh heat-resistant screens, and twilled dutch weave ultra-fine filter cloth. It also provides mesh tension measuring instruments, meters, and mesh counters to ensure high quality and consistent screen manufacturing.
