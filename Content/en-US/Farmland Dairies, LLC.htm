Cows keep Farmland Dairies flush with cash. The company (formerly Parmalat USA) produces fresh fluid and shelf-stable milk,  ice cream mixes, cream, half and half, and other dairy products. It also offers teas, fruit drinks, and juices. The company's brand names include Farmland Dairies, Welsh Farms (dairy products, juice, and iced tea), and Clinton (iced tea). Its rBST- (synthetic growth hormone) free lineup is distributed in New York, New Jersey, New England, and the southeastern US. Farmland fat-free milk lines, called the Special Request and Skim Plus, are touted as best-sellers in the New York and New Jersey metro areas. The company has been part of Mexico's Grupo Lala /Lala USA since 2009.
