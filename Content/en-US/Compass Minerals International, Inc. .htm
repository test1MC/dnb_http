Salt is Compass Minerals' true north. The company is one of the largest salt producers in North America. Its salt products include rock, evaporated, and solar salt and are used for applications such as water softening, road deicing, and food preparation. Highway deicing salt -- generally sold to state, province, or municipal governments -- accounts for almost half of its annual sales. Compass Minerals operates through subsidiaries North American Salt , Great Salt Lake Minerals (a top producer of the crop nutrient sulfate of potash), Sifto Canada, and Salt Union (based in the UK). It has 11 production and packaging facilities and operates a number of salt mines in Canada, the UK, and the US.

The company operates 12 production and packaging facilities, including the largest rock salt mine in the world in Goderich, Ontario, Canada, and the largest rock salt mine in the UK (in Winsford, Cheshire.) It also has salt assets in Chile.

Compass Minerals' solar evaporation facility in Ogden, Utah, is the largest solar salt production site in North America.

In 2012, the US accounted for 66% of the company's revenues.

Compass Minerals is the parent company of seven production and marketing companies engaged in salt and/or specialty fertilizer production.

North American Salt Company sells mined rock salt for highway and consumer deicing, and high-grade branded and private label mineral products for consumer and industrial uses.

Sifto is one of Canada's favorite brands, is well-known for its high-quality food salt, water care minerals, agricultural minerals, and many other consumer and industrial salt and mineral products. Itoperates the world's largest rock salt mine, making it a leading producer of salt for highway and consumer deicing.

Great Salt Lake Minerals Corporation is North America's leading producer of sulfate of potash (SOP), a vital specialty fertilizer for fruits, vegetables, tree nuts and other high-value crops.

Salt Union is the UK's largest supplier of rock salt and specialty products used for highway deicing.

DeepStore provides records management services to businesses throughout the United Kingdom. Its primary storage facility is a salt mine in Winsford, Cheshire.

Pristiva focuses on the North American salt water pool market.

Big Quill supplies sulfate of potash for the agriculture and turf markets and higher-purity SOP for specialty applications.

Compass Minerals provides highway deicing salt in the US, Canada, and the UK. Magnesium chloride is also sold in the US and Canada to deice highways and stabilize unpaved roads. The company's consumer and industrial products, which includes packaged deicing, water care, animal nutritional, and culinary salt, are sold throughout North America.

Compass Minerals' specialty fertilizer products are sold throughout North America and to export markets, including Latin America, Japan, Australia and New Zealand.

Revenue decreased by 15% in 2012 due to a drop in salt sales volumes. Salt sales volumes in 2012 decreased by 23% led by a decline in highway deicing products sales and a drop in consumer and industrial sales volumes, both due to the significantly milder than average winter weather in 2012 in the markets the company serves. In addition, Compass Minerals' experienced unprecedented preseason demand in 2011, while sales volumes in 2012 were lower from the effects of the 2011-2012 winter season which left customers with higher inventory levels and therefore, reduced the need for early fill orders in the latter half of 2012.

Net income decreased by 40% in 2012 due ot lower revenues, offset by a decrease in Selling, General and Administrative Expenses primarily due to lower marketing expenses; and a decrease in interest expense due to lower average interest rates on the company's outstanding debt, resulting from the expiration of interest rate swap agreements which fixed interest rates in the first quarter of 2011 and the refinancing of term loans in May 2012.

Except for revenue slumps in 2009 and 2012, company has seen an upward trend in revenues from 2008 to 2011. The revenue declines in 2009 and 2012 were primarily caused by milder than normal winter weather in North American markets.

To enhance Compass Minerals' deicing salt operations in Canada, subsidiary Sifto Canada acquired additional property in 2012 adjacent to the Goderich salt mine in Ontario. The C$2.8 million buy provided the company with low-cost rock salt storage capacity and additional land for increased ship-loading. Compass Minerals also expanded the mine, boosting annual production capability from 6.5 million tons to about 9 million tons.

That year, the company purchased the mining rights to 100 million tons of salt reserves in the Chilean Atacama Desert. It invested $6.5 million for the rights and will pay a small per-ton production royalty to the private seller if extraction commences in the future. The new assets further diversify Compass Minerals' rock salt mineral reserves and could enhance the company's North American highway deicing business as well as its consumer and industrial business, which processes rock salt to make many of its products. The acquisition secures Compass Minerals' access to one of the lowest-cost mining regions in the Western Hemisphere. It complements its existing salt businesses and provides the potential to further expand our highway deicing service footprint.

In 2011 Compass Minerals acquired Big Quill Resources, a Canadian potash producer, for $56 million. Saskatchewan-based Big Quill's high-purity sulfate of potash is used for non-agricultural specialty applications as well as nutrients for crops and turf. The deal strengthens and grows Compass Minerals' specialty fertilizer segment.

Beyond its minerals operations, the company has a unique and profitable way to use up all that leftover space in their old salt mines: records storage. Subsidiary DeepStore Records Management offers records management services to businesses throughout the UK. Its primary storage facility is located 500 feet below ground in the company's 160-year-old rock salt mine in Winsford, Cheshire, and offers the only secure facility of its kind in Britain.
