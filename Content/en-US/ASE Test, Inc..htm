Chip makers looking to ace their tests often call on ASE Test. Through subsidiaries in Taiwan, Malaysia, and the US, the company provides integrated circuit (IC) assembly, testing, and packaging services for customers that have included Atmel , LSI , NVIDIA , and VIA Technologies . ASE Test tests logic, mixed-signal, and memory ICs; performs wafer probing; and packages ICs for end users in the communications, automotive, and consumer electronics industries, among others.
