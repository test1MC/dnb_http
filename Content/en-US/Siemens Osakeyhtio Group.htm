Siemens Osakeyhtiö (SOY) provides a regional presence for electronics and industrial engineering giant Siemens AG . Its businesses cover wide-ranging areas including Communications, Industry (automation & electrification), Business Services (IT), Energy (generation/transmission/distribution), Building Technologies (HVAC & fire detection), Transportation (rail systems) and Health Care (imaging systems). SOY customers include insurer KELA , the Finnish Defence Forces, The Finnish Rail Administration, The Finnish Road Administration, and paper company UPM-Kymmene . Siemens established a subsidiary in Finland in 1898. Siemens Osakeyhtiö also oversees its subsidiaries located in Estonia, Latvia, and Lithuania.
