Killing bugs, sterilizing medical instruments, and keeping your car warm -- the products made by INEOS Oxide help with all of that. A member of the INEOS Group of companies, INEOS Oxide makes ethylene oxide (EO) and propylene oxide (PO), and their derivatives, as well as an array of solvents and specialty chemicals. EO is a highly reactive building block for products such as agrochemicals, antifreeze, detergents and shampoos, pharma-care products, and synthetic lubricants. PO is used in polyurethanes, industrial solvents, synthetic lubricants, and textile surfactants. INEOS Oxide works through third-party agreements with companies like Borealis , Dow Chemical , Praxair , and Momentive Performance Materials .

The company has manufacturing sites in Belgium, Germany, France, the UK, and the US.

INEOS Oxide produces a broad range of specialty and intermediate chemicals, primarily those derived from ethylene and propylene.

The company has long-term partnerships with about 13 third-party companies. INEOS Oxide is located within the world's second-largest petrochemical zone and has access to raw materials via pipeline, sea, road, and rail. It also helps companies start new operations in Europe.

The company's products are found in shaving cream formulations (including those used by major global brands), cleaning processes, pharmaceuticals, and other industry applications.
