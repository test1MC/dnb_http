Sensient Colors can color your world any color you want. The company makes natural and synthetic colors and dyes for food, beverage, pharmaceutical, cosmetic, personal, and home care product manufacturers. It also offers industrial dyes and display imaging dyes, as well as inkjet and specialty inks. Sensient Colors, which has created some 1,000 color blends, has manufacturing plants worldwide, including in Brazil, Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Mexico, Switzerland, the UK, and the US. Its colors and dyes are made from both chemical and natural sources, such as beta-carotene and turmeric. The company is a subsidiary of Sensient Technologies and accounts for about a third of its sales.

Sensient Colors reported 2012 revenue of $494 million, up just under 1% from the prior year on higher sales from both its food and non-food colors. Products for the food and beverage industry account for the largest portion of its sales.
