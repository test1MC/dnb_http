If you can walk through it or slam it shut, Designer Doors makes it. The company designs and builds solid wood entry doors, garage doors, shutters, and gates. It specializes in luxury doors and offers one-of-a kind handcrafted creations as well as limited editions of European-inspired and American panel designs. Its Woodscapes brand offers less expensive options. Doors are available in a variety of styles and can be made from wood, glass, or metal. Decorative steel hardware also is available. The company's network of professional design associates sell products directly to architects, builders, and homeowners throughout the US. Kent Forsland founded Designer Doors in 1987. The company is employee-owned.
