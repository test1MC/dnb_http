Celsius Holdings wants consumers to enjoy the taste of burning calories. The company develops, markets, and distributes nutritional drinks that claim to burn calories, raise metabolism, and boost energy. Its first product, Celsius, is a canned sparkling beverage that comes in a variety of flavors and is marketed as an alternative to soda, coffee, and traditional energy drinks. Although it has undergone independent clinical studies, results have not been US FDA approved. Its products, which also include non-carbonated Celsius green tea drinks and single-serving powder mix packets that can be added to water, are manufactured by third-party co-packers. Celsius Holdings was founded in 2004 under the name Elite FX.
