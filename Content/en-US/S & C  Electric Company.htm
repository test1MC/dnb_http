Products from S & C Electric help keep the power on. The employee-owned company makes switching and protection products for electric power transmission and distribution. S&C offers a broad range of engineering, laboratory, and testing services for electric utilities and commercial, industrial, and institutional power systems. Its devices can switch circuits, minimize damage to equipment, and automatically reroute power when there is an outage. Products include insulators, fuses, transformer protectors, fittings, switchgear, and software. ComEd , Intel , the US Army , and Walt Disney World have served as customers.

S & C has operations in Brazil, China, Canada, Mexico, the UK, and the US. In the US it has engineering offices and manufacturing facilities in Alameda, California; Chicago, Illinois; Duvall, Washington; Franklin, Wisconsin; and Orlando Florida.

S & C targets electric utilities and the wind energy, solar energy, data center, semiconductor, commercial, and industrial markets. It sells its products through its sales locations, authorized distributors, and sales representatives such as Chas. W. Ashby Co. and Peterson Company.

S & C is counting on growth in the alternative energy market to fuel growth in its own operations. The company is developing self-healing smart grid distribution automation technology, essentially using rules-based software in power distribution devices in the field to track system conditions and restore service when there is a fault. The company's Advanced Technology Center in Chicago gives it the ability to test products in-house. S&C has also teamed up with the likes of Oracle , Intergraph , and Alstom Grid to develop new products for the smart grid, primarily focused on system interoperability.

In 2012 S & C and Doble Engineering (a global leader in diagnostic instruments and consulting services) formed a strategic alliance to expand S&C's asset management capabilities, enabling S & C to monitor all critical equipment in an electrical substations.

S & C was founded in 1911.
