Uniguest wants to make your hotel room a little more informative. The company makes software that develops, installs, and manages Internet kiosks in the hotel industry. The kiosks provide hotel guests with information such as where to eat and what to do.  In addition, they provide services such as e-mail, maps, and directions, as well as a boarding pass printing station that allows users to print boarding passes and check in to flights remotely. The company also provides in-room guest directories to the hotel industry. Uniguest provides services in more than 2,500 hotels across America.
