Sonics supplies circuit designs to speed SoC creation. The fabless semiconductor company designs and licenses interconnect circuitry used in system-on-a-chip (SoC) devices. It touts its SMART Interconnect products as simplifying and shortening the design cycle for SoC chip makers. Other products include memory schedulers and integration software. The company's customers include such tech heavyweights as Broadcom , Samsung Electronics , Texas Instruments , and Toshiba . Sonics was established in 1996. The company has offices and sales representatives in Armenia, France, Germany, Japan, Taiwan, South Korea, Israel, and the US.
