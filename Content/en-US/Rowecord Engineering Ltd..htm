To connect with customers, Rowecord Engineering helps build bridges -- literally. The company is a manufacturer of steel beams and columns, footbridges, railtrack bridges, platework fabrications, portal frame structures, and trusswork. The company provides services including design, engineering, manufacturing, shop blasting, and structural erection to make its fabricated steel products. Rowecord Engineering has served such customers as Tata Steel Europe , GEC Alsthom, and Schal International. It has provided materials for projects including blast furnaces, commercial construction, and suspension bridges. The company, founded in 1967, has three plants in South Wales.
