Continental International rules as the Lord of the O-rings. Operating through joint manufacturing ventures, the company manufactures O-rings, seals, bumpers, isolators, grommets, rubber cords, switch protectors, valve stems, and related industrial machinery supplies for OEMs and distributors worldwide. More than 300,000 O-rings, seals, and allied rubber items are distributed by Continental International. Its lineup is used in brakes to four wheel drive and cooling systems, consumer lighting goods, and fractional motors. The company touts services including micro-encapsulation and Teflon coating, bar coding, and customized stocking systems, too. President Ron Clem founded the company as Continental Seal in 1970.
