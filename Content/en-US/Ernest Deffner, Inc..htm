Ernest Deffner has built a business helping old accordions sound like new again. The company, established in 1934, operates an accordion repair shop. It also sells accessories and spare parts, as well as repaired and refurbished used accordions. Affiliated companies Titano Accordion and Pancordion manufacture new accordions, and Ernest Deffner Publications distributes more than 6,000 titles of accordion sheet music. Ernest's wife, Faithe, is director of the accordian company.
