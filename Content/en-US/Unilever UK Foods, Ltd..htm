No longer known as Unilever Bestfoods , Unilever UK Foods is among the country's biggest food manufacturers . The division of Unilever UK , one of the worlds' largest suppliers of consumer goods on almost everyone's shopping list, produces a hefty line of food products. Core categories range from tea to dressings and condiments, soup mixes and cooking sauces, sold under more than two dozen brand names. Its brand-rich portfolio includes arguably the world's most famous mustard, Colman's, butter impersonator I can't believe it's not Butter!, weight loss staple Slim-Fast, and 1886 beefy Brit classic, Bovril. Operating through 11 locations, the food maker's products account for about one-third of Unilever's UK sales.
