China Packaging Group's strength lies in some pretty sturdy paperboard. Through three China-based companies held by wholly-owned subsidiary Evercharm Holdings, the group is the largest paper packaging manufacturer in the country, capable of producing about 440 million sq. meters of corrugated paperboard per year. It also processes paperboard into cartons used to package chemical, consumer electronics, food and beverage, household appliance, industrial machinery, and pharmaceutical products. Customers include Chinese subsidiaries of Panasonic and Samsung and top Chinese beverage maker Hangzhou Wahaha Group. In 2010 China Packaging, formerly a public shell company, completed a reverse acquisition with Evercharm.
