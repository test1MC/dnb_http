Sinopec Yangzi Petrochemical manufactures basic and intermediate chemicals for use by the textile, automotive, and agricultural industries. The company operates about 35 manufacturing and refining facilities, where it makes refined petroleum products, polyolefins (polyethelene and polypropylene), polyester materials, basic organic chemicals, and finish oils.
