Metrocorp Holdings has a thing for Beantown and Philly. The company publishes Boston Magazine and Philadelphia Magazine, two glossy monthly publications about about city life and events. The sister titles are best known for their "Best of" lists and regional features. The company also publishes related Web content, and ancillary titles such as Pennsylvania Home and Philadelphia Wedding , as well as New England Travel , Boston Homes , and Boston Weddings. Metrocorp Holdings was founded as Philadelphia Magazine: The City of Industry by the Trades League of Philadelphia in 1908.
