Vines planted in 1825 by Franciscan monks was the beginning of Sebastiani Vineyards. The storied firm produces middle-market California table wines. It turning out some 800,000 cases of vino annually. The vineyard's labels include its Cherryblock label, along with single-vineyard and multi-vineyard blends sold under the Sebastiani name. Long owned and operated by the Sebastiani family (Italian immigrant Samuele Sebastiani bought the vineyard in 1904), the firm was sold in 2008 to Bill Foley, the billionaire founder and chairman of title insurance giant, Fidelity National Financial , who folded it into Foley Family Wines , his growing California wine empire.
