American Blimp Corporation (ABC) is gaseous, and that's a good thing. The company manufactures the FAA-approved Lightship and SPECTOR helium-filled airships. ABC's A-60+ Lightship series -- translucent skin with internal lights -- flies commercially in Australia, Europe, South Africa, and North America, carrying advertising messages day or night. Major customers have included MetLife , Goodyear , and Sanyo North America . The SPECTOR series (A-150 and A-170) is used by military and law enforcement agencies for airborne surveillance. The airship gondolas can seat from four to nine passengers. American Blimp president James Thiele founded the company in 1987.
