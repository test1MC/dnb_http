Mark of the Unicorn sounds like a Tenacious D song spoofing Ronnie James Dio, so you know this company rocks out. Doing business as MOTU, the company is a manufacturer and distributor of computer hardware and software used for music and voice recording, mixing, and editing. Its Digital Performer is a package for Musical Instruments Digital Interface (MIDI) and audio sequencing. Other products include digital audio interfaces, audio sequencing software, sampling and orchestral add-ons, and a video and audio capture interface. MOTU's hardware and software are used by musicians, composers, and video game developers. Its products are distributed in more than 40 countries. MOTU was founded in 1981.
