This company gives you full authority to send out warm and fuzzy feelings. Carte Blanche Greetings is one of the leading makers of greeting cards and related products in the UK. Its cards are sold primarily under the Me To You brand; the company also markets stationary and other gift items. Carte Blanche Greetings is perhaps best known, however, for its Tatty Teddy plush bears. The company distributes products through retailers in 50 countries worldwide, as well as through its Web site. Chairman Stephen Haines founded Carte Blanche Greetings in 1987.
