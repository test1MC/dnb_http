Vintage Grapevine is the holding company for the Cosentino Winery, which produces ultra premium wines made mainly from California's famed Napa Valley grapes. The winery produces wine at three sites: Cosentino, CE2V, and Crystal Valley Cellars. Cosentino and CE2V produce wines that can cost $30 or $40 dollars a bottle or more; whereas, Crystal Valley, in California's Central Valley, is somewhat less pricey with one label, Ol' Red, costing about $12 per bottle.
