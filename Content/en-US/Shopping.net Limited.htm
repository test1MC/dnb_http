Shopping.net is a web marketplace and search engine that casts a net over thousands of shopping websites for features and prices on everything from financial products and fashion to electronics and vacations. The company is based in London, but operates websites in the US and 9 other European markets, including Belgium, Denmark, France, Germany, Italy, Spain and Sweden. The company is a subsidiary of digital marketing services company Conversant .
