North American Membership Group (NAMG) wants to get on your mailing list. The group runs 11 subscription-based "clubs" such as Cooking Pleasures , PGA Tour Partners , and the History Channel Club . It publishes each club's bimonthly magazine and does all the marketing, promotional, and organizational services, including Web sites, e-newsletters, arranging members-only events, and purchasing opportunities (such as discounts on fishing equipment for North American Fisherman members). NAMG clubs boast nearly 5 million members. Investment firm Pilot Group, which owns other consumer media properties, bought NAMG  from Doughty Hanson & Co. in 2007.
