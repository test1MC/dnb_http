Vista Paint provides a vast array of paints, wallpaper, and window coverings. The privately owned company distributes its products through a network of nearly 50 company-owned stores located in California and Nevada. Its paints and coatings are used on residential, commercial, and industrial projects by architecture, decorator, property management, and new-home construction firms, as well as do-it-yourself consumers. The company also offers spray equipment rental, sales, and repair services. Vista Paints is a retailer of Benjamin Moore paint products.

The company operates stores in California (including in Cathedral City, Encinitas, Fullerton, Orange, Huntington Beach, Laguna Niguel, Mission Hills, and Santa Clarita) and also in Nevada (Las Vegas).

Vista Paint operates a chain of 48 stores.

The independent Benjamin Moore paint retailer provides paint and related products to a range of customers, including architects, builders, contractors, designers, homeowners, and property managers.

Vista Paint was founded by president Eddie Fischer, who still owns the company, and his partner, Walt Jacobson. The company began operating in 1956 and was incorporated in 1960 as Grove Paints.
