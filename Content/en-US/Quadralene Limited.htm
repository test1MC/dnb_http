Keeping things clean is all in a day's work for Quadralene. The company manufactures and supplies cleaning detergents and disinfectants throughout the UK for several markets, including automotive, dental, food, healthcare, and micro-electronic engineering. Brands include the Teknon, Biocleanse, and Dentisan trademarks. The company also supplies products for private labels and exports to markets in Africa, Asia, Europe, and Canada in North America. Although its traditional markets are in automotive and janitorial cleaning, the company has broadened its healthcare and dental product lines.

The company and offers its services in more than 50 countries.

Quadralene offers its services in industries such as healthcare, industrial and retail. The company's customers included UK Valet Supplies and The Dental Practice.

The company is focusing on expanding its product portfolio.

In a move to counter the effects of the economic recession, the company has been branching out into new markets, especially in the healthcare industry. Quadralene's turnover in 2010 was reported as £3.2 million (about $5.1 million). Healthcare products contributed 30% of its sales. The company expected to grow at least 50% in 2011, and to make £20 million ($32.2 million) by 2015.

In 2012 the company launched its Biocleanse Ultra brand, part of the Dentisan product portfolio for medical device surfaces for dental practices. Biocleanse Ultra is a new version of the company's original Biocleanse; it is a wide-spectrum microbiocide based on alcohol-free technology with a pH neutral formulation. The cleaner and disinfectant has been developed to fight against mycobacteria, including TB, viruses such as Hepatitis B and C, HIV, fungi, and yeast.
