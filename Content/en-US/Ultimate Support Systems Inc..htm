It aspires to be the ultimate in stands, bags, and instrument cases for musicians. In addition to producing tripods for lighting and speakers, Ultimate Support Systems makes guitar, keyboard, microphone, and studio monitor stands. The company also designs and manufactures bags and cases for guitars, keyboards, and drums. Ultimate Support Systems outsources the majority of its manufacturing and assembly functions and sells through its catalog in the US and internationally. Founded in 1977 by Darrell Schoenig (a Christian musician and hang glider), who applied the technology of lightweight hang-glider tubing to music stands, the company is owned by Mike Belitz, who purchased it in 2007.
