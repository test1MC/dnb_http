Rembrandt Enterprises (doing business as Rembrandt Foods) has less to do with old European art masters and more to do with the modern American mastery of the art of egg production. As an egg producer and processor, the company sells egg products to the foodservice and food ingredients industries. Through its affiliates, it also produces egg ingredients for use in sports food supplements. Rembrandt, which buys grains from local farmers and produces its own eggs, operates facilities in Rembrandt, Iowa, and elsewhere in the Midwest. In 2009, the company expanded its operations after acquiring the assets of competitor Golden Oval Eggs.
