Seagate Systems Limited (also known as Xyratex) designs and makes storage subsystems and controllers, as well as PCI cards that analyze and manage network data. The company sells primarily to computer hardware vendors, such as NetApp , that sell the products under their own brands. It also develops products for disk drive manufacturers, such as EMC and Western Digital . Its disk drive products include production test systems, servo writers, and disk automation systems. Xyratex has been a subsidiary of Seagate Technology since the 2014 acquisition.

With the $374 million acquisition by Seagate in 2014 Xyratex began operating as a Seagate Technology. Xyratex was expected to provide up to $600 million in revenue a year to Seagate.

Xyratex has operations in Asia, Canada, Mexico, the UK, and the US.
