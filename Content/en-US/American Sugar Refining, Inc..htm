Business is sweet for American Sugar Refining (ASR). The company is the world's largest sugar refiner with a 6 million ton annual capacity. It owns plants in New York, Maryland, Louisiana, and California. ASR also owns Canada's s sugar largest refinery along with plants in Mexico and Belize, England, and Portugal. The company sells its sugars in the Americas, Caribbean, Europe, the Middle East, India, and Asia. In addition to its line of industrial, food service, and consumer sugar products, ASR also offers stevia, aspartame, and saccharine-based sweeteners. Florida Crystals and the Sugar Cane Growers Cooperative of Florida own the company.
