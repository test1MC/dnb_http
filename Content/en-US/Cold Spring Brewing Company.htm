The Cold Spring Brewing Company (formerly Gluek Brewing) keeps its cool. Using water from the Cold Spring in central Minnesota (which is so pure it needs no filtering), the company makes John Henry, Gluek, and Cold Spring ice brews, lagers, ales, and pilsners, along with hard beverages such as Cold Spring Hard Lemonade and Kingston Bay Mojito. In addition it makes non-alcoholic, vitamin and mineral fortified fruit drinks under the Guadalupe brand. The company bottles its pure spring water with nothing added under the Cold Spring Mineral Water label.
