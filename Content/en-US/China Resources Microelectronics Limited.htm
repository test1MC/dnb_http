China Resources Microelectronics operates a semiconductor contract manufacturing plant, or foundry, near Shanghai. The business focuses on the semiconductor market in China -- it provides manufacturing services for CMOS logic, mixed-signal, high-voltage, non-volatile memory and electrically erasable programmable read-only memory integrated circuits (ICs). China Resources Microelectronics also offers services in upstream IC design, downstream testing, and packaging. The company was established in 1997 and serves both domestic and overseas customers. China Resources Logic owns about 73% of China Resources Microelectronics.
