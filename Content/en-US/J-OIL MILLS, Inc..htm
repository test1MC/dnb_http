J-OIL MILLS would surely like to slide into the top spot as the edible oil producer in Japan and become a global player as well. Founded in 2002 to capture the fat and oil market, the company researches and develops, produces, processes, and sells about 600 products, including oils, fat, foods, and starches. It extracts its oils from soybeans, grapes, peanuts, and walnuts, as well as from other fruits and grains. The company is an affiliate company of Japan's largest seasoning maker, Ajinomoto . As for its aspirations, J-OIL is the #2 edible-oil producer and has begun exploring foreign markets.
