All of South Africa is a-flutter over Aspen Pharmacare's strides in treatments for AIDS and other ailments. The company is Africa's largest generic pharmaceutical manufacturer and a major supplier of branded pharmaceutical and health care products in Africa and more than 150 other international markets. Its products include pain medication, vitamins, cough and cold medicines, laxatives, medicine for indigestion, and aids to help people quit smoking and lose weight. Aspen Pharmacare also led the way in developing the first anti-retroviral medication in South Africa for AIDS treatment. The company, which has 24 manufacturing facilities on six continents, is expanding its international reach.
