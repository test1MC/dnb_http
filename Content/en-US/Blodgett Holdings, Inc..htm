Funny name, serious cooking equipment. Blodgett Holdings, through G.S. Blodgett, makes commercial cooking equipment including convection and conveyor ovens, ranges, fryers, and charbroilers. Blodgett claims the top spot as the world's leading manufacturer of commercial ovens, and has customers in the fast-food chain, hotel, hospital, institution, restaurant industries and serves both small businesses and large corporations. The company's products are sold under the Blodgett (ovens and ranges), MagiKitch'n (charbroilers and grills), and Pitco (fryers and filter systems) brands. In 2001 Middleby acquired Blodgett from Maytag Corporation .
