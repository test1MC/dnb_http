Sdix provides life sciences test, design, and development products and services. Offerings include antibodies, reagents, bio-processing, and custom assay design and development. Its products are used by and embedded in the products of pharmaceutical, biotech, diagnostic, and biomedical research customers around the world. The company (which was acquired by OriGene Technologies in 2013) also offers detection kits, for tasks such as food pathogen testing, to the food and beverage manufacturing and agricultural and agro-science industries. Companies that Sdix has forged research partnerships with have included BD Diagnostics , McDonald's , Monsanto , and Johnson & Johnson .
