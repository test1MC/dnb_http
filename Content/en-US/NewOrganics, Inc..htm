New Organics' master plan is to keep Americans healthy via nutritious ingredients. The company is one of the largest suppliers of non-genetically modified, certified-organic bulk ingredients and foods in North America. Founded in 1981 as American Health & Nutrition, the company provides organically grown barley, flax, peas, rye, soybeans, spelt, and wheat. The ingredients firm also offers edible oils and beans, grains, flours, seeds, protein powders, and specialized fibers and sweeteners. New Organics' customers include food manufacturers, co-packers, dietary supplement makers, ingredient distributors, and ranchers (for animal feed).
