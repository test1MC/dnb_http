Whether it's fact or fiction you seek, Macmillan Publishers can deliver. The UK-based unit of German publisher Verlagsgruppe Georg von Holtzbrinck is an international publisher of books and journals, operating in some 80 countries. Macmillan Publishers has units and imprints devoted to academic and scholarly books and business and training materials, as well as fiction and non-fiction publishing. Among its more notable holdings is scientific arm Nature Publishing Group, publisher of the journal Nature ; the respected title was founded in 1869. The company also offers production, fulfillment, and distribution services through its Macmillan Publishing Solutions business.
