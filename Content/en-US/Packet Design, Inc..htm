Packet Design develops hardware and software used to manage Internet protocol networks. Broadband and cable system operators, enterprises, and communications service providers use its products to monitor and optimize network performance. The company's Route Explorer and Traffic Explorer appliances collect and analyze routing path and network traffic information, allowing engineers to diagnose and repair problems. Packet Design has technology partnerships with Cisco Systems and Hewlett-Packard . The company has raised private equity funding from Advanced Technology Ventures , Allegis Capital , Juniper Networks , Masthead Venture Partners, and Mayfield Fund , among others.
