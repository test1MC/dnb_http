Just who makes that store-brand tile cleaner or floor polish you can buy at the corner market? Well, it's certainly not the retailer itself, that's for sure. Stanson Corporation manufactures more than 100 powders and liquids that it then markets to retail outlets for their private-label, store-brand products. The company's products run the gamut of cleaning products from hardwood floor cleaners to dish detergents to fabric softeners. Formed in 1949 and still owned and run by the founding Holuba family, Stanson Corporation is based in New Jersey and also has operations in Canada.
