Whether your stomach is grumbling from hunger or you have a restaurant starving for customers, Restaurant.com aims to please. The company operates an online directory of 18,000 eating places nationwide. Users can obtain restaurant information including menus, wine lists, and chef's bios, as well as search and filter results according to criteria, such as cuisine (Japanese, Italian) and atmosphere (romantic, family). The site also allows users to make reservations. The company additionally offers some 45,000 daily gift certificate options, as well as pre-paid gift cards and other promotional products for meals at participating restaurants. Restaurant.com was founded by CEO Cary Chessick in 1999.
