For companies that need a jump start in getting their projects off the ground, contract manufacturer Jumpsource offers global manufacturing of mechanical and electro-mechanical parts in China. Jumpsource produces customized mechanical and electro-mechanical components, fabrications, assemblies, and finished goods at competitive prices through its Asian manufacturing plants. In addition to its Shanghai-based engineering, project management, and quality assurance teams, the company operates a 105,000 sq. ft. contract manufacturing and assembly operation, complete with state-of-the-art quality laboratory and dedicated welding and machining facilities.
