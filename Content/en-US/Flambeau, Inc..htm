One word -- just one -- sums up Flambeau: Plastics. Founded in 1947, the company produced a frog-shaped fishing lure, and eventually found its future in plastics. Flambeau designs and manufactures a slew of injection- and blow-molded thermoplastic components and containers. Flambeau produces products for retail, Duncan branded toy yoyos to storage tools, mailboxes, floral containers, hunting decoys, and tackle boxes. Its automotive offerings include fluid systems, and its packaging and storage systems division, divided boxes and carrying cases. Flambeau is part of the privately owned Nordic Group of Companies.

Operating through facilities in the US, Mexico, and the UK, the company's creative capacity is nearly unlimited.
