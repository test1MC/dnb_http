Keeping workers safe is what Tronex is all about. The company makes a variety of disposable personal protection products primarily for the health care industry. Products include medical gloves, disposable and flame-resistant apparel, respiration masks, and safety goggles. Hospitals, nursing homes, dental offices, and laboratories make up a large part of the company's customer base. However, Tronex also develops products and equipment for the food service, industrial, retail, hospitality, and government sectors (federal and state). Its research and development division focuses on areas such as plastics, resins, and molecular bonding in order to bring new products to market in the US and internationally.
