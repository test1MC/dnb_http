Spectra Logic answers the phone when companies call for backup. The company provides tape- and disk-based data backup libraries, upgrades, and archive systems. Customers in the government, education, media, health care, finance, and energy sectors use its disk arrays and tape libraries to protect their data. The company creates customized storage systems that integrate third-party hardware and software with its own products. Spectra Logic maintains partnerships with major vendors that have included Avid Technology , Microsoft , NetApp , Symantec , and XIO .

Spectra Logic has operations in Australia, Canada, India, Korea, New Zealand, the UK, and the US.

The company's products include innovative features, such as its tape libraries with LTO-6 tape drives, integrated data encryption, media health monitoring, and low power consumption.

Growing its global reach, in 2014 Spectra Logic formed a worldwide relationship with Scale Logic to provide broadcast and media customers with HyperFS, a high-performance, cost-effective SAN application platform with tightly-integrated HSM-based archival workflow options.

In 2013 the company signed a distribution agreement with CMS Distribution, the largest independent distributor of IT data storage products and solutions in the UK and Ireland to expand its presence in Europe, the Middle East, and Africa.

Throughout 2012 Spectra Logic increased its global presence by investing in Australia and New Zealand. As part of this strategy, the company heightened its presence in Sydney and invested in sales support and technical staff. The presence will enable the company to offer enhanced, efficient local support and expertise to a growing market.

On the product development side of the business, in 2013 Spectra Logic launched a mobile app for its SpectraEDGE Channel Partner.
