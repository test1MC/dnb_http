CSI Compressco, a subsidiary of TETRA Technologies , understands when you've gone to the well once too often. The company sells, leases, and services its GasJack brand of compressor units, which are designed to increase the production of marginal natural gas and oil wells. The firm produces four types of GasJack, including both cold and warm weather packages. It also sells the E-pumper compressor unit, which allows users to remotely operate wells. CSI Compressco operates in the US, Canada, and Mexico, focusing on areas with the geological characteristics that best suit its technology.
