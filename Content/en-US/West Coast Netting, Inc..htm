West Coast Netting makes sure no one has to work without a net. The company manufactures nets for sports (volleyball, baseball, soccer, golf), construction, industrial usage, and amusement parks. It also makes netting and twine products for aerospace and fishing applications and suspension bridges for amusement parks. Some of West Coast Netting's customers have included Disney World , Knott's Berry Farm, and Universal Studios , as well as the Los Angeles Dodgers , Stanford University , Arizona Cardinals, and Duke University . In 2009, West Coast Netting netted a deal with the Defense Logistics Agency in Richmond, Virginia, for paradrogue canopies. The company was founded in 1951.
