This Michael Angelo didn't create the Pieta, or even a pita, but they do make a fine chicken piccata. Michael Angelo's Gourmet Food produces fresh and frozen Italian entrees, snacks, appetizers, and desserts, as well as pasta and pizza sauces. The company, which has no warehouse, claims to cook its meals from scratch every day and ship them to stores within 36 hours. Its products are available in major US food retailers, specialty food stores, and discount stores, including Albertson's , BJ's Wholesale Club , Publix , Whole Foods , Safeway , and SAMS CLUB . Michael Angelo's was founded in 1982 by CEO Michael A. Renna. The company was family owned and operated until its 2017 acquisition by private equity firm Advent International .

Michael Angelo's specializes in producing, packaging, and supplying Italian appetizers, entrees, and sauces to retailers nationwide.

The food maker sells its Italian items through major food retailers nationwide, specialty food stores, and discount stores. Customers include Safeway , Sam's Club , Publix , and Whole Foods , among many others.

Michael Angelo's is riding two trends that are sweeping the food industry: the desire for fast, convenient, in-home preparation; and preference for products made from quality ingredients without artificial flavors or chemical preservatives. To this end, in 2013 it launched a new line of Italian flatbreads, including Pepperoni & Salami Flatbread, Mediterranean Flatbread, Sausage & Mushroom Flatbread and Margherita Flatbread, available at Wal-Mart Stores .
