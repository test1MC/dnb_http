You can't hang General Magnetics on the refrigerator. The company is a leading manufacturer of audiocassettes and other magnetic media products in Southeast Asia. General Magnetics also makes compact discs (CDs), CD-ROMs, DVDs, and floppy disks. The company produces various cassette and CD cases and offers labeling, design, and packaging for its products as well. Its other services include CD and DVD replication and CD mastering. Founded as blank cassette maker Tony Wong Co. in 1973, General Magnetics (which operated a record label from the mid-1970s to mid-1980s) also manufactures recordable CDs.
