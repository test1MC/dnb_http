ABB Limited-Thailand (THABB) makes process control automatic. A subsidiary of Switzerland-based ABB, Ltd , THABB manufactures an array of industrial automation products for use in Thai industry. The company is comprised of five divisions: Power Products (voltage products and transformers); Power Systems (power generation and transmission); Automation Products (motors, power electronics, and drives); Process Automation (control and analytical systems, force measurement); and Robotics (industrial robots and software). In addition to its manufacturing center, THABB operates two electrical service workshops in neighboring Thailand provinces. Customers include state agencies and private companies.
