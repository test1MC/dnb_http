Channing Bete Company ("Bete" rhymes with "meet") publishes educational and promotional products such as booklets, handbooks, and workbooks on social and behavioral topics, including smoking prevention and cessation, substance abuse and violence prevention, and school success. The company also has content available on video and CD. In addition, the company provides promotion, publishing, and distribution services to corporate partners, including the American Heart Association and Prevent Child Abuse America .
