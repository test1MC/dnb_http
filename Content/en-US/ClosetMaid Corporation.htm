While children are scared of monsters in the closet, ClosetMaid customers are frightened by clutter there. The Emerson Electric subsidiary makes and markets shelving and storage products in the US and abroad. The company's products, sold through home improvement retailers, such as Home Depot and Lowe's , as well as through mass merchants, the company's representatives, and online, are used in closets, laundry areas, kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, and garages. Its products are made of wood, laminate, and wire. The company also offers professional design assistance and custom installation. Founded in 1965, ClosetMaid has offices and facilities in the US and Mexico under the ClosetMaid and Clairson names.

Based in Florida, ClosetMaid sells its products in the US, Canada, and Puerto Rico. Its facilities are located in the US and Mexico.

ClosetMaid specializes in making wire and wood laminate storage and organization items.

The company is part of Emerson Electric 's Commercial & Residential Solutions segment, which accounted for about 8% of its parent's revenue in fiscal 2013. Besides ClosetMaid, the segment comprises Emerson Appliance Solutions, Emerson Professional Tools, Emerson Storage Solutions, Flo Healthcare, InSinkErator, Lionville, MedDispense, METRO, ProTeam, and RIDGID.

The Commercial & Residential Solutions segment, which includes the ClosetMaid business, reported a $12 million drop in revenue in fiscal 2013, due in part to Emerson Electric having sold off the segment's Knaack business. The remaining operations within the segment saw growth, however.

The company boasts one of the nation's most widely recognized brand names among the ventilated wire storage and organization markets.

Its customer base includes individuals, large retailers, and independent authorized dealers. To reach out to potential customers and deepen loyalty among existing ones, ClosetMaid maintains a blog site (storageorganizationblog.com) to discuss tips and do-it-yourself projects.
