This company serves up a bowl of freshness to soup lovers short on time. The Harris Soup Company is a leading manufacturer of refrigerated, ready-to-eat foods for retail customers. Doing business as Harry's Fresh Foods, it makes chili, soup, and stew, as well as sauces and sides. The company also makes such ready-to-eat entrees as beef stroganoff, jambalaya, and pasta dishes. In addition to its Harry's brand, the company makes food products under private labels, and it distributes ready-to-eat foods to customers in the foodservice industry. Founded in 1978 by Rod Harris (a.k.a. Harry), The Harris Soup Company is a subsidiary of Basic American Foods .
