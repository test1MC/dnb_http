Genomma Labs works to bring relief for a myriad of conditions to people in Mexico and South America. It performs R&D and sales and marketing functions but outsources production. A majority of sales comes from the company's OTC pain, cold, and stress relief products Bengue, Next, and Dalay, respectively. Genomma's personal care products include the Jockey Club, Tio Nacho, and Ma Evans brands, among others. The company also offers about 300 branded generic medications under the company's Primer Nivel Por Tu Salud (Top Quality for Your Health) brand. Genomma's products are available in drugstores, warehouse clubs, and other retail outlets in Mexico, the US, and 10 South American countries.
