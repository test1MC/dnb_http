Creative Computing West (CC West) is an Austin, Texas-based printing firm that offers four-color digital reproduction services. Other services include layout and design, large format color printing, bindery, and mail fulfillment. Clients have included 3M , Dell , Texas Monthly , and The University of Texas . The company was founded in 1996 by Donna Couch (president), Rex Couch (VP Sales and Marketing) and James Diorio (VP Operations).
