Windham Castings envisions a warm breeze gently blowing through your hair as you recline in one of its patio chairs. The company's cast aluminum lawn furniture is sold through specialty retailers in the US. Windham Castings is best known for its lawn furniture and sells it in about 20 of Home Depot's EXPO Design Center home-furnishing showroom stores. Windham Castings' patio chairs have been touted by former President Jimmy Carter; the company is located in one of Carter's old peanut warehouses. Founder Debbie Young opened up shop in 1997.
