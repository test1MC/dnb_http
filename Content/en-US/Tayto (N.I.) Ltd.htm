Tayto (N.I.) is the undisputed king of crisps in Northern Ireland and has been busy expanding its empire to become one of the leading makers of snack foods in the UK. A family-owned company, Tayto has product categories including Tayto Standards (beef and onion, roast chicken, smoky bacon flavors), Rough Cuts (spicy Thai, char-grilled steak), Snacks (onion rings), Snacks (chips), Sharing Range (chilli, red peppers, steak, and sea salt) and Advantage Range (french onion and Texan grill). The company's head offices are in Tayto Castle, an actual castle located in the Ulster countryside.

Outside the main Tayto brand, the company sells crisps under Golden Wonder, Real, and Jonathan Crisp brands. It also sells pork scratchings through its Mr. Porky brand.

Tayto is headquartered in Co. Armagh in Northern Ireland, and its products are exported to 43 countries around the world.

Tayto is the major constituent of the parent company, Manderley Food Group, which generated revenue of £174 million in 2015.
