Able Industrial Products is ready and willing when it comes to pliable materials. The family-owned company custom manufactures gaskets, as well as specializes in rotary die cutting and fabrication of a slew of materials, including rubber and plastic, foam, sponge, cork, and felt products. Custom tape printing is also provided. In addition, Able Industrial distributes adhesives, abrasives, tapes, silicone caulk, and mold-making and safety products by blue chip companies such as 3M and Saint-Gobain . The company supplies a breadth of industries, from automotive to aerospace and computer, worldwide. Established in 1974, Able Industrial is led by Gilbert Martinez, the youngest son company founder Harold Martinez.
