When all the hoopla surrounding finding a new home gets overwhelming, turn to Zoopla Property Group. The company operates a website at Zoopla.co.uk that provides information about local areas in the UK. Zoopla combines hundreds of thousands of property listings (for sale and rent) with market data, local information, and community tools. Its online resources target property consumers, while its advertising products target UK estate agents, letting agents, and property developers. The company was founded in 2007 by Alex Chesterman and was taken public in mid-2014.
