Nutro Products hopes to become a big part of Fido's dinnertime ritual. The company formulates and manufactures dry and canned food, biscuits, and treats for dogs and cats. Its brand names include Natural Choice, Max, Greenies, and Ultra. Nutro's products, which compete in the premium pet food market, are available in more than 12,000 feed stores and pet supply shops (such as Petco and PetSmart ) across the US and Canada. The company's products are also distributed internationally. Nutro Products' roots reach back to 1926, when it was purchased from a British entrepreneur and transplanted to Southern California. It is a subsidiary of food, snack, and pet care giant Mars .

Based in California, Nutro Products operates nationwide and in Canada.

Nutro Products makes and markets pet food and treats under several brand names, such as Nutro Max, Nutro Natural Choice, Nutro Ultra, and Greenies.

The company successfully serves the natural foods market for pets. It offers a complete line of natural foods for both dogs and cats under the Natural Choice and Max brand names. For dogs, it has added the holistic nutrition dog food Ultra. The company has expanded its portfolio of products in recent years to also offer consumers a new line of dog desserts, an extensive line of dog biscuits, and treats for dogs and cats.

Nutro Products relies on several big-name retailers to get its products in the hands of consumers. It counts on Petco , PetSmart , Amazon.com , Chewy.com, and Wag.com, among others.
