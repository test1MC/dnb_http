The Sussman's ancestors might not have landed on Plymouth Rock, but the family did land a good idea when they founded the Plymouth Beef Company. The company processes and sells fresh, vacuum-packed and frozen ground beef, hamburger patties, and diced, cubed beef, including products made from certified Angus beef. It also offers soy protein patties. Owned and operated by the founding Sussman family, Plymouth Beef's customers include foodservice distributors, supermarkets, and restaurant and hotel supply houses. It is located in the Hunts Point Market, Bronx, New York.
