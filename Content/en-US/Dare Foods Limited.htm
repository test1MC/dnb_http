One really doesn't have to be double-dared to try Dare Foods' snacks. The company makes crackers under such brands as Breton, Cabaret, grainsfirst, Bremner, Vinta, and Vivant. Its fine breads bear the Grissol name while cookies carry the Breaktime, Bear Paws, Econo, and Whippet labels, among others. For those with an even sweeter tooth, Dare Foods makes gummie candies under the RealFruit and Juicee brands, as well as mints and jelly beans. The company distributes its goodies to supermarkets and food retailers in the US, Canada, Mexico, and more than 25 other countries. Consumers can also order snacks through Dare Foods' e-commerce site. Founded by Charles Doerr in 1892, the company is still owned by his family.
