Clogs may inspire visions of wooden footwear and dancing at Renaissance festivals, but they're what keep Dansko in step. The company, whose name means "Danish shoe," helped to rejuvenate the popularity of the wide-toed, arch-supporting style, which has fueled the company's growth since its founding in 1990. Dansko also makes sandals, boots, and other casual shoe styles for women, men, and children. The firm sells footwear through its own Dansko Company Store at its headquarters and via its e-commerce site. It also distributes products to more than 3,500 retailers and online merchants (such as backcountry.com and Amazon.com ).
