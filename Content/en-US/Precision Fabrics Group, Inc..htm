Precision Fabrics Group (PFG) brings precision to the material world. Its dual lineup offers woven fabrics, from airbags to parachutes, surgical barriers, and bedding, and nonwovens, including filtration and medical items, as well as protective apparel. The woven division weaves fine filament fabric, and dyes and finishes it, to market an array of goods, from greige items, performance and extended cold weather outerwear, to window treatments. A nonwoven division treats nonwoven fabric with coatings, laminations, and chemistries for customized end uses; the short list includes disposable wipes, home cleaning mitts, hazardous environment garments, fiberglass composites, and acoustic facing materials.
