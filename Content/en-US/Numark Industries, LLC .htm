Numark Industries is helping DJs worldwide improve their craft and leave a mark on the industry. The company manufactures and distributes professional DJ equipment. It specializes in making and marketing the highest-torque turntable motor worldwide. Numark's other products include amplifiers, beat-keepers, headphones, microphones, mixers, and single and dual transport CD players (known for pioneering the first dual CD players and digital sampler/mixers). Numark sells its products through such musical retailers as Guitar Center , Brook Mays Music , and Sam Ash Music , as well as to customers located in the US and more than 100 countries.
