If you're looking to unfasten the Beltway, try the National Journal Group (NJG). The firm publishes a handful of magazines on a daily, weekly, and monthly basis that delve into the inner workings of Washington politics and policy. It also publishes blogs and other online content, as well as government-oriented books. Its publications are aimed at those with professional interest in such matters; titles include the National Journal (politics and government weekly) and CongressDaily (twice-daily news service covering Capitol Hill). Its The Hotline offers Web-based coverage of politics, polling, and campaign development. NJG was founded in 1969 and is a subsidiary of Atlantic Media Company .
