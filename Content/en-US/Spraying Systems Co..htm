Spraying Systems Midwest has been spraying it and not just saying it for more than 75 years. The company is a leading manufacturer of industrial spray nozzles. Its vast line of spray nozzles are used in an array of industries including pulp and paper, automotive manufacturing, fire protection, chemical processing, and food processing. Other products include turnkey, automated spray controllers and software. The company also offers spray analysis services. Spraying Systems sells directly through more than 90 sales offices worldwide and through its website. The company has about 10 manufacturing locations in the Americas, Asia, and Europe.

Along with its manufacturing facilities in Americas, Asia, and Europe, the company also operates regional spray technology centers in the US and Canada.

Spraying Systems produces more than 90,000 product lines, which include spray nozzles, turnkey spray systems, air systems, and custom fabrication. Its spray fabrication unit designs and makes custom spray lances, spray manifolds, and spray headers for use in less-than-standard space configurations. It also makes custom mounting systems and adapters.

Spraying Systems focuses on developing new products. The company has developed the TankJet line of automated tank cleaners for use in cleaning food processing tanks, brewery vats, and other large containers. It has also introduced FullJet nozzles which are used for cleaning and rinsing products. Its AccuCoat system uses an internal temperature control system to apply heated coatings, used to apply chocolate, oils, and other ingredients to a variety of products.
