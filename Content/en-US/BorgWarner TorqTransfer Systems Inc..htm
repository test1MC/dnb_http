BorgWarner TorqTransfer (BWTT) is a division of BorgWarner and provides components to its parent company's Engine and Drivetrain segments. BWTT is a leader in drivetrain technology, which offers a full line of electronic synchronizers, transfer cases, its branded iTrac four- and all-wheel drive torque systems, and aftermarket products. The company also manufactures transmission systems, Morse TEC, thermal systems, and turbo and emissions systems. BWTT Systems develops air management strategies and products to optimize engines for fuel efficiency, reduced emissions, and enhanced performance, which help improve a car's stability, shift quality, and overall handling.
