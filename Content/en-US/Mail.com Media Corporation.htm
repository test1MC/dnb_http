Digital media company Mail.com Media Corporation, doing business as PMC, owns and operates a portfolio of websites focused on entertainment industry news. Assets include media industry reporter Nikki Finke's Deadline.com (formerly on LAWeekly.com) as well as other celebrity gossip sites such as Hollywood Life, Hollybaby, and TVLine. It also publishes Movieline, once a print magazine but now a news site about the film industry; BGR, a site devoted to gadget geeks; and OnCars.com, for car fans. PMC was founded in 2004 by Jay Penske (son of auto racer Roger Penske) and has offices in Los Angeles, New York, and Mumbai. In 2011 it sold its Mail.com portal and e-mail service to German Internet firm United Internet .
