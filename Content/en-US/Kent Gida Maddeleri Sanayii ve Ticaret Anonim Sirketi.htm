Kent Gida Maddeleri Sanayii ve Ticaret Anonim Sirketi wants to sweeten your worldview. The company, commonly known as Kent, makes candy, gum, and chocolate, all of which are distributed in more than 100 countries worldwide. Its products include Villa chocolates, Topi Top lollipops, Jelibon gummies, and Relax gum. Kent is a publicly traded company. Kent started making the traditional Turkish sesame-seed-based candy called tahin in 1927.
