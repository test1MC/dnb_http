China Metro-Rural Holdings Limited (formerly Man Sang International) sings the praises of pearls. The trading company and its subsidiaries purchase and process cultured pearls in the Asia/Pacific region. It deals in Chinese cultured and freshwater pearls, Japanese cultured pearls, Tahitian pearls, and South Sea pearls. It gets its Chinese freshwater pearls from farmers and suppliers in the eastern part of China, including the Jiangsu and Zhejiang provinces. The company runs a processing facility and distributes its jewelry worldwide to manufacturers and retailers. China Metro-Rural Holdings also owns commercial real estate in Hong Kong and residential and commercial buildings in China.
