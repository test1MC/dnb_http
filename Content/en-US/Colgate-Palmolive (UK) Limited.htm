Colgate-Palmolive (UK) Limited is brushing up in Europe. The consumer products powerhouse manufactures and markets a slew of oral care and personal care items, including manual and battery powered toothbrushes, mouthwashes and toothpastes, shower gel, bath foam, and bar soap, and deodorant, as well as food for Fido and felines. The company's lineup is sold under brand names, including Colgate, Palmolive, Softsoap, and Hill's Pet Nutrition. Colgate-Palmolive, part of holding company Colgate Holdings, is the UK commercial operations branch of the Colgate-Palmolive Company , the world's leading toothpaste producer.

The company is headquartered in Surrey, UK.

Colgate-Palmolive UK is part of the parent company's Oral, Personal and Home Care Segment (Europe/South Pacific), which contributed around 18% (some $2.8 billion) of the total.

In 2015, net sales in the segment decreased. Volume growth was more than offset by net selling price decreases and negative foreign exchange effects.
