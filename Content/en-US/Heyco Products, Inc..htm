Founded in 1926, Heyco Products makes molded wire protection products (fittings, conduit, power outlets, bushings) and stamped electrical products (blades, terminals, contacts). The family owned company's molded-product manufacturing plant houses 20 state-of-the-art closed-loop injection molding machines. These machines can handle custom colors, high temperature, and specialty materials in addition to nylon, acrylonitrile-butadiene-styrene (ABS), and polycarbonate molding. Heyco also offers design, engineering, and testing services. Its products are distributed to manufacturers through a global sales and representative network.
