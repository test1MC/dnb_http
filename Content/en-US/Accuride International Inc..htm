Business at Accuride International is on a finely crafted roll. The company is the world's largest manufacturer of ball bearing slides. Its lineup makes it easier to open and close drawers in residential and office furniture, or the doors of appliances and electronic enclosures, or rack mounts. The company's slides are also made for automotive accessories, including storage units and arm rests, and industrial equipment, such as cash registers and assembly lines. Accuride builds and maintains its own tools and machinery for manufacturing its products. Founded in 1962 by Fred Jordan, the company remains family owned.

Accuride has more than 1 million sq. ft. manufacturing space, three steel mills and more than 60 roll form machines, in six locations around the world. It has production sites in Mexico, Europe, China, and Japan.

Accuride sells its products through distributors such as Wilson & Bradley, Kent Impex, Hafele Gmbh & Co, Megaway Corporation, and Euro House Trading.

Accuride has grown from a California tool-and-die shop to a global manufacturer spanning 1 million-plus sq. ft. of production space and more than 60 roll form machines. The company purchases 100 million or more pounds of steel each year to develop a stream a new patented mechanisms that meet evolving demand.
