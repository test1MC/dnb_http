There's nothing small about the ambitions of euromicron. The company sets up wireless, wired, and optical networks for voice, data, and video across Europe. euromicron divides its operations into distribution, networks, and components, which includes its connectors, switches, cable systems, testing equipment, and monitoring station manufacturing. It also offers network design, planning, analysis, maintenance, and service. The company operates through a host of subsidiaries, including Telent (systems integration), Elabo (measurement and testing equipment), and Qubix (procurement and distribution), along with multiple euromicron-branded units. Though it serves the entire euro zone, Germany accounts for most of sales.
