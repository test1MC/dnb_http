BMT Defence Services doesn't want anyone sinking your battleship. The company (a subsidiary of BMT ) provides engineering and design consultancy services to customers involved in the naval sector. BMT Defense Services' offerings include model tests, concept design, electrical and systems engineering, cost estimating, and safety and risk management. Its clients in the private sector include Rolls-Royce , Thales , Alstom , and Raytheon . The company also serves the defense departments of the UK, Australia, New Zealand, Canada, and Norway, as well as the European Defence Agency.

BMT Defence Services operates from locations in Bath, Bristol, Fareham, and Weymouth.

The company' s partners include A&P , Babcock , BAE Systems , BC Hydro , DCNS , Drumgrange , MBDA , QinetiQ , Raytheon , Rolls-Royce , SEA, Thales , DSME, Lloyd's Register, Deloitte , Converteam , Dearsan, and EMEC.

The company serves national government departments of defense and security; armed forces and security services; security suppliers; and commercial shipyards. Its major clients include the UK Ministry of Defence's Defence Equipment and Support Organisation; Defence Materiel Organisation of Australia's Department of Defence; New Zealand's Ministry of Defence; Defence Science and Technology Laboratory; European Defence Agency; and Defence Materiel Organisation of Canada's Department of Defence.

In 2013 the company launched two innovative, new platform design concepts: the BMT Venator-110 Multi-Mission Reconfigurable Warship and the Vidar-7 Small Modern SSK Submarine, which will deliver maximum capability, flexibility and longevity within the limitations of current fiscal constraints.

That year it also opened a second office in Bath to support additional members hired due to growth in international projects.

BMT Defence Services has had major roles in UK naval programs that have included the Type 45 destroyer and the UK submarine program.

The company was founded in 1988.
