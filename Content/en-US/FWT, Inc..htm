Cleverly hidden antennas from FWT do not provide a direct connection to God. The company makes communications antenna towers disguised as church steeples, palm and pine trees (TREE-CELL), light poles, and flagpoles. Other products include monopoles, equipment shelters, antenna arrays, and Communication-Sites on Wheels (COWs), which combine antennas and temporary power supplies in mobile enclosures such as trucks and trailers. The company divides its operations between two divisions that serve the communications and utilities markets. Founded in 1959, the company was acquired by Sabre Industries in 2015.
