China Yuchai International hopes that China's highways will be its road to diesel success. Through its 76% interest in Guangxi Yuchai Machinery Company Limited, China Yuchai is one of China's leading makers of diesel motors. China Yuchai specializes in midsized diesel truck engines, and it is a major supplier to China's Dongfeng Motor (nearly 21% of sales). It also sells diesel engines to bus manufacturers and independent truck makers. China Yuchai's other products include diesel power generators and engine parts. The industrial trading arm of Singapore's diversified Hong Leong Group controls China Yuchai.
