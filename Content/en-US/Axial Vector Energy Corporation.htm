Need a push? Axial Vector Energy boasts clean, fuel-efficient horsepower. Formerly Axial Vector Engine Corp, under its current nameplate the company tackles the job of developing, financing, and licensing a new breed of internal combustion engine and electric power generator. The lineup meets pollution mandates for a range of vehicle, appliance, and industrial markets. Its patented lower weight machine is marketed as a cost effective, smaller system that drives more power and torque on less fuel, with fewer emissions. Moreover, the branded Axial Vector engine and generator-set portfolio is said to work in both fixed and mobile environments. Director Samuel Higgins owns more than 55% of Axial Vector.
