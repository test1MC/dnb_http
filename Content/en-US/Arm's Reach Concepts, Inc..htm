Arm's Reach Concepts lets moms and babies snuggle closer through the night. The company makes the Co-Sleeper family of products that enable parents to sleep next to their infants, a choice families make to enhance the bonding process and make nighttime caregiving more comfortable. Products include co-sleeper beds that can sit alongside parents' beds and convert into a playpen, changing table, or bassinet; a smaller version; and versions in a sleigh-bed style and with a floor-length liner. The company also offers accessories, such as leg extensions, a canopy, replacement bed liners, and fitted sheets. Arm's Reach sells its products in retail locations throughout the US and in Italy, Puerto Rico, and Singapore.
