Apex-BP Solar is reaching for the top of the solar energy industry. A subsidiary of BP , the company manufactures photovoltaic modules used in the collection and generation of solar energy in residential and commercial buildings. Its products are also installed in industrial applications, such as telecommunications. Public authorities have additionally tapped Apex-BP for modules that are used in the lighting and electrification of street lights, back-up electrical systems, and heaters. The company was established in 1991 and was acquired by BP in 1999.
