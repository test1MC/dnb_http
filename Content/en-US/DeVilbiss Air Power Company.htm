If you can't stand the pressure, then stay away from DeVilbiss Air Power. The company manufactures air compressors and pneumatic tools, along with parts and accessories for those products. Black & Decker (now Stanley Black & Decker ) bought the company, along with the rest of the former Tools Group of Pentair , for about $800 million in 2004. DeVilbiss authorizes a network of more than 2,000 warranty service centers throughout Canada and the US that do repair work and provide replacement parts and accessories. DeVilbiss Air Power is part of Power Tools and Accessories segment of Stanley Black & Decker.
