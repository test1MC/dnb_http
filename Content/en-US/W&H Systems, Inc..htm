Committed to making a material difference, W&H Systems provides industrial material handling equipment including conveyors, tray sorters, and pick and pack systems. The company serves the warehousing and distribution industry, specializing in material handling systems for the apparel, books and videos, catalogs/e-commerce, department stores, entertainment, liquor and wines, manufacturing, and sporting goods industries, among others. W&H Systems also supplies logistics-related software products such as WHizard Visual Control System, DynaPick and DynaPack Paperless Picking and Packing. It also maintains strong relationships with conveyor equipment makers, such as FKI Logistex (formerly The Buschman Company).
