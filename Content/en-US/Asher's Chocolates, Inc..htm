The Asher family subscribes to the belief that everything is better smothered in chocolate. Asher's Chocolates manufactures chocolate and confections, including chocolate-covered pretzels potato chips, and graham crackers. It also produces boxed assortments (including truffles, chews, nuts, cordials, and creams) and gift baskets, fudge, pecan-caramel patties, almond bark, and sugar-free and low-carb assortments. Asher's Chocolates sells its confections at its company-owned store (in Souderton, Pennsylvania -- near Philadelphia) and online. The family-owned company was founded in Philadelphia in 1892 by Chester Asher. It's run by the fourth generation of the Asher Family, including CEO David Asher.
