RAZ Imports wants to give 'em the old razzle dazzle. A wholesale importer, RAZ sells seasonal holiday decorations and home accessories to retailers, interior designers, and anyone else with a current resale tax ID number. Items include pillows, tabletops products, garden accessories, lamps, christmas trees and wreaths, among others. Minimum opening orders are $300. The company operates about five RAZ Imports showrooms, and sells products through showrooms in 10 US cities.
