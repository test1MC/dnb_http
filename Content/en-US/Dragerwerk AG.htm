Drägerwerk is in the business of protecting the world's workers. The company, which is controlled by the Dräger family, provides medical and safety technology to health care and public safety organizations, as well as industrial and mining entities. Dräger Medical's product range covers anesthesia workstations, ventilation units, infant warming equipment, and gas management systems. Dräger Safety manufactures hazard management equipment including mobile gas detection systems, respiratory gear, and alcohol and drug-testing instruments. Drägerwerk's headquarters are in Germany, but about 80% of its sales are generated outside of the country. The company's products are sold in more than 190 countries.
