Quayside Publishing Group has designs on book publishing. The company's Rockport Publishers and Creative Publishing international (CPi) imprints targets the professional design audience, with books on graphic and interior design, architecture, photography, and cinema. Titles include Urban Landscape Architecture , Idea Industry , and Experimental EcoDesign . Additional imprints include Fair Winds Press, Quarry Books, and Quiver Books. The company is a subsidiary of the London-based Quarto Group .
