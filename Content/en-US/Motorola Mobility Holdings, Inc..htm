Motorola Mobility is settling in as the mobile maestro of its parent company Lenovo . Motorola Mobility makes and markets lines of mobile phones, selling enough to make Lenovo the third biggest smartphone company. The phones, which run on the Android operating system, are sold under the Moto and Droid brands and aim the middle and lower ends of the smartphone market. Besides phones, Motorola Mobility makes and sells wearables and accessories. Lenovo bought Motorola Mobility from Google in 2014 for $2.9 billion.

Motorola Mobility was created when Motorola, which made semiconductors, wireless phones, and other devices, broke up in 2010. After a short time on its own, Motorola Mobility was bought in 2011 for $12.5 billion by Google, which wanted to pair phone hardware with its Android operating system. Deciding that wasn't the way to go, Google sold the phone unit to Lenovo in late 2014.

Motorola Mobility's phones are the Moto E and Moto X as well a the Droid Turbo 2 and the Droid Maxx 2. The unit also manages Lenovo's Vibe smartphone brand which is sold in China. The company manufactures phones in Asia and Brazil. It opened a plant in in 2015 in India to serve that market. The company's Moto 360 is a smart watch that tracks fitness metrics such as heart beat and calories expended. It is equipped with Wi-Fi.

Motorola Mobility is based in Chicago, Illinois and has offices and operations around the world.

The company sells their product through third-party distributors, representatives, and retailers. Customers include wireless carriers such as Verizon, AT&T, T-Mobile, and Sprint.

Motorola Mobility as found a level of stability at Lenovo. The phone unit provides Lenovo with a strong entry in the smart phone wars against Apple , Samsung , Ericsson , and others. Lenovo is tops in PCs and three in smart phones and servers (thanks to its acquisition of IBM's x86 server business).

Motorola Mobility is the engineering, design, and manufacturing engine for Lenovo's Mobile Business Group's smartphones and wearables. Going forward, the company plans to operate under the Moto brand. It plans to emphasize the Lenovo connection and de-emphasize the Motorola element. The company will keep the familiar bat wing logo.
