With a 95% market share, San Miguel Brewery (SMB) is the largest beer company in the Philippines. SMB brews about 175 million cases, or more than 2 billion bottles, from its five breweries every year, and its eight beer brands are distributed to almost 500,000 retail outlets across the Philippines. (The popular Pale Pilsen and Red Horse beers account for almost 80% of sales.) Subsidiary San Miguel Brewing International is responsible for exporting beer to almost 50 countries around the world. SMB was spun off from San Miguel Corporation in 2007; the company is now 51% owned by San Miguel and 48% owned by Kirin Holdings Company Limited .
