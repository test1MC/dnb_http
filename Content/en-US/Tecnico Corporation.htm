Técnico has the coastlines covered. With shipyards on the east, west, and Gulf coasts of the US, the company offers a breadth of marine and industrial services. Its slate includes manufacturing, maintenance and repair, installation, and alteration services for passenger, commercial, and naval vessels, as well as steel and sheet metal fabrication. Mobile capabilities allow it to even travel to where the ships are. Customers include the US Navy (it's largest), the Coast Guard , Northrop Grumman Shipbuilding , and various other commercial ship owners. Técnico was founded in 1990 by Rafael Torrech; it is held by American Maritime Holdings, which is led by the founder's son president and COO Michael Torrech.
