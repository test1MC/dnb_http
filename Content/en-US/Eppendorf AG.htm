Looking for a lab partner? Eppendorf develops, makes, and sells a variety of equipment used in biotechnology research laboratories, including pipettes, pipette tips, dispensers, centrifuges, and test tubes. (In fact, the 1.5 mL plastic centrifuge has become a genericized trademark known as an eppi tube). It also makes instruments and systems for cell manipulation, automated devices for handling liquids, and equipment for DNA amplification and biochips. Eppendorf distributes its products to academic and commercial research institutes and private sector biotechnology companies around the world. The company has offices in 20 countries, but its largest market is North America, followed by Western Europe and Asia.
