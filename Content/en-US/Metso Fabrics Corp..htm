Metso Fabrics (formerly Tamfelt) understands the fabric of industrial processes. The company makes paper machine clothing (PMC), custom-made fabrics and belts that move paper stock through each phase of production. Its filter fabrics are used by the chemical, forestry, and mining industries to separate liquids and solids. Metso Fabrics also supplies the European laundry industry with ironer felts. Formed in 1797, it is the second-oldest public company in Finland (after tool manufacturer Fiskars ). Metso Fabrics operates plants in Europe (more than 70% of sales), Asia, and South America. Equipment manufacturer Metso purchased Tamfelt in May 2010. It continues in Metso's Paper and Fiber Technology segment.
