Cordova Bolt helps its customers with their attachments. The company is a distributor of standard and special fasteners and hardware. It distributes fasteners including screws, rivets, nuts, bolts, anchors, pins, and washers. Specific items include expansion anchors, drywall screws, formed and forged fittings, lockwashers, metal building fasteners, special threaded products, socket screw products, and thread rod.
