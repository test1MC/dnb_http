Quite literally, Rollstud Limited helps its customers "keep it together." Doing business as Rollstud Fastener Company, the company is a manufacturer of studbolts, fasteners, and other precision components for the offshore oil and gas industry. To complement its main products, Rollstud stocks a wide range of anchor bolts, hexbolts, nuts, cap screws, socket head screws, pins, washers, as well as flanges, gaskets, and pipe-to-pipe fittings. Catering to the UK and Europe, it makes, coats, and tests all of its products at its three manufacturing plants (two in the UK and one in the UAE).

The company has its head office/plant in Aberdeen and other offices/plants in Hartlepool and Dubai.

To enable Rollstud to meet the varying requirements of the industry, it has installed a comprehensive range of machinery enabling it to produce a wide range of quality bolting in house, in all materials such as A193 B7, A320 L7; Inconel 718, 625; Stainless Steel; Super Duplex; Ferralium 255; and Monel K500, 400.

Rollstud's strategy is to provide a fast, reliable service of a competitively priced, quality assured range of products, backed by service and support.
