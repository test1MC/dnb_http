There's no uncertainty about CertainTeed's business. A subsidiary of French industrial giant Compagnie de Saint-Gobain , CertainTeed manufactures building products for both commercial and residential construction. CertainTeed's products include exterior and interior building products, including roofing, siding, trim, sheathing, fence, decking, foundations, windows, insulation, ceilings, drywall, performance wallboards, tile backers, underlayments, infrastructure, and pipe. CertainTeed sells its products under the Bufftech, CertainTeed, Form-A-Drain, Prestige, and Wolverine brands.

CertainTeed's corporate headquarters are located in Valley Forge, Pennsylvania. The company has approximately 6,000 employees and 65 facilities throughout the US and Canada.

The company has used partnerships to grow its business. In 2013 it formed a partnership with Angie's List . The deal allows CertainTeed to help local contractors build awareness for their services by providing access to exclusive advertising discounts, local business growth marketing opportunities, and support.

CertainTeed plans to build a new, state-of-the-art roofing manufacturing facility and warehouse in Jonesburg, Missouri.

In 2013, to raise cash, the company sold Pipe and Foundation Group, a leading producer of PVC pipe and fittings for municipal, water well, mining, agriculture and irrigation applications, to Westlake Chemical for $175 million.

CertainTeed traces its roots to the founding of the General Roofing Manufacturing Company in 1904. About 10 years later, the company was renamed CertainTeed, its name formed by contracting the slogan, "Quality Made Certain, Satisfaction Guaranteed."
