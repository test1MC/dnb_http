For workaday staples, shoppers count on Harveys of Oldham. The company is the UK's #1 manufacturer and stockist of industrial workwear, uniforms, and protective clothing for clients in many industries, including foodservice, hospitality, and healthcare. Harveys' garments include flame-retardant and high-visibility coveralls, overalls, tops and tunics, and chefs' wear. Regulatory protective workwear is made under the long-establsihed Harpoon brand. Its collection of activewear is sold under the Regatta label. Harveys offers embroidery of logos and other business insignia. Garments are distributed wholesale through contracts and brochures, as well as via the company's website.

Harveys also makes bespoke clothing to meet customers' individual requests and specifications for fabrics, coloring, and design. Its operations are supported by a manufacturing plant in the UK.

The company serves customers in a range of sectors, including Healthcare, Beauty Uniforms, Workwear, Activewear, Catering, Cleaning and Support, Hospitality, Bar Wear, and Business Wear. It also sells its products online.
