As the company likes to say: Rise, roll, rest, and ride! Golden Technologies is in the business of helping those who need a little assistance getting around do all of the above with its line of lift chairs, scooters, power wheelchairs, and adjustable beds. The company's brands include the Alante and the Alero power chairs, several types of scooters, including the Avenger, the Buzz Around, and the Companion Series, and the Compass wheelchair line. Adjustable beds come in standard or deluxe models. Golden Technologies' base of operations is in Old Forge, Pennsylvania; its products are sold through dealers in the US, Canada, and Mexico. The family-owned company was founded in 1985 by Robert Golden Sr. and Fred Kiwak.
