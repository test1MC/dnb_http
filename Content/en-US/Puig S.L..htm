Puig isn't just focused on rouge. Antonio Puig (rhymes with Scrooge) founded one of Spain's biggest beauty and fashion companies in 1914. The company's product lines include perfumes and cosmetics, clothing, luxury goods, and accessories. Puig boasts numerous designer names in its portfolio, including Paco Rabanne, Nina Ricci, Carolina Herrera, Prada , Jean-Paul Gaultier, and Comme des Garçons. Through a network of some 40 subsidiaries, Puig's products are distributed in more than 130 countries. Puig is run by its chairman and CEO Marc Puig. In 2011 the firm acquired control of the French fashion house Jean-Paul Gaultier, whose main asset is its perfume-making business.
