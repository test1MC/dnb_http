ICG/Holliston isn't going to tell you to wait until it comes out in paperback. The company produces fabrics for a myriad of coating applications. ICG/Holliston manufactures book cloth used to cover books ranging from best-sellers to textbooks and loose-leaf binders. Other products include cloth and vinyl substrates used for applications such as labels, cloth tapes, abrasive backing, and containers. Customers include the building materials, publishing, fragrance packaging, and album/looseleaf industries. The company, formerly Industrial Coatings Group (ICG), acquired Holliston Mills in 1998; both companies were founded in the late 1800s. The company has sold its sign and banner business line to Avery Dennison .
