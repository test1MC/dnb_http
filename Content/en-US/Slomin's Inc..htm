Slomin's wants to help keep you safe and keep you cool (or warm, depending on the season). The company provides security system installation and monitoring services for residential and commercial customers located throughout the eastern US (New York, New Jersey, Connecticut, Pennsylvania, Delaware, Maryland, Virginia, Washington, DC, and Georgia). In addition to security, Slomin's provides air conditioning and heating services to residential and commercial clients. The family-owned company was founded as a general store in 1923 by Jacob Slomin and later branched into kerosene fuel delivery and HVAC equipment services. Slomin's has been offering home security services since 1981.
