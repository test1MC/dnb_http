When the sparks fly, it helps to be under the aegis of Aearo Technologies. The company manufactures and sells a slew of personal protection and energy-absorbing equipment to the do-it-yourself retail market in more than 70 countries. Sold under brand names such as AOSafety, E-A-R, Peltor, and SafeWaze, its products include earplugs, goggles, face shields, respirators, hard hats, safety clothing, first-aid kits, and communication headsets. Aearo also supplies safety prescription eyewear and makes energy-absorbing foams that control noise, vibration, and shock. The company operates as part of 3M Company 's Safety, Security and Protection Services business segment.

Aearo operates throughout the world. The company has nine manufacturing locations worldwide and prescription safety eyewear labs in the US, Canada, and the UK.

With the Aearo purchase, one of 3M's largest, it added hearing protection, eyewear, and fall protection product lines to its offerings.

In addition to manufacturing, Aearo is also active in research and product development. Its customers come from industries such as manufacturing, the military, aviation, forestry, agriculture, and motor sports.
