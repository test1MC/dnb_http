Robb-Jack Corporation makes router bits, saws, end mills for high-speed machining of hardened metals, and related rotary cutting tools for industrial manufacturing machinery. The company can also modify standard saws and end mills or custom manufacture special saws and end mills according to customers specifications. Robb-Jack Corporation operates from its headquarters in Lincoln, California; it sells through distributors located throughout the US and through international distributors in more than a dozen countries, including Australia, Canada, Germany, Hong Kong, Israel, Portugal, South Korea, Taiwan, and the UK. Robb-Jack Corporation began operations in 1959.
