So many ways to generate power -- using thermal-differences. Marlow Industries manufactures a range of semiconductor-based thermoelectric coolers (TECs), and subsystems. Its lineup drives a host of solutions such as cooling, heating, temperature stabilization, power generation, and thermal modeling. Products are used for infrared sensors, fiber optic guidance systems, thermal reference sources, even refrigerators and freezers. Marlow taps into industries from aerospace and defense to electronics, medical, and telecommunications. The company was founded in 1973 by Raymond Marlow. As of 2004, it is an operating subsidiary of II-VI Inc. 's Compound Semiconductor Group.
