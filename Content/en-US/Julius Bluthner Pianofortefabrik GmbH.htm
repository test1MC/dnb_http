Julius Blüthner Pianofortefabrik helps to set the stage for a grand performance. The company makes Blüthner, Haessler, Irmler, and Breitmann grand and upright pianos. Julius Blüthner Pianofortefabrik provides Blüthner grand pianos in six sizes and its upright pianos in three sizes. The 150,000 instruments made by Blüthner have been used in concert halls, music festivals, and universities across Europe, Asia, and North America. The company started making pianos in Germany in 1853.
