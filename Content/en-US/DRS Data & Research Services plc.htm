DRS Data and Research Services wants to make its mark on a variety of industries. The company manufactures and markets optical mark reading and image scanning machinery. The company's products are used in more than 50 countries by schools, government agencies, health care providers, and businesses for such applications as examination processing, ballot counting, and survey tabulation. Customers have included the University of Bristol and the nations of Ethiopia and Kenya. Established in 1969, DRS Data and Research Services also provides data capture services, as well as training for its customers and distributors.
