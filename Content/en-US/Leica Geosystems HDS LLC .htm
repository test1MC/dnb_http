Leica Geosystems HDS is the High-Definition Surveying (HDS) division of Switzerland-based Leica Geosystems AG , which is controlled by Sweden's Hexagon . The division develops and manufactures 3D laser-scanning hardware and software products for as-built, detail, engineering, and topographic surveys for 2D and 3D mapping and modeling applications. The unit markets its products under the Leica ScanStation 2 and Leica HDS6000 names, along with the related Cyclone, CloudWorx, and TruView software packages. The company has customers and representatives in more than 40 countries. Leica Geosystems HDS was founded as Cyra Technologies in 1993 and was acquired by Leica Geosystems in 2001.
