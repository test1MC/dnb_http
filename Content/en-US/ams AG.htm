austriamicrosystems brings its analog microchips from Austria to the world. It offers various kinds of devices, including application-specific integrated circuits (ASICs) for power management, wireline communications, and medical applications; application-specific standard products (ASSPs) used in cordless phones and digital music players; and standard linear semiconductors, such as converters, amplifiers, and regulators. It customizes systems for power management, sensing, and mobile entertainment. The company also provides contract manufacturing (aka foundry) services through which it produces chips on behalf of other chip makers. The company gets over 60% of its sales in Europe, the Middle East, and Africa.
