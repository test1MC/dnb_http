Positivo Informática is positively enjoying top billing as Brazil's leading PC manufacturer. The company makes desktop and notebook computers, as well as servers, for numerous corporate clients, including PETROBRAS . In addition, Positivo expanded its product line to include multifunctional printers and educational laptops. In order to accommodate its increased production, the company more than doubled the size of its only factory, located in the southern state of Parana. Positivo exports its products across Latin America and is setting its sights on additional foreign markets. Founded in 1989, the company now competes in the retail market, as well.
