At this company, it's not soup to nuts, it's soy to nuts. US Soy works to make organic, non-genetically modified soy a staple product in the US and abroad. The company grows, processes, and sells whole soybeans, as well as soy flour, meal, oil, powder, and roasted soy nuts, pieces, and bits (salted and unsalted). It develops its own totally traceable seed stock. US Soy sells its products to consumer product manufacturing companies in the baking, beverage, health food, infant food, nutritional supplement, pet food, and snack industries. It has customers worldwide. US Soy is owned by Clarkson Grain Company , which acquired it in 2012.
