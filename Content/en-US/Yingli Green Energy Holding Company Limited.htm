Yingli Green Energy is green from top to bottom. The vertically integrated photovoltaic (PV) manufacturer makes polysilicon ingots and silicon wafers that it uses to create PV cells and modules, which it incorporates into solar energy systems that turn sunlight into clean energy. The company, with an annual production capacity of more than 1,000 MW each for polysilicon ingots and wafers, PV cells, and PV modules, is one of the few companies in China involved in all stages of PV manufacturing, sales, and installation. Yingli Green Energy sells its products in the US, China, Europe (primarily Germany, Italy, and Spain) and a few other markets.
