Arctic Glacier Income Fund is the trust structure that owns Arctic Glacier, one of the largest packaged ice manufacturing and distribution companies in North America. It has 46 production facilities with the capacity to make more than 11,000 tons of ice a day, as well as more than 50 distribution centers in the US and Canada. Arctic Glacier supplies packaged ice to more than 75,000 customers, including grocery and beverage retailers. For commercial and industrial customers, it supplies ice for baking and meat processing, among other uses. The firm also markets related products such as bottled water, dry ice, rock salt, and ice-making equipment.
