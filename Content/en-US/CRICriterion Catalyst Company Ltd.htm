Royal Dutch Shell subsidiary CRI/Criterion Catalyst Company helps the chemically challenged by producing catalyst products and technical services for refining and petrochemical plants. Through CRI Catalyst it operates research laboratories, development facilities, manufacturing plants and business units around the world. CRI/Criterion handles a range of products with a specific focus on environmental applications, hydrogen separation and recovery, selective oxidation and hydrogenation, dehydrogenation, and the production of renewable fuels. Through subsidiary Criterion Catalysts & Technologies, CRI/Criterion provides hydroprocessing chemicals used by petrochemical companies.

CRI/Criterion has operations around the world.

In 2011 BASF Catalysts acquired CRI/Criterion's global styrene catalysts business in a move to expand into markets in the Middle East and China.
