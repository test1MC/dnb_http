AB Mauri Food's business was made to rise. The company is one of the industry's largest makers and distributors of baker's yeast, sold as Fleischmann's in the US and Canada. AB Mauri Foods produces fresh yeast cakes, as well as traditional active dried and quick-rise yeasts for the bakery industry and home bakers. It also makes chemical leaveners, preservatives, anti-mold, and other technologically advanced ingredients under the ICS brand for industrial bakers. The company, formed by Associated British Foods in 2004, operates within its parent's ACH Food Companies group of US businesses, which include the Mazola corn oil, Karo syrup, Argo cornstarch, and Tone spice brand names.
