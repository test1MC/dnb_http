A hemato -grapher of sorts, Myriad RBM (formerly Rules-Based Medicine) is developing blood tests that it hopes will be used in the diagnosis of mental illnesses. Based on its proprietary multi-analyte profiling (MAP) platform, its Psychiatric Diagnostic Panel analyzes protein biomarkers found in blood to help psychiatrists diagnose conditions including schizophrenia, bipolar disorder, major depressive disorder, and to determine how well drugs are working. The Austin, Texas-based company was the first to develop blood tests for the diagnosis of mental illnesses. After canceling a planned IPO in 2010, the company was acquired by Myriad Genetics in 2011.
