Orsa Celulose, Papel, e Embalagens papers Brazil. The company produces woodpulp, wrapping paper, recycled paper, corrugated cardboard, and other packaging materials from six mills located across Brazil. While most of its products are sold domestically, Orsa Celulose also produces kraftliner paper for export. Its Marquesa forestry operation provides raw material for the company and sells wood. The company is part of the Brazilian conglomerate Orsa Group which includes the pulp, paper, and packaging operations of Orsa Celulose, Papel, e Embalagens, as well as Jari Celulose (pulp mill), Orsa Florestal (forestry), and Fudacao Orsa (foundation promoting education, healthcare, and social causes).
