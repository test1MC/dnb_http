S.C. Johnson Ltd. helps its customers clean up in the UK. The wholly-owned subsidiary of S.C. Johnson & Son is a Surrey-based maker of household and industrial cleaning and hygiene products. The company operates a marketing and manufacturing unit in Frimley Green, Surrey; a regional headquarters (for Europe, Africa, and the Near East) in Egham, Surrey; and a European Shared Service Centre in Edinburgh House at Slough in Berkshire. Established in 1915 as its parent's first subsidiary, the company markets cleaning products for nearly every room in the house. The company's brand names include Pledge, Sparkle, Toilet Duck, Mr Muscle, Glade, Oust, Brillo, Klear, Raid, and Goddards, among others.
