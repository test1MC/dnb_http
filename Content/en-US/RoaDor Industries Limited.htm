RoaDor Industries sends truckers speeding down the road without everything spilling out the back. The company makes polyvinyl chloride (PVC) roll-up overhead doors for commercial trucks, vans, and trailers. RoaDor's doors are designed to resist peeling and hinge and roller failure. Offering models for dry freight and insulated dry freight, the company has also developed a door for refrigerated loads. The PVC panels are manufactured by Royal Group Technologies . CXW Capital acquired truck door maker Proplas in 2003 and changed its name to RoaDor Industries.
