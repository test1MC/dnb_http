Hi Display Harlow (formerly PPE Limited) uses the leaves from one tree to build another. The company is Europe's largest producer of leaflet dispensers (also called trees). Hi Display Harlow also designs, makes, and sells point-of-purchase merchandising products, such as shelf-mounted brochure dispensers, in-store fixtures, and custom-built display systems. The company has manufacturing operations in the UK and sells to retailers worldwide. Hi Display Harlow is a unit of Hi Display UK Holding, itself a holding company subsidiary of Sweden-based HL Display AB.

Hi Display Harlow is the UK operating arm of the parent company, which operates in 47 markets throughout Western and Eastern Europe, the Middle East and Asia.

PPE was founded in 1969 by Ted Parker, who left the business to his twin sons, Neil and Nigel Parker. In 2009 the Parkers sold PPE to HL Display, representing the Swedish company's largest acquisition in its history. The Parkers left the business altogether in mid-2011.
