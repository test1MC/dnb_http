AXL Musical Instruments has earned a reputation from outfitting bands. The company makes electric and acoustic guitars and basses under the AXL, Johnson, and Lucida brands, as well as mandolins, banjos, ukuleles, and drum kits. Its pianos, stringed, brass, and woodwind instruments bear the Palatino brand. The company also makes Guardian gig bags and cases, cables, picks, pedals, tuners, and adapters. In addition, AXL provides OEM (original equipment manufacturing) services. The firm has about 20 manufacturing plants in China and distributors in a dozen other countries.
