As you scan store shelves, you might very well be reading material printed by Vestcom International. The company specializes in shelf-edge communications for the retail industry, which includes the production of labels and tags for store shelves that feature advertising messages, pricing and stocking information, nutritional messages, and other data. Vestcom also produces specialized marketing materials. Customers include leading supermarkets ( Kroger ), drugstore chains ( CVS ), mass merchandisers ( Target ), and manufacturers ( General Mills ). Vestcom has 10 production facilities across the US that print nearly a billion labels each year. Private equity firms The Stephens Group and Lake Capital own the company.
