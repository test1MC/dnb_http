You might say this company helps make the world a little thicker and stiffer. Roquette America is the regional operating subsidiary of Roquette Frères , a leading producer of starch products, syrups, and polyols (sugar alcohols). Roquette America sells some 700 by-products made from maize, wheat, potatoes, peas, and other agricultural commodities. The company's products are used in a wide variety of applications, including animal feeds, chemical products, and food ingredients. As part of its business, Roquette America also makes items for use by the manufacturers of industrial products and by pharmaceutical companies. Roquette America has production facilities in Illinois and Iowa.

Iowa-based Roquette America operates a sorbitol plant in Gurnee, Illinois, and a corn starch plant in Keokuk, Iowa. It also maintains an R&D center in Geneva, Illinois. The company is the US arm of France's Roquette Frères, and operates alongside other Roquette operations in Europe, Asia, and India, through commercial units, production sites, and research and development centers.

Nutralys pea proteins are extracted by Roquette America from the dry yellow pea; Nutriose is a soluble dietary fibers obtained from cereals; and Sweetpearl is a bulk sweetener produced from naturally occurring compounds in wheat and maize.

Roquette America focuses on product development for the human nutrition, pharmaceutical, animal nutrition, and industrial markets.

The company's French parent has been strengthening its presence in the US by adding to its operations. It's also working to develop products in high growth areas and sees the Americas, including Mexico, as a driver of growth.

To extend its reach, Roquette America partners with others. In 2014 the company signed an exclusive supply agreement with World Food Processing to meet the growing demand in North America for pea protein. The partnership bolsters Roquette's position as a leader in the pea protein market with its brand name Nutralys. Pea protein has numerous applications, including baked goods, snacks, cereals, and pet foods.
