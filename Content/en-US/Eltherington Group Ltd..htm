It's no lie: Eltherington Group is a fabricator. Catering to the UK and Europe, the company specializes in fabricating extrusion and sheet aluminum, transport body kits, and coated aluminum cladding. The range of products manufactured and distributed by Eltherington includes bars, coated and plain sheet, extrusions, and tubes. It also makes truck and trailer body kits as well as PVCu double-glazed windows and doors for the recreational vehicle industry. The company serves customers primarily in the construction and transportation industries. Eltherington Group subsidiaries include Eltherington Industries Ltd. and Eltherington Aluminium Ltd.

Eltherington offers more than 4,000 high quality aluminum and PVC-U products in sheet, bar and profile formats.

The company sells its products to companies in the architectural, construction, caravan, portable and modular building markets.
