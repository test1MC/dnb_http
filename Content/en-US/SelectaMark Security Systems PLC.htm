This company wants to make its mark by letting you mark what is yours. SelectaMark Security Systems provides its customers with tamper-resistant labels and tags used to identify property and assets in the case of theft. Its products, which contain the customer's name and identification coding, are used by schools, business offices, hospitals, police departments, and residential customers to protect items such as computer and electronics equipment, bicycles and boats, and boilers and heating equipment. In addition to its theft deterrent products and systems, SelectaMark also offers biometric access control systems and security consulting services.
