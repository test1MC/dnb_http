Hemlock Printers is a commercial offset printer serving Canada and the Pacific Northwest. The company produces such items as corporate brochures, catalogs, advertising materials, and fine art prints. Its services start with prepress (from electronic page assembly to digital proofing) and goes through post press (from binding to distribution). It also offers online ordering and proofing of customized print products like business cards and stationery. Hemlock Printers has offices in Burnaby and Victoria, British Columbia; San Francisco; Seattle; and Vancouver. The company was founded in 1968.
