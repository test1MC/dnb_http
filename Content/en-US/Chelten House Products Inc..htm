Chelten House rocks the house with salad toppings and meat marinades. The company makes all-natural, organic salad dressings and marinades for private-label customers and under its own Chelten House, Marinade Bay, and Simply Natural brand names. Its products, which also include marinades, rubs, salsa, mustard, and seafood, horseradish, soy, and pasta sauces, are sold in grocery stores in the US and Canada. Chelten's retail food customers include Albertson's , Giant , Kroger , Meijer , Whole Foods , and Wal-Mart . It also sells to the foodservice sector (Chef Select Recipes brand). The family-owned and -operated company was started in 1965 by Martin Dabrow.
