UMW's bread and butter comes in the form of auto manufacturing, industrial equipment, and oil and gas services. The group primarily makes vehicles through joint venture with Toyota Motor Corporation and Toyota Tsusho Corporation (known as UMW Toyota Motor) and through . It also produces national car models Alza, Myvi, and Viva Elite through Perodua. UMW manufactures and distributes heavy industrial equipment throughout Southeast Asia and produces auto parts and components for transportation equipment. The company's oil and gas operations consist of drilling services, tubing and casing threads, and the production of couplings. UMW has operations in about a dozen Asian and Middle Eastern nations.
