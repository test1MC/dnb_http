General Dynamics Ordnance and Tactical Systems - Scranton Operations (formerly Chamberlain Manufacturing Corporation) sticks to its guns. The company, which was previously owned by The Duchossois Group (formerly Duchossois Industries), is a maker of large-caliber (105mm to 155mm) ammunition projectile bodies for military customers, chiefly the US Department of Defense . Duchossois sold this division of Chamberlain Manufacturing to General Dynamics for an undisclosed sum in 2006. General Dynamics then changed the name of the company and organized it under its General Dynamics Ordnance and Tactical Systems division.
