Gemeinhardt thinks everyone should toot their own woodwind. The company makes and markets flutes (alto, bass, conservatory, professional, and student models) and piccolos (plastic, silver, and wood models) under the Gemeinhardt brand. In addition, Gemeinhardt manufactures cleaning kits, headjoints, and instrument cases. The company's Web site provides users with practicing tips and flute-care advice. Gemeinhardt is part of the Gemstone Musical Instruments subsidiary of Brookstone Partners. Other Gemstone brands that are part of the firm's products portfolio, include Stephanhouser, Sankyo, Trevor James, Artisan, Roy Seaman, W. Nirschi, and Andino.
