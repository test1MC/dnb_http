One company is fighting tooth and nail to sell music that is relevant to Christians and non-Christians alike. Christian indie label Tooth & Nail is home to artists such as Hawk Nelson, The Juliana Theory, and MxPx. Its imprint labels include Solid State Records (Christian metal and punk) and BEC Recordings (mainstream Christian pop). Tooth & Nail has major label-distribution and 90% of its sales are general market (not Christian retail outlets), with much of its Christian music having mainstream crossover potential. The company was founded in 1993 by CEO Brandon Ebel.
