Happy Worker makes GeekMan, SuperMom, BossMan, and other pop-culture and novelty figurines targeting the grown-up kids market. GeekMan, a six-inch, realistically sculpted figure, is posable and comes with glasses, a laptop, handheld computer, watch, and coffee mug. He boasts traits, such as "ungodly coding abilities" and "less than ideal personal hygiene routine." Happy Worker, which also makes custom toys for clients, also peddles posters, workplace prank signs, cards to e-mail friends and colleagues, and other items. President and CEO Kris Schantz founded Happy Worker in October 2002.
