YKK Europe designs, develops, and manufactures a range of fasteners that includes zippers, snap fasteners and buttons, and textile and plastic parts. Its zippers are used in a variety of applications and can be made airtight, flame resistant, lightweight, heavy duty, transparent, or waterproof. The company is the hub of Tokyo-based YKK Corporation's Fastening Products Group's operations in Europe, the Middle East, and Africa. YKK Europe does business from offices and facilities in about 70 countries.

YKK Europe manufactures and sells fastening and architectural products worldwide. It also makes other fastening products such as snaps and buttons, textiles, and plastics, hook and loop self-closing tape fastening systems, webbings, and metal and plastic notions and buckles. YKK also produces aluminum building products like window and entrance systems for low, medium, and high rise applications.

The London-based company operates in North and Central America, South America, EMEA (covering Europe, the Middle East, and Africa), East Asia, ASAO (covering ASEAN, South Asia, and Oceania), and Japan.
