Cementos Pacasmayo is a stone cold concrete company. Founded in 1949, it's the only cement manufacturer in northern Peru. It makes concrete blocks, ready-mix concrete, and produces quicklime, which is used by the mining industry to extract minerals. (Construction and mining are booming in Peru.) The company has two plants that can make about 3 million tons of cement and almost 250,000 tons of quicklime a year. It sold almost 2 million tons of cement and 120,000 tons of quicklime in 2010 through a network of more than 130 distributors. It also distributes third-party construction materials such as steel rebar, cables and pipes. Cementos Pacasmayo filed a stock offering in the US in early 2012.
