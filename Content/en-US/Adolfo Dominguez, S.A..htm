Adolfo Domínguez knows what men, women, and children want. The company designs, makes, and distributes fashionable apparel aimed mostly at upper-middle-class 30- to 45-year-olds. Adolfo Domínguez boasts more than 700 owned and franchised stores located in about 30 countries (some two-thirds in Spain). In addition to its men's and women's apparel, the company's products portfolio also includes cosmetics, eyewear, luggage, perfume, watches, and other items. Through licensing agreements with several firms, the company also takes in royalties through sales of cosmetics, eyewear, and other items.

Adolfo Domínguez opened a store in Mexico during 2016 as part of its effort to expand its footprint.

The company has expanded into the youth market with its U Line brand, aimed at 17- to 25-year-olds.
