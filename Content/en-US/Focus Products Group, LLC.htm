Focus Products Group designs and sells products for kitchens and bathrooms. The company's Electrics Group outfits commercial and residential kitchens with small appliances under the West Bend and Back to Basics brands. Focus Foodservice caters to caterers and other culinary professionals with products, such as carts, bakeware, shelving, and small hand-held appliances for commercial use. The Bath Group supplies personal care products for home and hotel bathrooms that include shower curtains and rods, hair dryers, mirrors, towel warmers, and more under the Shower Solutions, Steeltek, and Arcs & Angles brands. The company is owned by private equity firm Centre Lanes Partners LLC.
