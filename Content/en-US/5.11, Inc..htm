5.11 outfits some of the toughest men and women on the block. The company, which does business as 5.11 Tactical, makes a variety of clothing for law enforcement personnel, firefighters, and the military. Its products include tactical apparel, uniforms, leisurewear, outerwear, footwear, and accessories (such as eyewear, knives, holsters, and hats). Products are sold by arms and uniforms suppliers in the US and abroad, as well as by 5.11 through its catalogs and online store. Founded in 2003, the company is recognized for its signature 5.11 Tactical pants, which were originally designed for rock-climbing enthusiasts. It is majority owned by private equity firm TA Associates .
