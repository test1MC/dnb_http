This company might be responsible for the caffè at your café. illycaffè is one of Italy's leading coffee makers known around the world for its illy premium coffee brand. It roasts and packages whole bean and ground coffee for sale to consumers through grocery stores and specialty retail shops in 140 countries, as well as home brewing equipment and coffee accessories. illycaffè also supplies more than 50,000 restaurants, hotels and other hospitality operators with its 100% Arabica coffee products and accessory equipment. Francesco Illy, who invented the modern espresso machine, started the family-run business in 1933.
