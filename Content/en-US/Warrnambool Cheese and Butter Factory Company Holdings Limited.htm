Warrnambool Cheese and Butter Factory (WCB) is a top milk processor in Australia. Founded in 1888, it is also the country's oldest surviving dairy. It sources milk from dairy farmers located in the states of Victoria and South Australia and manufactures more than 83,000 tonnes of products each year. WCB makes butter, cream, cheese, milk powder, and whey protein in bulk for both domestic and international customers. Its most well-known product is Sungold milk. It also runs Cheese World. Situated across from the its factory, Cheese World is a tourist attraction that features a museum, a cheese and wine cellar, a supermarket, and a restaurant famous for its milkshakes. WCB is fending off takeover approaches.
