China Hewlett-Packard Company (CHP) has long represented its North American parent in Asia. Hewlett-Packard's operations in China include sales, manufacturing, research and development, and service units. Its broad array of offerings include personal computers, servers, data storage devices, printing and imaging products, networking equipment, enterprise software, and IT services. HP's Beijing-based operations include a branch of HP Labs . HP first established distribution operations in Beijing in 1981, and China Hewlett-Packard was established in 1985.
