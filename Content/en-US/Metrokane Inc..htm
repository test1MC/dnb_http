Metrokane's Rabbit, Bullet, and Houdini have been partners to quite a few parties. The company makes kitchen accessories such as barware, juicers, and other items, including the Rabbit Corkscrew, which can open a wine bottle in three seconds and appeared in more than 300 million catalogs its first year. The company's products also include the Houdini Wine & Beverage Chiller, Bullet Cocktail Shaker, and the Mighty OJ Juicer. Metrokane's products are peddled through online retailers ( Amazon.com ), big box retailers ( Target ), department stores ( Fortunoff and Stein Mart ), and specialty wine stores. President Riki Kane founded the company in 1983.
