Dogs, cats, guinea pigs, fish, chickens, canaries, pigeons, horses, turkeys, sheep, and llamas -- a whole Ark-full of animals give Blue Seal Feeds' products a first-class seal of approval. The company manufactures Blue Seal- and OrganicLife-branded wildlife, zoo and farm animal, and livestock feeds and supplements; canned and dry pet food; seed mixtures for wild and caged birds. On the people side of things, lawn and garden-maintenance products. Its lawn seed, feed, and weed-control products are sold under the Blue Seal and Safe 'N Simple brand names. The company sells its products in the eastern US and southern Canada. Blue Seal is a business unit of Kent Feeds .
