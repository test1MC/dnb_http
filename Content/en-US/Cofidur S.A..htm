Cofidur offers contract electronics manufacturing, including design and production of printed circuit boards and electronic components. It specializes in multilayer and double-sided boards, along with electronic cards, and provides logistics services in addition to assembly of products. The company was established in 1968 under the name Cirelec.

Cofidur has reported diminishing financial performance for the past two years. In 2015, the company reported revenue of €78 million, off from €85 million in 2014. Profit fell to about €2 million from €2.1 million.
