For Evolve Manufacturing Technologies (EMT), growth is all about change. The company provides turnkey, contract manufacturing services for companies in the semiconductor, biotech and medical device, and telecom industries. Founded in 1999, EMT began by manufacturing parts for machines that make computer chips. When Asian rivals began luring away Silicon Valley work, EMT shifted its sights to biotech and medical markets. EMT touts product assembly from electro-mechanical devices to the highly complex. Services include a swath of competencies: manufacturing engineering, supply chain management, product reliability and continuous improvement, and cost reduction solutions. EMT is led by its founder, Noreen King.
