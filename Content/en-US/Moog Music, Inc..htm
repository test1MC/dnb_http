Moog Music designs and manufactures analog effects modules, Theremins, analog synthesizers, and related products under names such as Etherwave, Moogerfooger, Little Phatty, and Minimoog Voyager. The company also offers repairs and servicing of its products built since 1996. And it sells hats, apparel, instructional DVDs, posters, and other "Moog stuff". The late Bob Moog, developer of the Moog analog synthesizer, founded Moog Music under the name Big Briar in 1978.
