Molds in buildings are usually a bad thing, but the molds of the type and strength made by Megamet Solid Metals are, on the contrary, a good thing. Specializing in metal injection molding, the subsidiary of CIC Group (formerly known as the Nooter Corporation) makes a variety of metal products for clients in such industries as medical, electronics, firearms, aerospace, and and automotive. Megamet Solid Metals utilizes a metal injection molding process that allows small metal parts such as door lock parts, hinges, power tool gears, and small piston tubes to be made with the precision shape ability of plastic injection molding but with the toughness of wrought and machined metal.
