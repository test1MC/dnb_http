Harlequin Enterprises has a deep, unquenched passion for romance novels. The publisher is synonymous with romance fiction, and has a portfolio of racy titles such as Incriminating Passion , Mistress on Loan , and Silent Desires . Imprints include Harlequin Teen, HQN, LUNA, MIRA, Red Dress Ink, and SPICE. Its books are published in series and as single titles, and are sold through retailers and directly to consumers. Harlequin publishes more than 110 titles a month in nearly 30 languages on six continents. It has offices in about 20 countries, including offices in Toronto, New York, and London. Its books are written by more than 1,200 authors worldwide. Harlequin is unit of Canadian media firm Torstar .
