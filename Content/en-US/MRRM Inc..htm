Perhaps spelling out "Mount Royal Rice Mills" got too time consuming, and thus MRRM was born. The company's business is split between two subsidiaries: Dainty Foods and Robert Reford shipping agency. The only rice milling company in Canada, Dainty Foods imports rice from the US, then processes and markets retail, industrial, and foodservice rice products under the Dainty, MRRM, and private-label brands. The Robert Reford business acts as a general or port agent to assist ships passing through Canadian waterways. Originally named Mount Royal Milling and Manufacturing, the company was founded by Robert Reford in 1882. It was acquired by a subsidiary of SIACOM Canada in 2015.
