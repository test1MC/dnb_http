Fab-Form Industries manufactures fabric forming systems used in the production of building materials. Concrete is poured into the forms to construct concrete footings, foundations, and walls for residences and commercial buildings. Among its products is the polyethylene Fast-Tube, which the company hopes will replace the cardboard cylinders generally used in making concrete columns on porches of homes. Fastfoot, its core product, is used to form concrete footings. President and CEO Richard Fearn and members of his family own a majority of Fab-Form.
