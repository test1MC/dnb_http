Mersen Group's brushes, which transfer the network current in a DC electric motor, fire up not only cars but industrial machinery and other products. The company, which specializes in carbon and graphite products, also makes permanent magnets (ferrite magnets) for cars and electrical protection fuses, as well as products for thermal, corrosion-resistant applications, such as graphite machined blocks and composite materials for brakes. Mersen's products serve a variety of industries from aerospace to power generation. The company has operations in more than 30 countries around the world.
