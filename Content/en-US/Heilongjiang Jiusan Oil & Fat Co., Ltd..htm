Heilongjiang Jiusan Oil & Fat Co. is an expert in soybeans. The company produces a variety of products from soybeans such as soybean oil (Grade 1), flakes, germs, isofavaone, and salad oil. Heilongjiang Jiusan also makes natural vitamin E, as well as soybean saponin, soybean mono-glyceride, soy tissue protein, and soy protein isolated. Its soybean oil, salad oil, flakes, and germs are certified NON-GMO (non-genetically modified organisms) products that have been tested by Hong Kong Dan Chips Limited. China Green Food Development Center has also certified the company's soybean and salad oil as green foods. The soybean industry in China, including Heilongjiang Jiusan, is heavily subsidized by the government.
