What with all the products the company makes, it is imperative that TECO Electric & Machinery stays current. The group is organized into industrial products (motors, fans, compressors), home appliances (air conditioners, TVs, refrigerators), IT (smart cards and readers, netbooks, motherboards), and construction and engineering (electrical and mechanical engineering and construction services). US-based subsidiary TECO-Westinghouse makes electric motors and generators. TECO followed rivals across the Taiwan Straits in 2005, investing in a compressor plant in mainland China. Founded in 1956 as an electric motor manufacturer, TECO has subsidiaries and affiliates located in Asia, Europe, and the Americas.
