Aerofil Technology specializes in aerosol filling, dry filling, and liquid filling and packaging services, using its 17 packaging lines. It also provides warehousing, distribution, and testing. In addition, Aerofil Technology's specialty packaging services include label changes, special promotions, and display assembly. It serves customers in a range of markets, including air dusters and cleaners, automotive products, fungicides and herbicides, household products, insecticides and pesticides, swimming pool chemicals, and pet products.
