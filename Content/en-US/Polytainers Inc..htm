Polytainers designs, manufactures, and distributes plastic containers for the dairy and food industries. Its thin-walled rigid plastic containers are used to package such products as cream cheese, margarine, sour cream, and yogurt. The company also provides graphic design and printing services. Polytainers has manufacturing facilities in Canada and the US, as well as about 10 sales offices from California to Nova Scotia. The company was founded in 1967. Customers have included Danone , Kraft , Loblaw Companies , and President's Choice.
