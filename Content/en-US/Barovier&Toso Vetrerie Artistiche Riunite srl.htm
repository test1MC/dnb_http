There's more than seven years of bad luck if you break a Barovier&Toso. The company's a leading glassware maker that creates designer vases, chandeliers, and light fixtures. It works with architectural types to produce custom-made pieces -- supplying hotels, public buildings, and more. Barovier&Toso's main showroom is in Milan. It also has franchised showrooms in Hong Kong, the Philippines, and Tokyo. The Barovier family, which has produced glassware for centuries, merged its business with the Toso family's (fellow glassmakers on the island of Murano) in 1936. Its history reaches back so far that the Guinness Book of World Records named the Baroviers as being the oldest family of glassmakers in the world.
