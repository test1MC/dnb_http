JDS Therapeutics develops and commercializes natural compounds for use in the marketing of drugs, nutritional supplements, and functional foods; the firm is primarily focused on psychiatry and women's health. Its products include Relizen, a supplement to help relieve menopause-related hot flashes, and Serenol, a supplement to ease the emotional symptoms of pre-menstrual syndrome. JDS also has a number of insulin-related compounds in its development pipeline. Subsidiary Nutrition 21 makes and sells chromium picolinate and other ingredients used in dietary supplement products and animal feed products; its ingredients are used in supplements including Chromax, Nitrosigine, Selenomax, and SelenoPure.
