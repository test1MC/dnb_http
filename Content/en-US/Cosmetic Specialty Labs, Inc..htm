Cosmetic Specialty Labs (CSL) believes in the healing power of aloe vera. The company, a contract manufacturer of private-label skin care, cosmetics, and health products that contain aloe vera, supplies more than 10,000 clients throughout the US and 20 other countries. Its Dream Valley Farms business, established in 1982, was one of the first to commercially grow hydroponic aloe under controlled greenhouse conditions. CSL was founded in 1973 by entrepreneur Edna Hennessee, who taught herself the aloe vera business and tested products on her salon clients. She died in 2011.
