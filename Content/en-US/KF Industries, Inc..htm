For KF Industries, one good turn deserves another, and another, and another. The company, part of Circor International 's Energy Products division, makes a swath of valve designs and sizes (ball, check, needle, butterfly), pneumatic and electric actuators, and pipeline closures. The company's flow control products are primarily used in the oil and gas, chemical, paper and pulp, metals and mining, and water and wastewater industries. Operating from a 180,000 sq. ft. manufacturing plant, KF Industries boasts sales of approximately 800,000 valves a year. The line is pedaled through a global distribution network. The manufacturer derives its KF moniker from the surnames of its founders, Gene Key and Odell Friar.
