Ecessa provides the high-speed ties that bind. The company makes and services components for wide-area data, voice, and video communications networks. Its PowerLink aggregators combine WAN links based on multiple technologies (DSL, cable, ISDN) for increased bandwidth. Applications for these components include high-speed Internet access, corporate networking, and videoconferencing. Ecessa sells to ISPs, telephone companies, corporations, and government agencies.
