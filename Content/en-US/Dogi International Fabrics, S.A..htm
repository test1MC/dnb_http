If you hear someone saying, "How much is that Dogi in the window," you can be pretty sure you're at a clothing shop rather than a pet store. Dogi International Fabrics, one of Europe's largest textile manufacturers, makes elastic fabrics and yarns for lingerie, swimwear, and sports apparel. Founded in 1954, the company now has subsidiaries located in the Americas, Asia, and Europe.
