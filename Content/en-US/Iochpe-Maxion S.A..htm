Iochpe-Maxion isn't trying to reinvent the wheel. As Brazil's top manufacturer of wheels, the company makes steel wheels for cars, trucks, buses, and farm machinery, as well as stamped metal frames, sidebars, crossbars, brake levers, and pedals for trucks, buses, and light commercial vehicles. Through Amsted Maxion, a joint venture with Amsted Industries , it makes railway freight cars, railway components, and industrial castings. Iochpe-Maxion has manufacturing plants in Brazil, China, and Mexico. Customers include car companies such as Fiat , Ford , and Volkswagen , as well as farm machinery and truck makers such as Caterpillar , CNH , John Deere , and Volvo .
