Founded in 1999, Maydream operates AdForum, an Internet portal for the global advertising, marketing, and communications industries. AdForum links to more than 24,000 agencies in 131 countries and provides information about advertising suppliers and vendors of advertising-related services; over 30,000 monthly searches are conducted by marketers, advertisers, and consultants. Subscribers also have access to as searchable library of more than 90,000 ads in various media (TV, print, interactive, outdoor). Maydream's headquarters are located in France and New York City, and the company also owns and operates offices in Canada and Switzerland.
