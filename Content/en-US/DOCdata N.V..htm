DOCdata has the prescription for digital sound. The company provides electronic fulfillment, online payment processing, and other e-commerce related services to businesses and consumers through its Docdata Internet services firm. The business also provides media services such as DVD and CD manufacturing and distribution for record labels, software developers, and film studios. The company's other main unit, IAI (Industrial Automation Integrators), employs optical and laser technology to build production systems used in a variety of applications, including film subtitles, packaging, and security printing. DOCdata has a strong presence in the Netherlands, Germany, and the UK.
