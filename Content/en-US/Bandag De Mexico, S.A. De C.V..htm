Bandag De Mexico helps clients Keep on Truckin'. The company produces and sells precured tread rubber and equipment used by its franchisees for the retreading of tires, primarily for trucks, but also for buses, light commercial trucks, industrial equipment, off-road equipment, and passenger cars. For businesses that maintain fleets of vehicles, Bandag De Mexico helps keep them road-ready with such commercial services as 24-hour emergency roadside services, trailer readiness, mounted wheel service, and scheduled fleet maintenance. Bandag De Mexico's US parent firm was purchased by Bridgestone Americas in 2007 for $1.05 billion.
