Founded by whirlpool pioneer Don Clarke in 1983, Clarke Products was the first manufacturer in the US to produce acrylic shower bases and sinks. Today it continues to hold a leading position in both of those market segments. The company designs and manufactures an array of luxury bathing products, including acrylic composite hydrojetted bathtubs, showers, and shower bases, as well as acrylic kitchen, bath, and laundry sinks for the home. These products are marketed to residential contractors under such brand names as Colorfloors and Sinksations.
