ABCO Refrigeration Supply keeps the northeast chilled. Through about 16 locations in Connecticut, Delaware, Maryland, Massachusetts, New Jersey, New York, and Pennsylvania, the company distributes air-conditioning and refrigeration equipment, parts, and supplies. Family-owned ABCO offers more than 250 brands of heating, ventilation, air conditioning, and refrigeration products including DuPont , Luxaire, Mitsubishi Electric , and Honeywell . ABCO provides online ordering and tracking for its contractor customers. The company also sells to home owners. About half of its proceeds come from customers in the New York metropolitan area.

The company's ABCO Engineering Resources offers authoritative, intelligent information about HVAC/R technologies, products and specifications. Apart from having on-site experts, ABCO Engineering Resources also operates a 24-hour Emergency hotline.

Training arm ABCO University offers its clients comprehensive training sessions in design, repair, and installation of new products and technologies.

The company markets its products through television advertising and via sales teams at its outlets. ABCO's seasonal 'Package Deals' organize product offerings in complete packages of related equipment, parts, and supplies at very competitive prices, which helps ABCO team members with their sales efforts to contractors. ABCO University is another component of its sales and marketing efforts.

Expanding its infrastructure, in 2013 ABCO opened its new 24,000 sq. ft. Hauppauge branch, featuring the company's largest ABCO University training center, with state-of-the-art facilities for training on design, repair and installation.

ABCO was founded in 1949 by Jules Gottleib; his son-in-law, Michael Senter, runs the company as CEO.
