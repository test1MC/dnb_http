If you like cold showers and dirty laundry, you'll have no need for what Haier Electronics Group has to offer. The company is the publicly-traded subsidiary of government-controlled Haier Group , China's largest appliance maker. Haier Electronics, once known for its wireless phone handsets, manufactures and sells a variety of products for the home, including air conditioners, water heaters, and kitchen appliances, as well as personal electronics (such as computers, cell phones, TVs, and Wi-Fi media players). The company also specializes in integrated home furniture design, appliance networking, and logistics services. Most of its sales are generated in mainland China.
