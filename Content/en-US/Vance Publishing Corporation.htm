Does your pig need a new hairdo? Vance Publishing can help. The company publishes about 15 business-to-business print publications, along with 30 related websites and 50 electronic newsletters, that serve a range of professionals from farmers ( Ag Professional ) to beauticians ( Modern Salon ). Vance's produce titles include Citrus & Vegetable and The Grower , while its protein division houses Pork and Dairy Heard Management . Its woodworking offerings provide information and education to professional woodworkers. The company also produces database products and hosts industry events. Vance has offices in Illinois and Kansas. Herbert Vance founded the firm in 1937.

Vance Publishing has offices in Illinois, Kansas, and Missouri. Its products are distributed throughout the US.

In addition to producing magazines, newsletters, and websites, the company also hosts a number of trade shows and events. Vance Publishing offers subscription-based data products and custom media products as well.

Vance Publishing is focused on expanding its operations in the digital media, data, and events markets. The company launched a new mobile application for publication The Packer in 2012 for instance. It has also invested in growing its Greenbook and Red Book Credit Services data offerings, and it hosted a new industry event for its Produce audience in 2012.

As part of this strategy, Vance Publishing sold off its nationally syndicated radio program, AgriTalk, to Farm Journal Media in 2012.
