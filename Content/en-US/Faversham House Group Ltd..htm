The Faversham House Group serves those who work to keep things green and pleasant. The company publishes about a dozen business-to-business magazines that cover four key industries: building services, the environment, home improvement, and visual communications. Titles include Air Conditioning & Refrigeration News and Housewares Magazine . Faversham House Group also organizes exhibitions, produces awards events and conferences, and operates about 10 websites, as well as related directories and special supplements. The Faversham House Group is controlled by the Gould family, which founded the firm in 1960.
