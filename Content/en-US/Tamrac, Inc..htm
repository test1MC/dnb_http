Tamrac's bags suit cameras to a "T." The company makes a wide variety of camera bags and camera-carrying systems designed for use in rugged conditions, such as those its customers face in outdoor and wilderness photography. Tamrac also makes and markets computer cases, backpacks, messenger bags, luggage, and accessories the likes of digital camera strap shock absorbers. The company's products are distributed in North America, Europe, and Japan, and they're sold in specialty camera stores, such as the Authentic Traveler, Amazon.com , and Wolf Camera . The company, founded in 1977 by amateur photographers, boasts manufacturing facilities in Chatsworth, California.
