Clover Industries' success in beverages has nothing to do with four-leaf luck. The holding company has grown from a single creamery in Natal to a giant South African food and beverage group, with certain products holding leading market positions due to a strong Clover brand. Operating subsidiary Clover S.A. (Pty) specializes in dairy, procuring some 30% of South Africa's raw milk from regional producers and processing it into a range of consumer products, from milk and yogurt to butter and cheese. These products are supplied to retailers, wholesale chain stores, and distributors nationwide. Clover S.A. also controls Clover Beverages, which produces and distributes branded fruit juices and flavored milk drinks.
