You don't need a passport to enter The Republic of Tea -- a taste for imported black, white, red, and green teas will get you there. The company specializes in the sale of whole tea leaves for home brewing but also sells tea bags and bottled, ready-to-drink teas. Among its numerous offerings are the wild blueberry black tea, ginger mango herb tea, Dancing Leaves green tea, and Emperor's White Tea from China. In addition to retail sales through its catalog and Web site, the company is a wholesaler to specialty food stores and retailers such as Whole Foods Market and Barnes & Noble . The Republic of Tea also offers accessories for serving and brewing tea.
