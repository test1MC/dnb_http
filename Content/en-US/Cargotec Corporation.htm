On the land and sea, Cargotec helps cargo move in harmony. The company provides materials handling equipment for loading and unloading goods through core brands Kalmar, Hiab, and MacGregor. Kalmar offers ship-to-shore cranes, shuttle carriers, container handlers, and other equipment, to port/terminal, distribution, industrial, and defense hubs. Hiab's on-road load handling lineup, including truck-mounted forklifts, is used by manufacturers as well as transportation, waste-handling, and rental companies. MacGregor specializes in cargo handling equipment (hatch covers and offshore cranes) for marine transports and the offshore industry. Europe, the Middle East and Africa account for about 50% of Cargotec's sales.
