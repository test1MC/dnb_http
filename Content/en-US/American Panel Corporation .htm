American Panel hopes that your next panel discussion is about how cool the company's products are. The manufacturer builds commercial solid door walk-in coolers, freezers, and insulated buildings. It also supplies blast chillers, and accessories from viewport windows to pressure relief vents and door kick plates. The company taps into restaurant markets, hotels, hospitals, supermarkets, and schools. American Panel's products are distinctive for their foam-in-place urethane insulation and use of fiberglass reinforced polymer door frames and perimeters, which provide efficient sealing. Its walk-in monitoring systems reportedly help achieve government mandated requirements for reducing energy consumption.
