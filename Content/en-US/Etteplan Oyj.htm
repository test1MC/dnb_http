Divided into two segments, Delivery Design and Product Development, Etteplan provides industrial engineering design services for large and midsized industrial companies. Delivery Design includes among its services mechanical, electrical, automation, and plant design and commissioning for equipment suppliers and plant operators. Product Development operates through long-term partnerships with clients. Etteplan also operates a laboratory that provides electromagnetic disturbance measurements. Etteplan focuses on clients in the automotive, equipment manufacturing, wood-processing, and electronics industries. Chairman Tapani Mönkkönen owns about one-third of the company.
