If you're looking for a taste treat with a distinct and spicy flavor, Chorizo De San Manuel is where it's at. Located in Edinburg, Texas, the company makes and distributes chorizo (an Hispanic spiced sausage, usually made with pork but sometimes beef). The firm's meat products, sold under the Chorizo De San Manuel and San Luis brands, are all-natural, with no added preservatives or fillers. They are available at food retailers including HEB , Wal-Mart and Sams Club , as well as through the company's Web site. Chorizo De San Manuel, founded in 1975, is owned and operated by the Flores family.
