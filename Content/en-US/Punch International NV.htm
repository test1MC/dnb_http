Punch International, an industrial holding company, tries to pack a punch with its diverse business segments. Its Punch Graphix unit makes digital printing (Xeikon brand) and prepress machinery (basysPrint brand) for the commercial, industrial, and newspaper markets. Punch Telematix, Punch's wireless applications division, develops telemetry and telematics gear. In 2009 the company sold a significant portion of its Motive (automotive) division, but it still manufactures continuously variable transmissions (CVT) for cars through its Powertrain division. SpaceChecker, part of Punch's Other businesses, provides tracking and tracing goods and vehicles via a geostationary satellite network.
