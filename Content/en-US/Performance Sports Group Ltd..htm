While best known for its ice hockey equipment and apparel, Performance Sports Group is also a major developer and manufacturer of roller hockey, lacrosse, baseball, and softball sports equipment and related apparel. It also makes and sells soccer apparel. The company claims the global leadership position in hockey, and the top North American position in baseball and softball. Its products are marketed under the BAUER (ice and roller hockey), MISSION (roller hockey), MAVERIK (lacrosse), CASCADE (lacrosse), INARIA (soccer apparel), EASTON (baseball and softball) and COMBAT (baseball and softball) brand names.

The company designs, develops, and manufactures high-performance sports equipment and related apparel.

Performance Sports' products are distributed by sales representatives and independent distributors worldwide.

The company's revenues grew by 51% in fiscal 2015, driven by a 50% growth in team uniform sales and a 50% growth in performance apparel sales.

Net income increase by 36% for the year.

Performance Sports claims to have the most recognized and strongest brands in ice hockey, roller hockey, baseball, and softball, and holds top market share positions in these sports, with an expanding presence in the fast-growing lacrosse market. It is focused on building its leadership position by growing market share in all product categories, including by pursuing strategic acquisitions.

Boosting its brand portfolio, in 2014 Performance Sports acquired the Easton baseball and softball business and the assets formerly used in Easton-Bell Sports, Inc.'s lacrosse business from Easton-Bell Sports, Inc., now named BRG Sports for $330 million in cash, plus a net working capital adjustment of $21.7 million. EASTON is one of the world's leading and most iconic baseball and softball brands.

In 2013 the company bought Combat Sports, a supplier of composite and hybrid composite products for both its own COMBAT brand as well as for high-end brand name companies in the baseball and softball bat, hockey stick and lacrosse shaft markets.
