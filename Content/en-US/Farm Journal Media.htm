Farm Journal Media has provided agricultural news for more than 125 years, and will likely print its flagship Farm Journal 'til the cows come home. The company publishes agricultural magazines and provides information via print and electronic media to farmers, ranchers, and agribusiness. Publications include Beef Today , Top Producer , and Dairy Today , while its Internet interests are served through AgWeb.com ("The Homepage of Agriculture") featuring online versions of its print magazines, as well as weather reports, market information, and agricultural e-commerce services. Farm Journal produces seminars and nationally syndicated TV and radio programs.
