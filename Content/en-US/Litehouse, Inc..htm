This company shines its light on salads. Litehouse is a leading maker of salad dressings, sauces, and vegetable dips under the Litehouse label. It produces a variety of dressings, including blue cheese, ranch, and vinaigrette, along with low fat and organic products. The company also makes fruit dips, glazes, and cheese crumbles, as well as apple cider, marinades, and freeze-dried herbs. With manufacturing facilities in Idaho, Utah, and Michigan, Litehouse products are sold through supermarkets and warehouse clubs in the US and Canada. Litehouse also supplies dressings and other products to food service distributors and restaurants. Founded by Edward Hawkins in 1963, Litehouse is run by the Hawkins family.

Idaho-based Litehouse serves North American consumers. It entered the Mexican retail market in 2011.

Litehouse makes, sells, and distributes refrigerated salad dressings, sauces, and dips. It operates manufacturing facilities in its home state of Idaho as well as in Michigan. It's expanding into Utah. As part of its business, Litehouse serves about 125 stores in the US and Canada.

The food firm has become a leader in refrigerated salad dressings, vegetable dips, sauces, instantly fresh herbs, blue cheese, caramel dips, fruit dips, and glazes. Banking on the growing popularity of Greek yogurt, Litehouse in late 2013 rolled out a new line of Greek style yogurt dressings under the OPA! by Litehouse name.

It's also building a new facility in Southern Utah, investing more than $10 million. The effort supplements its existing manufacturing plants in Idaho and Michigan.

Not known for making acquisitions, Litehouse has expanded in recent years through organic growth and through its purchase of Seattle's Green Garden Foods. Buying the maker of dressings, salsas, dips, mayonnaise, and more in 2010 has given Litehouse a deeper products portfolio. Like Litehouse, Green Garden also sells its products to grocery stores and food service providers and restaurants. The purchase has allowed Litehouse to expand its production capacity and product line.

Litehouse uses the slogan, "Because Taste Matters." Its products can be found in a variety of retailers the likes of Albertsons , Kroger , Target , Wal-Mart , Smart & Final , and AAFES , among others.
