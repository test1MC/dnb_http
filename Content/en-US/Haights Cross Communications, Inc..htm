Haights Cross Communications helps students buckle down on their studies. The company offers test preparation and learning materials for educators and school systems, public and school libraries, and consumers. Its Triumph Learning business includes imprints such as Buckle Down (state assessment and test preparation materials for grades 2-12) and Options Publishing (skills assessment products for math and reading students in K-8 grade).  Haights Cross also publishes audio books for public libraries and schools through its Recorded Books business.  Haights Cross was founded in 1997. Saddled with debt, the company filed for Chapter 11 in 2010; about a month later, it emerged from bankruptcy.
