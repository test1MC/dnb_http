La India Packing Company is actually situated closer to el México than to the home of the Taj Mahal. Located in Laredo, Texas, the company sells authentic Hispanic herbs, spices, and teas. It offers tejano and fajita seasoning, pica rico (for seasoning fruits), Mexican chocolate and more. Its products are sold in bulk, boxed, and bagged. They are available online at La India's Web site and at the company's store in Laredo, where the owners also operate a small restaurant called the Tasting Room Cafe. La India's spices are also carried by such retail outlets in Texas as Albertsons , H.E.B , Kmart , and Wal-mart . Owned and operated by the Rodriguez family, La India Packing Company was founded in 1924.
