St. Joseph Communications -- the largest private printer in Canada -- provides commercial printing services from two production facilities in Toronto. Its flagship St. Joseph Print business prints catalogs, inserts, and brochures for clients such as Sears Canada , Bell Canada , and Kraft . The company's 50-plus ePRINTit stores -- part of its Documents division -- offer document management services, including quick printing, online print job submission, and electronic and physical document storage. Additionally, its St. Joseph Content division handles brand management, design, photography, and digital pre-media. St. Joseph Media publishes magazines. Honorary chairman Gaetano Gagliano founded the company in 1956.
