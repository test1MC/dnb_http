Franco Manufacturing makes a wide range of products. The company produces home-furnishing textiles, including bedding, kitchen and bath towels, through a 480,000-sq.-ft. facility in South Carolina. One of the top US-based home textile companies, the firm makes a range of bedding and clothing products for both children and adults, as well as furnishings (including pillows and curtains) under many leading brand names, including Disney , Dreamworks , Hasbro , Nickelodeon , Paul Frank, and Warner Bros. Franco Manufacturing's products are sold through department stores, specialty stores, and mass market retailers.

New Jersey-based Franco Manufacturing enjoys a global reach. Besides its 480,000-sq.-ft. textile plant in Dillon, South Carolina, the company operates a showroom in New York and overseas offices in Pakistan and Shanghai.

Franco Manufacturing sells its textile items through department stores, mass market retailers, and specialty stores. Typical customers include Target , Wal-Mart , Kmart , Toys R Us , Amazon , and Bed Bath & Beyond .

The company's distribution reaches 90% of consumers located in the US and Canada.

The company is owned by the Franco family. Franco Manufacturing was founded in 1952 by David E. Franco and his three sons (Louis, Morris, and Jack). The five sons of brothers Louis, Morris, and Jack now run the business.
