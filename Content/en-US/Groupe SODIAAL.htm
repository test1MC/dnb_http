With 10,000 member/farmers, Sodiaal is France's largest dairy cooperative. It produces fluid milk, cheese, and industrial dairy products, and licenses and distributes Yoplait, the #1 worldwide yogurt brand, throughout Europe. (Yoplait is 51%-owned by US-based General Mills ; Sodiaal controls 49%.) Sodiaal's key unit, Candia, produces such brands as GrandLait and Babylait (milk). The company has operations in France and sells its products internationally in about 80 countries, primarily in Germany, Italy, Europe, the Middle East, Asia, and Belgium. In 2014 Sodiaal expanded through the merger of 3A Corporation, another large dairy cooperative in France.
