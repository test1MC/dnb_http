The Beggars Group feeds the hungry-for-independent-music-lover's soul. The company boasts a collection of recording labels, including 4AD, XL Recordings, Rough Trade, Beggars Banquet, Stolen Recordings, Young Turks, and the New York-based indie label Matador Records . The company has established the Beggar's Archive site to serve the back catalog of all of the Beggars Labels older artists and releases. Its Beggar's Music division expands the Beggars Group's publishing interests by supporting writers with a full service office backed with uniquely experienced management. Current Beggars Group artists the Cult, Dead Can Dance, Deerhunter, Dizzee Rascal, The Fall, The Go-Betweens, Giggs, and Grimes.

The Beggars Group operates in Canada, China, Germany, and the US.

Martin Mills launched the company as The Beggars Banquet in 1976, with the onset of the punk music scene in the UK.
