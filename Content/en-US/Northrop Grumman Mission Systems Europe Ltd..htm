Northrop Grumman Mission Systems Europe, by any other name, wouldn't be as descriptive. A subsidiary of US defense giant Northrop Grumman , it specializes in developing C4I (Command, Control, Communications, Computers, and Intelligence) systems for the US and its European allies. Typical defense systems include command support systems, communication/command/control systems, data communication systems and equipment, tactical information systems. Northrop Grumman Mission Systems Europe has also developed Common Operating Environment (COE) software that improves interoperability, provides a common interface, and reduces testing and integration costs.

As military communications and intelligence systems have grown more complex, defense contractors and governments have recognized the need to build systems on common platforms so that intelligence gathered in one location may be easily shared with allies without having to convert or massage the data into a new format. Northrop Grumman Mission Systems Europe specializes in providing such system interoperability.
