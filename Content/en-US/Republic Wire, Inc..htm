Pledge your allegiance to Republic Wire, 'cause its products are made in the USA. Republic Wire manufactures and distributes bare and tinned copper wire, insulated building wire, polyweatherproof wire, and tracer wire. Its wires come in spools, reels, coils and master reels and are sold to electrical wholesale distributors, industrial and commercial distributors, utility companies, and exporters and brokers across the US. Republic Wire also sells its products online, but Internet sales represent fewer than 25% of total sales. CEO Ron Rosenbeck formed the company in 1982.
