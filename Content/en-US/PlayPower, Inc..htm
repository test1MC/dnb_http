PlayPower knows that play is serious business. The company is a leading manufacturer of commercial playground equipment, floating dock systems, and personal water craft. PlayPower owns several companies, such as Miracle Recreation Equipment and Little Tikes Commercial, which both make playground equipment for schools and parks nationwide. Other companies in PlayPower's network include Soft Play (manufacturer of indoor play equipment for malls, restaurants, and amusement parks), HAGS (Swedish maker of wood and steel playground equipment), and EZ Dock (manufacturer of a floating dock system, boat lift, and personal water craft lift). PlayPower is owned by Investcorp SA .
