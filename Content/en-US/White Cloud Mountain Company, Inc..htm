White Cloud Mountain Company, which does business as White Cloud Coffee, produces specialty coffees for both retail and wholesale accounts. The Idaho-based company makes and sells dark roast, variously flavored, espresso, and decaffeinated coffees. Individuals can order White Cloud coffees through the company's Web site. Its CoffeeConnect program rewards customers for referring the company to friends and family. White Cloud was founded by company CEO, Jerome Eberharter.
