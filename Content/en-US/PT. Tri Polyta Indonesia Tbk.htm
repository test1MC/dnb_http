PT. Tri Polyta Indonesia wraps things up. As Indonesia's largest manufacturer of polypropylene resin, the company's primary products include film resins and yarn resins, used in food packaging, garment and fabric bags, tobacco wrap, paper lamination, and woven sacks. Other major applications include appliance and auto parts and furniture. It sells its products under the Trilene brand, primarily in Indonesia, but also exports to other points in Asia.
