Tubacex, one of the world's largest manufacturers of non-welded stainless steel pipes, makes a variety of pipes and other products used across the iron and steel industry. Known for its integrated production, the company specializes in producing mechanical tubes, pipe fittings, high-nickel alloys, and welding pipes. It serves several sectors, such as the aerospace, petroleum, and petrochemical industries. Founded in 1963, Tubacex has manufacturing plants located in Austria, Spain, and the US (in Houston, Texas). The pipe maker exports its products to more than 100 countries. The European Union represents its largest geographical market and brings in more than half of sales.
