Memorex Products never forgets to entertain. The company markets recordable optical media, including CDs, DVDs, and Blu-ray disks, used to store data, photos, video, and music. It also provides optical storage drives (CD-RW, DVD-RW), flash memory drives, and hard disk drives. Its consumer electronics offerings include digital music players and photo frames, accessories for Apple 's iPod players, portable DVD players, LCD and CRT televisions, karaoke machines, and voice recorders. Memorex is a subsidiary and brand of data storage media giant Imation .
