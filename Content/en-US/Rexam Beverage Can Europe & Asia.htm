Rexam Beverage Can Europe & Asia has a canny way with beverages. A business segment of Rexam , Rexam Beverage Can Europe & Asia is the largest can manufacturer in Europe. The company makes a wide variety of cans and can ends at its 20+ plants throughout Europe. True to its name, Rexam Beverage Can Europe & Asia also has plants in China, India, and South Korea, as well as Egypt. Its beverage cans range from small "stubby" cans to the slim cans used for energy drinks to one-liter cans. Product feature options include large opening ends, promotional tabs and ends, thermo-sensitive ink, and fluting. The company's cans are used for beer, fizzy drinks, energy drinks, iced coffee, juices, water, and wine.
