The chef has a restaurant full of hungry diners. He has to whip something up to please the palate. So he gets out his ingredients, including those from First Spice Mixing. The company makes a full array of spice blends, seasonings, sauces, marinades, breading, soup bases, and other food ingredients for its North American foodservice and grocery retailing customers. It also provides research, product development, batching, and packaging services. First Spice Mixing boasts facilities in New York, San Francisco, and Toronto. The ingredients company was founded by German immigrant Felix Epstein in 1940 and is operated by his descendants.
