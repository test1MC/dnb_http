Running full steam ahead, Anchor Brewers & Distillers offers more than a handful of craft brews. Led by its flagship Anchor Steam, its beers and ales also include Anchor Porter, Anchor Summer, Anchor Bock, Liberty Ale, Old Foghorn, and Christmas Ale -- a seasonal offering that changes each year. Anchor Brewers & Distillers sells its beer in the US, Canada, the Asia Pacific, and Europe. It also operates Anchor Distilling, which makes Junípero and Genevieve gins and Old Potrero Whiskey. Begun in the 1860s, the brewery was saved from bankruptcy in 1965 by former owner and brewmaster Fritz Maytag (an heir to the Maytag fortune). Maytag became chairman emeritus in 2010, when The Griffin Group bought the brewer.
