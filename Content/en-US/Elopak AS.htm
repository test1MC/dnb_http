Elopak has figured out how to make milk cartons curvier, and juice boxes more convenient! The company ranks as the world's #3 supplier of packaging for beverages, and boasts manufacture of the first paperboard carton for liquid foods, branded Pure-Pak. Elopak's carton portfolio includes an array of board technologies (foil laminate and non-foil, opacity and gas-tight barriers), sizes, and shapes. More than 90% of sales are garnered from customers outside of Norway, with promising growth in Asia. Elopak is owned by Norway's largest privately owned industrial group, FERD Holding, and accounts for the largest share of the group's revenue. FERD is held by the Andresen family, and led by Johan Andresen, Jr.
