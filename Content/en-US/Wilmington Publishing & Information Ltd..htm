Wilmington Publishing & Information is more than willing to serve up a heavy dose of business-to-business information. A publishing division of Wilmington Group , the company is a provider of business information across a variety of industry sectors, including UK charities, human resources and training, legal, media, and finance and accounting. Products include print and online directories, magazines, reports, mailing lists, legal diaries, and online data services. Its weekly legal magazine Solicitors Journal is a leading independent magazine for solicitors. It also publishes membership directories for The Institute of Chartered Accounts in England & Wales and Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors.
