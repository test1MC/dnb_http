Kingway Brewery Holdings is a leading beer maker in China with more than a half-dozen breweries. It distributes and markets beer in more than 20 provinces under such labels as Kingway, Kingway Draft, and Super Fresh Kingway. In 2011 a joint venture between international brewing giant Heineken and Singapore's Asia Pacific Breweries sold its roughly 20% stake in Kingway Brewery to government-owned Guangdong Holdings Group (GHG). The deal boosted GHG's ownership stake to 73%. Established in 1997, Kingway Brewery Holdings was acquired by China Resources Snow Breweries , a joint venture of SABMiller and China Resources Beer in 2013 for about $864 million.
