Interior Concepts Corporation is calling on those who call the man or woman of the house. The company's a custom manufacturer of furniture for use in call centers, computer labs, reception areas, technology stations, and school environments. Interior Concepts's primary product lines include thin-line paneling and wire-management systems. The company's products are sold under the Swurv, Pro-Fit Furniture, and Degrees brand names. The furniture, seating, and accessories at Interior Concepts are sold through the company's network of sales professionals.
