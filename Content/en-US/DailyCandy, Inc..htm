DailyCandy hopes to draw affluent women to its website like kids to a candy store. The site and free e-mail newsletters include content on fashion, travel info, restaurants, shopping, and other sweet activities. Companies such as Tiffany , HP , Disney , and Gap , place ads next to the yummy bits of information hoping viewers also have a craving for items such as jewelry, movies, and computers. DailyCandy offers newsletters for a dozen cities, including London, San Francisco, and (of course) the Big Apple. The company satisfies the trend appetites of more than 2.5 million subscribers. Editor-in-chief Dany Levy cooked up DailyCandy in 2000. Today it is owned by the Willy Wonka of media companies, Comcast Corp .
