DSG International leaves the competition all wet. The bulk of the company's business is making disposable baby diapers, including the Drypers and Fitti brands, as well as the economy-priced Baby Love line, among other brands. It has seven manufacturing companies, including US-located Associated Hygenic Products (AHP). DSG also manufactures its products in in Southeast Asia and China. The firm makes sanitary napkins, training pants, and adult incontinence products under the Certainty and Drypers names. In addition, it manufactures private-label diapers for supermarkets such as A&P and drugstores such as Walgreen . Chairman and CEO Brandon Wang owns some 46% of DSG.
