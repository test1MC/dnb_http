Oki Networks puts out the word the new-fashioned way. The company manufactures computer telephony equipment used to transmit, receive, and distribute voice and data over IP networks. Its products include voice gateways and wireless broadband systems. Oki Networks also makes systems that enable videoconferencing (Visual Nexus) and converged voice, data, and messaging functions for call center applications (CTstage), as well as communications network servers (CenterStage). The company sells directly and through resale agreements with other equipment vendors, including Toshiba and Panasonic . Customers include corporations, ISPs, and telecom carriers. Oki Networks is a subsidiary of Tokyo-based Oki Electric Industry .
