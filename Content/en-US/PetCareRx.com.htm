PetCareRx wants its customers to pay less to take care of their pets. The online retailer sells prescription and non-prescription medications and supplements at up to half off what they would typically cost when purchased from veterinarian offices. PetCareRx provides products for cats, dogs, birds, fish, chinchillas, guinea pigs, rabbits, and other small animals. The pet care company markets its items through a namesake website and offers additional pet care products, such as toys, treats, pet apparel, bedding, and grooming supplies. Additionally, staff pharmacists and veterinarians are available to answer customer questions online. The company was founded in 1998.
