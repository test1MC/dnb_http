Wise Metals Group's wise metals strategy is to make aluminum can stock for companies in the beverage and food packaging industries worldwide ( Wise Alloys is the world #3 producer). Wise Alloys uses recycled aluminum to produce its can stock, and sister company Wise Recycling is one of the top collectors of aluminum beverage containers in the US and operates shipping and processing facilities which support a nationwide network of neighborhood collection locations. Wise Metals also provides maintenance and repair services for manufacturing plants (Listerhill Total Maintenance Center), and electric motors (Alabama Electric Motor Services). Chairman and CEO David D'Addario controls Wise Metals.

The company has facilities in Alabama, Colorado, Florida, Kentucky, North Carolina, New Mexico, Tennessee, and Virginia. Wise Recycling operates 7 shipping and processing locations which support a network of more than 30 neighborhood collection centers.

Wise Metal recycles and reuses more than 1.6 million gallons of the oil it uses in the production of aluminum sheet per year. It also annually recycles and reuses more than 275,000 gallons of spent solvents.

In 2013 Wise Metals disclosed that GE Capital serves as administrative agent on $340 million in revolving credit facilities for subsidiaries of Wise Metals which enable it to grow and expand its businesses.

Seeking some guarantee of financial stability in terms of wage costs, in 2012 Wise Metals Group the company signed a new five-year labor contract with its largest union, United Steelworkers Local 200.

That year the company completed a $25 million expansion of its Alabama Reclamation Operation in Muscle Shoals, a move that expanded its capabilities and added more than 40 new employees.

In a move to be closer to its assets in Alabama (and to save costs), in 2011 Wise Metals Group move its corporate headquarters from Baltimore, Maryland, to Muscle Shoals, Alabama. The move relocated the head office to near Wise Alloys , Alabama Reclamation Operations, Alabama Electric Motors Service, Total Maintenance Center, and a number of recycling centers in the US Southeast.
