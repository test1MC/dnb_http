Roto Smeets De Boer Holding BV knows that a rose by a shorter name actually smells sweeter. Known as RSDB, the company offers printing and marketing services to businesses in nine Western European countries and the US. The company's Print Productions includes Roto Smeets (sales and production), Senefelder Misset (printing), Antok (printing) as well as sheet fed printers Roto Smeets GrafiServices and afterpress and pre-distribution by De Wit Graphic Projects, Nadruk, and Rotopack. Marketing communications fall to 2Organize, dem communications, DesignDomain, LogicUse, Media Partners, PlantijnCasparie DM, and SLiM. Magazines and commercial printing account for a majority of sales.
