CorMedix is a commercial-stage biopharmaceutical company which seeks to in-license, develop, and commercialize therapeutic products for the prevention and treatment of cardiac, renal, and infectious diseases. Its first commercial product in Europe is Neutrolin, a catheter lock for the prevention of catheter related bloodstream infections and maintenance of catheter patency in tunneled, cuffed, central venous catheters used for vascular access in hemodialysis, oncology, critical care, and other patients. CorMedix, controlled by a group of its officers, went public in early 2010.
