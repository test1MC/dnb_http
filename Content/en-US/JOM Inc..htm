Doing business as CHIPCO International, JOM is a leading manufacturer of professional gaming poker chips that are customizable and embedded with counterfeit protection for casinos, collectors, and retailers. The company operates a manufacturing facility that produces 40,000 chips per day. It makes both the ProTech and Unicorn series chips, which are played in casinos around the world. CHIPCO also makes chips with embedded radio frequency identification (RFID) technology, as well as such ancillary products as plastic chip trays and metal alloy tokens. CEO John Kendall has controlled the company since 1985.
