The mission of Access Intelligence (formerly PBI Media) is to provide business information. The company's portfolio of products includes magazines, newsletters, websites, conferences, data products, and e-media solutions. Access Intelligence covers about 10 industry specializations, such as aerospace, healthcare, and communications. Brands include Defense Daily , CableFAX Daily , Aircraft Value News , and PR News newsletters; Via Satellite , Studio Monthly , and Folio magazines; online health search engine CMRG.com; and the SATELLITE Conference & Exhibition. The company has about six offices in the US and one in Germany. A private equity affiliate of Veronis Suhler Stevenson Partners owns Access Intelligence.
