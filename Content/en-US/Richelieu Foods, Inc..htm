Richelieu Foods stocks pantries and freezer shelves with its private-label and contract-packaged foods. The company develops and markets store-branded products, including marinades, salad dressings, sandwich spreads, salsas, and sauces. Richelieu is the nation's top supplier of premium private-label frozen pizza to leading national retailers. The company's Bonne Chere division packages more than 300 types of dressings and sauces for foodservice customers. Catering to health-conscious customers, Richelieu's products can be prepared with organic ingredients. Richelieu Foods, which operates throughout four US states, is owned by the private-equity arm of Centerview Partners.

Massachusetts-based Richelieu Foods operates manufacturing plants in Illinois, Iowa, Ohio, and Wisconsin.

Richelieu Foods serves retailers, such as small chains, specialty food distributors, regional wholesalers, club stores, mass merchandisers, and hypermarkets, as well as corporate brand, foodservice, and contract packing customers.

Richelieu Foods operates three business segments: retail frozen pizza, retail dressings and sauces, and foodservice dressings and sauces. The company makes more than 50 million pizzas annually at its pizza-making plant in Beaver Dam, Wisconsin. It manufactures pizza crusts in Iowa; dressings and sauces for foodservice customers in Elk Grove Village, Illinois; and retail dressings and sauces in Grundy Center, Iowa.

The company rings up $250 million in annual sales.
