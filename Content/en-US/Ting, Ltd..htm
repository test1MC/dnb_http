A desire to stir up rich flavors gets Ting cooking. Doing business as The Wizard's Cauldron, the company is a leading producer of organic condiments, dressings, sauces, and marinades. It makes private-label products for strategic partners, which have included Whole Foods , Albert's Organics , Central Market , Spectrum , Earth Fare , and Lund Food Holdings . The Wizard's Cauldron also provides research and product development services. The company was founded by John "The Wizard" Troy in 1990.
