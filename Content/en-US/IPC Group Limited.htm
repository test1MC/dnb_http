Part of Britain's publishing royalty, IPC Group Limited is a ruler (though it has joined a large American media family). One of the UK's leading consumer magazine publishers, IPC Group is owned by Time Warner subsidiary Time Inc. , and has a collection of about 60 titles. Its brands are organized in groups that target three core audiences: men (IPC Inspire), mass-market women (IPC Connect), and up-market women (IPC Southbank). Among IPC Inspire's titles are Horse and Hound and Country Life , while IPC Connect counts Look and Now in its foster of publications, and IPC Southbank includes Marie Claire and InStyle . IPC Group was formed in 1963 from the merger of three UK publishers (Newnes, Odhams, and Fleetway).
