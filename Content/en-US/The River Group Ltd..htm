A steady flow of readers, that's what The River Group aims to deliver. The custom publisher, which does business as River Publishing, produces marketing-centered customer magazines for such firms as WeightWatchers International , Honda , Virgin , Liberty , and Holland & Barrett . It also publishes online versions of its customer magazines, as well as other digital content (videos, blogs, and websites). In addition, River Publishing offers promotions and special events planning and organization, as well as ad sales services for clients who want their publications to be self-funding. The company was founded in 1994.
