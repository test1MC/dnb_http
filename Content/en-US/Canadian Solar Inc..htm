Talk about a global company -- Canadian Solar makes solar modules in China and sell them to Europe. The company designs and produces standard and specialty solar modules for residential, commercial, and industrial use. It also customizes products (including solar car battery chargers) for OEMs and makes rooftop-mounted, ready-to-install solar system kits for customers in Canada and Japan. Canadian Solar has seven manufacturing plants in China; its first plant in Canada opened in 2010. The company sells its products to distributors, systems integrators, and manufacturers. Customers in Europe account for about 80% of sales. Founder, chairman, president, and CEO Shawn Qu owns about 30% of Canadian Solar.
