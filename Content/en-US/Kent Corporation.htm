Iowa's corn harvest is an integral part of this company's business plan. Kent Corporation (formerly Muscatine Foods) operates through a diverse group of agricultural enterprises. Its largest subsidiary, Grain Processing Corporation , is a corn wet miller that produces corn-based products for food and pharmaceutical use. The company's Kent Nutrition Group churns out animal feed that's sold under several brand names, such as Kent Feeds , Evergreen Mills , Blue Seal , and Sentinel Feeds, to commercial livestock producers. Subsidiary Precision Foods blends and packages dry soups, sauces, and mixes for sale to foodservice and retail customers.

Kent's business includes a handful of operating subsidiaries. Grain Processing Corporation , Kent Nutrition Group (feed for lifestock), and Kent Pet Group (pet food) are headquartered in Muscatine, Iowa. The company's Kent Precision Foods Group (blending and packaging for domestic and international foodservice customers) is based in St. Louis, Missouri.

The company operates manufacturing facilities throughout the Midwest, Mid-Atlantic, and Northeast US that cater to customers worldwide. Its flagship subsidiary, Grain Processing Corporation , is among the largest consumers of Iowa-grown grain, reportedly purchasing more than $400 million of corn a year from farmers. It turns corn into beverage and industrial alcohol, corn starches, maltodextrins (sweeteners), and corn oil.

From its headquarters in Iowa, Kent supports its global presence through operations in the US and Canada.

The company has evolved from a small mill that manufactured cattle feed to a conglomerate interested in producing, distributing, and marketing food and nutritional products for animals and people. Its growth continues to be fueled by improvements in technology as well as strategic acquisitions that expand its capacity and portfolio of branded products. In 2013 GSTC Logistics, Inc. of Walford, Iowa, began construction of a new 600,000-sq.-ft. warehouse and distribution center in Muscatine to serve Kent's Grain Processing Corporation, Kent Nutrition Group, and Kent Pet Group businesses.

In 2012 Kent Nutrition Group and J.D. Heiskell & Company formed a joint venture to provide dairy operators in the northeastern US with mixed dairy feeds, feed commodities, mineral packages, and other nutrition inputs. Operating under the name Gold Star Feed and Grain, LLC, the business gets its management expertise from J.D. Heiskell & Company while Kent Nutrition Group offers four feed mills and proprietary nutritional products.

Formerly named Varied Investments and Muscatine Foods Corporation, Kent is owned by the Kent family.
