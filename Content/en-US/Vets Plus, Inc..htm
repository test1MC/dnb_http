Vets Plus is a positive for animal professionals looking to perk up some pets or animate some animals. The company makes and markets healthcare products for livestock, farm, and domestic animals. Its products include skin and hair care products and dietary supplements. The company serves the international veterinary community through a network of distributors. Its brands include Horses Prefer, Pets Prefer, Goats Prefer, DVM Formula, and Probios. Vets Plus purchased Bomac Animal Health USA in 2006. It is now the exclusive US supplier of Bomac's products. Bomac's brand names include Feron 20 (iron deficiency treatment) and Paratak Plus (roundworm treatment).
