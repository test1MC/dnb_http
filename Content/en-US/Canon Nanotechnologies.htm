Canon Nanotechnologies makes specialized lithography equipment used to imprint circuit designs onto semiconductor wafers and to fabricate nanoscale devices, micromachines, and structures. Its Imprio systems use a technology called step-and-flash imprint lithography. The company has installed advanced lithography tools at Cardiff University in the UK, Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory , Pennsylvania State University , the University of Texas at Austin , and the University of Twente in the Netherlands. The company was established in 2001; it was acquired by Canon in 2014.
