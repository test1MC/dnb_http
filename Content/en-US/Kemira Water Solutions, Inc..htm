Kemira Water Solutions hopes to make rivers flow cleaner. The company manufactures a wide range of iron and aluminum coagulants for the wastewater and potable water industries. Kemira Water Solutions provides customer-specific sewage treatment, odor control, and soil erosion and related services for municipalities, industrial companies, and agricultural operations. The company, which has offices in the US and Canada is one of North America's largest suppliers of inorganic coagulants. Kemira Water Solutions is the US subsidiary of leading Finnish chemical company Kemira Oyj .
