Craftmade International is no celebrity, but it is accustomed to fans and bright lights. The company designs, manufactures, and distributes Craftmade brand ceiling fans, as well as lights for home use. Through its subsidiaries -- Woodard, Prime/Home Impressions, and Trade Source International -- it sells more than 80 fan models, separate light kits, doorbells, and accessories. It also offers bathstrip and outdoor lighting, lamps, and Woodard brand outdoor furniture. Asian manufacturers produce most Craftmade lines, sold to specialty retailers and mass merchandisers, including Lowe's (31% of revenues) and Costco (7%). Craftmade also owns a 50% stake in Design Trends, a Chinese distributor of lamps and shades.
