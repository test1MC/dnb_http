Tishcon's petri dish is filled to the brim with pharmaceutical formulations. The company is a contract manufacturer of over-the-counter pharmaceuticals, vitamins, minerals, and other nutritionals. The company has the ability to make a variety of oral drug-delivery systems, including capsules, chewable tablets, and softgels; it also makes instant, sustained, and dual release formulas. In addition to its core offering of vitamin supplements and over-the-counter (OTC) drugs, it also makes specialty items including powdered drink mixes, herbal extracts, and topical skin products. Tishcon also provides quality services including laboratory testing and chemical and biological analysis.

Tishcon's branded drug delivery offerings include Bio-Solv, a soft capsule formulation that is highly soluble, as well as the Rapid Solv (tablets and caplets) and Snipcaps, Snipgels, and Snipsules lines (gel capsules designed to be cut or snipped and the contents squeezed out). Its skin products include bath oil softgels and Dermagen topical ointments. The company has the capacity to produce up to 1 billion softgels and 3 billion tablets for its customers each year.

Tishcon operates manufacturing centers in Westbury, New York (softgels), and Salisbury, Maryland (capsules and tablets). The Westbury center also contains product development and quality control laboratories, while the Salisbury center provides packaging and distribution services. Quality testing for the Maryland operations is conducted at a satellite laboratory.

The company is focused on widening its product and service offerings in fields including formulation, nutrition research, product development, and food technology. It also looks to expand existing services into new geographic markets. In addition, Tishcon aims to grow through new or extended third-party manufacturing contracts.

The company was founded as a New York-based contract manufacturer of bulk products for wholesalers and mail-order marketers. It was formed in 1976 by CEO Raj Chopra and Vipin Patel.
