Hillandale is a supplier of shell eggs to supermarkets, foodservice operators, restaurants, and food processors throughout the US. The company produces eggs -- actually its hens do the producing -- at facilities located in the Northeast, Southeast, and Midwest. Its brand names include Hillandale Farms, Nearby Eggs, and Hartford Farms, and it is a licensed producer of Eggland's Best eggs. Hillandale also offers products that go with eggs: butter, cheese, bacon, English muffins, bread sticks, and bagels. Hillandale was one of two Iowa egg producers involved in the 2010 recall of some 380 million salmonella -tainted eggs that resulted in almost 2,000 illnesses. Hillandale is owned by Cal-Maine Foods .
