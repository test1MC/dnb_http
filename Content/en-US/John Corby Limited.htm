Was John Corby the first man to be mindful of the crease in his trousers? It's doubtful. But John Corby did start the company that for quite some time has been catering to such men (and women). Founded in 1930, the company makes and markets electric presses and other ironing products for sale to retailers and hotels. John Corby further caters to hotels by manufacturing hotel accessories, such as makeup or shaving mirrors, hair dryers, clotheslines, and electric kettles. The company, a subsidiary of Jourdan plc , distributes its products directly to hotels and travel specialty retailers throughout the UK, with select distribution elsewhere worldwide.
