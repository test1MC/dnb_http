All American Group makes modular housing and other structures. The company's residential housing segment includes the All American Homes and Mod-U-Kraf Homes subsidiaries, which build one-story ranch homes and two-story colonials. Its Ameri-Log Homes brand makes log homes and a line of energy-efficient houses are sold under the Solar Village banner. Subsidiary Innovative Design and Building Serives specializes in larger-scale housing projects such as apartments, hotels, and military and student housing. All American Group also sells a line of wheelchair-accessible buses through a joint venture with ARBOC Mobility. H.I.G. Capital acquired All American Group in 2011.
