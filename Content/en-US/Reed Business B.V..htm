RBI NL knows how much all those tulips are worth. Reed Business Information Netherlands (RBI NL) is a publisher of trade magazines, newsletters, and other business periodicals. Its Beleggers Belangen is the largest weekly investment magazine in the Netherlands. The company covers industries such architecture, healthcare, and high-technology. Titles include Bestelauto (automotive), Boerderij (agriculture), and Distrifood (food). The company also publishes related websites. RBI NL is the principal European operation (outside the UK) of trade magazine publisher Reed Business Information , itself a part of global business information publisher RELX Group plc .
