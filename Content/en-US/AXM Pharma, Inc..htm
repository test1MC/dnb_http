AXM Pharma puts its weight into selling pharmaceutical products in Asia. The company makes and sells prescription and OTC products through its AXM Pharma Shenyang subsidiary. It has more than 40 licenses to products but so far has only commercialized about a dozen of them, including LiveComf for dermatitis; Bodyward, an alternative to penicillin for infections; and Asarone, an anti-inflammatory for respiratory ailments. AXM Pharma also makes vitamins and supplements. AXM Pharma sells its products through direct marketing and independent distributors primarily in China, as well as other Asian countries such as Hong Kong, Singapore, and Taiwan. Chairman Wang Wei Shi owns nearly 30% of the company.
