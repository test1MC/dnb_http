Novartis UK is the UK arm of Swiss-based pharma giant Novartis AG . From nine locations across the UK, it develops, manufactures, and markets medicines, devices and diagnostic tests in the fields of pharmaceuticals, eye care and generics. Novartis Pharmaceuticals UK manufactures such products as tumor inhibitor Glivec and pain reliever Voltarol. The eye care business, Alcon, makes devices for surgical procedures and contact lenses and lens care products. Its Sandoz portfolio has over 150 generic and biosimilar products, including medicines covering cardiovascular, gastrointestinal and respiratory disease.

The company operates through three divisions: pharmaceuticals, eye care and generics.

Pharmaceuticals partners with the NHS, patient organizations, and academia to treat and improve the health of patients with cancer, and neurological, immunological, dermatological, cardio-vascular, respiratory, retinal and other conditions. Novartis' Sandoz division owns 150 generic and biosimilar products, including major and niche medicines covering cardiovascular, gastrointestinal and respiratory disease as well as anti-infectives, anti-virals, central nervous system conditions and pain management.

The eye care division, Alcon, is a world leader in eye care, and makes specialized equipment and intraocular lenses used in treating conditions such as cataracts. It also makes disposable and monthly contact lenses via its Vision Care business.

The Generics business develops technology to deliver transformative blood testing solutions.

The company is based in Frimley, UK, and has offices and operations across the north and south of England.

Novartis UK is the UK operation of parent company Novartis AG, and contributed 3% of Group revenue in 2015.
