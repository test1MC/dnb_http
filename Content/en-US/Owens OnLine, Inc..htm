Owens OnLine provides Web-based credit reports and background checks to more than 4,300 customers worldwide. The company also offers a free transliteration database that provides English spelling of Arabic names. Clients include exporters, insurance companies, banks, and government agencies. Owens OnLine was founded in 1986 by president Mark Owens.
