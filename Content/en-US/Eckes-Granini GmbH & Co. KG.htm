When Europeans want a shot of OJ, they can turn to Germany's Eckes-Granini, commonly referred to as Eckes International. The company pioneered the use of orange juice concentrate in German; however, all is not OJ -- the company makes a cornucopia of fruit juices, including apple, pomegranate, raspberry, and strawberry. Its most well known brands include hohes C, FruchTiger, Joker, YO, and Elmenhorster. The company's juices are distributed throughout Europe, Asia, and Africa. Newer offerings include functional beverages with added vitamins and minerals. Eckes-Granini often formulates juices to suit local consumer tastes.
