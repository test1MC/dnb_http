Whether you're farming, roofing, or mining, Manitou wants to help you handle your materials. The company manufactures material handling machinery and vehicles, such as concrete mixers, forklifts, all-terrain trucks, access platforms, wheel loaders, and telescopic handlers. Manitou's products are marketed primarily to the construction, farming, lumber, and waste treatment industries. The company operates through subsidiaries in Africa, Asia, Europe, the Middle East, and the Americas. Manitou began in 1957, when founder Marcel Braud transformed a farm tractor into the first rough-terrain forklift. In late 2008 the company acquired rival Gehl , a US-based construction equipment manufacturer.
