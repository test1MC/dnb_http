Lancaster Brewing Company is a regional microbrewer that creates a handful of beers and ales using traditional brewing methods. Its brews include Amish Four Grain pale ale, Hop Hog IPA, Milk Stout, and Strawberry Wheat Beer, as well as a number of seasonal varieties. Lancaster Brewing's craft beers are distributed throughout Maryland, New Jersey, Virginia, and Pennsylvania. The company also serves its beer at Walnut Street Grille, a restaurant located at the brewery. Brew master Christian Heim and partner John Frantz started Lancaster Brewing in 1995.
