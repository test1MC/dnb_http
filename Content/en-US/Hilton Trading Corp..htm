When "the count" really counts, you can turn to the AccuBANKER money-counting equipment of Hilton Trading. The company makes banknote and bill counters of various capabilities, depending on the volume of cash to be handled. Hilton Trading also supplies coin counters/sorters. Another, related line of business is counterfeit money detectors, ranging from portable units to countertop machines. Hilton Trading also combines a bill counter and  counterfeit detector in its top-of-the-line product, which can be connected to a PC. Clients include banks, casinos, and other businesses that handle large amounts of cash. Hilton Trading was established in 1981 by Margarita Gonzalez, the company's president, CEO, and CFO.
