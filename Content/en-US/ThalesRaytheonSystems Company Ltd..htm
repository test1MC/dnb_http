ThalesRaytheonSystems isn't exactly hands across the water, but it is a major transatlantic joint venture between defense electronics heavyweights Raytheon and Thales . The 50/50 venture concentrates on radar and communications systems. Radar products include short-, medium-, and long-range battlefield and ground-based weapon-locating radars; command and control products include fixed and mobile strategic and tactical communications centers, sensors, and components for air and ground defense operations. The joint venture has major facilities in the US and France, with operations based on French, US, and NATO business lines.
