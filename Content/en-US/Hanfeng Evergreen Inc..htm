They say you reap what you sow, and that's just fine with Hanfeng Evergreen. Through its operations in China, Hanfeng makes nitrogen and phosphate-based fertilizers for soybean, rice, corn, and wheat crops, as well as for lawns, trees, flowers, and other urban greenery. The company specializes in slow-release fertilizers, which are coated or chemically altered for slow and controlled release into soil. It produces about 800,000 tons of fertilizer annually, targeting its products primarily to the growing agricultural market in China. Hanfeng formed a joint venture with Beidahuang Agriculture Company in 2010 to produce fertilizers in the Beidahuang region. In 2013 Hanfeng agreed to be taken private.
