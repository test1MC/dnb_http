Pizza and a salad makes meal at K.T.'s Kitchens. The company manufactures salad dressings,sauces, and frozen pizzas. Its salad dressings and sauces, sold under the Bob's Big Boy brand name, are available at retail food companies in Las Vegas, Southern and Northern California, Arizona, and New Mexico. The dressings come in five different flavors; the sauces include seafood and tartar. K.T.'s private-label and contract-packed pizzas are supplied to retail food and foodservice firms across the US. K.T.'s was founded in 1987 by president and owner Kathy Taggares, who started the business after acquiring the Big Boy salad-dressing operations from Marriott International . Pizzas were added during the 1990s.
