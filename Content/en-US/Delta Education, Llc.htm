Delta Education is changing the way schools and teachers think about science. The company creates educational programs, aids, and other instructional materials that incorporate inquiry-based methods and hands-on opportunities into the math, science, and reading curriculum for prekindergarten through 12th grade. Its curriculum kits cover such topics as life science and scientific reasoning, and can be customized for individual school districts. Delta's flagship program Full Option Science System is used by teachers across the US, including New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, and Dallas. The company is owned by leading school supply distributor School Specialty .
