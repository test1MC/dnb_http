Cementos Lima has cemented its position as a top cement maker in central Peru. The company produces more than 3 million tons of cement per year at its one plant and is expanding production capacity to 5 million tons. It sells bagged cement to hardware stores and bulk cement to concrete product manufacturers. It also owns a 60% stake in a hydroelectric power plant, Compañia Eléctrica El Platanal S.A. (CELEPSA), and a 30% stake in BASF Construction Chemicals Peru. Its US-based holdings are controlled by Skanon Investments, which owns a majority stake in Drake Cement LLC, a cement plant in Arizona that produces about 660,000 tons of cement a year, and Ready Mix, Inc., which has seven cement plants in Arizon and Nevada.
