Hasee Computer makes personal computers on the eastern side of the digital world. The company designs and manufactures a line of desktop computers, laptops, and panel PCs (PCs that consist of a combined display monitor and computer case). Hasee also makes personal computer TVs, motherboards, computer peripherals, and graphics cards. It sells its products in retail stores in China and Korea, as well as in select international markets (international sales account for about 20% of the company's total sales). The company was founded in 1995 as a manufacturer of graphics cards.
