Spinning and dyeing is not as ominous as it sounds at Carolina Mills, which primarily produces synthetic spun yarns and also offers specialty fabrics for industrial and home furnishings applications. The company outsources it production to domestic and Asian producers. Its yarn products include cationic, dyeable, flame retardant, thermal, single and plied, and white and silver yarns. Major Carolina Mills brands include CoolMax, Delcron Hydrotec, EcoSpun, and 4t7 Silver; it also has products made from soybeans and bamboo. The company was founded in 1928 in North Carolina.
