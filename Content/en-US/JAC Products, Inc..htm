JAC Products and its Franklin Aluminum subsidiary design and manufacture products using rolled-form steel, hydro-formed aluminum, and injection molded thermoplastics. Products include roof racks, as well as step rails, carrier systems, and storage bins. It also makes frames, connectors, and support structures for solar panels. Utilizing an in-house design team, prototyping capability, and full testing facilities, JAC works with automobile manufacturers to design exterior trim and cargo systems for a number of automobile models made by Audi , Ford , Subaru , Toyota , and others. Founded in 1967, the company operates facilities in the US, Europe, and South America. JAC was sold to Wynnchurch Capital in late 2010.
