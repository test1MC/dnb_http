If Electronics Line 3000 has anything to do with it, you won't need to leave the porch lights on. The company provides electronic security products. It offers intelligent sensors, wireless transmitting devices, control panels, and closed-circuit television (CCTV) monitoring systems to residential and commercial customers worldwide through subsidiaries in the UK, the US, and France. Its wireless security applications and Internet-based access systems are used to control and monitor home appliances from remote locations.
