Preco Electronics makes the PreView line of vehicle safety and collision warning systems to the construction, waste, mining, and utility industries. PreView works by sending out radar pulses that bounce back to provide the location and distance of other objects in the area. Preco Electronics was established in 1947 as a rebuilder of automotive batteries, generators, starters, and water pumps.

The company's products include the WorkSight PreView sensor, an object detection safety system for heavy equipment; High Resolution PreView and Xtreme PreView, pulsed radar object detection sensors designed to detect moving and stationary objects in blind spots; and Standard PreView, a blind spot detection system that notifies a driver when an object obscured from view.

Preco is based in Boise, Idaho and that's where it makes the PreView products. It sells in Australia, Europe, South America, and the UK as well as the US

Preco sells to a range of industries that include construction, emergency services, mining, transportation, and waste. It sells through dealers in markets outside the US.
