Think lipstick grows in tubes? Cosmopak would beg to differ. The company is a contract manufacturer of cosmetics and their packaging. The company's design lab concentrates on developing new makeup application and packaging ideas, such as the massage bottle, semi-permanent pens, the brush pen, 3-in-1 pen, twist pencils, and mascara pencil. It specializes in staying ahead of the curve and innovating new ways to package cosmetics. Current and past clients include Stila Cosmetics , Avon , Blistex , Almay , IMX Cosmetics, and Ultima II . Established in 1996 in New York, Cosmopak now boasts operations in the US, Europe, and Asia.
