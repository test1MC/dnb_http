It's not "rendering unto Caesar the things that are Caesar's," and it's not the CIA's rendition program. It's the messy, "but somebody's gotta do it" industrial process of rendering uses heat and other processes to transform dead cattle and other animal remains into useful products. North State Rendering Company, based in the city of Oroville in Northern California, manufactures tallow, grease, and other animal by-products to make soap, candles, and pet and livestock feed.
