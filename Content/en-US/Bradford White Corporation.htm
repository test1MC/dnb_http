Bradford White Corporation helps folks get in -- and stay in -- hot water. The company makes and markets water heaters for residential, commercial, and industrial heating applications. It makes oil-fired products, gas power burners, and indirect-fired units. Through a pair of subsidiaries, Bradford White-Canada and LAARS Heating Systems, it also makes products, such as pool heaters, oil burners, and air handlers. Bradford White wholesales its products through a network of plumbing and heating professionals. Subsidiary Niles Steel Tank produces custom steel tanks for companies in the automotive, petrol-chemical, pharmaceutical, and refrigeration industries. Bradford White Corporation was founded in 1881.

Based in Ambler, Pennsylvania, Bradford White boasts manufacturing facilities in Michigan and New Hampshire. Its Canadian-based distribution center supports its business from Mississauga, Ontario.

Bradford White, which offers its products exclusively through authorized representatives and wholesale distributors, is a multichannel marketer, selling its items through wholesalers, contractors, specifiers, homeowners, and home builders. It sells to qualified installers and serves the residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

Bradford White, as part of its business, operates several subsidiaries, including Bradford White-Canada, Bradford White Water Heaters, LAARS Heating Systems, and Niles Steel Tank.

In 2013 Bradford White opened a new 18,500-sq.-ft. International Technical Excellence Center (iTEC) training facility that's located near its Middleville, Michigan, production plant. The facility boasts a 4,000-sq.-ft. mechanical room, complete with an energy-efficient, variable-frequency drive HVAC system with boilers, pumps, geothermal equipment, heat exchangers, and domestic hot water tanks.

Bradford White is unique in that it is one of the only water heater makers with all of its manufacturing facilities located in the US, at an 800,000 sq. ft. facility in Middleville, Michigan.
