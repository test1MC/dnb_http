Don't let the name fool you, Newly Weds Foods hasn't made a specialty cake since 1952 but rather works to discover new customized food coatings. The company specializes in making seasonings, marinades, glazes, sauces, batters, breadings, binders, and fillers for the food processing and foodservice industries in some 70 countries worldwide. Newly Weds uses its proprietary FlavorTrak database to monitor restaurant trends and then develop flavors it hopes will appeal to the most consumers. Newly Weds boasts that it has expertise in providing ingredients for more than 45 cuisines. As part of its business, the Chicago-based company owns and operates about 25 production plants and nearly 20 laboratories.

Newly Weds Foods operates its business worldwide, with 15 production plants in North America and another 10 located across China, Thailand, Australia, the UK, Holland, and the Philippines.

The company in early 2013 expanded its operations with its 26th manufacturing facility located in Erlanger, Boone County, Kentucky. Purchased for $7 million, the plant spans more than 325,000 sq. ft., employs 100-plus workers, and includes an in-house research and development lab. As part of the expansion, Newly Weds Foods in 2014 began operating all four production lines there. The entire expansion project was valued at about $57 million.

From its headquarters in Chicago, Newly Weds Foods serves food makers and foodservice companies globally in about 70 countries.

As part of its business, Newly Weds Foods makes products under private labels for retailers and foodservice companies.

The company's expertise lies in researching, developing, producing, and selling food ingredients to food processors and foodservice operators globally. Newly Weds Foods is #1 in food coatings and a leader in formulated seasonings. Aside from its focus on coatings, Newly Weds Foods makes and markets capsicums, liquid sauces, and functional ingredients.

It also regularly monitors markets, customers, and trends internationally to provide its clients with insight and strategic and efficient product sourcing.

In 2014 Newly Weds purchased Continental Seasoning, an industrial seasoning manufacturer ing New Jersey, for an undisclosed sum. It makes seasoning blends, spices, and ingredients for meat, poultry, meat snack, and seafood processors.

To build its core coatings business, Newly Weds Foods in 2012 purchased industrial food-coating company Continental Mills, based in Tukwila, Washington. The deal gave Newly Weds Foods a 55,000-sq.-ft. manufacturing facility located in Pendleton, Oregon, and a leg up in starch- and flour-based coatings and batters for both potato and poultry application and protein and vegetable type substrates.

Owner and President Charles T. Angell inherited the company, which his father founded in the 1930s. The elder Angell created a rolled ice-cream cake that "married" cake and ice cream, hence the name Newly Weds Foods.
