Anything less than total security may be the equivalent to no security at all. Total Security Protection installs and maintains security systems, including access control devices, closed-circuit television (CCTV) equipment, sensors, and alarm systems for commercial and government clients throughout the UK and internationally. Brands include Honeywell , Tyco , and Sony . Besides high-tech offerings, the company also provides such physical security products as turnstiles, barriers, and blockers. Markets served include construction, data centers, education, finance, industry, pharmaceuticals, and retail. Total Security Protection was founded in 1988 by managing director Richard Parnell.
