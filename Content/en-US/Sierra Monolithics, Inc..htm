Sierra Monolithics makes microwave modules for the military/aerospace industry and integrated circuits (ICs) for the telecommunications industry. The fabless semiconductor company uses silicon germanium in its designs for optical components, radio-frequency ICs, and other chips. Sierra Monolithics supports various forms of wireless communications, including the burgeoning WiMAX standard for wireless networking. Alvarion , a supplier of wireless network access equipment, is among its customers. Chairman Charles Harper, chief scientist Binneg Lao, and CTO David Rowe founded the company in 1986.
