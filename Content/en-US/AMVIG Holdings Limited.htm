AMVIG Holdings (formerly Vision Grande) prints cigarette packages and manufactures laminated paper and laser film used to make cigarette packages. The company sells to most of the top ten cigarette producers in China -- including Anhui Tobacco, Guangdong Tobacco, Hunan Tobacco, and China Tobacco Jiangsu Industrial. AMVIG has manufacturing plants located across China. While most of its plants offer either printing or manufacturing services, its Dongguan One-Stop Plant houses three business units that print, produce transfer paper, and manufacture holographic film. Former chairman Li Wei Bo founded AMVIG in 1998. Packaging giant Amcor has an ownership interest in AMVIG.
