Personal stories and prayers are the foundation of Guideposts . The company publishes Guideposts , an inspirational magazine that addresses topics of faith and hope, as well as the online companion Guideposts.com. In addition, its Angels on Earth bimonthly magazine includes stories that feature miracles. The company also publishes inspirational books, greeting cards, and music. Guideposts has a paid circulation of 1.5 million, while the Guideposts brand reaches some 5 million people. The firm was founded in 1945 by the late preacher Norman Vincent Peale (author of bestselling book The Power of Positive Thinking ) and his wife Ruth.
