San Francisco's trolley cars, cargo railways in Australia, and Bangkok's metro all depend on voestalpine Schienen rails to keep things rolling smoothly along. As part of voestalpine AG's Bahnsysteme division, the company manufactures rails for railroad customers around the world. Its products include flat-bottomed rails for heavy cargo railways and grooved rails for light rail public transportation. The company also makes specialized rails for its switch-making sister company VAE . voestalpine Schienen makes its products at a single steel mill in Donawitz, Austria.
