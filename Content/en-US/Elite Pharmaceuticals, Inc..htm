Elite Pharmaceuticals isn't above peddling generics. Subsidiary Elite Laboratories develops generic versions of existing controlled-release drugs whose patents are about to expire. Its commercial products include allergy therapeutics Lodrane 24 and Lodrane 24D, which are marketed by ECR Pharmaceuticals. Products in various stages of testing and preclinical development include oxycodone pain medications, anti-infectives, and treatments for gastrointestinal disorders. Elite Laboratories also provides contract research and development services for other drugmakers.
