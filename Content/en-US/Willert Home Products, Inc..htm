Willert Home Products doesn't want moths to its flame. The company is the largest manufacturer of moth prevention products in the US, with such brand names as Enoz and Reefer-Galler. It also produces other Enoz bug-kill products, as well as Bowl Fresh toilet bowl deodorizers. Its products are sold nationally in most major grocery, discount, and drug stores, including Kroger , Randall's , Target , and Wal-Mart . It also exports internationally to more than 40 countries. Willert Home Products was formed by August Willert and George Irwin in 1946. The Willert family still owns and runs the company.
