Toshiba Thailand believes in both the power of alliteration and of technology. Founded in 1969, the consumer electronics company is a subsidiary of behemoth Toshiba . Toshiba Thailand has built a business importing and distributing a wide array of information technology products, communication products, and audio visual items, as well as general household appliances. Toshiba Thailand's products include mobile phones, laptops, mini notebooks, TVs, and DVD players, as well as refrigerators, washing machines, vacuum cleaners, air purifiers, and electric fans.
