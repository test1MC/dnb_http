GHSP, formerly Grand Haven Stamped Products, designs, manufactures, and supplies mechanical as well as mechatronic control systems used in automotive, heavy truck, and other industries. For some 90 years the company has focused on shift systems for passenger vehicles. Its move to mechatronic systems, a marriage of mechanical elements, electronics, and software, expands the GHSP lineup to smart actuators and electronic controls. Products include actuator pumps for hybrid motor generator cooling, and electronic shift and adjustable pedal controls. The company's customers include Caterpillar , Chrysler , Cooper Standard , Ford Motor , GM , SPX , and Sub-Zero . GHSP is a unit of JSJ Corporation .

The company operates a dozen sales, engineering, and manufacturing facilities in Asia, Europe, and North America. Based in Grand Haven, Michigan, GHSP also has locations in Hart, Michigan, as well as in China, Germany, Japan, and Mexico.

GHSP is skilled in the art of integrating mechanical, electronic, and decorative elements in products that combine sensing, controlling, computing, communicating, and actuating dimensions. Its primary products are automotive shift systems, smart actuators, and electronic controls. The company designs and manufactures electronic controls that improve vehicle performance and appliance controls that enhance the user interface. GHSP Smart Actuators employ BLDC motor technologies and manufacturing processes in tandem with state-of-the-art software control strategies. It also creates mechatronic products that improve the performance of surface transportation vehicles.

The company is a global supplier of mechanical and electro-mechanical systems to the automotive, transportation, and appliance industries. Some of its customers include Fiat , Honda , Micropump, Nissan , and Toyota .

Expanding its operations to meet demand, in 2012 the company opened facilities in China (Shanghai) and in Mexico (Saltillo).

GHSP was founded in 1924 as Grand Haven Stamping Products.
