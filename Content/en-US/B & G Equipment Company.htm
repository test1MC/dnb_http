Weeds and pests beware! These sprayers are aiming at you. B&G Equipment manufactures professional pest control equipment (insect light traps, tanks, dispensers), industrial sprayers, lawn and garden products (sprayers, hoses), water fountains, and pet accessories (grooming necessities, coiled walking and yard leashes, washing systems). The company operates a manufacturing center in Jackson, Georgia. Its customers include pest control businesses and consumers. B&G Equipment was founded by Bill Brehm and George Gilmore in the 1940s.
