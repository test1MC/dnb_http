When you're on a quest to fill your tot's time, The Manley Group (dba Toy Quest) can help. Among the world's largest private makers of electronic games and toys, the company makes outdoor toys, as well as robotic pets, such as Tekno the Robotic Puppy. Other products include Planet Care eco-conscious toys, Stretch Screamers, role-play costume sets, and its Banzai line (all the makings of a backyard water park). Toy Quest makes toys under license with Disney , Nickelodeon , the NFL , Sesame Street , and Jeep . Toy Quest's products are sold by toy retailers, discount stores, and online. Toy Quest was founded by Chinese businessman Sampson Chan in 1975.
