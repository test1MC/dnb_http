Accton Technology helps tech companies act on their business strategies. The company designs and manufactures networking and communication products for computer and telecom equipment vendors. Its products include Ethernet switches and adapters, fiber-to-the-home equipment, network interface cards (NICs), and wireless access points and routers. Its SMC Networks subsidiary provides branded wireless and Ethernet networking products. The company's Accton Wireless Broadband unit makes WiMax PC cards, USB dongles, and customer premise equipment. Accton Technology was founded in 1988.
