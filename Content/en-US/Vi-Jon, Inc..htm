Vi-Jon has been keeping it clean for more than a century. The health and beauty care company makes such products as body washes, deodorants, lotions, mouthwashes, shampoos, and soaps. While it is primarily a private-label manufacturer for national retailer brands, Vi-Jon also develops and markets its own brands, including Germ-X hand sanitizers and Inspector Hector oral care and hand soap products for children. The company's products are supplied nationwide to retailers, such as Kroger , Target , and Walgreens . Vi-Jon was founded as Peroxide Specialty Company in 1908 by John B. Brunner, grandfather of chairman John G. Brunner. Vi-Jon has been owned by investment firm Berkshire Partners since 2006.

Vi-Jon is headquartered in St. Louis, Missouri, and operates five manufacturing and distribution facilities in Missouri and Tennessee.

Vi-Jon is one of the nation's oldest private-brand health and beauty care companies.

The personal care products maker serves North American grocery stores, drug stores, mass merchants, warehouse clubs, and dollar stores. Its customer list includes the likes of Costco , Dollar General , Family Dollar , Kroger , Safeway , Sam's Club , Target , Walgreen , and Wal-Mart .

Responding to the economic downturn in the US, Vi-Jon repositioned itself to meet demand for less expensive private-label products as financially strapped consumers traded down from pricier name-brand items. The company believes that many consumers will stay loyal to private-label offerings even as the economy continues to recover.
