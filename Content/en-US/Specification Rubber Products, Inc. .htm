Specification Rubber Products makes made-to-order products including compression, injection, and transfer molding parts up to and greater than 76" in diameter or length. The company produces difficult, large parts that other molders avoid. The polymers used include natural rubber, SBR (styrene-butadiene rubber), nitrile, EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer), neoprene, silcone, and fluoroelastomer. Customers are primarily in the water works industry. A subsidiary of American Cast Iron Pipe Company , Specification Rubber Products manufactures all of its products at its plant in Alabama. The company was founded in 1967 and is a member of the Southern Rubber Group.
