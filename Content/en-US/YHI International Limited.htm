Let the good times roll. For YHI International the rolling is just as important as the good times. YHI International, through its 19 subsidiaries, manufactures alloy wheels and distributes tires, wheels, automotive batteries, and other industrial products throughout the Asian and Australian markets. In addition to distributing such brands as Yokohama , Nankang Tyres, Enkei, and Advanti Racing, the company designs and manufactures its own alloy wheels sold under the Advanti brand. That is just how they roll. In 2005 the company acquired Konig (American), providing a toehold in the US wheel distribution market. Managing Director Richard Tay Tian Hoe owns about 60% of the company.
