Steelscape helps builders and architects change the landscape. The company produces metallic-coated and pre-painted steel for the construction industry. Its offerings are available in an array of thicknesses, widths, grades, and coating weights. Australia's BlueScope Steel acquired Steelscape and sister companies Varco Pruden Buildings and ASC Profiles, for $730 million from Ternium in 2008. Steelscape operates as part of BlueScope Steel's Coated & Building Products North America segment, which makes steel buildings and component systems and generates about 13% of the steel giant's revenues.
