A-Max Technology puts maximum effort behind the design and manufacture of portable digital audio players. Its flash-based products include MP3 players, CD players, and MiniDisc players. A-Max Technology also offers services in new product design, embedded software development, logistics arrangements, and after-sales support. It has partnered with Ibiquity Digital to develop HD radio receiver components and products. Key customers have included UK's largest electronics chain DSG , German retailer Medion , and NEC Retail. A-Max Technology got its start in 1996. It operates research and development centers in China and Scotland, a factory in China, and sales offices located in China, Germany, the US, and Macau.
