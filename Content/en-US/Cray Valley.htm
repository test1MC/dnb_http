Cray Valley (aka TOTAL Cray Valley) is a part of TOTAL 's Refining and Chemicals Business segment. That segment accounted for 65% of TOTAL's net sales in fiscal 2015. TOTAL Cray Valley is a major global supplier of specialty chemical additives, hydrocarbon specialty chemicals, and liquid and powder tackifying resins used as ingredients in adhesives, rubbers, polymers, coatings and other materials. It has pioneered the development of these advanced technologies, and has introduced hundreds of products aimed at improving the performance of products in the energy, printing, packaging, construction, tire manufacture, and electronics industries.

In 2016 the company started construction on its new C4 chemical manufacturing plant in Carling, France, which will be used for the production of liquid polybutadiene.

In 2010 French chemicals company Arkema acquired Cray Valley's resins segment (and other parts of TOTAL 's specialty chemicals businesses) in a deal valued at $730 million. The move allowed Cray Valley to focus on its refining and chemicals businesses.
