Moët Hennessy USA is a master blender. It imports and markets wines, spirits, and champagnes, supplying thirsty partakers throughout the US. The company distributes such as Moët & Chandon, Dom Pérignon, Krug, Veuve Clicquot , and Ruinart champagnes; Hennessy cognac; Belvedere and Chopin vodkas; and Green Point, Terrazas de los Andes, Cape Mentelle, Cloudy Bay, and Newton Vineyard sparkling and still wines. It also handles Grand Marnier and NAVAN liqueurs. The company is the US sales and marketing arm of luxury giant LVMH 's Moët Hennessy's Wines and Spirits Brands. It was formed in 2005 from the combination of Clicquot , Millennium Import, and Schieffelin & Co.

The Wines & Spirits division contributed about 14% of LVMH's 2013 sales; the US accounted for 23% of the division's sales during the reporting period. Overall, the Wines & Spirits division sold 57.4 million bottles of champagne (up from 56.8 million in 2012) and 69.1 million bottles of cognac in 2013, up from 2012's 67.1 million.

Moët Hennessy USA is the leading importer and marketer of luxury wines and spirits in the US. As a rule of thumb, the company regularly rolls out new products and product extensions to pique the interest of customers. In 2014, for instance, Moët Hennessy USA added to its vodka line by launching a Belvedere Mango Passion version. Its Cloudy Bay upscale New Zealand winery also rolled out a new ultra-premium Pinot Noir in 2013. Moët Hennessy USA also partnered with artist Shepard Fairey for a fourth edition release of the company's Limited Edition Hennessy V.S. label.

The company works to market and promote its various wine and liquor labels as upscale lifestyle brands. Moët Hennessy USA routinely sponsors sports and music events and the company often uses celebrity spokespeople to foster the image that "Hennessy" is synonymous with high class. Moët Hennessy USA positions its brands as luxurious options that are worth the price, in line with its value-creation strategy.
