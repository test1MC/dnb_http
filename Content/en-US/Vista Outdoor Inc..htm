Vista Outdoor serves the outdoor sports and recreation markets through a diverse portfolio of more than 30 brands. Its products include sporting ammunition and firearms, outdoor accessories, outdoor sports optics, golf rangefinders, and performance eyewear. Some of its more popular brands include Federal Premium (ammunition) and Bushnell (eyewear). Its customers include outdoor enthusiasts, hunters and recreational shooters, professional athletes, as well as law enforcement and military professionals. Its products are sold through specialty and independent retailers such as Wal-Mart , Cabela's , Gander Mountain , Bass Pro Shops , Dick's Sporting Goods , Sportsman's Warehouse , and Recreational Equipment .

Vista was spun-off from Orbital ATK (formerly Alliant Techsystems) in 2014.

Vista operates through 15 manufacturing facilities in Puerto Rico, Mexico, Canada, and the US.

The company's operations are divided into two segments: Shooting Sports (76% of net revenue) and Outdoor Products (24%).

Vista's revenues surged from $1.2 billion in 2013 to almost $1.9 billion in 2014, while its profits more than doubled from $64 million to $133 million over that same time period.

Vista has been growing its brand portfolio through the use of acquisitions. In 2013 its former parent purchased Bushnell Group Holdings , a global designer, marketer, and distributor of branded sports optics, outdoor accessories and eyewear, for $985 million in cash. Orbital ATK in 2013 also acquired Caliber Company, the parent company of Savage Sports Corporation , for $315 million in cash.
