Libra Industries provides contract manufacturing of printed circuit boards and other electronics, including box build (complete PCs and other electronic systems) assembly. The company also offers engineering design, prototyping, supply chain management, custom packaging and shipping, and product compliance testing services. Libra Industries, which has two facilities located in Ohio, also partners with offshore manufacturers to provide customers the cost benefits of manufacturing in regions with lower labor costs while reducing the risk of choosing a manufacturer. Libra Industries was established in 1980. The company is owned by its officers.
