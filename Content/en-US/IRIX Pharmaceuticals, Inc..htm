IRIX's ascension in the pharmaceuticals business is dependent on the success of others. IRIX Pharmaceuticals researches, tests, and manufactures drugs and drug ingredients for pharmaceutical companies. Services include discovery support, process development, clinical supply, manufacturing, and commercial production. It also helps clients work through the regulatory red tape. The company maintains several research and manufacturing facilities in South Carolina and serves about 150 pharma and biotech customers in North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia. IRIX was formed in 1996 by company executives and a group of South Carolina investors. It was acquired by Patheon in 2015.
