On a quest to find a good business-to-business publication? Questex Media Group is a trade magazine publisher serving such industry sectors as beauty and wellness, hospitality, travel, and technology. The company has a portfolio of more than 100 print and digital titles, including Hotel & Motel Management, GPS World , Travel Agent , and American Salon . It also organizes about 45 trade shows, conferences, and other events such as Sensors Expo & Conference. In addition, Questex offers consulting and business intelligence services, as well as direct mail list services. CEO Kerry Gumas and Audax Private Equity formed Questex in 2005 after purchasing media assets from publisher Advanstar Communications .
