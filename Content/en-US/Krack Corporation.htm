Krack's equipment can make it so cold that a lawyer will put his hands in his own pockets. An operating brand of Ingersoll Rand , Krack manufactures a line of commercial and industrial refrigeration, and process cooling products for climate control. Commercial evaporators, condensers, and condensing units protect a range of environments, from food processing plants to blood distribution centers. Condensers, as well as evaporators and condensing units, comprise the industrial line. These giants chill warehouses to blast freezers. Krack products are distributed through its parent company's US branch sales offices and dealers. The 2008 recession forced Krack's manufacturing to transfer from Illinois to Mexico.
