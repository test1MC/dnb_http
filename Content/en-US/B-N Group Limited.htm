Formed by John Britten and Desmond Norman in 1955, B-N Group is an airplane maker. Its models include the twin-engine and 10-seat Islander, the 18-seat Trislander, and the Defender 4000 (essentially a stretched Islander). B-N Group also buys used aircraft and restores them to meet customer specifications, and it provides training for pilots, technicians, and air crews. The company has sold more than 1,250 aircraft to civil and military customers in about 120 countries. Customers have included such noteworthy names as Honeywell , Rockwell Collins , L-3 Communications , and Universal Avionics . B-N Group operates from eight sites in the UK, as well as locations in the US and Australia.
