PLM Group is a commercial printer that provides prepress services, wide-format and web printing, inkjet finishing, and digital asset management. It specializes in providing direct marketing products and services. The company operates under the PLM, Mailer Magic, and Optium names. Clients come from the financial services, automotive, telecommunication, and pharmaceutical industries. The company was founded in 1987 as a sheet-fed printer under the name PLM Graphics.
