Diapulse Corporation of America makes and sells its Diapulse Wound Treatment System, a machine that generates non-thermal electromagnetic energy for the treatment of post-operative pain and edema for soft-tissue wounds such as bed sores, diabetic ulcers, and venous ulcers. Buyers of the Diapulse system include hospitals, assisted-living and long-term care facilities, clinics, physicians, and individual patients who use the equipment at home. Diapulse Corp. both rents and sells its technology, and the equipment is covered by Medicare and many insurance plans. Chairman and CEO David Ross owns 40% of the company.
