People of all ages --Â tots to seniors living in old folks homes -- can eat the food products made by Old Home Foods. Serving the four Midwestern states, the company offers cultured dairy products, such as cottage cheese, sour cream, dips, and yogurts under the Old Home brand name. It also makes organic fluid milk. Old Home supplies its products in bulk to other food-manufacturing companies. It also co-packs dairy products for other companies including Sara Lee , and offers private-label and custom manufacturing. Francis A. Davies founded the company in 1925. Former CEO Rick Hanson and his family own the majority of the Old Home Foods.
