Cains Foods has a can-do attitude when it comes to manufacturing and distributing dressings, condiments, sauces, and oyster crackers. The company provides products to retail food stores and foodservice operators, such as hotels, restaurants, and educational and health care facilities. Its brands include Cains and Olde Cape Cod. Cains also provides customers with private-label and co-packing services. It distributes its products throughout New England and upstate New York. The company was founded in 1914 by John Cain and was purchased by president Denis Keaveny in 1995.
