J. Filippi Vintage, which operates as Joseph Filippi Winery & Vineyards, produces merlot, syrah, zinfandel, chardonnay, and dessert wines.  The winery is known for its premium wines, but it also makes sacramental and altar wines and offers custom-labeling services. It has tasting and sales locations, one in Rancho Cucamonga, California, and another in the Guasti district, near the Ontario Airport. The family-owned vineyard and winery was founded in 1922.
