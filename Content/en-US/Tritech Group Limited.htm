Tritech Group specializes in casting -- but not the kind that replenishes Hollywood movies. The company is a fabricator of both ferrous and non-ferrous metal alloy castings catering to the UK and Europe. It is capable of producing castings utilizing CNC machining, casting simulation, rapid prototype, and robot ceramic mold production equipment. It supplies aluminum, stainless, carbon, and heat-resistant steel castings, as well as cobalt and nickel-based alloy vacuum castings to the aerospace and defense industry. Tritech Group's businesses include Tritech Precision Products and Tritech Engineering Solutions.

The company operates three UK facilities; two in Wrexham, and one in Barnstaple.

Tritech Group sells to the aerospace and defense industries. its customers include Aero Engine Controls, Airbus , AgustaWestland , BAE Systems , Boeing , Bombardier , EADS, Embraer , and GE Aviation .

The company grows through acquisitions and process improvement, and invests in upgrading its manufacturing capabilities and its other in-house resources.

Tritech Group was founded in 1982.
