Libby Laboratories (Libby Labs) wants to make shampoo for you and your dog. The company is a contract manufacturer of cosmetics and over-the-counter pharmaceuticals. Libby Labs also develops skin care, hair care, and bath products, as well as pet products (shampoos, conditioners, powders). The firm uses both natural ingredients and chemical preparations in its products, with everything from botanical blends and herbal extracts to antioxidants and peptides. Libby Labs has specialized in natural and botanical blends since the 1960s. It was founded by Dr. Henry Libby, father of current president Susan Libby, in 1959.
