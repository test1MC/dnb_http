Midmark Corporation operates in the health care equipment industry, providing medical and dental solutions for patients and doctors and corollary equipment for use in veterinary practices and animal care. Family-owned Midmark makes power and manual exam tables, anesthesia products, sterilizers, lighting, seating, cabinets, and dental operatory equipment for exam rooms, clinics, and physician practices. The company markets its Midmark and Ritter brands to general practice and specialty physicians and sells its dental products through distributors. Its IQ Digital Diagnostics System combines devices and software for patient monitoring.

Midmark produces sterilizers and an array of lighting, seating, and unique modular casework with storage solutions for healthcare facilities. Midmark Diagnostics Group, a subsidiary of Midmark, is a medical technology innovator that offers digital diagnostic medical devices through its IQmark product line.

Headquartered in Dayton, Ohio, and with production and corporate locations in Versailles, Ohio, Midmark has four subsidiaries in the US and also has operations in France, India, Italy, and the UK.

The company provides technical service and training programs both online and via a network of clinical specialists to service providers and regional managers who support Midmark products in the field.

Expanding its portfolio of products, in 2014 Midmark introduced its new cabinetry line (Artizan Expressions) and its Cardell Touch Veterinary Anesthesia Monitor. The following year, it introduced the new VetPro CR Digital Dental Radiography Reader, a compact imaging solution that rapidly provides high-quality readings.

The company is also looking to expand internationally through acquisitions and partnerships.

The company acquired the rest of hospital bed and furniture maker Janak Healthcare of India that it didn't already own in late 2014.

In 2016 Midmark acquired Versus Technology , which makes real-time location systems for the health care industry. That purchase enhances its Midmark Clinical Solutions tool, which helps health systems improve clinical workflows.

Midmark traces its roots to the Cummings Machine Company, which was founded to make construction machinery in 1915. It entered the medical equipment market in 1967.
