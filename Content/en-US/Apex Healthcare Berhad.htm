Apex Healthcare is at the top of Asian pharmaceuticals. The Company makes, markets, and distributes generic pharmaceuticals and other health care products to customers in Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia, and China. Subsidiary Xepa-Soul Pattinson (Xepa SP), the company's manufacturing arm, is Malaysia's largest generic drug maker with products including antibiotics, corticosteroids, and neuromuscular agents in tablet, capsule, liquid, cream, and powder formulations. Xepa-SP also makes nutritionals (vitamins) and surgical supplies. The company distributes its own products and those of other manufacturers to wholesale and retail customers through subsidiaries ABio, Apex Pharmacy Marketing, and Apex Pharma.
