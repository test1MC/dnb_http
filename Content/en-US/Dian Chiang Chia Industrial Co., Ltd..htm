Dian Chiang Chia Industrial Co. (dba Golden Wave) wants customers turn to them when they've had a long day. It manufactures a line of personal care items including personal saunas, bath massagers, and heating pads. Its core sauna line uses far infrared heat and is branded under the Metachamber, Solo System, and GoldenWave names. It also makes such varied products (some through joint ventures) as karaoke machines, amplifiers, golf putters, and wood furniture. The company was founded in 1976 as Golden Electronics Technology Co., a maker of telephone accessories and remote control devices.
