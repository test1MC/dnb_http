Zardoya Otis makes elevators, escalators, and travolators (moving walkways) in three factories in Spain and operates sales and assistance outlets throughout Spain and Portugal. The company also offers maintenance, installation, and monitoring programs for its products. Zardoya Otis exports products to more than 100 countries worldwide. Founded in 1972, the company is a publicly traded subsidiary of the largest manufacturer of elevators in the world, Otis Elevator Company , which in turn is a subsidiary of conglomerate United Technologies Corporation .

Spain generates 95% of Zardoya's net sales; the remainder stems from Portugal and Morroco. The company serves in 129 countries and also in the Far Eastern Countries like India, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam, Philippines, Cambodia, Malaysia, and Singapore.

Zardoya maintains and services about 250,000 elevators in Spain out of a total of 1.9 million worldwide for Otis Elevator, a unit of United Technologies Corp.

Zardoya's revenues decreased 3% from 2013 to 2014. This was fueled by reduced total service billings due to lower volumes of repairs and modernization. The company was also adversely affected by decrease activity within the construction industry, particularly residential construction, in 2014.
