When EWOS Canada rings the dinner bell, the fish they come a-swimmin'. Established in 1986, EWOS Canada manufactures feed for various fish species (bass, sturgeon, yellowtail, sea bream, flounder, rockfish, cobia and shrimp) at its mill in Surrey, British Columbia, but focuses particularly on controlling the hunger pangs of salmon and trout. Its sister company, Mainstream Canada, operates fish farms, as does EWOS Canada as part of its Pacific Coast operations. The company was the Canadian subsidiary of Oslo, Norway-based Cermaq ASA 's giant fish-feed business, the EWOS Group, before Cermaq sold the EWOS Group to Cargill in 2015.
