When it comes to pneumatics, Numatics is no neophyte. Since 1945 the fluid power and control products maker has pioneered parts for plumbing a variety of repetitive motion machinery. A billion cycle, lapped spool and sleeve valve led its series of first to market parts in 1952. Today's line caters to all core pneumatic applications -- valves, air preparation, motion control, and accessories. Numatics supplies far reaching industries, from automotive to commercial laundries, packaging, medical instruments, and newer uses in biofuels and nuclear power. Products are driven across the Americas, Europe, and Asia/Pacific through a network of distributors. Numatics is a business unit of Emerson Industrial Automation.
