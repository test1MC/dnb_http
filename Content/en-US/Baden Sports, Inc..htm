While Baden has made a name for itself by juggling ball manufacturing, the company also caters to customers by adding their names to Baden balls. Known for the basketballs it makes and sells to schools and other institutional customers, Baden Sports has long used infamous college basketball coach Bob Knight as an endorser. In addition the family-run company manufactures baseballs, footballs, playground balls, soccer balls, softballs, volleyballs, and water polo balls. Baden also provides customized promotional imprinting for its entire line of products. The company is the official supplier for sports teams such as the LA Lakers and the Harlem Globetrotters . Baden got its start in 1979.
