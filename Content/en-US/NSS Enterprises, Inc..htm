Clean me up, Scotty! NSS Enterprises produces an array of heavy-duty equipment for cleaning the floors of commercial, industrial, and institutional operations worldwide. The company's lineup runs from carpet vacuums to automatic scrubbers, sweepers, burnishers, pressure washers, tools and accessories, and buffing floor machines. Its catalog exceeds 40 different models and versions. The company offers sales and repair training on its products to maintenance equipment professionals through its NSS Institute. Backed by 600-plus distributors, NSS products find their way to more than 60 countries. Customers include Hilton , American Airlines , and K-Mart . NSS is owned and led by its founder's grandson, Mark Bevington.
