The name China Great Wall Industry Corporation (CGWIC) does little to betray the organizations higher purpose. Established in 1980, CGWIC runs the commercial and international space technology cooperation side of China's Aerospace Science and Technology Corporation (CASC). The company oversees commercial launch services, satellite operations, and a number of other consulting, contracting, and transportation services related to the aerospace industry. The company also pursues the commercial development of space technology.
