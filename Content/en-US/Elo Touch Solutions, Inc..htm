Elo Touch Solutions takes a hands-on approach to computing. Formerly a business unit of TE Connectivity , it was sold to Gores Group for $380 million in 2012. The company makes touchscreen products, including LCD monitors, touchscreen overlays, touch sensors, and touchscreen computers. Used in retail and informational kiosks, point-of-sale systems, process control equipment, and office automation systems, the company's products are sold to the education, gaming, government, financial, hospitality, industrial, medical, retail, and transportation industries. Elo Touch Solutions products are sold under the Elo TouchSystems brand directly and through distributors to customers worldwide.
