Howard Beef Processors, which does business as Ellison Meat Company, provides all the standard cuts of beef and pork, as well as ground beef in bulk or patties and pork sausage and link. In addition the company can produce custom cuts and offers marinating, rubs, and flavor-injection options to fulfill its customers' needs. It also provides portion-control, retail prepricing, private- label, and fresh or frozen meat. Located in the southwest corner of Minnesota, the company was founded by Edgar (Duke) Ellison in 1934 (during the Great Depression) as a neighborhood meat market. It now serves retail food and foodservice customers.
