There are plenty of people who think pulling a beer out of a hat is magic indeed. Founded in 1994, Magic Hat Brewing Company produces seasonal ales, three year-round beers, and several offerings it calls Odd Notions (including a lemon ginger beer). From its base in Vermont the company has expanded along the East Coast and its beers are sold in more than 20 states. The fast-growing craft brewer in 2008 acquired Pyramid Breweries , the owner of Oregon-based MacTarnahan's beer, and formed Independent Brewers United Inc. Two years later Rochester, New York-based North American Breweries (NAB) acquired the entire operation. NAB is owned by the New York-based private equity firm KPS Capital Partners .
