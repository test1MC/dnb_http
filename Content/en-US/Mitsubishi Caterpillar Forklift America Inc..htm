Mitsubishi Caterpillar Forklift America (MCFA) sticks a fork in it to make sure the job's done. Creating one source for three major material handlers, MCFA manufactures/distributes forklift trucks under the Mitsubishi and Jungheinrich brands and lift trucks under the Caterpillar brand throughout the Americas. Its affiliate, Rapidparts, Inc., distributes service parts of material handling equipment. Parts are sold under the Promatch (Caterpillar) and Mastersource (Mitsubishi) brand names. MCFA provides assembly, fabrication, and supply chain services. The company is a subsidiary of Mitsubishi Heavy Industries .

MCFA makes and distributes forklifts in the US, Canada, Mexico, and Latin America.

The company is engaged in forklift sales (direct sales, lift truck leasing, and forklift rentals); parts sales (the selling of original equipment and parts for other forklift brands); and support and services (warranty, fleet services, and forklift dealer services). It can produce 25,200 units annually. The capacity of the forklifts it produces ranges from 3,000 pounds to 15,500 pounds.

MCFA sells its products to more than 250 national accounts through a network of more than 440 dealer locations (178 dealers) throughout North America. In 2015, the company named TNE Equipment as an authorized Cat lift truck, Mitsubishi forklift truck and Jungheinrich dealer for the North Hampton, New Hampshire area.

In 2014 MCFA expanded its Jungheinrich brand with addition of two new products (ETV 110/112 Series of moving mast reach trucks and a new 7,000 lb. model capacity large electric pneumatic tire lift truck). It also launched a new line of 3,500 - 4,500 lb. capacity pantograph reach trucks.

In 2013 the company expanded its Houston-headquarters manufacturing facilities by more than 40% to enhance the company's production capacity.

Strengthening its distribution network, in 2013, the company acquired Jackson, Mississippi based Daily Equipment Company (DEC) from Emmerson Daily. DEC has five other full-service branches in Mississippi and Louisiana: Biloxi, New Orleans, Baton Rouge, Shreveport, and Lake Charles.

MCFA, which was founded in 1992, is a unit of Mitsubishi Heavy Industries .
