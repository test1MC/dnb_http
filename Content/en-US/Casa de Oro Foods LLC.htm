Casa de Oro invites you into its house of golden grain. The company is a producer of shelf-stable, refrigerated, and frozen corn and flour tortillas, tortilla chips, taco shells. It also offers taco, fajita, and Mexican dinner kits. (Casa de Oro produces more than 4 million tortillas every day.) It also provides private-label services and its customers include food-manufacturing, foodservice, and retail food companies. The company's products are sold throughout the US, Canada, Mexico, and in parts of Europe. Formerly part of the specialty grains unit of ConAgra , Casa de Oro is majority owned by company CEO John Stout through his investment firm, Plaza Belmont Management Group.
