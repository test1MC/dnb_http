Too much of the Flying Dog might lead to howling at the moon, so consume responsibly. Flying Dog Brewery makes a variety of ales and porters -- a litter of 10 or so, actually -- that are distributed throughout the US and in Canada. Its labels include In Heat Wheat, Raging Bitch, K-9 Cruiser, Road Dog Ale, and Tire Bite Golden Ale. Some of the company's brews have featured labels designed by Ralph Steadman, the artist and running buddy of the late writer Hunter S. Thompson. Flying Dog got its start in 1990 in Aspen, Colorado, not far from Thompson's adopted hometown of Woody Creek. It has since moved its headquarters to Frederick, Maryland.
