MMC Automotores do Brasil, a subsidiary of Mitsubishi Motors Corp ., sells over 40,000 Brazilian vehicles a year. MMC Automotores assembles and distributes Mitsubishi brand vehicles throughout its domestic market. MMC assembles the Pajero TR4, the Sport SUV, plus the Triton pick-up truck at its plant in the centrally located state of Goias. The plant began manufacturing its first 4-wheel drive flex-fuel vehicle in 2007, capitalizing on the demand for cars that utilize alternative fuels; the Pajero TR4 Flex can run on any mixture of gasoline and ethanol. News reports indicate that certain of Mitsubishi's manufacturing may be transferred to Brazil to take advantage of the tariff-free export agreements.
