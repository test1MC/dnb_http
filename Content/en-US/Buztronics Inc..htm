Buztronics makes it easy to create some buzz around your company. Through its four operating divisions, it manufactures and distributes promotional, novelty and lighted items -- from flashing buttons to bicycle spoke lights. Its Buzline unit wholesales and distributes premium promotional products while its Electrostar business develops and sells novelty, lighted, and toy items to retail outlets. Its Electronic Promotions unit provides custom-designs items for national ad campaigns. In addition, its Asia Direct unit allows clients to order directly from Chinese manufacturers. Edward "Buz" Lewis founded Buztronics in 1989.

Buztronics operates in four divisions: Buzline, Electrostar, Electronic Promotions, and Asia Direct. It operates through on-site sales, marketing, product development, warehousing, distribution, quality control, post production assembly, and printing facilities.

The company also provides pens, keychains, toys and gadgets, balls, pendants and necklaces, party products, buttons and reflectors, batteries, projector pens and keychains, votive led candles, presentation laser flashlights, play button USB game controllers, and light up dog tags, among other items.
