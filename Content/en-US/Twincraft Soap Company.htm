Twincraft was born from twins. Twincraft Soap, founded by twin brothers Bob and Dave Asch, makes bar soap for the private-label market. The company provides product development, manufacturing, and packaging services and produces more than 110 million soap bars annually. Twincraft's roster of clients includes specialty retailers such as Bath & Body Works , The Body Shop , and Victoria's Secret , as well as personal care industry behemoths including Johnson & Johnson and Unilever . Founded in 1971, Twincraft was sold to PC Group , a medical and skin care products firm, for nearly $27 million in 2007.

The company has facilities in Winooski and Essex, both in Vermont.

Twincraft is a contract manufacturer of specialty soaps and skin care. It also offers custom manufactured premium liquid soap formulations and skin care products, including soaps, foams, shower gels, and facial and body care products.

Some of the clients that the company works with are Aveda, Bliss, P&G, Avon, Origins, Mark Kay, Bath & Body Works, and Herbalife.

As part of the company's growth strategy it is expanding its manufacturing with a second plant in Essex.

Twincraft, which had been family-owned for more than 30 years, was the foundation for PC Group 's foray into a full-on skin care business. PC Group has served the medical and therapeutic skin care niche and has used the acquisition to extend its reach into additional skin care areas.
