Aerosonic helps pilots straighten up and fly right. The company makes digital and mechanical aircraft instruments and air data sensing and test equipment, such as altimeters, airspeed indicators, and vertical speed indicators. Air data sensor products include attack stall warning systems, angle of attack sensors, and integrated multifunction probes. Aerosonic products end up in destinations such as Sikorsky 's legendary Black Hawk utility helicopter and Bell 's Kiowa helicopter. Customers include Boeing , the company's largest individual commercial customer (15% of revenue), Hindustan Aeronautics , and Korean Aerospace Industries. Direct and indirect sales to US government agencies bring in nearly 45% of revenue.
