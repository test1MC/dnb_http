Linzer Products is the professional painter's pal and the supplier to homeowner Picassos. In business since 1892, the firm's primary products are paint brushes and roller covers. Its other painting tools and accessories include artist brushes, mini rollers, pad painters, and plastic drop cloths. Linzer's products are sold at paint and hardware stores as well as home centers under the brands American Brush, ArroWorthy, J.R. Edwards, Le Jour, Pintar, and World of Color. It also makes private label brands for retailers. Those products are manufactured and distributed from Linzer's sites in California and New York and distributed from facilities in Canada, China, and Mexico as well.
