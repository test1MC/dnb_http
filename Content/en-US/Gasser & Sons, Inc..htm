Gasser & Sons is much more about metal than air. The company provides metal fabrication and related services including engineering, press forming, deep drawing and stamping, tooling, and finishing. Gasser & Sons fabricates components using nickel, stainless steel, brass, copper, nickel iron, lead, aluminum, and several other metals. The company primarily serves industries such as electrical distribution, medical equipment and x-ray housings, lasers, electronics, and aerospace and defense. Gasser & Sons also boasts computerized manufacturing and warehousing systems that offer its customers a seamless parts supply chain. The company is controlled by the third generation of the family who founded it in 1916.
