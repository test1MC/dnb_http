Some of the world's great treaties have been signed with Montblanc pens. The company's classic fountain pen and other high-end writing implements have long been a tradition among those who appreciate such rites of luxury. The company also markets a growing portion of desk accessories, leather bags, watches, jewelry, eyewear, and fragrances. Montblanc products are sold through selected retailers, jewelers, and through about 360 Montblanc Boutiques worldwide. It has been concentrating on maintaining a strong presence in China, where is operates more than 90 boutiques. Founded in 1906, Montblanc is a subsidiary of Swiss holding company Compagnie Financière Richemont .
