Blue Ridge Networks specializes in virtual private network (VPN) security appliances, software, and services. Its BorderGuard network security appliances authenticate network users, help prevent network intrusions, and manage deployment of network resources. Blue Ridge also provides managed VPN services. The company originally specialized in secure networking for intelligence agencies and other government customers, and has branched out into supporting commercial clients in such sectors as financial services, manufacturing, and transportation. The company was spun out of Storage Technology in 1997.
