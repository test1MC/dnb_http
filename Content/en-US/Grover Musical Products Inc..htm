From cymbals to dulcimer pegs, Grover Musical Products helps the band play on. The company makes musical instrument parts such as guitar and bass machines and heads, banjo, ukulele, and violin pegs, and bridges. It also offers other types of instruments and accessories through its many divisions, including kazoos, recorders, and snare drums. The Cleveland Musical Instruments division consists of the Clevelander Drum Company, Pfrogg, and Orion cymbals, which imports its products from Brazil. Products are sold in music specialty stores across the US and in Europe.
