No need to bail on International Baler. This holding company, formerly known as Waste Technology, banks on its business being in the dumps. International Baler manufactures about 50 different types of waste baling equipment. It also sells replacement parts for waste haulers. In addition, International Baler produces accessories such as conveyor belts and "rufflers," which break down refuse for better compaction. Customers include rubber and polymer makers, solid-waste recycling facilities, power generating facilities, textile and paper mills, cotton gins, and supermarkets. International Baler makes about three-quarters of its sales in the US.
