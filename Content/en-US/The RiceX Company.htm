RiceX manufactures stabilized rice bran for use by food manufacturers in products such as cooking oil, cereals, baked goods, and health bars. The company also makes products for use in horse food. RiceX has developed a process for keeping rice bran from spoiling without using chemicals, microwaves, or other methods that can damage the bran's nutritional value. RiceX operates facilities in California, Idaho, and Montana and targets markets worldwide, including Indonesia, Asia, and Africa. RiceX was acquired by and made a subsidiary of NutraCea in 2005.
