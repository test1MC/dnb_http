Oberweis Dairy bottles, freezes, scoops, and delivers. The Midwestern regional dairy processor makes fluid dairy products, premium ice cream, and ice cream cakes under the Oberweis Dairy brand name. Headquartered in North Aurora, Illinois, its products are sold through grocery stores and companies in the foodservice and institutional food sectors. The company also operates company-owned ice cream and dairy stores in Illinois and Missouri. Offering an old-time convenience, Oberweis provides home delivery of its glass-bottled milk, along with other dairy and juice products. The company's milk is supplied by local farmers who agree not to use growth hormones in raising their dairy cattle.
