This company helps France stay connected. SFR Group (formerly Numericable-SFR) was formed when Numericable Group and SFR combined to create France's leader in the combined broadband and mobile markets. The company offers its customers a comprehensive range of Internet-access, fixed-line, mobile, and audiovisual services. SFR operates across all of France's telecommunications market segments, from consumer to B2B, local authorities, and wholesale. Its consumer segment includes the Numericable, SFR, RED by SFR, and Virgin Mobile brands. Its B2B segment operates under the SFR Business brand and serves more than 190,000 companies.

SFR currently serves a total of about 21.9 million mobile customers and 6.4 million households subscribing to broadband services in France.

The company reported revenue of more than $2.6 billion in fiscal 2014. That was an increase compared to the $1.8 billion it brought in during fiscal 2013.

The company plans to rapidly extend its fiber-optic and 4G coverage to every corner of France as it strives to provide optimal service quality.
