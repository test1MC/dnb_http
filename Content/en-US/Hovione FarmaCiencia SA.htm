Named from the first letters in its Hungarian founders' last names -- Horty, Villax, and Onody -- Hovione FarmaCiencia produces active pharmaceutical ingredients (known as APIs), which are used in making oral, topical, inhalation, and injectable drugs. In generics, the company makes antibiotics, corticosteroids, and imaging agents. For custom drugs, the company makes anti-AIDS drugs, cancer therapies, and Alzheimer's treatments, among others. Hovione makes bulk quantities of APIs and distributes them to pharmaceutical companies around the world. The company, founded in 1959, maintains manufacturing plants in China, Ireland, and Portugal, and a technology transfer center in New Jersey.
