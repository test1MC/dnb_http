Organitech USA is proof that technology and mother nature can coexist. The company engineers and manufactures self-contained hydroponic systems that combine custom software and robotic hardware inside large environmentally controlled metal containers. Organitech's products are designed to enable the cultivation of plants and such vegetables as lettuce. The company is also developing systems for use by biological and pharmaceutical researchers. Although headquartered in the US, most of the company's operations are carried out by Israel-based subsidiary, Organitech Ltd.
