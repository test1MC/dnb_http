Premier Pacific Vineyards puts its money where its wine is. The company is made up of a group of private investors who create vineyards. The group provides the capital, secures sites, and develops vineyards in California, Oregon, and Washington. Its customers include vintners who rent vineyards from Premier because they are need extra acreage to fill the demand for their products. Premier Pacific also offers financial services, including vineyard sale-buyback financing and other capital-based solutions. The company was founded in 1998by vineyard developer, William Hill, and real estate veteran, Richard Wollack.
