The drapes Amba Medical sells aren't used to pretty up operating rooms, they're used to keep the room clean and the patient healthy. Amba manufactures and distributes surgical supplies, including disposable equipment and procedure drapes under the Clinicon brand. Procedure drapes are used to cover different parts of a patient's body during surgery, providing sanitary protection, while equipment drapes cover surgical equipment, such as cameras, probes, and microscopes. Other supplies include drainage pouches, patient identification bracelets, and operating room and hospital footwear. The company is a division of P 3 Medical.

Amba Medical makes surgical products, anesthesia and airway products, general clinical products, and veterinary products. The company has been in peddling its wares for more than two decades. Along with selling its own products, it acts as a distributor for P 3 , selling items such as blade removers, ampoule openers, medication identifications, and lavage trays, among many other products.

Headquartered in the UK, Amba Medical serves the Americas, Africa, Europe, the Middle East, and the Asia/Pacific region.

Amba Medical sells its products in some 50 countries through a wide network of distributors.
