Cork Supply USA can help you put a cork in it. The company manufactures the cork stoppers used in wine bottles; its customers include some of the top wine companies around the world. Cork Supply USA makes natural corks under its own brand; it also distributes synthetic corks make by North Carolina-based Nomacorc, NewPak-branded screw caps, and Sparflex-branded foil enclosures. Cork Supply's 100,000-sq.-ft. plant in Northern California, in the heart of the US wine industry, can produce 1.5 million corks per day. (The cork wood comes from Portugal.) The company also has a 43,000-sq.-ft. warehouse and a laboratory for research and development. Cork Supply USA was founded in 1981 by President Jochen Michalski.
