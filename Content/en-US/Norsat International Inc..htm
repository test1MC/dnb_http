Norsat International serves those who dish it out. The company, which makes wireless communications equipment for the satellite and cable television industries, primarily sells such broadcasting equipment as satellite ground station receivers, transmitters, and transceivers. Norsat also supplies portable satellite terminal systems used for applications including on-location news broadcast transmission. The company sells directly and through a network of partners and distributors. Norsat does the bulk of its business in the US and Europe, but it ships products worldwide. Clients have included Boeing , CBS , Fox News , and the US military. It has international offices in Europe, Korea, and the US.
