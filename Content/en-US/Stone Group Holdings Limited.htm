The Stone Group has a veritable quarry of business interests. The boulder in the company's rock pile is its health care product business, which sells Naobaijin and GoldPartner branded vitamins, minerals, and over-the-counter drugs. The company's electronic products business makes and sells printers and industrial controllers. Stone Group has a stake in Internet café chain Sunnet Cafe, with some 80 shops in operation. The group holds minority interests in Me To You Holdings, an Internet Service Provider (ISP) and maker of global positioning systems (GPS), and cable operator China Cable Network.
