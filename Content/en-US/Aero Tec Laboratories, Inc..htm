Aero Tec Laboratories makes high-tech, durable, light-weight fuel bladders, tanks, and drums, which are made from flexible composite materials and are designed to stop leaks and keep fuel in the tanks of airplanes, helicopters, and cars. In addition to fuel cell tanks, bladder technology has spread to compensators, accumulators, portable water vessels, self-sealing ballistic fuel tanks, space containerization, bulk storage bladders, safety fuel tanks, and containment berms. Customers include Fountain Power Boats, Meggitt Defense , Northrop Grumman , Porsche , Sandia Labs , Toyota , and all of the US military forces.
