Founder Jerry Maahs may have gotten his name backwards, but he kept his company Alto-Shaam heading in the right direction. Originator of the low-temperature cook-and-hold oven, the company set a new standard within the industry and changed the way many products are cooked. The company manufactures and supplies first-choice equipment for commercial kitchens including ovens (cook and hold, combitherm, and convection), holding cabinets, quickchillers, heated buffet servers, rotisseries, smokers, and fryers. Its well-known heated display cases allow vendors to show hot foods on multiple shelves while keeping the dishes warm. Customers include restaurants, hotels, schools, and grocery stores.
