The golden goose of the growing demand for consumption of members of the Anatidae family is well looked after by Schiltz Foods, Inc., the largest Anatidae, or goose, producer in the Americas. The company offers geese fit for the table, including frozen whole geese, breasts, and livers, as well as specialty items including rendered and unrendered goose fat, goose feet, and goose chitterlings (intestines). Sister company Schiltz Goose Farms sells  goose eggshells for crafting and bulk feathers for bedding. Marlin Schiltz founded the family business with five Toulouse goose eggs in 1944.
