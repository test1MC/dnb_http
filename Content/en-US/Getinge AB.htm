Helping hospital patients stay clean and comfortable, keeping infections in check, and outfitting surgical theaters are all part of the big-picture at Getinge. The company serves the health care, pharmaceutical, and medical research industries worldwide. Its ArjoHuntleigh business manufactures beds and lifting systems for hospital and extended-care settings. Its also makes cleaning, disinfection, and decontamination equipment. Getinge's Maquet subsidiary makes equipment and workstations for operating rooms and critical-care facilities. The company operates in 40 countries and production is conducted at facilities in France, Canada, China, Poland, the UK, Sweden, Turkey, Germany and the US.
