L-3 EOTech has the law enforcement and military markets in its sights. The "E-O" in the company's name stands for electro-optics. It makes holographic weapons sights that members of SWAT teams, soldiers, and others use to focus on targets. The company has focus on homeland security and military applications of its technology. L-3 EOTech's customers include the federal Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives , as well as the federal Drug Enforcement Administration , the US Army , and the US Marine Corps . The company also markets its products to the consumer sportsman community, selling through retailers such as Cabela's and Sportsman's Warehouse . L-3 EOTech is part of L-3 Communications .

EOTech has its corporate roots in the Environmental Research Institute of Michigan (now the Altarum Institute ), a not-for-profit R&D organization that has developed technology in laser holography and other areas.

Other retailers through which EOTech's products are available include Some of the company's dealers include Shooters Vault, FX Tactical, Glockmeister, and 5 Boroughs LLC. The company is a regular exhibitor at the annual Shooting, Hunting, and Outdoor Trade Show and Conference (SHOT Show).
