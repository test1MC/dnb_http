Word got around about Alfred Rogers and his stone mill. The only flour and cereal mill in British Columbia, Rogers Foods was founded in 1951 by farmers Alfred and Pauline Rogers, who started the business when neighbors and local food stores began asking them to grind local grains into flour. Real growth for Rogers Foods began under the guidance of Alfred's son Stan.
