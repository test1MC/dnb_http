ABB Ltda. may be destined to walk in its parent's shadow, but there are certainly worse shadows to walk in. The company is the Brazilian division of Swiss-Swedish electrical engineering giant ABB Ltd . It targets customers in the utility and industry sectors and is a global leader in power and automation technologies. ABB Brazil operates through multiple plants and regional offices spread throughout the country. Its customers buy from them directly, as well as through distributors, wholesalers, system integrators, and other partners. The Brazilian unit got its start in 1912.
