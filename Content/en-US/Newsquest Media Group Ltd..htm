Newsquest Media Group is a leading regional newspaper publisher serving readers in the UK with about 20 daily papers more than 200 other publications. Its portfolio includes such mastheads as the Evening Times (Glasgow), The Northern Echo (Darlington), and the Southern Daily Echo (Southampton). The company's other publications include weekly newspapers, free papers, niche interest and lifestyle magazines, and traded publications. In addition to its traditional publishing, Newsquest operates a number of news Web sites and online classified advertising sites, and it has commercial printing operations. The company is a subsidiary of Gannett , the top newspaper publisher in the US.
