Thank Plochman for your ability to write with mustard. Aside from being one of the oldest manufacturers of good, old, American yellow as well as specialty mustards, the company introduced the nation's first squeeze-packaged condiment in the 1957. Currently, its products include flavored mustards such as Spicy Horseradish and Honey Dijon, and specialty mustards Kosciusko and Natural Stone Ground. The company's Web site has mustard facts, history, and recipes, a mustard-lover's club, and an online store. Family-owned and -operated, Plochman was founded in 1852 as Premium Mustard Mills. Fourth-generation Carl (Terry) Plochman, III, is the company's president and CEO.
