Fenner Advanced Sealing Technologies (FAST) made specialty seals used in hydraulic, pneumatic, and pipeline applications. The company's Hallite Seals International division marketed hydraulic piston seals and rod seals, as well as bearings and wipers, to fluid power customers through international distributors. Its CDI Polytek division made seals and packing for process applications. CDI -- which comprised the units of CDI Seals and EGC Plastics -- served customers in the agriculture, aerospace and defense, fluid handling, mining, pharmaceutical, oil and gas industries, and semiconductor industries. The company was acquired by Fenner in 2005 and eventually integrated into its operations.
