Aluminum shapes Aluminum Shapes' business, which manufactures aluminum shapes. The company's aluminum extrusion processes begins with making precision-formulated billets in its foundry. Its reverberatory furnaces melt and process aluminum ingots; these are later cast into finished logs. Aluminum Shapes creates billets for all of its extrusion needs, as well as billets for sales to external customers. The company's major customers include architectural, defense, and transportation industries. Aluminum Shapes is owned by investment firm H.I.G. Capital and also operates alongside sister company Signature Aluminum, a provider of aluminum alloys and fabrication services based in Canada.
