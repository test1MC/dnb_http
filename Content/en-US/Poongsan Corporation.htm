Whether you spend it or fire it, products from Poongsan Corporation show their metal. The company makes copper and copper alloy products, including sheets and strips, pipes and tubes, rods, wires, and bars. It is one of the top global makers of coin blanks used to make circulating and commemorative coins. Poongsan also supplies ammunition for tank, naval, recoilless, and antiaircraft guns to the South Korean armed forces, along with ammunition components, chemical propellants, and sporting ammunition for recreational hunting. Poongsan also makes a variety of precision dies, tools, semiconductor leadframes, and general machinery parts.
