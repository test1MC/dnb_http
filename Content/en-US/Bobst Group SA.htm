While companies all over the world are trying to think outside the box, Bobst Group is thinking about making the box. The company is a global supplier of packaging equipment and services specializing folding cartons, corrugated board, and flexible materials. Serving packaging manufacturers around the world, the company's products print, cut, stamp, glue, fold, and laminate all manner of boxes, bags, and packages used to contain and display the handiwork of consumer products manufacturers. The company's equipment is sold under various trade names, including Bobst, Champlain, Steuer, Astitrade, Rapidex, Rotomec, and Schiavi.
