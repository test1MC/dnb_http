Wimm-Bill-Dann Foods puts the milk mustache on Muscovites. The maker of fluid and cultured dairy products, fruit juices, nectars, and baby food is a market leader across Russia. Wimm-Bill-Dann brands include best-selling J7 juice beverages and Agusha baby food. Distribution of the firm's products extends to consumers throughout Russia and the other former Soviet republics. Hand-over-fist acquisitions of smaller dairies and plants have made it Russia's biggest dairy company. Wimm-Bill-Dann operates processing plants in Russia, the Ukraine, Georgia, and Asia. The company is a subsidiary of US beverage giant PepsiCo .
