When it comes to covering the creative industry in the UK, The Carnyx Group does more than toot its own horn and beat its own drum. The company (named for a bronze war horn used by the ancient Celts) publishes The Drum , a magazine that serves the creative communities, including the marketing, advertising, design, and public relations industries, in UK and elsewhere. The publication offers industry news, information, and job listings, while its Knowledge Bank features articles from experts in the field. The company additionally produces industry awards and events such as the Scottish Design Awards and The Law Awards of Scotland.

The Carnyx Group offers news, insights, and jobs through online, magazine, event, and social media channels.

The company is based in Glasgow, Scotland. It operates through four global offices, including one in London and one in New York City.

The Carnyx Group serves all sectors of the marketing industry including brands, media owners, agencies, and ad tech providers.
