Immecor designs and assembles customized industrial PCs and servers. Immecor uses third-party components to build customized computer systems for semiconductor makers, which use the equipment for automated wafer inspection. Besides PCs, the company also makes sheetmetal fabrications, custom cables, and PCB assembly. Immecor assembles industrial computers by Intel , motherboards, servers, and chassis by Super Micro Computer , and printers by Oki Data . Its products are used in the entertainment, medical, and telecommunications industries. Immecor was established in 1993 and has facilities in San Jose and Santa Rosa, California.
