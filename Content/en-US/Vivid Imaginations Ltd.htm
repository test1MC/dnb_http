Vivid Imaginations fuels the fantasies of British children. The company is a top UK developer and marketer of licensed toy properties, including Marvel 's Spider-Man, JAKKS Pacific 's iPets, and Wham-O 's Slip n' Slide. its licensed brands have included the Simpsons, Care Bears, Crayola , Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, and Disney's Frozen. The toy company also distributes Ganz 's popular Webkinz merchandise in the UK. It has extended its reach into licensing products intended for children-oriented movies and television programming. Retail partners include Amazon.com , Argos , WH Smith , Harrods , Tesco , and others. Vivid was formed in 1993 by former Matchbox (now part of Mattel ) employees.

Vivid is based in the UK with offices in Germany, France, and China. Its products are sold in more than 60 countries.
