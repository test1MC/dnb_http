Sterile and antiseptic aren't good attributes in a personality, but in medical wipes they're exactly what you're looking for. Alcavis International, also known as Alcavis HDC, makes chlorine-based, pharmaceutical-grade disinfectants. Its products include spray and liquid disinfectants, a machine that cleans and reprocesses kidney dialyzers, antiseptic hand sanitizers, and bleach disinfectant wipes. Alcavis' products are marketed to dialysis, dental, and veterinary care providers, as well as hospitals and nursing homes, through sales representatives and distributors in the Americas. Brands include AniosGel, Assist, Alcavis, ExSept, and Peracidin.
