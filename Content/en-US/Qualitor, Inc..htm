Qualitor keeps the big guys moving. The company makes automotive and heavy-duty truck parts, including brake components and wiper blades. Qualitor consists mainly of Anstro and International Brake Industries (brake manufacturers in the US), Sloan (truck and trailer components in the US), and Pylon (aftermarket wiper blades and replacement pieces in the US). Its switches, sensors, brakes, repair kits, water pumps, and wipers are sold to OEM parts manufacturers, rebuilders, retailers, mass merchants, and auto dealers worldwide; customers include Dana , Wal-Mart , and Standard Motor Parts . Qualitor is majority-owned by Thayer Capital Partners.
