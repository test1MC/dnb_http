Euthymics Bioscience wants to put patients in a pleasant state of mind. Named for a medical term defined as a moderate mood that's neither manic nor depressed, Euthymics Bioscience is a biopharmaceutical company focused on developing drugs that treat major depression and ADHD. The company's lead candidate, amitifadine, is a clinical-stage, orally administered treatment for depression that acts on the neurotransmitters serotonin, norepinephrine, and dopamine. Its other key candidate, also in clinical-stage development, is being developed to treat ADHD in adults. Founded in 2009, Euthymics Bioscience has received financial backing from Novartis Venture Fund, Venture Investors, GBS Partners, and other investors.
