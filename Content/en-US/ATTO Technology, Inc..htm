ATTO Technology keeps adapting to the data storage market. The company provides networking equipment for data storage networks, supporting a number of different technologies, such as Fibre Channel, Gigabit Ethernet, iSCSI, and SCSI. Its hardware products include bridges, controller appliances, and host adapters. The company also offers software for managing storage area networks (SANs). ATTO sells directly to manufacturers and systems integrators, and through distributors and resellers. Its customers include Apple , EMC , and Oracle . The company was established in 1988 by CEO Timothy Klein and CTO David Snell.
