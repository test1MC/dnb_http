Orient Semiconductor Electronics (OSE) orients itself around two main businesses. Its Semiconductor Group provides semiconductor packaging and testing services, through which it prepares clients' unpackaged microchips for shipment. The Finished Products Group offers contract manufacturing services, through which OSE builds electronic gear on behalf of other companies. OSE has manufacturing facilities in the Philippines, Taiwan, and the US, with a sales office in Japan.
