For Segerdahl, advertising ads up. The printing company specializes in digital pre-press, printing, finishing, and shipping services for advertising and marketing projects. It also provides digital photography (culinary, product, and other) for its clients. Segerdahl offers wide format (57-inch) web printing and in-line finishing. A member of the Segerdahl Group, the company produces brochures, catalogs, newspaper and magazine inserts, mailers, and other materials for a variety of national clients. Sister company Segerdahl Graphics offers photography, premedia, bindery, and mailing services.
