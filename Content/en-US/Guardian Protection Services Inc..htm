Guardian Protection Services wants to be your own personal guardian angel. The home and business security company offers burglary, fire, and personal emergency protection systems. Guardian offers design and installation services, as well as 24-hour monitoring and digital video surveillance. It also offers commercial access control systems such as intercoms, photo badges, card access, and biometric systems that include iris scans, fingerprints, and voice identification. The company protects more than 250,000 homes and businesses, including large corporations and small offices, in about 15 US states. Guardian was founded in 1950 by the Armstrong Company, a family-owned telecommunications services firm.

Guardian's fire protection services include smoke detectors and heat sensors, as well as 24-hour monitoring. The company's personal emergency service, mainly serving the elderly population and "latch-key kids," offers emergency pendants and key chains that are programmed to send a distress signal, prompting dispatch of EMS or 911 services.

The company's Guardian Home Technologies division designs, installs, and monitors advanced wiring in newly constructed homes. Offerings include internet, audio, home theaters, and intercoms. Guardian Home Technologies markets to approximately 500 builders in the US.

The company grows by adding to its partner network. Strategic partners include Alarm.com, Beam, Bose, Cooper lighting, Dirt Devil, Legrand, Kwikset, Leviton, Liftmaster, Klipsch, Honeywell , Premier partner security, Schlage, and Trane.
