E-Caps offers endurance athletes that extra energy boost they want without the unwanted legal side effects. The company makes legal performance enhancing supplements for endurance athletes. Its Hammer Gel and HEED are designed to provide athletes participating in short, fast-paced activities while its Sustained Energy and Perpetuem are made for more lengthy activities. On top of its performance enhancing products, E-Caps offers a line of dietary supplements geared toward athletes' overall wellness, including cardiovascular health and immune system support, as well as products that reduce fatigue and promote recovery. The company was founded in 1987 by CEO Brian Frank.
