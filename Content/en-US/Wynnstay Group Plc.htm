Wynnstay Group is here to stay for farmers in Wales and the UK. The company supplies feed, seed, and fertilizer for livestock and farms through its agriculture division. It also operates country stores under the Wynnstay banner that sell hardware and animal health products to farmers and rural residents, and the Just for Pets retail chain in the West Midlands, catering to pet owners. Wynnstay's roots reach back to 1918, when it was founded as a farmers' co-operative. Today most of its shareholders are local farmers.

The company operates two divisions, Agriculture, 75% of revenue, and Specialist Retail, 25% of revenue. The Agriculture Division covers the manufacturing and supply of a range of agricultural inputs to customers throughout Wales, the West Midlands, Lancashire and Yorkshire. The Agriculture Division has two divisions: the Feed Division, which operates two compound feed mills and one blending plant, offers animal nutrition products, and the Arable Division, which supplies arable and grassland farmers.

The Retail Division supplies specialist agricultural and retail products to customers throughout Wales, the Midlands and Lancashire. There are 42 Wynnstay Stores and 20. Just for Pets stores. Youngs Animal Feeds makes equine and small animal feeds.

Geographic Reach

The company sells in Wales, the Welsh border counties, the Midlands, Lancashire and Yorkshire as well as online.

Revenue was flat revenue in 2014 compared to 2103, although revenue in Specialist Retail increased 16% because of the acquisition of Carmarthen & Pumsaint Farmers.

Net income increased by 9% in 2014 from the year before with a decline in interest expense.

The company focuses on geographic expansion and organic growth. The company seeks minimize risks by operating in a broad agricultural portfolio.
