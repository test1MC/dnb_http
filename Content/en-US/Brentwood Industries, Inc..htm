Brentwood Industries can help you wheel it around, flush it, mold it, and cool it, among other things. The company manufactures a variety of plastic products, including wheelbarrows, cooling towers, wastewater treatment systems, and specialty plastic forming and molding services. Brentwood has expanded its product line with the additions of Polychem Systems (non-metallic chain and flight sludge collectors), NRG Products (non-metallic clarifier systems for municipal and industrial markets), and medical thermoform packaging. It is the world's largest manufacturer of plastic media, supplying more than 100 million cubic feet of material for cooling tower and wastewater applications.

Brentwood maintains and operates facilities for manufacturing raw materials, engineering, research and development, tooling, and product manufacturing in Arizona, Arkansas, Pennsylvania, and West Virginia, as well as in Thailand and the Czech Republic

Brentwood Industries is comprised of the Water Technology Group, the Specialty Products Group, and Polychem Systems.

The Specialty Products Group makes a range of plastic products from light gauge to heavy gauge, and serves customers in the transportation, agricultural, battery, medical, lawn and garden, construction, and electronics industries. It also designs, produces, and markets the AccuVent Attic Ventilation System for the home building industry.

Brentwood's Water Technology Group provides cost-effective, innovative and environmentally-sound solutions for the water, wastewater, and cooling tower industries.

The Polychem Systems Division and NRG Products Division provide non-corrosive, lightweight, durable, chain and flight sludge collection systems andsediment pumping systems for wastewater treatment and water clarification systems throughout the world.

Growing its portfolio, in 2012 Brentwood acquired Polytech Corporation. Polytech's expertise in helical skimmers and scum pipes will enhance Brentwood Industries overall product portfolio and provide additional market opportunities. The acquisition of Polytech's technology and knowledge of non-metallic and metallic helical skimmers and rotating scum pipe skimmers expanded Brentwood's product offerings for a surface removal systems.

Brentwood was founded in 1965.
