If you happen to be a military or commercial sea-faring craft, Babcock Marine has an open dock with your name on it. A division of Babcock International , the company provides a range of design, build, and support services that include refitting, upgrading, maintenance, and conversion. It provides engineering and support services to the Royal Navy (nuclear submarines, warships, naval bases, and aircraft carriers). Other customers include governments of Australia, Canada, and New Zealand. Devonport Royal Dockyard is now part of the Babcock Marine division, which was formed in 2007.

Babcock Marine provides engineering support either as an outsourced service or directly. It assembles surface ships, such as aircraft carriers and mine hunters, at Rosyth Dockyard, and offers offshore support vehicles, gas shipping, and floating production storage and offloading.

The company is based in Rosyth, Scotland, where it owns a waterside manufacturing plant, and Appledore in England.

The company provides platform design, integration and management services to the defense sector.
