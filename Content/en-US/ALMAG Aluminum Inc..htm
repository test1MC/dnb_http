Al and Mg are well-known symbols on the periodic table for aluminum and magnesium, respectively, but ALMAG has become a symbol for custom extruded aluminum parts. ALMAG Aluminum's products are used by producers of a variety of goods. The company provides services including fabrication and finishing, extrusion design assistance, extrusion punching, and surface anodizing. ALMAG manufactures such products as heatsinks for electronic devices and components for medical devices. Edward Peacock, father of company president Bob Peacock, founded the company in 1953 as a manufacturer of decorative door grills.
