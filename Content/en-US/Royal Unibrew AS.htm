Royal Unibrew has more than one brew to suit its customers' tastes. The company operates a dozen or so breweries located in in Denmark, Lithuania, Latvia, and other countries. Its flagship product line, Royal, offers a selection of lagers that are distributed primarily within its home country of Denmark. Its main export brand , FAXE, is exported to Germany, Africa, the UK, Canada, and several other countries. The brewer's other export products include Ceres and Thor. All in all, Royal Unibrew distributes to about 65 countries worldwide. In addition to its beer products, the company also makes enriched malt drinks and is the bottler and distributor of Heineken beer and selected Pepsi products in Denmark.
