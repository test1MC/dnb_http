The cast at Castings PLC is a group of three stand-alone manufacturing companies. William Lee operates an iron foundries and offers graphite and iron castings, machining, designing, and prototyping services to industrial manufacturers in the automotive, railway, and other industries. CNC Speedwell provides machining, surface treatment, assembly, and finishing. Its eponymous Castings PLC segment produces ductile iron castings, SG iron castings, austempered ductile iron (ADI) castings, Simo castings and Ni-resist castings. The group gets most of its sales from European customers, with the UK accounting for 33% of sales.

The UK represents its largest segment, representing 33% of net sales. Other major markets include Sweden (24%), the Netherlands (11%), North and America (3%), and other countries in Europe (28%).

The company operates through two chief segments: Foundry (86% of net sales) and Machining (14%).

Castings' revenues remained relatively flat from 2014 to 2015 due to decreased sales from its foundry business partially offset by increased sales from machining.
