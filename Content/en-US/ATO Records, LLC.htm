ATO (According To Our) Records is home to more than a two dozen artists, including David Gray, Mike Doughty, Gomez, Crowded House, Lisa Hannigan, Liz Phair, My Morning Jacket, Radiohead, Patty Griffin, The Firemen, and White Rabbits. The label distributes its records through Sony Music Entertainment . ATO Records seeks to find skilled songwriters, musicians, and performers "for whom there is genuine passion and belief." The company was co-founded by Dave Matthews (of the Dave Matthews Band). Other founders include manager Coran Capshaw and associates Michael McDonald and Chris Tetzeli.
