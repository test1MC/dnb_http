It's about more than just sound at Sonic Foundry. The company's Mediasite recorders and software enable educational institutions (more than half of sales), corporations, and government agencies to capture, stream, and archive online multimedia presentations. Its products are used for corporate meetings, media analysis, distance learning, and content publishing. Sonic Foundry also provides webcasting services, and it offers managed communications services -- including content hosting and delivery. It markets through resellers, its own sales team, and system integrator partnerships. The company's customers have included Thermo Fisher Scientific , BAE Systems , and Georgetown University .
