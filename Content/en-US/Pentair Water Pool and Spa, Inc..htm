Summertime and the living is, well, hot and sticky in many places, but Pentair Water Pool and Spa can break that heat. The Pentair subsidiary, also known as Pentair Aquatic Systems, primarily serves the US and manufactures vacuums, filters, controls, and other swimming pool accessories to keep that backyard oasis, as well as commercial pools, in inviting shape. The company's basic products include filters, pumps, and standard pool lighting. Additional features include automatic cleaning systems, automated controls, continuous color changing lights, fiber-optic lighting, heaters, and automatic chemical feeders. Pentair Pool Products, Sta-Rite, and Rainbow, are among the company's brands.

Pentair Water Pool and Spa is stationed in Sanford, North Carolina, and has operations primarily throughout North America. It has another location in Belgium and a presence in China, Europe, Latin America, and other countries.

The company's commercial line of products includes Acu-Trol, Paragon Aquatics, Aurora, and Berkeley brands. In addition to commercial and large residential pools, Pentair Water Pool and Spa also provides products for outdoor aquatic environments, which may include aquariums, zoos, and water parks.

The company provides equipment, accessories, and water technology solutions to two distinct markets: swimming pools and aquaculture. It offers swimming pool equipment for commercial, residential, and exterior water environments, such as aquariums, zoos, water parks, and resort swimming pools.

The company belongs to the Water Quality Systems segment of its parent company and accounted for 19% of its revenue in 2014.

Pentair Water Pool and Spa should benefit from a recovering US economy. Personal income, an indicator for spending on non-essential items, has slowly been on the rise since the Recession.
