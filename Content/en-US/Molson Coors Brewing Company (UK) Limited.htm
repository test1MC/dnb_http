Let's drink to Molson Coors Brewing Company (UK) (MCBC-UK). It is the UK's second largest brewer (behind Heineken ) and boasts more than 65 brands, led by the UK's best-selling Carling, along with Coors Light, and Cobra, which are also exported to Europe. Other cold ones distributed by the brewer include Worthington's, Blue Moon, Sharp's Doom Bar, and Grolsch (through a joint venture), as well as several brands under license. MCBC-UK operates three brewing facilities in Alton, Burton-on-Trent, and Tadcaster. It was formed in 2002 when the former Adolph Coors Company (now known as the Molson Coors Brewing Co. ) purchased the former business of Bass Brewers. MCBC-UK generates about one-third of its parent's sales.
