People who want to be fenced in can turn to Magal Security Systems. The company makes a line of security products used for perimeter security, motion detection, and security management. Its surveillance  products use methods such as taut wire perimeter intrusion detection, vibration detection, field disturbance, video analytics, and video motion detection. Clients include government and military agencies that use Magal's products to provide security at national borders, airports, prisons, and facilities. Israeli Ministry of Defense (MOD) accounts for nearly 15% of Magal's sales.
