Eminence Speaker comes in loud and clear with customers and audiences. The company makes loudspeakers and components for guitarists and the professional audio market. A large part of its business comes from custom speaker design and manufacturing for companies such as Yamaha , Marshall , and Crate. Eminence sells its products through a worldwide network of distributors and dealers, including Fender Musical Instruments . Artists such as bluesman W. C. Clark and vocalist Kim Wilson of the Fabulous Thunderbirds endorse the company's products. Eminence was founded by Bob Gault in 1966. His son, Rob, is the company's president.
