Manganese Bronze Holdings is the hands-down choice (or is it hand-up?) if you want a black London taxi. The company primarily operated through two companies: The London Taxi Company (Factory), which manufactures the London black taxi and The London Taxi Company (Factory) that provides financing, service, and repairs for the taxis. Its 48% owned Shanghai LTI, a joint venture with Geely Automobile Holdings and Shanghai Maple Automobile, manufactures London black taxis in Shanghai for sale in Asia. Manganese Bronze Holdings is also in talks to be Geely's European distributor. Toscafund Asset Management owns about 25% of the company.
