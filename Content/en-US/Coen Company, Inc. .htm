"Burn, Baby, Burn," could well be Coen Company's motto. Founded in 1912, Coen manufactures and services burner equipment and combustion systems (under brand names Coen and TODD), which are used in the cogeneration, industrial boiler, petrochemical, refinery, utility plant, and wood products industries. Other related products include control valves, igniter systems, and fluid handling systems. With its headquarters located in California, Coen owns and operates facilities located throughout the world that cover Africa, Asia/Pacific, Canada, Europe, Latin America, and the Middle East. In 2007 John Zink Company , a unit of Koch Industries , acquired Coen.
