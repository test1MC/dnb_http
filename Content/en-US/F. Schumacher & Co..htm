The "F" is for fabrics. F. Schumacher & Co. is a top designer and marketer of fabrics, home furnishings, wallpaper, and accessories. Its core brands include Schumacher Classic, Schumacher Modern, Schumacher Elements, and Schumacher Terra. The company also offers custom commercial and residential design services and is renowned for its historic reproductions. Schumacher's products are sold exclusively to design professionals through its roughly 15 showrooms in the US and its network of sales representatives in North America, Europe, Australia, Asia, and South Africa. The company is owned and run by descendants of founder Frederic Schumacher.
