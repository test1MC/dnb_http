CasC Systems' products go beyond traditional lock and key. The company, now named Intelligent Charging Limited, designs and manufactures electronic products under the trading names of Intelligent Charging, Intelligent Locking, and Intelligent Storage. It specializes in battery chargers and analyzers for the avionics industry, as well as electronic access controls and smart storage systems that can be used to secure everything from safes and display cases to weapons and police evidence tapes. The company is a military supplier of the UK Ministry of Defence (MoD), and it serves manufacturers and other commercial and industrial customers.The company changed its name in 2015 to better reflect its business.

Intelligent Charging is based in Norwich, UK, and sells worldwide.

Intelligent Charging sells to the aviation, military, transportation, and other sectors. Customers include the British Royal Navy, British Royal Air Force, British Army and Special Forces, Royal Netherlands Air Force, Bangladesh Air Force, and BAE Systems .

Companies that distribute Intelligent Charging's products include PT. Tunas Nusa Raya, Integrated Technology Consultants Co., and Habesis Ltd.
