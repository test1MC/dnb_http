If only our wardrobe pieces could speak up and tell us when and what to wear. Wear Me Apparel, with operations in the US and overseas, primarily designs and markets children's and infant's clothing under the name Kids Headquarters. Founded in 1972 by father-and-son team Arthur and Jason Rabin, Wear Me Apparel services major retailers nationwide and boasts an impressive portfolio of proprietary brands, character licenses, and national brands, such as Disney , Kenneth Cole Reaction , Timberland , and Skechers . It serves the branded women's apparel market through its licensing deal to manufacture clothing under the name of singer Beyoncé Knowles. In late 2009, Li & Fung acquired Wear Me Apparel.
