Prairie Thyme wants to show its customers' taste buds a good time. The company produces a variety of unusually flavored fruit salsas, vegetable chutneys, and flavored cooking oils. Its gourmet sauces include Roasted Tomato Chutney, Raspberry Jalapeño Ambrosia, Blackberry Jalapeño, Peach Habañero, and Chocolate Habañero Fudge Sauce. The company boasts some 1,200 retail customers, operates a shipping facility in Durango, Colorado, and works with two co-packing partners. Located in Santa Fe, New Mexico, Prairie Thyme is owned by president and founder Gary Hall.
