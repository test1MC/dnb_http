I-Bus helps customers climb aboard new technologies. The company designs and manufactures high-availability computing platforms used in medical, computer networking, telecom, and industrial settings. Its products include single-board computers, backplanes, power protection systems, and peripherals, and are used for functions such as medical imaging, industrial automation, computer telephony, and network infrastructure. The company was founded in 1982. Maxwell Technologies sold the business to its managers in 2002 for $7 million.
