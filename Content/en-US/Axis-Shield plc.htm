For Axis-Shield, success or failure turns on diagnostic testing. The company makes in vitro testing products for clinical laboratories and point-of-care (POC) diagnosis. Its POC division makes diagnostic analysis systems for doctors' offices under the brands NycoCard and Afinion; the analyzers run various on-the-spot tests, including ones that detect the cause of an infection and that monitor diabetes. Its laboratory product division makes proprietary tests for use on the automated analyzers made by such companies as Abbott Labs , Beckman Coulter , and Bio-Rad Laboratories . Axis-Shield was acquired by diagnostics giant Alere in 2011.
