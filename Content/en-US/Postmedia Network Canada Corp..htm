What's black and white and read all over Canada? Postmedia Network's newspapers, which span from Vancouver to Montreal. Postmedia Network publishes 10 English-language newspapers, including conservative Toronto paper the National Post , which has a weekly circulation of about 150,000 readers. Other papers include Ottawa Citizen , The Vancouver Sun , and The Windsor Star . In addition to newspapers, the company also publishes news website canada.com and owns media monitoring agency Infomart. Postmedia Network formed in July 2010 to acquire the newspaper assets from bankrupt media conglomerate CanWest Global Communications for $1.1 billion, including $927 million in cash.
