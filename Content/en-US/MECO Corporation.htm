Take a seat, then let MECO Corporation meet all of your metallic needs. The metal specialist designs, manufactures, fabricates, finishes, distributes, and warehouses metal products for businesses in the automotive, defense, transportation, home furnishings, retail hardgoods, and industrial manufacturing industries. It also makes residential and commercial folding furniture, including steel patio chairs, heavy-duty banquet tables, and covered swing sets under the SuddenComfort, Stakmore, and Sudden Solutions brands. Beyond furniture, MECO also makes barbecue grills and cooking accessories under the Aussie Grills, American Grills, Southern Country Smokers, and Easy Street Grill brands. Its products are sold from retailers nationwide.

The company operates three business lines: Meco Contract Manufacturing, OneFire Grills, and Meco Furniture. The company's top focus in recent years (as of early 2016) has been its Meco Contract Manufacturing business, which offers metal stamping, fabrication, powder coating, deep-draw metal forming, welding, CNC machining, assembly, packaging, and warehousing & distribution services.

Its Meco Furniture segment makes and sells folding furniture under the steel and steel & upholstered furniture Sudden Comfort brand, the steel-framed furniture Sudden Solutions brand, and the practical, residential-focused folding chair and table Stakmore brand.

The Greeneville, Tennessee-based manufacturer sells its products and serves clients across the US.

The metal specialist mostly serves businesses in the automotive, defense, transportation, home furnishings, retail hardgoods, and industrial manufacturing industries. It sells its furniture and grill products through retailers nationwide.

A leader in the outdoor living market, MECO sought to add a touch of comfort and permanence to its indoor portable furnishings. In 2007, the company, whose furniture was largely constructed of metal, began integrating memory foam cushions into its seating and hidden folding mechanisms into its craftsman-inspired wood tables and chairs.

MECO, a division of the Unaka Corporation, was founded in 1959 and has been manufacturing furniture since 1971.
