Allied Aerosystems Ltd (AAL) doesn't fix airplanes, but it does fix the machines that help others fix airplanes. AAL repairs and calibrates the testing equipment used by aircraft repair technicians. The company's operations include a laboratory in Wales and sites at London's Heathrow Airport, Hayes, and Nantgarw. Providing such products as altimeters, height gauges, and oscilloscopes (up to more than 12,000 items in all), AAL serves not only airlines, but also medical companies, armed forces, and telecommunication and ground support equipment manufacturers, including British Airways , General Electric , BAE Systems , and the UK's Ministry of Defense.
