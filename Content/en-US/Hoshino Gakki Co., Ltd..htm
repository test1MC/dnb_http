Hoshino Gakki has been enabling strumming and drumming for almost 100 years. Founded in 1908 as a musical instrument sales division of Hoshino Shoten (bookstores), the company manufactures and distributes the well-known Ibanez and Tama brands -- Ibanez guitars, basses, and acoustic and electronic items, as well as Tama drums, drum hardware, and parts. Hoshino Gakki has offices in the Netherlands, Germany, and Taiwan, as well as in California and Pennsylvania. The company's US subsidiary, Hoshino (U.S.A.) , has been serving the American market for about four decades.
