Gold Medal Products is a real winner when it comes to making the machines that make the treats that people like to eat. The company's specialty food machines include cotton candy machines, nacho warmers and cheese dispensers, snow cone and shaved ice machines, as well as gas and electric fryers. Gold Medal manufactures popcorn poppers that can fit on countertops and ones that are as large as countertops. It also makes machines can serve up hot dogs, steamed or grilled. Customers, typically small businesses, range from cinema concessions, to equipment rental shops, ice cream stands, and amusement park vendors.

From its Cincinnati headquarters, Gold Medal operates through nearly a dozen US branches (Chicago, Columbus, Greensboro -- N. Carolina, Indianapolis, Jacksonville, New Orleans, Orlando, Pittsburgh, and Tampa). and a global dealer network.

The company sells through its own sales force, dealers, and product and supplies catalogs (including electronic versions that customers can view online). As far as its own sales force, Gold Medal has a western sales manager and an eastern regional sales manager, under whom are seven zone managers each responsible for a different geographic area in the US (and some areas of Canada). Additionally, the company has an international sales manager who oversees sales activities outside of North America.

The company has expanded its global presence and has experienced recent growth in Brazil, Russia, India, and China. It has been helped by developments like the explosion of worldwide movie theaters and customers enjoying new concession concepts. In 2012 it bought a 42,000-sq.-ft. building near its headquarters for $1.7 million to accommodate growth, especially from international sales.

The company was founded as the Kings Ink Company in 1919. In 1931, under new ownership, the company broadened its ink-only line to include paste, glue, soft drink concentrates, cake flavors and coloring, eyeglass cleaners, window washing compounds, and other products. Food items began to be sold under the "Gold Medal Products" label.
