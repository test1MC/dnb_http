A big business in a small town, Ennis Steel Industries fabricates structural steel and provides such services as blasting, drilling, galvanizing, and welding. The company uses automated equipment including angle shear and punch machines, beam punch lines, robot beam profilers, and stub welders to prepare beams for construction companies. Its portfolio of completed projects includes work at the Dallas Museum of Art, the Delta Airlines Terminal at DFW Airport , the Fannin Holcombe Parking Garage in Houston, the General Mail Facility in Baton Rouge, Lakeline Mall in Austin, the Super Collider E-1 Project in Waxahachie, Texas, and the Texas Motor Speedway .
