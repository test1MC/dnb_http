No half nelsons are employed, but The Nelson Company does keep a firm grip on where its products are. The packaging management company specializes in "reverse distribution" by tracking, recovering, maintaining, and returning reusable packaging via its Nelson-ART Internet-based management tool. The Nelson Company makes and markets new and reconditioned custom-molded pallets, containers, metal shipping racks, and slip sheets, as well as angle board and void fillers. Founded in 1921 to supply wood packaging to Bethlehem Steel's tin mill in Baltimore, the company recovers transport packaging from 2,000 locations and operates facilities primarily in the Eastern, Southern, and Midwestern regions of the US.
