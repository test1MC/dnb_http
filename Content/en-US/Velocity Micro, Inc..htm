Building fast, nimble, and powerful PCs is the only thing that computes at Velocity Micro. The company builds custom high-performance desktop and mobile PCs that are designed for applications like gaming, digital graphic design, and home theater use, as well as common home and office use. Velocity Micro's product line includes its Edge, Raptor, ProMagix, and Vector series. Doing business mainly in North America, Velocity Micro sells its products through its e-commerce Web site; it also sells pre-configured PCs through retailers like Best Buy and MicroCenter . Velocity Micro was founded in 1997 by CEO Randy Copeland.
