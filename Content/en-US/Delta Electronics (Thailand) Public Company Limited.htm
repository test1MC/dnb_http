Delta Thailand isn't an exotic vacation spot for Western tourists. It's Delta Electronics (Thailand), the Thai branch of Delta Electronics , which is based in Taiwan. While its parent makes high-end goods for export to US computer makers, Delta Thailand makes mid-range products for export to Europe and the US, including switching power supplies, adapters, inverters, converters, magnetic components, solenoids, filters, and brushless fans and motors. Its products are used in computers, servers, networking equipment, LCD monitors, TVs, printers, modems, and other products. Customers include CISCO and Black & Decker . Founded in 1988, Delta Thailand has manufacturing operations in Thailand, India, and Slovakia.
