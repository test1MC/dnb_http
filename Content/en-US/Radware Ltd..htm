Radware could be seen as "totally rad" by those who want fast, secure communications. Its application delivery service consists of load balancing switches for applications, delivery, and WOC (WAN optimization controllers). The company also focuses in tandem on network security, which entails VPNs, unified threat management, intrusion detection and prevention, and network analysis, among others. Its applications include AppDirector, AppXML, APSolute Vision, Defense-Pro, and Inflight. Radware serves more than 10,000 clients worldwide such as Ace Hardware , Patagonia , Audiovox , New York Law School, ADP , the World Meteorological Organization, and Unicomm.
