If you have the material, Bache Pallets can probably handle it. The company is a UK-based manufacturer of material-handling equipment, offering products such as metal pallets, roll containers, and pallet converters. The manufacturer mainly serves customers in the automotive, construction, agriculture, foundry and forging, and retail industries, as well as the UK Ministry of Defence. Among Bache Pallets' recent clients are Land Rover , Royal Mail , and Caterpillar . The company utilizes computer-aided design to ensure its products meet specifications and automated production lines to ensure a proper build. Bache Pallets has been in business for more than 50 years.

Bache Pallet products are made to order, and are tailor fit to the client's exact requirements and specifications in regards to size, finish, budget, quantity, and durability.

To complete its orders, the company leverages its resource facilities, AutoCAD and SDRC C3P I-Deas Computer Aided Design workstations, factory development facilities (including production and welding facilities), two fully automated robot welding machines, and machine shop facilities for cutting, bending, presswork, electronic paint spray, and guillotining.

The UK-based company sells products across the UK, Belgium, Germany, Spain, and the US.

Some of its customers include Caterpillar , Honda , Ford , Jaguar , Royal Mail , Volvo , Land Rover , and Tenneco .
