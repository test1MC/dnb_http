Asahi Organic Chemicals really knows how to turn on the waterworks -- and how to turn them off, as well. The company manufactures and distributes valves, valve actuators and controls, gaskets, and polyvinyl chloride (PVC) plastic piping products. Its fluid control products can be found in the Georgia Aquarium, as well as Japan's famed Phoenix Seagaia Resort. Asahi Organic Chemicals also produces such items as flow meters and filtration equipment for use in automotive and industrial applications. The company maintains operations in China, Europe, Singapore, the US, and Japan, of course.
