GM Korea is one of South Korea's largest car makers by sales. It produces GM -branded vehicles (such as Chevrolet Cruze compacts, and Spark and Aveo minicars, Captiva SUVs, in addition to Malibu, Orlando, and Trax vehicles) at its five manufacturing plants in South Korea, and an assembly plant in Vietnam. Each year GM Korea sells 151,040 vehicles in Korea and exports more than 1.8 million complete vehicles and CKD (completely knocked down) products and kits to more than 150 markets around the world. It also provides vehicle kits for assembly at GM facilities in China, Uzbekistan, Mexico, India, Colombia, Russia, and other countries.
