China Valves Technology is a Nevada holding company that develops, manufactures, and sells large-scale industrial metal valves in China. Operating through three main Chinese subsidiaries, the company's valve products are geared towards the chemical, electricity, metal, nuclear power, oil and gas, and water industries. With a broad portfolio, it manufactures more than 800 models of low-, medium-, and high-pressure valves that come in a variety of specifications, with different diameter, pressure cap, and temperature ranges.
