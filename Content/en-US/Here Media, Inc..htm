Curious about queer media? Try Here. The media company owns nine companies targeting lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender consumers. Magazine titles include Out, The Advocate, and HIV Plus and their corresponding Web sites; OutTraveler.com, SheWired.com, and gay.com are its online properties without print versions. Here Media also owns publisher Alyson Books ( Heather Has Two Mommies, Daddy's New Roommate ), on demand TV network here!, and film acquisition and distribution company Here Films. The company has offices in New York, San Francisco, and Los Angeles. Here Media was formed in 2009 when Regent Entertainment founders Stephen Jarchow and Paul Colichman purchased PlanetOut and merged it with Regent Media .
