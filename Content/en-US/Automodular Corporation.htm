Automodular formerly provided sub-assembly and sequencing services to Ford Motor Company . Sequencing refers to the arrival of sub-assembled modules -- such as the cockpit, rear strut and axle, powerpack, radiator support, rear bumper, and door handles -- at a car assembly plant right at the time they are to be added to the vehicle. The company has had no active operations since its contract with Ford ended in late 2014.
