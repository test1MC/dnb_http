VPK Packaging Group makes boxes with a concern for the environment. The company provides a variety of packaging products and paper for customers primarily in western Europe. Its packaging products include corrugated board, solid board, and tubes. The company also practices recovered paper recycling to supply raw materials for its own business as well as other companies. VPK Packaging provides packaging products for chickens and eggs, the meat industry, and exporters of flowers and flower bulbs, among others. VPK Packaging Group has operations in Benelux, France, Germany, the Netherlands, Poland, and the UK.
