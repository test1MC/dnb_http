Mixing up a batch of colors is the primary activity of Performance Masterbatches. The UK-based specialty manufacturer has developed a system to make color-extruded polymer plastics to customer specifications. The masterbatch process is a mixture of pigments and additives encapsulated during heating into a carrier resin and then cut into a granular shape after cooling. The process allows manufacturers to select the color of their polymer products. Performance Masterbatches uses its Datamatch system to produce a wide range of colors and to ensure color consistency.

The company offers polymer specific and universal masterbatch color products for all polymer variants.

Performance Masterbatches was founded in 1975.
