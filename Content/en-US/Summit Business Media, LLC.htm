Summit Professional Networks (formerly Summit Business Media) wants to climb to great heights in the business-to-business media world. The company offers about 15 magazines, 20 websites, and 150 reference titles primarily to professionals in the insurance, financial, legal, and professional services industries. It publishes specialty titles such as National Underwriter Life/Health , Senior Market Advisor , and Agent's Sales Journal . The company also produces industry-focused conferences and events. Summit Professional Networks was acquired by ALM Media in 2015.

The company filed for Chapter 11 in 2011 in order to cut about $140 million of debt from its balance sheet, and exited bankruptcy later that year.

During 2013 the company changed its name from Summit Business Media to Summit Professional Networks before being acquired by ALM Media in 2015.

Summit Professional Networks is based in Erlanger, Kentucky. It also has office locations in Chicago, Denver, New York City, Philadelphia, and Washington, DC.
