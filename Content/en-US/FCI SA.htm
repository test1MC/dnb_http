FCI keeps the electronics industry well connected. The company designs and manufactures a variety of connectors and interconnect systems, including board and wire connnectors, backpanel connectors, cable input/output connectors, and power connectors. Other products include cable assemblies and sockets for integrated circuits. These products are used in a range of electronic devices from data storage systems and industrial control equipment to cell phones and other mobile devices. Although it's headquartered in France, the bulk of FCI's operations and sales are based in the Asia/Pacific region.

FCI has one manufacturing site in the Americas, one in Europe, and six in Asia/Pacific. It also has sales support, customer service, engineering, and process development operations in those regions. The Asia/Pacific region accounts for more than 70% of FCI's sales, though its also reaches customers in the Americas, the Middle East, Africa, and Europe.

FCI gears its products to customers in such industries as communications, data/storage, consumer electronics, industrial, instrumentation, medical, automotive, and renewable energy. Key customers and distributors have included such notable names as Avnet , HP , Samsung , and TTI .

Placing a heavy focus on R&D, FCI spends about 5% of its annual revenue on developing the next generation of high speed connectors and interconnect and power distribution systems. It's global R&D centers have logged thousands of patents and licensing agreements.
