Doing business as The Perfect Purée of Napa Valley, the company is a leading producer of fruit and vegetable purées for both the consumer market and the foodservices industry. It makes more than 30 flavors of purées, concentrates, and zests used to make beverages, sauces, and dessert items. Perfect Purée distributes its products to consumers through supermarkets and specialty food stores, as well as through its own mail-order Web site. The company also supplies restaurants and other foodservices businesses. President Tracy Hayward started the in 1989.
