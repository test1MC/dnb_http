Wellness Foods makes its living without the use of artificial flavorings, colorings, or preservatives. The company manages a family of food businesses that manufacture fresh and healthy breakfast, snack, and drink products for consumers in the UK and internationally. Its core brands include Orchard House & Paradise Foods , which makes fruit juices; and Stream Foods, which makes Fruit Bowl brand fruit snacks. From facilities around the UK, the firm distributes its food products through major UK supermarkets including Asda , Sainsbury , Morrison , Tesco , and Waitrose . Wellness Foods is owned by Irish horseracing tycoons JP McManus and John Magnier.

When possible and where food volumes are available, Wellness strives to source all of its products within the UK. Foods not found in the UK, such as its fruits, nuts, and seeds, are sourced in other parts of the world.

Wellness Foods distributes its products around the UK. Its head office is in Chertsey in Surrey, while subsidiaries Orchard & Paradise Foods and Stream Foods are located in Corby (Northamptonshire) and Wisbech (Cambridgshire), respectively.

Wellness Foods works with clients in the retail, food service, and food manufacturing industries in the UK and around the world to distribute its products. Some of its largest customers include major domestic grocers and supermarkets such as Asda , Sainsbury , Morrison , Tesco , and Waitrose .

The company has sold off some of its brands to concentrate on its core Orchard House & Paradise Foods and Stream Foods brands in recent years. In mid-2014 it sold off its its Rowse Honey brand to Valeo Foods (the Irish owner of Jacob's cracker brand); and sold its Dorset Cereals brand line to Jordans and Ryvita for Â£50 million.

Since its inception in 2005, the company has grown by acquiring established health food manufacturers, traditionally one business a year. However, Wellness Foods' plans to expand through future acquisitions have come to a halt as it deals with declining profits amid a global economic downturn and changes in the executive suite.

Focusing instead on organic growth, Wellness Foods-owned Grove Fresh is targeting European consumers as it refreshes the brand message and packaging design (with Elopak ) of its fruit juices and beverages. It has also launched a variety of new products, including exotic fruit blends. Other subsidiaries have managed to sustain a following in developing regions. Through Rowse Honey, Wellness-Foods is the leading player in Nigeria's honey market.
