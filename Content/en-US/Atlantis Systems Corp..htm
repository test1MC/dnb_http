It's okay to crash and burn a time or two, if you're operating an Atlantis Systems' simulator. AS's operating subsidiary Atlantis Systems International makes flight and maintenance training simulators for military and civil aviation customers, as well as for energy, medical, industrial, and transportation markets. Its Contracted Flying Training & Support (CFTS) division offers training simulators for flying helicopters, cockpit learning procedures, and testing and its Integrated Maintenance Training System (IMTS) trains military maintenance personnel to repair the complex systems on such military aircraft as the F-15E and the Blackhawk helicopter. It was acquired by Bluedrop Performance Learning in late 2013.
