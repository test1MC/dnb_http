PV Crystalox Solar provides the tools to help harness the power of the sun. Founded in 1982, the company makes multicrystalline silicon wafers and ingots -- components used by customers in constructing photovoltaic (PV) solar cells and modules. PV Crystalox Solar produces ingots at its plant in the UK and manufactures wafers at its production facility in Germany (for customers in Europe) and by a subcontractor in Japan (for customers in Asia). China and Japan, two of the countries leading the world in installing solar power systems, are PV Crystalox Solar's primary markets. Customers in the Asia/Pacific region make up more than three-quarters of its sales.
