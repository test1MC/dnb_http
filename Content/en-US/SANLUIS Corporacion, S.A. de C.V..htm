With its sales of leaf and coil springs and torsion bars, SANLUIS Corporación has sprung to the top of the list of Mexican auto parts suppliers. But the company's portfolio goes further. Under the SANLUIS Rassini brand, the company manufactures suspension and high tech brake components for heavy vehicles as well as pickups, minivans, SUVs, and other passenger automobiles. Its brake products include rotors, drums, and hubs. Operating from plants in North America and Brazil, SANLUIS' largest clients, General Motors , Ford , and Chrysler , account for over 65% of net sales. Other clients include Honda , Toyota , and Volkswagen .
