Southern Progress Corporation is proud of its Southern twang. The company publishes its popular Southern Living title, as well as Sunset , Cooking Light , Health , and Coastal Living . Its Oxmoor House unit publishes cooking and crafts books. Southern Progress also offers custom publishing services, and has produced titles for clients such as Lowe's, Publix Super Markets, and the State of California.
