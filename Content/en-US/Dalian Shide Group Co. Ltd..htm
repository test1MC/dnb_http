Dalian Shide Group is a leading manufacturer of plastic building materials. Founded in 1992 and headquartered in the city of Dalian, in northeastern China's Liaoning Province, the Shide Group is the country's largest maker of plastic door and window profiles. Its primary subsidiary is Dalian Shide Plastics Industry, which makes PVC sectional materials, including PVC profiles, windows and doors, pipes, pipefitting, sheet materials, and accessories. Shide Group companies also include petrochemical production, insurance and healthcare services, home-appliance manufacturing, a soccer team, called Dalian Shide Football Club.
