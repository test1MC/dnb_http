Kanai Juyo Kogyo has been saving nine with a stitch in time for over a century. The company manufactures and markets textile machinery parts and accessories, including spinning needles and various rings, as well as carding for non-woven fabrics. Kanai Juyo Kogyo also produces and sells non-woven fabric, which is used in industrial and bulk goods (air conditioner filters, abrasives, automotive interiors, and hat materials). Domestic affiliates, Tokusen Kogyo manufactures specialty steel wire, and Kosenkaku Hotel provides lodging at Japan's Arima Spa Resort. Foreign subsidiaries are established in China and the US. The textile parts maker was founded by Kumakichi Kanai and continues under his family's control.
