Badgerland Meat and Provisions stores, wholesales, and distributes beef, pork, and poultry products in its home state, as well as parts of Illinois and Michigan. It sells to grocery chains, meat markets, restaurants and warehouses stores. The company gets its meat from producers such as American Foods , Excel , Farmland Foods , Gold Kist , and Iowa Turkey . Badgerland also operates a case-ready business for which the company cuts, packs, and distributes more than 300,000 pounds of pre-packaged and pre-priced pork products every week.
