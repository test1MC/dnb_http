Femcare helps keep the babies away. The Femcare group, through subsidiary Femcare-Nikomed, manufactures and distributes the Filshie Clip System, a device used to block a woman's Fallopian tubes for contraceptive purposes. It also sells a number of other products, including HRT trays (used in hormone implantation procedures) and urological catheterization products. Femcare provides contract manufacturing services to other medical device firms, and it operates a medical equipment distributor in the UK, specializing in gynecological, surgical, and urological equipment. Femcare is a subsidiary of Utah Medical Products .

Femcare's portfolio includes both products that were developed in-house and products that have been sourced from other manufacturers.

The group is part of Utah Medical Products' Gynecology/Urology/Electrosurgery segment.

Femcare has offices in the UK and Australia. It distributes its products around the world.

In 2014, sales of Filshie Clips, applicators, and accessories represented nearly 40% of Utah Medical Products' total sales. The Gynecology/Electrosurgery/Urology segment, of which Femcare is a part, provided nearly 60% of the parent company's revenue. However, subsidiary Femcare-Nikomed's revenue fell 14% to $493,000 that year.
