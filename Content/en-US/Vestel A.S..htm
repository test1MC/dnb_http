Vestel develops, manufactures, markets, and sells a variety of consumer electronics, household appliances, and digital products including LCD and plasma TVs, digital receivers, wireless products, refrigerators, and air conditioners. Based in Turkey, the company primarily serves markets in Turkey and Europe (more than 70% of the company's revenues are from exports) and is intent on building its footprint in Russia, Ukraine, the Middle East, and Africa. The family of Chairman Ahmet Zorlu owns 51% of the company through Collar Holding BV.
