Icy waters produce a lot of frozen fish. Orca Bay Seafoods is a leading supplier of fresh frozen seafood, sourcing products from oceans all over the world. The company buys flash-frozen fish from suppliers, and keeps it frozen as it cuts individual portions for sale to foodservice companies, supermarkets, club stores, and restaurants across the US. Its products include Ahi tuna, Alaskan cod, Pacific Ocean pearch, sockeye salmon, mahi mahi, and tilapia, as well as Mexican white shrimp. Orca Bay was founded by Mike Samsel in 1985; the giant Japanese seafood company Maruha Nichiro owns a minority interest in the company; Japanese conglomerate Tokusui Corporation owns the controlling interest.
