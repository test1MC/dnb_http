Ah, food that gives Zorba his zing. Kontos Foods manufactures and markets Greek and Middle Eastern bakery products including more than 22 varieties of flatbread, including as pita and hand-stretched (not die-cut) fillo dough. The company also provides Mediterranean specialty foods such as gyro meats, feta cheese, grape leaves, and kalamata olives. Kontos sells its products wholesale to US supermarkets, ethnic grocers, specialty food stores, restaurants, and foodservice distributors. It also exports to Canada and other countries. Founded by Evris Kontos in the 1960s, the company continues to be owned and operated by the Kontos family.
