VeriSilicon HoldingsÂ is an application-specific integrated circuit (ASIC) design services firm. It provides software and services for customers to design a chip, then hands off the design to contract semiconductor manufacturers, such as Advanced Semiconductor Manufacturing , Grace Semiconductor Manufacturing , Semiconductor Manufacturing International , and Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing . The company was established in 2001. Founder and CEO Wayne Dai previously started Celestry Design Technologies, an electronic design software company acquired in 2003 by Cadence Design Systems . VeriSilicon Holdings has operations in China, France, Japan, South Korea, Taiwan, and the US.
