Industrial Convertidora converts plastic into fun. The company, better known as Conver, makes plastic films used for party products, food packaging, and industrial applications. Its four divisions, Flexible Packaging, Balloons, Casing, and Publicity make balloons, candy wrappers, banners, table cloths, and promotional items. Conver also makes custom packaging, including graphics, to clients' specifications. The Flexible Packaging division produces printed plastic bags from 25 millimeters to 1,600 millimeters. Kaleidoscope brand balloons includes more than 600 metallic designs. CASEMATIC makes sausage casings and the Publicity division puts clients' logos on balloons, wall hangings, and direct mail pieces.
