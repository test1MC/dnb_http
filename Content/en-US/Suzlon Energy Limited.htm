The Hindu god of wind should be renamed Suzlon. India's only major wind turbine maker is a global player in the renewable energy market. Suzlon Energy makes wind turbine generators for onshore use that range between 600kW to 6.15 MW. The vertically integrated company produces its own components -- blades, control panels, covers, generators, nacelles, rotors, and towers -- and also sells them to competitors. Boasting 24,726 MW of wind energy capacity installed across 30-plus countries, Suzlon maintains more than a dozen manufacturing plants in India, Europe, and the US (in Minnesota), as well as R&D facilities in Europe and India. Suzlon was founded in 1995 by Tulsi Tanti.
