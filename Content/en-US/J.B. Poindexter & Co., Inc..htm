Lookin' to jazz up your chassis? J.B. Poindexter (JBPCO) manufactures commercial truck and step-van bodies and installs them on ready-made truck chassis (by Ford , Freightliner , and Workhorse ) through its Morgan and Morgan Olson divisions. JBPCO's Truck Accessories Group (TAG) makes such products as hard pickup truck caps and tonneaus, under brand names Leer, LoRider, and others. The company's Specialty Manufacturing division provides precision machining services for specialty vehicles -- limousines and funeral coaches -- through subsidiaries Federal Coach, Eagle Coach, MIC, and EFP.

The company has 35 locations in Canada, Malaysia, Mexico, and the US.

JBPCO operates through several subsidiaries and divisions. Both Morgan and Morgan Olson make commercial medium truck and step van frameworks and bodies and account for almost 60% of the company's total sales.

Its Specialty Manufacturing division (about 25% of total sales) is divided into three business units: MIC (high-precision machining services for oil and gas services industry), SVG (makes funeral coaches and limousines), and EFP (expandable polystyrene and polypropylene foam for packaging applications). The Truck Accessories Group (TAG) segment generates the remainder of revenue.

JBPCO sells through a network of 1,300 independent dealers and distributors. Its major customers have included such big names as UPS and FedEx . Other customers have included Bimbo Bakeries , W.B. Mason , and ARAMARK Corporation , Schlumberger Limited , and Halliburton Company .

JBPCO achieves growth organically and through select acquisitions that augment its core manufacturing competencies and enhance its portfolio of truck component brands. It has made 30 acquisitions over the last 15 years to grow its operations. In 2015 it acquired Reading Truck Body, a truck manufacturer with a plant in Reading, Pennsylvania, as well as five company-owned installation facilities operating as Reading Equipment & Distribution LLC.

J.B. Poindexter began as a private equity investment firm in 1983. It transitioned into an investment partner before becoming a diversified manufacturing corporation.
