Sucrière de Pithiviers le Vieil Group puts its faith in the lowly sugar beet. Its Vermandoise Industrie subsidiary processes sugar in France. The company's Compagnie Nouvelle de Sucreries Réunies grows wheat, beans, and other vegetable crops. The Delloye family controls Vermandoise de Sucreries, which holds 56% of Sucrière de Pithiviers le Vieil Group.
