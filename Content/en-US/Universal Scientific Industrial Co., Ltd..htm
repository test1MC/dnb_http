Universal Scientific Industrial (USI) is all that and more. USI provides contract design and manufacturing of integrated circuits (ICs), printed circuit boards, notebook and desktop PCs, and handheld devices. The company also offers engineering, testing, and packaging services, as well as logistics and customer support. Founded in 1976, USI serves such industries as communications, consumer electronics, computing, and automotive electronics. Lenovo Group is among the company's top customers. Advanced Semiconductor Engineering (ASE), which previously owned about 23% of USI, purchased the rest of USI's stock in 2010. USI is among the largest providers of electronics manufacturing services in the world.
