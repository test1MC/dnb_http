Mars Petcare US is the king of kibble. A top maker of dry pet foods nationwide, the company makes and markets dry pet foods, soft-dry food, soft treats, and dog biscuits under the Royal Canin , Cesar, Pedigree, and Whiskas brands, among others. Mars Petcare US also makes products for other pet food companies, manufactures store brands for its retail customers, and sells its own regional brands. The company's customers include mass merchandisers (such as Wal-Mart , Meijer ), pet supply chains ( PETCO , PetSmart ), supermarkets, and farm and feed stores. The company was founded in 1954 as Doane Products and is part of candy and pet food behemoth Mars ' pet care division.

Mars Petcare US, headquartered in Tennessee, is one of the top units for Belgium-based Mars Petcare, which operates in some 50 countries.

Mars Petcare US boasts more than a dozen production plants nationwide to support its business.

The company's Pedigree, Royal Canin, and Whiskas brands each ring up more than $1 billion in sales annually.

Mars Petcare US serves several segments, including supermarkets, mass merchants, pet supply chains, and farm and feed stores. It makes its own name-brand foods for dogs and cats, as well as pet foods under private labels for others.

In a bid to grow its pet food business, parent company Mars in August 2014 acquired the IAMS , Eukanuba, and Natura brands in North America and Latin America (and other select countries) from Procter & Gamble for $2.9 billion. (The company plans to exercise its option to purchase P&G's pet food business in parts of Asia Pacific, the Middle East, and Africa.)
