This nearly century-old magazine can still churn out a few new ideas. The New Republic, Inc. publishes The New Republic , a print and online magazine that reports on world news, American politics, foreign policy, and culture. Topics include books and arts, the economy, and health care. The magazine is published every two weeks. The New Republic was founded in 1914 by political journalist Walter Lippman. Today it is owned by a consortium of investors that includes editor emeritus Martin Peretz, media banker Laurence Grafstein, hedge fund manager William A. Ackman, and the real estate developer Michael Alter. In 2012 Chris Hughes, a co-founder of Facebook , announced plans to purchase the company
