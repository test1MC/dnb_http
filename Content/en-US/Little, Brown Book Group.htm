Little, Brown Book Group has no problem populating the best-seller list. The company publishes works written by such notable authors as Norman Mailer, Gore Vidal, E. L. Doctorow, David Sedaris, and Candace Bushnell. Imprints include Abacus (literary), Sphere (commercial), Atom (teen and young adult), Virago (women's literature), and Orbit (science fiction and fantasy). Little, Brown Book Group, founded in 1837, published Louisa May Alcott's Little Women , as well as Emily Dickinson's poetry. The company publishes about 60 new titles a year, divided evenly between fiction and non-fiction. It releases audiobooks and eBooks through Hachette Digital. Little, Brown Book Group is part of Hachette UK .
