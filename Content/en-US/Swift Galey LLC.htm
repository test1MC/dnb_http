SWIFT GALEY is hoping that one day it will be too big for its britches. The company produces fabrics such as denim, twill, poplin, corduroy, and blends for major retailers. Customers have included Abercrombie & Fitch and Gap . It also makes specialty fabrics for the home, sportswear, and uniforms. No slouch when it comes to innovation, the company developed H 2 Tech, a technology that manages moisture and is repellent to oil and water. This technology includes color and shape retention as well as color fastness. The company also worked on the development of antimicrobial fabric treatments for specific markets. New York-based investment firm Patriarch Partners LLC owns Galey & Lord.
