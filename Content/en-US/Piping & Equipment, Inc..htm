Someone has to pipe up for this industry. Through eight locations in Alabama, Florida, Louisiana, and Texas, Piping & Equipment distributes industrial pipes, valves, and fittings. Products include aluminum and brass pipe, copper tubing, butterfly and globe valves, bronze and iron check, gate, nickel flanges, and socketweld fittings. Piping & Equipment's Transportation Products Group provides rail-car and other transportation valves and parts. The company serves the petrochemical, power, pulp and paper, refining, steel, textile, and transportation industries. Fairmont Supply Company (a subsidiary of CONSOL Energy ) acquired Piping & Equipment in 2007.
