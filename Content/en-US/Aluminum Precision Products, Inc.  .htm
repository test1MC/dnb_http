Aluminum Precision Products (APP) is no lightweight. Through its APP Forge division, the company is a leading maker of aluminum forgings for the auto, aerospace, medical, and diesel locomotive industries. APP also makes titanium forgings and offers complete milling, turning, drilling, and precision-honing services for aluminum, titanium, and steel forgings. The company produces its products from initial design through final assembly. Its Catalina Cylinders division manufactures high- and low-pressure aluminum gas cylinders for the scuba, beverage, medical, and automotive industries. Its Cliff Impact division makes fire extinguishers and paintball cylinders. The Keeler family controls the company.

Based in Santa Ana, California, APP serves North American markets and exports its products to Europe, Asia, Africa, South America, and Australia.

APP operates through several divisions, including APP Forge, Catalina Cylinders, and Cliff Impact.

A multichannel marketer, APP makes and sells its products to several industries. Its APP Forge unit caters to sectors such as auto, aerospace, medical, and diesel locomotive. It serves scuba, beverage, automotive, and medical customers through its Catalina Cylinders division. Through the company's Cliff Impact unit it sells paintball cylinders and fire extinguishers.

The company's key operating assets have grown since its inception to include nearly 50 hydraulic and mechanical forging presses. In addition to its presence in North America, the company exports its products overseas to Europe, Asia, Africa, South America, and Australia.

Its customer base is said to take in every major airfame prime contractor in the world, as well as more than 500 subcontracts. APP's reach into the automotive industry has deepened, too, as car makers use more aluminum to produce vehicles that meet federal and consumer demand for increased fuel economy, reduced emissions, and improved safety.
