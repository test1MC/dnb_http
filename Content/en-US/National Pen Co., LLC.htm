Your logo here! Self-promotion is always shameless at National Pen (formerly Internet Design Technologies). The company makes custom promotional items, such as bags, calendars, cups, mouse pads, pens, shirts, and other tchotchkes. National Pen's products are manufactured in production facilities located in Tennessee, Mexico, and Ireland. In Canada, the company operates as Perfect Pen and Stationery and as National Pen Ltd. in Europe, Australia, and New Zealand. The company was founded in 1948 as Modern Mold and Tool Corporation by Al Liguori. Sold to Berwind Corporation in 2004, National Pen has been owned by private equity firm Lincolnshire Equity Fund IV, LP, since 2012. In 2016, printing products company Cimpress N.V. agreed to acquire the company for $218 million.

Private equity firm Lincolnshire, which bought the company from Berwind Corporation in 2012, was attracted to National Pen for its organic growth potential. It also has plans to make strategic complementary acquisitions to boost the promotional products company.

Based in California, National Pen has a presence in the US, Canada, Ireland, and the UK. Its customer base, which has grown to 1 million in recent years, spans the US, Canada, Europe, Australia, and New Zealand. National Pen's manufacturing facilities are located in Tennessee, Mexico, and Ireland.

National Pen is a top-25 specialty company.

As a top supplier of pens, National Pen is focused on pen and promotional product development; product planning, manufacturing, and improvement; and quality testing. Its expertise is in selling highly decorated pens and other items in low minimum quantities at competitively low prices.

National Pen has benefited from licensing agreements through which it provides personalized promotional items to national football, basketball, baseball, and hockey leagues, as well as big-name corporate customers.

National Pen offers promotional products for both businesses and professionals. It does business primarily through the mail order channel, counting some 1 million customers. Most of its sales come from selected vendors who drop ship to their customers. It uses telemarketing, mail order, and the Internet to market to its customers.
