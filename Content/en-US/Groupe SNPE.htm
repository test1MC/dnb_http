Headquartered in France with operations in more than 70 countries, state-owned SNPE makes explosives and propellants for use in ballistic missiles, aerospace applications, and airbags. It also produces fine chemicals for pharmaceutical, agrochemical, and other uses; and specialty chemicals used in applications such as inks, fuel additives, and nail polishes. SNPE's subsidiaries and operating units include Bergerac NC (specialty chemicals). The company was acquired by Giat Industries in 2013.

SNPE's operations are grouped under Powders & Explosives and Redeployment & Services segments.

In the explosives field, subsidiary EURENCO works in the design, production and commercialization of explosives, and offers energy products used in the manufacture of warheads, ammunition and pyrotechnic devices for military or industrial use.

Other activities of the company include Powders, Combustible Objects and Additives. The Combustible Objects division offers modular propellant charges for artillery ammunition, combustible cartridge cases for tanks and artillery ammunition, and Nitrofilm, an explosive film.

The Powders division manufactures simple powders and multi bases for use in military ammunition, mortars, anti-tank missiles and hunting and shooting cartridges. It also makes military-grade Nitrocellulose.

The Additives division produces ethyl hexyl nitrate, a cetane improver for diesel fuel.

SNPE Redeployment & Services is responsible for managing the consequences of shutdowns of industrial units, and re-industrialize these sites after rehabilitation if necessary.

The company is located in France. EURENCO is based in Paris and operates 4 modern production plants in France (Bergerac, Sorgues), Belgium (Clermont) and Sweden (Karlskoga). EURENCO also runs a number of storage facilities worldwide.

Explosives, Propellants, Combustible Objects and Additives have production Plants in Sweden, Belgium and France, and commercial offices in Washington and Houston.

The company serves various industries such as Defense & Security, Demolition & Breaching, Oil & Gas Extraction, Mining & Quarrying, Rail Hardening, Warhead Disposal & Retrofit, Automotive and Tool industry.
