This company's growth has resulted from the pigging needs of other companies. Pipeline Engineering & Supply designs and manufactures pipeline pigging equipment (pistonlike mechanisms used to remove debris and impurities from existing pipelines), including brushes, pig launching and receiving traps, pressure vessels, and pipeline recovery tools. It also provides isolation and joint-testing systems. Its polyurethane engineering technology and services include the manufacture of products used for ceramics, cable protection, and buoyancy equipment. The company is a subsidiary of industrial valvemaker CIRCOR International .

Pipeline Engineering & Supply Co. is organized around three main divisions: pipeline engineering, pipeline engineering solutions, and polyurethane engineering. It supplies a full range of pipeline pigging tools, pig launchers and pig receivers, quick opening closures and pig signallers. It also designs and produces client-specific polyurethane products.

Customers include major oil, gas, petrochemical, and renewable energy companies and pipeline, refinery, and wind farm operators.

In response to the emerging offshore wind power market in the UK, in 2011 the company launched PEFLEX, a subsea cable protection system, which increases strength and rigidity along a full cable length by restricting the bending of cables).

The company was founded in 1969. CIRCOR International acquired Pipeline Engineering & Supply in 2009. That company combined the purchased unit's products and services with its Sagebrush Pipeline Equipment Company assets. The deal also allowed CIRCOR to expand its pipeline engineering services outside of North America.
