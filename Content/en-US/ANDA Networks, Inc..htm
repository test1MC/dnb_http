ANDA Networks products strive for ubiquity. The company provides equipment for fiber and copper-based networks that service providers use to deliver voice and data services to their subscribers. Its EtherTone product line enables services such as high-speed Internet access and voice-over-IP. ANDA sells through relationships with such equipment makers as Ciena to communications service providers worldwide. The company's investors included Venrock Associates , Highland Capital Partners , Amerindo Investment Advisors prior to its acquisition by Telco Systems in 2011.
