Internet firm Lycos Europe is spinning off its remaining Web properties across Europe. After declining revenues in almost all of its business units and the hiring of Kirkland & Ellis to advise it on restructuring, the company is working on the divestment of its online shopping business, and on the liquidation of its European portal and Web hosting businesses. At the end of 2008 it sold its Danish Web portal; in addition, in 2009 United Internet purchased its Domain Names business. Spain's Telefónica owns nearly a third of Lycos Europe through its LE Holding Corp.; German media giant Bertelsmann owns 20%.
