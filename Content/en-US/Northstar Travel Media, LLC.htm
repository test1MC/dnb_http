You might say this company guides professionals in the travel business. Northstar Travel Media is a leading trade magazine publisher that focuses on the hospitality, travel, and meetings industries with such titles as Travel Weekly, TravelAge West , and Meetings and Conventions . It also publishes industry information and directories; sells subscriptions to online data; produces industry trade shows; and provides marketing services. The company traces its roots to the 1939 publication of Hotel & Travel Index by Elwood Ingledue, the son of a hotel owner in Glendale, California. Today Northstar Travel Group is owned by Wicks Group, a private equity firm that invests in information, education, and media companies.

Based in Secaucus, New Jersey, Northstar also has offices in New York City; Glen Ellyn, Illinois; Los Angeles; and Winston-Salem, North Carolina.

Northstar targets travel agents, meeting planners, and other travel and hospitality industry professionals through its various product lines. All total, its reaches more than 1.4 million monthly unique visitors online, while it manages more than 2.7 million subscriptions to its content.

Northstar has evolved from a traditional business-to-business publisher to an integrated information and marketing solutions provider.

The company has been using acquisitions to grow in its core markets while at the same time diversify revenue streams. In fiscal 2014 Northstar acquired a majority stake in e-commerce booking technology company, Inntopia Inc.

That same year Northstar also acquired Mountain Travel Symposium (MTS), a conference that attracts travel professionals from all over the world in North America every spring. Also in 2014 Northstar acquired Travel Weekly China, Travel Weekly Asia, and Events China from Contineo Media, a multi-platform information provider based in Singapore, with locations in Beijing and Shanghai along with Web In Travel (WIT), a Singapore-based online travel site for the Asia Pacific region.
