Electronic Instrumentation and Technology (EIT) provides engineering and design and contract manufacturing services to OEMs (original equipment manufacturers) in the medical, communications, industrial process control, analytical instrument, and defense markets, among others. The company's engineering and design services include prototyping, electronic design, printed circuit board layout, and mechanical and process engineering. It also offers electronic manufacturing services, as well as assembly, cabling, and machining. Customers are located in some 30 countries. EIT was founded in 1977 by chairman and CTO Joe T. May.

The company has five facilities in the US, four in Virginia and one in Massachusetts.

To better serve its customers in the northeastern US and acquire new ones, EIT in early 2013 purchased a manufacturing facility in Methuen, Massachusetts.
