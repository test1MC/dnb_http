Great Wall Technology can help you scale all sorts of technical heights. The company manufactures computer products and other electronics like LCD TVs, storage products, magnetic heads, hard disk drives and disk substrates, power supply products, and monitoring terminal products. It also provides systems integration services, Internet content, and general IT services. Established in 1998, Great Wall Technology's ownership changed in 2006 when its parent, state-controlled China Great Wall Computer Group, was merged into China Electronics Corporation (CEC).
