If you're looking to acquire some media, Acquire Media can help with that. The news aggregator serves up text news, multimedia, and other time sensitive data to news, financial services, and information publishing companies (including Hoover's ). It distributes information from Dow Jones , PR Newswire , Business Wire , Canada NewsWire, The Washington Post News Service, The New York Times Syndicate , and other outlets, then delivers it to Web servers, corporate websites, and individual users. Acquire Media delivers more than 400,000 articles a day and claims zero latency. It counts Alpha Trade, Schwab , NASDAQ , Thomson , CNBC , and SNL Financial among its major clients. Acquire Media was formed in 2001.
