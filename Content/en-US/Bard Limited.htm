Perhaps a sonnet would help take one's mind off being catheterized, but that's as close to poetry as Bard Limited will get. Operating as Bard Medical UK, Bard Limited distributes the medical devices of its US-based parent, C. R. Bard , in the UK and Ireland. Its primary offerings in the urological field include infection-control catheters (Bardex IC Foley) that help prevent patients from developing urinary tract infections, traditional foley catheters (Biocath), and other devices for urine collection and incontinence treatment (Uriplan and Dignicare). Other offerings include vascular stents and catheters, chemotherapy equipment, and surgical supplies.

Bard Medical has its operational and sales offices in the London area (near Gatwick Airport) and in Dublin.

The company serves the healthcare sector in disease areas such as vascular, urology, oncology and surgical specialty products and services.

European operations account for 15% of parent company C. R. Bard 's annual revenues.

In 2013, C. R. Bard acquired Rochester Medical , the world's leading manufacture of silicone urological products, for $262 million. Rochester Medical develops and manufactures urinary incontinence and retention products including male external catheters, urethral inserts for women, silicone Foley catheters, and anti-infection catheters.

Bard Medical took control of Rochester Medical's UK operations.
