Crucible Industries makes high speed steel, stainless tool steel, and tool steel for a variety of industries. The company operates a steel mill in upstate New York with units that manufacture its metal products, which are distributed by contract affiliates. The company makes high-performance steel with a special manufacturing process that produces stronger, lasting steel that enhances production of alloys.

Niagara Specialty Metals distributes Crucible Industries' flat and sheet metal products. Crucible Industries reach international customers through distributor Robert Zapp Werkstofftechnik.

Crucible Industries shipped its first order as a new company -- 18 tons of steel billets -- in late 2009 from its plant near Syracuse, New York. The company reopened that year after a bankruptcy proceeding with 200 workers, compared to the 730 on its payroll two years earlier.
