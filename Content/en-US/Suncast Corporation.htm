Suncast won't let disorganization cast a shadow over your home, garden, or garage. The company manufactures and markets outdoor storage products made from injection-molded and blow-molded plastics. Outdoor items include gardening stations, fencing, hose reels, rain barrels, sheds, deck boxes, and snow shovels. Suncast's home storage products include resin storage systems, slat wall systems, storage bins, and golf and ceiling storage. It also offers pet products (dog houses, pet carriers, storages, pet feeders, and cat care items). Through its HomePlace Collection unit, Suncast also sells Amish-crafted wooden playhouses, cabins, garden buildings, garages, gazebos, and pergolas.

The company sells its plastic products in the US and Canada though more than 25,000 retail locations. Retailers include Costco , Wal-Mart , Lowe's , Ace Hardware , Home Depot , and Meijer , among others. Its wooden structures are sold directly to consumers in the US.

The company's products span several categories, such as lawn and garden, outdoor storage, home storage, pet products, and snow tools.

Suncast boasts nearly 2 million sq. ft. of space in Batavia, Illinois. The company designs and manufactures more than 15 million products each year.

Suncast's portfolio of products is full of more than 1,000 items.

The company ensures collaborate between product design, marketing, manufacturing, and distribution -- and an efficient product-to-market delivery -- by being vertically integrated.

Suncast was established in 1984.

To diversify its all-plastic product line, the company in 2009 acquired Pennsylvania-based HomePlace Structures, which makes and markets Amish-made wooden buildings. As part of the purchase, HomePlace's corporate headquarters remained in New Holland while its production operations remained in the nearby Lancaster area.
