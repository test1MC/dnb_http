Billionaire Phil Anschutz wants to help Web readers clearly examine local issues. Through his Anschutz Company , the investor owns Clarity Digital Group, which does business as Examiner.com. The Web property oversees a group of local news sites in more than 240 cities in the US and Canada.  Examiner.com relies on more than 60,000  "citizen journalists" for its user-generated content. Topics include news and information, sports and recreation, entertainment, and health and beauty. The site was launched in 2008 with coverage of approximately 70 cities.
