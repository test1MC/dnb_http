Davol keeps operating rooms in business. The company makes medical instruments, implants, and supplies for a number of surgical specialties, including hernia repair, hemostasis, laparoscopy, hysteroscopy, wound management, and orthopedics. Its hernia repair patches, plugs, grafting tissue, and fixation devices are offered under the Bard and Davol brands, while its hemostasis products include the Ativene brand of hemostat products used to staunch bleeding during surgery. The company also makes suction and irrigation equipment for wound care and laparoscopy, and an autotransfusion unit used in orthopedic procedures. Davol is a subsidiary of medical products company C.R. Bard .
