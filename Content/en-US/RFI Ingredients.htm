It's all in the recipe. RFI Ingredients knows this. The company manufactures natural ingredients for the food and dietary supplement industries. RFI's ingredient brands include OxyPhyte (natural antioxidants), Chocamine (cocoa extract), and Talin (flavor modifier/sweetness enhancer). It also offers fruit and vegetable powders, purees and concentrates, cereal grasses and greens, natural colors, botanical and nutritional ingredients, and custom formulations. The company maintains manufacturing facilities in North America, South America, and Asia, some of which are Kosher Certified and certified organic. RFI Ingredients was founded in 1989 by CEO Jeff Wuagneux.
