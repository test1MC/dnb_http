GCM Medical & OEM has a healthy appetite for making medical equipment. GCM (which stands for global contract manufacturing) & OEM offers engineering, manufacturing, and assembly services for the biotechnology, communication, industrial, integrated optics, medical, security, and transportation industries. It manufactures imaging and oncology equipment for its customers in a 90,000 sq. ft. plant in California. Companies such as GE Healthcare , Gillig , Hewlett-Packard , and Varian Medical Systems rely on GCM to build specialized equipment. The company was formed through the merger of M & Ko Manufacturing and GCM Holdings LLC in 2001.
