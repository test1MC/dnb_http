This company's Cristal doesn't flow into a glass and you certainly don't want to drink it. The National Titanium Dioxide Company, which does business as Cristal, is the world's second-largest producer of titanium dioxide, TiO 2 . The highly reflective mineral is used in many applications, from paint and coatings to sunscreen, pharmaceuticals, and food coloring; four million tons of it are consumed worldwide annually. Cristal's eight plants in six countries also make carbon dioxide, gypsum, hydrochloric acid, sulfuric acid, and other specialty chemicals. The company was formed in 2007 from the combination of Cristal Arabia and Millennium Chemicals ' inorganic unit.
