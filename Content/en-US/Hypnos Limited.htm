Best known for making bedding for Queen Elizabeth II, Hypnos Limited is a leading UK luxury mattress designer and manufacturer. Its products are sold under the Regency, Royal Comfort, Orthos, and Heritage brands, through retailers across the UK and through specialty mattress dealers in the US, Canada, Asia, and Australia. Founded by George Henry Keen, the family-run company sells various commercial mattress lines and upholstery to hotels, as well as sofa beds, fold-away beds, and bedding accessories for the home. Hypnos also specializes in hand crafting made-to-measure luxury mattresses for yachts and super-yachts.

Hypnos is known for its made to order and made by hand mattresses, even zip and link varieties that provide more flexibility by combining mattresses or separating them.
