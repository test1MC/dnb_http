HERIGE (formerly VM Matériaux) knows that building is all about the materials. The group provides materials and advisory services for the commercial, public, and DIY construction markets, primarily in western France. Its services include distribution, technical advisory, logistics, and transportation. The company also makes building materials from approximately 40 manufacturing plants in operation. Its products include industrial pre-cast and ready-mix concrete, wooden doors, aluminum windows, shutters, fences, and gates. HERIGE also provides support services including technical advisory, trading, logistics, and transportation.
