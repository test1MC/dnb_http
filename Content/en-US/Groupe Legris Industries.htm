Legris Industries is a triple threat when it comes to industrial equipment and turnkey plant engineering. Its Savoye unit designs and builds logistics systems for warehousing and distribution centers, and operates throughout Europe. The Keller unit designs systems to help build factories that make products for the clay building industry. The third unit, Clextral, designs and manufactures automated production lines from start to finish. Legris is present in over 75 countries with 40 sales subsidiaries in Africa, Asia, Europe, Oceania, and the US.

Legris' operations are divided into three main units: Savoye (51% of net sales), Keller (26%), and Clextral (23%).

The company was established in 1848 as a small valve factory in France.
