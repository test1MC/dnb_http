There's nothing foolish about hiring this firm to take care of your custom publishing needs. Wise Group Communications provides print and electronic custom communication services for corporate clients. Projects include newsletters, directories, brochures, annual reports, and magazines. In addition, the company provides custom digital solutions and event marketing services. Wise Group has worked on projects for clients such as the Lancaster, Pennsylvania Chamber of Commerce, the Cleveland Music School Settlement, and the Greater Cleveland Sports Commission.
