When the heat is on, companies look to Diversified Thermal Solutions to protect their heat-generating equipment. The company manufactures refractory products, such as clay and brick, for the linings of high-temperature furnaces and reactors. Diversified Thermal Solutions is expanding in the Southeast and Northeast of the US, primarily targeting non-ferrous industries such as aluminum, incineration, paper, and petrochemical operations. Prior to the refractory business, the company was involved in providing international long distance communications service. President and CEO B. Grant Hunter controls more than a third of Diversified Thermal Solutions.
