Viceroy Homes isn't Canada's seat of government, but it does provide regal housing. The company designs and manufactures modular, panelized, pre-engineered custom home packages for both owner/builder clients and professional contractors. Its packages, which include all materials needed to construct a home (such as doors, windows, and flooring), are sold throughout North America and also shipped globally. Viceroy constructs what is called a "healthy house" (built with products free of formaldehyde and other solvents). Founded in the mid-1950s, the company operates manufacturing plants in Ontario and British Columbia. Canada Wood Frame Solutions bought the company from Open Investments (OPIN) of Russia in 2013.
