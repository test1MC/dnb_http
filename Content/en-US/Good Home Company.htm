The Good Home Company develops and markets natural fragrances and cleaning products for the body and home. The company's products are sold nationally through major chain stores, specialty stores, and online. Good Home's products are made using essential oils and natural ingredients. Chain stores selling Good Home products have included Nordstrom , Anthropologie , and RH . CEO Christine Dimmick founded the company in her New York City home kitchen in 1995.
