This bubbly isn't confined to a champagne glass and it's not a term used to describe someone's demeanor. For Sundance Spas customers, their bubbly paves the road to rest and relaxation. As the world's largest manufacturer of acrylic spas, Sundance Spas manufactures and distributes spas (portable, in-ground), wood enclosures, and spa accessories. Spas also are used for hydrotherapy and aromatherapy. Founded in 1979, Sundance Spas' products are sold in about 750 retail locations in more than 60 countries. Its brand names include Maxxus, Optima, Cameo, Camden, Hamilton, Majesta, Altamar, and Solo. Jacuzzi Brands , which was sold to Apollo Management in 2007, bought Sundance Spas in 1998.
