Cereal Byproducts buys and sells raw ingredients derived from grains, grain byproducts, meat (beef, pork, and lamb), poultry, fish, peanuts, citrus, cotton, canola, beets, soybeans, malt, milk, and many other commodities. It trades in more than 300 different feed and food products, both domestically and internationally. The company's customers include animal-feed companies and food manufacturers. It also offers the Earth Friendly brand of corn-gluten meal for use as a fertilizer and weed-control substance to the lawn and garden product sector. The company was founded in 1917.

Working closely together Cereal Byproducts, Alexander Company and Cerco Ingredients handle different facets of the business. Cereal Byproducts handles procurement and marketing while Alexander takes care of milling. CerCo Ingredients supplies ingredients for use in food, sports nutrition, and pharmaceutical applications.

Cereal Byproducts provides its products in a variety of sizes through grinding and screening or as pellets. Products can be packaged in bulk, cardboard boxes, fiber or metal drums; frozen nude blocks; 5-, 10-, 25-, 50- or 100- pound paper bags; tote bags or boxes. They can be shipped by convertible, dump, hopper, pneumatic, refrigerated, tank, van or walking-floor truck; by box, hopper, air-slide hopper or tank railroad car; by barge or by vessel.

Cereal Byproducts is headquartered in suburban Chicago and it has seven offices, serving feed customers across North America.
