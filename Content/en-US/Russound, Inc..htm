Russound wants to surround you with its best sounds. The company makes and markets a variety of audio accessories and equipment, such as speaker selectors, volume controls, loudspeakers, and multichannel amplifiers, as well as source equipment and wall plate items. Russsound boasts more than 850 stock keeping units (SKUs) and ships to some 48 countries. The company also is a top manufacturer of multiroom systems -- including audiovisual equipment, intercoms, keypads, and touch screens -- used in residential and commercial settings. Russounds, which was founded in 1967, sells its products through a network of resellers and distributors worldwide.
