Honeyville Food Products mills a variety of grain products. The company's product include wheat, corn, and rice flour; whole, cracked, and mixed grains; whole grain and rolled oats and oatmeal; and textured soy protein. It makes consumer and commercial baking mixes; its non-grain products include freeze-dried fruits and nuts, edible seeds, and hot cocoa and smoothie mixes. Honeyville offers formulation, blending, packaging, warehouse, and logistic services. In addition to selling its products to retail food, foodservice, and food manufacturing firms, Honeyville operates retail stores Arizona, California, and Utah.

The company has served clients such as Costco , ConAgra , Domino's Pizza , Frito-Lay , and Del Monte .

Honeyville was founded in 1951 by Lowell Sherratt and has remained a family-owned business.
