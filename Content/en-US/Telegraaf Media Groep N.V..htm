This company gets the word out in print and over the air. Telegraaf Media Groep (TMG) is a leading Dutch media company that publishes the daily newspaper De Telegraaf along with popular free daily Sp!ts and a portfolio of regional free door-to-door papers. The company also publishes several general interest magazines, as well as specialty titles including puzzle magazines published through Keesing Group. Its broadcasting operations include Sky Radio (85%), a leading commercial station serving the Netherlands. TMG also operates Web sites for most of its publications. In addition to media activities, the company provides commercial printing and distribution services.
