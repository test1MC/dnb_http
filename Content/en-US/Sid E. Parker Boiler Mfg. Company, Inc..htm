Steam, the powerful force that drove the engines of capitalism during the heyday of the Industrial Revolution, still drives revenues at Sid E. Parker Boiler Manufacturing Company. The company, which does business as Parker Boiler Company, manufactures steam and hot water boilers, thermal liquid and indirect water heaters, low NOx (nitrogen oxide) burners, water softeners, and blowoff and storage tanks. It also makes hot water storage tanks and heat exchangers, feed systems, heat reclaimers, and feedwater deaerators. Parker Boiler's accessories include automatic blowdown systems, boiler chemicals, lead lag controllers, pumps, and vent materials. The company was founded in 1946 by Sid. E. Parker.
