Monster machines call for a Titan underbelly. Once part of US-based Titan International , Titan Europe manufactures and distributes steel wheels, brakes, and undercarriage frames and parts to support heavy-duty crawlers. Titan Europe's wheels are used in such equipment as tracked and wheeled off-road vehicles, tractors and combine harvesters, graders and cranes, materials handling and earthmoving loaders, and mining industry dump trucks. Its undercarriage division targets the same OEMs and aftermarkets with forged devices. Customers include Caterpillar , Agco , CNH , and Hitachi . Retaining about a 22% share in Titan Europe, Titan International has made an offer for the shares in the company it doesn't already own.
