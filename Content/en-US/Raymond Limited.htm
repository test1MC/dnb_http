Raymond Limited takes textiles every step -- from its beginning, on the back of the lamb standing in its pen, to its end, on the back of the man standing in the retail store. Raymond Limited manufactures textiles (from raw materials) and apparel in India. The company also operates more than 650 retail stores under The Raymond Shop banner in the Middle East, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, and Nepal. Founded in 1925, Raymond Limited comprises three divisions: the textile unit, the engineering unit (which makes steel files and rasps), and the aviation unit (a small fleet for hire). Subsidiaries Celebrations Apparel, ColorPlus Fashions, Raymond Apparel, and Silver Spark all produce men's, women's, and children's apparel.
