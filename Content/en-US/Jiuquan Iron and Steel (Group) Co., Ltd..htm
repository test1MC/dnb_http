Jiuquan Iron and Steel Co. (JISCO) makes products that clang. Located in the Gansu province of northwest China, JISCO's iron and steel products include wires and rods, rolled steel plates, and bars. The company's products are used in construction, welding, shipbuilding, automobile manufacturing, brewing, and transportation. Jiuquan Iron and Steel, which operates three manufacturing sites, produces about 8 million tons of crude and rolled steel annually. The company's steel-based primary activities include mining, beneficiation, sintering, iron making, steel making, and steel rolling; and non-steel sector activities.

The company is headquartered in Jiayuguan, China and has branches in China, South Africa, the Philippines, and Mongolia.

JISCO was founded in 1958.
