Chromcraft Revington has built a business by honing the craft of making home or office furniture. Under the Peters-Revington name, the company sells mid-priced wood furniture, such as bookcases, tables, and home office pieces. The furniture company's casual dining and commercial furniture (such as office chairs and conference tables) are made and marketed under its namesake Chromcraft brand. Its Cochrane Furniture line includes custom casual and semi-custom dining furniture. The company sell through retailers in the US and Canada. Chromcraft Revington, by way of its parent Sport-Haley Holdings, filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection in early 2015. In mid-2015, it was converted to a Chapter 7 liquidation.
