All Trail King Industries' friends know the low rider. The company makes lowbed and flatbed trailers, skid loaders, and dump bodies. A line of pneumatic dry bulk trailers also ramps up its stable of products. With trailers capable of loads from six thousand to 1 million pounds, Trail King captures the market for hauling jobs in construction, agriculture, transportation, and waste and recycling. The company also offers a full line of parts and accessories. In October 2010 Trail King was acquired by private equity firm CC Industries ( Great Dane Trailers is a unit of CC), which is controlled by investment group Henry Crown & Co. Trail King will continue as a stand-alone entity.
