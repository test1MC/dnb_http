Thrane & Thrane makes Inmarsat terminals and land earth stations for global mobile satellite communications and radio communications. It provides equipment for land-based, maritime, and aeronautical use. Its communication products are sold worldwide by distributors and partners and as OEM products under the brands Thrane & Thrane, SAILOR, and SKANTI. The company is focusing on portable high-speed terminals with the development of its broadband global area network (BGAN) terminal, the Explorer 500. The compact terminal can be used in areas having less-developed fixed and mobile networks. In 1981 president Lars Thrane and his brother Pers founded the company, which has expanded rapidly through acquisitions.
