Elma Electronic thinks your electronics equipment should be contained, not your enthusiasm. The US subsidiary of Elma Electronic AG manufactures and distributes an array of electronic enclosures, backplanes, and server racks. It also makes passive electronic components, from rotary switches to knobs and light-emitting diodes. Elma Electronic's slate of services includes component customization, design, engineering, manufacture, systems integration, and verification. Subsidiary Elma Bustronic offers custom backplane applications, and Optima EPS makes electronic enclosures. The company courts industries worldwide in telecommunications, medical electronics, industrial control, defense, and aerospace.

Based in the San Francisco Bay area, Elma Electronic has numerous sales, engineering, and manufacturing facilities in California, with sales and engineering offices on the opposite coast in Atlanta, Boston, and Washington, DC.

The company relies on making products that are superior in quality, reliability, performance, and consistently with innovative designs. Elma's product line encompasses more than 20,000 parts, including enclosures, cabinets, high quality switches, LED arrays, and knobs.

In 2012 Elma Electronic expanded Optima EPS by acquiring the broadcast cabinet (Stantron) and datacenter cabinet (APW Mayville) operations of Wisconsin-based Mayville Products Corp. The added brands bring an improved portfolio of enclosures for datacenter assets and broadcast audio, video, and related equipment.
