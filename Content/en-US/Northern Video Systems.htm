It is the job of Northern Video Systems (or rather, its products) to watch your back. The company is a distributor of security, surveillance, and Internet protocol video equipment. Its products include video surveillance systems, license plate cameras, and DVRs (digital video recorders) that can be wired to cash registers. The company also provides system design and integration services. Northern Video products are sold through dealers and online catalogs, and it has distribution facilities in Las Vegas; Memphis; and Cranbury, New Jersey. The company was founded in 1986.
