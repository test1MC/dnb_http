NextIO started out as a company designing semiconductors for large computer servers and made the transition to a systems developer, creating the Adaptive Connect platform for I/O virtualization. It provides hardware making use of the PCI Express industry standard to speed up data communications, and software to configure and monitor Adaptive Connect. The company's executives previously worked at AMD , Broadcom , Compaq Computer , and NetEffect . NextIO was founded in 2003. Adams Capital Management , Crescendo Ventures, Dell , and VentureTech Alliance (the venture capital arm of Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing ) have invested in NextIO. The company has raised about $55 million in private equity funding.
