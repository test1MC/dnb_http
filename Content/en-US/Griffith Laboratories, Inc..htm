A little pinch here, a little pinch there, pretty soon you have a business. Founded in 1919, Griffith Laboratories is a manufacturer of food ingredients with operations and customers worldwide. The company's clients include food manufacturers; food service operators, including restaurants, hotels, and cruise lines; and food retailers and wholesalers. Its products include seasonings, sauce and soup mixes, condiments, texturizers, and bakery blends. Griffith's subsidiaries include Custom Culinary (food bases and mixes) and Innova (meat and savory flavors). The company also offers customized ingredient services. Griffith Laboratories is family owned and run by third- and fourth-generation family members.

Griffith has global operations in the US and Canada, Belgium, Brazil, China, Colombia, Costa Rica, Japan, India, Mexico, Spain, Thailand, and the UK. Its US plants are in Alsip, Illinois, and Lithonia, Georgia.

Griffith's brands include Innova, Robust, Savor Notes, and Vegamine. The company also makes sauces and soup mixes under brands, such as Chef's Own, Master Touch, and Whisk & Serve. Griffith also operates consumerquiz.com, a website that offers consumer and market research assistance, analysis, and reporting for food and beverage manufacturers.

The company's geographic diversity is an asset in discovering and developing new flavors and keeping abreast of trends in the food industry.
