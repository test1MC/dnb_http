Hom/Ade's got the next-best thing to homemade dough. Hom/Ade Foods provides frozen bread dough manufacturing and distribution services. The company's Mary B's-branded and private-label products include frozen dinner rolls, buttermilk biscuits, corn flour biscuits, and tea biscuits that come in 10- to 24-count packages. Hom/Ade also offers frozen dumplings, chicken and dumplings dinners, and pecan sweet rolls. The dough maker distributes its products to grocery retailers. Founded by Howard Burris in the 1980s, the Florida company was acquired by J & J Snack Foods in 2007.
