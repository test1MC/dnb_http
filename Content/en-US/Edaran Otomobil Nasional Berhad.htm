Edaran Otomobil Nasional Berhad (EON) sells passenger cars, so it's no surprise that the company knows how to shift gears. EON's core business is marketing cars and spare parts. It operates business through five wholly owned subsidiaries. Euromobil distributes primarily Proton and other luxury cars, and is the retail arm for Audi . EON Auto Mart markets Mitsubishi vehicles and operates a network of 21 showrooms in Malaysia. Automotive Conversion Engineering (ACE) converts and modifies Proton vehicles into executive models and limousines. EON Properties invests in and manages properties, while EONMobil markets non-Proton motor vehicles.
