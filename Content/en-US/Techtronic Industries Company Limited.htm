Techtronic Industries (aka TTI) makes not only the tools for a home-improvement project but also the vacuum cleaners to tidy up afterward. The company manufacturers and markets Ryobi cordless power tools and Ryobi and Homelite outdoor power equipment. Its US subsidiary, OWT Industries in South Carolina, makes Rigid -brand tools that are sold exclusively at Home Depot . Through its Royal Appliance Mfg. unit it makes such floor-care appliances as Regina, Royal, Dirt Devil, and Vax. The Hoover and Oreck vacuum brands are also part of its business. TTI owns the former Atlas Copco electric tools business, including Milwaukee Electric Tool and Atlas Copco Electric Tools.
