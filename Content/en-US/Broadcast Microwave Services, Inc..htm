Broadcast Microwave Services (BMS) has its eye on you. The company develops microwave data links for transmission of video, audio, and telemetry. It makes wireless video equipment used for land, sea, and air broadcasting, and airborne surveillance systems for the military and law enforcement agencies. Its video cameras can be affixed to aircraft, maritime, and ground defense vehicles to record information and transmit images to command centers. The company's equipment employs coded orthogonal frequency division multiplexing (COFDM) technology, a digital signal scheme that helps eliminate "ghost" images in broadcasts. BMS was established in 1982 and acquired by Cohu in 1984.
