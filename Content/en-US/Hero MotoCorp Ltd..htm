Hero MotoCorp is putting forth an epic performance in the manufacture of two-wheeled motor vehicles. Serving the Indian market, Hero MotoCorp sells more than 3 million bikes annually with offerings ranging from fuel-efficient scooters to powerful motorcycles. The company, considered the largest two-wheeler manufacturing company in the world, exports to Africa, Asia, Eastern Europe, and Latin America. Hero MotoCorp sells about 6 million units each year and started as a joint venture between Hero Group (run by the Munjal family) and Honda Motors .

Hero sells its two-wheelers in as many as 18 countries including Peru and Ecuador in South America; Guatemala, Honduras, El Salvador in Central America; Kenya, Mozambique, Tanzania and Uganda in East Africa; Burkina Faso, Ivory Coast, Congo and Angola in West Africa; and most Turkey and Egypt.

The company has international assembly units in Kenya, Tanzania, and Uganda in East Africa through its distributors. Its manufacturing facilities reside in Gurgaon and Dharuhera in northern India; and Haridwar, in the state of Uttrakhand.

Hero serves 6,000 customers and sells its products through dealers, distributors, and service and spare parts outlets worldwide. It uses print, television, digital, and social media platforms for advertising.

The company's revenues increased 1% in 2013 primarily to an increase in its customer base and geographic expansion. Its net income dipped 11% in 2013, however, due to a spike in costs, especially depreciation and amortization expenses.

Hero's key strategies are to build a robust product portfolio across categories, explore growth opportunities globally, continuously improve its operational efficiency, aggressively expand its reach to customers, continue to invest in brand building activities, and utilize joint ventures, partnerships, and acquisitions. Throughout 2014 the company launched operations in Turkey and established a joint venture in Bangladesh. In 2013 it entered the African market through the launching of an assembly unit in Kenya.
