Hydratight wants to help its customers keep their equipment tightly connected. The company designs and manufactures mechanical pipeline connectors, hydraulic bolt tightening tools, and lifting equipment used by oil and gas, industrial, aerospace, and construction customers. Typical offerings include hydraulic bolt tensioners and torque wrenches, bolt load monitoring systems, nutsplitters, flange spreaders and pullers, and pumps. In addition to its products, the company provides on-site machining, training, and engineering services. Hydratight is key a subsidiary of Actuant Corporation , a diversified industrial company.
