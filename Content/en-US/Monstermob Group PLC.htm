Monstermob Group wants a stronghold on mobile content. The company develops and delivers mobile phone content, such as video-on-demand, interactive communities, music ring tones, high-resolution customizeable graphics, and games. Monstermob Group's products are aimed primarily at mobile phone users aged 14 to 30, and the company delivers content and services to customers in more than 12 countries across the globe. Zed Worldwide, a Spanish mobile content distributor, owns Monstermob Group. The company was founded in 2000 by former CEO Martin Higginson.

The company's Network Services division focuses on selling Monstermob Group products and services through the mobile entertainment portals of other mobile network operators. Its original direct-to-consumer operations of the company comprise its Consumer Services division.

Monstermob has used acquisitions to grow internationally. The company plans for continued international growth efforts in emerging markets.
