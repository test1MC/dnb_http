If your job involves digging, Kobelco Construction Machinery America can help you get to the bottom of things. The company manufactures heavy duty excavators and excavator-bulldozer combos. Products include compact, short radius, standard, long-reach, and full-sized units. Kobelco Construction Machinery's products are available through distributors and dealers to construction customers in the US market. Kobelco Construction Machinery America is a subsidiary of Kobelco Construction Machinery USA, which is itself a division of Kobe Steel (also known as Kobelco).

The global construction machinery division of Kobe, which accounts for about 15% of the parent's annual revenue, has operations in the Americas, Africa, Asia, Europe, and the Middle East.

Kobelco Construction Machinery serves customers in the aggregate, construction, landscape, material handling, transportation, rental, and site preparation markets, among others. Its products are sold through a network of equipment dealers and distributors.

Following its 2012 split-up with former partner CNH Global , Kobelco Construction Machinery is focused on promoting independent Kobelco-branded products in the US market. As part of a program to re-establish its own sales and service network in North America, in 2013 the company expanded its relationship with Scott Equipment Company, a distributor in the southeastern US. It is working to establish a network of dealers across the US. (Marketing functions were previously controlled by CNH in North America and other markets.)

Applying its technical machine muscle to sustainability issues, Kobelco Construction Machinery America conducts product research and development efforts to expand its product line with a focus on fuel efficiency and pollution reduction. Kobelco has also invested in expanding its production base, enhancing training programs, and improving online sales portals. The company partners with other equipment manufacturers to enhance or promote its product offerings.
