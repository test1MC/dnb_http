Nippon Metal Industry (also know as NTK) rolls out stainless steel and heat-resistant steel products for the automobile, construction, housing, and industrial machinery industries. The company's products include hot-rolled steel plates; cold-rolled medium-thick plates, sheets, and strips; and special products such as precision strip and foil. NTK's secondary processed products include stainless steel tubular products as well as roofing and membrane sheets for liquefied natural gas storage tanks. NTK was established in 1932 and operates through a handful of associated companies in Japan, Malaysia, and Singapore. In 2012 it merged with Nisshin Steel to become a subsidiary of Nisshin Steel Holdings Co., Ltd.
