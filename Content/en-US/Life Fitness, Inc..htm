Life Fitness thinks life is so much better when you're fit. The company manufactures fitness equipment for the consumer and commercial markets under the Life Fitness, Life Fitness Sport, and Hammer Strength names. The fitness firm's products include strength systems, stationary exercise bikes, treadmills, cross-trainers, and stair-climbers. Founded in 1977 as Lifecycle, the company distributes its products through a network of dealers in more than 120 countries. Leading recreational products maker Brunswick Corporation has owned Life Fitness since 1997.

Life Fitness remains one of the world's largest makers of consumer and commercial fitness equipment. It has a presence in Brazil, Germany, Hong Kong, Japan, the Netherlands, Spain, and the UK.

The company manufactures and sells its strength and cardiovascular equipment, productive well-being equipment, billiards tables and accessories, and game room furniture under the following brand names: Life Fitness, Hammer Strength, Cybex, SCIFIT, InMovement, and Brunswick Billiards.

Life Fitness is a part of Fitness Segment of parent company Brunswick Corporation.

Life Fitness sells its products via its distributors, a direct sales force, and US-based dealers. Its customers include health clubs, corporations, schools and universities, hotels, professional sports teams, retirement and assisted living facilities and the military and governmental agencies. Consumer products are available at specialty retailers, select mass merchants, and sporting goods stores, as well as through international distributors and the Life Fitness website.

The Fitness Segment of the parent company accounted for 18% of total revenues in 2015.

The company's strategy is expansion. It has built out its Ramsey facility and acquired a plant in Owatonna (both in Minnesota).

Life Fitness has also joined with Brunswick Billiards, a manufacturer of fitness products, to expand its access to Brunswick Corporation's direct salesforce and global distribution channels.

The addition of SCIFIT (provider of fitness products) in 2014 provided Life Fitness with an expanded product line-up and increased access to the growing active-aging market.
