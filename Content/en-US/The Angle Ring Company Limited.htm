The Angle Ring Company knows all the angles when it comes to fabricating metal. The company shapes and bends large steel and aluminum beams and tubes for use in the construction of structural frameworks. The company also has the capability to bend angle rings and segments using its large bending machines and to bend rolled steel channels, parallel flange channels, tapered flanges, and European parallel flanges. Other services include plate rolling, press braking, and welding. End uses for the company's fabricated structural materials include bridges, offshore construction projects, and sports arenas.

Angle Ring offers a comprehensive range of fabrication services, including section bending, press braking, plate rolling, heavy plate forming, tube manipulation, pipe forming and spiral and 3-D bending.

The company delivers products through its own fleet of vehicles, along with commercial haulers.

Angle Ring focuses on selling to several key markets. One of its most important markets remains the construction sector, where it makes and sells roof structures, circular frames, architectural metalwork, and other parts for structural building projects. Angle Ring targets the marine and offshore markets, supplying cylinders and pipes, and the power generation sector, where it supplies rolled sections and cylinders for offshore wind towers and wave and tidal energy projects. The company also has expertise in the press braking of heavy section armor plate, used to cover military combat vehicles and blast armor.

In 2015 the company installed two new sets of plate rolls, increasing its rolling capacity to a whole range of markets.

Angle Ring was founded in 1951.
