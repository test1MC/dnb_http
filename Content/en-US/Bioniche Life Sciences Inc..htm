Telesta Therapeutics (formerly Bioniche Life Sciences) is seeking its niche in the development of drugs to treat urology, oncology, and immune system disorders. Its main focus is on novel medicines that address major unmet medical needs, particularly in oncology. Its former anchor product, MCNA, completed a Phase III study in patients with non-muscle invasive bladder cancer; in 2016 the firm terminated research of MCNA. Formerly involved in the additional fields of animal health and food safety, Bioniche Life Sciences reorganized and rebranded itself as Telesta (with a sole focus on drug development for humans) in 2014. Canadian biotech ProMetic Life Sciences is now buying Telesta.
