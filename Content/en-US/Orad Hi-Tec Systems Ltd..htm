Orad Hi-Tec Systems supplies the television industry with broadcasting equipment, software, and services. Orad's product line includes virtual studios, on-air graphics systems, channel branding, virtual advertising, sports production solutions and hardware for the virtual reality industry. The company has experienced significant growth from the rise of HDTV technology, as more clients seek to upgrade their equipment. Customers include one of Europe's largest pay-TV operators CANAL+ Groupe . Orad, founded in 1993, is part of the Ormat Group of companies.
