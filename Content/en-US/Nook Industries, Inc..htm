Nook has the corner on manufacturing engineered motion-system parts. It covers robotic grippers to guided linear and rotational components that are electrically and pneumatically actuated. Nook takes on extreme-duty environments such as auto, chemical, communications, electronics, food and beverage, medical/diagnostics, and packaging and paper. Its line of explosion-proof modular guided actuators simplifies and improves the machine design process as well as prevents fires from sparking in caustic areas. For workplaces subject to dripping, spraying, or submersion in water (as in food processing), Nook protects power and data cabling with its stainless steel cable tray.
