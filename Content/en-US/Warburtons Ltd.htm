Warburtons is proof that a family business can rise nicely by loafing around. With roots that date back to the late nineteenth century, Warburtons is one of the the largest independent bread bakeries in the UK. The company bakes up breads, crumpets (English muffins in the US), rolls, fruit loafs, and teacakes day at its 13 bakeries. Its products are distributed in the UK under the Warburtons brand name. The company has grown through careful expansion, choosing to build new factories throughout the years, rather than acquire smaller businesses. The fifth generation of the founding Warburton family owns and operates the company.
