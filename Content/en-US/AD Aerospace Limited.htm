AD Aerospace has its eyes in the sky. The company makes video equipment for such aerospace titans as Boeing , Emirates , and British Airways . Its video products are designed for aircraft safety and security -- inside and out. Its FlightVu products use up to eight cameras around the outside of an aircraft, allowing all sides to be viewed simultaneously. The company's CabinVu line CCTV (closed-circuit television) allows the flight crew to monitor anyone attempting to enter the cockpit, as well as passengers, and the cargo hold. Video images can also be fed to each seat, giving passengers their own "window" seat. The company is part of UK-based Anglo Design Holdings .
