International Computer Appliances Corporation (which does business as ICA Corporation) is a contract manufacturer that makes custom metal component parts for analytical instrumentation, aerospace, medical equipment, and other products. The company provides sheet metal fabrication and laser cutting, precision machining, custom enclosures, and chassis assemblies. ICA Corporation also offers value-added services, from assembling medical diagnostic devices with multi-language software to packaging custom kits and inventory management. Its manufacturing facility takes up 60,000 sq. ft. in the northwest suburbs of Minneapolis.

The company is engaged in Sheet Metal Fabrication/Laser Cutting; Precision Machining; Mechanical/Electro-Mechanical Assemblies; Custom Enclosures; and Rapid Prototyping with 3D Printing.

ICA Corporation provides its products to some of the leading US-based OEMs in the medical, laboratory/analytical instrumentation, aerospace, defense, and electronics industries.

The employee-owned company was founded in 1970.
