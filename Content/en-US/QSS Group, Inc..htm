QSS Group keeps the government operating on the ground and in the air. QSS was founded in 1994 as an information technology contractor for NASA. Now the company provides services to a number of US government agencies. QSS researchers design, build, and test spacecraft and aviation systems. They also conduct studies in related fields, such as meteorology and astrophysics. The company's information technology sector offerings include database design, legacy migrations, and network integration and management. Clients have included the Executive Office of the President, the Department of Energy, and the General Services Administration. Early in 2007 Perot Systems bought QSS.
