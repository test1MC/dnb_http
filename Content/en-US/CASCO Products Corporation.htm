CASCO Products sees a future electric with promise. The company manufactures electro-mechanic and electronic equipment for the auto industry. CASCO's lineup comprises electronic sensors, for everything from climate control to management of fluid levels, lighting, and air quality. Its electro-mechanic offerings include cigar-lighters, LED interior lighting, and USB multimedia accessories. The company also provides research, design, engineering, and contract manufacturing. CASCO operates nine facilities and four development centers to serve auto OEMs and aftermarket demand worldwide. BMW , Toyota , and Volkswagen are among its roster of customers. CASCO and sister unit ARC Automotive are owned by The Jordan Company .

The company has facilities in the US and Canada (Bridgeport, CT; Morgantown, KY; Novi, MI; and Montreal), as well as Brazil, China, Germany, and Tunisia.

CASCO's products include sensors, connectivity aftermarket devices, DC/AC inverters, DC/AC converters, and electro-mechanical devices.

The company offers its products to a variety of automotive power distribution applications, including advanced start-stop and battery management systems. It provides a power source for 12V electrical accessories, including air pumps, cell phones, coolers, spotlights, and vacuum cleaners. CASCO's products can also be used in cars, boats, motorcycles, motor homes, snowmobiles, tractors, and truck bed liners.

The company sells through retailers across the US.

CASCO was founded in 1921.
