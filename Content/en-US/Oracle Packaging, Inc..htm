The prophecy foretold of a company that would make individually wrapped products easy to open! Oracle Packaging is a manufacturer and supplier of flexible and specialty paperboard packaging for the food and health care industries. It makes the aluminum foil lids for Mott's applesauce and Yoplait yogurt, the gum wrappers for Wrigley , and the orange juice cartons for Tropicana . After Oracle Packaging acquired TOLAS Healthcare Packaging in 2006, it started making laminated and foil pouches for pharmaceuticals and sterile medical devices. Oracle Packaging traces its roots back to 1919. Its current incarnation started in 2000 when attorney J. Scott Dickman bought it from Champion International .
