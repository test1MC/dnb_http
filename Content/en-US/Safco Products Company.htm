Safco Products Company does its best to bring some of the comforts of home to the office. The company is a distributor of organization products for the office, as well as pieces to make work feel as comfortable as home. Its 2,200-plus items include back rests and seat cushions, drafting tables, desks and chairs, filing and storage systems, footrests, shelving, and garbage cans. Safco distributes its products through dealers, national retailers (such as Office Depot , OfficeMax , and Staples ), and wholesalers ( S.P. Richards and Essendant ), as well as through a catalog and the Internet. A subsidiary of privately held Liberty Diversified Industries , Safco was founded in 1967.
