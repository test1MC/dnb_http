At Nielsen-Massey Vanillas its not a little brown jug, but little brown bottles, lots of 'em. The company produces vanilla extracts, powders, and bean pastes, as well as whole vanilla beans. It also offers other liquid extracts, including coffee, chocolate, almond, orange, and lemon. With US headquarters in Waukegan, Illinois, and an international office in The Netherlands, Nielsen-Massey supplies organic and conventional vanilla and other flavoring products to customers Africa, Europe, the Middle East, the Pacific Rim, and Central, North, and South America. Privately owned and operated, Nielsen-Massey was established in 1907 as a provider of aromas for the cleaning industry.

Nielsen-Massey Vanillas operates a production facility in Leeuwarden, the Netherlands.

The companies newer products include Mexican vanilla and alcohol-free vanilla.

Nielsen-Massey Vanillas products are used by makers of ice creams and novelties, and restaurants and bakeries round the world.
