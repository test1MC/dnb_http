AVVAA World Health Care Products manufacturers and sells skin care products to treat such conditions as acne, eczema, psoriasis, and other skin disorders and conditions afflicting people and animals. Its products for humans are sold under the NEUROSKIN brand and are sold in drug stores, mass merchandisers, and other retail outlets. AVVAA World Health Care is expanding its offerings to include more therapies for companion animals and horses. Such products include Dermalustre, a grooming aid; Derma-ear for cleaning animals' ears; and Bite Buster, an anti-inflammatory for mosquito bites. Big "D" Products distributes AVVAA's equine care products in Canada.
