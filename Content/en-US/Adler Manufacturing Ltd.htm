Doing business as Adler Global, Adler Manufacturing's specialty is high-end personalized and promotional products, including writing instruments, stationary, key rings, and pocketknives, among myriad items. It also offers diaries and calendars, eco-friendly and solar products, electronic products comprising chargers, adapters and accessories, phone cases and holders, USB flash drives, and media and accessories in addition to food and drink related products, such as sports bottles and tumblers, coffee mugs, glassware, and insulated bags. The company has opened an additional office in Cardiff, Wales, to serve as its sales operations headquarters. It was established in 1989.
