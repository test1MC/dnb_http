Ratti is always chic. Founded in 1945, the company specializes in making silk, cashmere, linen, wool, and cotton fabrics. Ratti produces most of its fabrics for women's apparel and accessories such as ties and scarves, but the company also makes fabrics for home furnishings. The company operates a few mills and distributes products worldwide, with offices in Italy, France, and New York. Ratti also participates in cultural textile exhibitions and publishes art books on ancient textiles. In August 2009 Marzotto approved the acquisition of a two-thirds stake in Ratti.
