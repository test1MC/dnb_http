Paul Mueller Company is in the "moo-d" for more than milk. The company manufactures stainless-steel industrial storage tanks and processing equipment and is a world-leading producer of dairy farm equipment, such as pasteurizers. Paul Mueller's industrial equipment unit makes processing equipment for brewery, food, chemical, and pharmaceutical applications. Mueller Field Operations, the company's field fabrication subsidiary, builds storage tanks that are too large to be manufactured and shipped from the factory. Its Mueller Transportation unit delivers the company's products and components. Operating in more than 100 countries, Paul Mueller makes about 45% of its sales in international markets.

Paul Mueller's manufacturing and sales operations are located in Osceola, Iowa; the Netherlands; and Vietnam. The US accounts for 55% of net sales; the Netherlands, 22%.

Sales are made by the company to independent dealers for resale. It sells through its sales force and dealers and serves the food, beverage, pharmaceutical, and chemical sectors worldwide.

Paul Mueller founded the Paul Mueller Company in 1940.
