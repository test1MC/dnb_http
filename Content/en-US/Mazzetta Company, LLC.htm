Mazzetta Company thrives on the knowledge that two-thirds of the Earth is water. Run by its founder and seafood industry veteran, Thomas Mazzetta, the company is a leading frozen seafood importer and distributor, serving the US and Canada. Much of its fish and farmed shrimp come from the seas around Africa, Australia, Brazil, China, the Netherlands, and New Zealand. It's primary products are shrimp, fish, lobster, and crab. Mazzetta distributes its seafood products to discount stores, warehouse clubs, and other retailers, including various supermarket chains, under the SeaMazz banner. It also serves foodservice establishments, such as restaurants.
