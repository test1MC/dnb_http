Stevens Graphics is a printer of catalogues and directories that produces 60 billion printed pages a year. The company churns out some 56 million directories and 550 titles. Its products have included telephone directories, health and medical guides, and home-improvement guides. Stevens Graphics also offers mailing services, ink-jet labeling, and automated job data collection and billing. Stevens Graphics is a unit of communications giant BellSouth . The company, which was started by H.G. Hastings in the late 1880s to publish the Hastings Seed Catalog, has locations in Atlanta and Birmingham, Alabama.
