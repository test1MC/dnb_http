Pochet's message in a bottle is scented. Founded in 1623, the company makes and distributes luxury glass perfume bottles and containers for cosmetics and perfume manufacturers. It caters to such top industry houses as Jean-Paul Gaultier, Christian Dior , and Calvin Klein . As many as 10 million bottles in a year have been made for perfume CK One! All perfume bottles are made at Pochet's sole factory in Guimerville, and finished at one of three sites. Bottles account for more than half of sales, and decorative glass containers for cosmetics, the remainder. In 2008 Pochet sold Lalique Group, a producer and distributor of luxury goods, to Art & Fragrance. Founders of Pochet, the Colonna family, own the company.
