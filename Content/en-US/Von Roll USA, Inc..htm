Sheep and geese aren't the only source of insulation. Von Roll USA, a subsidiary of Swiss Von Roll AG , manufactures insulating materials used in high-, medium-, and low-voltage rotating machines -- such as power generators, distribution and transmission equipment, and electric motors. Its manufacturing capabilities include coated fabrics and conductive coatings, and flexible materials lamination and slitting. Von Roll also offers testing services and tank maintenance programs, as well as field application and engineering services. It operates from four manufacturing sites in the US, serving energy, automotive, electronics, and consumer goods markets in North America and South America, helped by Von Roll Do Brazil.
