As one might expect from the name, The Peanut Roaster offers a variety of nutty snack foods. The company's products include raw, roasted, honey-roasted, gourmet, and chocolate-covered peanuts; whole cashews; frosted pecans; pistachios; almonds; and nut mixes. Through its Web site, The Peanut Roaster sells nuts packaged in tubs, gift boxes, and collectable tins. The company also operates three retail stores in North Carolina and also sells through mail-order. The business had its beginnings in 1949, when the Monahan family began roasting and selling peanuts at their restaurant; eventually, the restaurant closed and the business morphed into its current Peanut Roaster form; the Monahans still own and manage it.
