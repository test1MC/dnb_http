Looking for a product that will really clear the air? HMI Industries is up to the job. Under the Filterqueen brand name, the company (aka Health-Mor) sells portable vacuum cleaners and air filtration systems. Using high-efficiency filters and cartridges, HMI's systems are designed to rid homes of surface and airborne particles and allergens (dust, smoke, pollen, mold spores, pet dander) better than standard vacuum cleaners. Its chief products include its Majestic and Defender brands of vacuums and filtration systems. The company's products are sold through distributors in more than 40 countries. Founded as Health-Mor in 1928, the company made Filter Queen vacuums and initially sold them door-to-door.
