For readers who who just can't stand to look away from their PC monitor, Zinio Systems transforms print magazines into a digital format. Zinio allows readers to access popular titles such as People, Car and Driver , and Seventeen on the computer desktop via the Internet. Readers can interact with content using the Zinio reader for iPhone, iPad, Android, and Windows 8 and sharing through social networks. Zinio's portfolio includes more than 1,000 digital magazine titles and textbooks from more than 300 publisher partners such as Hachette Filipacchi , Hearst , S&P Global , and Playboy Enterprises . The company was formed in 2001 and has offices in New York, San Francisco, London, Ho Chi Minh City, and Barcelona.
