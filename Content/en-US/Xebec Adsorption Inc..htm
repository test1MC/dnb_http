When this company talks about clean energy, it's not a euphemism. Xebec Adsorption builds plants that purify natural gas, hydrogen, and methane. Its products remove carbon dioxide from natural gas as it comes out of the ground, from waste hydrogen leaving petroleum processing plants, and from biogas created in landfills so they meet quality standards and pipeline regulations. Xebec also makes natural gas dryers that remove moisture from compressed natural gas (CNG) at vehicle fueling stations. (Moisture in gas lines can freeze and cause corrosion.) Other products filter and separate compressed air and reclaim unused helium. The company serves clients worldwide from manufacturing facilities in Canada and China.
