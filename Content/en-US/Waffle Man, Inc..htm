No, he doesn't live on Drury Lane, but the Waffle Man might enjoy sharing a breakfast platter with the Muffin Man. The company makes frozen Belgian-style waffles and other grain products, including cinnamon rolls, pancakes, and baking mix, all under the King B brand name. It has developed a ready-to-go offering called the 54th Street Deli Pancake Sandwich (think Egg McMuffin ) for foodservice company Advance Food . (Advance carries a full-line of King B products.) Headquartered in Enid, Oklahoma, Waffle Man serves restaurants, educational facilities, and other foodservice customers throughout the US.
