A drink, a room, and a book: Fraser and Neave (F&N) is a diverse conglomerate. It makes and distributes beer, dairy products, soft drinks, and bottled water. Its F&N Foods is a top Singapore dairy and maker of the Magnolia milk brand. Through Frasers Centrepoint, F&N develops and manages shopping centers, business parks, apartments, condos, and hotels, including the Four Seasons in Shanghai. It also has printing and publishing holdings. It owned about 40% of Asia Pacific Breweries , a JV with Heineken , until late 2012. F&N has a presence in 20-plus countries in the Asia/Pacific region, as well as Europe and the US. In 2012 Thai billionaire Charoen Sirivadhanabhakdi made a $7.3 billion takeover bid for F&N.
