From the Trojan horse to Patton's non-existent 1st Army Group (complete with inflatable tanks and trucks), deception, decoys, and camouflage are as old as military history. Saab Barracuda LLC, the US arm of the Saab Barracuda Group and a subsidiary of Saab AB , continues the tradition of deception with the high-technology touches demanded by modern warfare. The company is one of the largest suppliers of personnel and vehicle camouflage systems in the US. Its products include screens, special operations tactical suits, collapsible concealment tents, and military tank and plane decoys.
