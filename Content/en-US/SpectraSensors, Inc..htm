SpectraSensors brings space technology down to earth. The company makes laser-based gas and petrochemical sensors/analyzers that can detect trace amounts of target gases, liquids, or contaminants. The company's products measure the absorption of laser light at specific wavelengths to detect carbon dioxide and water vapor in industrial and chemical process control and environmental monitoring applications. SpectraSensors was spun off from the NASA / Caltech Jet Propulsion Laboratory in 1999 to commercialize tunable diode laser technology (TDL), which is used along with absorption spectroscopy in a number of its analyzing products. Customers include Chevron , ConocoPhillips , and BP .
