OFS Fitel is looking for companies in need of a steady diet of fiber. Its fiber products include single-mode fibers, multimode fibers, and bend-optimized fibers. The company sells its optical fiber under the AllWave, LaserWave, UltraWave, and TrueWave brands. Furukawa Electric acquired the optical fiber cable business of Lucent Technologies (now Alcatel-Lucent ) and formed its US subsidiary OFS Fitel and its sister company OFS BrightWave , which makes fiber-optic cable. OFS began as Western Electric Company, the manufacturing arm of Western Union , in 1869.

Furukawa's US subsidiary operates fiber-optic plants in Denmark, Germany, Russia, and the US.

The company's operating divisions include Optical Cable, Optical Connectivity, Optical Fiber, Fiber to the Home, Specialty Photonics, and OFS Laboratories (which conducts research in optical fiber and photonics).

OFS Fitel's products and solutions are used in OEM's applications for wide variety of industries including the aerospace, defense, medical, telecommunications, and more.

The electronics and automotive systems segment contributed more than 25% of parent company Furukawa Electric's total revenue in fiscal 2013.
