MDI Entertainment owns the licensing rights to a portfolio of popular brand names that it licenses for use in lottery products. Its collection of properties includes consumer products such as automobiles (Chevrolet Silverado, Ford Mustang) and board games ( Monopoly, Risk, Trival Pursuit ), as well as such characters as Betty Boop and The Flintstones. Scientific Games , a leading supplier of lottery products and other gaming systems, acquired MDI Entertainment in 2003.
