Over the river and through the woods with Forest River products you go. The company makes recreational vehicles (RVs), cargo trailers, buses, boats, and modular homes/offices. Forest River's RVs include fifth wheels, motor homes, tent campers, and travel trailers. The company offers wheelchair-accessible vehicles, and customizes vehicles for use as personal and commercial shuttle vans. It also constructs manufactured homes branded Sterling Homes and Hart Housing and makes pontoon boats and truck bodies. It has a handful of facilities concentrated within the state of Indiana. The company is a subsidiary of Berkshire Hathaway , the diversified holding company controlled by investor Warren Buffett.

The company operates through several divisions: Recreational Vehicles, Forest River Marine, Park Model, Buses, Cargo Trailers, and Commercial Trucks. It sells its products through a network of independent dealers.

From time to time, Forest River expands by acquiring other businesses and adding new divisions. In 2014 it acquired the Startrans Bus division from Supreme Industries . The company spent $7 million on the deal and will manufacture the Startrans Bus line with the Starcraft Bus brand.

Forest River also grows by launching new facilities in select markets. In 2014 the company opened a new manufacturing location at Silverton, Oregon, where it makes utility trailers used by landscapers, contractors, and others. It also has plans to launch a new recreational vehicle trailer manufacturing plant in Hemet, California, in 2015.
