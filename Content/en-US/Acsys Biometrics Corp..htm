Acsys Biometrics wants you to keep your head up and your hands down. The company designs, develops, and manufactures facial recognition systems and other biometrics devices used to secure building and computer access, as well as to provide identity verification. Its products are marketed under the Custom Biometrics Solutions, Discovery, SDK, Veraport, and Verashield brand names. Customers have included the US government and The Department of Veterans' Affairs. Acsys Biometrics is a joint venture between NEXUS Group International and holographic technology provider AND Corporation .
