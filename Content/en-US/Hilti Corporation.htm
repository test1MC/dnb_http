Hilti supports the global building industry to the hilt. The company manufactures drilling, mounting, and fastening equipment and supplies for customers in the mining, solar energy, and construction and building maintenance industries worldwide. Hilti's products include drilling and demolition tools; direct and screw fastening systems, diamond coring and cutting tools (saws, sanders, and grinders); anchoring and positioning systems; firestop and foam products; measuring systems; and cutting and sanding systems. Hilti makes more than 60% of its sales in Europe. The family of the late Martin Hilti, who founded the company in 1941, still controls the firm through the Martin Hilti Family Trust.
