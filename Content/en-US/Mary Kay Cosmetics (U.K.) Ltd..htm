Independent Mary Kay consultants believe that the beauty of hosting makeup parties is in someday driving their own pink Cadillac or Mercedes. Mary Kay Cosmetics Ltd., founded in 1993, represents one of Mary Kay's more than 37 international markets. The UK retains more than 5,000 independent sales representatives who work full-time or part-time based out of their homes. These sales reps peddle Mary Kay products directly to customers at home parties or at work. Mary Kay's products include skin care, cosmetics, fragrances, nail polish, men's grooming products, and sunscreen. Mary Kay has extended the reach of its business to now boast a comprehensive men's collection for skin and fragrance.

Mary Kay develops, tests, manufactures and packages the majority of its own products at its facilities in Dallas and China.

The company's products are sold in more than 37 countries worldwide, through over 3 million Independent Beauty Consultants.
