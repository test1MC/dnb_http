Hartmann & Forbes is a natural at shielding your home from the sun. The company makes custom window coverings that are handwoven from natural materials, with a focus on recycling and reducing waste. Products include drapes, shades, panels, and screens made from such organic fibers as bamboo, grass, jute, and reeds. Customers, primarily interior designers for the luxury market, can also buy top treatments and accessories, such as pillows, wall coverings, and wood and metal hardware. Hartmann & Forbes imports its raw materials and assembles the finished products at three manufacturing facilities in Oregon. They can be seen and purchased at trade showrooms throughout the US and Canada.
