LaCie Group has la vie technologique on its mind. The company manufactures optical storage drives (CD-RW, DVD-RW), external hard drives, redundant array of independent disk (RAID) systems, and magnetic tape storage products. Other offerings include color monitors, software, and accessories. Its products are designed for use with Apple , Linux, and PC platforms. The company name is short for "La Compagnie," French for "The Company." The company has operations in the Americas, Asia, and Australia, in addition to Europe, which accounts for nearly two-thirds of LaCie Group's sales. Global hard disk drive maker Seagate acquired full ownership of LaCie in a series of transactions in 2012.
