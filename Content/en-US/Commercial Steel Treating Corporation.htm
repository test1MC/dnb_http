Commercial Steel Treating likes to give commerce a treat -- by processing steel. Operating one of the largest metal processing plants in the US, Commercial Steel Treating offers a wide array of heat treating and metal finishing services to the automotive, aeronautical and manufacturing industries. Its capabilities include continuous and batch hardening, salt bath nitriding and gas nitriding, among others. Curtis Metal Finishing, a subsidiary, operates three processing plants, which provide electrodeposition, dip-spin painting, and phosphating to enhance corrosion protection on automotive and other fasteners.
