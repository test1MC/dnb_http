We may not have the technology to teleport yet, but at least we can "beam" our voices around the world. Beam Radio distributes two-way radios and related accessories to customers in the southern US, Latin America, and the Caribbean Islands. It has majority ownership of Motorola distributor Enterprise Communications in Peru, as well as Beam Radio subsidiaries in Brazil, El Salvador, Jamaica, and Panama. The family-owned company was founded in 1976.
