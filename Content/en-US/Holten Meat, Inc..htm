Holten Meat pleases palates with its beef (including certified Angus), veal, and pork products. The company manufactures and distributes processed meat items, including steaks, patties, ribs, and breaded cuts, to foodservice institutions and retail food purveyors throughout the US. Its brand names include Thick 'N Juicy and Healthy Answers. It also offers custom cut services. Operating a 85,000-square-foot processing facility in the heart of St. Louis, Holten Meat is owned and managed by the Holten family. It was founded by Elwyn "Red" Holten in 1960.
