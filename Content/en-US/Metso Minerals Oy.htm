Metso Minerals makes equipment that crushes, screens, grinds, processes, enriches, and recycles mineral products. It also makes conveyor belts, wear protection products, and spare parts. The company is structured along business lines according to the primary industries it serves: Mining (processing of minerals and ores) and Construction (crushing and screening of aggregates used in civil engineering projects). It sells directly and through distributors to customers such as Minera Petaquilla, Norilsk Nickel , and Kalgoorlie Consolidated Gold Mines. Part of the Mining and Construction segment of Finnish equipment maker Metso , Metso Minerals is reorganizing its business into equipment and services.
