The Woeber's want you to spread it on thick. The family-owned firm, Woeber Mustard Manufacturing Company, makes mustards, vinegars, horseradish, chopped garlic in oil or water, tartar and cocktail sauces, and a myriad of other condiments for sale under the Crowning Touch, Sandwich Pals, Gloria's Finest, Supreme, Woeber's, and Woeber's Reserve labels. Woeber Mustard serves companies in the foodservice and commercial and retail food sectors throughout the US. German immigrant Carl Woeber founded the company in 1905 and his descendants continue to own and operate it.
