ReneSola is helping supply the renaissance in solar energy. Through its main operating subsidiary in China, Zhejiang Yuhui Solar Energy Source Co. Ltd., the company makes silicon-based ingots and wafers that go into producing photovoltaic solar cells and modules. ReneSola's customers have included JA Solar , Motech Industries, Solarfun Power , Suntech Power , and Topco Technologies. The company grows silicon ingots in its own furnaces, using raw feedstock or scrap polysilicon, then saws those ingots into wafers for manufacturing solar cells. ReneSola has offices and warehouses in more than 16 countries and an extensive production and sales network.
