Ormco wants to make sure orthodontists have the proper tools to get the most out of a smile. The company develops and distributes a variety of orthodontic equipment and supplies, including adhesives, cutting instruments, bands and crowns, brackets, trays, and wires. Its brands include Damon Smile, Titanium Orthos, Mini-Twin, and Prodigy SL. The company distributes its products directly and through distributors in the US and around the globe. Ormco also offers products through its online catalog. Danaher controls the company and its immediate parent firm Sybron Dental Specialties .

Ormco's products are sold in about 90 countries.

Ormco sells orthodontic supplies to doctors, medical staff members, consultants, and academic professionals. It uses online marketing strategies including a loyalty rewards point system.

To increase sales, Ormco widens its offerings through new product development. In 2012 it introduced its Insignia Clearguide Express system, an affordable line of orthodontic aligners. It also gained FDA approval for its Insignia Advanced Smile Design system, which uses 3D technology software and customized appliances to improve patient results and treatment efficiency.
