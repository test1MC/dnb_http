Polaquímia is one of four companies that makes up the Polak Group -- no joke. Polaquímia makes specialty chemical for the agricultural and industrial sectors. The company's agricultural division produces the active ingredients in herbicides, as well as the actual herbicides, fungicides, insecticides, and fertilizers. Its industrial division makes chelating agents, corrosion inhibitors, surfactants, wood treatment compounds, and a variety of other intermediate chemicals. Founded in 1915, Polaquímia joins sister companies Dr. Jose Polak, Polatecnia, and Metapol to comprise the Polak Group.
