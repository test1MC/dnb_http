AVI Limited keeps South Africa clothed and fed. The company maintains a portfolio of more than 50 brands of food, beverages, cosmetics, apparel, and shoes. Its Entyce unit makes and sells tea, coffee, and juice; Snackworx sells crackers, chips, and other snack foods; other divisions sell frozen seafood, packaged foods, and cosmetics by Coty and Puig . The company's Ciro Beverage Solutions unit supplies Lavazza , House of Coffees, and Ciro brand coffee and beverage services to airports, hotels, restaurants, and corporations. AVI Limited is also licensed to distribute Jimmy Choo , DKNY , Gant, Lacoste, Prada , Nina Roche, Salvatore Ferragamo , and Stuart Weitzman brand clothes and shoes in South Africa.
