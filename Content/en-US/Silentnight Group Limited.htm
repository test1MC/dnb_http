Silentnight Group wants to provide a whole night's sleep. One of the UK's largest manufacturers and distributors of beds and mattresses, the company produces more than 500,000 beds each year under brands Silentnight, Sealy Posturepedic (under license), Layezee Beds, Rest Assured, and Pocket Spring Bed Co. Silentnight offers a range of bedding from affordable comfort to luxury lines and estimates that one of every five beds sold in the UK is a Silentnight Group brand. The company was founded in 1946 as Clarke's Mattresses by Tom and Joan Clarke. Following three decades as a publicly held company, it returned to family control but fell into administration. Silentnight has been a part of the European arm of H.I.G. Capital since 2011.

Silentnight products are sold at retailers around the UK including Benson Beds, Furniture Village, Dreams , and John Lewis . Online, they can be bought at the company's website as well as Amazon.co.uk , Argos , and mattressonline, and TESCO direct.

Silentnight is based in Barnoldswick, Lancashire and that's where it makes most of its products. Its products are sold worldwide.
