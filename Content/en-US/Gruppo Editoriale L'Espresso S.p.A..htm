A number of Italians wake up with an espresso and L'Espresso. Editoriale L'Espresso (also known as Gruppo Espresso) is a leading newspaper publisher and media conglomerate in Italy. Its portfolio of more than 15 papers is anchored by the national daily la Repubblica , and includes such local titles as Il Piccolo, Il Tirreno , and La Tribuna di Treviso . The company also publishes magazines ( L'espresso, Le Scienze, National Geographic Italia ), owns radio stations (Radio DeeJay, Radio Capital, m2o), and operates digital TV networks. Carlo De Benedetti, former chairman of telecom giant Olivetti , has a majority stake in Editoriale L'Espresso through his family's stake in holding company CIR S.p.A.
