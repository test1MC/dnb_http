LiDCO Group cares about the status of your heart. The company develops and manufactures cardiovascular monitors using minimally invasive hemodynamic technologies that study a patient's blood circulation patterns. The company's products include the LiDCOplus monitor, PulseCO software, and disposable sensors, which work together (and with other manufacturers' equipment) to collect data electronically and monitor how well high-risk cardiovascular patients' hearts are performing, especially during surgery. The LiDCO Rapid system helps manage a patient's fluid and drug levels. LiDCO sells its products to hospitals worldwide, both directly (in the US and the UK) and through independent distributors.
