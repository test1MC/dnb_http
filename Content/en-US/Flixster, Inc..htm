Need an online forum to rave about the last flick you saw? Part social networking site, part movie-rating site, Flixster provides an online platform through which movie-goers can chat, network, and share movie opinions. The service, which boasts 25 million visitors, also offers synopses, movie trailers, theater locations, and showtimes. Its Rotten Tomatoes website provides movie reviews and ratings from critics and fans.  Flixster also streams movies and TV shows, and is is also available as a popular movie app on iPhone, Blackberry , Android, and other mobile devices. The company was founded in 2006 by Joe Greenstein and Saran Chari (CTO). Media giant Time Warner acquired Flixster in 2011.
