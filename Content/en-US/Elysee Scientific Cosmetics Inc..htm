Elyseé Scientific Cosmetics sees beauty in science. The company makes and markets women's cleansers, creams, cosmetics, and fragrances, as well as skin care products for men. It also develops skin treatments for anti-aging, acne, rosacea, and hyper-pigmentation. Products are sold through Elyseé's Web site and on home-shopping networks, such as HSN . Founded by Elizabeth Blumenthal in 1923, the company was purchased by president Linda Marshall in 1972.
