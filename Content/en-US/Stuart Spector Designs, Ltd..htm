Stuart Spector Design (also known as Spector Bass) makes electric basses. The company's basses range in price from about $600 to more than $5,000. The company also sells guitars and guitar accessories such as bags, cases, and strings. Products are sold at specialty guitar stores and national music retailers, including Brook Mays Music , Guitar Center , and Sam Ash . Founder Stuart "Thumbslinger" Spector has been designing and manufacturing Spector basses since 1976. In the 80s the company was acquired by Kramer. The company sold its goods under the Kramer brand until Kramer went bankrupt. In 1998 Stuart Spector began selling basses using the SSD label after winning a legal battle to recover the trademark.
