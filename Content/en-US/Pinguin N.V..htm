Greenyard Foods wants you to keep eating your greens. The company processes and distributes frozen and chilled vegetables in two dozen countries, many of which are in Western Europe. In addition to frozen vegetables, the company offers deep-frozen already-prepared vegetable dishes packaged for convenience, as well as canned fruits and vegetables. Greenyard Foods, which sells its products under the Noliko, Pinguin, Univeg, and Peltracom brands, sources its vegetables from West Flanders, southern France, and the UK with processing plants located nearby to ensure quality and freshness. Founded in 1965 as Pinguin, it primarily served the Belgium market until 1980, when increased demand from other countries drove the company's expansion.
