Kulthorn Kirby's business is cool under pressure. The company manufactures compressors for refrigeration and air-conditioning systems. Its menu of components includes hermetic and rotary compressors, condensers, motors, thermostat controls, and enameled copper-magnet wires. Established in 1980, Kulthorn Kirby produces more than 4.6 million units annually, with more than half its output exported to Asia, Australia, and New Zealand. Its compressors are also distributed throughout Europe, Africa, the Middle East, and North America. Major shareholders include James N. Kirby Pty. (of Australia), Tecumseh Products Company , and Industrial Finance Corporation of Thailand.
