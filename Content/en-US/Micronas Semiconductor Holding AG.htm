These days, Micronas Semiconductor is more concerned with what's in your car than what's in your TV. The company makes microcontroller and Hall sensor chips used in seatbelts, electric windows, braking systems, and dashboard instrumentation for cars. Its AutomotivePackage controller separates the chip modules for the engine and body units; since the body unit can be combined with various engines, the engine unit can be replaced by a new processor without needing to redesign or replace the vehicle's electronics. As part of a corporate restructuring to restore profitability, the company has discontinued making products for the consumer market.
