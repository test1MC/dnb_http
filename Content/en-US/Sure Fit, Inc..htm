Sure Fit is pretty sure it can outfit most upholstered furniture. The company is the nation's leading slipcover maker. With operations in New York and Pennsylvania, Sure Fit makes and markets fabric covers for wing chairs, love seats, recliners, ottomans, parson's chairs, and sofas. It also manufactures coordinating pillows and accessories. Under the Sure Fit name, its products are sold through department stores including Bed Bath & Beyond and JCPenney , as well as through catalogs and online. Its website provides consumers with seasonal decorating and measuring tips, as well as furniture-fitting advice. In December 2009 Sure Fit was purchased by Philadelphia-based private equity firm Guardian Capital Partners.
