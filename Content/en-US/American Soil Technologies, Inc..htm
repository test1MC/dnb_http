American Soil Technologies works to make sure your farmland isn't dirt poor. The company manufactures agricultural chemicals that help retain water in soil, the direct benefit of which is manifold. In addition to minimizing the frequency of irrigation, the chemicals also decrease the likelihood of erosion and reduce other damage. Its products are used by agricultural, residential, and recreational clients. Subsidiary Smart World Organics gives American Soil entrance to the organic turf and horticultural markets. Though primarily operating in the US, it also distributes internationally to the Middle East, North Africa, and China. The family of the late chairman Louie Visco controls 30% of the company.
