Tellermate is up for the count -- the count of cash, that is. The company makes bill and coin counting machines used by more than 250,000 businesses across the globe. The systems manage more than $2 billion daily in 20-plus currencies. Other products include automated systems that verify the contents of cash register drawers each time they are closed. Tellermate serves some big names in the banking, retail, and restaurant sectors, among others, including Best Buy , Staples , CVS , and Dollar Tree . The company operates from offices in France, Germany, Spain, the UK, and the US.

The company sells its products directly in its five core markets, but works with a network of distributors to supply products to clients in the rest of Europe, Australia and New Zealand, and South Africa. Its distributors include Sprintquip Australia, ILKA, Money Point, Albacon Slovakia, Perfomatic, SDSP Italy, and Cash Processing Technologies.

Tellermate is headquartered in Newport, Wales with regional offices in Atlanta, Paris, Heidelberg, and Barcelona.
