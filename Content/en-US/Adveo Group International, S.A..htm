Office and stationery products wholesaler Adveo provides paper, pens, printer ink, and Post-its across Europe. The company offers some 45,000 items (under brand names such as 3M , Bic , HP , and Canon ) through distribution networks Calipage, Plein Ciel, and Buro+, among others. Adveo serves some 17,000 office resellers and suppliers, department stores, and retailers who in turn serve more than 3 million small and midsized companies and individuals. The company generates most of its sales from ink and toner, with France its largest geographic market.
