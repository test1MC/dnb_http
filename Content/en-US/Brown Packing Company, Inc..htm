Brown Packing Company knows the difference between a foreshank and a flank. The Illinois company, which also does business as Dutch Valley Foods, slaughters, prepares, and packs veal and lamb for food retailers.  The company also offers other branded meat products, such as organic Dakota Beef, Hudson Valley Foie Gras, King's Pride Fresh Chilled Australian Lamb, and grass-fed Tallgrass Beef, and Tracy Lynns Veal, which contains no preservatives or other additives.  Brown Packing was founded in 1944.
