The company is Autocar, but its main product is a truck. Autocar makes heavy-duty low cab forward (LCF) trucks under the Xpeditor nameplate that are used primarily for garbage pickup. Typically, customers get an LCF truck that consists of a cab and a chassis and then add equipment from third-party manufacturers. In addition to refuse hauling and recycling, Xpeditors are used for concrete mixing and airport refueling. The company also supplies parts and selected services, including a mini-camera system that allows Autocar technicians to diagnose problems remotely. Autocar is a unit of Grand Vehicle Works Holdings .

Autocar's major customers include municipalities and private hauling companies throughout the US and some international customers as well.

The company sells its products online and through dealers, distributors, and sales representatives. Its LFC Class 8 trucks are generally used for refuse and waste disposal.
