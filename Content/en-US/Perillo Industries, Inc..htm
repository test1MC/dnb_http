Perillo Industries, doing business as Century Electronics, makes custom power supplies, inverters, and converters. Products include AC/DC and DC/DC single- and multiple-output power supplies, high-power systems, converters, and inverters. Its products are built to various US military standards and are used by the Air Force , the Army , the Coast Guard , the Navy , and the Marine Corps . The company also makes custom products and offers design and engineering services. Century Electronics was founded in 1973 by Dr. S. Chen as Chen Industries. President Mary Perillo and her late husband Joe bought the company in 1992 and changed its name to Perillo Industries.
