NanJing Iron and Steel is a master of metal production in the City of Stone. The company focuses on smelting and processing of ferrous metals, as well as producing coke and a variety of steel products (steel plates, bars, strips). Established in 1999, NanJing Iron and Steel operates 6 production facilities in China. The company produces more than 1 million tons of medium steel plates and 1 million tons of heavy steel plates and strips a year.
