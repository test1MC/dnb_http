In Enzymotec's view, fat is good for the body. Especially its fats. The company manufactures, markets, and sells lipid-based ingredients used in nutritional and dietary supplements. Its products include krill oil, an omega-3 fatty acid ingredient; InFat, an infant formula lipid that emulates human breast milk fat; and Sharp PS, a line of brain supplements. In addition to dietary supplement makers, Enzymotec makes ingredients for pharmaceutical manufacturers of specialty foods that help patients manage certain medical conditions. Founded in 1998, Enzymotec went public in 2013. It raised $61 million that will go toward working capital and other general corporate purposes.
