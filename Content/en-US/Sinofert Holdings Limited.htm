Sinofert produces fertilizers and other agricultural products and is the largest supplier of imported fertilizers in China. Sinochem Corporation , one of the biggest state-owned corporations in China, controls 53% of the company. Sinofert owns stakes in 17 fertilizer makers across China and operates 2,110 sales outlets. The company has also an established logistics system, including seaport delivery and inland warehouses stocks, owning more than 500 transit warehouses and 2 million tons pf stocking capacity. In 2013, annual fertilizer production capacity of the Group's subsidiaries, associates, and joint ventures totaled more than 12 million tons.

In 2013 the Group carried out more than 6,000 service activities, provide free testing on more than 50,000 soil samples for farmers, and provided other direct and indirect services to more than 1 million farmer households.

Subsidiaries of Sinochem Hong Kong include Sinochem International Oil (Hong Kong., Sinochem International Chemicals (Hong Kong), Dohigh Trading Limited, Rillfung Company, Franshion Company, and Sinochem Europe Capital.

Sinofert acquired the Sinochem fertilizer business in 2005; previously it had managed Sinochem's property investments and investment holdings. The company was founded in 1994 in order to trade on the Hong Kong stock exchange. PotashCorp owns a minority stake.
