Fremont Beef processes calf livers (along with beef and pork livers) that it supplies to US foodservice operators.Â It also exports its products to the Pacific Rim. Fremont sells its fresh and frozen vacuum-packaged meats under the Golden Forest label. In addition to liver, Fremont Beef also processes and sells offal, beef tongue and outside skirts, most of which are available in Asian-style cuts, which can be used for Korean barbeque or stir-fry. It also keeps extra processing and freezer space available for co-packaging opportunities. Established in 1990, Fremont Beef is owned by the Japanese meat company, S Foods.
