Sagus International is an institutional furniture and office products holding company that specializes in school furniture for grades K-12, as well as colleges and universities. Its companies also sell their products to health-care facilities, the hospitality industry, and the US government, among other customers. Sagus operates through three divisions: classroom furniture maker Artco Bell; LSI Corporation of America, a provider of laminate casework systems; and Midwest Folding Products, a maker of cafeteria tables with attached seating, folding tables, risers, and more. The company manufactures its products in plants in Illinois, Minnesota, and Texas. Sagus was acquired by HNI Corp . in late 2011.
