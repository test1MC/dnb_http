Those advertisements in the movie Minority Report that speak to individual shoppers by name may not always be science fiction. Digital View markets video systems for retail and outdoor advertising use that present customized content meant to engage consumers about a new product or service. The company provides media devices to playback digital video, controller devices to connect flat-panel displays to computers, and software for managing video and audio content. Digital View's VideoFlyer and RemoteFlyer players/monitors are meant to be placed on store shelves in hopes of influencing consumer purchases. The company was founded in 1995 by directors CEO James Henry and COO Richard Cobbold.
