Things are going wild, er, wait, make that, growing wild at Jasper Wyman & Son. The company's main product is wild blueberries -- canned, frozen, and juiced -- all grown and processed by the company, and all available under the Wyman label. It also offers frozen boysenberries, cranberries, raspberries, and strawberries. The company, also known as Wyman's, supplies retail food, foodservice and food manufacturing customers worldwide. It operates three processing plants, two in Maine and one on Prince Edward Island, Canada. Founded in 1874 by Jasper Wyman, the company is still owned by the Wyman family.
