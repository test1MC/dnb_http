Trilogy Communications plans to keep it up in the air. A technological leader in the telecommunications industry, Trilogy Communications' high-performance wireless products include AirCell Transline Cable (for wireless base stations), AirCell Radiating Cable (for wireless communication in restricted environments), and AirCell Plenum and Plenum Radiating Cable (for in-building wireless networks). AirCell products can be found in such big name spots as London's Heathrow Airport and the US Library of Congress , as well as the Beijing, Los Angeles, and Washington, DC metros. Its customers have included such big names as Adelphia , Cablevision , Time Warner , and Verizon Wireless .

The company has its customer base in over 40 countries.

AirCell was the first aluminum cable on the wireless scene; its electrical properties have proven better than copper products. The brand gets its name from the dry air that is included in the cable's polyethylene enclosure. The company's patented air-dielectric design makes the cable stronger, more durable, and more flexible. AirCell products are made exclusively in the US.

Trilogy Communications was founded in 1985 with the purchase of a business unit from General Cable .
