Srithai Superware Public Company Limited is Thailand's largest manufacturer of melamine tableware distributed under the brand names Ektra, Flowerware, Melamineware, Superware, UNICA, and Vanda. Srithai Superware exports melamine tableware to countries worldwide. It also manufactures other plastic products, including auto parts, bottle crates, furniture, garbage bins, and pallets. Srithai operates primarily in Thailand, though it does have a manufacturing facility in Vietnam as well; the vast majority of its sales are within Thailand. The company was originally incorporated in 1963.
