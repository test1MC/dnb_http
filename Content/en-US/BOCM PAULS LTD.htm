BOCM PAULS (also known as ForFarmers) is one of the largest animal feed makers in the UK. Its Homemix Nutrition business produces pig feed and food for cattle, poultry, and other livestock. In addition, its Dry, Moist and Liquid Feeds division purchases feed materials and ingredients in the form of byproducts from shippers, brewers, distillers, and the human food industry and uses therm to produce feedstuffs. The company operates through two subsidiaries in Russia and Romania and has 37 production facilities in the UK, Netherlands, Belgium, and Germany.
