Zaner-Bloser covers two of the three Rs from A to Z. The company publishes teaching guides, workbooks, and CD ROM instruction kits on reading, writing, vocabulary, handwriting, spelling, and other skills for kids in grades K through 8. Zaner-Bloser also offers workshops and seminars on topics like teaching cursive writing and reading fundamentals. Customers include teachers, curriculum supervisors, principals, and others in the education field. The company was founded in 1888 by Charles Zaner as the Zanerian College of Penmanship; it became Zaner-Bloser in 1895 and was acquired in 1972 by children's magazine publisher Highlights for Children .
