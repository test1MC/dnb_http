Blissfield Manufacturing makes products that will help keep you and your car cool. Specializing in heat transfer technologies for automotive, commercial, and industrial customers, the company manufactures cooling systems for power steering units and transmissions in cars and light trucks, open-drive compressors for refrigeration equipment, and all-steel condensers for HVAC systems. Other offerings include heat exchangers, oil coolers, and receiver tanks, as well as custom manufacturing services. In addition, the company is the North American supplier of Dorin Compressors, including open belt drive, semi-hermetic, and other models. Founded in 1946, Blissfield operates plants in Indiana and Michigan.
