Morinaga Nutritional Foods uses its soy foods to saunter into sweet, silky sales success. Morinaga Nutritional Foods, through its Pacific Nutritional Foods subsidiary, makes and distributes aseptically packaged tofu and dried nondairy pie-filling and pudding mixes under the Mori-Nu label. Its items are distributed through local and national chains of grocery stores such as Wal-Mart and Whole Foods Markets . The company also sells its tofu products directly to consumers on its Web site. Founded in 1985, the company is a subsidiary of Japanese dairy manufacturer, Morinaga Milk Industry Co .
