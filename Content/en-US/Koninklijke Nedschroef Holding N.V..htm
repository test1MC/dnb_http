Koninklijke Nedschroef Holding makes products that hold on tight. Through about 25 companies operating in 11 countries, Nedschroef makes fasteners for the automotive industry (majority of sales) and tools for forming hot and cold steel, also for the automotive industry. The company's trademark Nedshape components are pre-formed to cut down on further machining. Other key products include Nedpoint (self-aligning screws), Nedlite (light aluminum screws), and Nedcore (self-tapping screws). Nedschroef's manufacturing facilities are in Germany, the Netherlands, South Africa, and Denmark; the company also provides logistics and project management. Nedschroef began in 1894 making rivets for ship building.
