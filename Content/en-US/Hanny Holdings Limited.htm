Hanny Holdings Limited manufactures recordable compact discs (CD-R), DVDs, and USB Flash products. One of the world's leading producers of optical storage devices, Hanny sells its products throughout Asia, North America, and Europe. Growth in its USB Flash segment has significantly impacted sales in recent years. It is also involved in the sale of consumer electronics, trading of securities, and real estate development. The company sold its holdings in Memorex to Imation in May 2006. Computer-related products comprise 67% of turnover for the company.
