Symphony Group orchestrates the design and creation of finely tuned, harmonious kitchens. Founded in 1971, Symphony Group is the largest privately-owned UK manufacturer of custom-fitted kitchen, bedroom, and bathroom furniture. Customers include private individuals, housing developers, social housing providers, and merchants, as well as the British government. The company's base is a 750,000 square foot manufacturing and warehouse unit in Barnsley, Yorkshire. It distributes through more than a thousand retail outlets throughout the UK under the Aquadi, Linear, Laura Ashley, Milano, Gallery, Embrace, and Urbano brand names. The company is privately owned by chairman Douglas Gregory.

Symphony Group's products are manufactured in China, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, the Middle East, Norway, and Spain. It also has showrooms in Africa, Ireland, Europe, the Far East, Russia, the Middle East, and Scandinavia.

Symphony works in five market sectors: Retail, Private Development, Social Housing, and Student Accommodation, and Export.
