Every move you make, Time Domain is watching you. Based on UWB (ultra-wideband) radio technology, Time Domain helps clients track or secure important assets (such as employees, customers, products, or equipment) in real time. The company's PLUS (Precision Location UWB System) relies on sensors attached to assets for tracking purposes by retail, manufacturing, or healthcare clients. PulsON integrates tracking, digital communications, and radar for security applications, providing real time tracking within an established zone, regardless of physical obstacles (such as walls). Time Domain receives major financial backing from private equity investor Pharos Capital.
