Prince Castle dishes up some regal royal foodservice products to please it subjects (restaurants and other foodservice institutions) by providing commercial equipment. Its products are sold worldwide and include a lineup of fry baskets, condiment dispensers, egg rings, grill tools, toasters, holding bins, slicers, steamers, timers, mixers, and pizza ovens, among others. The food equipment maker also provides parts for its machinery and training on how to use them. The company, a unit of the Marmon Holdings (aka Marmon Group) has manufacturing facilities in both China and the US and a global network of sales offices.

Prince Castle maintains sales offices worldwide, including in Asia, Europe (France and the UK), Latin America, and the US. It has manufacturing plants in Illinois and Shanghai.

The foodservice equipment manufacturer develops new products to help solve problems across commercial kitchens worldwide.

Prince Castle provides retailers with shopping carts and restaurants with food preparation equipment. Partner 3Wire is the company's master distributor of parts.

It specializes in developing new technologies, has efficient global manufacturing facilities that produce high-quality equipment, a premier global distribution network, and highly-trained after-service support team.

Prince Castle was founded in 1955.
