Winner International aims to clobber the competition in the personal security and safety products markets with its popular vehicle security device, The Club. Users lock The Club over their steering wheels to discourage thieves. Winner International offers several versions of its signature product, including models intended for trucks and SUVs. The company also provides commercial, residential, recreational, and personal security products, including padlocks, trigger locks, pepper spray key chains, and a version of The Club designed for household doors. James E. Winner Jr. founded the company in 1986 after his Cadillac was stolen, despite the vehicle's alarm system.
