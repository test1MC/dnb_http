Beliefnet is a website devoted to religion, faith, and spirituality. The company is not affiliated with a particular religion or spiritual movement. Its content includes articles and interviews, as well as spiritual tools such as quizzes, prayer circles, and kits to help celebrate births, weddings, and other milestones. The firm also publishes a daily e-mail newsletter, which has a readership of about 12 million subscribers. Beliefnet's site is ad-supported and has some three million unique visitors each month. The company was founded in 1999 by Steven Waldman and Robert Nylen. Today it is owned by BN Media, an investment group behind inspirational video provider Cross Bridge and marketing firm Affinity4 .
