IRICO manufactures color picture tubes (CPTs) and CPT components. Established in 1978 as the first CPT producer in China, IRICO sells some 20% of the total CPTs purchased in the country. Its components menu includes glass bulbs, electron guns, shadow masks, deflection yokes, low-spectrum powder, and phosphors. IRICO manages seven different production lines and has inked two more. Changhong , TCL , Konka, Skyworth , and Hisense are among the television manufacturers who have used the company's products. IRICO additionally exports CPTs and components to more than 20 countries.
