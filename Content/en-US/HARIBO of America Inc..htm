Move over German rivals Trolli and Black Forest , candy maker HARIBO of America has been rooted in the US for more than 30 years. The sweets firm imports the gummi universe of candies made by its parent HARIBO GmbH . The company sells HARIBO brand gelatin-based candies such as Gold-Bear gummi bears, as well as worms, Army men, butterflies, an aquarium full of sea creatures, and gummi-everything-else, in packaged and bulk form. Its products are sold in all 50 US states and in Mexico through candy stores, convenience stores, drugstores, grocery retailers, mass merchandiser outlets, and other third parties. HARIBO of America is owned by Hans Reigel, as is the German parent.
