Antenova facilitates communication when one machine talks to another. The company's products are integrated into wireless communications gear, such as cell phones, mobile PCs, and other consumer electronic devices. The company's antennas appear in products from Samsung , Motorola , Sony , and others. Antenova also make antenna for the devices connected to the Internet of Things. It offers related services such as antenna design, integration, and test and measurement.

The UK-based company has manufacturing operations in Taipei. It has design and support operations and its North American headquarters in Chicago and opened a West Coast office in Santa Rosa, California. In Asia, it has sales and support sites in Shanghai and Shenzhen, China, and Seoul.

Antenova M2M sells through a number of distributors including Arrow Electronics , AEM , Avnet , and Digi-Key . It's website offers pages for speakers of Mandarin, Japanese, and Korean.

The company was founded in 1999 to commercialize antenna technology developed at the UK's University of Sheffield and Australia's Griffith University. Antenova M2M's investors have included Quester , Yasuda Enterprise Development, and The Cambridge Gateway Fund.
