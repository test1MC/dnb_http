Intergold is an innovator of championship sports heavy knuckle bling. The company makes and markets championship rings and other recognition jewelry for corporations, schools, and professional sports teams of all kinds. Previously having produced only class and corporate rings, in the early 1990's Intergold began to court professional sports teams as their official jewelers. Products are distributed through individual sales representatives, the company website, and one retail outlet store. The company was founded in the early 1980s by fifth-generation Armenian jeweler and CEO Miran Armutlu. It was taken private by Visant Holding , which acquired the firm in 2010.
