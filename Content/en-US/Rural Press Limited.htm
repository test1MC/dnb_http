A subsidiary of Fairfax Media , Rural Press Limited (RPL) publishes more than 160 regional newspapers and magazines throughout Australia. Its agricultural publishing unit operates more than 50 papers and magazines aimed at the agribusiness industry, including several titles in the US published through subsidiary Farm Progress Companies . In addition, Rural Press has commercial printing operations and publishes news online. The company's first publication, The Land , was founded in 1911.
