Megmilk Snow Brand Co. is a Japanese dairy company. Known as Meg Milk, the company's vast products portfolio includes fluid milk, yogurt, cream, margarine, fruit juices, and frozen desserts. Megmilk, a unit of Nippon Milk Community Co. since early 2011, also offers its customers milk delivery services to homes. The company had merged with Japan Milk Community in 2009 by means of a stock swap. Snow Brand Milk and Japan Milk were merged into the company most recently known as Meg Snow Brand Milk Co. The move reflected the consolidation in Japan's dairy industry.
