Eurosugar likes a healthy sweet tooth. One of Europe's leading groups for the marketing of sugar, the sugar-marketing joint venture began operations in 2007. It resulted from a bundling of the European sales operations of German sugar giant, Nordzucker , along with those of UK food group, ED&F Man, and French sugar group, Cristal Union. Eurosugar has some eight regional branches throughout Europe and handles approximately 2 million tons of sugar per year; sugar products offered range from classical sugars to fine fondant icings and syrups for beverages, and are sold under the SweetFamily, Daddy, and Erstein brand names. Nordzucker, ED&F, and Cristal Union each own one-third of the venture.
