Bjerke Forgings makes close-die forgings, open-die forgings, and rolled ring forgings for customers in a variety of industries, including manufacturers of aerospace components, heavy machinery, and power generation equipment. The company forges its products from several types of steel, including alloy, carbon, stainless, tool, and other specialty steels, as well as from aluminum, copper, copper alloys, and titanium. Bjerke Forgings also provides machining, heat treating, and destructive and non-destructive testing services. Bjerke Forgings was founded in 1988 and is owned by president Dale Bjerke.
