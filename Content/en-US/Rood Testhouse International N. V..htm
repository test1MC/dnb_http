No rudeness or rude boys here -- Rood Testhouse International politely and professionally provides know-how to the semiconductor and electronics industries. Among its chip-test services are software and hardware development for integrated circuit (IC) testing, wafer probing, final test, burn-in, marking, scanning, straightening, tape-and-reel, and programming. Founded in 1976, the company operates via three divisions in the Netherlands and Germany. While the firm has enjoyed solid returns from its Benelux operations over the years, Rood Testhouse has identified Asia as a key area of future growth. The company plans to expand manufacturing into China, where a significant portion of its customers resides.
