What with global warming and all, it's not a bad idea: Sunfresh Foods spreads jam to freezers throughout the US. The company makes its freezer jams and fruit sauces under the name Freezerves. Its customers include restaurants, supermarkets, and large foodservice distributors such as SYSCO , U.S. Foodservice , and Rinehart Foodservice. Its retail customers include such as Quality Food Centers , Larry's Market , Thriftway , and Albertsons ; items can also be ordered via the company's Web site. Sunfresh Foods was founded in 1986 by president Rick Cosgrave, VP Jill Morganpage, and Reed Hadley.
