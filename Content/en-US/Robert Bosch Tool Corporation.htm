Bosch likes DIYers to tool around the home with Skil. Robert Bosch Tool Corp. makes power tools and accessories for do-it-yourselfers (DIYers) and professionals. The manufacturer boasts a vast portfolio of brand name products, including Bosch, Dremel, RotoZip, Skil, and Vermont American. Its better-known lines are the Dremel rotary tool and the RotoZip spiral saw. Robert Bosch Tool also makes laser and optical leveling and range finding tools. The company's power tools are sold at The Home Depot , Fastenal , Grainger , Lowes , and other home improvement retailers nationwide. The company is a US subsidiary of Germany-based Robert Bosch .

Robert Bosch Tool's growth strategy focuses on driving new and improved tools to market. Indeed, the company boasts that it launches more than 100 new power tools each year.

In 2014 it formed an alliance with cooperative distributor group Associated Construction Distributors International as a vendor partner.

In late 2014 the company sold its lawn and garden tool business, which includes the Nelson and Gilmour brands, to Fiskars for some $30 million. The divestiture allows Robert Bosch Tool to focus on its core power tool lines.

The company sells its products online and through retailers, distributors, and dealers. E-commerce partners include Amazon.com , Build.com, Burns Tools, Alaska Industrial Hardware, ACME Tools, and AceTool.com.

Robert Bosch Tool, originally created through the merger of S-B Power Tool Company and Vermont American, combined with its parent's North American power tool, accessory, and lawn and garden divisions in 2003.
