Acorn Powell equips everything from prisons to high-end hotels with its wide range of stainless steel appliances and fixtures. Its easy-to-clean, no-fuss sinks, urinals, toilets, drinking fountains, kitchenettes, wash basins, and other plumbing systems are used in in commercial, residential, and institutional applications. The company distributes seven lines: Apinox Range, Neo-Metro, Whitehall Manufacturing, Acorn Aqua, Murdock Drinking Fountains, AcornVac, and Intra. Acorn Powell does business online and though mail order, but it also has a showroom at its Gloucester offices.

As well as its core steel appliances, the company makes and distributes vacuum plumbing systems, which have been installed in numerous supermarkets around the world including Hannford Bros. , Waitrose , Target , and Whole Foods Markets .

Acorn Powell has also made other installations for customers such as The Westin Hotel (Times Square, New York), Ras Laffan and Nakilat shipyards (Qatar), and even a mosque (Saudi Arabia).

Its partner Morris Group International provides Acorn Powell with the capacity to meet UK/European specifications and American standards.

Acorn Powell sell its products in more than 30 countries worldwide.

The company produces commercial sanitaryware products for a range of customers and end uses, including school building and refurbishment; local authority facilities such as public toilets; civil engineering projects such as shipyards, prisons, custodial units, and military complexes; and specialist products in hospitals, clinics and surgeries, mosques, and supermarket vacuum plumbing systems.
