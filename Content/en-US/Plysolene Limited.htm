Plysolene is known for leaving its customers high and dry. The company, a subsidiary of rubber and automotive products maker Watts of Lydney Group since 1999, makes elastomeric and thermoplastic sheeting for the construction industry. The company's products also have applications in point of sale displays, packaging, and insulation. Watts' plysolene PIB sheeting is used as a waterproofing and insulation material in a variety of commercial and industrial applications. Its products can withstand severe temperatures as well as chemical exposure. The company also manufactures other products used as liquid barriers in chemical plants. Plysolene was originally founded in 1968
