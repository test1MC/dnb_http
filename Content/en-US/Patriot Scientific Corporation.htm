Patriot Scientific proudly designs microprocessors for licensing to other parties. Advanced Micro Devices , which has an equity investment in the company, licenses Patriot's microprocessor patent portfolio. AMD also has the rights to manufacture and sell Patriot's Ignite 32-bit stack microprocessor. CASIO COMPUTER , Fujitsu , Hewlett-Packard , Hoya , NEC , Nokia , Philips , Sharp , and Sony , among others, have licensed technology from Patriot. The company also owns part of Scripps Secured Data, Inc. (SSDI) and Talis Data Systems, both network security software suppliers.
