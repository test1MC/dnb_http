Orion shines brightly over Scandinavia. At the heart of the health care group is Orion Pharma, one of Finland's largest drugmakers. Orion Pharma manufactures prescription, generic, and OTC pharmaceuticals, as well as veterinary medicines. Its Fermion subsidiary manufactures active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs) for use by Orion and other drugmakers. The company's research is focused on developing treatments in the areas of neurology, oncology, and urology, as well as respiratory and intensive care therapies. Orion Diagnostica develops and manufactures diagnostic tests to be used on site in physicians' offices.
