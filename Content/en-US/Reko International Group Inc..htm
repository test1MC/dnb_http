Founded in 1976, Reko International Group designs and manufactures plastic injection molds (compression molds, fixtures, and gauges and secondary equipment) and numerous metal dies. The company also provides metal cutting services using transfer lines, small intricate fixtures, and assembly and specialty machinery. Customers are in the aerospace, automotive, and consumer and industrial products industries. Reko International Group maintains 10 manufacturing facilities outside of Windsor, Ontario. It also maintains facilities, primarily sales offices, in Michigan.
