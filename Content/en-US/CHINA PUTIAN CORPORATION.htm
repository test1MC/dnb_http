CHINA PUTIAN, one of China's largest technology companies, makes telecommunications products, including switching systems, mobile communications equipment, fiber-optic equipment, and other networking products. The company's subsidiaries include Beijing Postal Telephone Equipment Factory, Great Dragon Information Technology , and Wuhan Putian Communication Equipment Group. CHINA PUTIAN has partnerships with major telecommunications heavyweights, such as Texas Instruments , Nokia , and Ericsson . The company has representative offices throughout the People's Republic of China. Its products are exported throughout the world.
