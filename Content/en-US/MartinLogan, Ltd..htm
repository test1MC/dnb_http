MartinLogan has put a premium price on quality sound systems. The company manufactures luxury-priced (ranging from about $600 to more than $100,000) electrostatic audio speakers for stereos and home theater systems. It's ELS Series (loudspeakers and subwoofers), Design Series (loudspeakers and subwoofers), and Custom Installation (in-wall and in-ceiling speakers) products are made for stereo and home theater use.
