Mondadori France hopes the French are tickled by its stable of magazine titles. One of France's leading magazine publishers, the company has more than 30 consumer titles, including women's fashion magazine Biba and television news and information magazine Tele Star . The company has also found particular success with French editions of popular UK titles such as celebrity news weekly Closer and young men's magazine FHM . Previously Emap France, the company became Mondadori France in 2006, when it was acquired from Emap by Italian media firm Arnoldo Mondadori Editore (also known as Mondadori Group).
