Vitacress Salads aims to make eating watercress hip, and fixing a salad much easier. While the UK company is Europe's largest provider of watercress, it also grows and packages washed rocket ( arugula) , spinach, and other baby salad greens. Beyond salad, it also produces and distributes specialty vegetables, such as potatoes, beets, and tiny peas and onions. In addition to the UK, Vitacress has associated growers in Kenya, Portugal, and Spain. The company supplies retailers and foodservice operators in the UK, Holland, Portugal, and Spain. Vitacress Sales, a subsidiary of Vitacress Salads, wholesales fresh produce and cut flowers and plants sourced worldwide. RAR Group, a Portuguese private food, packaging, real estate, and tourism company, owns Vitacress.

Vitacress has operations in the UK, Spain, Kenya, and Portugal. It sells its products in the UK, Spain, Portugal, and the Netherlands.

Founder Malcolm Issac started Hampshire Watercress Limited in 1951 with one acre of watercress beds. The company added growing operations in Spain and Portugal in the 1980s to secure a year-round supply of salad greens. Hampshire Watercress became Vitacress Salads in 1992 and was acquired by RAR Group in 2008.
