For the sake of security, Dedicated Microcomputers (DM) wants you to know who comes and who goes. The company, doing business as Dedicated Micros, designs and manufactures hardware used in closed-circuit television (CCTV) security surveillance equipment. It offers digital video recorders (DVRs), dome cameras, multiplexers (for recording multiple cameras to a time-lapse VCR), network servers, and peripherals. Its products are used in security systems at commercial and institutional sites around the world, including locations like BAA 's Heathrow Airport in London and France's La Poste .

The company pursues innovation with new product developments in video indexing, intelligent video analysis, and IP connectivity for both entry-level and professional installations.

Dedicated Micros sells its equipment through a global network of CCTV security and electronics distributors and resellers, including Accu-Tech , Hagemeyer , and Siemens .

The company's products are used in a wide-range of sectors including banking and financial services, healthcare, education, gaming and leisure, government agencies, law enforcement and high street retail.

Dedicated Microcomputers was founded by Mike Newton in 1982. Newton is credited with inventing the first commercially available CCTV multiplexer.
