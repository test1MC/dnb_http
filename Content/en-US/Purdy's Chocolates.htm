Purdy's is solid chocolate. The company is the second largest chocolate retailer in Canada (behind #1 Laura Secord ). Purdy's Chocolates owns some 50 confectionery stores located throughout British Columbia, Alberta, and Ontario. Its most popular product is a hedgehog-shaped hazelnut and chocolate candy. Looking beyond the usual chocolate-gifting holidays like Christmas, Easter, and Valentine's Day, Purdy's makes specialty chocolates to celebrate the Chinese New Year. In addition to its retail stores, the Vancouver-based company's chocolates are available for fundraising and on-line at the company's website. Founded in 1907 by Richard Cormon Purdy, the confectioner is owed by the Flavelle family.
