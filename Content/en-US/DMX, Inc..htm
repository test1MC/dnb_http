DMX gives the sights, sounds, and scents of your business a twist. The company provides what it calls "sensory branding" through the distribution of commercial-free music, video, on-air and on-hold messages, and environmental fragrances to companies around the globe. Clients include retailers, restaurants, hotels, health clubs, resorts, and casinos such as Gold's Gym , McDonald's , Burberry , and Whole Foods . DMX has license agreements with Sony Music Entertainment , Universal Music , EMI , and Warner Music , as well as more than 2,500 independent record labels. The company also provides music for cable television networks. Founded in 1971, DMX is owned by Mood Media Corporation .
