The road ahead is a brighter one thanks to Hella KGaA Hueck. Family-owned Hella makes components and systems for automotive lighting (headlamps, interior lighting) and electronics (energy management, driver assistance systems). Through multiple joint ventures, Hella produces automotive front-end modules and thermal-management control systems. Other products include a rain sensor integrated into a windshield and a retrofit cornering headlight. Hella's global aftermarket business supplies automotive parts and accessories. Its production facilities are located in in 35 countries.

The company operates around 100 locations in more than 35 countries across the EMEA, NAFTA/South America, and Asia /Pacific regions; these reside in Austria, Belgium, Bosnia- Herzegovina, Czech Republic, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, the UK, Dubai, India, Japan, New Zealand, Philippines, Singapore, and South Korea. Hella generates 15% of its sales from the German market; the remaining countries in Europe account for 37% of sales.

Hella operates through several segments: Automotive, Automotive Lighting, Automotive Electronics, Aftermarket, and Special Applications. The company also manages around 10 joint ventures, including HELLA Behr Plastic Omnium (HBPO); Behr HELLA Thermocontrol (BHTC); HSL Electronics Corporation; Behr HELLA Service; Mando Hella Electronics; HELLA Pagid; and Innosent GMBH.
