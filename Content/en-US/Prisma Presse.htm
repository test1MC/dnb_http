Prisma Presse knows what's on the tube. The company, France's #2 magazine publisher (behind Lagardère Active ) publishes 20 magazines, including the popular television listings Télé 2 Semaines , Télé-Loisirs , and TV Grandes Chaînes . Other Prima Presse titles include women's magazines Femme Actuelle and Prima , business magazines Capital and Management , as well as National Geographic France . Prisma Presse also publishes each magazine's Web site. Altogether, its magazines have an annual circulation of more than 270 million (the entire population of France is 62 million). Prisma Presse is a subsidiary of German publisher Gruner + Jahr .
