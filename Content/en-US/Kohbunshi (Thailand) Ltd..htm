Kohbunshi's business is a breeze. The Thai company manufactures fan parts for air conditioners. Its products include cross-flow fans, propeller fans, sirocco fans, and turbo fans. Kohbunshi also supplies fans used in cars and trucks. The company's plastic molding parts can be found in home appliances and automotive applications. Founded in 1989, the company operates four domestic production facilities in Thailand and markets its fan products globally. Kohbunshi is a subsidiary of Nippon Kohbunshi of Japan.
