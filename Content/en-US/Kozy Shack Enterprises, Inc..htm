The proof is in the pudding at Kozy Shack Enterprises. The company makes refrigerated puddings, sold under the Kozy Shack brand at groceries in the US and Canada, and a limited number of stores abroad. Beyond chocolate, the multi-flavor portfolio consists of such chilled dairy desserts as crème caramel flan (gluten free), bread and rice pudding, tapioca, and lactose- and sugar-free puddings. In addition, the company makes desserts for foodservice use and under private-label for retailers. Kozy Shack's manufacturing plants are in New York and California, and overseas in Ireland. Started in 1967, Kozy Shack is owned by Land O'Lakes , which acquired the business in 2012.
