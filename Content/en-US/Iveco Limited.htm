Iveco makes and sells distribution and construction vehicles, vans, and tractor units. Its portfolio of vehicles includes the Daily, Eurocargo, Trakker, and Stralis brands. In addition, its Iveco Astra brand manufactures off-road trucks and rigid and articulated dumpers as well as special vehicles. The company counts nearly 20 distributors and about 40 satellite offices within its sales network. Its Iveco Finance unit provides in-house financing options to customers. Iveco got its start in 1975 manufacturing the Magirus-Deutz, Fiat, Lancia, Unic, and OM brands of vehicles.
