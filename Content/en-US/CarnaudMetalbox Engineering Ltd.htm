CarnaudMetalbox Engineering (doing business as CMB Engineering) manufactures industrial machinery used to make steel and aluminum cans. Products include equipment for shaping, trimming, and lining metal containers. It provides its machinery to the beverage, food, and packaging industries worldwide through its manufacturing facilities and sales offices in the UK and the US. The company also reworks and refurbishes components such as clutches, brakes, crankshafts, pushrods, control rods, and other parts for its customers. CMB is a subsidiary of global consumer product packaging leader Crown Holdings .

CMB Engineering is based in Shipley, West Yorkshire, UK, with facilities in the US in the state of Connecticut. It maintains an international presence in Europe, Asia Pacific, South or North America.

The company sells its products through distributors and agents.
