Can you say Mighty Medical Manufacturer? 3M Health Care Ltd. is the British arm of manufacturing giant 3M 's health care operations and one of the largest 3M subsidiaries outside the US. It makes and distributes medical, dental, veterinary, and food safety products. 3M Health Care Ltd.'s products range from wound care dressings and stethoscopes to transdermal patches and microbiology testing equipment. The company's infection prevention items include surgical masks and sterilization equipment; it also makes and distributes dental care products such as adhesives, coatings, crowns, and orthodontic devices. As part of its business, the company provides information services and software to the health care industry.

The company operates four locations in the UK and Ireland (Loughborough, Clitheroe, Bridgend, and Dublin) and exports its products across Europe and the world.

3M Health Care Ltd. gets about 60% of its revenues from its operations in the UK and Ireland.

The company, a unit of US-based 3M Health Care , maintains research and development facilities, manufacturing, and sales and distribution facilities in the UK. The UK unit operates its business through three companies: 3M United Kingdom plc, 3M Health Care Limited, and 3M Ireland Limited. Its manufacturing locations primarily make asthma inhalers and microbiology products, while its sales office supports sales of all 3M medical equipment in the UK market. 3M Health Care Ltd.'s R&D center is focused on drug delivery technologies, and was responsible for developing the first metered dose inhaler. The division is working to extend its expertise into the field of transdermal medicine patches.

Selling through distributors and dealers, the company serves medical clinics and hospitals, pharmaceuticals, dental and orthodontic practitioners, health information systems, and food manufacturing and testing entities.

The company is part of the healthcare segment of 3M. The segment contributed 18% of the parent's 2014 revenues, with a 3% increase from healthcare operations in the Europe, Middle East, and Africa region.

In 2013 3M Health Care Ltd.'s revenues increased by 5.6%, with medical operations contributing 54%; Drug Delivery Systems, 34%; and Oral Care, 11%.

That year the company's net income increased by 7% due to higher revenues and lower operating expenses, offset by higher taxes.

As part of its business, 3M Health Care Ltd. aims to continue its selective acquisitions to achieve incremental growth. The company's also focused on boosting the number of products it launches each year while striving for shorter product-development cycles to market.

In 2011 3M Health Care Ltd. purchased the trade and net assets of Arizant UK Limited and Arizant UK Holdings Limited. Arizant, known for its forced-air patient warming products, now operates as a division of the company.

In 1929 3M chose the UK for its first expansion outside the US, distributing abrasives through a partnership with Durex Abrasives Ltd.

The UK subsidiary was formally set up in 1951 and, whilst 3M products have been marketed in Ireland since the early 1950s, 3M Ireland was established in 1975.
