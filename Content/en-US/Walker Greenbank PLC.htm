Walker Greenbank wants you to spend some greenbacks on its tasteful home decor. The luxury furnishings company designs, manufacturers, and markets wallcoverings, fabrics, furniture, carpet, trimmings and the like for the worldwide consumer market. Walker Greenbank's portfolio of brands includes mid-market ranges Harlequin and Sanderson, and upscale Zoffany. Others include Anstey, Morris & Co. and Standfast. Walker Greenbank manufactures its products in the UK and distributes them throughout Britain (its largest market) and some 75 countries throughout The Americas, Australia, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East. After several lean years, Walker Greenbank's sales have rebounded to exceed pre-recession levels.
