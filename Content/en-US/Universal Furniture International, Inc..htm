Universal may be a bit of a stretch, but Universal Furniture does have a face in the US and its feet in Asia. The maker of casegoods and upholstered home furnishings does all of its manufacturing in Asia. Universal's upholstery collection includes sofas, loveseats, ottomans, chairs, and chaise lounges.
