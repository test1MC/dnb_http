If you're sighting this Northstar, you're probably an aerospace mechanic. Northstar Aerospace manufactures complex machined components for airplanes and helicopters. Primary products include helicopter transmissions, helicopter rotor heads and shafts, accessory gearboxes, and components for auxiliary power units. Northstar also provides machining and fabrication services and maintenance, repair, and overhaul services (MRO). Clients include Boeing , Sikorsky , GE Aviation , and Rolls-Royce . Northstar Aerospace voluntarily filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection in 2012 and was acquired by affiliates of private equity firm Wynnchurch Capital for $70 million.

In mid-2012 Northstar Aerospace voluntarily filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection and was acquired by affiliates of private equity firm Wynnchurch Capital for $70 million.

Northstar Aerospace has operations in Chicago and Phoenix. Internationally it has locations in Milton and Windsor, Ontario.

Since it has emerged from bankruptcy, Northstar Aerospace has won work supplying transmission components for Boeing's AH-64 Apache Block lll program. It also makes precision aircraft gearing for both military and commercial aircraft for Rolls-Royce and is a major supplier to GE Aviation, Honeywell Aerospace, United Technologies' Sikorsky Aircraft, and other aerospace OEMs.
