Through its chief division, Texel, ADS makes needle-punched non-woven textiles for use in filtering, footwear, geo-synthetics, and sanitary products, as well as absorbent non-wovens for medical and personal care. The company also manufactures composites and plastic parts for buses, water vehicles, and aeronautics. Other divisions make liquid filtration systems that are sold in Asia, Europe, and the US. ADS' main customers are in the food processing, HVAC systems, medical care products, and petrochemical industries. Its flame-resistant non-woven fabrics are used by the military in protective garments. The company operates from three production facilities and a distribution center in Canada.
