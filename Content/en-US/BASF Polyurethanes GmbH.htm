BASF Polyurethanes makes specialty elastomers and more. One of the global leaders of polyurethane manufacturing, the subsidiary of world's No. 1 chemicals maker BASF makes polyurethane systems and polyurethane special elastomers. It is also a manufacturer and supplier of polyurethane raw materials. One of its products, Elastollan, is used in ventilation mesh for sports shoes and in the automotive and electronics industries. The unit operates about a dozen sites in Europe and works closely with affiliate BASF Coatings to sell its polyurethane products throughout Europe. BASF Polyurethanes opened its second polyurethane systems applications technology center in Russia in 2012.
