Solaria Energia y Medio Ambiente is looking on the bright side of renewable energy. Serving Spain's renewable energy sector, the company designs, manufactures, and distributes solar panels and provides related consulting services. Products include photovoltaic panels (converting sunshine into electricity) and solar kits (complete kits for solar installation). Solaria Energia y Medio Ambiente also provides project management services for large scale solar power projects -- its Turnkey Services business line helps clients clear regulatory hurdles, provides site selection services, offers planning, design, and engineering services; and manages construction and maintenance of solar installations.
