Nuvo Research finds new ways to get under your skin. The pharmaceutical development firm makes site-specific delivery systems that administer therapeutic drugs through the skin (transdermally). Such transdermal technology generally works by targeting only the affected area, avoiding the possible side effects of oral medications. The company markets topical wound healing treatment Oxoferin/Oxovasin in Europe, Asia, and South America, and it sells topical arthritis pain reliever Pennsaid in the US, Canada, and Europe. Nuvo Research added two more commercial topical pain medications, Pliaglis and Synera, through the 2011 acquisition of drug delivery technology firm ZARS Pharma .
