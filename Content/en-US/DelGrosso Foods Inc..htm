DelGrosso Foods has built a business on the foundation of family cooking traditions. The company produces a variety of tomato sauces, including pasta sauce, pizza sauce, and salsa, as well as Italian-style meatballs. Its spaghetti sauce flavors include meat, mushroom, three cheese, and tomato basil. It sells products through grocery retailers under the DelGrosso brand. For upscale markets, it packages sauces under the premium La Famiglia DelGrosso brand. It also makes an organic sauce line and packs private label brands. The family-owned company was founded in 1946 by Ferdinand and Mafalda DelGrosso; the family also operates DelGrosso's Amusement Park adjacent to its sauce facilities in Tipton, Pennsylvania.
