Intransa makes disk-based storage devices for use in storage area networks (SANs). Designed for Internet protocol (IP) networks, its disk enclosures help enterprises migrate from legacy, direct-attached storage (DAS) to networked storage systems. The company markets to customers ranging from small businesses to large enterprises. It also offers systems designed specifically for storing surveillance video and medical imaging data. The company provides support services related to networked storage integration, storage consolidation, data backup and warehousing, and Microsoft Exchange integration. Clients have included Baxter International and Comarco . Intransa was founded in 2000.
