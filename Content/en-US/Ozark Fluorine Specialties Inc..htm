Ozark Fluorine, as its name implies, produces specialty fluorine chemicals -- though it's based outside Philadelphia, not the Ozarks. The company was founded in 1924 as supplier of sulfuric acid to the petroleum refineries, and has been producing fluorine at its Tulsa, Oklahoma, manufacturing site since WWII. Ozark Fluorine is a subsidiary of California-based industrial recycling company TOXCO .
