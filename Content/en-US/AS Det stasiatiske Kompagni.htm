The mix may be motley, but Det Østasiatiske Kompagni has dibs on it all. The far-flung company, whose Danish name translates to The East Asiatic Company (EAC), has pork processing operations in Venezuela and offers Oscar Mayer and Plumrose brand meats to foodservice companies across the country. Additionally, the company manufactures and distributes industrial ingredients throughout Asia, and it provides moving and relocation services under the Santa Fe and Wridgways names in about 15 Asian countries and Australia. EAC extended its moving services to Europe by acquiring UK-based Interdean International Relocation Group in early 2011 for €65 million (more than $95 million). EAC was founded in 1897.
