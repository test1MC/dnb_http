Electrium is one of the UK's top suppliers of electrical wiring accessories and circuit protection devices. The company offers a wide range of low-voltage installation products, including everything from residential switch sockets to switchgear for industrial applications. Electrium's customers are mainly electrical contractors and large retailers. The company's brands include Crabtree, Wylex, and Volex; its products are distributed worldwide.
