Read my text: Intex DIY is into textiles -- woven industrial textiles to be exact. The bi-coastal company makes and distributes industrial and consumer cleaning towels, rags, wipes, canvas drop cloths, and sponges to retailers in the automotive, general merchandise, hardware, home center, janitorial, marine, paint, and tool markets. Intex is a leading importer of natural sheepskin chamois. Branded products include Detailn'Gear, Paint USA, Micro Gear (microfiber), ProLine Professional, and Marineworks. It operates manufacturing and distribution facilities in California, Georgia, and North Carolina. Formerly a subsidiary of The Tranzonic Companies , Intex DIY was acquired by Bollag International in February 2005.
