Orsus Xelent Technologies (Xelent) wants Chinese cell phone users to have an excellent experience. The company designs and sells about a dozen Orsus-branded models of low-end cell phones in China. It introduced the country's first watch mobile phone and LED screen. Xelent also offers the Proxlink brand of PDAs, which have special features for lawyers, stock brokers, doctors, and other professional groups. The company's products, made by third-party manufacturers, are distributed to retail outlets and through wholesale agreements with CEC Mobile, Beijing Xingwang Shidai Tech, and CECT-Chinacom Communications. Xelent was formed in 2004 as Universal Flirts; it took its current name in 2005.
