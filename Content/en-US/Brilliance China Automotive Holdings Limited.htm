Like its minivans, Brilliance China Automotive has a lot of get-up-and-go. Through its flagship subsidiary, Shenyang Automotive, the holding company manufactures and markets minivans in China. Its minibus brands JinBei and Granse have maintained the company's growth. The company also makes the Zhonghua brand sedan. With BMW , Brilliance China has introduced a series of premium Chinese-built sedans. The company partners, as well, with Toyota , Mitsubishi , and other car makers. Automotive components are made through multiple subsidiaries; Brilliance China's auto supplier business includes holdings in Mianyang Xinchen (gasoline engines for a range of vehicles) and Ningbo Yuming (car window molding and stripping).
