Novatel Wireless proves you can take it with you. The company designs wireless modems that let users access the Internet from anywhere. Its MiFi brand of mobile hotspot devices provides wireless connectivity for up to five users at the same time. Novatel also offers a series of wireless PC card modems (Merlin), embedded wireless modules for OEMs (Expedite), and desktop wireless gateway consoles (Ovation). Its MobiLink software, bundled with modems and embedded modules, connects mobile devices with wireless LANs. Novatel also offers activation, provisioning, and integration services. The company gets most sales from North America.

Novatel operate in two segments -- Mobile computing products and machine-to-machine (M2M) products. Mobile computing products account for 90% of sales. The segment includes its MiFi Intelligent Mobile Hotspot devices, USB and PC-card modems, and embedded modules that enable data transmission and services via cellular wireless networks. These products are manufactured in China and Thailand by Inventec and Hon Hai .

M2M products, which make up the other 10% of sales, include its M2M embedded modules and integrated M2M communications devices. Its products are manufactured in China and Thailand by Benchmark Electronics .

Novatel generates more than 90% of sales in the US and Canada. It has one international office in the UK.

Mobile computing products are sold to wireless operators either directly or through strategic relationships, as well as to OEM partners and distributors worldwide. M2M products are sold directly and through distributors. Verizon Wireless is its largest customer, accounting for almost 60% of sales. The company also has an agreement with Mobiltron for its distribution and repair services in Europe, the Middle East, and Africa.

Overall revenues dropped to $344 million in 2012, down 15% from 2011. Both of its mobile computing and M2M products saw decreased sales. Mobile computing was hit by increased competition at Verizon, its largest customer, and the M2M segment lost a top customer that year. Novatel Wireless has not recorded a profit since 2009.
