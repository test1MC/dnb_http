Daffin's is daffy for chocolate. Daffin's Candies sells chocolate candy at its three retail stores in Pennsylvania and one in Ohio, as well as online. It offers sweet and semi-sweet hand-decorated chocolates (including molded Peter Rabbit figures), as well as gourmet chocolates (truffles, nuts, and creams) under the Jean Marie label. Daffin's offers tours through its Chocolate Kingdom in Farrell, Pennsylvania, where visitors can view kingdom inhabitants and sights, including a 400-pound turtle, a 125-pound reindeer, 75-pound frogs, rabbits, a train, a Ferris wheel, castles, and a village -- all made of solid chocolate.
