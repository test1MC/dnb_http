Dürr Systems (also known as Paint and Assembly Systems) wants everyone to choose a car color, and it does not have to be black. Dürr Systems is the US unit of Dürr Inc., a wholly-owned subsidiary of Dürr AG . Dürr Systems is a leading global supplier of mass production paint shops for car manufacturers and suppliers, and designs and installs automobile manufacturing assembly systems. In addition, Dürr Systems designs and manufactures air-exhaust purification systems for a range of industries. Parent company Dürr AG, based in Germany, has operations in several countries in Asia, Europe, Latin America, and North America.

Dürr Systems has locations in the US (Michigan, New York, Ohio, and Alabama) and in Queretaro, Mexico.

North/Central America accounted for 21% of the parent company's revenues in 2015.

In 2015 Dürr Systems invested $40 million in its Southfield research, engineering facility. The company planned to consolidate its three Michigan locations (in Auburn Hills, Plymouth Township and Wixom) into one research, engineering and equipment testing facility at 26801 Northwestern Highway. The company also plans to construct a 100,000-square-foot testing and validation facility for its products behind the office complex. The basement of the building, which will be used for storage, is 30,000 square-feet.
