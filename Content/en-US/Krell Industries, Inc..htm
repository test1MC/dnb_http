Krell Industries wants to be seen as the hear and now. The company manufactures high-end audio and video equipment and accessories, including amplifiers, cables, home theater systems, iPod docks, loudspeakers, and SACD players. Its products are sold through specialty music retail stores in Asia/Pacific, the Americas, Europe, and the Middle East. All Krell components are designed by CEO and chief engineer Dan D'Agostino. The company was founded in 1980.
