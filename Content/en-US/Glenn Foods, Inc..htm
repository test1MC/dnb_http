Health nuts and diet junkies can settle in and make themselves right at home at Glenn Foods. Under the Glenny's brand name, the company makes about 20 different varieties of low-fat and low-sodium snack foods, including soy chips, as well as baked potato chips, veggie chips, and American fries. It also offers nutrition bars, cookies, marshmallow treats, 100-calorie brownies, and healthy candies, all of which are distributed to retail food and drugstore outlets throughout the US. Glenn Foods was founded by company president Glenn Schacher in 1979.
