Symrise stops to smell the flowers. The flavors and fragrance firm has earned 10% of the world market; about half of its sales come from its home market of Europe. Symrise operates through two divisions: Scent & Care and Flavor & Nutrition. It develops, produces, and sells some 30,000 fragrances and flavors and cosmetic ingredients based on about 10,000 mostly natural raw materials. Symrise sells its products in 160 countries to makers of fine fragrances, cosmetics, hair and body care products, detergents, food and beverages, and pharmaceuticals. It has development centers in Germany, Brazil, China, France, Singapore, and the US. Symrise was formed in 2002 when Dragoco and Haarmann & Reimer merged.
