Lopez Foods is the Fort Knox of the Hamburglar. The company's primary plant operates around the clock as it grinds, blends, molds, and quick-freezes millions of hamburger patties, pork sausage patties, and Canadian-style bacon for McDonald's restaurants. Lopez Foods also makes chorizo sausage and private-label meat products. Its retail brands include Country Cousin and Quick'N Eat. Besides McDonald's, Lopez Foods' customers are national food giants, such as Wal-Mart and Costco , Burger King , Applebee's , Sonic , and other regional retailers. Lopez Foods, which has plants in Oklahoma and Tennessee, is one of the largest Hispanic-owned meat processors in the US. Chairman Emeritus John Lopez controls the company.

Oklahoma City-based Lopez Foods exports its products to Asia, Mexico, the Caribbean, and Central and South America.

Lopez Foods' products are available worldwide through supermarkets and restaurants. Besides supplying McDonald's with a variety of meat products, the company also counts Applebee's , Burger King , Costco , Kroger , Sonic , Target , and Wal-Mart among its roster of customers.

Lopez Foods' operations consist of processing and packing beef, chicken, and pork. Some of the prepared patties are fully cooked. Except for beef, all of the products, are packed by machine versus by hand. In addition to supplying McDonald's and other fast-food restaurants, Lopez Foods manufactures private-label brands for regional and national retailers, and supplies food service and industrial customers.

The company boasts processing plants in both Oklahoma and Tennessee.

The meat company is focused on new product development and the customization of its products, tailoring size, taste, shape, and packaging for its clients' requests. It also looks to joint ventures to increase its manufacturing capacity. Lopez Foods partners with Tyson Foods , one of the world's biggest meat protein producers. In 2011 the venture, Dorada Poultry, reopened a former Tyson poultry plant located in Ponca City, Oklahoma. Another joint venture in South Carolina is held with McDonald's supplier Keystone Foods ; Rancho Poultry processes chicken for the fast-food giant.
