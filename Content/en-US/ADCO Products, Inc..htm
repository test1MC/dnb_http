ADCO Products adheres to its sticky operating mantra. The company, a subsidiary of ADCO Global , manufactures polyurethanes, acrylics, and butyls used in adhesives and sealants for the construction and transportation industries. ADCO's products make adhesives, sealants, tapes, primers, and caulk for the vehicle manufacturing, industrial assembling, and roofing industries. Some of ADCO's major customers include manufacturers of solar panels, wind energy turbines, and "green" construction materials. The Michigan-based company handles ADCO Global's North American operations.

ADCO Products and its parent company operate through about 12 facilities located in China, France, Germany, the UK, and the US. It has manufacturing locations in North America (Michigan and Ohio), Europe, and Asia.

ADCO Products operates through four primary divisions. Roofing specializes in the installation of reflective single-ply roofing products, while its construction division offers sealing, bonding, and weatherproofing products. To support the growing need for green products, its solar division makes solar panels and supports integrators and installers. In addition, the company's transportation division makes adhesives and sealants used in the construction and manufacturing systems markets.

To attract additional customers, ADCO Products is expanding the capabilities of its roofing products division with urethanes, advanced x-linked rubber, and other adhesion chemical methods. It aims to adapt the technologies to save time and labor, reduce VOC emissions, improve job-site safety, and reduce building disruption.
