This company is energetic about jazz, world, blues, and adult contemporary music. The Verve Music Group is one of the leading jazz record companies in the US, promoting such artists as Brian Blade, Diana Krall, Nellie McKay, Katharine McPhee, and Lizz Wright. It also reissues recordings from its formidable back catalog, including classic tracks by John Coltrane, Count Basie, Ella Fitzgerald, Charlie Parker, and Sarah Vaughan. Founded by producer Norman Granz in 1956, Verve today is part of Universal Music Group (UMG). The label operates alongside other UMG properties, such as Motown Records and Island Def Jam .
