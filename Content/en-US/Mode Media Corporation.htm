One company publishes Web content for both the glamorous and the brash. Mode Media's flagship website Glam.com is an entry point for users to access the Glam Publisher Network, which is made up of women's interest websites from more than 1,400 publishers. The sites target women between the ages of 25 and 35 and address topics such as entertainment, family, food, fashion, and health. Mode Media also targets the men's market through Brash Media, which covers topics such as sports, technology, and cars. In addition, its Tinker.com is a social media offering that lets users share information across sites such as Facebook and Twitter . Mode Media was founded in 2004 by Samir Arora, who formerly worked at Apple .
