Hill & Smith has a steely resolve to make an impact on road safety. The company makes the FlexBeam steel highway barrier system, which can be set in concrete, driven into the ground, or surface mounted on road sides and bridge decks. It also makes off-road safety barriers to protect vehicles around buildings, delivery bays, industrial work zones, private roads, and racetracks. Hill & Smith makes both tensioned and untensioned corrugated barriers, as well as open box beams, which are suited to center dividers on highways and high-speed roads. The company is part of Hill & Smith Infrastructure Products, a subsidiary of Hill & Smith Holdings .

Hill & Smith makes steel barriers (under the Flexbeam, Flexbeam Plus, and Hi-Flex brands), wire rope barriers (Brifen), hybrid impact protection steel barriers and wire rope (Briflex), and industrial barriers.

The company is based in England and has infrastructure businesses operating in Australia, France, India, Norway, Sweden, Thailand, the UK, and the US.
