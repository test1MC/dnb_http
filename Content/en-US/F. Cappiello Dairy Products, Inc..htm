Family-owned F. Cappiello Dairy Products makes Italian cheeses your family can't live without -- mozzarella, smoked mozzarella (called scamorze), and ricotta. It also has a line of specialty cheeses, including marinated and hand braided mozzarellas. The company's customers include retail food and foodservice providers. It also sells its cheeses directly to the consumer via its online store. Francesco Cappiello founded the family-owned company in 1921.
