Looking for a magic bedtime story? Check out these tomes from the Magic Kingdom. Disney Publishing Worldwide (DPW) is the children's publishing arm of Walt Disney 's consumer products division. Most of its titles are based on characters and content licensed from other parts of the Disney media empire. Its Disney Global Books publishes books based on characters such as Winnie the Pooh and Mickey Mouse, while Disney Global Magazines magazines include Disney Princess and Donald Duck . Each year, DPW sells some 250 million books and more than 400 million magazines. Its books and magazines are published in 85 languages in 75 countries. DPW also owns Disney English, which operates English language schools in China.
