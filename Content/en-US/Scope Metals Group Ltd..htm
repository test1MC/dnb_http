Finished, semi-finished metals, and engineered plastics all fall under this company's scope. Scope Metals Group imports, exports, processes, and markets an array of products, including aluminum, stainless steel, brass copper, bronze, titanium, and nickel alloy. Its stock is configured into sheets, coils, plates, bars, pipes, flanges, fasteners, valves, and a slew of other off-the-shelf items. Headquartered in Israel, the company's products reach industrial manufacturers across the Middle East. Acquisitions and organic growth have opened the door to tapping the US, Europe, and China, too. Scope Metals Group serves clients in such industries as aerospace, chemicals, electronics, construction, and energy.
