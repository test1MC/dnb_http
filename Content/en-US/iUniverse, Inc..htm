The publishing world just got a little bigger. iUniverse is an online publishing company that provides individual writers publish, market, and sell their books. Services include author support, copy-editing, contracts, distribution, and cover design services. iUniverse offers six separate packages, ranging in price from about $600 to more than $4,000, depending on how many services an author wants. Its iUniverse online bookstore has distribution agreements with retailers such as Barnes & Noble (through BarnesandNoble.com) and Amazon.com . iUniverse was founded in 1999 by Richard Tam and is a subsidiary of Author Solutions , the country's largest self-publishing firm.
