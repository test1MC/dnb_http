Things are a snap for Canon Marketing (Malaysia). The company, a subsidiary of imaging giant Canon , supplies digital and SLR cameras, as well as digital camcorders, plain paper faxes, scanners, printers, projectors, calculators. The company also provides combination equipment that prints, scans, copies, and faxes all in one unit. It offers supplies and parts such as  copy and photo paper, ink cartridges, toners, and drums. Along with hardware, the company offers business imaging solutions, such as printing, faxing, and CAD (computer aided design) services. Established in 1987, Canon Imaging (Malaysia) is headquartered in Selangor and has seven branch offices located throughout the country.
