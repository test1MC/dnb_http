Iroko Pharmaceuticals takes great pains to develop and commercialize its drugs. A specialty pharmaceutical company, Iroko focuses on developing, marketing, and selling nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs used to treat acute and chronic pain. The company has several pain management candidates in its pipeline including its SoluMatrix Fine Particle Technology platform, which helps make pain killers more effective. Approved products include Vivlodex, Zorvolex, Indocin, Tivorbex, and Aldomet. In late 2015, the FDA approved Vivlodex, which was launched in the US early the next year.
