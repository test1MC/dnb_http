These artists don't mind if you say they've gone Hollywood. Hollywood Records is the pop music label of media giant Walt Disney . It produces and distributes albums from such popular artists as the Jonas Brothers, Hillary Duff, and Miley Cyrus (the star of the über-pupular Disney series Hannah Montana ). The label also administers the catalog of operatic rock band Queen, and its catalog includes soundtracks to Disney movies such as Freaky Friday , The Wedding Planner , and Zoolander . Hollywood Records additionally includes the Mammoth Records and Buena Vista Records labels. Hollywood Records operates as part of the Disney Music Group, a unit of Walt Disney Studios .
