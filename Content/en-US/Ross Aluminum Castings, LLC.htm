Casting obstacles aside, Ross Aluminum Castings (formerly Ross Aluminum Foundries) specializes in high-performance aluminum castings. The company utilizes aluminum alloy melting processes and continuous degassing techniques to filter molten metal to remove oxides, inclusions, and hydrogen gas porosity. A subsidiary of Advanced Metals Group since 2007, Ross is one of its parent company's four foundries, in addition to US Aluminum Castings, Oberdorfer, and Mabry Castings. Ross's casting processes include dry sand, green sand, permanent mold and drag, and squeeze casting. Products include aerospace castings and housings, automotive castings, and turbo charger housings.
