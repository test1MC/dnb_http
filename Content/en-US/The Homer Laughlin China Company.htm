It may not be a household name, but you might just find Homer Laughlin in as many homes as the other Homer (Simpson, that is). Homer Laughlin China (HLC) makes lead-free china products including iconic Fiestaware, its most popular line. Introduced in 1936, Fiestaware is now highly valued by collectors and museums. Despite such prestige, HLC continues to sell the boldly-colored dishware at affordable prices. (Periodically, the company retires certain colors from its collection.) In addition to selling to individual consumers through mass retailers and a Web site, HLC also supplies the hotel and foodservice industries. Founded in 1871 by Homer Laughlin, the company acquired chinaware maker Hall China in 2010.
