No petulant poultry here, only blissfully frolicking fowl. Petaluma Poultry is one of the top US producers of certified organic chicken. It produces free-range, vegetarian-fed, antibiotic-free, and organic chickens and chicken hot dogs. The company's products portfolio includes such labels as Rocky the Range, Rocky Jr., Rosie Organic Free Range, and Rocky Dogs. Rocky the Range chicken was the first USDA -approved free-range chicken while Rosie is the first US chicken brand to be certified as organic. Petaluma's products are sold through retail grocery stores and restaurants in California, Illinois, Maryland, and New Mexico. Founded in 1969 by the late Allen Shainsky, Petaluma is owned by Coleman Natural Foods .
