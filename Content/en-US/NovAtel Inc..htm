NovAtel makes Global Positioning System (GPS) receivers and related components for aerospace guidance and navigation, precision agriculture, surveying and mapping, and other applications. The company's products include GPS antennas, receiver units, and signal generators designed for use on land, sea, and in the air. NovAtel's customers have included Leica Geosystems , Beijing BDStar, and YC International. The company's systems -- GPS, GNSS, BeiDou, and Galileo -- are compatible with the major satellite systems on which GPS is based. In 2015 NovAtel got a major boost in reaching customers through a distribution agreement with Arrow Electronics, which has a worldwide reach. NovAtel is a subsidiary of Hexagon .

Also in 2015, NovaTel introduced the GPS-704-WB antenna, a wideband antenna that supports L-Band as well as the frequencies of all GNSS constellations (GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and BeiDou), including B3 and E6 signals. The antenna provides flexibility to customers who can use it GPS-only or multi-constellation applications.
