Heeding an old saying, Enprotech puts its eggs in more than one basket. The company, a subsidiary of ITOCHU International , manufactures, upgrades, and retrofits production equipment used in a slew of businesses. Its lines range from automation and control products, beverage processing and refrigeration equipment (subsidiaries BevCorp and RDM Technologies) to industrial equipment, machinery repair, and rebuild (Enprotech Mechanical Services), steel production equipment manufacturing (Enprotech Steel Services), and, even construction of manufacturing plants (subsidiary Xytel ). Enprotech's customer roster includes North American automobile, beverage processing, petrochemical, steel, and various heavy industries.
