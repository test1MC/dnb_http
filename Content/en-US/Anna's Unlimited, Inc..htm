Anna's Unlimited was whipped up by Anna Olvera-Ullrich, who got her start making salsa for her Austin, Texas, friends and neighbors in 1997. Today, Anna's Unlimited makes Southwestern salsas, pepper jellies, along with herbs and spices all under the Ana's label. The company's products are sold in grocery stores such as Albertson's , HEB , Market Basket , and Sun Harvest . Distribution of the company's products is currently limited to Texas and Louisiana; however, Anna's does accept orders by phone or mail. Olvera-Ullrich owns and operates Anna's Unlimited with her husband, Jim Ullrich.
