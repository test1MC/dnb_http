Esprit Log & Timber Frame Homes, doing business as Fireside Log Homes, designs and builds log homes made from eastern white pine. It offers floor plans for homes that range in size from about 865 sq. ft. to more than 3,200 sq. ft. In addition to logs, beams, and rafters, the company provides accessories used in home construction, such as siding, trim, ceiling panels, stair systems, and decking. The company sells Fireside Log Homes through dealers in Alabama, the Carolinas, Ohio, Tennessee, and West Virginia. The company got its start in 1976 with a kiln-drying and re-sawing operation in Georgia.
