You don't have to be particularly nutty about beer to enjoy Beer Nuts' snacks. Despite its name, the company's nut varieties (including original, spicy & hot, bar mix, glazed peanuts, chocolate-covered nuts, almonds, cashews, macadamias, and pecans) do not contain any alcohol. The family-operated company started out as a small confectionery shop in Bloomington, Illinois, in the late 1930s. As their "redskin" nuts (called so because they were made with skins intact) gained popularity, the father-son team of Edward and Arlo Shirk began selling them to local liquor stores and bars. Beer Nuts' snack offerings are now sold in retail food and convenience outlets throughout the US as well as overseas.
