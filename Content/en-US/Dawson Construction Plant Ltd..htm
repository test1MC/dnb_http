Dawson Construction Plant believes in creating strong foundations. Headquartered in the UK, the company manufactures piling equipment used to install contraction pilings for anchoring bridges, buildings, and roadways to the earth. The company's products include hydraulic hammers, piling and drilling rigs, sheet pile threaders, and sheet pile guide frames. The company sells it products in the UK and has distributors throughout Europe and North America, Australia, South America, and Asia. The company also offers piling installation through its Dawson Contract Piling subsidiary.
