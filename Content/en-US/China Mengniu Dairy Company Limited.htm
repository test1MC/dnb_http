China Mengniu Dairy Company ranks among the top Chinese fluid milk producers. China Mengniu and its subsidiaries produce and distribute dairy products in China, Hong Kong, and Macao, primarily under the Mengniu brand. The company's full line of offerings include liquid dairy products, such as pasteurized milk, yogurt, milk beverages, and ice cream. Its other products include aseptically packaged milk, milk powder, and cheese. Headquartered in Hong Kong, the dairy has some 30 manufacturing sites in 17 Chinese provinces. Mengniu Dairy has a production capacity of 7.8 million tons of milk products per year. Mengniu is pursuing the fast-growing drinkable yogurt market in China with French partner Groupe Danone .
