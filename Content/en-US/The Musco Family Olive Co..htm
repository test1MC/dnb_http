The Musco Family Olive Co. is one of the nation's leading purveyors of canned and brined olives. The company sells its products primarily under its flagship Pearls brand. It also sells olives under the Early California brand, but also offers private-label services. Musco's products, which include black, green, and Mediterranean olives in sliced, chopped, pitted, low-salt, and whole varieties, are sold through retail food outlets, as well as to restaurants and other foodservice providers nationwide. The family-owned and -operated company was founded by Nicolo Musco, who immigrated to the US from Italy in the 1920s to start a food-distribution company.

The Musco Family Olive Co. has a canning facility and its headquarters on a 350-acre site in Tracy, California.

The Musco Family Olive Co. supplies US restaurants and foodservice companies with table olives, such as Black Ripe, Sicilian, Spanish green and queen, Fresh Cured green ripe, Kalamata, and stuffed queen jalapeno and garlic olives. It taps more than 500 growers, 350 employees, and many vendors and business partners to keep its company running.

The company relies on a host of retailers to get its products in the hands of consumers. It sells its olives through Costco , Fry's Food City , Safeway , Fred Meyer , Basha's , Kroger , Target , and Wal-Mart , among others.

To cater to the growing snacks (and mobile) category of the food industry, The Musco Family Olive Co. in 2013 rolled out new single-serve 1.2-oz. snack cups filled with black, pitted olives. Under the name Pearls Olives to Go, they're liquid-free and ready-to-snack in easy open cups.
