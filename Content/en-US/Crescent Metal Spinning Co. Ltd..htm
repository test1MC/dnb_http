Crescent Metal Spinning knows how to spin a tale with a little fabrication thrown in for good measure. The family owned company is a producer of metal components and machine parts. It offers services including fabrication (guillotine, rolling, pressing, punching and press-brake bending), machining (lathe turning and milling), metal spinning (hand and hydraulic spinning), and welding (arc, gas, spot, and stud). The company works with aluminum, mild steel, and other materials, but specializes in stainless steel. Catering to the UK, Crescent Metal Spinning serves customers in the automotive, electronics, leisure, pharmaceutical, and textiles industries, among others.
