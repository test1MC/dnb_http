Mitsubishi Motors North America (MMNA) oversees the US operations of Japan's Mitsubishi Motors . It develops, manufactures, sells, and finances (through Mitsubishi Motors Credit of America ) Mitsubishi brand vehicles through a network of about 400 dealers. MMNA's manufacturing division in Normal, Illinois is Mitsubishi Motors' only passenger car assembly plant in North America. There, it builds the Outlander crossover vehicle for the US market and several export markets. MMNA recently revamped its lineup and phased out the Eclipse couple, the Endeavor SUV, and the Galant sedan in 2012. Its current lineup is the Evolution, Lancer, the Mirage, the Outlander, and its first electric vehicle, the i-MiEV. It sells about 85,000 vehicles in the US every year.
