Tevco Enterprises has nailed down its corner of the US fingernail polish manufacturing industry. Tevco makes nail enamel polishes and polish ingredients for such iconic companies as Chanel , Revlon , and Shiseido . The company, which operates in the Americas, was the North American unit of Durlin (which, in turn, was part of French chemicals powerhouse SNPE , a maker of nitrocellulose, an ingredient in propellants, inks, and varnishes). In mid-2006 SNPE sold Tevco Enterprises to an independent investment group. The company was founded in the mid 1950s.
