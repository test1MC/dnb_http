When it came to dancing, Gene Kelly could hardly contain himself. When it comes to containers, Kelly Container can. The company sells and rents containers and trailers, specializing in refrigerated containers. The company offers refrigerated containers and freezers (providing refrigeration and freezer capability to minus 30 degrees Fahrenheit.) for a wide range of business and industries as an economical alternative to cold storage. Its regular wind and water tight steel storage containers, available for long or short term storage, come in 40 ft and 45ft and larger sizes.
