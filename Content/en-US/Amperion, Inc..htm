Amperion hopes to amp up signals across the country. The company makes equipment and software that makes it possible to transmit high-speed data signals over medium-voltage electrical power lines, using wireless mesh networks and broadband over power lines technologies. Service providers and utility companies use the products to enhance their service offerings with broadband Internet access. Progress Energy , Southern Company , and AEP (who is also an investor) are among the company's customers. Other investors include leading equipment maker Cisco and commercial lender Redleaf . Amperion was founded in 2001.
