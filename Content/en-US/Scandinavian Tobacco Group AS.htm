All you need is a little fire to get a taste of Scandinavian Tobacco Group (STG). One of the world's top tobacco products companies, STG is a producer and distributor of several brands of pipe tobacco, cigars, and rolling tobacco. Its prominent brands include Café Crème (cigarillo), Skandinavik (pipe tobacco), and Escort (rolling tobacco). Its products are popular in Denmark, but the firm also distributes to more than 100 countries, including most of Europe, Canada, and the US. STG runs production facilities in several countries and imports most of its tobacco from Indonesia, North and South America, and Asia. STG, which is 49%-owned by Swedish Match , bought Reynolds American 's Lane Limited subsidiary in 2011.
