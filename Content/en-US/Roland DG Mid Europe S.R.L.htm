Electronic musical instruments are BOSS at Roland Europe. The company produces and distributes electronic musical instruments such as keyboards, synthesizers, digital pianos, and amplifiers under names such as Roland and BOSS. Roland Europe also markets computer peripherals (CAD/CAM equipment, printers) made by its affiliated company, Roland Digital Group. It distributes its products in Europe, the Middle East, and North Africa. Roland Europe is a subsidiary of Japan-based Roland Corporation , whose founder, Ikutaro Kakehashi, has been recognized for technical innovations in musical instruments.
