How does Howden North America do? Formerly Howden Buffalo, the company actually does quite well making air movement equipment. Products, including axial and centrifugal fans, blowers, compressors, cooling systems, hydraulic drives, and rotary heat exchangers, are sold under brand names American Blower, Buffalo Forge, Canadian Blower & Forge, and Gýrol. The lineup is used in myriad applications, such as emission control; highway, tunnel, and mine ventilation; waste water treatment; wind tunnels; and a variety of other industrial processes. The company is an indirect subsidiary of Charter International . Howden North America has facilities in Michigan, New York, Ohio, South Carolina, and also in Mexico City.
