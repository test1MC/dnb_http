Things can get hazy when Rosco puts you in the spotlight. Rosco Laboratories is the world's largest producer of fog machines. The special-effects company also manufactures and markets gobo rotators, projectors, color filters, stage flooring, and other set design products. Rosco sells its products for use in theaters, clubs, and concerts. Its products are distributed across the US and worldwide through a handful of regional subsidiaries and sold in specialty lighting stores. Rosco Laboratories acquired the assets of DHA Lighting (the UK's largest supplier of custom gobos for theatrical and other applications) in 2005.
