Société Anonyme Belge de Constructions Aeronautiques (SABCA) designs, tests, and manufactures aircraft parts for civil aviation, space, and military clients. A partner of Airbus and Dassault , SABCA supplies such integral parts as tailcones, wing panels, and subfloor structures used in private and commercial aircraft. It also manufactures structures and thrust vectoring systems for European space customers such as Arianespace and Europropulsion. SABCA's military operations include production, overhauls, and upgrades of military helicopters and airplanes, supporting nine air forces worldwide.
