From hair cream to stretch mark cream, The Stephan Company manages a vast portfolio of products as a maker of branded and private-label personal care items. The company's brands include Cashmere Bouquet, Quinsana Medicated, Balm Barr, Stretch Mark Crème, Protein 29, Stiff Stuff, Wildroot, and Frances Denney. It sells them worldwide by mail order and in retail stores and salons through its subsidiaries, including Morris Flamingo-Stephan, Old 97, American Manicure, Lee Stafford Beauty Group, Williamsport Barber and Beauty Corp., and Scientific Research Products, among others. Chairman, president, and CEO Frank Ferola owns about 21% of the firm, which bought Bowman Beauty and Barber Supply Company in August 2008.
