TeligentEMS provides electronics manufacturing services, including mechanical, electronic, and system-level assembly. Specialized services include prototyping, new product introduction, material procurement, supply chain management, printed circuit board assembly, in-circuit board testing, functional board testing, final system box build, depot repair, order fulfillment services, printed circuit board development, and functional test development. TeligentEMS was established in 2002 by president and CEO Chris Eldred, who purchased the assets of another company.

TeligentEMS provides a mix of engineering, new product introduction, program management, supply chain management, manufacturing and support services to customers in the communications, aerospace, military, medical, life sciences, industrial, and instrumentation businesses.
