Redline Communications wants to lead a WiMAX revolution. The company provides access and backhaul products used to build wireless broadband networks. Used by WiMAX network operators, the company's RedMAX products -- base stations, indoor and outdoor subscriber units, and network management software -- support broadband Internet access, voice- and video-over-IP, and VPNs. The company's RedCONNEX (point-to-point) and RedACCESS (point-to-multipoint) broadband wireless infrastructure products are targeted toward the education, financial services, municipal, oil and gas, public safety, and utility sectors.
