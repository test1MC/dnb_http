Contract Chemicals began life in 1977 as a gun-for-hire in the world of specialty chemical manufacturing. Its chemicals (including bromides, chlorides, intermediates, and salts) are made for the agrochemical, food, fragrance, detergent, food, personal care, pharmaceutical, and photography industries. The independent chemical company's products are used in antibiotics and anti-hypertension drugs as well as in salt and vinegar potato chips. The bulk of Contract Chemicals' revenues come from exports to the US, France, Germany, India, Italy, Ireland, Japan, Korea, the Netherlands, and Switzerland.
