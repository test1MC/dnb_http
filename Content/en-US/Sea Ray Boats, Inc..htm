When you must go down to the sea again, Sea Ray Boats can help you out. The company, a subsidiary within Brunswick Corp. 's boat business, builds a line of 18- to 61-ft. pleasure craft, including sport boats, cruisers, and yachts. Models, sold under the Sea Ray and Meridian Yacht brands, are aptly named Sundeck (sport boats), Sundancer (sport cruisers), and Sedan Bridge (sport yachts and yachts). Bose stereos, wet bars, DSS satellites, and washer/dryer units are a few luxury options the boat maker offers. Sea Ray operates as part of the Brunswick Boat Group operations.

Sea Ray has offices in the US and the Netherlands, plants in Tennessee and Florida, and works with more than 400 dealers operating in 86 countries.

Sea Ray Boats was founded in 1959 and acquired by Brunswick Corp. in 1986.
