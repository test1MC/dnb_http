Young's Seafood has real net worth. Formerly The Seafood Company, it is part of European parent Findus Group. Effective 2011 the company represents the combined operations of The Seafood Company, Young's Seafood, and Findus UK. Through more than a dozen UK sites, Young's Seafood is the country's largest supplier of fish and seafood to retailers and consumers. It makes value-added smoked fish and shellfish, such as smoked salmon appetizers and terrines, smoked haddock gratins, and kippers with butter. It also processes local and imported crab, lobster, prawns, and other shellfish. Customers include grocery stores, caterers, and other food manufacturers. The company is ultimately owned by Lion Capital .
