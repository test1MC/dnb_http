Pathwork Diagnostics is hoping to offer doctors the ability to predict what health conditions a patient may have. The company is developing methods to identify biomarker patterns that indicate certain types of diseases. It is focused on molecular diagnostic technologies, as many traditional tests cannot identify diseases in their early forms. Pathwork Diagnostics is working on identifying the earliest possible signs of a given disease, such as cancer, to help doctors determine the best therapies to treat that disease. Its Tissue of Origin test has been approved by the FDA to analyze certain cancerous tumors. The company was founded in 2003.
