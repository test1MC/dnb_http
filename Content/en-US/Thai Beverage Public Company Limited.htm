Whether the alcohol goes in your tank or your car's, Thai Beverage is happy to pour. Known as ThaiBev, it makes beer and distilled spirits, as well as ethanol through more than 60 affiliated companies. It makes est-brand soda; Chang beer, bottled water, and soda; a distilled line of wine coolers, fermented herb drinks, whiskey, rice wine, sake, and SangSom rum; Bionic fertilizer; and other items like animal feed, bricks, and oak barrels. ThaiBev's Thai Alcohol company is the country's largest producer of ethanol, which refiners like Shell and Exxon Mobil turn into alternative fuels. ThaiBev was created in 2003, though it traces its roots to 1786, when the Bangyikhan Liquor Distillery began operation.
