Henry Group Holdings Limited (formerly known as Zida Computer Technologies) believes that the computer industry holds a lot of promise. Henry Group is an investment holding company with subsidiaries that operate in the manufacturing, marketing, and research and development of computer motherboards, networking products, computer components, and peripherals. Director Ng Chun controls a majority of the voting power of the company through Henry Jewellery Holdings Limited.
