Wondering who makes the 12-foot airblown Easter Bunny that appears on your neighbor's lawn each holiday? Thanks go to Gemmy Industries, which manufactures a wide variety of outdoor airblown products, including holiday and animated characters, movie screens, and costumes. The company also makes holiday decorations (indoor and outdoor) and animated gifts, as well as novelty and seasonal items, including the Dancing Hamsters, Turtle Dancers, and Frogz. The company gained fame with its animatronic singing Big Mouth Billy Bass. Its licensing partners include Peanuts, Sesame Street, and Disney . Gemmy's products are sold through national chains including J. C. Penney , Target , Toys 'R Us , and Wal-Mart . The company was founded in 1984.
