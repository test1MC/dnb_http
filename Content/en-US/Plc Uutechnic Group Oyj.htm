Uutechnic Group's business is worth the paper its customers write on. The Finnish company (formerly Vaahto Group) manufactures industrial machines that are used by the paper, board, and pulp industry. Uutechnic's rebuild services are particularly popular -- refurbishment of paper, board, and pulp drying machinery is among its most-requested services. It additionally designs agitators, pressure vessels, and heat exchangers for the process industry, and produces stainless channels and drains. Uutechnic was established in 1874 and markets its products to industrial clients throughout Europe, China, and North America.
