Diversified conglomerate LOTTE Holdings, with interests in retail, sports, and restaurants, is best known as one of Japan's leading confectioners. Its Lotte Co. unit makes a wide array of candies and other treats, including gum, chocolate, candy, ice cream, and cookies. LOTTE also owns fast-food chain, Lotteria, which has locations in Japan, South Korea, and Taiwan. It also owns several retail chains including Korea's LOTTESHOPPING . In addition, LOTTE owns the Chiba Lotte Marines professional baseball club. Other business interests include soft drinks, hotels, golf courses, and real estate. Named after Charlotte, a character created by German author Johann Goethe, the company was formed in 1948.
