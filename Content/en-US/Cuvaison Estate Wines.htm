Cuvaison Estate Wines produces a variety of red and white wines from its vineyards located in the northern Napa Valley region of California. The vintner's wines (some 63,000 cases a year, 65%of which is chardonnay) are sold across the US and overseas, and are available online through the company's Web site. Cuvaison Estate's wines are also available in 25 international markets and on airlines such as British Airways and China Airlines ) and on cruise ships including those of Holland America , Seabourn , and Carnival .
