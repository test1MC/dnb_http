Fifty Fifty Music (formerly 1-800-Prime CD) is a record label with an eclectic roster that includes musicians such as Aztec Two-Step, Chris Chandler, Mindy Jostyn, and Northern Lights Band. The company's music can be purchased through its Fifty Fifty Music Online Store. Music producer and company president David Seitz has engineered recordings for a wide range of artists, including Dar Williams and Jeff Buckley.
