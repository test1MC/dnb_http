What is black and white and read all over Hong Kong? Why, the Oriental Daily News , Oriental Press Group's flagship newspaper. First published in 1969, today the Oriental Daily News is one of Hong Kong's most popular papers, with an average daily readership of more than 1 million. Oriental Press also publishes The Sun , another of Hong Kong's best-selling newspapers. Launched in 1999, The Sun attracts younger readers with a trendy, youthful voice; some 1 million people read The Sun daily and about 30,000 subscribe to its e-paper version. Oriental Press Group also dabbles in property holding and leasing, printing, human-resource services, and Web-site services.
