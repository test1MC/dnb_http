Crompton Greaves (CG) is one of the largest private sector enterprises in India and is engaged in the design, manufacture, and marketing of advanced electrical products and services related to power generation, transmission, and distribution. CG caters to the needs of utility, electricity, and high-tech companies, as well as consumer products makers. The company's three business divisions are Power Systems (transformers, switchgear), Industrial Systems (motors), and Consumer Products (fans, light sources, pumps). In late 2014 it announced plans to demerge its Consumer Products operations. CG has manufacturing facilities in nine countries and operations in 85 countries across Asia, Europe and North America.
