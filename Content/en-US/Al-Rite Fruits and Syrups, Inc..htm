Al-Rite Fruits and Syrups wants your margarita to taste al-right. The company manufactures beverage concentrates and syrups for fruit drinks, ice cream toppings, sno-cones, liquid teas, hot chocolate, and energy and functional drinks. It also makes ready-to-drink cocktails (Blood Mary, Long Island Tea and others) under the Steve's brand; however, the firm specializes in private-label non-alcoholic concentrate products and boasts that it can manufacture "just about anything drinkable" (other than carbonated and diary products). Al-Rite's customers include wholesale food, foodservice, and other food-manufacturing companies in the Americas. Located in Miami, Al-Rite Fruits and Syrups was founded in 1970.
