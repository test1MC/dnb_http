At Mediatech the medium is more of a goo than a message. The company develops, manufactures, and distributes molecular biology and cell culture reagents used by research labs. Its customers include pharmaceutical companies, government agencies, and academic institutions. Products are sold under the Corning cellgro brand, and include cell culture media, reagents, salt solutions, antibiotics, and bioprocess collection containers. The firm also provides custom formulation and contract manufacturing. Its products are sold directly in the US and through third-party distributors internationally. Formed in 1984, Mediatech was acquired by glassmaker Corning in 2011 and became part of the Corning Life Sciences division.
