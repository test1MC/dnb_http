Omitec tries to make auto makers and mechanics omniscient when it comes to car trouble. The company offers a range of diagnostic equipment and services used at every stage of an automobile's life cycle. Car manufacturers use the company's equipment to ensure that their designs for everything from engine exhaust systems to power windows work in the real world. Mechanics use other gear to detect problems after cars have seen some use. Omitec's products include its flagship OmiScan diagnostic tool that covers systems such as climate control, brake systems, suspension, and security on vehicles from about 30 manufacturers. Omitec has five locations in the UK, as well as offices in Frankfurt, Paris, Detroit, and Shanghai.
