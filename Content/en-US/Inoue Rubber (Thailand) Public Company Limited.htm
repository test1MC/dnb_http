Inoue Rubber (Thailand) has built its business by tiring out its customers. The company makes a wide range of industrial elastomer parts (gaskets, valves, hoses, seals, and other components) for cars and motorcycles, as well as tires and tubes for motorcycles and scooters. Inoue also makes gaskets, valves, and other rubber products for use outside the auto industry. The company manufactures IRC branded motorcycle tires and tubes for sale directly to motorcycle manufacturers and dealers, and supplies wheel set assemblies for Thai Suzuki and Thai Yamaha. Other clients include Honda , Nissan , Yamaha , and Toyota , among others.
