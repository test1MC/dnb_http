Singha , Leo , Thai Beer -- these are four words you'll likely hear on the lips of the Thai beer connoisseur, and Boon Rawd Brewery is responsible for supplying them. Boon Rawd is Thailand's oldest and largest brewery, established in 1933. Since then the company has grown to produce more than 800 million liters of beer annually under the Singha, Kloster, Leo, and Thai Beer brand names. Boon Rawd doesn't stop at beer, though; it also makes soda water, drinking water, and ready-to-drink green tea. The Boon Rawd network encompasses three breweries and six water factories located across the country.
