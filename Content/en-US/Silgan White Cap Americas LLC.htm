Silgan White Cap Americas, part of Silgan Holdings ' global closures business, concentrates on keeping products in -- and non-products out -- by making metal, plastic, and composite vacuum closures for bottles, cans, and jugs. Typical closure applications include beverages, condiments, pickles, sauces, soups, beer and liquor, vegetables, fruit, baby food, preserves, and dairy products. The unit also provides sealing and capping equipment for use by its customers. Silgan White Cap Americas serves the food and beverage industry in North and South America, while Silgan White Cap Europe/Asia in Germany serves the Europe and Asia-Pacific markets.

Silgan Holdings acquired former partner Amcor 's stake in US joint venture Amcor White Cap in 2003 and renamed it Silgan Closures; Silgan had previously held a minority stake in the company. The unit was part of the Silgan Containers division until Silgan Holdings acquired Amcor's international White Cap operations in 2006 and 2007. The businesses have been reunited under the name Silgan White Cap, with the Europe/Asia business (Silgan White Cap Deutschland GmbH) headquartered in Germany and the North American and South American operations (Silgan White Cap Americas LLC) headquartered in Illinois.
