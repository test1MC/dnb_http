Sound quality is paramount for Parasound Products. The company makes high-end audio equipment including tuners, amplifiers, speakers, and related accessories for stereo and home entertainment systems. Its electronics are grouped into four product lines by function and price range: Halo (highest quality and price), NewClassic (a redesign of traditional products), Zcustom (compact components), and C-series (architectural speakers). Its items come with a 10-year warranty. Founded in 1981, Parasound products are sold in retail and specialty electronics stores around the world.
