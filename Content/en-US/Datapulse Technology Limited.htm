Datapulse Technology has its finger on the pulse of Singapore's digital storage needs. The company manufactures storage media, including CDs, DVDs, and diskettes. Datapulse Technology also offers mastering, replication, and packaging services. Customers include computer software, hardware, and game makers, as well as companies in the home video and music industries. Founded in 1980, Datapulse Technology expanded its reach into Taiwan in 2000 by establishing subsidiary Datapulse Technology (Taiwan). The company is expanding into new product types (DVD-R) and locations across the Asia/Pacific region.
