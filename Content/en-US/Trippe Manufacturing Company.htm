Trippe Manufacturing trips the light fantastic by selling protection from power trips. Doing business as Tripp Lite, the privately owned company makes more than 3,000 products used to protect, power, and connect electronic equipment. Its surge suppressors guard against surges, spikes, and over-voltages that can damage personal computers and other electronic equipment. The company's uninterruptible power supply (UPS) systems provide battery backup power, while its inverters are used to power laptops and other products when other power sources are not available. Tripp Lite products also include power strips, cables and connectors, laptop accessories, and power-management software.

Tripp Lite sells its wares from 80 locations in more than 60 countries worldwide.

The company's portfolio includes UPS systems, surge protectors, cables and connectors, power inverters and inverter/chargers, KVM switches, power strips, power distribution units, rack and rack cooling systems, power management software, UPS replacement batteries, laptop accessories, audio/video systems, as well as specific products for healthcare, government, and education segments.

In addition to consumer and home office products, Tripp Lite supplies equipment for use in environments where access to reliable power can be critical, such as health care and industrial facilities. Other markets the company targets include telecommunications, networking, construction, maintenance, government, education, and global enterprises.

Tripp Light's products are sold through authorized distributors including Tech Data , Ingram Micro , D&H , and SYNNEX . They are also sold through reseller partners and exporters such as Avnet , Super Technologies, and Intcomex Miami.

Tripp Light aims to increase its market share in the Middle East, India, Russia, and other emerging markets by partnering with local distributors and through installations at major organizations including HP , Cisco , and McDonald's .

It also maintains its growth by introducing new products within its vast portfolio. In 2014 the company introduced a 16-port USB charger with syncing function; a four-port USB charging station that doubles as an On-The-Go hub; and expanded its line of premium fiber cables for high-density data center applications.

In 2013 it launched three new power strips under the Power It! brand and five new surge suppressors under the Protect It! brand.

Founded in 1922, Trippe Manufacturing initially made automotive headlights. The company introduced the first UPS system designed for personal computers in the 1980s.
