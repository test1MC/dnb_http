Instrument Technology Limited (formerly Caburn-MDC Europe) does not make items that create music -- it instead manufactures ultra-high vacuum components. The company's product line includes more than 2,000 components, ranging from basic nuts and bolts to flanges, fittings, valves, feedthroughs, manipulation devices, and a variety of thin-film deposition and sputtering tools. Instrument Technology also provides custom engineering and fabrication services. Primarily serving customers in the electronics manufacturing industry, the company has operations and sales offices in France, Germany, Italy, and the UK. It traces its historical roots back to 1991.
