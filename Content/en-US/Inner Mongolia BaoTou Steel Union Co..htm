Inner Mongolia BaoTou Steel Union, part of the Baogang Group, makes steel rods, beams, columns, and plates. The firm also makes heavy tracks, square steel, and seamless pipes. The union sells steel processing machinery and machine parts and offers steel and iron production consulting services. Northern China is a main market. The Baogang Group, which boasts a strategic alliance with Baoshan Iron & Steel, produces about five million tons of steel products each year, most of which consists of steel rails for China's high-speed train system. It also operates a rare earth mine that produces niobioum and thorium; it inked restructuring agreements in 2014 with nine rare earth upstream businesses.
