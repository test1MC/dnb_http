Robert Bentley wants you to know your engine from your axle. The company publishes automobile manuals and other transportation-related books and items through its Bentley Publishers unit. Book topics include automobile histories ( Porsche: Excellence Was Expected , Volkswagen Beetle: Portrait of a Legend ) and technical and how-to books ( Jeep Owner's Bible, Volkswagen Sport Tuning for Street and Competition ). Its Web site offers a forum for technical discussions. In addition, Robert Bentley has a book retail location in Boston. The company was formed in 1950 by Robert Bentley and is operated by his son, Michael.
