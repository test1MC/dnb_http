SGK LLC (formerly Schawk) has designs on consumers' products. The company provides digital prepress and other graphic services primarily for consumer product packaging, advertising, and point-of-sale marketing. It offers digital imaging, color separations, electronic retouching, and platemaking services for lithography, gravure, and flexography. Ultimately the company is responsible for manipulating images used to entice consumers to buy a product. The family of founder and chairman Clarence Schawk owns a controlling stake (more than 50%) in the company. It has been in operation since 1953 and was acquired by Matthews International in 2014.

SGK operates from more than 155 facilities and more than 20 countries in the Asia/Pacific region, Europe, North America, and South America.

Because its work is delivered through multiple mediums, SGK classifies its business into four categories. "At home" includes catalogs, circulars, and direct mail; "at the store" covers point-of-sale displays, in-store merchandising, and interactive displays; "on the shelf" refers to packaging; and "on the go" includes outdoor and mobile advertising. Graphic services account for most of SGK's sales. In addition, the company provides brand strategy and creative design services under the Anthem Group brand, as well as digital asset management, workflow management, and online proofing software.

The company markets its services nationally and internationally through its website, social media, media engagement, and highly focused marketing programs targeted at existing and potential clients.

SGK sells its services through a group of approximately 200 direct salespeople who call on consumer products manufacturers in the food and beverage, home products, pharmaceutical, and cosmetics industries.

SGK reported $798.3 million in revenue for fiscal 2015, compared to $497.3 million in fiscal 2014.
