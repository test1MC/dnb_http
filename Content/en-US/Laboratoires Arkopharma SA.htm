Laboratoires Arkopharma's products offer a glimpse of modern worries: Its offerings include ampoules of fat reducers, tablets to help quit smoking, creams to tackle cellulite, and capsules to alleviate anxiety and stress. Arkopharma produces plant-derived OTC drugs and nutritional supplements. The company also manufactures homeopathic remedies and cosmetics. Arkopharma markets its products under a variety of brands, including Arkocaps, Arkofluide, 4.3.2.1. Slim, Phyto Soya, and Plante System. Subsidiaries of the company can be found in Australia, Europe, and the UK. European private equity firm Montagu acquired control of Arkopharma from the founding Rombi family in mid-2014.

Montagu plans to help develop Arkopharma, taking advantage of the fractured natural medicine market.

Arkopharma is one of Europe's leading phytotherapy (using medicinal plants to promote health) firms; it holds more than 1,850 product licenses. The company benefits from the public's growing taste for natural medicines.

Arkopharma sells its products in 60 countries, primarily in Europe. Most of its manufacturing is done in France; the company also has subsidiaries in Belgium, Ireland, Italy, the Netherlands, Spain, and Switzerland.

Arkopharma was founded in 1968.
