How do you say "Avon calling!" in Japanese? How about Pola? Pola Orbis is the holding company for two prestigious skin care lines in Japan -- Pola and Orbis. Pola's pricey skin care products and cosmetics are sold door-to-door through a network of 135,000 direct sales representatives, who are known as "Pola ladies." The company also operates 530 retail locations. The Orbis line, known for its oil-free products, is sold primarily online and through mail order. The company also distributes ORLANE, a French brand, and sells cosmetics under the brands decencia, Future Labo, pdc, and Three. In addition, Pola Orbis owns US-based skin, body, and hair care line H2O and Australian organic skin care line Jurlique .
