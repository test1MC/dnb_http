Artsana knows that taking care of the family can be considered an art form. The company (whose name combines the Italian words for "art" and "heals") makes products for health, beauty, and baby that are distributed worldwide. Artsana's health-related offerings include such consumer items as Control brand condoms, Lycia sanitary pads, and Serenity incontinence products. Under the Chicco, Boppy, and neoBaby labels, the company markets clothing, toys, and feeding and hygiene essentials. Its beauty lines are geared to women and infants. Additionally, Artsana makes clinical health-care supplies through its Pic Indolor subsidiary.
