Olidata manufactures and sells desktop PCs, graphics workstations, notebook computers, and servers designed for customers ranging from individual consumers to large enterprises. Its primary market is in Europe, where it sells its products under its own name. Corporate clients include Enel and INPS. The company also offers LCD televisions and computer peripherals, such as monitors and storage drives. Olidata is geographically expanding, selling its wares in France, Germany, and the Netherlands. While sales volumes are up, the weakness of the dollar against the euro has driven revenues down. Acer Europe, a subsidiary of Acer , owns nearly 30% of Olidata.
