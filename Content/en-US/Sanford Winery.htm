Sanford Winery makes Sanford Pinot Noir, La Rinconada Pinot Noir, and other wines from four estate vineyards in California's Santa Ynez Valley. The Terlato family has a majority ownership in Sanford Winery, which credits itself as the first to plant Pinot Noir in California's Santa Barbara County. The Terlato Wine group invested in Sanford Winery in 2002 taking its majority interest in 2005 and helping the winery expand its operations. Though the winery still bears his name, Richard Sanford no longer works there. He has moved on to other endeavors.
