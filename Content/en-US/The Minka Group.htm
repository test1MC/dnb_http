Light and air are the two elements that dominate the world of The Minka Group, which manufactures and distributes lighting and ceiling fan fixtures through more than 2,100 dealers in the US. The company has an extensive product line from chandeliers to table lamps, floor lamps to wall sconces, and mirrors to wall art, as well as heaters and dehumidifiers. Minka markets its products under the Ambience, George Kovacs, GK Lightrail, The Great Outdoors, Metropolitan, Minka-Aire, and Minka Lavery brand names. The company has over 500 patents for its lighting creations. Customers range from independent lighting showroom dealers to larger accounts such as Home Depot and Lowe's .
