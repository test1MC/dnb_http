If this company has its way, traditional keys, passwords, and ID cards will become obsolete. RCG Holdings develops biometric and RFID (radio frequency identification) security and surveillance products for commercial and personal use. Products include ready-made and custom solutions that require facial or fingerprint verification to gain access including door locks, security systems, and employee time clocks. The company targets multiple industries including health care, banking, entertainment (theme parks and stadiums), and retail. RCG focuses on Asia/Pacific markets, but sells through nearly 1,900 distributors and dealers worldwide, including in the US. Manufacturing facilities are in Malaysia.
