Taihan keeps Korea and the world wired. Taihan Electric Wire provides a wide variety of products such as stainless steel coils and sheets, magnet wire, extra high voltage underground cables, copper rod, optical fiber cables, and control cable. Among Taihan Electric Wire's services areas are engineering, system design, testing, and installation. The company serves clients in the power, communications, and metal industries among others. Taihan is also seeking to expand into home networking, solar power generation, and the rental and leisure business. Founded in 1955, Taihan has subsidiaries in Canada, China, Mongolia, South Africa, and the US. Samyang Metal Co. leads a group of investors who own 55% of Taihan.
