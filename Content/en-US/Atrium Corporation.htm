The way to Atrium's heart? Right through that sliding door. Atrium Corporation (formerly Atrium Companies) makes and distributes vinyl and aluminum windows and patio doors for residential use. Its brands include Atrium, Champion Window, North Star, Superior Windows, and Thermal Industries impact-resistant windows, doors, and shutters. Its products are used by builders, distributors, dealers, lumberyards, contractors, home centers, and architects. Atrium also offers installation and repair services. The company traces its historical roots back to 1948.

The company operates four manufacturing plants, each at Union Gap, Washington; Denver; Dallas; and Welcome, North Carolina.

Atrium Corporation offers products to builders, remodeling contractors and distributors, and national home center retailers and homebuilders in the residential construction industry. It targets builders, distributors, dealers, lumberyards, home centers, contractors, and architects in the new construction and replacement/remodel sectors.
