Daheng New Epoch Technology is taking China's high-tech industry into the information age. Through a number of subsidiaries, the company makes a variety of electronic products, including optical, laser and infrared components; IT and office automation equipment; computer hardware and software; satellite positioning and navigation systems; household electronics; and medical equipment. The company also makes equipment supporting digital television systems in China. Serving the domestic Chinese market, Daheng New Epoch Technology sells its products primarily in the cities of Beijing, Shanghai, and Zhejiang.
