Microsemi Corp. - Rf Integrated Solutions (formerly AML Communications) wants to pump up the volume with its microwave amplifiers for wireless communications. The amplifiers support defense-related radar, satellite, and surveillance systems, as well as commercial wireless applications. It also makes higher-power microwave amplifiers for the defense market through subsidiary Microwave Power. Customers include defense equipment manufacturers (such as Raytheon and Boeing ), systems integrators, and commercial wireless operators ( AT&T Mobility and Verizon Wireless ). In 2011 AML was acquired by Microsemi for about $28 million.
