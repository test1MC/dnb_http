This company is a sure winner. America's Cup Coffee manufactures and distributes cappuccino and other coffee products to foodservice operators such as convenience stores, as well as businesses in the food and beverage wholesaling sector throughout the US. However, the company does business primarily with agencies and departments of the US government. America's Cup Coffee has contracts with the US Army and the US Air Force . Located in Slidell, Louisiana, and privately owned by its founder and president, Bob Barrett, the company has been in business for more than 10 years.
