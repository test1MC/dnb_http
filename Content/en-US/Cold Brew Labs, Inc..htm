Take an old-fashioned concept like a pin board then make it modern by putting it online and you get Pinterest. Cold Brew Labs, which operates the Pinterest social-networking-via-shared-images website, hit on a new way for people to connect. Users, who can join by invitation only, bookmark interesting, beautiful, or otherwise share-worthy things they find online and sort them into boards so others can peruse, like, comment on, and repin them. The site's traffic has been growing significantly lifting it to #3 behind Facebook and Twitter ; it's become so popular even the US Army is pinning with more than 25 boards of its own. Cold Brew Labs, formed in 2008, also builds mobile shopping applications.
