When businesses need help handling money they turn to a chip off the old block, Block and Co. The family-owned business manufactures and distributes money-handling supplies and security equipment primarily to financial institutions and, to a lesser degree, gaming, hospitality, and vending businesses, libraries, and restaurants across the US. Products -- almost everything needed to operate -- include money bags; coin and currency-handling materials such as cash boxes and teller supplies; and bank drive-through envelopes and ATM supplies. Block and Co. manufactures a significant number of the products that it offers.

The company operates facilities in Wheeling, Illinois, and Bristol, Tennessee and has customers in more than 69 countries.

The company's products include coin and currency handling, check handling and document storage, coin and currency bags, drive-up and ATM supplies, forms, imaging supplies, signage, lobby supplies and crowd control products, identification and security products and office, breakroom and janitorial supplies.

Its divisions include MMF Industries, and MMF POS (Point of Sale).

MMF Industries (which has more than 70 years of metal fabrication experience) offers a full line of cash handling products, key cabinets, and desk accessories.

The MMF POS division supplies cash drawers and accessories to POS/Retail distributors and OEMs.

Block and Co.'s My Block Power Suite enables customers with multiple branches to manage their online orders. It offers controls for authorizing users, setting spending limits and approved product purchases, consolidating orders, and tracking shipments.

Through dealers, an online store, and a product catalog, the company distributes cash handling and office supplies and equipment to businesses in a number of industries, including banking, gaming, hospitality, vending, restaurant, educational, and business services.

Some of its dealers include BFS, Crowd Control Company, SHOPLET, and Staples .

Block and Co. has focused on expanding its offerings for handling cash as well as handling its customers. Through new programs and industry-leading partnerships it is strengthening its focus on providing innovative products, backed up by superior customer service.

On the supply side, Block and Co.'s website hosts more than 200 money-handling related products. It has introduced a see-through locking security bag. The Ultima Seven Clear View Security Bag, which is largely sold to businesses that use security cameras in their cash rooms or processing centers, touts assurance that the bag will be completely emptied, as well as a pick-resistant metal zipper and rip-stop material construction.

The family-owned and women-owned corporation was founded in 1934 by Mitchell Block.
