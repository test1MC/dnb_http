Star Micronics America, the US branch of Japan's Star Micronics , provides point-of-sale (POS) printers, thermal card reader/writer systems, and audio components. Its dot matrix and thermal printers are used for printing receipts and other business forms. The company also sells printers for use in kiosks, as well as printer mechanisms used in applications ranging from ATMs to medical devices. Star Micronics America's audio components line includes microphones, speakers, electronic buzzers, receivers, and transducers. Star Micronics established its US subsidiary in 1976.

Star Micronics America offers a line of kiosk printers and releases a new product about twice a year. The company's printers can be found in ATMs, boarding pass kiosks, gift registries, gas pumps, and DVD burner kiosks.

In 2016 Star Micronics released the CD3 Cash Drawer Series in White and TSP100III for point-of-sale printing. In 2015 it expanded the Cash Drawer Line With the CD3 Tradition Series.
