Lorentzen & Wettre (L&W) follows the paper trail worldwide to serve its customers. A subsidiary of industrial door giant Cardo AB (itself part of lock maker ASSA ABLOY , since 2011), the Swedish manufacturer provides quality control and product-testing equipment for the pulp and paper industry. L&W makes process optimization machines (permeability meters, moisture sensors), laboratory automation (computer testing software), testing devices (micrometers, resistance testers), and sample preparation equipment (cutters, trimmers) used to measure and check the size and strength of paper products. It also provides maintenance, training, and customer support services. ABB acquired L&W in late 2011.
