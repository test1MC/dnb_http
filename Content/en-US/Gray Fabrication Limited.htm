Gray Fabrication specializes in rolling and forming metal plate using hydraulic brake presses and plate rollers into cans, cones, tubular, and unconventional shapes. The specialty manufacturing company's services include cutting, materials supply, plate and pipe rolling, steel fabrication, and welding. Gray fabricates materials used for general engineering, offshore energy, and pressure vessel applications among others, and it makes and warehouses its products at its main facility in the UK. The company primarily serves customers in Canada, Norway, and the UK.

The company's specialties includes rolled or formed cans, cones, multi-can tubulars, and other custom-made shapes.

In 2014 Gray secured a contract from Scotrenewables Tidal Power to make the hull for the world's most powerful tidal turbine.

The company was founded in 1979 in Perth, and moved to the present site at Cupar in 1989.
