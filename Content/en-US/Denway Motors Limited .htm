Denway Motors keeps things motoring in China. The investment holding company's subsidiaries and associated companies manufacture, assemble, and sell motor vehicles and electrical equipment and components for automobiles in China. The company also makes and sells audio equipment in Hong Kong. Denway Motors is comprised of two companies, Guangzhou Storage Battery Enterprise and Shanghai Guangchee Automotive Trading and Services; jointly controlled Guangzhou Honda Automobile; and seven directly held subsidiaries. The group produces nearly 300,000 vehicles per year. Parent Guangzhou Automobile Group plans to go pubic via Denway, which will be privatized in the transaction.
