Makers of both bagels and beer rely on the malt products of Malt Products. The company supplies malt in dry and liquid forms for use primarily in the baking and brewing industries. It offers both organic as well as conventional varieties. In addition to malt, Malt Products and its International Molasses unit offer agave, brown rice syrup, corn syrup, high-fructose syrup, and molasses. Malt Products has manufacturing plants in the US (New Jersey and Ohio) and Canada (Ontario), as well as in the UK and the Netherlands. The company's products are available worldwide; it has sales offices in the US, Europe, Asia, Africa, and South America.
