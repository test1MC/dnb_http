San-Ai Oil is all about energy. The company has its hands in several petroleum related businesses including San-Ai gas stations; gasoline, kerosene, oil, liquefied petroleum gas, and gas appliances sales to businesses; and supplying airplane fuel to the Haneda airport where the company has proprietary refilling equipment. San-Ai also makes and sells chemicals used in antiseptics and fungicides and some of its subsidiaries offer consulting services to help companies reduce their energy consumption as well as construction, building management, and insurance. San-Ai, which is part of the Ricoh Group, purchased Exxon Mobil partner Kygnus Oil in 2004.
