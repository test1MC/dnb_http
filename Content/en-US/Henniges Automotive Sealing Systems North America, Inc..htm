Road warriors worldwide thank Henniges Automotive for their peace and comfort. The company designs and manufactures a slew of automotive sealing systems, as well as a glass encapsulation and modular systems. Henniges' lineup includes inner and outer seals, integrated glassrun and quarterlight systems, and engine compartment seals. It operates about a dozen plants in North America and China that also produce windshield, sunroof, and fixed side glass, and body moldings, such as backlight modules, and liftgate and obstacle detection systems. Auto giants GM , Chrysler , Ford , and BMW are Henniges' customers. In 2010 private equity Littlejohn & Co. acquired the sealing systems maker from Wynnchurch Capital Partners.
