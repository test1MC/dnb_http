Cementir - Cementerie del Tirreno SpA is working to build a concrete future. The company makes and sells Portland cement as well as concrete and related products, such as composite concretes made for use in blast furnaces and other applications. Founded in 1947, it sells products primarily to sister companies in the Caltagirone Group, of which Cementir is a subsidiary. Its cement is used in water projects, bridges, and dams as well as in roads, tunnels, and other infrastructure. Cementir has operations spanning four continents in 15 countries, including China, Denmark, Egypt, Malaysia, Turkey, and the US. Each year it sells around 9.7 million tons of cement and 3.2 million tons of aggregates.

Turkey is Cemintir's largest market, generating 27% of total sales during 2013. Other major markets include Denmark (25%), Italy (11%), Egypt (5%), the Far East (7%), and other Scandinavian countries (21%).
