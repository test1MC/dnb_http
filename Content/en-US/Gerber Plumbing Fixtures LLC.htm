No cute babies here: Gerber Plumbing Fixtures manufactures residential  plumbing products, including vitreous china sinks, toilets, and bidets in more than 20 colors and styles. It also makes faucets, drains, fittings, and accessories for kitchens, bathrooms, and laundry rooms. The company has a line of toilets, lavatories, urinals, and mechanical and electronic faucets and flush valves for commercial clients such as hotels, schools, and health care facilities. It sells to builders, plumbers, and homeowners through wholesale distributors across the US and Canada. Gerber is majority-owned by home-improvement product maker and distributor Globe Union Group.
