What's in a name? If yours is Springfield, it carries more than 200 years of firepower behind it. The firm, which does business as Springfield Armory, makes pistols and rifles (including the 1911, M1A, and XD brands) for personal, military, law enforcement, and competitive use. Its Springfield Custom unit builds guns with customer-specified upgrades and modifications. While its firearms are primarily sold through US retailers and international distributors, Springfield offers logoed apparel and sporting accessories, such as scopes and cases, on its Web site. The original Springfield Armory was designated as the country's first arsenal by George Washington in 1794.
