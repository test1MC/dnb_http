COFCO East Ocean Oils & Grains Industries (Zhangjiagang) Co., Ltd (formerly Eastocean Oils & Grains Industries) has found its fortune in oil -- the edible kind. The company manufactures, processes, and refines soybean, wheat, and rice from which it produces oils, flour, and a variety of other foods. It sells its products under the Fortune, VITOIL, and Four Seas brand names. In addition to serving the domestic market, it exports selected products throughout Asia. COFCO East Ocean Oils & Grains Industries (Zhangjiagang) Co., Ltd is a joint venture between Chinese companies state-owned China National Cereals, Oils, and Foodstuffs (COFCO) and Wilmar International .
