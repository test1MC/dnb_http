MTS Medication Technologies gives pills a safe home while they head to the patient who needs them. The company makes proprietary medication packaging systems and related products. Institutional pharmacies use them to conveniently and accurately package the drugs they give to patients; the company's custom technology allows caregivers at some 6,000 pharmacies located within assisted-living, nursing, and correctional facilities to administer medicine or vitamins to many people while keeping close tabs on dosages. Retail pharmacies also use MTS' products in medication distribution. MTS is a subsidiary of Omnicell .

In addition to 50 types of drug packaging consumables, the company sells a line of about 20 packaging machines. Products include labels, printers, and other related products. It makes automated machines for manual and semi-manual dispensing, as well as a fully automated system (dubbed OnDemand).

The company's MTS-350 is a table-top machine that fills prescriptions, while the MTS-400 handles larger volumes. Other items include the MTS-500, designed for high volumes and automated pre-packaging and labeling.

The company operates its business in the US, the UK, and Germany. It has distributors in Australia, France, Belgium, the United Arab Emirates, Ireland, and Canada.

The company's largest customers include drug wholesalers such as McKesson , Cardinal Health , and AmerisourceBergen . MTS Medication Technologies operates its own sales organization in the US.

Customers fall in the institutional, retail, and long-term care markets.

MTS Medication Technologies is part of Omnicell's Medication Adherence segment, which contributed about 20% of the parent's revenue in 2015.

Considered to be a leader in adherence packaging systems in Europe, the company generates most of its sales in the US, where it has its own sales organization. It has also been working to expand its distribution network in international markets, specifically in Australia, France, Belgium, the United Arab Emirates, Ireland, and Canada. To maintain its market share, MTS Medication Technologies regularly pumps out new products.
