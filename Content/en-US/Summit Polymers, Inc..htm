Plastic that's molded-to-order is Summit Polymers' high purpose. The company engineers, manufactures, and assembles thermoplastic injection-molded automotive interior trim. Its products include air conditioning registers and consoles, and instrument panels, primarily for SUVs and trucks. In addition to its manufacturing plants in Kentucky and Michigan, Summit Polymers operates a design and sales office, and a tech center, as well as manufacturing facilities in Matamoros, Mexico, and Nanjing, China. The collapse in automotive industry sales drove the supplier in 2009 to close a Tennessee plant.
