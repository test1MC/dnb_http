Even irrational people might enjoy a well-cooked meal. Rational AG is a top maker of industrial cooking appliances used in professional kitchens. Its SelfCookingCenter and CombiMaster Plus are self-cleaning, programmable ovens that control time, temperature, and humidity. They range from tabletop-size to as large as vending machines, and can cook up to 500 meals per day. French subsidiary FRIMA manufactures the complementary VarioCooking Center, which instantly cooks food in liquid (for boiling, frying, or poaching). Rational AG's ovens cook food faster and use less space and energy than traditional appliances. The company, which was founded in 1973, sells its products in more than 100 countries.
