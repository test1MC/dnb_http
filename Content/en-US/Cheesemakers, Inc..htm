Cheesemakers has the cows and goats to thank. The company produces fresh cow and goat cheeses from locally raised and range-fed animals. It sells Mexican-, Texas-, and farmer-style cheeses, along with mozzarella, goat's milk cheeses, milk, and yogurts. Its brand names include under the Lone Star, Jaimito, and Texas Fresh brands. The company also sells bulk deli cheeses. Its products can be found at grocers such as HEB , Kroger , and Whole Foods . Cheesemakers also sells its cheeses to restaurants and hotels. The family-owned and -operated company was founded in 1997 by general manager, James Keliehor.
