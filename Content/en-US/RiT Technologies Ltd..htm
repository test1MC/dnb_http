RiT tames tangles. RiT Technologies' PatchView and PairView cable management systems are used to organize and track network computer cable connections. The products combine hardware and software to show when, where, and why connections within a computer network break. The systems include a control panel, scanners, and smart cables, and are used by Internet, cable, and telephone service providers. RiT's SMART system combines outlets, plugs, cords, and patch panels for managing cabling in large commercial buildings. Customers include Deutsche Telekom , Fujitsu , and O2 Germany . Russia's STINS COMAN, which distributes RiT's products in the Commonwealth of Independent States, owns about 60% of the company.
