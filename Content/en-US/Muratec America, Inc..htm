Muratec America, a subsidiary of Japan's Murata Machinery , represents its parent in North America and South America. The company sells fax machines and multifunctional office equipment that combine copy, print, scan, and fax functions. It also sells document management systems that combine hardware and software to speed the handling of documents across corporate networks. James D'Emidio became president of Muratec America in 2008; he joined the company in 1987. Murata established Murata Business Systems in 1982 to sell its fax machines under private-label deals with US companies and began selling fax machines under the Murata brand three years later. The subsidiary changed its name to Muratec America in 1992.
