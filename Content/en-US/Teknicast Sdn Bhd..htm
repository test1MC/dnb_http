Technically speaking, Teknicast makes die cast and machined aluminum alloy components used in the manufacture of motorcycles, automobiles, office equipment, and industrial machinery. The company's motorcycle products include cylinder heads, hubs, brake levers, clutch covers, and cowling fans. Automotive products include compressor cylinder heads and starter and wiper motor housings. Most of Teknicast's production is used to manufacture end-products for its customers' Malaysian subsidiaries. These customers include Suzuki , Robert Bosch , DENSO , and ZF Friedrichshafen . Teknicast also exports appliance and measuring equipment parts to customers in China, Germany, Singapore, the US, and the UK.
