MTI Electronics makes PCBs, not acronyms. The contract electronics manufacturer provides printed circuit board (PCB) assembly and encapsulation, liquid-crystal display (LCD) touch screen integration, and box build (complete system) manufacturing services. MTI also provides in-circuit, functional, optical inspection, and ionic contamination testing services. In addition, the company can provide thick film hybrid circuit prototyping and high volume production services. Customers include companies in the medical, telecommunications, military, automotive, and industrial markets, among others. MTI was established in 1976 as Micro-Technology Inc. The company operates from a facility located in Wisconsin.
