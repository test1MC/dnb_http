Who'd have guessed? ORC Foods did. The company, formerly The Original Rangoon Company, makes crab rangoon, the Asian-inspired appetizer that's purely an American invention. It also makes other wonton-wrapped frozen foods, as well as seafood appetizer, such as bacon-wrapped scallops and shrimp. In addition to seafood, ORC offers meat-based appetizers, including Philly-style steak-and-cheese egg rolls. It has branched out into other cuisines and food types as well, adding fresh falafel to its offerings. ORC's products are sold at US supermarkets and warehouse clubs, as well as at US military commissaries around the globe. The company supplies foodservice operators with a line of appetizers.
