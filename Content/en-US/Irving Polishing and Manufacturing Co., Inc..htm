A polished performer and an accomplished fabricator. Not just the description of a successful national politician, but also of Irving Polishing and Manufacturing. The company fabricates and polishes stainless steel fittings, filters, pumps, valves, and tubing for the beverage, biotech, pharmaceutical, and semiconductor industries. Products include long radius stainless steel plumbing elbows, manifolds, miniature fittings, special valves, tanks and Y joints. Irving Polishing and Manufacturing operates a 25,000 sq. ft. facility in an industrial park in Kenosha, Wisconsin.
