We all know what pet is man's best friend. But did you know BOPET is Fuwei Films' best friend? The company develops, manufactures, and distributes high-end plastic film known as BOPET (biaxially oriented polyethylene terephthalate) film. Its products include stamping, aluminum plating, laser holographic, matte, and high-gloss film. They're largely used to package processed foods, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, tobacco, and alcohol, but are also used in the imaging, electronics, and magnetic products industries. Fuwei Films sells to more than 300 customers primarily in China, but also in Canada, India, South Korea, and the US.
