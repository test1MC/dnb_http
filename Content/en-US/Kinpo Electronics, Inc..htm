Kinpo Electronics makes addition as easy as 1-2-3. From its initial success as one of the world's leading manufacturers of electronic calculators, Kinpo has gone on to develop more advanced digital gadgets, including fax machines, tablet PCs, printers, electronic dictionaries, storage products, digital still and video cameras, cable modems, and GPS devices. The company has delivered its products and services to the likes of Texas Instruments , Hewlett-Packard , Casio , Ricoh , and Canon . Its products are marketed throughout Asia, the US, and Europe. Kinpo Electronics was formed in 1973 and operates factories in Taiwan, China, and Thailand.
