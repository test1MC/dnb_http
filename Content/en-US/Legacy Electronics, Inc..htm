Founded in 1993, Legacy Electronics is home of the patented Canopy chip-stacking alternative, a three-dimensional printed circuit board subassembly and process technology. Legacy is an ISO 9001:2000-certified manufacturer, designer, and tester of high-speed, high-density memory modules, printed circuit boards, and other computer products. Legacy Electronics has facilities in Germany and the US, with representatives in Hong Kong, Japan, and Singapore. CEO Jason Engle is a co-founder of the company; the other founding partner sold out in 1995. Legacy Electronics is owned by its officers.
