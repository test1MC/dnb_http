SystemOne Technologies lets used industrial solvent live again. The company makes and sells a line of self-contained recycling industrial parts washers. SystemOne's proprietary technology distills contaminated solvent into pure solvent and an oil-like by-product. The process allows the solvent to be used, treated, and re-used. The washers are used in the automotive, aviation, and marine industries, and for general industrial repair. The company filed for bankruptcy protection in the fall of 2009.
