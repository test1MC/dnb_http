QuatRx Pharmaceuticals hopes to capitalize on the bright ideas of others. The biopharmaceutical company acquires compounds in the pre-clinical stage from other companies and academic institutions and takes them through clinical studies then licensing them to pharma giants. The company focuses on developing new therapies for cardiovascular, endocrine, and metabolic conditions. Its research efforts include treatments for conditions such as age-related hormone deficiencies in women and men, as well as high cholesterol, thyroid conditions, and psoriasis. Hormos Medical, QuatRx's Finnish subsidiary, conducts most of its preclinical and discovery programs. The company was founded by a group of researchers in 2001.
