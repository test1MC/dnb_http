BiTMICRO Networks has a solid understanding of data storage. The company specializes in flash memory-based, solid-state disk (SSD) storage. Its products include flash drives used in large-scale network storage systems for military ( General Dynamics , Harris , and Lockheed Martin have all used its solid-state drives), industrial, and enterprise customers. BiTMICRO's products are used in applications such as cloud computing, big data, data warehousing, and high-performance computing. It has R&D centers in India and the Philippines. Technology partners include Ionics EMS, Cadence , and Synopsys . BiTMICRO was established in 1995 by the Bruce brothers, Rey and Rudy.
