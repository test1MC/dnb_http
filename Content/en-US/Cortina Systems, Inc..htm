Cortina Systems wants to help us all connect better, faster, and with streaming video. Its system-on-a-chip products deliver high speed digital, analog, and mixed signals to networking service providers, data centers, and the home market. The fabless company sells its communications semiconductors to computer networking OEMs including Alcatel-Lucent , Cisco , Juniper Networks , and Nokia Siemens . Its products are also used by telecom providers China Telecom , Deutsche Telekom , Telefonica , Vodafone , and others. It also offers broadband routers and network storage systems that deliver streaming video, games, telephone, and other services to home users. Cortina was formed in 2001.

Headquartered in Sunnyvale California, Cortina has sales offices in Canada, China, Japan, Hong Kong, Korea, Taiwan, Europe, and the US.

Cortina has historically made about one large acquisition a year, buying start-ups that moved it into China and other parts of Asia. It intends to continue that strategy while using its global operations to continue market penetration and to offer new product innovations.
