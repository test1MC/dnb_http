Performing seals at the circus make people happy. But the well-performing seals made by Novagard Solutions (formerly Foam Seal), while less entertaining, also keep the paying public in a good mood. The foam products company uses RTV and acetoxy silicone to manufacture vinyl foam sealants, gaskets, weather stripping, padding, PVC (polyvinyl chloride) foam and foam tapes, and adhesives and coatings, for a variety of industrial and commercial applications, including automotive, electrical, medical, and construction. Novagard also makes grouts and tile adhesives for applications in the pool and spa industry. The company serves customers in North America, Europe, and Asia.
