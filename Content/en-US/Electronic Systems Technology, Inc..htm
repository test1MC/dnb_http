Electronic Systems Technology (EST) makes wireless modems that it markets under the ESTeem brand. EST targets the modems for applications in industrial automation, the military, and public safety. The ESTeem line includes Ethernet radios that can be used for handling video and voice over Internet protocol (VoIP) transmissions. EST buys parts from Hitachi , Intersil , Integrated Microelectronics, Mitsubishi , Murata Manufacturing , Rakon, and Toko America for its products. Assembly of EST's products is farmed out to Manufacturing Services.
