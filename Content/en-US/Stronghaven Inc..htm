Stronghaven is determined to build a stronghold in the corrugated products manufacturing world. Established in 1982, the company makes corrugated containers for industrial and consumer products manufacturers. With four container plants throughout the Southeastern US (Florida, Georgia, and two in North Carolina), Stronghaven primarily specializes in short-run orders and customized corrugated products with graphics-intensive, value-added features. It also provides design services as well as specialty label and display capabilities, such as direct print flexo, litho label, and POP display. The company is controlled by investment firm TransOcean Capital, which acquired the corrugated box manufacturer in 2003.
