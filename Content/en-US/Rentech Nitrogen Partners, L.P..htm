Rentech Nitrogen Partners knows that, when it comes to growing crops, sunlight and water sometimes need a little help. The company produces ammonia and liquid fertilizer (made from urea ammonium nitrate) mostly for midwestern agricultural customers. Its plant is capable of producing up to 830 tons of ammonia and 1,100 tons of liquid fertilizer per day. Rentech Nitrogen typically sells to customers within a 200-mile radius of its facility, including corn growers and other farmers in Iowa and Wisconsin. CVR Partners LP agreed to acquire Rentech Nitrogen Partners in 2015 for $533 million.
