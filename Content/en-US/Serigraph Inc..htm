Specialty printer Serigraph knows the importance of presentation. The company designs and produces graphic overlays for use on automotive products, electronics, appliances, computers, outdoor equipment, sporting goods, and point-of-purchase displays, among other items. Specifically, it makes name plates for popular brands Maytag and Kenmore . Serigraph's graphics services include design, screen, offset, and digital printing, along with stamping, die-cutting, and embossing; The company also offers fulfillment services. Serigraph was founded in 1949.

Serigraph operates more than 10 offices and factories worldwide, including facilities in the US, Asia, and Mexico.

Serigraph serves point-of-purchase and in-store advertising markets through its Specialty Graphics Group division.
