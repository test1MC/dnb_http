ALL Holding Company (a combination of the Alderfer and Leidy's companies) offers pork, beef, and turkey products, including fresh pork, hams, bacon, hot dogs, roasts, ribs, rolls, scrapple, ground pork, and various smoked and fresh sausages, including Italian, chorizo, kielbasa, and braunschweiger, and andouille. Its brand names include Alderfer, Leidy's, and Nature's Tradition. The company touts its exclusive use of wood rather than liquid smoke or MSG to flavor its meats. ALL Holding also offers foodservice meats and private-label services. The company's customers include those in the retail food, foodservice, club store, and industrial food sectors.
