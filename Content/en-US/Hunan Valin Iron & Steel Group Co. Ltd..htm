Hunan Valin Iron & Steel makes steel pipes, bars, wires, sectional products, and hot-rolled steel plates along with copper plate pipes and inner-twisted pipes. Its annual output is about 22 million tons of steel and 12 million tons of steel products; hot-rolled steel plate is the company's biggest revenue generator. Hunan Valin Iron & Steel's products are distributed in mainland China and exported throughout much of Asia as well as to the US. Global steel conglomerate ArcelorMittal holds about 30% of the company; the Hunan Valin Group, 40%.

Hunan Valin Iron & Steel exports its products to Malaysia, Singapore, South Korea, Thailand, Vietnam, and the US.

The company has three steel making subsidiaries in China's Hunan province: Valin Xiangtan Iron & Steel Co., Ltd.; Valin Lianyuan Iron & Steel Co., Ltd.; and Valin Steel Pipe Holding Co., Ltd.

Hunan Valin Iron & Steel was formed in 1999. In 2005, the company sold about a one-third stake in publicly listed subsidiary Hunan Valin Steel Tube & Wire Company to what is now ArcelorMittal .
