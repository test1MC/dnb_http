Bernhardt gives new meaning to outfitting a family room. Bernhardt Furniture is a family-founded and -run manufacturer of upscale casegoods and upholstered furniture. Founded in 1889, the company makes custom-built furnishings for the living room, dining room, and bedroom and sells them through retailers, such as Dillard's and Star Furniture . The company's contract division, Bernhardt Design, was established in 1981 to sell furniture to outfit corporate offices and public areas. Founded by John Mathias Bernhardt, the company also makes the Martha Stewart Furniture and Smithsonian Collection by Bernhardt furniture lines, which are sold through stores nationwide.

Based in North Carolina, Bernhardt boasts an extended footprint, selling its products in more than 50 countries.

The company operates nearly 10 facilities in North Carolina and half a dozen offices in Asia. Nestled among the nation's furniture country, Bernhardt operates a 75,000-sq.-ft. showroom in High Point, North Carolina.

The company's Bernhardt Design works with architects and designers to meet contract and institutional needs.

Bernhardt, which serves the residential, contract, and hospitality industries, sells its furniture and accessories in the US, Canada, and overseas through dealers, retailers, department stores, and furniture outlets.

In recent years, Bernhardt has been extending its reach into new niches of the market by partnering with iconic names, such as the Smithsonian and multi-dimensional media maven Martha Stewart. Its Smithsonian Collection comprises pieces fashioned after historical collections of the Smithsonian Institute .

To keep up with demand, Bernhardt in 2014 began to expand its operations in North Carolina's Caldwell County.
