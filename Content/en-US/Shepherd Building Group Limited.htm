Need a building that can move? Shepherd Building Group manufactures bulk handing systems, equipment housings, prefabricated park and leisure homes, and its Portakabins line of portable and modular buildings to public and private sector clients in the UK. Its equipment housings portfolio features process-control, switching, and automation equipment housings for the oil and gas sectors, packaged traction substations for the rail industry, and power distribution substations for the energy sector. In addition, Shepherd makes systems for the storage and handling of particulate materials under the Portasilo brand name.

Shepherd manufactures bulk handling systems, storage systems, equipment housings, and modular buildings through two divisions: Portakabin (which contains its Yorkon, Paton Plant, Allspace, Konstructa, Foremans, and Oecon operations) and Shepherd Group Engineering (Portasilo and Portastor).

Shepherd has been selling non-core operations in order to focus on its two main segments. In 2015 it completed the sale of two of its remaining built environment businesses (Shepherd Engineering Services and Shepherd FM, its facilities management company). In addition, the company sold its Shepherd Homes business to Galliford Try in 2015.

Founded in 1890, Shepherd Building Group (also known as Shepherd Group) is owned by the Shepherd family.
