General Extrusions makes no exclusions with its extrusions. The company produces standard and custom aluminum extrusions for manufacturers in the appliance, medical equipment, transportation, and automotive markets, among others. General Extrusions is engaged in fabrication, anodizing, and powder coating, as well as aluminum finishing and machining. It also provides design assistance and engineering support. General Extrusions makes simple and intricate aluminum shapes, through both long and short production runs. The company's manufacturing operations, which include a Youngstown, Ohio plant, comprise 220,000 square feet. General Extrusions is owned and run by the Schuler family.
