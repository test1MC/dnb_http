When it comes to energy and maintenance issues, Goulds Pumps has a fluid answer that folks listen to. The company manufactures water utility, irrigation, and industrial pumping products. For more than 160 years Goulds Pumps has been producing pumps; it boasts more than a million of its centrifugal and turbine pumps installed worldwide. Goulds Pumps also offers replacement parts and repair services for its lineup. Its products are sold under brands including Goulds, A-C Pump, PumpSmart, and ProServices. The company's industrial pumps cater to the chemical, pulp and paper, power generation, oil refining, and mining industries. Goulds Pumps is a subsidiary of diversified manufacturer ITT .

The company is stationed in Seneca Falls, New York, and has offices, warehouses, and manufacturing facilities in 100 countries spanning the Americas, Europe, the Middle East, and the Asia/Pacific.

Goulds Pumps targets the chemical, mining, oil and gas, power generation, and pulp and paper industries. The company markets its products through distributors.

Goulds Pumps operates under the Industrial Process segment of its parent company; this segment contributed about 45% of its parent's total revenue in 2013. Industrial Process sales were also up 16% from 2012 to 2013 due to a previous acquisition made in late 2012. The segment was also helped by growth of 21% in the North American oil and gas market.
