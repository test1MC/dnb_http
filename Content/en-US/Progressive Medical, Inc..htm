Progressive Medical hopes its pressure therapy will make progress in getting rid of bed sores. The company sells a variety of therapeutic air and foam mattresses and related medical products to patients with wound care needs, pulmonary complications, and immobility (i.e. paraplegics and quadriplegics). Its mattresses help to prevent and also heal patients' bedsores. The company's other products include negative pressure (vacuum) wound therapy systems and durable medical equipment (wheelchairs, walkers, and commodes), as well as products for bariatric patients. Progressive Medical also provides consulting and condition assessment services.
