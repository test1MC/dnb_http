Robotically delivering the right stuff, Wright Industries makes and delivers automated assembly systems and robotic integrators. It is a leader in nuclear automation and fiber formation and provides engineering, design and manufacturing services, including concurrent and reverse engineering, solid-modeling, prototype and process development, and full-scale production for the healthcare, automotive, aerospace/defense, energy, textiles, and nuclear industries. The company is a subsidiary of Doerfer Companies, an application manufacturer specializing in automation systems, batteries, controls integration, press systems, metal stamping, and forming dies.

Though Doerfer Companies is headquartered in Iowa, it operates from facilities in Illinois, South Carolina, and Tennessee. Wright Industries has kept its corporate name and maintains its offices and facilities in Nashville, where it utilizes its 300,000-sq.-ft. manufacturing plant.

Wright Industries targets the consumer, electronics, energy, fibers and textiles, health care, and transportation markets.
