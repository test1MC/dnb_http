FLYHT Aerospace Solutions, formerly AeroMechanical Services, products are not entirely mechanical, but they are used in aerospace applications. The company's Automated Flight Information Reporting System (afirs) monitors aircraft operations to collect and transmit flight information data from onboard the aircraft. Its ground-based UpTime database collects, stores, and reports on that information. FLYHT's AeroQ Cabin Cleaning System cleans microorganisms from cabin surfaces in order to reduce passenger risk of infection. Other offerings include FlyhtStream (air-to-ground data streaming service), underfloor stowage units, and consulting services. FLYHT has international offices in China, Switzerland, and the US.
