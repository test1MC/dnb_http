AutoComm manufactures LCDs, light-emitting diodes (LEDs), plasma displays, and other types of electronic signs, such as scoreboards and tickers, along with the software needed to operate them. The displays are used in corporate communications, education, financial services, government, hospitality (hotels, motels, and restaurants), retail, and transportation. AutoComm also is a reseller of components and software. Clients include Lands' End , Harley-Davidson , and HowardJohnson International . The company, which was established in 1998, is owned by its officers.
