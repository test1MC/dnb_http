QPL International Holdings can help you get the lead out...or, err, in. The company provides leadframes, heatsinks, and stiffeners for the semiconductor industry through subsidiary QPL Limited. The company offers a variety of custom designs for clients for both etched and stamped leadframes, as well as standard products including SOIC (Small Outline Integrated Circuit), QFP (Quad Flat Package), and PLCC (Plastic Leaded Chip Carrier). QPL Limited operates manufacturing sites in China and has sales offices across the US and Asia/Pacific. The company also owns more than a 40% stake in customer ASAT Holdings , which offers semiconductor test, assembly, and packaging services.
