Scientific Components, doing business as Mini-Circuits, designs and manufactures radio-frequency, infrared, and microwave parts for aerospace, commercial, industrial, and military applications in signal processing. The company has facilities in China, India, Israel, Japan, Malaysia, Taiwan, the UK, and the US, with distributors and sales representatives worldwide. Mini-Circuits produces over 20 product lines for WiMax, CATV, cellular wireless, medical and test equipment, receivers, transmitters, and satellite markets. Its more than 4,000 electronic components are available through Mini-Circuits' catalog and its Web site. The company, which was established in 1969 by Harvey Kaylie, is owned by its officers.
