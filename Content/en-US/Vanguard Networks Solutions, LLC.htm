Vanguard Networks Solutions provides broadband network access routers for businesses. Hardware products, sold under the Vanguard name, include compact routers designed for use in remote settings like branch offices and ATM terminals. Its Vanguard Access Services Gateway router can replace several single-purpose network devices. The company also offers product implementation, testing, and support services to customers in North America. Clients come from such industries as finance and retail; it also serves government agencies. The company has been a subsidiary of Raymar Information Technology since 2013.

Headquarters in Massachusetts, Vanguard also is represented in Europe, Latin America, and the Asia Pacific region.

Vanguard's sales vanguard is its channel partners, which sell to service providers, financial services, retail, industry; and the public sector.

Vanguard was bought by Raymar Information Technology in 2013 from the Platinum Equity. Vanguard's access gateways and IP routers were seen as complementary to Raymar's communications products.
