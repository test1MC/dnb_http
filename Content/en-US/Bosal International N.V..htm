Bosal is boss for myriad metal products. Bosal International's automotive offerings include catalytic converters, jacks, towbars, and roofbars. It also produces sprinkler irrigation pipes made of galvanized light steel and aluminum. Bosal's warehouse racking division manufactures body panel storage, draw units, long-span shelving, and perforated display panels. It also makes oval, perforated, preformed, and round tubes made from aluminized, cold and hot-rolled steel, and stainless steel. Through Bosal-Sekura Industries, it makes cabs for agricultural and industrial vehicles. The company was founded in 1923 by Karel Bos, whose namesake son still runs Bosal.
