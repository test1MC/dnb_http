EDGE Manufacturing keeps its edge in serving the meat-cutting industry by providing cutting tools, such as grinders, handsaws, and knife sharpeners, as well as German-made Giesser Messer cutlery blades. It also manufactures wood-cutting bandsaw and circular saw blades and coil stock. Additionally, it makes butt flash welding machines for metal joining and bandsaw blade welding machines. EDGE also provides repair and rebuild services for customers' existing welders. The company operates facilities in the US, Germany, Brazil, and China. EDGE was founded in 1993.
