BORIDE's brushes and bobs help keep the industrial world polished. BORIDE Engineered Abrasives develops, manufactures, and distributes mold and die polishing products (brushes, bobs, diamond files, and wood sticks), diamond wheel dressing products, and specialty abrasive products that include private-labeled products. The company began operations in the 1960s.
