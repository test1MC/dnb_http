Don't knock Dawson Metal's open-door policy. Dawson Metal (which does business as Dawson Doors) manufactures custom-made stainless steel, aluminum, and bronze doors for businesses and storefronts. The company also manufactures balanced doors, which open in an elliptical arch. It mainly serves US corporations and the construction industry, and makes doors for private residences, as well. Dawson Metal was established in Jamestown, New York, in 1946 as an industrial and architectural metal fabrication business by Axel Dawson and his son George. The Dawson family continues to own the company.
