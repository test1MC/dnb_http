Milbank Manufacturing will keep the power turned on, at home and at work. The family owned company is one of the largest designer and manufacturer of electrical meter mounting equipment, including sockets, enclosures, power outlets and pedestals. Its lineup of more than 16,000 items also offers circuit breakers, test switches, and safety switches. Manufactured at five plants, Milbank's products are channeled through a network of manufacturer's representatives to electric utility companies, contractors, industrial distributors, and OEMs across North America.

Milbank has operations in Kansas City, Missouri, where it houses its National Training Center and R&D Lab, and in El Dorado, Arkansas, and Concordia, Missouri. It has about 22 stocking locations across the US.

Milbank's range of products includes residential and commercial meter sockets, meter pedestals, recreational vehicle and mobile home power supply systems, enclosed controls, switches, hubs, generators, and energy management products.

Major product lines include meter sockets, enclosures and controls, generators, and transfer switches.

Serving the utility, contractor, industrial and OEM segments, Milbank markets its products through a national network of manufacturer's representatives (including electrical enclosure, wholesale/contractor sale, utility sale, retail, and international representatives) and via wholesale electrical distributors.

With more than 16,000 SKUs designed and manufactured to date, the company specializes in custom-built enclosures and has the engineering expertise to accommodate customers' requests for specific designs and products.

Determined to fuel its growth in Europe, Milbank in 2013 entered into a strategic alliance with Sentec, a firm based in Cambridge, UK. Sentec provides smart grid and energy technologies and is partnering with Milbank to establish operations in the UK and develop new green technology products.

Boosting its portfolio, in 2014 Milbank acquired the Vigilant Automatic Transfer Switch product line from DynaGen Technologies for an undisclosed amount.

The company was founded in 1927 by Charles A. Milbank. It is owned by the third-generation of the founding Waldrop family.
