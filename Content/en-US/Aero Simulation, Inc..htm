Learning by trial and error isn't acceptable when the product in question is a multimillion-dollar aircraft, so Aero Simulation, Inc., (ASI) makes and upgrades commercial and military flight simulators used in pilot training. ASI has created simulators for aircraft made by companies such as Bell , Boeing , Lockheed Martin , Northrop Grumman , and Sikorsky . The company provides custom-built, task-specific simulators to address various training needs as well as more complete flight simulators. ASI's customers have included airlines, government agencies, military branches, and private jet operators. ASI also provides logistics support and program management services. It was founded in 1983.
