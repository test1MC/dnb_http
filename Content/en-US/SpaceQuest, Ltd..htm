For SpaceQuest, the trip usually ends in low Earth orbit. The company develops advanced satellite technology for government, university, and commercial use. SpaceQuest specializes in the design, development, testing, and manufacturing of spacecraft and the components necessary for operation of low-Earth orbiting satellites. SpaceQuest produces such communication components as antenna systems, radios and modems, in addition to power systems, command and data handling systems, attitude determination and control, and software. The company has built micro-satellites and provided components for such clients as Aprize Satellite, Bigelow Aerospace, and NASA . The company was founded in 1994.
