When they can't find the answer the clinical information they are looking for, doctors don't need to run for the hills. Instead they can turn to Medscape. The company provides medical information and educational services for physicians and other health professionals. Its content includes more than 125 medical journals and textbooks, offering review articles, medical news, and commentary. Content is organized around more than 30 specific medical specialties, with each specialty having its own customized Web site. In addition, the company offers more than 850 online continuing medical education (CME) activities. Medscape is a subsidiary of health information services firm WebMD Health .
