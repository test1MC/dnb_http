Uniflex Co. Ltd. (formerly Teleflex-NHK Automotive) has got a spring in its step. The company, a part of NHK Spring , specializes in the manufacture of automotive driver control systems. The company's products include steering columns, adjustable pedal systems, and electronic throttles. Uniflex primarily serves automobile makers and manufacturers of industrial machinery. Customers include Fuji Heavy Industries , Honda , Isuzu , and Nissan .
