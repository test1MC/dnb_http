Sometimes plain old diamonds just aren't good enough. Lazare Kaplan International specializes in cutting, polishing, and marketing premium-priced diamonds, as well as diamond jewelry. The company buys rough diamonds (primarily from Diamond Trading Company, a sales arm of behemoth De Beers ), cuts and polishes the gems for maximum sparkle, and laser-inscribes the branded Lazare Diamonds with the company's logo and an ID number. Lazare boasts manufacturing plants in Puerto Rico, Russia (in Moscow and Barnaul), and South Africa (specifically Namibia) to handle processing of the diamonds. As part of its business, Lazare sells to wholesalers, manufacturers, and authorized jewelry retailers worldwide.
