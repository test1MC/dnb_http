Solarfun, operating through subsidiary Linyang China, makes solar photovoltaic (PV) cells and modules, as well as offers PV cell and module processing services. The vertically integrated company's Yangguang Solar subsidiary produces silicon ingots and wafers used to make its products. Solarfun sells its wares through distributors, OEM manufacturers, and systems integrators for use in utility, governmental, commercial, and residential applications. A majority of the company's sales come from Europe, primarily from Germany, Italy, and Spain. Solarfun has offices in China, Germany, Spain, South Korea, Australia, and the US. In late 2010 Hanwha Chemical bought around a 49% stake in Solarfun for about $78 million.
