The road to success for Nature's Path Foods is paved with organic products. The company is a leading maker of organic grain-based foods and best known for its namesake brand of hot and cold breakfast cereals. The company also markets toaster pastries, energy bars, and other snacks under the Nature's Path banner. Under the EnviroKidz name it makes such kid-friendly products as Koala Crisp and Panda Puffs cereals; for grown-ups it offers Optimum energy bars. The company sells its products through specialty food stores and other retail outlets in Canada and the US. Arran Stephens started the family-owned business in 1985.
