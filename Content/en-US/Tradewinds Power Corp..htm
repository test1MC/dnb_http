No fair-weather supplier, Tradewinds Power is an all-season manufacturer and distributor of industrial machinery, such as power generators, pumps, engines, and motors. The company makes marine propulsion and power generation units as well as associated marine parts. It also makes a range of engines for the power generation industry as well as power units, pump sets, and transmissions for industrial and irrigation use. Tradewinds Power's 109,000 sq. ft. of warehouse space (across three warehouses in Florida) stocks more than 30,000 items. The company also provides a full maintenance and after-sales parts service.

Tradewinds Power's primary manufacturing location and corporate headquarters is located in Miami and caters to Latin America and the Caribbean. It also has a branch office in Sebring, Florida, serving the Central Florida agriculture market.

The company sales its products through dealers in Connecticut, Louisiana. Oklahoma, Florida, New Jersey, Texas, New York, Illinois, Kentucky, North Carolina, and Virginia.

Tradewinds Power evolved from a John Deere engine distributor with territories in Central and South America to an OEM in 2006. It was at that time that the company began developing John Deere-powered generator sets and water pumps.
