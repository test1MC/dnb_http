Shafer Vineyards produces mostly cabernet sauvignon wines from its family-owned vineyard, which has been in operation in California's Napa Valley since 1972. Other varietals grown by Shafer include chardonnay, merlot, and syrah. The vintner's wines are sold under numerous labels, including Hillside Select, One Point Five, and Relentless. The company, which is led by the father-and-son team of John and Doug Shafer, distributes its wines across the US and overseas.
