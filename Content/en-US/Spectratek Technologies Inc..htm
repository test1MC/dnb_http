It's no illusion ... Spectratek Technologies makes metallized and transparent holographic films that give video boxes, cigarette cartons, and other consumer packaging products the dazzle of holographic effects. Also, by cutting its films into tiny pieces, the company produces holographic particles for use in glitter and geometric pigments. Its geometric pigments are used by makers of cosmetics, fabrics, paints, and inks. Spectratek Technologies offers its holographic film in 13 patterns and supplies the film in a variety of forms, including adhesive backed, laminated to paper, and laminated to paperboard.
