Though its origins lie in coffee and tea trading, Elementis is now a purveyor of paint additives, chemicals, and surfactants. Its Specialty Products division makes additives for paints and coatings, personal care and oilfield drilling products. It also makes specialty additives such as defoamers, colorants and pigments, dispersants, and lanolin. Its Chromium chemicals division is a top producer of chromic acid, chromic oxide, sodium dichromate, and liquid chrome sulfate, used in metal alloys, metal finishing, timber treatment, and leather tanning. Elementis also makes surfactants used in oilfield and construction chemicals, and it owns a hectorite mine. Elementis bought SummitReheis for $360 million in 2017.
