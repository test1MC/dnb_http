Having some leafy greens with that steak? Ask Ken's Foods for some dressing. The condiments company manufactures and markets more than 400 varieties of bottled salad dressings, marinades, and sauces under the Ken's Steak House brand name, among others. Its products are distributed to retail food companies, such as Albertsons , Wal-Mart , Hy-Vee , Kroger , Meijer , HEB , and others, and to foodservice operators nationwide. As part of its business, Ken's Foods, which is family-owned and -operated, also offers production, labeling, and packaging services for other food manufacturers. The dressings manufacturer owns and operates production facilities in Massachusetts, Georgia, and Nevada.

Serving customers nationwide with hundreds of salad dressings, marinades, and sauces, Massachusetts-based Ken's Foods supports its business with production facilities located in Georgia, Nevada, and Massachusetts.

From its plants in Massachusetts, Georgia, and Nevada, Ken's Foods makes available more than 400 varieties of dressings and sauces. It boasts retail grocery products and food service divisions, producing custom-made dressings and sauces for other firms.

For itself and others, Ken's Foods markets products under several brand names, including Ken's Steak House, Wish-Bone , Ken's Foods, Kraft Foods , and Hidden Valley Ranch. It also offers services for food products, such as production, labeling, selling, and packaging. Its customers include both big-name food retailers and foodservice operators that operate across the US.

Ken's Foods has focused on developing and rolling out more health-conscious items as it works to provide products with more straight-forward ingredient lists and dressings with enhanced functionality. Catering to customers who are looking to boost their heart health and reduce fat or sodium intake, Ken's Foods has launched several new dressings in recent years. Its Ken's Steak House Healthy Options Salad Dressing is available in several varieties -- from Balsamic Vinaigrette to Raspberry Walnut -- and contains omega-3s sourced from fish oil.

The company gets more mileage out of its operations by making custom dressings for others. Ken's Foods has partnered with the likes of Paul Newman's Newman's Own line of dressings and sauces, acting as a private labeler and bottler.

Ken's Foods was founded in 1958 by Frank and Louise Crowley, friends of the proprietors of Ken's Steak House (Ken and Florence Hanna), located in Framingham, Massachusetts, where the salad dressings originated.
