Afexa Life Sciences bets that Mother Nature is always right, but it offers a guarantee just in case. The company uses its ChemBioPrint technology to identify and measure the contents and biological activity of natural health products to ensure batches of herbs and natural compounds are safe and consistent. Afexa focuses on the development and manufacturing of natural health products for disease prevention and health maintenance for everyday consumers, especially athletes. COLD-FX is its lead product and works to strengthen the immune system to prevent colds and the flu. Canadian drugmaker Valeant Pharmaceuticals took the company private in 2011.
