If it's as cold as ice, Vilter Manufacturing has done its job. The company makes a variety of commercial refrigeration products, including industrial compressors (single screw, rotary screw, and gas), evaporative condensers, air units, heat exchangers, packaged refrigeration systems, and computer controls. Vilter sells its products through a global network of distributors to customers in the food and beverage, microelectronics, petrochemical, and pharmaceutical industries. Ice rinks such as Tulsa Expo Square Center Ice Arena also use Vilter's products to maintain their cold conditions. Peter Weisel and Ernst Vilter founded the company as Weisel & Vilter in 1867.
