Permar Systems, doing business as Electromark, wants you to watch out, keep out, and stay away from danger zones. The company designs, manufactures, and markets safety and traffic signs, identification tags, and warning labels used along roadways and by utilities as well as in industrial plants, manufacturing facilities, and construction sites. Its other products include bar-coded identification tags, hard-hat labels, hazardous waste labels, pipe and pole markers, and warning tapes. Electromark was founded in 1970 by Harold Rickles. It is a subsidiary of Brady Corp. , a maker of industrial identification and specialty coated material products.
