By land, sea, or air, Icon Polymer is a top gun among high performance rubber product manufacturers. The company's polymer-based products focus on sealing and safety applications in aerospace and defense, energy, industrial, and transportation markets. Products include wheels and track guards for tanks, bearings for marine vessels, and refueling hoses for aircraft. Icon operates as part of the Icon Polymer Group of companies, which also consists of Silentbloc, which makes technical parts for power (expansion joints), energy and construction (seals and pads), and general industry (vacuum belts). Over the years, the group has counted BAE Systems , Stork Fokker , and Bissell as prominent customers.

The company is based in Retford, Nottinghamshire England, and has two high capacity manufacturing sites located in the UK.

Significant brands residing in the group's portfolio include: Northern Rubber, Silentbloc UK, Endurance, LoTenz, Flexiflow, Hover-Wear, ICO-STOP, Thermi-Flo, and Silverline.
