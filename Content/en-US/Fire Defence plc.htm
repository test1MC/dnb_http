Fire Defence can keep a fire from becoming an inferno. Serving the UK, Fire Defence designs and installs automatic sprinkler and fire-suppression systems. The company also provides sprinker system modification, testing, maintenance, and support services. Selected products include wet pipes (pipe-fed sprinkler systems); alternate systems (combination water/dry air pipe systems); and deluge and recycling installations (used in special cases for industrial risks). Fire Defence has installed its systems in shopping centers, warehouses, private residences, historic buildings, schools, hospitals, and airports. British Airways , Barclays , and the BBC have all availed themselves of the company's services.

The company offers a range of emergency electrical fire protection and fire suppression products manufactured by companies such as Advance Electronic, Hochiki , Hyfire, Kentec, Nittan , and Prolift M.

Fire Defence serves a wide range of industries include warehousing, distribution, manufacturing, retail, commercial, public sector, defence, aviation, heritage, residential, hotels, process, special risk, and education. Supermarket customers include Tesco , WM Morrisons , and Waitrose .

Fire Defence plans to continue to grow both organically and through acquisitions.

The company was formed in 1981 by Bill Johns and his wife Sharon. The company is still owned by the family and managed by Bill Johns (chairman) and his son Keiran (managing director).

In 2011, the company announced that it had won contracts to install fire sprinkler systems in the 2012 London Olympic Village. The win tops off the company's impressive growth over the last three years - including more than doubling its revenue and headcount.
