China BlueChemical's products come in a rainbow of colors. The company manufactures nitrogen fertilizers, methanol, food-class carbon dioxide (used in carbonated beverages), and plastic bags used to package its fertilizer. China BlueChemical mainly produces nitrogen gas, used to make granular and prill urea, a fertilizer ingredient. Methanol is an alcohol-based solvent used to make fiber, fuel, plastic, and pesticide. China BlueChemical operates two manufacturing plants that produce urea and methanol. Fertilizer products are sold under the Fudao and Tianye brands in China, and less than 10% of the company's sales are outside the country. CNOOC controls about two-thirds of its stock.
