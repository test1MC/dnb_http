Drum Workshop (DW) can improve your paradiddle and your double pedal. The company designs and manufactures drums, pedals, hi-hats, and other hardware and accessories for musicians of many styles. It also sponsors clinics by professional drummers. Products are sold at Guitar Center , Sam Ash , and other retailers in the US and worldwide. DW also markets its goods through its drum, pedal, and hardware catalogs, and features musicians with their products in Edge Magazine . President Don Lombardi founded the company in 1972.
