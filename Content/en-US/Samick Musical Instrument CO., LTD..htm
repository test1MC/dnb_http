From traditional acoustic classics to a digital series, Samick offers a vast collection of pianos. Samick's brands include Wm. Knabe, Greg Bennett, Silvertone, Kohler & Campbell, Pramberger, Sohmer, and namesake Samick. It makes and sells acoustic and electric guitars, mixers, amplifiers, and equalizers and sells them through independent dealers.
