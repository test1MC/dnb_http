The written word has been good to Brill. Established in 1683, the publishing house specializes in English-language books, encyclopedias, and journals covering a variety of academic subjects. Brill's publications focus on such fields as the ancient Near East and Egypt, Middle Eastern and Islamic studies, social sciences, and public international law (among others). Its publications are predominantly found in higher-learning institutions and libraries in Europe, the US, and Japan. Brill produces its publications in both print and electronic forms.
