CellAntenna wants to rid the world of bad cell phone reception. The company distributes and installs external antennas, signal boosters, and other equipment that improves reception and reduces the number of dropped calls. It also installs in-building repeater systems to improve signal strength in office buildings and sells cell phone jammers to the federal government. CellAntenna's more than 18,000 customers have included Sun Microsystems and AT&T Mobility .
