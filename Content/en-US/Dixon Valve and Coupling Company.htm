Like fitting Tab A into Slot B, Dixon Valve and Coupling brings disparate equipment together and makes it work as a whole. The company manufactures valves and hose couplings though operating divisions Dixon Boss, Dixon Brass, Dixon Powhatan, Dixon Specialty Hose, Dixon Bayco, Dixon Quick Coupling, and Dixon Sanitary. Products include brass hose fittings and couplings, stainless steel fittings and valves, and pneumatic coupling and hydraulic products. Industries served include fire protection, oil and gas, chemicals, food, and industrial manufacturing. Dixon Valve and Coupling operates from a dozen locations throughout the US.
