Pomegranate Communications is a leading publisher of calendars, postcards, Christmas cards, and other consumer products. Its calendars and cards are targeted towards such niche interests as art, nature, and women's studies, as well as culture, music, and photography. The company sells its products direct to consumers through its Web site and catalog; it also distributes through other retail stores. Company president Thomas Burke started Pomegranate Communications in the late 1960s.
