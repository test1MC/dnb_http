While the cola wars may leave the ground littered with empty Coke and Pepsi bottles, Big Red proves that niche soft drinks can still win over followers one gulp at a time. The firm bottles and markets the Big Red strawberry-bubble-gum-flavored soft drink throughout most of the US. Other flavors offered by the company include Big Red Float, Big Blue, Big Peach, Big Red Retro, and Big Pineapple. The company, which makes its products available in 43 states, also offers stevia-sweetened SANS Soda, as well as the Honey Lemonade and NuGrape labels through its North American Beverages operations. The Dr Pepper Snapple Group distributes about 80% of the company's beverages. Chairman and CEO Gary Smith owns the company.
