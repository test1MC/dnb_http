Al Baddad International is not your average camping-in-the-woods tent maker. Al Baddad manufactures marquees (large mobile tents) and prefabricated aluminum buildings used for conferences, weddings, sporting events, exhibitions, military camps, and warehousing. Its marquees, which come in a variety of shapes and sizes, range from small domes to giant warehouse-sized tents and are typically made out of hard and soft PVC, glass, sandwich panels, and aluminum. Al Baddad also provides customers with furniture, windows, air conditioning, decorative doors, and wood flooring. The company's pre-fab buildings include military barracks, storage units and warehouses, and living quarters. Al Baddad was founded in 1971.
