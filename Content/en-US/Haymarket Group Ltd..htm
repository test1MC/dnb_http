Making hay while the sun shines means making magazines to The Haymarket Group. The company publishes more than 100 consumer, professional, trade, and customer publications in some 20 languages and more than 40 countries. Titles include campaign (advertising and communications), Stuff (gadgets), What Car? (automobile buying guides), and FourFourTwo (football). The company also publishes websites related to its publications and hosts events, including exhibitions and conferences. In addition to its primary market in the UK, the company has subsidiaries and joint ventures operating in such markets as Australia, Belgium, China, Germany, India, Japan, Singapore, and the US. Haymarket Group was founded in 1957.
