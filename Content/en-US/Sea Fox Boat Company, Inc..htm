Being called "all wet" is not always a bad thing -- just ask the folks at Sea Fox Boats. The company makes fiberglass recreational fishing boats, along with deck boats. The company offers vessels for both saltwater and freshwater use. Its models range from 16 to 28 feet in length and include bay fishers, center consoles, dual consoles, flats boats, and walk arounds. Its hand-crafted Mariah brand includes bow riders, cuddy cabins, deck boats, and fish and ski models. Sea Fox boats are sold through dealers across the US, as well as more than a dozen international dealers. Brothers Fred and Ed Renken own Sea Fox; they are the third generation of Renkens to run the company.
