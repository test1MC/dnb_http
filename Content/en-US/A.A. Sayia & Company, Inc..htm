A.A. Sayia can put the spice back in your life, or at least in your recipes. The import company offers a variety of spice products for wholesalers, processors and traders. Sayia's product offerings range from the exotic, such as fenugreek and annatto, to the more familiar, like black pepper and poppy seeds. The company's products are sourced from growers throughout the Americas, Europe, and Asia -- and like its spices, from places familiar (Canada) to exotic (Morocco). A.A. Sayia was founded in 1917 by Anthony Alfonso Sayia (thus, the "A.A." of the company's name) and is still owned and operated by the Sayia family.
