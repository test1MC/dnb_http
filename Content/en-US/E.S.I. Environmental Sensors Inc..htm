The oil and water sectors mix well for E.S.I. Environmental Sensors. The company manufactures and distributes water content and moisture monitoring equipment used by industries where water management is important including oil production, agriculture, and waste management (primarily landfills). Its software-operated Gro-Point System is suited for water management in vineyards and other crop operations of high value, while its Flo-Point sensor is useful during crude oil extraction in areas with high water content. Founded in 1973, E.S.I. provides its products to customers in more than 40 countries.
