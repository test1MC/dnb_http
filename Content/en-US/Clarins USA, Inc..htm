Clarins USA is a natural. As the first subsidiary for French skin care and cosmetics group Clarins , the US-based business provides department and specialty stores and spas across the country with its popular line of botanical-based products. Clarins, which boasts an annual capacity to make 7,400 tons of products, specializes in skin care for women and men (ClarinsMen), make-up, and fragrances. In addition to its own Clarins brand, the company owns and markets fragrances by Thierry Mugler, Azzaro, Porsche Design, David Yurman, and Swarovski . Incorporated in 1980, Clarins USA also operates more than 15 Skin Spa locations in half a dozen US states and overseas. Clarins was founded by Jacques Courtin-Clarins in 1954.

Based in New York, Clarins USA sells its products nationwide and caters to spa customers globally.

Clarins USA consists of two divisions: Clarins Brand and Clarins Fragrance Group.

The beauty company supports its retail sales through a logistics center located in Orangeburg, New York.

It also operates Skin Spa boutiques in several US states, as well as in Brazil, Canada, China, France, Germany, Hong Kong, Italy, Japan, Malaysia, Mauritius, Mexico, Morocco, Panama, Russia, Saudi Arabia, Seychelles, Singapore, South Korea, Spain, Switzerland, Taiwan, the UK, and Vietnam.

Clarins USA and InterParfums USA (a subsidiary of Inter Parfums ) formed InterParfums Luxury Brands in 2011. The business, a subsidiary of Clarins USA's fragrance division, markets luxury beauty items from both firms, including Burberry, Jimmy Choo, Lanvin, and Montblanc. As part of the partnership, the two companies manage their combined sales forces together and share the costs of InterParfums USA. Clarins USA also provides administrative and logistical support for the subsidiary.
