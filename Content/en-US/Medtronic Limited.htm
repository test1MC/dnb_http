Medtronic Limited keeps hearts thumping across Britain. The company is the UK arm of medical technology giant Medtronic , which focuses on developing and selling devices and therapies in such areas as cardiac rhythm disease management, cardiac surgery, vascular medicine, neurological stimulation, diabetes, and spinal disorders. The cardiac rhythm management division's products include pacemakers, implantable defibrillators, external defibrillators, and electrophysiology systems; the cardiac surgery division makes valves used in surgical procedures to repair or replace heart valves. Medtronic Limited's products are marketed to hospitals and physicians in diagnostic, therapeutic, and surgical procedures.

Medtronic Limited is part of the Europe, Middle East, and Africa geography of its parent company.

The company is part of Medtronic's EMEA division, which provides some 25% of total revenues to the group.

Medtronic Limited contributes to growth in Medtronic's UK market by focusing on increasing sales of existing products, as well as by supporting the group's development of new, innovative products that will better serve patients in the core fields of pain management, health restoration, and life extension. Focus areas of technology include improved pacemaker, defibrillation, and monitoring devices for cardiac patients; neuromodulation devices for pain and movement disorders; heart valves and stents to improve vascular flow; new diabetic drug delivery and monitoring devices; and minimally invasive surgical, biologic, and dynamic stabilization devices for the spine.

Medtronic Limited has been in operation since 1972; it moved to its current location in Watford in 1987. The company's US-based parent was founded in 1949.
