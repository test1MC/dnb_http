With a name like STORServer, you'd think the company stored something or had something to do with servers -- and you'd be correct. STORServer makes a range of STORServer Backup Appliances that keep computer data safe and sound on a dedicated backup server. STORServer appliances use SCSI disks and have tape options as well; applications include data backup, archiving, and disaster recovery. The employee-owned company also provides enterprise, agent, and client software. STORServer was established in 1995. The company has offices in the Netherlands and the US.
