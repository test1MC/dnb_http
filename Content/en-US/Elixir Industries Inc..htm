Elixir Industries is a diversified manufacturer of aluminum extrusions, aluminum fabrication, and custom metal fabrication products. Its portfolio of offerings includes doors, siding and roofing, window guards, recreational vehicle products, cargo trailers, and aluminum painted coil. It also makes a variety of coatings, sealants, and tapes. The company operates from about a dozen locations in half a dozen states, serving clients across the US as well as in selected international markets. Roland Sahm founded what is now Elixir Industries in 1948.

The company's locations reside in Douglas and Fitzgerald, Georgia; Mishawaka, Indiana; Waco, Texas; Tuscumbia, Alabama; and Leola, Pennsylvania. It is headquartered in Mission Viejo, California.

Elixir Industries operates through two million sq. ft. of production space and 12 divisions nationwide.

Elixir Industries targets recreational vehicles; factory built homes; cargo trailers; and lighting, academic, and community athletic programs as customers.
