If you like your eggs frozen, liquid or powdered, Sonstegard Foods can serve you. The company wholesales egg products to food processors, manufacturers, and distributors, as well as other customers in the food industry. Its products include bulk egg solids and frozen and liquid eggs, which are used to make salad dressings, candies, pasta, and other food items. Sonstegard also provides egg products for use in school cafeterias and other foodservice venues. Its subsidiary, Estherville Foods, provides similar services. The company is owned by president Dr. Philip Sonstegard.
