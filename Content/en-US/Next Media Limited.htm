Next Media Limited may be a print media conglomerate but it isn't confining its activities to paper. The company publishes and prints a variety of Chinese-language newspapers, magazines, and books for distribution in Hong Kong and Taiwan. Publications include Apple Daily , Next Magazine , Easy Finder and Eat & Travel Weekly . The company publishes separate versions of Apple Daily and Next Magazine for distribution in either Hong Kong or Taiwan. Next Media also distributes its content on the Internet through its atnext.com portal. In addition to supporting its publishing operations, Next Media's print division also prints books, calendars, catalogs, and promotional materials.
