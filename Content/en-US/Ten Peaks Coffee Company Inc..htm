Ten Peaks Coffee hopes drinking its brew is a peak experience. The Canadian firm owns Swiss Water Decaffeinated Coffee Company (SWDCC), which is its primary operating subsidiary. SWDCC uses water from the mountains of British Columbia in a chemical-free process developed in Switzerland to remove caffeine from green coffee. It decaffeinates on-demand using customers' coffee and buys beans to decaffeinate then sell to roasters. SWDCC sells coffee directly in North America and through distributors in Europe, Australia, and Asia. It also owns and operates Seaforth Supply Chain Solutions, a green coffee handling and storage business located in Metro Vancouver.
