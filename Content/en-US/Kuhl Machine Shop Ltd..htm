Kuhl Machine Shop primarily designs and manufactures hydraulic cylinders. The company also makes other industrial machinery, including hydraulic shafts, lathes, mills, pistons, and presses. Kuhl Machine Shop provides for its customers such services as custom cylinder design and fabrication, piping, millwrighting, and welding. Its facility has more than 15,000 sq. ft. of workspace. Customers include Goodyear Tire & Rubber , Hydro One , Ontario Power Generation, Pilkington Glass , and Tenneco . The company was founded in 1989 by David Kuhl, its president.
