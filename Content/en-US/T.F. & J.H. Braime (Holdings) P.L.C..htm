T.F. & J.H. Braime knows the ups and downs of the elevator business. The company makes material handling components including elevator components, conveyor and elevator chains (forged chain and round link), elevator buckets, and related items under the 4B Components brand. T.F. & J.H. Braime also manufactures custom elevators and conveyors. Its Braime Pressings subsidiary makes metal pressings and sub-assemblies. In addition to serving customers in and around its UK base, T.F. & J.H. Braime has operations in Australia, France, South Africa, Thailand, and the US. The company's roots date back to 1888.

The group comprises two core segments: the manufacture of deep drawn metal presswork, and the distribution of material handling components and monitoring equipment. The material handling components segment includes its subsidiaries that trade from over six countries and export to approximately 10 countries. The metal presswork segment operates across several industries including the automotive sector.

Distribution accounts for 85% of sales; manufacturing accounts for the remaining 15%.

Braime's subsidiaries include Braime Pressings Limited, 4B Elevator Components Limited, 4B Braime Components Limited, 4B France sarl, 4B Africa Elevator Components Limited and 4B Australia Limited.

In 2015, sales increased 9% on prior year to £26.5 million due to a strong performance in distribution. Net income increased 83% to £1.6 million due to increased sales and profits on the disposal of tangible fixed assets. Cash flow from operating activities was down £1.4 million to £0.4 million due to changes in trade and other payables.

In 2015, the company sold the grade-II listed Braime Pressings factory in Leeds to the newly opened University Technical College, sparing the company the annual running cost of the building and its long-term maintenance.
