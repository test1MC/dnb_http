Imagine yourself on the biometric aisle of the security products and services supermarket, not knowing exactly what you need. That's where security consulting and integration firm International Biometric Group (IBG) comes in. IBG helps companies and government agencies select and integrate biometric security devices, including voice-scan equipment; fingerprint, iris, and retina scanners; and facial recognition systems used for access control and monitoring. IBG's BiometricStore is a hands-on technology showroom featuring more than 100 biometrics systems. Clients have included Citibank , the Federal Aviation Administration , and Microsoft . IBG, founded in 1996, is owned and run by brothers Raj and Samir Nanavati.
