Sta-Rite Industries stores up a lot of water, but it's not like it's waiting for the apocalypse or anything. The company manufactures pumps, water systems, filters, and accessories, which it sells in more than 100 countries. Sta-Rite manufactures its products for two distinct markets: pool and spa customers and residential, commercial, and municipal water supplies. It operates domestic plants in California; Nebraska, New Hampshire, and Wisconsin, and international facilities in Australia, Germany, Italy, Mexico, and New Zealand. Founded in 1934, Sta-Rite is a part of water products company Pentair .

Sta-Rite is expanding internationally. In recent years it has added joint venture operations in China, India, and Kazakhstan.

The company markets its products in more than 100 countries globally. Its dealers in North America include Valnes Well Drilling, Braunwarth Well Company, Wheels Enterprise, and Hydro Engineering.

Sta-Rite is part of the Process Technologies segment of its parent. This segment contributed 24% of its parent's net revenue in 2013.
