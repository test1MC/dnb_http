Laser Atlanta makes laser-based speed and distance measuring equipment used in applications such as surveying, mapping, traffic monitoring, facilities management, and blast profiling. Its laser systems are light and portable, and include the SpeedLaser, a radar detector used by law enforcement that can be upgraded to provide accident mapping capabilities. The US Navy uses Laser Atlanta's proximity measurement equipment to help keep its ships from colliding at sea by measuring the distance between vessels, information that is valuable when one ship is loading cargo onto another. International customers include the Middle East, China, South/Central America, and the UK. Laser Atlanta was established in 1989.
