ECA Group dares to go where humans should not. The company develops and makes defense and civil sector systems, such as robots and unmanned vehicles, simulators, and security and control equipment, that are intended to reduce risk to human life in perilous environments. These automated systems are typically geared towards government, aeronautics, offshore, and nuclear industry customers. They perform tasks ranging from identifying and destroying mines to training marine, naval, and ground crew via computer simulation. Geographically, ECA's revenues are split fairly evenly between France and the export market, with key customer bases in the US and Asia. The group is controlled by Finuchem .
