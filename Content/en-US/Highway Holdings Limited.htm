Highway Holdings wants to help its OEM clients speed up their production process. Through nearly a dozen subsidiaries, the company makes metal and plastic parts and subassemblies for German, Japanese, and US manufacturers, including Braun , Casio , and Swatch . It also manufactures finished products such as LED lights and radio chimes. Highway Holdings' metal stamping business offers spray painting, screen printing, and plastic injection molding. To focus on its core business, the company exited its singe use camera, clock, and watch operations and sold off rights to the Kienzle brand of clocks. Although Highway Holdings operates factories in China, Europe accounts for about about 50% of sales.
