Librarians everywhere know H.W. Wilson knows how to research. The H.W. Wilson Company publishes reference books and databases, such as the Readers Guide to Periodical Literature , found mostly at libraries. Its print and electronic products include nearly 80 research databases covering periodicals, biographies, and art images. Its Core Collections includes the must-have resources that public, school, and children's libraries should have on their shelves. The company has sales representatives located throughout the US. The H.W. Wilson Company was founded in 1898 by Halsey William Wilson. It was acquired by EBSCO Industries in 2011.
