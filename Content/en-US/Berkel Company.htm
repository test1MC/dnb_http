One could say that Berkel's foodservice equipment is the best thing since sliced bread. In fact, it helps produce sliced bread. Berkel makes bread slicers, food processors, heavy-duty mixers, meat slicers, meat tenderizers, stir-fry cutters, and vacuum packing equipment. The company also makes planetary mixers, bowl-type food cutters, vertical cutter/mixers, food scales, and a range of meat grinders. Berkel, which traces its 19th-century beginnings to the Netherlands, is associated with Vulcan-Hart and is a trademarked division of Illinois Tool Works ' ITW Food Equipment Group. Berkel Company serves the US, Canada, and Puerto Rico; sister companies include Avery Berkel and Berkel International.
