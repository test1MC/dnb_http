Like a veteran goalie, CIC International holds its own on defense. The company makes military equipment and systems for governments and defense contractors worldwide. CIC's main products include night vision systems, munitions, and aerospace components, including helicopter parts. Its naval services include upgrading weapons systems and providing engineering for surface warfare problems. The company's CIC Communications division makes radios and other electronic equipment for use on the battlefield. The company also acts as a prime contractor for the supply of munitions for the US government.
