Business gets its buzz from soft drinks at Nichols. The company makes and markets Vimto, a fruit-flavored fizzy drink sold in more than 85 countries, and, in the UK, the name brands Sunkist (licensed), a carbonated fruit drink, and Panda fruit juices and flavored waters. Customers include major retailers, wholesalers, and cash-and-carry food and beverage businesses. The company ranks as the UK's #3 player in the UK soft drink draft sector, supplying the Cabana, Dayla, and Ben Shaws soft drink labels on tap to pubs, clubs, restaurants, and other recreational outlets. Founded in 1908, Nichols generates nearly 80% of its sales in the UK, with the remainder from export markets.

Nichols divides its business into Still and Carbonated, which each account for 50% of revenue. Brands include Vimto, Feel Good, Levi Roots. Through Nichols Dispense, the company dispenses soft drinks such as Vimto, Coa-Cola, Irn Bru, Sunkist, and Ocean Spray. The company also does a range of sweets, including Vimto-branded jelly beans.

Outside of the UK, the Group operates in the Middle East, Africa, and the Rest of the World. The UK brings in about 80% of sales, the Middle East 11%, and Africa 7%.

Nichols has brands in the still, carbonated, dispense and frozen drinks categories, and they can be found in supermarkets, discount retailers, cash and carries, pubs, clubs, bars, restaurants, and leisure outlets.

The company advertises on TV, radio, outdoor, and through digital advertising and social media.
