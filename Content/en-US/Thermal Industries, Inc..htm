Thermal Industries manufactures custom-made vinyl windows, doors, sunrooms, patios, decking planks, railings, and boat dock systems. The company distributes its products in the US through branches in states east of the Mississippi. It also has an international division that serves its customers worldwide. Through its partnership with GE Retail Sales Finance , Thermal Industries also provides financing services. The company operates research and manufacturing facilities in the suburbs of Pittsburgh. Thermal Industries was founded in the early 1960s, and it is a subsidiary of Atrium Companies , which filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy in 2010, but quickly emerged.
