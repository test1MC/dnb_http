True Temper Sports' golf shafts have withstood many a temper tantrum on the links. The company is a leading manufacturer of golf club shafts, which are marketed under the True Temper, Grafalloy, and Project X names and used by club makers such as Callaway , Golfsmith , TaylorMade-adidas , and Mizuno . True Temper also produces hockey sticks, lacrosse shafts, and components for bicycles and automobiles. The company boasts operations in the US, Europe, China, Japan, and Australia. True Temper restructured its business and slashed its debt load while enduring a stint in Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection in 2009.
