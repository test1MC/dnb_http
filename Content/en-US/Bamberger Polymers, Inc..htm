Need a few tons of plastic? Bamberger Polymers distributes plastic resins for molders, extruders, compounders, and manufacturers that use plastics to make everything from automotive components to garbage bags. Bamberger distributes resins from international manufacturers (such as Ineos , Westlake Chemical , and INVISTA ), as well as its own lines of polyethylene, polypropylene, and polystyrene (under the Bapolene and Bapolan brands). Bamberger delivers more than 850 million pounds of bulk and packaged resins annually via subsidiaries throughout North America and the rest of theÂ world. The company is owned by its executive management.
