Salada Foods Jamaica wants to help you wake up and start your day no matter where you are. The company is Jamaica's largest coffee producer with exports to the rest of the Caribbean, North America, Europe, and China. In addition to a variety of blends of Arabica and Robusta coffee beans, including decaf, it produces instant coffee, chicory-coffee blends, cocoa mix, and bagged black tea. Other products include sugar, a fruit-flavored drink mix powder, and fruit-flavored gelatin. It sells to hotels, retailers, and restaurants through about 35 distributors. Formed in 1958, Salada Foods has been trading publicly since the country's stock market opened in 1969.
