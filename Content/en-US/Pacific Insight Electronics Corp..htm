Pacific Insight Electronics lights the way. The company manufactures and distributes electronic components and assemblies primarily for the automotive, commercial truck, off-road, and specialty vehicle markets. Products include daytime running lamp modules, headlight control systems, and light-emitting diode (LED) light bars for cars, trucks, and boats. It also makes keyless ignition systems, gauges, security systems, and wiring harnesses. Customers include Caterpillar , Ford , Toyota , Harley-Davidson , Volkswagen , and Peterbilt . Co-founder, CEO, and CFO Stuart Ross owns about 13% of Pacific Insight. The company has operations in Canada and Mexico.
