SWCC SHOWA HOLDINGS (formerly Showa Electric Wire & Cable) likes to remain well connected. The company makes electric power, optical fiber, communication, and industrial cable assemblies and related electrical connectors and parts. It also makes insulated, magnetic, aluminum, and superconducting wires, as well as vibration isolators (used to help buildings withstand earthquakes) and other precision devices. SWCC is developing low-frequency sound absorption and insulation materials. The company sells products to telecommunications, electronics, and utility companies, and to makers of copy machines and printers. It gets more than three-quarters of sales from energy related products.
