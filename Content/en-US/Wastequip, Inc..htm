Wastequip doesn't talk trash -- it takes care of it. Wastequip provides large and small metal containers for on-site waste collection; balers and compactors for waste processing; and containers, trailers, hoists, and other mechanical equipment used to transport solid, liquid, and hazardous waste. Its brands include Wastequip, Toter, Galbreath and Accurate, Pioneer, Parts Place, Mountain Tarp, and Cusco. Customers are national and regional solid-waste disposal haulers, commercial businesses, and public agencies. Private equity firm Centerbridge Partners controls the company.

In mid-2012 the company was acquired by Centerbridge Partners in a deal that allowed it to eliminate $550 million of its debt and obtain $40 million for making investments.

Wastequip operates through nearly 25 waste handling equipment manufacturing plants in California, Colorado, Florida, Illinois, Indiana, Kentucky, Louisiana, Massachusetts, Minnesota, Missouri, North Carolina, New Jersey, New York, Ohio, Oklahoma, Oregon, Tennessee, and Texas. International locations reside in Ontario, Canada; and Coahuila, Mexico.

Wastequip manages its business across four units: containers (including hoppers), mobile products (including tarps, hoists, and vacuum trucks), plastics (Toter brand carts and containers), and technical (compactors, balers, and intermodals).

Wastequip serves a range of North American markets including chain stores, convenience stores, environmental services, grocery and food retail, malls, shopping centers, oil and gas production, restaurants, and waste management companies. The company's brands include Wastequip, Toter, Galbreath, Pioneer, Accurate, Cusco, Mountain Tarp, and Parts Place.
