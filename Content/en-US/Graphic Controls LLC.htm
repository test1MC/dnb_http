Graphic Controls has printing and other graphic-related activities under control. The company manufactures precision charts and pens for hundreds of models of industrial and medical recording instruments. Other products include ink jet fluids, pressure sensitive bar code label printers, case coding printers, and continuous ink jet printers. It also produces a wide range of thermal printing and papers used for ticketing, as well as plastic magnetic-strip cards. The company markets its product worldwide to businesses in the manufacturing, medical, and oil and gas industries.

The company has more than 50,000 customers in over 80 countries worldwide.

It has warehouses in US and Canada and manufacturing plants in the US and the UK.

Graphic Controls has sales offices in Belgium, Canada, the UK, and the US. Customers include ABB , Honeywell , Leeds & Northrup,Partlow, and Yokogawa .

In 2011 the company acquired DIA-Nielsen Group Companies (with operations in the US, Germany and France). This transaction allows Graphic Controls to better serve the Industrial Recorder Supplies market.
