Evogene is looking for the next (man-made) evolution in plants. The plant genomics company uses biotechnology and advanced breeding methods to produce cotton, rice, corn, soybean, and wheat seeds designed to be more resistant to environmental stress like drought and disease, pests, and insects. The company realizes revenue from research and development and when it creates or enhances certain traits desired by its customers, which include Monsanto , Bayer , DuPont , and Syngenta . Evogene also develops agricultural chemicals and seed for biodiesel feedstock plants. The Israeli company has additional testing facilities in Brazil and Argentina. It was formed in 2002 and began trading publicly in the US in 2013.
