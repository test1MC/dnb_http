Biohit develops, manufactures, and sells diagnostic test systems for health care, research, and industrial laboratories around the world. Its Biohit HealthCare products include testing assays and instruments used to diagnose gastrointestinal ailments and prevent cancer and other diseases. Its main products include blood sample-based GastroPanel tests used to diagnose stomach illnesses; biopsy specimen quick tests to diagnose lactose intolerance and H. pylori infections (a cause of gastric ulcers); and ColonView examinations used for the early detection of intestinal bleeding, a risk indicator of colorectal cancer. Biohit has sold its liquid handling business.
