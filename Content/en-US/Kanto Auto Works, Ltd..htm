Kanto Auto Works brings a can-do attitude to its service for Toyota Motor . Starting with the Toyopet car, Kanto Auto Works has been making auto bodies for Toyota since 1950. Its Higashi Fuji plant makes the bodies for eight Toyota and Lexus models, including the Corolla sedan and the Isis minivan. Kanto's Iwate plant builds bodies for five Toyota vehicles. The company was founded in 1946 as Kanto Electric Motor Works when a Japanese naval base was reconstructed for peacetime industry; it took its present name four years later. Kanto also manufactures car-lifting equipment for vehicle maintenance. In late 2011 the company, previously 50%-owned by Toyota, became a wholly owned subsidiary of the titan automaker.
