Barry Manilow might write the songs, but Sencorp builds the machines that keep the world's workplace humming. The company designs and manufactures packaging equipment, from thermoforming machines to blister packagers, lab sealers, as well as custom-built machines for specialized wraps. Its line appeared in 1946, when a predecessor company launched the Sentinel sealer. Sencorp equipment packages electronics, food, and pharmaceuticals, as well as testing material seals and car parts shields. It holds global manufacturing rights to Enviroshell, an eco-package sealer. The privately held company evolved from a management buyout of DT Industries ' Converting Technologies, backed by investment firm Management Capital.
