Printer Wetzel Brothers specializes in producing collateral for "point-of-purchase" displays, meaning advertisements or other marketing paraphernalia located in store aisles, at checkout counters, and elsewhere where products are sold. The company's services include design, printing, finishing, and fulfillment of such products as counter displays, standing cardboard likenesses of celebrities, and logoed floor decals and carpets. Wetzel Brothers is a subsidiary of commercial printing industry leader Consolidated Graphics .
