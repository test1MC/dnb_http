TRANSGENE's expertise in gene therapy may help it transform into a cancer fighter. The biotech company is focusing its research and development efforts on its cancer vaccine candidates, which target oncology conditions including breast, lung, prostate, liver, and other cancers. It also has vaccines in development targeting infectious diseases such as hepatitis C virus (HCV) and human papillomavirus (HPV). Much of the company's research is conducted through partnerships and licensing agreements. The Mérieux family owns a controlling stake in TRANSGENE through Institut Mérieux Group.
