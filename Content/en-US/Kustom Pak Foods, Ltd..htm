Kustom Pak Foods produces packaged meat products, including portion-controlled and vacuum-packed pork, beef, and poultry. The company operates from a 175,000 sq. ft. production center, which is situated just half a mile from two cold storage facilities. Its products are sold primarily to foodservice, retail, and industrial customers. Kustom Pak also offers private-label services. Founded in 1983, the company is controlled by the Sherman family and led by CEO Stan Sherman Sr.
