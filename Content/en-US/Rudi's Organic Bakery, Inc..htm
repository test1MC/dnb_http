Rudi's Organic Bakery is on a natural roll. The company produces organic artisan breads in flavors such as Country French and Tuscan Roasted Garlic and sandwich bread in low-carb and whole grain varieties. Other products include hamburger buns, hot dog rolls, and tortillas. It is launching its Rudi's Gluten Free Bakery line, with sandwich, multigrain, and cinnamon-raisin varieties, in July 2010. Its baked goods are available at supermarkets and natural food stores throughout the US. Rudi's Organic Bakery was founded as a local bakery in Boulder, Colorado in 1976 and began producing organic products in 1990. Rudi's was acquired by The Hain Celestial Group in 2014 from investment firm Charterhouse Group .
