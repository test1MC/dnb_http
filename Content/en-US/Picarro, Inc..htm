Picarro manufactures optical instruments for bioscience, industrial, and medical diagnostic equipment. The company has operations in California and Ontario. Founded in 1997, Picarro has received funding from Benchmark Capital , Greylock , Skymoon Ventures, Stanford University , and Weston Presidio, among others. The company sold its Laser Products business to rival Newport in order to focus on its ultra-trace gas analyzer and isotopic analyzer products. Picarro's analytical instrument business has applications in atmospheric monitoring, automotive emissions, food fraud detection, petrochemical process control, and semiconductor manufacturing.
