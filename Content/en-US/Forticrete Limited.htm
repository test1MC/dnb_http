If you want your home to be a literal castle, Forticrete can supply the masonry products to build it, but you're on your own with the moat. Forticrete's home, castle, and commercial building offerings include masonry products (standard and specification masonry), cast stone products (walling stone, cast stone, landscape walling), and roofing products (ventilation systems; ridge systems; and clay, stone, and slate products). It also makes retaining wall systems under the Kiveton brand. The company, which operates from about 10 locations in the UK, has served clients such as Cambridge University and Nuffield Hospital . Founded in the 1920s, Forticrete is a subsidiary of cement and concrete group CRH .
