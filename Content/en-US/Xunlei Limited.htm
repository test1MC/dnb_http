Cayman Islands-incorporated Xunlei is an Internet content provider in China. Through its subsidiaries, the company operates a cloud computing platform that enables Internet users to access digital media content, such as online games, software, and high-definition movies and TV. It also offers a series of download and streaming services, including an application that speeds up download access (Xunlei Accelerator) and a video streaming portal (Xunlei Kankan). Xunlei makes its revenues from subscriptions, advertising on the Downloader and Kankan sites, and other services. The company went public in 2014.

Xunlei raised almost $88 million through its public offering. The company will use the net proceeds to invest in technology and product development, including potential acquisitions.

Although the competition in China is fierce, Xunlei is working to set itself apart by offering increased Internet access and speed. The company hopes to provide a superior user experience allowing easy access to digital media content anywhere, anytime, and on any device.
