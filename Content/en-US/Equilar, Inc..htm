Equilar earns its bread and butter by finding out how much everyone else gets paid. The executive compensation research company manages a database compiled with the salary information of more than 20,000 executives of public companies registered with the US Securities and Exchange Commission . Equilar reports and analyses are available through a subscription; customers can also buy printed copies of its reports. Fortune 500 corporations use the information to determine executive and board member pay; consulting firms such as Deloitte , and media firms such as Bloomberg and the Wall Street Journal also use Equilar's data. The company was founded in 2001 by CEO David Chun.
