Wintec Industries is trying to rack up big wins in the high-tech world. The company, which was founded in 1988, makes computer memory modules for OEMs, including customized products for server, storage, and telecommunications applications. Wintec also provides memory modules and third-party products to resellers and systems integrators. In addition to components, the company makes a variety of computer systems, servers, and peripherals for corporate and government end-users, and sells many of its products on its Web site. Wintec also distributes computer hardware and software. It has facilities in China, Hong Kong, Singapore, Taiwan, and the US. The company is owned by president Sue Jeng.
