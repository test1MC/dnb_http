Family owned and operated Sokol Blosser, which does business as Sokol Blosser Winery, produces Pinot Noir and Pinot Gris wines, which can be bought off the winery or via mail. The Oregon winery also produces Evolution, a blend of nine white varietals. Paterno Imports distributes the company's wines to more than 30 US states as well as Alberta and British Columbia, Canada. The family-owned and -operated winery was founded by husband-and-wife team Bill and Susan Sokol Blosser in 1971.
