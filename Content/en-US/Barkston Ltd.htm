One of the largest plastics companies in the UK, Barkston manufactures and distributes a variety of plastic materials for customers, including fabricated components and prototyped parts. The company uses such materials as PVC, polycarbonate, polypropylene, acrylic, recycled plastic, and others. Barkston serves customers in the protective equipment, medical, architectural, nuclear, renewable energy, and chemical processing markets, among others. The company also supplies golf clubs, schools, and visitor attractions with plastic outdoor furniture. Barkston additionally provides manufacturing services that include design, machining, and laser welding. Formed in 1969, Barkston was bought out by management in 2003.

Barkston serves various sectors such as architecture, chemical processing, energy, medical, nuclear, protective equipment, and transportation.

In order to grow, the company continues to invest in the most up-to-date CNC equipment enabling it to quickly and competitively provide repeatable, high volume, accurate, high-quality finishes, batch consistency, and close tolerance work.
