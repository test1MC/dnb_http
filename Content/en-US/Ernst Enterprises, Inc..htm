Ernst Enterprises knows the value of a solid foundation. Operating as Ernst Concrete, the company makes and distributes ready-mix concrete and related products in Georgia, Indiana, Kentucky, and Ohio. The company also provides concrete pumping services for residential, industrial, commercial, and government clients. Ernst Enterprises makes standard concrete mixes as well as custom and specialty products at the approximately 30 plants it operates. Other products sold include trowels, marking implements, and other finishing tools; concrete nails, cures, and stains; and general construction supplies. The firm also sells used mixers to other concrete suppliers. The family-owned company was founded in 1946.
