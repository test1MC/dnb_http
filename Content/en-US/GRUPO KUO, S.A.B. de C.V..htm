Diversified Mexican manufacturer GRUPO KUO (formerly DESC, S.A. de C.V.) has a hand in wheels, meals, and pork belly deals. KUO's Automotive division's products include axles, clutches, stamped parts, pistons, wheels, and transmissions. Its Consumer division offers food products, including salsas, tomato paste, and tuna, which it supplies mainly to Mexico and the southwestern US; brands include Herdez, La Victoria, and Doña Maria. KUO markets its Kekén brand of pork meat Mexico. It also makes decorative laminates and particle board through its subsidiary Rexcel. KUO's Chemical division produces such products as synthetic rubber, polystyrene, carbon black, adhesives, sealants, and pigments.
