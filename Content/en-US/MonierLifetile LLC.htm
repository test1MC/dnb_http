MonierLifetile is one of the largest manufacturers of concrete roofing tiles in the US. The company serves the residential building market, offering a variety of tile colors and tile accessories; it operates about a dozen plants in the US and Mexico and has been looking to Florida for growth. More than 95% of the company's sales are generated online. Formed by the combination of Monier and Boral Lifetile in 1997, MonierLifetile was a joint venture between PAI Partners' Monier and Boral until 2010, when the latter firm bought out its partner's stake. Lafarge , a former partner in the business, sold its interest in 2007.
