Lung Kee (Bermuda) has nothing to do with lungs and isn't in Bermuda. The Hong Kong-based company, known as LKM, makes mould base and die steel products including cold and hot working tool steels, hot and cold working steel, powder steel, porous steel and specialty mould steel. It produces steel at five factories in China and one each in Malaysia, Japan, and Taiwan. LKM also distributes tool steel from Assab Steel (Sweden) and Daido Steel of Japan. It sells and distributes its products in Asia, Europe, North America, and Australia. LKM was founded in 1975 and went public in 1993.
