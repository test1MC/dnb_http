Columbia-Staver Limited cleans up with heat sinks. The company makes heat sinks under brands that include Thermofin, Thermofab, Thermopower, and Thermoflag, as well as semiconductor manufacturing hardware and precision metal pressings. Columbia-Staver's products additionally include EMI/RFI screenings, clips, spacers, and other accessories used in the assembly of semiconductors. The company also designs multi-slide, multi-stage, transfer, and lamination tools. Columbia-Staver serves customers in the aerospace, automotive engineering, electronics, and technology manufacturing industries, among others.

The company operates its design and manufacturing facility at Wickford, UK. It also maintains sales and service centers for the Chinese market at Shenzhen, Suzhou, Shanghai, and Beijing with a distribution hub in Hong Kong.

Columbia-Staver primarily targets the consumer, LED, telecommunications, power, medical, and automotive sectors. Its distributors are located at Singapore, Czech Republic, Switzerland, Germany, Taiwan, the US, Belgium and France. The company also operates a subsidiary trading company (Techford International Co. Ltd.) in Hong Kong.
