United States Pipe and Foundry Company (US Pipe) manufactures ductile iron pipe and related fittings, valves, and gaskets. The company makes pipe at 20 plants in the US and Mexico. US Pipe also manufactures coated, fabricated, and lined pipe used in wastewater treatment facilities. In addition, US Pipe manufactures pipe in metric dimensions for the international market. US Pipe traces its historical roots back to its founding in 1991. In 2016 it was acquired by Forterra Building Products.

US Pipe recycles waste material (ferrous scrap) into ductile iron pipe for use in water and wastewater systems throughout the world. The company operates through 20 plans in the US and Mexico.

The company sells its products through its multiple branches and as well as through its sales representatives and outlets nationwide. It also sells its products throughout North America, Panama, Columbia, Australia, Europe, and the Middle East.

US Pipe fortifies its operations through the use of acquisitions. In 2015 it bought Custom Fab, a fabricator of pipes and fittings mainly for the waterworks industry. The deal gave US Pipe facilities in Orlando, Florida; Atlanta; Phoenix; Portland; Philadelphia; and San Antonio, Texas.
