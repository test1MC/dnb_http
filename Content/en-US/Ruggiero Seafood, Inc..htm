Ruggiero Seafood is not squeamish when it comes to squid. The New Jersey has company processed and distributed some 300 million pounds of domestic calamari (squid) since its beginnings in 1938. Along with squid, Ruggiero offers other shellfish under its own Fisherman's Pride and other, imported brand names. Its products, available throughout the US,  include calamari, squid ink, mussels, clams, conch, and seafood salads, all of which it delivers directly to customers using its own fleet of refrigerated trucks. Ruggiero Seafood was founded by Andrew Ruggiero and is owned and operated by his nephew and company president, Rocco Ruggiero.
