Business plan? It's in the bag for Webster Industries, which manufactures plastic trash bags, kitchen bags, and food contract bags. In addition to selling its own brands, the company is a leading private label supplier to many retailers worldwide. It serves both the retail and commercial and institutional markets through a network of brokers and sales representatives. Webster Industries is a leading maker of certified recycled content trash bags; some of its products are made with recycled polyethylene that decomposes after exposure to sunlight and/or otherwise biodegrades more rapidly than traditional plastics.
