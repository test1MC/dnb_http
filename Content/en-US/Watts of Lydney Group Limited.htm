To know watt's going on in the UK transportation industry, ask Watts of Lydney. Watts of Lydney (doing business as Watts Group) is a holding company that oversees several businesses. Watts Truck & Van Centres, a network of commercial vehicle dealers, sells and services parts, components, and new and pre-owned DAF trucks, and Piaggio and Fiat vans through a half dozen locations across the south midlands and South Wales. Plysolene Limited makes waterproofing products for the building sector, while Watts Urethane processes a wide array of molded polyurethane products and components such as wear pads, dampers, rods, tubes, pulleys, shock absorbers, springs, and wheels.

The company is based in Lydney, Gloucestershire, UK and offer services in South Wales (Cardiff, Swansea, and Newport). In addition to the aforementioned operations, Watts of Lydney owns Watts Properties, which manages a portfolio of 12 retail and trade counter properties and industrial units in the UK.

The company's divisions serve a range of customers, with Watts Truck & Van serving commercial fleet owners and individual businesses; Watts Urethane supplying applications to clients from motor sport components to furniture making; Plysolene serving customers including point of sale display, packaging, construction industry, and insulation; and Watts Properties catering to a number of Watts Group subsidiary companies and external tenants.

Watts of Lydney traces its history back to a general store and bakery that was established in 1850.
