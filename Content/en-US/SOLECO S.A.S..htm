SOLECO, one of Europe's largest prepared salad and vegetable producers, specializes in mixed bags. SOLECO (also known as The Florette Group) markets more than 200 products under the Florette brand, including salads, salad dressings, frozen vegetables, and soups. The company sells to retail and catering customers in Europe and the UK. The Florette brand is the European market leader in prepared salads. The products are prepared at five company production plants located in France, Spain, and the UK. A subsidiary of the agri-giant Agrial , SOLECO secures its mostly French produce from sister companies Prim'Co and Européenne Fruitière.
