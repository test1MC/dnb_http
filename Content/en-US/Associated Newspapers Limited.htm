Associated Newspapers is the flagship newspaper publishing subsidiary of UK media giant Daily Mail and General Trust (DMGT) with its mainstay national publications The Daily Mail (with a circulation of about 2 million) and The Mail on Sunday . The subsidiary also publishes the Irish Mail on Sunday , along free dailies London Metro and London Lite . Other titles include Loot (free advertiser) and 7DAYS (a newspaper in Dubai). In addition to its print publications, Associated Newspapers distributes news and information online through a number of websites.

Like other newspaper publishers, DMGT has been struggling with declines in readership and advertising revenue. It has been focused on cutting costs throughout its publishing operations, including a number of staff reductions.
