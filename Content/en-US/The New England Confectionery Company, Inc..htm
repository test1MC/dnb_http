This company's products will beg you to "Be good" and "Tweet Me." One of the nation's oldest confectioners, The New England Confectionery Company (NECCO) is the maker of the familiar Valentine's Day candy, Sweethearts Conversation Hearts candies. (It makes some 6 billion conversation hearts a year.) The company is also know for its powdered-sugared eponymous NECCO Wafers, Clark bars, Candy Buttons, Mary Janes (saltwater taffy named after the creator's favorite aunt), and Squirrel Nut Zippers (a chewy mixture of caramel and peanuts). NECCO's confections are sold through retail food outlets across the US. American Capital , a Maryland-based investment firm, controls the candy maker.
