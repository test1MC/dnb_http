Diversified manufacturer Ube Industries operates more than 100 subsidiaries in about half a dozen core businesses. Its largest segment is chemicals and plastics, which includes the production of caprolactam (nylon raw material), nylon resins for industrial plastics, ABS resins for common plastics, and polyethylene and butadiene rubber. Cement and construction materials manufacturing is the company's other major segment. In addition, it makes machinery and metal products, imports coal and operates a power utility, and produces the active pharmaceutical ingredient (API) for several brand-name drugs. Ube Industries has operations in Japan as well as in the Americas, Europe, and throughout Asia.
