Viva Optique has set its sites on keeping its customers in shades worldwide. It operates Viva International Group, a leading worldwide eyewear and sunglass manufacturer. It has about 15 international offices and distributes its products in more than 60 countries. Brand names include Bongo , Candie's , Ermenegildo Zegna , Escada , Givenchy , Guess? , Harley-Davidson , and Tommy Hilfiger . The company, which was founded in 1978 by Harvey Ross, has in-house design teams located in Italy and New Jersey that pump out some 1,000 designs yearly. The company is part of HVHC, a subsidiary of health insurance provider Highmark .
