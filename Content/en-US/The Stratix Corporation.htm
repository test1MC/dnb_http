The Stratix Corporation helps sales and distribution professionals straddle the technology gap when they're on the road. The company provides managed mobile services primarily to Fortune 1000 firms. Its services are divided into planning (business process review, mobile readiness), deploying (purchasing, provisioning, installation), supporting (technical support, inventory and repair, decommissioning), and monitoring and measuring (reporting, shipping history and management, repair status and history). Stratix works with a host of hardware, software, communications, and other partners -- Honeywell , Samsung , SAP , Ingram Micro , AT&T -- to ensure it can support the broad range of products available to clients.

The company serves large clients in the transportation, logistics, and retail industries, among others; customers include Abercrombie & Fitch , American Airlines , Caterpillar , ConAgra , Lowe's , Office Depot , and Walt Disney .

Formerly a seller of hardware and software for mobile users, Stratix is focused now on managed mobile services. In early 2013 it sold its software division to the UK's ServicePower Technologies.

In 2012 the company partnered with Canadian firm PiiComm to enable both managed mobile services providers to better serve clients with business in the US and Canada.
