With its crisply traditional red-and-black tartan packaging, Walkers Shortbread has taken Scottish shortbread from the Isles to the world. The family-run company manufactures more than 170 products including packaged biscuits, cakes, fruitcakes, meringues, oatcakes, shortbreads, and mincemeat tarts, all sporting the Walkers brand. The company also makes cookies and biscuits under the Weight Watchers label in the UK, and organic oat biscuits for the Duchy label. Walkers' customers include food retailers and foodservice operators worldwide. Its products can also be ordered through the company's website. Walkers began baking shortbread in the Scottish Highlands in 1898.
