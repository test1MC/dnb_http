Ace thinks its Kaces beat the band. Ace Products Enterprises makes cases and bags for musical instruments and audiovisual equipment under the Kaces and Reunion Blues labels. Its cases and bags are constructed of fabric, leather, and wood, and are available through instrument makers and music stores in the US and Canada. In addition to providing gig bags, Ace Products manufactures RocknRoller heavy-duty carts, which can be used for moving electronics, music gear, industrial equipment, and other unwieldy items. Carts and accessories are distributed internationally to music dealers, hardware stores, and commercial equipment suppliers.
