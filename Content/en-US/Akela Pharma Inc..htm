Drug developer Akela Pharma is hoping to be there before the last dose of pain meds wears off and the next dose kicks in. The company's lead product candidate is Fentanyl Taifun, an inhaled formulation of cancer pain fighter fentanyl, to be used in conjunction with other drugs to manage severe pain. Akela Pharma is also working on research in areas not related to pain relief; it is developing a growth hormone stimulator to help treat frailty and malnutrition in patients with kidney failure. Akela Pharma's subsidiary PharmaForm provides contract drug development services and specializes in controlled-release drug delivery technology.
