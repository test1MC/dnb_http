Better Baked Foods thinks that if you're gonna have some pizza, you'd better choose its products. As a supplier to the foodservice industry, the company produces frozen, pre-baked bread products, including pizzas, French bread, breadsticks, foccacia, and breakfast and dessert pizzas as well as garlic bread, Texas toast, and appetizers. It also offers kid-friendly food products like cheeseburgers, grilled cheese sandwiches, s'mores, and cinnamon breadsticks. Better Baked offers custom product-development and -packaging for its customers. It has operations in New York and Pennsylvania. Better Baked Foods was founded in 1970 as B&B Pizza by Robert Miller and Bruce Underwood.
