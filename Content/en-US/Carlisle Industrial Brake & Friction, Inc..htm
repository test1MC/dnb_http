Carlisle Industrial Brake & Friction puts the stop in stop-and-go traffic. The subsidiary of Carlisle Companies makes braking systems for large trucks, as well as for industrial vehicles and equipment used in the agriculture, construction, industrial, military, and mining sectors; it also makes rotor brakes for use in wind power applications. Products include brakes for parking, emergency, and trailers, as well as drum and combination brakes, hydraulic controls, and friction products. OEM customers include AGCO , Case New Holland, Caterpillar , Komatsu , and General Dynamics . Carlisle Industrial Brake & Friction operates through facilities in China, India, Japan, the Netherlands, the UK, and the US.
