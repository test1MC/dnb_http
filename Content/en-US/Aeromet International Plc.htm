Aeromet International doesn't fish, but it does do a lot of casting. The company casts aerospace, defense, and medical components from aluminum and magnesium. Products include avionic housings, escape doors, fuel pumps, aeroengine heat exchangers, and air intakes. Aeromet's patented A20X is a version of a high property alloy with advantages that include more stress resistance. With agents in Germany, Italy, Japan, Korea, and the US, Aeromet operates a sand foundry in Sittingbourne to make aluminum and magnesium castings. In Rochester and Worcester it operates two foundries for making aluminum investment castings. In 2015 the company was acquired by investment firm Privet Capital.
