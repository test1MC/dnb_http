If you enjoy munching cherry sours while watching the latest flick, you can thank Judson-Atkinson Candies. The San Antonio company manufactures jelly beans, hard candies, fruit slices, gum drops, salt water taffy, and sour balls in a variety of flavors, as well as other confections such as taffy, pralines, and Christmas, Easter, Halloween, and Valentine's DayÂ specialty items. Chairman, president, and CEO Dr. Basil Atkinson Jr. bought the company in 1983; it started out in business in 1899 as Jenner Manufacturing and sold five-cent fudge bars. Atkinson and his family also own sister company, Atkinson Candy .
