Transamerican Technologies International wants to put the focus on the medical examining room. The company, also known as TTI Medical, makes video and digital camera adaptors for surgical microscopes and endoscopes. It also makes suction and irrigation tubes for minimally invasive surgery, as well as handpieces, micromanipulators, and other accessories for laser surgery. The TTI Imaging product line includes image capturing equipment and related software. Products are sold to hospitals and other health care providers in North America and internationally, primarily under the ACCU-Beam and ACCU-Surg names. Transamerican Technologies International was founded in 1984.
