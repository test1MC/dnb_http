It's not the aurora borealis; Aurora Fashions is the holding company behind several stellar apparel brands. Aurora operates through Britain's luxury women's labels Coast, Warehouse, and Oasis. Each targets a consumer niche: the Coast label is stitched to premium occasionwear for affluent women; Warehouse brands are designed for the high-style, trend-conscious urban woman; and Oasis offerings attract the aspirational, fashion-forward woman in her 20's. The company's lines are sold online and through some 1,260 stand alone retail stores and concessions in the UK, as well as Europe, Australia, the Middle East, Sandinavia, and the Far East. Managers of predecessor Mosaic Fashions and Iceland's Kaupthing own Aurora.
