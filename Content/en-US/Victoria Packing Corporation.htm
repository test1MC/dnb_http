Victoria Packing makes and markets more than 150 mostly Italian food products, including canned vegetables, edible oils, sauces, condiments, spices, and dips. The food maker peddles its products in grocery stores and warehouse clubs, as well as through foodservice companies throughout the US. Victoria Packing has operations in New York, the Midwest, and New England. The company also provides private-label and co-packing services for more than 50 firms. Victoria Packing was founded by Pelligrino Aquilina in 1929 and was a family-owned business until it was purchased by US-based private-equity firm Huron Capital Partners in February 2011.
