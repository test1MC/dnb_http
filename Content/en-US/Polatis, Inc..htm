Polatis develops ultra-low loss optical switches based on its DirectLight optical beam-steering technology. The company's switching hardware and software serves clients in the communications, defense, and instrumentation industries, among others, for carrier networking, testing and measurement, mobile communications and satellite systems, data centers, and video distribution. It touts its DirectLight platform as enabling high-quality, low-energy products designed for demanding environments. Polatis also offers services such as training, field support, integration, custom configuration, and new product design.

Polatis is headquartered in Bedford, Massachusetts, as well as Poland and the UK.

To grow, Polatis depends upon partnerships and affiliations with other companies. Partners that have helped with manufacturing tests have included JDSU Corporation , Logic Media Solutions GmbH, Phoenix Datacom, and Marubun Corporation .

In 2012 Polatis and JENCO Technologies, a global manufacturer of low-loss optical switching technologies, signed a reseller agreement. Per the agreement, JENCO will focus its selling efforts with Polatis on the government and defense industries, which are a core emphasis for both companies.
