TAT Technologies helps militaries keep their cool. It makes environmental control systems, heat transfer equipment, cooling systems, heat exchangers, precoolers, oil coolers, cold plates, and brazed assemblies used in commercial and military aircraft, defense systems, and other electronic equipment. The company also makes and overhauls aerospace emergency and secondary power systems for F-16 fighters and air conditioning systems for military shelters. TAT Technologies owns 70% of Bental Industries, an Israel-based motion industries company. The company gets more than half of its sales in North America.
