You wouldn't be able to keep the pedal to the metal without DURA Automotive Systems' driver control systems. The company is a leading supplier of pedal systems, parking brake mechanisms, manual and automatic transmission gear shifter systems, and auto cables. DURA also designs and makes engineered assemblies such as latches and seating adjustment controls, as well as structural door modules and exterior trim. The company sells to auto OEMs and many suppliers in the Americas, Europe, and Asia.

Headquartered in Auburn Hills, Michigan, DURA has more than 35 manufacturing operations in 15 countries spanning North America, South America, Europe, and Asia.

With a presence on more than 300 models around the globe, DURA markets its automotive products to North American, Asian, and European OEMs and many leading Tier One automotive suppliers. Some of its customers have included Aston Martin, Ford , GM , Chrysler , Honda , Daimler, VW Group , Audi , Toyota , Renault, Nissan , BMW , and Subaru .
