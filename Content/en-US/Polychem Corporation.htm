Polychem Corporation makes and sells plastic package strapping, as well as power tools, bundling equipment, and other strapping-related gear. The company produces three kinds of strapping: polypropylene (economical strapping), polyester (superior strength and tension retention), and woven and bonded cord (flat rope). Polychem also makes and sells strapping machines and related supplies. Subsidiaries include Anderson Parts, Evergreen Plastics, Polychem OMS Systems, and RC Packaging Systems.

Polychem operates a PET bottle recycling and polyester strap manufacturing plant, Evergreen Plastics, in Clyde, Ohio. It also has a Distribution in Los Angeles to serve West Coast customers. The company has a distribution network spanning 70 countries.

Its operating subsidiaries include Anderson Parts (a premier custom machine parts company), Evergreen Plastics (a plastic bottle recycler), Polychem OMS Systems LLC (a provider of equipment systems), and RC Packaging LLC (a cord strapping and metal buckles conducting business).

Polychem serves a range of industries, including can and bottle, corrugated, brick/block and paver systems, lumber and panels, graphic arts, textile fibers, and cut paper and totes.

Polychem's strength in the polyester and polypropylene plastic strapping segment has led to an expansion of several related product lines and services such as strapping equipment and systems, strapping tools, cord strapping, equipment parts and service, and accessories. In a related move, in 2013 the company opened a new distribution center in Mentor, Ohio.

Founded in Willoughby, Ohio in 1975, the company moved to a larger manufacturing site in Mentor, Ohio in 1978.
