Kanthal Corporation wants to get you heated up. Kanthal (dba Kanthal Palm Coast) is a part of Kanthal AB, itself a subsidiary of Sandvik 's Materials Technology unit. Kanthal has four lines of wire products: heating/resistance (used to regulate electricity), conductive alloys (aluminum, beryllium, and copper wire), medical (gold and platinum plated for use in catheters, implants, and sensor coils), and specialty (custom designs). Its precision and ultra-fine wire products are used in electronics, aerospace applications, disk drives, and medical devices. Kanthal's manufacturing facility is located in Florida; capabilities include wire drawing, annealing, plating, anodizing, and roll milling.
