BPL Business Media gives people in the telecommunications, technology, and broadcasting industries something to talk about. BPL Business Media publishes IBE (which stands for international broadcast engineer), a magazine for the electronic media industry in the UK. The company also produces IBC Connected World, an annual event for professionals engaged in the creation, management, and delivery of entertainment and media content worldwide. In addition, its Comms Dealer magazine, event, website, and electronic news bulletin focus on the Voice and ICT (information and communication technologies) industry. BPL Business Media was founded in 1991.
