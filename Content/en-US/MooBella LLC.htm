Frozen assets are a beautiful thing for MooBella. The company uses proprietary technology to make vending-type machines that store fresh ingredients and mix, flash freeze, aerate, and dispense ice cream on demand in about 45 seconds. Users can choose a variety of flavors and mix-ins from the machines that are designed to be located inside cafeterias and other food-service locations. MooBella is currently field-testing a few machines in New England with plans for nationwide, and perhaps international, availability. The company intends to sell the machines to food-service operators rather than franchise. Paul Kateman launched MooBella in 2001 with help from ice cream industry veteran Bruce Ginsberg (president).
