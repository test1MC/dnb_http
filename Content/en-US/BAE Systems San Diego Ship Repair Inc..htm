You have to shape up before you ship out, and that's just what BAE Systems San Diego Ship Repair helps with. The company repairs, converts, upgrades, and overhauls non-nuclear ships. Its facilities boast five piers and two floating dry docks. It offers services such as comprising blast/paint marine coatings, carpentry and electric, structural welding, and crane operations. Owned by BAE Systems, Inc ., it makes its berth along side of the US Navy 's Pacific fleet, which gives it access to military and government vessels such as destroyers to cruisers, and landing ships. All types of commercial customers are engaged, too, including cruise ship owners, barges, tankers, container ships, and research vessels.
