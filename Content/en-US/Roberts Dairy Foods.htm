Holy cow! Roberts Dairy Foods is a leading producer of fluid, cultured, and frozen dairy products. It offers milk, yogurt, sour cream, cottage cheese, and other dairy products. A division of Hiland Dairy , the firm operates production plants in Omaha and Kansas City and 10 distribution centers located in the Midwest. The company markets its products under the Roberts and Hiland-Dairy brands; it also provides private-label services and school milk. Through a joint venture named Hiland-Roberts, the company makes ice cream products from a facility in Norfolk, Nebraska. Founded as a milk route by J.R. Roberts in 1906, Roberts Dairy Foods serves retail food and food service customers throughout the Midwest.

Roberts Dairy Foods has two production plants and 10 distribution centers in Nebraska, Iowa, Kansas, and Missouri. Roberts Dairy serves a region that includes Nebraska, Iowa, Missouri, Kansas, and parts of Colorado, Illinois, and South Dakota.

In 2011 the company changed its name from Roberts Dairy to Roberts Dairy Foods to reflect its expansion beyond milk. It also adopted a new logo that year to emphasize the freshness of its products. Non-dairy products include juices, lemonade, fruit punches, and teas. Other specialty markets for the company include kosher and gluten-free products.
