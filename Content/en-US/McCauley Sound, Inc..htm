McCauley Sound is serious about loudspeakers. The company manufactures and sells professional loudspeaker systems and raw-frame loudspeaker components. The US-based audio company continues to have control of its engineering and manufacturing in-house. Its professional systems might be too much for a house, but they're appropriate for stages, theaters, and touring groups. McCauley's MLA3 large-scale three-way array is made specifically for touring applications. McCauley sound has worldwide sales, with representation across the US and in more than 10 foreign countries. President and CEO Tom McCauley founded his company in 1979.
