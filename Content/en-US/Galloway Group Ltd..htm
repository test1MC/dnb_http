A manufacturer of ventilation ductwork, Galloway Group makes products that include Guardian Fluoropolymer-coated ductwork for the pharmaceutical and semiconductor sectors and fire-rated ductwork for any structure that requires it. Beyond ductwork, Galloway makes acoustic and noise-control products and installs electrical systems in new housing developments. Its Industrial Maintenance and Support Services division provides fabrication and engineering services. Galloway has installed systems in such facilities as airports, hospitals, prisons, hotels, retail, schools and colleges, and stadiums.

Galloway's modern 68,000 sq. ft. fabrication facility in Dundee is complete with overhead cranes and can manufacture large and complicated fabrications. In addition, it has a stainless steel fabrication plant in Dewsbury, West Yorkshire, for the manufacture of stainless steel only projects.

The company has manufacturing plants in Dundee, Scotland; and Dewsbury, England.

The company targets a range of industries and segments that includes airports, hospitals, hotels, leisure facilities, offices, pharmaceutical/semiconductor plants, prisons, retail offices, sports stadiums, and schools and colleges.

Galloway is owned by members of its management team who bought out the previous owners/managers in 2008. Serving as the European distributor of DuctMate products the company also holds exclusive right to the Fenland Fire Duct system. The company provides a slue of other related and not-so related products and services that capitalize on its manufacturing capabilities and industrial expertise such as making and selling underfloor heating panels, offering electrical contracting services, and providing bespoke industrial maintenance and support.

The company began in 1872 making sheet metal products.
