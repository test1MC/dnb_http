The Lion Group oversees a large pride of goods and services. The company's divisions include steel (bars and wire rods), motor (automobile distribution), tires, PC component manufacturing, and agri-products (lumber, palm oil, rubber, and cocoa plantations). It's also investing in communications networks and retail operations in China. The Lion Group's service divisions include retail and trading (Parkson department stores, construction equipment, and industrial lubricants) and real estate development. The company has operations in nine mostly Asian countries, though it's also in the US and Mexico. Though it traces its roots to a 1930s confectionary, the Lion Group officially came together in the 1970s.
