HellermannTyton has a tight lid on the manufacturing of communication products. The company manufactures a wide array of cable management products, including fasteners, clips, cable ties, tubing, binding ducts, electrical ducts, security seals, markers, printing and labeling systems, enclosures, racks, and connectors. It primarily targets the electrical (OEMs, electrical wholesalers, contractors, and resellers), automotive (OEMs and suppliers), and data communication (data centers) markets. It also caters to non-cable management markets like agriculture, forestry, and packaging. It has nearly 12 facilities spanning nine countries.

HellermannTyton operates facilities in nearly a dozen primary locations across nine countries, producing over 12 billion items annually from the more than 20,000 products offered. It sells its products directly in almost 35 countries, particularly in its core geographies of Brazil, Europe, Japan, and the US. Its focus on China, India, Russia, and South Korea is also increasing.
