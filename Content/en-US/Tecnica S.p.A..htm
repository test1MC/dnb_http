Skiers and skaters alike turn to Tecnica to get them on their way. The company produces footwear for active lifestyles, including ski and snowboard boots, hikers, and athletic shoes, as well as apparel. Its brands include Dolomite, Lowa, and Think Pink. Besides shoes, the firm manufactures snowboards, skis, and related gear under the Marker, Nitro, Nordica, and Blizzard labels. Tecnica's Rollerblade unit makes in-line skates. Founded in 1960, the company originally made shoes for Italian workmen. It gained worldwide recognition in the 1970s through its Moon Boot brand.
