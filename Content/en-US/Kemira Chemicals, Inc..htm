Kemira Chemicals brings a Nordic approach to the chemical industry in the New World. The company is the North American subsidiary of Finnish international specialty chemical maker Kemira Oyj . It manufactures specialty and process chemicals for the pulp and paper, water treatment, mineral slurries, and chemical industries. Products include biocides, colloidal silica, de-foamers, dispersants, hazardous waste stabilizers, polishing slurries, sodium aluminate, and water treatment polymers. It also produces bleaching agents for detergents and titanium dioxide, as well as organic acids, salts, and blends.

The company is organized in three customer-based segments: Paper (serving customers in the pulp and paper industry); Municipal & Industrial (serving customers in municipal and industrial water treatment areas); and Oil & Mining (serving customers in the oil, gas, and mining industries).

Kemira Chemicals' US-based research and development facility is responsible for global research related to tissue and recycled fiber products, oil and mining activities, and de-foaming and polymer chemistry developments.

The company has production centers in Georgia and Washington.

Kemira Chemicals was incorporated in 1942. In Kemira Oyj opened a North American Research and Development Center in Atlanta, the fourth of five planned global centers.
