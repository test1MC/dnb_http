Eaton Hydraulics is a flow business celebrity. It designs and makes hydraulic and fluid distribution systems and for industrial and transport equipment. Pumps, power units, motors, and a slew of controls and sensing devices (valves, cylinders, electronic components), as well as assemblies and filtration systems are part of an extensive hydraulics portfolio that make its parent, Eaton Corp. , a leader in power management products. Eaton Hydraulics' brands, including Aeroquip, Boston, and Vickers, are familiar to a diverse group of industries, from agriculture to construction, forestry, marine, material handling, oil and gas, renewable energy, and mining. Eaton Hydraulics operates manufacturing plants worldwide.
