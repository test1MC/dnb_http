One group is all about magazines. The Magazine Group (TMG) is a custom publisher of magazines and Web sites. It also produces podcasts and other multimedia content. The group's custom publishing services include editorial, design, production, ad sales, distribution and circulation, and strategic consulting for its varied clientele. The company serves some 50 clients, which include associations, corporations, and non-profit agencies such as American Folk Art Museum ( Folk Art magazine); Hilton Grand Vacations Company ( Grand Times magazine); and Scripps Networks ( Food Network Magazine ). Business partners Richard Creighton (principal) and Jane Ottenberg (president) co-founded TMG in 1981.
