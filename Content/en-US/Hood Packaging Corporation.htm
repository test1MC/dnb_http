Hood Packaging makes no veiled references to its primary products -- bags. The company manufactures multiwall bags, small paper bags, industrial plastic bags, and soft- and rigid-handle bags. Hood Packaging also makes a variety of coated and laminated paper products and nylon and polyethylene films. The company serves the food products, pet foods, building products, lawn and garden, and paper and wood markets. Hood Packaging owns and operates manufacturing facilities throughout the US and Canada. It traces its roots to 1906, when it was called Canadian Bag Company. The company expanded in 2006 when it acquired Glopak, a maker of food and dairy packaging and filling equipment.
