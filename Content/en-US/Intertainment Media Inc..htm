Intertainment Media has something for business owners and young adults alike. Founded in 2006, the company develops traditional and digital advertising and marketing platforms, including its desktop and mobile phone application Itibiti , a private-label social media and entertainment platform that allows businesses to market their brands to Itibiti's users. In addition, the company owns graphics design business Magnum Fine Printing, which caters to commercial clients. Intertainment's other operations include entertainment-based Web sites and social networking sites like Eyerock Digital (themed channels and amateur programming for the 18-34 age range) and NGTV (celebrity video content).
