Frank J. Zamboni & Co. makes some slick machines. The company manufactures ice resurfacers familiar to hockey players, ice skaters, and Olympic athletes around the world. Zamboni makes seven models of ice resurfacers and three edger models. It also buys used ice resurfacing machines to sell or rent. The company has sold more than 9,000 machines. Inventor and entrepreneur Frank J. Zamboni, his brother Lawrence, and their cousin opened the Iceland Skating Rink in 1940 in Paramount, California. From his experiences with the rink, Frank developed his Model A Zamboni Ice Resurfacer in 1949 and formed the Frank J. Zamboni Company in 1950. The company continues to be owned and operated by the Zamboni family.
