This snack company wants you to indulge in some guiltless pleasures. Eat Your Heart Out! makes a line of freeze-dried fruit and vegetable snack foods, including apple cinnamon wedges, peach wedges with mango, and sweet and sour green soybeans. The company sells its products through health food stores and supermarkets in select markets around the country, as well as through its own online store. Amy Sinaiko started Eat Your Heart Out! in 1999.
