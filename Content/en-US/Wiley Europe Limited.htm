The wily brainiac probably already knows that Wiley Europe is home to many books of interest. A subsidiary of US-based publisher John Wiley & Sons , Wiley Europe includes operations in the UK (John Wiley & Sons Ltd.) and Germany (Wiley-VCH Verlag GmbH). The company targets the scientific, technical, and medical (STM) and academic markets with its publications, including reference books, professional journals, and electronic information products. Wiley Europe publishes its products primarily in English, but also in German. It has publishing offices in Berlin, Bern, London, and Paris.
