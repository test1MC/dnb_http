Pacific Scientific-OECO -- the electromagnetic components company that made Milwaukie famous. The company makes custom power supplies and magnetic components used in commercial and military aircraft, including various transformers and transformer rectifiers. It also manufactures circuit card assemblies, custom molded cable assemblies, and high-voltage assemblies. Pacific Scientific-OECO's customers have included Boeing , Honeywell , IBM , Lockheed Martin , Medtronic , Northrop Grumman , and Raytheon . Founded in 1946, it was acquired in 2003 by Pacific Scientific Company, a subsidiary of Danaher , and then by Meggitt PLC in 2011. The company remains headquartered in Milwaukie, Oregon.
