China GrenTech provides customized wireless coverage and radio frequency (RF) technology. Its products enable Chinese telecommunication operators to transmit and receive signals and expand wireless communication networks to areas with weak coverage, such as buildings, tunnels, and remote regions. Key customers include China Unicom and China Mobile . China GrenTech also provides installation, connection, and testing services. In 2012 the company was acquired and taken private by chairman and CEO Yingjie Gao and CFO Rong Yu through a company they co-owncalled Talenthome Management Limited, along with an outside party known as Xing Sheng Corporation.
