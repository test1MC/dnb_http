Premier Foods has been cleaning out its pantry, disposing of stale brands, to focus on its best sellers. Britain's biggest branded food producer, Premier Foods makes bread, flour, cakes, desserts, preserves, sauces, stocks, gravies, and more. Top brands include Ambrosia, Batchelor's, Bisto, Hovis, Loyd Grossman, Mr. Kipling, Oxo, and Sharwood's. Premier's branded and non-branded offerings are sold by major food retailers and to food service suppliers throughout the UK. (Britain accounts for more than 90% of sales.) The company has reorganized to form two divisions: grocery and bread. Premier Foods has sold its canned grocery and meat-free businesses to reduce its substantial debt and focus on its core brands.
