Avensys manufactures optical components used to build electronic environmental monitoring systems. The company's fiber sensors report changes in temperature, humidity, and structural strain. Other products measure water flow in the field, and analyze air samples for the presence of combustible gases. Avensys' core customers are in the chemical, construction, manufacturing, oil and gas, and telecommunications industries, and include the City of Toronto, GE , and Oclaro . In 2006 Avensys acquired the assets of ITF Optical Technologies , another supplier of optical components, which became the firm's ITF Labs unit. Avensys was acquired by French photonics maker 3S Photonics in 2010.
