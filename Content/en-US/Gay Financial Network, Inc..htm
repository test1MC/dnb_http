Gay Financial Network provides financial, investment, and business news, information, and resources to the gay and lesbian community through its Web site at gfn.com. Topics on the company's Web site include career, health, personal finance, politics, travel, real estate, and technology. The Web site also features a Professional Directory listing service. Gay Financial Network was founded by stock broker Walter Schubert (first openly gay member of the New York Stock Exchange) in 1998.
