Acer India provides desktop and portable personal computers to the consumer, education, enterprise, and government sectors. The regional subsidiary also markets computer servers ranging from devices designed for small workgroups to enterprise-level models. Its peripheral products include digital projectors and LCD and CRT displays. The company also provides IT support services with its AcerCare centers. Acer India sells through resellers located across the country; it also operates branded retail stores called Acer Malls. The company's parent, Taiwanese PC stalwart Acer , established its Indian unit in 1999.
