Founded in 1954, Productos Laminados de Monterrey, S.A. de C.V. (PROLAMSA) fabricates cold-rolled, hot-rolled, or galvanized steel coils into steel tubular shapes, such as rectangular, round, and square tubing. In the company's early days it provided primarily smaller products with regional demand, such as hen cages. The company's main customers today are in the construction, furniture, and industrial manufacturing industries throughout North America.
