Ductwork Projects' motto might be, "If you can't stand the heat, stay in the kitchen ... but use its ventilation systems." The niche manufacturing company designs, manufactures, and installs ductwork and kitchen ventilation systems. Its products are used for such applications as heating and air conditioning and include filters, stainless steel ventilation canopies, and ceilings as well as service distribution units. The company also installs related fire suppression, lighting, and filtration systems. Ductwork Projects maintains offices and a factory in Southern England.

Ductwork Projects' installation teams cover the UK, Ireland, and mainland Europe.

The company's Kitchen Ventilation segment offers a wide range of canopies and accessories.

Its completed installations include Benihana St. Pauls, London; Broxbourne Campus, Broxbourne; Limewood Hotel, Lyndhurst; Royal Shakespeare Theatre, Stratford-upon-Avon; West Hertfordshire College, Ware; and The Grange Hotel St. Paul's, London.

Ductwork Projects was founded in 1980.
