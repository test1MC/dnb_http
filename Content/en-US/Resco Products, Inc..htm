Resco Products is all about preventing burnout. The company manufactures and supplies pressed and monolithic refractories -- heat-resistant materials used to line the inside walls of furnaces -- such as chemical-bonded, low-cement, and extreme service castables. The company's materials are used by oil refineries, cement manufacturers, paper mills, iron and steel makers, and construction brick makers, among others. Along with about a dozen facilities in the US, Resco Products has operations in Canada and the UK. The company was founded in 1946. In late 2011, Resco was acquired by two private equity firms: Wellspring Capital Management and Hancock Park Associates.
