Herlitz wants every kid to be inspired by the first day of the school year, and every adult to be re-inspired by the sight of a blank page. Herlitz AG is one of Germany's leading manufacturers and distributors of paper goods, office and school supplies, and stationery. The company also makes Susy Card-brand greeting cards and gift accessories. Its eCom Logistics subsidiary offers supply chain management services to customers, while Mercoline deals with business integration and SAP systems. Products are distributed throughout Europe and the US. Carl Herlitz founded the wholesale paper and stationery company in 1904. Malaysia-based Pelikan International plans to acquire a 66% stake in Herlitz for €45 million.
