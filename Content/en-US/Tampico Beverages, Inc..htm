Tampico Beverages manufactures refrigerated juice drinks and punches under the Tampico brand name. They are available in a variety of serving sizes and an array of fruit flavors, including mango, kiwi-strawberry, and peach. It offers bottled water flavored to appeal to Hispanic taste buds (piña colada, mamey-mango and grapefruit-tangerine). The company's beverages are distributed throughout the US and more than 50 other countries. Tampico (formerly known as Marbo) began making its juice products in the early 1980s and claims to be the #1 refrigerated juice drink in the US. It was acquired by food retailer, Houchens Industries , in 2008.
