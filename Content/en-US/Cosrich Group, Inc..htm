Cosrich Group wants to wash whatever shouldn't be there right out of your little man's (or little woman's) hair. The company, a subsidiary of international firm PMC Group, makes and markets children's bath products (shampoos, conditioners, cleansers, bath bubbles) and play cosmetics. Its products feature characters including Batman, Superman, Hello Kitty, My Little Pony, Strawberry Shortcake, and Bob the Builder (among other kid-friendly characters) and can be purchased through supermarkets, and specialty and mass retailers (such as Kmart , Kroger , Target , Toys 'R' Us , and Wal-Mart ) nationwide.
