Content with containment, Pretium Packaging makes blow-molded plastic bottles and containers using polyvinyl chloride (PVC) compounds, high-density polyethylene (HDPE), and polyethylene terephthalate (PET) resins. Products include salad dressing containers, peanut butter jars, pickle jars, large containers for snacks, shampoo and hand soap containers, and bottles for juices, sauces, and jellies. It caters to a high-margin niche, comprised of small to medium orders for custom containers. Its industrial and consumer customers have included General Mills , Colgate-Palmolive , Proctor & Gamble , and Spectrum Brands . Founded in 1992, Pretium supplies 1.2 billion packages each year to more than 700 customers.

Pretium operates through almost 10 facilities in the US and two in Canada.

Pretium sells its products through its direct sales force and on the Internet.

Pretium in 2015 purchased Intertech Corporation, a manufacturer of high-quality stock, custom, and proprietary plastic bottles. The acquisition invoked Pretium's strategy to grow both organically and through selective acquisitions that expand its markets, technologies, and geographic reach to allow it to better serve its customers.
