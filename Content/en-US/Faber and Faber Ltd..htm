Whether your taste runs to T.S. Eliot or Ricky Gervais, Faber and Faber has a book for you. The midsized independent publisher is known for its emphasis on poetry, but it also publishes fiction, non-fiction, film, drama, and children's books, including Flanimals , written by comedian Ricky Gervais. Its published poets include Ted Hughes and Sylvia Plath. Faber and Faber was founded in 1929 by Geoffrey Faber. Poet T.S. Eliot was an active director in the company until his death. (For several decades the company benefited from the popularity of the musical Cats , based on Eliot's book of poems, Old Possum's Book of Practical Cats .) Today Eliot's widow Valerie and the Faber family own the company.
