Navistar Canada (formerly known as International Truck and Engine Corporation Canada) is Navistar International 's manufacturing presence in The Great White North. The company manufactures International brand commercial trucks, school buses, and diesel engines. The firm also provides diesel and truck engine parts and services, as well as the Workhorse brand chassis for motor homes and step vans. Through a joint venture with Ford Motor , the company builds medium-duty commercial trucks and sells components and accessories for trucks and diesel engines. In addition, Navistar Canada provides insurance and financing for its customers.
