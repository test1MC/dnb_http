Whether you're dodging bullets or linebackers, Point Blank Enterprises aims to have you covered. The company makes flak jackets and other bullet-resistant vests and body armor through subsidiaries Point Blank Body Armor (PBBA) and Protective Apparel Corporation of America (PACA). Other major brands include PARACLETE and Protective Products, two lines of body armor. Point Blank produces both concealable and tactical (worn over clothes) armor. Sales to the US military and federal law enforcement agencies account for a majority of the company's business. It operates through a few facilities in Florida and Tennessee.

Headquartered at Pompano Beach, Florida, Point Blank sells its products through sales representatives throughout the US, Central America, South America, the Caribbean and Europe. It also caters to customers in Mexico, Ecuador, France, Afghanistan, Iraq, Panama, and Macau. It has two manufacturing facilities in Florida.

Point Blank's customers include the US Army , Department of Defense , Marine Corps , Defense Logistics Agency , Department of Justice , Department of Homeland Security , and other government agencies.

In an effort to grow its business, Point Blank in 2012 completed its purchase of Protective Products Enterprises. Through the deal the company gained a portfolio of well-known and trusted global brands, including Point Blank Body Armor, PACA Body Armor, Protective Products, and PARACLETE.
