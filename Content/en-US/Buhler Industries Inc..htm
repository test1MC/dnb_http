Agriculture -- deemed by some as the only honest way to wealth -- is Buhler Industries' business. The company operates through two divisions. The tractor division designs and manufactures a series of tractor models, sold under the Versatile brand name. Its short line division, long known for its Farm King branded lineup, manufactures agricultural implements used in small-scale farming. Offerings include wheel loaders, front-end loaders, augers, cultivators, landscape rakes, mowers, and hay and forage balers. The company operates and distributes throughout North America and parts of Eastern Europe. Russia's Combine Factory Rostselmash Ltd. owns an 80% interest in Buhler and leads the company's management.
