Expect power supply converters from XP Power. The company makes power converters for manufacturers in the aerospace, communications, medical, instrumentation, and industrial equipment sectors who don't already have their own in-house power supply expertise. XP Power's more than 5,000 standard products include AC and DC power supplies, DC-to-DC converters, secure power systems, and ancillary products such as filters and fans. The company also provides custom manufactured products. XP Power has about 27 sales and manufacturing locations worldwide; most of its sales come from customers in North America and Europe.
