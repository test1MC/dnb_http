Hyundai could be the Korean term for "diverse." The trading corporation operates through four divisions: trading (manufactured goods), overseas investment (steel processing, real estate), shipbuilding, and resources development (natural resource investments). The trading division has seven lines of business: shipbuilding; plant (offshore substructures, petrochemical plants, and power plants); machinery (automobiles and automobile parts, material handling equipment); automobile and rolling stock (cars, military vehicles, trucks, parts); steel; chemicals (rubber and plastic resins); and information and telecommunications (audio/video equipment, digital cameras, digital satellite receivers, and LCD monitors).
