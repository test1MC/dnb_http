Nimgrove knows how to manipulate metal; it specializes in the manufacturing of sheet metal using mild steel, aluminum, and stainless steel. Doing business as Nimgrove Sheet Metal Fabrications, the company produces its sheet metal using a variety of fabricating techniques, including aluminum punching, bending, plasma cutting, and shearing. Its products are used by manufacturers of earth moving, materials handling, and office equipment, and the company also caters to the automotive and defense industries. Founded in 1980, Nimgrove caters to customers in the UK and throughout Europe. The company entered Administration in 2014.
