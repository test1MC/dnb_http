Yes, Virginia (and anyone else who might be wondering), there was a real Betsy Ann behind Betsy Ann Candies. The company produces boxed gift, novelty, and seasonal handmade gourmet dark and light chocolates, as well as fudge and roasted and salted nuts, which it sells online and via its catalog. It also lends its chocolate confections to not-for-profit organizations for fund-raising activities. Betsy Ann Candies operates four retail candy outlets in and around Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. The company was founded by Betsy Ann Helsel in 1938.
