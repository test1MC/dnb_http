Seiko Corporation of America (SCA) keeps US time for Japan's Seiko Holdings Corporation , a firm best known for its watches, clocks, and athletic timing devices. SCA markets watches and clocks under popular brands the likes of Pulsar and Seiko. Watches in Seiko's Spring Drive series feature a glide motion that claims to reflect the true nature of time. Its Kinetic series watches are powered by wrist movement rather than batteries.The manufacturing company is focusing more on fashion than function by adding the kinetic features to some of its fashion watches. SCA also offers personalized touches such as custom engraving and diamond settings. The US subsidiary was formed in 1970.
