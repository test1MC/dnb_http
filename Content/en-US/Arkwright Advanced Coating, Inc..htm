Whether its customers are printing digital photos or large-format engineering plans, Arkwright Advanced Coating (AAC) has the right stuff for the task. The company, formerly named Arkwright, manufactures coated and uncoated papers, films, vellums, and other specialty substrates for narrow- and wide-format printing. Its customers include sign makers, reprographics shops, and photo reproduction companies. AAC has two distribution facilities in the US and operates half a dozen manufacturing centers internationally. The firm is a subsidiary of Italy's Diatec Holdings.
