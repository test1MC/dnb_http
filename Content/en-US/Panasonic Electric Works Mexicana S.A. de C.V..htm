Panasonic Electric Works Mexicana is the Mexican subsidiary of Panasonic Electric Works Corporation of America , itself a unit of Panasonic Corporation . The company distributes electrical construction materials, home appliances, residential building materials, automation control products, wiring devices, and electronic components. Its consumer electronics line includes power tools, massage chairs, and personal hygiene systems. The company also makes GPS, lighting control, and time-switch products. Founded as Aromat Mexicana in 1996, the company was renamed in 2005.
