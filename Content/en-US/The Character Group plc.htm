Toying with the business world takes character or maybe just a game changer like The Character Group (TCG). The group develops, manufactures, and distributes an array of toys, games, and gifts. Since 2003 it has focused heavily on the development of its own products. Approximately 70% of TCG's products are created by its in-house team, and sold in more than 30 countries worldwide under brand names Scooby-Doo!, ZhuZhu Pets, Peppa Pig, Doctor Who, and H.M. Armed Forces, among others. International sales, which account for more than one-third of the groups' top line, are served by operations in the UK, China, and Hong Kong. The group was founded in 1991 and started trading on the UK stock market in 1995.
