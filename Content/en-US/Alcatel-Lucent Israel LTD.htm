When people in Israel reach out and touch someone, on the phone or online, it's probably Alcatel-Lucent Israel equipment that makes the connection possible. The company provides wire line and wireless, broadband, optical networking, network security, enterprise networking, convergence, and communication equipment. It also offers network design, deployment, integration, operation, and maintenance as well as fundamental research in nanotechnology and computer sciences. Its customers are businesses and government agencies and telecom service providers. Bezeq , Israel's largest telecom provider, is a major customer. Alcatel-Lucent Israel is part of French telecom equipment firm Alcatel-Lucent .
