While it's not quite L.A. Confidential , Baseline StudioSystems can give you the low-down on Tinseltown. The company is a provider of subscription-based movie and TV industry information, including cast and crew credits, box office sales, celebrity biographies, and studio project schedules. Its databases also include information on entertainment companies, with contact and deal information and development slates, as well as an archive of articles from industry publications such as Variety and The Hollywood Reporter .  Clients include major movie studios, broadcast networks, representation firms, and media outlets. The company is owned by The New York Times , and falls under the operations of the NYTimes.com .
