Online news junkies use Newser. The company operates Newser.com, an aggregator of news stories that compiles headlines from more than 100 sources, including The Wall Street Journal , The New York Times , and the Associated Press . Newser's style is to keep its content short and sweet -- it condenses articles into brief summaries, adds graphics and video, and a hyperlink to the original story. Newser.com also offers widgets (applications that users can install on their own Web pages, such as Facebook ), RSS feeds (a free subscription to receive a news feed), and a mobile application for Apple's iPhone. Newser was launched in 2007 and spun off from HighBeam Research the next year.
