Russell's Technical Products manufactures blowers and fans and industrial machinery for testing laboratories. The company began as a commercial and industrial refrigeration sales and service company. In 1972 it began designing and manufacturing environmental test chambers. The company supplies test chambers for the automotive, consumer, pharmaceutical, and aerospace industries, among others. Russell maintains its own sheet metal equipment, welders, lift trucks, and paint booths. Russell's Technical Products was acquired in 1972 by Donald Bench, a veteran of the environmental and test chamber manufacturing industry who first began work in this area in the 1950s.
