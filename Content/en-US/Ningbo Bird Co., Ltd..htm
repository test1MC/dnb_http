Ningbo Bird wants Chinese callers to flock to its phones. The company, also known simply as Bird, manufactures mobile phones primarily for the domestic market in China. It makes a wide range of cell phone models, as well as cordless household phones and wireless Internet adapter cards. Bird sells handsets domestically from regional offices and its products are distributed through authorized dealers and retailers located throughout China. The company exports phones to Australia, Egypt, Mexico, and the US, among other countries. Bird is a part of the Ningbo Electronics Information Holdings group of technology and electronics companies.
