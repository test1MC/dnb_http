Ashland Specialty Ingredients (formerly Ashland Aqualon Functional Ingredients) manufactures chemicals that add desired traits to products ranging from food to construction materials. An Ashland subsidiary, the company produces both water-soluble and solvent-soluble polymers. It makes specialty additives and functional ingredients such as cellulose ethers and synthetic polymers, as well as wood and gum rosin resins for adhesives, food and beverages, and construction materials. Customers include makers of coatings, oil field chemicals, paper, pharmaceuticals, and personal care products.

The Specialty Ingredients unit of Ashland operates about two dozen manufacturing facilities in eight countries in the Americas, Europe and Asia Pacific, and also holds stakes in two joint ventures.

Ashland Specialty Ingredients offers industry-leading products, technologies and resources for solving formulation and product performance challenges in key markets including personal care, pharmaceutical, food and beverage, coatings and energy.

The Specialty Ingredients unit is comprised of a number of lines of businesses, including Care Specialties, Pharmaceutical & Nutrition Specialties, Coatings & Construction Specialties, Energy Specialties, and Performance Specialties. The unit sold 392,100 tons in fiscal 2013.

The Care Specialties business is made up of the Oral Care, Hair Care, Skin Care and Home Care product lines. The Pharmaceutical & Nutrition Specialties business includes the Pharmaceutical and Food and Beverage product lines. The Construction Specialties business (a leader in rheology solutions for waterborne architectural paint and coatings) is also a major producer and supplier of cellulose ethers and companion products for the construction industry. The Energy Specialties business makes guar-, synthetic- and cellulosic-based products for drilling fluids, oil-well cement slurries, completion and workover fluids, fracturing fluids, and production chemicals.

The Performance Specialties business offers a broad range of organo- and water-soluble polymers made from both natural and synthetic resources. Its product lines include derivatized cellulose polymers, synthetics, guar and guar derivatives for a variety of industrial markets and specialized applications.

The company markets and distributes its products and services directly and through third-party distributors in the Americas, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and Asia/Pacific. Key market segments include pharmaceuticals, personal and home care products, food and beverages, paints, coatings and construction materials, and oilfield service companies.

In 2013 Ashland Specialty Ingredients' revenues decreased by 9% primarily due to lower pricing (and to a lesser extent a decline in volumes), and changes in product mix. The decline in pricing was primarily in the energy market as a result of much weaker demand in for certain guar products for use in fracking activities.

The company contributed 33% of its parent's total revenues in 2013.

Expanding its infrastructure, in 2013, Ashland Specialty Ingredients added a coatings and construction lab in its Shanghai Technical Center. The newly expanded technical center houses laboratory facilities for research and development , application technology and services for coatings and construction, personal care, pharmaceutical, food and beverage customers in China. That year the company also opened a battery testing laboratory in Wilmington, Delaware, to serve as Ashland's technology development and support center for lithium-ion battery customers, and a 6,000 sq. ft. research and development laboratory in The Woodlands, Texas, to serve as Ashland's technical support center for the oil and gas industry.
