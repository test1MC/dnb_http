Aberdeen Fabrication (A-Fab) is steel going strong in the Granite City. The company,, fabricates steel components and pipe work and provides services such as installation, maintenance, and welding. A-Fab primarily serves the offshore oil and gas, marine, and steel fabrication industries in the UK. The company also provides quayside services which include equipment provisions and sea fastening work. (The Quayside is an area along the banks of the River Tyne in Newcastle in the northeastern UK.) A-Fab has manufactured parts including submarine structures and skidding systems for offshore drilling rigs. Its list of clients have included Dolphin Drilling , Transocean , Houlder, MI Swaco, and MI Drilling.

The company manufactures fabricated structural metal and steel and other metal products for structural purposes. Its projects include Mud Tanks for the Borgsten Dolphin, Pipe Deck Extensions, Pollution Control Unit, Mud Skid Stations, Liquid Mud Plants, and Conductor Guide Frames.

Expanding its infrastructure, in 2014 A-Fab bought a new burning table for its workshop, a HyPerformance Plasma HPR400XD.

The company was founded in 1983.
