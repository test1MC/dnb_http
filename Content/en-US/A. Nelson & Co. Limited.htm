A. Nelson & Co. is Europe's oldest and the UK's largest manufacturer of homeopathic medicines and other natural health care products. Its homeopathic creams, ointments, sprays, liquids, and tablets address a range of health problems from common colds to skin conditions. Its formulated remedies include Arnicare for bruising, Travella for travel sickness, Teetha for teething, Coldenza for cold and flu symptoms, Noctura for insomnia, and Pollenna for allergies. Nelson also makes Bach Original Floral Remedies, including the popular Rescue Remedy, and Spatone iron supplements. Nelson distributes its products globally and operates specialized manufacturing labs in the UK.

Nelson offers alternative first-aid treatments as well as treatments for most common ailments; its philosophy is that its products can help the body safely and easily heal itself.

Headquartered in London, Nelson also has a manufacturing facility in London's Wimbledon district. The company also has international distribution offices in Hamburg and Massachusetts.

The company's homeopathic products are carried in UK health food markets, pharmacies, and larger chains. In the US, its products are sold in natural foods stores and pharmacies.

Founder Ernst Louis Ambrecht studied under the founder of homeopathy, then moved to England, changed his surname to Nelson, and established the company in 1860. The Wilson family has owned the firm since the early 1970s when remaining members of the founding family died in an airplane crash.
