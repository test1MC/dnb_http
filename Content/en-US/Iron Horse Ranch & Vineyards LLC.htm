Iron Horse Ranch & Vineyards produces red, white, and sparkling and still wines. Among the vineyard's varietals are Chardonnay and Pinot Noir. Iron Horse's 160 acres are located in California's cool, misty, grapevine-friendly Green Valley (just 13 miles from the Pacific Ocean). Its wines, which have been served at the White House, are available at the winery and on the company's Web site to those US states which allow direct shipments of alcohol to consumers. Iron Horse was founded in 1976 by Barry and Audrey Sterling. Their children, including CEO Joy Sterling, now run the company.
