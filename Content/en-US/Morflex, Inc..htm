When the insects begin to flex their pestilent muscles, Morflex helps you outflex the bugs. A leading producer of DEET, an active ingredient in insect repellents, the company also makes organic esters (scent agents), plasticizers (used to give resins and rubbers flexibility), and solvents. Founded in 1927, Morflex was a subsidiary of specialty chemical maker Reilly Industries until that company was merged with Rutherford Chemicals to form Vertellus Specialties .
