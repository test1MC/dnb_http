Those who don't have the time or effort to sunbathe can develop a tan at home in a breeze. Breeze Products is a contract manufacturer of indoor tanning products. Its BreeZe brand of lotions and gels promote varying levels of tan while some are packed with antioxidants, fruit fragrances, aloe vera, and anti-aging ingredients. The firm also bottles self-tanning spray. In addition to its products, Breeze offers research and development, private labeling, customization options (fragrances, colors), and tube and bottle filling. The company was founded by president Mike "Zeke" Lemle in 1999.
