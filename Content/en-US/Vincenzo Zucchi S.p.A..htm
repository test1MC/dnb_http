There's nothing shameful to Vincenzo Zucchi about closing the curtains, hiding under the bedspread, or sweeping something under the rug -- especially if you're using its linens. The company makes and sells household linens, including sheets, bed coverings, towels, rugs, tablecloths, curtains, and upholstery fabrics.
