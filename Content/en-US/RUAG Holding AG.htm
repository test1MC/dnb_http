The Swiss say they're neutral, but they've got a good defense with RUAG Holding's armored vehicles and bullets. RUAG is a group of companies involved in military and civilian aerospace, security, and defense, and it's the primary contractor for the Swiss Armed Forces. RUAG manufactures and services parts and systems for aircraft, satellites, space launchers, and armored vehicles for companies such as Boeing , Cessna and the European Space Agency . It also develops simulation and training systems for armed forces and government agencies, and makes ammunition for police, military, hunting, and sport. RUAG Holding was founded in 1998 and is owned by the Swiss government.
