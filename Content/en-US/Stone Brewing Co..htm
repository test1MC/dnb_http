Fear not the stone gargoyle. It's just the company mascot of Stone Brewing, which employs the gargoyle -- along with some aggressive attitude -- to sell its collection of ales. Arrogant Bastard Ale headlines a family of microbrews that are sold in some 30 US states. In addition to its own brands the California company distributes the beers of more than 20 other craft breweries. Stone Brewing was founded in 1996 and produces about 100,000 barrels of beer a year. The brewery also operates an onsite restaurant, which features 32 craft and specialty beers on tap. It is considering starting brewing operations in Europe.
