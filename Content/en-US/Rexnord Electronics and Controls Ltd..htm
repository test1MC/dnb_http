There are plenty of fans of Rexnord's fans. Rexnord Electronics and Controls is one of India's largest manufacturers of instrument cooling fans and motors, which are used in a variety of industrial and residential applications. Its axial and brushless fans cool condensers in air conditioners and refrigerators while its exhaust fans are found in computers and photocopiers. The company also makes induction and gear motors that are put to use in power tools, textile machinery, and injection presses. Rexnord was founded in 1988 by managing director K. C. Talwar.
