Murata Electronics North America (MENA) is the North American sales arm of Murata Manufacturing . MENA designs, manufactures, and sells power supply modules, capacitors, sensors, and ceramic passive electronic components such as crystal oscillators, piezoelectric buzzers, inductors, and wireless communications filters. The company serves manufacturers of audio visual equipment, automotive, and wireless communications devices, and targets telecommunications and automotive industries. Murata Manufacturing established its North American sales operation in 1965.

The company has sales operations throughout North America.

MENA has two operating divisions. Murata Wireless Solutions (MWS) and Murata Power Solutions (MPS).

MWS provides wireless connectivity solutions to the broad market by creating complete solutions including hardware (world-class RF performance), software (drivers and application), and localized technical support. MPS is a market leading supplier of DC/DC converters, AC/DC power supplies, magnetics, digital panel meters, and data acquisition components, with six engineering design centers and eight manufacturing facilities and partners worldwide.

Major distributors include Arrow Electronics , Avnet , Digi-Key Corporation , Future Electronics , Mouser Electronics, Sager Electronics, and TTI .

The company works to constantly innovate and improve its products. MENA has grown as its parent buys product lines, often from competitors, then integrates them into its North American operations.

In 2012 the company acquired RF Monolithics (RFM). With the combined resources of Murata, RFM will be able to take advantage of market channels globally and development of innovative wireless solutions, particularly for the healthcare, energy, and industrial markets.
