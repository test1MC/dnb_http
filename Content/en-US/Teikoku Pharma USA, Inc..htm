Have trouble taking your medicine? Teikoku Pharma USA (TPU) wants to help. The company, a subsidiary of Japan's Teikoku Seiyaku, focuses on developing and manufacturing transdermal patches for the topical administration of medications, which it hopes patients find more palatable than oral medications. Lidoderm is the company's lead product. The prescription Lidoderm patch treats nerve pain. TPU has a range of patches in its pipeline to treat back pain, Alzheimer's disease, and breakthrough pain.
