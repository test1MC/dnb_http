Wicksteed Leisure's products are really swinging, and twirling, and sliding! The company is one of the UK's top makers of commercial playground equipment and related products. As part of its business, Wicksteed Leisure also provides design, maintenance, and inspection services and will do everything from designing and installing a complete playground to replacing one piece of equipment. Play structures include themed systems like tropical, woodland, and nursery rhymes. Other products include agility and fitness courses for children and adults, BMX and skateboard courses, and safety surfacing.

Wicksteed Leisure provides outdoor fitness equipment, multi-use games areas and impact absorbing surfaces; as well as outdoor furniture and fencing. Its steelwork color range includes black, light blue, dark blue, brown, light green, dark green, orange, purple, red, yellow, grey, turquoise green, ruby red, and beige.

The company supplies play equipment to local authorities, architects, schools, builders, holiday and leisure parks, and the general public.

The company's survival through economic downturns has been aided by its diverse customer base. As part of its design services, Wicksteed Leisure incorporates the guidelines of the Disability Discrimination Act of 1995 with relation to outdoor play areas. It also offers a bespoke (customized) service, allowing for special orders to be made.

Founded in 1876 as an engineering company, Wicksteed Leisure began making and installing playground equipment in 1918.
