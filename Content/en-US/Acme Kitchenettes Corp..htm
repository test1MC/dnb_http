When space is a commodity, and the conveniences of a kitchen are called for, customers turn to Acme Kitchenettes. The company manufactures kitchenettes that include cabinets and countertops, as well as appliances (refrigerators, ovens, cook tops, sinks, ice makers, coffee makers, and wet bars) that fit well in small homes, trailers, and boats. It offers stock and custom models, including solid wood cabinetry centers and a line of barrier-free kitchenettes for disabled users. The company markets its products under the King Mini-Kitchens name. Newer products include the Guest Bar and Combo-Serv units for the hotel industry.
