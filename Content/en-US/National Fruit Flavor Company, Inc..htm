Next time you're at the state fair and get brain freeze on the midway from a sno-cone, thank the National Fruit Flavor Company (NFF) that the pain at least comes with a nice taste. NFF makes colored syrups used to make the frozen treat. It also makes fruit-flavored beverage mixers and syrups, flavor shots, and concentrates, oils, extracts, and emulsions for the food-manufacturing industry. It has customers in the dairy, bakery, beverage, foodservice and vitamin-supplement industries. The company's products are sold under the its proprietary labels and it offers private-labels services.
