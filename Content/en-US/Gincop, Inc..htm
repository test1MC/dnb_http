OneTouchPoint Southwest (formerly Gincop, also doing business as Ginny's Printing), offers a variety of copying and printing services to companies and universities in the Central Texas area. The company was founded in 1971 by Ginny Rogers in a shop near the Austin campus of the University of Texas . It has grown from its roots serving the UT community to providing a wide range of digital and offset printing services. Ginny's also offers direct mail and fulfillment services, as well as management of in-house copy centers for large clients such as Dell . OneTouchPoint acquired the company in 2011.
