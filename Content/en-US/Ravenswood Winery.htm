When it comes to vino , Ravenswood Winery prefers rosso . The company's red Zinfandel is its top seller, but it also produces Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Chardonnay, and other red and white wines. Ravenswood buys most of its grapes from third parties and its wines are sold in the US and abroad through retailers, restaurants, and online. Known for its complex wines, the company's brands include value-priced Vintners Blend, mid-priced County Series, and higher-end Vineyard Designate. Ravenswood Winery was founded by Joel Peterson and W. Reed Foster in 1976; it is now owned by wine behemoth, Constellation Brands .
