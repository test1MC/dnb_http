Celtro is a manufacturer of communications equipment used to build the landline portion of wireless networks. The company's DynaMate line of voice and data network switches are designed to enable service providers to increase the transmission capacity and speed of their networks. Celtro was founded in 2002 as a business unit of communications equipment manufacturer ECI Telecom . It was spun off the following year as an independent private company. ECI retains a stake in the company and other investors include such venture capital providers as Genesis Partners and Momentum Management.
