APRIL showers the globe with pulp and paper in hopes it may bring business, if not flora and fauna. Asia Pacific Resources International Holdings (APRIL) produces bleached hardwood kraft pulp, and uncoated, wood-free paper, including its flagship PaperOne brand of office paper. Its core subsidiary, Riau Andalan Pulp and Paper clears annually nine million tons of wood to produce some two million tons of pulp and 750,000 tons of paper. APRIL sells nearly 90% of its product outside of Indonesia, to Asia, and to a lesser extent, Europe and the Middle East. Solidifying its market in China, APRIL holds a 90% share in China-based SSYMB Pulp and Paper. Raja Garuda Mas Group, a company of Sukanto Tanoto, owns APRIL.
