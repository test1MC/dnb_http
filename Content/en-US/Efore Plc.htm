Before companies even make their products, Efore wants to empower them. The electronics group manufactures and markets power supplies and electronics for the telecommunications, industrial automation, and health care industries. The group offers both custom-designed power conversion products and standard power supplies. It provides uninterruptible DC power supplies, DC battery backup systems, inverters, rectifiers, and electronics design and manufacturing services (EDMS), as well as related service and maintenance. Efore has production facilities in China and Estonia, as well as product development and marketing units in China, Finland, Sweden, and the US.
