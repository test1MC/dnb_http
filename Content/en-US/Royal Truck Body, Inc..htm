For blue-collar professionals, getting the right service truck for the job can be a royal pain. But, as the largest maker of truck service bodies in the Western US, Royal Truck Body delivers chassis cabs tailored to clients' workaday needs. Bodies range from high-end commercial utility to maintenance flatbeds and stakebeds. Features include adjustable shelving, electro-galvanized coating, diamond steel plate floors, kick plate mounted taillights, and lockable, weatherproof compartments. Royal Truck Body caters to government contractors, heavy-equipment workers, and small service businesses. Its California site boasts 200,000 sq. ft. of manufacturing and warehouse space. Founder Dudley DeZonia owns the company.
