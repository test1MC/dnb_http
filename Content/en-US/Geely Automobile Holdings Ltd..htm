Geely Automobile is one of the top 10 automobile makers in China. It manufactures cars that are exported to more than 40 countries and makes automotive parts for use throughout China. Geely's nine manufacturing plants produce about two dozen sedan models sold under the brands GLEagle, Emgrand, and Englon. Its economy-model cars are sold from about 1,000 dealerships located across China; the export market accounts for about 25% of sales. Geely is growing, adding more upscale sedans to its lineup, and exporting to new markets thanks to its acquisition of Volvo Car Corporation from Ford for about $1.8 billion in 2010.
