RADA Electronic Industries designs, manufactures, and sells defense electronics, such as flight data/video recorders, inertial navigation systems for air/land applications, avionics for unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), and radar sensors for land vehicle protection, as well as its CATS (commercial aviation test stations). RADA derives almost 90% of its revenues from military agencies. The company has formed partnerships with customers Hindustan Aeronautics , GE Aviation , Embraer , and Israel Aerospace ; it also provides digital recording equipment for Lockheed Martin 's F-16 fighter aircraft. It exports about two-thirds of its products. Chairman, Herzle Bodinger served as former commander of the Israeli Air Force.
