New York fish processor Harbor Seafood banks on international shores. The company is a shellfish supplier that provides critters like crab, lobster, shrimp, squid, and clams. It carries a limited array of fin fish, including halibut, tuna, swordfish, and salmon. Harbor's products are sold under brand names such as Oyster Bay (retail) and Grub for Pubs (foodservice) in frozen, bulk, and prepackaged varieties. Harbor also manufactures value-added seafood, such as breaded shrimp and crab cakes. In addition to its own brands, the company offers private-label services. Harbor Seafood has global operations and purchases and processes seafood that it sources in Washington State, South America, the Caribbean, and Asia.
