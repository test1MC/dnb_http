Well-dressed tabletops and mantels thank company founders Pat Fitzpatrick and Bob Floyd. Fitz and Floyd is a leading manufacturer of giftware, dinnerware, and collectibles. The company also sells seasonal decorative items like cookie jars, decorative birds, Christmas ornaments, and Halloween candy plates. Fitz and Floyd sells its products through other retailers and  online through its company Web site. In 2009 the company sold its Charming Tails brand of resin figurines and related assets to gift retailer Enesco .
