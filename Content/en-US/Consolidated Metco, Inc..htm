Consolidated Metco (ConMet) looks to be a market heavy-weight, without the weight. The company manufactures lightweight components for heavy-duty commercial trucks. Its aluminum castings and assemblies include standard and preset wheel hubs and brake drums. ConMet offers structural plastics such as instrument panels and fairings, too. OEMs opt for the aluminum lineup as a way to simplify vehicle construction and reduce weight yet capture structural near-steel strength. ConMet parts can be found on heavy-duty truck brands Freightliner, and Daimler , Mack , Peterbilt , Kenworth, and Volvo . ConMet is a subsidiary of Amsted Industries .

The company has six manufacturing plants the US (three in North Carolina, and one each in Oregon, Tennessee, and Washington) and one each in China and Mexico. It also operates a warehouse in Canada.

ConMet's products include CastLite, PreSet, PreSet PLUS, and TruTurn.

In North America ConMet markets its products through authorized distribution channels such as truck and trailer dealers and independent distributors.

ConMet is looking to expand its portfolio of products.

The company was acquired by Amsted Industries in 1999.

ConMet was founded in 1964.
