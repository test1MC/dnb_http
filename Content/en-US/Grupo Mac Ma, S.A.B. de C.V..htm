Grupo Mac Ma is partial to sweets. Operating through a number of subsidiaries, the holding company produces, distributes, and sells cookies, biscuits, and chocolates throughout Mexico. Focused on high quality and gourmet markets, the company sells its treats under the Mac'Ma and Chocolates Ferback brands. Grupo Mac Ma also makes and sells quality pasta products under the Pinerolo brand. The company sells its products through department stores, grocery stores, and its own chain of about 60 Mac'Ma stores, which account for about 35% of sales.
