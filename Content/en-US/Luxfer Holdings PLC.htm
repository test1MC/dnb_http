What do luxury printing, portable oxygen tanks, catalytic converters, and jet engines have in common? If you guessed Luxfer Holdings, you are correct. Its Elektron division uses magnesium, zirconium, and rare-earth elements to produce corrosion- and flame-resistant alloys, plates, and powders for the aerospace, automotive, defense, and printing industries. Luxfer's Gas Cylinders group makes Luxfer oxygen, compressed natural gas, and carbon dioxide canisters for healthcare, industrial, transportation, and firefighting applications as well as Superform brand complex molded equipment casings, train fronts, auto body panels, and airplane air intakes. Luxfer has 16 manufacturing plants in six countries.
