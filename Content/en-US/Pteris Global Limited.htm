Pteris Global keeps the wheels of industry moving by making mechanical automation systems. Its products -- mostly aimed at the airline industry -- include baggage handling conveyors, air cargo handling systems, and in-flight kitchen equipment. Other lines range from automated systems used for parking cars to handling industrial materials. Wheelabrator air pollution control products feature fabric air filters and electrostatic precipitators. The company also offers planning, design, installation, and integration services. Customers include the Changi Airport in Singapore, the New Bangkok International, Ecuador's Quito International, and the US Phoenix Sky Harbor International airports.
