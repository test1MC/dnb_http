Carlin Combustion Technology burns more than just the midnight oil. The company makes natural gas, propane, fuel oil, and furnace oil burners, as well as igniters, controls, and related components such as motors, oil valves, circuit interfaces, and burner cover kits, for both residential and commercial applications. It also offers one-day professional training sessions on the components, setup, and testing of its residential oil, residential gas, and commercial oil and gas burners. Carlin Combustion Technology distributes its products throughout the US, as well as in Puerto Rico and Canada.
