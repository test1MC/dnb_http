Formerly Aruze Corp., Universal Entertainment is a leading manufacturer of gaming machines in Japan. It makes and distributes Pachinko and Pachislot machines, which are extremely popular among Japanese gamblers. Universal Entertainment also has a 20% stake in Wynn Resorts , a casino business with gaming resorts in Macau and Las Vegas. The company changed its name in 2009 after selling its US-based gaming machine manufacturing business, Aruze Gaming America , to chairman Kazuo Okada.
