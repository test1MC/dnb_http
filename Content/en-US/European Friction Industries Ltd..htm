Some may have a need for speed, but European Friction Industries is the place to shop to stop. The company, part of the UK-based Freeman Automotive Group, makes braking systems for agricultural machinery, automobiles, industrial vehicles, motorcycles, mountain bikes, and trains which are sold under the EBC brand name. Other European Friction products include machine gears, marine blocks, and wind turbine components. Founded in 1986, European Friction Industries was acquired in 2002 by UK-based EBC Brakes Group, which is owned by European Friction Industries' managing director Andrew Freeman.
