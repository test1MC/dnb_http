Garden State Fire & Security Alarm installs burglar and fire alarm systems for residential and commercial customers in -- you guessed it -- New Jersey. The company specializes in the installation of systems made by GE Security . Alarm monitoring services are provided by a contractor. Garden State Fire & Security Alarm was founded in 1969 by Eugene Ash, father of company president and owner Michael Ash.
