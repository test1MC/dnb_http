Thanks to Archimedes, Greek mathematician first to develop a block and tackle pulley system, Ancel Products is in business. Ancel Products is a job shop that manufactures small to medium-size orders for pulleys. Batch jobs are run for both standard-size and custom-designed pulleys, based on cast iron, steel, aluminum, and plastic. The company makes pulleys that range in diameter from one inch to 35 inches. Ancel Products also specializes in machining services -- drilling, boring, cutting, honing, arc welding, and grinding -- for a breadth of industries including aerospace and automotive. Founded in 1947, the company operates as a subsidiary of J.K. Pulley Company, Inc., owned by the Koch and Nichting families.
