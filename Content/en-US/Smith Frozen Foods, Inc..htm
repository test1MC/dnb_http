Getting food frozen is what fuels the fire of this company. Smith Frozen Foods is a processor and packager of frozen vegetables, including peas, corn, beans, and carrots. Purchasing most of its produce from growers in northeastern Oregon, the company's plant and 250,000-sq.-ft. cold storage facility can process and hold more than 130 million pounds of vegetables at a time. It packages vegetables for customers under various brand and house names. Founded in 1919, Smith Frozen Foods is owned by former US Senator from Oregon, Gordon Smith, and his wife, CEO Sharon Smith.
