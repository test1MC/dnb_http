Kongsberg Automotive's wants to be king of automotive components. The company manufactures seating components - head restraints, arm rests, lumbar supports, seat heaters, and pedals -- for vehicle manufacturers around the world. It also makes under-the-hood parts for the driveline and chassis, such as steering columns, gear shifts, cables, fuel lines, tubing, hoses, couplings, clutch actuators, stabilizing rods, electronics, and displays along with fluid transfer and driver control components. Its ePower Nordic subsidiary serves electric and hybrid vehicles. Kongberg has more than 30 plants in 20 countries including China where it owns 74% of Shanghai Kongsberg Automotive, which makes commercial truck pedals.
