Players fell in love with Bridgestone Golf's Lady, and now they're pretty fond of the Laddie, too. The company sells golf balls, including the Laddie and Lady lines, as well as golf clubs and accessories under the Precept and Bridgestone names. Touring pros Fred Couples, Matt Kuchar, Shigeki Maruyama, Nick Price, and Lee Trevino have played with Bridgestone Golf's gear, which is made at the company's Georgia plant and sold around the world. The Distance iQ ball, originally designed for women golfers, earned a place in men's bags when it was discovered the ball could be hit great distances by players with slower swings. Bridgestone Golf is a wholly owned subsidiary of Bridgestone Sports Co., Ltd .
