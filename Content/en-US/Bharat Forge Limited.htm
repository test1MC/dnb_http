Bharat Forge is revving up its presence as a global manufacturer of automotive engine components. A part of the Kalyani Group, the company supplies forged (close die, open die, and ring-rolled) and machined engine and chassis parts for commercial and passenger vehicle manufacturers and suppliers. Bharat Forge manufactures more than 130,000 tons of steel and aluminum crankshafts, connecting rods, and camshafts for passenger cars; SUVs; light, medium and heavy commercial vehicles; tractors; and diesel engines. It has about a dozen manufacturing plants across India, Germany, Sweden, China (through a joint venture with FAW ), and the US. Bharat Forge earns about 40% sales outside of India.
