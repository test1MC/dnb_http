Virginia is for lovers -- of communications testing equipment. Avcom of Virginia makes spectrum analyzers and related accessories for testing broadcast, cable, satellite, military communications, and wireless networks. The company's distributors in the US include Broadcast Depot Corp, Tessco Technologies , and Grove Enterprises; international distribution partners include Richardson Electronics (worldwide), Satcom Service (Russia), and Toner Cable Equipment (UK), to name a few. Avcom of Virginia also works with distributors in such countries as Canada, Dubai, and India. The company started its business producing satellite receivers in 1976.
