Bet Coronado never thought to look in Arkansas. LSB Industries did, though, and found its own El Dorado there. El Dorado Chemical is part of the chemical manufacturing division of LSB. It makes agricultural products and industrial chemicals, including nitrogen-based fertilizers, nitric acid, sulfuric acid, and industrial-grade ammonium nitrate for the mining industry. The company operates a manufacturing facility in El Dorado, Arkansas; it has additional plants in Texas, Alabama, and Oklahoma. It ships products to wholesale customers by truck or railcar; it also serves retail customers. El Dorado Chemical's products go into fertilizers, explosives, and other chemicals.
