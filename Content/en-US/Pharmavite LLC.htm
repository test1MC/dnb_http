Pharmavite manufactures pharm-fresh nutritional supplements. Pharmavite's Nature Made brand of vitamins, mineral supplements, and herbal remedies are sold through major mass retailers and drugstore chains. Like all successful supplement makers, Pharmavite's product lines shift and adapt and new product development is key. It has entered the snack food segment with its SOYJOY nutrition bars, which are made from soybeans and offered in flavors such as blueberry and strawberry. It also makes Voots Veggie-Fruit Tarts among other products. Pharmavite is a subsidiary of Japan-based Otsuka Pharmaceutical .

Every year the company produces more than 120 different kinds of supplements and fills more than 120 million bottles with 15 billion tablets, capsules, and softgels. It also markets more than 100 products in Iran, Japan, Mexico, South Korea, and Taiwan.

Pharmavite is headquartered in Northridge, California, and has facilities in San Fernando, and Valencia (both in California), and also in Opelika, Alabama.

The company markets its products internationally throughout Iran, Japan, Mexico, South Korea, and Taiwan.

Pharmavite is part of Otsuka's North American segment, which provides some 41% of its total revenues.

Pharmavite's strategy to capitalize on the growing global consumer trends on health and wellness and meet increased demand for its products.

In 2014 the company expanded its Nature Made adult gummies product line. The expanded line includes Adult Gummies Multi + Omega-3, Adult Gummies Energy‡ B12, and Adult Gummies Immune Complex with Zinc.

In late 2014, Pharmavite acquired FoodState, which makes the MegaFood and Innate Response brands of whole food supplements. FoodState continues to operate as a subsidiary of Pharmavite.

The company was founded in 1971 and acquired by Otsuka in 1989. Otsuka has since introduced the Nature Made vitamin brand to Japan.
