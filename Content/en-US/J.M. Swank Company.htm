If you have a taste for swanky food, J.M. Swank's the place. The company provides ingredients to "swank up" the flavor of foods by sourcing and distributing more than 4,000 ingredients for the dairy, beverage, meat, bakery, snack, confection, ethnic, and prepared-foods industries. Its major product categories include dairy, sweeteners, and specialty items. The company also offers such support services as ingredient blending, logistics, labeling and palletizing, inventory-management, and quality-control. It operates its own fleet of delivery trucks under the J.R.R.W. Transport operation. J.M. Swank Company was founded in 1954 and is owned by investment firm Platinum Equity . The company was previously owned by US food giant ConAgra Foods from 1992 to 2016.
