ICON Health & Fitness has brawn as one of the leading US makers of home fitness equipment. Targeted to consumers, ICON manufactures and sells treadmills, elliptical trainers, and other fitness equipment under the NordicTrack, HealthRider, ProForm, Image, iFit, Weslo, and Weider brands. ICON also offers commercial equipment through its FreeMotion Fitness business unit. The company makes most of its products in Utah but operates in Asia, Australia, Brazil, Europe, and Mexico. Its products are sold through major retailers, as well as through infomercials and its brands' eCommerce sites. The company was founded as a housewares importer in 1977. Bain Capital , Credit Suisse , and founders Scott Watterson and Gary Stevenson collectively own ICON.

Utah-based ICON maintains manufacturing facilities in its home state and additional operations in Asia, Australia, Brazil, Europe, and Mexico. The company operates more than 40 facilities around the world.

Multichannel marketer ICON serves both the consumer and commercial markets. It sells its products via direct sales through 800 numbers and online channels, catalog, and infomercials on the Home Shopping Network and QVC . It also sells through retailers the likes of Wal-Mart , Amazon.com , and Dick's Sporting Goods , as well as to membership and non-membership based fitness facilities, such as Gold's Gym and 24 Hour Fitness .

Although it sells primarily to consumers, the company also caters to commercial customers through its FreeMotion Fitness business unit.

To its benefit, ICON holds nearly 223 issued patents and has 107 patents pending. It aims to help customers "fit fitness into their home," particularly through its commitment to innovation. As part of its portfolio of products, ICON sells Space Saver folding treadmills and ellipticals for easy storage.

NordicTrack teamed up with fitness trainer Jillian Michaels (of TV's Biggest Loser fame) to amp up its product line. Michaels began promoting NordicTrack's Incline Trainer treadmill, which features programmable workouts narrated by the TV fitness star. ICON has also introduced other features to keep people glued to its workout gear. Some fitness machines come with built-in LCD screens, MP3 player ports, stereo speakers, and interactive fitness games.
