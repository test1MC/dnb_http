Bryant Rubber keeps it eye on the ball as a manufacturer of engineered, precision molded polymer components, using liquid injection, injection, transfer, and compression processes. End products include connector grommets, thin wall diaphragms, fluid and gas flow seals, septum closures, and a range of medical products. Bryant Rubber has developed the Select Primerless Adhesion Polymer System, a technology which allows primerless adhesion of silicone rubber to plastic substrates.
