ZyXEL Communications wants broadband to flow through its equipment. The company manufactures computer networking equipment, including modems, ISDN terminal adapters, security gateways, DSL routers, switches, and wireless LAN systems. It markets its products worldwide, selling directly and through distributors, resellers, and retailers to communications service providers, as well as consumer and corporate markets. ZyXEL counts Chunghwa Telcom , Earthlink , Orange , Sprint Nextel , Time Warner , and Verizon among its customers. The company was founded in 1989.
