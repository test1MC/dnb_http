Recticel's business is all filler -- but in a good way. The company produces flexible polyurethane foams used in bedding, insulation, and automotive products. Recticel makes spring, latex, and foam mattresses sold throughout Europe under a variety of brand names that include Beka, Lattoflex, and Superba. The company's insulation products serve both the construction and industrial manufacturing industries. Recticel's automotive products include molded seat cushions as well as both exterior and interior trim components. It operates 11 manufacturing facilities; eight are in Europe, two are in the US, and one in Asia.
