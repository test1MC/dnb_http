Windhover Information provides analysis and commentary on the health care industry, including biotech, pharmaceutical, and medical device and diagnostic sectors. The company's editorial and research staff creates newsletters, in-depth market reports, and databases. Publications include IN VIVO: The Business & Medicine Report and Start-Up: Windhover's Review of Emerging Medical Ventures . It also hosts a variety of pharmaceutical, biotech, and medical conferences including Euro-Biotech Forum and Pharmaceutical Strategic Alliances. Windhover Information was founded in 1989. It was purchased by global information firm RELX Group in 2008 and operates as part of health and science publisher Elsevier .
