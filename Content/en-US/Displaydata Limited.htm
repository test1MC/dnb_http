Displaydata, formerly known as ZBD Displays, is out to kill the paper or plastic labels deployed on store shelves. Its electronic displays offers retailers dynamic control (via wireless connections) of product and pricing information right where product meets customer. The company offers the displays in black and white with its Aura display and in three colors in its Chroma product. Displaydata offers Bluetooth-enabled labels that can communicate with smartphone apps. Customers are primarily retailers, including Orange , Coop Danmark , and T-Mobile .

Displaydata is headquartered in the UK and has offices there and in the US.

Displaydata made several moves in the past couple of years that indicate its strategy.

It sold its ZBD Germany unit to Delfi Technologies. The sale removes Displaydata from the German market, freeing resources for expansion elsewhere.

At its headquarters, Displaydata increased the size of its operations to make room for growth. And in the US it hired a senior vice president of sales for the Americas.
