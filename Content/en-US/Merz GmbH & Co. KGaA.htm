Merz might have made both the drug your doctor prescribed and the promotional pen she used to write the prescription. The company's specialty pharmaceutical business, Merz Pharma Group, focuses on therapies for central nervous system disorders (depression, Parkinson's, and Alzheimer's) and dermatology products. In Europe, the company also offers naturopathic OTC products. Its US subsidiary, Merz Pharmaceuticals , is focused primarily on dermatology products. Pharmaceuticals account for more than 75% of the company's revenues. Other Merz businesses include Merz Dental, maker of dental equipment and artificial teeth, and Senator GmbH, which produces Senator-brand pens and promotional items.
