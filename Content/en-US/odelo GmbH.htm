For odelo (formerly Schefenacker Vision Systems Germany), the light isn't at the end of the tunnel, it's at the rear of your vehicle. The auto parts company makes tail, brake, interior, and turn signal lights for major car manufacturers in Europe, including Audi , BMW , Ferrari , Porsche , Volkswagen , and Volvo using such light technologies as LED (light-emitting diode) and electroluminescence. Most of its tail lights are sold to customers in Germany, where it holds roughly 20% market share. The company also operates a manufacturing facility in Slovenia. In North America, about 2% of all tail lights are produced by odelo.
