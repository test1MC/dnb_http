Best Maid Products is a company that enjoys being in a serious pickle. Founded in 1926 by the Dalton family, the company distributes a variety of pickles, sauces, dressings, and other condiments under the Best Maid, Del-Dixie, and Farm to Market brand names. Best Maid provides products to retail stores, including Wal-Mart , H.E.B. , and Randall's . It also serves wholesale clients the likes of foodservice chains and distributors across the southwestern US. Best Maid operates its own fleet of delivery trucks. The family-owned company also sells pickles and other items directly to consumers via its website.

Texas-based Best Maid operates a 125,000-sq.-ft. distribution facility and a production plant in the Lone Star State.

Best Maid gets its products in the hands of consumers with the help of several companies, including grocery stores, food distributors, online stores, and restaurants. Its products are found mostly throughout the Southwest.

Best Maid Pickles launched a new line in 2013 to keep its portfolio of pickle products fresh. It introduced five flavored pickles -- Bloody Mary, Chipotle Lime, Garden Fresh, Garlic Baby Dills, and Sea Salt and Peppercorn -- under the Farm to Market specialty label.

In 2014 the company added to its portfolio by rolling out Best Maid mayonnaise that's packaged by Amcor Rigid Plastics in 12-oz. lightweight and break-resistent PET bottles.
