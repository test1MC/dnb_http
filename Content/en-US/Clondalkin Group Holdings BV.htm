Clondalkin Group Holdings likes to be very flexible when it comes to pleasing customers. Through its Flexible Packaging segment, the company manufactures plastic, foil, paper, and paperboard products, including seals, laminates, and lids for the agriculture, food, beverage, healthcare, and consumer goods industries. Clondalkin serves many of the same industries, as well as other industrial markets, with its Specialist Packaging segment which makes folding cartons, labels, leaflets, paper bags, and plastic cards. The company has over 40 operations scattered throughout Europe and the US, the two primary regions where its customers are located. Clondalkin Group is owned by private equity firm Warburg Pincus .
