Forget winding watches, changing clock batteries, springing forward and falling back, and other archaic timekeeping practices. Primex Wireless sets the right time, every time. Its Primex Wireless GPS Clock System, including Global Positioning System (GPS) transmitters and clocks, uses a satellite-linked technology to wirelessly synchronize all clocks in a building. Among many portfolio expansions, a Power over Ethernet (PoE) clock series hooks up to a resident network to enable remote control of devices from a browser interface. Primex Wireless sells its systems as a productivity tool to schools, universities, hospitals, manufacturing plants, corporations, and public agencies. It is a division of Primex Inc.
