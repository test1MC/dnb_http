If you dust the fingerprint-recognition technology sector, you'll find the prints of Cross Match Technologies all over the scene. The company makes forensic-quality fingerprint recognition and identification systems for law enforcement agencies and corporate customers. Among Cross Match's biometric products are fingerprint scanning, facial recognition systems, iris capture, and other identification systems. The company also offers identification systems for airports, national borders, nuclear power plants, and sea ports. Founded in 1996, Cross Match is owned by investment firm Francisco Partners Management .

Cross Match was acquired by investment firm Francisco Partners Management in mid-2012.

Cross Match has locations in Canada, Peru, Thailand, the UK, and the US.

Cross Match serves more than 10,000 customers around the globe. Its customers include the US Department of Defense , the Department of Homeland Security , the US State Department , and various other state and local government agencies. It also caters to numerous foreign governments and law enforcement agencies and provides biometric products and services to customers in the transportation, critical infrastructure, financial services, education, and health care sectors.

With security and terrorism still in the forefront of many, Cross Match should not have a problem finding a market for its products. The company has launched a number of new products and extended existing product lines over the last couple of years. In 2013 it launched SEEK Avenger, a handheld mobile credential reader. The year before, it introduced its WEBS web-enabled biometric software application and its first ten-print scanner with touchscreen and wet/dry finger capture.

In 2013 Cross Match formed Cross Match Ventures, an entity focused on the licensing and commercialization of emerging technology products within the biometric and identity management spheres. It partners with other companies to bring these products to the market and attract additional revenue.

To achieve a deeper penetration in Europe, in 2014 Cross Match opened a new sales office in Reading, west of London, in order to serve the UK and other countries in Europe.
