Beretta USA is right on target. It's the North American manufacturing and marketing arm of Beretta , the oldest gunsmith company in the world. Its products include handguns, rifles, and shotguns, as well as knives, accessories, and sporting apparel. Beretta USA sells to law enforcement agencies through exclusive distributors and peddles its products in its six high-end Beretta Gallery stores worldwide. The gun maker also provides weapons to the US military. As part of its business, Beretta USA also offers hand-finished firearms, specialty books, gift items, and custom-made hunting apparel. Berretta USA, established in 1977, showcases its guns in on cable TV via The World of Beretta .

The company's Gallery stores are located in New York, Texas, and Tennessee.

Some of Beretta USA's firearm and optics brands including Benelli, Burris, Franchi, Sako, Steiner, Stoeger, Tikka, and Uberti.

The company manufactures firearms for the US Military, Commercial, and Law Enforcement markets.

In 2016 Beretta USA opened a manufacturing and engineering center in Gallatin, Tennessee. The gun maker has transferred production operations for the M9 9mm pistol to its new 156,000 sq. ft. Gallatin facility, as well as the manufacturing operations for the variety of other firearms the company makes. In addition to the construction of the new facility and campus, Beretta USA has plans to employ an additional 200 employees in North Central Tennessee.

In 2014 the company introduced the M9A3 to the US Army.

Focused on customer service as it works to boost sales, Beretta USA implemented a three-part initiative. It opened regional Beretta service centers in Connecticut, Missouri, and Texas to add to its existing one in California. The gun maker has also partnered with fellow arms supplier Brownell to provide spare part sales, storage, and distribution for customers nationwide. And Beretta USA enhanced the customer service functionality on its website to include order and service status, warranty registration, product schematics, owner's manuals, and other information available around the clock. The company has changed its production method to a "just-in-time" manufacturing process. The move has allowed Beretta to reduce its inventory levels and better meet customer needs during different seasons.

Established in 1526, Beretta has been making firearms for several hundred years. The company's products have become a mainstay for the US during times of war. It set up Berretta USA in 1977.
