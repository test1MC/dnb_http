LEIFHEIT depends on demand for brands that make cooking, cleaning, and bathing a breeze. The company's household products division makes cleaning items (cloths, brooms, brushes), non-electric kitchen appliances, step ladders, and shopping trolleys under the LEIFHEIT brand. Other household product brands include Dr. Oetker bakeware, Soehnle personal and professional scales, and Birambeau kitchen tools and accessories. LEIFHEIT's bathroom furnishings division makes shower curtains, bathroom rugs, towels, toilet seats, and accessories. The company, established in 1959, operates about 10 production facilities and established Leifheit International USA in 1996.
