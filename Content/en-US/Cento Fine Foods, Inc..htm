Cento Fine Foods brings the enjoyable scents of its fine Italian foods to American dinner tables. The company makes, imports, and distributes more than 1,000 Italian food items, including canned tomatoes (peeled, diced, and pureed), dry pasta, olive oil, rice, pastries, and confections, throughout the US. Its products are sold under the Cento, AnnA, Bellino, Ferrara, La Florentine, and Little Gina's brands. Founded in 1963, Cento Fine Foods sells its products through its website, a direct sales force, and a network of brokers; it also operates a fleet of distribution trucks.
