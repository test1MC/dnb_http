Cloyes Gear & Products knows that timing is everything, in life and in internal combustion engines. The company makes timing chains, gears, and sprockets for leading car manufacturers and the automotive aftermarket. (Timing components synchonize the sparking of the plugs with the movement of the pistons in an engine.) The company's customers have included Chrysler , Ford , and General Motors . Cloyes Gear & Products also makes a variety of gears and sprockets for high performance vehicles, off-road equipment, and industrial machinery. The company operates two manufacturing facilities in Arkansas and one in Oklahoma. Cloyes Gear & Products was founded in the early 1920s.
