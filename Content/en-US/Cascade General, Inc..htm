Cascade General provides ship repair, overhauls, and drydock services, such as mechanical, electrical, and structural repairs; blasting/coating; and piping/rigging services. The company operates from the Portland Shipyard in Oregon, a leading West Coast ship repair facility. It works on a wide variety of vessels, including barges, cruise ships, factory trawlers, ferries, oil tankers, and tugboats. The company is a subsidiary of privately held Vigor Industrial LLC , which provides ship repair and construction services through subsidiaries in Alaska, California, Oregon, and Washington. Government contracts make up more than half of the company's business; the remainder comes from the repair of commercial vessels.
