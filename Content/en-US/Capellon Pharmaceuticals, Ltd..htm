Capellon Pharmaceuticals is helping curb the common cold. The company's product portfolio includes Liquibid, Rescon, Tekral, and Tussi-bid, prescription remedies for cough, colds, allergies, and congestion. Other products target various ailments including vitamin deficiencies, chronic pain, and gastrointestinal conditions. Capellon Pharmaceuticals markets its products to a customer base of health care providers and consumers. The company develops new products through its own research efforts and through collaborative partnerships or acquisitions. Capellon was founded in 1999.
