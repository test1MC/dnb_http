The flush heard around the world might be the one made by AS America. Doing business as American Standard Brands, the manufacturer of bath and kitchen products is a leader in the toilet and bathroom fixtures sectors in the US, Canada, and Mexico. Other products include faucets, sinks, bathtubs, showers, bathroom furniture, and accessories. The company targets both the residential and commercial markets; its products are found in homes, as well as airports, hotels, and stadiums, and are sold through a network of dealers, wholesale distributors, online retailers, and stores such as The Home Depot . LIXIL Corporation acquired the company from private equity firm Sun Capital Partners in 2013.

In 2013 Sun Capital Partners sold ASD Americas Holding Corp. (the parent company of American Standard Brand) to building products group LIXIL Corporation in a transaction valued at $542 million. LIXIL is a major manufacturing group based in Japan with a presence in 30 countries; it sells windows, tiles, front doors, kitchens, bathrooms, and toilets under the brand names INAX, Tostem, and American Standard.

American Standard Brands markets products under the brand names of American Standard, Jado, Porcher, Safety Tubs, Crane Plumbing, Eljer, Fiat, and Decorative Panels International.

In 2013 American Standard Brands expanded its product portfolio with the introduction of New Curved Tub Apron, Dual Flush Toilets and Invisia Collection of bath and shower accessories. That year it grew its American Standard Cadet PRO toilet collection to provide a full portfolio of five different bowl and tank options, in both standard and water saving models, exclusively for wholesale distribution.

American Standard Brands was formed in early 2008 through a merger of three companies: American Standard Americas, which was the bath and kitchen products division of American Standard Companies (now Trane ); plumbing fixture maker Crane Plumbing ; and vitreous china fixture manufacturer Eljer . The combination created a major force in the industry with a broader product and brand portfolio (including the American Standard, Crane, Eljer, Porcher, Fiat, and Jado brands) and enhanced global distribution opportunities.
