Customers' feet thank them. The Orthotic Group makes custom foot orthotic devices and customized footwear, including a variety of shoe styles such as sandals, boots, walking shoes, golf shoes, and sneakers. The Orthotic Group makes footwear in partnership with Brooks Sports , Crocs , and other manufacturers. The company also offers the GaitScan gait analysis product, a diagnostic software tool used by medical practitioners to assess foot biomechanics. Its products are distributed in Canada as well as throughout the US, the UK, Scandinavia, and the Asia/Pacifc region. The Orthotic Group was founded in 1985.
