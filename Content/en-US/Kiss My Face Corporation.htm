Pucker up -- Bycmac (dba Kiss My Face) is widening its reach into mainstream markets and riding the wave of the popularity of organic products. The company makes and markets more than 200 natural personal-care products that are sold in nearly 20 countries. Its products include toiletries, such as bar soap, deodorant, shampoo, and toothpaste, as well as household products, including candles, canvas bags, and cold and flu room spray. Kiss My Face products are sold through the company's website, through specialty shops, and natural products stores, such as the Vitamin Shoppe and Whole Foods in the US and Yeauty in Germany. The company was founded by Steve Byckiewicz and Bob MacLeod in the 1980s (hence Bycmac).
