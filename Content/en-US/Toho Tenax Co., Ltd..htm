Toho Tenax (formerly Toho Rayon) is stretching the reach of its fibers. The company specializes in the production of materials used in aerospace, aircraft, apparel, sports and recreation, and industrial products -- everything from high-fashion clothing to fishing rods, spacecraft, and pipes for offshore oil extraction. Toho Tenax's carbon fiber products business is its largest and fastest growing business; its textile business produces acrylic and cotton products, having divested its rayon business. The company's other businesses include polyurethane molding machinery and commercial linen supply services. Leading polyester maker Teijin owns Toho Tenax.
