For Kubota Engine America (KEA) it is the little things in life that matter. The company, a subsidiary of Japan-based Kubota Corporation , makes compact diesel and gasoline engines for use in agricultural, construction, industrial, and power generation equipment. KEA's engines range in size from about 10 to 100 horsepower. KEA also manufactures 60 Hz generators. The company sells its products through more than 50 independent distributors throughout the US and a network of some 300 dealers worldwide. Although Kubota-branded compact engines first reached US shores in 1976, KEA was formed in 1998.
