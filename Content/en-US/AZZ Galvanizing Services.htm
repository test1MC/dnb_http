AZZ Galvanizing Services (formerly North American Galvanizing & Coatings) is glad that rust never sleeps. Operations include hot-dip galvanizes fabricated structural iron and steel components to provide protection against corrosion. The company galvanizes its metal products by submerging them in molten zinc. Typical galvanizing customers work in the petrochemical, irrigation, food processing, highway and transportation, wastewater treatment, telecommunications, energy, and utilities industries. It operates facilities in eight states and sells its services directly to customers, primarily in the US. Texas-based galvanizer AZZ paid more than $125 million to acquire North American Galvanizing & Coatings in 2010.
