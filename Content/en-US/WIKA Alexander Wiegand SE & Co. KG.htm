WIKA Alexander Wiegand (WIKA) is best known for performing exceptionally well under pressure and has done so for more than 50 years. Founded in 1946, the family-owned company makes electronic and mechanical pressure measuring devices used in chemical, energy, food, gas distribution, and other industrial manufacturing applications. Other products include analog and digital thermometers, transmitters, thermowells, diaphragm seals, and accessories. The company also makes testing and calibration instruments and software; many mechanical products made by the company are computer-compatible, allowing for automated data collection. WIKA has worldwide manufacturing and distribution operations in more than 75 countries.
