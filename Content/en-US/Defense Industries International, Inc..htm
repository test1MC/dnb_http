Just because you're paranoid, it doesn't mean that people aren't out to get you. If someone is out to get you, Defense Industries International can help your odds of getting through the encounter unscathed. The company's primary projectile-proof products include body armor, bulletproof vests, and bomb disposal suits as well as ballistic helmets, wall covers, and one-way windows. Other offerings include harnesses, backpacks, battle pouches, tents, vehicle covers, winter suits, and sleeping bags. Customers include military (NATO, UN Peacekeeping Forces, Israeli Defense Forces), law enforcement, border patrol agencies, and corporations.
