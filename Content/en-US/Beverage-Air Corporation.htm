If you like a cold beer or a hot pizza, raise your mug to Beverage-Air. The company manufactures commercial foodservice and beverage equipment under such brands as Breeze and Maxi Marketeer. The company makes refrigerated display cases, coolers, freezers, beer dispensing equipment, and pizza and food preparation units. With manufacturing plants in South Carolina and Pennsylvania, Beverage-Air sells its products around the world, and has sales offices throughout the US, in Asia, Central and South America, and in the Middle East. Founded in 1944, the company was acquired by Italy's Ali in 2008. The deal joined Beverage-Air with more than 65 other brands under the global food service company.
