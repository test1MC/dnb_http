Montgomery Research publishes books, magazines, and Web sites on a wide range of business and technology topics. Subjects include supply chain management, financial performance management, and mobile commerce. The company targets clients in markets such as health care, utilities, financial services, and semiconductor manufacturing. Its Revenue magazine focuses on Internet affiliate marketing. Montgomery Research was founded in London in 1995.
