Uniweld Products manufactures welding equipment, pressure gauges and thermometers, and heating, ventilation, air-conditioning, and refrigeration (HVAC-R) tools. Products include air and fuel kits, flame tools, balloon inflators, medical gauges, vacuum pumps, leak detectors, automotive manifolds, tubing tools, and more. The company serves the welding, air-conditioning, refrigeration, plumbing, heating, automotive, and maintenance industries from its warehouses in Las Vegas and Houston, and its offices in Canada, Ecuador, Lebanon, Singapore, and the US. President David Pearl Sr. founded Uniweld Products in 1949. The company continues to be owned and operated by the Pearl family.
