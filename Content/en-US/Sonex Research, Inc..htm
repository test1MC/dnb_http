Sonex Research is developing and marketing its Sonex Combustion System to reduce fuel emissions and improve fuel mileage. The system, also called Ultra Clean Burn technology, consists of redesigned pistons used in existing car, truck, boat, and motorcycle engines. Development and demonstration programs with major OEMs are in various stages of completion. Sonex holds contracts with the US military to use its proprietary starting system and modified engine design in developing unmanned aerial vehicle engines that use safer, less volatile jet fuel in place of gasoline.
