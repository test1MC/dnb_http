Hutchison Harbour Ring is an original equipment manufacturer that specializes in the production of a wide range of toys for leading international toy brands. The company's toy operations serves original equipment and original design manufacturers, specializing in the production of promotional and conventional toys for major toy marketing companies. The production facilities of Hutchison Harbour Ring are mainly located in Guangdong province. The company also operates subsidiaries engaged in the design, marketing, and sale of telecommunication accessories, premium and electronic products, and property investment. Hutchison Harbour Ring is a subsidiary of CK Hutchison Holdings .
