Wafers and chips are vital to the health of STATS ChipPAC. The company assembles and tests integrated circuits (ICs) for semiconductor manufacturers on a contract basis. STATS ChipPAC receives wafers of ICs from manufacturers that it inspects for flaws, sorts, and cuts into individual chips. It then assembles, packages, and tests them. Analog Devices , Broadcom , Freescale Semiconductor , Intel , Marvell Technology , and QUALCOMM are among the company's top customers. About three-quarters of the company's sales come from the US, where many of the industry's fabless semiconductor companies are based. Singapore Technologies (which is owned by the Singapore government) owns 84% of STATS ChipPAC.
