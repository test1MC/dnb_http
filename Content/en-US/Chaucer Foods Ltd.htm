With few snippets here, a couple of sprinkles there, and powders and crumbles everywhere, Chaucer Foods adds bulk, body, crunch and flavor to packaged foods. The UK company makes croutons, bacon bits, fast-cooking pastas, mushroom extracts, and freeze-dried fruits, vegetables and cheeses for use by other food manufacturers. Its ingredient products (which are called re-hydrators in the food-manufacturing business) are added to instant soups, packaged salads, ready-meals, and other easy-to-prepare convenience foods. Chaucer Foods sells its products to other food companies. The company operates manufacturing sites in China, France, and the UK.
