H. Bauer Publishing is reaping the rewards of its magazine holdings in the UK. The British firm is a unit of European publishing powerhouse Bauer Verlagsgruppe , German publisher of some 280 magazines in 15 countries. Its popular lifestyle, TV listing, and magazine holdings in the UK include women's titles Bella , Take a Break , and That's Life! TV publications include TV Quick and TV Choice . The company also produces puzzle publications, and several of its magazine concepts have made the leap to North America through sister firm Bauer Publishing USA . Bauer Verlagsruppe expanded greatly with the acquisition of Emap's radio and consumer magazine holdings in 2008, adding titles such as FHM and Heat to its portfolio.
