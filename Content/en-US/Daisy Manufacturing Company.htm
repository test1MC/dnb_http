Daisy Manufacturing, the world's oldest and largest maker of pellet and air-powered guns, ammunition, and accessories, aims to please. Its products are sold at online retailers, gun dealers, and sporting goods stores such as Bass Pro Shops and Dick's Sporting Goods . Founded in 1882 as the Plymouth Iron Windmill Company, Daisy's first air gun was introduced in 1886 as a premium for farmers who purchased windmills. Charter Oak Partners owns a majority interest in the company.
