As the publisher of weekly magazine The Economist, The Economist Group has been in the thick of the British media scene since 1843. The Economist has a circulation of 1.4 million, with most of its readers living outside the UK. Its Economist.com includes full editorial from The Economist 's print edition, as well as Web-only features. Its CQ-Roll Call publishes titles covering legislative and political news and analysis, and are widely read by lawmakers, policy makers, and lobbyists. Its Economist Intelligence Unit conducts research, while its EuroFinance produces conferences and training in finance. The Financial Times Group , a subsidiary of British media giant Pearson , is selling its 50% stake in The Economist Group to existing shareholders.
