Menasha Packaging wants you to have your cake and carry it, too. Cake circles and boards and deli boxes are just a few of the products designed and manufactured by this corrugated package maker. The largest of Menasha 's three operating units, Menasha Packaging produces a slew of specialty packaging as well as point-of-purchase retail aids, such as displays, paperboard and paperboard substrates, protective cartons, and commercial printing products. Menasha Packaging also offers consumer goods manufacturers structural and graphical design services.

Headquartered at Neenah, Wisconsin, Menasha Packaging operates through 30 locations (16 fulfillment and 14 manufacturing plants) in about 15 states in the US and also in Canada. It has seven "retail integration institute campuses" located in Arkansas, Minnesota, Ohio, Wisconsin, Illinois, Pennsylvania, and California.

Menasha Packaging operates through nine groups: RII (Retail Integration Institute Group), Display Group (P-O-P DISPLAYS), Folding Carton Group, Food Group, Fullfillment, OTC HealthCare Group, Packaging Group, Preprint Group, and Services Group. Its sister company Orbis develops plastic reusable packaging programs for longer-term cost savings and sustainability.

Menasha Packaging serves customers in the food and beverage, automotive, storage and distribution, pharmaceutical, household products, retail, and environmental sectors.

The company bolsters its operations through the opening of additional facilities and through acquisitions. As a part of its focus on providing point-of-sale expertise to targeted customers, in 2014 Menasha Packaging purchased Pennsylvania-based Strine Printing Company in a deal that enhanced its graphic packaging, merchandising, and fulfillment capabilities and boosted its presence on the East Coast.

In 2013 it obtained Rand Diversified, a POP-focused display firm, in a deal that extended its presence along the East Coast and enhanced its expertise in the areas of contract packaging and fulfillment for over-the-counter pharmaceutical and consumer packaged goods companies. Menasha Packaging in 2012 fortified its traditional packaging operations with the pickup of The Strive Group, an integrated merchandising supply chain company based in the important metropolitan region of Chicago.
