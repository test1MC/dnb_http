CMC Magnetics has a history with the media. The company manufactures optical and magnetic data storage media. Its products include CDs, DVDs, floppy diskettes, and media cases. The company is the exclusive distributor of Philips -brand discs in the Americas; it also distributes HP -branded USB flash drives. CMC provides mass replication services for CD-ROMs, audio CDs, and DVDs. The company also and manufactures various types of product packaging through its packaging group.
