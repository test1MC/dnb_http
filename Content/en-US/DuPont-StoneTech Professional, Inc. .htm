Granite may be tough, but even tough guys need some extra protection every now and then. And that's where DuPont StoneTech Professional comes into the picture. The company, a business unit of E. I. du Pont de Nemours (DuPont), researches, develops, and manufactures protective chemicals and care products for stone, ceramic, grout, and masonry. StoneTech manufactures sealers that take preemptive steps to protect your stone, cleaners for everyday spills and stains, and refinishing products for when older materials.
