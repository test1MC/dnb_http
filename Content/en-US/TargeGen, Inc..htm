TargeGen is developing small molecule therapeutics to treat hematological malignancies, or for the layperson, cancer and other disorders of the blood, bone marrow, and lymph nodes. The development-stage firm's lead (and only) drug candidate is TG101348, which is in early clinical trials for the treatment of a bone marrow disorder called myelofibrosis. The biotech company had been eyeing an IPO but opted in mid-2005 to stay private and took on an additional round of venture capital financing to fund trials of TG101348.
