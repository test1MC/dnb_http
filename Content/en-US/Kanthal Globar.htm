Kanthal Globar markets high-temperature ceramic products for industrial and residential applications. The company's product lines serve two main categories including electronic components, such as resistors (produce charges), capacitors (store charges), and thermistors; electric heating elements for furnaces comprise the second category. Kanthal Globar is a subsidiary of Sweden's Kanthal AB, which in turn is a part of Sandvik AB , a manufacturer of tools and materials for the mining and construction industries. Kanthal Globar is part of Sandvik's Materials Technology business area.
