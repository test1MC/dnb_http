The Gardner family's business has grown and a rich crop of specialty instruments. Paul N. Gardner (Gardco) makes testing instruments for paint and coating inspection in the automotive, aerospace, chemical, petroleum, plastic and pharmaceutical industries. The company's products include moisture checking and heat monitoring devices, meters for measuring gloss, microfilm applicators, relative humidity measurement devices, salt contamination detection kits, and a host of other specialty monitors and devices.
