Medicure develops drugs for the treatment of cardiovascular diseases and stroke. Its first commercial product, Aggrastat, is an injectable treatment for acute coronary syndrome. Medicure is seeking partnerships to expand its product portfolio. In late 2016, the company acquired a majority stake in Apicore, which develops and manufactures specialty active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs) and pharmaceuticals. That acquisition added two manufacturing facilities -- one in New Jersey and another in India. Medicure was established in 1997.
