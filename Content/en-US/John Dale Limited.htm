John Dale wants to keep its customers completely wiped down, regardless of their ages. The company makes baby and children's wipes (Baby Dream, Mucky Faces, and Me and My Baby), skin-care wipes (Cherish and Skin Deep), and facial tissues (Cherish, Fleur, Countess, and Viscount). Included in John Dale's brands is Skin Deep Self Tanning Wipes, which work in about three hours and can last for several days. The company also manufactures private-label products for other major retailers. Operating at a 60,000 sq. ft. factory in Wales, the company was founded by its managing director, John Dale.
