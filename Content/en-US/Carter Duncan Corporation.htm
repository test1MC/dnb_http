Carter Duncan sells "soapbars," but you can't wash your face with them. Doing business as Seymour Duncan (the name of its founder), the company makes electric and acoustic guitar and bass pickups (which translate sound waves for amplification). In addition to its Phase II Passive Soapbar, it also sells products under brand names such as Custom 5, Five-Two, Humbucker, Lightnin' Rod, Stratocaster, Trembucker, and Twang Banger. The pickups are sold at music retailers throughout the US and internationally. It partnered with Hottie Amplifiers in 2009 to make Hottie Guitar Pickups, which are sold as singles or sets and with the option to customize.
