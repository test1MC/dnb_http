Insight Equity A.P. X (dba Vision-Ease Lens) is quite comfortable creating spectacles. The company specializes in designing, making, and distributing the increasingly popular polycarbonate prescription lenses used in eyewear today. While the company focuses on polycarbonate lenses for their impact resistance, light weight, and ultraviolet-filtering properties, Vision-Ease also manufactures and markets other styles. It produces plastic and glass lenses, as well as lens treatments such as polarized, anti-reflective, scratch-resistant, and photochromic (allowing lenses to darken automatically according to light levels). The company was founded in 1930.

Minnesota-based Vision-Ease sells its ophthalmic lenses nationwide, as well as in Canada, Latin America, and Europe.

Vision-Ease's lineup includes Novel and Novella progressives, LifeRx photochromic, and Coppertone and SunRx polarized lenses. The company manufactures its complex polycarbonate products, as well as new items, in Minnesota. Its standard polycarbonate and glass items are made in Indonesia.

The company sells its products through independent opticians, optometrists, and ophthalmologists, as well as a host of retail chains and markets that dispense eyewear.

Customers who rely on Vision-Ease to supply them with eyewear include the likes of ubiquitous global retailer Wal-Mart and optical specialist LensCrafters . As an added service, Vision-Ease offers sales and marketing training to its customers in Latin America, Canada, and the US in an effort to help them grow their own clientele.

To expand its products portfolio, Vision-Ease in 2013 launched its Novel and Novella conventional front-side progressive lenses, which are made using an iterative design process. Taking into account wearer comfort, the lenses are developed to be both smooth and soft.
