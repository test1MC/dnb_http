Alès Groupe hopes to make you look, feel, and smell better. The France-based company manufactures and distributes hair care, cosmetics, coloring, skin care, and perfume products. Its Phyto and plant- and vegetable-based shampoos and conditioners are available worldwide in stores; such lines as Phytologie and Secret Professional are available only at professional salons. Alès Group also makes products exclusively for other salon chains. The Paris-based firm sells over 80 skin care and cosmetic products, including anti-aging and anti-wrinkle products. International markets include Asia, North America, and Western Europe. Alès Groupe (formerly Phyto-Leirac) was founded by its chairman and CEO Patrick Alès.

Alès Groupe's key brands are Leirac, Phyto, Phyto Specific, Caron, and Laboratoire Ducastel. Its cosmetics products bring in around 50% of revenue. The haircare business brings in 46%, with the remaining 4% brought in by perfumes.

The company has operations in ten European countries, and two in each of North America (US and Canada) and Asia (Turkey and Hong Kong). France is the country's largest market, at around 43% of sales.

The company sells its products through variety of distribution channels such as pharmacies, hair salons, and luxury department stores, among others.

In 2015, revenue increased 7% on prior year to €244.2 million.

Alès Groupe established e-commerce websites for its Phyto, Lierac and Caron brands.

Alès Groupe acquired two French wholesalers of professional products for hairdressers, Boy Diffusion and Distri-coiff, for a combined €15 million.
