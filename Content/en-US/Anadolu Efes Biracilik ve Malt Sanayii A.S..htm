Effervescence keeps business bubbling at Anadolu Efes. The company produces and markets beer and soft drinks throughout its home country of Turkey, as well as the Middle East, Russia, Eastern Europe, and Central Asia, serving some 600 million consumers. In the #1 position, the Efes brand commands an impressive 76% of the beer market in Turkey. In the soft-drink segment, the company is Turkey's #1 Coca-Cola bottler and distributor (through subsidiary Coca-Cola Icecek). Anadolu operates 16 breweries and 20 soft drink bottling plants.
