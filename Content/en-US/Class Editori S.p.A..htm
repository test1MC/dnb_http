Class Editori is a leading publisher of newspapers and magazines in Italy. Its portfolio of titles includes newspapers Italia Oggi and MF/Milano Finanza , along with such magazines as Case & Country, Lombard , and Luna . In addition to publishing, the company distributes financial news and information through its e-class unit, and it operates radio station Radio Classica along with digital television channels Class CNBC (business news) and Class News.
