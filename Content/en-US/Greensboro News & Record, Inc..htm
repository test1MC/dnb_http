Greensboro News & Record publishes the News & Record , one of North Carolina's leading newspapers with a weekday circulation of more than 80,000. It also publishes Go Triad , a free entertainment and leisure weekly serving the Peidmont Triad area, including Greensboro, High Point, and Winston-Salem. In addition, Greensboro News & Record operates websites for its newspaper and weekly magazine. The News & Record was formed in 1982 through the merger of two smaller papers, the Greensboro Daily News (started in 1909) and The Daily Record (founded in 1890).
