I.D. Systems has taken its tracking business on the road. The company's products track, analyze, and control the movements of objects such as packages and vehicles. Its systems use radio-frequency identification (RFID) technology and tiny computers attached to the object to be monitored, and users can access tracking data via the Internet. The company is focused on vehicle management, rental car, package tracking, and airport ground security applications. Customers include 3M , the FAA , Ford , Hallmark Cards , Target , the US Postal Service (42% of sales), and Wal-Mart Stores (41%).
