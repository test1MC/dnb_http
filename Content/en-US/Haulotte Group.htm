The Haulotte Group (formerly Pinguely-Haulotte) puts construction workers on top of the world. Haulotte manufactures aerial platforms and about 60 other products, including telescopic and self-propelled cranes, backhoe loaders, telehandlers, and scaffolding, as well as event staging and seating. Considered one of the largest aerial manufacturers in the world, Haulotte has subsidiaries in Asia, Eastern Europe, South America, and six manufacturing plants in France, Italy, Romania, and the US. The company maintains sales, rental, and distribution offices worldwide, including a service center in France to support global customers. The Solem Group holds about 53% of Haulotte, as well as 73% voting rights.
