Located in California's famed Napa Valley, Franciscan Vineyards serves up a few traditional fine red and white wines, but it also has a few custom blends up its sleeve. Franciscan's signature wines include Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Chardonnay, and Sauvignon Blanc. The winery also offers limited release wines, such as Cuvée Sauvage, made using native yeast; and Magnificat, a Cabernet Sauvignon/ Merlot blend. Founded in the mid 1970s by Raymond Duncan and Justin Meyer, the winery is today owned by spirits giant Constellation Brands . Franciscan operates its own 240 acre vineyard, but as the fine wine division of its parent, the winery also manages other Constellation-owned wineries, including Ravenswood .
