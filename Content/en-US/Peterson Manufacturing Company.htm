Peterson Manufacturing is full of bright ideas. Although it controls 10 diversified business units, the family-owned company is primarily a maker of automotive and trailer lighting products. Peterson's lighting offerings include spotlights and halogen accessories, fog and off-road lights, flashers, dome and interior lights, and back-up lights. The company also makes automotive mirrors. Lighting customers include Harley-Davidson and Caterpillar . Peterson's other operations include Anderson Marine (boat and trailer lights), Maxi-Seal (heavy-duty truck wiring harnesses), Mission Plastics (injection-molded plastic parts), and D&D Aviation (air charter services).

The company's product line covers more than 2,200 different types of vehicle safety lights, reflectors, mirrors, antennas and other related products. Its catalog features more than 250 new products, including 75 LED lights.

Peterson's 670,000 sq. ft. manufacturing facility in Grandview, Missouri houses management, sales, engineering, R&D, manufacturing and assembly, purchasing, shipping, receiving and warehousing departments.

Its major customers include proprietary OEMs ( Caterpillar , Harley-Davidson , and John Deere ); Non-Proprietary OEMs ( Heil , International, McNeilus , and Timpte); and Fleets ( Allied Waste , Transport America, and Waste Management ); and other customers ( Fleetwood , Lowe's , and Wal-Mart of Canada ). It is represented in Central and South America, the Caribbean, and portions of Mexico by John Prior; in New Zealand and Australia by CDL Autoparts; and in the UK and Continental Europe by UK-based Peterson Europe.

Peterson uses its vast production facilities, warehousing space and well-trained staff to maintain a 97% fill-rate and order-turnaround of three-to-five days. It is seeking to expand its product line covering more than 2,200 different types of vehicle safety lights, reflectors, mirrors, antennas and other related products.

In 2013 Peterson moved into the rapidly developing LED forward lighting market, launching a new 7-inch round LED headlight.

To expand its worldwide presence in regions where it did not currently have an international sales partner, in 2012 Peterson formed a strategic alliance with Dorian Drake International, to represent the company in certain international markets.

Peterson was founded in 1945 and has been under its current ownership (the Armacost family) since 1956.
