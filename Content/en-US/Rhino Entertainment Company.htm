This record company is helping to save endangered species of the music world. A subsidiary of Warner Music Group , Rhino Entertainment specializes in anthologies, compilations, and reissues. Its releases have included boxed sets covering such artists as Ray Charles, John Coltrane, The Doors, and Joy Division. It is well known for novelty oriented material such as Like, Omigod! The '80s Pop Culture Box and soundtracks from such TV shows as Mystery Science Theater 3000 and ABC 's Schoolhouse Rock series from the 1970s. It also distributes DVDs and videos of classic television shows, concert films, and documentaries.
