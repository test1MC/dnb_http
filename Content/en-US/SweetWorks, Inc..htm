You might say this food company offers sugary employment. SweetWorks is a candy maker serving the retail market with chocolates, gum balls, and jawbreakers sold under such brands as Bubble King, Oak Leaf, and Ovation. Its confections are sold mostly through supermarket chains, drug and convenience stores, warehouse clubs, and mass merchandisers in the US and Canada. In addition to its retail brands, SweetWorks offers contract manufacturing for other candy makers through its three plants in New York and Ontario. The company was formed in 2002 through the merger of three candy companies, Niagara Chocolates, Oak Leaf Confections, and Whetstone Candy.
