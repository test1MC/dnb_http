fSONA's products help customers maintain happily unattached relationships. The company is a manufacturer of optical wireless transmission systems designed to bridge gaps in wired and wireless telecommunications networks. Its line of SONAbeam products use lasers instead of radio signals to transmit and receive data over line of sight paths. fSONA's broadband products are designed for use in corporate networks, ISPs, and other telecommunications systems. The company's customers including Creo , the Indy Racing League , and the United States Army .
