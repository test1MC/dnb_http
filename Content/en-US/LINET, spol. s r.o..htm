Hospitals just wouldn't be hospitals without patient beds, and that's where Linet comes in. A manufacturer of furniture for the health care industry, Linet offers patient beds and mattresses for hospital rooms, intensive care units, and nursing homes. Other products include carts for instruments, food service, laundry, and general purposes, as well as bedside cabinets and other accessories. The manufacturer exports to more than 70 countries, including the US, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East. It distributes through subsidiaries located in select countries and through partnerships with third-party suppliers. A fast-growing company, Linet was founded by managing director Zbyněk Frolík in 1990.
