Bringing good 'ole American chewing gum to mouths across the UK, The Wrigley Company Limited, or Wrigley UK, sells the products made famous by its parent Chicago-based Wm.Wrigley Jr. Company . The company's gum brands, which are produced at its 45-acre plant in Estover, Plymouth, include Extra, Orbit, Hubba Bubba, Juicy Fruit, Spearmint, and Doublemint. It also makes and distributes Airwaves, a gum that contains a menthol "kick" designed for European tastes (and only sold there). Airwaves' varieties include Lemon Ice (menthol and lemon), Cherry Menthol, and Spicy Cocktail (orange and menthol). Wm. Wrigley Jr. Company founded Wrigley UK in 1911.
