Prince Agri Products is royally concerned with the health of livestock -- cattle, swine, poultry and pets worldwide. The company, a subsidiary of Phibro Animal Health (formerly called Philipp Brothers Chemicals), refines trace minerals such as cobalt, copper, iodine, iron, manganese, molybdenum, and zinc, as well as additives to be used as premixes for animal feed. It is one of the largest trace minerals providers in the industry. The company's Chromax (chromium tripicolinate) is a nutritional supplement fed to sows. Prince Agri acquired the manufacturing assets and patents and trademarks of Animate, a feed additive technology and anionic mineral supplement for cows, from Granco Minerals in 2011.
