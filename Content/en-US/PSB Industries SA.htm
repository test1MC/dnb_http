The next time you spring for a new Chanel cologne, Clarins cream, or Lancôme lipstick because of its pretty packaging, you may have PSB Industries to thank (or blame). The company specializes in creating and manufacturing packaging products, particularly for the beauty industry. PSB Industries' subsidiaries include Baikowski, which makes ultra-pure alumina powder and other fine mineral oxides for industrial use; CGL Pack, which makes custom packaging; and Texen, which specializes in plastic injection for beauty products packaging. PSB Industries has operations in France, Japan, Mexico, and the US. In 2008 Texen bought Mar-Lee Companies, an American maker of precision injection molds.
