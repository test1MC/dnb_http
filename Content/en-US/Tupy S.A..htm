Tupy is heavy into cast iron parts. With two industrial plants in Brazil, and an investment in a machining plant in Mexico, the company the world leader in iron blocks and cylinder heads for engines. Its parts (designed for brake, propulsion, transmission/steering, and axle/suspension systems) are made for trucks, buses, utility and passenger vehicles, tractors, and other types of agricultural machinery. Tupy also makes malleable cast iron parts, iron and steel shots and grits, and continuous cast bars for the civil construction, marble and granite, and other industrial engineering markets. The company, founded in 1938, sells to Ford , Chrysler , Cummins , and Mack Trucks , among others in more than 40 countries.
