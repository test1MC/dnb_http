Total media saturation is all in a day's work for Sanoma Corporation. The group is a leading media company in Finland and the Nordic region; it also operates in other European regions (a total of some 20 countries). While its bread and butter is magazine publishing (Sanoma Magazines), the company also has subsidiaries devoted to newspaper publishing (Sanoma News); TV, radio, and Internet (Sanoma Entertainment); bookstores and distribution (Sanoma Trade); and educational materials (Sanoma Learning). The company's Helsingin Sanomat is the largest daily newspaper in the Nordic region. Sanoma history stretches back to 1860 when Gustaf Söderström founded his book printing business.
