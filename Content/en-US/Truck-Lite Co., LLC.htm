No, Truck-Lite does not contain half the calories as most trucks; instead it helps truck drivers see in the dark. A subsidiary of diversified transportation company Penske Corporation , Truck-Lite makes LED signal and forward lighting products for the automotive, heavy-duty truck, off-highway, marine, military, and construction vehicular markets. Its headlamps and lighting products are also used by recreational vehicles and buses. Additional products include wiring harnesses, mirrors, and other safety accessories. Truck-Lite has plants and offices in the US, China, Mexico, and Europe.

Truck-Lite operates several US manufacturing plants and offices in Michigan, New York, and Pennsylvania. International offices reside in France, Mexico, Poland, the UK. German-based subsidiary FER Fahrzeugelektrik gives Truck-Lite a presence in Eastern Europe through facilities in Poland and Belarus, as well as in Spain.

The company sells through dealers and sales representatives. Its customer segments include automotive, off-highway transportation, construction, agricultural, marine, heavy-duty truck, trailer, recreational vehicles, and military markets. Customers have included such noteworthy names as Chrysler , Ford , General Motors , Mack Trucks , Navistar , PACCAR , the US Army , and Volvo .

Truck-Lite's strategy is focused on product improvements and portfolio expansion, as wells as finding more innovative ways to produce safety lighting. In 2013 the company released its full-line catalog featuring more than 2,700 products.

Truck-Lite was founded in the 1950s by George Baldwin.
