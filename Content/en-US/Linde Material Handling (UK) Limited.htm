Linde Material Handling (UK) is there when you need to move something big from Point A to Point B. One of the largest forklift and warehouse equipment makers in the UK, the company manufactures electric and gas-powered pallet trucks, counterbalance trucks, stackers, order pickers, side loaders, and container handlers. Its flagship product is branded under its Linde X range of reach trucks. The company operates under the Linde Material Handling, Linde Severnside, Linde Castle, Linde Jewsbury, and Handling & Storage Equipment Co. names and is owned by material handling equipment provider Kion Group AG.

Linde's list of customers includes Garden Buildings Direct, The Co-Operative, Poundland, Regatta, Twyford Bathrooms, and Blue Print.

Linde accounted for 60% of its parent company's net sales in 2015.

Linde grows by introducing new products and vehicles. In 2016 it launched the new Linde Roadster, a range of trucks designed to maximize field of view and safety to operators. The Roadster is an alternative configuration which applies to its models E20 to E50R.
