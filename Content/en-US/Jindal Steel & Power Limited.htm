Jindal Steel & Power (JSPL) is not a superhero, though it is a company made of steel and power. Through its subsidiaries, JSPL operates across a handful of industries, including steel production, power generation, and coal mining. Steel is by far its largest segment, producing some 4.5 million tons of steel, 1.4 million tons of sponge iron, and millions of tons of pig iron per year at its plant in Chhattisgarh, India. Products include railroad track rails, beams, coils, and wire rods. JSPL's power generation business produces about 4,500 megawatts of electric power, while its mining business helps fuel its electricity and steel production. JSPL is part of Indian conglomerate O. P. Jindal Group.
