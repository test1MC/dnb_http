Chicken soup isn't just good for colds -- it's good for business. Health Communications (HCI) publishes books in the self-help and inspirational categories, including the popular Chicken Soup for the Soul . Other top sellers for the company include Adult Children of Alcoholics and A Child Called It . In addition, the company publishes Counselor, The Magazine for Addiction Professionals ; and organizes conferences, training seminars, and other events for professionals in the addictions and mental health fields through its U.S. Journal Training and Institute for Integral Development subsidiaries. In 2008 the company lost the right to publish and distribute new titles in the Chicken Soup for the Soul series.
