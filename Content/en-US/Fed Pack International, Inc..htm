Looking for the next boxing champion? Fed Pack International promotes more than a pretty package. The packing and crating company specializes in packaging for high-tech equipment and heavy machinery, whether the product is traveling across the street or around the globe. Design and manufacture of Fed Pack International's lineup is in-house, and includes plywood shipping crates and skids. Warehouse space for short-term storage of customers' inventory is also offered. Formerly known as Future Packaging, Fed Pack International stretches back to 1973, established by its president, Ed Vega.
