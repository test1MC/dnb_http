O-I Limited loves to hear the clink (and even crash) of beer bottles in pubs around the UK; it probably made the bottles making the noise. The leading glass container manufacturer for the UK spirits business, O-I Ltd. operates two manufacturing plants -- one in Harlow and the other in Alloa. Other operations include glass packaging for premium spirits and glass recycling. Major customers include Anheuser-Busch InBev and Diageo . The company, which has operations in more than 10 European countries, is a subsidiary of Owens-Illinois , Europe's largest glassmaker.
