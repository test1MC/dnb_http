AOL UK knows that Americans aren't the only ones who like being online. The British subsidiary of American Web portal AOL is focused on providing content and managing online advertising. The site offers communication services such as free e-mail and instant messaging, as well as news, shopping, and entertainment content. AOL (then called America Online) originally launched its UK service in 1996. AOL UK sold its broadband and dial-up Internet access business, complete with more than 2 million subscribers, to The Carphone Warehouse Group in 2007 for some $690 million. In 2015 Verizon acquired AOL for about $4.4 billion.
