The Graduate's Mr. McGuire and this company's founder and chairman Chiang Chen agree on one thing: There's a great future in plastics. Chen Hsong Holdings is one of the world's largest manufacturers of injection molding machines, used to make such products as home appliances, electronics, and toys. The company produces about 15,000 machines each year for customers in more than 80 countries around the globe (though most of its revenues are generated in China and Hong Kong). The company is now run by Chiang Chen's daughter, CEO Lai Yuen Chiang.
