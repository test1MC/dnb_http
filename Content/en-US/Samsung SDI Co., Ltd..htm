Samsung SDI wants you to want one of those huge flat-screen televisions. The company, 20% owned by Samsung Electronics , specializes in the dazzling world of display technology, manufacturing such prized consumer products as plasma display panels and liquid-crystal display panels. Bigger is always better in such things, so Samsung SDI offers screens as large as 102 inches. Its technologies also find applications on a smaller scale, such as in computer monitors, cell phones, and other handheld mobile devices. Established in 1970, Samsung SDI also produces rechargeable batteries and is working on next-generation displays. It formed a joint venture in 2008 with Robert Bosch GmbH to make lithium-ion batteries.
