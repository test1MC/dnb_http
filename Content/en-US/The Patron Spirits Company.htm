The Patrón Spirits Company hopes to make ultra-premium patrons of as many people as possible. The world's #3 tequila maker produces five varieties of Patrón "ultra-premium" tequila in Jalisco, Mexico (where its main ingredient, the Weber Blue agave plant, grows). It also makes Patrón Citrónge, an orange liqueur, and Patrón XO Cafe, a blend of coffee and tequila. Patrón's liquor lineup includes Pyrat Caribbean rum (US distribution only) and Ultimat Polish vodka (which it owns). The company distributes its various products in 100 countries, where it targets upscale retailers and duty-free shops. Patrón Spirits International (formerly Caribbean Distillers Corporation), owns Patrón Spirits Company.
