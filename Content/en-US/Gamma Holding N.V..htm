Whether you're garbing a car seat, yourself, or a conveyor belt, Gamma Holding probably made the cloth. The textile maker offers an array of goods through two units. Industrial Solutions makes industrial textiles, such as coated conveyor belts and process belts; filters and screens; coatings, anti-ballistic materials, and industrial knitted cloth; and high-tech sailcloth. Lifestyle Fabrics makes patterned fabrics for mattress ticking, automobile upholstery, and African-print cloth. Gamma has operations in about 40 countries (including the US) and makes more than half of its sales outside Europe. In 2011 two Dutch private equity firms acquired a the company; Gamma subsequently delisted from the Amsterdam market.
