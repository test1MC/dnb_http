There are no half measures for Nelsons Labels, the UK and Europe's premier supplier of specialist labels to the bedding, upholstery, and carpet industries. The venerable company has occupied a modern purpose built factory in the city of Manchester since 1999, housing the highest standards of plant and equipment to allow it to service the needs of the markets it supplies. The company's status is also helped by the fact that the factory is located next to the world famous Manchester United Football Club. The company opened its doors for business in 1919 as a supplier of fabric labels to the raincoat, cap, and hat industries.
