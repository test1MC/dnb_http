Inscape improves office landscapes on the inside of the building. The company designs, manufactures, and distributes cubicles, movable walls, filing cabinets, bookcases, and other ergonomic furniture. The furniture maker boasts showrooms in Chicago and Toronto, as well as independent representatives reaching across North America and the UK. Inscape's manufacturing facilities are located in Canada and the US. Originally named Office Specialty, the company was founded in 1888. It adopted the Inscape name in 1999.
