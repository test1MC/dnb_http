Ask a horticulturist who can, and the answer just may be, "Florikan." The company manufactures and distributes Florikote and Nutricote controlled-release fertilizers. The fertilizers are dispersed gradually over time rather than all at once; the staged release is aligned with the crop's growing patterns and negates the need for multiple fertilizer applications. Florikan primarily serves the southeastern US, though it also sells elsewhere in North America. The products are intended for the residential, professional landscaping, and sports turf markets. It distributes its products partly through a joint venture with Sun Gro Horticulture . Ed and Betty Rosenthal founded Florikan in 1981 and still own the company.
