Air Enterprises knows how to handle things. The company custom designs, manufactures, and maintains commercial air-handling systems throughout the world. Founded in 1964, Air Enterprises offers specialties ranging from installing lights and switches to providing electrical and temperature control equipment, as well as piping. The company, which operates from its facility in Akron, Ohio, works on new construction and expansion projects. Clients include pharmaceutical companies, health care, educational, and commercial office buildings, and manufacturing facilities. Air Enterprises assembles its aluminum air-handling systems on-site and online unit design software.
