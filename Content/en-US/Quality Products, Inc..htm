Holding company Quality Products makes and distributes hydraulic jacks and ground support equipment for commercial and military aircraft through its ColumbusJACK/Regent Corporation subsidiary. Its Multipress subsidiary, which began operations in the 1940s as Denison Multipress, makes a variety of industrial hydraulic bench and floor presses and accessories primarily for automotive, appliance, electrical, and food-compaction industries throughout the US. Multipress has sold over 60,000 units; it also provides aftermarket parts and services. Quality Products sells its products through a small, in-house sales force and more than 50 outside sales representatives.
