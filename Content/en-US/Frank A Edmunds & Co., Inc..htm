Wood is good for Frank A Edmunds & Co., which manufactures wooden products such as dowels, posts, and other turnings as well as wooden displays, moldings, and custom pieces. Its turning products include balls, craft items, furniture parts, game parts, handles, knobs, spindles, and novelty items. Moldings include architectural moldings, frames, and furniture parts. The company also makes specialty parts (CNC work, rollers, specialty finishing, assembling, packaging, and order fulfillment) and through its F.A. Edmunds unit, makes fine needlework and quilting frames.
