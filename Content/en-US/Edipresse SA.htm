Edipresse is a leading European newspaper publisher serving French-speaking Switzerland with such titles as 24 heures, Le Temps , and Tribune de Genève . It also publishes about 180 magazine titles in 15 European and Asian countries, including Beijing Tatler (lifestyle, China), Lecturas (celebrity news, Spain). In addition, Edipresse operates websites for many of its publications. It also has print facilities offering commercial printing services. Rival Tamedia acquired Edipresse's Swiss operations in 2012. Edipresse is controlled by the founding Lamunière family (including chairman and CEO Pierre Lamunière).
