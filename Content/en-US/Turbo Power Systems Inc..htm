Turbo Power Systems gets electric users off the grid. Turbo Power makes direct drive permanent magnet-based motors and generators, as well as high-voltage power supplies, converters, inverters, and motor drives. Its high-speed alternators can be used either as a generator or, when supplied with electrical power, as a motor. Turbo Power sells to OEMs, systems integrators, and end-users in the energy, industrial, transport, and defense sectors. Most of the company's sales come from the US, and its customers include Bombardier 's transportation division and Eaton 's aerospace unit.
