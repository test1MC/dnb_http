Canoga Perkins wants to get the message across your network. The company manufactures fiber-optic and data communication products, including fiber-optic modems, multiplexers, long-distance extensions, videoconferencing products, and test equipment. It has more than 1.5 million products in networks around the world and supplies managed Ethernet devices to the telecommunications sector for use in offices and data centers. It also caters to service providers, government agencies, and military institutions. Canoga Perkins sells its products around the world primarily through a network of resellers.

Canoga Perkins sells its products through a network of resellers that includes Advantage Telecom (in Texas), Data Connect Enterprise (Maryland), FONEX (Canada), LTI DataComm (Virgina), Norseman Defense Technologies (Maryland), and PacStar (Oregon.)

Canoga Perkins was founded in 1965 as Perkins Research & Manufacturing and originally specialized in outside plant telephone equipment.
