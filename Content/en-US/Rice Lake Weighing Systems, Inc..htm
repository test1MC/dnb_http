Rice Lake Weighing Systems enables customers to weigh their options. The company has designed and manufactured industrial scale and process control weighing equipment. Its inventory boasts more than 35,000 products varying in size and purpose from small retail scales, bench scales, and balances to scales for livestock, and on-board weighing devices for semi-trailer vehicles and trains. Rice Lake Weighing Systems also carries related uninterruptible power supplies, printers, and software. Its divisions offer custom machining, pressure instrumentation, spray equipment, and fishing, home, and outdoor accessories.

The company has facilities in North America, South America, India, and the Netherlands.

Rice Lake Weighing Systems operates through about 10 subsidiaries and divisions. Condec markets products for pressure instrumentation that include digital wight indicators, pressure indicators, and pressure intensifiers. Intruder serves the recreational and home markets with accessories for hunting and fishing, home food preparation, and all-terrain vehicles and trailers. The subsidiary also offers mousetraps and other rodent control products.

Mattson Spray Equipment makes spray equipment for the automotive and industrial markets. Measurement Systems International, acquired in 2012, serves a variety of industries, including aerospace, forestry, petrochemicals, and transportation, with such products for industrial weighing and process control as electronic scales, tension dynamometers, and load monitoring instrumentation. Rice Lake Custom Machining provides such services as reverse engineering, design, and prototyping. Operating through a partnership with weighing and packaging maker Ishida, Rice Lake Retail Solutions offers products that include price computing scales as well as other equipment, such as food slicers and tabletop automatic wrappers.

International operations are conducted by Rice Lake de Mexico, which provides Rice Lake products in Latin America; Rice Lake Weighing Systems Europe, based in the Netherlands for distribution and support throughout Europe; and a joint venture in India, Rice Lake Weighing Systems India, operated with Strategic Weighing Systems Limited. The Indian company provides electronic weighing systems, material handling systems, and batching and blending products. Rice Lake Weighing Systems' main laboratory and manufacturing facility are at the corporate headquarters in Rice Lake, Wisconsin.

Growing in Europe, in 2013 the company acquired Heteren, Netherlands-based belt-scale manufacturer Master Engineering, a provider of static and dynamic weighing, batching, and dosing systems in the Netherlands and Belgium. The acquisition establishes a manufacturing presence in Europe and expands Rice Lake Weighing Systems' distribution network.

Rice Lake Weighing Systems was established in 1946 by Donald B. Johnson and Warren Thomas.
