Printing Methods Inc. (PMI) provides pre-press services, sheet-fed and web-fed printing as well as finishing and fulfillment services for about 90 commercial clients. Its clients have included Disney , Kodak , Bank of America , and the University of Rochester . The company was founded in 1969 by Myron Babiuk before being sold to its current CEO Lanre Olotu.
