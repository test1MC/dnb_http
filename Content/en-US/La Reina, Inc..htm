If the mention of Mexican food is music to your ears, this company will have you dancing the macarena. La Reina makes a variety of Mexican foods (tortilla chips, taco and tostada shells, burritos, chimichangas, tamales, enchiladas), as well as frozen Mexican entrees and tortillas under brands names that include La Reina, Anita's, Sierra Madre, and La Tolteca. Headquartered in Los Angeles, the company's customers include food retailers and foodservice operators throughout the US, as well as in the UK, Canada, Mexico, Japan, Korea, China, New Zealand, and Australia. La Reina was founded in 1958 by Mauro Robles, father of company president Ricardo Robles.
