ZAG Industries helps keep the disorganized from doing too much useless zigzagging with its line of plastic boxes and bins and other organizational items. Its products include a range of toolboxes, workbenches, portable storage kits, and tool bags. The world's largest maker of plastic toolboxes, ZAG sells its wares around the world through the marketing network and Web site of its tool and hardware parent company, Stanley Black & Decker , and through its own subsidiaries. Customers include home-improvement chains and mass-merchandise stores. Stanley acquired ZAG from its founder Zvi Yemini in 1999.
