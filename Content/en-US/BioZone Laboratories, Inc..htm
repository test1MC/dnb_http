BioZone Laboratories takes you on a journey into a wondrous land of face masks, shaving cream, vitamins, and cough and cold medicines. The company is a private label manufacturer of OTC drugs, cosmetics, supplements, and personal care products. It also provides contract development and manufacturing services. The firm's proprietary drug delivery technologies include oral, topical, liquid, and sublingual substances. Founded in 1989, BioZone Laboratories was acquired in 2014 by sports nutrition company MusclePharm Corporation. MusclePharm now plans to sell the company to Flavor Producers for $9.8 million.
