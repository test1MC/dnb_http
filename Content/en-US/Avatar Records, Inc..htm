Avatar Records is a rap, hip hop, and R&B record label that is home to artists such as Bishop, Official Pham, and Blaxuede. The company's Avatar Publishing Group provides worldwide copyright exploitation, US sub-publishing, film and television licensing, music supervision, and royalty administration. Its Avatar Digi division provides digital distribution services to independent artists and labels; the division has working relationships with iTunes , as well as other online retailers including Napster , Yahoo! , MSN , and Sony Connect . Avatar Records was founded in 1985 by company president Larry Robinson.
