RADCOM puts networks to the test. The company's test equipment and software aid the creation, installation, and maintenance of corporate computer networks, as well as those using VoIP and ATM technologies. Its equipment identifies, diagnoses, isolates, and resolves network problems. RADCOM sells to telecom carriers, Internet service providers, and communications equipment developers. The company has more than 70 distributors around the world, in 50 countries, and multiple manufacturers' representatives across North America. RADCOM is part of the RAD Group (15 networking and telecommunications companies, including Ceragon Networks , Radvision , Radware , and Wisair ).
