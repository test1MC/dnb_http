In German, "besser" means "better," and this company declares that "better" applies to its machinery. Besser manufactures equipment for the worldwide concrete products industry. It serves all segments, including landscape, pipe, precast, ready-mix, and masonry. The company sells both new and used equipment, along with molds and machine parts. It also offers training for maintaining, operating, and troubleshooting machinery. Besser has manufacturing facilities in the US (Boone, Iowa; Compton, California; Holland, Michigan; San Antonio, Texas; and Sioux City, Iowa), and in Canada (Montreal, Quebec), and Germany (Emden).

The company provides equipment to producers in more than 120 countries.

Besser supplies complete manufacturing systems for the pipe, masonry, hardscape, precast and ready-mix customers and makes curing systems for all segments of the concrete industry.

Besser was founded in 1904 as the Besser Manufacturing Co. by Jesse Besser.
