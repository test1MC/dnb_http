Automation is central to Cencorp products. Cencorp makes automated assembly and test equipment for the telecommunications, industrial, and automotive electronics industries. The company's products include conveyers, loaders, buffers, and other printed circuit board handling and test equipment. Its material feeding product line includes axial, radial, tube, tray, and other feeders for odd-form components. Cencorp has added a fiber laser grooving machine for silicon wafer applications through a development partnership with Corelase (a subsidiary of ROFIN-SINAR ). PMJ automec took on the name and brand of subsidiary Cencorp in 2005 as part of a plan to focus on standard automation products.
