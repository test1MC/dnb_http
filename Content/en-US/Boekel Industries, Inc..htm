Before there was electricity, before there were cars, there were Boekel Scientific water baths and incubators. For more than 100 years, molecular biologists, physicians, and chemical engineers turned to the Boekel family for their lab equipment. Though the family sold the business more than 35 years ago, Boekel still manufactures equipment used for clinical, environmental, food, cosmetics, and biotech testing. From hybridization ovens and incubators to autoclaves and histology products, Boekel makes the full gamut of research tools, which are then sold through a huge network of distributors in North, South, and Central America.
