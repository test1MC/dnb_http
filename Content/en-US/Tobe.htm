Tobé is a consulting service that provides fashion and retail analysis, trend forecasting, product and brand development, merchandising, and strategic planning and market news. The company publishes The Tobe Report, a fashion and merchandising publication for the retail industry. Clients include department stores, chain stores, fashion media, and colleges. Tobe Insights, the company's consulting division, offers customized research projects for retail clients and industry leaders. Tobé also publishes shopping guides for New York and Paris. The Tobe Report was first published in 1927.
