Canon Solutions America (formerly Océ North America) combines the canon of Netherlands-based office equipment and supplies maker Océ (pronounced "oh-say") with that of Canon . (Océ was taken over by Canon in 2010.) The new Canon Solutions America provides sales and support for Canon and Océ hardware, software, and services in the US. Its software is used to manage documents across networks; services include consulting, support, training, and outsourcing. The company targets the general office, production print, and large format markets with wide-format, multi-function office and high-production printers.

Canon Solutions America operates from 150 offices located throughout the US, including previous Océ wide format printing systems and production printing systems locations in Chicago, Ill. and Boca Raton, Fla.

Canon Solutions America has customers in a variety of industries ranging from financial services to legal, from manufacturing to government, and from education and healthcare to o commercial printers.
