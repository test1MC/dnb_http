As the US operation of Japan's largest sporting goods maker Mizuno Corporation , Mizuno USA is hoping to win big stateside with its performance lines of sports apparel and equipment. Some of the company's apparel labels include Breath Thermo and Arctic Touch, which specialize in helping to control body temperature and keep athletes dry. Mizuno USA caters to several markets, including golf, baseball, softball, running, volleyball, and track and field. The company regularly sponsors professional athletes, while also maintaining its flagship support of Japan native Ichiro Suzuki, who plays in the MLB . The company was formed in 1988; parent Mizuno Corporation was founded in 1906 to sell Western goods in Osaka.

Despite its name, Georgia-based Mizuno USA markets its sports equipment and apparel internationally.

While parent Mizuno Corporation does not report separate financials for Mizuno USA, the company reported that sales revenues in the Americas rose some 33% in fiscal 2013 versus 2012 on a local currency basis. Increases were led by expanded running shoes sales channels within specialty stores and major sports retailers. Steady performance of baseball products and gains by its golf products business due to continued promotion of custom-fitted clubs also contributed to year-over-year growth. Mizuno USA generated about 17% of its parent's total revenue in fiscal 2013.

In 2014 the company announced it will relocate all of its distribution facilities and manufacturing operations to a 520,00-square-foot facility in Braselton, Georgia by 2015 to support expected growth. To support growth in the Americas, Mizuno has expanded its Portland Design Center to assume responsibility for global performance running footwear. Mizuno also reorganized in 2013 by combining its Diamond Sports, Volleyball, and Golf divisions under unified leadership.

Mizuno USA sells its products online and through retail stores, serving several markets, such as softball (both fast pitch and slow pitch), baseball, running, golf, volleyball, and soccer. The company's partnership with Olympian Jeff Galloway to head up nationwide workshops has already begun to help the company achieve its goal of building its specialty running shoes business by 2% to 3% annually.

Overall, the company concentrates on developing products that are both unique and enhance athlete performance. In 2013 Mizuno USA debuted the first Mizuno Beach Volleyball apparel line and signed on three new volleyball professionals to promote its products.
