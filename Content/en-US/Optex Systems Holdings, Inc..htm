Optex Systems Holdings has a sharp eye for the boots on the ground in foreign lands. Through its Optex Systems operating subsidiary, the company manufactures gun sights, periscopes, assemblies, and other optical products used by US military personnel. Its products are designed for rifles and military land vehicles (such as Abrams and Bradley fighting vehicles) and armored security vehicles. Optex manufactures its products at its 49,000 sq. ft. facility in Texas. It contracts primarily with the government and defense companies; core customers include the US Army and General Dynamics Land Systems .
