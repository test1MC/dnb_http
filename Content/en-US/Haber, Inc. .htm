All that glitters may not be gold, but Haber makes the shiny stuff that really is gold a little easier to get. The company's Haber Gold Process is used to extract gold much the same way as cyanide does, but without the toxic side effects. It licenses its technology to gold exploration companies. Haber's other operations include the development of electromolecular propulsion (EMP), an electrochemical process used to evaluate and identify biological stains. The company, along with joint venture partner Etruscan Resources , owns a gold exploration concession in Ghana and has properties in the US it owns with exploration company Gold City.
