Brown bears, black bears, snow-white polar bears -- BEHR Process Corporation manufactures all the colors in the bear rainbow. The Masco -owned company produces coatings such as paint, stains, and varnishes that are sold primarily through The Home Depot ; its best-selling line is exclusive to the home-improvement warehouse giant. The architectural paint supplier and exterior wood care products company has online tools to let do-it-yourselfers coordinate and preview their paint projects by looking at color swatches, magazines, and mood cards in their home.

The company mainly serves customers in the US and Canada. Expanding from its North American base, BEHR introduced its branded paint products to China in the mid-2000s. BEHR also sells in Mexico, and South America; almost all of its manufacturing is in North America. It also has a coatings research center in India

BEHR has built its "Customer Solutions Centers" in more than 2,200 Home Depots throughout the US, from which it sells not only its BEHR Premium Plus products but also a line of Disney -branded paints meant primarily for children. Its relationship with the Home Depot is much like Valspar 's with Lowe's .

Innoivation and the regular introduction of new products is key to BEHR's growth model. In 2012 it launched BEHR PREMIUM PLUS ULTRA® Interior Stain-Blocking Paint & Primer in One which combines a multi-purpose primer with innovative paint to offer superior stain-blocking attributes.That year it also offering a new self-priming, zero volatile organic compound (VOC) interior paint. The upgraded self-priming paint seals uncoated or previously painted surfaces without the need for a separate primer-reducing paint times and project labor needs while helping professionals, such as painters, contractors and remodelers, hide imperfections and provide excellent coverage.

Other 2012 innovations included the launch of BehrProSM Web Enabled Business (WEB), a free service that allows pre-qualified professionals to create a customized website to more effectively market their business, and a redesigned Exterior Wood Care Centre (an in-store resource allows consumers to find the right products and learn about proper preparation and execution of projects using for exterior wood)

In 2011 BEHR also introduced ColorSmart applications for iPhone, iPad, and Android mobile devices. The app enables consumers to preview more than 2,000 colors and digitally paint a room. Users can browse through color families to choose a particular color, or can enter a BEHR color name or number. The app also lets users save their favorite colors and calculate the cost of the paint.
