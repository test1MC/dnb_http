Kyocera Document Solutions (formerly Kyocera Mita) wants to pulverize your printing predicaments. The global document company provides a wide array of office equipment and products, including copiers, fax machines, printers, and multi-function document devices. Its printer line range from standard to wide-format and small desktop models to departmental models designed for large corporations. The company also provides document management software, as well as service and support. It sells directly and through independent dealers and resellers to customers in the enterprise and government sectors. Kyocera Document Solutions is the document imaging division of manufacturing giant Kyocera .
