You've gotta hand it to 'em, Redman Fisher certainly knows how to grate on you. A subsidiary of Hill & Smith Holdings PLC , Redman Fisher makes industrial metalwork products, including floor grating, handrails, ladders, beams, and stairtread. The company's open grid flooring products are primarily made of aluminum, stainless steel, and GRP (glass fiber reinforced plastic). It also fulfills custom design orders for flooring, staircases, access platforms, balconies, and balustrades. It has a facility in England and two in Scotland. Bromford Iron and Steel, another Hill & Smith subsidiary, is the company's primary supplier of raw materials. Redman Fisher Engineering was founded in all the way back in 1897.
