PTC Alliance manufactures and markets welded and cold drawn mechanical steel tubing and tubular shapes, fabricated parts, and precision components at about a dozen plants in North America and in Germany. Customers  include steel service centers, automotive and truck, construction and agricultural equipment, machinery, and appliances manufacturers. Its tubular products include electric resistance welded and drawn over mandrel tubes.  The company has three business units: Automotive and Truck, Service Centers, and the OEM group. The company emerged from Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection in late 2010 with a new owner.
