Wireless Xcessories Group dresses up cellular phones. The company markets and distributes about 3,000 accessory products for wireless phones and other portable devices. Decorative carrying cases, hands-free kits, rechargeable batteries, chargers, antennae, and other items are sold primarily in North America through retailers and distributors, and from its e-commerce Web site; a majority of its products are manufactured in China and Taiwan. Wireless Xcessories also offers ancillary products and services such as retail packaging, displays, posters, and marketing. In addition, the company distributes third-party cell phone products made by Motorola Mobility , Nokia , and other cell phone manufacturers.
