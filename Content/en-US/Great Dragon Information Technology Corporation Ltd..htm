Great Dragon Information Technology gets beastly for telecommunications equipment. The company manufactures telecommunications equipment used to build fixed-line and wireless networks for telephone service providers and corporations primarily in China. It also provides systems integration services. Great Dragon has six domestic holding subsidiaries, two overseas joint ventures, and other stock-owned domestic companies. The company also has partnerships with companies in Canada, Israel, Russia, and the US to export large-scale digital switching systems. Great Dragon has offices in China, Columbia, Cuba, and Bangladesh.
