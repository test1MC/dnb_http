Vigor refers to physical health; Vigor Shipyards refers the state of military and commercial vessels. Formerly known as Todd Pacific Shipyards, the Seattle shipyard operator mainly repairs and overhauls ships, such as frigates and destroyers, aircraft carriers, and icebreakers, for the US government, including the Navy and Coast Guard . It also offers new construction and conversion; Vigor has built some of the largest ferryboats (owned by Washington State Ferries) plying Puget Sound. Founded in 1916, Vigor and Everett Shipyard, subsidiaries of Todd Shipyards , span some 3,700 feet of berthing space, the biggest private operation in the Pacific Northwest. Todd Shipyards was acquired by Vigor Industrial in 2011.
