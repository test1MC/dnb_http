NKT Holding has a diverse portfolio of industrial and commercial manufacturing operations. Through over 100 subsidiaries in the Americas, Asia, and Europe, NKT Holding makes a wide range of components and devices. Primary offerings include telecommunications and power cables (NKT Cables), commercial vacuum cleaners and other floor care equipment ( Nilfisk-Advance ), fiber-optic cables and lasers (NKT Photonics), and flexible pipes for offshore oil and gas transportation (NKT Flexibles, 51%-owned). Subsidiaries include KOHERAS (fiber laser products), Crystal Fibre (optical fibers), LIOS Technology (temperature-monitoring systems), and Nanon (products made by modifying polymers and glass).
