Marathon Cheese is a leading independent packaged cheese manufacturer with four production facilities in Wisconsin, Idaho, and Mississippi. It produces a wide variety of cheeses for third-party customers and offers custom packaging services. Its products are sold to consumers through retail outlets and to customers in the foodservices industry. Ray and Marie Goldbach started Marathon Cheese in their basement in 1952.
