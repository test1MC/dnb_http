ILEOS' main area of business is consumer packaging for beauty and health care items, including paperboard boxes and plastic cases. Its Axilone division manufactures plastic components for the make-up, fragrance and skincare markets, including caps, jars, dispensing caps, galvanized, and metallized collars. Its ILEOS Graphic division -- comprised of Alliora and Packetis -- specializes in cardboard packaging, folding boxes, coffrets, leaflets, and labels. Alliora manages the entire graphics technical process, while Packetis makes folding boxes, leaflets, wedge systems, adhesive labels and after-sales service and maintenance for clients to fold their leaflets on their production lines.

Based in Paris, ILEOS has sales offices and manufacturing sites in Europe (France, Spain), China, and the US. The company has 12 industrial sites including eight in France.

ILEOS mainly targets the perfumery/cosmetic, pharmaceutical and spirit sectors. The company's clients include such big names as Dior, P&G, Guerlain, Coty, Puig, Givenchy, Unilerer, Loreal, and Clarins.
