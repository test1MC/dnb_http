Button. Button. Who's got the button? Levcor does. Buttons and other accoutrements are the core of Levcor International's business. The company makes and sells buttons, decorations, craft products, and complementary product lines, including iron-ons, kits, and fashion and jewelry accessories. Levcor's products are sold to the home sewing and craft markets through mass merchandisers and retailers, such as Wal-Mart and Jo-Ann Stores . The company is led by chairman, president, and CEO Robert Levinson. Levcor's roots reach back to 1964, when it was established as Pantepec International.
