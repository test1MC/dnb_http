Mediwatch designs and manufactures medical devices used in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of urological disorders. Its PSAWatch product helps to detect and monitor prostate cancer. The Venus pelvic floor rehabilitation system treats incontinence. The company's other products include ultrasound imaging devices, urinary flow monitoring devices, and products to help with erectile dysfunction. Mediwatch has offices in the UK, Europe, and the US; it sells its products worldwide. Medical equipment maker LABORIE acquired Mediwatch in early 2014.
