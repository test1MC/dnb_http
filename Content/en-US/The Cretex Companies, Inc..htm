Its results may be concrete, and the same can be said about many of its products. The Cretex Companies is a conglomerate of companies that manufacture a diverse range of products, spanning from steel heart-pacemaker components to reinforced concrete pipe. Primarily operating in the Midwest, the company is organized into three operating units: commercial products (medical implants, machines stampings, plastic moldings, and steel machining); concrete products (reinforced concrete pipe and other precast and prestessed concrete products for infrastructure use); and construction products (precast and prestressed concrete products used in buildings, stadiums, bridges, and other structures).
