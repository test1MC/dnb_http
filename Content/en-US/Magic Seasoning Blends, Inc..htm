Magic Seasoning Blends, owned by celebrity Louisiana chef Paul Prudhomme, wants to add some magical bayou spice to family meals. The company, which makes packaged seasonings, dehydrated mixes, sauces, and marinades, was formed as an extension of Mr. Prudhomme's popular New Orleans restaurant, K-Paul's Louisiana Kitchen. The company also sells other products including cookbooks and videos, smoked meats (andouille and tasso), rice, and coffee blends. Magic Seasoning products are sold in retail stores throughout the US, as well as to international distributors and institutional foodservice customers.
