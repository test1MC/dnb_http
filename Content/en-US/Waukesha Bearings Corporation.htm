Power's great, if you can get it. For those in need of it, Waukesha Bearings can help you generate power. The subsidiary of Dover Corporation makes fluid film and active magnetic bearings primarily for the power generation and oil and gas sectors. Waukesha's bearings go into gas, steam, and hydro turbines; motors; compressors; gearboxes; generators; and marine and industrial machinery. Customers include Mitsubishi Heavy Industries , Rolls Royce , Dresser-Rand , and Siemens . Waukesha Bearings has manufacturing, sales, and engineering facilities in the US and UK; the company also sells through representatives located in China, India, Italy, Japan, Russia, Singapore, and South Korea.
