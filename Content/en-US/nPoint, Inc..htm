No matter how small, nPoint wants to make the point over and over. nPoint makes nanopositioning devices used for precise and repeatable positioning and motion control at submolecular and nanometer scales. Products include systems and sensors, scanning-probe microscope tips, and closed-loop controllers. Its closed-loop scanners can be integrated with atomic force microscopes made by Quesant Instruments and Veeco Instruments . nPoint was founded in 1997 by chairman Max Lagally.
