When endotoxins seep in and sepsis sets in, Spectral Diagnostics is there to help. The company's lead product is its Endotoxin Activity Assay TM (EAA), a diagnostic test used to identify patients at risk of developing sepsis (infection). Spectral used the diagnostic test to create a companion sepsis treatment device, Toraymyxin, which works to remove endotoxins from patients' blood. The device is available in Europe and Japan and is in late-stage clinical trials to gain FDA clearance. Spectral also provides biochemicals, such as polypeptides and other reagents, used in diagnostic procedures. Toray Industries' Italian subsidiary distributes Spectral's EAA in combination with Toraymyxin in Saudi Arabia and Turkey.
