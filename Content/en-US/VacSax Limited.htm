That great sucking sound might be VacSax making money by sucking up waste materials. The company specializes in the design and manufacture of disposable suction products for medical wastes, for use in surgical and general hospital settings. Products include suction systems, aluminum collection canisters, liners, filters, tubes, vacuum devices, and gel (used to solidify liquid waste before suction). Brands include VacSax, PreGel, GelSax, and Advance. The company's liners are PVC-free and produce only carbon dioxide and water upon 100% combustion. It also makes disposable bedpans and surgical smoke suction devices. VacSax's products are sold directly and via distributors.

The company has a presence in more than 25 countries across the world.

VacSax has the largest and most comprehensive product range available in the disposable suction market.

The company is able to channel all its resources from market research, innovative product design, automated manufacture and customer service to this medical equipment market. This results in a better understanding of customer needs and the development of innovative products to make the collection and handling of medical waste as safe as is practically possible.

The latest product added to the company's portfolio is the VacSax BactiClear Antimicrobial Disposable Suction Liner system, which has been proven to reduce the growth of harmful bacteria, including MRSA and E.coli.

VacSax was founded in 1996.
