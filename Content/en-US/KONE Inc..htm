KONE Inc., the US subsidiary of Finland's KONE , sells, manufactures, upgrades, and maintains elevators, escalators, and autowalks. The company's line of products range from eco-friendly (using no oil) traction elevators designed for low-rise office buildings to high-performance elevators used in skyscrapers standing more than 100 stories tall. KONE's escalators and autowalks, designed for indoor or outdoor use, are commonly installed in airports, stadiums, subways, and shopping malls. The company accounts for about one-fifth of its parent company's elevator and escalator sales.

KONE provides elevators, escalators, and autowalks. It offers maintenance and modernization services to low-rise buildings, skyscrapers, shopping malls, hotels, airports and transit hubs, cruise ships, stadiums, and concert halls.

The company has parts and service centers, sales offices, and more than 90 branches in the US, Canada, and Mexico.

The construction market's demand for new equipment has prompted KONE to expand its capabilities. The company aims to capitalize on infrastructure opportunities to modernize the elevators and escalators operating in urban markets, where approximately 20% of elevator and escalator systems are more than 20 years old.

KONE improves its domestic footprint through select acquisitions by its parent company. In 2016 it acquired the service and repair business of Elevators Unlimited. The deal expanded KONE's scope inside the greater Denver metropolitan area.
