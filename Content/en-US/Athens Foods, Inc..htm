You say "fee-lo." I say "fi-lo." But it's all Greek to us -- the products of Athens Foods, that is. The company makes the popular Greek specialty item -- frozen phyllo dough. The dough comes in paper-thin sheets, which can be layered and filled to create appetizers, entrees, and desserts. Athens Foods also offers frozen ready-to-fill phyllo shells and baklava pastry. It also sells value-added frozen pre-made phyllo foods, including spanikopita (spinach and cheese appetizers), tyropita (three-cheese appetizers), and baklava (dessert pastries) under the Apollo and Athens brand names. The company's products are sold in retail grocery stores throughout the US; it also distributes to foodservice operators.
