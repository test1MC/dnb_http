Southern Container likes to present itself as the whole package. The company manufactures custom corrugated boxes (cardboard and plastic) and folding cartons in windowed, laminated and labeled varieties. It also makes flexible foam, polybags, and stock boxes, and it offers a wide range of value-added services to its clients. These services include CAD design software to depict 3-D computer design packaging concepts, a 24-hour customer service system , and a JIT (Just In Time) logistics program. Southern Container also offers to warehouse clients' materials and ship on demand.
