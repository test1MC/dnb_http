Lift up your heads, O ye gates, and be ye lifted up, ye everlasting doors! GTO, or "gates that open," makes automatic electric gate openers that remotely open chain link, panel, tube, wrought iron, wooden, and vinyl swing or slide gates. Products include the Mighty Mule brand of gate openers for the do-it-yourself market, and the GTO/PRO line of access control systems with solar battery charger options for professional installation. Gates are designed for home, ranch, and industrial use. Since the company's start in 1987, over half a million GTO products have been installed. The company is a subsidiary of NTK Holdings (formerly Nortek).
