Monticello Vineyards produces cabernet cauvignon, chardonnay, merlot, and pinot noir wines under the Monticello Vineyards, Corley Reserve, and Corley labels. The company's limited-volume wines produced from the company's five Napa Valley vineyards can be ordered by phone or fax. Jay Corley founded the vineyard in 1970 and his sons still run the business. Monticello Vineyards was named among the top 10 Merlot producers in 2005 by Wine & Spirits Magazine .
