SKYDEX Technologies doesn't want to impact your life -- not negatively, at least. Founded in 1998, the company develops high-performance cushioning and protective materials designed to limit or absorb the negative effects of impact -- including blast, shock, and vibration limiting -- on the human body. SKYDEX's cushioning materials use polymers and proprietary geometric technologies (imagine two opposing halves of a ball squeezed together) to produce padding that works better than foams, gels, and other cushioning materials. Its materials are used in athletic, commercial, and military applications including footwear, helmets, bike and snowmobile seating, playground surfacing, protective gear, and automotive.
