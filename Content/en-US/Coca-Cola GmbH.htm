Since 1929 Coca-Cola has been serving its legendary soft drink in Germany, its third-largest market after the US and Mexico. Coca-Cola GmbH, the German arm of The Coca-Cola Company , offers all the usual Coke products the rest of the world enjoys. The subsidiary, which poured its world-famous beverage at the historic 1936 Olympics in Berlin, now produces, markets, and distributes "the real thing" and some 60 other nonalcoholic products in Germany.
