The Campagna - Turano Bakery kneads you. Doing business as the Turano Bakery, the company manufactures fully- and partially-baked European-style artisan breads. The company's more than 400 varieties include French baguettes, ciabatta bread, focaccia, kaiser rolls, and croutons, as well as sweet baked goods, including cannoli, biscotti, cakes, and cookies. Operating three commercial bakeries in Illinois, Georgia, and Florida, Turano serves customers nationwide in the restaurant, in-store bakery, and retail grocery markets. The company's foodservice operation supplies customers in Illinois, Wisconsin, and northwestern Indiana with par-baked and fully-baked goods.

Situated in the West Chicago suburb of Berwyn, Campagna - Turano Bakery serves food retailers and foodservice operators nationwide.

Based in Berwyn, Illinois, Campagna - Turano Bakery has worked to extend its geographical reach and product capabilities in recent years. Located in Villa Rica, Georgia, the company opened the baking plant (operating under the Turano Georgia Bread name) in 2009. It has the capacity to bake 140 million pounds of bread a year. The company also has plants in Berwyn and Bolingbrook, Illinois, and has a fourth site (Turano Florida Buns) in Orlando, Florida.

Campagna - Turano Bakery sells its products through food distributors, restaurant chains, and grocers in the greater Chicago area, Wisconsin, Northwest Indiana, and the Midwest.

The company's retail food customers include more than 40 of the Top 100 National Chain Operators. They include, in part, Butera , Dominick's , Jewel-Osco , Meijer , Peapod , Strack & Van Til , and SAM'S CLUB . Its Turano bread can be found on deli shelves in grocery stores throughout most of Chicago.

Most of the products made at the company's Bolingbrook facility are frozen.

Campagna - Turano Bakery has leveraged new technology, electronic processes, social media, and other tools to build its business. To this end, it's creating new artisan products via high-speed lines and achieved a longtime goal of reproducing its original 2-lb. Pan Turano. The company has also successfully extended its reach into value-added product lines by making artisan croutons in several flavors using its ciabatta and focaccia breads.

The company was founded as Capagna Bakery in Chicago by Mariano Turano in 1962 and for many years it offered home delivery in its surrounding neighborhood.
