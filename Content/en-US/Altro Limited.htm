Whether you're walking the floors or climbing the walls, Altro is happy to have your business. Founded in 1919 in the UK, the company makes flooring and walling under brands such as Altro Aquarius and Altro Whiterock for the retail, medical, and food industries, among others. In 1947 the company invented its Altro safety flooring, a sheet vinyl floor covering that promises a smooth, slip-resistant surface. Its smooth flooring is available for spaces where appearance takes priority over safety. Altro also provides flooring for buses and trains. To help customers understand flooring and walling issues, Altro offers training seminars. The company distributes its products in more than 70 countries worldwide.
