Ward Manufacturing hopes your need for fittings is more than just its pipe dream. The company is a maker of pipe fittings including flanges, pipe unions, plugs, and bushings. Its WARDFLEX gas piping system is a corrugated stainless steel tubing that offers flexible pipe as an alternative to traditional steel pipe in transporting fuel gases. Ward Manufacturing maintains offices in Canada and the US and sells its products to a network of more than 600 wholesalers throughout North America. The company has been a subsidiary of Hitachi 's metal unit ( Hitachi Metals America ) since 1990.

The company sells its products in all 50 US states, Canada, and around the world.

Ward Manufacturing operates three units: Ward Fittings, WARDFLEX, and Wisconsin Nipple and Fitting Corporation (a manufacturer and distributor of pipe nipples and couplings).

Ward Manufacturing's products are used in housing projects, sprinkler and plumbing applications, and industrial uses. Its product lines include standard and extra heavy malleable fittings, pipe unions, plugs and bushings, beam and "C" clamps, standard cast iron pipe fittings, flanges, companion flanges, flange unions, flanged fittings, and cast iron drainage fittings, and WARDFLEX Gas Piping Systems.

The company's products are sold through a distribution network of 50 locations in addition to plumbing, heating, and HVAC wholesalers in Canada and the US. It has about 28 sales offices and 22 warehouses across the US. Ward's products are distributed through manufacturers' representative groups, such as Carr Company, CK Dorman, Ferro Resources, and Harry Eklof & Associates.

Ward Manufacturing was established in 1924 by Joseph Ward.
