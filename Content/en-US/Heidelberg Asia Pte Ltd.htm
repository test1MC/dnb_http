Heidelberg Asia helps printing and publishing companies meet their deadlines. The company provides a variety of products and services for all stages of the printing process. Heidelberg Asia's product line includes printing presses, plate imaging devices, finishing equipment, and software components. It also offers spare parts and used equipment, as well as training programs through its Print Media Academy. The company is part of Heidelberger Druckmaschinen AG, which serves customers in more than 150 countries around the globe through its network of sales, service, and marketing specialists.
