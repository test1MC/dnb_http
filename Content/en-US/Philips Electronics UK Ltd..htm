Philips Electronics UK is a fillip to electronics consumers. The British arm of consumer electronics giant Royal Philips Electronics, Philips Electronics' operations are varied, from making semiconductor components to selling men's shavers. Philips Electronics UK makes and markets appliances (including personal care items, irons and hand-held vacuum cleaners), consumer electronics, and consumer and industrial light bulbs. It also offers business communications products and services, develops integrated chips for semiconductor devices, and sells medical equipment. Additionally, the UK is home to one of Philips' major research laboratories, located in Redhill, Surrey.

The UK subsidiary has a similar focus to that of its parent, working to develop, make and sell electrical goods in a variety of fields. It groups its products into seven categories: sound and vision (headphones), personal care (hair removal and toothbrushes), mother and child (bottles, breast pumps), household products (cooking, ironing, vacuums), lighting (natural lighting products), personal health (blood pressure monitors), and accessories (complementary items to the other six categories).

The company is based in Guildford, Surrey, in the UK.
