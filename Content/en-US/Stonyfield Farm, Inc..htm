Stonyfield Farm is banking on choosy moms to spend a few cents more to save the planet -- or at least buy the company's natural organic yogurt, smoothies, ice cream, frozen yogurt, and fluid milk. Stonyfield uses certified organic milk and uses no gelatins, fillers, artificial flavors, colors, or sweeteners in its products. For customers allergic to cows' milk, the company offers a line of soy-based yogurts (O'Soy); it also makes Oikos Greek-style yogurt. A longtime leader in health-food brands, Stonyfield's products are now mingling in mainstream America, distributed nationwide through supermarket chains, natural food stores, and wholesale clubs. Stonyfield Farm is a subsidiary of France's Groupe Danone .
