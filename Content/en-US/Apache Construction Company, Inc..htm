This company wants to fence you in. Apache Construction Company, Inc., provides residential and industrial fence construction in New Mexico, Arizona, and Nevada. The company, doing business as Valley Fence Company, builds and installs fencing for residential and commercial customers -- including prisons, schools, sports facilities, and parks -- and guardrails for highways and bridges. The company is ranked as one of the 500 largest Hispanic-owned companies in the US. Apache Construction was incorporated in 1981 by its officers and is owned by members of the Chavez family, including CEO Mariano Chavez Jr. and president Paul Chavez.
