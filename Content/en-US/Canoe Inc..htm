This company's canoe navigates the waters of the Internet. A subsidiary of Canadian publishing conglomerate Quebecor , Canoe owns and operates the CANOE network of news and information Web sites serving primarily Canadian audiences. The network of sites, including the flagship canoe.ca, draws nearly 8 million unique monthly visitors in Canada for a mix of news, sports, and entertainment content. It also operates an online shopping mall for Canadian merchants. Other sites include Jobboom.com (job listings); Autonet.ca (automobiles); ReseauContact.com (personals); and ClassifiedExtra.ca (classifieds). Canoe also provides communication and IT consulting services.
