O'Sullivan Films is making its comeback. Formerly O'Sullivan Corporation, the company was sold to The Geon Company (a predecessor of PolyOne ) in 1999. In 2007 O'Sullivan Films, reemerged from PolyOne's divestiture of its Engineered Films business unit. The firm provides customized polymer films used in applications such as automotive instrument and door panels, flooring, wall coverings, and pool liners. O'Sullivan also offers film-finishing technologies including embossing, laminating, and painting. Its customers serve global auto makers like Ford , General Motors , Honda , and Toyota . In 2010 the company was bought by German based film producer The Hornschurch Group, providing O'Sullivan with capital for growth.
