NOV Process & Flow Technologies UK Limited (formerly Mono Pumps) manufactures and sells cavity, dosing, widethroat, hygienic, and solar pumps; pumping systems; grinders; munchers; screens; and universal parts. It distributes products to industrial sectors including the water and wastewater, pulp and paper, chemical, food and beverage, mining, oil and gas, pharmaceutical, solar, and agriculture markets. The company's products are sold through a network of distributors worldwide. The company is a subsidiary of oilfield equipment specialist National Oilwell Varco .

NOV Process & Flow Technologies has manufacturing plants, offices, and distribution centers in Manchester, UK; Melbourne, Australia; Auckland, New Zealand; Shanghai, China; and Rebeuville, France, as well as in Houston, TX in the US.

NOV Process & Flow Technologies' products are marketed through distributors that include Tomlinson Hall & Co, Marshall Pump Systems, Industrial Pumps, G.M. Treble Limited, and Clyde Associated Engineers Limited. The company's client portfolio includes Water Utilities, Millennium Inorganic, GlaxoSmithKline , Balfour Beatty plc , BHP Billiton, Dyno NobeL, CIBA Speciality Chemicals, Colgate-Palmolive, Iggesund Paperboard, and Taiwan Pulp & Paper Corporation.Strategy

The company has been trying to capture more of the hygienic market, which includes customers from the food, beverage, chemical, and pharmaceutical industries. It offers solar-powered pumps for water supply and irrigation applications in remote regions.

NOV Process & Flow Technologies is also focused on expanding its service offerings, which includes a full service exchange package, upgrades, and spare parts that are interchangeable with most other brands of pumps. It provides installation and maintenance services for its portfolio of products.
