Terralene Fuels (formerly Golden Spirit Enterprises) has had many lives -- and many names. Founded in 1993 as Power Direct, an oil-and-gas exploration company, this development-stage firm has undergone several different incarnations. It took a gamble on online poker through its goldenspiritpoker.com site. After Congress passed the Unlawful Internet Gambling Enforcement Act of 2006, Golden Spirit again switched gears seeking to develop waste-recycled products. In 2010 the company launched its Terralene Fuel product, an alternative to ethanol-blended gasoline. It changed its name to Terralene Fuels in 2011 to reflect its core business.
