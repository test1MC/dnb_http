Is experiencing sheer ecstasy worth a half-million dollars to you? If so, you can be accommodated. Rolls-Royce Motor Cars NA LLC is the North American sales and marketing arm of BMW's venerable UK-based Rolls-Royce Motor Cars brand. With its Spirit of Ecstasy hood ornament (also known as the "Flying Lady") and classic chrome grille reminiscent of Roman architecture, Rolls-Royce is an instantly recognizable brand. Its model lineup leads with the Phantom, the Phantom Coupé, the Phantom Extended Wheelbase, the 101EX, and Phantom Drophead. The Rolls-Royce Ghost began production in late 2009. A new Rolls-Royce price tag, which averages around $450,000, is not for the faint of heart nor small of wallet.
