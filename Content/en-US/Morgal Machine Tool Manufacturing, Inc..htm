It's "the more the merrier" for Morgal Machine Tool, which manufactures split steel pulleys, stamped metal sprockets, and contract stampings for more than 200 clients in the motor vehicle & transportation (seat frames, steering assemblies, suspension systems), agricultural and lawn & garden (sprockets and gears), bicycle, fitness, material handling (pulleys), and door operator industries. Morgal's four sister companies include Carmichael Machine, Ohio Stamping and Machine , Rose City Manufacturing , and South Carolina Metal Products. Morgal Machine Tool manufactures its products at a facility with over 100,000 sq. ft. The company was founded as a tool & die manufacturer by Frank Morgal in 1939.
