Blydenstein-Willink wants you to have it made in the shade. The company, known as Verosol, designs and manufactures custom window coverings for residential and commercial applications. Its Verosol Fabrics produces the fabric while Verosol Netherlands and Verosol Australia make pleated, roller, and vertical blinds along with panel tracks from metallic fabric (ultra transparent to black out) and non-metallic fabric (ultra transparent to non-transparent). Verosol's trademark Silverscreen fabric is a fine mesh aluminum screen used in large offices. The company sells its products through sales offices in Belgium and the UK and distributors in more than 50 countries worldwide. Verosol was founded in 1801.
