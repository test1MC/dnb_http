CRU Acquisitions Group is highly driven to get data moving. The company, which does business as CRU-DataPort, makes high-capacity portable data storage and security devices (such as external hard drives) for businesses, as well as for the education and government sectors. Its brands include its flagship product DataPort, as well as Data Express and RhinoJR. It sells its products globally through its e-commerce site and through third-party Internet resellers and distributors like Synnex and Ingram Micro . Customers include the Department of Defense , HP , and other federal agencies and corporations. CRU-DataPort was founded in 1986.
