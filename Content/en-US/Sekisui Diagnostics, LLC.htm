The mysteries of health can be revealed in a drop of blood and Sekisui Diagnostics (formerly Genzyme Diagnostics) is ready to help. The company develops, manufactures, and distributes in vitro diagnostic products, including point-of-care tests. It produces diagnostic intermediates, or raw materials such as enzymes and antibodies, that are used internally and sold to other diagnostic and research firms. Sekisui Diagnostics also makes reagents, or basic chemical ingredients, for tests to detect conditions such as diabetes and pancreatitis. The company's point-of-care (rapid result) products include tests for flu and pregnancy. Japan's Sekisui Chemical acquired the business from former parent Genzyme in 2011.
