Westerbeke makes waves with its line of marine engines and generators. Many of the company's engines are used for auxiliary power for sailboats; some models are used as the primary power source for other craft. Westerbeke's marine generators are used to power lights, navigational gear, and other equipment on pleasure and commercial vessels. The company also makes industrial generators for use on land. Most Westerbeke engines and generators are diesel-powered, but the company also makes gasoline-powered models. Westerbeke sells products worldwide through distributors and dealers. President and CEO John Westerbeke Jr. owns the company, which was founded by his father in 1937.
