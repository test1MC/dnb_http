Born and raised in corn country, LifeLine Foods makes a living by using every last bit of its corn kernels for either food or fuel. The company manufactures corn products that end up in corn flakes, brewing and distilling ingredients, snacks and soups, corn meal and flour, tortillas and taco shells, and corn oil, as well as industrial lubricants and ethanol.
