Audio Partners Publishing Corp. spins other people's yarns. The company has published more than 400 audiobook titles, literary works ranging from bestsellers such as The Hunt For Red October to classics such as To Kill a Mockingbird . The company also offers non-fiction, personal improvement, and children's titles in its catalog of audio books. Its titles are sold in cassette, CD, and MP3-CD formats. The company's books are sold primarily through it Audio Editions catalog by sister company The Audio Partners, Inc. CEO Grady Hesters and president Linda Olsen, who are married, own both firms. Titles are distributed to the book trade by Publishers Group West.
