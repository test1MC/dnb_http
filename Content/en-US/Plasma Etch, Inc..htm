Pleased to ply its plasma technology, Plasma Etch is a manufacturer of surface deposition and plasma surface etch equipment. This equipment is used to produce microelectronics devices, such as microelectromechanical systems (MEMS), thin-film heads, optoelectronic components, and compound semiconductors. Plasma technology is used to remove organic and inorganic contamination, boost wettability, increase bond strength, and remove residue. Plasma etching is also known as dry etching. The company provides its patented plasma etching technology for a wide range of industries, including aerospace, automotive, electronics, medical, and toys. Customers have included NASA , Boeing , Lockheed Martin , Honeywell , Motorola , and Bayer .
