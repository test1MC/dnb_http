Catalyst Media Group's holdings help kick-start some activity among gaming enthusiasts. The UK-based company's assets include an ownership stake of more than 20% in Satellite Information Services, which supplies live television footage of horseracing and greyhound racing to betting outlets in the UK and Ireland, as well as other countries in Europe, the Caribbean, and Sri Lanka. In addition, Satellite Information Services produces the "At The Races" channel on the Sky Satellite platform. Catalyst Media Group also operates an online gaming website called Spoof.com, which offers online casino games for real money. The site was launched in 2010.
