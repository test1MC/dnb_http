Hewlett-Packard Limited is the UK arm of technology giant Hewlett-Packard (HP). The company markets HP products in the region, including tablet, notebook, and desktop PCs, printers, copiers, multifunction imaging devices, scanners, ink cartridges, and cameras. HP Limited markets PCs that are predominately Microsoft Windows-based systems with either AMD or Intel processors. The company's UK-based operations include one of its largest international research facilities, the Bristol branch of its HP Labs unit.

HP Limited is part of HP Inc. , which sells PCs, printers, and services. It was created with the break up of Hewlett-Packard in 2015. Hewlett-Packard Enterprise , the other entity, sells servers and services to big companies.

HP Limited is based in Belfast.
