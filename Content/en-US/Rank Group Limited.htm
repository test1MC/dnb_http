The Rank Group ranks as New Zealand's top investment firm. The company (not affiliated with the UK's Rank Group Plc ) is owned by billionaire Graeme Hart, the richest man in New Zealand. It has built up a portfolio of businesses, mostly in the packaging industry, bought primarily through leveraged buyouts. Rank Group owns forest products company Carter Holt Harvey , auto parts manufacturer UCI International , and filter maker Champion Laboratories . Through Mr. Hart's ownership, Rank Group is affiliated with, but does not legally own Reynolds Group Holdings and its main subsidiary, Reynolds Food Packaging .
