Is your business bursting at the seams? Williams Scotsman will accommodate your needs. The company makes, leases, sells, and manages mobile offices, storage containers, and related structures for the public and commercial markets in North America. Customers include government agencies, schools, and hospitals. The company maintains a fleet of some 100,000 offices, portable classrooms, and storage units, which it leases through a network of about 100 branch locations. Formed through the 1990 merger of Williams Mobile Offices and Scotsman Manufacturing, Williams Scotsman is part of Algeco Scotsman (along with European counterparts Algeco and Elliott).

Williams Scotsman offers a number of modular space products, providing solutions related to: office space, storage space, living space, instructional space, health space, and specialty space.

The company offers several innovative products, including: AS Flex, for customers who need temporary space solutions that offer maximum flexibility and design customization; rÄ“MOD, which comes in three different sustainable options and provides flexibility for jobsites with different requirements; and Redi-Plex buildings, which are durable, secure, and easily customizable to allow for advanced versatility to a client's space needs.

Williams Scotsman operates about 100 branches across the US, Mexico, and Canada. Its parent company, Algeco Scotsman , offers space solutions globally.

Serving more than 25,000 customers, from startups to Fortune 500 companies among 450 diverse industries, Williams Scotsman offers space solutions for the construction, education, energy, industrial, commercial/retail, healthcare, and government markets. It serves a wide array of industries, providing mobile space for such uses as workforce housing for remote locations and on-site construction trailers.

Customers include the likes of Rainbow Kids Learning Center, Merced County Airport Authority, Speedway Casino, Newmarket Courthouse, and Southeastern Technical College.

Another niche it serves is the disaster recovery market, for which it is ready to provide on the quick temporary housing, medical clinics, schools, and other sites. Williams Scotsman also builds permanent modular structures to suit.

Williams Scotsman continues to provide space solutions to companies when they need it fast. In early 2014, for example, the company greatly aided companies covering the Super Bowl on February 2, providing 180 units to multiple New York/New Jersey companies (including the Fox Sports Network ) that used its modular products before, during, and after football's Big Game.
