Life sciences researchers go shopping for molecules at Research and Diagnostic Systems The company (aka R&D Systems) is the primary subsidiary of TECHNE and manufactures and sells the tools used in life sciences research. Its biotechnology division offers thousands of proteins, antibodies, stem cells, and other handy stuff for researchers. Its BiosPacific subsidiary supplies biological ingredients to makers of in vitro diagnostic kits. R&D Systems' hematology division sells the blood components and equipment used to calibrate blood analysis equipment. Clients include pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies and academic and institutional research facilities.
