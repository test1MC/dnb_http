Cisco Systems Limited provides routers and switches used to direct computer network traffic. Its switch line includes equipment based on Ethernet, Gigabit Ethernet, and ATM technologies. The company also supplies remote access servers, IP telephony equipment, optical networking components, data storage network equipment, and network security products. A regional subsidiary of US-based Cisco Systems , the company markets to telecom service providers, government agencies, healthcare providers, and large enterprises, as well as smaller businesses and consumers.
