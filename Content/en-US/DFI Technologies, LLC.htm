DFI Technologies makes build-to-order computers for the casino/arcade gaming, digital signage, industrial, medical instrumentation, and travel/hospitality markets. The company makes a variety of computer systems, including flat-panel LCD touchscreen computers and retail point-of-sale workstations. DFI Technologies also sells a line of customizable Pentium-based PCs under its own Tiger brand and under private labels from resellers and distributors. It also offers contract manufacturing and systems integration services. David Lu, DFI's president, founded the company in 1985 in his garage. DFI Technologies is an affiliate of DFI Inc., a Taiwanese manufacturer of PC motherboards.
