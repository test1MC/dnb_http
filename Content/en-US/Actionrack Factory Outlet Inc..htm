Actionrack Factory Outlet cleans up in the aisles. The company makes stock store fixtures and displays, such as brochure holders, garment racks, mannequins, metal shelves, sign holders, tables, and counters. Actionrack, which sells its products by phone and through a catalog, supplies its fixtures and displays to a variety of retail formats. The fixtures firm boasts manufacturing facilities in China and Korea and warehousing at its headquarters operations in Brooklyn, New York. The company also makes custom metal and wood units, such as counters and showcases, and creates prototypes for special projects.
