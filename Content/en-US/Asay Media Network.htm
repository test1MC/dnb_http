Thanks to Asay Media Network, old business equipment and office machines never die -- they just change hands. The company has carved out a niche in publishing trade publications, books, and Web sites that help bring together potential buyers and sellers of devices such as computers, printers, scanners, fax machines, typewriters, and copiers. Among Asay's publications are Locator Magazine and International Edition Locator Magazine . Web sites include yesterdaysoffice.com and thecopiernetwork.com. Roger Asay founded the company in 1987.
