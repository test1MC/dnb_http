Getting an education is Common Sense for this publisher. Common Sense Press publishes texts in language arts, math, and science used by schools and parents who home school. The company publishes curricula such as Learning Language Arts Through Literature and Great Science Adventures. Common Sense Press products emphasize hands-on learning activities. The company's products rely on an integrated approach to learning emphasizing context; its language arts course integrates spelling, reading, vocabulary, grammar, handwriting, and phonics into a single lesson rather than separate lessons for each component.
