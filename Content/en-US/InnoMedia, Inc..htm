InnoMedia gets innovative with videoconferencing media. InnoMedia is a manufacturer of computer telephony and videoconferencing equipment. The company's products include VoIP (voice-over-IP) network connection devices, video phones, and multimedia terminal adapters. InnoMedia has supplied its services to the likes of Asia Pacific Online and Net2Phone . Chairman Kai-Wa Ng and president Nan-Sheng Lin founded the company in 1995. InnoMedia operates from offices in the US and Asia.
