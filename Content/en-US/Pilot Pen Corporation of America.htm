The apple doesn't fall far from the tree in Pilot Pen Corporation of America's case. The US arm of Tokyo-based PILOT Corporation (the oldest and largest writing instrument manufacturer in Japan), the company is a leading maker and marketer of writing instruments. As one of the top writing instrument firms in the US, Pilot Pen operates facilities in the US and Mexico. Its products include an assortment of pens (gel, roller ball, ball point, fountain, calligraphy), mechanical pencils, highlighters, styli, markers, and high-end Namiki fountain pens. The company's products are sold under brands such as Dr. Grip, G2, Precise, Razor Point, and EasyTouch. Pilot Pen was established in the US in 1970.
