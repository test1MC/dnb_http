High Liner Foods doesn't ride the high seas. It leaves that to the fishing boats. What the company does do is process the catch of the high seas, offering value-added frozen seafood products. High Liner Foods peddles its products through retail food and club stores in Canada, Mexico, and the US. High Liner's most well known brand names are High Liner and FPI (processed frozen fish) and Fisher Boy (fish sticks). Other brands include Mirabel, Sea Cuisine, and Royal Sea. It also produces private-label products for both foodservice and retail customers such as Wal-Mart and Costco Wholesale Canada . Located in Nova Scotia, Canada, High Liner Foods boasts processing operations in the US and Canada.
