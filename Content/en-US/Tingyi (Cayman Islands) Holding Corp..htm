Tingyi (Cayman Islands) Holding Corp. and its subsidiaries specialize in the production and distribution of instant noodles, beverages, and egg rolls in China. The company is the Chinese market leader in instant noodles, ready-to-drink teas, and bottled water. It also makes fruit juices, egg rolls, and sandwich crackers. Tingyi distributes its products under the Master Kong brand throughout China via a countrywide sales network consisting of sales offices and warehouses that serve wholesale and retail customers. Company management owns a controlling interest in Tingyi. Partnering with US beverage giant PepsiCo , the two companies opened a new beverage manufacturing plant in China in fall 2012.
