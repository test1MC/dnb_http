You won't find anyone milling around at Kolbe & Kolbe Millwork. Instead, workers are busy making wooden and aluminum-clad windows and doors in a variety of styles. The family-owned company also includes Kolbe Vinyl Windows & Doors and Pointe Five, which offer custom-made and Craftsman-style doors and garden windows. The company provides installation and maintenance through its ServicePro unit. Sales and distribution of Kolbe & Kolbe products is handled by K-K Sales and K-K Way. Brothers Herb and Ervin Kolbe founded the company in 1946. Their first office was their mother's wash house, where they repaired window screens and built door frames and window frames from rough lumber.

The company has five locations concentrated in Wisconsin; it has one location each in Colorado and Minnesota.
