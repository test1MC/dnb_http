Dairy Manufacturers plays surrogate mother to all manner of creatures, including calves, puppies, and white-tailed deer. The company makes synthetic dairy products used as livestock, pet, and wildlife milk replacements. It also manufactures human-grade dairy and dry fat ingredients and additives for the food processing industry; and it produces dairy blends, baking mixes, and beverage mixes for industrial food customers. Dairy Manufacturers offers custom blending, laboratory, product-development, and sales-support services. The company was founded in 1986 and serves customers throughout North, Central, and South America.
