Steamaster is working to bring the therapeutic benefits of the sauna and steam bath culture to America's tired, poor, huddled masses. The company, doing business as Steamist, makes steam bath systems, steam generators, controls, maintenance products, and accessories for residential and commercial applications. The company also offers sauna rooms and heaters for those who prefer dry heat. Steamaster operates from offices in Rutherford, New Jersey and Lancaster, California. It distributes its products throughout the US, as well as in Canada and Mexico.
