Bionostics aims to predict and control the future with tight quality controls. The company manufactures and markets medical diagnostics equipment and products used to monitor, and manage health problems. Its Oxford Cryosystems division makes cooling systems including the Cryostream and Cobra devices used in x-ray crystallography. Bionostics' RNA Medical unit develops and supplies hospitals with materials and supplies to control and calibrate blood monitoring equipment used to track diabetes and other conditions. RNA Medical also provides quality control statistical evaluation services. Bionostics' manufacturing facility fills ampoules, bottles, and vials with pharmaceutical products.
