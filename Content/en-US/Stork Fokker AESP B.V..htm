Born to serve the aviation industry, Stork Fokker delivers the goods that help airplanes get off the ground. With competencies in manufacturing, engineering, and supply chain management, Stork Fokker designs and builds structural components, such as tail and fuselage panels, doors for landing gear systems, electrical systems, and other aircraft parts for aerospace and defense companies. Its clients include commercial aircraft manufacturers Airbus and Boeing , business jet companies Cessna and Gulfstream , and military contractor Lockheed Martin . Stork Fokker represents the aerospace division of conglomerate Stork B.V. , which is ultimately owned by UK-based private equity firm Candover .
