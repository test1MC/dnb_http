Sam Kane Beef Processors is a leading slaughterhouse that supplies beef products to customers throughout the country and in some foreign countries. Under the Kane's brand, it markets a variety of traditional cuts of beef and portion-controlled steaks, as well as such specialty products as liver, hearts, tongue, and other exotic cuts. The company's warehouse facility has storage capacity for more than six million pounds of fresh and frozen meat.
