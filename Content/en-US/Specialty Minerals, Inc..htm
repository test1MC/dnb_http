Specialty Minerals Inc. (SMI) has a knack for developing industrial minerals and chemicals for the paper, coatings, building materials, ceramic, glass, polymer, and food industries. Its primary product is precipitated calcium carbonate (PCC), which brightens and whitens paper, teeth, and paint. In addition to the paper industry, pharmaceutical companies also are big users of PCC (as a buffering agent in tablets). SMI operates 54 PCC plants in 16 countries. Other mineral products made by SMI include quicklime, limestone, and talc. SMI is a subsidiary of Minerals Technologies , accounting for about two-thirds of its parent's annual sales.
