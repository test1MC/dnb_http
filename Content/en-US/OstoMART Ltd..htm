OstoMART is a friend to ostomy patients in the UK. The family run company sells a range of garments and prescription ostomy appliance products to patients throughout the UK. (An ostomy is a surgical opening in the body for releasing body wastes). Products include ostomy pouch covers, adhesive tape, protection shields, and other specialized supplies. OstoMART sells MRSA-resistant support garments through sister company Hi Line Ltd. and makes underwear and swimwear at its sewing facility in northern England. The company offers overnight home delivery of appliance prescriptions from its service centers in Nottingham and Peterborough. In 2014 OstoMART Ltd was acquired by T.G.Eakin Ltd.

In October 2014 OstoMART Ltd was acquired by worldwide medical device manufacturer T.G. Eakin Ltd, which is dedicated to the production of high-quality skin protection products for use in stoma and wound care treatments.

OstoMART products include ostomy pouch covers, adhesive tape, protection shields, and other specialized supplies.

The company directs a high percentage of its profit toward research and developing new products and services. All of its products are available through NHS prescription.

OstoMART also provides educational booklets for new patients leaving the hospital and for those awaiting surgery.

The company continues to explore new and innovative stoma products to enhance the lives of those affected by bowel related conditions.

OstoMART was established in 1991 by nurse (and former owner) Dorothy Waller.
