Shermag wants its US-based customers to remark, "Oh, Canada!" when they discover the origin of their wooden dining room set. Shermag is a residential furniture maker and importer, specializing in contemporary wooden dining room, bedroom, and living room furnishings. Products are distributed through large department stores, independent specialty retailers, and mass-market chains in North America. Most of the wood used by the company is processed at its 13 facilities. Exports to the US account for about two-thirds of sales. In 2009 Quebec-based rival Groupe Bermex acquired a controlling stake in Shermag, which had been operating under the Companies Creditors Arrangement Act (the equivalent of bankruptcy).
