Garage door getting too old? Amarr designs, manufactures, and distributes garage doors for residential and commercial customers. Residential doors are made from steel, wood, aluminum, and wood composite; commercial doors come in aluminum, glass, or steel. Other products include garage door openers, screen doors, and vinyl garage flooring. The company offers door brands such as JELD-WEN , LiftMaster, Dynasty, and Linear . Its products are sold by major retail companies such as Sears and Lowe's . Amarr is owned by ASSA ABLOY Entrance Systems and operates under the Entrematic umbrella.

The company is engaged in the design and manufacturing of doors for residential garages, condominiums, commercial buildings, shopping malls, warehouses and industrial applications. It owns four manufacturing facilities. Its Lawrence, Kansas, plant is the primary manufacturing facility for Amarr Garage Doors.

Amarr has 85 distribution centers in the US, Canada, and Mexico.

Amarr's products are sold to 3,000 dealers and major retailers such as Sears , Lowe's , and Costco .

Amarr continues to drive growth and expansion through its extensive dealer network across North America. Entrematic (of which Amarr is a part) has declared its presence over the years as a strong player in North America by opening its new 6,000 square foot showroom (the Entrematic Academy) in Carrollton, Texas.

Amarr was founded in 1951 by brothers Abe, Morris, and Herb Brenner.
