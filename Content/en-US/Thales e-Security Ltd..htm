Thales e-Security is the network security business of French defense company Thales , operating within that company's larger Information Systems Security operations. Thales e-security offers services and products for card payment systems, smart card technology, internet and email security, network encryption, and mobile security. The company has worked on such diverse projects as providing identity cards for Hong Kong's citizens, creating a secure electronic payment system at UK post offices that allows patrons to withdraw money from their bank accounts, and providing encryption products to the US State Department and US Department of Defense .

Thales e-Security's products include 3D Security module (for the security and acceleration of operations for online payments), Guardisk (to protect classified information stored on laptops), Datacryptor (hardware that ensures data security across networks), and SafeDial (used by workers to access web services and email and transfer files from home or while traveling). The company has had strategic partnerships with companies such as MasterCard , Computer Associates , Visa , and Applied Communications .
