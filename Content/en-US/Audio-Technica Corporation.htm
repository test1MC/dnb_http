Audio-Technica is best known for making high-performance sound equipment for home and professional users. The company's products include wired and wireless microphones, headphones, karaoke systems, audio mixers, and related accessories. Its microphones are used at high-profile events, such as the GRAMMY Awards and at the 2010 Winter Olympic Games. Audio-Technica also makes the Autec brand of sushi-related food equipment and the TechniClean cleaning system for electronic parts and silk screen printing. Other products include acrylic and polycarbonate films. Founded in 1962, Audio-Technica was initially known for its phonograph cartridges. Its US arm, Audio-Technica U.S. , was established in 1972.
