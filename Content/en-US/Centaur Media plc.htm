It's no myth that Centaur Media is a major UK business information publisher. The company publishes a wide range of business-focused magazines covering industries such as legal, financial, creative and marketing, and construction and engineering. Its titles include Marketing Week , The Lawyer , and Creative Review . Centaur Media also publishes newsletters and directories, and operates websites for its publications and niche communities. The company additionally holds exhibitions, conferences, and other events that complement its print and online products. Centaur Media's Perfect Information subsidiary publishes an online database of corporate financial information. The firm was founded in 1982.
