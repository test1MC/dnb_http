SportPaint will spiff up that sports car or crotch rocket with a new coat of paint. The company provides custom finishing services to automotive and motorcycle OEMs, as well as replacement part and accessory manufacturers. SportPaint operates spray equipment, downdraft spray booths, and bake ovens at its manufacturing facilities in Louisville, Kentucky. It provides various colors, two-tone applications, and pinstripe and decal applications for plastic and metal components. The company was founded in 1995 by CEO Thomas Cunningham.
