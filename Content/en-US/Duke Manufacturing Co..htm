Duke Manufacturing dukes it out with the competition in the food equipment manufacturing industry. The company specializes in the manufacture of about 20 different product lines including stainless steel tables, salad bars, serving systems, hot/cold food buffet-style units, and convenience store cabinetry and equipment. The manufacturing company's customers include convenience stores, fast food restaurants, hospitals, hotels, and schools. The company also helps its various customers to design their kitchens by providing custom fabrication services.

Duke targets markets through a handful of sales offices and distribution centers in Canada, China, the Czech Republic, the UK, and the US, and through 285 service centers.

The company looks for strategic acquisitions and alliances as a way to grow. To that end, in 2011 Duke signed a comprehensive distribution agreement with Compass Group North America . Duke works as an approved supplier through Supply America and Foodbuy, the exclusive procurement division for UK-based Compass Group 's North American business.

It offers its products and services through a network of service agents and through sales offices across the world. Duke supplies foodservice equipment to schools, colleges, hospitals, and restaurant chains. Taco Bell is one of its customers.

Duke was founded by Marsh P. Duke. Formerly a sales manager at the Majestic Stove Company, Mr. Duke left in 1925 to start his own workshop to produce commercial foodservice equipment.
