Times-World Corporation (dba The Roanoke Times ) serves Southwestern Virginia with news, sports coverage, and lifestyle features. In addition to its flagship publication, Times-World produces niche publications and websites including Inside Out , Job Market, Neighbors , News River Valley Current, SWVA Cars, and SWVA Homes . The company also publishes the regional business publication Blue Ridge Business Journal . First published in 1886 as The Roanoke Daily Times by founding publisher M. H. Claytor, The Roanoke Times was the area's first daily paper. The newspaper was acquired by BH Media Group, a Berkshire Hathaway company, in 2013.
