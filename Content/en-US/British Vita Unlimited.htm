Vita, one of the UK's largest foam manufacturers, produces foam for use in furniture, construction, packaging, and carpet underlay. Vitafoam is its most popular brand. Vita also makes industrial polymers, thermoplastic sheeting, and compounded plastics for a variety of uses. Its Nonwovens segment makes polyester fibers for filters, diapers, and insulation. The company operates through approximately 35 sites in almost 15 countries, primarily in Europe. Vita was established in 1949 and was listed on the London Stock Exchange as British Vita PLC from 1967 to 2005. After that, it was acquired by an international private equity group.
