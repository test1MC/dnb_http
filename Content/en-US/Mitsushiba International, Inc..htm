Mitsushiba International manufactures golf clubs and related golf accessories, including balls, gloves, and hats. The company, a US subsidiary of Maibor Corp. (a Taiwanese manufacturer of products ranging from rain coats and plastic wardrobe hangers to tennis rackets and golf club shafts), sells its golfing collections through pro shops and sporting goods stores nationwide. To strengthen the presence of its brand, Mitsushiba International sponsors the annual Northern Order of Merit golf tournament played throughout England.
