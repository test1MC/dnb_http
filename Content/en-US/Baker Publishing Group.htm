Baker Publishing Group has a spirit in its step. The Christian publisher publishes books through seven market-focused divisions: Baker Books, Baker Academic, Bethany House Publishers, Brazos Press, Cambridge University Press, Chosen Books, and Revell. Its Baker Books division includes titles for pastors and church leaders, featuring books on worship, preaching, counseling, and leadership. Its Cambridge division focuses on bible publishing. Other imprints publish Christian fiction and self-help titles, as well as books on subjects such as theology, discipleship, and Christian living. The company was founded in 1939 by Herman Baker.
