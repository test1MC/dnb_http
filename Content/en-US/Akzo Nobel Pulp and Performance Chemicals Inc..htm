As its name spells out, AkzoNobel Pulp and Performance Chemicals (formerly Eka Chemicals) serves the pulp and power industry. A subsidiary of global chemical giant Akzo Nobel , it operates in North America through locations in Georgia, Mississippi, Washington, and Wisconsin. In addition to bleaching and water treatment chemicals, it markets specialty products like colloidal silica (which is used by the electronics industry) and high-purity alkalies. Akzo Nobel has declared pulp and paper chemicals to be strategic areas of focus. The company operates as a business unit within the AzkoNobel Specialty Chemicals group.
