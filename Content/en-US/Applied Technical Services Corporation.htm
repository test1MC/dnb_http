Assembly required? Give Applied Technical Services a call. The company, also known as ATS, is a contract electronics manufacturer serving the aerospace, computer, fitness equipment, industrial control, medical, military, test and measurement, and telecommunications industries. ATS provides surface-mount and through-hole assembly of printed circuit boards, along with wave soldering, conformal coating, system integration, and PCBA wash. The company also offers engineering and test services. ATS was founded in 1984. George Hamilton -- not the famously tanned actor, but the CEO of ATS -- owns the company, which has plants in Mexico and the US. Customers include Leviton , Precor , and Verathon.
