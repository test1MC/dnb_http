MC Hammer can't touch them, but if you've frequented a bar or pub lately, maybe you have laid a finger on a digital jukebox provided by TouchTunes Music. The company makes digital jukeboxes and entertainment systems, which can be found in some 38,000 restaurants, bars, retailers, and other businesses throughout the US and Canada. Revenues come from jukebox sales and leases, as well as music services. TouchTunes has rights to play music from major record labels such as Universal Music Group , Warner Music Group , EMI , and Sony Music , and several independent labels. Canadian subsidiary TouchTunes Digital conducts research and development for the company.
