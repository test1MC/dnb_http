Ahlstrom manufactures and sells a swath of web-forming technologies and fiber-based materials. Applications for its products include masking tape, automotive filters, self-adhesive labels wallcoverings, medical fabrics, life science and diagnostics, flexible packaging, and roofing. Ahlstrom's segments include Advanced Filtration (serving the water, energy, health care, food and beverage, and environmental control industries), Building and Energy (the construction, energy, fabric care, marine and transportation industries), Food (the food, beverage and construction industries), Medical (the medical and health care industries), and Transportation Filtration (the automotive industry). In 2017 it was purchased by and merged into Munksjön AB, a fellow provider of fiber-based products.
