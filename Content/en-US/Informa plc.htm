This company tries to help its customers be well informed. Informa (formerly T&F Informa) publishes academic and scientific, commercial, and professional data though electronic and print information services. Products include about 72,000 books, journals, newsletters, and research reports. The firm also provides consulting, education, and training services, and produces more than 8,500 events and exhibitions for professionals in such industries as finance, law, life sciences, technology, telecommunications, and transportation. The company reached an agreement to buy US-based Penton, an information services company, for $1.56 billion in 2016.
