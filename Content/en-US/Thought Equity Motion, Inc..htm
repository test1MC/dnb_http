From LeBron James dunking to a tropical sun setting, if it's in motion, Wazee Digital (formerly T3 Media, and before that, Thought Equity Motion) probably has footage of it. The company provides licensed and royalty-free moving stock images (in digital and HD formats) to advertisers, filmmakers, TV broadcasters, corporations, and other customers. It also provides research, clearances, and other digital asset management tools. Its online footage collections include content from National Geographic , Sony Pictures , HBO Archives , NCAA , and Paramount Pictures . Wazee Digital has offices in in Denver, Los Angeles, New York, Chicago, and London.
