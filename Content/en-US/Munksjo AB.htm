From wood laminate on furniture to metallic labels on beer bottles, specialty paper company Munksjö has it all wrapped up. Its decor products include wood-based panels and wear-resistant papers used on furniture, cabinets, doors, and flooring. The release liners line includes label stock, tapes, and other pressure sensitive items along with product labels and environmentally friendly long fiber pulp used in specialty applications. Munksjö's print-base papers are used in graphics and consumer goods packaging. And industrial applications covers industry-specific papers. The subsidiary of Ireland-based Smurfit Kappa Group has several manufacturing and pre-printing facilities in Norway, Poland, Spain, and Sweden.
