Sothys Paris knows how to maintain its youthful glow. The firm, which traces its roots to 1946, develops and manufactures high-end cosmetics and skin care products under the Sothys, Bernard Cassiere, and Simone Mahler brands. Its lineup includes facial care (cleansers and moisturizers), body care, hair care, and sun care products, as well as men's grooming items (detoxifying, age-defying, and hydrating). Sothys Paris' products are sold by spas and beauty professionals in more than 110 countries. The company operates an R&D lab, a distribution center, and two factories in France and the US. Its flagship institute and international training center are located in Paris.

Sothys has its headquarters in France and sells its products in 110 countries. It stays up to date on the needs of hotels and spas through more than a dozen subsidiaries in the UK, US, Singapore, Malaysia, the Netherlands, Japan, Switzerland, Italy, Finland, Austria, and Belgium.
