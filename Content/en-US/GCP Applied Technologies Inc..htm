GCP Applied Technologies graced the world as a brand new publicly traded company in 2016 as the result of the spin-off of W. R. Grace & Co. 's (Grace) construction products and packaging technologies businesses. Grace spun off its Grace Construction Products segment (which makes concrete and cement products, including specialty construction chemicals and specialty building materials) and its Darex Packaging business as GCP Applied Technologies. The separation was made by Grace to create value for shareholders and sharpen its strategic focus on its customers. In 2017 Henkel agreed to acquire Darex Packaging Technologies from GCP Applied Technologies for $1 billion.

GCP has operations on six continents (including 65 manufacturing plants) and serves customers in 110 countries.

The company makes specialty construction chemicals with a focus on cement and concrete products; and specialty building materials, primarily including air and water barriers used in commercial, infrastructure, and residential construction.

Its specialty construction chemicals segment provides products, technologies, and services that reduce the cost and improve the performance of cement, concrete, mortar, masonry, and other cement-based construction materials. GCP's specialty building materials segment produces and sells sheet and liquid membrane systems and other products that protect structures from water, air, vapor penetration, and fire damage. GCP also manufactures and sells specialized cement-based and chemical grouts used for soil consolidation and leak-sealing applications.

The company's construction products are found in buildings around the world, from One World Trade Center to the London Underground, to the 115-story Ping An International Financial Center in Shenzhen, China.

GCP's Darex Packaging Technologies business produces and sell sealants and coatings for consumer and industrial applications to protect the integrity of packaged products, including a portfolio of can coatings. Darex seals or coats more than 300 billion food and beverage cans and millions of other packages.
