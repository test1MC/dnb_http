International Barrier Technology wants to put up a wall between your stuff and a fire. The company makes fire-resistant structural wood panels under the Blazeguard brand for use by homebuilders and other construction industry customers. The panels, which are coated with a material that actually releases water when exposed to heat like that generated by fire, are used for roof decking and structural wall assemblies in townhome and condominium developments across the US.  Customers include D.R. Horton , KB Home , and Lennar , while Lowe's , 84 Lumber , and ABC Supply are among its distributors. International Barrier Technology was established in 1986.
