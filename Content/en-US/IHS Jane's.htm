IHS Jane's (formerly Jane's Information Group) is a print and electronic publisher. It publishes defense, security, public safety, transport, and law enforcement information (handbooks, magazines, summaries, and other publications) for global military, government, and corporate decision-makers, as well as academics. The company uses independent sources (journalists, researchers, correspondents) to gather its intelligence. IHS Jane's also offers consulting services in such areas as security threats and risk assessment. The company is a subsidiary of technical document publisher IHS .
