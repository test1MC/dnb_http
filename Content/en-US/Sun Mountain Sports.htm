Sun Mountain wants to provide the tools before you tee off. Sun Mountain Sports makes and markets golfing bags, rental cars, and push carts, along with an assortment of men's and women's golf outerwear and accessories. The company peddles the Eclipse brand of outerwear, as well as 15 other names. Through its embroidery division, the manufacturer offers items, such as golf clubs, company bags, and outerwear, that are embroidered with a particular logo. Sun Mountain Sports has partnered with about 600 college and high-school coaches to provide gear for teams. The firm's products are sold through sporting goods and specialty golf retailers nationwide.
