Combine green algae, ionized silver, shark cartilage, and pomegranate juice and you might find the fountain of youth, or at least the ingredients for a multi-level marketing company like AMS Health Sciences. The company's 60 products consist of dietary supplements, weight management products, and hair and skin care products -- all of which are manufactured by third parties. A network of 7,000 independent distributors sell the products in Canada and the US. Its products are sold under the Advantage, AMS, Prime One, and ToppFast brands. The company also markets and sells promotional material to its distributors. AMS has restructured and emerged from Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection.
