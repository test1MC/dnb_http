Thinking of packing it up? Cascades S.A. has your boxing supplies. Cascades S.A., the European branch of Canadian packaging product giant Cascades Inc. , manufacturers cartonboard products (folding boxboard and white-lined chipboard) for customers in the food, health and beauty, and hygiene industries. End uses for its products include trays for meats and pastries and packaging for makeup and health care products. Cascades S.A. has maintained a presence in Europe since 1985 and operates virgin boxboard and chipboard mills. Parent Cascades Inc.combined the unit's recycled board operations with Italian firm Reno de Medici in 2008, creating CAREO.
