Vecima Networks keeps cable and wireless companies covered. The company makes equipment and components that connect end users with cable and wireless broadband networks. Cable companies such as Comcast and Shaw and their equipment suppliers such as Cisco and Motorola incorporate its hardware into cable set-top boxes and cellular network base stations to transmit date between subscribers and the network. All of Vecima Networks' products are made in Canada; the company maintains a software development office in India. Subsidiary Spectrum Signal Processing develops communications platforms for defense applications. Vecima Networks was founded in 1988 by CEO Surinder Kumar as Wavecom Electronics.
