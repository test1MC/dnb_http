With products ranging from citrus and baby soft to green apple, lemon lime, and raspberry, at least the company's factories shouldn't smell too bad. Bell Flavors & Fragrances manufactures flavors, botanical extracts, and aroma chemicals for the food, beverage, household products, personal care, and pharmaceutical industries, among others. Founded in 1912, it has established itself as a relied-upon source for commercial quantities of raw materials, manufacturing most of the ingredients incorporated in the products sold by its Flavor and Fragrance divisions.

The company has manufacturing operations in Brazil, Canada, China, Germany, Mexico, and the US. It also has research and development facilities in 10 countries, and sales offices in 40.

Bell Flavors & Fragrances' operations consists of Flavors, Fragrances, Botanical Extracts, and Ingredient Specialties Departments.The company offers liquid, powder, spray dry, and paste flavor products, including flavor enhancers, bitterness suppressors, salt reducers, replacers and extenders for vanilla, cocoa, honey and sweeteners, and masking agents.

It provides fragrances to personal care, household care, and related companies and develops compounded fragrance oils, encapsulates, dry blends and solubilized fragrances for finished goods makers.

The company supplies Belltanicals brand of botanical extracts for personal care use.

Bell Flavors & Fragrances also provides ingredients for natural chemicals, aroma specialties, aroma chemicals, and citrus concentrates.

The company serves food, beverage, cosmetic, household care, personal care, oral care, and tobacco companies worldwide.

To meet rising demand, the company is growing its market share in Central and South America. In 2013 Bell Flavors & Fragrances opened an new innovation center in Guadalajara, Mexico.
