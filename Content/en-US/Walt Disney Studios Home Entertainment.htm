Walt Disney Studios Home Entertainment can turn the living room into a Magic Kingdom. The company, a unit of Walt Disney Studios , is responsible for the DVD and Blu-ray high-definition format releases of all films and TV shows produced by Disney's various labels, including Touchstone Pictures, Walt Disney Animation Studios , Pixar Animation Studios , and Marvel Entertainmnet . It has about 1,800 live action and animated titles available in the US market, and some 2,700 available internationally. Walt Disney Studios Home Entertainment titles are sold to retail outlets such as Wal-Mart , Redbox , and Best Buy . The company also distributes films digitally, including via Apple's iTunes and Netflix instant streaming.
