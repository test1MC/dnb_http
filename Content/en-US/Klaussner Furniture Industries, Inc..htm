Klaussner Furniture Industries makes accoutrements for the American dream. As one of the nation's largest makers of home furniture, Klaussner sells fabric- and leather-upholstered sofas and recliners, chairs, ottomans, occasional tables, home entertainment, and dining furniture under the Distinctions and Klaussner names. It also licenses such brand names as Sealy and Dick Idol. The furniture company boasts manufacturing and distribution facilities in North Carolina and Iowa, as well as a handful of licensed Klaussner home stores and about 150 Klaussner Home Furnishings Galleries. Founded in 1963, the company was owned by Hans Klaussner from 1979 to 2011, when it was bought out by management.

Funded by an asset-based loan from Wells Fargo Capital Finance , Klaussner Furniture's CEO, CFO, and other senior managers purchased the company in 2011 through a management buyout from owner Hans Klaussner.

North Carolina-based Klaussner Furniture boasts showrooms in High Point, North Carolina, and in Las Vegas. It also maintains manufacturing and distribution facilities in North Carolina and Iowa. The company's retail operation consists of a handful of licensed Klaussner home stores and some 150 Klaussner Home Furnishings Galleries.

Along with showrooms, manufacturing and distribution facilities, and owned and licensed retail operations, Klaussner Furniture partners with affiliated company Prestige Fabricators, an Asheboro, North Carolina-based foam manufacturer from which Klaussner Furniture sources the foam used in its domestic products.

Klaussner Furniture has been ranked by industry publication Furniture Today as the nation's fourth largest residential furniture manufacturer with estimated sales of $500 million. It's working to maintain its ranking by turning to a luxury clientele. To this end, Klaussner boasts a permanent 90,000-sq.-ft. showroom in High Point, North Carolina, so that furniture market buyers can shop its full lineup.

The company is also pursuing niche furniture markets. Klaussner introduced Comfort Design, a business focused on making high-end leather and fabric upholstery. The Comfort Design collection includes reclining chairs and stationary sofas and sectionals. To cater to the growing number of customers with media rooms, Klaussner has rolled out home-theater seating and motion sofas.

Klaussner Furniture, which prides itself on its product breadth and upholstery options, is able to produce custom-order furniture domestically in fewer than 21 days. The company considers this its core competency. It offers 500-plus styles, 600 fabric choices, and more than 75 leathers.

The company sells its furniture through a network of retailers and through dealers.

It is rolling out a new Solutions Studio format that ranges in size from 2,500 sq. ft. to 5,500 sq. ft. to offer dealers a simple way to sell the products in Klaussner's portfolio. Studios, which offer designer assistance, feature custom-order upholstery across many product lines and price points. Studios in Montreal, Canada, and Cape Girardeau, Missouri, represent its first two locations. The company has since added two locations in Wisconsin.

The company was founded as Stuart Furniture Industries in 1963.
