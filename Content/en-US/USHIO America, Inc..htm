USHIO America discharges its duties by designing, manufacturing, and distributing discharge and halogen lighting. A subsidiary of USHIO , the company has an arsenal of over 3,500 general and specialty lighting products. Its main customers are in the biotechnology, electronics, image information processing, medical, and semiconductor industries. Ushio America's sister affiliate production bases include Ushio America-Oregon, USHIO Philippines, BLV Licht-und Vakuumetechnik GmbH, Taiwan USHIO Lighting, and USHIO Hong Kong. The company puts a high premium on the research and development of advanced photon and electron beam technology and their adaptation for use in industrial lighting and processing equipment.

The American unit sells to customers in Canada, Central and South America, and the US. It has sales offices throughout the US.

The very technology of the halogen bulb gives the company a competitive edge over more traditional incandescent and fluorescent lighting products. A halogen lamp is an incandescent lamp, but with a tungsten filament that is housed in a transparent package along with a halogen element such as iodine or bromine. The resulting chemical yields a longer bulb life, and halogen lamps can be made in smaller sizes for use in optical systems.

USHIO America caters to a wide array of industries such as general illumination, audio-visual, photographic, stage, studio and television, semiconductor, printed circuit, video projection, cinema, UV curing, germicidal, horticulture, graphic arts, flashlight, scientific, medical, dental, ophthalmic and infra-red heating, among others.

The company's growth strategy included the regular introduction of new products. In 2014 it launched its Uphoria LED PAR Lamp series and Uphoria LED Downlight Retrofit kits. In 2013 USHIO America introduced the Uphoria LED Reflector Lamp series, Uphoria LED MR-16 Series, and the high-output Midori ULB35i LED fiber-optic illuminator.

To boost its portfolio of LED lighting products, in 2104 the company invested in Zylight LLC, a privately-owned manufacturer of intelligent LED lighting instruments for the cinema and broadcast production industries.

Parent company USHIO was founded in Japan in 1964. It set up USHIO America in 1967.
