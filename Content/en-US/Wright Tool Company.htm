Every mechanic should have a Wright to work hard. Wright Tool makes the wrenches, screwdrivers, pliers, hammers, and other tools that professionals need to get building and repair jobs done. It also offers tool cabinets and a variety of master sets. In addition to hardware, the company markets men's and women's Wright Tool-branded apparel and accessories. Peddled under the Wright and Cougar brand names, the company's tools are sold through online retailers and representatives in the US, Canada, Mexico, and other countries; its 4,000-plus products are not distributed to the do-it-yourself mass market. Owned by the Wright family, Wright Tool was founded in 1927.
