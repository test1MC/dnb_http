Planning on starting a company bowling club this year? Koza's can make that group into a team with personalized hats and T-shirts. The company manufactures a variety of promotional products, such as badge holders, bottle openers, cups and mugs, duffel bags, flashlights, hats, luggage tags, mouse pads, shirts, stress squeeze toys, trophies and safety awards, and umbrellas. It provides product customization with art and graphics, embroidery, and screen-printing capabilities. Koza's was founded in 1965; it's run by Joseph Koza.
