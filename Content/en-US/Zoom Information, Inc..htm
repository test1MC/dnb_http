This company wants to be speedy with its business information. Better known as ZoomInfo, Zoom Information is a provider of information about industries, companies, and executives. Its website and search engine, with profiles on more than 5 million companies and approximately 50 million people, is used by customers for such purposes as recruitment, sales leads, and competitive intelligence. The company generates revenue mostly through advertising and subscription fees, as well as through content licensing. ZoomInfo was founded in 2000 by CEO Jonathan Stern, Michel Décary, and Jeremy Rothman-Shore. It is backed by venture capital firms including Ascent Venture Partners, Commonwealth Capital, and Vulcan Capital.
