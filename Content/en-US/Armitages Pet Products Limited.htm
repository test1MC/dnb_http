Cats, dogs, hamsters, birds, and fish, are happier thanks to Armitages Pet Products. The company is Europe's #1 manufacturer and distributor of pet products. It makes and sells 2,000-plus pet accessories, food, and treats under brands such as Good Girl and Good Boy (cat and dog toys, treats, collars, bowls), Rotastak (hamster houses and runners), and Gussie (goldfish products). Armitages produces about 85% of its pet lines through a handful of manufacturing facilities in the UK. Internationally, Armitages exports its products to some 60 markets. Sales to pet retailers are generated via a website and catalog. Founded more than 200 years ago, Armitages holds a royal warrant as pet food manufacturer to the queen.

Armitages sell its products in the UK and across the globe. It exports to over 20 countries, with a wide-ranging presence in Europe and a dedicated Asia/Pacific sales office in Australia.

The company produces more than 2,000 products. It has a sophisticated stock control system and a rapid distribution network servicing all parts of the globe.

Armitages primarily distributes its products through local, national, and international pet product retailers and pet stores. Sales to pet retailers are generated via a website and catalog. Customers range in size from small website operations to international retail chains and include Petcara, Alpa, KPC, ASDA , Batleys , bstpets, Garden and Leisure, makro , just cages, and pets in time.

Armitages stays ahead of the competition by launching new and innovative products, based on extensive market research with pet owners. These products are branded consistently throughout, making them eye catching and encouraging brand loyalty.

In 2014 the company launched a new wild bird range. It also opened a new showroom at its Nottingham location.

In 2013 Armitages launched Pawsley & Co., as a pet food brand.

The company traces its roots to 1775 and founder Samuel Fox Armitage.
