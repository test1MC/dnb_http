The global tile market holds Ceramiche Ricchetti against the wall, which suits this maker of interior and exterior glazed ceramic and stoneware tile just fine. Its collections of tile patterns and surfaces include rustic, elegant, classic, and technical pieces for both floors and walls in the kitchen, bathroom, and other areas. Exports make up most of Ceramiche Ricchetti's sales; in the US the company sells its products through Ricchetti Ceramic. Acquiring Industrie Ceramiche Cisa-Cerdisa solidified the company as one of Italy's largest tile makers. Chairman and managing director Oscar Zannoni owns almost two-thirds of the company, held through Fincisa and Cisf.
