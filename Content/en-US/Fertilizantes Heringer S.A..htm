Whatever the state of its finances, Fertilizantes Heringer's status as a growth company is secure. The company manufactures and distributes more than 6 million tons of fertilizer every year from 21 production, commercial, and distribution units (13 of which are company-owned) across Brazil. Its primary products are basic fertilizers (ammonia sulphate, potassium chloride, and reactive phosphate, among others), NPK formulas (combinations of basic fertilizers), and special fertilizers tailored to suit particular types of crops. Heringer also provides services, including soil analysis and technical support. The company mainly serves producers of soybeans, corn, sugar, and coffee in Brazil.
