AlarmForce Industries is a leading provider of home alarm systems and monitoring services in Canada, with more than 90,000 subscribers using its warning systems. The company's AlarmVoice system alerts monitoring stations of a possible break-in, activates an alarm, and enables two-way voice contact between the AlarmForce staff and the suspected intruder. It also links clients with company staff in medical emergencies. The AlarmPlus add-on system uses wireless technology to notify controllers of a cut telephone line. In addition to Canadian customers, AlarmForce serves selected markets in the US.
