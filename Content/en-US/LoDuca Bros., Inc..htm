No one can accuse LoDuca Bros. of putting all of its eggs in one basket. The company, established in 1941 by a pair of brothers, operates its business in two diverse markets. First, it's a manufacturer of accordions and distributor of printed music, music equipment, and music accessories, such as adapters and electronics stands. LoDuca's second market is making and importing wines and specialty cooking and seasoning oils. The latter operation, which commenced in the 1980s, accounts for about a quarter of LoDuca's sales. LoDuca Bros. is run by the sons and grandchildren of founders Tom and Guy LoDuca.
