APEM is always ready to make the switch. The company makes sub-miniature and miniature switches, keyboards, and integrated assemblies under brand names CH Products and mec. Its products are used by the aerospace/military, computer, electronics, machinery, medical, security, and telecommunications industries. APEM's switches include key, piezo, push button, pushwheels, rocker, rotary, slide, snap-action, and toggle models. Other products include joysticks, LED indicators, switch panels, and terminal blockers. APEM was established in 1952 and has a presence in 11 countries.

APEM has a presence in 11 countries and has a network of more than 130 distributors and agents on five continents with production plants in Europe, North Africa, the US, and Asia.

APEM develops and manufactures professional switch products for diversified markets including instrumentation, medical, communications, industrial automation, defense, and transport.
