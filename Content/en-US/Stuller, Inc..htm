Stuller and its shiny objects boost its bottom line. For decades, the company has made and distributed fine jewelry, including diamonds and bridal jewelry, as well as charms, colored stones, fabricated precious metals, jewelry supplies, and watch bands. It serves more than 40,000 accounts -- consisting of mainly professional jewelers -- throughout North America and abroad with some 200,000 different items. Stuller also wholesales jewelry to clients through its just-in-time delivery service. Stuller's manufacturing and administrative facilities (located in Louisiana) measure almost 600,000 sq. ft. Chairman Matthew Stuller founded the company in 1970.

Aside from its manufacturing and administrative headquarters located in Lafayette, Louisiana, the company has locations in Chattanooga, Tennessee; Houston, Texas; Davenport, Iowa; Toronto, Ontario; Merida, Mexico; Ramat Gan, Israel; and Bangkok, Thailand.

Stuller owns nearly 10 operations on three continents.

The company's e-commerce website (www.Stuller.com) accounts for about 30% of the company's total sales. It primarily sells diamonds, gemstones, jewelry, mountings, and tools and supplies through its website. Some of the tools and supplies sold include abrasives, benches and bench tools, burs and drills, gauges and scales, and pliers and tweezers. Stuller also sells religious and family jewelry lines. For the sports enthusiast, the company markets jewelry with the college and NFL team's logo embossed on the jewelry.

Stuller has become one of the largest manufacturers and distributors nationwide and is a leader in both domestic and international wholesale jewelry sectors. One of Stuller's areas of focus is "just in time" delivery, as founder Matt Stuller discovered that the industry is not known for its speedy product delivery. To this end, Stuller specializes in next day delivery of its merchandise.

The company has devoted time to inking several licensing deals in recent years to add new products and brands to its portfolio. In 2013 Stuller partnered with BlingGuard to become the exclusive distributor of BlingWraps and BlingDots to retailers. The invisible ring guards keep jewelry from twisting, turning, and pulling. An agreement with Quadamas, entered in 2013, designates Stuller as the distributor of the new patented line of Asscher Cut Princess sapphires to the US market for Quadamas. Making it the exclusive distributor of the Bfly line to US independent retailers, Stuller partnered with the company, which is known for its patented Butterfly Gem cut.

Catering primarily to more than 40,000 professional jewelers, Stuller wholesales jewelry to clients. It also sells jewelry through its website.
