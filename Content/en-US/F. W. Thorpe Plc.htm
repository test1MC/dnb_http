F. W. Thorpe knows how many engineers it takes to design a light bulb. The company designs, manufactures, and supplies professional lighting systems for customers around the globe. Its Thorlux subsidiary, which accounts for about 80% of the group's sales, manufacturers professional lighting systems for industrial, commercial and controls markets. The company's Mackwell Electronics unit makes emergency lighting components and LED conversion kits. Other units include Sugg Lighting (decorative lighting), Compact Lighting (retail space lighting), Solite Europe (cleanroom lighting equipment), and Philip Payne (emergency exit signage). The company was founded in 1936.

F. W. Thorpe's business segments operate in the three main areas of the UK, the rest of Europe, and the rest of the world. The company has a presence in Ireland, the UK, Germany, UAE, and Australia, and its products are sold throughout the world.

F. W. Thorpe's revenues jumped 14% from 2013 to 2014 due to growth experienced from its Thorlux subsidiary. In addition, its net income climbed 7% during that same time period.
