The United Kingdom buys loads of memory from Kingston Technology Europe. The company, which is a subsidiary of Kingston Technology , is a top supplier of computer memory in the UK. Kingston offers a variety of memory products, including system-specific memory modules, solid-state drives, USB flash drives, flash memory cards, and add-in random-access memory (RAM) modules. Through its website, customers can find memory for just about any sort of computer or consumer electronics device. As with its California-based parent company, Kingston Technology Europe has received broad recognition for its employee-friendly environment.

The company has offices in the Paris, France and Munich, Germany along with its headquarters in Sunbury-on-Thames, UK.
