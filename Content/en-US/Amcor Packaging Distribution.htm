Orora Packaging Solutions (formerly Amcor Packaging Distribution) offers its goods as a package deal. The company is a total packaging solutions supplier as well as a fully integrated corrugated manufacturer. With more than 50 locations in 7 countries, Orora Packaging Solutions makes a wide array of corrugated products, and also provides custom and stock packaging materials, facility supplies, equipment and automation, and primary packaging services. Its products are used for packaging goods in a range of industries, including agriculture, food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and technology. A business unit of Australia-based Orora Ltd, Orora Packaging Solutions comprises its parent's North American operations.

Headquartered in Buena Park, California, the company has operations mostly concentrated in California but has nearly 55 distribution centers and 10 manufacturing plants spanning North America.

Orora North America operates in two segments: Packaging distribution (under the Landsberg brand); and corrugated board manufacturing (under the Manufactured Packaging Products and Corru-Kraft brands).

The company operates through a handful of subsidiaries: Landsberg Co. coordinates the distribution and sales of shipping and janitorial supplies; Manufactured Packaging Products manufactures custom corrugated packaging; and Corru-Kraft produces corrugated sheets.

The company serves various industries including agriculture, technology, food and beverage, pharmaceutical, and automotive.

Orora North America generated 40% of its parent's total revenues in 2014. This represents the largest percentage of any of Amcor 's region/segments.

The company was created in 2013 after Amcor spun off its Australasian Fiber and Beverage and North American Packaging Distribution businesses as Orora Ltd., with the operating business units of Orora Australasia and Orora North America.

Working with its sister company, in 2014 an Orora Australasia mill exported 8,500 tons of recycled paper to Orora North America.

Orora North America also uses acquisitions as a means for boosting its business.

In 2014 Orora North America made a small bolt-on acquisition of a Texas-based distributor of plastic packaging products for the food and produce sector.
