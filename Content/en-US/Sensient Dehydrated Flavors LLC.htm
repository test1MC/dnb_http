Sensient Dehydrated Flavors has exceptionally good taste ... just add liquid and you do too. The company manufactures flavorings and other products for the food processing, foodservice, and beverage industries. It specializes in dehydrated spices and vegetables such as parsley, celery, and spinach, but also makes flavors for condiments, dairy products, gravies, marinades, and beverages. Formerly called Rogers Foods, it is a business unit of Sensient Technologies , which has worldwide operations, including in the US, the UK, Canada, France, and Germany.
