Benson Systems watches the systems that watch over you. The company provides integrated security system installation and monitoring services to commercial customers in the US (primarily the Southwest) and Mexico. Its products included card access, gate operators, closed-circuit television (CCTV) systems, and fire alarms. Benson Systems also provides dispatch, asset tracking, and inspection services. Customers have included AT&T , Cox Communications , Nestlé USA , Southwest Airlines , Taco Bell , Target , and U-Haul . President and CEO Shawn Benson founded Benson Systems in 1986 at the age of 21, with his $10,000 savings. The company is owned by the Benson family.
