Old Orchard Brands processes and sells bottled fruit juices, frozen juice concentrates, juice blends, nectars, and nonalcoholic drink mixers. Flavors offered by the company include apple, cranberry, fruit punch, grape, grapefruit, and lemonade. Old Orchard, which began operations in 1978 selling only apple juice, now offers 27 frozen and 63 bottled flavor choices. The company produces about 55 million bottles and 45 million cans of frozen concentrate every year. Privately owned, Old Orchard is headquartered in and maintains a manufacturing site and warehouse in Sparta, Michigan. Its products are sold throughout the US.
