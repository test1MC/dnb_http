Going up? Allow Fujitec to be of assistance. Fujitec designs, develops, manufactures, markets, installs, and maintains elevators, escalators, moving walks, vertical parking equipment, and new transportation systems. Fujitec's principal products include train station elevators, machine-room-less elevators, and standard elevators; escalators, wheelchair accessible escalators, autowalks, and vertical automated bicycle park systems; and large-scale underground mechanized parking facilities. Fujitec has production bases in 20 countries and conducts business worldwide.
