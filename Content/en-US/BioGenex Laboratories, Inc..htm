BioGenex Laboratories isn't slacking when it comes to helping researchers unfold the mysteries contained in tissues and cells. The company develops technologies that perform automated analyses useful in drug discovery and development, life science research, and cellular and molecular diagnostics. The firm's products include antibodies, probes, imaging systems, labeling, and staining systems; reagents; and detection kits. It sells primarily to biotechnology and pharmaceutical companies, reference labs, hospitals, cancer treatment centers, and academic medical institutions.
