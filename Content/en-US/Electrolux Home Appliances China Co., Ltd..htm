Electrolux Home Appliance China might have Swedish roots but its reach extends across China. The company is the Chinese arm of global appliance giant AB Electrolux and trails competitor Haier , the largest appliances maker in China. Electrolux Home Appliance China makes, distributes, markets, and sells air conditioners, refrigerators, microwave ovens, vacuum cleaners, and just about anything else needed to keep a home running smoothly. The company's brands include AEG, Electrolux, Eureka, Frigidaire, Husqvarna, and Znussi. Electrolux Home Appliances also conducts research and development for its parent, creating brands and products exclusive to the Chinese market.
