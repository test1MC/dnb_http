"Wine makes daily living easier, less hurried, with fewer tensions and more tolerance," according to Benjamin Franklin. Davis Bynum Winery is a small, family-owned business that makes a variety of wines (from chardonnay to pinot noir sold under the Davis Bynum label) for those wanting more ease in life. Customers can order its wines, made from grapes grown in the Russian River Valley, on the company's Web site. Founder Davis Bynum entered the wine business in the 1950s as a hobbyist but by the early 1970s, owned a winery in Sonoma County. In 2007 Bynum sold the winery to Tom Klein and the Klein family.
