Tropicana and Minute Maid may dominate the juice market, but Naked Juice makes a splash with its juices. The company, a subsidiary of PepsiCo and part of its Americas beverage arm, focuses on preservative-free (thus "naked"), no-sugar-added juices, smoothies, and other beverages. Its Naked Juice line is spiked with combinations of proteins, probiotics, vitamins, and herbs and it's divided among seven functional families, such as Well Being and Antioxidant. Its products are sold throughout the US at health food stores and neighborhood markets, as well as major food retailers and club stores. Naked Juices are also available in Canada and the UK.

Based in New York, Naked Juice makes and distributes juices and fruit smoothies in the US, Canada, and the UK.

Naked Juice is part of PepsiCo Americas Beverages (PAB), which accounted for 32% of PepsiCo's fiscal 2013 revenue. Within the PAB segment, the Naked Juice business generates about half of sales.

Naked Juice sells its products through health food stores, club stores, supermarkets, and neighborhood outlets. Customers include Costco , Kroger , Wal-Mart , Target , and Whole Foods Market . In the UK, Naked Juice sells its products through Sainsbury's , Starbucks , WH Smith , and Tesco .

In line with its focus on health, the company is the first nationally distributed brand to offer its beverages in 100% post-consumer recycled plastic bottles (PET), called reNEWabottles.

While parent PepsiCo does not break out financials specifically for Naked Juice, the company's PepsiCo Americas Beverages segment posted 2% decreases in revenue in 2013. It attributes the decline to sales slips across its carbonated soft drinks (5% decreases) and its non-carbonated beverages (down 2%). The latter reflected a high single-digit decline in its overall water portfolio.

The company traces its roots to the beaches of Santa Monica in 1983 but received a boost in 2001 from North Castle , a private equity firm that put Naked Juice under an umbrella with several of its other beverage companies that went by the name Ultimate Juice Company. Pepsi acquired the company from North Castle Partners in 2007.
