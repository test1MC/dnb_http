NSF Controls can't stand insufficient motion control. The company makes a wide range of solenoids and switches used in motion control applications. These devices find their way into automotive gear shift locking assemblies, camera shutter controls, printer paper feeders, and the locking and feeding mechanisms on ATMs. The company's NSF Solutions division offers design, sourcing, manufacturing, and support services. NSF Controls serves customers in the aerospace, automotive, machine tool, medical, and robotics industries, among others. The company became independent in a 1997 MBO from LucasVarity.

NSF has a UK production of 900,000 assemblies annually, with a supply of over 2.5 million solenoid activated gas valves in service to date. In excess of 1,000 of these solenoids are supplied for use in combat vehicles.

Typical applications for the company's switch products include communication, test equipment, stage lighting control consoles, and temperature control systems; its rotary solenoids are utilized in vacuum circuit breakers.
