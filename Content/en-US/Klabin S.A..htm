If life gives you lemons, make lemonade, if an Amazon forest, paper --Brazil's Klabin has for more than a century. The company is the country's #1 paper producer, exporter, and recycler. Its activities span manufacturing paper, packaging, industrial sacks, as well as forestry. Klabin owns and leases a million plus acres of timberland, and operates 18 plants. Core products are eucalyptus and small logs for sawmill industries, packaging paper (Kraftliner, Klabin Boards), corrugated boxes, and multi-wall sacks. Klabin sells to markets from construction materials to food and beverage, chemicals, and agribusiness. Unilever is among its largest customers. It is held by industrial family group Klabin Irmãos & Cia.
