Next time you're drifting off into La La Land, consider whether the effort has been supported by the craftsmanship of Hilding Anders Holdings Limited, aka Slumberland. Founded in 1919, Slumberland is a leading UK manufacturer of mattresses and bedsprings. The company was one of the first to develop posture springing in mattresses in 1964. As part of its business, Slumberland has a counterpart in production and sales located in the Czech Republic. It also sells bedding products such as pillows and sturdier accoutrements the likes of headboards. The bedding maker is owned by Hilding Anders , the largest bed manufacturing group in Europe that boasts 30 subsidiaries in Europe, Asia, and Russia.

The company engages in manufacturing high quality mattresses and divans and sell to local and export, in both retail and institutional channels.

Slumberland has an entire department dedicated to the production of mattresses under the IKEA brand. It also holds two warrants granted by the Royal family (Her Majesty the Queen and the Queen Mother) for its standards of quality.

The company sells to local and export markets, in both retail and institutional channels.

Slumberland also sells through stockists, including Bensons for Beds, and Furniture Village. Company Background

Looking to build on its reputation for rolling out innovative items, Slumberland in 2012 launched eight new models of beds. Each has high-density foam to maintain edge-to-edge support and Sensity fabric with probiotic micro-capsules to help keep mattress covers clean.
