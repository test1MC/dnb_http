New Harbinger Publications helps readers usher in a new era. A publisher of self-help books for consumers, the company's psychology and medical books are written by clinical professionals and feature step-by-step models. New Harbinger covers topics such as chronic illness, depression, anger, stress reduction, relationships, and self esteem. Titles include The Depression Workbook, Angry All the Time , and Self Esteem . The company's The Anxiety and Phobia Workbook came out in 1989 and has sold more than 750,000 copies. Psychologist Matthew McKay and writer Patrick Fanning founded New Harbinger in 1973.
