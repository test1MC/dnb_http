Sometimes, a new view is all one needs to see the world in a different light. New View Gifts & Accessories is a designer, importer, and manufacturer of gifts and home décor accessories. The company's products portfolio is filled with a variety of items, including candles, candleholders, picture frames, photo albums and boxes, clocks, pillows, and wall décor. The private company wholesales its wares and distributes its products to major retailers, such as Bed Bath & Beyond , across the US. New View Gifts & Accessories operates from several locations in Pennsylvania. Associates Anne Marie Mendlow, John Brennan, and Joseph Narzikul started New View in the late 1990s.
