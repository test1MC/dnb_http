It's likely that Sleepeck Printing hasn't had a nap in decades. The printing company has kept the presses rolling since 1904, churning out printed products from catalogs to scratch-and-sniff inserts. Sleepeck Printing also prints posters, calendars, book covers and inserts, and point-of-purchase displays. Along with sheet-fed printing, the company also offers web-offset capabilities through its subsidiary Dixonweb Printing. As part of its services, Dixonweb produces brochures, mailers, and national newspaper and magazine inserts. The company was founded by, and is still owned and led by, the Sleepeck family.
