D3 LED makes bright lights for big cities. Short for Dynamic Digital Displays, D3 designs and manufactures indoor and outdoor digital LED (light-emitting diode) displays, such as video walls, LED signs, large video screens, marquee systems, ticker displays, and reader boards. The company's displays can be found in New York City, Atlantic City, Hong Kong, Tokyo, and other cities. It has designed displays for companies like Walgreens , JVC , and Chase , as well as stadiums and arenas. Founded in 2006, D3 operates from offices in California and New Jersey.
