Double Life Corporation keeps oil fields shaking. The company designs, makes, and distributes solids control components and centrifugal pumps for drill rigs used in oilfields, mining operations, and on construction sites. It also offers vibration screen shakers, valves, centrifuges, vacuum degassers, desanders and desilters, mud agitators, and other related equipment. Founded in 181, Double Life also offers replacement parts and provides equipment recommendation and system evaluation services.
