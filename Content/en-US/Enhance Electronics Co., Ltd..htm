Enhance Electronics makes switching power supply products used in desktop PCs, Internet appliance servers, self-service kiosks, telecommunications instrumentations, and other applications. It also manufactures remote-access power hubs, using Ethernet or modem connections. Enhance delivers some 800,000 of its products monthly. Founded in 1986, the company boasts locations in China, France, the Netherlands, South Korea, Taiwan, and the US.
