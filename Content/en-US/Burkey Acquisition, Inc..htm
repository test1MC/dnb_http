Burkey Acquisition, which does business as Popcorn Alley, is banking on visions of all things associated with autumn dancing in your head. The Dover, Pennsylvania-based company makes and packages caramel and candy-coated popcorn snacks under the Stonehedge Farms brand. In addition to making the gooey treat, Popcorn participates in fundraising efforts for organizations including marching bands, cheerleaders, scout troops and various school clubs, churches, and civic organizations. Formerly a family-owned corporation, president John Hornick bought the caramel-corn company in 1998.
