Sandow Media caters to the discriminating magazine reader. The company publishes six titles, including the bimonthlies Surface , a design publication; Watch Journal , about high-dollar watches; and Worth , a financial magazine for high net-worth individuals. It also offers two home design magazines, the monthly Interior Design and the quarterly Luxe Interiors + Design . Another quarterly magazine, NewBeauty , is tied in with product promotions that allow users to sample new beauty products. Sandow Media also publishes the weekly industry newspaper Furniture Today and oversees its publications' websites. In addition, it owns 50 newsstands located at private airports. Sandow Media was formed by CEO Adam Sandow in 2002.
