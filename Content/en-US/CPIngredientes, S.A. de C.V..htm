It may be corny, but if your company needs ingredients, this company can provide them. CPIngredientes refines corn into sweeteners (sorbitol, glucose, high fructose corn syrup, dextrose, and maltodextrin), starch, oil, and caramel coloring agents. Its products are used by companies in the food and beverage, brewing, personal care, pharmaceutical, packaging, and textile industries. CPIngredientes has nearly 35 facilities in more than a dozen countries. The company, which was founded in 1930 as Productos de Maíz, is owned by US food ingredients firm Ingredion .
