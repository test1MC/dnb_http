Omega Cabinets, a division of MasterBrand Cabinets (a Fortune Brands Home & Security company), manufactures all-wood cabinets for the kitchen and bath from hardwoods (alder, cherry, maple, oak, pecan, red birch, and walnut) in a variety of styles and finishes. Omega makes other all-wood products such as entertainment centers, mantles, bookcases, and custom-designed pieces. The company sells its products under the Dynasty and Omega brands through dealerships across the US. Started in 1997 in a barn in Iowa, Omega Cabinets was acquired by Fortune Brands in 2002.
