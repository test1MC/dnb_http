Whether you're treating yourself to a manicure in a cushy salon or in the comfort of your own boudoir, Orly International will stand by your side. The company makes nail lacquers and assorted nail care items that are used internationally in salons and spas in more than 65 countries. Its products also are sold through beauty supply stores, including Ulta and Sally Beauty . Orly's nail treatment products include the Original French Manicure, Fungus MD, Calcium Shield, and 3 Step HandSpa, among others. The company boasts the Get a Grip-brand nail polish cap for ease of use. Founder and CEO Jeff Pink established the personal care products manufacturer in 1975.
