The Roasterie spills the beans on a daily basis. The company is a specialty importer and coffee roaster that offers almost 100 different packaged blends of coffees and about 25 teas. Its products include regular, dark roast, decaffeinated, organic, and fair-trade coffees (which are guaranteed to give third-world farmers a fair price for their crops so that they can support their farms and families). The Roasterie sells its products in select US retailers and through its Web site. Customers can sign up to receive regular orders through the company's Pilot's Club. The Roasterie Café is the company's flagship coffeehouse. The Roasterie was founded in 1993 by President Danny O'Neill.
