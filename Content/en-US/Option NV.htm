This company aims to be the best option for the mobile professional in need of a communications solution. Option manufactures and markets broadband wireless equipment, such as data cards, embedded wireless modules, USB modems, wireless routers, and wireless software. Its products are used primarily in mobile phones and laptop computers. Founded in 1986, the Option depends largely on European customers including Vodafone , Telenor , Orange , and Bouygues Telecom. Other customers have included AT&T , Acer , ASUS , Samsung Electronics , and Sony .
