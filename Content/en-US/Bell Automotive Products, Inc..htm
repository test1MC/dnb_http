Need to add some handy accessories to the vehicle? Then, ring Bell for assistance. Under the Bell and Cobbs brand names, Bell Automotive Products supplies a line of aftermarket accessories for motor vehicles, including air fresheners, cargo carriers, compasses, covers for seats and steering wheels, deer-warning equipment, drink holders, license plate frames, sun-protection visors, and yes, those ever-popular fuzzy dice to hang from the rear-view mirror. The product line is carried by a number of retailers, such as AutoZone , Kmart , Pep Boys , Shopko , Target , and Wal-Mart . In 2015 Bell was acquired by Hopkins Manufacturing Corporation.
