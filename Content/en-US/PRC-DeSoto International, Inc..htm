PRC-DeSoto's sealants do a little bit more than just keep a drafty house warm or a back deck waterproof; they help keep F-22 jet fighters and the Space Station in one piece. The company, a subsidiary of PPG Industries residing within its Aerospace unit, provides sealants and coatings to aerospace companies like Airbus and Boeing . Other customers include airframe manufacturers, airline operators, insulating glass unit makers, and the telecommunications industry. The Aerospace unit is one of the world's leading supplier of sealants, coatings, aircraft maintenance products, transparencies, and application systems, serving OEMs and maintenance providers for the aviation industry.

PRC-DeSoto operates manufacturing facilities in France, the UK, and the US; it sells its products worldwide. It has its regional offices in Africa, Brazil, France, Hong Kong, India, Japan, and the US, and application support centers in Australia, Canada, China, France, Germany, India, Japan, Singapore, the UAE, the UK, and the US.

The company operates as part of PPG 's Aerospace segment. PPG's aerospace coatings business supplies sealants, coatings, maintenance cleaners and transparencies for commercial, military, regional jet and general aviation aircraft and transparent armor for specialty applications and also provides chemical management for the aerospace industry. PPG supplies products to aircraft manufacturers and maintenance and aftermarket customers around the world both on a direct basis and through a company-owned distribution network.

PRC-DeSoto has benefited from post-recession overall industrial growth in the US (in particular in the aerospace industry) due in part to declining natural gas costs.

In 2013 it expanded its aerospace business operations in Japan with the relocation of its application support center to a site in Miyoshi that supports increased production capacity, adds warehouse space and affords new service capabilities. The new site in Miyoshi Kurozasa Industrial Park has expanded capacity for custom packaging of premixed frozen sealant with this larger plant to support increased commercial and regional aerospace demand.

Through a series of strategic acquisitions, PPG has assembled a team of industry leaders that builds on its own long history as a transparencies aviation leader, with an emphasis on product innovation.

Adding to PRC-DeSoto's aerospace coatings capabilities product line, in 2013 PPG acquired California-based Deft Incorporated , a privately-owned specialty coatings, which makes structural primers and military topcoats for the North American aviation industry. It also produces coatings for the architectural and general industrial markets.

The company, formerly known as Courtaulds Aerospace, was founded in 1945.
