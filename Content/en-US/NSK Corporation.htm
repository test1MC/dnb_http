NSK Corporation, a subsidiary of Japan's NSK Ltd. , designs, markets, and manufactures automotive anti-friction bearings. The company's other products include precision machinery and parts, automotive products such as steering columns and shafts, and mechatronics (mechanical and electronics engineering). NSK also offers complete pinion and column type electric power steering systems. In addition to its automotive customers, NSK also serves industries such as mining and construction, food and beverage, and paper manufacturing. The company distributes over 100,000 types of bearings, linear motion, and automotive component products.

Based in Michigan, the company operates manufacturing plants in Bennington, Vermont, and Dyersburg, Tennessee; along with a subsidiary operating at Guanajuato, Mexico.

The company serves a wide range of industries, including Agriculture, Construction, Electric Motors, Food and Beverage, Lawn and Garden, Machine Tool, Material, Handling, Medical, Metal Mills, Mining, Aggregate and Cement, Office Equipment, Oil and Gas, Power Transmission, Pulp and Paper, Pumps and Compressors, Recreational Products, Renewable Energy, Semiconductor, and Utilities.

NSK Corporation is part of NSK Ltd. 's Americas operations, which accounted for 15% of the parent company's 2014 revenues.

The company has become one of the world's leading bearing manufacturers. It has fostered a network of authorized distributors who work in tandem with NSK sales and engineering teams. The company has strategically located its distribution centers in the US to be in proximity to OEMs and industrial distribution markets, in order to meet customers' needs.

Expanding its North American operations, in 2014 NSK Corporation opened a plant in Guanajuato, Mexico, to produce high-quality bearings for automakers and automotive parts manufacturers.

The company announced in early 2009 its bearing reconditioning program, which was borne out of the recessionary economy. Since bearings are used in just about every manufacturing and industrial application, expensive replacement can be circumvented, at least for a time, by utilizing NSK Corporation's reconditioning program. Bearing damage can occur because of the wrong lubrication, misalignment, excessive heat and vibration, and improper installation.

"Think NSK" is the tagline for the new brand campaign NSK Corporation launched in October 2008. The campaign is designed to get consumers to connect with the product and the company, thus enlightening them to NSK's attributes in the motion control industry.

NSK Corporation was formed in 1997 after NSK Ltd. acquired Hoover Ball and Bearing.
