Warrell Corporation (dba Pennsylvania Dutch Candies) has plenty of sweet treats. The Pennsylvania confectioner's more than 1,000 products include peanut brittle, butter and party mints, peanut-butter pillows, sanded candies, nut crunches, dried fruits, and, not forgetting all the chocolate lovers out there, chocolate-covered peanuts, cashews, and almonds. In addition to its namesake brand, Pennsylvania Dutch Candies, the company makes the Katherine Beecher confections, as well as private-label candy. In 2009, it acquired the Classic Caramel Company . Founded in 1965, the Warrell serves accounts throughout the US. The company is still owned and operated by the founding Warrell family.
