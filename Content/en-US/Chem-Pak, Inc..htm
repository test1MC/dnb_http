Chem-Pak packs a chemical punch into its private-label products. The company provides contract chemical manufacturing services for specialty coatings, cleaners, paints, music accessories (instrument cleaners), lubricants, and degreasers. Chem-Pak also provides packaging services for steel aerosol cans and specialty filling of aluminum tubes, pails, and drums. It operates from a facility in West Virginia. The company was founded in 1966 to provide aerosol packaging services to marketers. Chem-Pak moved to its present manufacturing location in 2001.

The company serves the industrial trades with equipment maintenance and process needs; plastic injection molders with patented coatings and mold releases; the music industry with lubricants, polishes, and cleaners; and the firearms trades with lubricants, polishes, finishes, and protective products.
