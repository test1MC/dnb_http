Gard Plasticases helps you get squared away when you need to get around. The family-owned company manufactures plastic cases that can hold objects that range from less than an inch to eight feet in size. Gard Plasticases makes executive cases (briefcases), flight cases, and polypropylene boxes. Other products include compartmentalized plastic boxes and petri dishes. Gard Plasticases also offers foam and inserts to protect products housed within the company's cases, as well as silk-screen printing for labeling cases. Industries served include aerospace, automotive, broadcasting, and sports.

The company manufactures and supplies a variety of plastic cases and storage products that can hold objects ranging from less than an inch to more than eight feet in length.

Gard Plasticases serves customers in a range industries, including aerospace, military and defense, medical, promotional, broadcasting, sports and leisure, control and instrumentation, photography, and automotive.

The company was founded by Graham Tickle in 1972.
