The company and its NB Linear Motion subsidiary manufacture roller bearings, spindle units, stainless steel ball splines, stud rollers, and other related linear motion products used for such applications as automated factory production lines and shipping systems. Nippon Bearing, which was founded in 1959, primarily serves customers in the industrial manufacturing sector. The company does business worldwide from branch offices and subsidiaries located in Japan, the Netherlands, and the US, and makes sales in part through a network of distributors.
