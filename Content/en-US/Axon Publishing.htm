Axon Publishing gets glossies into customers' hands. The firm is a contract publisher of customer magazines commissioned by companies to market their products. In addition, Axon produces business-to-business magazines and internal staff communications. The company offers services in media design, production, and distribution for both print (catalogs, newsletters) and digital (Web and intranet content, mobile apps) publishing. Clients have included Cable & Wireless , Guinness , Marks & Spencer , and Procter & Gamble . Axon Publishing was founded in 1994 by Ellen Brush and Paul Keers. In a 2012 management buyout, Lisa Clarke and Ellen McGonigal acquired the firm from the founders after the death of Brush.
