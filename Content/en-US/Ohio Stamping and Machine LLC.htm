Drawing a blank is never a problem for Ohio Stamping & Machine. The company manufactures stampings and compound blanks, such as operational die-cut blanks, progressive blank dies, front and rear seat back frames, and other automotive components. It also makes blanks and medium to large stampings for the truck and appliance industries. The company operates automatic press lines producing wide stampings and compound blanks. Blanks are processed and either die-cut configured or cut-to-length.
