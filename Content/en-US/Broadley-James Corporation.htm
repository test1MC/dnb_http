Broadley-James manufactures measurement and control instruments and accessories for use in bioprocess and fermentation applications. The company's BioNet product line includes a bioreactor, a controller, a motor controller, and a utility tower. Each controller can network and control several dozen bioreactors. Broadley-James was established in 1967 by Leighton Broadley (CFO), Marcile Broadley, and Richard James. James left the business in 1976. The company has operations in California and Pennsylvania, and overseas in the UK. Broadley-James is owned by its senior officers.
