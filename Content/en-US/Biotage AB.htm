What do you get when you splice a genetic analysis company with a chemical synthesis company? Biotage. The life sciences firm was created when Pyrosequencing acquired Personal Chemistry in 2003. Its Discovery Chemistry division offers small-molecule drug discovery purification and separation systems. Biotage's customers include some of the world's leading pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies, as well as academic research organizations. Biotage in 2010 bought MIP Technologies, SPE Extraction, and Evaporation Products. Previously, in 2008 Biotage sold its Biosystems unit, which facilitated DNA sequencing, to QIAGEN NV . Biotage boasts offices in Sweden, the US, the UK, and Japan.
