Thai Union Frozen Products knows how to lure a tuna into a can and a cephalopod into a freezer. The company is one of Thailand's largest producers of frozen and canned seafood, which it exports to the US, Japan, and Europe. The company's subsidiary Tri-Union Seafoods owns the US tuna brand, Chicken of the Sea. Its other subsidiaries make and distribute value-added products such as microwaveable meals; snack foods, including the Fisho brand of fish snacks; and the Sealect brand of canned tuna. Thai Union also operates its own can production facilities and fishing vessels, and has holdings in fruit and vegetable canning companies, animal-feed operations, and commercial printing.
