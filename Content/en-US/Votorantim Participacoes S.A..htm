Votorantim Participações is an expert at the game of Rock, Paper, Squeezers. The industrial conglomerate, known as Votorantim Group, is a Brazilian mining, metals, and paper manufacturer and a leading global exporter of concentrated orange juice. Through its business units and subsidiaries, it operates not only in its native Brazil but also across the globe. Its manufacturing operations produce cement and concrete ( Votorantim Cimentos ); metals such as aluminum, zinc, and nickel ( Votorantim Metais ); and paper and pulp (through its 29% stake in Fibria ). Other divisions generate power from some 38 hydroelectric and co-generation plants and provide retail and commercial financial services.
