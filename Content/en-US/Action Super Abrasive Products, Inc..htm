Thanks to the inventor of the wheel, Action SuperAbrasive (ASA) is grinding out a living. The company makes and supplies superabrasive grinding wheels used in machines for cutting a range of hard materials. ASA's resin, polyimide, metal, and vitrified bonding for diamond and CBN (cubic boron nitride) grinding wheels offer manufacturers options for cutting finish, coolant flow, and wheel speed. Wheels are sold in a variety of diameters, too. ASA makes electroplated wheels as well, and offers computer-aided design and on-site technical services. Among many worldwide, customers include cutting tool, auto, and aerospace parts makers, and mold and die, high tech ceramics, and quartz fabricating businesses.
