Consumer electronics maker Sichuan Changhong Electric (Changhong) is one of China's largest television manufacturers. The company also makes air conditioners, batteries, audio-visual equipment, mobile phones, refrigerators, and washing machines. Changhong Electric was founded in 1958 to develop military radar systems. In 1985, the company moved into the consumer electronics sector. Changhong Electric has established overseas operations in Australia, Europe, the Middle East, Russia, Southeast Asia, and the Americas.
