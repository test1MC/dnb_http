Where there's smoke, there's fire, and detecting them both is job number one for Apollo Fire Detectors, a manufacturer of smoke and flame detectors, primarily for commercial customers, including banks, manufacturing plants, and restaurants. Graced with more than 3,000 product approvals worldwide, the company's product line includes -- along with detectors -- interfaces, sounders, beacons, and mounting accessories. Apollo Fire Detectors has offices in the UK, the US, China, Germany, and Ireland and additionally operates through partners and distributors in other areas of the world. The company was founded in 1980 and was acquired by electronic, safety, and environmental technology firm Halma in 1984.

The company's offices are located in the US, China, and Germany, and it has operations across all five continents. It exports its products to over 100 countries worldwide.

Apollo Fire Detectors' products are sold through a network of partners and distributors who sell across a wide range of sectors, including banking and finance, commercial, data processing, government and education, health care, marine, offshore, power generation, residential, transportation, and telecommunications. Its devices can be found in famous places such as The Statue of Liberty, New York; The Royal Albert Hall, London; and The Kremlin, Moscow.
