NitroSecurity wants to be the one safeguarding your organization's computer network. Founded in 1999, the company provides a suite of hardware and software products designed for security monitoring, analysis, and protection of computer networks. Its systems prevent network intrusions and allow for the viewing and analysis of network traffic flow and security event data relationships, with the goal of quickly identifying and responding to potential network security threats. NitroSecurity sells its products to midsized and large companies in such industries as education, financial services, health care, managed services, and retail, as well as government agencies. In 2011 it was bought by McAfee .
