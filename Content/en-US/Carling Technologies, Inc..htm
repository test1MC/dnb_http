Carling Technologies gives circuits a break. The company designs and manufactures a variety of electrical circuit breakers (hydraulic, magnetic, and thermal); electrical switches and assemblies; power distribution centers; digital switching systems; and electronic controls. It provides custom product design and tool-and-die fabrication, as well as technical and engineering services, to suit its clients' individual needs. Products are used by industrial and electronic makers for applications such as electronics, industrial controls, appliances, commercial food equipment, generators, HVAC equipment, security systems, and factory automation.

The company conducts its business through locations in Plainville, Connecticut; Brownsville, Texas; Matehuala, Mexico; Devon, UK; Kowloon, Hong Kong; and Zhongshan City, China.

Carling Technologies sells directly and through numerous distributors worldwide, including in Australia, Barzil, China, Germany, France, Hong Kong, India, Indonesia, Japan, Korea, New Zealand, Philippines, Singapore, South Africa, Taiwan, Thailand, Vietnam, the UK, and the US.

Its main industry groups include the on/off highway, marine, renewable energy, telecommunications, military, commercial food equipment, floor care, HVAC, security system, and small appliance markets.

The company is focused on developing and enhancing its products on regular intervals. In 2014 launched a new L-series circuit breaker with 3-phase approvals, along with a new V-Series Contura XIV switch. It introduced a new toggle switch Hexboot in 2015.

Carling Technologies was founded by Arthur Carling in 1920 as Carling Tool & Machine. The company is run by Richard Sorenson, the grandson of the founder.
