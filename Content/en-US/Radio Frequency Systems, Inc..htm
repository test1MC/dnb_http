Radio Frequency Systems has radio antennas and related products to enable everything from the rush hour traffic report to battlefield communications. The company, which does business as RFS, designs and manufactures a wide range of wireless and radio network infrastructure equipment used by broadcasters, telecommunications service providers, network equipment manufacturers, and government agencies. Products include radio-frequency (RF) systems, antennas, filters and combiners, cables, connectors, accessories, and related software. The company is owned by network product maker Alcatel-Lucent .

The company operates globally from 35 sales and support offices and nine manufacturing facilities located in Australia, Brazil, China, France, Germany, India, the UK, and the US. It also has five research and development centers.

RFS divides its operations across four business units: Wireless Infrastructure Solutions (base station antennas and RF conditioning products); RF Transmission & Distribution Solutions (transmission lines and wireless indoor equipment); Radio Link Networks (microwave antennas); and Broadcast & Defense Systems (TV, radio, high frequency and defense products).

RFS serves OEMs, distributors, system integrators, operators, and installers in the broadcast, wireless communications, land-mobile, and microwave market sectors. It sells its products through sells representatives, and as well as through its partners.
