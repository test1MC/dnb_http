Wheat's going on? Well, at Pamela's Products, it's gone. Wheat, that is. The company makes wheat- and gluten-free cookies, biscotti, and cakes (including cheese cakes such as white chocolate raspberry). It also offers baking mixes for bread, cornbread, pancakes, cakes, cookies, brownies, and frosting. The company's goods are geared toward people with food allergies and intolerances. They are available at specialty and natural food stores and supermarkets throughout North America. They can also be ordered online. Pamela's Products was founded by Pamela Giusto-Sorrells in 1988.
