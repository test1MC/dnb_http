TEAC Corporation's a hit with audiophiles and has mass (storage) appeal. Through its Data Storage division, TEAC makes computer peripherals such as optical disk, CD, and DVD drives. Its Information Products division makes audio and video recording devices, mass storage products, and video players for in-flight entertainment. Audio pros and music junkies know TEAC for its Audio & Visual Products division, which makes recorders and mixers for studio recording, as well as consumer stereo equipment like CD players and stereo speakers. Founded in 1953 as Tokyo Television Acoustic Company, TEAC markets its products under TEAC, TASCAM, and Esoteric brand names.
