This company came clean. The first certified organic dairy operation in the UK, Rachel's Organic Dairy is committed to treating cows nicely, as well as the consumers of the products of its well-looked-after cows. The company produces yogurts, dairy desserts, butter, cultured dairy products, and fluid milk, all of which are sourced from certified organic dairy farmers. Rachel and Gareth Rowland (organic farmers themselves) founded the company and now act as consultants. Rachel's Organic Dairy was owned by Horizon Organic and was part of the acquisition when Dean Foods purchased the Horizon company in 2004. Dean is now a subsidiary of France's Groupe Lactalis .

The company is located in Aberystwyth and has about 10 farms in UK.

The company sources milk from organic dairy farms in Wales.

The dairy's products are sold through such UK food retailers as Morrisons, J Sainsbury , Tesco , and Waitrose . Faced with difficult economic conditions, Rachel's has looked to TV advertising for the first time in its history to improve market share and sales.

As the demand for organic products has grown, Rachel's has added to its production facilities by boosting its manufacturing capacity.

Dean Foods sold the company to France's Groupe Lactalis in 2010.

During 2011, Rachel's transferred production of its rice-based desserts to France from the UK.

In another reorganization effort, owner Lactalis announced in 2012 that its Lactalis Nestle Chilled Dairy unit planned to integrate the Rachel's business into its operations in the UK and Ireland.
