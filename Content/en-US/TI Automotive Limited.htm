TI Automotive helps vehicles manage their liquid assets. The company is one of the largest manufacturers of automotive fluid storage, carrying, and delivery systems. It operates through five product groups: fluid carrying systems, powertrain components, tank systems, pump and module systems, and HVAC fluid systems. TI Auto serves major auto makers and the automotive aftermarket via some 130 locations in about 30 countries. Headquartered in suburban Detroit, TI Automotive operates facilities in 29 countries. In mid-2015, the company was bought by Bain Capital , one of the world's largest investment firms.

TI Automotive's units keep the flow going inside vehicles in various capacities. The TI Automotive Fluid Carrying Systems Division designs and manufactures fluid-carrying systems; TI Automotive Powertrain Systems Division develops products that contribute to the improved fuel economy and performance and lower emissions; TI Automotive's Fuel Tank Systems Division develops and manufactures fuel tank systems for diesel, gasoline, and flex-fuel applications; TI Automotive designs pumps and modules for gasoline, diesel, and flex-fuel vehicles; and TI Automotive's HVAC Fluid Systems Division provides a complete portfolio of fluid-carrying products.

TI Automotive's operations around the world keep it close to key customers. In 2014 the company opened a fuel tank systems production facility in Guangzhou, China.
