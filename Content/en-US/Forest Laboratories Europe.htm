Forest Laboratories UK, dba Forest Europe, plants medical seeds in the UK and Ireland. The company is the European arm of American drug firm Forest Laboratories (a subsidiary of drugmaker Allergan ). It produces both prescription and OTC medications for a wide array of ailments such as rashes, ear aches, and indigestion. Its prescription division markets Colomycin for cystic fibrosis and Colobreathe for chronic pulmonary infections, whiles its health care division sells OTC products including skin creams (Sudocrem and Exorex), colic medicines (Infacol), and heartburn pills (Bisodol). Products are marketed through a direct sales force in the UK and Ireland.

Forest Europe Pharma is building a portfolio of prescription drugs to address challenging conditions such as cystic fibrosis and bronchiectasis.

The company has divisions in the UK and Ireland. It is also working on expansion in Austria, Belgium, France, Germany, Italy, the Netherlands, Scandinavia, Spain, and Switzerland.

Flagship product Sudocrem is sold in more than 40 countries around the world.

Forest Europe is part of Allergan's international operations of its North American Generics and International reporting segment. The international arm provided 38% of the segment's revenue in 2014.

In early 2014, Forest Laboratories acquired Aptalis Pharma for some $3 billion. The deal added cystic fibrosis and ulcer medications, as well as sales networks in the US and Europe. The company then acquired drug development firm Furiex for about $1 billion, boosting its gastroenterology line.
