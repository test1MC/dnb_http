TriTech Coatings, also known as Sinnett-Elpaco Coatings, makes industrial paints and powder coatings for products including ammunition, appliances, and automobiles, as well as for manufacturers of industrial machinery, consumer goods, marine, and office furniture. Its products include high-temperature coatings, clear polyester powders, enamels, solvents, epoxies, urethanes, and wash primers. TriTech Coatings operates two production facilities in the Midwest and is owned by its executives.
