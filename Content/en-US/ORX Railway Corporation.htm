They've been working on the railroad all the live long day. ORX manufactures and repairs all types of railroad axles, gear units, trucks, and wheel sets. The company also provides a wide range of wheel shop services, including freight car wheel set repair, locomotive U-tube assembly, and the manufacture of new axles for all types of rail vehicles. Among its other jobs, ORX acts as a subcontractor that refurbishes Amtrak 's Acela locomotive wheel sets under a maintenance subcontract. (Maintenance of Amtrak's Acela trains is the responsibility of Bombardier Inc . and Alstom , the consortium that built the train).
