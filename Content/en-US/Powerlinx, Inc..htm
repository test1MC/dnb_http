Powerlinx designs, manufactures, and distributes products that transmit voice, video, and data over power lines. Through its Security Products segment, the company provides closed-circuit television systems. The company's DC Transportation segment offers view enhancement systems that transmit data through vehicle wiring to allow truck, school bus, and RV drivers to see objects in what otherwise would be blind spots. Through its Marine segment, Powerlinx provides customers with underwater surveillance equipment under the SeaView and SeaMaster brand names. Its Audio Products Division sells audio accessories to the home entertainment market. HSN accounts for 29% of Powerlinx's revenues.
