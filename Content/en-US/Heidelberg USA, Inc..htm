Heidelberg USA is a manufacturer of sheetfed printing presses and prepress, printing, and binding equipment for customers in the print media industry. The company's main packaging printing equipment, branded as Prinect, allows the user to make labels, folding cartons, or leaflets that are attached to packaging materials. Heidelberg USA also sells such consumables as plates and film, and it is the main North American subsidiary of German printing giant Heidelberger Druckmaschinen (known as Heidelberg), along with Heidelberg Canada Graphic Equipment, Heidelberg Print Finance Americas, and Heidelberg Mexico Services.

Heidelberg USA develops and manufactures precision printing presses, devices for plate imaging, post-press finishing, and digital inkjet systems for packaging manufacturers. It also offers prepress, press, post-press, and digital printing products, as well as consumables, parts, remarketed equipment, and business consulting services.

The company has regional offices in Deer Park, Illinois; Cranbury, New Jersey; and Edina, Minnesota.
