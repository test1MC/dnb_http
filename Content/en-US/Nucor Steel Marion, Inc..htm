Nucor Steel Marion isn't a square, but it does have an angle on steel shapes. The steel mini mill uses recycled steel to make reinforcement bar (rebar), small and midsized signposts, cable barrier systems, and delineators. Nucor Steel Marion makes a complete line of sign supports for highways, including Federal Highway Administration -approved U-channel posts (Rib-Bak, Bend-Bak) and breakaway U-channel products (Lap-Splice, Slip-Safe) used in producing signposts. The company's main customers are in the highway, construction, and agricultural industries. In 2005 steel giant Nucor Corporation acquired Marion Steel for about $110 million. The company's name later changed to Nucor Steel Marion.
