GuideTech, LLC (formerly Guide Technology) makes time interval analyzer (TIA) instruments used to test semiconductor chips as they are being produced. Customers include Advanced Semiconductor Engineering , ChipMOS , Siliconware Precision Industries , STATS ChipPAC , Unisem, and VIKO Test Lab. The company works cooperatively with the leading vendors of automated test equipment for semiconductors, including Advantest , Credence Systems , Xcerra , and Teradyne . GuideTech was founded in 1988. In 2008 Guide Technology was acquired by GuideTech Inc., a company headed by Ron Sigura, one of the original founders of Guide Technology. Its name was subsequently changed to GuideTech LLC.
