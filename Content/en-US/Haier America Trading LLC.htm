Haier America is the US sales and marketing arm of Chinese home appliance and consumer electronics giant Haier Group . Haier Group makes refrigerators, freezers, wine cellars, air conditioners, dishwashers, laundry machines, and small appliances (such as coffee makers, food processors, and ice makers). Down the entertainment aisle, the group produces flat-screen TVs, Blu-ray disc and portable media players, and headphones. Haier's products are sold by leading retailers, such as Wal-Mart , Bed Bath & Beyond , Best Buy , Office Depot , and Target . The Haier Group subsidiary has sold more than 40 million products since its founding in 1999.

Headquartered in New York, Haier America operates domestic sales offices in some 13 states and warehouses in three states. The state of Georgia serves as its its logistics hub serving the Southeast US. International sales offices reside in Argentina, Mexico, and Canada. The firm also has a research and development center in South Carolina.

Haier America offers products through noteworthy retailers like Wal-Mart , Amazon , Office Depot , Target , and Best Buy . The company sells its products throughout the Americas and serves home appliances and consumer electronics markets.

Haier America became a wholly-owned subsidiary of Haier Group in 2013 after acquiring the shares held by minority joint venture shareholders. The share purchase was made to further align the Chinese company's global resources toward growth of the Haier brand in North and South America. Further underlining its commitment to the North American market, Haier is expanding its operations here with a new headquarters in Wayne, New Jersey slated to open in the second half of 2014.
