As the world's largest grower and processor of almonds and pistachios, Wonderful Pistachios & Almonds (formerly Paramount Farms) earns more than peanuts in its market. The company's nuts are sold to consumers under the Wonderful label. The company also offers private-label products and services and sells nut products to food manufacturers and foodservice customers across the US and worldwide. Wonderful Pistachios & Almonds offers in-shell and shelled pistachios, along with various almond products, including whole, sliced, slivered, or diced, all of which are supplied in raw, roasted, blanched, and flavored forms. Wonderful Pistachios & Almonds is owned by The Wonderful Company LLC.
