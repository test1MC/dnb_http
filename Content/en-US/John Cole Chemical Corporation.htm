John Cole Chemical makes a living by keeping the workplace clean. The company, which does business as JOSCO Products, manufactures wiping rags and towels, disinfectants, mats, protective clothing, and other clean room supplies. It also offers technical-grade chemicals, packaging and shipping supplies, fiberglass-reinforced containers, and first-aid kits. John Cole Chemical serves the biotech, commercial, and manufacturing industries, as well as municipal customers. The company was established in 1980 by John Cole and his wife.
