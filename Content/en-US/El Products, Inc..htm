Bright-idea company EI Products (formerly Austin Innovations) makes lights that look and feel cool. The company has developed a series of energy-efficient night-lights that stay cool to the touch. Its Power-Fail LimeLite runs on batteries for 72 hours after a power failure. Other products include night-lights and clocks sold under the LimeLite and Indiglo names. The company also makes a line of 110 volt and solar charged exit signs for commercial use. EI Products has a marketing agreement with GE to sell its products under the GE brand name to retail markets.
