Employees of Vie Cosmetics Group don't have a problem with taking their work home. Formerly known as Victory Corporation, and doing business as Virgin Vie At Home (cosmetics) and apparel outlet Virgin Ware, the make-up and skincare company markets Vie beauty products under the Vie at Home banner. Its wares are sold through parties hosted by home-based consultants, a retail website, and catalog. Founded in 1997, it was once part of Virgin Group and publicly traded on the London AIM until 2007, when it was sold to management. Leading the buyout, Ros Simmons owned 50% and chairman Ratan Daryani the other 50%. Vie Cosmetics was acquired in 2011 by Network World Alliance, owned by German entrepreneur Helmut Spikker.
