Canrig Drilling Technology manufactures top drive drilling systems for oil and gas exploration. The company is a leading supplier of AC top drives for deep-water drilling applications, as well as automated catwalks, floor wrenches, and control systems. It offers fixed and portable top drives in 175-, 275-, 350-, 500-, and 750-ton capacities for installation in most land-based and offshore rigs. Canrig, which was founded in Calgary, Canada, in the late 1980s, also provides engineering and field services throughout the world. A subsidiary of Nabors Industries , Canrig makes about 60% of its sales to other Nabors units. Canrig and Epoch Well Services, a drilling data and analysis supplier, merged in 2008.
