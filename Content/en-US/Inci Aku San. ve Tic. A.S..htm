Inch by inch, Inci Aku is becoming a major battery manufacturer to carmakers. The company makes heavy-duty lead acid batteries for cars, boats, RVs, and commercial vehicles. It also makes stationary batteries that supply uninterrupted power to telephone exchanges and security systems. Customers include Iranian vehicle manufacturer SAIPA and Russian carmaker Tagaz, as well as Volvo and Peugeot . Inci Aku has two manufacturing plants in Turkey that make 5 million batteries per year; another in Ukraine makes 300,000 batteries per year. Its sales and distribution company, Inci Energy, is located in Germany. The batteries are exported to more than 70 countries (but not to the US). Inci Aku is owned by the Inci family.
