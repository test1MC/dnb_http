Don't confuse this Oscar Mayer with the US Kraft Foods brand of the same name. Oscar Mayer Limited is one of the largest food processors of chilled ready meals -- producing approximately 2.5 million a week -- for sale to grocery stores J Sainsbury and Wm Morrison under their own private-label. Oscar Mayer, which operates three facilities in the UK, also makes 1.5 million frozen ready meals a week under the Ferndale Foods brand for Waitrose . The business started with Chairman John Bigwood and his family took over the Oscar Mayer UK franchise operations and hot dog brand. Oscar Mayer Limited later pushed into the prepared meals market.

Oscar Mayer is based in Chard, Somerset, and has other locations in Erith, Wrexham, and Grantham.

The company produces and sells bacon, hot dogs, cold cuts, and other products. Oscar Mayer also provides rope sausages, such as beef, turkey, and Italian herb sausages. Oscar Mayer's food processing facilities operate all day, every day of the week. Its nonstop shift enables the company to consistently meet customer requirements for timely, quality products, as well as fend off rival ready meal manufacturers.

Oscar Mayer supplies prepared foods to grocery stores, including Sainsbury , Waitrose , Morrison , ASDA , The Co-Operative Food , and Tesco .

The company's owners, John Bigwood and his family, also hold Rowan Foods, which contracts with foodservice businesses, as well as Ferndale Foods. Bigwood ranks among of the wealthiest people in Kent, England.
