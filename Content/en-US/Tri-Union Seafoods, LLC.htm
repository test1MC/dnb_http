Tri-Union Seafoods tries hard to unite seafood lovers under the Chicken of the Sea and other banners. The company, which does business as Chicken of the Sea International, makes and markets some of the top brands of canned tuna in the US. Tri-Union cans tuna (Albacore and light), salmon, crab, shrimp, oysters, clams, sardines, and mackerel for sale in North America. The company also offers value-added seafood products, such as frozen and shelf-stable pouches (including smoked salmon), and "peel & eat" cups (salmon and tuna). In addition to its own brands, Tri-Union also offers product-development and private-label services.

The company is based in San Diego, California and has a plant in Lyons, Georgia.

Tri-Union's shelf-stable seafood product portfolio ranges from tuna, salmon and sardines. The company caters to consumers who prefer to have healthy choices. To this end, Tri-Union offers its Genova brand of canned tuna and salmon. The gourmet-style line contains no additives or preservatives.

The company sells its products through grocery stores, convenience store, and big box stores to hospitals, restaurants, and schools.

In 2015 Tri-Union began a major project to combine its sustainability data systems and to share best practices.

Most of its canning operations have been located in Southeast Asia. The seafood supplier, however, has brought some of its canning operations back to US soil in recent years. It established a facility in Lyons, Georgia, in 2009.

Known as California Tuna Canning Company in 1914, Tri-Union has been a subsidiary of Thai Union Frozen Products since 2000.
