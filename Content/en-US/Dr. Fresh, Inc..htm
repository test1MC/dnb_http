Dr. Fresh isn't a doctor, but it does sell fresh breath and other oral health care products. The company makes and markets Firefly, a top kids' toothbrush brand in the US, as well as dental floss, breath freshener, mouthwash, power toothbrushes, toothpaste, and travel kits under the Reach, Dr. Fresh, and Binaca names. In addition to oral care, Dr. Fresh distributes personal and home care products, such as razors, soaps, wet wipes, air fresheners, humidifiers, and thermometers. They are sold to leading retail chain stores, including the likes of CVS , Kmart , Kroger , Safeway , Target , and Wal-Mart . Dr. Fresh boasts sales offices, distribution centers, and sourcing facilities in the US, Europe, and Asia. In 2017 consumer care products company High Ridge Brands (a Clayton, Dubilier & Rice portfolio company) acquired Dr. Fresh.
