This company wants to find out what music you like and help you tell the world. iLike operates a social music discovery service with more than 60 million registered users. The service and its applications allow users to discover and share music recommendations, playlists, and concert alerts. The iLike application is popular on such social networking sites as Bebo , Facebook , and Google 's iGoogle. The company also provides applications that integrate with Apple 's iTunes software and Microsoft 's Windows Media Player. iLike also runs GarageBand.com, a Web site that spotlights independent music.
