Continental Web Press wants its clientele to span the continental US. It offers four-color web offset printing, digital prepress, and bindery services at plants in Itasca, Illinois, and Walton, Kentucky. It also operates a sales office in New York City. Clients have included Time , Newsweek , and Sports Illustrated . The company also prints books, annual reports, catalogs, coupons, inserts, and brochures. To save a trip to the print shop, customers can upload files and view their proofs through the company's website. Continental Web Press is owned by president and CEO Ken Field and EVP Diane Field. The company was founded in 1973.
