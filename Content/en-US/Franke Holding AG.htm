Franke Holding understands that even a kitchen sink requires technical perfection. The company is one of the leading suppliers of kitchen sinks and much of the equipment that goes around them, such as faucets, ovens, vent hoods, and waste disposal systems. Franke also manufactures foodservice equipment, beverage containers (including beer kegs), coffee systems, aerospace and gas turbine parts, and gear for disinfecting medical equipment. Franke turned to acquisitions to spur rapid growth in its Washroom Systems and Coffee Systems divisions. Founded in the early 20th century, the Franke Group has operating subsidiaries in more than 40 countries around the world.
