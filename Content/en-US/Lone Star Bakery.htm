Round Rock doughnuts -- or, more properly, Round Rock Donuts -- are a Central Texas taste sensation. The yellow-orange creations bring doughnut enthusiasts from miles around to the Lone Star Bakery, established in 1926 and now known as Round Rock Donuts, after its most popular product. The doughnuts get their distinctive color from farm-fresh eggs, with their dark-yellow yolks, and a dash of Bakers Egg Shade food coloring. Round Rock Donuts also makes and sells cookies, pies, cakes, breads, and other pastries. The bakery actively solicits wholesale business, and it takes retail orders by phone for local delivery. Lone Star Bakery is owned and operated by Dale and Jan Cohrs.
