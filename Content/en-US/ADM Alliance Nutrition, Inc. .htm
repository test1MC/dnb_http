ADM Alliance Nutrition is a subsidiary of one of the largest processors of oilseeds, corn, wheat, and other agricultural commodities, Archer Daniels Midland (ADM). ADM Alliance supplies ingredients for feeds used primarily in the livestock industry. Its list of ingredients, including proteins, vitamin blends, and amino acids, are marketed to improve animal quality. They're also used in the production of feeds for poultry, beef and dairy cattle, horses, sheep, and swine. ADM Alliance also produces dog and cat food under the Proud Paws brand and health supplements, such as dewormers, probiotics, insecticides, and vitamin-enhanced and medicated pellets.

Illinois-based ADM Alliance has an international reach.

The company maintains manufacturing facilities in the US, Canada, Mexico, Puerto Rico, and China.

ADM Alliance serves the livestock industry. It makes and markets ingredients, such as proteins, amino acids, and vitamin blends, to be used in animal feeds. It also produces dog and cat food under the Proud Paws name.

ADM Alliance makes regular acquisitions to boost its capacity and expand its business.

In 2013 the company extended its reach in Puerto Rico by buying a pair of feed manufacturers -- NutriMix Feed Company and Granossa Corporation -- that gave ADM Alliance a feed mill with port access, extensive storage, and strengthened its foothold in the Caribbean.

In 2011 ADM acquired English River Pellets (ERP), a grain elevator and animal feed manufacturing business in Iowa.

Subsequently, ADM Alliance entered the liquid animal feed business with the purchase of Colorado-based Cattleman's Choice Loomix, a producer of liquid animal feed supplements and ruminants, and in mid-2012 Nebraska-based Liquid Feed Commodities, another maker of liquid feed animal supplements. Both acquisitions are anticipated to increase ADM's share of the Midwest cow/calf industry by providing competitively better service at a lower price.

The company grew from a merger in 2002 of MoorMan's (acquired in 1997 by its parent company) with several independent animal feed businesses: Consolidated Nutrition, Supersweet, Master Mix, and Tindle Feeds.
