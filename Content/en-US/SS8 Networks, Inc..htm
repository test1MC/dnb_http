SS8 Networks extends the long arm of the law across networks. The company provides lawful intercept and cyber monitoring products that communications service providers use to assist law enforcement agencies with electronic surveillance. It also provides forensic tools for law-enforcement agencies that chart communications activities, map locations, and create reports about evidence, among other tasks. Its products are compliant with the Communications Assistance for Law Enforcement Act (CALEA). Outside the US, SS8 has operations in a handful of other countries, including Dubai, Mexico, and the UK.

SS8 has provided more than 250 deployments in 30+ countries.

The company's AXS passive probe is used for IP networks that don't have an active intercept capability. The AXS probe can examine and assess packets while remaining independent of a network and therefore creating no danger of interference.

Useable with more than 100 network interfaces and 20+ delivery standards, SS8's Xcipio network interception product configures network traffic and signaling into a standard format and creates and delivers metadata in real time.

Intellego is an Internet monitoring application that monitors Internet exchanges, including email, online chat, VoIP communications, and Web browsing, as they happen in real time. Intellego provides both real-time viewing and analytics on a single screen.

SS8 maintains interoperability agreements with about a dozen companies, including Alcatel-Lucent , BroadSoft , Cisco Systems , and Nortel Networks .
