Reno de Medici (RDM) would like to help you pack. One of Europe's largest manufacturers of cardboard products, RDM is recycling its renaissance. RDM produces folding boxboard, uncoated board, and coated chipboard. After growing unwieldy through acquisitions and mergers, the company disposed of non-core operations in order to focus on manufacturing recycled cardboard. In 2008 the company merged with the European recycled cartonboard operations of Cascades S.A. , its largest shareholder. Products from both Cascades and RDM are sold through a common sales company, CAREO.
