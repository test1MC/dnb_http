This company is definitely not a fan of lip-synching. AKG Acoustics is a leading manufacturer of professional-grade audio gear and known for its high-quality microphones. Its mics are used for both studio recordings and live sound, as well as in broadcasting, film production, and intercom systems. AKG is also a noted headphone manufacturer for both the professional and consumer markets. In addition, the company makes headsets, audio signal processors, and related accessories. AKG is owned by US-based audio products manufacturer Harman International Industries . The company was founded in 1947 by Dr. Rudolf Goerike and Ernst Pless.
