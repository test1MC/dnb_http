This company doesn't like the image of Chinese rice farmers bent over a lush green field. It wants those farmers riding a brand new tractor. YTO Group (also known as China Yituo Group and formerly China First Tractor & Construction Machinery Corporation) makes agricultural machinery, tractors, combine harvesters, and road construction machinery including pavers and rollers. Other products include wind turbines and diesel generators. YTO Group's 20 subsidiaries and 50 factories export its wares to more than 70 countries worldwide. Subsidiary YTO International is responsible for the export business and runs overseas construction projects. YTO Group was established in 1955 and sanctioned by Mao Zedong.
