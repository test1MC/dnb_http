When traveling by air, departing on time is important. Even more important? Stopping on time. That's where Hydro-Aire, an operating brand of Crane , helps. Hydro-Aire makes anti-skid brake systems, landing systems, and fuel pumps for commercial, regional, business, and military aircraft. Not content to stop with stopping piloted planes, the company's brake systems are selected for the evolving market of military unmanned aircraft vehicles. Hydro-Aire also makes and sells hydraulic components including shutoff valves, check valves, and fluid reservoirs. The company, in business since 1943 and making anti-skid aircraft braking systems since 1946, contends that most of the world's aircraft use its braking systems.
