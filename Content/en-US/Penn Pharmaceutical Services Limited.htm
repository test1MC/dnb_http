Because big pharmaceutical companies can't do all of their own research and manufacturing, PCI Pharma Services (formerly Penn Pharmaceutical Services) is ready to help out. PCI Pharma offers a variety of products and services, including clinical trial supplies; formulation and analytical development; chemistry, limited batch manufacturing, and controls (CMC) consultancy; and qualified person (QP) audits. The company's facilities provide contract services for small- to medium-batch manufacturing. PCI Pharma also offers logistical management services, such as exporting and reporting.
