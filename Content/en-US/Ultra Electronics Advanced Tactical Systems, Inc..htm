Ultra Electronics Advanced Tactical Systems designs real-time tactical command and control systems for the US military and foreign governments. Its Air Defense Systems Integrator (ADSI) products are used to receive, forward, and display tactical information. Ultra Electronics also provides support and training services. The company's customers include all branches of the US Armed Forces. Formerly called Advanced Programming Concepts, the company was acquired by UK-based Ultra Electronics in 1999.
