Furman Foods has firm ideas about tomatoes and other food products. The Pennsylvania company has built a business producing a complete line of canned tomatoes and tomato products. It also offers canned beans; spaghetti, pasta, and pizza sauces; bean salads; vegetables; and ketchup and other condiments. The company's brand names include Furmano's, Conte, and Bella Vista. Furman Foods boasts customers in the foodservice (restaurants, schools, and hospitals), retail (supermarkets and grocery stores), export, branded, manufacturing, and private-label sectors along the US's East Coast. Furman Foods is a family-owned business founded in 1921 by J. W. Furman.

Based in Pennsylvania, Furman Foods supplies the East Coast with a variety of tomato products, condiments, beans, and vegetables.

Each year, Furman Foods farms more than 600 acres of its own land. Together, with its company-owned farms and those of its growers, 80% of its tomatoes are grown in Pennsylvania. Nearly half of these come from within 20 minutes of its processing facility. The rest of its tomatoes are grown within a four-hour truck drive to the plant, which allows Furman Foods to can them within hours of harvesting.

Furman Foods is a multichannel marketer, serving retailers and foodservice operators. It also supplies its products to exporters, manufacturers, and private labelers.

The company's products are found in Wal-Mart stores and retail grocery stores along the East Coast.
