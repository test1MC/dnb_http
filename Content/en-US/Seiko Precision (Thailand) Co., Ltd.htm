I shutter to think what customers would do without Seiko Precision (Thailand) Co., Ltd. (formerly Seiko P&C [Thailand] Co. Ltd.). Seiko Precision (Thailand) produces camera shutters, distance measuring units, control units, and injection parts for cameras. The company also manufactures time recorders and time stamps, as well as plastic injection molds and exterior parts for various Seiko products. To boot, it has a hand in making camera modules and lenses for mobile phones. Seiko Precision (Thailand) supplies sister Seiko companies and other personal electronics manufacturers in China, Japan, Korea, and numerous countries throughout Southeast Asia. Founded in 1988, the company is a subsidiary of Seiko .
