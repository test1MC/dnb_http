Sanwa Shutter intends to shut out the competition. The Japan-based manufacturer specializes in heavy-duty and lightweight shutters. Through its subsidiaries, Sanwa Shutter also makes steel and aluminum building materials such as sliding doors, metal fittings, and shutter- and window-related pieces. The company targets builders of commercial property and residential housing, and offers a bundle of maintenance services. Sanwa Shutter's US subsidiary, Overhead Door Corporation , produces residential, commercial, and industrial overhead doors and openers, commercial sectional doors, and Genie-brand remote-control garage doors. Sanwa Shutter is part of over 100 companies held by Sanwa Holdings Corporation.
