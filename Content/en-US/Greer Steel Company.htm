Greer Steel, one of the operating companies of Greer Industries , manufactures cold-rolled strip steel. The company processes both low-carbon and high-carbon steel into products in a variety of finishes and sizes. Greer Steel's strip products are used in the metal stampings, appliance, automotive, hardware, and hand tool manufacturing industries, among others. The company operates a manufacturing plant in Ohio and a service center in Michigan. Outside of steel production, Greer Industries is involved in everything from newspaper publishing to limestone mining to radio broacasting.
