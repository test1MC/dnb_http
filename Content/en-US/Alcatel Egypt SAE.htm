Alcatel Egypt does the bidding of its parent, telecom equipment firm Alcatel-Lucent , in the Middle East and Africa. The company operates in nearly 70 countries from Afghanistan to Guinea selling hardware for voice, data, and video transmission to telecom carriers and ISPs. Major customers include Pakistan Telecommunications , Dubai Metro, Etisalat , Vodafone , Nile Online, Saudi Telecom , and Orascom . Alcatel Egypt supports both fixed line and mobile operators, running a dozen mobile networks in infrastructure-challenged locales like Yemen, Algeria, and Afghanistan. Its also offers voice, video, and data services directly to customers in Kuwait and Pakistan. The company began operations in Egypt in 1986.
