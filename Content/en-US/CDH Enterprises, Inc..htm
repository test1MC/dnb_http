Flavors from the desert might not seem like something to be harvested, but Cheri would definitely disagree. CDH Enterprises, doing business as Cheri's Desert Harvest, makes southwestern-themed jellies, jams, syrups, candies, and bread mixes from such desert produce as prickly pear cactus, mesquite beans, tart apples, red limes, green and red chilies, and habanero and jalapeno peppers. The company's products can be ordered by bulk customers and individual consumers from the company's website. CDH was founded in 1985 by owner and president Cheri Romanoski.
