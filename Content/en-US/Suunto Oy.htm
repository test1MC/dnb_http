For those of us who like to get around and reach new heights or depths, there's Suunto. The company makes sports instruments for diving, running, cycling, and mountaineering, among other recreational activities. Suunto's product portfolio is led by its line of wrist-top computers -- wrist watches that provide an altimeter, barometer, thermometer, compass, and heart rate monitor. The firm also produces field and marine compasses, clinometers, training heart rate trackers, and wireless performance monitors, as well as Bare-branded wetsuits. Its products are distributed to about 60 countries. Founded in 1936, Suunto is a subsidiary of sporting goods manufacturer Amer Sports .
