ACW specializes in CEM. ACW Technology offers contract electronics manufacturing (CEM) services for makers of electronic equipment. The company offers complete product manufacturing, including printed circuit board assembly and test, box build and configuration, system test, packing, and global shipment. It also offers related logistics and aftermarket upgrade and repair services. The manufacturer's customers are primarily in aerospace and defense, broadcasting, communications, industrial, and security industries. Founded in 1989 by executive chairman Chris Woods, ACW Technology operates facilities in China, the UK, and the US.
