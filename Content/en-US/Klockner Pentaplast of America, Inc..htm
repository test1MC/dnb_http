It's not a police force, but Klöckner Pentaplast of America is here to preserve and protect. The company (KpA) is one of the largest manufactures of rigid plastic films and sheets for packaging and printing in North America. Uses for its products include pharmaceutical blister packaging, credit card production, food packaging, general-purpose packaging, shrink sleeve/label making, and stationery. The company has several manufacturing facilities in the US. KpA is a division of German plastic films producer Klöckner Pentaplast Group , which was founded in 1965 and is owned by private equity group Strategic Value Partners LLC.

Since taking root stateside in 1976, KpA has grown to operate facilities in Ohio, Virginia, and West Virginia.

KpA has diversified into manufacturing veneer coating and vinyl for furniture, as well as furniture components, such as door and cabinet fronts, and moldings, under name brand Pentadecor.
