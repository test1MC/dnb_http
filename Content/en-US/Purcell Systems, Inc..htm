It's not the United States Postal Service , but Purcell Systems makes sure that "neither rain nor hail nor sleet nor snow nor heat of day" stops its customers from safely operating their outdoor electric power and communications systems. The company makes outdoor power cabinets and enclosures and outside plant equipment systems that protect equipment from the elements. These cabinets are integrated with battery backup, climate control, DC power, equipment protection, surge suppression, and additional rack space. Founded in 2000, Purcell Systems was acquired by battery making giant EnerSys in late 2013.

Purcell Systems was acquired by EnerSys in October 2013.

Purcell Systems serves the cable, telecommunications wireless and wireline, and utility industries from locations in the US, Sweden, and Poland.

The company works directly with service providers, tower companies, and construction firms to provide customized on-site installations that support a wide range of power and environmental applications. It's all part of what Purcell calls its "Fanatical Service."
