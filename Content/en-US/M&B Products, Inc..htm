Whether it's with juice drinks, milk shakes, or just some plain ol' cold water, M&B Products can quench your thirst. The company bottles orange and other fruit juices, water, and dairy drinks and distributes them to schools, hospitals, hotels, and other foodservice venues in more than 30 US states. The company's flagship brand is Mr. J Premium Orange Juice. Other products include Mr. J Klear Splash water, a line of Mr. J frozen fruit juice dessert bars, and dairy and juice drinks with rotating ("limited edition") flavors such as strawberry kiwi and blue raspberry. M&B Products operates a production facility near Tampa, Florida. The family-owned company was founded in 1987 by Dale and Mary McClellan.
