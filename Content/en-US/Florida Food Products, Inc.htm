Don't let the name fool you. Florida Food Products doesn't stop at food ingredients, it also makes plant-based products for manufacturers in the personal care, beverage, pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, and agricultural industries. For the foodies, the company makes vegetable juice concentrates, blends, and powders, as well as dried fruit juices. In addition, it specializes in growing and processing aloe vera into liquids, powders, gels, and extracts for addition to consumer products. The company's brands include Veg Con, Veg Blend, Veg Dry, Juice Dry, Crystal Dry, and Aloe Con. Florida Food also produces an organic pesticide called INVITE that deters corn rootworm beetles.
