Choosy about online news? Take the YellowBrix road. The company provides real-time industry news, content analytics and syndication, and business intelligence solutions to corporate clients for websites, intranets, and wireless devices. YellowBrix's proprietary software offers customized content and tools covering news, sports, weather, and company information. Its sources include media outlets such as the BBC, USA Today , Washington Post , PR Newswire, and LA Times , as well as thousands of industry trade journals and magazines. YellowBrix once targeted Web startups seeking content, but has found a niche with larger corporate clients such as Clear Channel and Sprint . CEO Jeff Massa founded the company in 1997.
