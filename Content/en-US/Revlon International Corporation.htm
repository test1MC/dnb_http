A subsidiary of cosmetics giant Revlon , Revlon International markets its parent's makeup, fragrances, and skin care and personal care products. Brands include Revlon, Almay, Gatineau, and Ultima II. The company sells products through wholly owned subsidiaries established in about 15 countries outside the US as well as through several distributors and licensees worldwide. Revlon products are distributed to drugstores, hypermarkets, mass-volume retailers, general merchandise and department stores, and perfumeries. Revlon International's top markets include South Africa, Australia, Canada, the UK, and Venezuela. It generates about 40% of its parent's sales.
