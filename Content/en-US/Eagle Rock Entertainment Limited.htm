Eagle Rock Entertainment knows that rock and roll will never die. Through its Eagle Records label, the company releases new recordings by such artists as The Rolling Stones, Queen, and Peter Gabriel. Its Eagle Vision division releases music videos, DVDs, concerts, and documentaries from its 2,000 hour library. The company also owns the television and DVD rights to footage from nearly four decades of Montreux Jazz Festival performances (artists range from Bob Dylan to Radiohead). Other best-sellers include One Night Only by The Bee Gees and Classic Albums: The Doors by The Doors . Founded in 1997, Eagle operates offices in London, New York, Toronto, Paris, and Hamburg.
