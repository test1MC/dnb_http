PBM Graphics is a full-service commercial printing company with printing and fulfillment locations in North Carolina and South Dakota. The company offers a broad ranging of printing services, including card printing and printing for packaging, as well as binding and finishing and on-demand printing. In addition, PBM Graphics provides specialized services for advertising campaigns and direct mail marketing, along with warehousing and fulfillment services. Founded in 1983 by former CEO Terry Pegram, PBM Graphics is a subsidiary of Consolidated Graphics .
