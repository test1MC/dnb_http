Aldila has a heart of graphite. The company's passion is in making graphite golf shafts used in golf clubs under the Aldila brand name, and some prepreg lines for recreational and non-recreational use. Offerings include arrows and archery products, sold under the Victory Archery name. Aldila's golf shafts and other products are assembled and marketed worldwide by major and custom golf club manufacturers and sporting goods distributors. Callaway Golf , Ping , Acushnet , and TaylorMade-adidas represent more than a third of Aldila's sales. Aldila-made shafts are reportedly the #1 graphite shaft used by PGA TOUR players. The company, which operates facilities in China and Vietnam, generates some 40% of sales abroad.
