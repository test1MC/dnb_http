Jel Sert makes a variety of frozen treats, including Flavor-Aid and Wyler's powdered drink mixes; Fla-Vor-Ice, Otter Pops and Wyler's Italian Ice freezer pops; and MONDO Fruit Squeezers fruit drinks. Jel Sert also makes My*T*Fine and Royal pudding mixes, purchased from Nabisco . The company offers contract manufacturing services, as well. Jel Sert's products are available throughout the US and Canada. Founded in 1926, Jel Sert is still owned by the Wegner family. The company made its name in the early 1960s with its popular Pop-Ice "stick 'em in the freezer and squeeze 'em out of the tube" treats.

From its Chicago headquarters, Jel Sert operates its business across the US and Canada.

The company's known for making and marketing freezer bars, powdered soft drinks, dessert mixes, and ready-to-drink beverages. Jel Sert also licenses brands and manufactures under contract products from companies in the food, beverage, and health and wellness industries. Its Jel Sert Canada operation focuses on selling a wide variety of freezer bars, powdered drinks crystals, and pudding and jelly mix items exclusively for the Canadian market.

Jel Sert's manufacturing and distribution facilities are located in West Chicago, with manufacturing comprising 400,000 sq. ft. and distribution another 100,000 sq. ft. Jel Sert maintains a national warehousing, logistics, and distribution facility in Canada. The company is considering adding manufacturing capabilities in the country.

Besides making its own freezer pops, powdered soft drinks mixes, dessert mixes, and ready-to-drink beverages, Jel Sert makes products for others through licensing agreements and contract manufacturing deals.

Jel Sert serves the food, beverage, and health and wellness industries. It has a dedicated Canadian sales office supported by a national broker network.

The company, which takes its name from its first product (a jelly dessert mix called Jel Sert), has expanded and updated its products portfolio in recent years. It has added licensed freeze bars, including Soda Pops with Dr Pepper , 7UP , Orange Crush, and A&W Root beer flavors. It also debuted Diet Snapple Singles to-go powdered soft drink mix.
