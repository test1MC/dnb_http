Idaho Fresh-Pak, also known as Idahoan Foods, manufactures a number of different dehydrated potato products under the Idahoan brand for sale in retail food outlets and foodservice businesses across the US. Its products include instant mashed potatoes, hash browns, scalloped and au gratin potatoes, as well as potato ingredients such as flakes, shreds, and slices. Idaho Fresh-Pak's foodservice potato products are marketed and distributed by J.R. Simplot . In addition to potatoes, the company's foodservice products include frozen fruit, vegetables (from asparagus to zucchini), value-added pasta mixes, and microwaveable pancakes.
