Known as Europac, Papeles y Cartones de Europa uses recycled and natural fibers to manufacture paper, corrugated cardboard (in two, three, five, and seven layers), and packaging materials. Europac's products include kraftliner paper, recycled paper, white and brown testliners, and corrugated boxes. The company, which operates more than 30 industrial plants in Spain, France, and Portugal, serves clients in more than 15 countries, primarily in the European Union.
