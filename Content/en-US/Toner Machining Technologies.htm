Toner Machining Technologies (TMT) doesn't make toner for photocopiers; it manufactures holding fixtures, gages, lathes, boring mills, grinding machines, saws, inspection machines, welding equipment, and other special machinery. The company caters to the aircraft, automotive, electric power generation, heavy truck, medical, and nuclear industries. Customers include ArvinMeritor , Bombardier , Caterpillar , Eaton , Ingersoll-Rand , Makino Milling Machine , Northrop Grumman , Rockwell Automation , and TRW Automotive . TMT is owned by president Jim Toner and his wife, Iris.
