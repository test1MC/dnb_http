Ever wanted to record your children's dance recitals or cross-country road trip but shied away because video cameras appeared complicated and unwieldy? Pure Digital Technologies has simplified that for even the timid shutterbugs. The company's high-definition Flip digital camcorders are easy to use and fit inside your pocket. They feature on-board editing software and plug into a computer's USB port, enabling users to save videos on their PCs and Macs or share them online. Flip cameras are sold via Pure Digital's e-commerce site and by such retailers as Amazon.com , Best Buy , and Wal-Mart . To expand its presence in the consumer electronics market, Cisco Systems acquired the company for $590 million in 2009.
