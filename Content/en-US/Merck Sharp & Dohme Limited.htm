Merck Sharp & Dohme (MSD) is the UK subsidiary of American pharmaceutical manufacturer Merck . The company markets such Merck products as arthritis pain treatment Arcoxia (which is approved in Europe but not the US), high blood pressure medication Cozaar, osteoporosis drug Fosamax, and asthma treatment Singulair. It also operates its parent's largest manufacturing facility outside the US, making more than a dozen medicines at its Cramlington, Northumberland, plant. MSD's Hoddesdon headquarters handles sales, marketing, and administrative functions; it also includes a R&D center that runs clinical trials and performs research and development activities specializing in organic and synthetic chemistry.
