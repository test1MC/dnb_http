Elkem is one of Norway's largest industrial companies and a global supplier to the silicon products and solar energy industries. The company produces high-grade silicon for the solar and computer industries. Its principal products include solar-grade silicon, silicon, ferroalloys, carbon, and microsilica. Divisions include Elkem Solar, Elkem Silicon Materials, Elkem Foundry Products, and Elkem Carbon. State-run specialty chemicals manufacturer China National BlueStar (80%-owned by China National Chemical Corporation) acquired Elkem, formerly a subsidiary of Norwegian industrial conglomerate Orkla , in 2011. Elkem holds production facilities worldwide.
