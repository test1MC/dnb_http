Its roots are in Dixie, but its products are found coast to coast. Alabama Metal Industries Corporation (AMICO) manufactures industrial flooring and grating and expanded metal products. The company, a subsidiary of Gibraltar Industries , also offers complementary lines that include access floor systems; concrete forming products; fiberglass structural products; perforated metals; plastering, stucco, and drywall products; and security barrier products.
