Proview International has no problem with its products constantly being on display. The investment holding company cradles a global network of companies that manufacture and sell CRT (cathode ray tube) and LCD (liquid crystal display) monitors, as well as LCD and plasma display TVs. As the market has declined for CRT monitors, Proview has begun looking toward its flat-screen digital TV business for future growth. The company, established in 1989, makes and sells products under its own brands, including Proview, MAG, and EMC.
