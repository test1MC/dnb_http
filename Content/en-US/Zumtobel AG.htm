Zumtobel can always see the light. Through its many subsidiaries, the Zumtobel Group makes light fixtures and light management devices with commercial, industrial, and residential applications. The Zumtobel subsidiary manufactures products for professional indoor lighting. Thorn Lighting Group makes light fittings used for indoor and outdoor lighting. TridonicAtco produces electronic and magnetic lighting components for the car industry. The company's ZLG subgroup includes its Reiss (OEM production) and Luxmate (digital and electronic lighting) operations. Zumtobel gets more than 80% of its sales in Europe. The Zumtobel family owns around one-third of the company.
