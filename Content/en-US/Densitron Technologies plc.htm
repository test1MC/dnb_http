Densitron Technologies makes products that put information on display. The company makes flat-panel displays primarily for industrial instruments and consumer electronics. Its products include touch screen panels, TFT (thin-film transistor) displays, OLED (organic light-emitting diode) displays, LCD modules and glass panels, and LED backlighting products. Customers use the displays in medical instruments, mobile consumer electronics, digital cameras, handheld instruments, and industrial meters. It also offers custom manufacturing and design services.
