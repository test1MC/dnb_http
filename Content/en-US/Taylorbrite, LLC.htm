Taylorbrite develops, manufactures, and markets advanced lighting systems for the marine, truck, and RV markets. Products include cold cathode fluorescent lighting, LED lighting, solar warning lights, and fiber optic lighting systems. Taylorbrite, a joint venture of Taylor Made Group 's Taylor Made Technologies unit and Everbrite , addresses the problems inherent in the marine, recreational vehicle, and truck markets -- small-cramped areas, limited power capabilities, ambient heat issues, and constant shock and vibration -- to produce reliable light supply and functional features. The Taylor Made Group traces its beginnings to 1908 and the custom canvas trade.
