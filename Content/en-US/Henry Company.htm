Henry Company can put a roof over your head and pavement under your feet. It operates in the US through several divisions. Its Building Envelope Systems unit provides roofing, waterproofing, and air barrier systems. The Henry Building Products arm offers roof coatings and systems and driveway maintenance products. The Bakor division sells commercial roof coatings, cements, and driveway maintenance systems. Henry also makes specialty products such as marine sealants, rust-proofing products for the auto industry, green roof systems, and protective coatings for industrial and commercial applications.
