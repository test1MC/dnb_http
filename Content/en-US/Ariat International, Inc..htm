Basketball players have technologically advanced shoes, so why can't cowboys, cowgirls, and the world's top horse riders? Ariat designs and produces namesake Western and English style riding boots, work boots, casual shoes, denim, and riding apparel and accessories. Its footwear is designed to maximize performance, comfort, and style, and with competitive and casual horsemen in mind. The company sells its footwear in the US and Canada through more than 4,000 retailers, such as Cavenders Boot City , Tractor Supply , and Nordstrom , as well as through the catalogs of Orvis , State Line Tack , and others. It sells its equestrian footwear in the international marketplace to more than 600 retailers.

Based in California, Ariat operates in 30 countries in Europe, the Americas, Asia, and Australasia.

The company manufactures innovative performance equestrian footwear and apparel.

During the past few years, Ariat has extended its reach outside the US to the UK, Australia, Germany, Belgium, Holland, France, Luxemburg, and Scandinavia. It expanded presence in Europe took the form of the subsidiary Ariat Europe.

The company has maintained an average of 75% growth per year.

Looking to morph its business into a lifestyle company, such as Tommy Bahama has, with a popular brand that lends itself to a variety of products, Ariat has been adding to its products portfolio by debuting its own brand of denim for men and women. The equestrian company enlisted the help of designers that hailed from 7 for All Mankind and Levi's Premium to create a line of Ariat denim.

Ariat spends its marketing dollars by promoting its products through sponsorships and by staying in front of existing and potential customers. The company participates or sponsors more than 10,000 local, regional, national, and international events across both Western and English disciplines. It became the official boot of the Professional Bull Riders, sponsors the World Equestrian Games in Kentucky, and sponsors bull riders Adriano Moraes, Cord McCoy, and Wiley Petersen. To connect with customers virtually, Ariat developed a presence on Facebook and has been growing its number of fans.

Ariat products are sold via a network of retail outlets throughout the world and by online retailers. It's retail clients include Boot Barn, Cavendars Boot City , Country Outfitter, Drysdales, Nordstrom , Shepler's, and Zappos.com , among others.

Ariat changed hands in mid-2012. A shareholder group, which included LNK Partners and Brentwood Associates, sold the company to another group that comprises both company management and the Fisher family (who founded The Gap, Inc. )

The company was founded in 1990.
