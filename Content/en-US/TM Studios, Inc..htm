TM Studios has a jingle in its pocket. The company provides weekly compilations of new music releases for radio stations, and compiles copyrighted tunes by original artists in a variety of genres. TM Studios also writes and produces station identification jingles, background music, and sound effects that broadcasters use in commercials and promotions. The company has in-house recording and replication capabilities, and sells its CD and CD-ROM production libraries to radio and television stations, postproduction facilities, ad agencies, and Web sites worldwide. TM Studios is a subsidiary of independent radio network operator Triton Media Group .
