Timing is everything for customers of Colorado Time Systems (CTS). The company makes electronic display systems, from scoreboards to timers and wireless judging terminals, used in arenas, stadiums, and aquatic centers. CTS displays flash messages (GO TEAM!), as well as the game's score, the amount of time left in a game, or a competitor's scoring marks. CTS' Colorado Display Systems division makes the Olympex line, used for airport flight information, highway and transportation system alerts, sports-venue live action and replay display, and theater marquees. Products are sold through US and international distributors and resellers. CTS was established in 1972 by former employees of Hewlett-Packard .
