Silver Spring Foods has sprung up to become something big. The company is the world's top grower and processor of prepared horseradish and horseradish sauces. It also makes mustards, tartar sauces, and other condiments under the Silver Spring and other brand names. In addition to retail items, the company offers products  for foodservice, private-label, and industrial-food manufacturing customers. Its condiments are available worldwide. Silver Spring Foods, which grows its horseradish on more than 9,000 acres of farmland in Wisconsin and Minnesota, has been family owned and and operated since it was founded in 1929 by Ellis Huntsinger.
