Quintum Technologies makes equipment and software for VoIP networks. Its Tenor switches, routers, and session border controllers enable service providers to deliver VoIP services; they also let corporations with multiple office locations  link different phone systems and extend the functions of their traditional PBX phone systems to remote users. Quintum sells its products worldwide using a direct sales force and through a network of resellers and distributors. The company's product partners include 3Com , AT&T , Avaya , and Microsoft . Quintum Technologies is a subsidiary of Network Equipment Technologies .
