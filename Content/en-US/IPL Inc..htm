Formed in translation: L'Industrie Provinciale Enregistrée, French for today's IPL, manufactures and markets more than 400 injection- and extrusion-moulded plastic products for the agricultural, chemical, food processing, construction, waste management, and other industries. The company's industrial offerings include waste management/materials handling products (such produce crates) and engineered (custom-molded) products like heated hockey skate blades for Wayne Gretzky-owned Thermablade. Packaging products include industrial-size rigid containers and pails, as well as smaller containers for retail sales. IPL operates four factories in Canada. Novacap and Fonds de Solidarité (Fonds) acquired IPL in fall 2010.
