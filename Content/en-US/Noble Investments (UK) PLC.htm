Noble Investments is out to show that money itself is a fair and decent investment. Noble Investments (UK) invests in and trades items including rare UK and ancient European coins, including Greek, Roman, Byzantine, English, European, Modern Greek, and World Coins. Besides coins, the company trades banknotes and paper ephemera, military orders and decorations, and world stamps. It also offers certified appraisal and consignment services to collectors and potential clients. Noble Investments, which expanded its business by buying out rival A. H. Baldwin & Sons and Baldwin's Auctions, is the only publicly traded numismatic company on the London AIM Exchange. It is owned by Stanley Gibbons Group .

Nobel Investments operates several businesses. Its Baldwin's unit, established in 1872, focuses on numismatics while the company's Apex Philatelics auctions world stamps. Trading at Nobel Investments is conducted through wholly owned subsidiary A. H. Baldwin & Sons Limited, which runs auctions in London, New York, and Hong Kong.

Though previously strictly an in-house dealer and appraiser of coins, Noble Investments trades its shiny goods through a retail website to satisfy its some 45,000 collector clients.

Nobel operates in the UK, Europe, North American, and Asia.
