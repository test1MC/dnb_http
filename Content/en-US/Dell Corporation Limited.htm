Dell Corporation Limited provides the UK market with desktop and notebook personal computers, professional workstations, servers, and storage systems. Other products include inkjet and laser printers, Ethernet switches, and wireless networking gear. The company offers a range of services, including customer support, computer recycling, and financing. Dell also sells third-party products such as Microsoft applications. Dell serves the consumer, enterprise, education, government, and health care markets. The company sells directly and through retailers and other channel partners. Its UK-based customers have included such diverse organizations as Knowledge Technology Solutions , University of Oxford , and Rexam .
