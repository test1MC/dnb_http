Country Wood Products is no friend to the trees, but it does make friendly additions to your kitchen. The company handcrafts specialty hardwood bowls from maple and beech trees dating back to American colonial times. Using a drying and sanding process developed in the 1800s, the company's products include mixing bowls, salad bowls, cutting boards, serving utensils, napkin holders, pepper mills, and serving trays. The products, which customers can order through the Web site, come in a variety of sizes, and some feature decorative touches, including pewter handles and antique or stonewash finishes.
