For those who trek into the great outdoors and commune with wildlife, American Recreation Products (ARP) might be behind the items on their packing list. The company makes and markets outdoor gear including tents, backpacks, sleeping bags, outdoor clothing, outdoor furniture, and related camping accessories. ARP's brand names include Kelty, Royal Robbins, Sierra Designs, Slumberjack, Wenzel, and ISIS. Its products are sold through sporting goods stores and outdoor specialty stores, as well as through mail order via brand websites. ARP is a division of women's apparel manufacturer Kellwood Company , which in 2008 was purchased by private equity firm Sun Capital Partners (SCP).
