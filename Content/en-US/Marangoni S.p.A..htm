Marangoni -- where the rubber meets the retreader. Through its subsidiaries located in Italy and elsewhere, the company makes and distributes retreading materials and machinery, as well as new and retread tires. The company also sells its Ringtread system to other tire retreaders. Marangoni sells its retread tires (for cars, off-road vehicles, and more) and new tire products primarily in Italy and other European countries. Other products include tires for industrial machinery such as for fork lifts, excavators, and loading machines. Products are sold both wholesale and through about 20 sales outlets; Marangoni sells its wares in more than 100 nations worldwide.
