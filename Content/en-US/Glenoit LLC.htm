Glenoit doesn't quite like it when its customers cut a rug, particularly if it's one of theirs. The company designs and manufactures accent rugs, area rugs, and welcome mats. Its Ex-Cell Home Fashions subsidiary makes decorative home furnishings. The company's products are distributed to mass merchandisers, department stores, discount retailers, and specialty retailers nationwide. Barry Leonard, Glenoit's president and CEO, also heads up home furnishings maker Croscill . Both businesses are portfolio companies of private equity firm Patriarch Partners .
