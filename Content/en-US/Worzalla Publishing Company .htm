Though it got its start as a newspaper publisher, Worzalla Publishing Company now prints books and books only. The company provides pre-press, printing, binding, and fulfillmentÂ services through its Stevens Point Wisconsin facility. Worzalla produces a variety of books including juvenile, coffee table, medical, and directories offering its expertise for short or long runs. The company, founded in 1892 by brothers Joseph and Steven Worzalla, has both web and sheetfed capabilities, as well as digital and conventional prepress services. It also operates a full-service bindery. Worzalla is fully-owned by its employees.
