When its customers take a load off and sit down, Caldeira wants to make sure that they are comfortable. The company is a designer and manufacturer primarily of cushions and curtains, operating through four chief divisions: Caldeira UK, Caldeira China (known in China as Hao Sheng Textiles), Caldeira USA, and Caldeira Retail (its chain of some 15 home textile stores in the UK doing business as The Fabric Warehouse). The company stocks roll fabrics and supplies a large selection of leading brand names. Caldeira's divisions also sell cushion covers, seat pads, throws, bed sets, and window fashions.

Caldeira China represents the company's strongest effort to expand internationally. Located near Hangzhou in East China, the division makes curtains, throws, and seat pads for retailers in more than 20 countries around the globe. In addition, Caldeira's US division is based in New York City and sells UK and Chinese made products directly to retailers across the US.

Leading brand names supplied by Caldeira include Ashley Wilde, Bennetts Silks, Bill Beaumont, Blendworth, Crowson, Fibre Naturelle, Filigree, Fryetts, Gordon John, Sundour, Hardy, Jeff Banks, Montgomery, Prestigious, Richard Barrie, SMD, Style, and Voyage.

In 2014 the company introduced a range of products for Caldeira UK including tapestries, digital prints, woven chenilles and a wide range of stunning velvets. Its Caldeira China products that year included a commercial range of home textiles with jacquards, prints, chenilles, velvets and faux furs.

Tony Caldeira founded the company in 1991.
