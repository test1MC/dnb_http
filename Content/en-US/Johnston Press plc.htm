Johnston Press is a leading newspaper publisher with a collection of more than 300 papers serving predominantly small towns and communitees in the UK and Ireland. Its portfolio includes about 20 daily papers and 300 weekly titles. Johnston Press also distributes news and information through more than 300 websites. Other publishing operations include some lifestyle magazines and classified advertising circulars. In addition to its newspaper business, the company is a leading provider of commercial printing with about 10 printing plants throughout the country.
