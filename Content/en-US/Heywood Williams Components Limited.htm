Heywood Williams Components, doing business as Mila Hardware UK, literally opens the door to a slew of fenestration products. The company designs and supplies both window and door hardware to customers in the UK and Australia. It carries some 10,000 stock items in its Daventry distribution warehouse. Offerings, sold under its own name and other European brands, include multipoint locks, door cylinders and viewers, letterboxes, tools, adhesives, and door and window strikers. Heywood Williams Components also provides training and delivery services. The company was initially formed in 1980 as part of building products group Heywood Williams .
