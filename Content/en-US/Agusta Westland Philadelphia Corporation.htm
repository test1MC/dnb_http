Agusta Westland Philadelphia, the US branch of AgustaWestland , has sold and serviced its UK parent's helicopters and helicopter parts since 1981. Agusta Westland Philadelphia manages the US headquarters as well as parts supply depot and full-service repair facility for customers in the Americas. In addition to sales and services for AgustaWestland products, it provides training and technical support, and helicopter customization and local maintenance for the AW 119, AW 139, and AW109 Power and Grand models. The company also procures US and Canadian components for its parent's European operations.

Since taking off stateside, Agusta Westland Philadelphia has expended to service growing demand for AgustaWestland's commercial lineup. It is AgustaWestland's third main helicopter final assembly workspace, and a primary product support depot for the Americas' market.

Agusta Westland Philadelphia customers are largely corporate, emergency medical, law enforcement, offshore, and utility businesses.

The company grows by introducing new and improved models of helicopters. In 2014 it established an assembly line for the new generation AW169 helicopter at its AgustaWestland Philadelphia facility located at the Northeast Philadelphia Airport. The new model will be delivered to its first customer in 2015. The new production line will aim to produce about 20 AW169 helicopters per year by 2017. With the commercialization of the new model, the company will be phasing out the production of its AW119.
