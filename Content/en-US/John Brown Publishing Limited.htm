Customers of John Brown Publishing aren't looking to go under ground. The company is one of the largest contract publishers in the UK. It specializes in publishing customer magazines (publications produced on contract for companies) designed to strengthen ties with consumers. John Brown Publishing has produced publications for clients such as Virgin Group and NatWest . The company also produces catalogs and other marketing materials, manages direct mail efforts, and develops digital marketing and social media campaigns. John Brown Publishing was founded in 1987.

The company has offices in London, South Africa, Dubai, and Hong Kong.

John Brown Publishing is focused on clients that come from industries such as food, fashion, home, health, finance, and children's products.
