SHS Group is one of the UK and Ireland's largest consumer goods holding companies. The group owns a dozen companies known for such brands as Merrydown hard cider, Bottlegreen cordials, and WKD fruit-infused vodka drinks (made by Beverage Brands). Others lines include British Pepper & Spice seasonings, Clayton Love 1st Choice frozen foods, Gordon's condiments, and Maguire & Paterson matches. Caledonian Bottlers produces drinks for SHS companies and others. Through subsidiary SHS Sales & Marketing, SHS also distributes brands such as Nivea, Jordans, and Finish to retailers. Wait, there's more. The company also provides outsourcing for logistics, customer service, business insight, finance, and customer marketing.

SHS conducts business in four divisions. tHE Condiments & Sauces Division, which trades under British Pepper and Spice, specializes in processing, packing, and marketing of herbs, spices, seasonings, and wet condiments. Its Corporate Services Division handles corporate and financial functions for the group. The Drinks Division makes ready-to-drink beverages in the UK. Sales & Marketing provides a range of business services for clients.

SHS reported revenue of £374 million in 2014, off from £393.3 million in 2013. Profit after tax was also down, to £11.3 million in 2014 from £11.9 million.
