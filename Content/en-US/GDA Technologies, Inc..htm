GDA Technologies offers a good deal of design and development services. The company provides electronic design services for everything from semiconductor chips to printed circuit boards to embedded software to complete systems. GDA's designs appeared in a variety of high-tech goods, including PCs and servers, networking devices, and audio and video gear. The company's customers are such heavyweights as Microsoft , Sony , and Toshiba . GDA, which was founded in 1996 (its name came from "global design automation"), has operations in India and the US. GDA Technologies is a wholly owned subsidiary of Larsen & Toubro Infotech Limited .
