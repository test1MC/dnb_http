How can you go on a road trip and still sleep in your own bed every night? Newmar knows. The company manufactures fifth-wheels, Class A motor homes (gas- and diesel-powered), and diesel-powered motor coaches. Nameplates include Bay Star, Canyon Star, Cypress, Dutch Star, Essex, King Aire, Mountain Aire, Torrey Pine, Ventana, and X-Aire. Newmar's vehicles feature dual ducting air-conditioning systems, dishwashers and refrigerators, icemakers, ovens, full bathrooms with tubs and/or showers, and hardwood cabinetry. It provides service support for both Newmar and non-Newmar coaches. Mahlon Miller owns the company, which was founded in 1968 by Marvin Miller (no relation to Mahlon) and Marvin Newcomer.
