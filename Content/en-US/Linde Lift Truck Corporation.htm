Need a lift? If you're on a pallet, Linde Lift Truck can help. The company makes forklifts and other material handling equipment including hydraulic control devices, transmission systems, and warehouse equipment. The company's line of forklifts includes models powered by diesel, electricity, natural gas, and propane. The company provides sales and service from about 200 locations across North America. Linde Lift Truck had been part of KION Group, Linde 's forklift holdings. In late 2006 Linde sold KION to a group of investor including Kohlberg Kravis Roberts & Co. and Goldman Sachs Capital Partners for about €4 billion.
