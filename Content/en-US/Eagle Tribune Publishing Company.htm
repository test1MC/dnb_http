You might say this company keeps an eagle-eye on the news in northern Massachusetts and southern New Hampshire. Eagle-Tribune Publishing publishes four daily newspapers in Massachusetts, including The Salem News , the Gloucester Daily Times , and the Daily News of Newburyport , as well as its flagship The Eagle-Tribune (North Andover). It also publishes several weekly papers serving in the region. The company traces its roots back to its first paper,Â the Lawrence Daily Eagle , which was formed in 1868. Community Newspaper Holdings (CNHI) acquired Eagle-Tribune Publishing from the Rogers family in 2005.
