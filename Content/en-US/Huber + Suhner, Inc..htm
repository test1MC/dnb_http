It's in Huber + Suhner's best interest that you stay connected. The company is the North American subsidiary of Switzerland's HUBER SUHNER AG . It manufactures radio-frequency (RF) and microwave cables, connectors, assemblies, antennas, and lightning protection for the automotive, communications, industrial, instrumentation, medical equipment, military/aerospace, and transportation industries. The company also manufactures fiber-optic cables, as well as other wire and cable products. Huber + Suhner has distributors, manufacturers' representatives, and sales offices across the US, and in Canada.
