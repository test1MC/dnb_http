Cirrascale (formerly Verari Technologies) hopes its cloud future is more high-flying than wispy. The company develops blade-based platforms for cloud computing and storage for data centers. Its BladeRack platforms, the largest supporting 96 blades, are cooled with its patented Vertical Cooling Technology, which it claims makes its racks denser and more efficient. Cirrascale also offers its FOREST Container modular data center, which supports equipment from other manufacturers. Additionally, it provides server, GPU, and workstation blades, desktop consolidation, and rackmount servers, as well as licensing of its products and technology.
