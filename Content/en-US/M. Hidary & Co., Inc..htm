M. Hidary knows that clothes make the man...or boy...or girl. The company makes apparel for men and children under numerous brand names, including Bon Bebe, Russell , Everlast , Amy Byer, Maui & Sons, and Pacific Trail. M. Hidary also makes licensed items under a dozen agreements for Marvel Entertainment and Disney , as well as apparel under private label. It sells its products through big box chains ( Wal-Mart , Target ), specialty stores ( Sports Authority ), national retailers ( Sears ), and catalogs (Global Sports Interactive). The company, founded in 1948 by Max and Moe Hidary, operates under The Hidary Group, an investor group that has holdings in consumer products, real estate, technology, and financial services.
