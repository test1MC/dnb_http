Gaymar Industries happily produces products to ease your pain. The company makes a variety of products for pressure ulcer management, temperature therapy management, muscle and joint pain, and animal health. Wound management products include mattress overlays, chair pads, gel mattresses, and powered patient-rotation mattresses. Its temperature therapy offerings include warming and cooling systems, including blood and fluid warming and external therapy for human and veterinary applications. The company also offers educational seminars and technical support. Gaymar serves US and international clients worldwide. The company, which was founded in 1956, is owned by medical device giant Stryker .
