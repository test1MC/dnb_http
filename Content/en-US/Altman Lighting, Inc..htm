Trusting Shakespeare's insight, "All the world's a stage" and a business opportunity for Altman Lighting. The company manufactures lighting equipment designed for theatrical illumination of stages, stores, museums, churches, even outdoor venues. Altman Lighting's broad assortment offers commercial, industrial, and institutional fixtures including electric lamps, bulbs, and tubes for use in film and video and architectural markets, among others. Extending its reach, the company operates a rental subsidiary, too, Altman Rentals. It restructured in 2009 to cope with the economic slump. Charles Altman founded the company in 1953. It is owned by the Altman family, led by his son, Robert, and grandson Randall Altman.
