The namesake for Carroll Shelby International (CSI) is one of the world's great walking brand names. From his days with the Aston Martin racing team in the 1950s to his work with Ford Motor , Carroll Shelby is an automotive living legend. CSI's Shelby American (formerly Shelby Automobiles) subsidiary builds Shelby Cobra component cars (kit cars with no engine or drivetrain), which it sells through a network of 20 dealers in North America and Europe. CSI also licenses the Shelby name for use by makers of everything from auto accessories and apparel to collectibles and golf gear. Carroll Shelby has rejoined forces with Ford to build a 550-horsepower Shelby Expedition SUV and a Shelby Mustang.
