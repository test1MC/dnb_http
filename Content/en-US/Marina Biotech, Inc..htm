Marina Biotech is a clinical-stage drug discovery and development firm with a focus on rare diseases. company is developing treatments using gene silencing approaches such as RNA interference (RNAi) and messenger RNA (mRNA) blocking. It conducts clinical and preclinical tests on RNAi treatments for a variety of cancers and precancerous conditions. Marina Biotech has built up its holdings by licensing peptides with potential RNAi therapy applications from numerous pharmaceutical companies and universities including the University of Michigan . In late 2016, the struggling Marina Biotech merged with IthenaPharma, a firm with a focus on pain, arthritis, hypertension, and cancer.
