If you have to know where you've been to know where you're headed, Ficosa International should be in good shape. The company is a maker of interior and exterior rear-view mirrors for automobiles, motorcycles, heavy-duty trucks and agricultural vehicles, buses, and RVs. Ficosa's line of automotive components also includes parking brakes, gearshifts, vehicle communication systems, antennas, security systems, drive cables, air intake products, parts for vehicle doors and seats, and fluids systems. Founded in 1949, the company has operations in about 20 countries worldwide. In 2017 Panasonic announced it was buying up to almost 70% of Ficosa.
