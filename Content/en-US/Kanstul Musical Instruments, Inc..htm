Kanstul is the likely culprit behind the bugle corps blaring a tune to get sleepy cadets up and at 'em. Kanstul Musical Instruments makes brasswind instruments (bugles, cornets, euphoniums, flugelhorns, trombones, trumpets, and tubas), mouthpieces, mutes, and related accessories. The company's signature line of brass instruments include trumpets, flugelhorns, piccolo trumpets, and cornets. The musical instrument maker maintains manufacturing facilities and a showroom in Anaheim, California, and sells its instruments through authorized dealers worldwide. The company was founded by Zigmant Kanstul in 1981; Jack Kanstul is the firm's spokesperson.
