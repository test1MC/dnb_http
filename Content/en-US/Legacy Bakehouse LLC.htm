Legacy Bakehouse wants to be remembered for its scrumptious snacks. The company makes bread-based baked, toasted, and fried snack products, such as rye chips, bagel chips, and snack mix. Its products are manufactured at its 59,000 sq. ft. facility in Waukesha, Wisconsin, and are sold under the Pinahs brand name. The snacks are distributed to retailers, catalogs, and food service companies. Legacy Bakehouse also offers private label custom products, bulk sizing, and research and development services. The company traces its roots back to 1917, and is owned by CEO Mike Heyer.
