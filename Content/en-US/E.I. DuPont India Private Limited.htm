DuPont began operations as an explosives maker in 1802, and black powder imported from India was essential to the early success of the company. Today E.I. DuPont India's products are still booming, as its business lines include the manufacture of crop protection products, engineering polymers, and automotive paints, as well as R&D for crop seed production. DuPont India also markets its parent company's wide range of products for the health care, construction, electronics, and transportation markets. DuPont opened its first office on the subcontinent in 1974 after India adopted a more multinational friendly economic development policy, and commenced manufacturing operations in the mid-1990s.
