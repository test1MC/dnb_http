Mitsumi Electronics, a subsidiary of Mitsumi Electric Co. , distributes radio frequency devices (RFD) and receiver/transceiver modules -- AM/FM, SDARS (satellite digital radio), Bluetooth, Wifi, Wimax, and others -- to systems manufacturers. Additional electronic components include circuits, coils, connectors, imagers, motors, and switches. With research and development facilities based in California, Mitsumi operates manufacturing plants throughout the world and distribution centers in the US (California, Illinois, and Georgia) and Canada. Its Michigan facility designs and tests vehicle antennas. The Japanese company was founded in 1954 and developed international production throughout the 1990s.
