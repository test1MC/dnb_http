Concord Fabrics caters to the crafty. The decorative textile manufacturer mainly produces polyester circular knits and fleece that appeal to patchwork and quilting enthusiasts. The company's Andover Fabrics unit specializes in fabrics used in quilts, made in a variety of colors and patterns. Its Makower UK unit, headquartered in Oxfordshire, designs and produces cotton fabrics for quilters. Andover Fabrics sells to apparel makers and retailers in the US, Canada, and South America. Makower prints its products in South Korea and distributes them throughout Europe from its warehouse in Oxfordshire. Concord Fabrics is owned by Safer Textiles.
