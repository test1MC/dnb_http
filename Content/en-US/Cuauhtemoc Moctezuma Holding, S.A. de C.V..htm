Looking for something frothy and flavorful south of the border? A subsidiary of international brewing giant Heineken , Cuauhtémoc Moctezuma Holding makes, markets, and distributes beer from six breweries in Mexico. Its branded beers include Carta Blanca, Dos Equis, Tecate, Bohemia, and Sol. The Heineken label was added to the lineup when its namesake acquired Cuauhtémoc Moctezuma (formerly FEMSA Cerveza) in early 2010 from Fomento Económico Mexicano (FEMSA). Cuauhtémoc Moctezuma controls more than 40% of Mexico's beer market. In addition to brews, the company produces glass bottles, aluminum cans, and crown caps through its subsidiaries Fábricas Monterrey (FAMOSA) and Sílices de Veracruz (SIVESA).
