Geberit's products allow clean water in and take waste water out. Through its global subsidiaries, the company makes two primary product lines for residential, commercial, and industrial applications: sanitary systems (which include pneumatic and electrical faucets, flush actuators, water-saving and bidet-style toilets, and installation mounting fixtures) and interior and exterior piping systems (which include plastic pipes and fittings for drainage and supply). It also trains plumbers and architects at its 25 training centers. Established in 1874, Geberit has offices in over 40 countries, with sales focused on Europe and growth opportunities in North America and Asia.
