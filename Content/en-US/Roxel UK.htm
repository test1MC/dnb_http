The thrust of Roxel UK's business is rockets. The company, a joint venture between SAFRAN and MBDA , makes rocket motors, propulsion units, gas generators, and related products used in tactical and cruise missiles, artillery rockets, guided bombs, and decoy systems, among others. Roxel UK also offers propulsion systems design, safety and hazard analysis, and training and support services. In addition, the company operates a variety of facilities for testing its products. Roxel UK was established in 2003 following the merging of Royal Ordnance Rocket Motors of the UK and CELERG of France.

Roxel targets the market segments of surface-to-air missiles, anti-ship missiles, anti-tank missiles, air-to-air missile, and others.

Headquartered in France, Roxel owns three production facilities in France (Saint Médard, Bourges, La Ferté Saint-Aubin) and one in the UK (Summerfield). The company has propulsion systems operations across South America, Middle East, Europe, Asia, and North America.

Roxel often grows its business through the launching of new manufacturing facilities in strategic locations. In 2014 Roxel began building a new plant in Indonesia. The plant will produce extruded double base (EDB) propellant and composite propellant for rocket motors and spherical powders used in small and medium ammunition.
