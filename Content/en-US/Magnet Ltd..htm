Creating an irresistible pull toward upscale kitchens and appliances is one of Magnet's goals. A UK kitchen design and furniture manufacturer, the company -- through its Magnet Retail unit -- sells complete kitchens, cabinets, flooring, and other interior products through more than 175 showrooms. It also offers one-on-one design consultations, home surveys, and installations to retail customers. The company's Magnet Trade business operates some 160 branches that sell its products to independent craftsmen and professional builders. In addition to these brick-and-mortar businesses, Magnet supplies third parties with kitchens through its Interior Solutions division. Founded in 1918, the company has been owned by Swedish firm Nobia since 2001.

Magnet offers three collections of increasing price: Simply, Purely, and Uniquely. The company makes kitchens in 50 different styles.

Magnet's partners include AEG, Astracast, Caple, Electrolux, Whirlpool, Smeg, Zanussi, and Schock.
