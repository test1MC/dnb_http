If you're presently surrounded by cubicle walls, you might have Cano to thank. The company manufactures office furniture, including modular office systems, seating, tables, and storage units, at its facilities in Massachusetts. It also offers custom design and manufacturing services. Products are sold under the Canospace, Executive, Infinity, and Performance Seating brand names. Customers include FORTUNE 500 companies, government agencies, and individuals in search of home office essentials. The late Odd Bjorn Kapstad founded Cano in 1971. His sons, Jan (president) and Bjorn (VP), now run the family-owned company.
