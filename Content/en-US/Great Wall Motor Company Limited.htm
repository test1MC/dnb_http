You've heard of Chevy and GMC, but how about a pickup truck made by Great Wall Motor (GWM)? The Chinese automaker produces a variety of sedans, pickup trucks, SUVs, multipurpose vehicles (MPVs), and even a "stretch" limousine that is 22 feet long. The company has four vehicle production facilities and a production capacity of 800,000 units per year. China accounts for more than 90% of sales, but GWM has exported vehicles to regions like Europe, Africa, Central and South America, the Middle East, and to other countries in the Asia Pacific region.
