TOMY wants us all to have a long and happy childhood. The company manufactures toys, games, and apparel for children and infants and is one of Japan's leading toymakers. About 90% of  its toys and games are sold in Asia, with Europe and the US accounting for the rest. TOMY's key toy product lines include baby & toddler, bath, preschool, and Transformers. TOMY also makes such game products as Screwball Scramble, Pop up Pirate, and Rumble in the Jungle. Founded in 1924, TOMY operates in Japan, China, Hong Kong, Thailand, France, the UK, and the US. Seeking to expand its brand portfolio and global reach, TOMY acquired US-based baby products makers RC2 and Boon as well as supplier Keen Distribution in 2011.
