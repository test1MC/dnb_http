For those nuts and bolts giving you a hard time, Channellock makes the right tools for the job. The company makes and markets 120 sizes and types of pliers, wrenches, screwdrivers, and other non-powered hand tools. Channellock sells its products to more than 4,000 wholesalers and retailers in the US and abroad. The signature color of the firm's tool grips is trademarked as CHANNELLOCK BLUE. It holds several other trademarks, such as PermaLock, LITTLE CHAMP, GRIPLOCK, and TOG-L-LOK, among others. Channellock, founded in 1886 by blacksmith George B. DeArment as the Champion Bolt & Clipper Company, is now run by the fifth generation of the DeArment family.
