No paper tiger, Mitsubishi Paper Mills (MPM) claims to be the only manufacturer in the world to tackle the entire paper making process -- from wood selection to resign coating of base paper. Founded in 1898, the company produces, processes, and sells coated paper for printing, wood-free paper, and publishing paper for books to brochures. Its lines include communication paper, digital imaging media, as well as pulp and specialty lines. MPM also sells printing systems, printing plates, and specialty printing materials. The company has operations in Japan, Germany, and the US. MPMs' core market is Asia, however it exports paper products worldwide. It also operates sports facilities, warehouses, and factories.
