Don Miguel Mexican Foods helps tastebuds say "Olé!" The company makes and markets frozen and fresh Mexican meals and snacks for sale in the retail market. Its products include Don Miguel-branded burritos, chimichangas, empanadas, enchiladas, tacos, taquitos, tamales, quesadillas, party trays, and family-style dinner entrees. Don Miguel's customers include convenience stores (such as 7-Eleven ), as well as wholesale clubs (the likes of Sams Club and BJ's ) and supermarkets. The company manufactures its products at a Dallas facility. Founded in 1908, Don Miguel is owned by MegaMex Foods , a joint venture between Hormel Foods and Herdez del Fuerte , which bought the company from a private equity firm in 2010.

The late 2010 acquisition paired Don Miguel's Mexican-flavored appetizers, snacks, and other items with MegaMex's popular La Victoria and Embasa brands, among others.

Based in Dallas, Don Miguel maintains both manufacturing and distribution facilities in its home city.

Don Miguel is a multi-channel marketer. The company sells its products through vending machines, convenience stores, wholesale clubs, and supermarkets.
