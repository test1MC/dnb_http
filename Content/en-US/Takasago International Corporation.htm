The flavor of success is a sweet one for Takasago International. The company is a leading flavor and fragrance developer, manufacturer, and supplier in North America, Europe, and especially the Asia/Pacific region. Through four business divisions, it offers flavors and extracts for the food and beverage industry; fragrances for perfumes, cosmetics, and other household and personal care products; aroma chemicals for fine fragrances; and fine chemicals for pharmaceuticals and electronics. Founded in 1920, Takasago International maintains offices, production sites, and R&D facilities in some 24 countries around the world. Subsidiary Takasago International (U.S.A.) is a US-based flavor maker and seller.
