A unit of health care giant Johnson & Johnson (J&J), Johnson & Johnson Medical comprises the UK operations of a number of J&J's medical equipment and diagnostic subsidiaries. The company sells sutures and other surgical and medical products made by J&J's Ethicon unit, including women's health products (under the Gynecare brand), skin glue (Dermabond), biosurgery products, and hospital disinfectants. Johnson & Johnson Medical also handles the UK marketing business of J&J's orthopedic device firm DePuy and surgical instrument maker Ethicon Endo-Surgery .

Ethicon Products is a global leader in developing, manufacturing, and marketing wound closure and reinforcement products such as sutures, skin adhesives, hernia repair technologies, and wound drainage lines.

Johnson & Johnson Medical has a number of contracts with the UK National Health Service (NHS) to supply orthopedic, cardiovascular, endoscopic, and other surgical equipment and consumables to the country's government-run medical facilities.The firm also serves a number of hospital group purchasing organizations.
