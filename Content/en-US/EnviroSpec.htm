It all comes out in the wash for Hi-Tech Jetting Technologies. Primarily though its EnviroSpec division, the company sells mobile pressure washing equipment, parts, solvents, and soaps. Among the items sold by the company are pumps, fire cannons, ball valves, nozzles, check valves, chemical filters, battery accessories, chemical injectors, and pressure washers. The products are used to clean building exteriors, degrease restaurant exhaust hoods, wash trucks, and clean concrete surfaces. Hi-Tech Jetting Technologies is stationed in Georgia and primarily caters to the southern US.
