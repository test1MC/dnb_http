Alpha Sensors, doing business as Alpha Technics, designs, develops, manufactures, and markets a wide range of standard and precision negative temperature coefficient (NTC) thermistor (thermally sensitive resistor) components and assemblies, RTDs (resistance temperature detectors), HVAC sensors, industrial probes, and glass-encapsulated and epoxy thermistors, primarily for industrial, life sciences, and medical applications. Alpha Technics also provides calibration services for its products. The company was established in 1979.
