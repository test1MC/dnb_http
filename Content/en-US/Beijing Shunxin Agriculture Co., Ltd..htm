Beijing Shunxin Agriculture produces and exports spirits and vegetable and fruit juices throughout Asia and beyond. Its spirits are marketed under the Huadeng and Niulanshan brand names, while its juices are typically branded as Qianshou products. The company additionally processes pork and raises livestock. Beijing Shunxin retails its products through some 347 juice outlets, 439 delicatessens, and more than 1,400 raw-food outlets. Its products are also sold in 17 provinces in China as well as Hong Kong, South Korea, Singapore, Mongolia, Malaysia, Brunei, Canada, and the US.
