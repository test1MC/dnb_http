Pull up a chair for a fireside chain. Fireside Coffee's instant beverages include regular, decaffeinated, and sugar-free coffee and chai tea, all of which are available in many gourmet flavors. Among the company's coffee flavors are chocolate-covered cherry, vanilla nut fudge, butter rum, and coconut cream. Its chai tea selection offers chocolate, spiced, and vanilla. Fireside's products are available to wholesalers throughout the US and via the company's Web site. (It ships its tea and coffees all over the world.) The company was founded in 1988 by Carol Davis, who started her business after her homemade coffee blends garnered rave reviews from friends and relatives.
