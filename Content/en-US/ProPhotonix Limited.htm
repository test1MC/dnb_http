ProPhotonix (formerly StockerYale) lights the way with lasers and LEDs. The company makes light-emitting diode (LED) modules used in semiconductor manufacturing, machine vision, biomedical, and other applications. It also makes custom LED modules for OEMs, systems integrators, and end users with unique lighting needs. Its Photonic Products, Ltd. (PPL) subsidiary makes custom laser modules and other electro-optical subassemblies and optoelectronic components based on semiconductor laser diode technology. It also distributes precision optical lenses from Panasonic to OEMs in the industrial, medical, scientific, and defense markets. ProPhotonix has operations in Ireland, the UK, and the US.
