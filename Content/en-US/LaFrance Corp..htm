"Viva LaFrance!" would make a nice nameplate, and this company could design it. LaFrance Corp. makes nameplates, trim, plastic enclosures, metal letters and other products through several divisions. Its Benmatt Industries unit makes a range of customized products including key fobs, license plate frames, and trailer hitch covers that are used as promotional items by auto dealerships. The company's J.A.T. Creative Products division provides products like bottle openers, bottle and can huggers, and keychains to promote and advertise companies and special events. PacTec is a producer of standard and custom modified plastic electronic enclosures.

In addition to its headquarters in Concordville, Pennsylvania, the company has an 85,000 sq. ft. manufacturing and distribution facility in Sarasota, Florida. Its Asian headquarters is located in Shenzhen, China, and LaFrance has an additional office in the country located in Guangzhou. Two other international offices are located in Tokyo and Hong Kong.

LaFrance offers metal letters, aluminum nameplates and trims, stainless steel logos, coined aluminum, domed products, die cast nameplates, plastic products, custom modified plastic electronic enclosures, license plate frames, key fobs, and on-car advertising and promotional products.

The company operates through several divisions. Benmatt Industries manufacturers key fobs, license plate frames, and trailer hitch covers that are used by auto dealerships. J.A.T. Creative Products provides bottle openers and bottle and can huggers, while PacTec is a leading producer of standard (more than 350 sizes) and custom modified plastic electronic enclosures.

LaFrance's Industrial Design works with clients' product placement and brand strategy when supporting the creation of designs. It utilizes a variety of 2D and 3D modeling programs to create and review the design with our customers prior to creating the specific parts.

The company serve a diverse range of consumer and professional product manufacturers who produce such items as computers, motorcycles, appliances, cell phones, eye ware, and cosmetics. Customers have included Dell , Dirt Devil, LG , Mizuno , and Zyliss.

LaFrance was founded in 1946 by Joseph Teti.
