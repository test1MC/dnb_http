CoCo Communications is good to have around in an emergency. The company develops networking equipment (Mesh Routers) and software (CoCo Node) designed to help first responders (police, fire fighters, and emergency medical personnel) communicate more effectively. Its products bridge technical gaps between varying communications systems enabling users to transmit voice, video, and data over multiple networks. CoCo's technology links public safety networks, commercial carrier lines, the Internet, and satellite networks. Clients have included local, state, and federal agencies in the US such as the Coast Guard and the Transporation Security Administration . CoCo has satellite offices in Dallas, Texas and Laurel, Maryland.
