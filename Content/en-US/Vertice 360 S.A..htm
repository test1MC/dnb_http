Vértice 360 has all the angles covered when it comes to visual media. The Spanish holding company is divided into four segments -- cinema, theater, television, and audiovisual services, but its focus is on film. Vértice 360 has produced more than a dozen films since it formed in 2006 and has a library of about 4,500 titles. It was created as the audiovisual subsidiary of Spanish telecom and media giant Avánzit , and became the first film and video company to be listed on the Spanish Stock Exchange when it debuted in 2007. That year, Vértice 360 also bought post-production company Apuntolapospo. Vértice 360 now has more than a dozen companies.
