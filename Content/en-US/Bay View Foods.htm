It's a pickle-lover's paradise. Bay View Foods is one of the nation's largest briners of pickling cucumbers. The company washes, grades, sorts, and pickles the cukes, using either its in-house recipe or custom-made brines created to customers' specifications. Bay View's wholly-owned subsidiary, Mr. Chips, prepares and packages dill pickle chips (smooth or crinkle cut) and pickle relish (cube-cut, sweet or dill) for private-label accounts. The company, as well as its contract growers, are located in northeastern lower Michigan, near the shores of Lake Huron. Bay View Foods was founded in 1946.
