Much has been made about Metalcraft of Mayville's many metal fabricating and machining capabilities. The company is a metal fabricator and industrial contract manufacturer that offers precision cutting, welding, pressing, shearing, machining services, and conventional and powder painting services. The company also provides complete product assembly services and bar coding. Metalcraft of Mayville's main facilities are in West Bend and Mayville, Wisconsin, and collectively span a 630,000 sq. ft. of manufacturing space.

Metalcraft targets US customers in the construction, agriculture, military, material handling, and lawn and garden sectors.

Metalcraft traces its roots back to its founding in 1981.
