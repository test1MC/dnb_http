You might say a steady diet of red meat has made this company big and strong. OSI Group is one of the largest suppliers of meat products to US foodservice operators. Through its operating companies, OSI boasts a bulging menu of products that includes a variety of beef, pork, and poultry items, such as beef patties, hot dogs, sausages, bacon, and chicken nuggets. The company enjoys a longtime relationship with #1 fast-food chain McDonald's as its beef supplier. As part of its business, OSI also offers other food processors services such as contract manufacturing, packaging, and supply chain management. It has more than 60 production facilities in 16 countries.

Across its operations, OSI counts more than 60 facilities, including 40-plus manufacturing sites, in 16 countries in the Americas, Europe, and Asia.

In the US, OSI owns South Carolina-based Amick Farms, Fair Oaks Farms in Wisconsin, and Nations Pizza Products and On-Cor Frozen Foods , both of which are located in Illinois (where it is headquartered). To expand its footprint in its home state, OSI partnered with refrigerated warehousing and distribution services company Millard, based in Geneva, Illinois. OSI has a plant that is attached to the Millard operation, from which it produces branded and private label frozen entree dishes, as well as frozen pasta products. These are marketed nationwide to grocery stores, mass merchandisers, club stores, foodservice, and other customers.

OSI maintains a presence in Brazil through a strategic alliance with Marfrig Alimentos, S.A. The company's European operations include Alpenrind in Austria and Germany, Meatworks in Russia, and Kazatin Meat in Ukraine.

In the Asia/Pacific region, it owns K&K Foods in Taiwan, Vista Processed Foods in Japan, and Orange Bay Foods in India. In 2012 OSI expanded its footprint in southern India by opening a third plant through member group Vista Processed Foods Pvt., Ltd.

From its base in Aurora, Illinois, the company enjoys a global reach into promising markets. OSI has half a dozen offices nationwide in Illinois, Iowa, Utah, South Carolina, Wisconsin, and Kansas. In Europe, OSI has a presence in the UK, Germany, Hungary, Poland, Spain, Ukraine, Austria, Serbia, and Russia. The meat processor's business also reaches to China, Australia, India, Japan, the Philippines, and Taiwan.

OSI serves as a contract manufacturer for restaurants the likes of McDonald's , which includes provision of the hamburger chain's meat for stores in China. Through alliances with others, such as refrigerated warehousing and distribution company Millard, the company sells branded and private label products to supermarkets, big-box retailers, warehouse stores, and foodservice operators.

OSI is expanding overseas.

To streamline its operations, OSI established German-based MPO Global Trade, GmbH, a new global trading platform for meat, poultry, and other products. The entity focuses on poultry products from Brazil and Thailand and supports the primary processing assets in Europe and Asia.

The company is embroiled in a legal battle in China, where it supplies Yum Brands restaurants which include Pizza Hut , KFC , and Taco Bell . Employees of OSI China have received jail sentences and the company has been fined for violating food safety standards. OSI has paid fines, but it also is appealing some rulings made by Chinese courts.
