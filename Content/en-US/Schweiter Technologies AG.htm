Everyone knows that manufacturers assemble goods, but what assembles the assemblers? A specialized machine manufacturer, Schweiter Technologies makes various products through a handful of divisions, including SSM Textile Machinery, which makes rewinding, dyeing, and texturing machines for the textile sector; and Ismeca, a manufacturer of machines that assemble, test, inspect, and package solar cells, light-emitting diodes, and other products made by semiconductor companies. The company also makes light-weight composite panels used in the construction of wind turbines, boats, and graphic arts displays through its 3A Composites unit. Schweiter sells the bulk of its products to customers in Asia and Europe.
