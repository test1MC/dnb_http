ACS Motion Control keeps it moving. The company makes programmable motion control products for use with automated systems. Its line includes stand-alone motion controllers, interfaces, development tools, and modules that combine motion controllers and digital amplifiers. Its motion control products are used in equipment for semiconductor manufacturing, electronic testing and assembly, medical scanning, robotics, and laser printing. Customers include Heidelberg and Orbotech . ACS Motion Control has other offices in China, Germany, and South Korea, and its products are distributed in 20 countries around the world.
