Whether you crouch to step into your sports car or stretch to climb into your SUV, Kraco Enterprises might very well greet you when you put your feet on the floor. The company makes aftermarket automotive floormats for cars, SUVs, trucks, and vans, as well as cargo floormats for vehicle, home, and shop use. Its products are sold at more than 15,000 auto supply stores ( Advance Auto Parts , AutoZone , NAPA) and general merchandise retailers ( Sears , Target , Wal-Mart ) in the US and Canada. Kraco was founded in 1954. Sun Capital Partners , a US-based investment firm, acquired Kraco in late 2008.
