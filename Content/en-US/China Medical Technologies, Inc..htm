China Medical Technologies leaves no doubt as to what the company is all about. The company's ECLIA system is a luminescence immunoassay diagnostic system used to detect thyroid disorders, diabetes, reproductive disorders, infectious diseases, and tumor markers. It also offers reagent kits for use with the ECLIA system. China Medical also offers the FISH and SPR molecular diagnostics systems, which detect certain genetic diseases, infectious diseases, and cancers. The company's products are sold in China through the company's sales force and via third-party distributors.
