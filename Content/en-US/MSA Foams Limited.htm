MSA Foams has a good feeling about folks who want to "pack it in." The Peli UK Group company specializes in designing and manufacturing foam packaging and other foam products, including die-cut, CNC-machined, and molded inserts for cases; trays for tool controls; and foam component parts. It also provides project management services, offering training and help for engineers on how to best design and implement their foam products. MSA Foams' customer list has included such names as BAE Systems , Caterpillar , Ford , Valeo , and Vauxhall . The company caters to the Continental Europe and UK markets.

Through sales staff located across the UK, the company serves customers in the automotive, aerospace, broadcast, defense, engineering, logistics, marketing, medical, and museums and heritage segments.

MSA Foams supplies customized reusable and single trip packaging products for all types of equipment and also offers protective packaging products tailored for a perfect fit.

The company's design team provides a complete service using 3-D solid modeling CAD systems.

MSA Foams was founded in 1969. The company became a part of the Peli UK Group in 2012.
