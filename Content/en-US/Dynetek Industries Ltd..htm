Dynetek Industries thinks it's natural that cars should run on gas. The company manufactures lightweight natural-gas and hydrogen-fuel storage cylinders and complete fuel systems used by manufacturers of cars, heavy-duty trucks, school buses, and commercial vehicles. Other products include fueling systems, stationary fuel storage, and bulk fuel transportation systems. The company has partnered with JFE Containers, Ford , and Mitsubishi Rayon , and includes among its customers Magna Steyr , Heuliez Bus, Hyundai Motor , Nissan , and Toyota . Dynetek, which was founded in 1991, also has a subsidiary in Germany. In 2012 Dynetek was acquired by UK-based Luxfer for more than CA$5 million (more than $5.1 million).
