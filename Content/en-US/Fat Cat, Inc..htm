Fat Cat brings da' funk to people and their pets. The company designs toys and specialty products for dogs and cats, including non-traditional catnip-filled and rubber toys in the form of various animals and, of course, politicians. In addition to the catnip-filled character toys of Fat Cat's past (resembling former presidents Bill Clinton and George "Dubya" Bush, as well as Ross Perot and Bill Gates), its lines also feature likenesses of Hillary Rodham Clinton and California governor Arnold Schwarzenegger. Fat Cat toys are sold through pet product retailers, supermarkets, animal clinics, and gift stores nationwide. Fat Cat is a subsidiary of infant and pet product designer and manufacturer Munchkin, Inc.
