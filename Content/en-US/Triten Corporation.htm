Eschewing the trite, Triten Corp has been fast tracking oil refining projects for more than half a century. Its activities are driven by two subsidiaries. The Alloy Products Group makes abrasion resistant sheet steel and welding consumables. The hard-metal lineup, complemented by engineering and fabrication services, is marketed through a direct sales force and independent distributors. The International Alliance Group courts petroleum operations. It touts a low cost, one stop shop of general contracting services for start ups to turnarounds for fluid catalytic cracking (FCC) revamps and emergency repairs. The company is led by its founder's son, John Arnoldy.
