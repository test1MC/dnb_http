Intelligrated helps businesses move products -- literally. The company makes and installs automated material handling equipment, including conveyors, sorters, and airport baggage handling equipment, as well as order fulfillment and warehouse control software. Its InControlWare software system manages conveyor and sorting systems, while its Real Time Solutions software aids in order fulfillment. It targets the consumer goods, distribution, logistics, pharmaceutical, postal, and warehousing markets. Customers have included such notable names as Dick's Sporting Goods , The Hershey Company , Big Lots , and McKesson .

Intelligrated owns four manufacturing plants in Kentucky, Missouri, and Ohio. Regional operations are located in Georgia, Illinois, New Jersey, Ohio, Texas, California, as well as in Brazil, Canada, and Mexico.

Intelligrated's New York-based subsidiary company, Knighted, offers web-enabled warehouse management software, warehouse control software, and labor management software.

The firm plans to extend its customer base further into North America and introduce additional product offerings and global capabilities through investment and strategic acquisitions. It also plans to penetrate select markets it believes are ripe for growth. More specifically, it is focused on the advanced light-to-medium unit segment of the market, which is defined as case conveyor and sortation systems that function at more than 150 cases per minute and are the most technologically sophisticated systems available.

Intelligrated has its eye specifically fixed on Brazil, an emerging market it believes is experiencing a rising demand for handling automation in the manufacturing and retail sectors. It notes a surging uptick for advanced sortation and palletizing systems within the country. Intelligrated launched its operations in Brazil in 2012, through a joint venture with SDI Group USA, a material handling equipment provider that has operated in South America since 1992.

In 2013 the company improved its software offerings through the acquisition of Datria Systems , a provider of voice-enabled technology used on mobile devices for distribution, logistics, and retail management purposes. Datria will become part of Intelligrated's software subsidiary, Knighted.
