Don't be surprised if that pregnancy test has "Made in China" stamped on the box. ACON Laboratories markets the rapid diagnostic tests made by its sister company, ACON Biotech, the first Chinese company licensed by the FDA to produce these diagnostic tests. The company's product line includes tests for strep A and H. pylori, pregnancy and ovulation tests, and tests that screen for a variety of illicit substances, including marijuana, cocaine, and methamphetamines. The tests are marketed to hospitals, clinical labratories, blood banks, and consumers.
