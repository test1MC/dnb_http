Mansueto Ventures has more than an inkling about entrepreneurship and the fast-changing business environment. The company publishes Inc. , which provides content aimed at entrepreneurs , and Fast Company , a chronicler of trends and practices in the quick-to-change business world. Both magazines publish related Web versions. Inc. is known for its Inc. 500 annual ranking of the fastest-growing, privately-held companies in the US. In addition to its two magazine titles, the company also has events, custom publishing, and integrated marketing operations. Joe Mansueto (founder and CEO of Morningstar ) formed the publishing company in 2005 after buying both titles from Gruner+Jahr for about $35 million.
