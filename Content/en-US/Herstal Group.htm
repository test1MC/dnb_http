Herstal has heaps of history behind its guns. The company, which operates through its FN Herstal and Browning units, is a top maker of firearms. Soldiers and peace officers use FN Herstal's products, which include both small-caliber firearms and mounted weapons. Among its most famous products is the FAL rifle, which has been used by fighting forces around the world. Herstal also makes Browning and Winchester firearms for hunters and recreational sharpshooters. It also offers firearm accessories, hunting apparel, and sporting goods such as golf clubs, tennis and squash rackets, and gear bags. In addition to its headquarters in Belgium, Herstal maintains production facilities in the US, Japan, and across Europe.
