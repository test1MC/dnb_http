Automotive fluid exchange and diagnostic equipment maker MotorVac develops, manufactures, and distributes equipment and maintenance service kits to the automotive aftermarket. The company introduced the CarbonClean gas fuel cleaning system, and it quickly became the industry standard. MotorVac operates a factory in Santa Ana, California, and holds about 20 patents (and more, pending approval). A select group of auto parts suppliers and tool and equipment dealers distribute MotorVac's products throughout the world. The company was incorporated in 1992.
