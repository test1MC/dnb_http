Healthy sales put the stamp of approval on Mid-South Industries' products. Through its various subsidiaries and affiliates, Mid-South designs and fabricates metal stampings, and electronic and mechanical equipment, which are used in the appliance, automotive, and telecommunications industries. The company's operations include Alabama-based Mid-South Electronics, Mid-South Products Engineering, Inc., and Stamped Products Inc., and Kentucky-based Mid-South Electronics. Gobar Systems, a joint venture with Ohio-based Select Tool & Die, operates metal stamping and injection molding operations on the Texas-Mexico border.
