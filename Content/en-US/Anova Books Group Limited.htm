Anova Books Group, which specializes in illustrated books and digital content for the UK and international markets, is a relatively new company with some old names. The independent publisher's flagship Batsford imprint began publishing books in 1843, and addresses topics such as fashion and design, embroidery and textiles, chess, heritage, horticulture, and architecture. Other imprints include Collins & Brown (how-to books), Conway (naval history and maritime culture), and Portico (humor). The company is wholly-owned by its three founding partners, who established the firm in 2005 when it acquired the book division of Chrysalis PLC .
