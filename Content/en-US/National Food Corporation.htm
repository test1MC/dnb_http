National Food is in the international egg business. The company operates feed mills, egg-production farms, processing plants, and distribution centers in Washington, Oregon, Montana, and South Dakota. Its fresh shell eggs and egg products (liquid or frozen whites, yolks, whole eggs, and fortified mixtures) are available in the Pacific Northwest and the Midwest, as well as in Alaska and Hawaii; the company also exports its products to the Pacific Rim. The company's eggs, which come in a numerous of sizes and colors, as well as in cage-free, fertile, and organic varieties and are offered in retail and bulk packaging. National started out in business in 1956 as an egg-grading operation called Northwest Egg Sales.
