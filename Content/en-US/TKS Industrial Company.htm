TKS Industrial designs, fabricates, and installs paint finishing systems, specialized drying ovens, industrial ventilation systems, and commercial clean rooms for automakers and other manufacturers. The company operates offices and plants in the US in Michigan and Ohio, and it has international subsidiaries in Brazil, Canada, and Mexico. Customers include Toyota Motor , Chrysler , Ford Motor , Honda , and General Motors . TKS Industrial, established in 1981, is the US subsidiary of Taikisha Ltd., a Japanese supplier of clean room systems, electrical and mechanical equipment for buildings, and paint finishing systems, which was founded in 1913.
