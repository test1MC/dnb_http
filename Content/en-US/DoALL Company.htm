DoALL is trying to be the end-all and be-all of metal cutting. For more than eight decades, the company has engineered and manufactured saw machinery and accessories and distributed industrial products. Its four businesses include DoALL Sawing, which makes saw blades, sawing machines, and cutting oils; Greenlee Diamond Tool makes diamond and super-abrasive products; Continental Hydraulics makes hydraulic pumps, valves, and power units; and DGI Supply distributes some 1,500 industrial brands through a national network of more than 40 supply centers.

The company's DGI Supply unit operates in with more than 40 locations across the US.

In addition to cutting tools, DoALL manufactures material handling equipment, including conveyors, staging tables, and fully automated systems. The company designs the systems for its own DoALL saws, as well as competitors' saws. Additionally, through its ASRS-Remmert unit, DoALL designs compact warehousing systems that include tower, honeycomb, and bridge styles.

DoALL's Sawing division makes metal-cutting instruments of bi-metal (carbon steel backing); tungsten carbide (for use on alloys); carbon steel (for greater flexibility); and grit-edge (to cut hardened steels and brittle materials such as glass, silicon, and quartz); among others. Its line of sawing machines includes general purpose (fixed or swivel head); vertical contour (for cutting plastic, wood, and fibrous materials); miter cutting; and production power saws (numerical control or automatic). This division also produces cutting fluids, such as synthetics (water soluble), semi-synthetics (water soluble with lubrication), soluble oils (for tool life and surface finish), and direct application fluids (applied directly to cutting tool edge).

DGI is represented nationally through its own sales force in more than 40 sales offices and a major call center.

DoALL offers integrated Cutting Tool Division and Machine Tool Division expertise to address customers' requirements. It has the ability to address process establishment and future refinement, installation, service, and sales through the manufacturers the company represents. DoALL broadens it reach and fortifies its product portfolio through the use of acquisitions

In 2013 it obtained Merwin-Stoltz, an industrial machinery maker based in Wisconsin.

In 2012 DGI Supply business obtained K & H Sales in a deal that gave it three locations in Northern California. Also that year, DGI Supply purchased Anich Industries, a metal tool and machining distributor based in Florida.

DoALL is owned by the Wilkie family. The company traces its roots to 1927 when it was founded as Continental Machine Specialties Company.
