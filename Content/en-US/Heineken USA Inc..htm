Heineken USA taps a lush market thanks to America's thirst for foreign suds. The US beer-importing subsidiary of Heineken (Netherlands) offers Heineken, Amstel Light (one of the US's best-selling imported light beers), Indio, and Strongbow. Through its parent's joint acquisition with Carlsberg of Scottish & Newcastle , Britain's largest brewer, Heineken USA has the import rights to Newcastle Brown Ale. Heineken USA is also the sole US importer and seller of Mexico's former FEMSA Cerveza 's beer brands, Tecate, Dos Equis, Sol, Carta Blanca, and Bohemia. For drinkers who want the brew without the buzz, the company sells Buckler, a nonalcoholic beer. Heineken beer was first imported into the US in 1880.

The US arm of the Dutch giant, Heineken USA has offices in New York City, and White Plains, New York, as well as regional offices in Atlanta; Downers Grove, Illinois; and Long Beach, California.
