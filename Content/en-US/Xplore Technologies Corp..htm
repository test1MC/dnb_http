Xplore Technologies ensures that you can take your computer with you no matter what difficult terrain you're exploring. The company manufactures and sells ruggedized tablet PCs and handheld computers. The company primarily targets manufacturers, distributors, and systems integrators that supply field service personnel, factory workers, public safety officials, military personnel, and other customers that require durable mobile computers. Its products incorporate wireless networking technology and can be mounted in vehicles such as carts and forklifts. Xplore was founded in 1996.
