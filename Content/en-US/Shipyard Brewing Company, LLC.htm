Although a shipyard sounds like a dubious place to brew up some suds, brew aficionados will tell you that Shipyard Brewing's beers (and sodas) taste pretty good. Founded on Portland, Maine's historic waterfront, the microbrewer distributes English-style and seasonal ales. In 2008 it shipped approximately 1 million cases of ales and 41,00 cases of soda. Shipyard's libations are available in about 40 US states.
