GLG Life thinks its product is sweeter than sugar. A producer of the natural, plant-derived sweetener stevia, GLG Life develops, breeds, grows, and harvests the stevia plant and then produces high-grade stevia extract that it sells to food and beverage manufacturers. While it sells to more than 30 companies, its biggest customer is agribusiness giant Cargill , which makes its own branded zero-calorie sweetener, Truvia, from GLG Life's stevia extract. Such sweeteners produced from stevia extract can be 200-300 times sweeter than sugar and are considered a natural alternative to artificial sweeteners. Though a Canadian company, GLG Life grows its stevia plants, and operates four production facilities, in China.
