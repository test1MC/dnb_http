A word to the wise (investor) -- that's what MPL Communications is all about. The company publishes print and online investment advice; publications include The Investment Reporter , Investor's Digest of Canada , and such specialist publications as The MoneyLetter , Canadian Mutual Fund Adviser , The TaxLetter , and Blue Book of Stock Reports . The company's website (adviceforinvestors.com) provides online investment advice to subscribers. MPL Communications also handles Canadian distribution of Louis Rukeyser's Wall Street . The company is controlled by officers Barrie Martland and Stephen Pepper through Marpep Publishing.
