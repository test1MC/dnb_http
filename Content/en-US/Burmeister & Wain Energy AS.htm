Burmeister & Wain Energy (BWE) is energized about boilers. The company is a manufacturer of boiler systems for power-generating plants and utility companies. It builds industrial Ultra Supercritical coal-burning boilers (regarded as a clean burning alternative to traditional coal-burning methods), as well as Biomass boilers capable of burning organic materials such as hay and specialized pellets. BWE's other products include emissions control systems and equipment for controlling sulfur dioxide emissions at power plants. BWE's roots date back to the 1800s; it has been a subsidiary of Italian industrial manufacturer STF S.p.A. since 2002.
