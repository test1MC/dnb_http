Kemps Publishing plays matchmaker for people seeking business information. The company is a publisher of information on UK businesses. Its products include magazines and directories for local and regional UK Chambers of Commerce and other trade organizations. Directories include the London Chamber of Commerce & Industry Directory of Members , while magazines include In Business , the bi-monthly publication for the Northamptonshire Chamber of Commerce. The company additionally operates online UK business directory SearchMe4.co.uk, and provides publication design services. Kemps Publishing was founded in 1912.

The company's SearchMe4 online directory lists more than 1.8 million UK businesses.

Searchme4 also publishes a monthly newsletter that is emailed to subscribers and advertisers. Called 4Sight, the newsletter provides business news and advice to owners and managers of UK companies.

Kemps' publications reach more than 200,000 readers. The company primarily serves the B2B (business-to-business) sector.

Businesses owners wishing to promote their companies can register their contact information for free, and can also post news stories on the site.
