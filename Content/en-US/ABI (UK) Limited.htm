The first order of business of ABI (UK) is making manufactured second homes, known in the UK as holiday homes. It offers more than a dozen models with two or three bedrooms, with options including electric fireplaces and kitchen appliances. Prices range from about £16,500 to nearly £60,000 ($25,500 to $92,000). The company sells its homes to dealers and holiday parks throughout the UK and Ireland and in about a dozen other European countries. In the past, some of its top customers have included Parkdean Holidays and Park Resorts . ABI (UK) was formed as Ace Belmont International in 1972 following the merger of travel trailer company ACE International and leisure home company Belmont.

Beverley-based ABI (UK) has a dealer presence across England, Northern Ireland, and Ireland.

ABI (UK) in 2015 continued to emphasize its quality product and customer-oriented business model, with a "focus that has always been on quietly putting more into its holiday homes, rather than on big marketing campaigns and singing its own praises."

The company helps potential buyers consider all of the factors involved before making a holiday home purchase, such as choosing the right holiday park, the size of the pitch, the direction the windows need to face, the cost of utilities, and a home price that's within the shopper's budget.

Accordingly, ABI (UK) offers homes from its affordable entry-level Summer Breeze home listed at just under £16,500 to the luxury and quality-built Westwood home model with a starting price just above £56,700. Shoppers may spend more on upgrades to add extra features that suit their tastes and land plot conditions.
