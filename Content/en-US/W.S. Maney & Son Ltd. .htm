Maney has many things of interest to academia. W.S. Maney & Son, operating as Maney Publishing, is an independent international academic publisher with offices in the UK and US. The company primarily publishes journals and books in specialty fields, including archaeology, architectural history, biomedical topics, decorative arts, language, literature, and social history. It also offers outsourced journal publishing and distribution services. Many of its journals are available online to subscribers and other users (libraries, researchers) through Ingenta and HighWire Press. W.S. Maney & Son traces its roots to 1900, when it was originally established as a typesetting and printing firm.
