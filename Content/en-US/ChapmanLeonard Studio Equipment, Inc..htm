Chapman/Leonard Studio Equipment helps the movies move. The company rents motion picture equipment such as camera cranes, dollies, and tracks to producers of feature films, commercials, television series, music videos, and infomercials. The company operates from three US locations (California, Florida, and Texas) and two international ones (Canada and the UK). Chapman/Leonard was formed in 1965 when Chapman Studio Equipment (itself founded in 1945) teamed up with Leonard Studio Equipment.
