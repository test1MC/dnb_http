Quotient Technology (formerly Coupons.com) connects great brands and retailers with consumers. The company provides digital coupons and coupon codes for more than 2,000 brands from 700-plus consumer packaged goods (CPGs) companies and retailers the likes of Procter & Gamble , Crayola , Walgreens , and Albertsons . It offers display advertising through multiple channels, such as the Internet, mobile, and social media. Quotient Technology taps a network of 30,000 third-party websites to give traction to its coupons and advertising. The media firm logs revenue from 1 billion-plus transactions -- involving a selected digital coupon or redeemed coupon code -- via its platform.

Quotient Technology primarily caters to North American consumers, companies that make consumer packaged goods, and retailers.

The company primarily serves consumer packaged goods (CPG) companies, North American retailers, and consumers.

Quotient Technology delivers promotions and media advertisements to consumers where they are most engaged and likely to make a purchasing decision. To this end, it's targeting existing and potential customers through their smartphones to supplement its focus on social media and the Internet.

The company makes money through digital promotion transactions. It's paid a fee -- whether digital coupons are redeemed or not -- when a consumer selects a digital coupon through the Quotient Technology platform by printing it for redeeming it in person at a retailer or by saving it to a retailer online account for automatic digital redemption. The company also earns a fee when consumers uses a coupon code to make a purchase.
