Acusphere micromanages its products, but then how else would you handle microparticles? The company uses a proprietary technology to create porous microparticles smaller than red blood cells to develop cardiovascular drugs for the detection of coronary artery disease. The company's primary candidate, Imagify, is an injectable drug designed to identify coronary artery disease. The company is completely focused on getting Imagify to market in the US and Europe. Acusphere believes it can successfully market Imagify to echocardiologists (a subset of cardiologists) by touting its ability to improve ultrasound detection of coronary artery disease.
