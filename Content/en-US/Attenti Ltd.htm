Attenti (formerly Dmatek) keeps tabs on felons by keeping tags on them. The company provides electronic monitoring systems for the law enforcement market, including prison operators and government agencies. The system consists of a tamper-proof tag worn on the wrist or ankle that transmits a wireless signal to a monitoring station. Through this system, an offender can be monitored within a designated geographic area, such as a home, workplace, or prison, or checked for alcohol consumption. Attenti operates through subsidiaries Pro Tech and Elmo-Tech as well as Home Free Systems, which offers monitoring of the elderly for nursing homes. Attenti, which was formed in 1990, has customers in more than 25 countries.
