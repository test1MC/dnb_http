T A B C (Toyota Auto Body California) keeps tabs on Toyota within the US. T A B C makes body components for Toyota vehicles (primarily the Corolla, Matrix, and Tundra models) assembled in North America such as truck beds, catalytic converters, steering columns, weld sub-assemblies, and sheet metal components. Hino Motors Manufacturing USA, the US subsidiary of Toyota's Hino Motors subsidiary, also builds Hino brand trucks at the T A B C facility. T A B C was Toyota's first and is the longest-operating manufacturing facility in the US. It traces its historical roots back to 1972 when it was founded as Atlas Fabricators.
