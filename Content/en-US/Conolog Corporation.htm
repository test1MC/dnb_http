Conolog makes small electronic and electromagnetic components that military, industrial, and utilities customers use for microwave, radio, and telephone transmission. Its products include transducers, receivers, electromagnetic-wave filters, and signal-processing equipment. Its products for commercial customers, electrical and industrial utilities in particular, are carried under the INIVEN brand name, taken from a company Conolog acquired in 1981. Leading customers include the US military and power utilities Bonneville Power Administration , NSTAR , and Tucson Electric Power .
