Victron offers contract electronics manufacturing services (EMS), including printed circuit board and box build (complete system) assembly, design and engineering, and quick-turn prototyping, along with order fulfillment and supply chain management services, such as repair depot, test and inspection, and materials management. The company serves customers in aerospace/defense/security, wireless communications, networking equipment, medical gear, and instrumentation, as well as automotive and industrial manufacturing. Victron was established in 1983 by chairman and CEO Todd Yun. Another EMS company, OnCore Manufacturing Services, acquired Victron in 2011.
