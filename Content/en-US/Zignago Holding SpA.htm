In the glass container industry, Zignago Holding SpA believes in "shape and substance." Through its Zignago Vetro Group (Zignago Vetro, Verreries Brosse, Vetri Speciali), it manufactures specialty glass containers, luxury perfume containers, and glass containers for food and beverages. Zignago also produces and sells bottled (and unbottled) wine through its Santa Margherita division. Zignago is additionally involved in real estate and electricity production through its Zignago Immobiliare and Zignago Power divisions, respectively. The company has facilities in Italy and France. The Marzotto family, through its Italian holding company FIMIZ, owns the company. About 33% of Zignago's revenues are generated abroad.
