Del Monaco Specialty Foods makes products that any Tuscan, Roman, or Venetian mama would give her blessing to. The company cooks up and freezes Italian-style food items for the foodservice industry. Del Monaco's certified-organic food products include pastas, sauces, ravioli, tortellini, gnocchi, polenta, pestos, and desserts. It offers American-style foods as well, including barbecue sauce, pot pies and New England clam chowder.  It makes its own Del Monaco products and also offers services such as recipe creation, duplication, and enhancement, plus the manufacture of custom products. The company was founded in 1964 by its namesakes, the late Mike Del Monaco and his wife Ernestine.
