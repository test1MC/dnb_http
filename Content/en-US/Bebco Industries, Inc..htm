Customers enclose checks made payable to Bebco Industries for the enclosures supplied by this manufacturer. The company makes customized metal enclosures (custom brackets, desktop consoles, instrument stands, wall mount cabinets, and instrument access doors), environmental control units (hazardous location ventilation and air conditioner units) and industrial shelters (metal and fiberglass portable modular buildings, blast resistant buildings). Bebco Industries targets clients in the petroleum, chemical, utility, and pharmaceutical sectors, among others. Founded in 1967, the company has two manufacturing facilities in Texas.
