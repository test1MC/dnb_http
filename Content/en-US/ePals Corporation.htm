Not sure that Facebook is educational enough for the classroom? ePals might agree. A provider of educational technology platforms, ePals offers social learning networks and digital content to K-12 school districts, teachers, and parents. The company's offerings include ePals Global Community, a social network that facilitates electronic communication (i.e. email and community forums) between students from different classrooms; the SchoolMail 365 email system for school districts; virtual workspace LearningSpace, and the In2Books eMentoring program. ePals also offers K-12 digital and print magazines through subsidiary Carus Publishing , which publishes Cricket for teens and Babybug for toddlers, among others.
