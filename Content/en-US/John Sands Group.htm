Australians looking for that last minute birthday card for Mom can thank John Sands. A unit of American Greetings , the company supplies customers in Australia and New Zealand with cards, gift wrap, and other items. Brand names include John Sands, Momentum Greetings, Creative Stationery, and The Ink Group. Acquired by American Greetings in 1996, the company was founded in 1837 by John Sands, the son of an English engraver. It's Australia's second oldest registered company.
