Airxcel makes sure you're comfortable and well-fed on your RV road trips. The company manufactures air conditioners, furnaces, water heaters, cooking appliances, and compressor refrigeration units mainly for recreational vehicle OEMs (generates the majority of sales), including Fleetwood Enterprises , Thor Industries , and Winnebago . RVP, the RV products division, makes Coleman-Mach brand air-conditioning equipment. The Marvair division makes specialty wall-mount ACs, environmental control units, and heat pumps for utilities and schools and for modular construction, telecom companies, and marine applications. The Suburban Manufacturing Company division makes RV gas furnaces, water heaters, ranges, and cooktops.
