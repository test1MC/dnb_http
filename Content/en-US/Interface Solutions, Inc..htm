Interface Performance Materials (IPM) does not have its lips sealed, but it can just about seal anything else. The company makes a broad range of seals and insulating material with applications ranging from automobiles to electric motors and transformers. It makes gasket products for automotive, heavy-duty, and industrial markets, and felt backings for vinyl floors. Interface Solutions also produces electrical barriers for appliances, computers, lamps, and power tools, and thermal barriers for high temperature applications. IPM was founded in 1911. In the 1970s it developed the world's first non-asbestos soft gasket materials.

The company is headquartered in Pennsylvania and has three materials plants in the US and one in Germany. It also has a presence in China and India.

IPM serves the light vehicle, commercial vehicle, off-highway, power sports and recreational vehicle, consumer products, and aftermarket sectors.
