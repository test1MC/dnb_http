Knauf Insulation believes that an R rating is good for the family. (R-values are assigned by resistance to heat flow.) The company makes thermal and acoustical fiberglass insulation products for residential, commercial, industrial, marine, OEM, and metal building applications. Knauf also produces polystyrene custom shapes, insulation, panels, and other products. Knauf Insulation has a range of products that have met the stringent chemical emissions standards to achieve the GREENGUARD Gold Certification and is verified to be formaldehyde-free. The company is a subsidiary of Knauf Gips KG , a multi-billion-dollar family of building materials companies owned by the Germany-based Knauf family.

Knauf Insulation is based in Indiana and operates in more than 35 countries (including more than 30 manufacturing plants) in Asia, Europe, Australia, the EMEA, Russia, and North America.

Knauf Insulation offers building insulation products (batts, rolls and blown insulation); air handling products (duct board, duct liner, duct wrap and rigid plenum liner); industrial/commercial products; metal building products (blanket insulation) and OEM products (blanket insulation, equipment liner, insulation board, acoustical insulations and uncured blanket).

Products are distributed in the US and Canada through independent insulation distributors and retail building materials outlets in addition to sales representatives. It offers its products for residential, commercial, industrial, OEM, marine, and metal building applications.

The company posted revenues of more than $1.3 billion in 2013.

Knauf Insulation enters joint ventures to extend its reach and augment its insulation product portfolio. In 2013 it launched a joint venture with Exeed Industries to acquire a holding in its subsidiary, Exeed Insulation. The company will be renamed Knauf Exeed Insulation and makes glasswool insulation products in Abu Dhabi.

The company was established in 1978 as Knauf Fiber Glass.
