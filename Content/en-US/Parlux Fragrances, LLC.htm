If scents could speak, Parlux Fragrances (from the French verb parler ) might be its lexicon. The fragrance and beauty products designer and manufacturer owns and licenses to third-party manufacturers the perfumes and beauty-related products of a handful of celeb brand names, including Paris Hilton, Jessica Simpson, Marc Ecko, Nicole Miller, Queen Latifah, Rhianna, and others. Parlux markets designer and other fragrance blends that are sold primarily through national department stores ( Belk , Macy's , Boscov's ) and 340 Perfumania retail stores in the US. Distributors from Canada to the Caribbean cater to retailers, mainly perfumeries, in 80 countries. Partner fragrance purveyor Perfumania acquired Parlux in 2012.
