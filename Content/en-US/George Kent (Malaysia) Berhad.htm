George Kent (Malaysia) Berhad (GKM) has water on the brain -- in a good way. The company's manufacturing division makes a line of brass components including water meters, stopcocks, ferrules, and fiberglass-reinforced polyester panels for water storage tanks. GKM's industrial marketing division offers products that serve the water, building, power generation, and oil and gas industries. Specific products include industrial process controls and instrumentation, valves and fittings, boilers, water meters, and automation systems. The company's infrastructure division develops turnkey water projects for privatized water service. GKM's export division markets the company's products abroad, primarily in South Asia.
