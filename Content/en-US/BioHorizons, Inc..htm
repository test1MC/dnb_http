BioHorizons offers its customers a beautiful set of choppers with its tools. The company manufactures and sells dental implants -- or individual screw-in teeth -- as an alternative to conventional dentures. It also sells soft tissue regeneration products, called biologics, to help patients' gums grow back around the implant. Targeted to oral surgeons, BioHorizons offers tools and specialty instruments (including those by Hu-Friedy ), as well as a software program under the Virtual Implant Placement (VIP) name to map treatment procedures. BioHorizons sells its products in more than 80 countries and online. The company is 60%-owned by Henry Schein , a leading global distributor of medical, dental, and animal health products.

BioHorizons operates in 85 markets throughout Asia, the Americas, Africa, Australia, and Europe. The US accounts for most of its sales.

In 2015, the company launched a suite of xenograft (cross-species) products, the MinerOss X family, which are grafting products made from bovine bone and cartilage. The line is complementary to its already successful MinerOss allografts (same species grafts).
