Beiersdorf Canada works to keep its customers' skin smooth and supple. The subsidiary of Beiersdorf AG makes NIVEA, Eucerin, La Prairie, tesa, and Aquaphor brands, as well as Labello lip balm. Beiersdorf's products are sold in more than 100 countries. The parent company's Beyond Borders executive training program places some of Beiersdorf's best in Canada. Typically through a six-month placement, program participants work for Beiersdorf Canada, which acts as an international affiliate.
