Strobic Air designs and manufactures patented laboratory fume hood exhaust systems, heat recovery systems, specialty blowers, silencers, Tri-Stack branded roof exhaust systems, and industrial fans for ventilation applications in university, government, and other testing laboratory facilities; hospitals; semiconductor manufacturing facilities; and pharmaceutical, chemical, and petrochemical plants. It also offers engineering, design, and technical support. Strobic Air has sales offices in Belgium, Canada, Puerto Rico, Spain, the UK, and the US. The company is a subsidiary of Met-Pro and part of its Product Recovery/Pollution Control Equipment segment, which generates almost half of Met-Pro's sales.
