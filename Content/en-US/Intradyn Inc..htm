Intradyn provides disk-based email archiving appliances and related software. Designed to work with a wide range of email systems, its systems provide mailbox management and message storage optimization. Its products are touted for helping businesses comply with government regulations such as the federal Sarbanes-Oxley Act. Intradyn targets small and mid-sized businesses with its Orca Seawolf and Blackfish appliances, and mid-sized and large enterprise customers with its ComplianceVault series. Intradyn was acquired by email software and server provider Mirapoint in 2008.
