Toye Group knows that formal recognition is no game for most of its customers. The company makes and sells corporate apparel and promotional products, commemorative items, jewelry, and military and ceremonial medals, uniforms, and regalia. Its Benton & Johnson division manufactures gold and silver embroidery threads and cords, and the London Badge & Button division makes cufflinks and other accessories. The Chas C. Stadden unit sells collectible military figurines, while Toye, Kenning & Spencer makes sports trophies, furniture, badges, and other items. The company has been in operation since 1685.

Toye's products are used by governments, corporations, fraternal and friendly societies, and clubs and associations.

Toye operates from three UK locations: Birmingham, Bedworth and London. Its products are sold worldwide.

The company supplies retailers in the UK and overseas. It also sells online.
