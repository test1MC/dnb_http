A manufacturing company by any other name would smell as... sweet? Rose City Manufacturing (which took its name from the founding MacGgregor family's 19th century mail order floral business) manufactures metal stampings, blanks, laminations, and welded assemblies; and provides services such as swaging, secondary forming and piercing, resistance welding, robotic MIG welding and assembly operations. By way of its sister companies Morgal Machine Tool Co. , and Ohio Stamping and Machine LLC , Rose City Manufacturing also offers in-house design and build capability.
