To TOTO, going to the restroom is not just a bodily function, it is a sublime experience. The #1 toilet brand in Japan, TOTO toilets incorporate electronic, water-saving, and grime-resistant technologies, and feature options from elevating toilet seats for the elderly to heated-seat toilets with warm-water bidet functions. The company makes plumbing and other home products and bathroom and kitchen units and is engaged in the remodeling of public and private toilet systems. TOTO also develops new metals, ceramics for the semiconductor industry, and photocatalyst technology used in making films, tiles, and auto body coatings. Its Hydrotect film is used to protect car mirrors.

TOTO has manufacturing and sales operations in Brazil, China, France, Germany, India, Indonesia, Korea, Malaysia, Mexico, the Philippines, Singapore, Taiwan, Thailand, the UAE, the UK, the US, and Vietnam.

To improve operating and financial efficiency and raise cash, in 2012 TOTO spun off its sanitaryware manufacturing business as an operating subsidiary, TOTO Sanitechno Ltd.

The company was founded in 1917.
