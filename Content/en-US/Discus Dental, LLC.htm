Discus Dental delivers the stuff for dazzling teeth. The company develops, markets, and distributes dental aesthetics products, including teeth whitening items and restoratives. It also offers oral hygiene products, impression materials, and endodontic supplies. Discus Dental's brands include Zoom!, BriteSmile, BreathRx, and DayWhite. While the company develops many of the items in its portfolio, it also distributes products from other companies. As part of its business, Discus Dental also provides practice management software, training, and marketing services. It distributes products directly to dentists throughout North America and in 100-plus countries worldwide. Royal Philips Electronics owns Discus Dental.

Royal Philips acquired Discus Holdings, the holding company of Discus Dental, in 2010 and added the company to its Consumer Lifestyle division's Health and Wellness business. Discus' oral care lineup complements Philips' Sonicare portfolio of tooth cleaning and polishing systems.

Discus Dental operates within the Health and Wellness business of Royal Philips's Consumer Lifestyle division. Discus Dental offers dentists a range of aesthetic dental products -- from high end light-activated whitening systems to less expensive simple gel products. The company also offers home-use whitening products, which are dispensed through dental offices.

Prior to being acquired, the company expanded its offerings through the acquisition of ZAP Lasers. The 2010 purchase added development and manufacturing operations in Southern California; ZAP Lasers already provided contract manufacturing services for Discus Dental's soft-tissue diode lasers. The acquisition also expanded the company's international distribution network.

The previously privately-held Discus was founded in 1989 by Dr. William Dorfman.
