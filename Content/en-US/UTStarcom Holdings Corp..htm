UTStarcom answers the great call of China. The company provides multimedia communications products and wireless and wireline network infrastructure equipment. Telecom service providers use its Internet protocol-based mSwitch product to deploy voice, data, and video services on a single platform. Its broadband infrastructure products include multiservice access nodes, DSL access multiplexers, and optical transport devices. In 2012 UTStarcom divested its Internet television (IPTV) business, which included central office and customer premise equipment; the IPTV product line will operate as a privately held, standalone company.
