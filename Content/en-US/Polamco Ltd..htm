Polamco knows how to stay insulated and connected at the same time. The company manufactures connectors, backshells, and interconnect components for electronic products. Other products include connector covers, screenbraid, screen fixing bands, heat shrink boots, silicon stretch tape, lacing tape, and adhesives. It sells directly and through distributors worldwide to customers in the aerospace, medical, mass transit, oil, communications, shipbuilding, and space sectors. Polamco is also the exclusive European distributor for Breyden lacing tapes, used in civil and military aircraft applications. Its distribution network spans some 20 countries. TE Connectivity, the Switzerland-based components giant, acquired Polamco in 2015.

After 39 years as an independent company, Polamco agreed to TE Connectivity's buyout offer. Polamco will operate under its name while having access to TE Connectivity's worldwide network of suppliers and distributors. TE Connectivity reported nearly $14 billion in sales in 2014.

Polamco operates its own research and development program using CAD/CAM equipment. In the UK, distributors include PEI-Genesis UK, HUB Electronics, Kapco, and FC Lane Electronics. Outside the UK, Polamco has distributors in North America (the US and Canada) and across Europe and the Asia/Pacific regions. Some of its overseas distributors include PEI-Genesis, Intex, S.C.P. / Sintersa, RSG Connexion, and T&G Elektro.

Based in the UK, Polamco has sales offices in the US and France. It is seeking to expand its operations in Bath.

Polamco are used in a range of industries that include aerospace, defense, communication, industrial, medical, mass transit, shipbuilding, energy, and space. On its customer roster are BAE Systems , General Dynamics , Northrop Grumman , Rolls-Royce , Rockwell Collins , GKN Aerospace , and Lockheed Martin .
