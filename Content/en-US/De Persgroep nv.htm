De Persgroep is a diversified media company with operations in publishing, broadcasting, and digital media. It owns a portfolio of local and regional newspapers, including Het Laatste Nieuws , the most widely read daily newspaper in Belgium, and Het Parool in Amsterdam. Its magazine operations include Flemish weekly Dag Allemaal and other special interest titles. De Persgroep's broadcasting operations include commercial TV stations ATV and vtm, along with radio stations Q-music and JOE fm. In addition, the company operates Web special interest Web sites focused on used car sales, careers, and fashion. Tracing its roots to the 1880s, the company is controlled by the Van Thillo family.
