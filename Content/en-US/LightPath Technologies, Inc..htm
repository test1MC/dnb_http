LightPath Technologies is lighting the optical networking way. The company, which has traditionally used its patented GRADIUM glass to make distortion-reducing lenses for inspection equipment, is developing new applications for its technologies in the optoelectronics and fiber-optic communications fields. Its optoelectronics products include collimators (optical network components) and optical isolators (filters that prevent light waves from reflecting backwards).
