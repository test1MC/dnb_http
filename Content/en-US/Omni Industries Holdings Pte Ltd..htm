Omni Industries wants to make, not break, the mold. Through its subsidiaries, the company designs and builds plastic components and molds for plastics injection molding machines. Its precision processes include two-shot, insert, and gas-assisted molding. Its manufacturing facilities in Singapore, China, Mexico, Malaysia, and Thailand provide initial product design and development, to fabrication and molding, as well as product manufacture and assembly. Although the focus at Omni is on plastic parts for consumer goods OEMs, it produces molds for everything from syringes to car cup holders for automotive and medical markets, too. Since 2008, the company operates as a subsidiary of Adval Tech Holding AG.
