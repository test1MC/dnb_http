U.S. Traffic Corporation wants to get you out of a jam. The company makes electronic traffic controllers, vehicle sensors, pedestrian and vehicle signals, power backup systems, and traffic conflict and malfunction monitors. Its SmartToll system collects the magnetic signatures of vehicles to count and classify automobiles on toll roads. The company also makes vehicle detection systems, video equipment, and cabinets. The SmartToll system is sold directly to customers; other products are sold through distributors in the US and Canada. U.S. Traffic is a division of Peek Traffic; privately held traffic safety company Signal Group bought U.S. Traffic and Peek Traffic from Quixote Corporation in 2008.
