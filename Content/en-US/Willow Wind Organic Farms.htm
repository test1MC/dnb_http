The wind in the willows helps bring forth good produce at Willow Wind Organic Farms. The agricultural company grows certified organic vegetables on its 640-acre farm in Washington State (it is developing a 2,000-acre farm in Oregon). Willow Wind's vegetable products include frozen broccoli, carrots, green beans, peas, mixed vegetables, sweet corn, and potato products (hash browns, french fries, nuggets). Its frozen veggies are available under the Earth's Finest Organic label. The company's customers include national food chains and club stores including Costco . Willow Wind Organic Farms was founded by Steve and Margaret Walser in 1980.
