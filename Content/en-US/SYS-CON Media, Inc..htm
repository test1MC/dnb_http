SYS-CON Media publishes magazines and newsletters that specialize in topics of interest to IT professionals, such as Java, ColdFusion, Linux, and XML, as well as wireless technologies. Titles include Java Developer's Journal ( JDJ ) and Wireless Business & Technology ( WBT ) . The company also publishes books and runs about 100 Web sites devoted to technology topics. Its SYS-CON Events business specializes in technology-related conferences, trade shows, and educational seminars.  SYS-CON Media's readership consists of more than 1.2 million IT professionals. CEO Fuat Kircaali founded the company in 1994 using $1,200 he borrowed off his credit cards.
