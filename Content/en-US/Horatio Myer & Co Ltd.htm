Known as Myer's Comfortable Beds, Horatio Myer & Co. was among the largest manufacturers of beds and mattresses, and headboards. Among its distinctions, Myer's boasted a proprietary Silverdry anti-bacterial mattress technology. Its products, sold under such soporific names as Dream Charm and Serenity Memory, were distributed in the UK through bedding specialty shops, department stores, and its website. Founded by Horatio Myer in 1876, the longtime family-owned company was acquired by South Africa-based Steinhoff International in 2013 and was integrated into its operations.
