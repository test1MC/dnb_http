Amada America is pressing for your business. The company makes and sells sheet metal fabricating equipment, including CNC turret punch presses, lasers, press brakes, shears, flexible manufacturing systems, and related software. It primarily sells its fabricating equipment to the aerospace, appliance, electronics, farm equipment, gaming, medical, and telecommunications industries. Through its Amada Capital subsidiary, Amada America finances and leases its equipment. Its Japan-based parent company, Amada Co. , has about 80 affiliates and subsidiaries around the world.

Amada America's headquarters is located in Buena Park, California. It has its sales and services office in Texas, Georgia, and Massachusetts. It also has solution centers in Illinois, Ohio, and Oregon.

The company targets the aerospace, air conditioning, appliance, computers, electronics, farm equipment, gaming industry, medical equipment, metal office equipment, restaurant equipment, telecommunications, and transportation sectors. Its sales offices are located in Byfied, Massachusetts; Covington, Georgia; Troutdale, Oregon; and Irving, Texas.

Amada America was founded in 1971.
