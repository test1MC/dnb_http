Periodical powerhouse or event organizer extraordinaire? However you say it, Top Right Group fits. Formerly called Emap, the business reorganized in 2012 under the new name Top Right Group. As part of the restructure, it divided into 3 separate units: The magazine division, home to about a dozen titles including Drapers , Retail Week , and Nursing Times , continues to use the Emap name. Its exhibition and conferences arm, which produces events such as World Retail Congress, is called i2i, while the insight and data operation, which includes the retail trend-analysis firm WGSN , is branded 4C. Top Right is owned by Eden Bidco, made up of private equity firm Apax Partners and newspaper business Guardian Media Group .
