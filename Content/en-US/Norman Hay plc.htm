When a job needs to be finished, Norman Hay is ready to begin. Through its many divisions, the company has a hand in processing, coatings, and metal finish surfaces. Surface Coatings utilizes process chemistry for coating and finishing a wide variety of metal components. These coatings are designed to prevent corrosion and provide surface release, primarily for customers in the oil and gas industry. Catering mostly to automotive companies, its Ultraseal Impregnation Sealants Division manufactures and supplies resin sealants and related chemicals used in metal castings. Other divisions design and make a range of chemical process plants and provides operations and management for these plants.

Norman Hay is headquartered in the UK and has operations across five continents and a network of distributors, agents, and resellers in over 25 countries.

The company's noteworthy customers include BMW , Mercedes Benz, Toyota , Airbus , Rolls Royce , Messier Services, Shell, Phillips Conoco, and Halliburton .

Norman Hay often extends its geographical footprint through the use of acquisitions. In 2013 it purchased Sterr & Eder Industries, a manufacturer of cast metal parts and services catering mostly to the automotive supply chain sector, located in Germany.
