Tropical Oasis hopes to be exactly that, offering much needed refreshment to bodies in need of liquid. The company sells nutritional drinks, vitamins and minerals (in capsule, liquid, and powder form), joint health products, and, for our four-legged friends, a line of animal health care products. Pet offerings include joint health, vitamins, and weight control products. Tropical Oasis' brands include its namesake, Tropical Oasis, Liquid Solutions, and Organoz. The company's variety of nutritional and sports drinks are said to boost energy, replenish certain nutrients and vitamins, and assist in weight loss efforts. Tropical Oasis, which was founded in 1996, sells its products online and through distributors.
