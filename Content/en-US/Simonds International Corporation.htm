Whether or not you measure a thousand times, Simonds gives you an edge for cutting once. Founded in 1832, Simonds International is North America's oldest manufacturer of industrial cutting tools. The company maintains more than a million square feet of manufacturing space for producing a variety of tools for cutting through metal, wood, paper, and plastics. Its metal-cutting products include carbon, bimetal, and carbide-tipped bandsaw blades. Other products include wood-cutting items, such as bits, shanks, circular saws, machine knives, wide bandsaws, and circle saws, as well as files (a hand-cutting tool for producing a smooth finish on materials), steel rules, and farrier rasps.

Based in Fitchburg, Massachusetts, Simonds is a global company with offices in the US. It has an extended reach into Canada, Germany, and the UK.

As part of its business, the company operates Simonds Canada Ltd. and Simonds International - Wespa Metallsagenfabrik - Germany. Altogether, it boasts more than a million square feet of manufacturing space.

Simonds has achieved the status of the oldest maker of industrial cutting tools in North America by serving several industries. The company caters to nearly a dozen customer groups, such as aerospace, automotive, lumbermill, metalworking, pallets, pulp/paper, tree care/tree service, woodchip processing, and fabricators, among others.

Simonds has steadily developed its presence in the marketplace by expanding its operations to about a dozen facilities in Canada, Germany, and the US. In 2012 the company established a new Central US Weld & Distribution Center in Louisville, Kentucky. Focused on bandsaw welding and finishing systems, the facility provides faster order turnaround for customers, higher order fill rates, and high weld quality.

Acquisitions have also provided Simonds with a vehicle for boosting its customer base, as well as for solidifying its position as an industry-leading manufacturer of cutting tools. To further expand in the Western Canadian saw mill market and strengthen Simonds as a top supplier of wide band saws, the company in late 2012 purchased the wide band saw manufacturing assets and circular saw finishing assets -- located in Langley, British Columbia -- belonging to Cal Saw Canada.
