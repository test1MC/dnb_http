Kumho Tire can really get around. The company makes tires for passenger cars, SUVs and trucks, and off-road vehicles in its eight foreign and domestic plants. The company's affiliates include Hyundai , Mitsubishi , and Volkswagen . It serves customers in about 180 countries. Kumho's technical center in China provides technological and research services for its various subsidiaries throughout that country. The company has R&D centers in China, Germany, the UK, and the US, as well as in its homeland. Kumho Tire is South Korea's second largest tire manufacturer, after Hankook Tire .
