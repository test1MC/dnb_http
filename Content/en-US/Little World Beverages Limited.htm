Little World brews divine beverages for Australian creatures of legal drinking age. Through its Little Creatures Brewing subsidiary, Little World Beverages makes the Little Creatures line of craft beers. The company's award-winning varieties -- all of which sport its trademark cherub logo -- are Little Creatures Pale Ale, the amber Rogers' Beer, and Little Creatures Pilsner. It exports its beers to New Zealand and the UK.
