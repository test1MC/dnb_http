Tjernlund Products manufactures and distributes ventilation products. Its offerings included fixed- and variable-speed draft inducers, and commercial and residential air intake systems, air cleaners, ventilation and cooling fans, sidewall vent systems, and replacement parts. The company also builds custom exhaust and ventilation systems to client specifications. It distributes its products throughout the US and Canada. Emil Tjernlund started the company in 1938 as a manufacturer of oil-fired forced-air furnaces; it continues to be owned and operated by his grandsons, Robert and Tom, and his great-grandson Bob.
