You won't find Neo or Morpheus in this Matrix. Phase Matrix develops and manufactures microwave and radio-frequency (RF) test equipment, such as microwave frequency counters, pulse generators, microwave synthesizers, and power amplifiers. The company designs and manufactures instruments on a contract basis for OEMs, and also provides repair services and spare parts for EIP-branded instruments. The company also supplies microwave components, such as high-speed clocks and oscillators. Phase Matrix has been a subsidiary of National Instruments (NI) since the 2011 acquisition.

A number of Phase Matrix's products have been rebranded as National Instruments offerings. They include the QuickSyn frequency synthezisers, integrated microwave assemblies, and component products.

In 2015 Phase Matrix designated its VXIbus instrument products as obsolete. It stopped selling them at the end the third quarter of 2015.

Prior to the acquisition by NI, founders Pete Pragastis and Charanbir Mahal held the majority ownership in Phase Matrix.
