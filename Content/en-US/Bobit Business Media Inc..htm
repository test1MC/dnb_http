Bobit Business Media is brewing with business-to-business coverage. The company publishes 17 trade publications covering five different markets (automotive, automotive aftermarket, ground transportation, protection, and beauty). Titles include Automotive Fleet, Auto Rental News, and Business Fleet . In addition, the company has more than 40 Web sites and about 25 e-newsletters (PoliceMag.com), NailsMag.com). Bobit also offers custom publishing and organizes trade shows and conferences, such as the Green Fleet Conference. It operates 15 regional offices throughout the US. The company was founded in 1961 by Ed Bobit with the launching of Automotive Fleet .
