Searching for a soul mate? Then don't bother browsing Avid Life Media's dating sites. The company runs websites catering to various sexual tastes and non-monogamous relationships. Its flagship site, AshleyMadison.com, is for married people looking to have an affair; women join for free, but men must pay to register and solicit profiles. Established Men is for high-net-worth men looking for casual relationships and CougarLife is for older women seeking younger men. Avid Life claims more than 40 million members across the globe. A massive data breach in which Ashley Madison user details were leaked caused a media sensation and PR nightmare for the company in 2015.
