This company is concentrated around the local news. American Consolidated Media owns and operates about 100 community newspapers serving primarily small to medium-sized markets in Texas and Oklahoma, and more than a half dozen states in the Upper Midwest. Its portfolio includes such titles as the Athens Messenger (Ohio), the Grand Rapids Herald Review (Minnesota), and the Midlothian Mirror (Texas). Jeremy Halbreich, former general manager of the Dallas Morning News (owned by A. H. Belo ), founded American Consolidated Media in 1998. A group of lenders acquired the newspaper business from Australia's Southern Cross Media in 2010.
