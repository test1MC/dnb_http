While NMB Technologies always has its bearings (miniature, precision bearings at that), it also has electric motors, system cooling fans and blowers, and lighting products in its portfolio. It products end up in industries such as medical, automotive, industrial, toys, home electronic appliances, and others. It also makes bearing assemblies, custom fan trays, and impellers, as well as a touch-screen computer keyboard called COOL LEAF. The company is part of the North American operations of Japan-based MinebeaMitsumi , one of the world's largest manufacturers of miniature precision ball bearings.

NMB's products include LED lighting. The LED lineup includes a line of programmable and dimmable LED drivers and LED Modules with Ultra Thin Lens technology. The programmable drivers offer the capability to program the output current of the driver to match the application.

NMB is headquartered in California and makes its products at 36 manufacturing facilities around the world.

NMB depends primarily on distributors to sell its wares in the US and Canada. Arrow , Avnet Electronics Marketing , Digi-Key , Newark , and Sager are among its many national distributors.

MinebeaMitsumi's North American operations accounted for 10% of its revenue in 2014.
