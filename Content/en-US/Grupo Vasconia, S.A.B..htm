Viva la cocina! Grupo Vasconia (formerly Ekco S.A.B.) is Mexico's largest housewares company. It manufactures steel and aluminum cookware, bakeware, cutlery, dinnerware, flatware, coffee makers, and kitchen gadgets. The company sells its kitchenware in Mexico, Argentina, and across Central America under the brands Ekco, Presto , Regal , Thermos, and Vasconia. After Lifetime Brands bought a 30% stake in the firm for $22 million in 2007, the company introduced Vasconia brand kitchenware to the US and Canadian market. In turn, Ekco became the Latin American distributor for Lifetime's popular US brands Faberware, KitchenAid, Cuisinart, Pedrini, and Pfaltzgraff. Grupo Vasconia traces its roots back to 1934.
