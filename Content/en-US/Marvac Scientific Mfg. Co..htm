The vacuums made by Marvac Scientific aren't the ones you use to clean crumbs from the carpet. The company's vacuum pumps are mechanical high-vacuum pumps used for scientific and industrial applications. Its heavy-duty belt drive pumps are used in neon sign manufacturing, as well as in laboratory settings. Its heavy-duty direct drive product line is designed for dehydration and some scientific uses, while the standard-duty direct drive models are used in the refrigeration service field. Marvac has phased out manufacturing of vacuum pumps due to overseas competition, but it continues to provide spare parts and repair services for its products. The company has branched out into radiator adaptors and testers.
