Ameriscan Designs keeps a sharp eye out for the latest trends in custom cabinets and woodwork. The company manufactures and installs custom cabinets, architectural woodwork, and millwork. The company, which operates primarily in the Chicago metropolitan area, serves residential and commercial clients, including hotels, restaurants, and banks. Subsidiary Okumura Designs offers custom Japanese interior woodworking pieces, including screens, gazebos, and cabinetry.
