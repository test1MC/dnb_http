UCI Holdings might not bring together all the parts of an automobile, but it's got a good start. UCI's component divisions include Airtex Products (fuel pumps and cooling systems), ASC Industries (cooling systems and water pumps), Champion Laboratories (oil, fuel, and air filters), and Wells Manufacturing (engine management products). All told, the company makes more than 60,000 products for light and heavy-duty vehicles. The company operates a network of manufacturing facilities and distribution centers throughout North America, Europe, and Asia. New Zealand-based Rank Group Limited acquired UCI in early 2011 from The Carlyle Group .
