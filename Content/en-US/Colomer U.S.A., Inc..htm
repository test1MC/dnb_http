Colomer USA is out to make every day a good hair day for its customers. The company makes a variety of hair care formulas, specializing in ethnic styling and treatment products. It primarily markets to African-American and Hispanic consumers. Colomer USA's salon lineup is sold under several brands, including ABBA, American Crew, Art & Science, Mop, and TIGI. Labels for consumer products include Lotta Body, Creme of Nature, Revlon Realistic, Fabu-Laxer, and Fanci-Full. Once part of Revlon , Colomer USA operated under Barcelona's hair and beauty products concern The Colomer Group (owned by former Revlon exec Carlos Colomer and investors) until 2013, when Revlon bought the business for $665 million.

In late 2013 Revlon's wholly-owned operating subsidiary, Revlon Consumer Products Corporation, acquired The Colomer Group and its Revlon Professional business. Colomer sells to salons and other professional channels not currently served by Revlon. As part of the transaction, Revlon gained Revlon Professional, Intercosmo, Orofluido, and UniqOne hair care brands; CND nail polishes and enhancements, including the Shellac innovation; and American Crew men's grooming products. New parent Revlon also took control of certain brands, such as Natural Honey body lotions and Llongueras hair care, that Colomer sells directly into retail channels, and the multi-cultural haircare brand Cr√®me of Nature. Buying Colomer expands Revlon's geographic reach, as Colomer earns about 50% of its net sales in Europe, Middle East and Africa, and approximately 40% in the US.

Despite its geographic name, Colomer USA boasts a handful of research and development centers located in the US in Jacksonville, Florida, and Vista, California, as well as abroad in Barcelona, Spain, and Bologna, Italy. The centers focus on developing new products for the care of hair, skin, and nails, as well as creating multicultural products.

Colomer USA targets both the professional and consumer markets with its beauty products. It makes products under private label for other manufacturers, as well.

In addition to manufacturing products, Colomer USA is the d/b/a (doing business as) for Roux Laboratories, which engages in research and development, laboratory testing, package design, and product formulation. Colomer USA offers some custom manufacturing of personal care products, as well as logistic support services, warehousing, distribution, and inventory management.
