Panasonic Healthcare Company (formerly Panasonic Shikoku Electronics) helps put the pieces together. Its Health Care and Medical Business Group makes blood glucose monitoring, lactate monitoring, and lancet systems, while its Visual Business Group produces a variety of electronic goods and parts, including TVs, DVD players, camcorders, and cameras. The company also operates a Devices Business Group that makes such products as Blu-ray Disc, Super Multi, and DVD-ROM drives. Panasonic Healthcare, which pioneered the first electric heater tables, is a contract manufacturing subsidiary of consumer electronics giant Panasonic Corporation .
