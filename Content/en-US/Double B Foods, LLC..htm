Double B Foods gives hungry folks a hand. The Texas company makes ready-to-eat refrigerated and frozen handheld foods, including appetizers, entrees, and desserts. Its kolaches (the traditional Czech specialty, smoked sausage rolls) and rolls come in a dozen different styles including a sausage kolache with cheese, a chicken and turkey morning sausage roll, and a whole-grain jalapeño luncheon roll. The company also offers scrambler biscuits and rolls, empanadas, tacquitos, burritos, sandwich wraps and dips. Double B's customers include retail food stores and foodservice operators, such as schools and convenience stores. Double B is owned by investment firm, Atlantic Street Capital.
