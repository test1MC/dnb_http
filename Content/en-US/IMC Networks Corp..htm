IMC Networks is a master of mixed media. The company makes media conversion, optical access, and bandwidth management products. Its media converters translate signals that run on the copper wiring employed in LANs to signals that can be transported over fiber optic cables. Other products include repeaters, remotely managed access points, and wavelength division multiplexers (WDM). Its products support multiple networking protocols, including asynchronous transfer mode (ATM), Ethernet, Fast Ethernet, Gigabit Ethernet, Fiber Channel, and T1/E1. Customers include government and education installations and ISPs in more than 60 countries. Founded in 1988, it became a subsidiary of B&B SmartWorx in 2012.
