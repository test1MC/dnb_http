Angang Steel (Ansteel) has its steely gaze fixed on the Chinese market. The firm produces such steel products as billets, cold rolled sheets, color coating plates, wire rods, medium and thick plates, and heavy rails. Ansteel exports only a small portion of its products beyond the Chinese borders. Customers include companies in the automotive, construction, shipbuilding, railway, and pipeline construction industries. Ansteel was formed in 1997 when Anshan Iron and Steel Group consolidated its cold rolling, wire rod, and thick plate operations under the Angang Steel name. Though publicly traded, it still is controlled by Anshan Iron and Steel (a holding company owned by the Chinese government).
