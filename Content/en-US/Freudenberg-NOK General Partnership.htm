Freudenberg-NOK, the Americas joint venture of Freudenberg & Co. (Germany) and NOK Corporation (Japan), makes elastomeric sealing products, lubricants, and custom molded products used primarily in the automotive industry. Doing business as Freudenberg Sealing Technologies, its brand names include Automotive Sealing and Corteco (automotive aftermarket sealing and gasket products), Simrit (industrial and medical seals), and TransTec (transmission and power steering seals). Other markets it serves include aerospace, oil/gas, marine, and chemical processing. Founded in 1989, Freudenberg-NOK has operations in the US, Canada, Brazil, Malaysia, and Mexico.

Headquartered in Michigan, the company has 23 locations across Austria, Brazil, Canada, China, Czech Republic, Denmark, England, Estonia, France, Germany, Hungary, Italy, Malaysia, Netherlands, Poland, and the US.

Freudenberg-NOK markets its products through distributors, including ARAT S.A.S., A C Depuydt, A. Volz Industriebedarf OHG, Abbey Seals International, ABES Technoseal, ACBI, Acorn Industrial Services, and ADIFOR. It serves customers in the automotive, civil aviation, mechanical engineering, shipbuilding, the food and pharmaceutical, and the agricultural, and construction machinery sectors.

In 2014 the company acquired Tobul Accumulator, a global developer and manufacturer of hydraulic accumulators. The acquisition enhanced Freudenberg-NOK's portfolio of technical products in its accumulator business.
