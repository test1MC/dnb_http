Crown Prince hopes to ascend the throne as king of the specialty canned seafood industry. The company's products include canned abalone, anchovies, clam juice, clams, crab meat, kipper snacks, mackerel, oysters, salmon, sardines, and shrimp. Crown Prince imports seafood from Canada, Mexico, and Scotland, as well as South American and Middle Eastern countries. Founded in 1948, the company also offers a line of natural products that contain no preservatives. Crown Prince's seafood is available in the US and Mexico through natural food retailers such as Whole Foods and Wild Oats , as well as at major US food retailers, including Wal-Mart .
