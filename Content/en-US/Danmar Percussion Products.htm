From furry kick beaters to wicked stick holders, Danmar Percussion Products has the accessories that will turn the little drummer boy into a rock god. The company makes and sells drum hardware, accessories, and replacement parts. It also manufactures acoustic percussion instruments such as castanets, chimes, cowbells, triangles, and wood blocks. The company was established in 1970 by president Frank DeVito, who has worked with Elvis, Frank Sinatra, and The Monkees.
