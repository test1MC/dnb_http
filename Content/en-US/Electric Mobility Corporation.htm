Electric Mobility is keeping people on the move. The company, which does business as The Rascal Company, makes electric-powered scooters and other mobility products for the elderly and disabled. Its three-wheeled and four-wheeled scooters come in models for indoor or outdoor use, and some are lightweight and foldable for easy travel. Electric Mobility also makes four-wheeled power chairs, ramps and vehicle lifts, and other home health aids, including stair lifts and electric lift recliners. It primarily sells its products in North America and Europe. The family-owned company was founded in 1974.
