Small World Toys wants to introduce children to a world of fun and learning. The company manufactures toys and educational items for children ranging in age from newborn to age 10. Its brands include IQ Baby, Gertie Ball, Neurosmith, and Ryan's Room. Products are sold in retail, educational, and specialty toy stores including Whole Foods , Learning Express , and Target . Small World stuffed animals, games, and puzzles are also found at children's museums, gift shops, and zoos. The company grants its retailers credits that can be used for co-op advertising on its merchandise. Small World Toys filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection in 2007.
