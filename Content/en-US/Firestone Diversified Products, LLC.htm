Don't come to this Firestone looking for a spare tire. A subsidiary of tire giant Bridgestone 's Bridgestone Americas , Firestone Diversified Products handles many of the non-tire aspects of Bridgestone's business. The company includes three businesses: Firestone Building Products, which makes building and roofing products; Firestone Industrial Products, which makes air springs (for trucks, trailers, and industrial applications); and Firestone Fibers and textiles, a maker of natural and synthetic rubber products, and fibers and textiles. Though it doesn't make tires itself, Firestone Diversified does supply its parent and others with both natural and synthetic rubber, which is used to make tires.
