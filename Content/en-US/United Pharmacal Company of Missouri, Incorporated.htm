United Pharmacal Company (UPCO) of Missouri has answered the call of the wild, as well as the domesticated. The company provides some 15,000 pet products through its Web site and through a pair of catalogs. UPCO boasts a full catalog and one published specifically for feathered friends. The retailer ships books and training tools, collars, grooming supplies, medicines, food, and toys for family members as big as horses or as small as hamsters. The family-owned and -operated company was founded by Charles Evans in 1952 to sell serums and vaccines for farm animals. A second and third generation of the Evans family now runs the company.
