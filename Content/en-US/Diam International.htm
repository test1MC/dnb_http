Diam makes the displays that make the sale. The company designs, makes, installs, and maintains custom retail displays for retailers and manufacturers. Diam's point-of-purchase displays and retail fixtures include back wall cosmetic systems, counter displays, fixtures for electronics requiring power and security, floorstands, kiosks, and stock shelving solutions. Operating globally and comprising Diam USA and Diam Europe subsidiaries, it has manufacturing facilities in the US and overseas. Founded in 1953, Diam's customers have included Clinique , L'Oréal , Microsoft , Nintendo , and Wal-Mart . Michel Vaissaire became the company's CEO in February 2008.
