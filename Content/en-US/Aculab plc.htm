Aculab is helping to make landlines a thing of the past. The company manufactures and sells hardware, software, and cloud-based products to support digital phone platforms. Its voice, data, and fax products are designed for such uses as inbound and outbound call center applications, speech-enabled interactive voice response (IVR) and self-service systems, conferencing and messaging, unified communications, and fax and voice broadcasting. The company also provides professional services such as consulting, training, and co-marketing. Aculab serves clients in 80 countries across the globe from offices in the UK, Australia, Germany, and the US (in Massachusetts). Managing director Alan Pound founded Aculab in 1978.

Aculab offers inbound and outbound SMS in six countries: Belgium, Canada, Spain, Sweden, the UK, and the US. It expects to add more countries to its SMS service list.

The company operates in the UK, Germany, and US.

Customers include NSF Telecom, MyForce, and IT Sonix.
