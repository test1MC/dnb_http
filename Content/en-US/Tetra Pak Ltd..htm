Tetra Pak Ltd. manufactures aseptic laminated paperboard packaging materials used to store liquids such as milk and juice and shelf-stable processed foods such as tomatoes and tuna. Food packages include Tetra Recart, designed for such foods as beans and soups that could previously be stored only in cans and jars. Packaging for chilled foods includes Tetra Brik, Tetra Rex, and Tetra Top. The company's recyclable cartons are comprised of 70 to 90% paperboard. Tetra Pak Ltd. is one of three UK branches of Swiss packaging manufacturer Tetra Pak , itself a subsidiary of Tetra Laval International .
