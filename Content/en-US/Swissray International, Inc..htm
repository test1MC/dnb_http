Giving Superman's X-ray eyes a run for their money, Swissray International makes and sells film-less X-ray imaging equipment. The company's products, based on its direct digital Radiography (ddR) technology platform, include X-ray systems used in nearly all fields of radiology, including orthopedic and bone density scans. The company's eXpert workstation and SwissVision software can enlarge X-ray images and transfer the data through central networks. Swissray markets its products to care providers worldwide through its own sales force, as well as through partnerships with other medical equipment firms and distribution companies. Switzerland-based affiliate Swissray Medical handles international operations.
