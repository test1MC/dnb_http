In a complex world, it's nice to find a company with a name that points to what it does -- transport materials. Materials Transportation Company (MTC) designs, engineers, and manufactures an array of material handling equipment for industries from food processing to automotive, clothing, grocery distribution, and pharmaceutical, among others. The company's food processing equipment includes blenders, conveyors, cookers, and dumpers. Its battery handling equipment boasts gantry and man-aboard changing systems, and portable side extraction units. A microprocessor-controlled unit even automatically charges, changes, and helps to maintain industrial batteries. MTC builds the equipment at its sole facility in Texas.
