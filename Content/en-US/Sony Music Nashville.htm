Heartbreak and agony are staple ingredients for the hit makers at Sony BMG Nashville. The division of Sony Music Entertainment operates country music labels Arista Nashville, BNA Records, Columbia Records Nashville, and RCA Nashville. Together they boast a roster of top talent including Keith Anderson, Brooks & Dunn, Kenny Chesney, Martina McBride, and Carrie Underwood. In addition to its country music offerings, Sony BMG Nashville oversees Provident Music Group , a top label for Christian music featuring such artists as Casting Crowns and Jars of Clay.
