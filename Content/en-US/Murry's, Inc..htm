Though its first product was sandwich steak, Murry's hit the road to success with its frozen French Toast Sticks. The company now offers more than 150 frozen prepared beef, chicken, and pork products, along with its "we've achieved the American dream" French Toast sticks. Its products can be found in more than 10,000 grocery stores throughout the US; Murry's also offers private-label services and sells to restaurants, institutions, and other foodservice businesses. In addition, the firm operates some 30 Murry's supermarkets in Washington DC, Maryland, Virginia, and Philadelphia. The company, founded in 1948, is owned and operated by the third generation of the founding Mendelson family.

Murry's National Sales Division is focused on getting the company's products into grocery stores nationwide. Murry's "Route Sales" territories extend throughout the mid-Atlantic region and include several thousand restaurants, diners, and other foodservice businesses.

Products are sold through retailers the likes of Wal-Mart .

As part of its business, Murry's operates a production facility in Lebanon, Pennsylvania, that has been recently expanded by some 100,000 sq. ft. Its warehouse is located in Maryland near its corporate headquarters.

The company operates 30 company-owned stores in the Baltimore, Philadelphia, Norfolk, and Washington, DC, markets.

Murry's, based in Maryland, serves customers nationwide.
