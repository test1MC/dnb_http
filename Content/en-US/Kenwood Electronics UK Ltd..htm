Asian electronics take on a British accent at Kenwood Electronics UK. The UK-based business operates as one of nearly two dozen European affiliated sales companies owned by Japanese electronics giant, JVC KENWOOD . Kenwood UK primarily markets and sells automotive electronics, including audio equipment (amplifiers, receivers, and speakers) and navigation systems (all-in-one technologies, packages, and related accessories). The company also sells communications products, from amateur (ham) and business radio equipment to two-way portable walkie talkie radios. Kenwood Electronics UK supplies car makers ( Hyundai , SAAB , Skoda , and VW ), as well as after-market dealers and retailers, such as Halfords and Maplin .
