Naver say naver. NAVER (formerly NHN Corp.) operates the popular search engine Naver.com in South Korea and Japan, as well as the online game portal Hangame. Naver.com's success in South Korea has made NAVER the country's largest Internet company. Naver.com offers users e-mail, a toolbar, maps, phone service, and dedicated sites such as Naver News and Naver Books. It also offers user-generated content such as blogs, photo albums, and free home pages. NAVER has since expanded with a children's portal, Jr. Naver, and an online donation portal, Happybean. Hangame has some 30 million members and offers more than 300 games, from casual games to MMORPGs, and has operations in China, Japan, and the US.
