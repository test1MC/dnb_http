Olympus Keymed Group Limited has built a business taking pretty pictures inside and out. A subsidiary of Japanese camera maker Olympus Corporation, it makes binoculars, endoscopes, and a variety of medical and scientific diagnostic equipment. In recent years KeyMed (Medical and Industrial Equipment) was tucked in under the Olympus Keymed Group umbrella, along with Olympus UK Ltd., which was established in 1975 to provide marketing and sales support to the region. The group's Medical and Industrial Equipment division is a top Olympus endoscopic production unit and the exclusive UK distributor of such Olympus products as flexible and rigid endoscopes.
