Taiwan Cement is a hard act to follow. The company manufactures and sells a variety of cement and ready-mixed concrete products for distribution throughout Asia, the Middle East, and the US. As the largest cement supplier in Taiwan, the company produces some 25 million metric tons of cement annually from its plants in Taiwan. It plans to double capacity and expand by building new plants, especially in the growing Chinese market. In 2010 it bought Prosperity Minerals Holdings ' cement unit for about $500 million, enhancing its holdings on the mainland. In addition to its cement operations, the company produces paper, paper bags, and sludge treatment agents.
