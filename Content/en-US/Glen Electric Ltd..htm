When damp and cold set in, Glen Electric helps take the edge off, electrically. Glen Electric is the Northern Ireland division of the Glen Dimplex Group , one of the world's largest manufacturers of domestic heating appliances. The group makes more than 400 products such as portable convectors, oil-filled radiators, skirting heaters, and panel heaters. Its products are distributed in North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia. Brands include Glen, EWT, Electromode, Pelgrim, and Unidare. Aeronautical engineer Martin Naughton founded the company in 1973; he became sole shareholder in 2004.
