This is no shell company! Ise America is an egg producer with more than 6.7 million layers in its barns. Products from the company's processing facilities include fresh shell eggs, pasteurized shell eggs, hard-cooked eggs, and liquid and frozen egg products. Ise America is entirely integrated, operating its own breeder, hatchery, feed mills, and pullet farms. It has offices in Maryland, New Jersey, and South Carolina. The company is a wholly owned subsidiary of Ise K.K., a Japanese egg producer. The US subsidiary was established in 1980; the parent company dates back to 1912.
