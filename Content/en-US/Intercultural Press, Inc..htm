If the world is your oyster, Intercultural Press has a world of information for you. The company, a unit of the UK's Nicholas Brealey Publishing , specializes in books geared toward travel, and working and studying abroad. Its more than 150 titles range from cross-cultural fiction and travel memoirs to books on intercultural issues and doing business in foreign countries. It also publishes a number of books on student exchange programs and handbooks for host families and schools. Intercultural Press welcomes authors to submit their own manuscripts for consideration.
