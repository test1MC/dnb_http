R & Z Ventures, which does business as Kennesaw Fruit and Juice, is a leading supplier of fruit juices to foodservice operators. The company produces traditional and organic, fresh and pasteurized juices, including orange, lemon, lime, apple, grapefruit, and tangerine (seasonal only), along with lemonade and tropical fruit punch for its mainly Florida customers. The company also offers cut fresh fruit in bulk containers. In addition to its foodservice supply business, Kennesaw makes private-label fruit juice for supermarket chains nationwide. Bubba Humphreys founded the company in 1923; R & Z Ventures was formed in 1990 by president Len Rosenberg and partner Ed Zuckerman to purchase Kennesaw.
