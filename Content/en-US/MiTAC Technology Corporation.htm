MiTAC Technology Corporation specializes in making computer notebooks. The company also develops digital audio and video, MP3, LCD TV, wireless communication, software, and power products. MiTAC Technology produced the first notebook computer to use the desktop Intel Pentium 4 processor. The company has manufacturing sites in China and Taiwan. MiTAC makes its products for industrial and military clients. Established in 1989, the company is a subsidiary of MiTAC Synnex Group, which focuses on providing electronic chemicals, IT products, and Web services.
