Deep Foods incorporates exotic flavors from the Far East into its recipes. The company manufactures and markets a range of dry, fresh, and frozen Indian food products, as well as Thai and Chinese entrees. Located in Union, New Jersey, Deep Foods sells its products primarily in specialty food emporiums throughout the US and Canada. The company's offerings, sold to food retailers and foodservice operators, under labels including Bansi, Bhagwati's, Mirch Masala, Reena's, Tandoor Chef, and Udupi, range from appetizers and meals to delicacies and desserts. The family-owned and -operated company was founded in 1977 by Bhagwati and Arvind Amin.
