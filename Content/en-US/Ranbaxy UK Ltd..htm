Ranbaxy UK is making a run at the British generic pharmaceuticals market. The company, a subsidiary of Indian drug firm Ranbaxy Laboratories , offers a wide variety of generic drugs (both branded and unbranded) in the UK for central nervous system disorders, cardiovascular diseases, metabolic disorders, respiratory ailments, and infections. Some of the company's products include gabapentin for epilepsy, simvastatin for high cholesterol, and venlafaxine for depression. Ranbaxy UK represents a major foothold for the Ranbaxy group's operations in Europe, which accounts for about 20% of global annual revenues.
