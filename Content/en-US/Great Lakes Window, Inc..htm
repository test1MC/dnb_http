The goal at Great Lakes Window is crystal clear: to make the highest quality windows and doors for homeowners across the US. The company, a subsidiary of Ply Gem Industries , manufactures custom vinyl replacement windows and patio doors that offer such benefits as energy efficiency, weather resistance, and security. Specific products include single-hung, double-hung, bay, casement, garden, sliding, and awning windows, as well as hinged and sliding patio doors. Brands and product lines include BayShore, GrandView, PlyGem Lifestyles, Seabrooke, and UniFrame. Homeowners and consumers can purchase these products through a nationwide network of remodeling contractors, distributors, and lumberyards.
