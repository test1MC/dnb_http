Lead, follow, or get out of the way, Hyundai Translead is on the move. The subsidiary of Hyundai Motor manufactures refrigerated (reefer) and dry-freight van trailers and domestic containers, as well as container chassis and converter dollies. Its reefers offer improvements of reduced weight and increased strength and airflow. Hyundai Translead containers are also produced per US EPA specifications and used as options for transportation customers wanting a qualified SmartWay fleet. Made in Mexico, trailers are sold both directly and through a network of dealers dotting the US, Canada, and Latin America.
