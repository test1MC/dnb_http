Hisense Group is a well-known brand in China for consumer electronics and household appliances. Its main product lines include TV sets, LCD computer screens, mobile phones, air conditioners, refrigerators, and washing machines. The company operates more than 20 domestic marketing centers, 200 after-sale service stations, and roughly 10,000 repair outlets. Its international operations include about 10 subsidiaries and several offices in North and South America, Europe, Australia, South Africa, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East. Hisense Group operates factories in Africa and China. The firm exports its goods to some 100 countries worldwide.
