The people at Kelvin want you to elevate the degree of style in most of the rooms of your home. The company is a manufacturer of kitchen, bedroom, and bathroom furniture and furniture sets. Kelvin makes kitchen cabinets and islands, bathroom vanity furniture, wardrobe door systems, sliding mirrored doors, freestanding bedroom furniture, and bedroom suites. Key brands include Kerrera, woodland, Groovy, and Haddington. The company targets its products to renovation contractors and customers who do their own home remodeling. As an additional service, Kelvin will complete installation of any built-in furniture. The company also caters to local UK homebuilders, including Cala, Muir, and Gladedale .

Though it deals in bedrooms and bathrooms, the company itself has only one showroom, located in the Glasgow, Scotland-area, from which it services most of the UK.
