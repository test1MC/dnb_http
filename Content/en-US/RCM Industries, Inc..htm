RCM Industries knows the true mettle of aluminum. A custom aluminum die-casting company serving North America, RCM Industries provides tool manufacturing, die casting, CNC machining, and assembly on parts for manufacturers in the appliance, automotive, communications, industrial equipment, lawn and garden, office product, and recreational markets. Offering everything from initial concept design to final production services, the company has four manufacturing facilities in the Midwest and Southeast US and additional operations through partners in China. RCM Industries is owned by president Robert C. Marconi.
