Cision US doesn't underestimate the value of a precise message. The subsidiary of Sweden-based research firm Cision AB provides software and services for public relations and marketing professionals. The company also monitors, plans, and analyzes news and information from electronic media sources; maintains a database of media contacts, and distributes press releases. The company began as a news clipping service (Bacon's Information) in 1932. Cision AB acquired Bacon's in 2001; it was re-branded as Cision US in 2007. In 2015 it agreed to acquire PR Newswire for about $841 million and the company closed the deal in 2016.

Cision US has about a dozen offices throughout the US.

The company is focusing on social media over print, radio, and TV media. The PR Newswire deal gave Cision a much bigger footprint for global press release distribution.
