CNH Industrial is one of the world's largest makers of agricultural combines and tractors (by sales volume). It also makes commercial vehicles, construction vehicles, powertrains and transmission systems, and offers leasing and rental services. Its major brands include Case IH, New Holland Agriculture, and Steyr for tractors and agricultural machinery, Iveco for commercial vehicles and buses, Iveco Astra for quarry and construction vehicles, Magirus for firefighting vehicles, and FPT Industrial for engines and transmissions. CNH Industrial was formed from the 2013 merger of CNH Global and Fiat Industrial .

CNH Industrial has 11 manufacturing facilities and 13 research and development centers in North America.
