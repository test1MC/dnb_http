Universal Beverages Holdings Corporation sells certified kosher bottled seltzer water under the Syfo brand name. Distributed in the southeastern US, Syfo is available in original and flavored varieties, such as lemon-lime, wild cherry, and tangerine-orange. The Leesburg, Florida, company also offers bottled sparkling and still waters as part of its business. Universal's water products are sold nationwide at food and beverage retailers the likes of Publix and Winn-Dixie . Syfo waters, which originated in 1949, are also exported outside the US to the Caribbean and Bermuda.
