You might say this company is more interested in book learners than page turners. McGraw-Hill Ryerson (MHR) is a leading Canadian book publisher focused on the education market and a subsidiary of McGraw-Hill Education . It produces textbooks and multimedia materials for the higher education and elementary and secondary school markets (through its Higher Education and School divisions) covering math, hard sciences, technology, and social sciences. The company also has a Professional division that publishes reference materials for professional in such fields as business, computers, and medicine. McGraw-Hill Education is owned by Apollo Global Management .
