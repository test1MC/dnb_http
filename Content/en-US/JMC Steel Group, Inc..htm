JMC Steel Group knows more about tubes than a London subway commuter, a California surfer, or an old-time TV repairman. Founded in 1877 as John Maneely Company, JMC specializes in the production of steel pipe and tubing used, among other applications, in commercial and industrial buildings, oil wells and refineries, bridges, fencing, machinery, solar panels, and utility poles. The company operates 15 manufacturing facilities and produces upwards of 2.5 million tons of products each year. Its oldest division is Wheatland Tube ; other divisions include Atlas Tube Energex Tube, Sharon Tube, and Picoma. JMC is owned by Barry Zekelman and his brothers, Alan and Clayton.

Based in Chicago, JMC Steel has locations in Illinois, Ohio, Pennsylvania, Arkansas, Texas, Alabama, and Michigan. In Canada, it has plants in Alberta, Manitoba, and Ontario.

The steel pipe and tubing maker's customers include the non-residential construction industry, as well as the energy, transportation, and utilities markets, and the US government.

Wheatland Tube's products include fire sprinkler piping, electrical conduit, well casing pipe, couplings, and hydraulic shafts, along with standard steel pipe. Atlas Tube specializes in structural tubing in round, square, and rectangular shapes used in construction, trailer hitches, agricultural and recreational equipment, and lighting systems. Picoma manufactures galvanized elbows, couplings, and threaded pipe. JMC also offers oil and gas pipes and tubes though its EnergeX unit and precision tube through Sharon Tube.

In 2014, the company implemented a $35 million revitalization program for its Council Avenue plant in Wheatland, Pennsylvania. The project included new equipment, realigning the manufacturing footprint, and automating many processes.

In early 2012 JMC acquired Canadian oil field supplier Lakeside Steel for $58 million and established its EnergeX Tube division, which provides piping and tubes to oil and gas customers. JMC combined assets from Lakeside Steel and its existing oil and gas product lines to form EnergeX.
