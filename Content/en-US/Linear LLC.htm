Linear has a system and it's sticking with it. The company manufactures access control systems, intercom systems, wireless security alarms and emergency response systems, as well as smoke and fire detection systems. It is a leading maker of radio frequency (RF) security and alarm systems for both the residential and commercial markets. Its Audio, Video, and Control group markets consumer electronics, such as music/communications systems, intercoms, and central vacuum systems. Founded in 1961, the company is a subsidiary of Nortek, Inc. ,which makes air conditioning and heating systems and other building products.

While the company conducts its engineering, research, and development at its headquarters in California, the vast majority of its manufacturing is done at its China facility. Regional distribution facilities reside in Vista, California; and Summerville and Charleston, South Carolina.

Linear is part of Nortek's Home Technology Products segment. It works alongside other Nortek units, including Niles Audio , Xantech , and ELAN Home Systems .

The company sells its products through more than 400 distributors and has two regional distribution facilities located on the East and West coasts. Its major customer groups include: security and control, assess and integration, health and wellness, and home technology.

While its focus has been on RF security and alarm systems for almost 50 years, Linear has been diversifying its portfolio to include products such as consumer electronics, audio/video systems, central vacuum systems, speaker systems, and structured wiring systems.

Its parent also uses acquisitions to beef up its core security software line. In 2013 Nortek purchased 2GIG Technologies, an integrated security systems manufacturer, for $135 million. The deal expanded Linear's expertise in providing smart home technology security products. It is keeping the 2GIG brand name.
