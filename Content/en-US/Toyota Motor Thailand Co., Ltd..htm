Toyota Motor Thailand is nurturing the Thai that binds. The largest auto manufacturer in Thailand, the company makes the Land Cruiser Prado SUV and the Hilux one-ton truck, as well as the Altis, Camry, Vios, and Yaris sedans, and the Wish minivan, among other models. Toyota Motor Thailand is doing its part to help revamp Thailand's automotive industry by increasing the use of locally produced parts for its vehicles. Most of Toyota Motor Thailand's export sales are made in Australia, but the company also exports to Singapore and Brunei. The company and its affiliates have more than 100 dealers, with nearly 300 showrooms.
