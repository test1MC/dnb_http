Cathay International Holdings is a diversified holding company with interests in pharmaceuticals, food ingredients, and hospitality. Its Lansen Pharmaceutical Holdings subsidiary makes prescription drugs to treat rheumatic disease, while Xian Haotian Bio-Engineering Technology is focused on manufacturing plant extracts used in making beverages, cosmetics, and food products. In addition, Cathay International owns and operates the Landmark Shenzhen, a luxury hotel in China that is part of the Crowne Plaza Hotel & Suites chain (part of InterContinental Hotels ).
