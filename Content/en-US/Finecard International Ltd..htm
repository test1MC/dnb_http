Finecard International, doing business as the Ninja Corporation, makes it easier for children's imaginations to run wild in the backyard or the gameroom. The Ninja Corporation makes pop-up play structures for children. Products include the Animal Barn Puppet Theater, the Family RV, and the Bouncy Castle. The company also makes pop-up shelters and gazebos for outdoor activities, golf practice structures, food protector tents, and executive toys, such as desk basketball. Its best-selling Sun Protection collection boasts UV protection. Items are distributed through general goods department stores. The UK-based company is owned by founders Anthony Brereton and Dee Baxter.

The Ninja Corporation works from its home office in the UK along with those in Hong Kong and the United Arab Emirates. It caters to consumers in Europe, the Far East, and North America.

The Ninja Corporation serves its target audience by continuing to roll out new products. More recent product launches include the 3D PlayScapes line of indoor/outdoor play products. These pop-up structures include bed tents, "castles", and miniature "shops" designed to help kids role play and use their imaginations.
