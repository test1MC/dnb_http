People at this company will definitely tell you it's the egg that comes first. Cargill Kitchen Solutions (formerly Sunny Fresh Foods) is a leading manufacturer of value-added processed egg products for distribution to foodservice operators, schools, the US military, and institutional organizations, as well as consumer food manufacturers throughout the US. It makes refrigerated and frozen liquid egg products, hard-cooked and peeled eggs, diced eggs, cholesterol- and fat-free liquid eggs, and egg entrees (egg and ham or sausage patty on a bagel). The company, whose products are available under the Sunny Fresh brand name, is a unit of food and agriculture giant Cargill .
