Loggins Meat Company is a leading supplier of portion-controlled meat products for restaurant operators and other foodservice industry customers. Through its two meat processing facilities, the company provides a wide variety of beef, chicken, and pork products, including flavored and breaded meats. The company also custom-created products for its customers. Loggins Meat was founded by Bob D. Loggins in 1940. Chairman and CEO Bobby G. Loggins controls the company.
