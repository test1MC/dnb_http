Fastrack Design doesn't make the computer chip, it makes the blueprint for the company that makes the computer chip. It designs custom integrated circuits and application specific integrated circuits (ASIC) to produce a "tapeout" or guide that semiconductor fabs use to make circuits. Fastrack's ASIC turnkey services include packaging, testing, and assembly; the company also offers sign-off, implementation, and methodology services. It has created circuit designs for chip companies NuCORE , MetaRAM, Legend Silicon, and eASIC. President and CEO Moazzem Hossain and VP of professional design services Gabriel Ho formed Fastrack Design in 2001 after working together at Magma Design Automation .
