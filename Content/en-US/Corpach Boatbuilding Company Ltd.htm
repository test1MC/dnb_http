Corpach Boatbuilding can help you to get your sea legs. The Scotland-based company builds, repairs, and modifies boats of various sizes. Additionally, Corpach can perform services for both commercial and pleasure boats that range from painting and marine engineering to blast preparation and steel fabrication. The company's pier and landing facilities can handle crafts of up to 200 tons. The facilities can also provide berthing, mooring, and drying out for vessels up to more than 260 feet in length. The company builds workboats, which have included fish farm landing craft, mussel harvesters, and inter-island ferries, up to about 100 feet long.

The company delivers its boats across the UK and Europe.

In addition to building boats, Corpach also offers a repair and breakdown service to yachts/boats in transit through the Caledonian Canal. It also operates charters and cruises, through Clyde Classic Sailing and Caledonian Discovery Ltd.

The company serves a number of marine sectors, including fish farming, inter-island ferries, inshore and offshore fishing, workboats, private yachts, and other commercial craft.

Corpach seeks new markets and develop its range of services to meet the needs of core customer and industry developments.

The company is also focused on upgrading its plant and equipment and maintaining its facilities to the highest operational and safety standards.

Corpach was founded in 1992.
