Pervo Paint hopes the elements won't penetrate its coatings. The company makes a variety of paints and industrial coatings ranging from exterior and interior house paint to those used to mark highways and parking lots. Its products include curb coatings, interior and exterior paints, industrial enamels, waterproofing systems, and thermoplastic marking materials. Pervo Paint operates two manufacturing facilities and three retail outlets. It serves customers throughout the western US, as well as in Asia. The company, family-owned and -operated since 1929, was acquired by Ennis Prismo in 2010, and will operate as a subsidiary. Pervo means impervious in Dutch.
