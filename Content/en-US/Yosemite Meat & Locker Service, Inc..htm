Since "Yosemite" means "those who kill," it is a fitting name for this meat processor. Yosemite Meat & Locker Service specializes in the slaughter and cutting of fresh pork. The company also offers fresh beef and chicken, along with specialties such as beef tripe, goat, quail, and headcheese. Its brand names include Yosemite, Yosemite Gourmet, and Azteca (aimed at California's large Latino population). The company's customers include Hispanic and Asian supermarkets, as well as restaurants and carnicerias (Mexican meat markets) located primarily in California and Nevada.
