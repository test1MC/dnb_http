CIE Automotive has the mettle to supply more than just metal for carmakers. Operating worldwide, CIE organizes its activities into biofuels and automobile parts manufacturing and marketing. Its auto parts lineup includes components for engine and powertrain systems, as well as chassis and steering systems and exterior and interior trim. It also also produces and sells biofuels. Manufacturing facilities specialize in aluminum, forge and machining, metal, and plastics. CIE does business in the Americas, Europe, Asia, and in developing countries through joint ventures with local suppliers. Top OEM clients include Ford , GM , Chrysler, and Volkswagen . Effective 2014, it owns Advanced Comfort Systems outright.
