Scholar Craft Products wants to be at the head of the class and all around the room. The company manufactures ergonomic, durable and practical (primarily plastic) classroom furnishings through its six facilities in Alabama, Arkansas, and Massachusetts. Products include stacking and folding chairs, plastic chair desks, open-front and lift-lid desks, computer and activity tables, and teachers desks. In addition to supplying the education market, the firm makes rental and commercial products under the Scholar Craft Commercial name. The company made its first school furniture in 1956.
