When the financial markets dance the can-can, Fininfo is front and center. The French company provides online financial information and analysis tools covering both domestic as well as international markets to stock traders, fund managers, and other financial professionals. It also provides access to information databases covering mutual funds and markets transaction software for financial institutions such as trading houses and banks. In addition, Fininfo disseminates other commercial information such as risk and liability data, credit reports, and legal information. In 2007 the company was aqcuired by financial information firm The Telekurs Group, which is a joint venture between Swiss banks.
