BIOQUELL uses hydrogen peroxide vapors to quell uprisings of biological contaminants. The company's low-temperature, residue-free technology eliminates bacteria, fungi, and viruses in rooms and buildings. The company's services and products, which include vapor generators and specialist filtration equipment, are used for applications in the biotechnology, defense, food, health care, laboratory, and pharmaceutical industries. The company's Cape Engineering subsidiary offers environmental stress testing and software-based stress analysis. BIOQUELL operates in Europe and North America.
