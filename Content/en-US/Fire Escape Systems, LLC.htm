Your office building probably has a fire escape of some kind, but your house might very well not, and that's where Fire Escape Systems comes in. The company manufactures and distributes fire escape ladders for residential as well as commercial markets. Fire Escape Systems offers a dozen types of ladders, including portable chain ladders and permanent ladders that be installed inside or outside a window. Its ladders come in 55 different sizes. In addition to ladders for indoors and out, Fire Escape Systems also sells emergency kits and evacuation hoods. The hoods are plastic bags that fit over the head and filter out smoke and bacteria; emergency kits contain ladders, light sticks, air horns, and hoods.
