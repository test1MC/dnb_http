Every breath you take creates microcontaminants, which are hazardous in the ultraclean conditions of pharmaceutical and semiconductor manufacturing. Huntair makes air handling equipment for high-tech clean rooms and other specialized applications. It also serves the aerospace, biotech, and pharmaceutical industries. It makes ceiling systems (plenums, grids, and fans) for clean rooms, along with electrical enclosures. Huntair operates six facilities in Arizona and Oregon and serves customers in 27 countries. Huntair was established in 1994 by Ed Hunt (former chairman) and Dave Benson (president and CEO). In 2006 Huntair's assets were acquired by NTK Holdings (Nortek), which filed Chapter 11 in October 2009.
