GridPoint can help you chart a course off the grid. The company's smart grid software manages load distribution, energy storage, and renewable power integration for corporations. Its intelligent energy management appliances provide traditional and renewable energy users with solutions to monitor and manage their energy usage, as well as provide automatic backup power. GridPoint's services include hardware such as controllers and submeters, enterprise software, professional assistance and mobile solutions. This energy-saving technology also offers the potential to sell back excess energy produced by solar cells to utility companies. GridPoint is a subsidiary of Washington, DC-based Twenty First Century Utilities, which owns and operates utilities.
