ADCO Global would prefer that you seal your windows, doors, and roofs with its line of adhesives and sealants rather than with kisses. Through its worldwide group of subsidiaries -- including ADCO Products (US), Kömmerling (Germany), and ADCO Kömmerling (China) -- ADCO Global provides products for construction, automotive, and specialty industrial markets, serving huge corporations and individual customers. The company operates from 10 global locations and sells its products in more than 65 countries. ADCO Global was acquired by private equity firm Arsenal Capital Partners in mid-2013. After the transaction, Arsenal merged ADCO with its own Royal Adhesives and Sealants business.
