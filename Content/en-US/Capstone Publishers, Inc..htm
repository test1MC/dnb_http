Capstone Publishers makes at least one of the three Rs exciting. The company publishes children's books under six imprints -- Capstone Press, Compass Point Books, Heinemann-Raintree , Picture Window Books, Red Brick Learning, and Stone Arch Books. Capstone publishes fiction and nonfiction books, picture books, literacy books in English and Spanish, and educational comic books for the pre-kindergarten to high school set. Its more than 13,000 titles are distributed to schools and public libraries nationwide. The company also offers online content, such as the Capstone Interactive Library, which has more than 200 e-books available for kids to read online or in CD-ROM format, and other educational Web sites.
