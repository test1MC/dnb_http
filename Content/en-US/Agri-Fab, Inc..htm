So you need to mow the back 40? Agri-Fab has the tools to get the job done. Since 1973, the company has manufactured lawn and garden attachments for tractors and in recent years for three-wheeled all-terrain vehicles (ATVs). Among its lineup of rust-resistant tow-behind lawngrooming equipment are sweepers, aerators, carts, levelers, and mowers. Agri-Fab operates a 350,000-sq. ft. manufacturing facility in Illinois. Its products are sold through select dealers, such as the Army and Air Force Exchange Service , Lowe's , Sears , and True Value . The company's customers also can order replacement parts through its website.
