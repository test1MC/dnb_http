Puzzled about what to do with your time? Buffalo Games has some suggestions. The company makes and markets puzzles of all shapes and sizes -- many through licensing agreements with the likes of Disney , Warner Bros. , and the Audubon -- as well as board games under the iMAgiNiff name and puzzle-preservation product Puzzle Presto! The company's products portfolio includes traditional jigsaw puzzles and specialty puzzles, like 3-D spherical versions. It also makes party games, card games, and educational games. Buffalo Games sells its items nationwide through retailers (such as Target and Wal-Mart ) and online via such e-tailers as Amazon . Founded in 1986, Buffalo Games began manufacturing its own products in 1996.
