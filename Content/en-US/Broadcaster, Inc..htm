Broadcaster has something to say about game developing and mobile phone value added services.  The company operates through two businesses. Broadcaster's LampLighter Studios, acquired in 2008, provides graphic art for online games, cell phone games, video games, and commercial video projects. Its Eyecandy Media subsidiary, also acquired that year, offers video technology and value added services for mobile phones in China. In 2007 Broadcaster discontinued its Social Media Network, an Internet entertainment network that allowed users to upload and view videos, publish and access live Web cam feeds, and chat with other users.
