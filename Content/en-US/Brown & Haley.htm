Brown & Haley will have you seeing brown with its chocolates and other confections. Its products include Almond Roca, Mountain Bars, Caramel Clusters, Buttercrunch Thins, as well as its boxed assortments. The company's Mocha Roca products are available at retail food stores throughout the US. It also operates two factory outlet stores in Tacoma, Washington. The company was founded in 1912 by Harry Brown and J.C. Haley and is owned and operated by Haley's descendants. The confectioner sells its products in more than 60 countries worldwide through retailers and its website.
