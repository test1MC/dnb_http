For those who like to savor a good meal, SavvyDiner.com offers online reservations to some of the nation's finer eating establishments. The Web site's restaurant index includes nearly 1,000 locations in more than 40 US cities, from Atlanta to Tampa Bay, Florida. The company also works with upscale hotels to refer guests to quality restaurants. It surveys the concierge staff of the top hotels in the cities it covers for their restaurant recommendations, and also follows local and national food critics' comments and reviews. SavvyDiner.com was founded in 1987.
