Foxconn Technology is known by the company it keeps. The company is an electronics manufacturing services (EMS) firm that makes a range of computer, communication, and consumer electronics products. While the Taiwan-based company focuses primarily on the production of mobile handsets, it also manufactures computers, computer heat sinks, and magnesium aluminum alloys. Customers include a number of high-profile consumer product makers, including Apple , Dell , Hewlett-Packard , Nokia , and Sony . Incorporated in 1990, Foxconn Technology has manufacturing facilities in Taiwan and China. It is part of the Hon Hai group of companies.
