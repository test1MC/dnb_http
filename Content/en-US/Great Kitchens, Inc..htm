Great Kitchens thinks great take 'n bake is its ticket to great sales. The company is one of North America's largest private-label manufacturers and distributors of frozen deli pizzas and appetizers. Its pizzas are prepared fresh and then frozen for sale to major US grocery retailers, who resell the products under their own brands usually in their deli or prepared foods sections. Customers include grocery chains, club stores, and foodservice distributors, such as Wal-Mart , Target , and Trader Joe's . Meatballs, mini quesadillas, potato skins, and stuffed jalapeno peppers round out its menu offerings. The Swiss baked goods supplier ARYZTA AG acquired Great Kitchens from former owner Arbor Investments in 2010.
