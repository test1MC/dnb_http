Runco hopes to keep you firmly planted on your sofa, with a bare minimum of running around. The company is a leading manufacturer of high-end home theater products. Runco's product line includes plasma and LCD displays, DLP projectors, processors, and signal enhancers. Its brands include CineWide, Video Xtreme, and Vidikron. The manufacturer primarily targets the profitable home theater market. The company was founded in 1986 by CEO Sam Runco, who had developed and sold video projectors since the early 1970's under the name Runco Video. Runco was acquired in May 2007 by Planar Systems , a supplier of hardware and software for specialty display.
