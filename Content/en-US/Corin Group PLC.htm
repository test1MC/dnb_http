Corin Group does its best work right at the literal crux of arthritis problems. The company designs and manufactures reconstructive orthopedic devices used in joint replacement procedures. Most specifically, it manufactures replacement ankles, hips, knees, and ligament augmentation materials targeted towards the needs of younger, active arthritic patients who wish to maintain a high level of activity. The company has handful of international subsidiaries in Australia, Germany, Japan, and the US. Corin sells its products directly and through distributors in some 40 countries. The company -- which operates one main manufacturing and supply center in the UK -- was established in 1985 and went public in 2002.
