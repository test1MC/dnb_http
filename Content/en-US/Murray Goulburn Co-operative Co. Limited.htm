If you drink milk in Oz, chances are you've encountered Murray Goulburn (MG), Australia's largest dairy foods company. Also known as Devondale Murray Goulburn, MG supplies dairy ingredients (milk powders, cheese, milk-fat, whey, specialty proteins) globally. In Australia it also sells Devondale, Liddells, Cobram, and Kiewa milk, cheese, and other dairy items. Other offerings include specialty nutritional ingredients, farm and hardware supplies, and domestic milk distribution. MG also participates in joint ventures with Groupe Danone (R&D), and INTERMIX Australia (ingredient production). Formed in 1950, the co-operative is owned by its members.
