Koninklijke Wegener is one of the largest publishers of daily newspapers and free door-to-door weekly papers in the Netherlands. Its portfolio is anchored by flagship title De Gelderlander , along with regional papers Brabants Dagblad and de Stentor . The company also operates about 20 Internet sites including JobTrack.nl and AutoTrack.nl. In addition to publishing, Wegener has operations in graphics services and commercial printing. UK-based Mecom Group , a publisher of international newspapers, owns more than 85% of the company.
