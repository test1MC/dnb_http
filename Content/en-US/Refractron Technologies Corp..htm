Refractron Technologies acts on technologies related to ceramics. The company produces advanced engineered ceramics, some of which employ proprietary zirconia and alumina components. Its products boast a range of precisely controlled porosity and are used in applications such as filtration, aeration, and diffusion, and in devices including dispersion parts, pumps, gas probes, orthopedic implants, fingerprint pads, and vacuum chucks. Refractron Technologies' ceramic alloy components are used in chemical, healthcare, and petrochemical industry applications. The company also collaborates with clients to custom-design ceramics products.
