Sofame Technologies is famous for its environmentally-efficient direct-contact water heaters and heat recovery systems. The Canada-based company creates systems that extract heat from natural gases and wastewater and then return the heat to industrial, commercial, and institutional buildings. Sofame set up a joint venture in the US in 2010 as a way to grow business there. A year before, Sofame announced plans to buy TransPacific Energy in California. However, that deal fell through. The company also has a French subsidiary (Sofame Europe) that serves international markets in Europe, North Africa, and the Middle East.
