A couple of drinks from Guinness Anchor Berhad might give you the gift of GAB. The company makes and distributes beer and stout in Malaysia under the brands Anchor Smooth, Anglia Shandy, Guinness, Heineken, Kilkenny, Malta, and Tiger. The company has weathered a tough business climate in Malaysia since taxes on beer and stout have increased significantly. GAB has introduced new dispensing methods and in-house efficiency programs; it has also upgraded its headquarters and brewery. The company enjoys a 57% market share in Malaysia. GAPL, a joint venture holding company between Diageo and Asia Pacific Breweries (owned by Heineken and Fraser and Neave ), owns 51% of GAB.
