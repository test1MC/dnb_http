Investors seeking information (and the advertisers who'd like to reach them) might give Digital Look a looksie. Its DigitalLook.com is a financial information portal for investors and financial, IR, and media professionals. Its Corporate Solutions service aggregates and distributes data to corporate clients via hosted websites or data feeds. Digital Look also includes ProShare Investment Clubs (seminars and other resources for investment clubs) and ShareCast (financial news alert service), and its Advertising Solutions target financial professionals on its DigitalLook, ShareCast, and ProShare websites. Digital Look was founded in 1998 by entrepreneur Mark Pritchard. Today it is a part of Web Financial Group.
