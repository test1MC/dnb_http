Gemfire hopes to crystallize multiple optical components into one hot product lineup. Under its PhotonIC brand, Gemfire is developing optical networking devices -- such as attenuator arrays and pump laser source arrays -- that replace separate components with single, highly integrated units. Gemfire also touts its expertise in planar processing methods for lithium niobate wafers, from which the components are made. The company hopes these design and production advances will allow it to produce components much more cheaply than competitors. Investors in Gemfire, which was founded in 1995, included heavyweights Cisco Systems , Corning , Eastman Kodak , and Intel , and top venture capital firm Kleiner Perkins .
