Celebriducks, a company hatched by an animation artist and his product design major daughter, doesn't make a rubber ducky you'd see bobbing around in Ernie's bathtub. The firm offers rubber ducks with eerily detailed features that mirror James Brown, Mae West, Shakespeare, Betty Boop, Elvis, Bill Clinton, Mr. T, and The Osbournes. They're sold through major department stores, smaller specialty shops, and its Web site. These fowl are also commissioned by major sports teams for game giveaways. Its Allen Iverson duck, made for the Philadelphia 76ers and complete with cornrows and tattoos, was Celebriduck's most difficult design yet. The nontoxic-vinyl ducks are manufactured in China.
