Fish oil is easier to swallow if you can't taste the stuff, which is where Soft Gel Technologies helps out. The company makes hermetically sealed gelatin encapsulations of dietary supplements -- vitamins, minerals, fatty acids, and herbal extracts. The company manufactures more than 150 products, both private label and branded, to promote digestive health, immune system support, urinary tract wellness, and weight loss. Its brands include Carnisol, Femalgen, GlucoFit, and LyCoQ. Soft Gel Technologies also provides contract research and development, formulation, manufacturing, and sales and marketing services for other nutritional products companies. The company is owned by Japanese chemicals firm Kenko.
