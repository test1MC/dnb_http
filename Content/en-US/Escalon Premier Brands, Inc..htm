Escalon Premier Brands, Inc., got thee to a cannery. The company prepares and packs tomatoes for the US foodservice industry. (Its main customers are restaurants.) Processing approximately 400,000 tons of tomatoes per year, Escalon manufactures canned tomatoes under the brands 6-in-1, Allegro, Bontá, Bella Rosa, Christina's (an organic line), Cristoforo Colombo, and El Verano Sabroso. Canned tomatoes are available peeled, ground, diced, or crushed; the company also offers ready-to-eat pizza sauce and tomato puree. Escalon Premier Brands, a subsidiary of Heinz Foodservice , is the largest employer in the town of Escalon, Calif.
