Yahoo Japan Corporation operates Yahoo Japan, a Japanese online information portal that offers auctions, entertainment, news, and online shopping in addition to its search engine and Internet directory. It also provides registered users with e-mail, personalized Web pages, and access to topical message boards. Revenue is generated from advertising, e-commerce, and consumer and business services. The acquisitive company is working to capitalize on the growth of smartphones in the Japanese market. Tokyo-based technology investor SOFTBANK and US-based Internet portal Yahoo! jointly set up Yahoo Japan in 1996; the two firms each own about 35% of the company.
