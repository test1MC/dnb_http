Meade Instruments has been opening windows to the universe for decades as a leading telescope maker. Made under the Meade and Coronado brands, its telescopes are popular with universities, scientific laboratories, and aerospace firms, as well as with amateur astronomers. Founded in 1972, the company also offers binoculars, microscopes, weather stations, clocks, and telescope software and accessories. Products are sold through more than 300 specialty retailers and mass merchandisers worldwide, including Wal-Mart , Sam's Club , Costco , and Target , and by mail-order dealers. Jinghua Optics & Electronics Co., Ltd., which boasts a pair of manufacturing facilities in China, agreed in 2013 to acquire Meade.
