Simply Fresh Foods has first pick of the produce its parent peddles. The company offers a wide variety of chilled, pre-packaged stir-fry kits, vegetable side dishes, snacks, sauces and dips, soups, and hydroponically grown bean sprouts. Products sourced by Morrisons (Worsley) are available in bulk or individual servings. They're primarily sold through grocery retailer Morrisons and other sister supermarket chains under the owner's umbrella. The packaged foods provider was acquired in 2010 by Morrisons Produce Ltd., the manufacturing division of Wm Morrison Supermarkets , to become a dedicated produce packing and distribution service for the company.

The company operates through its stores and online.

In 2010 Wm Morrison Supermarkets bought the grocery giant bought Simply Fresh Foods, known for stir-fry mixes, prepared vegetables, and ready-to-eat salads, to boost its capacity and to consolidate sourcing for these products in-house. The move was necessary to help Morrisons meet the demand of some 10 million customers each week as the supermarket operator reaches across the UK for growth.
