Green, lush, and overflowing with cows, New Zealand makes more milk than its citizens can drink. They use what they can, and Fonterra Co-operative Group markets the excess and value-added dairy products through its principal marketing arms, New Zealand Milk Products (NZMP) and Fonterra Brands. While NZMP is the world's #1 supplier of industrial dairy ingredients, Fonterra Brands produces fluid milk, yogurt, cheese, and ice cream for consumers. Its products (both industrial and consumer) are sold worldwide. Fonterra also operates more than 55 RD1 rural supply stores in New Zealand. Fonterra represents some 11,000 dairy farmers who supply the cooperative with 15 billion liters of milk each year.
