Stores and offices would probably run much slower without Toshiba TEC products. Through three business units, the company provides point-of-sale (POS) terminals and electronic cash registers for retailers; office peripherals such as digital copiers, fax machines, and printers; and barcode and radio frequency identification (RFID) printers and terminals for the logistics and financial markets. Toshiba TEC has a worldwide manufacturing and sales network. Its products are generally sold under the Toshiba brand by both domestic and foreign subsidiaries and distributors. In 2012 the company made its first installment of about an $850 million payment for IBM 's retail store solutions business.
