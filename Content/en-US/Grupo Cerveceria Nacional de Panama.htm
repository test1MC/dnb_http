Grupo Cervecería Nacional de Panamá (Cervecería Nacional) has been quenching thirsts since the nearby interoceanic water passage was just a construction site (for the history-challenged, that's the Panama Canal). The company's beer brands include Atlas and Balboa but it also distributes the Miller and Corona brands. Through its subsidiaries, Cervecería makes and distributes Pepsi , 7 UP , Canada Dry, Orange Crush, Squirt, as well as Brisa bottled water and La Chircana shelf-stable milk products. Founded in 1909 as the Panama Brewing & Refrigerating Company by five local businessmen, Cervecería Nacional has grown to become one of the leading brewers in Panama. It is a subsidiary of SABMiller .
