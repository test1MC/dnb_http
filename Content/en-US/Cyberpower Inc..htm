Cyberpower manufactures custom PCs, primarily for the gaming set. Its desktop configurations include systems optimized for video game play, business and graphics workstations. and multimedia PCs. The company also offers notebook PCs ranging from entry-level models to high-end gaming devices. Its systems incorporate processors from both AMD and Intel , and peripherals such as Viewsonic displays and Sony optical drives. Cyberpower markets its products through its Web site to businesses, government agencies, and schools, as well as to individual consumers.
