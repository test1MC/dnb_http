Jacob Leinenkugel gets its kicks filling kegs. The Jacob Leinenkugel Brewing Co. makes several varieties of hand-crafted beer, producing seven year-round and four seasonal beers at its two Wisconsin breweries. Sold in 45 US states, its year-round brands include Leinenkugel's Original, Sunset Wheat, and Honey Weiss. Seasonally, it offers Oktoberfest, 1888 Bock, and Fireside Nut Brown. The company, the seventh oldest operating brewery in the US, is part of MillerCoors . However, the fifth generation of Leinenkugels continue to run the brewing operation.
