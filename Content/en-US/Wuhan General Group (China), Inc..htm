Wuhan General Group helps keep the lights on in China. The group operates through two subsidiaries that manufacture industrial machines for use in power plants. Wuhan Blower makes axial fans, centrifugal blowers, and noise-reducing exhaust silencers. The company is the largest manufacturer of industrial blowers in China, producing about 300 units per year. Wuhan Generating Equipment manufactures 300 MW steam turbines and 200 MW water turbines for use in electrical power plants. Customers come from the iron and steel, power generation, and chemical industries and include Mitsubishi Heavy Industries .
