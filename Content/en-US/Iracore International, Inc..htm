Iracore International (formerly Industrial Rubber Products) manufactures and supplies rubber and polyurethane protective materials to heavy industries, such as mineral-processing, power, and pulp and paper processing. Operating through five subsidiaries (Irathane/Elliot, Industrial Rubber Applicators, Iracore, Irathane Systems, and TJ Products), the company makes Iracore-brand cast parts, pipe and pipe-lining products (rubber lining for overland slurry pipes and other pipes used in mineral processing to remove tailings), standard rubber products, and grinding mill parts. It was acquired by Lime Rock Partners and Thompson Street Capital Partners in 2008.
