ADM Tronics has had its own Industrial Revolution. While the company previously focused on the making of medical devices, ADM has shifted its main focus to water-based chemical products for industrial use. These products include coatings, resins, primers, and additives, primarily for the printing and packaging industries. The firm licenses many of its medical products, which include the Sonotron line of devices (used to treat osteoarthritis and inflammatory joint ailments with radio waves). Its Pros-Aide unit makes adhesives used in professional makeup products. ADM spun off Ivivi Technologies in 2006 but still owns about a third of the company. The founding DiMino family owns nearly half of ADM Tronics.
