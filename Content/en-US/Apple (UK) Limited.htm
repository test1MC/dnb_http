Apple 's UK arm provides the iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, desktop (Mac Pro and iMac) and notebook (MacBook) personal computers, servers, and peripheral displays. The UK subsidiary also offers Apple TV, multimedia and operating system software, wireless networking equipment, and third-party hardware and software. Apple sells music, movies, and TV shows through its iTunes service and offers a music subscription service, Apple Music. The company markets to consumer, government, education, and corporate customers. It sells directly online and from its chain of retail stores (about 40 in the UK), as well as through electronics retailers, distributors, and resellers.

Europe contributed 22% revenue of the parents' revenue in 2015. Revenue increased by 14% compared to previous year driven primarily by increased net sales and unit sales of MacBook Air, MacBook Pro and Mac Pro.
