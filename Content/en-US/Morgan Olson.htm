Morgan Olson's products are as close as your next FedEx delivery. The company, a subsidiary of J.B. Poindexter & Co. , makes walk-in van and truck bodies used for delivery and cargo vehicles. Morgan Olson's riveted aluminum bodies are sold under the brand name Route Star and are built on three chassis: Freightliner , Ford , and Workhorse . Morgan Olson also sells parts and accessories ranging from wipers and windshields to bumpers and grilles. In addition to FedEx delivery and mobile workshops, customers include fleets that deliver baked goods, laundry, and newspapers.

Morgan Olson operates from two locations. The Sturgis Michigan location is responsible for walk-in van manufacturing and distribution. The North Charleston, South Carolina, manufacturing facility produces Sprinter dry freight delivery bodies. The company has its presence throughout North America.

Founded in 1946 the company was formed as a result of the merger of Morgan Corporation and Grumman Olson, which Poindexter bought in 2003.
