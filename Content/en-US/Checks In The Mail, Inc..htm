It's not difficult to imagine what the folks at Checks In The Mail think of the bank check card. The company makes paper bank checks for business and personal use and sells them throughout the US via its catalog and website. It offers more than 200 different types of check designs (including religious, cartoons, animals, and floral) as well as coordinated checkbook covers, personalized address labels, and custom stamps. Checks In The Mail also markets organizers, stationery, business card cases, luggage tags, coin purses, and coasters. Founded in 1922, the company is part of M&F Worldwide 's Harland Clarke unit.
