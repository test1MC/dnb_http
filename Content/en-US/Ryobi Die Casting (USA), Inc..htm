Ryobi Die Casting (USA) knows pressure. The company is North America's largest supplier of aluminum transmission cases. In fact, it manufactures high pressure aluminum die-castings for an array of automotive applications. The list of products includes transmission cases and housings, engine blocks, and structural parts. In addition, Ryobi makes parts for manufacturers of lawn and garden power tools. Ryobi operates two casting plants and a machining plant in Shelbyville, Indiana. In business for more than two decades, the company is a subsidiary of casting and power tool maker Ryobi Limited , Japan.

Headquartered in Shelbyville, Indiana, Ryobi has facilities that include two die casting plants and a machining facility covering 560,000 sq. ft. of manufacturing space.

Ryobi's die cast machine sizes range from 500 to 3,500 tons, and its casting weights range from 3 to 40 pounds. Its product range varies from small ladder brackets to large transmission cases and engine blocks. All in all, Ryobi melts over 170 million pounds of aluminum per year in service of some of the world's top automotive manufacturers.

The company caters to automakers, from GM to Ford , Honda , and Toyota , as well as outdoor-equipment maker Tuff Torq Corp.
