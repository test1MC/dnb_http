From the highway to the doorway Ceco Door Products gives old cars new life. The company's variety of steel doors and frames are made from up to 63% recycled steel, much of which comes from old cars. The company also uses recycled cardboard packaging. Ceco's eco-friendly products include electrified doors, fire doors, storm doors, and custom doors and frames used in commercial, industrial, and institutional, construction. Ceco distributes its doors to builders through a network of 500 dealers in 15 countries around the world. It makes its doors at two manufacturing facilities in Mexico and the US. Ceco Door was founded early in the 20th century and acquired in 2001 by Swedish lockmaker ASSA ABLOY .
