You might say news travels fast thanks to this publisher. Swift Communications is a leading regional newspaper publisher with more than 30 papers in Colorado, Nevada, Oregon, and a few other Western states. Its portfolio includes the Vail Daily (Colorado), The Greeley Tribune (Colorado), and The Union (Grass Valley, California). In addition to its papers, Swift operates local news and information portals for Reno and Tahoe, Nevada, and offers commercial printing services. Its 1st Hour Communications unit publishes and distributes some 830,000 newsletters for 15 local school districts. Phil Swift started the family-owned business in 1975.
