Amherst Records is an independent record label specializing in genres such as big band, jazz, country, and pop music. The label has released music from artists such as Doc Severinsen, Nancy Kelly, Glenn Medeiros, The Stylistics, and Spyro Gyra. The company's music library also includes the compilation album A Tribute to Edith Piaf . Amherst Records was founded in 1957 by company president Leonard Silver, who also owns Record Theatre, an independent music retail chain with a handful of locations (Amherst, Buffalo, and Hamburg) in Western New York.
