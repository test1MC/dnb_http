The Comex Group keeps adding colors to its palette. The company manufactures and distributes architectural paints and coatings through five regional operations/brands ( Color Wheel , Frazee, General Paint, Parker Paint, and Kwal). It sells its products mainly in the western US; though Color Wheel sells throughout Florida and the Southeast and General Paint covers western Canada. Each brand formulates its products especially for the climate in its home region. The company comprises the US, Canadian, and Central American operations of Mexico's largest paints maker, Comex. In 2014 PPG acquired Comex for $2.3 billion, making PPG the largest coatings company in the world.
