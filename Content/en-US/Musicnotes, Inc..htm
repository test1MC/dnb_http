This company strikes a chord with musicians looking for digital sheet music. Musicnotes offers a catalog of digital sheet music and guitar tabs for nearly 110,000 songs.  Users download the music directly to their computers via the company's Web site at Musicnotes.com. The company has agreements with such content providers as Alfred Music Publishing , Cherry Lane Music Company , Sony/ATV Music Publishing , and Theodore Presser . It also offers an online catalog of more than 260,000 traditional music books, CDs, and videos. Musicnotes has some 2 million customers. The company was founded in 2000 by CEO Kathleen Marsh, Tom Hall, and Walter Burt.
