Veridien takes a swipe at germs, bugs, and even the sun. The company develops, manufactures, and markets antiseptic and disinfectant products. Flagship brand Viraguard is a patented formulation that is offered as a hospital disinfectant to clean hard surfaces and as antiseptic hand wipes and sprays for doctors, surgeons, and other health care workers. Other products include SunSwipe (sunscreen towelettes), BugSwipe (insect repellent towelettes), and the SitSecure dispenser, which holds Viraguard toilet seat wipes. Veridien primarily serves health care customers, including the American Red Cross . The company has a distribution agreement with DuPont for its Viraguard products marketed in the US.
