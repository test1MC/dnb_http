Vacon will manufacture you anything you want, as long as it's an AC drive. Vacon focuses its production efforts completely on drives and frequency converters, which are modular and customizable to the client's conversion needs. The company has research and development and manufacturing bases in China, Finland, Italy, and the US. Its sales offices are located in about 25 countries worldwide. Vacon got its start in 1993, when a faction of workers from Vaasa Factories established Vaasa Control. The company's first frequency converter was launched in 1995. Its 2008 acquisition of the AC drives business of TB Wood's for $29 million added operations in India, Germany, Italy, and the US.
