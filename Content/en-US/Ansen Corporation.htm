Ansen offers electronics design and manufacturing services to makers of microelectronics systems and components. Ansen provides services such as printed circuit board surface mounting and assembly from manufacturing facilities in China, Hong Kong, and the US. It also offers design, prototyping, and engineering services, as well as packaging, distribution, warranty, and support. The company's products are used in medical instruments, computers, telecommunications gear, and industrial controls. Ansen was taken private by management in 2003, and then combined with fellow EMS firm InnerStep in 2004. Ansen divested two US plants in 2005 in favor of expanding Chinese operations.
