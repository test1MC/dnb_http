Hedy Holding finds IT intoxicating. The company designs and manufactures a variety of computer hardware products including tower cases, speakers, power supplies, CRT and LCD displays, and accessories. It also makes laptop and desktop computers and mobile phones. Hedy got its start as a manufacturer for other information technology products makers like Dell , but it is staking out a claim at home where its sells IT products and consumer electronics under its own brand. The company has European sales offices in Poland. Chairman Yi Xianzhong and vice general manager Chen Zhou founded Hedy Holdings as Guangzhou Hedy Computer in 1997.
