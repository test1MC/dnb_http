Lee & Man Manufacturing manufactures paper, man. In fact, the company is one of the top suppliers of paper products to the Chinese market. It specializes in containerboard, which is used to make shipping and packaging cartons. Lee & Man operates paper factories in China's Dongguan province, in the Yangtze River Delta outside of Shanghai, and in Vietnam. Together, the plants are able to produce about 4.81 million tonnes of containerboard and 130,000 tonnes of pulp each year. Lee & Man also owns a pulp mill in California. Chairman Patrick Lee runs the company with his sons, CEO Raymond Lee and managing director Man Bun Lee; the Lee family owns about 60% of Lee & Man.
