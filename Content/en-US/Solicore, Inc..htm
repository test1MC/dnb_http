Solicore makes lithium polymer batteries, a compact energy source that utilizes the company's dry electrolyte technology. Its Flexion technology is used in smart cards, micro medical devices, and radio-frequency identification (RFID) tags, among other applications. Solicore's partners include Innovative Card Technologies , Parlex , Giesecke & Devrient , and Gemalto . The company was formed as a result of a 2001 merger between Skylab Technologies Group and Leading Edge Technologies. Investors in Solicore include Rogers Corporation , Firelake Capital Management, Draper Fisher Jurvetson , Hydro-Québec CapiTech , and OPG Ventures .
