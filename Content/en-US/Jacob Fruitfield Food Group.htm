Jacob Fruitfield Food Group has found its sweet spot. The company is a producer and distributor of biscuits (cookies), crackers, candies, chocolates, jams, and sauces in Ireland. It makes the popular Jacob's Fig Rolls (much like a Fig Newton), Cream Crackers, and the Mikado, Kimberley, and Coconut Cream cookies. Marmalade and jams are manufactured under the Fruitfield brand, and its Chef brand ketchup, barbecue sauce, pickles and salad dressings enjoy a 30% market share. Jacob Fruitfield Food Group is majority-owned by chairman Michael Carey. Amid tough economic times, the company was acquired by Origin Enterprises ' Valeo Foods Group in 2011.
