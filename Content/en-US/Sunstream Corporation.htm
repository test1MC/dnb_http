It's not just the sun streaming down that lifts Sunstream Corporation's spirits and floats its boats -- it's the power of hydraulics and innovative engineering. The company makes hydraulic boat lifts and canopies. Accessories include solar and AC-powered remote controls, dock ladders, guide-ons and load guides, boat bunks, shallow water foot pads, and underwater lighting systems. Other products include floating platforms and docking stations for seaplanes and boats. Sunstream also provides custom engineering and design services for unique boat lifts and canopies. Founded in 1996, Sunstream introduced the SunLift hydraulic boat lift (made from grade aluminum and stainless steel) that same year.
