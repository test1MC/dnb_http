This company knows how to take care of people's dirty laundry, and despite its name, it's not even a law firm. Tingue, Brown & Co. makes and distributes industrial textiles and finishing equipment serving commercial laundries, in North America and abroad. Its lineup includes plastics molding, metal fabrication and machining, flatwork ironer paddings, aprons, and a host of services. Over the last 100 years Tingue, Brown has pioneered industrial textile advances, from laundry bags touting strength, and mold and mildew resistance, to sling handling and molded polyethylene in laundry carts, hampers with lids and side doors, as well as cleaning treatments. Family owned, the company is led by CEO David Tingue.
