Like the Vikings of yore, VPI Acquisition Corp. (dba Viking Plastics) has conquered a successful niche in making plastic components. Established in 1972, Viking Plastics provides injection molded products, such as air-conditioning closures and fuel system parts, as well as brackets and connectors for motor vehicles. It also offers engineered products including HVAC products, caps, protective enclosures, and fluid control components. Other products include plastic fasteners, such as screws, bolts, nuts, washers. Customers have included Eaton , General Motors , Honeywell , Lennox , Motorola , and Visteon . The company was established in 1972; company president and CEO Kelly Goodsel bought the company in 2006.
