A bride needs to budget more for herself if she wants to walk down the aisle in a wedding dress from Vera Wang Bridal House. With gowns reaching $20,000, Vera Wang has designed bridal gowns for celebrities the likes of Sharon Stone and Mariah Carey. Vera Wang Bridal House also designs evening gowns, bridesmaid dresses, and sportswear, and licenses its name for other apparel, as well as for china, crystal, eyewear, footwear, fragrances, jewelry, linens, silver, and more. Vera Wang's products are sold through upscale department stores, specialty stores, and a pair of company-owned flagship boutiques located in New York City and Los Angeles. Chairman, CEO, and owner Vera Wang founded the firm in 1990.

Based in New York, Vera Wang Bridal House enjoys a global reach through operations in the Americas, Asia, Europe, the Middle East, and Australia.

A multichannel marketer, Vera Wang Bridal House sells its products through upscale department stores, specialty stores, and boutiques worldwide.

Vera Wang Bridal House designs and produces ready-to-wear collections, bridal gowns, and bridesmaids dresses. Through licensing agreements, the designer's namesake firm also has a hand in fine jewelry, footwear, eyewear, fragrances, linens, books, and fine papers. The fashion house produces the majority of its bridal collection in its company-owned workrooms located in the US.

As part of its business, Vera Wang Bridal House operates nearly 20 bridal boutiques globally that are both licensed and owned. In the US, it has stores in New York, Massachusetts, Illinois, and California. Internationally, it has a presence in the UK, Canada, South Korea, Hong Kong, Japan, Australia, Russia, China, Greece, Mexico, Kuwait, and Singapore.

Vera Wang Bridal House has extended its reach through licensing deals in recent years and the move has helped the design firm branch out from bridal gowns. The company makes Vera Wang-branded lingerie under license with Komar Co. Its noteworthy deal with Kohl's , which took a couple years to iron out, has been viewed as the designer's successful transition into mainstream fashion. The company has been extending its reach further into new niches, in addition to attracting a broader audience for its products. As part of the Kohl's agreement, Wang provides the design inspiration and Kohl's sources the apparel production. The collection, named Very Vera, comprises women's apparel, intimate apparel, handbags, leather accessories, footwear, jewelry, linens, and towels.

A former ice skater, Wang also designs costumes for skaters, including former Olympic ice skater Nancy Kerrigan.
