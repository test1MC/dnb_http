ApaTech has an appetite for orthopedics. The company's Actifuse product is a new type of synthetic bone graft material, which incorporates silicate to enhance new bone growth better than other synthetics or even autograft materials. ApaTech's ApaPore product is more conventional synthetic bone graft material used in the treatment of fractures and bone defects, as well as in spinal and joint surgeries. The company's headquarters and manufacturing facilities are in England, but ApaTech has operations in the US and Germany as well. ApaTech is a subsidiary of medical giant Baxter International .

ApaTech and the Actifuse product are part of Baxter's Regenerative Medicine product category within its BioScience segment.

The firm's product development work consists of ongoing clinical studies on Actifuse and potential new uses for the material.

The company offers its products in Australia, Austria, Germany, Greece, Ireland, Italy, Kuwait, New Zealand, Norway, Portugal, Spain, Sweden, Syria, Turkey, and the UK. It has operations in London, Berlin, and Foxbourough, Massachusetts.

ApaTech sells its products through distributors.
