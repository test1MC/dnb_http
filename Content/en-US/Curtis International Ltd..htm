Curtis International's electronic creations keep its customers wired. The firm designs and markets value-priced consumer electronics, including telephones, clocks, GPS navigation systems, LCD TVs, karaoke machines, and CD and DVD players. Curtis International also makes appliances, such as compact refrigerators, microwave ovens, and water coolers. The company's products are sold under the Curtis, RCA, Sylvania, and Igloo brand names, as well as under private labels for a number of retailers. Curtis International distributes its goods across North America and Europe.
