The solution to health and beauty is simple thanks to Simple Health & Beauty. The company manufactures and markets skincare products for almost every need. It offerings include cleansers and moisturizers for face, and bath and shower gels for body, as well as bar soaps, and haircare and suncare products. Products are touted as color- and fragrance-free; most are sold under the brand Simple and identified as Kind to Skin, Radiance, Derma, Regeneration, and others, depending upon the product's use. Baby and men's skincare products are also manufactured and distributed under the Simple label. The company, once known as Accantia Health & Beauty, is the UK subsidiary of Alberto-Culver , which is owned by Unilever .

Simple Health & Beauty's takeover by Unilever in 2011 followed an agreement to license the Simple and other soap brands to rival manufacturers to assuage competition concerns. Nonetheless, Simple has sustained its position as one of the UK's top facial skincare brands in terms of number of packs sold. All told, Simple Cleansing Facial Wipes are among the biggest-selling facial skincare products in England and Ireland.

The company is most well known in the UK and Ireland. It also has operations in South Africa and distributors in Australia, China, Ireland, New Zealand, Norway, and Southeast Asia.
