Rytec has an open-and-shut job. The company designs and manufactures high-speed roll, fold, slide, and swing doors. Rytec doors conserve a building's energy, as well as keep occupant traffic moving more efficiently and safely. Its doors are used in facilities with moderate to high-volume activity, such as food processing, cold-storage, and general warehousing. In 2010 Rytec moved into the carwash industry by acquiring Wynd Star Doors. Clients include the US Postal Service , Merck , and Sysco . The company sells directly and through dealers under brand names Arctec, Bantam, and Predadoor. Rytec is credited for introducing the first high-speed folding door to the US market. It is owned and led by Don Grasso.
