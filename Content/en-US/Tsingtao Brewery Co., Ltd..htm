China, the land of almost 1.4 billion people, needs a lot of beer to handle its people's thirst and relax their minds. The good news is that Tsingtao Brewery is up to the task. With some 60 breweries in total, it is one of China's largest domestic brewers (along Snow Breweries and Beijing Yanjing Brewery ) and is also its biggest beer exporter, accounting for more than 50% of China's beer exports. The company's Tsingtao, Dragon, and Phoenix brands are exported to more than 90 countries in Asia, Europe, and North America. Japan's Asahi Breweries has a minority stake in Tsingtao.
