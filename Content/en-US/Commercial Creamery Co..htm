Say "cheese" and, poof!, you get the Commercial Creamery Co., a manufacturer of spray-dried food powders. The company's products include cheese and dairy powders, including cream and cheese powders, as well as flavors, seasonings, shortenings, sauce mixes, and shelf-stable cheese cubes (Chunkettes) for use by food manufacturers. It serves customers in the US and overseas through its locations in Washington and Idaho by providing custom drying, blending, and packaging services, in addition to research and development. Commercial Creamery started in business as a traditional dairy-products distributor in the early 1900s. It is still owned by the founding Gilmartin family.
