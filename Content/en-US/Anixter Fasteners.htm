Chicagoland manufacturer Anixter Fasteners (formerly Quality Screw & Nut, a.k.a. QSN Industries) has threaded its way to success by specializing in industrial fasteners and 'C' class components. Its parts portfolio lists more than 200,000 product lines. Customers include OEMs in agricultural and construction machinery, recreational and commercial vehicles, and electronic markets. Among its technical services, the company offers joint, hole-size, and clamp load evaluation, and failure and torque strip analysis. Customer specific services include direct line feed, subassembly, kitting, stores management, and aftermarket spares. In 2008 Anixter International acquired the assets and operations of QSN.
