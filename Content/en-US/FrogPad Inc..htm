Kissing this frog can turn your keyboard into an ergonomic friendly, mobile keypad. FrogPad develops add-on keyboards for desktop and portable PCs that are designed to be operated with one hand. The company has introduced the iFrog, an input device with Bluetooth wireless networking capability, and a product for left-handed people, addressing a neglected market. Founded in 1999, the company has offices in Canada, Germany, Japan, the UK, and the US.
