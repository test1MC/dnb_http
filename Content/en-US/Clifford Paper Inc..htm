Clifford Paper keeps the presses rolling. The company distributes book publication papers, coated and uncoated freesheet, supercalendared and coated groundwood papers, newsprint, and other specialty papers. Each year it distributes 750,000 tons of paper from more than 60 mills, including Domtar Industries , Georgia-Pacific , and Wausau Paper . Clifford Paper serves the book, catalog, magazine publishing, commercial printing, and advertising industries. The company operates sales offices in California, Connecticut, Florida, New Jersey, New York, Ohio, and Wisconsin, as well as in Canada (Montreal, Toronto). The company, which traces its roots to 1985, is owned by CEO John Clifford.
