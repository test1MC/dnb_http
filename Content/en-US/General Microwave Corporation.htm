General Microwave Corporation, operating as Herley New York, makes microwave components and electronic systems. The company's products include attenuators, phase shifters, couplers, power and radiation meters, modulators, switch filters, and oscillators that are used in military and commercial equipment. The company has a novel place in the wireless industry; it provides telecom companies with radiation hazard meters used to indicate dangerously high levels of microwave radio frequencies. A subsidiary of Herley Industries , General Microwave Corp. was founded in 1960 and acquired by Herley in 1999.
