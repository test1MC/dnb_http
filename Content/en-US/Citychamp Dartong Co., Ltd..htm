Who's the Champ? Well, in the Chinese enameled wire market it could be Citychamp Dartong. Citychamp Dartong makes and sells enameled wires, steel core aluminum stranded conductors and various other wire products primarily for a growing portfolio of clients in China. The company operates from two manufacturing plants located in Fuzhou and Huaian, China. Citychamp Dartong also has interests in residential and commercial property development projects in Beijing and other parts of China. Fushou Yingrong Investment Co. owns about a quarter of the company.
