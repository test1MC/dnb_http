Another company might develop some high-tech gadget, but Elma Electronic makes the container to hold it snug and the glowing light-emitting diode (LED) component to tell you if it's turned on. The company produces computer enclosures and components, backplanes, power supplies, fans, and systems platforms. It also makes switches, knobs, and LEDs for electronics, and provides design, engineering, and manufacturing services. Elma Electronic was founded in 1960.
