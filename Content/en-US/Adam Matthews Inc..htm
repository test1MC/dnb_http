Who would have thought that a Louisville, Kentucky-based company could excel at baking New York-style cheesecake? Adam Matthews manufactures fresh and frozen cheesecakes in some thirty different varieties, including original, apple cinnamon crisp, turtle, and double double chocolate. The company also makes cakes such as peanut butter chocolate fudge and Italian creme. Its customers include major grocery store chains, as well as restaurants and other foodservice operations in the US, Canada, Mexico, and parts of the Caribbean. The company's retail food customers include Albertson's , selected Ahold locations, Kroger , Meijer , Publix , Shop-n-Save , Wal-Mart Super Centers , and Winn Dixie .
