Hampson Industries makes highly engineered products for aerospace, automotive, and industrial applications. The company is divided into three divisions: aerospace composites and transparencies, aerospace components, and automotive turbocharger. Aerospace offerings include airframe structures and products including wing edges, fuselage sections, composite panels, and cargo/cockpit/undercarriage doors. Automotive products include turbocharger and fuel system components. The company also makes laminated shims, seals, and gaskets. Hampson has manufacturing facilities located in India, the UK, and the US. Nearly 70% of sales come from North America.
