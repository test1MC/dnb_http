Making machinery to save "time in a bottle" keeps Kortec humming. The company designs and manufactures industrial injection mold equipment used to make a diversity of air-tight plastic bottles. Its proprietary co-injection technology produces high-barrier, multilayer packaging for juice to beer, as well as for chemicals, prescriptions, and even re-sealable plastic cans for paint. Kortec touts turnkey processes for co-injection systems, tailoring the machinery to suit customers' specific needs. Facilities in Belgium, Mexico, Hong Kong, the UK, and the US, cater to a global list of customers in plastics and packaging industries. The company in 2014 was acquired by Milacron , a provider of plastics processing technologies and industrial fluids.
