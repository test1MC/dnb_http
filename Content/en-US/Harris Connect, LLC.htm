This company would hate for you to miss a class reunion. Harris Connect maintains membership databases for more than 4,900 institutions, including schools, alumni associations, and other membership-based organizations. It produces more than 600 directories a year both in print and online. Among its clients are colleges and universities, fraternities and sororities, and private and public high schools. The company has built more than 500 online communities for alumni groups and professional organizations to network and organize. The information hubs offer message boards, online chat, and e-mail services.  New York-based private equity fund Wicks Communications & Media Partners III owns the company.
