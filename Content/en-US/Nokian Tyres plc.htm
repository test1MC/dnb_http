Selling its products in 59 countries worldwide, Nokian Tyres makes tires for cars, forestry and heavy trucks, and vans and is one of the largest retreaders in Europe. Its Vianor unit operates 1,475 retail tire outlets in 26 countries. The company also provides a "tire hotel" service in Europe that allows drivers to store their summer or winter tires until needed once again. Nokian Tyres produces branded passenger car tires such as Nokian, Hakkapeliitta, Hakka, and Nordman to the Europe, North America, Russia, and Nordic markets. Russia and Europe collectively account for more than 70% of the company's sales each year.
