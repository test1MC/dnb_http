Lexmark Canada sells and supports printers and supplies to consumer, corporate, and government customers across Canada. Its product line includes both ink jet and laser printers; multifunction devices with print, scan, copy, and fax capabilities; document management software; and print cartridges. The company, which has sales offices in seven cities, sells through and network of dealers and retailers. Lexmark's Canadian operations also include customer support units for handling parts requests and warranty service. Lexmark Canada is a subsidiary of US-based Lexmark International .

The head office of Lexmark Canada is in Montreal, where it also operates a warehouse and integration center.

Lexmark Canada sells through dealers, retailers and distributors. Customers include the City of Toronto, Markham Stouffville Hospital, University of Regina, York Region District School Board, Mount Royal University, and Westcomb Inc.
