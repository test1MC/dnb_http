SVA would make it easy to stay at home and never leave, should you decide to do that. Through publicly traded SVA Electron and SVA Information Industry, along with 30 other subsidiaries, the company makes appliances (air conditioners, refrigerators, washing machines, microwave ovens), computers, digital still cameras, DVD players, phones (mobile and wired), and TVs (cathode ray, plasma, and LCD). If you do need to connect with the outside world, SVA also provides two-way satellite communications, home networking equipment, and Internet access. The company was founded in 1995. The government of Shanghai recapitalized SVA in 2009, investing $680 million, due to the company's losses and debt.
