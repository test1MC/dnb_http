This company only wears one hat -- manufacturer. Capps Manufacturing makes and assembles aircraft parts, including door assemblies, wings, components, and sheet metal pieces, at its 80,000 sq. ft. plant. Services include machining, roll forming, hydro forming, extrusion and skin stretch forming, component assembly, and heat treating. Customers include Boeing , Cessna , Raytheon , Northrop Grumman , and other major aircraft makers. Capps differentiates itself by doing assembly work few other companies have the desire to take on. Owner and president Barney Capps started Capps Manufacturing in 1983.
