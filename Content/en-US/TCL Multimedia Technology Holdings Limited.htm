While TCL Multimedia may not necessarily be a household name, its products are. As a top television manufacturer, TCL (aka The Creative Life) markets its products globally under the TCL, THOMSON , and RCA brand names. Its core product line features flat-screen, projection, and various high-definition TVs and also DVD OEM/ODM, set-up box, digital camcorders and cameras products. It also makes TCL and Alcatel mobile phones, as well as audiovisual products. TCL has offices in Europe, the Americas, and Asia. TCL develops liquid-crystal display (LCD) panels through a joint venture with LG Display ; its joint venture with AU Optronics produces TFT-LCD (thin-film transistor liquid-crystal display) panels.
