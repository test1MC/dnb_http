National Scientific keeps an eye on kids, and it doesn't mind riding the bus. The company makes location-tracking products that incorporate digital video recording (DVR) devices, Global Positioning System (GPS) technology, and software. Its Gotcha! radio device alerts parents when a child wanders too far away, while the Travado IBUS system provides position tracking and video monitoring of school buses. The company's Travado Mini product tracks the location of government and first responder vehicles. National Scientific has yet to be profitable. The company is owned by its executives and other shareholders.
