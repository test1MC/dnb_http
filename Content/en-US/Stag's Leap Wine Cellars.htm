Established in 1972 by founder and owner Warren Winiarski, Stag's Leap Wine Cellars is in the heart of Napa Valley, where it grows wine grapes on some 40 acres of land. The winery is best known for its cabernet sauvignons --CASK 23 (costing more than $100 per bottle), S.L.V., and FAY. In addition to the namesake Stag's Leap wines, the winery also produces "house brand" (more affordable) wines under the Napa Valley, Atremis, Karia, and Rancho Chimiles labels. The wines of Stag's Leap are available throughout the US, as well as in about 30 other countries.
