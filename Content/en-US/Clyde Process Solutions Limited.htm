Clyde Process doesn't mind being pushy if it means getting the job done. The company designs and makes pneumatic conveying systems (controlled streams of air) that move bulk materials -- cement, metals, ethanol, and chemicals -- through a production process. It has particular expertise in making bulk materials handling valves, diverters, and terminal boxes. It also makes air filtration systems for manufacturing customers, which help to save on energy and maintenance costs. The company operates through 30 locations worldwide. Clyde Process is a subsidiary of The Schenck Process Group, a provider of measuring and process products and technology based in Germany.
