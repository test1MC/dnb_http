Coperion K-Tron Pitman (formerly K-Tron International) helps manufacturers watch their weight. K-Tron makes feeders that let manufacturers control the flow (by weight or volume) of bulk solids and liquids during manufacturing processes. The group's expertise lies in the building of pneumatic conveying systems that use vacuum and pressure to precisely control the flow of ingredients to make pharmaceutical, food, chemical, and plastic products. Coperion K-Tron Pitman is owned by casket maker Hillenbrand , which merged K-Tron International with bulk handling system maker Coperion in 2013.

K-Tron's geographic reach in the Americas, and Europe, the Middle East, Africa and Asia (EMEA/Asia) coupled with capabilities in material handling equipment and systems fit well with Hillenbrand's need to diversify.

K-Tron holds more than 100 patents for feeding, weighing, mechanical components and control equipment catering to the chemical, food, pharmaceutical, plastics, detergent, and nonwoven industries. Its operations consist of three segments: K-Tron Material Handling & Feeding Systems, K-Tron Feeding Equipment, and K-Tron Pneumatic Conveying Equipment.
