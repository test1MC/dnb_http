When it comes to selling hot dogs, Bar-S Foods is serious about being top dog. The company manufactures hot dogs, corn dogs, bacon, lunch meat, smoked sausage, and hot links primarily under the Bar-S value brand. Its more than 120 meat lines are sold to deli, retail grocery, warehouse club, and military and other institutional customers throughout the US, and sometimes abroad. The company's Bar- S Jumbo and 12 oz. classic hot dogs are among the best-selling in the US. Bar-S Foods is a subsidiary of Mexico-based meat and cheese processor Sigma Alimentos , part of industrial behemoth Alfa SAB .

Bar-S Foods markets its products in all 50 US states and in a number of other countries.

The company's product include pre-packaged meats and franks, bacon, lunchmeat, and sausage.

Bar-S Foods sells its products though a number of retailers, including Allen's Grocery, Alps, Apple Market, Bentonville Butcher & Deli, Bob's AG Market, Brookshire Grocery, , C.V. Family Foods, and Country Market.

Founded in 1981, Bar-S Foods was acquired in 2010 by Sigma Alimentos .

The Bar-S brand was originally a part of the Seattle Packing Co. The Cudahy Company (a meat packer that dates back to 1890) acquired the Seattle Packing Co. in 1957.
