Before facing live fire and other hazards, soldiers and police officers can test their mettle with the products of Advanced Interactive Systems (AIS). The company makes training simulators for law enforcement, military, and security agencies. Training products include the PRISim video-based judgment training simulator, the SVS PC-based training system, and QuickRange complete live-fire shooting ranges. It also designs and builds anti-terrorist training facilities. Other shooting range products include shooting stalls, bullet traps, and ceiling guards. AIS also provides services, including training facility design, content development, and a staff of firearms instructors.
