Uniden makes communication more convenient, with no strings attached. A leading manufacturer of wireless communications devices, the company makes cordless and voice-over-IP (VoIP) telephones for the consumer and business markets. Other consumer products include LCD televisions, digital television tuners, scanners, radar detectors, portable car navigation systems, CB radios, and marine electronics. The company has development and manufacturing operations in China, Japan, and Vietnam. It also has marketing arms in Australia, Belgium, New Zealand, and the US.
