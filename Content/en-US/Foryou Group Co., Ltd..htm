Well, we know who it's for. It, in this case, is a whole host of things including home and car entertainment systems including DVD players and stereos. It is also chemicals (paints and inks), digital electronics, home theater systems, precision molded tools, and medical instruments. Foryou makes and distributes all these items via more than 20 subsidiaries in Hong Kong and Beijing. The company's research and development department works with Chinese and foreign universities; it holds more than 20 invention and 50 design patents. Foryou began in 1986 as Shinwa Industries and became Foryou in 1990.
