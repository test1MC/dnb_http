British motorists don't necessarily need to book passage through the Chunnel to buy a Peugeot. As the UK subsidiary of France's Peugeot S.A. , Peugeot Motor Company PLC provides such an opportunity on British soil. Peugeot models sold in the UK include the iOn, 107, 207, and 308, along with the 3008 crossover vehicle and 3008 Diesel HYbrid4. Peugeot Motor Company also sells a line of light commercial vans, such as the 207 Van, Bipper, Boxer, Expert, and Partner models. In addition, the company exports cars for British and UK-based US military personnel, diplomatic officers, and individuals, and it offers business fleet and rental car services. Peugeot S.A. is among the largest purely European car companies.
