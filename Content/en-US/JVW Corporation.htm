JVW Corporation grows and harvests grapes, makes wine and bottles and sells it as the Jordan Vineyard & Winery. Located in the Sonoma Valley in Healdsburg, California, the winery, designed to look like an 18th century French chateau, produces cabernet sauvignon and chardonnay wines, which are distributed in throughout the US, as well as in the UK and the Far East. The winery keeps vintages dating back to 1976. JVW also grows olive trees on its land and produces limited-edition olive oils for retail sale. JVW Corporation is the brainchild of oilman Tom Jordan.
