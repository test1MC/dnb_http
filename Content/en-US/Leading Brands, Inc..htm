Leading Brands seeks leadership in the Canadian market for energy and health drinks. The company is an independent manufacturer, bottler, and distributor of soft drinks and what it calls "new age" beverages, including Infinite Health bottled water, as well as Stoked and DIE HARD energy and TrueBlue blueberry juice drinks. It also bottles several beverages under license, including Icelandic Glacial Water and Stewart's classic sodas. In addition, Leading Brands offers private-label bottling and co-packaging, product-development, logistics, and marketing services for customers throughout the US and Canada. Board member R. Thomas Gaglardi owns about 10% of the company through his Northland Properties company.
