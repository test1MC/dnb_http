Ibstock has literally helped build Great Britain (and its own bottom line) brick by brick. The company, which started as Ibstock Collieries in 1899 to quarry clay and mine coal for brick manufacturers, is one of the largest brick manufacturer in the UK. With nearly two dozen brick and paver manufacturing plants located throughout the UK, Ibstock has an annual capacity of more than 900 million bricks -- about 15 per UK resident. Rather than doling it out to the populace, however, its brick is used primarily in residential housing construction. In late 2015 Ibstock became a public company.
