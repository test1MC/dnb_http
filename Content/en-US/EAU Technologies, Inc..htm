Of all the vowels, O is EAU's bread and butter (as in H 2 O). Using water electrolysis technology, EAU Technologies (formerly Electric Aquagenics Unlimited) makes equipment and process systems that clean and disinfect surfaces and foods. Its Empowered Water generators are sold and leased to companies in search of improved cleaning and sanitizing. The firm's water-based, non-toxic products reduce bacteria, viruses, spores, and molds in food processing, living surfaces, and other environments. Director Peter Ullrich, individually and through his Water Science firm, is EAU Technologies largest shareholder. Water Science is also EAU's biggest customer and it licenses EAU technology in Latin America.
