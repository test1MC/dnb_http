TGE Marine exists to handle hazardous, cold, and wet conditions. The company designs and builds cargo handling systems for ships that transport cryogenic gases (frozen, liquefied gases) like ethylene, as well as liquefied petroleum gas (LPG) and liquefied natural gas (LNG). Offering engineering, purchasing, and construction management services, it focuses on serving smaller-sized carriers and midsized commercial shipyards. It also offers services to offshore, or "floating," cryogenic gas production and storage platforms. The company, which has offices in Germany and China, operates primarily in Europe and Asia. TGE Marine was founded in 1982.
