This company uses a group approach to keep in touch with its core plastic manufacturing identity. The INTEC Group makes precision insert molded and injection-molded thermoplastic parts for the automotive, electronics, and telecommunication industries. The company's products include antennas and related items, custom insert molded parts, custom injection-molded parts, and single cavity preproduction tools. The INTEC Group has a 60,000-sq.-ft. manufacturing facility in the US, and additional plants in Mexico and Singapore. The automotive industry accounts for more than 90% of the company's production output.
