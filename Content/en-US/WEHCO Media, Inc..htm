Although the name is pronounced like the Texas town of Waco, this media company is based in Little Rock, Arkansas. WEHCO  Media -- WEHCO is an acronym for Walter E. Hussman Company -- operates two divisions: WEHCO Newspapers and WEHCO Video. The company's portfolio includes daily and weekly newspapers, cable television networks, and Internet companies serving several states including Arkansas, Missouri, Tennessee, and Texas. It publishes one of the nation's largest newpapers, the Arkansas Democrat Gazette, which has a circulation of over 180,000. Its Internet and cable service, Cablelynx, is provided to customers through subsidiaries in Arkansas, Oklahoma, Mississippi, and Texas. The company was founded in 1909.
