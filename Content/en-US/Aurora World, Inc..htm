Little boys and girls (and some of their parents) would like to get lost in Aurora World. Formerly A&A Plush, the company makes and distributes plush toys and gifts (from traditional teddy bears and dolls to backpacks, puppets, and seasonal giftware). Aurora makes plush pets with their own carriers (think Reese Witherspoon in Legally Blonde ) and accessories such as dress up clothes and key chains. Aurora also develops custom plush toys (often incorporating corporate logos) for businesses. Its lines of toys are sold through more than 25,000 retailers (department stores, specialty toy and gift stores) globally including Hallmark , Learning Express , and Neiman Marcus . It also boasts about a dozen US showrooms.

California-based Aurora World has operations in Hong Kong, South Korea, the UK, and the US.

The company has been looking to expand its products portfolio. In 2013 Aurora World inked a licensing deal with toy-giant Hasbro to launch a new line of soft and sparkly MY LITTLE PONY plush ponies.
