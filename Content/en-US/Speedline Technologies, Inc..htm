Speedline Technologies makes electronics assembly equipment, including systems used to print and clean printed circuit boards. It has installed more than 5,000 stencil printing and dispensing systems around the globe. The company also makes reflow ovens, coating equipment, wafer bumping systems, and packaging and encapsulation equipment used in semiconductor manufacturing. The company is part of Illinois Tool Works ' Specialty Systems Group. The company was established in 1988. Illinois Tool Works (ITW) bought Speedline Technologies in late 2006.

The company has manufacturing and customer support facilities in the US, Asia, Europe, and Mexico. Speedline Technologies leverages its parent company's network which spans 750 businesses and nearly 50 countries.

Speedline Technologies' major customer base includes OEMs and contract electronic manufacturers, as well as companies in the telecommunications, automotive electronics, and medical electronics industries.

The company has used partnerships to build its business. Speedline Technologies also attends trade shows and conferences to get the work out about its products.
