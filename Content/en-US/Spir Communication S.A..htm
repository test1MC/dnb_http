Spir Communication is a leading publisher of free newspapers and real estate publications in France and other European countries. Its portfolio of more than 200 publications includes 20 minutes, Top'annonces , Logic-immo and DeHuisaanHuisKrant . It also publishes news and other content online through various websites associated with its print products. In addition to publishing, the company has commercial printing and distribution operations focused on advertising flyers. Holding company Sofiouest Group has a controlling stake in Spir Communication.
