A pea in the Royal Dutch Shell pod, Shell Chemicals manufactures all manner of petrochemicals and is a global leader or among the leaders in producing olefins, aromatics, solvents, phenols, glycols, and styrene monomers that make up the raw materials for products such as plastics, coatings, and detergents. Shell Chemicals has manufacturing facilities worldwide and operates a number of joint ventures. Infineum , a joint venture with with ExxonMobil Chemical , makes lubricant additives. Shell Chemicals also teams up with BASF , Saudi Basic Industries , CNOOC , and others.

The company supplies over 17 million tonnes of petrochemicals per year to large industrial customers. Products include aromatics, ethylene oxide/glycols, lower olefins, phenol/acetone and nonene, propylene oxide, solvents, and styreme monomers.

The company is based in London, and has a global manufacturing base.

The company has nearly 1,000 customers.
