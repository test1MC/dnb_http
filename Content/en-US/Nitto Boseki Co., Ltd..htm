Nitto Boseki, operating as Nittobo, oversees a group of textile, fiberglass, environmental, and health businesses. The company operates in five segments: Textiles (core-spun thread, stretched, and interlining products), Glass Fiber for Industrial Materials (glass fiber products and electronics related materials), Glass Fiber Yarn (yarn, roving, chopped strands, raw fiber), Glass Fiber Cloth, and Environment and Health (glass and rock wool products, acoustic works, the operation of plant factories, maintenance, and security services, medical products, specialty chemicals, and soft drinks). Other business activities include real estate, golf courses, sports centers, and insurance services. Nittobo was founded in 1918.
