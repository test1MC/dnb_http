If ribs are your thing, or you "ham it up" for ham, Trim-Rite Food is your company. The custom pork boning company's products include boneless hams, St. Louis-style ribs, rip tips, plus boned blades, butts, and loins. Trim-Rite also provides custom cuts and packaging for customers. The company's pork products are available to food manufacturers, distributors, and restaurants throughout the world. Spun off in 1980 from a Boston-area meat supplier that served mainly Chinese restaurants, Trim-Rite relocated to Illinois to be closer to fresh meat supplies.
