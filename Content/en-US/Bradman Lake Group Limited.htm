Not a resort, Bradman Lake is a manufacturer of packaging automation machinery used by customers in the consumer goods (cosmetics, CD cases, and phone cards), food and beverage (candy bars, frozen foods, and tea bags), and health care (pharmaceuticals) industries. Bradman Lake's machines help with product distribution, flow wrapping, carton erection, loading, case packing, and shrink wrapping, among other uses. The company has three chief operating brands -- Autowrappers, Bradman Lake Ltd., and Europack -- and also provides refurbishing and maintenance services. Bradman Lake is a subsidiary of machinery maker Langley Holdings PLC .

Bradman Lake operates across five business areas: Bakery and Biscuits, Chocolate and Confectionery, Consumer and HGealthcare, Dry Foods, and Frozen Foods.

Bradman Lake maintains international sales and service partnerships to supply its products worldwide. These partners include Interbion, HBM Plastics & Packaging Technologies, Qualitechs, and Hawapack, among others.
