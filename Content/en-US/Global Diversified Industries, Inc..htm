Global Diversified Industries is the new mod squad. Through its Global Modular subsidiary, the company makes pre-fabricated portable modular buildings, mainly for use as classrooms. It also constructs permanent one- and two-story structures. Clients include public and private schools, universities, child-care facilities, and municipalities. The company is active throughout California. Global Diversified divested its MBS Construction subsidiary, which provided construction site management services, in 2006. Company president Phil Hamilton has voting control of more than 20% of Global Diversified's stock.
