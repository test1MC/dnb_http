IBIDEN's ken is high-tech materials. The company's primary products include plastic packaging substrates that protect semiconductors (from dirt and electric shocks) and printed circuit boards used in wireless devices and communications networks. Its ceramic division makes silicon carbide diesel particulate filters used for emissions control in diesel engines, along with fine ceramics, ceramic fiber, and graphite products used in semiconductor manufacturing equipment, combustion equipment, and fuel cells. The company's housing division makes decorative melamine laminates, while its construction division provides landscaping and erosion control services. IBIDEN was established in 1912.
