Inabata & Co. is all over the map, literally and figuratively. The company operates in such varied segments as IT and electronics, housing materials, chemicals, plastics, and food, offering its products around the world. The company's IT and electronic goods including flat panel displays, copiers and printers, while its chemical products include dyestuffs, paper chemicals, paint, ink, and adhesives. Offerings in the plastics segments include resins, performance plastics, and packaging materials. Inabata also offers building materials, housing equipment, and other ecologically friendly materials such as particle boards. It has operations in 20 countries worldwide and gets half its sales overseas.
