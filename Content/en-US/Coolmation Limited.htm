Chilling is a hot topic of conversation at Coolmation Limited. The company is a manufacturer of air-conditioning and cooling systems. Coolmation's blast coolers, cooling towers, circulating fans, evaporators, heat pumps, and water chillers are used for industrial cooling of large buildings. Each year the company makes and distributes thousands of fan coils, air conditioning systems, chillers, and heat pumps. The company serves customers in such industries as aerospace, automotive, beverage bottling, food processing, plastics, and telecommunications. Coolmation operates primarily in the UK and Europe. In 2013 it was acquired by Sweden-based Swegon AB.

Headquartered in Hampshire, Coolmation has other locations across the UK residing in Leicester, Hoddesdon, and Leigh in Lancashire.

The company operates through three major divisions: Coolmation BlueBox, Chillers 4 Hire, and Service4chillers. Coolmation BlueBox acts as the UK sales operations of Blue Box, a major manufacturer of chillers and heat pumps. Coolmation and Blue Box are both part of the Swegon group of companies.

Chillers 4 Hire provides temporary chillers for hire for industrial cooling and HVAC applications. Service4chillers is operated by Coolmation Service, a specialist chiller service organization with a nationwide team of trained service and maintenance engineers.

Coolmation markets its products through experienced sales teams. The company serves a range of sectors, including plastics and rubber, food and services, and aerospace and telecommunications. Major clients have included Glaxo Smith Kline Beecham, Everards Brewery, Canary Wharf, Ford Motor, Rolls Royce, Boots Pharmaceuticals, and Nestle.
