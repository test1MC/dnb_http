Kongsberg Power Products Systems (formerly Capro) makes motion control systems and cable assemblies. Its automotive products include cables that operate hood and fuel door releases, heating and air conditioning controls, trunk releases, seat recliners, and door and window systems. The company also makes mechanical control systems and cable assemblies for power and industrial equipment OEMs. Kongsberg Power Products offers services that include engineering, design, and prototyping. A former subsidiary of diversified manufacturer Teleflex , Capro was sold in 2008 to global auto supplier Kongsberg Automotive Holding ASA , which changed the company's name but kept the Capro brand.
