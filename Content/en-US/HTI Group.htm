HTI Group doesn't want children to grow up too fast; they'll outgrow the company's products. The UK's largest toy retailer, HTI Group (known as HTI), makes a variety toys under brands such as Teamsterz, Smart, and Cosy Cottage, as well as licensed brands like Bob the Builder, Mamas & Papas, Peppa Pig, Hello Kitty, and Barbie. It also makes Christmas decorations, stationary and gifts, and seasonal products like garden furniture. HTI supplies grocery, value and major high street retailers, mail order and internet retailers, and wholesalers across more than 60 countries. Founded in 1952 by David Halsall, HTI has offices in the UK as well as in China, Hong Kong, and Germany.

HTI's product lines number in the thousands. Its own label brands include its vehicle range Teamsterz as well as role-play ranges Smart and Cosy Cottage.

HTI's warehouse facility near its headquarters in Fleetwood, Lancashire provides the company's warehousing, storage, and distribution operations. As of 2015, the 30,000 square-meter freehold warehouse facility had a capacity of 22,000 racked pallet locations, and provided an additional 3,500 square-meter space for bulk storage. Product orders can be filled directly from the UK warehouse or directly from Hong Kong.

HTI's toys are primarily sold across Europe, the Middle East, Asia, Australasia, and the Americas. In the past, the company has reported that over 25% of sales are generated from then non-UK markets. The company has offices and showrooms in the UK, Hong Kong, and Germany.

HTI reiterated in 2015 that its key focus has been to grow its business internationally, both in existing territories as well as into new geographical markets. It's investing heavily in ensuring that it gets the right strategies, product breadth, and infrastructure to achieve its international expansion goals. Its business has been growing rapidly in the Far East in recent years, where it first established operations in 2006.

HIT opened its China office a few years ago to oversee engineering and quality control operations in the region. It is located in mainland China to strategically check the manufacturing side of the business.
