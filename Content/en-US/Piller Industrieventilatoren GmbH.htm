Piller Industrieventilatoren makes fans -- not the kind that scream at celebrities, nor the kind that ladies use to gently create a breeze, but the kind used in industrial furnaces, power and evaporator plants, and industries ranging from food to textiles to petrochemicals. Piller also offers installation, maintenance, repairs, and remote diagnosis. The company has offices and representatives in Asia, Australia, Europe, the Middle East, and the US. Exports account for more than half of sales. Founded in Germany in 1909 by Anton Piller, the company is now owned by the families of Bernd Klostermann and managing director Nils Englund.
