Here's a business story with a twist -- Weaver Meats makes and distributes beef jerky and meat snack sticks under its own name as well as under the Chubby Checker Snacks, Jeff's Jerky, Pacific Mountain Farms, GP's, and Wild Ride brands. The company was founded in 1981 by brothers Dave and Mike Weaver. In 2001 Weaver Meats teamed up with 1960s pop icon and meat aficionado Chubby Checker and developed a meat-snack line bearing the rock and roll legend's name.
