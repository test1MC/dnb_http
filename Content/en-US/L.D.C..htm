If dinner is to be Coq au Vin or Poulet au Vinagre, L.D.C. has the bird you'll need. As the leading poultry producer and processor in France, the company offers fresh chicken and turkey under such brands as Loué and Landes. Its prepared poultry is sold under the Le Gaulois and La Toque Angevine brands. L.D.C. also processes rabbit and offers ready-made meals, pizzas, and sandwiches. In addition to its home base in France (about 30 plants), the company has operations in Poland and Spain. The founding Lambert and Chancereul families, who gave L.D.C. its "L" and "C," respectively, formed the company in 1968.
