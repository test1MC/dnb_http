Asia Pacific Breweries (APB) puts a Tiger in your tankard. The company produces its flagship beer, Tiger, along with Anchor , Heineken , and ABC Stout. It distributes those and 40 other national and regional brands in some 60 countries worldwide. The Singapore-headquartered brewer operates more than 30 breweries in about a dozen countries, including Cambodia, China, Laos, Malaysia, Mongolia, New Zealand, Papua New Guinea, Singapore, Sri Lanka, Thailand, and Vietnam. APB was a joint venture between the Netherlands' Heineken and Singapore conglomerate Fraser and Neave until late 2012, when Heineken paid some $4 billion for the remaining 40% stake held by Fraser and Neave.
