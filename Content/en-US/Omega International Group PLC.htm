Omega International Group (OIG) wants to be sure it has the last word in kitchen furnishings. Omega International is a leading UK designer, manufacturer, and distributor of branded kitchen furniture and appliances. Founded by chairman Bob Murray in 1992, the company focuses on the middle to upper end of the market with its four main brands: Mackintosh, Chippendale, English Rose (formerly Omega), and Sheraton. The company's products are sold by more than 650 kitchen specialty retailers around the UK. Five years after it first floated on the London AIM stock exchange, Omega International was taken private by its management in 2009.
