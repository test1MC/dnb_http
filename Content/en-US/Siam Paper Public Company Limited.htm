When playing the game Rock! Paper! Scissors! with Siam Paper Public Company Limited, paper always wins. A Thai special paper manufacturer, Siam Paper Public Company Limited (SIAMP) produces a variety of products including coated and uncoated wood-free, coated wood-containing, eye care (reduced glare), non-carbon required, and thermal paper. The company has production capacity of more than 140,000 tons of paper a year. SIAMP's products are used for writing, photocopying, commercial printing, book and magazine publishing, and other specific applications. The company was established in 1967.
