Breen wants to inject some color into things. Breen Color Concentrates formulates and manufactures custom plastic and vinyl colorants, primarily for blow molders of consumer packaging, extrusion and injection molders, and wire and cable manufacturers. It also provides application assistance, custom packaging and labeling, and analysis certification. Breen Color Concentrates serves customers throughout North America; it operates plants in Lambertville, New Jersey, and Leominster and Niles, Illinois. The company was founded in 1976 and acquired by investment firm Capital Partners in 2014.
