HLS Systems International (doing business as HollySys) wants to keep machines in China moving in time. Through its operating subsidiaries, HollySys designs and builds automation and control systems and related components such as software platforms, motors, drivers, sensors, and other devices and hardware for industrial facilities, power plants, and rail companies. The company's products include safety control systems for power and chemical plants, automation and control systems for manufacturing companies, and signal control systems for railways and transit lines. It targets its products and services primarily to companies operating in China. HollySys's roots date back to 1993.

Although its customer based has traditionally consisted of power plants and chemical companies, HollySys has been making efforts to target other customers in industries such as food and beverage processing and pharmaceutical manufacturing.

In addition, as part of its overall strategy, the company also intends to expand its presence in the rail transportation and nuclear power industries, which are two markets that the company has only recently entered.
