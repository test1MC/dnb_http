Walker Die Casting doesn't leave things to chance. The company is a producer of high-pressure aluminum castings for industrial applications. Walker Die Casting provides custom die fabrication services to customers in the automotive, appliance, lawn and garden, marine, and power tool industries. Products include parts such as adapter plates, brackets, oil pans, gear cases, and housings for transmissions, engines, axles, and flywheels. Walker Die Casting also offers product design, finishing, testing, machining, and warehousing services. The company was founded in 1958 by Robert Walker.
