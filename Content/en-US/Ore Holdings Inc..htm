Ore Holdings aims to make its mark in the drug development business by licensing or selling potential therapies to research firms. The company is a self-proclaimed pharmaceutical asset management firm, focused on acquiring drug programs and then licensing or selling them to other companies. Compounds in its asset portfolio include potential treatments for metabolic disorders, cardiovascular diseases, and inflammatory conditions. Ore Holdings halted its direct involvement in development programs and reduced its workforce in 2009, and it has undertaken additional restructuring measures as it works to resolve financial difficulties. The company is currently seeking to monitize its drug development assets.
