Addison is into comfort. The company designs and manufactures heating, cooling, and dehumidification products, primarily for commercial and industrial use. Addison specializes in 100% outdoor air units, including rooftop and split systems and heat pumps. The company also makes automated controllers and air handling units. Addison began operating in 1949 and has had a series of parent companies: Addison was acquired by Heat Controller in 1995, then by Fedders Corporation in 2004, and finally by heating system manufacturer Roberts-Gordon in 2008.
