In times of stress, please watch your step around Adhesives Research's products -- they're pressure-sensitive. The company manufactures pressure-sensitive adhesives for the medical (for wound care and intravenous attachments), electronics (used in the assembly stages for mobile devices, hard drives, etc.), industrial (automotive, glass treatment), and splicing (pulp and paper industry) markets. Adhesives Research manufactures its products on a custom basis, working with customers worldwide. It operates technical design and manufacturing facilities in Colombia, Ireland, and the US.

The main operations of the company are in Glen Rock, Pennsylvania, but it also operates manufacturing plants in Colombia and Ireland and has sales and marketing offices in Singapore and the UK, a representative office in China, and sales representation in Korea, Japan, and Taiwan. It operates technical centers for the design and development of custom adhesives, coatings, and film components in Pennsylvania and Limerick, Ireland.

Adhesive Research operates two businesses, Adhesives Research and ARx, LLC. The former makes specialty films, pressure-sensitive adhesives tapes, coatings, films and laminates in key markets: medical, pharmaceutical, electronics, engineered tapes, authentication, and splicing. ARx provides drug and biopharmaceutical delivery materials and technologies for the pharmaceutical industry.

Adhesives Research uses its extensive product development and analytical support to provide customized products encompassing advanced adhesive polymerization, mixing, adhesive coating, and release liner design.

In 2012 the company launched an innovative line of long-term wear and gently-removing adhesives for use in treating serious wounds, able to secure a dressing in place for up to seven days and with the added value of being residue-free and virtually painless when removed.

Adhesives Research was founded in 1961 by chairman Erwin Huber.
