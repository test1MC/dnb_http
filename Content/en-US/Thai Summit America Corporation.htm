Thai Summit America (formerly Ogihara America) takes note when the price of a stamp goes up. The US company is a Tier I supplier of stamped automotive metal panels and modules. Thai Summit's line includes major exterior parts and structural welded assemblies, such as doors, hoods, trunks, fenders, dashes, frames, and roofs. The company also serves appliance, medical, and alternative energy OEMs with all phases of tool and die manufacture, from design to refurbishment. It caters to major carmakers and has made stampings for Nissan Altimas and Lincoln Town Cars.

Thai Summit works primarily from its southeastern Michigan location as ready access to interstate highways and railway lines enable the company to efficiently service customers throughout the Midwestern US and Ontario, Canada.
