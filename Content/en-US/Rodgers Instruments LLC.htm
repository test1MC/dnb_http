For those who like to make a joyful noise, Rodgers Instruments makes, designs, and installs pipe and digital organs. The company has built a business primarily serving religious organizations and churches; customers count on it for its sound quality and its Parallel Digital Imaging, a digital-generation system that mimics a pipe organ. As part of its operations, Rodgers Instruments also makes and markets the speakers and amplification systems that accompany these electronic instruments. The instruments manufacturer's other products include pipes and other organ accessories, such as adjustable benches, file players, and recorders. Rodgers Instruments is owned by the US subsidiary of Japan-based Roland .
