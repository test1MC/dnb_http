India's leading and Asia's fourth-largest paint manufacturer, Asian Paints serves customers in more than 65 countries. Besides the Asian Paints label, the group also operates around the world through subsidiaries Berger International Limited, Apco Coatings, SCIB Paints, Taubmans, and Kadisco. Asian Paints' PPG Asian Paints Pvt Ltd (a 50-50 JV between Asian Paints and US-based PPG , one of the world's largest automotive coatings makers) serves the growing demands of the Indian automotive coatings market. A second JV with PPG (Asian Paints PPG Pvt Ltd) serves the protective, industrial powder, industrial containers, and light industrial coatings markets in India.

Asian Paints manufactures a wide range of paints for decorative and industrial uses.

The company has 26 paint manufacturing plants in 19 countries.

In the Decorative paints segment, Asian Paints provides interior wall finishes, exterior wall finishes, enamels, and wood finishes. In the Home Improvement and Decor markets, the company is present in the kitchen and bath fittings space with various products under the Sleek and Ess Ess brands.

Asian Paints' Industrial products include waterproofing, wall coverings, and adhesives.

Asian Paints pursues vertical integration and has diversified into chemical products such as phthalic anhydride and pentaerythritol, which are used in the paint manufacturing process.

The company was founded in 1942. It has been the Indian market leader in paints since 1967.
