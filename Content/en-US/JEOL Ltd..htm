JEOL makes scientific and analytical instruments used in biotechnology, materials inspection, medical diagnostic, nanotechnology, surface treatment, and semiconductor manufacturing applications. The company's products include scanning electron microscopes, scanning probe microscopes, mass spectrometers, laser lithography systems, electron-beam guns, coating systems, and amino acid analyzers.
