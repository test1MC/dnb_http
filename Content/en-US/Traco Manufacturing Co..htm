Shrink products translate to growth revenues for packaging equipment and materials maker Traco Manufacturing. The company specializes in shrink wrap packaging products (including printed shrink labels, PVC shrink film, Super Shrink polyolefin bags, and tamper evident bands). It can make all the packaging DVDs, for example, from vinyl binders to jewel cases and display materials. Traco Manufacturing also makes shrink wrap machines and equipment, including portable bag sealers and heat guns. It sells its products across the US and Canada through a network of stocking distributors. Traco Manufacturing was founded in 1985.
