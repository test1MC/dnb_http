Independent photo agency Zuma Press has zoomed in on digital images. The company maintains an archive of more than 4 million pictures, about 2 million of which are available for download via the company's website. Images from Zuma have appeared on the covers of magazines such as The New York Times , People , Time , and Newsweek . Zuma represents the work of more than 3,000 photographers worldwide, including Pulitzer-prize winning photographers. It also has a large collection of movie stills that date back to the 1800s. Founded in 1995, Zuma has bureaus in Hollywood, London, and New York.
