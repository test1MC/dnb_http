Thermal Ceramics' products keep the hot side hot and the cool side cool. The company has three product lines: it manufactures insulating fiber, firebricks, and monolithic products. Its insulation products are used in industrial furnaces, household ovens, pools and spas, and steam generators, among many other applications. Brand names include 80 Firebrick, Kaowool Ceramic Fiber, Kaocast Castables, and Pyro-Bloc Monolithic Modules. Thermal Ceramics operates in 50 countries worldwide and manufactures in 34 of them. Thermal Ceramics was sold in 2015 by its long-time parent company, British insulation giant Morgan Advanced Material , and now operates under Sitek Insulation.

The company has 34 facilities and 50 sales offices worldwide.

Thermal Ceramics' customers are found in industries such as Petrochemical, Iron and Steel, Energy, Aluminum/Non-Ferrous, Ceramics & Glass, and Domestic OEM Appliances.

The company achieved revenue growth of 11% on prior year at constant currencies in 2015, driven by large orders from North America and the Middle East.
