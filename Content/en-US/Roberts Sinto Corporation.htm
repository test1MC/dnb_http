Roberts Sinto manufactures material handling machinery and foundry equipment for use in production facilities. Specifically, the manufacturer produces robotics and conveyors for material handling, mold handling systems, and flask and flaskless molding. Its list of core products is divided across several categories: Molding, Finishing, Robotics, Auto Grinding, and Automation. Roberts Sinto is part of the North American operations of Japanese foundry equipment maker Sintokogio, which has a worldwide engineering and service network of subsidiaries and affiliate companies.
