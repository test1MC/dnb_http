You don't need a spy to learn that S.P.I. makes Stoli. Also known as Stolichnaya, the vodka comes in the classic 80-proof red label, as well as fruit flavors and premium blends. Stoli is exported to 100 countries ( William Grant & Sons distributes it in about 15 countries, including the US). S.P.I. also makes sparkling wine and vodkas (Moskovkaya, Kaznacheyskaya, and Kremlyovskaya) for the Russian market. It owns and operates distilleries, raw alcohol plants, wheat fields, and retail and distribution centers in the Baltic region. Besides its 2011 investment in Argentine winery Achaval-Ferrer, S.P.I. has interests in commercial real estate and Italian wineries. S.P.I. was founded in 1997 by chairman Yury Shefler.
