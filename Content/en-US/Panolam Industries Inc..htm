Panolam Industries makes table and counter tops, and that just scratches the surface. The company produces thermally fused melanine panels, decorative surfaces, and high pressure laminates that are used in commercial, institutional, and residential projects across the US and Canada. Its thermofused panels, sold under the Panolam and Pionite trade names, are used in store fixtures and displays, kitchen and bath cabinets, office furniture, and shelves. Under its Conolite Aircraft Laminates brand, the company also manufactures cargo liners designed for aircraft underbellies.
