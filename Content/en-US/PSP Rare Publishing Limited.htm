PSP Rare Publishing wants to be a rare breed -- a firm that brings creativity to contract publishing. The company produces magazines, catalogs, websites, social media, and digital content for corporations and organizations across a wide range of industries, providing communications planning, editorial, design, commercial, production, and distribution services. PSP Rare has produced work for such clients the British Heart Foundation, the City of London, and Kia Motors . Digital publishing and website design services are conducted through the company's PSP Rare Digital division. PSP Rare has three offices in the UK, in Clerkenwell, Manchester, and Bristol.
