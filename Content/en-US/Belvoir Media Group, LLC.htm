Special interest magazines are of special interest to this company. Belvoir Media Group publishes a portfolio of magazines and newsletters focused on seven subjects, which include aviation, boating, guns, health, home decorating, horses, and pets. In addition, the company publishes audio CDs and operates Web sites in conjunction with many of its titles. Publications include Kitplanes Magazine: The Independent Voice for Homebuilt Aviation and Wholedog Journal , about natural dog care and training. Founded in 1972, Belvoir is controlled by chairman and CEO Robert Englander.
