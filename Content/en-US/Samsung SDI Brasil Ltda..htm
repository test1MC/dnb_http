Samsung SDI Brasil lights up the screen with its display technology. Founded in 1996, the company focuses on making liquid crystal display panels, as well a variety of color picture and color display tube products. Applications for its technologies include televisions, computer monitors, cell phones, and other handheld mobile devices. Samsung SDI Brasil exports about 75% of its color display tube products to plants owned by Samsung Electronics or LG Electronics in China, Korea, and Taiwan. The company is a subsidiary of Samsung SDI Co. , a part of Samsung Group .
