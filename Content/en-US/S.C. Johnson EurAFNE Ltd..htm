For those who want to know insights into consumer behavior and what's on the forefront of household products, call on S.C. Johnson EurAFNE (stands for Europe/Africa and Near East). The company serves the Near East region of household cleaning products company S.C. Johnson & Son and is a unit of S.C. Johnson Ltd. The region maintains about a dozen products in its products portfolio to clean just about every room of the house and kill insects. The EurAFNE office houses the regional and functional division executives, as well as the European Information Services and the Consumer Environmental Responsibility divisions. Its Central Strategic Procurement division is based in the Netherlands.
