EMC Computer Systems (UK) provides data storage systems. A regional unit of storage leader EMC , the company sells cloud-based storage systems, storage networking equipment, data management software, and applications for big data and content management. It also offers a wide range of consulting and support services. It markets to banks, manufacturers, ISPs, retailers, government agencies, universities, and other organizations that manage large amounts of data. EMC's UK-based customers have included British Airways , Marshalls , The Financial Times , and Tesco . Europe is EMC's largest non-US market with $6.9 billion in revenue. In 2015, Dell Inc. agreed to buy EMC Corp. for $67 billion, and the deal closed in fall 2016.

The company is based in Brentford, UK.

It markets via direct sales and through various distribution channels.

The company is part of the Europe, Middle East and Africa division of the parent company, and which contributed 28% of total sales.
