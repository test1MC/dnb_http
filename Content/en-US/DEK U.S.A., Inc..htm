DEK U.S.A. supplies stencils, but not the kind kids use in making drawings, or Martha Stewart employs in decorating walls. The company's precision screens and stencils are used to manufacture automotive parts, biomedical sensors, displays and membrane switches, fuel cells, and printed circuit boards and other electronic products. DEK also makes screen printing machinery, with related consumables, software, and tooling. The company partners with BTU International to provide metallization equipment for manufacturing photovoltaic solar cells, offering the mass image processing of its Europa and Galaxy printer platforms with BTU's thermal processing systems. DEK is part of Dover 's Electronic Technologies segment.
