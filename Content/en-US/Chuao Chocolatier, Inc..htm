This company hopes you'll say "wow" when you chew its chocolate. Chuao (pronounced chew-WOW) Chocolatier makes a variety of specialty chocolates including bars, bonbons, hot chocolate mix, baking chocolate, and brownie mixes. Varieties include unusual taste combinations like chocolate, chipotle, salt and popping candy (Firecracker) and chocolate, chile paste, and cayenne pepper (Spicy Maya). Chuao takes its name from Venezuela's famed Chuao cacao-growing region; the company's founders, Michael and Richard Antonorsi, brothers and president and chairman, respectively, were born there. Formed in 2002, Chuao includes three chocolate and gelato cafes and a wholesale operation serving national specialty retailers.
