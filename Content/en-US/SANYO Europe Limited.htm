SANYO Europe is the electronics giant's connection to Europe. One of more than 200 subsidiaries comprising SANYO Electric , which is owned by Panasonic , SANYO Europe has historically accounted for about 10% of SANYO Electric's revenue. The European unit sells a slew of consumer electronics (digital cameras and LCD TVs) and appliances (washing machines, refrigerators, and car navigation systems), office equipment (projectors and television systems), as well as commercial equipment (air conditioners, compressors, and medical equipment). It also markets energy-saving lithium-ion batteries, fuel cells, and smart gas-meter-use devices. SANYO Europe operates via a network of representatives across 30 countries.

SANYO Europe Limited is part of Europe and Appliances segment of its parent company, Panasonic Corporation.

The company sells its products across 30 European countries including Austria, Belgium, Denmark, Germany, the UK, and Switzerland.

SANYO Europe's regional presence and capabilities appreciably complement Panasonic's objective to tailor its lineup to market needs. In 2011, for example, Panasonic successfully introduced a lineup of energy-saving refrigerators and washing machines, products for which SANYO Europe has an established customer base.
