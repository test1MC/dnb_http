This company helps people hunt down retail deals and hot new wheels. Scout24 Holding operates a network of websites offering a variety of services for consumers and job seekers in Europe. It has nearly 10 sites that help people find discounted prices on new and used cars, consumer electronics, and travel and financial services, as well as employment, real estate, and life mates. Scout24 serves Internet users in Germany and more than 15 countries. The company was founded in 1998 by Joachim Schoss and Christian Mangstl. It is a subsidiary of Deutsche Telekom , Europe's leading telecom company.
