Life is all but dull for Criterion Machine Works, a manufacturer of boring products and related machine tool accessories. Its products include boring heads, bars, shanks, adapters, and other machine tool accessories made from premium alloy steels. Its boring heads feature threaded backs that can be fitted with interchangeable shanks. The company serves customers around the world, with facilities in the UK and in the US, and has been in business since the 1940s.
