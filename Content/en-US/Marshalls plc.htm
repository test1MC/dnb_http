Marshalls doesn't mind being stepped on. The company produces landscape, garden, and patio products for use in residential, commercial, and public projects. Its products include paving blocks, walling, drainage systems, greenhouses, garages, and street furniture. The company, which operates its own quarries, utilizes a variety of materials, including concrete and natural stone, as well as iron, steel, wood, glass, and polyurethane. The do-it-yourself home improvement and home building markets are the largest users of Marshalls' products. The company a network of service centers and two manufacturing and distribution sites in the UK.
