EZchip Technologies tries to make the lives of telecom gear makers just a littler EZier. The company designs 10-Gigabit network processors and related products used in high-speed telecommunications equipment such as routers and switches. EZchip says that its devices reduce cost and power consumption because they combine various functions onto a single chip. The fabless semiconductor company was founded in 1999 by CEO Eli Fruchter; its customers include Cisco , Huawei , Juniper Networks , and ECI Telecom . After buying out other investors, including Goldman Sachs , EZchip Semiconductor (formerly LanOptics) has made EZchip a wholly owned subsidiary.
