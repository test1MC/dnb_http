Ultra-Scan manufactures fingerprint identification technology used for access management and other security purposes. Its fingerprint readers use ultrasonic sound waves to measure and identify fingerprints, while its integrated hardware and software systems (TouchLink, TouchPoint) use that data to allow authorized access to computer systems. Ultra-Scan's systems are also used for facility access and other security applications. In addition to software and hardware systems, the company provides security consulting services. President John Schneider started Ultra-Scan with Frank Keeney, former president of Nanodata Computer Corporation, in 1989.
