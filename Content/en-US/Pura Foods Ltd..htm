Pura Foods (aka ADM Pura) runs a slick business. A subsidiary of US agricultural giant Archer Daniels Midland , Pura Foods refines and processes edible oils, shortenings, margarines, and other spreads. The company caters to the bakery, foodservice, and retail food sectors in the UK, as well as in selected overseas markets. Oils produced by Pura Foods include coconut, palm, rapeseed, and sunflower. Because of concern about genetically modified grains and livestock, as well as allergy and health issues, the company does not refine animal fats, soya, maize, or ground nuts. Popular brands provided by Pura Foods include Bake-lite, Peerless, Wesson Green, Pura, Leonara, and Frymax.

The company has offices, a bottling plant, and R&D facilities in Kent, as well as manufacturing facilities in Essex. In addition, Pura Foods blends and packs many of these oils at its specialty oil refinery and packaging facility in Purfleet, Essex.

The company refines liquid oils including rapeseed and high-oleic rapeseed, linseed, sunflower seed, and high-oleic sunflower and winterized sunflower seed. It also refines tropical oils including palm, palm olein, palm stearine, palm kernel, palm kernel stearine and coconut.

Pura Foods, which supplies some 45% of the UK's bulk vegetable oil requirements, serves some of the country's top food manufacturers, bakeries, caterers, and technical industries. The company specializes in providing its customers with individual oil streams and oil blends.

The company serves customers ranging from small craft bakers to large, multinational manufacturers.
