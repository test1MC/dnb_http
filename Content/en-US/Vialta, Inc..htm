Vialta makes videophone systems (color video enables users to see the person whom they are calling) under the Beamer name, as well as VistaFrame digital picture frames (units display photographs from internal memory or from a digital camera memory card), and markets them through retailers such as Best Buy and Fry's Electronics . Founded in 1999, Vialta has offices in California and Hong Kong and distributes its products to some 60 countries. The company's chairman Fred Chan owns 100% of the company's stock when he took the company private in 2005.
