SynQor makes AC/DC and DC/DC power converters and EMI (electromagnetic interference) filters for communications, computing, industrial, medical, military, railway and transportation applications. The company has many distributors, manufacturer's representatives, and sales representatives around the world; some have included Avnet Electronics Marketing and Nu Horizons Electronics . Its client base is comprised of the 12 largest telecom OEMs in the world. SynQor has offices in China, France, Malaysia, South Korea, and the US. The company was established in 1997 by Martin Schlecht, an MIT professor.
