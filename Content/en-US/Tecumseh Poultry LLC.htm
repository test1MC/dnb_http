Tecumseh Poultry makes sure its chickens keep their cool. The company produces natural, free-roaming, grain-fed, and certified organic fresh chicken products which are air-cooled instead of chilled in standard (non-potable) communal water baths, which is the system that most producers use. Air-cooling eliminates eliminates water absorption and cross contamination. The company's chicken is sold at retail food outlets in the US under the Smart Chicken brand name. Its products include whole and cut-up chickens, ground chicken meat, chicken franks, and sausage, including Italian sausage and bratwurst made with chicken.
