From its 80-acre estate winery, Badger Mountain Vineyard produces certified organic wines under the Badger Mountain label. The company also makes non-organic wines from fruit grown elsewhere under the Powers Winery label. The Badger Mountain Vineyard transitioned to organic cultivation in 1988. Its wines are available throughout the US through various distributors. Chardonnay, merlot, syrah, and cabernet sauvignon are some of the varietals available.
