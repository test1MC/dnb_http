Jamestown Coating Technologies (formerly Jamestown Paint) helps its customers to paint their wagons, and much more. The family-run company makes more than 2,000 varieties of coatings used by automotive OEMs and other manufacturing companies, including producers of heavy machinery, pipes and containers, general industrial products, and office furniture. The coatings manufacturer also provides custom formulation, process optimization, training, and waste reduction services. Jamestown Coating Technologies serves both domestic and international customers, with a primary focus on clients in the northeastern US.

Jamestown Coating Technologies also develops a broad range of coatings for packaging. Some of these coatings are protective and decorative (such as urethane and UV-curable coatings for cosmetic packaging, or epoxy coatings for disposable aluminum cookware). Others are functional, like heat seal coatings and print primers for flexible aluminum or plastic. The company produces batches in any quantity, from 1-ounce touch-up vials to 4,000-gallon tankers.

It also operates the Jamestown Powder Coatings unit.

The company serves a range of industries, providing industrial applications, packaging applications, and specialty applications.

Jamestown Coating Technologies was founded in 1885 as the Jamestown Paint & Varnish Company in Jamestown, Pennsylvania by Victor Hugo Von Ehrhart.
