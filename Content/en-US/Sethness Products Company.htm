Color is the thing at Sethness Products -- a specific range of colors. From pale yellow to dark brown, the firm helps make colas look dark and foods look "done." The family-owned business manufactures caramel coloring for food and beverage companies. Its products are used by makers of baked goods, sauces and seasonings, candy, soup, syrup, pet food, and beverages (beer, tea, soft and protein drinks, wine, and distilled goods). Sethness, which makes liquid and powdered caramel products, has production facilities in the US, France, and India and serves customers worldwide. The company, founded in 1880, is owned and operated by members of the fourth- and fifth-generation of the Sethness family.
