DMI Music & Media Solutions has helped propel the jingle into the 21st century. The music branding agency creates and markets tunes to represent consumer brands and products. DMI works with brands to create their "soundDNA" - the audio "footprint" that consumers recognize immediately and equate with a brand. DMI's in-house production and entertainment companies include DMI Music Branding Group, DMI Music & Media Networks (A/V programming), Big Rig Interactive (design studio), 5 Alarm Music (music library), and Firehouse Recording Studios. Clients include the AARP , Build-A-Bear Workshop , Victoria's Secret , and United Airlines . DMI Music & Media Solutions was founded in 1997 by CEO Tena Clark.
