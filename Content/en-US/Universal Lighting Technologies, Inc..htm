In order for you to see your name up in lights, you need a smooth flow of electricity, which is where Universal Lighting Technologies comes in. The company manufactures compact fluorescent, dimming, electronic, high-intensity discharge (HID), and magnetic ballasts and neon transformers. It also supplies light-emitting diode (LED) sign illumination products and energy management lighting control products. Universal Lighting Technologies' products are marketed under brands including Universal, Triad, and Signa. Universal Lighting Technologies is a part of the Panasonic group of companies.
