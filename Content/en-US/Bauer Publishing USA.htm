Feeling out of touch? Turn to Bauer Publishing USA, the publisher of celebrity and style magazines In Touch Weekly and Life & Style Weekly , women's magazines First and Woman's World , soap magazine Soaps in Depth , and teen magazines TWIST , M , and J-14 . Bauer Publishing USA is a top seller of women's, teen, and entertainment magazines on the newsstand. The company is owned by German parent company Bauer Verlagsgruppe (also known as The Bauer Publishing Group). The Bauer Publishing Group operates in the UK through Bauer Publishing USA sister companies H. Bauer Publishing and Bauer Consumer Media .

The company's magazines are sold at supermarkets, drug stores, convenience stores, bookstores, and newsstands across the US. It has offices in New York, California, Illinois, and Texas.

Since its founding, the firm has grown to publish a total of about 10 magazine titles in three distinct categories: entertainment, women's, and teen. Woman's World consistently ranks among the top-selling magazines at newsstands (behind Hearst's Cosmopolitan and Time Warner's People) with about 1.2 million single-copy sales.

Bauer Publishing USA uses a European approach to publishing, which includes a focus on newsstand, not subscription, sales.

The company has been expanding its portfolio with the launch of new magazines. It plans to roll out three new titles in the second half of 2013.
