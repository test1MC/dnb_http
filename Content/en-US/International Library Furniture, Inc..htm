Shhhhhh! International Library Furniture (ILF) hopes you'll curl up quietly with a good book and make yourself comfy on one of the furniture pieces from its lineup. The company makes upholstered lounge seating, reading chairs, study carrels, shelves, tables, book trucks, and other assorted library furniture all at its factory in Keene, Texas. ILF offers a 15-year warranty on all of its products. Founded in 1985, International Library Furniture sells primarily to public, university, and corporate libraries worldwide.
