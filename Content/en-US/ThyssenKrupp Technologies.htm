Give smart people high-tech toys and, voilà! ThyssenKrupp Technologies. The ThyssenKrupp AG division operates in four business areas, across more than 80 countries. Its Elevator Technology business builds and services motion systems, from elevators to escalators and moving walks. Marine Systems engineer ships and vessels, launching non-nuclear submarines and frigates for military use, and yachts for private owners. Industrial camshafts and crankshafts, tunnel driving machinery, undercarriages for earthmovers, and automotive parts and systems are churned out by its Components Technology business. Plant Technology builds refinery, chemical, and cement factories, as well as tools for mining and material handling.
