China Huaren Organic Products (dba Jilin Huaren) plays in a healthy arena. The company markets organic rice and grains, and grain-based products (biscuits, cakes, and crackers). It also makes organic nutritional supplements from ginkgo leaves and blue-green algae (spirulina). Jilin Huaren also offers organic cosmetics. All of its product manufacturing is outsourced. Aimed at the growing urban class in China, Jilin's products are available through a multilevel distribution network, made up of 90 branch units and 140 specialty stores, all of which are independently owned. Warner Technology & Investment owns 19% of the company; former officer Fang Jinzhong owns 16%; former officer Zhang Chengcai owns 10%.
