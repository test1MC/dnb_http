Morinda (formerly Tahitian Noni International) forages for fortune beyond  traditional corporate culture. The company, dba Morinda Bioactives, markets juices, skin- and hair-care products, and nutritional supplements made from green noni, a tropical fruit reputedly high in health benefits. In addition to its core line, Tahitian Noni Bioactive Beverages, offerings include teas, energy drinks, and weight-loss and protein blends. Morinda Bioactives has manufacturing operations in China, Japan, Tahiti, Germany, and the US, and sells its products through multi-level distributors in some 70 markets. Researchers Stephen Story and John Wadsworth founded the subsidiary of Morinda Holdings in 1995 with Kerry and Kim Asay.
