If you aspire to acquire wire, then Nexans is the supplier you desire. The manufacturer of copper, aluminum, and fiber-optic cables and wires provides products for the infrastructure, industry, building, and local area network (LAN) markets. Nexans' main focus is in the energy infrastructure sector where its cables are used for power production, transmission and distribution. Its industry segment offers cables used inside vehicles or for oil and gas materials handling. Structures such as office buildings and industrial complexes also utilize Nexans' cables for Ethernet networks and fire safety systems. Global customers include engineering companies, installers, power utilities, and wholesalers.
