Matrox Electronic Systems developed a matrix for graphic success. Matrox focuses on computer graphics, video, and imaging hardware and software. Its Matrox Graphics unit makes multi-display graphics cards for business and gaming applications. Matrox also makes professional video hardware and software for real-time editing, Web streaming, and DVD authoring applications. Its imaging division makes hardware and software for machine vision, image analysis, and video surveillance. Matrox also offers medical display controllers for use in operating rooms and medical presentations. The company is owned by president Lorne Trottier and chairman Branko Matic, who founded Matrox in 1976.
