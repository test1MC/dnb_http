R&S Robertson's work is more than a bit illuminating. The company designs, manufacturers, and sells commercial and architectural lighting products. The company's interior and exterior products include LED floor lights, office lighting, wall mounted lights, and weatherproof lights that address the stylish and functional needs of its customers. R&S Robertson primarily serves the hotel, retail, and leisure industries. The company's customers are located in the UK as well as overseas; clients have included such notable names as Burger King , Holiday Inn, and Sainsbury's . R&S Robertson is owned by Steeple plc, an electrical wholesaler based in the UK.

The company's major projects have included Amarone Restaurant, Aberdeen; The Anchor Line Restaurant, Glasgow; Sohe Restaurant, Newcastle; Hilton Stansted Airport; Guoman Charing Cross Hotel, London; Hilton Craigendarroch, Ballater; Hilton Hotel, Glasgow Grosvenor; New Northumbria, Newcastle; The Dome, Edinburgh; Village Hotel, Solihull; and Meditteraneo Restaurant, Glasgow.

R&S Robertson serves the Hotel and Leisure industry, with clients ranging from Michelin-starred restaurants and boutique hotels to a small chain of public houses.

It interacts with its customers primarily through its sales teams.
