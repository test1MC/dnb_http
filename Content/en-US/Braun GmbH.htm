Braun GmbH, a manufacturer of electric appliances worldwide, keeps its customers' skin stubble-free and teeth sparkling clean. Product categories include shaving and grooming, oral care, beauty care, and clocks and calculators. The company also makes household products (blenders, juicers, steam irons) that are licensed to Italy's De'Longhi Group. Its products are sold through mass merchandisers, grocery stores, drug stores, and department stores, among other locations. Founded by Max Braun in the '20s to market radio components, Braun company became part of Gillette in 1967. The company (and Gillette) became part of Procter & Gamble in 2005.
