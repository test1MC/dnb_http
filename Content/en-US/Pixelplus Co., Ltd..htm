Pixelplus puts pixels galore onto your cell phone. The company designs and markets high-resolution image sensor chips used in mobile phone cameras. It offers both CMOS and application-specific integrated circuit (ASIC) designs. Image sensors from Pixelplus incorporate several functions, including image processing and autofocus, on a single device. The fabless semiconductor company outsources manufacturing chores to South Korean silicon foundry Dongbu HiTek and to Taiwanese semiconductor contract manufacturer United Microelectronics . Top Pixelplus customers include BYD , Cresyn, Dongbu HiTek (23% of sales), Logitech , Seiko (20%), and Sharp .
