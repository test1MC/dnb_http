Ansun Multitech is India's largest manufacturer and exporter of zipper sliders, zippers, personalized pullers, alloy buttons, luggage fittings, pant hooks and eyes, and other fastening accessories, as well as die casting for precision components. It serves the leather goods, luggage, and garment industries, among others. The company, which was founded as a private limited company in 1985 by brothers and co-CEOs Anil and Sunil Monga, was converted to a public limited company in 1995; its name is derived from the two brother's names. The Monga family controls Ansun Multitech.
