Wegener doesn't mind broadcasting its business. The company, through its Wegener Communications subsidiary, makes transmission and receiving equipment primarily for the broadcast and data communications markets. Its products include digital and analog compression equipment that increases satellite channel capacity, cue and control products that enable cable networks to insert local commercials, devices that feed data to news and weather services, and equipment that transmits background music to businesses. Customers include MUZAK and Roberts Communications.
