Intarcia Therapeutics likes to think long-term for its drug delivery system. The development-stage company's DUROS device is a matchstick-sized mini-pump that can be inserted under the skin to deliver drugs steadily over an extended time period, even up to one year. The FDA-approved device is being tested for delivery of existing drugs, drugs in development, interferon, and peptides. The company's pipeline includes potential treatments for Type 2 diabetes, hepatitis C, and obesity. It has products in both clinical and preclinical trials.
