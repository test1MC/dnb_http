It's not that other pills aren't special, but the SmartPill is above average. The SmartPill is a medical device development company that thinks small. Really small. A patient swallows a SmartPill capsule-based device which then tracks its progression through the digestive tract, including location, pressure, and the time it takes to pass through the intestines. Data measurements are transmitted via radio frequency and recorded by an external receiver for several days. Designed as a less invasive alternative to endoscopic methods that often require anesthesia, the SmartPill can help physicians diagnose and treat gastrointestinal disorders on an outpatient basis.
