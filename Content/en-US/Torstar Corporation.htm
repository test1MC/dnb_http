Newspapers and romance novels orbit around this company. Torstar is one of Canada's leading newspaper publishers, anchored by its flagship daily the Toronto Star . Its portfolio of newspapers also includes such local dailies as The Hamilton Spectator and The Mississauga News, as well as some 100 community newspapers. Torstar also publishes local editions of the free weekly Metro through a partnership with UK-based Metro International . In addition to newspapers, the company owns Harlequin Enterprises , the world's leading romance novel publisher, and it operates several websites and other digital media assets.

Despite owning Canada's leading daily newspaper, Torstar has been hurt by sharp declines in advertising revenue that have plagued most in the industry. In response, the company has been focused on cutting costs through efforts including job reductions.

In 2015 Torstar acquired a 56% interest in VerticalScope, a leading North American vertically focused digital media company that owns and operates more than 600 consumer enthusiast online forums and premium content sites across North America.
