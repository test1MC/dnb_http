Delta Fluid Products has absolute control over fluids and gases. The company is one of the UK's largest makers of controls, valves and fittings for fluids and gases. Applications include domestic heating and oil refineries. The company makes bronze valves and industrial pipe-fittings under the Brownall brand, gunmetal safety valves under NABIC, and brass compression fittings under the Wade brand. Beyond fluid control, the company also makes such gas control components as regulators and ball valves, as well as sight flow indicators, through its Sperryn and Rhodes divisions. The company's IAT brand of valves is used for potable water installation. Delta Fluid Products is owned by Crane Co. .

The company supplies products to the building services market, including safety and pressure relief valves for commercial and industrial hot water, steam and air applications and industrial pipe fittings and accessories.

The company is based in Merseyside, UK.
