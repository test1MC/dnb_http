Check out M & F Worldwide. The holding company's Harland Clarke Corp. business manufactures checks and related products, forms, treasury supplies, and delivery and fraud-prevention services. Its Harland Financial Solutions provides lending and mortgage applications, risk management and business intelligence solutions, and customer management software for commercial banks and credit unions. M & F's Scantron Corporation offers testing and assessment solutions to schools in North America, while its Mafco Worldwide is one of the world's largest makers of licorice extract, used primarily for flavoring tobacco and candy products. Diversified holding company MacAndrews & Forbes Holdings owns M & F.

M & F Worldwide is headquartered in New York City and does business throughout the US.

The company has four business segments, which are operated by its subsidiaries Harland Clarke, Harland Financial Solutions, Scantron, and Mafco Worldwide.
