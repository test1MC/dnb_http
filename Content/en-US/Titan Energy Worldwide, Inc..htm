Titan Energy Worldwide is a distributor of standby and emergency power equipment and a manufacturer of disaster relief systems. It offers power generators from such manufacturers as Generac Power Systems . It also makes and markets the Sentry 5000 mobile utility system, an all-in-one trailer unit that provides electrical power, heating and cooling, water purification, communication, and lighting. Titan Energy markets these products to first responders, relief agencies, defense and homeland security agencies, and municipalities. In early 2015, the company was acquired by Pioneer Power Solutions.
