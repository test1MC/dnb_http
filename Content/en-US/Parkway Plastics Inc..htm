Parkway Plastics' products line the shelves of convenience stores nationwide, but you'd likely never know it. The company designs, manufactures, and markets more than 150 sizes of stock plastic jars and bottles, from 1/8 ounce to 40 ounces, in customizable colors. In addition to jars, Parkway Plastics makes metal and plastic closures, and dust disk liners (popular in the cosmetics industry), jewelry cleaning baskets, and wax containers (commonly used in the automotive and household products industries). The company boasts more than 8,000 customers across the US. Entrepreneur Edward W. Rowan, Sr. founded Parkway Plastics in 1951; his son, Edward Jr., is chairman and president.
