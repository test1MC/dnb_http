Who wants to deal with a snooty croupier when there are Novomatic's many gaming machines lighting up all over the casino floor? Novomatic primarily designs and manufactures electronic betting machines, such as slot machines, video poker machines, video lottery terminals, multiplayer games, electronic table games, and other automated betting terminals, which are sold in some 80 countries. Novomatic operates more than 235,000 gaming terminals in more than 1,600 traditional and electronic casinos as well as via rental units. Subsidiary Admiral Sportswetten operates more than 200 sports betting outlets in Austria. In addition, the company owns the Hotel Admiral am Kurpark in the resort town of Baden, Austria.
