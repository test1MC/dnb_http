Springs Global Participações wouldn't mind taking blanket share of the home linens market. The Brazilian holding company is one of the world's leading bed linen and bath product businesses through subsidiaries Springs Global US and Coteminas . Through them, the company designs, manufactures, and markets its own branded and licensed set of products -- bed sheets, comforters, duvets, pillow cases, shower curtains, towels, bath rugs, and bath accessories -- under such names as Artex, Garcia, Magicolor, Regal, Santista, Springmaid, and Wamsutta, among others. With plants in Argentina, Brazil, Mexico, and the US, Springs Global Participações sells mainly to large retailers in North and South America.
