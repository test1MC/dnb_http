Maval Manufacturing aims to steer in the direction of profits. The company, which has been in business since 1987, offers a line of new and remanufactured automotive steering components, including power rack and pinion units, power steering pumps, electric steering columns and gears, and power steering gear boxes. Maval's steering systems are made for on- and off-road specialty vehicles. Its brand names include Unisteer and Rackzilla. Maval products are used as replacement parts for vehicles made by a wide range of US-based and overseas auto manufacturers. Its remanufactured products are sold to OEMs and aftermarket parts distributors. In 2015 the company was acquired by Remy International .
