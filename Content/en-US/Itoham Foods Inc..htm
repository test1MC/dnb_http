Itoham Foods specializes in protein in a variety of forms. The Japanese firm makes and sells fresh and processed meat products, including fresh beef and pork meat, as well as bacon, ham, sausage, ready-to-eat meals, and frozen and chilled meat and poultry value-added foods, such as pizza and breaded chicken sticks. It also makes noodles and dairy products. Itoham's customers include retail food purveyors and the restaurant sector. Its US subsidiary, California-based American Peptide Company, makes ingredients used by pharmaceutical and biotech companies to research and develop new drugs.
