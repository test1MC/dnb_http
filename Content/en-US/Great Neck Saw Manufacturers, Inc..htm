Great Neck Saw Manufacturers makes, markets, and distributes some 3,000 hand tools. Its portfolio of products includes screwdrivers and saws, as well as levels and layout tools, knives and sets, and flashlights. Great Neck Saw also markets a line of professional-grade auto repair tools. The manufacturer sells its products online through a variety of retailers under the brand names Mayes, Sheffield, Bulldog, OEM, GreatLITE, and Great Neck, among others. Founded in 1919 by Samuel Jacoff making 10 saw designs, the company operates about half a dozen factories and distribution facilities across the US.

Based in New York, Great Neck Saw runs factories and distribution centers nationwide.

Great Neck Saw makes and distributes tools for the hardware, automotive, and do-it-yourself markets. It sells its products through retail distribution partners and online.
