If you've ever dodged some bad food because it was past its "use by" date, thank Linx Printing Technologies. The company manufactures specially designed inkjet and laser printers that are used to mark product packaging (containers, labels, outer cases) for batch identification purposes. Linx also develops and sells specialty inks and accessories such as optical sensors and mounting attachments. Customers include food, beverage, automotive, cosmetics, and electronics companies that need to identify products in batches or by date. Linx uses its own sales force in the UK and France and a network of distributors in 50 other countries. The company, which was formed in 1987, is a subsidiary of Danaher Corporation .
