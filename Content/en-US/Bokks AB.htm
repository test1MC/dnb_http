Bokks designs media player hardware and software used for digital signage and portable entertainment products. It also provides customization and system integration services and provides decoder technology to the security industry. Its Bokks CM software is a customizable content management tool that runs on the Linux operating system. Another product, the Bokks media player, has multiple display options and supports many movie formats and image formats. The company has worked with digital media players since 2000 and has developed custom media-playing technology for companies such as Axis Communications , Shopvision, and Bredbandsbolaget.
