Roadies can move it, you can play it, but König & Meyer can hold it. König & Meyer (K&M) manufactures music stands; guitar and keyboard stands; microphone, speaker, monitor, and speaker stands; keyboard benches; drummer's thrones; music holders; and light stands. The company also makes related music accessories such as adapters, brackets, mounts, and stackers. K&M was founded in 1949 by Karl König and Erich Meyer. Karl's son Martin König continues to run the company.
