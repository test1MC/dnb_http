Groupe Gorgé (formerly Finuchem) makes robotic systems for underwater operations, such as minesweeping and laying cable, and for mechanical and industrial uses, such as appliance, automobile, and semiconductor manufacturing. It also makes high-tech composites used in race cars and medical equipment. The company sold its fine chemicals business and its grinding and mixing unit (Grenier Charvet), and is making acquisitions to strengthen its global standing in the automated food processing and aeronautics industries. About three-quarters of Groupe Gorgé's sales derive from France.
