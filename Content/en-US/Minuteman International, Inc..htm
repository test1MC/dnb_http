Janitors with wood-handle mops and tin buckets dream about Minuteman International. The company makes commercial vacuums, floor and carpet care products, cleaning chemicals, and bio-hazard vacuums, as well as carpet sweepers, scrubbers, and accessories. Its brands include Minuteman, PowerBoss, and Multi-Clean. Minuteman's customer base includes government agencies, contract cleaners, and cleaning equipment dealers and rental companies. In 2004 the company was acquired by German cleaning equipment maker Hako-Werke International, which is owned by German holding company L. Possehl & Co.

The company provides its products for janitorial, manufacturing, municipalities, parking facilities, government and GSA, healthcare, aviation, education, food and beverage, retail and public areas, hospitality, and warehouse/distribution markets.

Minuteman was founded in 1981 as American Cleaning Equipment Corporation.
