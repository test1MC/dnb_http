BMG is a real hit factory. BMG Rights Management (known as BMG Chrysalis in the US, UK, and Sweden) is one of the last few major music publishing firms in the world. BMG boasts a library of almost 1 million songs from more than 1,000 musicians, from UK soul sensation Adele to 70's rockers ZZ Top. Music publishers also handle copyright administration, licensing, and royalties for when songs are distributed over the Internet or used in television or other media. BMG has built its catalog through acquisitions, buying up other publishers such as Bug Music, Cherry Lane , Chrysalis , Crosstown Songs, Evergreen Copyrights, and Stage Three Music.
