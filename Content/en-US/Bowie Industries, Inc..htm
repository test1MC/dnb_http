Bowie Industries believes that the grass can be greener on this side of the fence as well. As a manufacturer of landscaping and erosion control machines, Bowie Industries sells a line of mulching machines (Hydro-Mulchers) used for maintaining and laying turf on residential lawns, golf courses, and sports fields. The company also manufactures machines used for hay mulching, seed distribution, and landfill covering. Bowie's customers include landscaping contractors, city water and flood control agencies, and land developers. The company distributes its products internationally through sales offices and third-party dealers located in the US, Canada, Mexico, Australia, Spain, and other countries.
