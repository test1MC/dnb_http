Catering primarily to South East Asia, highly diversified Jebsen & Jessen specializes in seven core operating segments through its some 40 subsidiaries and affiliates in Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, and Vietnam. Jebsen & Jessen Chemicals is a chemical producer, armed with a portfolio of more than 500 products, while Jebsen & Jessen Communications offers telephony products and services. Its Jebsen & Jessen Marketing segment provides brand management services, and its MHE-Demag business specializes in material handling equipment. The firm also owns subsidiaries in the packaging, bio-pharmaceutical engineering, and technology consulting sectors.
