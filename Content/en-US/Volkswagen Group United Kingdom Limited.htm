Volkswagen Group United Kingdom can be seen rolling along the carriageways on the British Isles. As one might suspect, the company is the UK arm of Germany-based Volkswagen AG . Volkswagen Group United Kingdom imports Volkswagen vehicles and automotive parts, as well as other brands from its parent company. The vehicles are sold through a nationwide network of more than 250 independent dealers. Volkswagen Group UK also provides support services to its retailers and customers. Volkswagen, which first entered the UK market in 1952.
