Nebraska Popcorn hopes popcorn consumption isn't confined to the movie theater experience. The company produces grows and manufactures raw and processed popcorn for retail, wholesale, and export. Its products are sold under the Morrison Farms label and include microwave popcorn, bulk popcorn, and popped and raw packaged popcorn. Nebraska Popcorn also offers private-label services. It customers include grocery stores and other food retailers, as well as companies in the foodservice industries.
