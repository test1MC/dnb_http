Tmp International, dba Todd McFarlane Productions (TMP) has spawned a comic book empire. TMP is the comic publishing firm of Spawn creator Todd McFarlane. After cutting his teeth as an artist for Marvel 's Spider-Man , McFarlane left that company (taking several artists with him) and helped found Image Comics . (TMP is an autonomous unit within Image, providing the firm with publishing, marketing, and distribution services). In addition to Spawn, TMP also publishes the ghostly comic Haunt . Archived series include Spawn spinoff Hellspawn and the crime noir Sam & Twitch. McFarlane owns separate firms engaged in the toy business, film and video production, and graphic design.
