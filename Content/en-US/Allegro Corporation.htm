Allegro Corporation is one of the largest independent distributors of music in North America. The company distributes releases by more than 200 alternative, blues, classical, jazz, new age, and pop independent record labels. Allegro works with labels that have no fewer than 12 artists and/or 25 titles, including Knit Media's Knitting Factory Records, E1 Music , and K-Tel International .
