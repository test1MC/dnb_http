CustomVis envisioned a future without glasses or contacts. The Australian company designed, manufactured, and sold surgical equipment that utilized solid state lasers to correct nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism. The company's flagship product was the Pulzar Z1, which had not yet been approved by the FDA for use in the US. CustomVis also made a portable digital camera used to take pictures of the retina. The company had research and development facilities in Australia and marketed its products to eye doctors and clinics through distributors in about 20 countries worldwide.
