Who needs wind? Revcor has the products that generate a cool (and hot) breeze. The company utilizes advanced airflow technology to manufacture metal and plastic wheels, fans, and blowers for use in the heating and air conditioning, automotive, agricultural, ventilation, appliance, and off-road original equipment manufacturer markets. In addition to providing its products, Revcor offers wheel and fan replacement and repair services. It also offers system analysis and project software. Revcor has a facility in the US and in Mexico and was established in 1946.

Revcor products are manufactured at a plant of 300,000 sq. ft. in Carpentersville, Illinois (near Chicago) and an axial fan assembly facility of 50,000 sq. ft. with other manufacturing capabilities in Mexico.

Revcor sells its products through contractors and wholesalers as well as through its sales team in the US.
