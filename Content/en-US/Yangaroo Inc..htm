Yangaroo, formerly Musicrypt, is dead serious about making sure media can be digitally delivered securely and inexpensively. The company helps recording companies, radio stations, advertising agencies, and other businesses deliver and receive audio and video content via its Web-based DMDS (Digital Media Distribution System) platform. Its BRM (Biometric Rights Management) system verifies media-access rights. Yangaroo has offices in Canada, the US and the UK. The company was founded in 1999.
