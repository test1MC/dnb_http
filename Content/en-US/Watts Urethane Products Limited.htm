A key subsidiary of Watts of Lydney Group , Watts Urethane Products manufactures castable polyurethane and ultrathane products such as industrial tires, printing squeegees, and roller coaster guide wheels. Additional Watts Urethane products include outdoor products such as furniture, road barriers, and fencing. The company's products are also used to coat metals, form liners used inside pipe, and as a packaging medium. The nature of polyurethane and ultrathane makes them resistant to oil and chemicals, abrasions, extreme temperatures, and environmental degradation.

The company's business is organized into five sectors, which serve many different markets: Watts Industrial, Watts Squeegee, Watts Street Furniture, Watts Wheels' Tyres, and Watts Wheels' Tyres.

Watts Urethane Products sells squeegee blades across 75 countries and exports 90% of its production through the two brands of Watts and Ora. Clients include manufacturers who screen print textiles, electrical circuits, electronics, glass and ceramics, CD and DVDs and solar panels; and industrial and retail floor cleaners using push-along and drive-on machine scrubbers.
