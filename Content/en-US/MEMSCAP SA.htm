MEMSCAP makes microelectromechanical systems (MEMS) components, such as radio-frequency switches, variable capacitors, inductors, optical switches and attenuators, and sensors. The company also offers design, integration, and manufacturing services. It caters to a wide variety of markets, including network and medical equipment suppliers, aircraft manufacturers, research institutes and universities, and cosmetic businesses. Aerospace and defense customers account for some 40% of the company's revenue.

MEMSCAP's four markets are: aerospace/defense, 41% of revenue; optical communications, 25% of revenue; medical and biomedical, 26% of revenue; and IT and consumer market, 8% of revenue.

Revenue rose 4% in 2015 from 2014 and profit leaped 26% higher on the revenue growth and reductions in research and development costs. Cash flow generated from operations slowed 15% in 2015 from 2014.
