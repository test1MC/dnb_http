Ericsson Canada stretches mobile telecommunications networks and services across Canada's vast expanse. Part of the North American operating arm of Swedish mobile telecom giant Ericsson (along with US-based Ericsson Inc. ), the subsidiary sells such products as mobile and wired broadband, metro-area Ethernet, and optical transport infrastructure gear. It also offers such services as consulting, network management, design, and integration. The company markets mobile phones through a joint venture with Sony called Sony Ericsson Mobile Communications . Established in 1953, Ericsson Canada is one of the country's leading investors in research and development. Customers have included Hydro-Quebec and Rogers Wireless .
