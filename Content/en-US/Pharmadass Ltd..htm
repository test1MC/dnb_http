HealthAid (formerly Pharmadass) wants to lend a helping handful of supplements toward a healthier lifestyle. Launched in 1982 with a single product, the company sells more than 560 products: vitamins and minerals, beauty care items, weight-loss formulas, essential aromatherapy oils, and natural body care products. It also sells anti-smoking products, herbal cigarettes, anti-snoring spray, and such supplements as fish oils and bee products. Specialty lines focus on such areas as mood enhancement, women's health, the immune system, and vitality. HealthAid sells its products in about 4,000 pharmacies and health food stores in the UK and in more than 60 countries worldwide.

The company's products are all additive-, flavoring-, and dairy-free.

HealthAid uses distributors to sell its products to international retailers, pharmacies; it also sells via e-commerce and telephone. In the UK, the company has a dedicated field sales force.
