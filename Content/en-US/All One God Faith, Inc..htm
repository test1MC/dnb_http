"Absolute cleanliness is Godliness!" and other exclamations can be found on the company's heady Dr. Bronner's Magic Soaps labels. All One God Faith, operating as Dr. Bronner's, sells its products primarily through natural foods retailers. It makes items for body washing, tooth brushing, shampoo, and laundry. The company's liquid and bar soaps (made of vegetable oil) rely on low prices and word-of-mouth for advertising. All One God Faith makes lip balms, lotions, shave gels, and all-purpose cleaners, as well as ALPSNACK hemp-nut nutrition bars. Founded by master soap maker, world peace visionary, and "Pope of Soap" Dr. Emmanuel Bronner, the company is still family owned and operated by his descendants.
