Omron Automation and Safety (formerly Omron Electronics) likes to be in control. The industrial automation company makes sensors and programmable controls to keep machines and factories running like clockwork. Other products include timers and counters, push-buttons and switches, temperature and process controllers, printed circuit connectors, and digital panel meters. The company's products are used in the automotive, electronics, food and beverage, and semiconductor industries, among others. It also offers custom product fabrication services. Omron Automation and Safety is a major subsidiary of Japan-based OMRON Corporation .

Omron Automation serves customers in the US, Latin America, and Canada for its Japanese parent company Omron .

In addition to its industrial automation products, the company makes safety products through subsidiary Omron Scientific Technologies, Inc. (OSTI). The OSTI division makes various safety devices and components -- including safety lights and lasers, bumpers, switches, safeguards, and relays -- for industrial equipment.

Omron Automation plans to grow OSTI aggressively. The company has also looked to the gaming industry to expand its business as nearly all casino games are now automated.
