Nemschoff is an upscale remedy for furniture needs. The company makes not only high-end chairs, but tables, casegoods, and other furniture for use in hospitals and various health care facilities nationwide. Its seating options span styles for patient, lounge, guest, and bariatric settings. Its Foxxman line caters to health care customers with its Lancaster recliner and Beaumont sleep settee. For children, Nemschoff makes furniture, playhouses, and wall activities under the Playscapes brand. Nemschoff operates three manufacturing facilities in Wisconsin and Iowa. The firm was founded in 1950 by Leonard Nemschoff. Furniture designer and supplier Herman Miller acquired Nemschoff in 2009 and made it a subsidiary.
