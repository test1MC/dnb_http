Elizabeth Arden (UK), the London-based subsidiary of Elizabeth Arden, focuses on creating fragrances and cosmetics for its European clients. Its parent has targeted the market with Prevage anti-aging formulas and other skincare and makeup lines, and by launching a fragrance under the licensed name of Italian fashion designer Alberta Ferretti. Other fragrances made and distributed by Elizabeth Arden Ltd. include Britney Spears, the #1 celebrity brand in the UK. The company opened its famed red doors in 1997; it boasts a sole retail outlet, which shares space with the company's headquarters and a Red Door Spa. The UK generates about 15% of the parent's foreign sales.
