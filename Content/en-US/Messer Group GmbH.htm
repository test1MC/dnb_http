The Messer Group operates in more than 30 countries in Europe as well as in China and Peru. In addition to supplying gases such as oxygen, nitrogen, and helium, Messer provides welding and cutting services and, through subsidiaries, makes medical instruments and welding products. The company's products are used by medical facilities and a variety of manufacturers. It delivers gas through on-site facilities and pipelines, as well as by truck and in cylinders. The company sells primarily to customers in Central and Eastern Europe, Western Europe, and Asia. The company came into its current form in 2004 when it sold 70% of its business to industry giant Air Liquide for more than $3 billion.
