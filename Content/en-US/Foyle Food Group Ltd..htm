Foyle Food Group is really focused on only one food group, meat from steers, heifers, and white fatted cows. The group can process more than 200,000 cattle a year through its five sites located at three main locations in Northern Ireland. The facilities slaughter, debone, and render cattle sourced from specialist producers clubs of mostly small family-run farms. Foyle Food's fresh, frozen, and processed beef products are sold under its namesake brands, as well as Blackmoran Angus, Glenslieve Hereford, and private labels. Retailers and foodservice operators, Tesco , Ahold , and McDonalds , are among Foyle's customers. The group operates as a subsidiary of Faughan Ltd., which is wholly owned by the Acheson family.
