Shinco Electronics Group Co. focuses on producing a variety of consumer electronics and air-conditioner products in China. The company's products include portable DVD players, as well as DTV products and EVD players. Shinco Electronics Group sells its product line throughout more than 50 countries around the globe. The company has more than 60 offices and sales branches, as well as more than 300 technology services locations. Shinco Electronics Group plans to expand its operations in Australia, Europe, Japan, and Russia.
