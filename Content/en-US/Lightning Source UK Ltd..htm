Lightning Source UK can print a book in no time. The company provides publishing services for print-on-demand and e-book fulfillment outside the US. Along with its parent company Lightning Source , it has printed more than 50 million books for more than 5,000 publishers worldwide. It ships to distributors and wholesalers including Ingram , Bertram Books , and Baker & Taylor and some retailers including Amazon.com . The company stores books digitally and prints them as-needed for distribution in Africa, Asia, Australia, and Europe, saving shipping costs by printing closer to the destination. Lightning Source UK and its parent are subsidiaries of distribution giant Ingram Industries .
