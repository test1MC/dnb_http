Loea (pronounced "low-ay-ah") manufactures high-speed, high-bandwidth radios that operate in millimeter-wave bandwidth free space (71-76GHz and 81-86GHz range, also known as E-Band). The company has installed its wireless communications equipment at several federal agencies and at the University of Hawaii and other institutions. Loea, a subsidiary of military contractor Trex Enterprises , has facilities in California, Hawaii, and Massachusetts. Loea is the Hawaiian word for technical knowledge and expert.
