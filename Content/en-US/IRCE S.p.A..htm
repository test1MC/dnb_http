IRCE conducts itself well in the world of energy and electrical equipment. The company, which began in 1947, now makes isolated cables for power and data transmission on local-area networks (LANs) and conductors (copper and aluminum enameled thread, or wire) for use in electric generators, motors, transformers, relays, and other electric devices. The company sells almost half of its enameled wire outside Italy. IRCE operates five manufacturing plants, three in Italy, one in the Netherlands, and one in the UK. The company also has distribution operations in Germany, Spain, and Switzerland.
