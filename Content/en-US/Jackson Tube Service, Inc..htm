Jackson Tube Service manufactures welded steel tubing at its facilities in Ohio and North Carolina. To meet its customers' specifications, the company provides such services as annealing, flash rolling and scarfing, re-cutting, and slitting. It has a production output of about 200 million feet per year. Jackson Tube Service was founded in 1972 by Sam Jackson and still is run by his family.
