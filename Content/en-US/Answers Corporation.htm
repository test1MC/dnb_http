This company just needs you to pose the right question. Answers Corporation runs two free, ad-supported Internet search services: ReferenceAnswers.com and WikiAnswers.com. Its ReferenceAnswers supplies users with editorial content covering millions of topics licensed from reference works (including encyclopedias, dictionaries, and atlases from publishers such as Houghton Mifflin Harcourt and Encyclopedia Britannica ). Its WikiAnswers.com is a dynamic questions-and-answers website collaboratively written and edited by a community of users.  Answers Corporation was founded in 2005. An affiliate of investment firm Summit Partners owns the company.
