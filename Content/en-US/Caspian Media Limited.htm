Caspian Media is real interested in business. The company publishes the magazines Real Business and Real Deals . The titles focus on content for entrepreneurs and small and medium-sized enterprises ( Real Business ) and on private equity and venture capital ( Real Deals ). Clients -- such as IBM , HSBC , and EAEC (European Automobile Engineers Cooperation) -- also turn to Caspian Media for their own specialized publications through the company's contract publishing operations. Caspian Media also provides marketing services and organizes events that range from business- and finance-themed conferences and seminars to rugby awards ceremonies. The company was founded in 1996 by Matthew Rock and Stuart Rock.

The company's work is conducted by a team of editors, journalists, graphic designers, event managers, marketers, sales and sponsorship teams, and print and production specialists. Caspian Media also employs an in-house digital development team that creates websites, mobile apps, and social media campaigns.

A recent effort in the digital realm is its Telepresence video conferencing tool, which it developed in collaboration with Tata Communications . Designed to reduce the need for corporate travel, the service provides Public Telepresence Rooms in more than 35 cities around, equipped with microphones, cameras, lighting, and other audio and video capabilities.
