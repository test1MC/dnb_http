Pasta is the prologue and center plate at Monterey Gourmet Foods. The company makes refrigerated prepared pasta meals, including raviolis and tortellinis stuffed with an array of fillings, as well as various savory sauces and specialty items, like gnocchi and polenta, and shredded parmesan cheese. Monterey Gourmet sells the products primarily under its namesake label and the CIBO Naturals brand name. Its customers are mainly retail grocery and club stores in the US, and certain parts of Canada, the Caribbean, Latin America, and the Asia/Pacific region. The company is held by Pulmuone Foods USA, a subsidiary of Korean tofu and noodle maker Pulmuone , which acquired the business in 2009 for more than $45 million.
