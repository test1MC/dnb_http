Eleva Alimentos (also known as Avipal) keeps Brazilians well-fed. The company provides a variety of meat (chicken and pork), dairy, and grain (soy, corn) products. Processing about 1 billion liters of milk each year, Eleva sells a line of dairy products including milk, cheese, and butter under brands such as El Vaquero and Dobon. The company offers poultry and swine, which it processes at its three meat-processing sites. Founded in 1959, Eleva markets its products in Brazil and regions abroad, including Africa, Asia, Central America, Europe, and the Middle East. The company operates four distribution centers in Paraná, Rio de Janeiro, Rio Grande do Sul, and São Paulo states. Eleva is a subsidiary of Brasil Foods.
