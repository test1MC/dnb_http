This company takes your product and puts a cap on it. CAP Industries, also known as Custom Aerosol Packaging, is a contract packager and private labeler of aerosol, liquid, and powder products for the personal care, household, agricultural, and automotive industries, among others. CAP puts products in a number of different container types, from glass bottles and barrier systems to pouches and tubes. It also provides custom paint color matching for sale in a variety of forms - aerosols, cans, and even pens and markers. The company was founded in 1969.
