Faultless Starch/Bon Ami cashes in on keeping you crisp, clean, and nicely scented. The company makes and sells the market-leading laundry brands, Faultless Starch and Magic Sizing, and Bon Ami and Kleen King household cleaners, Garden Weasel outdoor tools, and the Trapp and Evoque home fragrances. It also produces starches and detergents for commercial laundries and dry cleaners. Although once in decline, the Bon Ami line has won fans as an environmentally friendly cleaner, and starch is on the rebound with the return of cotton in garments. The company sells to 30 international markets dotting Europe, South and Central America, and Asia. Faultless Starch/Bon Ami is owned and led by the founding Beaham family.
