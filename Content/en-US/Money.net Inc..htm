Somebody show this site to Jerry Maguire. Money.net provides streaming real-time financial data and services to investors, media companies, and financial institutions. Offerings include real-time quotes, company and market news, and portfolio tracking to individual investors through its Web site. It also distributes information to financial institutions through licensing agreements and customized software systems. In addition, Money.net also owns PCQuote.com and stock screener MarketScreen.com. The company makes money from consumer-based subscription fees, custom development and licensing fees, and Web site and e-mail advertising sales.  Money.net was founded in 1998.
