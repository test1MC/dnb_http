If you hold a job, Simon Jersey can dress you for success. The company makes men's and women's work attire and uniforms, specializing in bespoke apparel (made-to-measure clothing designed for client companies). Simon Jersey distributes apparel, including off-the-shelf lines and footwear, through a 300-page mail-order catalog or online in the UK and internationally. It fills orders for just a few pieces to outfitting companies with 500-plus employees. The clothing ranges from business suits to uniforms for restaurant, hospitality, salon, and medical personnel. Founded in 1971 by Simon Moyle, entrepreneur David Ross bought control of the company in 2014.
