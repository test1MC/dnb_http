Ambu supports life with its respiratory products. The medical device maker develops and markets diagnostic, life support, and training equipment for hospitals and emergency rescue services. It specializes in ventilation products for artificial respiration, such as bags and masks, immobilization devices for the head and neck, and single-use electrodes used to diagnose brain, muscle, and nerve diseases. Ambu also makes mannequins for basic and advanced first aid training. The company sells and exports its products through international distributors and also directly markets them through sales offices in Europe and the US. It also maintains production facilities in Asia.
