Willerby Holiday Homes makes manufactured vacation homes, which are sold through dealers and mobile home parks in the UK and Europe. The upscale homes and lodges are generally equipped with most appliances and furniture as well as stainless steel and porcelain fixtures. Willerby, which began as a travel trailer manufacturer, has brought innovations like central heating, double glazed windows, cathedral ceilings, and pitched roofs from traditional homes to the manufactured home market. One of the largest manufactured home companies in the UK, Willerby is owned by Scaid Investments. It was established in 1946.

Willerby has more than 500 holiday parks. Its brand names include Linear, Skyline, Sheraton, and Chambery.

The company manufactures all of its homes in the UK.
