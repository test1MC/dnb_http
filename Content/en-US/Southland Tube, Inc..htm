Operating in the industrial heart of Dixie, Southland Tube makes carbon steel pipe and tubing. The company manufactures square, round, and rectangular tubular products for the oil, construction machinery, and transportation industries. It produces square tubing in sizes from ½" to 10" along with comparable rectangles. Wall thickness ranges from 18 gauge to ½" wall. Round tubing is available in sizes from ½" pipe through 12" pipe. It operates a single production mill in Alabama. Southland Tube contracts with independent carriers to deliver products to its customers. In 2016 the company agreed to be acquired by Nucor for $130 million.
