What a cute, little bulldozer you've got there! Bobcat Company specializes in compact construction and utility equipment. Its products include mini-track and skid-steer loaders, loader backhoes, excavators, and attachments for all of them. Its brand lines include the M-Series (mini), CT-Series (compact tractor), Toolcat (utility), and VersaHandler (telescopic tool). Based in North Dakota, the company operates manufacturing facilities in the US, the Czech Republic, and France. There are more than 1,000 Bobcat dealers in 100 countries. Bobcat is a subsidiary of Korea-based construction equipment maker Doosan Infracore .

Stationed in West Fargo, North Dakota, Bobcat has its European marketing and distribution center in Belgium, which supports Bobcat sales throughout Europe, the Middle East, Russia, and Africa. The Asia Pacific market is supported from its office in Singapore. Its Japanese headquarters resides in Yokohama, Japan, and the Latin American market is served from offices in Miami.

Bobcat compact loaders have been drafted into service to defend lives. In conjunction with Bobcat, QinetiQ developed a kit to transform the company's compact or skid-steer loaders (in as little as 15 minutes) into remote-controlled robots that can dig up roadside bombs and improvised explosive devices (IEDs). The kits include cameras and microphones that transfer sound and images to the remote operator.

The company provides compact equipment to the construction, rental, landscaping, agriculture, grounds maintenance, government, utility, industrial, and mining sectors. Bobcat has 1,000 independent dealers in more than 100 countries worldwide, serving its customers through more than 3,000 sales specialists.

In 1958 farmer Eddie Velo asked blacksmith-inventor brothers Louis and Cyril Keller to make a compact loader light enough to be lifted to the second floor of his barn. The brothers partnered with North Dakota-based Melroe Manufacturing Company (which later became Bobcat) that same year, and the skid-steer loader was created.
