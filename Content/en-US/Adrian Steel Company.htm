A cluttered work truck is a business opportunity for Adrian Steel. The company makes cargo management products for service trucks and vans, such as bins, drawers, files, ladder racks, partitions, plastic liners, shelving units, and toolboxes. On board, Ford , General Motors , and Nissan have each offered various programs that make Adrian Steel an official upfitter of select new trucks and vans for vocational needs. The company's products are also sold through more than 300 independent distributors throughout the US, Canada, and Puerto Rico. Adrian Steel was founded in 1953 by Bob Westfall, a former All-Pro football player with the Detroit Lions and the father of company president Harley Westfall.
