Reinco helps shape landscapes. The equipment supplier makes power mulching and hydrograssing machinery, which can mulch, seed, and fertilize areas that are disturbed by residential and commercial construction. Landscape contractors use the machines to revegetate soil and control erosion near homes, schools, golf courses, cemeteries, mines, landfill reclamation sites, and other large, hard-to-access areas. Some of its products use emissions-certified diesel engines for a cleaner environment. Reinco sells its equipment and provides service maintenance through a US network of dealers. The company is family-owned and operated.
