The last thing telecommunication equipment needs is a power surge, which is where Surge Technologies comes in. Through its operating subsidiary Surge Technologies, Inc., the company manufactures a variety of electrical surge protection equipment, including protection modules and building entrance terminals. These devices preserve telecommunications gear from the harmful effects of power spikes. The company, which has operations in Canada and the US, also offers related services. Surge Technologies' customers include TELUS Corporation and Verizon Wireless .
