Cummins Filtration gets frustrated when it encounters an unclean machine. The subsidiary of engine manufacturer Cummins is among the world's largest makers of engine-related component products. Cummins Filtration's 7,000-plus offerings include filtration, coolant, and fuel additive products, filters for air, fuel, lube, chemicals, exhaust system technology products, hydraulics, and other filtration systems. Marketed under the Fleetguard brand, the lineup is used primarily on heavy- and medium-duty diesel trucks, in addition to construction, mining, agricultural, marine, and power generation equipment.

The company operates 13 manufacturing plants (in nine countries), 10 distribution centers, and two joint ventures.

Cummins Filtration offers a wide variety of OEM and aftermarket systems for diesel engines, transmissions, and industrial applications worldwide, including engines ranging from 3 to above 100 liters in displacement. It holds more than 200 active global patents for product technology. It portfolio of products include the Centriguard centrifugal filtration system; Enviroguard open crankcase ventilation system; OptiAir air filtration system with disposable parts; and user-friendly lube and hydraulic filters made of advanced composite polymers.

The company targets markets such as heavy, medium, and light duty trucks; industrial; construction; mining; agriculture; marine; power generation; and locomotive. It distributes through more than 30,000 dealer/distributor outlets. Fleetguard branded products are sold through thousands of distributors in more than 160 countries spanning North America, South America, Europe, Asia, Africa and Australia.

Cummins Filtration is a part of parent Cummins' Components Segment, which contributed 21% to the parent company's net revenues in 2013.Components Segment sales increased by 8% in 2013 due to increased sales within the emission solutions business, mainly related to improved on-highway OEM and aftermarket demand in North America, a full year of sales from the Hilite acquisition in 2012, 2013 pre-buy activity in anticipation of higher emission standards in Europe, and growth in the medium-duty Brazilian truck market.

In 2013 the company introduced a new Fleetguard Product Catalog mobile phone application to help users to quickly find products that fit a specific vehicle or engine.
