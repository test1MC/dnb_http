This M86 is made to protect against attacks on a virtual battlefield, not a physical one. The company's secure Web gateways (a combination of software and hardware) protects businesses and schools from malware, data loss, and other email and Web hazards. Its products also manage corporate policy compliance and e-mail archiving and offer web filtering and reporting. M86 offers its products as hosted, cloud-delivered versions in addition to appliance and hybrid options. The company markets its products worldwide from offices in the US, the UK, France, Germany, the Netherlands, Australia, New Zealand, Israel, South Africa, Singapore, and Taiwan. In 2012 it was bought by cyber security provider Trustwave .
