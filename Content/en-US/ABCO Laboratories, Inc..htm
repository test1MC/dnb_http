ABCO Laboratories knows the ABC's of ingredients. The company offers custom, contract, and private-label ingredient manufacturing. Its services extend to clients in the food, nutritional, over-the-counter pharmaceutical, and personal care industries. ABCO provides ingredient custom blending, packaging, and encapsulation for foods (including seasonings, herbs, and marinades), nutritional supplements and homeopathic remedies, and many personal care staples. In addition to the ingredients themselves, ABCO offers such services as product research and development, laboratory analyses, and microbiological testing. It tests focus on yeast, as well as mold, E. coli and other coliforms, salmonella , and other pathogens.
