Just when modern life gets to be too much, Calhoun Bend Mill offers up some good, old-fashioned, soothing, Southern, comfort food. Located in Jonesville, Louisiana, the company produces corn bread, fry, and dessert mixes using genuine stone-ground cornmeal that the company grinds itself. Calhoun Bend products are available to food retailers and foodservice providers throughout the US and Canada, as well as to individual consumers via the company's Web site. Dating back to 1846, the company was founded and is still owned and operated by the Calhoun family.
