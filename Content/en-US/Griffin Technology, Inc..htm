Griffin Technology understands that fashionable and functional electronics must be properly accessorized. The company makes accessories and peripherals for computers and digital music devices. The bulk of its products are designed for use with Apple 's computers, iPod digital media players, and iPhones. Computer products include laptop stands, laptop lights, and computer microphones. On the music side, Griffin is one of the leading makers of iPod accessories; products include car chargers, car holders, FM transmitters, laser pointers, flashlights, and microphones. The company sells its products worldwide through retailers including Best Buy , Amazon , and WH Smith .
