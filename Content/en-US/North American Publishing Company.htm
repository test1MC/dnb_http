If reading about publishing doesn't make you want to take a nap, turn to NAPCO. The North American Publishing Company (NAPCO) publishes about 15 business-to-business magazines, many of which relate to the publishing industry. Its titles include Publishing Executive , Book Business , and Promotional Marketing .  NAPCO also creates and distributes some 20 e-mail newsletters, and produces trade shows such as the Publishing Business Conference and Expo, and the Book Business Conference and Expo. In addition, the company sells Webinars, rents postal and e-mail lists, and provides licensed content to clients' websites. NAPCO was founded in 1958 by Irvin J. Borowsky with the publication of Printing Expressions .
