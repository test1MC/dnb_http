Breeder's Choice Pet Foods provides premium choices for picky pet owners. Founded in 1947, the pet food maker offers a variety of cat and dog food products that not only meet the nutritional requirements of pets but are also specially formulated to help treat specific health needs. The company's AvoDerm brand dog food promotes better skin and coats, while its Active Care line promotes joint health in older dogs. Its Pinnacle line offers a holistic approach for canines and felines. Breeder's Choice other product lines include cat food, skin and hair care products, and dog treats. The company is owned by Central Garden & Pet Company , which spent about $25 million in cash to acquire Breeder's Choice in 2006.
