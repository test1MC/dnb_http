Got a gadget? Well, Belkin’s got a product to cover it, connect it or charge it. The company makes a variety of products for mobile devices made by Apple, Samsung, and LG as well as eReaders, laptops, netbooks, and TVs. A leading manufacturer of Universal Serial Bus (USB) devices, such as networking hubs, Belkin also provides media player accessories, surge protectors, and battery backups. Belkin owns router maker Linksys and WeMo, a maker of smart home devices. For commercial and government customers, Belkin provides security, infrastructure, and server-room products that include Keyboard-Video-Mouse (KVM) switches, rackmount consoles, and converters and adapters.

California-based Belkin operates in more than two dozen countries around the world.

Belkin’s products are available via online and brick-and-mortar retailers such as Amazon, Newegg, Best Buy, Staples, and Target. The company also works with Dell, CDW, Graybar, Ingram Micro, as well as distributors such as Anixter, Graybar, and Brightstar.

From covers and cables, Belkin has expanded to sell connections with its Linksys and WeMo brands. The WeMo smart home devices have benefited from the wave of digital assistants from Google and Amazon that have taken up residence in households. Consumers can control home functions by speaking to WeMo devices through the digital assistants.
