Jubilant Organosys is more than a mouthful. It's one of India's leading drug development and custom research and manufacturing services providers. The integrated pharmaceutical company serves other global pharmaceutical companies with products and services ranging from active pharmaceutical ingredients and generic dosage forms to clinical research and medicinal chemistry services. Customers include AstraZeneca and Orion . Jubilant announced in 2009 it would demerge its other business segment -- which focuses on agricultural and performance products, such as adhesives, fertilizers, food polymers, and latex -- to allow each to pursue continued growth independently.
