Ampro Computers may be high-tech, but it's no stranger to factory floors and other rugged environments. The company makes single-board computers and computer-on-module products, specializing in embedded applications in industrial automation. Its products are well-suited for the harsh environments of factories and other places where computer gear is expected to keep working despite the debilitating effects of dust, heat, and vibrations. Ampro supports various computer operating systems, including Linux, QNX Neutrino, VxWorks, and Windows. The company was acquired by ADLINK Technology in 2008.
