Richard Burton acted, Lance Burton makes magic, Tim Burton makes movies, and this Burton makes medical equipment. Located southeast of London, Burtons Medical Equipment manufactures, distributes, services, and repairs a wide range of products for medical, dental, and veterinary health care providers. The company specializes in autoclaves and gas regulators as well as ultrasonic baths for cleaning instruments. Burtons' extensive veterinary product catalog includes equipment for anesthesia and sterilization, patient monitors, kennels, surgery tables, scales, and more. Burtons Medical Equipment is owned and managed by David Burton.

The company operates five divisions: Medical, Veterinary, Grooming, Vehicle-Systems, K9 Cages, and Manufacturing.

Burtons Medical designs, makes and services a wide range of leading infection control products, including the 'Instaclave' sterilizer range, 'Instawash' washer disinfectors, and a reverse osmosis systems.

Its veterinary equipment and services cover everything from anaesthesia to X-ray.

Grooming equipment and services cover everything that a grooming saloon needs.

The company's K9 Cages offers a range of services, from simple vehicle conversions to animal ambulances and mobile practices.

Where it is able, Burtons Medical Equipment oversees UK-based in-house manufacturing processes, from raw materials sourcing to finished products.

Burtons Medical Equipment was founded in 1981.

Since its founding, the company has grown organically through ongoing investments in developing and creating new and sustainable products. It has also acquired complementary businesses including Bowmed Limited (vaporizers; 1994); the sterilizer services division of Goldsworth Engineering Ltd. (bench top autoclaves; 1998); and K9 Cages in 2011.
