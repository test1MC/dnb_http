Jungbunzlauer is an old company that caters to some of mankind's oldest needs: food and medicine. Tracing its roots back to 1867, Jungbunzlauer is a food additive and ingredient producer that serves food and beverage, pharmaceutical, cosmetic, and industrial companies around the globe. The company specializes in natural and biodegradable ingredients, including citric acid (used for pH-control in food and drugs), xanthan gum (thickener and stabilizer in food and other products), gluconates (plasticizing agents), sweeteners, and salts. Jungbunzlauer operates four manufacturing sites in Austria, Canada, France, and Germany, as well as a global network of regional offices that serve clients in 130 countries.
