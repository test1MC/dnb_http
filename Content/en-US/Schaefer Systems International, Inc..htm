Better Schaefer than sorry -- Schaefer Systems International knows how to keep warehouse and distribution centers on track. The company makes, distributes, and installs a range of equipment for heavy materials handling and transport, as well as waste handling. Products include systems for shelving, containers, and packaging, along with conveyor systems for storage and retrieval, high-density polyethylene pallets, plant and shop equipment, and waste and recycling systems. Schaefer Systems International is the North American subsidiary of Germany's Schaefer Holding International, which operates a dozen different steel and plastics manufacturing plants, and has subsidiaries in 22 countries worldwide.
