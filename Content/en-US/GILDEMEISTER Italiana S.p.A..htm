With a name that spins off tongues both German and Italian, GILDEMEISTER Italiana is a specialist maker of multi-spindle automatic lathes. The company also makes universal lathes, computer numerical control Swiss-type lathes, and other machine tools and accessories. It sells itself as being expert in "straight turning" technology, in which the length of the turned parts are quite a bit larger than the diameter. GILDEMEISTER Italiana has production facilities in Brembate di Sopra, Castelleone, and Tortona, Italy. Along with parent company DMG Mori (formerly GILDEMEISTER AG), the group aims to dominate the global lathe market.
