Endocare wants to end cancer and start something good. The business develops and markets medical devices used in the minimally invasive process known as cryoablation to treat cancer. Approved for tumor removal, the Cryocare surgical system freezes targeted cells, which are broken up and reabsorbed by the body. Endocare sells its products mainly to US urologists, radiologists, hospitals, and other health care providers; it also offers related clinical support and maintenance services. Its products are primarily used in renal and prostate cancer treatment. Endocare is a subsidiary of urological equipment firm HealthTronics , which is in turn a unit of drugmaker Endo International .
