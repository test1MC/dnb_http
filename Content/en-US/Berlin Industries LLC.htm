Postal carriers with sore backs can thank Berlin Industries. The commercial printing firm specializes in producing such items as catalogs, direct mailings, newsletters, digests, and advertising inserts. It specializes in quantities of 50,000 and above. In addition to printing, the company's offerings include database management and list services for direct mailings. Berlin Industries was founded as I.S. Berlin Press in 1919 as a family-run printing shop. Smith Investment Company (controlled by the family of CEO Bruce Smith) owns Berlin Industries.
