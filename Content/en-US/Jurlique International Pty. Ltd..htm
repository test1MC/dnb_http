You won't exactly find Jurlique's natural skin care products at the farmer's market. The company's line of premium skin care products are formulated with botanicals and plant extracts grown on its organic farm in Australia. Jurlique offers cleansers and creams for the face and body as well as aromatherapy oils. Its products are sold at specialty retailers in almost 20 countries; it also operates more than 50 standalone stores, most of which are located in the Asia/Pacific region. In the US its products are sold at Sephora and Whole Foods , and its three stores double as spas. Private equity firm JH Partners sold Jurlique to Japanese skin care giant Pola Orbis Holding for almost $300 million in February 2012.
