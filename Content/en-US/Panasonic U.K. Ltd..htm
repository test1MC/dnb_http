Panasonic U.K. sells electronics for consumers to use at home, at the office, or in transit. Panasonic U.K.'s products include such items as flat-panel TVs, DVD players including Blu-Ray, mobile phones, digital cameras, camcorders, dictation equipment, security systems, and commercial ovens. They're sold under the Panasonic and Technics brand names. Most of the high-end TVs (LCD, plasma, rear-projection) the company manufactures in the UK are integrated digital television sets. Panasonic U.K. operates several manufacturing plants. Formed in 1972, Panasonic U.K. is a subsidiary of Japan-based Panasonic Corporation .
