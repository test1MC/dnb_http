Just say cheese! Original Herkimer County Cheese Company combines local and family cheese-making traditions. The New York company, which does business as Herkimer Foods, manufactures specialty dairy products, including aged cheddar, cheese balls and logs, flavored spreads, dips, and blends. Herkimer Foods sells its products to retail delicatessens and grocery stores, including Giant , Kroger , Safeway , and Winn Dixie . It also has a corporate gifting program. Herkimer Foods has been in business since 1949 and is owned and operated by the founding Basloe family.
