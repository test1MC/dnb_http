There's a whole lotta shakin' goin' on at Scientific Industries. The company manufactures research laboratory equipment, featuring the Genie line of mixers, particularly the Vortex-Genie mixer, as well as orbital, magnetic, and microplate mixers and refrigerated and shaking incubators. The company's products are generally used by clinics, hospitals, and universities, among other customers. In business for more than five decades, Scientific Industries has distributors worldwide. The company also carries spinner flasks made by Bellco Biotechnology. Scientific Industries gets more than half of its sales outside the US.
