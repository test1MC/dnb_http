Even if they may prefer the left side of the road, Rockwell Automation Ltd. allows industries to go on cruise control, worry free. The company serves as the UK arm of US-based Rockwell Automation .  Among its operations in the UK is drives, software, and control equipment for the UK water industry. For the UK power industry, Rockwell Automation provides service in reducing greenhouse gases. It expanded its global footprint into Asia and other regions with the 2011 acquisition of Lektronics, a UK-based industrial automation repairs and service provider.
