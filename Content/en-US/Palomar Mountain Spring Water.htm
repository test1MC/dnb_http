Palomar Mountain Spring Water bottles water from Palomar Mountain near San Diego and from springs in the northern and southern parts of California. The company ships its water in single-serving sizes and gallon containers. Palomar distributes its water to retail and club stores throughout California and specific locations in Arizona, Nevada, and Mexico. The company is owned by investor group Kizer & Walker.
