The sun doesn't set on EMRISE. Through its worldwide subsidiaries, the company makes electronic components and communications equipment for customers in the aerospace, military, and telecommunications industries. Its CXR Larus units produce network transmission and access equipment and a range of testing gear. EMRISE's EEC Corporation subsidiary manufactures power converters, digital and rotary switches, and subsystem assemblies. The company counts BAE SYSTEMS , EMS Technologies , Harris , ITT , Raytheon , Rockwell Collins , and Thales Air Defence among its top clients. EMRISE gets more than 80% of sales outside the US, principally in the UK.
