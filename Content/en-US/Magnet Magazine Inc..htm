Magnet Magazine publishes MAGNET , a bi-monthly, internationally distributed, glossy music magazine that focuses on indie rock and other alternative music. The magazine is sold through subscriptions, as well as in independent bookstores and chains such as Barnes & Noble , Borders , MTS 's Tower Records and Hastings . Magnet Magazine was founded in 1993 and is owned by editor and publisher Eric T. Miller.
