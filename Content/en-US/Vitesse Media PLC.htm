Brits who want to "XL" in business should get to know Vitesse Media. The company publishees Business XL , a magazine that targets CEOs of growing companies. Vitesse Media also publishes other UK magazines and websites for entrepreneurs and investors. Its Growth Company Investor focuses on fast-growing public companies, while Information Age is a monthly technology magazine and M&A magazine provides information on mergers, acquisitions, and IPOs. Vitesse Media's websites target investors and small  and growing businesses. The company also sponsors and produces events, including business awards, seminars, and exhibitions. Vitesse Media reaches a monthly audience of approximately 1.1 million people.
