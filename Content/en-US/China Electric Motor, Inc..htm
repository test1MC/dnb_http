This company's motto should be "Good things come in small packages." China Electric Motor, Inc. manufactures micro-motors, which are small direct current (DC) and alternating current (AC) motors used in a variety of applications for household appliances (hair dryers and kitchen gadgets); automobiles (power seats, windows, locks, and roofs); and other consumer electronics (power tools and toys). The motors are sold to original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) and distributors in China, Hong Kong, and Korea under the brand name Sunna. China Electric Motor operates through subsidiary Shenzhen YuePengCheng (Shenzhen YPC). The company went public in the US in early 2010.
