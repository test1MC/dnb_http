Claverham Limited makes the gizmos (actuators) that make aerospace and industrial components change position on command. Aerospace and defense actuator applications include air brakes and landing gear. The company is owned by industrial diversified giant United Technologies . Customers have included Boeing , Curtiss-Wright , and Eurocopter /HAIG. Claverham's actuators utilize electro-hydraulic, electro-mechanical, hydraulic, and hybrid technologies for use in units that include military and commercial helicopters, military fast jets, unmanned aerial vehicles, and military turboprop trainer aircraft.
