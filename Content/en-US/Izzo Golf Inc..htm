This company has more fun developing golf accessories than it does playing the game. Izzo Golf sells its golf club bags and clubs through golfing retail outlets worldwide. The company's carry and cart golf bags are sold under names such as Legacy, Buddha, and Fat Daddy. It also makes straps, travel trunks, shoe caddies, and training aids (practice nets and putting mats). Izzo's flagship accessory brand is Gold Eagle, but it also boasts a licensing agreement with Top-Flite Golf , under which it makes golf bags and accessories for the Top-Flite brand in North America. CEO Bruce Dan purchased the company from founder T. J. Izzo (inventor of the dual-strap carry system) in 2000.
