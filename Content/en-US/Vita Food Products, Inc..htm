Vita Food Products won't lead you astray with a red herring -- their herring are properly pickled. Under the Vita and Elf brand names, the company produces refrigerated and frozen kosher salmon products (lox, smoked, cured) and pickled herring in various sauces. It markets salad dressings and specialty condiments (sauces, seasonings, syrups, jellies) under license for Jim Beam , Budweiser , Dr Pepper , 7UP , and Jelly Belly . Vita is also home to Scorned Woman Hot Sauce. The company's products are distributed to US supermarkets, wholesale clubs, and foodservice providers, and they are sold online. Vita Food went private in 2009 when board member and stockholder Howard E. Bedford bought the company.
