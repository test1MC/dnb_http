No habitats have been harmed in the making of Natura Cosméticos' products since its founding in 1969. The socially and environmentally conscious Brazilian cosmetic giant manufactures more than 600 products using sustainable extraction, carbon neutral processes, and recycled and recyclable packaging and materials. Its product lines range from make-up to fragrances and skin cleansers, moisturizers, sunscreen, shampoos, and conditioners for men, women, and children. Brands, including Natura Chronos, Ekos, Tododia, and Una, are sold through a direct sales force of more than 1.4 million consultants, primarily in Brazil, and less so, Argentina, Chile, Columbia, Mexico, and Peru, and local distributors elsewhere.
