Spin Master is a master of child's play. The company offers a veritable Santa's workshop of toys, from radio-controlled airplanes to teenage dolls to toys that morph for Bakugan Ultimate Battle Tours. It also sells food-making toys branded by the likes of McDonald's , Hershey , and ICEE, and other toys and activity kits bearing the names of characters from The Simpsons and Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles . Its Marshmallow unit makes children's room decor including pillows, headboards, and storage. Spin Master's lineup is sold in major toy and department stores in more than 50 countries. Chairman and co-CEO Ronnen Harary, president and co-CEO Anton Rabie, and EVP Ben Varadi own the company, which they founded in 1994.
