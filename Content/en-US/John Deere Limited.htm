Reaping, sowing, and mowing are all in a day's work for John Deere Limited. The company is the sales and marketing branch in the UK and Ireland for agricultural and construction equipment maker Deere & Company . Its mechanical parts are divided among six segments, agriculture, residential, commercial, golf, forestry, and engines and drivetrain. Services and support include emissions information, remanufactured warranties, and PowerGard maintenance coverage. Established in the UK in 1966, John Deere Limited also offers financing for equipment needed for agriculture, commercial, forestry, and golf-course uses.
