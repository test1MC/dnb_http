Terrapin International can put up a building lickety-split. Founded in 1949, the company designs, manufactures, and constructs temporary and permanent prefabricated buildings throughout the UK, mainly through its Terrapin Limited subsidiary. Its buildings are used for retail space, showrooms, offices, laboratories, conference facilities, training rooms, prisons, and schools, in addition to apartments, social housing, army barracks, and hotels. The company also makes and instsalls bathroom pods. It serves clients in the education, health care, and government sectors, among others. Terrapin offers its products for sale and lease and offers financing programs, as well.
