Industries Qatar (or IQ for short) has a head for business in its home country and beyond. As one of Qatar's largest companies, it has holdings in a handful of businesses, including wholly-owned subsidiary Qatar Steel, a producer of steel products, and such joint ventures as: QAPCO, a petrochemical products producer; QAFAC, a fuel additive products maker; QAFCO, which makes fertilizer products; and Fereej Real Estate Company, which deals in real estate investment and development. IQ boasts production facilities in Qatar and the United Arab Emirates. Its products are sold in markets worldwide, including the US, Australia, and India. The company is controlled by Qatar Petroleum.
