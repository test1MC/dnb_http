Maquet strives to ensure optimal surgeon performance. Maquet makes what could be some of the most crucial modern components of hospital operating theaters and post-op care facilities. The company is organized into three divisions: surgical workplaces (operating tables, surgical lights, ceiling service lights, and prefabricated operating and intensive care suites); cardiovascular (beating heart positioners, pumps, oxygenators, and grafts for open-heart and vascular surgeries); and critical care (anesthesia and ventilation systems for the operating theater and intensive care). Additionally the company provides  software, disposables, and maintenance services. Maquet is a subsidiary of Getinge AB .
