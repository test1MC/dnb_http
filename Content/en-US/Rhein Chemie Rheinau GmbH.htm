Denial ain't just a river in Egypt, and Rhein adds up to more than some flowing water in Germany. Rhein Chemie manufactures chemical additives for rubber, lubricants, plastics, and polyurethane from its home base in Germany and half a dozen other locations in Europe, the Americas, and Asia. Its rubber chemicals go into tires, wire and cable products, and belts and hoses. Rhein Chemie's lubricants make metalworking fluids, greases, and turbine and compressor oils; its engineering plastics include polycarbonates and polyurethanes. In 2011, it grew significantly by buying the tire release agents business of Wacker Chemie AG . The company is owned by LANXESS , and operates as Rhein Chemie Corporation in the US.
