Allergan Limited's mission is to improve vision and visage in the UK. The company is the UK subsidiary of Irish pharmaceutical giant Allergan plc . Like its parent, the company is focused on eye and skin care medications and neurological disorder treatments. Products available in the UK include eye twitching and wrinkle removing drug Botox, glaucoma treatment Lumigan, the Naturelle line of breast augmentation products, and the Surgiderm dermal facial filler product. The company is also the headquarters of its parent's research and development operations; its efforts target areas including retinal disease, skin disorders, and pain. The parent company was reverse-acquired by Actavis plc in 2015; Actavis assumed the Allergan name.

The company is based in Buckinghamshire, UK.

European sales account for about 20% of the parent company's sales. Allergan Limited employs a direct sales force; it also markets products through partnering drug companies and wholesalers. The firm targets specialty physicians, medical centers, and consumers with its promotion efforts.
