Spectrum Laboratories is wrapped up in a full gamut of filtration products. Spectrum's membranes and filters are used by pharmaceutical, diagnostic, industrial, and food manufacturers to filter, isolate, purify, and concentrate fluids. The company also offers customized filtration and separation products for original equipment manufacturers (OEMs). Other products include laboratory supplies (dialysis tubing and purification devices, chromatography instruments) and disposable operating room products (orthopedic and arthroscopic drapes, sterile microscope covers, tissue carriers, and other disposables). Spectrum also does contract manufacturing to create customized products with filtration applications for laboratory use.

Spectrum has three production facilities in the US (in Dallas, Houston, and Rancho Dominguez, California) along with sales offices in the Netherlands (Breda) and Japan (Otsu-City, Shiga). The company sells its products in about 45 countries around the globe.

Spectrum has more than 2,000 products, ranging from small disposable membrane modules to high volume systems. its products are used for the filtration, isolation, purification and concentration of pharmaceuticals, diagnostics, food, beverages and industrial fluids. Its membranes and membrane modules are used for macrofiltration, microfiltration, ultrafiltration, diafiltration, dialysis and extraction.

The company makes separation products for R&D scale through pilot and production scale biotech and pharmaceutical applications. Spectrum's hollow fiber filters for Tangential Flow Filtration are used in a wide range of ultrafiltration and microfiltration applications including cell, protein, virus and nanoparticle concentration, clarification and diafiltration. Spectrum also makes the world's largest selection of laboratory dialysis tubing and ready to use dialysis devices for protein purification, buffer exchange and electro-elution. Its chromatography products include a fraction collector and an ultra-violet light detector used in liquid chromatography applications.

The company's customers include hospitals and universities as well as pharmaceutical, industrial, and biotechnology companies. Spectrum distributes products through international wholesalers and through partnerships with entities including Mark Medical and DivBio Science. It also sells its products online through Spectrumlabs.com.

Spectrum invests in R&D programs to develop advanced separation technologies. It has teams dedicated to membrane separation products and to OEM customization projects.
