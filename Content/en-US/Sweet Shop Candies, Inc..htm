Take a toothbrush on this company tour. Sweet Shop USA (formerly DFG Confectionery) produces more than 100 varieties of gourmet sweets. The company offers handmade chocolates, fudge, truffles, and toffee under the Price's Fine Chocolates, Mrs. Weinstein's Gourmet Toffee, and Sweet Shop brand names. Sweet Shop's confections are available at candy and gift shops, boutiques, floral, and gourmet food retailers throughout the US. Its some 8,000 customers include Whole Foods , Neiman Marcus , Dillard's , Lord & Taylor , Hallmark Cards , and various Federated stores.
