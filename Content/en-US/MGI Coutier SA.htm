MGI Coutier would like to show you the door -- the car door, that is. The company manufactures auto parts, primarily for French automakers. Rival French carmakers Renault and Peugeot S.A. are the company's largest customers. MGI Coutier's plastic and metal products include window and door systems; parts for powertrain, fuel-injection, and ventilation systems; and mechatronics (products with both mechanical and electrical components). MGI Coutier also offers distribution and other services. The Coutier family controls about two-thirds of the company.
