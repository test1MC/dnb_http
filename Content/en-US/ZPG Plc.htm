ZPG (formerly Zoopla Property Group) operates a website at Zoopla.co.uk that provides information about local areas in the UK. The company combines hundreds of thousands of property listings (for sale and rent) with market data, local information, and community tools. Its online resources target property consumers, while its advertising products target UK estate agents, letting agents, and property developers. ZPG was founded in 2007 by Alex Chesterman and taken public in 2014.
