Plainville Farms (formerly Plainville Turkey Farm) is a leading US producer of turkey and turkey products. Its antibiotic-free, vegetarian-fed turkey products, available under the Heart Lite and Veggie Grown labels, include deli and fresh turkey items. Plainville also offers turkey go-withs, including turkey gravey and homestyle dressing.The company's meats are sold in supermarkets and natural food stores throughout the US. Feed for the turkeys is grown on the company's farm, which uses wind power to run its operations. Plainville became part of organic chicken producer, Hain Pure Protein, in 2007.
