No matter where you stand, Sabine will make sure the audience can hear you. The company makes professional audio systems, including wireless microphone systems, feedback exterminators, tuners, and signal processors, for the sound industry. It sells them through worldwide distributors. Customers vary from musicians (Waylon Jennings' tour sound system) to universities (Michigan State) and sports venues (2000 Sydney Olympic Games). Even the Vatican uses a Sabine system. President and CEO Doran Oster founded the company in 1971 as a guitar repair and flute manufacturing shop headquartered in Gainesville, Florida. The company has since moved to Alachua, Florida.
