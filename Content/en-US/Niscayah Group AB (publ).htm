Niscayah Group designed and installed security systems for large and midsized companies. Its products included video surveillance, access control, burglary intrusion, and fire alarm systems. Niscayah's customers operated mainly in the high security risk banking, retail, and industrial sectors. The company had operations in more than 15 European countries, as well as Australia, Hong Kong, and the US. Stanley Black & Decker acquired Niscayah in September 2011 for about $1.2 billion.
