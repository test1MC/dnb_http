For a label dedicated to the preservation of acoustic music, Acoustic Disc Records isn't shy about adopting technology to record, distribute, and sell its wares. Acoustic Disc Records releases acoustic bluegrass, classical, Latin, jazz, and folk recordings by artists such as Tony Rice, John Hartford, and David Grisman. The record label was founded in 1990 by Grisman, a mandolinist, composer, bandleader, and producer. Acoustic Disc has released some 60 productions -- five of which have received Grammy Award nominations. The company's Web site offers a streaming audio feature which allows users to listen to songs from every company release in additon to selling digital downloads, CD's and other merchandise.
