Verbatim encourages you to get it on tape, or the current equivalent. The company makes recordable digital data storage media including CDs, DVDs, floppy disks, memory cards, and tapes. Its DVD line includes standard recordable and rewritable disks, as well as high-definition disks in both Blu-ray and HD DVD formats. Other products include USB flash drives and hubs, mice, printer supplies, and media storage cases and labeling kits. The company's products are distributed worldwide. Verbatim is a subsidiary of Mitsubishi Chemical Corporation . The company was founded in 1969.
