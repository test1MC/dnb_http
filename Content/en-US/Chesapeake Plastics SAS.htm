Hollywood glamour aside, Chesapeake Plastics is in love with plastic! The company does business as Chesapeake Specialty Chemical Packaging, designing and manufacturing high-density polyethylene (HDPE) and polyethylene terephthalate (PET) plastic bottles and containers. Its lineup, from 100 ML to 30 liters, in various shapes and colors, packages products for agro-chemical, industrial (cleansers and adhesives), inks and coatings, and flavorings and fragrance markets. Multinational and specialized customers include Abbott Laboratories , A.G. Barr , The Coca-Cola Company , Dow AgroSciences , Eastman Kodak Company , Glanbia , and Syngenta . The company is part of the UK-based consumer packaging giant Chesapeake Ltd.
