Noritsu Koki helps make sure your photo lab can process your photos in a flash. The company manufactures and sells photo processing equipment, primarily minilabs, as well as film processors, editing and manipulating software, image archiving equipment, digital dry and wide-format printers, and other photo-imaging and printing-related equipment. The company also sells optional and spare parts for the equipment it manufactures. Noritsu operates through about two dozen subsidiaries and associated companies, and markets its products in more than 180 countries. Sales to Walgreen accounted for more than 13% of the company's 2007 revenues.
