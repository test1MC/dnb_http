Microvision thinks tiny images have big potential. The company's PicoP display technology can be used to create high-quality video and image displays using an ultra-miniature projector that is embedded into mobile devices such as cell phones, DVD players, gaming devices, and laptops. The projector enables users to display images and data onto a variety of surfaces from mobile products. Microvision's first product -- the SHOWWX accessory projector -- connects via cable to a video-out connection on a mobile device. It is sold directly and through distributors in Asia and Europe. The company also produces prototypes based on its light scanning technology under government and commercial development contracts.
