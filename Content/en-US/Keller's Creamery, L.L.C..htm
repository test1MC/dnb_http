How can butter be more buttery? Keller's Creamery knows it's a matter of more butter -fat. Many of its products contain a higher butter-fat content than other butters. The company whips up salted and unsalted butter and butter products for retail, foodservice, and industrial customers. In addition to the familiar sticks and tubs of butter, Keller's also offers butter sculptures for Thanksgiving, Christmas, and Easter. It also offers regional and national premium brands such as Borden, Breakstone's, Falfurrias, Hotel Bar, Mid-America Farms, and Plugrá. Keller's also offers private-label manufacturing services. Founded as Branch Valley Creamery in 1906, it became a subsidiary of Dairy Farmers of America in 2005.
