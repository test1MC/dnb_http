MonoSol Rx helps the medicine go down with its thin film pharmaceutical products. The company develops thin film versions (about the size of a postage stamp) of new and existing prescription and over-the-counter drugs for colds, nausea, pain, and other ailments. MonoSol creates its thin film products, which are easier to take than tablets or capsules, primarily through partnerships with other pharmaceutical companies. Its portfolio of commercialized products includes Zuplenz (for chemotherapy-induced and post-operative nausea and vomiting) and Suboxone (for opioid dependence). The company focuses on creating products for the US, Europe, and Asia/Pacific markets.
