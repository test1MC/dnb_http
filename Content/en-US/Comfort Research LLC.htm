Researching comfort may sound like a pretty cushy job, but Comfort Research founders Matt Jung and Chip George know it actually requires a good deal of hard work. Their company, founded in 1998, manufactures foam chairs, pillows, and cushions, in addition to making traditional bean bag furniture. The company's products are available in sports, fruit, celestial, and outdoor styles. The executive duo created FUF Chairs (bean bag-inspired seating) while still in college and now distribute the chairs through specialty furniture and department stores nationwide. It has expanded its products portfolio to include environmentally friendly bean bag chairs named Green Beans.
