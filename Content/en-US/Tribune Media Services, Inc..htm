At the nexus of big data and entertainment you'll find Tribune Media Services (TMS). The company provides entertainment and marketing data to about 4,000 customers worldwide. Its On Entertainment line serves up the info for entertainment guides and applications to TiVo , Virgin Media , Roku , DIRECTV , and Time Warner Cable ; the companies also receive data for measuring and analysis. TMS' Zap2it online hub connects fans with TV shows, movies, and celebrities. Its News and Features group syndicates comics, articles, games and puzzles, and opinion pieces as well as news and infographics through McClatchy-Tribune Information Services. TMS is part of Tribune Media .
