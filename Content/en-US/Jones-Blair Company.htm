Jones-Blair makes coatings in the US and Canada. The integrated research, manufacturing, and marketing company produces coatings and epoxies for industrial, construction, and other customers. The privately owned company's high-performance industrial coatings division makes acrylic-urethane, alkyd, and acrylic coatings for decorative and protective applications, including automotive manufacturing, oil-field service, imaging, signs, and woodworking. Jones-Blair's Neogard division produces elastomeric coatings (waterproofing and roof and wall coating systems, and seamless epoxy flooring) used in construction.

The company has US locations in Louisiana, Tennessee, and Texas, and a Canadian service center in Alberta.

Jones-Blair serves customers in a range of industries, including automotive parts, coil coating, maintenance, off-road equipment, oilfield services, recreation, service station imaging, storefronts and signs, and transportation.

The company sells through distributors in US, Canada, Mexico, and Singapore, including Howard Supply, Katalyst Coatings, and NCH .

Jones-Blair was founded in 1928.
