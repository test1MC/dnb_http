Makino Milling Machine manufactures and sells machine tools, such as numerical control electrical discharge machines, milling machines, machining centers, and tool grinding machines. The company's primary market focus is in mold dies for plastic, die cast aluminum, and rubber, with plans to expand to press dies. The company provides its advanced machining technology to global customers from service centers and manufacturing facilities located around the world; more than 40% of its sales are made in Japan. Makino Milling Machine was founded in 1937 by Tsunezo Makino. The company is still led by the Makino family.
