Stanley Security Solutions is putting burglars out of business. The company, a subsidiary of hardware maker Stanley Black & Decker , provides security, communications, and time and attendance products and services. Its security items include closed-circuit TV, access control, parking management, and fire detection. Its communications operations, mostly for hospital use, extend to nurse notification, patient monitoring, and paging systems, and also includes fire alarm systems for the deaf. Stanley Security Solutions offers timeclocks and payroll systems through its time and attendance division. It also installs community-wide satellite systems for digital TV reception.

The company has offices across Europe.

The sectors that the company serves are education, finance & banking, healthcare, hotels and leisure, biotech & pharmaceutical, commercial buildings, retail, small & medium companies, transport & logistics, ports & airports, government & local authority, industrial, and residential.

The majority of the company's sales come via retailers, which includes home centers, mass merchants, hardware stores, and retail lumber yards.

The company's growth strategy includes organic expansion and new products. Organic sales in Europe increased 9% year on year, and volumes increased 2%, due to new product introductions and an expanding retail footprint.
