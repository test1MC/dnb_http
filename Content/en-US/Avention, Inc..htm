Avention (formerly known as OneSource) serves up business information, offering data on millions of companies and executives worldwide. Avention aggregates company, industry, and financial information from a variety of sources, including news articles, websites, and regulatory filings. The company's products and solutions target sales, marketing, and research professionals. Subscribers access the information through Avention's software or the data can be integrated into a customer's enterprise information system or CRM. In early 2017, Avention was purchased by Dun & Bradstreet for $150 million.

In early 2017, Avention was purchased by Dun & Bradstreet for $150 million. The deal enhanced Dun & Bradstreet's Sales Acceleration operations, which provides sales and marketing professionals with actionable market and company information.

Avention has offices in North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific.

The company currently has more than 4,000 customers worldwide. It markets its products through a direct sales force.
