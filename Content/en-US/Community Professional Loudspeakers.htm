Even the people in the back row can hear, thanks to Community Professional Loudspeakers. The company makes loudspeakers and accessories for indoor and outdoor venues. It manufactures its products under brand names iBOX, CLOUD, ENTASYS, VERIS, Wet Series II, and others. Community's speakers are sold worldwide, and can be heard at places like Walt Disney World , the Mirage Casino , Warner Brothers Film Studios , minor league baseball franchises like the Traverse City Beach Bums, and even the White House. It was founded as Community Light & Sound in 1968.
