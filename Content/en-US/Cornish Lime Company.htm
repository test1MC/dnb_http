Cornish Lime's products can be found in structures throughout the UK. The company manufacturers lime-based products such as high calcium mature lime putty (guaranteed to be at least four months old), lime mortars, lime plasters, and lime washes. All of these products are made in Cornwall using high quality Derbyshire limestone. The firm serves as the southwest regional distributor for the St. Astier range of pure natural hydraulic limes. Cornish Lime supplements its line of lime products with an array of sands, decorative aggregates, chippings, natural and mineral paints, cements, landscaping stones, and tools.

The company manufactures lime products including lime putty, ready-mixed non-hydraulic mortars and lime washes, as well as a range of Cornerstone Drymix Mortars. Cornish Lime also stocks blended sands and decorative aggregates and chippings from across the country.

Cornish Lime is based in Cornwall.

Cornish Lime serves as the regional distributor for St. Astier Natural Hydraulic Limes, and the national distributor for Prompt Natural Cement and Beeck Mineral Paints.

Managing director Phil Brown started the company in 1992 to service his property restoration firm, Darrock & Brown Building Conservation.
