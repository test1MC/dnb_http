MTM Recognition Corporation wants to be sure hard work is celebrated in a memorable way. The company creates and implements employee recognition plans that celebrate achievement, reward performance, honor service, and reinforce company image. MTM Recognition manufactures a variety of awards and recognition trophies, plaques, pins, custom castings, medallions, and watches. About 80% of sales are generated through MTM's corporate recognition business, but the company also provides awards for the Super Bowl, Olympics, World Series, and NCAA Championships. Clients have included Adobe , Farmer's Insurance , O'Charley's , and Westar Energy . MTM, formerly named Midwest Trophy, was founded in 1971 by CEO David Smith.

MTM is headquartered in Oklahoma with manufacturing facilities there and in Nebraska and Illinois.
