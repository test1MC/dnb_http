Nanotechnology meets microfluidics in the pursuit of biotechnology at GeneFluidics. The company is developing technology to find and identify DNA and RNA, proteins, and small molecules without the use of amplification methods, reducing the time and cost of research. Some of the markets it serves include clinical diagnostics, animal testing, environmental monitoring, and biothreat detection.
