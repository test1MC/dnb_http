Eaton Metal Products is into heavy metal. The company is an engineering and fabricator firm specializing in pressure vessels, storage tanks, pilings, bulk storage silos, towers, and columns and reactors. Materials used include carbon, low alloy, and stainless steel. The company's three manufacturing facilities, all of which are serviced by the Union Pacific Railroad , are located in Colorado, Utah, and Idaho. Eaton Metal Products is capable of building mammoth pressure vessels up to 20 feet in diameter, 200 feet long, and weighing up to 550 tons. The company's products serve customers in a range of agricultural and industrial applications. The company began in 1880 in Arizona.
