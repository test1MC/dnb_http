Indage Vintners (formerly Champagne Indage) is one of India's top wine makers and suppliers. With some 32 labels, it produces a variety of red, white, and sparkling wines, which it exports throughout the world. Domestically, the company operates vineyards in the Narayangaon region that produce the Chateau Indage and Indage Vineyards brands. Top sellers include Riviera, Figueira, and Chantilli. Its international operations include the Thachi Vineyards in Australia. Subsidiary Indage (UK) imports wines from around the world and distributes them in the UK to major supermarkets, such as J Sainsbury , and other retailers. Indage Vintners is controlled by chairman Sham Chougule and his family.
