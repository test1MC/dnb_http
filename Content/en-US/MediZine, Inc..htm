Chockfull of health information, MediZine's publications are found in drug stores and doctors' offices across the country, reaching more than 100 million readers. The company's flagship publication is MediZine Healthy Living , a magazine that provides expert news, information, and advice on staying healthy. The title is targeted at readers over age 40. Other MediZine titles include REMEDY and Diabetes Focus . Its REMEDYMD education program delivers information to patients that provides explanations on how to manage health conditions. The company also runs a medical information website RemedyLife.com. MediZine was founded in 1994.
