Magnell Associate, dba ABS Computer Technologies, designs and builds made-to-order personal computers. Based on processors from firms including AMD and Intel , its N-Series and Commander X desktop systems can be customized for gaming, multimedia, or professional applications. ABS has grown into one of the largest custom specialty PC suppliers. The company also resells third-party software and accessories. ABS was established in 1990 by Fred Chang, who then went on to establish Newegg.com , the popular Web portal for electronics. ABS now operates as a division of the younger venture.
