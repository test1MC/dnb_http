Southeastern Metals Manufacturing Company (SEMCO) doesn't mind if you think its mind is in the gutter. The company, founded in 1963 and a subsidiary of Gibraltar Industries since 1997, makes more than 3,000 metal building products, including gutters, roofs, frames, corner beads, storm panels, support wire, trim, vents, and concrete and masonry hardware. Its products are engineered and manufactured from steel, aluminum, copper, and galvanized materials. SEMCO has a Florida manufacturing plant and sells its merchandise through a distribution network of more than 1,900 locations primarily in the Southeastern US.
