One assumes that there are always enough people to have a productive, well-lit meeting at Quorum International. Quorum International makes decorative residential-lighting fixtures and ceiling fans. The company, which claims the position of being the world's largest fan and lighting fixture maker, also makes ceiling mounts, chandeliers, entry lighting, pendants, outdoor lighting, track and recessed fixtures, vanity lighting fixtures, wall mounts, and sconces. Additionally, it manufactures door chimes and lampshades. Quorum International markets its products throughout the US as well as Central America and the Caribbean. The company is owned by Fort Worth-based Davoil, Inc.
