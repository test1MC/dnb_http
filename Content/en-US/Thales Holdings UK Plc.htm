Thales Holdings UK (aka Thales UK) is the British division of French defense giant Thales SA . Thales UK serves as a defense contractor for military and civilian commercial markets. Operations include Aerospace (air training and flight simulators, air traffic management systems, avionics and sensors, unmanned aerial vehicles, airline services, satellite communications), Defense (vehicle, soldier, missile, electronics, naval, and under- and above water systems, warship design), and Security (for finance, large enterprises, energy facilities, airports, among other institutions). Customers have included Boeing , Dassault Aviation , the US National Security Agency , and the UK Ministry of Defense.

Thales UK offers transportation systems, including revenue collection systems, integrated communication and supervision systems, rail signaling for main lines, and rail signaling.

The company offers equipment, systems, and services to manage critical information for the defense and security sectors. Clients include Transport for London , Network Rail , London Underground , DLR, Transport for Greater Manchester, and many other train operating companies.
