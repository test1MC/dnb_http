Global Motorsport Group (GMG) is now known as DAE-IL USA, after it was acquired in March 2008. Through its Custom Chrome division, DAE-IL USA is one of the world's largest independent providers of aftermarket parts and accessories for Harley-Davidson motorcycles. It sells parts via catalogs and a global network of motorcycle dealers and repair shops. Products, including transmission assemblies and gear sets, wheels and tires, engines, frames, and headlight kits, are sold under brand name RevTech, Santee, Highway One, MotorFactory, and Jammer Cycle Products. GMG filed Chapter 11 in early 2008. DAE-IL is a subsidiary of South Korea-based DAE-IL Corporation, a maker of vehicle and motorcycle parts.
