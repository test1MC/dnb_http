In the UK, the number to call for the 411 is actually 118 118. The Number UK Limited is the country's largest directory-assistance service, providing a range of information including private and business telephone listings, movie listings, train schedules (with directions to the closest station), and directions to various other locations. Its 1818 Answers services fields questions to queries on a variety of topics. The 118 118 service is available via phone, text messaging, or the company's website. The company charges a fee per minute on the phone, and one flat fee per text. It is free to search the 118.com website. The company, founded in 2003, is a subsidiary of the US-based directory-assistance company kgb .
