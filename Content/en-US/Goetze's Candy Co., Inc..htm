It might sound gross, but you really can eat a Cow Tale. Goetze's Candy makes confections including Caramel Creams and Cow Tales (cream-filled caramel strips). The company's candy products come in vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry variations. Goetze's Candy sells its confections through retail venues throughout the US, as well as online. Goetz's customers include Kmart , Wal-Mart , Costco , BJ's Wholesale Club , CVS Drug , Walgreen's , and Target . The family-owned and -operated company was founded when August Goetze purchased Baltimore Chewing Gum Company in 1895. Its factory has been located in Baltimore, Maryland since 1895 and is in its 5th generation of operation and ownership.
