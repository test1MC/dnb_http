For people in business, any time is the right time for this newspaper. Financial Times Group is a leading international business newspaper publisher anchored by its well-regarded flagship title. The Financial Times is distributed in about 25 cities worldwide and claims a readership of about 2.1 million. In the UK and Ireland, the paper boasts a circulation of more than 100,000. Financial Times Group also distributes news online. In addition to its mainstay publications, the company owns a 50% stake in The Economist Group , which publishes the weekly magazine The Economist . Financial Times Group is a unit of publisher Pearson .
