Norstar Office Products, which also does business as Boss Office Products, has taken the chair in the workplace furniture market. The company specializes in the design and manufacturing of ergonomic office chairs and other seating products. Its products include drafting stools, medical stools, upholstered executive chairs, leather chairs, side chairs, task seating, reception furniture, workstations, conference tables, filing cabinets, and folding chairs and tables. Headquartered in California, Boss Office Products also operates a branch in Georgia.
