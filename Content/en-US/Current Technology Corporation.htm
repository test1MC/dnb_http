Current Technology Corporation aims to help users keep their current full head of hair. Current Technology develops and sells electrotherapeutic devices for the treatment of hair loss. The company's ElectroTrichoGenesis (ETG) device works via electrostatic charges; ETG treatments are not available in the US. Current Technology sells the CosmeticTrichoGenesis (CTG) device to spas and other markets in the US. The company also manufactures security detection systems through its SpyTell Security venture. It has entered the global positioning system (GPS) tracking market by purchasing a majority stake in telematics firm Celevoke.
