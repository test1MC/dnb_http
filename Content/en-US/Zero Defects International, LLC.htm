Zero Defects provides printed circuit board and backplane assembly test, diagnostic, and repair services and products. The company, which was founded in 1983, also offers software development and other support services. Customers include Cisco Systems , Flextronics , Honeywell , Jabil Circuit , LSI Corp. , Solectron , and Teradyne . Zero Defects filed for Chapter 11 protection from creditors in 2003 and its assets were sold to a partnership named Zero Defects International.
