Always pushing for the next technological breakthrough in textile production, Nextec Applications manufactures windproof, water resistant, and breathable fabrics used primarily in apparel, but also in vehicle covers, outdoor gear, dog beds, and medical applications. While other performance fabrics rely on coatings, laminates, or durable water repellent treatments to provide protection, the Nextec encapsulation process places the protection inside the fabric. This encapsulation process wraps an ultra thin polymer around individual woven fabric fibers. Nextec Applications' customers include Lands' End , Orvis , Patagonia , and Bibler Tents. Its fabrics are also used in making apparel for the military.
