Talon Therapeutics (formerly Hana Biosciences) has its claws sunk deeply into the fight against cancer. The development stage pharmaceutical company acquires and develops new drugs with a focus on cancer treatments. Specifically the company seeks to find treatments that kill tumors without damaging surrounding healthy tissue. Talon's lead product candidates include Marqibo, a unique formulation of an existing cancer drug targeting forms of leukemia and melanoma, and Menadione, a topical lotion meant to treat rashes caused by some cancer treatments. The company has other oncology pharmaceuticals in its pipeline, including therapies to treat solid tumor cancers (including lung and ovarian).
