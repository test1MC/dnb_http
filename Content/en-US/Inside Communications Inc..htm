Inside Communications targets athletes who want to go the distance. The company produces publications that promote outdoor sports. Its VeloNews: The Journal of Competitive Cycling covers all types of cycling (road racing, mountain-biking, exercise, fitness, and touring), while its Inside Triathalon covers the ins and outs of triathalon races.
