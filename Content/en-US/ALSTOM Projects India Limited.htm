If you're ALSTOM Projects India (APIL), geography has predetermined your life's course. Created by French energy giant ALSTOM , the India-based company is counted on to win business in developing markets. APIL's power operations, its largest segment, manufactures power generation equipment and constructs plants and add-ons, such as combined cycle, conventional steam, and hydro power plants, and nuclear conventional islands. A slate of services is also supplied by APIL power, from repair to on-site field tasks. APIL's transport segment produces and installs rail vehicles, along with network infrastructure. Four plants dot India, and marketing is run from APIL headquarters. ALSTOM is APIL's majority stakeholder.
