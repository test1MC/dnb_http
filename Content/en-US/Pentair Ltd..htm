Pentair is perpetually pumped about water. Valves & Controls manufactures water flow and filtration products for the residential and commercial construction projects and municipal markets. Pentair's Water Quality Systems segment segment makes custom enclosures that house and protect sensitive electronics as well as thermal management systems used in industrial, communications, electronics, and energy industries. Although incorporated in Ireland, the US is its largest market, generating about 50% of its net sales each year. In mid-2016, Pentair agreed to sell its Valves & Controls operations to Emerson Electric for $3.15 billion.

In early 2015 the company reorganized its business across three segments: Flow and Filtration Solutions, Water Quality Systems, and Valves and Controls.

The company has almost 90 plants, 60 distribution facilities, 130 sales offices, and 60 services centers. It reincorporated in Ireland in 2014 for tax and regulatory reasons.

Products are sold to a network of distributors, OEMs, water treatment facilities, retailers, and pool and electrical contractors.

Pentair's revenue increased in 2014 primarily due to increases from its former Process Technologies and Technical Solutions segments. Its net income has fluctuated 2012; it decreased in 2014, mainly due to a loss on a sales and the impairment of discontinued operations.

Growth in Technical Solutions segment climbed 24% due to increased volume in the US, China, and Canada. Process Technologies spiked by 26% due to higher sales of certain pool products serving North American residential housing and increased demand for global food and beverage products.

A key component to Pentair's growth strategy has involved getting its cost structure under control. During 2014, 2013 and 2012, it initiated and continued certain business restructuring initiatives aimed at reducing its fixed cost structure and realigning its business.

Pentair is also on the look-out for acquisitions in order to grow. In the summer of 2015, it agreed to purchase ERICO Global Company for $1.8 billion. Based in Solon, Ohio, ERICO is a global manufacturer and marketer of engineered electrical and fastening products for electrical, mechanical and civil applications. ERICO has a presence in 30 countries with recognized brands including CADDY fixing, fastening and support products; ERICO electrical grounding, bonding and connectivity products; and LENTON engineered systems.
