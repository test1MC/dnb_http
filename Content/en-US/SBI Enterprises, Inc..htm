Thank SBI Enterprises for that mobile trampoline, the pogo stick. The company manufactures and distributes pogo sticks. Its Master Pogo and Maverick Pogo brands are sold at Toys "R" Us stores across the country. Products are made of foam, wood, or metal. It also manufactures the Pogo Pony -- a pogo stick with a stuffed horse head on the handle bars.
