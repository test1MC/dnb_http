Squeaky clean is only the start of clean for MEDIVATORS. The company makes and markets a variety of sterilants and medical devices. Its kidney dialysis products include hemodialysis concentrates, dialyzer reprocessing systems, and hemodialysis blood pumps. MEDIVATORS also makes water filtration and disinfecting products used by drug companies, hemodialysis providers, and semiconductor makers, among others. Its namesake MEDIVATORS division makes reprocessing systems and chemicals that disinfect endoscopy equipment. A subsidiary of Cantel Medical , MEDIVATORS sells its products to hospitals, clinics, and kidney treatment centers through distributors worldwide.

The company counts several business groups among its operations. Aside from its largest Endoscopy Group, MEDIVATORS runs its business through three other groups: Renal Systems Group, Therapeutic Technologies Group, Infection Prevention and Control Chemistries Group.

MEDIVATORS' endoscopy products include CO2 and water irrigation pumps, single-use valves, and sterile irrigation tubing.

Outside the US, MEDIVATORS operates its business in Africa, Central Asia, the Asia/Pacific region, the Caribbean, Europe, Japan, Latin America, the Middle East, and Canada.

MEDIVATORS serves hospitals, clinics, and kidney treatment centers. The company sells its products worldwide to several sectors through distributors and its own sales force in certain markets. The company sells and services its products through its own direct US field sales and service organizations. Independent distributors sell its products in Europe, Canada, Asia, Australia, and Latin America, while MEDIVATORS' own sales people sell to and service customers in the Netherlands and Singapore.

The company's net revenue increased 31% to $248.6 million in 2015; it contributed 44% of parent Cantel Medical's total earnings.

The company is working to expand its business into new niches by leveraging its core technologies in hollow fiber membrane devices, chemical sterilants, dialysis solutions, and electronic products.

Cantel Medical entered the UK market in 2014 when it acquired PuriCore International (since renamed Cantel Medical (UK) Limited). The purchase both strengthened MEDIVATORS' endoscope reprocessing business and aligned with the group's international growth strategies.
