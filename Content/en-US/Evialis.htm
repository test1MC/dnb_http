French cuisine is an art form, but for Evialis, animal nutrition is all about science. The company's nutrition business develops, manufactures, and distributes feeds for livestock and exotic animals. Its health division develops and manufactures medicinal feed additives, while its premix and specialties business exports feeds and premixes and provides advisory services to other feed manufacturers. In 2008 the company acquired the French feed operations of Cargill . With manufacturing throughout the world, Evialis' products are available worldwide. The company is owned by the French agricultural cooperative, Union InVivo.
