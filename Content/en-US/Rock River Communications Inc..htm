Rock River Communications licenses music from record labels in order to create compilation CDs for retailers to play in stores. Customers have included The Gap, Williams-Sonoma's Pottery Barn, and Starwood Hotels & Resorts. Rock River also partnered with Volkswagen to release VW Street Mix Volume 1 , a compilation CD featuring music from Volkswagen's first commercials.
