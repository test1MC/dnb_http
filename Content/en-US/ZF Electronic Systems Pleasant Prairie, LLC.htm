For ZF Electronic Systems (dba Cherry Electrical Products) the cherry on top is manufacturing a wide variety of switches, magnetic sensors and controls, and automotive modules. A business unit of ZF Friedrichshafen , its products range from microswitches to sensors, electronic controls, and touch controls based on infra-red technology. CHERRY switches, sensors and controls can be found in a broad range of applications and markets such as white goods and appliances, automotive, gaming, vending, drives, switchgear, control engineering and powertools. Parent ZF has offices and factories across Europe, North America, and Asia/Pacific.
