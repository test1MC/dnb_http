Restonic counts on cultivating customers to rest their weary heads on its products as they count sheep. Restonic is a leading manufacturer of innerspring and specialty mattresses that are sold through home furnishing and specialty bedding retailers nationwide. The company, founded in 1938, specializes in latex, memory foam, magnet, and air mattresses. Its lineup of mattress collections, which feature its patented Marvelous Middle technology (formally trademarked in 1965), includes ComfortCare, SpiraLok, and SuperEdge Plus. Restonic's commercial unit supplies bedding products to hotels and motels, colleges and universities, and the recreational vehicle industry.
