Valcor Engineering makes fluid control products with aerospace, scientific and light industrial, and nuclear applications. Valcor's Aerospace division offers 50,000 varieties of solenoid valves, pressure regulators, relief valves, and accumulators. Valcor Scientific has a library of more than 18,000 solenoid-driven fluid control devices, including cryogenic service valves and dispensing/metering pumps. The company offers another 18,000 components, including solenoid valves, check valves, and flow regulators for nuclear plant applications.

Valcor is based in Springfield, New Jersey, and caters to the markets of the US, Mexico, Spain, Korea, China, and Taiwan.

The company operates through four divisions: Aircraft, Missiles and Aerospace, Nuclear and Power, and Scientific and Industrial. Valcor Aerospace receives contracts from customers including the US Navy and the Defense Logistics Agency . The Scientific division specializes in the design and manufacture of pumps and valves for medical, biomedical, analytical chemistry, and scientific instrumentation. Its Nuclear division provides flow control devices to global commercial nuclear power facilities.

Valcor sells its products through distributors such as Transaero and Sojitz Aerospace Corporation . It caters to the primarily aerospace, nuclear, light industrial, and scientific industries.
