American Beverage Corporation (ABC) keeps American consumers well supplied with beverages of their choice. The company is a manufacturer and marketer of ready-to-drink cocktails, non-alcoholic cocktail mixers, and fruit drinks. Its brands include Daily's Cocktails, Little Hug Fruit Barrels, Big Hug, and Guzzler which are distributed across the US in grocery, drug, mass merchandisers, warehouse clubs, and liquor stores. ABC has manufacturing facilities in Verona, Pennsylvania and Phoenix, Arizona. Its state-of-the-art manufacturing includes bottle blow molding expertise. The company is owned by Harvest Hill.

In 2015 Harvest Hill acquired American Beverage Corporation (ABC) from Royal Wessanen for $55 million. The addition of its brand Little Hug to its retail juice portfolio enables Harvest Hill to participate in all segments of the juice aisle, strengthening its position with its retail partners.

The company manufactures its products at a company-owned facility in Verona, Pennsylvania, and a leased facility in Phoenix, Arizona.

ABC's products are distributed across the US through grocery, drug, mass, warehouse club, and liquor store channels. Additionally, Daily's products are sold through foodservice distributors to bars and restaurants. ABC relies on an extensive network of licensed brokers and distributors to sell its beverages to retailers.

ABC was founded as Daily Orange Juice Company, Inc. in 1960.
