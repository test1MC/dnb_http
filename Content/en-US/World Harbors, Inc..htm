World Harbors is a leading manufacturer and marketer of cooking sauces and marinades. The company's products include Australian-style barbeque sauce (Bar-B Sauce), Asian- and Mediterranean-inspired sauces, steak sauces, and Buffalo-style wing sauce. It also makes mixers and sauces under the Angostura brand, as well as a line of natural products marketed under the label Acadia Naturals. Founded as C.V. Finer Foods in 1990, World Harbors was a unit of spirits producer Angostura Holdings until 2010, when it was sold to Mizkan Americas, the US operations of Japanese food company Mizkan Group, which has been acquisitive in the US during the past couple years.
