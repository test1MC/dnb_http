Just like K-Tel , K-Tool doesn't sell its products in stores. The company distributes hand tools, power tools, industrial equipment, and other professional products made by more than 250 manufacturers to more than 3,000 auto parts stores, government agencies, hardware stores, industrial distributors, and tool distributors. Customers may order by phone or through the company's Web sites. In addition K-Tool offers repairs and provides spare parts. The company also makes hand tools, power tools, and professional equipment through its K-Tool International division. K-Tool's Preferred Tool & Equipment division sells exclusively to jobbers, warehouses and distributors. K-Tool was founded in 1981 by Edward Kay.
