Biopharmaceutical firm MediWound is focused on developing new products to address severe burns (and other hard-to-heal wounds) and connective tissue disorders. It is commercializing NexoBrid, which uses a patented proteolytic enzyme technology to non-surgically and rapidly remove dead or damaged tissue in adults with severe burns. The firm launched NexoBrid in 2013 in Europe through its German subsidiary, targeting a focused audience of burn specialists. It also plans to initiate a Phase 3 study in the US in 2014 as part of a plan to win the support of the FDA . To fund these efforts, in 2014 MediWound raised $70 million in an IPO. Post - IPO, parent company Clal Biotechnology Industries held 48% of the firm.
