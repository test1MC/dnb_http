Marshfield just adores portals. Marshfield DoorSystems is a leading maker of commercial architectural wood doors in the US. The company sells its door and accessory products to the commercial construction industry nationwide. It offers more than 3 million possible made-to-order combinations of doors. The company's history dates back to Axel Hatteberg's establishment of Hatteberg Veneer Company in 1890. Since its founding, Marshfield DoorSystems has transformed itself through five names and two devastating fires. Door manufacturer Masonite bought the company in 2011 from private equity firm Wind Point Partners and Marshfield DoorSystems' management.
