Ever hold in a sneeze? NABCO does the same thing -- but on a much bigger scale -- with bombs. The company designs and manufactures so-called total explosive containment vessels (TCV) used by military forces, security units, and bomb squads. NABCO's explosive-blunting offerings include the aforementioned TCVs, storage containers for suspect luggage, and portable/wheeled detonation units. The company also makes gas-tight products used to contain biological or chemical agents and treatment systems used to neutralize harmful chemical or biological agents.
