Dorling Kindersley's stable of titles includes page turners, travel planners, parenting primers, and gardening bibles, among others. The UK publishing house is a market leader in adult and children's non-fiction sales; tomes on the arts and culture, business, health, history, and travel abound at Dorling Kindersley. The company publishes books internationally via subsidiaries in the US and Germany. It has offices in New York, London, Munich, New Delhi, Toronto, and Melbourne. Dorling Kindersley was founded in London in 1974 by Peter Kindersley and Christopher Dorling, initially as a book packager for other publishers. It was purchased by Pearson in 2000, and operates as part of Pearson's Penguin Publishing .
