Holton Food Products has a hold on the American love of eating pies. The company manufactures food-preparation products, primarily dried-egg consumables, for use in baked goods, including pies. It sells its products wholesale to food manufacturing companies in the US. The was founded by CEO Paul Holton in 1946. Mantrose-Haeuser Co. (a subsidiary of RPM International Inc. ) acquired Holton Food Products in February 2016. It planned to keep the Holton brand and expand it internationally by leveraging the Mantrose-Hauser distribution network.
