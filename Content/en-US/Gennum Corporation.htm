Gennum takes its customers down the information highway with high-definition TV, the Internet, and e-commerce. The company designs, develops, and markets high-performance semiconductor products and intellectual property (IP) designs used to preserve transmitted data signal quality. These analog, mixed-signal, and optical devices are used in networking, home entertainment and broadcasting, and data communications, as well as security and surveillance. The company primarily caters to video broadcast, data storage, consumer and telecommunications connectivity, and IP markets. In 2012 Gennum was acquired by Semtech .
