Sandvik Thermal Process (formerly MRL Industries) makes specialized heating equipment used in semiconductor production and other manufacturing applications. The company's products include heating elements, furnace systems, insulation materials, gas systems, and cantilever loading systems. It also provides support services, including installation and repair. Thermal processing is used in semiconductor manufacturing for annealing, deposition, oxidation, and silicide formation. Established in 1979, the firm has been a part of Sandvik since 1999. The company has customers in the Americas, Europe, the Asia-Pacific region, and Japan.
