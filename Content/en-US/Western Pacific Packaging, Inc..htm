Western Pacific Packaging Inc. (WPPI) can wrap up a wide range of products, from sunscreen and shampoo to fuel additives and car wax. The contract packager bottles, pillowpaks, pouches, and tubes are filled, printed, and repackaged in display kits to make items ready for the store shelves. WPPI's facility has bulk storage for liquids, and its bottle filling lines can imprint labeling anywhere on a bottle or cap. Pillowpaks can be completely sealed or affixed with various dispensing tips. Company clients include Amway , Bocchi Laboratories , and Prolong International .
