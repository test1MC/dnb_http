It's time for apps, and Great American Appetizers helps its customers dress up the table and bar with treats. The company adds some finery to onions, jalapeño peppers, cheese, and vegetables and creates breaded and battered appetizers, including deep-fried pickle spears and slices, onion rings, and stuffed jalapeños, for restaurants, specialty stores, and other foodservice operations. The firm's products are sold under the Wahoo! brand name. Great American Appetizers also creates custom appetizers for private-label customers. The company was founded in 1955.
