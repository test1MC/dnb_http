Perform knows what to do with gorgeous digital images of sport. The company owns the rights to digital content from more than 200 global sports leagues, events, and tournaments. It uses this portfolio, combined with editorial and data content, to create websites, stream live events, and host online gambling. Through offices in seven countries and subsidiaries in another three (Australia, New Zealand, and the Netherlands), Perform works with its sports partners and earns money from subscriptions, content distribution, advertising and sponsorship, and technology and production. Key content providers include Wimbledon , the Australian Open, the PGA , and Volvo Ocean Race. The company went public in mid-2011.

Headquartered in the UK, the company operates through more than 20 offices in the US, Europe, and the Asia/Pacific region. Perform gets the majority of its revenue from its European business.

In the subscription category, Perform manages more than 250 websites and mobile products for teams, sports governing bodies, and broadcasters as well as its own LIVESPORT.tv and Sportal Scoreboard.

Perform's distribution channels include Watch&Bet, live video feeds to online gambling houses; OMNISPORT, multimedia sports news; Global Sports Media, live and historic sports stats; and Watch&Trade, live video of events to sportsbook traders. Its partners in these ventures include Abu Dhabi Media , Betfair , William Hill , and Univision .

Perform's growth strategy involves enhancing its digital sports rights portfolio, expanding geographically, expanding across new digital platforms, launching new products, and pursuing complementary acquisitions. Football and tennis are the key sports in the company's live events portfolio.
