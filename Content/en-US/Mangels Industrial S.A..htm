You won't see this company's hot dip served at any cocktail parties. A Brazilian manufacturer, Mangels Industrial operates through three divisions: steel, wheels, and cylinders. The steel division houses the company's galvanization business which produces highway guardrails and provides hot dip galvanizing services. It also produces cold rolled steel strips, steel strapping, and stamped steel parts for automobiles, tools, electronics, household appliances, agricultural machinery, and industrial packaging. Its wheel group makes aluminum wheels for domestic and international automakers. The cylinders sector produces liquefied petroleum gas (LPG) cylinders, as well as air and fuel tanks for trucks and buses.
