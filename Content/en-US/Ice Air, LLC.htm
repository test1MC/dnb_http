Ice Air can keep its cool, but it also knows when it's time to turn up the heat. The company manufactures, installs, and services heating, ventilating, and air conditioning products such as packaged thermal air conditioners, water source heat pumps, ductless split units, and fan coil units for both new construction and renovations. Its products can be found in government offices, hospitals, hotels, office buildings, schools, and residential construction. Ice Air serves the New York high-rise market, but also counts Illinois Masonic Home, the University of Tennessee , and the Pennsylvania National Guard as customers. The company was founded in 1972.
