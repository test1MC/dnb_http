XING AG is looking to cross the road from social networking to professional networking. The company (formerly Open Business Club) is an online business community with global aspirations. It offers free registration and content, but premium membership services are by paid subscription. Like other networking websites, XING offers member profiles and contact information for sales and career opportunities, but XING is supported in 16 languages; has offices in Beijing, Istanbul, and Barcelona, Spain; and is looking to expand to the United Arab Emirates. The company was founded in 2003 by CEO Lars Hinrichs and now has about 5 million registered members and 400,000 paying members.
