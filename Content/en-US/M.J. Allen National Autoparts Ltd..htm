M.J. Allen National Autoparts (formerly National Auto Parts) is a leading UK manufacturer of automotive brake and clutch components for cars and light commercial vehicles. The company's products include brake discs and drums, clutch assemblies, clutch concentric slave cylinders, and flywheel replacement assemblies. M.J. Allen National Autoparts also remanufactures auto parts and operates through a worldwide network of distribution centers. It sells directly and through distributors in about 30 countries across Europe and Asia/Pacific. The company is a member of the MJ Allen group of manufacturing companies.

M.J. Allen National Autoparts specializes in clutch and brake components for passenger cars and light commercial vehicles.

The company's product range covers more than 95% of the UK and European vehicle parts, which includes more than 900 clutch components covering about 4,600 applications, along with over 1,500 products covering about 9,200 applications.

M.J. Allen National Autoparts sells its products directly and through distributors worldwide, including Degert Auto SH.A, Australian Clutch Services, De-Mi Promet D.O.O., AKC - Automotive, KOSSER, ANET Ltd., ETEX Ltd., Renovak, Balti Autoosad AS, and Parkanon Autovaraosa OY.

The company is looking to expand across Belarus, Germany, Slovakia and Ukraine, through distributors.

In 2015 M.J. Allen National Autoparts launched a new brake pad range (about 250 parts) and has plans to further expand the line.

The company was established in 1977.
