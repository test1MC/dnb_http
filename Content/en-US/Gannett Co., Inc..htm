Gannett spreads the news all over the USA and UK through digital, mobile, and print products. The publishing company operates the national newspaper USA TODAY along with more than 90 local media organizations in 33 states and Guam. Gannett also operates more than 160 local news brands online in the UK. The company claims its digital properties attract 95 million unique visitors per month. Its US newspapers claim a daily audience of about 9 million readers. Gannett was formed in 2015 through a spin-off transaction which separated its publishing and broadcasting operations.

The company's newspapers cover world news but are distributed mainly in the US and UK.

USA TODAY is especially popular with business travelers and other Americans staying in hotels. Its marketing and advertising are geared towards business people.

Gannett is focused on local news that could be of national interest. USA TODAY has a truly national following, and it is considered one of the few national newspapers. However, the paper is not as highly regarded as the New York Times or the Wall Street Journal .
