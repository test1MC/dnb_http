Hikari Tsushin means "brilliant communication" in Japanese, and when the company was founded in 1999 it rode the dot-com bubble and boom quite brilliantly. Since then Hikari Tsushin has evolved into a cell phone and Internet service provider and distributor, a life insurance agency, and a seller of office equipment, such as copiers, fax machines, and telephones. The company's e-machitown subsidiary provides Web hosting services in Japan, and Newton Financial Consulting sells medical insurance policies to individiuals.

Hikari operates in three segments: Corporate, Shop, and Insurance. Corporate, 40% of revenue, sells OA equipment, telecommunication line services, and enterprise services. The Shop business, 58% of revenue, sells mobile phones through its nationwide sales channels. The company sells insurance, 2% of revenue, sells insurance policies through its nationwide call centers.

Hikari focuses on small, medium-sized enterprises. It operates more than 2,000 shops across Japan.

The company's revenue climbed 13% in 2014 from 2013. It posted sales increases in all segments. Net income rose even more, 74% on the higher revenue and gains in non-operating income. Cash flow from operations was 14% higher in 2014.

Hikari acquired a controlling stake in iFLAG Co. in 2015 and will operate it as a subsidiary.
