While its parent company is well-established in the US, Estée Lauder International enjoys the same prestige worldwide. The company is a subsidiary of Estée Lauder Companies and is the arm of the company that extends outside the US and Canada. The company sells cosmetics, fragrances, and skin care products under the Flirt!, Tom Ford Beauty, Bobbi Brown, Clinique, Prescriptives, and Donna Karan Cosmetics names, among others. Founded in 1946 the Estée Lauder company is known for its upscale beauty items and dedication to research and product testing. The company develops and markets men's fragrances (such as Sean John Fragrances and Aramis), as well as men's Lab Series Skin Care for Men.

This subsidiary of beauty behemoth Estée Lauder is responsible for all markets outside of North America, particularly in Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and Asia Pacific.

Estée Lauder International is responsible for conducting the parent company's travel retail business worldwide. Selling skin care items, cosmetics, and fragrances to travelers in airports across Asia Pacific, specifically, has become big business in 2012. Estée Lauder International also cultivates relationships with area sales affiliates and distributors for the larger Estée Lauder business.

The company is working to boost profitability in European perfumeries and pharmacies and in Asian department stores. It's also focused on its online, specialty retail, and prestige salon businesses.
