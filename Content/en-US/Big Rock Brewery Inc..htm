Big Rock Brewery (formerly Big Rock Brewery Income Trust) is a leading regional beer maker that produces unpasteurized premium craft beers in accordance with the 1516 Bavarian Law of Purity. Its brands include Grasshöpper, Jack Rabbit Lager, McNally's Extra, and XO Lager. Big Rock Brewery also produces seasonal beers and Rock Creek dry apple cider. Its products are sold in all the Canadian provinces except Quebec and New Brunswick. In addition to its own beers, Big Rock Brewery bottles and packages beverages for third parties. In January 2011, the former trust reorganized as a corporation under the name Big Rock Brewery Inc. and began trading on the Toronto Stock Exchange under the symbol BR.
