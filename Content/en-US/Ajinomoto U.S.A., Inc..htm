Ajinomoto U.S.A. wants to extend the reach of monosodium glutamate worldwide. The company is the US arm of one of East Asia's best-known companies (and brands), Ajinomoto . It manufactures and distributes Chinese-, Japanese-, and Korean-style foods and ingredients, including the aforementioned Ajinomoto brand MSG and Aspartame, Savor-Rich chicken seasoning, Crispy Fry breading mix, and Hon-Dashi fish-and-seaweed-flavored broth. Ajinomoto U.S.A. also makes amino acids for use in cosmetics, industrial chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and nutritional supplements. Its Amino-Vital supplements are used by athletes and its Capsiate Natura supplements (derived from peppers) are available through health professionals.
