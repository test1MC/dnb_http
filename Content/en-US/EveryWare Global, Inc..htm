EveryWare Global has doubled down and put it all on the tabletop. The parent company of Anchor Hocking and Oneida Ltd. went public in mid-2013 after the controlling entity of all three, Monomoy Capital Partners, effected a merger of the two subsidiaries into the new company EveryWare. Oneida and Anchor Hocking are both staples in the tabletop industry, known as manufacturers of dinnerware, cutlery, glassware, culinary gadgetry, buffetware, kitchen accessories, and candleware, among other items. They make articles for the foodservice industry as well as the consumer market. EveryWare markets its merchandise perhaps not everywhere, but throughout the Americas, Asia, and Europe.
