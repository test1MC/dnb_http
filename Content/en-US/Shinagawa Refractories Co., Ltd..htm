Shinagawa Refractories helps manufacturers take the heat. The company makes a comprehensive line of shaped and unshaped refractories. Its primary product is heat-resistant ceramic material. Shinagawa's diverse line of fire-resistant goods is used for industrial purposes, primarily by steel manufacturers. It designs and services industrial furnaces and licenses furnace technology. The company also produces a line of fine ceramics and ceramic fibers. Shinagawa has nine affiliate businesses that provide related goods and services. Shinagawa has production facilities and marketing offices throughout Japan and sells its products worldwide.
