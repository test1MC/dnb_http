Sing Hallelujah! Amen! for Miracle Recreation Equipment. The company designs and manufactures park and playground equipment, including modular playscapes, slides, swings, outdoor fitness equipment, basketball goals, bleachers, picnic tables, grills, and water slides. It also handles equipment installation arrangements, site surfacing and furnishings, and fundraising plans. Customers include churches, city parks and recreation departments, day care centers, retail operations (like auto dealerships and hotels), and schools in the US and abroad. The firm maintains a network of more than 100 sales consultants across the US and some 40 international distributors. Founded in 1927, Miracle is a division of PlayPower .
