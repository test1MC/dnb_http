Healthcommunities.com helps doctors navigate the Information Superhighway. The company designs, hosts, and maintains websites for physicians and health practices. It also offers some 30 physician-monitored patient education websites that enable visitors to learn about medical conditions and treatments. These sites also allow users to connect with others who have similar health interests, share personal health stories, search for doctors, investigate current clinical trials, and shop for health care products. Site topics range from senior health, to sleep disorders, to pain management. Healthcommunities.com was established in 1998 by board-certified urologist and company chairman Dr. Stanley Swierzewski.
