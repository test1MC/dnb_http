ELMOS Semiconductor develops and manufactures application-specific integrated circuits (ASICs) used in the automotive industry and in consumer and industrial electronics. The company's chips are used in automotive applications (about 85% of sales), such as remote control keys, seat heating systems, ventilation controls, throttle positioners, fuel pumps, air bags, tire pressure gages, and braking systems. Top customers include Autoliv , Saia, and Valeo . ELMOS gets around three-quarters of its sales in Europe. The company has approximately 130 customers around the world.
