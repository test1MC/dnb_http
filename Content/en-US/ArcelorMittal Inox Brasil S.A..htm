ArcelorMittal Inox Brasil is South America's leading maker of silicon and stainless steel. It also produces carbon and alloyed steel, thick bars, and stainless steel tubes. The company's products are used as raw material for heavy components in trucks and tractors, household appliances, and construction products. ArcelorMittal Inox Brasil maintains a 90% domestic market share for its flat stainless steel and silicon products. It also exports its products to over 55 countries. The company operates a single manufacturing facility in the state of Minas Gerais. It is a subsidiary of the world's #1 steel maker, ArcelorMittal .
