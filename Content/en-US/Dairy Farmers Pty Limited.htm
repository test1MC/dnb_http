Everyone wanted to nibble at this company's cheese when it went on the chopping block in 2008. Dairy Farmers Pty Limited (formerly Australian Co-operative Foods) was acquired that year by Australia's National Foods , which since has been integrated into Lion . The dairy co-op's farmers produce cheese, milk, yogurt, and other dairy items under such brands as Dairy Farmers, Thick & Creamy, SKI, Cracker Barrel, COON, Shape, and moove. It also sells dare-brand iced coffee beverages and Berri juices. The group, founded in 1900, owns and operates some 10 processing plants in Australia and exports its products to Asia, Eastern Europe, and the Middle East. Products are sold through about 40,000 Australian retailers.
