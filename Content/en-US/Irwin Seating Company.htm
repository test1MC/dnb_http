Irwin Seating invites you to take a seat, literally. The company manufactures, restores, and markets seating for a range of public and home theater venues. It serves various customers, including corporations, hospitals, movie theaters, musical centers, places of worship, schools and universities, home media rooms, and sports stadiums. Irwin Seating has installed its products at Best Buy 's corporate headquarters, Boston College , Carnegie Hall, and the Staples Center in Los Angeles . The company boasts manufacturing facilities in the US and Brazil, and sales offices in Canada and the Middle East. Founded in 1907, the family-owned business is the operating subsidiary of Irwin Seating Holding Co.

The Michigan-based seating company operates globally in North America, Brazil, and the Middle East.

Irwin Seating maintains manufacturing facilities in the US and Brazil. It has sales offices in Canada and the Middle East. It makes its auditorium seating in Grand Rapids, Michigan, and its bleachers and telescopic platforms in Altamont, Illinois.

The company's new product development focuses on formulating and marketing products to make sitting more convenient and comfortable. Among its patented designs, Irwin Seating owns the rights to a foldable bleacher seat. The seat back is pivotable, which allows for both an upright and a folded down position.

Irwin Seating has become an expert in developing seating solutions for some of the largest and most prestigious stadiums worldwide. In 2012 the company partnered with London's Blue Cube (GB) Ltd. to launch Solara and Integra beam-mount chairs. The agreement extends the use of the Integra chair, which has been in use across Europe since 2011. The products are installed in Soccercity Football Stadium in Johannesburg, South Africa, and in Wembley Stadium in London. The alliance with Blue Cube allows Irwin Seating to make the chairs using its own supply chain.

The company sells its seating products worldwide through sales representatives. Its sectors include theaters, operas, churches, lodges, public areas, offices, educational facilities, juries, arenas, and convention centers.

Irwin Seating is positioned to win business from the growing number of organizations that recognize the importance of state-of-the-art seating. High-profile opportunities are also particularly helpful in raising brand awareness -- for the customer and for Irwin Seating. The company has manufactured and installed a mix of custom fixed, removable, and swivel theater seats for The Valley Performing Arts Center, opened in 2011 at regional higher ed institution California State University . Internationally, Irwin Seating has installed bleachers in such public centers as the O2 Arena, UK, and Siauliai's stadium, Lithuania, among several.
