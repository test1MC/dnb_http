The future of this company is tied to the fortunes of the publishing world. Future (formerly The Future Network) is a publisher of consumer magazines, primarily in the UK, with a portfolio of more than 40 print titles. Its content covers topics such as games, film, music, technology, cycling, automotive, and crafts. Best-selling titles include T3, Total Film, Classic Rock, Guitar World, and the Official Xbox Magazine . In the US, the company's Future US subsidiary produces titles such as MacLife and CyclingNews. In addition to its magazines, Future also publishes about 20 websites, produces some 25 annual live events, and offers contract publishing for businesses or organizations to communicate with clients.
