The many-armed Aditya Birla rivals even Shiva's many incarnations. With nearly 70 manufacturing units, operations, and subsidiaries in 26 countries, the Indian conglomerate produces fabrics, industrial gases, aluminum and copper, cement, palm oil, apparel, carbon black, chemicals, fertilizers, iron, and insulators. Aditya Birla is the #1 global producer of viscose staple fiber (a man-made fiber akin to cotton) in the world as well as the world's #1 producer of rolled aluminum. It also provides business, financial, and IT services. Subsidiaries include metals company Hindalco , and industrial giant Aditya Birla Nuvo . In 2016 it acquired Jaiprakash 's cement unit for $2.4 billion.
