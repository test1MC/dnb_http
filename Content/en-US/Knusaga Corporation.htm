Riders in recreational vehicles, conversion vans, and other specialty vechicles who can adjust their seats exactly to their liking might have Knusaga to thank. The company makes power seat adjusters for a variety of motor vehicles, as well as for commercial and recreational boats. Its product line includes adjusters for both bench and bucket seats. Knusaga formerly supplied hydraulic tubes and fittings to truck manufacturers; it exited that business in 2004. The company was established in 1971.
