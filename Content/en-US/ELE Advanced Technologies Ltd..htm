ELE Advanced Technologies really has it made when it comes to making blades. The company is a UK manufacturer of highly engineered precision components for the aerospace, gas turbine, and automotive industries. Products include turbine blades, nozzle guide vanes, compressor blades, variable guide vanes, heat shields, and combustor linings for aerospace and industrial gas turbine use, as well as turbocharger components and turbine housings for automobiles. ELE also makes inflation adjusters and valves for the aircraft. Customers have included such noteworthy names as Rolls-Royce , Alstom , Fiat , Raytheon , and Fuji Heavy Industries .

The company is headquartered in Lancashire, UK. It regularly ships to the UK, Germany, France, Italy, and Romania.

ELE primarily caters to the industrial gas turbines, aerospace, and automotive turbochargers sectors.
