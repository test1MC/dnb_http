American Dawn manufactures textile products -- industrial aprons and towels, bed linens, blankets, and surgical towels and patient gowns, as well as chef and butcher uniforms -- that are distributed to wholesale, commercial, and retail markets. The company was founded as Overseas General Business in 1972 and caters to the health care, home fashion, laundry and institutional, and salon industries. American Dawn has expanded its operations through the years by acquiring Ambassador and KWB/Calco Manufacturing in 1989 and Tres Manufacturing in 1994.

American Dawn has its headquarters in Compton, California and operates 10 distribution centers throughout the US and in Canada.

The company also operates in Mexico, Egypt, Turkey, India, China, Nepal, Thailand, the Philippines, Pakistan, and Bangladesh.
