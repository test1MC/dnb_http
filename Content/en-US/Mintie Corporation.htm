Not only do surfaces in hospitals need to be clean, the air does, too, and that's where Mintie comes in. The company cleans and maintains specialized HVAC systems used in health care facilities. It also maintains ceilings, fire dampers, and fire walls and works on systems to contain infectious materials and prevent water intrusion. The company's Mintie Technologies unit makes indoor air-quality systems used in the health care industry. Besides health care facilities, Mintie's clients include aerospace, entertainment, manufacturing, and real estate enterprises, along with universities. The company operates from offices in Arizona and California. Mintie was founded in 1940.
