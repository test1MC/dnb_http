If you think wine is just peachy, try a bottle from The Peachy Canyon Winery. It operates four vineyards on 100 acres in the coastal range of Central California. The company produces a number of wine varieties including Cabernet Sauvignon, Cabernet Franc, Chardonnay, and Zinfandel. Wines are available through distributors and via the company's Web site in selected states. The winery is owned and operated by Douglas Beckett and his family.
