M-I, known as M-I SWACO, supplies drilling and completion fluids and additives to oil and gas companies in more than 75 countries worldwide. Its fluids cool and lubricate drill bits, remove rock cuttings, and maintain the stability of the wellbore. Through M-I's SWACO division, the company provides pressure control, rig instrumentation, and drilling waste management products and services. It is also a worldwide producer of barite and bentonite used by the oil and gas and industrial markets. Drilling fluids provide the company with more than half of its sales. M-I SWACO is owned by Schlumberger Limited .

As a service provider to the global oil and gas industry, M-I SWACO does more than two-thirds of its business outside the US, primarily in West Africa and the Middle East.

M-I SWACO is a leading supplier of drilling fluid systems engineered to improve drilling performance by anticipating fluids-related problems. Its fluid systems and specialty tools are designed to optimize wellbore productivity while its production technologies boosts production rates. It also offers environmental solutions that safely manage waste volumes generated in both drilling and production operations.

Its Research and Engineering Support Services Group, based in Houston,is comprised of Analytical Services and Quality Assurance. Both groups support the efforts of the other technical disciplines throughout the company.

The company is part of the Drilling Group segment of its parent company, which contributed 38% of the total revenues of Schlumberger in 2015.

Growing its technical portfolio, in 2010 M-I SWACO acquired Idrabel Italia, an environmental technologies firm specializing in production tank cleaning and petroleum sludge treatment as well as oil recovery and volatile organic compound control for the petroleum and petrochemical industries. The company focuses specifically on solving problems related to hydrocarbon recovery from tank sludge while preventing toxic gas emissions.

Formerly a joint venture 60% owned by Smith International , M-I SWACO became a wholly-owned unit of Schlumberger following Smith's acquisition by that company in 2010.
