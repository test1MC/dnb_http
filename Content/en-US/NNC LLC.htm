One might say Naturade is all about products that are "nature made." With roots reaching back to the 1920s, the company markets meal-replacements, supplements, beverages, and other products touted to enhance weight loss or anti-aging capabilities using natural ingredients. Its biggest selling brands -- Ageless Foundation, Naturade, and Symbiotics -- are sold via health food stores and mass marketers. Some of the main outlets for their products include Whole Foods , Wal-Mart , and military commissaries.  The company also develops private-label products for some retailers.
