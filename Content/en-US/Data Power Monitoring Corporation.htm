Data Power Monitoring has your back ... er ... your back- up . The company provides products and services used to remotely monitor and maintain programs for critical battery systems. Products are geared toward enterprises that manage and maintain mission-critical IT facilities and depend upon uninterrupted back-up power, including the financial services, government, telecommunications, and Internet data center sectors. Clients have included Oracle , Pac-West Telecomm , Charles Schwab , and the California Health and Human Services Agency . Data Power Monitoring was founded in 1992 by CEO Bart Cotton and is today owned by the Cotton family. It operates two offices in Northern California.
