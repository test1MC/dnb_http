Nippon Avionics doesn't always have its head in the clouds. The company, which also does business as Avio, designs and manufactures electronic components and equipment for use in aviation, data communications, and medicine. The company's products include digital projectors, infrared thermographs, precision machining systems, printed circuit boards, software, and telecom equipment. Subsidiaries include Yamanashi Avionics, Avionics Fukushima, NEC San-ei Instruments, and Avio Sales. Nippon Avionics was formed in 1960.
