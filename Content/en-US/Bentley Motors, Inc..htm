What do entertainers and blue-blooded aristocrats have in common? They both have a passion for expensive luxury automobiles. And cars don't get much more extravagant than Bentley. Bentley Motors Inc. is the US marketing and sales division of Volkswagen 's Bentley Motors Limited subsidiary. The extremely expensive and extravagant Bentley models include the Arnage, Continental GT, and Continental Flying Spur, as well as the Mulsanne, the company's new flagship sedan, which debuts in the fall of 2010 (starting price is $285,000). The company prides itself on craftsmanship and the fact that it takes some 150 hours to hand-build a single Continental GT and 400 for the Arnage. Bentley was founded in 1919.
