Local Motion wants to spread the joy of Aloha shirts beyond the backyard barbeque. Designing and manufacturing the classic Hawaiian shirts since 1937, the company's Kahala brand is the one that springs to mind when one considers Aloha shirts. The colorful shirts feature tropical scenes of plants, flowers, and people and are designed by artists. Local Motion distributes to retailers worldwide. The manufacturer and retailer operates about a dozen stores in Hawaii and sells its apparel across the the US, as well as internationally in the likes of Japan, Norway, and Spain.
