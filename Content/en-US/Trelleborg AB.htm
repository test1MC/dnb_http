Trelleborg is a global manufacturer of polymer-based industrial rubber products, including hoses, seals, and anti-vibration components. It sells to the aerospace, automotive, construction, industrial, oil and gas, and transportation markets across more than 40 countries. To a lesser degree the company also makes tires for the agricultural and industrial markets. Trelleborg operates through five segments: Coated Systems, Industrial Solutions, Offshore & Construction, Sealing Solutions, and Wheel Systems. Besides its core markets of Western Europe and North America, Trelleborg is selling more products in the Asia/Pacific region. Scandinavian industrialist Henry Dunker founded the company in 1905.
