Brunico Communications provides print and electronic trade information for such industries as advertising, broadcasting, entertainment, and film production. Publications include Kidscreen (children's entertainment), Realscreen (non-fiction programming), 'Boards (commercial production), Playback (Canadian broadcasting, film, and interactive media), and Strategy (Canadian marketing). In addition, the company produces entertainment and marketing conferences, and organizes events related to its publications. Brunico Communications was founded in 1986.
