Golden Peanut and Tree Nuts Co. (formerly Golden Peanut) shells and processes peanuts and pecans for retail and commercial distribution to customers worldwide. Its products include raw shelled and in-shell peanuts, as well as peanut-derived ingredients: flour, oil, extract, and seed. GPC's peanut lineup is sold to snack-food makers, industrial food manufacturers, and animal-food makers. Its seeds are sold to commercial farmers and home gardeners. The firm also processes other farmers' peanut crops. GPC serves growers in the southeastern and southwestern US, and in Argentina. A subsidiary of Archer-Daniels-Midland Co. , Golden Peanut added Tree Nuts to its name following the acquisition of Harrell Nut Co.

The company acquired the processing facilities and certain other assets of the Harrell Nut Co. in October 2014. Harrell Nut is a leading processor and sheller of pecans. Following the purchase, the company changed its name to Golden Peanut and Tree Nuts.

Founded in 1986, GPC was a 50/50 joint venture of Archer Daniels Midland (ADM) and Alimenta (USA) until year-end 2010, when ADM bought out the venture and gained control of GPC.
