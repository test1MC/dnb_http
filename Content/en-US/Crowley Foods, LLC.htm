Regional dairy producer Crowley Foods keeps East Coast folks' dinner tables nice and creamy. The company produces fluid and cultured dairy products, including milk, half & half, cream, cottage cheese, sour cream, and yogurt. It serves the retail grocery and food service industries primarily in upstate New York. In addition to a full line of Crowley products, its offers six other brands: Heluva Good!, Rosenberger's, Axelrod, Maggio, and Penn Maid. Its customers include Giant , Shoprite , Weis Markets , and Wegmans . Crowley Foods has been producing dairy products at its plant in Binghamton, New York since 1915. The company is owned by HP Hood .

In addition to Crowley and Hood branded products, the company produces milk for retailers under store brands, such as Weis Quality Milk.
