Need to get something printed on the cheap? Need to get some clients for your printing business on the cheap? WebMart is equipped for both jobs. The print management company connects businesses with printing needs to printers. The firm specializes in gravure, Web offset, sheetfed, screen, and digital printing services. Webmart has expertise in magazines, point of sale, direct mail, catalogs, and inserts. It also offers a print procurement and management software platform, Printelligence, for organizations to conduct (and improve) their own print buying and management. WebMart serves clients throughout Europe; customers have included Thomson , Ubisoft , and Tesco . Webmart was founded by Simon Biltcliffe in 1996.
