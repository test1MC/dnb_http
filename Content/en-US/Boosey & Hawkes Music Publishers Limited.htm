Boosey & Hawkes Music Publishers is one of the leading classical music publishers in the world, owning the rights to works by such modern composers as Aaron Copland, Sergei Rachmaninoff, and Igor Stravinsky. It licenses the use of the compositions in film and television projects, as well as for use in advertising. It also markets printed music for professional and educational use. Founded by Ralph Hawkes in 1930, Boosey & Hawkes has offices in Berlin, London, and New York. ImagemMusic, an investment vehicle of Dutch pension fund Stichting Pensioenfonds ABP, acquired the company in 2008 from private equity firm HgCapital .
