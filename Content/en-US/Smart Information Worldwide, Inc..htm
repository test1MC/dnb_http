Smart Information Worldwide publishes Sulekha, an online portal for the worldwide Indian community. ("Sulekha" originates from the word "su" in Hindi meaning "good" and "lekha" meaning "writing".) The company has also published Sulekha Select , a collection of its Web site's best content. Sulekha was first published in 1998, and has since grown to include offices in Chennai, India, and Austin, Texas
