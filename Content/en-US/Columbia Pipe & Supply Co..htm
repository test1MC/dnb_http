Profits are in the pipeline for Columbia Pipe & Supply. Columbia Pipe has built a business as a leading distributor of industrial pipe, plumbing supplies, and HVAC (heating, ventilation, and air conditioning) equipment. Based in Chicago, the company also has sales offices covering the rest of Illinois, Indiana, Michigan, and Wisconsin, and an agreement with Affiliated Distributors that expands its distribution network to a national level. The company's target markets are construction, maintenance and repair, and OEMs, among others. CEO William Arenberg and family own the company, which was founded in 1935.

Based in Illinois, Columbia Pipe maintains some 20 locations across Illinois, Indiana, Wisconsin, Michigan, and Minnesota.

As part of its business, Columbia Pipe supplies its clients with pipes, valves, fittings, plumbing products, hydronic products, and HVAC equipment and supplies.

Columbia Pipe counts more than 10,000 local, regional, and national customers among its list of clients.

The company serves several sectors, such as automotive, agricultural processing, biotechnology, chemical, food and beverage, medical, OEM, petrochemical, pharmaceutical, power generation, primary metals, pulp and paper, wastewater, wet and dry milling, government, healthcare, commercial real estate, property management, and K-12 schools and universities.

Columbia Pipe benefits from maintaining alliances with affiliates, such as the Midwest Distributors Association, National Safety Council, and the American Society of Plumbing Engineers, among many others.
