Solid Data Systems has a solid backup deal for that backed-up system. The company makes solid-state data storage systems targeted for telecommunications, finance, and other markets. Its file caching systems offer instant-access storage in compact rack-mount units. Solid Data's proprietary system-level analysis software (I/O Dynamics) identifies "hot" files -- those files that place the highest demand on a system's input/output routing -- and helps reduce bottlenecks. Customers have included HSBC USA and Critical Path . Brothers George and Wade Tuma started the company as Disk Emulation Systems in 1993.
