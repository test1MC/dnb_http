Pallets everywhere get a lift from UniCarriers Americas Corp. (formerly Nissan Forklift Corporation, North America), a maker of forklifts for the North American market. Products include more than 100 models of engine-driven and electric pallet stackers, rider trucks, tow tractors, and other material handling equipment under the Nissan and Barrett brands. Its engine-powered vehicles include cushion and pneumatic lift trucks. The electric motor machinery include stackers, tow tractors, and pallet trucks. UniCarriers Americas was formed by the 2013 merger of Nissan Forklift and TCM America and is the North American arm of Tokyo-based UniCarriers Corp., which was acquired by Mitsubishi Heavy Industries in 2015.

Marengo, Illinois-based UniCarriers Americas Corp. (UAC) distributes its forklifts through more than 200 dealerships with 300-plus locations across North America, and in Central and South America.

The company has five primary brands: Nissan Forklift, TCM, Atlet, Barrett, and UniCarriers.

By joining forces, Nissan Forklift (a subsidiary of Nissan Motor Co. ) and TCM America (a unit of Hitachi Construction Machinery ) became the seventh-largest lift truck maker in the world. The merged company will focus on growing its forklift business by adding new, more efficient products, and addressing its customers' logistical problems.

In 2014 UniCarriers Americas improved its position in the South American market through the opening of a unit in Brazil.
