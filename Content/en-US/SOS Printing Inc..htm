SOS Printing can't save you at sea, but it can answer your distress call on land. The mid-size commercial printing company serves a wide range of clients in the San Diego area, who need professional-quality books, brochures, catalogs, invitations, manuals, posters, press kits, and other marketing materials. SOS Printing provides pre-press, web, and sheet-fed printing in one to five colors. Its pre-press equipment takes care of layout, color correcting, scanning, film output, and proofing. Its finishing services include cutting, folding, stitching, as well as binding. The company was founded in 1967.
