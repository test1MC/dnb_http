Conair helps Canadians and others to have perfectly coiffed hair, shiny teeth, and neatly pressed clothes. Conair Consumer Products, the Canadian division of Conair Corporation , makes and markets hair styling appliances (Conair, Easy Start, Super Clips) designed for home and professional salon use. The division also manufactures lighted mirrors, electric razors and battery-operated trimmers, oral irrigators, facial saunas, foot massagers, nail care centers, showerheads, and steam irons. Additionally, Conair makes electronic scales sold under the Weight Watchers brand, as well as Interplak and Opticlean branded motorized toothbrushes.

The company has its headquarters in Woodbridge, Ontario. It has a presence across Canada, and in the UK, US, France, Germany, Pacific Rim and Mexico.

Conair sells its products through retail stores.
