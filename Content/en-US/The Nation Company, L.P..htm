The Nation Company publishes The Nation , a political magazine that gives a liberal voice to current events and issues. The weekly opinion journal tackles national and worldwide topics and has regular commentaries and features. Contributors have included Albert Einstein, Eleanor Roosevelt, and Ralph Nader. With departments devoted to topics such as art, corporations, defense, environment, and peace and disarmament, the magazine boasts a circulation of about 200,000. In addition to its print magazine, The Nation Company publishes content online. The Nation was established by abolitionists in 1865.
