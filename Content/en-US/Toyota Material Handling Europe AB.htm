Handling the material world with care is Toyota Material Handling Europe (TMHE) business. Known as Toyota Industries Sweden AB prior to May 2009, TMHE manufactures and sells electric and counterbalanced warehouse trucks, used to move boxed goods and bulk materials. Its lineup includes forklifts, and pallet and stacker trucks, as well as material handlers designed to move within narrow aisles. TMHE operates as a subsidiary of Toyota Industries , overseeing 90-plus material handling companies worldwide. Under the BT Industries, Raymond, Lift-Rite, and CESAD brands, the company serves businesses in Europe, Japan, China, and North America, including wholesale and retail distributors Home Depot , IGT , and Food Lion .
