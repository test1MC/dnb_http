Hamworthy designs and manufactures marine fluid handling equipment that treats and transports oil, gas, and water for offshore vessels and facilities. Its pumps and compressors are used in drilling, engine starting, rig tensioning, and seismic surveying, among other applications. Hamworthy is a leading maker of inert gas systems for the marine and offshore markets. Its equipment also serves crude oil tankers, dry cargo carriers, passenger ferries, LNG vessels, and naval ships. The company has operations throughout Asia, Europe, the Middle East, and North America. In early 2012, Hamworthy was acquired by Wärtsilä , an industrial manufacturer of marine propulsion systems and power plants.
