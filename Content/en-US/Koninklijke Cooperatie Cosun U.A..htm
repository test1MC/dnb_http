Cosun believes it has all the components for success. The food ingredient cooperative has four member companies that produce items used by food companies and restaurants to make their products. Suiker Unie and Sensus sell sugar and insulin, respectively. Sugar is the firm's biggest segment. Its Aviko unit makes chilled, frozen, and dried potatoes, as well as prepared potato dishes. SVZ specializes in frozen fruit and vegetable purees, concentrates, and compounds. In 2011 Cosun sold its Royal Nedalco business, which makes alcohol and ethanol, to Cargill . Also that year Cosun sold commercial baking supplier Unifine Food & Bake Ingredients to Dawn Food Products and bio-ethanol maker C5 Yeast to Royal DSM .
