Curds and whey are not just for Little Miss Muffet. Davisco Foods International, which asserts it's "leading the whey," makes cheese and cheese curds used for production of cheese products and related ingredients, as well as various whey protein products used as ingredients in food products such as nutrition bars, infant formula, dry mixes, beverages, and dietary supplements. The family-owned company produces nearly 200 million pounds of whey and 370 million pounds of cheese annually and supplies food processing companies like Kraft Foods and others. Davisco in 2012 tapped Jon Davis as president and CEO; he succeeds his father Mark and grandfather Stanley Davis, who founded the company in 1943.
