Euphonix has built a business on mixing it up. It makes digitally controlled audio mixing consoles under the brand names Max Air and its flagship System 5 series. The systems can be configured for multiple applications such as audio recording, film and television post-production, and broadcast production. The company also makes the S5 Fusion audio post console and R-1 multi-track digital hard drive recorder, routers, and converters. The company's systems are used by top production houses in music and film around the world. The firm distributes its products through about 60 dealers in Canada, Japan, the US, and the UK. Euphonix was acquired in 2010 by rival digital audio and video technology firm Avid .
