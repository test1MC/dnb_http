GN Hearing (dba ReSound), a subsidiary of GN Store Nord , offers a range of digital and non-digital hearing instruments under the ReSound, Beltone , and Interton brands. Its products are programmed to adjust to the acoustic environment and the client's range of hearing. Its GN Otometrics unit sells audiological diagnostic systems and measurement equipment under the Aurical, Hortmann, ICS, and Madsen brand names; ReSound is selling Otometrics to Natus Medical , but it will collaborate with Natus on technology and product development in the future. ReSound operates in more than 80 countries in North and South America, Europe, and the Asia/Pacific region.
