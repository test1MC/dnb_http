Xomox knows valves backwards and forwards. The Crane subsidiary's products include sleeved plug valves, ball valves, butterfly valves, and lined valves. The company's products are used in the chemical, petrochemical, pulp and paper, nuclear power, and hydrocarbon processing industries. Xomox also offers a full line of vane and rack and pinion pneumatic actuators, hoses, tank wagon systems, pipe fittings, spacers, and expansion joints. The company has a half dozen manufacturing operations in France, Germany, Hungary, India, Mexico, and the US.

Xomox is part of the Fluid Handling segment of the Crane Valve Group which is made up of Crane Pumps & Systems and Crane Supply. Xomax is also the trade name for a number of the products it manufactures.

The company's principal product is the Tufline sleeved plug valve, which is able to work without the need for lubrication because it's lined with Teflon. Its actuators are sold primarily under the Matryx brand name.

Xomox was founded as Continental Manufacturing Company in 1956 and was acquired by Crane Co. in 2001.
