Surgical Specialties Inc. gets surgeons in and out of an operation. The company designs and makes high performance surgical knives and wound closure products that are used in a number of specialties including ophthalmology, plastic surgery, orthopedics, urology, dental, and veterinary medicine. Among the company's products are LOOK sutures, Sharpoint ophthalmic microsurgical knives and sutures, and the Quill knotless tissue-closure device. Surgical Specialties makes devices for other companies and private labels. The company sells in the US and throughout the world through its own sales staff and distributors. It has a manufacturing operation in Tijuana, Mexico. Surgical Specialties is a subsidiary of Angiotech.

Angiotech emerged from bankruptcy in 2011 and sold some groups of assets including its interventional oncology division. It renamed and rebranded its operational subsidiary as Surgical Specialties.

In early 2016 Surgical Specialties bought FSSB Chirurgische Nadein GMBH, a Germany-based maker of surgical needles. The companies said their combination created one of the largest manufacturers of surgical needles. FSSB has two manufacturing facilities in Germany. The deal expands the products and geographic markets of both parties. Terms were not disclosed.
