H3C Technologies provides Internet protocol-based routers, switches, network security devices and other wireline and wireless networking equipment. The company also offers network management software and support services. It markets its products worldwide. Customers have included China Telecom and Citroën . Initially a joint venture between US-based 3Com and China's Huawei Technologies called Huawei-3Com Technology, H3C became a wholly owned subsidiary of 3Com when that company bought out Huawei's stake for $882 million in 2007. H3C Technologies continues to partner with Huawei on product development, along with NEC and other companies.
