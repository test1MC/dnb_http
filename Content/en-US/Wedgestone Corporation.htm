Wedgestone wants to help your truck look its best. The company, which sells products under the Westin Automotive brand name, makes aftermarket accessories such as bed rails, driving lights, grilles, grille guards, step-ups, taillight guards, and tailgate ladders. Wedgestone also manufacturers both factory replacement and universal bumpers. The company also offers cargo management products such as pickup bed extenders, roof racks, and luggage trays, as well as the mounting kits need to install the products. Westin Automotive products are sold to warehouse distributors and retail chains.
