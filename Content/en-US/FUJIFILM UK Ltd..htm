FUJIFILM UK is a subsidiary of Japanese film and imaging equipment maker FUJIFILM Holdings . The unit comprises three specialty areas: document, imaging, and information. Besides film, product lines include digital, instant, and single-use cameras, as well as video, audio, CCTV, and computer media backup and data storage items. FUJIFILM UK also manufactures media and equipment for the medical, print, photo development, and motion picture industries. Its latest offerings include a photo book for smartphone pictures called POP BOOK.
