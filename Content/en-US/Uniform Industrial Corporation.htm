Uniform Industrial Corporation (UIC) makes products that allow for the perfect swipe every time. The company is a manufacturer of magnetic stripe card and check readers, encoder and decoder components, PIN pads, and other peripheral devices for electronic payment and data collection. These products primarily serve the banking and retail markets. The group's other divisions include Asante , a developer and supplier of routers, switches, connectors, network cameras, and other communication components, and Alinking, a maker of IP video security surveillance equipment. Established in 1985, UIC maintains operations in Asia, Europe, and North America. It boasts a global network of clients, resellers, and distributors.
