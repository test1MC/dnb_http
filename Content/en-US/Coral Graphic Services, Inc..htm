Coral Graphic Services provides commercial printing services from facilities in Kentucky, New York, and Virginia. The company offers prepress, printing (including digital printing), and finishing services. Specialties include book components, such as covers, inserts, and jackets, that incorporate such graphic effects as die cutting, embossing, and foil stamping. Coral Graphics also produces annual reports, corporate brochures, and various forms of marketing materials, including posters. The company is a unit of arvato , a Germany-based printing and customer relationship management services company that itself is part of the Bertelsmann media conglomerate.
