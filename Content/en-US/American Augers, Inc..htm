Industry's need to bore holes in America has augured well for American Augers. The company, a former subsidiary of Astec Industries ' Underground Group, manufactures earth boring machines and horizontal directional drilling systems. Its lineup includes fluid and mud pumps, cleaning systems, and product tooling and accessories. The equipment is used in underground construction, oil and gas well drilling, and pipeline and utility installations. It holds the distinction of manufacturing the largest maxi-rig system available on the market. In late 2012 Astec Industries sold American Augers and its Underground Group to The Charles Machine Works for $55 million in cash.

The company makes a range of underground and utility construction equipment, including large directional drills, auger boring machines, mud pump and cleaning systems, and trenchers, as well as tools and accessories.
