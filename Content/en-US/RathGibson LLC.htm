RathGibson makes welded finished stainless steel and special alloy pipe and tubing (stainless steel, nickel, and titanium tubing) for a range of processing industries. Its primary products include stainless steel, duplex stainless steel, nickel alloy, and super austenitic (a non-magnetic allotrope of iron with an alloying element) tubing and pipes; and super ferritics/ferritics pipes and tubing. RathGibson's products include welded (as well as welded and drawn) tubing and pipes; commercial tubing and pipes; encapsulated tubing; and U-bend tubing. The company is an operating subsidiary of complex metal components and products manufacturer Precision Castparts .

RathGibson was acquired in 2012 by Precision Castparts , which then became part of that company's Forged Products segment.

The company has offices in Argentina, Australia, Austria, China, India, Korea, Singapore, the UAE, and the US (Arkansas, New Jersey, and Wisconsin).

RathGibson's products are used in a range of oil and gas, chemical and petrochemical processing, and power generation applications, as well as in other commercial markets such as beverage, pharmaceuticals, and biopharmaceuticals.

The parent company's Forged Products segment's revenues increased by 12% in 2013, thanks in large part to the acquisition of RathGibson.

Forged Products accounted for 43% of Precision Castparts' revenues in 2013.

Upon emerging from bankruptcy in 2010, the company's name was changed to RathGibson LLC.

Bankruptcy in 2009 came on the heels of a record 2008. The problem was caused by demand dropping by half coupled with a tremendous debt load. The company suffered a significant decrease in orders in 2009 due to the global economic crisis and was forced to seek protection.

A series of earlier deals expanded the company's operations. Rath Manufacturing bought Gibson Tube in 1999 to produce an expanded line of commercial and sanitary tubing; the company took the RathGibson name in 2005. It acquired Greenville Tube in 2006, adding to its offerings and geographic scope.
