There's nothing like the sound of Imagem Music Group's music to inspire the imagination. As a European-based music publisher, Imagem Music Group controls the rights to more than 100,000 songs by popular artists, including Kid Rock, Rihanna, Kelly Clarkson, and Justin Timberlake. Through its Boosey & Hawkes and Rodgers & Hammerstein subsidiaries, the company also owns the rights to classical music compositions by modern composers, as well as stage and songs featured in popular musicals such as Oklahoma and The Sound of Music . Formed in 2007, Imagem Music Group is the product of a partnership between music publisher CP Masters BV and Dutch pension fund APG.
