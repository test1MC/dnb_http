White goods keep Arçelik in the black. Operating more than a dozen plants in Turkey, Romania, Russia, China, and South Africa, the company sells air conditioners, cooking appliances, dishwashers, refrigerators, vacuum cleaners, washing machines, water heaters, and other appliances in more than 100 countries. It also makes electronics and furniture, and offers kitchen design services. The company's products carry the Arctic, Beko, Blomberg, and Grundig brand names, among others. Arçelik was founded in 1955 as Turkey's first manufacturer of refrigerators, washing machines, and water heaters by Vehbi Koç, who in 1963 founded Koç Holding, which controls Arçelik.
