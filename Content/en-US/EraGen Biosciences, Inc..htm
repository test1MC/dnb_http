EraGen Biosciences may just be an usher for the next era of genomics tools. The company is a developer and marketer of molecular diagnostic products for clinical laboratory and medical research customers, primarily in North America. It uses its MultiCode technology platform to develop diagnostic assays for the clinical detection of conditions including genetic and infectious diseases. EraGen Biosciences sells its products directly and via international distribution firms. It also has some development and marketing partnerships with other molecular diagnostic companies. The company was acquired by diagnostics firm Luminex in 2011; it was previously backed by venture capital financing entities.
