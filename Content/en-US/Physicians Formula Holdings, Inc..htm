Physicians Formula Holdings (PFH) boasts the professional formula for skin imperfections. The company develops, makes, and markets prestige cosmetics products, focusing on both face and eye makeup. The Physicians Formula label includes facial powders, bronzers, concealers, blushes, foundations, eye shadows, eye liners, brow makeup, and mascaras. It sells its products through more than 25,000 stores, including mass merchandisers ( Wal-Mart and Target ), drugstores ( CVS and Rite Aid ), and specialty cosmetics shops ( Ulta ). PFH caters to the US but also sells its beauty aids in Canada, Australia, and beyond. Founded in 2003, PFH is owned by beauty products maker Markwins International .

Based in California, PFH distributes its beauty products worldwide across North America, as well as in Australia, Canada, Costa Rica, El Salvador, France, Guatemala, Honduras, Italy, Panama, Ireland, Belgium, South Africa, Mexico, Malaysia, Nepal, and Turkey.

The beauty firm develops, markets, and sells both cosmetic and skin care products.

PFH serves the mass market channel. It caters to a variety of retailers in the food retail, drug chain, mass volume, specialty retail, and wholesale channels. Customers include the likes of Wal-Mart , Target , Kmart , Kroger , and Meijer .

PFH and its parent company Markwins share similar sales channels and boast non-competing brands. PFH is looking to leverage an opportunity to broaden its customer base.

It's also working to grow by regularly rolling out new products, as they tend to generate a larger portion of gross sales. PFH's forward momentum builds upon development and introduction of new products in less-served yet commonly needed categories.
