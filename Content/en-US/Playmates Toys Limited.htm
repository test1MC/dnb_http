When kids wish upon a star, they may be wishing for one of Playmates' toys. Playmates Toys makes dolls and action figures through licensing agreements with companies such as Disney and Nickelodeon . The company's lineup includes Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles and Disney's Little Princess (featuring characters such as Ariel, Cinderella, and Belle from both classic and newer animated films). It also makes other dolls including Water Babies, Strawberry Shortcake, and Amazing Amanda. The majority of Playmates Toys' revenue comes from North America; it has offices in California and Hong Kong. Chairman Thomas Chan owns almost 40% of the company, which was founded in 1966 by Sam Chan as an OEM doll maker.
