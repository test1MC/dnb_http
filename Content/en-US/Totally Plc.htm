Totally absolutely, positively wants to be everybody's everything, at least for members of the Jewish communities in the UK. Through its Jewish News & Media Group, the company operates the website TotallyJewish.com and publishes free weekly newspaper The Jewish News in London. It also publishes Pulse (Jewish food, travel, and lifestyle topics) and TotallyJewishSimchas (weddings, Bar Mitzvahs, and other celebrations) magazines, and produces TotallyJewishSimchasLive, an annual celebrations exhibition. Through subsidiary Totally Communications, the company also offers digital marketing services, including website and software design and development, consultancy and systems integration, and online marketing.
