LamTech's quite a smooth operator. With manufacturing, distribution, and sales offices in Pennsylvania, New York, and Virginia, the company makes custom countertops, surfaces, and vanities made from laminate, cultured marble, solid surface, butcher block, natural quartz, and granite for the home and office. The laminates, polymers, and other coating materials used in LamTech's products are made by such manufacturers as DuPont , Formica , Nevamar , and Wilsonart International . The company also offers delivery and installation of its custom-crafted products. LamTech formed out of the 1997 merger between LamTech Industries and Marblux Company.
