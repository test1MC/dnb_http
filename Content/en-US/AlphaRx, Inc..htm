Pills can be hard to swallow, but sometimes they're even harder to dissolve. AlphaRx develops drug formulations that make it easier for compounds that are poorly soluble in water to be absorbed by the human body. The company improves on existing FDA-approved drugs and health supplements for the treatment of inflammatory and infectious diseases. AlphaRx's main bet is on Indaflex, a topical non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) for the treatment of osteoarthritis. The company is also developing drugs for the Asian market, including Indaflex.
