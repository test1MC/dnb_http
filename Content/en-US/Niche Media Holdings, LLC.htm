Niche Media serves a pretty exclusive readership. The company's glossy luxury publications aim to meet the needs of the rich and influential in metropolital locales such as New York, Los Angeles, Boston, Washington, DC, and Aspen, Colorado. Niche Media targets readers who maintain annual household incomes of at least $200,000 and have liquid assets of more than $1 million. Its Gotham magazine covers the New York City scene while its Hamptons magazine details the happenings of the resort community. Its LA Confidential magazine focuses on the jet-setters on the West Coast, and Aspen Peak blankets the ski set. Niche Media was founded in 1992 by CEO Jason Binn. It is a subsidiary of Greenspun Media Group.
