J.C. Potter Sausage Company, a subsidiary of Atlantic Premium Brands , processes and distributes sausages and other meats under the J.C. Potter brand name. The company's products are a staple in grocery stores and supermarket chains in Arkansas and Oklahoma, although they are available nationwide. Potter's products include breakfast sausage in link, patty, and bulk forms; sausage biscuits; bacon, cured salt pork; hickory smoked ham; hot links; and smoked sausages. It also processes private-label products for other organizations. The company was founded in 1949 by its namesake, J.C. Potter.
