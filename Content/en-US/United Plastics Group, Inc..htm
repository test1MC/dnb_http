From an amorphous blob to a cell phone casing or car dashboard, United Plastics Group (UPG) shapes its profits by molding raw plastic into finished products. The company makes plastic products and components utilizing mold building, injection molding, and micro molding processes. Its divisions are based on the automotive, consumer, electronics, industrial, and medical markets. Value-added products include heat transfer decals, heat staking, pad printing, and sonic welding. UPG has a handful of facilities in the US and one each in Mexico, China, and the UK.

UPG has facilities in Fremont, California; Minneapolis; Houston; Suzhou, China; Mexico; and the UK.

UPG operates more than 450 plastics processing machines and has 1 million sq. ft. of manufacturing space. Because of the slow US auto industry, UPG has diverted its energy towards other endeavors, such as the medical industry. In recent years the company has expanded its clean room facilities at its Minnesota manufacturing facility and also doubled its clean rooms in California and China, and will continue to focus on this market.

Each year the medical market accounts for around 40% of UPG' total sales; the remainder is divided among the electronics, consumer, industrial, and custom automotive sectors.

The company sells its products in over 30 countries, primarily catering to the medical, data center, consumer, energy, and automotive markets.
