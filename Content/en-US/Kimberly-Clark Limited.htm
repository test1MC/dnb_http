Kimberly-Clark Limited, in operation in the UK since 1955, has a foothold in the area's paper products market. It manufactures such items as Kleenex (tissues), Andrex (toilet tissue), Fiesta (disposable towels), Huggies (diapers/nappies), Kotex (feminine care), and its Professional line. The company's professional unit caters to businesses in the manufacturing and hospitality industries by making products for wiping, workwear, and the washroom. Kimberly-Clark Limited also develops a health care product line for hospitals and medical professionals that includes disposable gowns, drapes, and gloves, as well as sterilization wrap. It's a business unit of Kimberly-Clark Europe .
