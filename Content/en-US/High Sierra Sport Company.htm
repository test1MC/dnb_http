When folks head out for some high adventure on the slopes or in the Outback, High Sierra Sport Company hopes they pack their bag. The company makes and distributes backpacks, day packs, and other gear for carrying stuff into the great outdoors, onto airplanes, and on their backs. The company's bags are made under the High Sierra, Sierra Expedition, ATQ, Pinnacle, Next Level, and other labels. High Sierra also makes popular hydration bags for trekking customers. The company, which sells to some 1,500 retailers worldwide the likes of Sports Authority , Kohl's , and Cabela's , was founded in 1979 as the H. Bernbaum Import & Export Company. In mid-2012 the company agreed to sell its business to Samsonite .
