APN News & Media is a leading publisher of newspapers and magazines in Australia and New Zealand. The company's flagship paper, The New Zealand Herald , boasts a circulation of more than 180,000. It also publishes The Herald on Sunday , a portfolio of regional papers, and such magazines as The Listener . In Australia, APN runs more than a dozen daily papers in New South Wales and Queensland, along with some 75 community papers. The company also operates a number of news Web sites in conjunction with its papers. In addition to publishing, the company has outdoor advertising and radio broadcasting operations in Australia. UK-based Independent News & Media owns about 40% of the company.
