Ajax United Patterns and Molds can take plastic and turn it into something fantastic. The company, doing business as Ajax Custom Manufacturing, builds plastic and metal components for the medical, semiconductor, communications, and food processing industries. Services offered by the company include design and engineering, thermoforming, tooling and machining, plastic welding, and assembly and finishing. The company has two manufacturing facilities totaling 75,000 sq. ft. in California and a 2,000 sq. ft. logistics and distribution center in Texas. Ajax Custom Manufacturing, which was established in 1945, is noted for designing and building fresh pineapple peelers for Dole .
