It's gumball machines galore at Ford Gum & Machine's factory. The company makes and markets gumballs and gumball dispensers in classic and modern styles under the Carousel brand name. It also sells chewing gum and hard candy under such brand names as Big League Chew, Chunk-A-Chew, and Yowser!! Ford Gum & Machine sells its chewy confections through product distributors, retail outlets, and its website, as well as in bulk to vending companies. The company also offers made-to-order and private-label manufacturing services. Entrepreneur and former roofing salesman, Ford Mason, founded the company in 1913.
