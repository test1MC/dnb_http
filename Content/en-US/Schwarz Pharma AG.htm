For those with cardiovascular, neurological, and gastrointestinal problems: May the Schwarz may be with you. Germany-based Schwarz Pharma, which is part of Belgium's UCB , makes and markets a stable of prescription drugs that fall into one on those three therapeutic categories. Historically a generic drugmaker, the company has been moving into proprietary drug development, with a pipeline of experimental therapies focused mainly on neurological disorders including Parkinson's, epilepsy, diabetic neuropathic pain, and restless leg syndrome. Leading drugs include Neupro, a transdermal patch for the treatment of Parkinson's disease; Vimpat, for epilepsy; and Toviaz (marketed by Pfizer) for bladder problems.
