The next time you're waiting to see your doctor, you can thank TIDI Products for that paper gown you're wearing. The company manufactures disposable medical and dental products made from paper, gauze, cotton, and polyethylene, including gloves, capes, gowns, and exam table paper. TIDI Products also manufactures plastic films with medical and other commercial applications, as well as products for the foodservice market, such as aprons, gloves, and bags. The company markets its products through distributors worldwide. TIDI Products is a portfolio company of health care private equity firm RoundTable Healthcare Partners (which bought the company from J.H. Whitney Capital Partners in mid-2015).
