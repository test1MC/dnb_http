Chu nin chien kang! When this toast for good health is made, it’s often with a glass of Chinese rice wine in hand ... rice wine made by China Shaoxing Yellow Wine Group. One of the largest producers of rice wine in the country, China Shaoxing Yellow Wine Group makes more than 110,000 tons annually for domestic and overseas markets. The wine is used for cooking, drinking, and as a solvent for Chinese herbal medicated ointment. Sometimes referred to as the "Champion of the Famous Orient Wine," the company sells its products under the brands Gu Yue Long Shan and Shen Yong He. Shaoxing is in southeastern China where the subtropical climate is ideal for producing rice and grains, two of the wine’s key ingredients.
