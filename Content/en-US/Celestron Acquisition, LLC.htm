Celestron Acquisition has its eyes toward the skies. The company makes computerized and non-computerized telescopes, spotting scopes, binoculars (which can come equipped with digital cameras), and microscopes, as well as related optical accessories. Products are sold in the US and Canada through major retailers and specialty stores (including Canadian Tire , Dick's Sporting Goods , and Sixth Avenue Electronics ), as well as online. Celestron-branded gadgets are also available abroad through international distributors. The company got its start manufacturing telescopes in 1965. It is owned by Taiwan-based Synta Technology.
