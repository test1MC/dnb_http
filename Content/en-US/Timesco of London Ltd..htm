Timesco Healthcare (formerly Timesco of London) is a medical supplies company that designs, manufactures, and sells surgical and non-surgical medical and dental instruments. Its specialties include products for anesthesia, surgery, podiatry, and primary care. Besides instruments, Timesco also offers disposable gloves, medical equipment bags, the Cordura Exmouth Pioneer Bag for use by midwives to hold basic life support and home delivery equipment, and other medical supplies. Timesco uses its own direct sales force, as well as distributors and exhibit appearances, to serve the National Health Service (NHS) in the UK and the private sector worldwide. The family-owned company was founded in 1964.
