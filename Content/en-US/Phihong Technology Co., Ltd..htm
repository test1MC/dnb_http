Phihong Enterprise knows how to adapt. Founded in 1972, the company is a leading manufacturer of power supplies, voltage adapters, wires, and cables for a variety of electronic games, digital still cameras, and cell phones. It also offers a variety of products for transmitting electrical power over Ethernet-based data networks. Other products include hands-free devices, cradles, and battery chargers for cell phones. Customers have included companies such as Acer , Cisco , Hewlett-Packard , Panasonic , SANYO , and Sony . Phihong Enterprise has international offices in China, Taiwan, and the UK.
