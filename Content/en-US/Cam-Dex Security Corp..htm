Cam-Dex Security designs, assembles, installs, and maintains integrated security systems through its offices in Kansas and Missouri. Its products include access control, closed-circuit television, command and control, ID badging, intrusion detection, patient monitoring, surveillance, and X-ray and metal detection systems. The company offers service throughout the US and Canada through its affiliation with SecurityNet, a group of North American independent security systems integrators. Cam-Dex was founded in 1957.
