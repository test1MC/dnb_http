Malaysian Oxygen Berhad (MOX) has got moxie when it comes to producing industrial gas. As the largest gas producer in Malaysia, MOX manufactures and supplies industrial, medical, and specialty gases in addition to supplying welding electrodes and related equipment. The company's products are used in a variety of applications such as metal fabrication, medical oxygen and carbon dioxide, cryogenic freezing systems, glass furnaces, and iron and steel enrichment. MOX supplies its gas products through pipelines, in storage tanks (liquid form), or inside cylinders (gaseous form). It operates about 40 production and customer service sites nationwide and is part of German gas giant Linde .
