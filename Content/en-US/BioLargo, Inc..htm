After morphing identities several times over the past few years, BioLargo (formerly NuWay Medical) hopes that it has found its way. The company went from car dealership and casino ownership to being a medical device maker and application service provider (ASP). It believes it has found its future in designing sanitizing chemicals for specialty packaging (such as pads, protective liners, and surgical drapes) used for shipping blood, biohazardous materials, meat and poultry, and other items requiring sanitary containment. Consultant Kenneth Code controls 56% of BioLargo.
