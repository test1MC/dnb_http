Lancope keeps an unobtrusive eye out for network problems. The company's StealthWatch system uses flow data from existing routers and switches to help network administrators monitor performance, detect weaknesses, respond to threats, and improve communications across physical and virtual networks. Lancope markets its products worldwide to academic institutions, government agencies, and large organizations in the financial services, health care, retail, transportation, and manufacturing industries. The company counts networking equipment makers Cisco Systems and VMware among its partners. In 4Q 2015 Cisco became more than a partner when it bought Lancope for $452 million. The deal is expected to close in 2Q 2016.

Lancope has operations in the US, Europe, and Middle East, and serves customers around the world. It maintains sales offices in London and Dubai.

Using pervasive insight across distributed networks, including mobile, identity, and application awareness, Lancope improves forensic investigations, speeds up incident response, and reduces enterprise risk. The company holds seven US patents and more than 130 proprietary algorithms. Its technology partners include Cisco, Brocade , VMWare, 3Com , Palo Alto Networks , and Citrix .

Lancope expands its reach through direct, reseller, and distribution partners who serve customers such as Cisco, Alpha Natural Resources , Chick-fil-A , the Detroit Tigers , Gibraltar Industries , Electra Net, Oldcastle APG , and Westinghouse .

Bookings from new and existing customers increased 67% for Lancope, which did not disclose dollar figures. It said revenue rose 60% in 2013 as the size of its average deal tripled. As revenue rose, so did the size of its staff, doubling in 2013.

Lancope has benefited from trends such as virtualization, globalization, and user mobility that require networks to securely handle high data traffic and increasing complexity. The company has a number of technology partners and more than 700 customers worldwide, and is looking for continued growth in the health care, higher education, and government sectors. In 2014 the company launched its global Secure Partner Program to extend the capabilities of its StealthWatch System to additional resellers and their customers in the European and Asia Pacific regions.

The company was founded in 2000.
