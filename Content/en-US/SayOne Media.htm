Hennik Research (formerly SayOne Media) puts the 'fact' in manufacture. The company publishes The Manufacturer, a trade magazine that includes content related to the manufacturing industry. The title has both print and digital editions. Hennik also publishes Lean Management Journal , which addresses the management philosophy of minimizing waste and increasing efficiency within the manufacturing industry. Both publications provide industry news, analysis, case studies, and reviews. In addition, the company's ERP Connect is a conference for manufacturers that addresses topics on the use of enterprise software.

As well as its two trade magazines, Hennik Research runs industry events such as Solidworks and the Manufacturers Annual Leaders Conference. It also runs a thought-leadership network.

Hennik Research is based in London.
