Lincat ensures your food gets fried, toasted, or cooked. The company manufactures kitchen, bar, and food processing equipment through different brands. Under the Lincat brand name, it produces water boilers and electric and gas powered ranges, primarily for commercial use in pubs and restaurants. Its IMC brand covers commercial kitchen and bar machinery, including specialized coolers, vegetable peelers and choppers, as well as disposal units. Fans and other cooking ventilation products are found under its Britannia name. Lincat serves customers primarily in the UK and throughout Europe. The company is a subsidiary foodservice equipment maker The Middleby Corporation .

Lincat's range of over 450 items of commercial catering equipment is supplied through a network of dealers, both in the UK and overseas. Its dealers include Nisbets, Cater-Kwik, Hopkins, Nextday, LCE Online, and Shopequip. The company's cooking and warming equipment is used across all types of foodservice operations, including quick-service restaurants, full-service restaurants, convenience stores, retail outlets, hotels, and other institutions.
