Whether it's tea-time or cheese-time, Britannia Industries makes products that compliment the occasion. As one of India's largest biscuit manufacturers, Britannia produces more than 10 brands of savory and sweet biscuits (referred to crackers and cookies in the US) including NutriChoice, Good Day, and Marie Gold. The company also sells bread, cakes, chips, and rusk (hard, dry biscuits often dipped in hot beverages), as well as a line of cheeses and other dairy products. It targets its products primarily to consumers in India, but it also serves markets in the Middle East and Sri Lanka. Britannia is the food-production arm of Indian conglomerate, The Wadia Group.
