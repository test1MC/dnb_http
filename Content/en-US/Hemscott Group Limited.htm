You might say Hemscott's strength is in numbers. Hemscott Group Limited(formerly CoreData Group) provides data to such customers as financial institutions, major corporations, and data redistributors. It supplies information on publicly listed companies in the US, Canada, the UK, and Ireland, industry groups, and major stock markets. The company's data products include annual and quarterly financial information, pricing histories, stock tables, earning estimates, compensation, and historical index levels. In 2008 Morningstar acquired the Hemscott data, media, and investor relations Web site businesses from Ipreo Holdings for $52 million.
