When it comes to the private-label tools of retailers, what's theirs may be Jore's. Jore makes power tool accessories and hand tools under retailers' private labels and for power tool manufacturers. Products include drill sets, driver bits, quick-change systems, and related accessories. Customers have included Stanley Black & Decker , and Sears . In addition to private-label manufacturing, Jore markets products under its Rocky Mountain Twist and Montana Brand names. The company was founded in 1990. It was acquired out of bankruptcy in 2002 by Western Mortgage and Realty for about $30 million.

The company is based in Ronan, Montana, and has sales offices in Chicago and Atlanta.

Jore's retail products are sold under private labels to the industry's largest power tool retailers and manufacturers.
