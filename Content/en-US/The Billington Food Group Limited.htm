The Billington Food Group (dba Billington's) is a sweet proposition. The group supplies unrefined cane sugar, as well as unrefined fair trade and organic sugar for use in baking and as a sweetener for tea, fruit, or cereal. Its sugar selection includes dark and light brown, demerara, caster and granulated, icing, and muscovado (dark and light), as well as molasses. It also offers glac√© cherries. The fair trade sugar is produced by co-operatives in Malawi, Zambia, and Mauritius; other sugars are grown in Brazil. Billington's packs its sugar at its facility in Ipswich, for sale primarily to food retailers. Billington's is a brand of the Silver Spoon Co., owned by Associated British Foods .

The company is based in Liverpool, England and has distributors in Africa, Australia, Croatia, Cyprus, Egypt, Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, Germany, Greece, Holland, Hungary, Kuwait, Lebanon, Malta, Middle East (including Saudi Arabia), New Zealand, Norway, Portugal, Romania, Russia, Singapore, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden and Switzerland.

The company mainly sells its product through distributors which includes Kimco (Africa), GMA Imports (Egypt) and Delimondo Handels GmbH & Co.KG (Germany).

Among its roster of major food retailers, Billington's sweet lineup is available at ASDA , Morrisons , Safeway , Sainsbury's , Tesco , and Waitrose . Billington's products are also exported to the US, and other countries worldwide.

The company's history of supplying British-grown sugar reaches back more than 150 years. Although many sugar processors have focused on selling refined white sugar, Billington's has preserved the traditional unrefined cane sugar products, which are only partially refined and offer varying degrees of molasses-rich flavor and color. The company was first to market organic unrefined sugars and was among the first to participate in fair trade unrefined sugars, intended to allow fair prices for farmers.
