This company is a machinist for metalworks. Seco Tools manufactures metal-cutting products which cover four main areas of machining: milling, holding, turning, and holemaking. Items include milling cutters, carbide endmills, thread turners, drills, boring heads, reamers, and tooling systems. Seco Tools also manufactures an array of solid carbide drills, and its Secomax product line consists of a range of high-performance cutting tool grades developed for use on hard-to-machine metals. Some of its latest tool presetters feature digital imaging. The group holds strong market positions in Western Europe and North America and is investing in Asia and Eastern Europe. The company is a subsidiary of Sandvik .
