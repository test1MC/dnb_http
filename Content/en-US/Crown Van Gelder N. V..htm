Spider Man nemesis, Crown, has super strength, super speed, and the ability to alter his body into any shape or size he wishes. Crown Van Gelder, too, boasts super (financial) strength, super (production) speed, and the ability to transform its product offerings into anything its clients wish. Crown Van Gelder (CVG) is a paper manufacturer that targets customers in the graphics and publishing industries, as well as those in retail, transportation, and business services, primarily in the Netherlands. Its coated and uncoated papers are used for such varied end products as labels, packaging, airline tickets, marketing materials, business forms, and office papers.
