The Hogue family has lived off the land for more than 50 years, growing a wide assortment of crops durng that time, but Mike Hogue's experiment with grapes in 1979 marked the beginning of The Hogue Cellars. Today, the Washington State winery produces some 500,000 cases of wine per year under the Hogue, Genesis, and Reserve labels. Its offerings include cabernet sauvignon, chardonnay, gewürztraminer, merlot, pinot grigio, and syrah. They are available in the US as well as abroad. Vincor purchased The Hogue Cellars in 2001. In 2006 Vincor and its subsidiaries, including Hogue, were acquired by Constellation Brands .
