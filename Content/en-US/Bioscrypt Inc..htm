Security is only a touch away for this company's customers. Bioscrypt, which does business as L-1 Enterprise Access Solutions, provides fingerprint scanners and recognition software used for equipment and facility access control. Its Door to Desktop product line allows users access to both facilities and computer networks. In addition, L-1 Enterprise Access Solutions has a presence in the IT security market through its Cognizance Security subsidiary; its VeriSoft offering is an access management software application.  Bioscrypt has installed fingerprint readers for customers including American Express , Continental Airlines , NASA , NATO , and the US Army . The company was acquired by L-1 Identity Solutions in 2008.
