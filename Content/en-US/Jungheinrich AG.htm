Jungheinrich is getting rich from the development of urban jungles around the world. Jungheinrich is one of Europe's leading manufacturers of material handling equipment, such as pallet-lifting trucks and forklifts. Other products include high-lift order pickers, racks, reach trucks, and sidelift trucks. The company manufactures its products under the Jungheinrich and MIC brand names. It also services, leases, and resells equipment. Jungheinrich offers product logistics consulting, radio data transmission services, and warehouse administration software. The company sells its products worldwide directly, as well as through distributors and dealers.
