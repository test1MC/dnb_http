Cornelius understands the attraction of a frosty pint on a hot day. The company is a worldwide supplier of professional beverage dispensing and cooling equipment. It makes draft beer dispensers, bottle coolers, cellar equipment, ice makers, and refrigerated foodservice merchandisers. In addition to producing a stream of new equipment, Cornelius remanufactures legacy beverage dispensers. The company operates a dozen manufacturing facilities, located in six countries, with direct and authorized sales distributors crisscrossing the globe. Cornelius is a subsidiary of The Marmon Group, a Berkshire Hathaway company.

Cornelius has about 20 offices and six engineering centers in Mexico, the UK, Germany, China, the US, and Ukraine.

The company divides its operations across three strategic business lines: coling and dispense, merchandising and print, and supply chain.

The company serves markets including health and wellness; beer and cider; fountain dispensers and frozen. Customers have included McDonald's, Sara Lee Coffee and Tea, China Huiyuan Juice Group, and Vivreau.
