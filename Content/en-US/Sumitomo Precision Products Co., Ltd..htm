It's tough to say precisely how many industrial products Sumitomo Precision Products (SPP) makes. The company operates in two main divisions: aerospace and hydraulic equipment and heat control products. The aerospace division manufactures landing gear, propellers, and pneumatic components for engine and air systems. Hydraulic equipment includes parts such as pumps, valves, and cylinders as well as control systems. SPP's heat exchangers are used in Japan's bullet trains, electric power generation, and the oil and gas industry. In fact, SPP is the world's largest supplier of liquefied natural gas vaporizers. Other divisions include environmental systems and microelectronics technology. Most of its sales are in Japan.
