This company believes in the power of words. Electric Word publishes newsletters, magazines, websites, books, and special reports covering a range of industries. The company also conducts research and produces events. The company operates through three divisions: Education, Health, and Sport & Gaming. Most of the company's content primarily addresses topics in the sport, education, and healthcare sectors. Electric Word streamlined its operation by disposing of several non-core activities in recent years. Revenues come from subscriptions, advertising and sponsorship, exhibition space at events, custom publishing, and sales of third-party products. The company was founded in 2000.
