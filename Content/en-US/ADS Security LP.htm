ADS Security is a leading provider of security alarms and monitoring services for commercial and residential customers primarily in the Southeast. The company installs and monitors alarm systems for more than 70,000 customers. In addition to burglar and fire alarms, ADS Security offers carbon monoxide alarm systems, medical alert monitoring, and video surveillance services. Founded in 1989, ADS Security has branch offices in about a dozen states.
