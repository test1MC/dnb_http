Beer lovers hanging ten on the big island can pull up a chair at the Kona Brewing Company, which operates three pubs -- two on Hawaii's big island and the second in Hawaii Kai, Oahu. Kona Brewing produces a number of craft beers including Castaway, Fire Rock, Longboard, Big Wave, and Wailua. The company's draft-only brews include Black Sand, Duke's Blond, and Hula Hefeweizen. Kona's beverages are available in Japan and throughout the US. The brewery, which produces more than 12,000 barrels of beer per year, has a sales and marketing agreement with Craft Brewers Alliance , which distributes its beer on the mainland, as does Anheuser-Busch . Kona launched its first organic beer in 2009.
