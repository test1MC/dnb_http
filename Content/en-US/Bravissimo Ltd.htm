Before you know it, Bravissimo will have everyone applauding and shouting "bra-va." The company designs, manufactures, and distributes bras, swimsuits, and other lingerie in a variety of styles. It caters to women who are well-endowed above the waist, offering items for ladies with cup sizes ranging from D-KK. The intimates apparel company also provides a line of clothing for the same target customer that sells under the Pepperberry name. Bravissimo sells women's apparel in the UK through some 20 retail shops, through its website, and by mail order catalog. Founded in 1995 by Sara Tremellen in her living room, the company opened its first retail store four years later.
