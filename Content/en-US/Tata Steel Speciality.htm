Tata Steel Speciality specializes in manufacturing high-performance steel products for specialist markets worldwide. A unit of India-based Tata Steel , it makes high-grade steel -- including alloy, carbon, and stainless -- designed to meet the specifications of markets such as aerospace, oil and gas, industrial bearings, and automotive industries (primarily heavy goods vehicles). It uses electric arc furnace steelmaking and mill processing to produce ingots, billets, and bars. Its aerospace steel is used for landing gear, engine shafts, casings, and other aircraft components.

Headquartered in the UK, the company has two manufacturing sites in Rotherham and Wednesbury.

The company serves industries such as Aerospace, Automotive, Construction, Consumer Products, Energy & Power, Lifting & Excavating, and Packaging.
