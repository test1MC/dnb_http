P.L. Thomas & Co. provides flavor for thought. The company makes ingredients for the food, nutraceutical, and cosmetic industries. It provides hydrocolloids ( i.e. , water-soluble gums), stabilizers, flavors, and other ingredients for food manufacturers. It also offers botanical extracts, specialty nutraceuticals, herbal powders, mineral gluconates, and other items for nutritional supplement makers. For cosmetic maters it offers oils, vitamins, antioxidants, collagen producers, and ceramides. The company provides global sourcing, custom ingredient manufacturing, and product development services. P.L. Thomas was founded by Marvin Flowerman and Paul Greene in 1955 and is owned by the Flowerman family.
