Eaglemoss Publications keeps a sharp eye on what readers like. Eaglemoss specializes in partworks, an industry unique to the UK. Partworks are adult and youth magazine-style, special-interest series covering such subjects as toys, collectibles, and hobbies. (Partworks are distinguished from regular magazines by their collectibility and limited duration; for example, a series may be designed to have an issue each month for six months only.) Eaglemoss Publications' partworks cover such topics as popular movies ( The Lord of the Rings ), crafts for kids, and birding. Some series are marketed with collectible figurines or craft materials. The firm was founded in 1978 by Patrick Cavendish and Beverley Hilton.
