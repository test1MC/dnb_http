Keystone Powdered Metal knows how to get its business in gear. The company designs and manufactures powdered metal (PM) products, primarily for the automotive industry. Its lineup includes bearings, gears and gear components, magnetic actuators, pinions, diodes, hot forged parts, and sprockets. Other customers include home appliance, outdoor power equipment, and power tool manufacturers. PM technology allows powdered metal to be formed into shapes that produce minimal scrap and require less machining. Keystone Powdered Metal traces its historical roots all the way back to 1927.

The company maintains two manufacturing facilities in Pennsylvania and one in North Carolina. Keystone also distributes its products in Asia, Canada, Central and South America, Europe, and Mexico.

The company's products are widely used in automotive applications, appliance applications, outdoor power equipment applications, and industrial production applications. Some of its customers include Ford Motor and Chrysler .

Keystone claims to generate $70 million in annual sales. Of this amount, 90% comes from the automotive sector.
