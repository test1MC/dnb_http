The Mobility Depot makes a variety of electrical powered wheelchairs, lifts, scooters, and related products for the handicapped. The company offers a luxury, gentle, and casual line of lift chairs. Mobility Depot also provides accessories such as crutch holders, headlights, canopies, and bumpers. The company has six locations in Louisiana and Alabama.
