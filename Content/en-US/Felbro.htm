Felbro displays don't come right out and say "Buy Me!," but customers still get the idea. The company designs, manufactures, and markets temporary and permanent point-of-purchase displays and fixtures. Products are made using acrylic, molded plastics, sheet metal, tubing, and wood to catch buyer's eyes, and hopefully their money, too. The company's customers have included Pepsi , Nestle , Kwikset , Clorox , Shimano, West Marine , and Frito-Lay . Felbro was founded in 1945.
