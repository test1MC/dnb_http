AnaCon Foods offers nuts and sweets of a different variety. The company manufactures snacks foods that taste nutty, but are made from wheat germ and contain no actual nuts. It also manufactures flavor-particulate products for use by other food manufacturers as extenders and replacements for nut pieces, natural fruit, and sweet flavor bits. AnaCon's brands include Bits-O-Flavor, Bowlby's Bits, Mix-Ups, Nuts'N'Pops, and Wheat Nuts. It also sells caramel corn, snack mixes, and gourmet chocolates, peanut brittle, and chocolate-covered potato chips under the Bowlby's label. In addition to its own brands, AnaCon offers custom product formulation.
