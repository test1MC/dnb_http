The squeaky wheel is said to get the grease -- actually it's Lincoln Industrial's lubrication system. The global manufacturer builds lubrication equipment and fluid pumping products to help modify friction, improve fuel economy, reduce noise, and lengthen component life. Among its products are general lubrication pumps and grease guns, heavy-duty pumps for handling viscous materials, and automatic lubrication systems to direct the right amount of grease or oil to bearings or other close-fitting points. In late 2010 Sweden's Aktiebolaget SKF (SKF) purchased Lincoln Industrial for $1 billion from manufacturing and distributing conglomerate Harbour Group Industries .
