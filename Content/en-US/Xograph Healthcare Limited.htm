Xograph Healthcare is a specialist company that supplies medical imaging and radiography equipment to health care providers involved in general diagnostic radiography, mammography, and mobile imaging. In addition to X-ray equipment made by other companies, Xograph designs and manufactures quality control instruments for X-ray equipment. It also provides technical support for its equipment. The company's sales efforts focus on the NHS , private and military hospitals, and primary care trusts in the UK and Ireland. Other clients include chiropractors, veterinary practices, labs, forensic associations, and industrial users. Xograph sells its products in more than 35 countries.

The company's medical imaging systems and services include general, fluoroscopic, mammographic, dental imaging, surgical, and mobile imaging equipment, as well as health care information technology and direct digital imaging solutions. Other offerings include veterinary X-ray equipment, such as CR imaging, cephalostat and panoramic imaging systems, field deployable imaging systems, and quality and research and solutions. Xograph also partners with specialist medical and veterinary finance providers to finance and lease equipment.

Besides providing equipment, Xograph provides support and assistance to such associations as The Association of Forensic Radiographers by quickly responding to natural disasters and terrorist attacks, deploying portable digital radiography (DR) systems to affected areas. The company also contributes to scientific and research groups, as in its participation in the Discovery Channel 's expedition into Egypt to identify Queen Nefertiti and in the University of St. Andrews ' studies of Scottish salmon.

Customers include the NHS, private and military hospitals, primary care trusts, chiropractors, laboratories, forensic associations, and industrial clients.

In 2014 Xograph and its partner, Delft DI (part of Canon ), launched new versions of Triathlon and Intuition DR systems; the new systems feature a 10.4" interactive color touchscreen display and image preview capability.

Also that year, partner Ziehm launched the new Ziehm Vision RFD 3D, which combines 2D and 3D functionality for added versatility.
