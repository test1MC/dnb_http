The buffet line, and all its patrons, would be lost without The Delfield Company. Delfield makes foodservice equipment, including plate dispensers, mobile serving counters, blast chillers, and refrigerated and heated display cases, serving counters, and reach-in refrigerators. Its lineup features a green series of reach-ins designed for durability, performance, and reduced energy consumption, along with an eco-friendly foam-insulation. The company's products are typically customized to fit the specific needs of hotels, institutions, and restaurants. Delfield operates as a part of the Manitowoc Foodservice family of brand names.

Delfield sells its products through dealers, sales representatives, and dealers on the Internet in the US and overseas.

Delfield traces its historical roots to its founding in 1949.
