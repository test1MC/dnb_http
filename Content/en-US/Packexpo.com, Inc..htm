Need to sell some packaging products, or need to buy some packaging products? Packexpo.com, a Web site devoted to the packaging industry, brings packaging product suppliers and buyers together online. The company also bundles news with industry information and resources as well as offering networking opportunities for users. Users of Packexpo's Web site can research products and the companies that make them, conduct price comparisons, contact potential clients, and arrange transactions, as well as catch up on the latest industry headlines.
