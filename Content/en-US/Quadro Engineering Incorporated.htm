Quadro Engineering keeps things moving. The company's Solids and Liquids divisions manufacture equipment for fluid mixing, dispersion, emulsifying, and milling (crushing methods), while its Material Handling division makes pneumatic conveying and lifting products -- for use by customers in the food production, pharmaceutical, chemical, cosmetics, and biotech industries. Brand names include Quadro, Comil, Ytron, and Quadro Vac. The company offers installation and testing services. It has operations in Canada and the US, with sales representatives worldwide. Quadro Engineering was acquired in 2007 by IDEX for $31 million in cash; it operates as a stand-alone unit of IDEX's Fluid & Metering Technologies segment.
