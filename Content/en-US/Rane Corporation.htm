Julie Andrews or not, Rane Corporation is into the sound of music. The company manufactures mid-range professional audio equipment including compressors, equalizers, mixers, preamplifiers, network products, amplifiers (headphone and loudspeaker), transformers, and accessories. Rane, which was founded in 1981, gets its name from an anagram that was created from the names of company's original founders. Rane celebrated its 25th anniversary in 2006.
