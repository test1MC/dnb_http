Kelley Foods of Alabama is a leading meat processor and distributor serving foodservice customers, convenience stores, and retail locations in parts of Alabama, Florida, Georgia, and Mississippi. The company makes a variety of pork and beef products, including bacon, ham, sausage, and beef patties, as well as processed products made from turkey. In addition to food products, Kelley Foods distributes such items as paper packaging and kitchen supplies. It also sells gift and holiday food packages to consumers through its Web site. Lester Kelley started the family-owned business in 1957. In 2016 food service distributor Ben E. Keith Foods agreed to acquire the company.
