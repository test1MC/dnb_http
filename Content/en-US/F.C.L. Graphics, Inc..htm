F.C.L. Graphics wants to give its customers some TLC. The commercial printer, which operates as FCL Graphics, specializes in promotional and marketing materials for retailers, direct-mail marketers, and financial services providers. It offers digital prepress, color control, bindery, finishing, and fulfillment services for such clients as the Chicago Tribune , eBay , Sears , and Wells Fargo . FCL Graphics owns and operates several types of presses, including sheet-fed, half-web, and full-web. The company was founded in 1973 by former chairman and CEO Frank Calabrese. It has since grown into one of the largest direct marketing printers in the US, logging some $60 million in annual sales.
