Buses and heavy-duty trucks are the stock and trade of Hino Motors Sales. The company manufactures and distributes heavy trucks and buses throughout Thailand. Hino also produces and supplies auto parts to several Southeast Asian countries. A domestic network of sales and service centers supports Hino's Thai operations, along with a mobile service operation. A subsidiary of Japan's Hino Motors , Hino first began supplying buses to Thailand in 1952. The company was officially established 10 years later as the exclusive distributor (and later, manufacturer) of Hino trucks and buses in the country.
