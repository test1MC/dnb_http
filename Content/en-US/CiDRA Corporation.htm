What's down that pipe? CiDRA can help you tell. The company offers process monitoring through the combination of optical component technology and its SONARtrac line of process measuring instruments, used in mineral processing, paper and pulp manufacturing, oilsands, power generation, and other industrial applications. SONARtrac keeps track of gases, liquids, slurries, and various fluids. The company has 250 customers in over 20 countries; three include ArcelorMittal , Anglo American , and Kairos Mining. Founded by president and CEO Kevin Didden in 1996, CiDRA has received funding for its work from Axiom Venture Partners , Cisco , and Morgan Stanley .
