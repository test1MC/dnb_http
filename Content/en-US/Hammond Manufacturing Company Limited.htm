Hammond Manufacturing makes enclosures -- not playpens or corrals, but the kind that enclose electrical and electronic components. Its offerings include aluminum and stainless steel box enclosures and mounting racks. Along with these goods, the company offers electrical components including electronic transformers, surge suppressors, and outlet strips. The company spun off its electrical transformer group in 2001 as Hammond Power Solutions . Hammond Manufacturing has facilities in Australia, Canada, the UK, and the US. The company owns about 40% of Taiwan-based enclosure manufacturer RITEC Enclosures; the company distributes RITEC products through its worldwide sales network.
