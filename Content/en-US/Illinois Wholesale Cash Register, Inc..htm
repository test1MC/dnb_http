Ka-ching! The sound of cash registers is music to Illinois Wholesale Cash Register (IWCR). The company sells and services refurbished point-of-sale (POS) equipment from such manufacturers as IBM , MICROS Systems , NCR , and Panasonic , among others. Products include POS terminals, processors, scanners, printers, keyboards, and spare parts for that equipment. IWCR also offers printed circuit board repair and technical support services. Established in 1976, the company serves clients in such industries as hospitality, supermarkets, and general retail. Al Moorhouse, the president of IWCR, owns the company.
