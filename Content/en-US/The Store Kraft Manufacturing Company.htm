Store Kraft wants retailers to display their merchandise on one of its pedestals. Store Kraft Manufacturing designs and creates custom store fixtures such as metal and wood retail shelves, display cases, and racks. The company's clients have included major retailers such as Banana Republic , Cabela's , Foot Locker , and the Hilton hotel chain. The firm's Shanghai-based subsidiary, Gaohuo, supplies wood and metal retail store fixtures to China and international markets. Store Kraft began in 1920 when candy retailer William Morton, frustrated with product presentation in his own store, sketched out an idea for superior candy displays and began selling them to interested buyers.
