Yell Publicidad, formerly Telefónica Publicidad e Información (TPI), publishes the Spanish telephone directories Páginas Amarillas and Páginas Blancas and operates the online Spanish yellow pages (PaginasAmarillas.es) and white pages (PaginasBlancas.es). In addition the company operates sites such as RestaurantesHoy.com (restaurant listings), and CalleaCalle.com, which gives information about more than 400,000 retail outlets and includes georeferencing. The company also provides directory assistance through its 11 888 telephone service. Outside Spain the firm serves Argentina, Chile, and Peru with similar directory and other informational products. Yell Publicidad is 95%-owned by Yell Group .
