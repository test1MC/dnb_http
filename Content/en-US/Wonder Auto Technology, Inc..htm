Every journey has a beginning, and Wonder Auto Technology makes products that get the journey started. Through its operating subsidiaries, Wonder Auto designs and manufactures starters, alternators, and other automotive electrical parts for passenger vehicles. It also manufactures rods and shafts, which are used in suspension and shock-absorber systems. Although the company earns some of its sales revenue from international customers, most of Wonder Auto's products are sold to OEMs operating in China. Some of its larger customers include Chery Automobile Co . and Shanghai VW , as well as Chinese subsidiaries of Mitsubishi Motors and Hyundai Motor Company .
