Agfa-Gevaert Ltd. is no digital dinosaur. Although the company (aka Agfa UK) made its mark in photographic films, today it develops, manufactures, and sells analog and digital imaging systems, workflow management software, and other related IT products. It targets the printing and healthcare industries through businesses Agfa Graphics and Agfa HealthCare. Agfa UK also makes film-based products and other related high-tech components for industrial applications through Agfa Specialty Products. A subsidiary of Belgian imaging technology group Agfa-Gevaert NV , Agfa UK runs one of its parent's major production centers in Leeds. Gevaert started in the UK in 1909, and Agfa in 1950; the two companies merged in 1964.
