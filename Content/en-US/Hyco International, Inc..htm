What do a CAT ( Caterpillar ) and a Deere ( Deere & Company ) have in common? They both keep moving with a little help from Hyco International, a leading maker of hydraulic cylinders used by OEMs in construction, material handling, waste handing, and agricultural equipment. The company's customers, some of which are also competitors, also include AB Volvo , J C Bamford , and Dover Corporation . Hyco's single-stage and multi-stage hydraulic cylinders end up in products including excavators, backhoes, forklifts, garbage trucks, and combine harvesters.

The company has manufacturing plants in Brazil, Canada, Germany, and the US.

Hyco's products are used in forestry and construction machinery, refuse handling equipment, dump trucks, oil and gas field installations, and cranes and mining and agricultural equipment.

Hyco has identified a number of key strategies aimed at growth and increased profitability. The company wants to capture more business from existing customers, especially those with in-house hydraulic cylinder manufacturing capability.

At the same time the company wants to increase sales to regional heavy equipment component distributors to gain more OEM customers as well as a strengthened presence in the hydraulic cylinder replacement market. Hyco is also keeping its eye out for acquisition opportunities -- including the hydraulic cylinder manufacturing operations of some OEMs.

Hyco was founded in 1988. It filed for an IPO in 2006 but withdrew its registration statement in early 2007.
