At factories in China, Finland, Indonesia, Sweden, and the UK, Evox Rifa makes electrolytic, paper, and film capacitors. Electrolytic capacitors are used in automotive, consumer and industrial electronics, lighting, power-supply, and other products. Film capacitors are used in electrical and electronic equipment for precision, pulse, and electromagnetic interference (EMI) suppression, among other uses. Paper capacitors are used for EMI suppression in power supplies for such products as TVs, industrial machines, and medical equipment. Customers include Valeo and Vacon . In 2007 KEMET acquired Evox Rifa for more than €21 million in cash, plus the assumption of debt.
