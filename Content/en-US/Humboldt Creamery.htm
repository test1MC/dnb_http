Foster Dairy Farms churns out milk and more, primarily in northern California. The dairy processor owns the Crystal Creamery and Humboldt (organic products) brands; it also owns the Mccoll's Dairy Products brand. Its two processing plants produce fluid and powdered milk, ice cream, cultured dairy products, and juices. The company is also a co-packer for other dairies. Customers include McDonald's , Wal-Mart , Costco , and Trader Joe's . Foster Dairy Farms was founded in 1941, but its dairy brands date back to the 1901 (Crystal Creamery) and the 1920s (Humboldt).
