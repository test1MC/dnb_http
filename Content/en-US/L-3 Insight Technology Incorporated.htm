L-3 Insight Technology works to keep its business on target. The company makes and markets tactical weapon lights and lasers adaptable to most handguns, shotguns, and rifles. The company's products are designed for use by military and law enforcement personnel. L-3 Insight Technology's gun-mounted aids, which are activated by a button or pressure switch, assist its customers in shot placement and target identification. Its products are sold through dealers. The majority of L-3 Insight Technology's sales are generated by military and police contracts. The firm, which was acquired by L-3 Communications in April 2010, was founded by engineer Kenneth Solinsky in the 1980s.
