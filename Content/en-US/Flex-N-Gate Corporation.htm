Flex-N-Gate makes its easier for drivers to Sit-N-Drive. The manufacturing company makes metal and plastic automotive components and assemblies, such as bumpers, grilles, hinges, interior panels and pillars, latches, pedal systems, parking brakes, and running boards. The company also offers prototyping, mechanical assembly, and sequencing services. Flex-N-Gate makes products for a wide range of car platforms; customers have included such big names as BMW , Chrysler , Ford , Honda , General Motors , Toyota , and Volvo . Flex-N-Gate was formed in 1956.

The company divides its operations across several divisions: Low-Run and Prototyping, Front End Module (FEM), Plastics, Metals, and Lighting.

The company's name comes from Flex-N-Gate's first product: a stock rack for pickup trucks that included a roll-up rear gate. More recent offerings have included step bumpers with integrated tow hitches.
