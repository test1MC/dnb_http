Founded in 1856 in Sacramento, H.S. Crocker Co. is a commercial printer, focusing on food and specialty printing as well as pharmaceutical cartons and labels. The company's products include foil lids for dairy products, such as yogurt, and well as non-foil lidding for other types of containers. It also makes thermoformed and injection molded product-specific cups.
