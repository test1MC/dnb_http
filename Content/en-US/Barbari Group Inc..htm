Products from Barbari Group may not give you that new-car smell, but it can give you a new-car look. The company manufactures products for the automotive aftermarket industry, including seat, car, dash, truck bed, and spare tire covers; car bras; floor mats; and tailgate nets. Barbari's products are designed for passenger cars, light trucks, vans, and SUVs, and fit a variety of domestic and import makes and models from the 1970s to today. The company also makes covers and accessories for ATVs motorcycles, boats, watercraft, and snowmobiles. Founded in 1999 by president Mansur "Manny" Barbari, the company operates an office in Boise, Idaho, and one in Cairo, Egypt.
