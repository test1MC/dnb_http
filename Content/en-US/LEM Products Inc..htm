LEM Products offers game-processing equipment and supplies for hunters who want to do their own thing after the big take-down. Its products are also sold at sporting goods stores (including Gander Mountain and Academy Sports & Outdoors ) and other North American retailers, as well as through its catalog and Web site. LEM's inventory includes meat grinders, sausage casings, burger presses, skinning knives, vacuum sealers, dehydrators, and tanning supplies, along with instruction books and videos. Among its more colorfully named products is the Jerky Cannon, which doesn't launch but makes jerky. (No heavy artillery involved -- it looks similar to a caulking gun.) CEO Larry Metz and his family own the company.
