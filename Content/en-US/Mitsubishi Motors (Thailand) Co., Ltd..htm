Mitsubishi Motors (Thailand) Co., Ltd. (MMTh and formerly MMC Sittipol Co.), the Thai subsidiary of Japan's Mitsubishi Motors , builds and distributes Mitsubishi passenger cars and pickup trucks. MMTh operates manufacturing plants in Thailand that produce vehicles for the Thai domestic market (about 10%) and for worldwide export (about 90%). Models include the Lancer sedan, the Pajero Sport SUV, the Space Wagon, and the Triton and Triton Plus pickup trucks. Founded originally as MMC Sittipol Co. in 1987, the company's named was changed to Mitsubishi Motors (Thailand) Co., Ltd. in 2003.
