Sellers Publishing publishes calendars, books, journals, greeting and holiday cards, and e-cards and invitations, which are sold through the company's Web site, RSVP.com. It also offers print catalogs for its calendars and greeting cards. Sellers Publishing's calendar selection includes wall, daily, and miniature versions, as well as wall and weekly planners; its books include address books, wedding and humorous books, as well as other specialty gift books. The company was established in 1994 by founder Ronnie Sellers. Before launching its Web site in 1994, Sellers Publishing was solely dedicated to visual media publishing.
