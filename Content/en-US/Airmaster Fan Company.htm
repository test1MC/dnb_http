Airmaster Fan creates products to help master the elements. The company manufactures fans, including air circulators, blowers, and ventilators. Commercial and industrial products include ceiling fans, pedestal fans, panel fans, man coolers, duct blowers, intake louvers, exhaust fans, and roof ventilators. Airmaster Fan makes its products in Mississippi, warehouses them at facilities across the US, and sells them throughout the world; it markets its products through a network of affiliates. The company traces its roots back to one of the first patented electrical fan makers in the 1800s, Diehl Fan.
