Biopharmaceutical company Genfit zeroes in on inflammatory, cardiovascular, and metabolic diseases with the development of drugs and supplemental anti-inflammatory applications. Genfit employs more than 100 scientists and partners with major drug companies including Sanofi , Merck , Pierre-Fabre, and Servier Laboratories. It's associated research center Cardialpha helps Genfit's clinical development operations maintain a more efficient cost structure than other businesses. Established in 1999, the company owns and operates facilities in France and the US.
