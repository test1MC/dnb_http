Nomadix doesn't want you to ever have to suffer without Internet access, no matter where you wander. The company provides wireless LAN equipment (modems and routers) and support software for Wi-Fi distributors, including service providers and venue operators. Its flagship Nomadix Service Engine (NSE) product, with more than 50,000 units sold, supports clients' billing, authentication, wholesale, and roaming activities within wireless networks. The company's networking equipment is used to enable Wi-Fi hot spots in such settings as retail shops, hospitals, hotels, and apartment buildings. Nomadix is a subsidiary of NTT DoCoMo 's interTouch unit, a provider of technology products to the hospitality industry.
