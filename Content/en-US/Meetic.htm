Want to meet your amour? Your amor? Your amore? Let Meetic help. The Group operates one of Europe's leading online dating services through regional and international brands: Meetic, MeeticAffinity, Match.com (Europe), Lexa, LoveScout24, Neu.de, and Secret.de. The Web matchmaker is established in over 15 European countries and Latin America, and is available in a dozen-plus languages. Its sites are free to join and browse, but charge a fee to contact other members. The group also offers dating categories (sexuality, religion, nationality, race), and hosts singles events in metropolitan areas. Since its launch in 2001, some 28 million profiles have been registered with Meetic. Match Group acquired Meetic in 2011.
