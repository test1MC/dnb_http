Can you imagine a fuel cell-powered bicycle? Palcan can. Palcan Fuel Cells is a development-stage company that makes hydrogen storage products for laboratory demonstrations, but has plans to make products for electric bikes and scooters. Other products in the works include portable and backup power supplies and auxiliary power plants for engines in trucks and recreation vehicles. Europe and the US have been targeted for early product launches, but Asia (particularly China, Japan, South Korea, and Taiwan) will be Palcan's primary market for scooters and bicycles. The current incarnation of Palcan was formed in early 2002 after a reverse takeover by Cosworth Minerals.
