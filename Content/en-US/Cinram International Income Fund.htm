Like the music and movies it reproduces, Cinram International Income Fund has changed with the times. Established in 1969 as a maker of eight-track cassettes, the firm is now the largest provider of pre-recorded multimedia products (mostly DVDs and Blu-ray formats) for movie studios, music labels, video game publishers, and software firms in North America and Europe. It also has packaging and distribution operations, and provides distribution and logistics services to telecommunications firms through its wireless subsidiaries.  Najafi Companies, a private investment firm whose portfolio companies include Direct Brands, and SkyMall, has announced plans to acquire Cinram.
