Hornby's products have remained on track for almost 100 years. The company makes model railways, miniature trains, and other vehicles mainly for the collector and hobby markets. Hornby boasts more than 650 products and its primary brands include Hornby and Scalextric. The company was founded in 1907 by Frank Hornby as Meccano Ltd. More than 70% of Hornby's sales are generated within the UK. Hornby expanded into the Spanish and Italian markets through its purchase of Madrid-based Electrotren, as well as Italian-based model railway maker Lima, whose brands include Lima, Rivarossi, Jouef, Arnold, and Pocher.
