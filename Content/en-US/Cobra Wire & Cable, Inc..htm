Like its reptilian namesake, Cobra Wire & Cable is easily coiled. The company makes insulated wire and cable products used in range of industries, including aerospace, appliance, automotive, telecommunications, and utilities. Its product applications include cables for battery systems, boats, diesel locomotives, fiber optics, high-voltage power lines, mining, special construction, robotics, and submersables Cobra Wire & Cable also provides custom-made specialty wires. In late 2011, Cobra Wire & Cable was acquired by EIS , an electrical products distributor owned by Genuine Parts Company . It now operates as a division of EIS.
