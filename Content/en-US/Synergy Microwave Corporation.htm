Synergy Microwave makes a host of electronic components, including couplers, filters, mixers, modulators/demodulators, switches, transformers and voltage-controlled oscillators. The company began putting components into surface-mount packaging, which has the advantage of making slimmer printed circuit boards, in 1985. The company was founded in 1982. Synergy Microwave is owned by Ulrich Rohde, the company's founder and chairman, and by Meta Rohde, its president.
