Talan Products uses its talent to extrude aluminum, fabricate tubes, and stamp metals. Founded in 1986, the company offers custom metal stamping, tube forming, and tube fabrication services; it also provides machine processing services such as bending, countersinking, drilling, and welding. Talan Products makes a range of tooling and engineered parts for customers in the automotive, construction, consumer appliance, defense, and hardware markets. Talan Products' primary business is mid- to high-volume metal stampings. Talan Products was founded in 1986 with one metal stamping die, running production for a single customer.
