Unicity International prescribes health and wealth. Unicity distributes nutritional supplements and skin care products through a multilevel marketing network of independent contractors who sell direct to consumers. Unicity's products include Bios Life branded weight management products, and a variety of herbal remedies for allergies, anti-aging, diabetes, digestive problems, heart health, and stress reduction. As a management-controlled entity, Unicity operates offices in about 20 locations and sells its products in more than 30 countries.

In addition to its US headquarters in Utah, the company has offices in countries including Australia, Canada, Columbia, Hong Kong, India, Indonesia, Japan, Laos, Malaysia, Mexico, New Zealand, Nigeria, the Philippines, Singapore, South Korea, Switzerland, Taiwan, Thailand, and Venezuela.

Unicity markets its products through its contracted franchise representatives, as well as partners in international markets. The company also hosts seminars and events to increase brand awareness.

The firm has expanded in recent years by developing new products and widening its geographic presence into new markets. Unicity has also increased marketing and promotional efforts for its offerings. New product offerings including two natural energy drinks launched in 2011 that work to boost mental focus (Bios Life Matcha) and manage glucose levels (Bios Life Slim G), as well as the Be PREMIUM personal skin care line introduced in 2012. International growth included establishing businesses in Indonesia and Laos during 2012, as well as the opening of new Canadian and Indian offices in 2013.

The company enhanced its web and mobile applications with the launch of the Unicity Office and Unicity Sales apps in 2012 and the redesigned Unicity.com site in 2013.

Unicity was formed in 2001 when network marketing firms Rexall Showcase International and Enrich International merged. Formerly known as Unicity Network, it changed its name to Unicity International in 2003 when the company's management severed ties with Royal Numico and took the company private.
