Election Systems & Software (ES&S) casts its vote in favor of e-lections. The largest voting machine company in the US provides systems used to manage all stages of elections, including voter registration, ballot creation, voting, and tabulation. Its products include voting terminals with features such as touchscreens and paper audits, as well as ballot counters. Its election management software helps voting officials maintain databases, format ballots, and program election equipment. ES&S also provides maintenance and support services. Its equipment supports elections in 4,500 election jurisdiction in more than 41 US states and four countries, including Canada.

ES&S operates through two units. Data Information Management Systems (DIMS), in turn, has the DIMS.net suite of election management software, which runs on Microsoft products. The units offers customers election management, optical voting, scanning, imaging, ICR, GIS, bar code recognition, report writing, and office automation. The company's FileOne unit provides back-office and online business service systems for sectaries of state throughout the US. It offers software and services through SystemWORKS, and consulting services division, ServiceWORKS.

The company is based in Omaha, Nebraska and has operations in Canada.

The company's strategy is to offer products and services that enable elections to be held with accuracy and trust with a variety of voting formats. In 2014 the company launched New ExpressPass Technology, which offers greater convenience and flexibility to voters. Also in 2014, ES&S won a contract to provide the City of Chicago's 2,069 precincts with electronic poll books.
