Stanadyne's products give engines their growl. The company manufactures fuel pumps for gas and diesel engines, and injectors for diesel engines. The line focuses on diesel engine components used by OEMs of agricultural and industrial off-highway equipment. The company also makes fuel heaters and oil pumps for diesel engines, and related parts distributed by diesel engine aftermarkets. On an exclusive contract basis, its services are tapped by businesses for precision manufacturing, assembly, and testing.

The company serves customers around the world from manufacturing centers in China, India, Italy, the United Arab Emirates, and the US.

The company's engine applications serve agriculture, construction, power generation, industrial, marine, and on-highway vehicles. Customers have included such big names as Deere , General Engine Products , and Cummins .

Stanadyne extended its geographic footprint in 2016 with the opening of its first facility in the Middle East. The new location in Sharjah caters to the Gulf Cooperation Council states (Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, the United Arab Emirates, Qatar, Bahrain, and Oman), Pakistan, and Turkey. The company also plans to double its capacity in three years.

In addition, the company in 2016 acquired the remaining equity in its joint venture company Stanadyne Amalgamations Private Limited from The Amalgamations Group (an Indian company comprised of 47 companies and focuses operations on engineering industries including automotive). The joint venture, which was formed in 2001, was renamed as Stanadyne India Private Limited after the acquisition.

Stanadyne was founded in 1876 as the Hartford Machine Screw Company.
