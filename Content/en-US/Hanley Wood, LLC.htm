Founded in 1976, Hanley Wood has built a big business one page and show at a time. Operating through four divisions, it serves the building industry with publications and exhibitions. Its Business Media division publishes some 30 magazines and trade journals, including Builder, residential architect , and EcoHome , that target the residential and commercial construction markets. The unit also publishes e-newsletters (BuilderJobs) and websites (Builder Online). Its Exhibition division produces trade shows, conferences, and other industry events, such as World of Concrete . Hanley Wood Marketing offers marketing communication services and Hanley Wood Market Intelligence provides real estate research and information.

Based in Washington, DC, Hanley Wood has offices in Chicago; Dallas; Los Angeles; Minneapolis; Costa Mesa, California; and Williston, Vermont.

Hanley Wood operates a handful of divisions: Business Media, Exhibition, Marketing, and Market Intelligence.

The company is working to expand its business and portfolio of services through acquisitions. In 2013 Hanley Wood entered a strategic partnership with the US Green Building Council whereby Hanley Wood acquired the group's Greenbuild International Conference and Expo. Also in 2013 Hanley Wood purchased Metrostudy, one of the nation's top providers of primary and secondary market information to the housing and related industries. The move further strengthens the company's commitment to providing customers with actionable information, tools, and knowledge. Hanley Wood intends to fold Metrostudy into its Market Intelligence business while retaining the Metrostudy brand name.
