Like Mona Lisa, this Lisa retains her reputation for the renown. In German automotive circles, connoisseurs recognize Lisa Dräxlmaier's contribution to what's inside a car. The company makes complete interiors (cockpits, center consoles, door panels), auto electric, and other integrated electrical systems for Bugatti, BMW , Jaguar , Mercedes-Benz ( Daimler ), Porsche , and Volkswagen/Audi . Its lineup integrates wood, stone, and leather materials with electrical management systems. Lisa Dräxlmaier operates more than 50 facilities in about 20 countries around the world. The family owned company was founded by Lisa and Fritz Dräxlmaier in 1958 to make wire harnesses for the Goggomobil produced by Hans Glas GmbH.
