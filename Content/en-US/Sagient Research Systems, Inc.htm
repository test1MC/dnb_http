Sagient Research Systems publishes salient research. The company provides independent research and data services to the financial services and institutional investment communities. Its research products include PlacementTracker (private investment in public entity market), BioMedTracker (biotech and pharmaceutical industries), and CatalystTracker (identifies and analyzes future events that will materially impact publicly traded companies). In addition, Sagient provides outsourced marketing and development services for third-party independent research providers. Robert F. Kyle (CEO) and Brian M. Overstreet (chairman) co-founded the company.
