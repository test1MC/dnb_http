Intaglio is very image conscious, but not at all concerned with fashion. The company provides a variety of printing and design services including lithographic and digital printing, pre-press, graphics, and Web design services. Itaglio specializes in providing clients with a consistent corporate image in all forms of media, from business cards and letterhead through a corporate Web site. The company primarily serves clients in the Los Angeles area.
