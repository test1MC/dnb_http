Insulectro supplies materials used to make printed circuit boards (PCBs) used to manufacture personal computers and other electronics. It carries copper-aluminum-copper foil (CAC), aluminum foil, dry film, epoxy laminates, and pressure sensitive (PSA) tapes, among other products. Insulectro manufactures some of its product line and distributes products made by such companies as DuPont , Isola , and Saint-Gobain . The company was founded in 1964 and has distribution locations across the US, including offices in Arizona, Illinois, and Texas, and its headquarters in Lake Forest, California. Insulectro was founded in 1991 and is owned by its officers.
