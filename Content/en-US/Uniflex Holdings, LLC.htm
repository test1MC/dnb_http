Uniflex has its business in the bag. The company makes custom-printed plastic bags and other plastic packaging products for promotional, advertising, and retail security applications. Its sells bags directly and through distributors to retailers, government agencies, and other large firms. Uniflex offers a slew of bag styles including soft loop handle, drawstring, over-the-shoulder, and zipper lock bags, as well as gloss laminated, white and natural kraft, and metallic kraft paper shopping bags. Uniflex is a subsidiary of retail packaging supplies firm S. Walter Holdings.
