Rockwell Collins (UK) is the main UK subsidiary of US-based aerospace systems and services provider Rockwell Collins . Its operations include government communications systems (integrated air-ground-sea data link systems, secure digital radios, and GPS-based navigation systems for military applications); aviation services (modification, repair, and overhaul services and test equipment); on-board services (in-flight cabin electronics support and service); and training services (courses for avionics maintenance and communications systems). Rockwell Collins' business in Europe includes a 50% stake in Quest Flight Training, a joint venture with Quadrant Group which trains aircraft crews for the UK Ministry of Defence.
