Pioneer Speakers wants to blow you away -- or make you hard of hearing trying -- with its sound equipment. The company is the North American sales outpost for Tohoku Pioneer Corporation, a publicly traded, 97%-owned subsidiary of Pioneer Corporation . Tohoku Pioneer, through its handful of business units, manufactures and markets speakers sold to automobile makers and mobile phone manufacturers, as well as electromechanical components used in CD players. In addition, the company makes organic light-emitting diode (OLED) displays for mobile phones and factory automation equipment. Pioneer Speakers operates several manufacturing plants in Japan.
