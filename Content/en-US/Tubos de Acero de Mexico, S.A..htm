Tubos de Acero de México knows how to play the pipes seamlessly. The company, which does business as TenarisTamsa, is the sole maker of seamless steel pipe in Mexico and a major operating unit of Luxembourg-registered Tenaris . Besides seamless pipe, which it sells primarily to customers in the oil industry, TenarisTamsa also makes mechanical and cold-drawn pipe for use in a variety of other industrial applications. Argentina-based Siderca bought a substantial interest in Tamsa in the early 1990s. Tenaris acquired Siderca, Tamsa, and Dalmine in 2002 to begin its operations.
