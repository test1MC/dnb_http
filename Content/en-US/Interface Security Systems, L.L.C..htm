Interface Security Systems is a leading provider of security systems and monitoring services for businesses and residential customers. It installs and monitors alarm systems for access control, fire and life safety, and intrusion detection. The company also offers video monitoring and alarm verification, remote managed access monitoring and support, and Internet-based alarm monitoring. Interface Security Systems has offices in about a dozen states, mostly in the South and Southwest. The company was founded in 1981.
