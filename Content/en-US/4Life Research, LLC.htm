Whether man or man's best friend, 4Life Research wants to protect the health of bipeds and quadrupeds alike. The multilevel marketing company manufactures more than 60 different nutritional supplements for humans and animals and sells them through its global network of independent distributors. 4Life Research's flagship product is Transfer Factor, a supplement made from egg yolks and cow colostrum that is meant to enhance the body's immune system. The firm makes Transfer Factor formulations for humans, dogs, cats, horses, and livestock. The privately held company was founded in 1998 by CEO David Lisonbee, who had previous experience with multilevel marketing organizations.

Based in Sandy, Utah, 4Life boasts a global network of independent distributors on five continents: the Americas, Oceania and the South Pacific, Asia, and Europe. It has offices in 22 countries.

4Life offers more than 60 different products (general health and wellness lines, personal skincare, and weight management products) and multiple delivery choices.

4Life's other (human) products include weight management supplements, nutritionals to support various systems of the body for general health, and body care products. Its products are based on a form of research invented by the company and coined "transferceutical science," or the idea that molecules contain information that can be transferred from one immune system to another, which, the company says, means that the body can borrow the immune education it needs to recognize, respond to, and remember unwanted threats.

Despite difficult market conditions, 4Life reported a 18% rise in global sales in 2012.

4Life's growth strategy is centered on expanding its global presence further by establishing distribution teams in new overseas markets.

In 2014 4Life launched four new products in India (NutraShake, 4Life NutraShake Kids, 4Life Vitamin & Mineral Complex, and 4Life Transfer Factor® Classic).

In recent years it has expanded its office in Peru with new meeting rooms to accommodate distributor networking. Additionally, 4Life Philippines opened a new office and business center in Baguio City, Philippines. In 2013 4Life opened a new office for Transfer Factor México located in Mexico City, as well as an office in East Java, Indonesia, and new office in Santiago, Chile.

In 2013 4Life Thailand launched NutraStart, while 4Life Singapore launched 4Life Transfer Factor Vista (a targeted transfer factor product to support eye health) and Bountiful Harvest Plus (a daily serving of nutrient-rich greens to support overall wellness).

As part of a global branding strategy, in 2013 Transfer Factor Mexico changed its name to 4Life Mexico.
