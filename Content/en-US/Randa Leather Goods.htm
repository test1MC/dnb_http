Thank Randa for helping you keep your pants on and your neck dressed up. Randa Leather Goods makes men's and boys' belts and other personal leather goods as part of Randa Accessories , which is known for its neckwear expertise. The division also makes and distributes lines of suspenders, packaged gifts, and boys' neckwear. Parent Randa Accessories boasts more than 10 licensing agreements to make accessories under the Levi's , Dockers , Dickies , Columbia , Izod, and Arrow brand names, among others. The company also markets products under its own handful of proprietary labels, such as Bill Adler Studio, Reward, and Dante. Randa Leather Goods sells its products through retail and specialty stores and catalogs.

Along with manufacturing, the Randa Leather Goods division offers its customers marketing and merchandising services. It also has the capacity to assist with sales, sourcing, and quality assurance.

The division, also known as the Randa Humphreys Mexico office, is in Mexico City. New York City-based parent company Randa Accessories operates in 11 countries across five continents (as of early 2016), with offices and facilities in the US (New York City; Bloomfield, New Jersey; St. Rose, Louisiana; Rosemont, Illinois; and McCarren, Nevada), Europe (London; Gelnrothes, Scotland; Milton Keynes; and Como, Italy), Mexico City, South Africa, Southeast China, and Southeast Australia.

Randa Accessories in June 2001 bought Humphrey's Accessories, which became Randa Leather Goods.
