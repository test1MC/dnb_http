L.A. Darling Company (LAD) gives shoppers their retail therapy. LAD manufactures and markets an array of wood and metal store fixtures, shelves, racks, and point-of-purchase displays. For over a century, the company has customized displays to the unique needs of retailers; services range from metal, and tube and roll forming and stamping to various welding, machining, finishing, and assembly processes. With facilities and service centers in the US and Mexico, LAD caters to discount, department, and specialty retailers worldwide, including Wal-Mart and Best Buy . Founded as the Ideal Fixture Company, and subsequently acquired and renamed by Lewis Archer Darling, LAD is owned by Marmon Group, which is a subsidiary of Berkshire Hathaway .
