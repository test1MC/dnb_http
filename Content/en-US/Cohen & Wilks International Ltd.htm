Cohen & Wilks International (CWI) is a leading manufacturer and supplier of outerwear and casualwear for several major retailers in the UK. Known for its market-leading childrenswear, CWI also makes customized clothing for men and women (under the Brit Island brand). The company also offers licensed apparel and is able to produce apparel in virtually any quantity or physical size requested by its customers. CWI's distributors have included Debenhams , House of Fraser , Mackays Stores , Sainsbury , and Marks and Spencer . Founded in 1974, the majority of CWI's manufacturing is done by third-party suppliers and trading partners located in Asia.

Though the company has mostly remained a family-run operation, one of its main suppliers of ready-made garments is Mitsui & Co .

CWI's ladieswear teams source and develop garments for High Street Stores, and also make clothing that appeals to brands in the equestrian market. Regarding its men's clothing, the company's product development has led it into areas of high-performance ski, snowboard, and watersport gear.

The company also provides work-related, customized clothing for commercial teams. Additionally, CWI makes licensed clothing for its childrenswear products, which carry themes and characters with licenses from various Disney movies, the Olympics, Thomas and Friends, Octonauts, and more.

Headquartered in Leeds, Yorkshire (in England), the company's manufacturing operations are conducted by ethically-compliant partners around the world.

Some of CWI's clients include: Babies R Us , Bhs , Costco , Debenhams , Disney , Dunnes , John Lewis , Littlewoods, Mackays , Mothercare , Marks and Spencer , Monsoon , Next , Tesco , Quba, and Sainsbury , among others.

The company's marketing and design teams are customer focused. To grow business, the company plans to use its teams to design up-to-date fashion trends, innovate new products and services, provide products of quality and value that its competitors can't match. Additionally, CWI's goal is to be flexible with its customer requirements when designing custom apparel, and promote excellent service from product development to in-store delivery.
