Glassman High Voltage produces high-voltage DC power supplies for test equipment and vacuum-processing gear. Its power supplies are used to power semiconductor fabrication equipment, medical diagnostic systems, and flight simulators, among others. Glassman's power supplies range in power from 15 watts to 50 kilowatts. The company has a manufacturing facility in New Jersey and sales and technical support offices in the UK and Japan. The company was founded in 1977 by owner and CEO Sanford Glassman.
