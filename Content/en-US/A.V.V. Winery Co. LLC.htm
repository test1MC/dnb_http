A.V.V. Winery (which does business as Alexander Valley Vineyards) produces some 20 varietal wines and proprietary blends. Prices are in the $20 to $50 range. Some 75% of its output is red wine, such as chardonnay, merlot and cabernet sauvignon, pinot noir, zinfandel, gewürztraminer, syrah, sangiovese, viognier, and cabernet franc. Producing 100,000 cases of wine per year, A.V.V.'s brand names include Alexander Valley Vineyards, CYRUS, Sin Zin, Redemption Zin, Temptation Zin, New Gewurz, Two Barrel, and Alexander School Reserve. The California vintner's wines are available across the US through distributors; they also can be purchased directly from the company's Web site.
