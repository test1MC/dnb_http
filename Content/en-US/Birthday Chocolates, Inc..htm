Birthday Chocolates helps employers tell their employees "Happy Birthday!" The company packages and ships boxed chocolates nationwide to employees' homes. Birthday Chocolates' products come in individual gift boxes, along with a card, and include plain chocolate, walnut, and fudge varieties. Corporate clients can order gift items to be shipped to their employees' residences on or before each employee's birthday. The company is owned by president Connie Watts.
