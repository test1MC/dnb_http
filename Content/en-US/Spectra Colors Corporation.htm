Spectra is all about the colors. Spectra Colors Corporation manufactures colorants and dyes for everything from Polaroid 's imaging products to Samsung ink jets to Kiwi shoe polishes. The company, which was founded in 1987, maintains a laboratory, production area, and warehouse at its New Jersey headquarters and also has a distribution network that spans the globe.
