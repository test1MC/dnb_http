A division of Wolters Kluwer , Lippincott Williams & Wilkins is a publisher of health care information for the medical industry and students. The company publishes the information in print format (textbooks, journals), as well as electronic formats (CD-ROM, Internet, and Intranet). Titles include Stedman's Medical Dictionary and Griffith's 5-Minute Clinical Consult . The company also publishes more than 275 medical journals, newsletters, and loose-leaf products with such titles as Implant Dentistry , Journal of Immunotherapy , and American Nursing Student . Lippincott Williams & Wilkins has offices in the US, Australia, Hong Kong, and the UK.
