Met-Pro tests its mettle by producing professional cleaning equipment. Its largest segment, Product Recovery/Pollution Control Technologies, manufactures products for industrial air quality and odor control, as well as laboratory fume hood exhaust systems. The Fluid Handling Technologies segment makes pumps (under the Dean, Fybroc, and Sethco brands) for the petrochemical, pharmaceutical, plastics, and food-processing industries. The Filtration/Purification Technologies segment makes products for drinking water and industrial air applications. The Mefiag Filtration Technologies segment makes products for handling corrosive, abrasive, and high-temperature liquids. It was acquired by CECO Environmental in 2013.

To create greater operational efficiency, in 2010 Met-Pro consolidated its Flex-Kleen , Duall, and Met-Pro Systems units as Met-Pro Environmental Air Solutions, under the recovery and pollution-control segment. That year it acquired Bio-Reaction Industries, a provider of air pollution control systems utilizing biological technology to eliminate volatile organic compounds, hazardous air pollutants, and odors.
