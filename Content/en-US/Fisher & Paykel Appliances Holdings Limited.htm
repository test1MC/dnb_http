Fisher & Paykel Appliances Holdings specializes in cooking, cooling, washing, and drying. The holding company designs, manufactures, and markets a range of household appliances -- from ovens and refrigerators to dishwashers and laundry machines -- through subsidiary Fisher & Paykel Appliances Limited. It has manufacturing sites in New Zealand, Italy, Mexico, and Thailand. While appliances account for most of the company's revenue, its financial services business unit contributes as a provider of retail point-of-sale consumer finance cards, insurance services, and rental and leasing finance. The company was founded in 1934 as an importer of Crosley refrigerators and Maytag washing machines from the US.
