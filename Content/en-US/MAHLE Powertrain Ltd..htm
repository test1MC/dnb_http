MAHLE Powertrain manufactures aluminum alloy casting powertrains and other vehicle engine parts using precision sand casting. The company's products include cylinder blocks, heads, and structural castings. Other products include adaptive and modular diagnostic tools used in industrial manufacturing. It also undertakes powertrain design and development and produces integrated powertrain assemblies for the automotive, non-automotive, and construction equipment markets. Customers have included such high-profile names as Audi , Automobili Lamborghini , and Ford Motor . MAHLE Powertrain is a subsidiary of Germany-based auto parts manufacturer Mahle .
