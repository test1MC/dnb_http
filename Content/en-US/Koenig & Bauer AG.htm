Koenig & Bauer AG (KBA) manufactures newspaper web presses, web offset presses (for printing books, commercial products, and telephone directories), web presses for publication rotogravure, presses for conventional and digital offset and security printing, and peripheral systems for paper logistics and counterfeit-proofing. Sheetfed Offset Presses and Web Presses comprise the company's two major divisions. KBA owns and operates four facilities in Germany, with sales and service facilities operating worldwide. About two-thirds of the company's sales derive from the European market. The company was founded in 1817 by Friedrich Koenig and Andreas Bauer.
