You don't hitch Bessie up to Carriage Works products. You sidle up to them and place your order. The company manufactures food and merchandise carts for use in shopping malls, casinos, hotels, resorts, convention centers, and amusement parks. Carriage Works makes standard-design and custom vending, food, coffee, and espresso carts and kiosks. Its products are sold throughout the world. The company also offers awning graphics and printing services under the Sunbrella Graphics System name. Carriage Works is owned by husband and wife team John and Barbara Evensizer.
