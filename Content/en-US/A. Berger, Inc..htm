A. Berger's machines are much too sophisticated to just make a burger. The company is a manufacturer of steel, aluminum, and brass machine components, primarily for the automotive industry. Among the company's chief products are motor shafts, ABS brake components, and automatic transmission valve components. A. Berger also makes components for airplanes, power tools, and hydraulic heavy machinery. Its precision parts and assemblies range from 4 millimeters to 1,800 millimeters in diameter, of a variety of metals, at its 12,000 sq.-ft. plant. The company is part of Berger Holding GmbH & Co. KG (The Berger Group), which operates 11 other companies, primarily in Germany, but also in Poland and Switzerland. The company is owned by the Berger family.
