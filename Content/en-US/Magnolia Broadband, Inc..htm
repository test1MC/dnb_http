Magnolia Broadband develops radio-frequency and mixed-signal chipsets for wireless antenna applications. The fabless semiconductor company offers the DiversityPlus line, covering the CDMA2000, WCDMA/UMTS, and WiMAX protocols in wireless communications, with applications in handsets and mobile terminals. Magnolia Broadband's major technology partners include telecom carriers such as SK Telecom . The company's chips are fabricated by Jazz Semiconductor . Magnolia Broadband has offices in South Korea and the US, with sales representatives in other countries. Investment backers have included Draper Fisher Jurvetson , Grosvenor Funds, Intel Capital, Selway Partners, Silverstar Holdings , and Uniquest.
