While it has nothing to do with Shakespeare's canon, UBM Canon does put out an influential body of technology-based manufacturing work. The division of business-to-business (B2B) media firm UBM publishes about 30 print and electronic publications and organizes some 60 trade shows in areas such as medical manufacturing, pharmaceutical development, and packaging. In addition to magazines such as Medical Product and Manufacturing News and PlasticsToday , and trade shows including Green Manufacturing Expo and Assembly & Automation Technology Expo , UBM Canon operates a number of websites, such as Qmed.com and Pharmalot.com. Its Medical Device Register (MDRWeb.com) is a leading online directory for medical suppliers.
