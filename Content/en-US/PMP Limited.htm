PMP Limited pumps ink and paper into Kiwi households by providing printing and related services in Australia and New Zealand. Its core business unit, PMP Print, offers web offset printing for magazines, catalogs, newspaper inserts, books, and directories. PMP also offers physical and digital magazine distribution and circulation management services through its Gordon and Gotch subsidiary. Another PMP company, Dimension Studios, provides creative and photographic services to the retail, fashion, corporate, and advertising industries. Although PMP targets Australasia, slightly more than 80% of its sales are derived from the Australian market. Key customers include Coles Group and Foster's Group .
