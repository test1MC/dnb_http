Iron and steel are girding China's economic development, and Jiangsu Shagang is big part of that process. The company processes some 31.9 million tons of iron annually in order to roll out about 39.2 million tons of steel each year, and 37.2 million tons of rolled products, making it one of China's top-five steel producers. Jiangsu Shagang's product line includes rebar, wire rods, slabs, and hot-rolled coils. In addition to iron and steel, Jiangsu Shagang produces stainless steel and zinc-plated steel sheets. The company ranks as China's largest private steel producer.

Jiangsu Shagang's products are exported to more than 50 countries in East Asia, South Asia, the Middle East, Western Europe, South America, Africa, and other regions.

The company's broad range of products (in more than 60 series and more than 700 varieties with almost 2,000 specifications) include wide heavy plate, hot-rolled strip coil, high-speed wire rod, large bundle wire rod, ribbed steel bar, and special steel round bar.

Jiangsu Shagang serves a range of end users market such as shipbuilding, boiler and pressure vessels, pipelines, buildings, bridge, machinery, and automobile manufacturers.

The company focuses on innovative products and technology, upgrading the efficiency of its operations, and on improving its marketing strategy.

Since 2006 Jiangsu Shagang has acquired four smaller, rival steel companies.
