Wellborn Cabinet wants to be part of your remodeling projects. The company makes and markets wooden cabinets and drawers primarily for remodeling and building kitchens and bathrooms. Its products, which are sold through a dealer network of specialty shops, are also used for home office, closet, and entertainment media storage. The company's portfolio of brands includes namesake Wellborn Cabinet, Estate Custom Collection, Elegant Bath Collection, Distinctive Storage Solutions, Home Concepts, and Contractor's Advantage. Wellborn manufactures its products in the US and operates its own timber processing mill. The family-owned business was established in 1961 by brothers Paul (president and CEO) and Doug Wellborn.

Based in Ashland, Alabama, Wellborn sells its products nationwide through dealers.

As part of its business, Wellborn operates Factory Direct Kitchen and Bath as a division, located in Lineville, Alabama. It serves customers throughout the US.

Wellborn sells its cabinetry and other items through a dealer network of specialty shops. The company markets its products to consumers and remodelers focused on kitchens, bathrooms, home offices, closets, and entertainment areas. Altogether, it boasts more than 30,000 door style and finish combinations.

The company works to get its name out through its collaboration with Home and Garden TV (HGTV) for its Dream Homes programs. Wellborn has provided cabinetry and products for nearly every room of each home for a handful of years.

The company has expanded by purchasing the operations of Karman Kitchens, as well as Rutt HandCrafted and Heritage Custom Cabinetry.

To extend the reach of its products, Wellborn in 2012 rebranded its home-organization product line from Distinctive Closets to Distinctive Storage Solutions. The line features organization items that can be repurposed for several areas of the home, such as laundry rooms, garages, mudrooms, lockers, media centers, craft rooms, desks, offices, bedrooms. Its HandiSolutions brand is also part of this line. It significantly boosted its combination choices in 2014 when it unveiled new cabinet finishes and finish options.
