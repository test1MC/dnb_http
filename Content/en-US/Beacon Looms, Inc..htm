Beacon Looms looms large as a designer, manufacturer, and supplier of knitted products worldwide. The company makes window treatments, bathroom linens, and private-label bedding. Its products are sold in big box chains ( Bed Bath & Beyond and Wal-Mart ) and at leading department stores ( Dillard's and J.C. Penney ) throughout North America. While its window business remains its main focus, the firm's bedding division has been expanding rapidly, thanks to flourishing sales. Founded in 1914, Beacon Looms manufactures alongside its overseas partners and imports goods primarily from the Asia-Pacific region. Sy Sadinoff, chairman and CEO, succeeded the founder of the company and his grandfather, Max Sadinoff.
