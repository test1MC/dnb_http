When customers are seeking the star treatment, they give Star Nail Products (dba Star Nail International) a shot. The company primarily manufactures and markets manicure and pedicure supplies for use by professional cosmetologists. It also offers spa products, such as hand creams, salts and scrubs, and soaking leaves. Star Nail peddles its goods under several brand names, including namesake Star Nail, All Season Nails, All Season Professional, Cina, Cina Pro, and Cuccio Pro. Manicurists and beauty schools typically request product samples from the company website before they buy. Its products are distributed throughout the US and Europe.
