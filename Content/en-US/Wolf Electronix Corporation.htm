Wolf Electronix manufactures printed circuit boards, including quick-turn prototypes, at facilities in Utah, Colorado, and in Mexico. The production facility at its Utah headquarters has five manufacturing lines for surface-mount technology, a specialty in board assembly. The company was established in 1996. Founder and chairman Jim Trent Sr. owns Wolf Electronix. Trent and his four sons, all of whom are in Wolf's management, gained experience in the board-making business through working at such companies as Compaq Computer, Hewlett-Packard , IBM , and Toshiba .
