Powerstax makes the products that feed the power hungry. Specializing in power conversion and high efficiency products, the company makes AC/DC converters, bulk power modules, power supplies, and controllers used in communications, industrial, transportation, test and measurement, and medical applications. The company also provides custom engineering design and manufacturing services, and distributes power supplies, frequency converters, and inverters in the UK and Ireland. Powerstax operates its own factory in England and outsources volume production to a manufacturer in China. It also has a stand-alone R&D center in the UK.

Powerstax has expertise in the designing and manufacturing of AC-DC and DC-DC power supplies; this includes DC-DC converters, transformer rectifiers, configurable, modular, and bulk-power switched mode power supplies (SMPS).

Powerstax has primary offices in the UK (Farnborough, Hampshire, UK) and the US (Sun City West, Arizona). Its sales partners are located in the EMEA, North America, Australia, and Asia.

Powerstax serves a wide range of markets including the defense, medical, electro-chlorination, cathodic protection, aerospace, fuel cells, and oil and gas sectors.

Focused on engineering, the company has been able to grow by providing custom solutions for its commercial and military markets. The company has managed to grow its export business despite the slow economy and continues to look toward the US for growth while pursuing opportunities in Europe and Asia.
