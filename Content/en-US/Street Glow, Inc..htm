Worried that your ride doesn't stand out in a crowd? StreetGlow manufactures and distributes automotive lighting products, ranging from neon license plate frames and under car lighting kits to interior strobe lights, shift knobs, and glow dice. The company makes a dash-mounted switch panel used to connect and control lighting systems. StreetGlow also makes lighting kits that can help jazz up your home. Products are sold through a network of dealers under brand names such as StreetGlow, OPTX, and GlowFX. The company's products were featured in the 2001 street racing movie The Fast & The Furious . StreetGlow was founded in 1991 by CEO Jack Panzarella.
