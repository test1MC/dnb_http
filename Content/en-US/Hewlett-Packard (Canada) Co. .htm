Hewlett-Packard (Canada) supplies businesses and consumers in Canada with a wide range of high-tech equipment. The subsidiary, also known as HP Canada, provides data storage devices, networking equipment, servers, PCs, and printers. Its consumer products are sold directly online and through distributors and retailers. HP Canada's software portfolio includes operating systems and print management tools, and business software that handles application, network infrastructure, and product lifecycle management. The company also provides consulting, financing, maintenance, outsourcing, and support services. HP Canada, a subsidiary of Hewlett-Packard , was established in 1961.
