Independent Stave has literally staved off the competition by producing the majority of the wine and whiskey barrels in the world. The company manufactures a range storage containers (mostly oak barrels) for the wine and liquor industries. Established in 1912, Independent Stave is owned by the Boswell family (descendants of founder T.W. Boswell) and operates facilities and sales locations in approximately 20 countries. The company also makes walnut bowls, cutting boards, picture frames, and other gift items, and it manufactures and distributes decorative mulch and other landscaping items from oak by-products.
