Agrium U.S. operates south of the border for Canada-based fertilizers manufacturer and distributor Agrium . The parent company produces and distributes four major crop nutrients: nitrogen, phosphate, potash, and sulfate fertilizers for the wholesale market. Agrium U.S. operates a network of retail farm centers and other retailers in the US and Latin America, and is the largest independent retailer of fertilizers, seeds, and chemicals in North America. Agrium U.S. produces more than 1.3 million tons nitrogen per year. The US provides Agrium with about two-thirds of its overall revenues.

The company has plants in Alabama, California, Georgia, Idaho, Ohio, Texas, and Washington.

Agrium rebranded all of its US and Canadian retail outlets under the Crop Production Services name in 2009.

Agrium U.S. began originally as Cominco in 1931. It changed its name to Agrium U.S. in 1993.
