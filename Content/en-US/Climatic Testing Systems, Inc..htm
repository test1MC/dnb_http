Climatic Testing Systems (CTS) isn't afraid to make a few high-pressure sales -- as a product, not a tactic. The company designs and manufactures equipment used to simulate and control such environmental conditions as temperature, humidity, and pressure. Clients include manufacturers of automotive and HVAC equipment, components, and systems. Products include calorimeter and durability test stands, psychrometric rooms (used to measure thermal performance of air conditioners and heat pumps), and drive-in chambers. CTS also makes environmentally controlled rooms for industrial testing or life science applications. Environmental rooms include freezer rooms, incubators, and temperature and humidity controlled rooms.
