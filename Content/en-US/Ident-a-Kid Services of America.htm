The photos of children on milk cartons help track down missing children, and so does Ident-a-Kid Services. The company provides child identification cards through participating schools and day care programs. The laminated plastic cards contain pictures, physical descriptions, names and addresses, and fingerprints.  The cards are kept in the possession of parents or guardians, who can show the ID to police if their child is reported lost or missing. Ident-a-Kid Services operates through some 250 franchise locations in the US. The company was established in 1986.
