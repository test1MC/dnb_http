Low-tech fermentation begets stuff like sauerkraut, but Lonza Biologic's high-tech fermentation produces far more amazing ingredients. Lonza Biologics plc, one of The Lonza Group's four biologic contracting manufacturing sites, is a leading contract manufacturer of monoclonal antibodies, recombinant proteins, and diagnostic monoclonal antibodies that are used to produce pharmaceuticals. Lonza Biologics conducts research and development on mammalian cell culturing involving cloning. From its manufacturing facility near London, the company also supplies its products to other biotechnology companies.

The company serves pharma & biotech market.
