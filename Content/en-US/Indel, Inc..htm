The heat is on at Indel. The company is the parent of an international group of about 60 engineering and technology companies producing a variety of products, primarily for the metals industry. It operates through two groups. Inductotherm Group includes about 40 companies that make melting and thermal processing systems. Its Diversified Technology Group includes about 25 firms that design and manufacture metal products and components, welding equipment, electrical components and systems engineering electronics, engineering products, plastic products, and network communications products. Services range from metal fabrication to silkscreen printing. Indel has operations in the Americas, Europe, and Asia/Pacific.
