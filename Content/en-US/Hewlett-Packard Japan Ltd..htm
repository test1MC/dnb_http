Hewlett-Packard Japan helps its customers adapt to a technological society. The company provides a broad selection of technology and office equipment to customers in the consumer, corporate, small business, and government sectors. Products include personal computers, printing and imaging devices, network servers, and enterprise software. The company also offers a range of IT services including consulting, maintenance, and product integration. Hewlett-Packard established a presence in Japan in 1983 when it formed a joint venture with Yokogawa Electric called Yokogawa Hewlett-Packard. HP eventually took a majority ownership in the business and it was renamed Hewlett-Packard Japan.
