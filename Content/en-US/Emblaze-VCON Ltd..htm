Emblaze-VCON (formerly VCON Telecommunications) makes videoconferencing hardware and software. The company provides computing and peripheral equipment to enable desktop and group audio- and videoconferencing. The associated software products include applications for video network management, conference scheduling, and transmission security. Emblaze-VCON serves customers in such industries as education ( University of Virginia ), telecommunications ( Telekom Austria ), and energy ( Harken ). The company is a subsidiary ZONE-IP, which is part of the Emblaze group of companies.
