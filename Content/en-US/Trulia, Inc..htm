Looking for a home you'll truly love? Try Trulia, an online marketplace for real estate listings. Trulia.com offers a Web-based database of more than 4.5 million homes and apartments for sale or rent in the US. Incorporating data from multiple listing services, real estate brokerages, and apartment managers, the site also offers information on local schools, businesses, market data, and crime statistics, as well as user-generated content for local insight into communities. While Trulia.com is free to search, the company does offer a subscription-based product, Trulia Pro, for real estate professionals. Founded in 2005, Trulia was acquired by Zillow Group in 2015.
