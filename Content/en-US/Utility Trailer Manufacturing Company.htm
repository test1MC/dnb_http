Utility Trailer Manufacturing likes to play it cool. The company is one of the largest manufacturers of refrigerated trailers (aka reefers) in the US. The company also produces dry freight trailers, flatbeds, side skirts, and its branded Tautliners curtain-sided trailers. Its refrigerated trucks include special designs for the whole food distribution and fast-food markets, such as trucks with separate chambers for frozen and dry goods. A handful of factories in the US makes its more than 140 brands of trailers and vans, which are distributed through a network of 100 independent dealers. The privately held company is family operated.

Utility operates five trailer factories across the US. Utility's multi-temperature refrigerated trailers are made at the Marion, Virginia and Clearfield, Utah plants. Dry vans are manufactured at the Glade Spring, Virginia and Paragould, Arkansas plants. Drop deck and double flatbeds as well as the Tautliner are all made at the Enterprise, Alabama facility.

Utility's dealer network spans 100 locations throughout Canada, Mexico, South America, and the US. Its major customer is Stevens Transport .

Utility has also tackled rising fuel costs and the onslaught of environmental regulations impacting the transportation industry by shifting its manufacture to lightweight trailers. It has launched several new models including an improved refrigerated trailer, followed by a lighter weight, composite material-based dry freight van, the first in the industry to earn EPA (Environmental Protection Agency) SmartWay certification, and a lighter, more durable flatbed.

In 2014 the company launched a new mobile application for Apple iPhone and Android smartphones, which uses Global Positioning System capabilities to search for a Utility dealership within a user's geographic location.

Utility also gradually launches new locations and adds more manufacturing space in order to grow. In 2014 it opened a new dealership in Spokane, Washington. In 2013 it opened a new location in Delta, British Columbia.

In 2012 Utility opened a new dealer location in Queretaro, Mexico, and added 56,000 sq. ft. of manufacturing space to its Glade Spring, Virgina facility.

Two brothers, H.C. and E.W. Bennett, founded the company in 1914.
