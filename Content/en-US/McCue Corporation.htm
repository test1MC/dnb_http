McCue tries to perfect the art of the cart. The company offers protective and decorative bumpers for shopping carts; specialty carts for kids; and parking-lot cart corrals. Founded in 1988 by David McCue, McCue's customers have included major retailers, including Albertson's, The Home Depot , and Wal-Mart Stores . The company's specialty carts have gained popularity with local grocers as a gimmick to help them compete with mega-stores. Its "Bean" carts are designed with front ends like trucks, with the cart placed in the bed of the truck. Children ride in an enclosed cab, designed to keep them safe (and amused) while grown-ups shop.

McCue offers various products such as bumpers, sales area corners and rails, back rooms, beans, and outdoor products for retail customers. It also supplies bollards, rack protection, rails, and barriers for warehouses and material handling applications.

Its customer list includes Food Lion , Meijer Stores , Price Chopper, Schnucks Markets, and Trader Joe's .
