Sinopec Tianjin Petrochemical has no problem working with raw materials. One of Sinopec 's major oil refining and petrochemical processing subsidiaries, the company's activities include crude oil processing, production of ethylene, polyethylene, polypropylene, and manufacturing polyester chemical fiber components. The group consists of 15 oil refining units (with a throughput of 110,000 barrels per day), 15 chemical production units, and nine chemical fiber production units. Sinopec Tianjin Petrochemical's end products include gasoline, kerosene, jet fuel, and asphalt in addition to its chemical components. In a major expansion in 2009, it completed a new 200,000 barrel-per-day crude oil refining plant.
