So, you want to be a Washington insider? Inside Washington Publishers (IWP) offers print and electronic newsletters with the skinny on federal policy covering five areas: defense, trade, health care, energy, and the environment. The company publishes about 20 newsletters, such as Environmental Policy Alert , Inside the Pentagon , and Energy Washington Week . Web sites include InsideEPA.com and InsideHealthPolicy.com. IWP offers certain documents and articles for sale on a pay-per-view basis. The company was founded in 1980.
