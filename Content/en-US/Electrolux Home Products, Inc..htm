Keeping things clean keeps Electrolux Home Products in business. The company makes and markets Beam, Eureka , and Electrolux brand central vacuum systems for boats, RVs, and residences (tubing is installed in the walls and the vacuumed debris is sent to a collection bin in the home's basement or garage). Its products are sold through certified dealers and online, serving customers in more than 50 countries worldwide. Owned by AB Electrolux , the vacuum maker was known as Beam Industries until 2007. The company's roots go back to WWII, when it manufactured washing machines, dishwashers, and scooters -- a product that became popular during the wartime gas-rationing period.
