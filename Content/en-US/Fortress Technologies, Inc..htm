Fortress Technologies puts locks on the doors of wireless networks. The company designs and manufactures security devices for wireless local-area networks to prevent unauthorized access to proprietary or private information. Health care providers, manufacturing and transportation companies, and financial institutions use the company's gateway products and secure clients, as do government agencies (including the departments of Defense and Veterans Affairs ) and public safety entities. Fortress Technologies sells through such partners as Intermec , Hewlett-Packard , and CACI .
