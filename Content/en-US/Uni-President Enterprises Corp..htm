Uni-President Enterprises may have "uni" in its name but "multi" would be more fitting. The firm, Taiwan's largest food company and one of its largest conglomerates, exports everything from food products to animal feeds through its companies and affiliates. In Taiwan it controls the 7-Eleven franchise and owns the President Lions professional baseball team. In China and the Asia/Pacific region, the company has joint ventures or stakes in companies ranging from Kikkoman (sauces) to French retailer Carrefour (hypermarkets in Taiwan). It has global investments in biotech, electronics, finance, and other industries.
