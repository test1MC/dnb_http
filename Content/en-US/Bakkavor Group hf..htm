Busy folks can still get a hot -- or a cold -- meal thanks to this company. Bakkavör is a leading supplier of fresh prepared and ready-to-eat meals, supplying grocery retailers with more than 5,000 products through its more than 50 production facilities in 10 countries worldwide. The Icelandic company produces fresh pizza and pasta, prepared vegetables, soups, and specialty bread items, as well as prepared fruits and salads and fresh produce. Its operating units include Hitchen Foods, a leading UK supplier of chilled foods, vegetables, and pizza toppings. The company also has significant foreign operations including in continental Europe, South Africa, China, and the US.

Bakkavör has 32 facilities in the UK, along with 20 locations in Belgium, China, Hong Kong, Italy, Spain, and the US. Almost 90% of its sales come from the UK.

To sharpen its focus on catering to the UK, its primary market, the company has been selling other units in other countries. In early 2014 it sold Spring Valley Foods, a prepared fruit business in South Africa, to In2food Group. Months earlier Bakkavör sold Heli Food Fresh, its former Czech ready meals business, to Yazico Investments Ltd and Axentia Financial Consultants Ltd.

In mid-2012 Bakkavör opened its new facility at Caledonian Produce in West Lothian, Scotland. The state-of-the-art facility specializes in the handling and production of nut-based products in the UK.
