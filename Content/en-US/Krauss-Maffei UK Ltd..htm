Paper or plastic? This company's machines make the latter. KraussMaffei Group UK (a unit of Germany's KraussMaffei Technologies) makes injection molding, extrusion, and polyurethane foam molding equipment used to process plastics. Customers use the machinery to make products such as DVDs, plastic bottles, sports equipment, dashboards, and PVC pipe. KraussMaffei Group UK markets its machines under the KraussMaffei, KraussMaffei Berstorff, and Netstal brand names and offers spare parts and repair, maintenance, and training services. It sells to OEMs in the appliance, automotive, industrial, medical, and pharmaceutical sectors.

German parent KraussMaffei Technologies currently employs around 4,000 people. With a global network of more than 30 subsidiaries and more than 10 production plants, supported by around 570 sales and service partners, the company is close to customers around the world.

KraussMaffei Group UK covers all areas of injection molding machinery, extrusion technology and reaction process machinery.

The company serves customers in range of industries, including the automotive, packaging, medical, and construction markets.

In 2014 KraussMaffei Technologies' net revenues increased primarily due to improved sales in all segments, primarily in the automotive and infrastructure industries. KraussMaffei Group UK contributed 36% of the total sales of the parent company in 2014.

Parent KraussMaffei Technologies has been headquartered in Munich since 1838. Krauss-Maffei UK was founded in 1970 as a maker of plastics machinery (injection molding and polyurethane), and process technology (centrifuges, dryers and separators).

In 2013 the parent created a stronger presence in the UK by merging Netstal UK and Krauss Maffei UK to create the KraussMaffei Group UK Ltd.
