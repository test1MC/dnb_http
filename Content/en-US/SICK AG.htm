SICK AG provides its customers with a special brand of healthy en-light-enment. The company produces sensory devices for the automotive, electronics, food and beverage, material handling, and packaging industries. Products include bar code scanners, color sensors, contrast and luminescence scanners, encoders, light grids, photoelectric sensors, and position finders. In 1946 Erwin Sick obtained a license from the US military to practice engineering; today, the company operates more than 40 subsidiaries worldwide. The Sick family owns more than 75% of the company.
