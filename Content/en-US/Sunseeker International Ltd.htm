Sunseeker (International) shines brightly as a manufacturer of high-end powerboats, most of which it exports from its Poole boat-building headquarters on the south coast of England. Sunseeker models include Manhattan, Portofino, and Predator. As an added feature, Sunseeker Charters offers trips on the company's yachts, ranging from day-long excursions to extended journeys around the world, that start with limousine pickup service and even private jet rentals. Sunseeker boats are sold through distributors in nearly 60 countries, including the US, China, Germany, and Japan.

Sunseeker has dealerships worldwide, from Helsinki to Hong Kong and San Diego to Sydney. It has a presence in Africa, Asia, Australia, Europe, North America, and South America.

The company's distributors include Sunseekeri London; Sunseeker Torguay; Sunseeker Germany; Nautikos Mexico; and Burevestnik.

The company also uses joint ventures as a means for expansion. In 2015 musician Nicole Scherzinger launched Predator 57.

In 2014, the company launched Sunseeker 155 Yacht, the largest and most advanced yacht ever produced and which can accommodate up to 12 guests and 10 crew in luxury surroundings.

In 2012 it entered joint ventures with companies in Spain and the Channel Islands.

The company began molding and making its own boats in the 1970s, when the firm was known as Poole Power Boats.
