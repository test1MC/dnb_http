Polywell Computers manufactures PCs, workstations, servers, and storage systems. The company builds computers for general desktop use, as well as specialized workstations for 3D animation, video editing, and CAD applications. It also offers network servers and mass data storage systems. It sells directly and through resellers to government and corporate customers. The company also provides consulting and support services. Established in 1987, Polywell is owned by its officers.
