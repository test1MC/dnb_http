Onto every car a little rain must fall -- that's where Trico Products comes in. One of the leading manufacturers of its kind, Trico designs, makes, and distributes windshield wiper systems and electronics for about two dozen of the world's car makers, as well as wiper blades and accessories for the consumer replacement market. Trico produces millions of wiper products a year, including wiper motors, wiper arms, windshield washer systems, and linkages. The company also makes wiper systems for heavy-duty trucks and RVs. Its electronics division makes electronic controls for automotive wiper system rain sensors. Wiper brands include Force, Onyx, NeoForm, Flex, Shield, Ice, and Chill.

In 2014 Trico agreed to be acquired by aftermarket and OEM component parts supplier Crowne Group with funds managed by Kohlberg & Company . Trico represented an excellent strategic fit for Crowne due to its balanced mix of business between aftermarket and OEM, and manufacturing and distribution synergies.

Trico operates eight manufacturing facilities and serves customers in more than 50 countries in North America, South America, Europe, Asia, Australasia.

The company's business is classified into two segments; aftermarket wipers and accessories (Aftermarket) and original equipment manufacturer wipers systems (OEM).

Strategy

Trico focuses on improving the quality of its products. It has built its strong reputation by combining an obsession with product performance and customer service with the ability to deliver on our commitment to quality.

In 2014 it has added 12 new part numbers to the Trico Exact Fit blade line and four new parts numbers to the TricoForce beam balde product line.

That year Trico announced the launch of a new website designed to provide customers in Mexico with comprehensive details on all available TRICO wiper blade products.

Trico's performance depends on its ability to service its OEM and consumer markets with an assortment of advanced products. Tailoring its lineup to about 50 automotive models, it courts Ford Motor to General Motors , Daimler , Chrysler , Toyota , Nissan , Honda , Volkswagen , and Renault .

Trico sells its products worldwide through distributors such as RepWorks Marketing, The Shepherd Group, Action Sales & Marketing, NA William Company, Siskel Sales, and Kitchin & Sons.
