Bursting at the seams? Sculptz is likely to have the right fit. The company makes and sells hosiery under the Silkies brand in more than a dozen styles and nearly as many colors. It also manufactures and sells knee-highs, socks, and tights under the Sculptz Shapewear and Legwear labels, Silkies Enriche anti-aging skincare products, and PainVanish, a pain-relieving cream. Founded in 1974, Sculptz claims to be the largest direct marketer of pantyhose, counting 1.5 million-plus member customers (mainly women) worldwide. Made in the US, the hosiery is sold through a direct mail and online continuity program, whereby additional pairs continue to be sent. Formerly HCI Direct, the company changed its name in 2010.
