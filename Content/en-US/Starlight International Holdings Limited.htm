Starlight International Holdings helps to bring your favorite entertainment to your living room. The company develops and markets a variety of audio and video electronics, such as compact disc, DVD, and MP3 players, as well as televisions, through its subsidiaries. Starlight International has counted companies the likes of Sony , Hitachi , Samsung , and Sharp among its customers. The electronics manufacturer sells its products in North, South, and Central America; Asia/Pacific; and Africa. Most of its sales are generated in Europe and the US. The company operates a pair of manufacturing facilities in China. Chairman Philip Lau founded Starlight in 1969.
