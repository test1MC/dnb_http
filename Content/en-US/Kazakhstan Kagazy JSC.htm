Kazakhstan Kagazy (kagazy means "paper" in Kazakh) is one of Central Asia's largest paper, container board, and corrugated packaging product manufacturers through subsidiary Kagazy Recycling. It primarily uses recycled wastepaper collected in Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Russia, and Uzbekistan. Customers include dairy, fish, and meat producers. The company also operates a commercial and industrial real estate property business called Prime Estate Activities Kazakhstan (PEAK), which owns more than 700 hectares of land for construction of warehousing and logistics facilities. A third subsidiary, Astana Contract, owns existing warehousing and container terminal facilities.
