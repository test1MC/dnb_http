Alpan Lighting Products is illuminating a brighter future for consumers' homes and the environment. The company specializes in design, development, and manufacture of solar lights for residential settings. Alpan's lineup includes stainless steel, bronze, cooper, and plastic solar garden lights, solar stepping stones, decorative outdoor post lamps, as well as low- and line-voltage powered lights, and replacement parts and batteries. The company is headquartered in California and operates manufacturing facilities in China, Malaysia, and Hong Kong. Alpan sells its products through its website and, to a lesser extent, through major retail and home improvement centers in the US, such as Ace Hardware and Wal-Mart .
