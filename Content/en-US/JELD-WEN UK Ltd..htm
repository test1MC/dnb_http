JELD-WEN UK Ltd., a subsidiary of US-based JELD-WEN , manufactures windows and doors. Its doors include interior, exterior, patio, and garage varieties. JELD-WEN UK's windows include thermal, bay, and custom products. The company also makes custom-made and stock stairway kits, newels, rails, spindles, and treads. JELD-WEN UK was formed when JELD-WEN purchased the John Carr and Boulton & Paul companies from the Rugby Group in 1999. JELD-WEN UK operates manufacturing plants in Sheffield, Leicestershire, and Melton Mowbray.
