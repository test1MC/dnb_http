Enjoying the taste of beer without popping open a cold one may seem impossible, but the experts at Butter Buds Food Ingredients have made it possible with their dairy enzymes. Snack manufacturers can even spray its beer flavoring on pretzels! What's next, milk flavors? Well, as a matter of fact, that's one of the company's product offerings -- Milk Buds. Others include include its namesake Butter Buds, as well as Bacon Buds, Cheese Buds, Cream Buds, Cream Cheese Buds, Buttermilk Buds, Cocoa Butter Buds, and Olive Oil Buds. For even more beer taste, the company offers Dried Beer Extract, which can be used to flavor batters, sauces, marinades, dips, and snacks. Butter Buds is owned by Cumberland Packing .
