Quantel designs and manufactures laser systems used in medical and scientific applications, from treating acne to guiding missiles. Quantel makes laser diodes used in industrial, medical, military, and scientific applications, and solid-state lasers used by the military and aerospace industries. Quantel Medical and Quantel Derma manufacture lasers used in ophthalmology and dermatology. The group's lasers are exported all over the world; the US represents about 7% of sales. The company runs manufacturing operations in France and the US.

Customers in France account for 45% of Quantel's revenue. Those in Israel generate 10% of revenue, followed by the US, 7%, South Korea and Germany, 5% each, and China, 3%. About a quarter of revenue comes from the other countries where Quantel has customers.

In 2015 Quantel Group posted 21% increase in revenue to 62 million from 51.3 in 2014. Revenue from industrial and scientific lasers rose 48% and medical and ophthalmological laser sales rose 6% year to year.
