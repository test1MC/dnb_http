Businesses turn to Wilmington for a ton of professional information. Wilmington Group publishes information on professional markets through its Professional Publishing & Information unit, while its Professional Training & Events unit offers training services and organizes industry events. Information is published and distributed in a variety of formats, including print and online databases, directories, and magazines. Wilmington Group's content is geared toward the banking, finance, legal, government, and media and entertainment industries, as well as charities. Key brands include Central Law Training for legal professionals, and Waterlow, which publishes legal and professional databases for various markets.
