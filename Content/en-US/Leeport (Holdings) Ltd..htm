Through its subsidiaries Leeport (Holdings) Ltd. can provide the tools and equipment necessary to manufacture almost anything. Leeport Precision Machine Tool makes metal cutting machinery. Leeport Metalforming offers machinery for bending and forming metal. Leeport Metrology makes measuring instruments. Leeport Tools provides all manner of industrial tooling. Leeport Electronics makes machinery for printed circuit board manufacture and assembly. Other operations include Leeport Technology and Rapman (prototyping), and Leeda Machinery (grinding and milling machines), and Hoffman Quality Tools. The company also provides sourcing and consulting services. Leeport was founded in 1967 by Joseph Lee.
