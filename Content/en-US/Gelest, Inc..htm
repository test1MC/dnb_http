Gelest is an expert in the manufacturing of silanes and silicones. Founded in 1990, Gelest supplies silicone, tin, and germanium compounds to both the commercial and research markets. Product applications include adhesives, ceramics, coatings, composites, metallization, and semiconductors, as well as synthesis and organic chemistry processes. Gelest offers more than 3,000 different materials in gram to commercial quantities. It has distributorships throughout North America, Europe, and Asia. Gelest is expanding its offering of other metal-organics, particularly metal alkoxides and diketonates. In 2010 it teamed up with Matherson Tri Gas to supply high purity germane to the electronics and photovoltaic markets.
