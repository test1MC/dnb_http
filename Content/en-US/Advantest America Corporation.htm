Advantest America Corporation manufactures and distributes electronic measuring instruments and semiconductor test equipment used in the development and manufacture of computer and telecommunications products. Products include of test systems, test handlers, device interfaces, and software. The company is the North American subsidiary of Advantest Corporation , established to expand the automatic test equipment business (ATE) in the US. It has more than a dozen offices throughout the US and an R&D Center located in California. The Japanese parent company gets about 15% of its annual sales from the Americas. Advantest established its first representative office in the States in 1973 and founded Advantest America in 1982.

Advantest America serves such markets as consumer electronics, semiconductors, medical instrumentation, and digital TVs, to name a few. With about 88% of Advantest's sales originating outside Japan, Advantest America has become more essential to the corporation's business, holding a market share of about 64% of semiconductor and component test systems sold in the US.

Test measurement technology must adhere to perpetual transformation to keep up with the monitoring of difficult and costly production processes, in order to keep them cost effective. Advantest spends more than $375 million annually on research and development to verify the quality of chip functions during manufacturing. Its test systems enable manufacturers to have more control over device fabrication.
