Technic's technique allows it to specialize in electroplating chemicals and technology. The company makes plating chemicals for electronic manufacturing, specializing in precious metals plating. It also produces wafer processing chemicals and powders and flakes for electronic applications. Additionally Technic manufactures plating equipment like wafer plating benches for semiconductor makers, automatic hoists, and reel-to-reel plating machines. Its specialty chemical products are supported by three equipment groups: The Technic Equipment Division, Surface Finishing Technologies, and Technic Future Automation.

Technic has more than 20 global facilities in 14 countries in North America, Asia, and Europe. Subsidiaries reside in Canada, China, France (three locations), Germany, Italy, Japan, Korea, Taiwan, Singapore, Hong Kong, and the UK. It owns 15 customer analytical support laboratories around the world.

It serves its domestic customers through regional offices and warehouse facilities in Chicago; Dallas; and Anaheim and San Jose, California.

The company operates through three equipment groups: The Technic Equipment Division (innovative technology, including the patented Spouted Bed Electrode plating tool); Surface Finishing Technologies (continuous vertical processing equipment for both rigid and flexible materials, including c-Si and thin film photovoltaic processing tools); and Technic Future Automation (diversified electronic plating equipment).

Technic serves customer in a variety of markets, including decorative applications, electronic connectors, industrial finishing, photovoltaic cell manufacturing electronic components, printed circuit boards, and semiconductors (front end manufacturing, wafer level packaging, amd lead frames).

To grow its business and maintain an edge in innovative technology, the company collaborates with industry partners, including (for solar applications) Industrial Technology Research Institute of Taiwan, Photovoltaic Manufacturing Consortium of New York, and Owens Design. The company's equipment partners include Surface Technology Solutions and Dynatronix. Printed circuit board partners include include Alfachimici Spa, and Circuit Automation.

In 2013 Technic released its new liquid photoimageable solder mask for LED applications. The new sold mask provides superior flexibility and high reflectivity and is resistant to discoloration.

The company was founded in 1944.
