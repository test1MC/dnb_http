H&R WASAG manufactures crude-oil based chemical and pharmaceutical raw materials and high-precision plastics parts. The company has expertise in the making of precision-molded parts, industrial explosives, gunpowder, and blasting machines. Catering mostly to Germany, the company has main sites in Germany, the UK, the Netherlands, Czech Republic, Australia, Thailand, Malaysia, South Africa, and China. Each year H&R WASAG sells a total of 734,000 tons of products within its chemical pharmaceutical raw materials division. The automotive industry counts as its plastics division's main customer, but the division also makes plastic parts for medical devices and other industrial applications.
