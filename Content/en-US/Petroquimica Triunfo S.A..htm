Petroquimica Triunfo hopes to triumph in the world of chemicals. The company, which has the capacity to produce 160 thousand tons of resins per year, makes chemical compounds and coatings. Its Trithene branded products include low and medium density polyethylene and mixtures used in food and pharmaceutical packaging. Trilene includes mixtures used to coat films used in food and liquid packaging while Tritheva copolymers of ethylene and vinyl acetate are used for shoes, asphalt, laminated films, and food packaging. Triunfo's customers are domestic as well as international companies. Petroquisa, a subsidiary of Brazilian-government run Petrobras , owns about 85% of Triunfo.
