Grupo Leche Pascual strives to be the cream of the crop. The company prepares and packages more than 300 dairy, cereal, and beverage products, including fluid milk (fresh and shelf-stable), cream, fruit juices, mineral water, yogurt,  liquid egg products, and breakfast cereals. Its brands include Bezoya, Essential, Funciona, MásVital, Pascual, ViveSoy, Yosport, and Zumosol. In 2000 the company was the first foreign company to earn "Grade A dairy" status from the USDA and now exports yogurt to the US. With the exception of South and Central America, Grupo Leche Pascual sells its products worldwide. The company is owned by Spain's Pascual family.
