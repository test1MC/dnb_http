Tollgrade Communications helps those who test themselves. The company's system test devices let telephone and utility companies diagnose problems in their networks from remote locations. It serves some of the world's largest telecom firms with products that verify service order readiness, diagnose subscriber problems, and provide physical layer fault detection and metallic testing, among other functions. Tollgrade's LightHouse product offers power grid monitoring and fault detection for utilities. It also offers professional and managed services such as migration planning, project management, network monitoring, system audits, and repair. Private equity firm Golden Gate Capital owns the company.

Tollgrade serves customers worldwide, but primarily operates in the US and Europe. It has US facilities in New Jersey, Pennsylvania, and Virginia. Its international locations are in Germany and the UK.

The company has been slowly expanding its geographic reach.
