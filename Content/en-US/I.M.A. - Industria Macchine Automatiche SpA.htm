Don't blame I.M.A. - Industria Macchine Automatiche if they get wrapped up in their work. I.M.A. designs and makes automatic machines for processing and packaging various drugs, cosmetics, coffee, and tea. I.M.A. plies tea bagging machines to sterile processing equipment and end-of-line machines, as well as solid dose manufacturing services, liquid filling, freeze-drying, labeling, blistering, and cartoning. Its headquarters, and branches and agents in Europe, America, and Asia drive sales -- the bulk of comes from outside of Italy. Customers include Novartis , Unilever , and R.C. Bigelow . Founded in 1961, IMA was transformed into a joint-stock enterprise in 1963 by the Vacchi family's purchase of a 52% stake.
