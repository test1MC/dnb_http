Dongbu Steel produces an array of steel products that are sold internationally. Founded in 1967, the company manufactures cold-rolled steel, steel sheets, pipes, H-beams, wire products, and steel and iron coatings, among many other steel products. With four manufacturing mills in  South Korea, Dongbu produces 2.5 million tons of steel annually. Offices in China, Thailand, Japan, and the US help market Dongbu's products throughout the world. Though publicly traded, the company is a part of the Dongbu Group, which ranks among South Korea's largest conglomerates, which also includes the likes of Dongbu Insurance and Dongbu HiTek .
