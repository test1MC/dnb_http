Oxygen Biotherapeutics prescribes some good ol' O 2 for whatever ails you. The development stage biotechnology company develops products that help deliver oxygen to tissues. Its Dermacyte topical cosmetic line is designed to improve the appearance of skin. A concentrate is currently available and the company is developing Dermacyte formulas specifically for acne, rosacea, sunscreen, and other applications. Pipeline products include Oxycyte, an IV emulsion created to speed surgical and other healing being tested in Israel and Switzerland and Wundecyte gel and bandages in pre-clinical trials. Oxygen Biotherapeutics was formed through a reverse merger with Synthetic Blood International in 2008.
