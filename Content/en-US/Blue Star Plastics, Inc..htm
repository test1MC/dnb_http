Delivering products once in a blue moon is just the opposite of Blue Star Plastics' work ethic and track record. The company provides custom injection molding of thermoplastic components for companies in the automotive, consumer electronics, industrial controls, material handling, and paper products industries. It also offers contract manufacturing services for plastic assembled products, as well as providing additional services such as assembly, heat staking, hot stamping, pad printing, and sonic welding. Blue Star Plastics operates 17 thermoplastic molding machines, ranging in size from 44 tons to 720 tons, at its 60,000 sq. ft. manufacturing and warehousing space.
