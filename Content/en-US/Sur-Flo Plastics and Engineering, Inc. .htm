Sur-Flo Plastics and Engineering keeps automobile manufacturers stocked with plastic parts. The company is a tier-1 supplier of injection molded plastic parts to automobile manufacturers such as General Motors , Ford , and Chrysler . Sur-Flo uses injection and insert molding, vacuum forming, and gas assist processes to create products such as fan shrouds, wheelwell splash shield assemblies, and center console trays; non-automotive products include radio knobs, sinks, and stanchions. Manufacturing capabilities range from single prototypes to large capacity production runs. Sur-Form, Sur-Flo's sister company, makes custom thermoformed and injected molded plastic products. Sur-Flo is owned by the Wolfbauer family.
