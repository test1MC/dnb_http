MS Instruments (MSI) can help you dodge a bullet. The company designs and manufactures a variety of ballistic measuring instrumentation systems. MSI's products include velocity measuring, targeting, training, and range systems that are designed for army, police, air, and naval forces. It also makes instruments used to detect hostile fire and protect security forces from attack. The company's clients have included the Royal Air Force, the Austrian government, the Malaysian Ministry of Defense, the US armed forces, and the Saudi armed forces and police. MSI has been in business since 1972.

The company has partners located across Canada, Taiwan, France, Hong Kong, India, Indonesia, Israel, Japan, Mexico, Singapore, the US, and Turkey.

MSI's products are used by manufacturing companies, governments, and armed forces.
