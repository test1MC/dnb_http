Ancient Prometheus was thinking ahead when he gave humans fire. Now we can enjoy what Prometheus International has to offer. The company is a leading importer and wholesaler of cigar and cigarette lighters and cases, ashtrays, cigar cutters, humidors, and pipes. The firm's products are made in a plethora of countries (including Italy, Spain, Germany, Japan, France, Korea, and Austria) and are sold in about 25 countries worldwide. Tobacco and duty free shops, such as Barclay Rex, Grand Havana Room, and The Ultimate Cigar, carry Prometheus' smoking items and related accessories. The company was founded in 1992.
