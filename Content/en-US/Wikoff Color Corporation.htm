Wikoff Color makes a rainbow of color products for use by graphic arts companies. The company is dedicated to developing tailor-made inks to maximize printing pressroom efficiencies and providing highly technical, service-oriented approaches to ink making. Wikoff Color makes aqueous coatings and overprint varnishes in addition to its line of inks marketed around the globe. Its products are primarily used by customers in the graphic arts industry for lithography, flexography, letterpress, and gravure printing processes. Wikoff Color also makes functional coatings and specialty items.

The company operates approximately 30 manufacturing plants and service centers, mainly in the Eastern half of North America but also in British Columbia and the UK.

Wikoff Color Corporation is a manufacturer of printing inks, varnishes and coatings for the graphic arts industry. It makes all of the major printing processes' product lines including flexo, gravure, and litho, as well as functional coatings, energy curable products, and specialty items.

Wikoff Color makes inks and coatings for the Commercial, Digital, Packaging, Security, Tag and Label, and other markets. Its major distributors include Antalis GMS; Cano Industrial; Destin Soluciones, SRL; Eurotex Cia, LTDA; Inversiones Kaluca, C.A.; and Serviflex Arco Internacional S.A.S.

The company is ramping up its US operations. In 2013 it converted a space in its Fort Mill, South Carolina manufacturing plant into an energy-cure inkjet ink manufacturing facility and cleanroom to help it become a major player in the growing digital market. The facility houses some of the industry's most advanced manufacturing and test equipment, providing an ideal environment for manufacturing inkjet inks. It also opened a branch it Sacramento, California in 2012 to better serve its West Coast customers.

To meet growing demand in Europe, in 2012 the company established a manufacturing plant in the UK (in Leeds), Wikoff Colors' first plant outside of North America.

The company is owned by its employees.

Wikoff Color was founded in 1956 by Fred Wikoff.
