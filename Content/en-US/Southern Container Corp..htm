Southern Container Corp. has packed a lot into its nearly 60 years of existence, and not just in the nation's South. A manufacturer of containerboard and corrugated packaging, Southern Container designs and produces a hefty array of shipping containers, point-of-purchase displays, and customized packaging. Products cater to large and small food, beverage, cosmetic, and pharmaceutical companies, among others. Its offerings are enhanced by a range of related printing and graphic services. The company operates joint ventures with microflute packager GraphCorr, pre-print operators SCI-California and SCI-Canada, and specialty folding carton-maker Pohilig Brothers.
