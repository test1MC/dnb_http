Husbands in the Columbus, Ohio, area might avoid the dog house by paying a visit to an Anthony-Thomas Candy store. The company makes boxed chocolate confections, including buckeyes (peanut butter-filled), truffles, cordials, butter cremes, and chocolate-covered nuts. It also makes candy bars, novelty shaped chocolates, and jelly beans. The company's products are sold in and around Columbus through the company's 15 retail candy stores (Anthony-Thomas Candy Shoppes).
