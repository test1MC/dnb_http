Alamance Foods/Triton Water manufactures branded and private-label water, flavored drinks, and freeze pops under brand names such as Freeze Pops, Fun Pops, and Happy Drinks. Its branded water is sold under the Triton Water label. Sold under the Classic Cream brand, Alamance's aerosol whipped cream comes from a can, providing a quick and easy way to take a few shots of the sweet stuff when no one's looking. Alamance's customers are located throughout the US. Chairman and CEO Bill Scott's family owns and operates Alamance Foods, which was founded more than 100 years ago in North Carolina as a seller of home-churned butter and cream.
