Archant is a regional UK newspaper publisher with a portfolio of daily and weekly papers and associated websites. Its daily mastheads include the Evening News (Norwich) and the East Anglican Daily Times . The company also publishes a number of regional periodicals, as well as such consumer magazines as The English Home, Professional Photographer , and Sport Driver . Other operations include custom publishing and commercial printing. A family-owned company, Archant traces its root to the founding of Norfolk News in 1845.
