The world's top steel producer is getting more advanced. China Gerui Advanced Materials is a contract manufacturer of cold-rolled, high-precision narrow strip steel products. It's the largest Chinese manufacturer of cold-rolled steel not owned by the government. China Gerui has a processing capacity of about 250,000 tons, and its chromium coating production line has an annual capacity of 50,000 tons. (The company is expanding its capacity to 500,000 tons). China Gerui does not export; its products are sold to domestic Chinese customers in the food, packaging, construction materials, telecom cable and equipment, and electrical appliances industries. The family of CEO Mingwang Lu owns 72% of its stock.
