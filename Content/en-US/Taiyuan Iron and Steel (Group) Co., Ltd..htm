This company's position in the steel industry has been hard fought. For some time Taiyuan Iron & Steel (TISCO) has been China's largest stainless steel producer, but the company was never satisfied with that distinction alone. Expansion efforts have increased its annual production to 3 million tons, making it the world's largest supplier. TICSO's total steel production output is more than 12 million tons. Its other products include boiler, naval, pure iron, vessel, low alloy, die, and alloy structure plates; quality carbon steel; and structure carbon steel. Taiyuan has a joint venture with Harsco Corp. which processes and recovers by-products from stainless and carbon steel slag production.

TISCO has operations in China's North, Northeast, Northwest, Central, South, Southwest, and East, including in Shanxi and Shandong provinces, as well as in Hong Kong, Germany, and the US.

The company has an annual capacity of 12 million tons steel (of which 4.3 million tons are stainless steel).

TISCO engages in the research, development, production, and processing of stainless, special, and carbon steel. The company produces hot rolled strips, nonmagnetic medium plates, premium carbon medium plates, and forged stainless steel products.

The company serves the industry such as, Petrochemical, Power, Ship-Tower and Container, Automobile, Boiler and Pressure Vessel, Food and Environment Protection, Tower Decoration and Mechanical Products.

TISCO is focusing on upgrading its product mix and quality.

In 2014, the company signed the agreement with China Minmetals for the long term cooperation in terms of metal raw materials and steel products, including logistics and storage, steel e-commerce, international resources, and market exploration.
