Hey there, what is that field of red in the valley of the golden sun? It must be VSP Products' bins of tomatoes laid out to dry. Formerly Valley Sun of California, the company contracts with tomato growers in the central section of the Golden State to purchase their "raw" material, and produces and markets sun-dried tomato products in packages and jars. Its customers include food manufacturers, foodservice operators, and retail food outlets throughout the US. VSP also provides custom tomato processing (diced, chopped, powdered, or julienned) and packaging services. Its retail customers include Golub (operator of Price Chopper supermarkets) and Costco .
