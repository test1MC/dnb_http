Construcciones y Auxiliar de Ferrocarriles (CAF) doesn't ride the rails, but it makes it possible for others to. The firm makes a variety of railroad vehicles and related equipment, such as diesel trains, trailer cars, freight cars, and bogies (locomotive undercarriages with wheels that swivel for negotiating curves). Components include wheels, axles, tires, couplers, and brake blocks. CAF also offers related services, such as maintenance and refurbishing, and works with other railway industry companies, utilities, governments, and financial institutions to provide complete turnkey railway solutions. The company has plants in its home country of Spain, as well as in Brazil, France, Mexico, and the US.
