Marcy Laboratories has mercy for companies that need a little polish. The company is a contract manufacturer of fragrances for the personal care products and cosmetics industries. Its products include private label cosmetics fragrances, soaps, and nail polish. Marcy Laboratories also provides filling and packaging services. The family-owned and -operated company was established in 1963 by William Martin.
