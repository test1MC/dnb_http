Zodi Outback Gear takes the "rough" out of "roughin' it." Whether you're mule packing in the Sierras, running a wilderness camp in the Idaho mountains, or dove hunting with buddies on your Texas ranch, you might want to end your day with Zodi, which offers more than 50 camping and hunting comfort products. The company pioneered the portable hot shower, and also manufactures and markets shower shelters, water heaters, and tent heaters in 75 countries around the world. Additionally, Zodi targets military and industrial customers with hot decon (sometimes referred to as haz-mat or hazardous material) showers used by soldiers, fire fighters, and emergency medical teams worldwide.
