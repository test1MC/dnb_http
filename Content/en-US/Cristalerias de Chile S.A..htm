Cristalerías de Chile, known as Cristalchile, just can't contain itself in the bottle-making business. The company dominates Chile's glass container industry. Its 200-plus domestic and foreign customers include laboratories and makers of wine, beer, nonalcoholic drinks, pharmaceuticals, and food products. Customers include Compañía Cervecerías Unidas , Viña Concha y Toro , and Viña San Pedro, of which Cristalerías controls. The company boasts a technical agreement with Owens-Illinois , as well as stakes in broadcasting businesses. Cristalchile's primary stakeholder is Electro Metalúrgica (Elecmetal) and affiliates. María Luisa Vial, widow of Cristalerías magnate Ricardo Claro, holds more than 45% of Elecmetal.
