Inner Mongolia Yili Industrial Group (Yili Group) manufactures milk products. Its lineup of more than 1,000 products includes fluid milk, milk powder, yogurt, shakes, cheese, as well as iced treats. The Chinese firm has processing sites in Inner Mongolia, Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Heilongjiang, Hebei, Shanxi, Guangdong, and Xinjiang. Yili is one of only dairy producers that has catered to both the Olympic Games and the World Expo in China. The company was founded in 1993, has nearly 100 subsidiaries, and trades on the Shanghai stock exchange.
