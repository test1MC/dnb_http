ZT Group International assembles components from A to Z to supply computer systems to its customers. The company builds and supports Microsoft -based desktop PCs, notebooks, and servers in a wide variety of configurations. Selling directly and through resellers and systems integrators, it targets businesses of all sizes, government and education organizations, and consumers. Its online and retail partners include Costco Wholesale , OfficeMax , QVC , Staples , and Target . The company builds its systems with processors from AMD and Intel . It also counts Fujitsu and Seagate among its strategic partners.
