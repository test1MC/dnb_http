Town & Country Linen imports and manufactures textiles like table linens, kitchen textiles, decorative pillows, throws, and bath products for city and rural dwellers. Its products are sold nationwide at department and discount stores like Wal-Mart , Kmart , and other retailers that sell textiles. The company got its start back in 1953 when David and Jeffrey Beyda's paternal grandfather sold handkerchiefs from a pushcart in the streets of Manhattan, New York.
