Kingfield Electronics wants to be a king in the field of contract electronics manufacturers (CEMs). The company specializes in low- and medium-volume printed circuit boards (PCBs) using surface mount and through-hole assembly. It also offers product design, coating, rapid prototyping, test and inspection, complete systems (box build), cable assembly, system integration services. Manufacturers use Kingfield's wares in aerospace and defense, process control, scientific and laboratory instrument, industrial, and other equipment. Kingfield, which was founded in 1985, continues to be family-owned and operated.

Kingfield Electronics has expanded into a one-stop-shop manufacturer by offering high-volume PCB production through a partner in Asia. It has also added product sourcing to its supply chain services, using vendors located in the UK, US, Asia/Pacific, and Eastern European regions. This allows the company to provide services that range from engineering and design through finished product shipping directly to customer and aftermarket repair and spares supply.

Kingfield works mostly in businesses in process control instrumentation, scientific and laboratory instrumentation, defense, aerospace, and oil and gas.
