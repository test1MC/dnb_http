Luxury jeweler Theo Fennell offers the designs of its namesake founder at prestigious locations (such as its flagship Fulham Road store and at Harrods and Harvey Nichols -- Dubai). The firm also designs and fabricates silver gifts, and tableware, as well as corporate trophies and awards. Products are pricey, but the company was forced to introduce a lower cost line of silver jewelry amid the global economic crisis. Customers have included celebrities such as Liz Hurley and Victoria Beckham.

The company has its stores operating in UK, Barbados, Beijing, Hong Kong, UAE, Kazakhstan, Kuwait, Russia, Republic of Tatarstan, Ukraine, and Azerbaijan. It has 19 stores in the UK and 15 international stores.

Theo Fennell has been concentrating on expanding its retail efforts into Eastern Europe and the Middle East.
