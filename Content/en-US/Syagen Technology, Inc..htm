Syagen Technology makes mass spectrometry technology instruments (the Guardian, Narcotics Trace Portal, and FieldMate) used for a variety of molecular analysis applications, including high-throughput chemical analysis for drug discovery, explosives detection for aviation security, and chemical and biological detection for federal, state, and local defense. The company's atmospheric pressure photoionization (APPI) sources are used in instrumentation made by Agilent Technologies , Thermo Fisher Scientific , and Waters . Customers have included the US Department of Defense , Homeland Security , and Northrop Grumman . In 2011 Syagen was acquired by Morpho Detection, a subsidiary of French defense firm SAFRAN .
