TurboSonic Technologies has a snooty attitude towards soot. The company provides industrial customers with air-pollution control equipment and products designed to enhance the performance of industrial process equipment. Selling its products worldwide, the company serves clients in the food and beverage, forest products, mining, oil and gas, waste management, and other industries. In early 2013, TurboSonic Technologies was acquired by MEGTEC Systems , a maker of equipment (dryers, splicers, and oxidizers) for the commercial, newspaper, packaging, and digital printing industries.
