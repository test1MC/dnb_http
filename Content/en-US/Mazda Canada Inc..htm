"Zoom-Zoom" has the same meaning in English and French at bilingual Mazda Canada. Established in 1968, Mazda Canada is a wholesale distributor of Mazda vehicles and parts. The company has about 160 franchised, independent Mazda dealers across Canada. Models include the Mazda3 and Mazda6 sedans, the CX-7 and CX-9 crossover vehicles, the MX-5 convertible roadster, the RX-8 four-door sports car, the Tribute SUV, B-Series pickups, and the Mazda5 minivan. Mazda Canada, a subsidiary of Japan's Mazda Motor Corporation , has regional offices in Montréal and Vancouver, in addition to its headquarters in Richmond Hill, Ontario. The company maintains a parts distribution center for all of Canada in Mississauga, Ontario.
