As the North American agent for Columbian fruit puree and juice concentrate processor Compania Envasadora del Atlantico, Dennick FruitSource sells tropical fruit juices, concentrates, and purees made from acerola, guava, mango, and passion fruit, as well as from less exotic fruits such as apricot, banana, peach, pear, and pineapple to industrial customers in North America. Its fruits are sourced from growers all over the world, including in Brazil, Peru, Columbia, India, and South Africa. Dennick's standard, organic, and kosher fruit products ship in drums, bins, or tankers and used by other food and beverage manufactures who use them to make various products, including yogurt, baked goods, jams, and jellies.
