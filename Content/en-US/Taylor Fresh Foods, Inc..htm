Taylor Fresh Foods could be considered a custom seamstress of edible veggie creations. The company fashions prepackaged salads and fresh-cut fruits and vegetables for the US foodservice and wholesale food industries. Its products are sold under the Taylor Farms label. The company's Taylor Organic unit produces and packages USDA certified organic foods. A leading supplier of salad and fruits and vegetables, Taylor Fresh Foods operates about a dozen processing plants in the US and in Mexico. Founded in 1994, the food company is owned by CEO Bruce Taylor. The manufacturer has grown through numerous acquisitions over the years, including the purchase of competitor River Ranch Fresh Foods .

Taylor operates processing plants in California, Arizona, Colorado, Tennessee, Texas, Florida, Maryland, and a single location south of the border in Mexico.

Taylor markets its fruits and vegetables under the Taylor Farms and River Ranch labels. It also sells USDA certified organic foods.
