There's no glass ceiling on growth at AGC Glass Europe. The company is an arm of Asahi Glass , the world's #1 glass producer. AGC Glass Europe operates through four divisions, each owning multiple business units: the Raw Glass division manufactures float, coated, patterned, and laminated glass, and mirrors; the Processed Glass produces architectural, industrial, and appliance glass products; Solar Glass, products for photovoltaic use; and Automotive Glass, tempered and laminated windows for vehicles. The company boasts 18 float plants, seven automotive, and 100-plus processing and distribution units Europe wide. Formerly known as Glaverbel, the company evolved through the merger of Glaver and Univerbel in 1961.
