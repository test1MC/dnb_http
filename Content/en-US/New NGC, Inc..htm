New NGC is always board at work. Doing business as National Gypsum Company, it is one of the largest gypsum wallboard manufacturers in the US. National Gypsum sells wallboard under the Gold Bond brand name. The company also produces other building products such as joint treatment compounds (ProForm) and cement board (PermaBase). Its testing services division tests the acoustical, fire, and structural properties of building materials. National Gypsum has about 40 plants in the US and Canada and sells its products worldwide to the construction industry. The company is a subsidiary of Delcor, itself a subsidiary of privately held Golden Eagle Industries.

National Gypsum has nearly 40 operating plants, mines, quarries, and support facilities throughout the US and Canada. It owns 17 operating gypsum board plant locations strategically located near metropolitan areas and four cement board plants.

The company sells its products through distributors such as Allied Building Products Corp. (National Gypsum Drywall Products), Gypsum Management & Supply, Inc. (distributor of gypsum); and retailers and wholesalers such as Avanti Tile & Stone (tile and tile related installation products), Do It Best Corp (Online hardware store), Guardian Building Products (supplier of building materials), and Component Assembly Systems, Inc. (Northeastern US-based full service commercial carpentry contractor).

Key to National Gypsum's strategy is vertical integration. In an effort to control the quality and cost of its products, it owns three paper mills that make liner paper for drywall, as well as eight gypsum mines and quarries.
