Novogen has novel operations in prescription drug development. Its Australian prescription drug division and its US subsidiary ( MEI Pharma ) conduct research and development efforts in areas including oncology, cardiovascular health, and inflammatory conditions. MEI Pharma develops drugs for cancer treatment, targeting conditions including prostate, ovarian, and pancreatic cancers. Novogen sold its consumer drugs division in 2011. The following year it sold its Glycotex subsidiary, which was developing topical wound medications. It also announced that it would explore further strategic options, including the spinoff of MEI Pharma.
