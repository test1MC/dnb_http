Unrelated to the Strategic Defense Initiative or to the military-industrial complex for that matter, SDI Industries makes material handling equipment and supplies software for distribution center automation. SDI provides consulting services and designs, builds, and installs logistics systems for retail, wholesale, fulfillment, and e-commerce clients worldwide. The company's products range from high speed unit sortation systems, to advanced picking systems and IT infrastructure support. The UK-headquartered company sets its sights on international markets, positioning operations in Europe, India, and South America.
