Sunshine Homes hopes the sun is always shining through the windows of its houses. The company makes single-section manufactured homes, which include two to three bedrooms, and multi-section manufactured homes, which feature three- to five-bedrooms, under the Brookwood, Sunshine, and Georgetown names. Homes range in size from about 1,680 sq. ft. to 2,160 sq. ft. Features may include hand-laid rock fireplaces, crown molding, cathedral ceilings, and oak cabinets and window sills. Sunshine Homes are available through dealers in 18 states in the southeastern, south-central, and midwestern US. Sunshine is owned by president John Bostick. Bostick family holdings also include pet food maker Sunshine Mills.
