3M Touch Systems UK provides touch systems and technologies for those who want to reach out and touch their displays. The UK branch of the touch systems segment (headquartered in Austin, Texas) of consumer and electronics giant 3M , the company sells and distributes two major types of touchscreens: capacitive touch (using electrostatic disruption) and, invented by 3M in 2002, dispersive signal technology (DST, pressure-based recognition). It also offers a range of touch monitors. The global Touch Systems unit was formed in 2001 with the acquisitions of Massachusetts-based MicroTouch Systems and the Canada-based Dynapro Thin Films. The UK branch is responsible for sales throughout Europe, the Middle East, and Africa.
