Great walls -- decorative china not included -- for homes and other establishments are the business of British Gypsum. One of the UK's leading makers of plasterboard for walls and ceilings (sold under the Gyproc and Glasroc brands), British Gypsum also makes metal studs and channels (through a process that includes the patented UltralSTEEL hardening technique) for its wall systems and produces Thistle brand plaster products. The company's Beautiful Acoustics unit makes performance acoustic tiles used by hospitals, schools, and offices. The company has five manufacturing plants in the UK. British Gypsum is a subsidiary of BPB , which is itself owned by mega materials group Saint Gobain .

British Gypsum operates five manufacturing plants across England.

The company's innovative products and services help to create partitions, provide comfort, protect against fire, and insulate against sound. Marketed under the Gyproc, Thistle, Gypframe, Glasroc, and British Gypsum brands, its products provide the basis for the UK's leading range of partition, wall lining, ceiling, flooring, insulation and fire protection systems.

Thistle plasters offer a full range of specific and multi-purpose solutions for all internal plastering needs and are designed to suit either hand or machine application.

The modern, engineered alternative to traditional timber and blockwork construction, Gypframe-based wall, ceiling and floor systems are quicker and easier to install, whilst meeting the highest performance requirements.

British Gypsum produces acoustic ceilings. It also offers a comprehensive bespoke service for Gypframe metal profiles, Gyproc plasterboard, Glasroc specialist board, and Gyptone Acoustic Ceilings.

British Gypsum's technical advice center handles more than 80,000 enquiries per year.

The company serves a range of sectors, including Education, Healthcare, High-Rise Multi-Occupancy, Offices, Off-site, Renovation, and New Build Residential.

British Gypsum projects have included main contractor work for the Derby Riverlights development and a Leeds Carnegie College dormitory, as well as ceilings for the Loughborough Magistrates Court and a student center at Glasgow Caledonian University.

British Gypsum accounted for 26% of Saint Gobain 's revenues in 2014.

To support the future generation of plasters and dryliners the company invests heavily in its Thistle Partnership which supports 69 colleges around the UK with free products an information. The Learning HUB, a lifetime members' scheme, provides access to relevant literature, exclusive videos, health and safety information, product data sheets and posters. It also hosts a forum where students, lecturers and its Technical Academy team can interact with each other.

As part of its strategy to pursue environmental transparency and make it easier for specifiers to gather evidence for building certification schemes, in 2015 British Gypsum launched seven new Environmental Product Declarations covering nine products. These include declarations for Thistle BoardFinish, MultiFinish, BondingCoat, Browning, Universal OneCoat, DuraFinish, SprayFinish, ToughCoat and HardWall.

British Gypsum launched Thistle Magnetic Plaster in 2014. This innovative magnetic plaster provides a smooth, high quality surface creating an interactive wall that attracts magnets, ideal for home, as well as working environments. It also unveiled The White Book System Selector online tool to help streamline the specification process for construction professionals. The tool allows specifiers to search and filter through tested British Gypsum plaster and drylining products to select the right specifications for their projects.
