You could say Capricorn Pharma has an oral fixation. The contract manufacturer makes time-release capsules and dissolvable tablets for prescription drugmakers, as well as over-the-counter (OTC) pharmaceuticals such as chewable antacids, cough drops, and children's grape-flavored chewable acetaminophen tablets. In addition, Capricorn Pharma makes dietary supplements, including chewable fiber, glucosamine, and calcium tablets. Customers include Teva and International Specialty Products . Capricorn Pharma operates a 40,000 sq. ft. plant in the US; it also has a research and development center in India. The company was founded in 1998 and majority-owned by CEO S. Rao Cherukuri.
