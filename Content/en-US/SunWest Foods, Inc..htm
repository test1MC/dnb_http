From rice (not soup) to nuts, SunWest Foods has got the goods. The company supplies brown, white, wild, and other specialty rices and rice blends to retailers and wholesalers in the US. Its rice products are sold under the SunWest Organics and SunWest Rice brand names. SunWest Foods also distributes nuts (almonds, pecans, pistachios, hazelnuts, and walnuts) under the SunNuts label, and has divisions devoted to wild and specialty rices, and rice pilafs and blends. The company's rice milling facility is the second largest mill in the State of California. SunWest Foods was founded in 1986.
