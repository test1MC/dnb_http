If you love to read the funnies, turn to Reed Brennan Media Associates (RBMA).  The company collects, scans, edits, assembles, and distributes comics and non-news syndicated features, including advice and lifestyle columns, as well as puzzles and games. RBMA provides services for more than 300 newspapers. It is owned by the Hearst Corporation and falls within its Hearst Entertainment & Syndication operations. RBMA content primarily comes from King Features Syndicate , also owned by Hearst.
