Tri-Star Electronics makes custom cable assemblies, specialty connectors, wire processing equipment, and electrical contacts. The company's core product lines are contacts, connectors, crimp splices, and wire processing equipment. It also offers training and engineering services. The company's customers are primarily equipment manufacturers serving the aerospace, avionics, and electronics industries. Tri-Star Electronics has facilities in the US and Switzerland, and it sells worldwide through distributors, systems integrators, and resellers including Powell Electronics and Avnet . Carlisle Companies acquired the company in 2011 for $285 million.
