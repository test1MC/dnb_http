Makers of counterfeit products beware -- Authentix has the tools to knock you out of business. The company provides product authentication technology to help detect and deter counterfeit or unauthorized products. It makes labels and authenticated packaging, authentication markers for use inside of products, and technologies for tracing and tracking products. Active in 18 countries, Authentix sell its products to customers in industries such as health care, oil and gas production, and consumer goods manufacturing, as well as financial services providers and government agencies.  Formed in 2003 through the merger of Biocode, Calyx, and Isotag, the company was bought out by The Carlyle Group in 2008.
