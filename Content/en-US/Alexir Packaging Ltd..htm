Running a successful company is always a gamble, an while Alexir Packaging may know when to hold 'em, its real business is knowing how to fold 'em. The company, which also does business as The Alexir Partnership, manufactures printed folding cartons primarily for the retail, cosmetic, food, health care, and pharmaceutical industries. With plants based in Kent and East Sussex, it produces more than 150 million cartons catering to the UK and Europe each year. privately owned Alexis also offers in-house graphic and design capabilities, as well as services related to labels, leaflets, vacuum formings, and foil-block printing.

The company has packaging and carton manufacturing plants in Edenbridge, Kent and Uckfield, East Sussex. It service customers in the UK and Continental Europe.

Alexir's in-house design studio is an independent full-service design agency that has been creating branded packaging solutions for more than 20 years. Its areas of expertise include name generation, brand creation, and graphic and digital design, as well as structural and design packaging.

The company's Todhunter brand offers an array of bespoke hand woven, fitted hampers, and food gifts all filled with specialty foods and wine. Alexir's sells toiletries under the Wimborne & Sloane brand.

Alexir's customers include Tesco , Batchelors, Sainsburys , Lurpark, Higgidy, Arla , and Peppersmith.

The company has developed many innovative packaging solutions for its customers and has been first to market with carton-based packs in industries dominated by plastic.

Alexir's proximity to London is ideal to service the major airline caterers and distributors who recognize the benefits of working in partnership with a local specialist food packaging and co-packing group.

In 2015 the company launched a range of branded National Trust food gifts and hampers.

Alexir was founded in 1989.
