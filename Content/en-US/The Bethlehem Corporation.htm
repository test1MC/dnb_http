The Bethlehem Corporation doesn't build mangers or sew swaddling; for more than a century the company has engineered and marketed material processing equipment for a range of industries. The short list of Bethlehem's thermal process equipment includes the Porcupine Processor, used to heat and cool chemicals, and the Thermal Disc Processor, to cool, dry, heat, and sterilize coal fines. Its Tower Filter Press handles mineral to food filtration processes. For specialized equipment that's gone caput, Bethlehem will rebuild it. Manufacturing has been outsourced since 2000. The company sells globally, both directly and through representative agreements, to companies in chemical, plastics, and refining industries.
