SpaceAge Control makes miniature position transducers (position sensors, displacement sensors, string pots) and air data products (air data booms, air direction vanes, temperature sensors, trailing bombs). The company has more than 900 customers worldwide in a variety of industries, such as aerospace and automotive manufacturing, including BAE SYSTEMS , Boeing , Ford Motor , General Motors , Honeywell International , Lockheed Martin , and Northrop Grumman . SpaceAge Control was established in 1968 (the year of the first manned Apollo space mission, Apollo 7).
