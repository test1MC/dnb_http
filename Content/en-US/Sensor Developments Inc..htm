Sensor Developments makes force and torque sensors and instruments for testing brakes, engines, drive trains, steering, suspensions, brakes, tires, power windows, sunroofs, aircraft controls, spot welders, and power tools. The company also offers repair and calibration services. Established in 1976 by CEO Albert Brendel, Sensor Developments was acquired in 2012 by HITEC Sensor Solution.
