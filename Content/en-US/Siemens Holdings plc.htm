Siemens Holdings plc is the UK holding company for German electronics and electrical engineering group Siemens AG . The Siemens UK portfolio of companies provides services and designs, develops, and manufactures electrical and electronic systems through its parent company's global business sectors: Industry (customized motors, fire safety systems, water treatment, high speed trains); Energy (industrial gas and wind turbines, power quality and transmission); and Health care (scanners, laboratory automation, and hearing systems). More than half of UK revenues come from the provision of services. Siemens plc's client roster includes Barclays and Citigroup . Siemens Holdings has had a presence in the UK since 1843.

Siemens plc has a significant presence throughout the UK, with 13 manufacturing sites and more than 25 major offices. With the head office in the UK is based in Frimley, it also has other sites in and around major towns and cities including Manchester, Glasgow, Poole, Oxford, Nottingham, Durham, and Lincoln.

The UK company is primarily engaged in the manufacture of superconducting magnets for Siemens MRI (magnetic resonance imaging) scanners that are used worldwide; the production of variable speed drives that control the speed of motors driving production lines and baggage-handling systems; industrial gas turbines; automotive components; and traffic signals. The company also offers a range of support services, including everything from real estate planning and development to maintenance of electric train fleets.

The company offers its services across the power generation services, energy management, mobility, digital factory, process industries and drives, financial services, and health care sectors.
