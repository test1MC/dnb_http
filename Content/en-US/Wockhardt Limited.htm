Wockhardt works hard in the pharmaceutical world. The research and pharmaceutical manufacturing firm employs hundreds of scientists who are focused on developing recombinant biopharmaceuticals as well as generic and new drug discovery. Wockhardt's products include injectables, tablets, liquids, and topicals (creams and ointments) that it manufactures to sell itself and for AstraZeneca , Johnson & Johnson , and Pfizer , to name a few of its pharmaceutical partners. Wockhardt operates 15 manufacturing plants in France, India, Ireland, the UK, and the US, and has filed more than 250 patents for biotechnology products. The company is a subsidiary of Khorakiwala Holdings and Investments.
