Quabbin Wire & Cable is fighting to protect the future of copper wire makers as the new order of fiber optics is steadily gaining control in the world of connectivity. Quabbin Wire & Cable makes low temperature thermoplastic shielded and unshielded cables used to connect audio and video devices, computers, control and instrumentation systems, LAN systems, point-of-sale equipment, and telecommunication products. The company developed a polyurethane jacketed cable for manufacturing high-strength Industrial Ethernet patch cords. It has been developing other cable jackets that can accommodate various environmental stresses. Quabbin Wire & Cable was founded in 1975 by CEO Paul Engel.
