Corporate communication is the language this company speaks. PR Newswire Association is a leading distributor of corporate news and public relations materials all around the world. The company serves tens of thousands of clients from offices in the Americas, Europe, Middle East, Africa, and the Asia-Pacific region. PR Newswire broadcasts press releases, photos, and multimedia presentations in 40 languages to thousands of news organizations, websites, online databases, and other information outlets. In 2015 Cision agreed to pay $841 million to acquire the company and in 2016 the deal closed.

Although the company distributes material around the world, the US accounts for more than half of its revenue.

PR Newswire has managed to maintain its usefulness in the digital age. The company provides tools that allow its customers to target their communications to specific audiences. It also offers its clients more sophisticated analytics so companies can measure the effectiveness of their campaigns.
