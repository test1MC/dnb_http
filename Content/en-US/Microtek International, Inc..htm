Microtek International is an image expert. The maker of digital image processing equipment specializes in flatbed image scanners that create photos, illustrations, filmstrips, and negatives. Microtek's products range from its ScanMaker line of desktop scanners for consumers and small offices, to the high-end ArtixScan series of scanners for pre-press, graphic design, and digital printing. The company also makes video projectors, LCD monitors, and digital video cameras. Microtek, which was founded in 1980 by chairman Benny Hsu, sells its scanners under its own brand names and manufactures them for other companies.
