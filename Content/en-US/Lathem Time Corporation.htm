Lathem Time is a stickler for punctuality. The family-owned company develops automated time and attendance systems that help employers track when their employees come and go. Lathem Time's products include employee time and attendance systems, employee management and scheduling software, and mechanical and electronic time recorders that workers use to punch in and out. Its TouchStation biometric system uses fingerprints to identify employees and record their total working hours; its PayClock series combines badge readers with time calculating systems for businesses of varying sizes. Established in 1919, the company also makes analog and digital clocks, as well as time cards and time and date stamps.
