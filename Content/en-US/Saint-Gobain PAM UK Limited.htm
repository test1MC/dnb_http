It may have a great set of pipes, but Saint-Gobain PAM UK is only interesting in singing the praises for its pipe fitting products. The UK operating unit of Saint-Gobain 's global pipelines division is a leading supplier of ductile iron pipes, fittings, valves, access covers, and gratings in the UK. It is also the UK's top-ranked producer of cast iron above- and below-ground drainage systems. The company's markets include utilities, water and sewerage, telecommunications, highways, civil engineering, construction, and housing. Saint-Gobain PAM operates in the UK and Ireland through three locations in Ilkeston, Melton Mowbray, and Telford.

Saint-Gobain PAM UK supplies ductile iron and cast iron products to UK utilities, telecoms, highways, civil engineering, construction and housing companies. It has two foundries and a distribution center.

The company operates more than 1000 sites in the UK & Ireland.
