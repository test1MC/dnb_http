Blackhawk Industries Products Group ascribes to a "clothes make the warrior" tradition. The company makes a slew of tactical gear and apparel for people in the military, law enforcement, and other mission-critical operations, and shooting sports. Products include belts and boots, helmets and holsters, knives and weapon accessories, EMS entry tools, and rappelling equipment. Items are sold by catalog or online to the government, dealers, and consumers. After acquiring US-based Knoxx Industries in 2007, Blackhawk added recoil-suppression stocks for rifles and shotguns to its offerings. Blackhawk was founded in 1993 by CEO Mike Noell, a former Navy SEAL. Orbital ATK acquired the company in 2010.
