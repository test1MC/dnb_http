Banks.com is hoping lend a hand to online consumers seeking financial advice and services. An operator of financial services-focused Web properties, Banks.com provides stock quotes and tracking, financial news, tax preparation and advice, mortgage information, and financial calculators. In addition to banks.com, the company's core properties include proprietary search and shopping sites look.com and searchexplorer.com, as well as domain names like irs.com, which redirects users to Banks.com's tax preparation services site. Other assets include InterSearch AdNet, a pay-per-click advertising network that supports third-party advertisement listings displayed on its websites. It is owned by Remark Media .
