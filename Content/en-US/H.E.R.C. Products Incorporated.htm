H.E.R.C. likes to run a clean ship. The company makes chemicals for treating water scale and corrosion on ship surfaces and in pipeline systems, and provides marine tank cleaning services. H.E.R.C. products such as Pipe-Klean and Well-Klean are combined with water and flushed through water delivery systems, sprinkler systems, waste systems, and water wells. Many of H.E.R.C.'s products are biodegradable and don't require the expensive clean-up that other, more toxic chemicals do. H.E.R.C. also sells products for use in cleaning industrial systems.
