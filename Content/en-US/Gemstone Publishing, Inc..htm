Gemstone Publishing helps comic collectors find the diamonds in their collections. The company publishes a variety of comic price guides including The Overstreet Comic Book Price Guide and Comic book Marketplace . Gemstone and sister company Diamond International Galleries publish Scoop , a free, weekly information e-mail newsletter. The company also has a deal with Walt Disney to publish a revived line of comics featuring Mickey Mouse, Donald Duck, Goofy, and pals.
