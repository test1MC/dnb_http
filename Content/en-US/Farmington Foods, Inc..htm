Farmington Foods takes food from the farm, adds value, and sells the results. The company processes, markets, and distributes Value-added pork products, including pork loin, chops, and ribs. Its Lean N' Juicy product line consists of case-ready enhanced pork, such as bone-in or boneless loin chops, baby backribs, spareribs, pork shoulder, and tenderloin. Farmington also offers a variety of marinated pork products in flavors like Teriyaki, lemon pepper, and Italian.
