If you sip it, SIG Combibloc wants to hold it. The company manufactures packaging products for the beverage and food industries. Its offerings include aseptic cartons in a variety of sizes to hold everything from water and juice to soups and baby food. SIG Combibloc also sells a complete line of filling machines that can top off 6,000 to 24,000 carton packs an hour. The company operates plants in Germany, Austria, Saudia Arabia, China, and Thailand. Its service division includes project design and engineering, machine parts and servicing, and training. SIG Combibloc traces its roots to 1853. Today it is part of New Zealand's Reynolds Group Holdings , but is being acquired by Onex .
