Read all about it: Aylesford Newsprint makes recycled newsprint for European newspaper publishers. The company recycles about 400,000 tons of newspapers and magazines annually. About 1% of the world's newsprint is supplied by the company. Aylesford Newsprint takes recycling one step further by using waste materials to generate electricity and heat at a plant on its mill land that is owned by Npower Cogen, but operated by Aylesford employees. The energy is then used at the paper plant. It also recycles about 90% of the water used in production.
