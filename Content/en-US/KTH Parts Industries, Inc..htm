KTH Parts Industries has a desire to put its stamp of approval on the automobile industry because, well, it manufactures stamped and welded automobile parts. The company utilizes an automated welding process and specializes in frame components and fuel tanks, which it supplies primarily to Honda . KTH Parts operates facilities in Alabama and Ohio and in Ontario, Canada; all are located near Honda's North American assembly plants. Through its affiliation with Japan-based auto parts maker H-One, the company also has about 10 offices in Japan and additional offices in China, Mexico, Thailand, and India. KTH Parts was founded in 1984 and began production the next year.
