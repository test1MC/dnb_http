For Gold Pure Food Products, horseradish is paydirt. The company makes and distributes sauces and condiments including horseradish sauce, as well as horseradish-added wasabi sauce, mustard (Dijon, honey mustard), tartar sauce, ketchup, hot dog relish, and salsa. Gold Pure Food Products sells its tangy items under the Gold's, Nathan's, Uncle Dave's, and Chef Allen brand names. It offers its products in glass jars and in "squeeze me" packages. The Gold Pure Food website offers recipes and sells products online. Tillie and Hyman Gold founded the company in 1932.
