Jameson Publishing, formerly Corry Publishing, produces national trade magazines focused on information technology. Its titles include Business Solutions Magazine (growth strategies for the IT channel), Integrated Solutions Magazine (capturing and accessing data at all points of work), and Integrated Solutions for Retailers (for retail executives). Jameson Publishing also partners with other companies to promote and produce conferences and trade shows. The company was founded in 1980 by James Peterson; it is currently owned by his sons, Rick and Terry Peterson.
