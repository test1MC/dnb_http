For some, throwing money down the drain is wasteful. For Anaheim Manufacturing, it earns money from the drains themselves. The company is a leading manufacturer of food disposal products. The company also makes and markets Quick & Hot hot water dispensers and designer faucets, as well as decorator sink flanges. Anaheim Manufacturing sells its products to residential and commercial consumers, alike, under such brand names as Sinkmaster, Cool Water, Clear Water, Legend, Waste King, and Whirlaway. Anaheim Manufacturing was acquired in 1994 and operates as a subsidiary of Western Industries.
