What does Dedoes Industries do best? It makes paint-mixing equipment, stirring lids, paint shakers, cabinets, and related accessories for manufacturers of automotive and industrial finishes worldwide. The company's sole manufacturing and engineering facility drives a slate of production processes, including die-making and die-casting, metal forming, plastic injection molding, and powder coating. Dedoes also offers private labeling, and a range of custom services. The company was founded by Arnold Dedoes in 1937 as an industrial manufacturer of dies, tools, and metal-forming processes. Dedoes Industries shifted into the mixing machine business in the 1950s. The company is led by members of the Dedoes family.
