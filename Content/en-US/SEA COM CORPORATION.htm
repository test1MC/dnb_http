SEA COM makes marine and land radios and navigational instruments, such as short, medium, and long-range two-way radios, marine loud hailers, guard receivers, and automatic antenna tuners. The company specializes in narrowband radio equipment for the land mobile radio market and marine mobile radios for commercial and fishing vessels and oceangoing yachts. It also makes Inmarsat-C satellite communications systems that support two-way radio and written data transmission, as well as distress calling from marine locations. SEA COM makes sales primarily through a network of regional marine equipment dealers located in the US, Canada, UK, Greece, Australia, and other countries.
