La Flor Spices hopes its flowery array of offerings will please you with its aromas and flavors. The company packages and sells dried spices and herbs, seasoning blends, marinades, and liquid extracts. It also offers dried beans, peas, and lentils, as well as a number of herbal teas. The company's customers include retail and industrial food companies, as well as food distributors and foodservice operators. Its products can also be ordered from the company's Web site. La Flor products include the fixings used in traditional Mexican, Jamaican, Dominican, and other Hispanic cuisines.
