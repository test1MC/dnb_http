LEONI keeps the world current and connected. The global manufacturer provides cables and cable systems, wires and wiring systems, and optical fibers for the automotive, healthcare, electric appliance, and communication and infrastructure markets. Its slate, which runs from single-core cables to wiring systems with integrated electronics, is used by auto OEMs the likes of Fiat , Chrysler , BMW , General Motors , Johnson Controls , and Renault . It also offers standard cables; special, hybrid, and optical cables; and assembled systems for industrial customers in solar, traffic, and datacom projects. Comprising about 88 subsidiaries, LEONI generates more than a third of sales in Europe; Germany brings in another third.
