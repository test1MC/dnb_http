Snowbirds take to the road in search of warm weather, not isolation. MotoSAT makes and sells mobile satellite communications equipment to keep them and other ramblers connected as they travel. The company designs satellite systems for use primarily on recreational vehicles. MotoSAT's DataStorm products enable road trippers to get their favorite channels and connect to the Internet using services from such providers as DIRECTV and UNASAT.  The company's equipment is also compatible with systems made by VT iDirect . It makes sales across the US through satellite equipment and RV dealers. MotoSAT was founded in 1984 as Mobile Technologies Inc.
