Gold Group International (GGI) wants its customers to share in the golden opportunities created by its founding brothers. Its Ann Summers division sells sex toys and lingerie in its more than 130 stores in the UK, Spain, and Ireland. Its 8,000 home party organizers arrange about 200,000 gatherings per year that are much more titillating than Tupperware parties. The company also sells Ann Summers products online, and owns the Knickerbox brand of lingerie, which sells its wares online and at in-store concessions within Ann Summers. Chairman David Gold, who co-founded the company with his brother Ralph, owns Gold Group International. Ann Summers is run by David Gold's daughter, Jacqueline Gold.
