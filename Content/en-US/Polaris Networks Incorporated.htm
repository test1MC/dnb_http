Polaris Networks prides itself on being the next generation of switching systems for wired phone service providers. The company makes optical network transport switches (OMX) and accompanying network management software (IntelliOp) used in metro core networks. OMX is intended to cut down on equipment costs by combining the functions of SONET-based multiplexers and digital cross-connect device into a single product. Polaris was founded in 2000.
