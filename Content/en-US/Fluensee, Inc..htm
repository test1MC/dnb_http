Fluensee aims to influence the technology behind inventory control. The company develops end-to-end radio-frequency identification (RFID) chips, software (termed AssetTrack), and support and training services for asset management and tracking. RFID technology can be thought of as futuristic bar codes that track in real-time products of all shapes and sizes. The technology is particularly useful for IT equipment and reusable/returnable assets. Fluensee was founded in 2005 as Applied Logistics. In 2008 the company bought rival RFID manufacturer TrenStar .
