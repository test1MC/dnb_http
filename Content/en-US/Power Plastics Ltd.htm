Whether you're designing a koi pond, fumigating a building, or just doing some tumbling, Power Plastics has a product for you. Like its name suggests, the company makes industrial-strength plastic items such as pond and tank liners, fumigation sheeting, and protective padding such as gym mats. Power Plastics makes other products including construction sheeting, thermal liners, pool covers, secure environment chambers, and germination enclosures. The company is also a major supplier of foam-filled landing areas (think pole vaulting) for UK athletic stadiums. It has conducted work for such notable companies as British American Tobacco , Enpure , and Veolia Water .
