Körber makes the machines that make a lot of the products you buy. Körber's 30 companies operate in a number of European and Asian countries and the US. Its Hauni division, generating over 50% of total sales, designs machines and systems that process tobacco and produce cigarettes. Körber PaperLink includes several subsidiaries responsible for machines used by paper companies to make paper, cardboard, and absorbent hygiene products. Schleifring Group creates custom machining processes and systems for customers in the aircraft, automotive, mechanical engineering, and tool & die industries. Körber Medipak creates machines for pharmaceutical packaging. Körber Foundation owns the company. Most sales are in Europe.
