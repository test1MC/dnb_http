A giant in an industry where many yarns are spun, Titan Textile, doing business as Dillon Yarn Corp., manufactures textiles, up to 90 million pounds of polyester and nylon yarn a year! The privately held company stands as the #2 producer of textured yarn in North America. Its yarns run from textured and flat polyester and nylon to spun, flat, and industrial yarn, as well as a variety of cotton and rayon blends. Titan Textile also imports and distributes cotton and polyester/cotton spun yarns to apparel markets. Its yarn and fabrics are sold worldwide to manufacturers of industrial and consumer goods, including automotive fabric, lining, ribbon, rope-and-cordage, shoelace, tarp, and wall covering threads.
