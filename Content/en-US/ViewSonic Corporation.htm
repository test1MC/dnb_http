ViewSonic likes the sound of "having all eyes on its products." The company makes LCD and LED computer displays, including models for high-end computer-aided design and desktop publishing, and for office, home, gaming, Internet browsing, and multimedia applications. ViewSonic also offers LCD and LED TVs, projectors, all-in-one and mobile PCs, digital signage and software, and digital photo frames. The company produces a Microsoft Windows-based ViewPad tablet PC to compete with the Apple iPad; other versions use Google 's Android operating system. Chairman and CEO James Chu founded the company in 1987.

ViewSonic has offices around the globe, including in Australia, China, France, Finland, Germany, Hong Kong, India, Russia, Singapore, South Korea, Taiwan, the United Arab Emirates, the UK, and the US.

The company focuses on producing display-centric products such a LED monitors, tablets, projectors, digital signage displays, smartphones, and cloud computing solutions. Some of its products include ViewPad tablets, pocket camcorders, nd home theater projectors, desktop monitors, digital photo frames, digital signage, and LCD TVs.

ViewSonic markets its products globally through direct sales and via resellers, outlets, and distributors to consumer, corporate, government, and education customers.

A leading provider of displays, ViewSonic has managed to hold its own in an industry where Asian giants such as NEC , Samsung , and Sony vie for market share. The company is trying to raise the visibility of its brand by rolling out new products that address a more diverse selection of consumer electronics niches. In the digital devices arena, the company offers 2-D and 3-D pocket camcorders, pocket camcorder projectors, eBook readers (some with Wi-Fi), and high-definition digital media players.

ViewSonic made two moves designed to increase its market in India. The company has partnered with Savera, an IT products distributor in India, for Savera to distribute the ViewSonic monitors. The goal is to leverage Savera's reach in India. In a smiliar deal, ViewSonic teamed with Creative Peripherals India, also a distributor, for ViewSonic's projector business.

In 2016 ViewSonic released several higher resolution displays with a special aim to garnering games as customers.
