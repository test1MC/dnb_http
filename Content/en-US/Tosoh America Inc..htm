Tosoh America is a holding company for several chemical and specialty materials subsidiaries of Japanese parent company Tosoh Corporation , providing corporate services for Tosoh's American businesses. Those operations include the production and manufacture of quartz materials (for semiconductor and electronics companies) and silica glass products, as well as specialty chemical products and the sales and marketing of Tosoh Bioscience's diagnostic systems. Tososh America's Tosoh USA division handles sales, marketing, and business development for the company's US operations.

The company maintains operations throughout the US. Headquartered in Grove City, Ohio, it has offices in the Bay Area and the Philadelphia region. It operates two manufacturing plants in Oregon and Ohio; and sales offices in California, Georgia, New York, Ohio, and Pennsylvania.

Under the Tosoh America umbrella, Tosoh companies in the US are engaged in advanced technology fields ranging from semiconductor, solar, medical diagnostic instruments, high performance liquid chromatograph, and bio-separation media, to fine chemicals.

The company operates through three business divisions; Bioscience, Electronic Materials, and Chemicals. Its Bioscience division is covered by Tosoh Bioscience, Inc. and Tosoh Bioscience, LLC; Electronic Materials, by Tosoh Quartz and Tosoh SMD; and Chemicals by Tosoh USA and Tosoh Specialty Chemicals USA.

Tosoh SMD is an international supplier of sputtering targets and high purity thin film deposition materials for use within flat panel, and solar applications. Tosoh Specialty Chemicals USA markets and sells a variety of chemical products, including ethyleneamines, polyurethane catalysts, and methylene diphenyl diisocyanate.

The company provides manufacturing, distribution and sales, regulatory and technical support as well as equipment servicing for customers in the chemical, medical, pharmaceutical, semiconductor, and solar industries.

In addition to integrating the corporate activities of its subsidiaries, Tosoh America seeks to expand Tosoh's business lines and operational capabilities in North America. In Europe and North America the parent company is concentrating on increasing its brand recognition while boosting its market share and, based on market trends, plans to adjust its product mix in favor of high molecular weight amines.

For greater efficiency, in 2012 Tosoh America merged its Tosoh SGM USA unit (silica glass materials) into Tosoh USA.

Tosoh America was founded in 1989.
