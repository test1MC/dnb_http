Can R2D2 be far behind? Transbotics Corporation makes control hardware and software for automatic guided vehicles (AGVs) and carts (AGCs). AGVs and AGCs are unmanned, computer-controlled vehicles that transport materials around factories, and can be used to automate conventional material-handling equipment such as forklifts and pallet jacks. The company is promoting its Lazerway laser-based guidance system over traditional wire and nonwire technologies. Transbotics also provides engineering and maintenance services, and automatic truck loading and conveyor systems. Its AGV systems use technology licensed by the Industrial Technologies' Motion segment of Danaher .
