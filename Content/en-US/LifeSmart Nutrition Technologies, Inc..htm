LifeSmart Nutrition Technologies, through its wholly owned subsidiary LifeSmart Nutrition, manufactures chewable nutritional supplements that are similar to caramel candies. Its products include calcium, creatine, and Vitamin C supplements, as well as a chewable weight loss product. LifeSmart sells its products primarily to retailers to be sold to consumers under its private label brand. President Mike Van Dyke owns about 20% of the company stock, and CFO Raymond Woolston owns some 30%.
