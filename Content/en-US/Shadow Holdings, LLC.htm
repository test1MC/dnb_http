Shadow Holdings is the man behind the curtain, so to speak. The company, operating as Bocchi Laboratories, is a contract manufacturer of personal care and over-the-counter preparations. It specializes in such products as skin care items, cosmetics, hair products, soaps, and fragrances. Bocchi Laboratories is a supplier to many well-known retailers. The company was originally founded in 1988 to provide the salon industry with personal care product lines. Bocchi Laboratories was acquired in mid-2008 by Shadow Holdings, an investment holding company, from Clearlight Cream, Inc. Its sister firm, Medicia Corp., which operates in New Jersey, was purchased by Joshua Partners in December 2008.
