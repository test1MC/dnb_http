Mind your own net. Northstar Electronics makes the NETMIND underwater wireless acoustic sensor. Customers use it to monitor gigantic fishing nets while being towed. The NETMIND system has fully serviceable sensors that are contained in stainless steel cylinders. Other Northstar Electronics products include sonar detection systems and submarine control consoles. Customers have included the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA). In addition, Northstar's subsidiaries Northstar Network and Northstar Technical make electronic systems for the defense and aerospace industry. Lockheed Martin is a major customer. The company agreed to be acquired by Empower Technologies Corporation in 2011 for CDN $1 million.
