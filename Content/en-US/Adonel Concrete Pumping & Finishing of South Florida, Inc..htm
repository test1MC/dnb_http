Adonel Concrete has its footprint firmly planted in South Florida. With six concrete plants, the privately-owned company is a materials supplier to residential and commercial construction projects from Key Largo to Palm Beach. It makes concrete and special mixes that repel water, inhibit corrosion, and self-compact. Clients have included homebuilders Caribe Homes and Lennar , Perini Marine Construction, and Trans Florida Development. President Luis Garcia, an immigrant from Nicaragua, started the company in 1984 with a single employee and a single truck.
