Instead of hammer and anvil, Forge Europa shapes environments with cool luminesence of light-emitting diodes (LEDs). The company makes high-performance LED components and assemblies, including red, green, blue, and white LED lamps, backlights, and printed circuit board assemblies. Forge Europa offers various types of displays, including seven-segment models (the kind that display numerals on digital alarm clocks) and dot matrix displays, as well as LED drivers and power supplies. The company, which was founded in 1993 by managing directing Peter Barton, is the designated Cree Solution Provider to northern Europe. Forge Europa products are marketed in Finland, Russia, Sweden, and the UK through distributors.

Forge Eurpoa designs and makes several kinds of LEDs. Its LED Luminaires products are used in commercial, healthcare, and municipal indoor lighting. The lights include down lights, ceiling lights, and recessed troffers (the rectangular space for lighting in a dropped ceiling). More Forge Europa products are its LED light engine range for inclusion in other manufacturers' fixtures. They come in 280mm and 560mm x 24mm linear strips, a 270 x 270mm square tile, and a 200mm diameter round PCB.

Forge Europa sells through its own sales staff as well as through distributors.

Forge Europa works with OEMs to design its lighting into upcoming products and it works with LED manufacturers to offer the latest technologies. In 2016, the company introduced a line of flexible LED lighting products.
