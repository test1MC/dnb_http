Webster Engineering & Manufacturing Co. turns up the heat. The company manufactures multi-fuel (gas and oil) boiler-burners mainly for use in boiler assemblies for commercial heating and industrial use. Its products include air heaters, dryers, incinerators, and steam and hot water boilers. Webster also offers such specialty and custom products as control panels and oil pump sets. The company's latest products can run off of alternative fuels such as waste gas, biodiesel, animal fats, and methane gas. The company was founded in 1993.
