If you drink beer until you're a blithering idiot, you are likely to miss the finer points of Weyerbacher Brewing Company's beers. The Pennsylvania microbrewer makes Belgian-style craft beers and ales. Also offered is Blithering Idiot barley ale, meant to be carefully sipped and savored before a warm hearth. For those who lack the firewood, Blithering Idiot is still a good choice. At 11% alcohol, it will warm you up. The company's other colorfully named brands include Blasphemy, Decadence, Heresy, Insanity, and Old Heathen. Weyerbacher beers, both bottled and draft, are sold through retailers and taverns in about 15 US states, mainly along the East Coast.
