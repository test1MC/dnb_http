Globeride (formerly Daiwa Seiko) has everything a sportsman needs for a day on the lake, links, or tennis court. Through its subsidiaries, the company makes, sells, and repairs fishing gear, including fishing line, lures, reels, rods, tackle boxes, coolers, and more. Globeride's fishing products, sold under the Daiwa brand, account for the majority of its sales. The company also offers licensed Prince brand tennis gear and GIII brand golf gear, as well as Bottecchia bicycles and other outdoor products. Globeride, founded in 1958, operates from offices throughout Japan and internationally from subsidiaries in Australia, China, France, Germany, Taiwan, Thailand, the UK, and the US ( Daiwa Corporation ).
