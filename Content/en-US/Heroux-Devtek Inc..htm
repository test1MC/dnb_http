Héroux-Devtek is concerned with perfect touchdowns every time. The company designs, manufactures, repairs, and sells landing gear systems to commercial and military customers. The company also makes electronic enclosures, heat exchangers, and cabinets for airborne radar, electro-optic systems, and aircraft controls. Customers have included Boeing , Bombardier , France's Dassault Aviation , as well as the US Air Force and US Navy . Formed in 2000 by the merger of Héroux and Devtek, the company maintains facilities in Canada and the US; the latter is where it makes the majority of its sales.
