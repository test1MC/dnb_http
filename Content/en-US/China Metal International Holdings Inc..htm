China Metal International Holdings (CMI) is the largest metal casting foundry in China. Its customized products include compressor parts for air conditioners and refrigerators; automobile components; and other mechanical parts. CMI also provides molding, machining, and coating services. The company serves Japanese and Korean air compressor and refrigerator compressor manufacturers; automobile parts and component manufacturers; automobile manufacturers; and other industrial manufacturers for water pumps and agricultural equipment. CMI operates two foundries in China -- Tianjin CMT Industry and Suzhou CMS Machinery -- with machining facilities, six molding lines, and one coating line. CMI was founded in 1992.
