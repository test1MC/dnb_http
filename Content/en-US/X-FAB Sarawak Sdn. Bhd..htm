X-FAB Sarawak (formerly 1st Silicon) marks the spot for semiconductor contract manufacturing in Malaysia. The company, which was founded in 1998, offers semiconductor foundry (contract manufacturing) services, including design, mask making, fabrication, sorting, packaging, and testing. The company's wafer fabrication facility (or fab) makes semiconductors on silicon wafers measuring 200mm (about 8 inches) across, and can produce more than 27,000 wafers per month. X-FAB Sarawak serves the automotive, industrial, consumer, and medical markets; customers included Atmel and Sharp . Multinational chip maker X-FAB Silicon Foundries , based in Germany, acquired 1st Silicon in September 2006 and renamed it.
