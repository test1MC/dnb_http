Whole Harvest Foods helps restaurants reduce the fat in their fried food offerings. The company purchases soybeans and canola seed crops from North Carolina farmers and processes them into commercial and consumer cooking oils. It makes Whole Harvest soy and canola commercial cooking oils; they are trans fat-free alternatives to hydrogenated and partially hydrogenated oils. The company's other products include consumer-oriented Whole Harvest Culinary Oils (for salads and baking) and Whole Harvest SmartCoat (pan spray). It distributes its products in the US. The company is owned by food and feed ingredient company Bunge North America .
