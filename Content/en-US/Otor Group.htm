Not just another pretty package, Otor Group is one of the largest box makers in France. The company primarily makes containerboard, corrugated boxes, and other paper products. It also has a division that collects and recycles waste paper for use in its product manufacturing. Otor makes and sells box-making equipment as well. The company operates paper mills mostly in France, but has sales and manufacturing operations in Germany, Poland, Switzerland, and the UK. In late 2010 DS Smith , the top paper producer in the UK, acquired 95% of Otor shares owned by US-based private equity firm The Carlyle Group , with the intent to squeeze-out the remaining 5% owned by Otor itself.
