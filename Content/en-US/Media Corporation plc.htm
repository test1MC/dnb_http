While the company's name is generic, Media Corporation hopes its advertising and online publishing services are anything but. The company's Eyeconomy|NASH division provides online ad placement for advertisers, advertising agencies, and Web publishers. Its Publishing holdings include websites such as OnTheBox.com (TV listings and entertainment guide), Flightcomparison.co.uk (international air fares), and Sport.co.uk (sports portal). Media Corporation was founded in 2000 by Justin Drummond. It became listed on the London Stock Exchange a year later via the reverse takeover of investment firm Chrome Technology.
