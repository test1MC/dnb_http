Wilkins Rogers Mills is a flour and corn meal milling company with three mill locations in Maryland and Pennsylvania. In addition to milling and selling flour and cornmeal, the company also offers packaged baking mixes, breadings, and coatings. It makes pretzel, cake, pastry, cookie, whole wheat, all purpose and self-rising flours, and yellow and white corn meals. Its brands, available in the Eastern US, include White Lilly and Washington flours and Indian Head, Cross, Beattie's, and Old Time corn meals. The company's customers include large commercial bakeries, grocery stores, private-label clients, food processors, restaurants, and foodservice distributors. Family-owned Wilkins Rogers was founded in 1913.
