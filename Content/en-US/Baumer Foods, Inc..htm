Baumer Foods is unsure whether it wants you to have a crystal clear or slightly smoky view of its products. The company produces a variety of sauces and condiments, including hot, steak, wing, teriyaki, soy, and Worcestershire sauces, spicy mustards, and jams and jellies, all under the Crystal brand name. It also sells Figaro brand Hickory Liquid Smoke and Mesquite Liquid Smoke. Manufacturing 3 million gallons of its Crystal hot sauce yearly, Baumer Foods distributes its foods to some 75 countries worldwide. The family-owned and -operated company was founded in 1923 by Alvin and Mildred Baumer.
