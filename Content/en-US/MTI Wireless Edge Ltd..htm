MTI Wireless Edge helps to make the armed forces strong by keeping them connected. The company makes flat panel antennas used in defense, as well as commercial wireless communications applications. The company's defense products include antennas for aircraft, ships, submarines, and terrestrial use. It offers fixed wireless antennas and RFID (radio frequency identification) products for commercial use. MTI Wireless Edge also maintains three outdoor antenna test ranges that are 8 to 300 meters in length. The company operates from locations worldwide and has sold its products in more than 100 countries.
