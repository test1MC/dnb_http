Alfred Franks and Bartlett (AFB) is dedicated to protecting the eyes of its customers and providing accessories that bring their look together. The company designs, makes and distributes its own The Sunglass Company brand and children's licensed sunglasses in the UK through major retailers. It specializes in kids' shades that carry well-known licensed brands, such as those of Hello Kitty, The Simpsons, and Disney 's portfolio of characters. AFB is also a leading supplier of reading glasses. Its other lines of business include designing and sourcing home products (kitchen, bathroom, garden, and storage) and health and beauty items. Founded in 1918, AFB has been importing products from Asia for nearly 100 years.

The company designs, manufactures, and distributes home, health, and beauty and fashion products.

AFB sells and distributes products in Europe (including Scandinavia), Asia, and the Middle East.

The company sells its products through all categories of the retail sector, including department stores, apparel shops, home stores, discounters, and major supermarket and drugstore chains.

In recent years AFB has expanded its reach into sport-branded sunglasses for adults.
