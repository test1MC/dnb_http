Nicolás Correa manufactures milling machines, including CNC bed types, double column bridge types, fixed bed/traveling columns, floor types, and gantries. It also makes high-speed milling machines, including a vertical center as well as milling-boring machines. The company makes machine parts, manufacturing dies and large moulds, for clients in the aerospace, railway, and energy sectors. The company exports 90% of its production to Germany, China, France, Canada, the UK, and the US. Nicolás Correa was founded in 1947 by Baldomero Nicolás-Correa.

The company manufactures milling machines in two of their productive plants in Spain, Burgos, and Itziar. Nicolás Correa also has two technical support centers in Kunming and Shanghai in China.

Nicolás Correa sells its products through distributors and subsidiaries such as Tiger Machinery & Parts Co., Joymatech, Design and Technical Services, and GNC Mepro.
