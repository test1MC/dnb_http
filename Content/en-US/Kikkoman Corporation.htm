Ubiquitous and indispensable, Kikkoman is in every Japanese pantry and almost every US one, as well. The company is the world's #1 producer of soy sauce. Offerings include varieties of soy and other cooking sauces, seasonings, and soy milk, foodstuffs, sake and wines, and some biochemical products. In the Asia/Pacific region, it sells ketchup, canned corn and fruits, and other Western foods under the licensed Del Monte brand. Business is divided into four segments: manufacture and sale of domestic foods (the largest), and others; and manufacture and sale of overseas foods, and wholesale. Kikkoman's roots run deep -- more than 350 years old, the company is controlled by the descendants of its founding families.
