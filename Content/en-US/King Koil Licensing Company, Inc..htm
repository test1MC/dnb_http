With 100-plus years under its belt and former Charlie's Angel Jaclyn Smith at its side, King Koil Licensing Company is ready to rumble. The company, which celebrated its centennial anniversary in 1998, is a leading mattress maker in the US. King Koil has manufacturing facilities in 70 countries, including Argentina, Belize, Hong Kong, Mozambique, Saudi Arabia, Scotland, and the US. Actress Jaclyn Smith, also well known for her Kmart television endorsements, partnered with King Koil in 2003 to develop mattresses for her home bedding collection.
