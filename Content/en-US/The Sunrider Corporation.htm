Sunrider Corporation has blended bits of traditional Chinese herbal medicine with modern multilevel marketing. The company manufactures and sells more than 415 health, beauty, food, and household products enhanced with herbs and herbal extracts. Its brands include Kandesn, Vitalite, SunBright, SunLiving, and SunSmile. Sunrider has manufacturing operations in China, India, Singapore, Taiwan, and the US. Its products are sold in more than 40 countries through multilevel marketing via distributors and directly to customers. However it is expanding its network of franchised stores in several Asian countries. Owners (and spouses) Drs. Tei-Fu Chen and Oi-Lin Chen started Sunrider International in 1982.

Sunrider operates globally through about two dozen offices, selling its products in 40-plus countries.

The multilevel marketer operates an Authorized Business Owner (ABO) business system in China, Korea, Malaysia, Singapore, and Taiwan. Through an Independent Business Owner (IBO) business system, Sunrider operates in the US, Canada, Israel, Western Europe, Hungary, Eastern Europe, Russia, and East Asia.

Sunrider boasts 7,000 authorized stores. ABOs own and operate retail stores under the Sunrider banner name and sell Sunrider-branded products. The company's IBOs own a franchise-like business the sells Sunrider-brand items.

Sunrider sells its products through distributors and directly to consumers. Its IBOs market and sell products to customers through their websites, product fairs and expos, home-shopping parties, and one-on-one interactions.

While based in the US, the company is focused on geographic expansion.

It has worked to get its products in Asia. To avoid regulations on direct-selling in China, Sunrider first entered that country by establishing a franchise system with thousands of stores. Following the success of its stores in China, the company has set out to repeat it with its Authorized Business Owner (ABO) franchise division in other Asian countries. It has focused on Australia, Japan, Malaysia, Singapore, Korea, Israel, Turkey, and Taiwan in recent years.

In 2013 the company opened a wellness center in Torrance, California. SunLiving Health & Wellness offers holistic treatments for pain, overall wellness, and injury-recovery. Additional treatments also include weight-loss programs, pain management, acupuncture, prolotherapy, and Sunrider herbal supplements.

To further penetrate China, Sunrider in 2013 partnered with nearly 10 Shinsegae Department Stores to sells its products there. Shinsegae is a department store franchise that's based in Seoul, South Korea.

The company entered the consumer foods sector in 2009 when it opened the Kunshan Manufacturing Facility at a cost of $80 million in China. Built to house Sunrider Food, the plant produces instant noodles and a variety of canned foods.

Sunrider has diversified further by acquiring and operating several hotels in China. In Beijing, the company operates the Sunworld Dynasty Hotel and the Sunworld Hotel. It also owns the Sunworld Dynasty Hotel in Taipei. Both of its Beijing hotels are "Olympic" hotels, which means they are pre-booked by government officials.
