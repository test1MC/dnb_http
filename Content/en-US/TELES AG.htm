TELES manufactures telecommunications switching systems for voice and data services. The company is divided into two segments -- TELES Communication Systems (TCS) and TELES Wireless Broadband Internet (skyDSL.) TCS provides next generation networks (NGN) and VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol) for more than 200 businesses in Germany, Austria, Italy, India, Israel, and Bolivia. skyDSL has more than 100,000 customers across Europe. TELES announced in 2006 it's introducing an Intellectual Property Rights (TIPR) Division and a Customer Premises Equipment (TCPE) Division.
