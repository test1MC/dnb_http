Rover's "good boy" treats start at American Dehydrated Foods (ADF). The company manufactures ingredients for the specialty pet-food industry. ADF's meat, protein, and fat products, which include spray-dried, dry-blended, pasteurized, and homogenized items, are used by pet-food and exotic and custom animal-feed makers. They are distributed worldwide. ADF also conducts research and development that leads to custom products for its customers. The William Darr family owns ADF (founded in 1978) and its sister companies, International Dehydrated Foods and Food Ingredients Technology Company.
