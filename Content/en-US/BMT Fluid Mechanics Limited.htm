BMT Fluid Mechanics can help you go with the flow. The company (a subsidiary of engineering and technical services provider BMT ) specializes in wind engineering and offshore marine consulting services, addressing fields such as fluid dynamics and naval architecture, as well as physical and numerical modeling. Customers come from such industries as civil engineering, oil and gas, rail, shipping, automotive, and defense and have included Carnival Cruise Lines , the UK Ministry of Defense, ExxonMobil , Fugro , Kellogg Brown & Root , and London Underground . The company has offices in Hong Kong, the United Arab Emirates, Malaysia, the US, and the UK.
