In Japan, when you say tomato, you mean Kagome. It's one of Japan's leading ketchup makers and dominates the market for canned tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato puree, and tomato sauces, including salsa. The firm also bottles teas and fruit and vegetable juices (including tomato juice). Kagome cultivates its own variety of tomatoes for production but also sells them at supermarkets such as AEON . It has expanded its core product line to include seasonings, prepared meals, soups, and probiotic dairy drinks. Kagome boasts operations in China, Italy, Taiwan, Turkey, and the US, as well as units involved in distribution and real estate. It expanded into Australia in late 2010 by buying a tomato farm and processor.
