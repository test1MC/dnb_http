Ricoh Canada, an operating arm of Japan's Ricoh , supplies copy and facsimile machines, printers, scanners, and related parts and supplies. Its line of imaging products include monochrome and color printers and multifunction devices (print, scan, copy, fax) for general office applications; it also provides wide-format printers and copiers used by architects, engineers, and others that work with large documents. Ricoh's other products include document management and security software, and the company provides network consulting and support services. Ricoh has some 2,200 employees in Canada.

The main office of Ricoh Canada is in Toronto, Ontario. The subsidiary also offices and dealers located throughout Canada. It has technical service centers in Ottawa, Montreal, Quebec City, Toronto, London, Calgary, Vancouver, and Burlington/St. Catharines.

In 2015, Ricoh Canada Inc. reached a reseller agreement with ACCO Brands Canada to offers a range of offline/near-line finishing products. This adds to Ricoh Canada's Pro Series System and Workflow solutions with ACCO's range of off-line/near-line products that includes binding equipment, booklet makers and trimmers, die cutters, folders, and slitter-cutter-creasers.
