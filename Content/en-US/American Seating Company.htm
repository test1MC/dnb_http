American Seating wants you to be comfortable and take at least one of its seats. A leading manufacturer of seating for many industries, the company has furnished offices, public transit vehicles, schools, auditoriums, and stadiums and entertainment venues such as Busch Stadium in St. Louis and the Tabor Opera House in Colorado. The company sells its products in the US and Canada. American Seating was the first company to manufacture the combination student desk and chair unit when it was founded under the name Grand Rapids School Furniture in 1886.
