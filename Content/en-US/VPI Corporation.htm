VPI manufactures a full line of plastic flooring products including rubber flooring, green flooring (made from 40% reprocessed material), static control tile, transition molding, and wall base. It also makes accessories including rubber stair treads, risers, stringers, and installation accessories. The company markets its products to companies in the construction, electronics, education, healthcare, and sports industries. VPI was founded as Natural Products, Inc. in 1946 to manufacture and sell a newly developed form of plastic: polyvinyl chloride (PVC). The company was reorganized as Vinyl Plastics, Inc. in 1951.

ESD (static electricity discharge) static control tile was invented by VPI in 1946. The flooring, which is sold under brand names Conductile and Statmate, contains no lead or any other toxic heavy metals, and is resistant to acids, alkalis, and caustic chemicals. It is used in applications including flight control, ammunition, explosives, and manufacturing.

The company sells its products to its customers through a network of distributors worldwide. It targets the health care, electronic, education, and general purpose markets.
