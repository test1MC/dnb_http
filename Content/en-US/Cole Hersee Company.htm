Cole Hersee has a device for whatever flips your switch. The company manufactures electromechanical, electronic, and digital switches and connectors, primarily for heavy duty trucks, industrial equipment, and pleasure boat applications. Its products include battery-related equipment, buzzers and alarms, circuit protection devices, relays, senders, solenoids, switches, monitoring devices, and wiring harnesses. Cole Hersee sells products directly to product manufacturers and through a network of distributors. Founded in 1924, the company is a subsidiary of circuit protection device maker Littelfuse .

The company is stationed in Boston and has manufacturing operations in Mexico and the US.

Cole Hersee sells its switching products through sales representatives, distributors, and dealers throughout North America. It serves major auto makers such as BMW , Caterpillar , Chrysler , Daimler Trucks , Ford , GM , Hyundai Group , and Volkswagen .
