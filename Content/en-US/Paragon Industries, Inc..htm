A paragon of virtue when it comes to steel products, Paragon Industries manufactures steel pipe and tubing used by the oil industry and as structural steel for the building industry. The company also provides cutting and beveling, pipe bundling, precision threading, and trucking services. Paragon has its headquarters in Oklahoma. Its coil processing facility is located at the Port of Muskogee in Muskogee, Oklahoma. The manufacturer serves a range of customers in the oil industry in the US, Canada, and South America. The company was founded in 1970 by its president, Jack Wachob.
