Those who say media companies are soulless machines have not heard of Integrity. The company produces and distributes Christian-oriented music, books, videos, and software. Integrity Media's music offerings range from praise and worship music to adult contemporary and children's titles, and include top selling compilations Shout To The Lord and Holy Ground. The company also owns Christian music label INO Records, and conferences and events division Integrity Live. Integrity Media distributes music and videos through retail stores in the US and more than 160 other countries, as well as through Integrity Direct, which operates direct-to-consumer channels (mail-order catalogs, music clubs, and the Web).
