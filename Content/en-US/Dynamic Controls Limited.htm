Dynamic Controls Limited is all about going with the flow -- andÂ creating products that regulate, direct, or control it. The company is a manufacturer of valves for high- and low-pressure industrial applications, and it makes cartridge valves, regulator control valves, and bellow sealed globe valves. The company's products are used primarily by the military, nuclear, petrochemical, and bulk gas-handling industries; its valves are used in everything from submarines and power plants to smoke-making machines and rocket loading systems. It produces its valves at its plant outside of Manchester in the UK, where it has been making valves for more than 40 years.
