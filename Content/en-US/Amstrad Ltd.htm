Amstrad has got great connections. Once upon a time, Amstrad made inexpensive home computers, but now the company makes digital satellite receivers and PVRs (personal video recorders). Amstrad's e-m@iler product is a telephone with LCD screen and keyboard that enables anyone with phone service to send and receive email. Amstrad's nearly 90%-owned Amserve joint venture (with UK retailer DSG International ) connects more than a million subscribers to email and advertisements using the e-m@iler. The company also supplies satellite receivers to Sky plc and Italian broadcaster, Sky Italia. Sky has owned the company since 2007.
