Chocolate and snacks are at the top of Zetar's shopping list. A leading independent confectioner in the UK, the company produces boxed chocolates under its Lir brand and under the licensed Baileys and Famous Grouse labels. It also makes character-licensed chocolates for media firms such as Pixar and Sanrio . Zetar's natural snacks division rolls out dried fruits and nuts under the Humdinger and Fruit Factory names, as well as under license for Marmite, Reggae Reggae, and Vimto. Zetar's offerings are sold by major food retailers in the UK. Founded in 2004, Zetar has expanded its operations by acquiring local confectioners and snack suppliers. In 2011 it bought UK-based chocolatier Derwent Lynton for $1.3 million.
