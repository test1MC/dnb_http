Over hill, over dale, and all throughout Yorkshire, folks are reading Dalesman . Dalesman Publishing Company produces that magazine and sister publications Countryman (features and news about the North England countryside), Down Your Way (Yorkshire memories, history, and traditions), and Cumbria (regional publication for the Lake District). The company also publishes books and operates a website with Yorkshire-related content (classified ads, genealogy information, travel, and entertainment news). The site also features an online shop offering books, videos, DVDs, audiocassettes, calendars, and gifts of regional interest. Dalesman Publishing was established in 1939.
