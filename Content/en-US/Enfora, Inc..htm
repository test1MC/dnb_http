Enfora's customers are in for a wireless treat. The company makes integrated and modular electronics platforms sold under the N4 brand that are used to power wireless communications devices. For the asset management market, it offers gateway software such as its Enabler product that enables clients to access, monitor, and control a wide range of connected devices. Enfora also develops wireless applications used by telecommunications service providers for deploying and managing wireless networks. Its services include consulting, integration, implementation, and support. The company has international sales offices in Hong Kong, Bogotá, and the UK. Enfora is a subsidiary of Novatel Wireless
