When horn players hear a fanfare, they've gotta getta Getzen. The Getzen Company makes brass musical instruments (trumpets, cornets, fluegelhorns, and trombones) and accessories. The company distributes its products under the Capri, Eterna, and Getzen brand names through dealers in Asia, Australia, Canada, Europe, and in the US. It also sells German-made French horns and euphoniums. T. J. Getzen founded Getzen Musical Instrument (initially focused on band instrument repair) in a converted dairy barn in 1939. The company began manufacturing trombones (and later trumpets, cornets, and bugles) in the 1940s.
