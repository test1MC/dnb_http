One company is counting on user generated content to solve the puzzle of how to find business people. Jigsaw Data is an online business directory of names and contact information that is contributed and evaluated by members of the company's service. Jigsaw has more than one million members (including sales people, recruiters, and market researchers) and a database of about 25 million contacts. Members can download up to 50,000 contacts at a time. The company was founded in 2004 by sales executives Jim Fowler (CEO) and Garth Moulton (VP), and engineer Peng-Chong Sien. Jigsaw is a subsidiary of software firm salesforce.com .
