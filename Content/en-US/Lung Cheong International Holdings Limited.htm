Lung Cheong International Holdings has built a business out of toying around. The company manufactures plastic, electronic, and radio-controlled toys under its own brands, including KG Flighter and Buggies vehicles and the Bendos bendable action figures line (created by its Kid Galaxy subsidiary). It has also produced toys under license for the NBA and FIFA and is an original equipment manufacturer for such toy brands as Little Tikes and TOMY . Besides playthings, Lung Cheong produces consumer electronics, such as digital cameras, baby monitors, and Bluetooth earpieces. It also has operations in aviation leasing.
