AdvoCare International can be your ticket to good health, or your ticket to entrepreneurship. The direct sales company offers a variety of vitamin supplements and nutritional products for weight management, sport and athletic performance, skin care, and general wellness. In addition to its grown-up offerings, the firm also sells supplement products for children. AdvoCare markets its products through a force of more than 225,000 independent distributors throughout the world. Bolstering the company's image is its slate of endorsers, ranging from professional and collegiate athletes to bodybuilders and musicians.

AdvoCare has more than 225,000 independent distributors who get their products for distribution through FedEx or pick up the products on-site.

The company is in the early stages of a real estate search for a large distribution center for its products. The new distribution center plans follow AdvoCare's recent adding of a second production line in its current distribution facility in Plano. The facility packages products for fulfillment six days a week.

AdvoCare's strategy lies in helping its distributors succeed by providing professional training, education, and support programs.

It also seeks to add new, innovative products to its offerings, and by adding new celebrity spokespersons. AdvoCare became the sponsor for Shreveport, Louisiana's Independence Bowl title football game in 2009 and more recently AdvoCare signed a jersey sponsorship deal with Major League Soccer team FC Dallas.

The company was founded by athlete Charles Ragus in 1993. CEO Richard Wright took the helm of AdvoCare in 2007 after leading several other direct sales distribution organizations.
