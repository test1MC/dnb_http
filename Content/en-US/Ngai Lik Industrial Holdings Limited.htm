Yuan Heng Gas Holdings is engaged in China's energy sector, with activities in oil and gas, liquefied natural gas, and bulk commodities. Primary subsidiary Guangzhou Yuanheng Gas invests in upstream natural gas production facilities (it owns two major LNG production bases in Ordos Basin and Sichuan Basin) and develops pipeline and logistics projects. Another subsidiary, Fully World, trades products including crude oil, fuel oil, aviation fuel, gasoline, diesel, natural gas, and chemicals; it trades oil and gas all around the globe. Formerly operating as Ngai Sing Industrial Holdings, the group changed its name and business focus after acquiring LNG processor and distributor Union Honor Limited in 2014.
