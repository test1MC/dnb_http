Interpump Group likes high-pressure when it comes to its business. The company makes a range of products to serve four sectors: hydraulics (oil pressure power take-offs), industrial equipment (high and very high-pressure plunger pumps), electric motors (used in high pressure washers and compressors, and in gate openers), and cylinders (hydraulic pumps). Interpump exports its products to more than 60 countries worldwide.
