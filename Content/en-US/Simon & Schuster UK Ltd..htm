Bestsellers are the order of the day at book publisher Simon & Schuster UK. The company publishes commercial fiction, literary fiction, general non-fiction, and children's publications. It operates through three imprints: Simon & Schuster, Simon & Schuster Illustrated, and Simon & Schuster Children's Books. The company also publishes eBooks, which are available for download on computers, laptops, PDAs, and eBook readers such as Amazon's Kindle and Apple's iPad. In addition, the company distributes books for third-party independent and international publishers, including VIZ Media, Duncan Baird, Andrews McMeel Publishing, and Black Library. Part of Simon & Schuster , the UK division was formed in 1987.
