Concept Packaging Group packages a lot more than original ideas. It manufactures corrugated paperboard, corrugated plastic, wood packaging, and other packaging materials. The company's thermoformed trays can be made to precisely fit customers' products while its corrugated products provide disposable and reusable packaging options. For products that fall outside the box, Concept Packaging's wood packaging and metal racks accommodate bulkier items, ranging from heavy equipment to motors. The company also provides contract packaging, logistics services, and inventory management.

The company's 13 distribution and manufacturing facilities are located in five states in South Carolina (six locations), Georgia, Indiana, Kentucky, and Mississippi.

Concept Packaging has a wide array of machinery capable of producing a broad range of end products from intricate die cuts to bulk boxes and large furniture. Its products include corrugated cardboard products, as well as wood crates and pallets, and honeycomb packaging and metal, plastic, and foam packaging materials.

Concept Packaging began operations as Southland Container Corp. in 1983 with 12 employees and a 20,000 sq. ft. plant in Inman, South Carolina. The company moved to establish a competitive position by acquiring several packaging companies and subsequently changed its name to reflect its broader service capabilities in 1993.
