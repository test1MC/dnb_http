Andrew Peller is a leading winemaker in Canada with vineyards and wineries in British Columbia, Nova Scotia, and Ontario. The company markets wine under more than a dozen labels, including premium brands Hillebrand, Peller Estates, and Trius, as well as value-priced Domaine D'Or and Hochtaler. It also imports wines from around the world. Through its Global Vintners unit, Andrew Peller offers winemaking supplies to consumers under the Winexpert, Vineco, Wine Kitz, and Artful Winemaker names. Products are sold through state-owned distributors and in more than 100 company-owned retail outlets operating as Aisle 43, Vineyards Estate Wines, and WineCountry Vintners. Chairman Joseph Peller controls the company.
