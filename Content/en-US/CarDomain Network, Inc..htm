You don't have to be a grease monkey to be the automotive master of your domain. CarDomain Network operates CarDomain.com, a community Web site for car enthusiasts where members can showcase their vehicles, share information and tips on vehicle enhancements and modifications, and provide feedback to each other. All total, it features pictures and videos of more than 700,000 cars and trucks. CarDomain also publishes blogs and covers events such as the LA Auto Show and the SEMA (Specialty Equipment Market Association) Show. The site earns money through advertising, which targets the 18-to-34-year-old male demographic. CarDomain Network was founded in 1998. It was acquired by Source Interlink in 2012.
