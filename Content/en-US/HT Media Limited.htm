HT Media can't print newspapers for all of India's different languages, so it settles for Hindi and English. The company's flagship newspaper is the English-language Hindustan Times , with a 1.7 million circulation. Its business newspaper, Mint , is a joint venture with the Wall Street Journal , while its other daily paper, Hindustan , is the country's third-largest Hindi-language newspaper. HT Media also publishes two magazines in Hindi, along with a job search Web site, Shine.com; a social networking site, DesiMartini.com; a radio station, Fever 104, (along with Virgin Group ), and the Miss India Worldwide India pageant. In addition, HT Media entered the mobile marketing segment in a partnership with Velti PLC .
