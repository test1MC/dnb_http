Extending the reach of its horseshoe logo, Etienne Aigner has expanded its classically styled leather goods and apparel beyond its mainstay handbags and belts. The brand name adorns such items as costume jewelry, briefcases and computer bags, and shoes, and Etienne Aigner licenses its name for cosmetics, eyewear, men's and women's apparel, and watches. Its goods are sold in approximately 40 countries through about 135 company stores and some 350 point of sales worldwide. Founded in 1965, Etienne Aigner oversees distribution of Etienne Aigner products in Africa, Asia, Australia, and Europe. Etienne Aigner Group , a separate company, distributes the brand in the Americas.
