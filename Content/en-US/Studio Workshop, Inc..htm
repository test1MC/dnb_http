Studio Workshop wants to stand up and be recognized for its own recognition efforts. The company makes and markets hardwood plaques, trophy bases, and desk accessories, such as paperweights and business card holders. From its 6,300 sq. ft. manufacturing facility in Nebraska, the firm produces solid hardwood products made from American walnut, red alder, cherry, and oak woods. Studio Workshop, which was founded in 1968, peddles its products through select dealers nationwide.
