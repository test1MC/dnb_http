Tangled TV cables got you in a tizzy? Well, AMIMON offers a wireless alternative for those who hate the hassle of wires and snakes. The fabless semiconductor company makes modules and chipsets that enable wireless transmission between high-definition video and audio sources and consumer electronic displays, such as DVD players, LCD and plasma TVs, gaming consoles, and multimedia projectors. AMIMON's uncompressed wireless link boasts the same functionality and quality as a wired link. Manufacturers such as Sharp use AMIMON's technology in LCD TVs that are sold in Japan. The company has also joined forces with Belkin to develop wireless home theater setups.
