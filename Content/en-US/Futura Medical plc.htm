Futura Medical's focus for the future is for less pain and more sexual health. The company develops topical products mostly for the consumer health care market aimed at helping people manage pain, as well as products that aid with erectile dysfunction and premature ejaculation. Its primary technology is DermaSys, which is used as a base for many of its drug candidates including MED2002, a topical gel being developed with Reckitt Benckiser (RB) to treat erectile dysfunction. Futura is exploring other applications for DermaSys such as CSD500, a condom to designed to help maintain firmer erections (also being developed with RB). The company is developing PET500, its premature ejaculation treatment with Ansell Ltd .
