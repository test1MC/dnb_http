If you need electrical enclosures, you may still need Maysteel. Maysteel is a manufacturer of custom precision sheet metal enclosures, assemblies, and components; it also manufactures its own proprietary furniture and electrical and telecommunications enclosures and switches under the SmartBank and Trinetics brands. Products range from small fan plate enclosures to large computer server cabinets, electrical distribution enclosures, and vending machine and ATM machine bodies. Its contract manufacturing clients have included IBM , Cisco , and Rockwell Automation . The company operates three manufacturing facilities -- two in Wisconsin and one in Mexico.

The company additionally offers assembly and testing services, prototyping, painting/finishing, and welding/joining capabilities. Its aluminum TH enclosures are more impact-resistant than fiberglass and are lighter in weight and price than stainless steel.

Maysteel was founded in 1936. For more than 35 years Maysteel has been controlled by Everett Smith Group, a privately held investment company established in 1858.
