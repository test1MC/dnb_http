Delta Electronics wants to turn you on -- and your computer and your printer and all your office equipment, too. The company is one of the leading manufacturers of switching power supplies, including products that power personal computers, servers, workstations, and office equipment. Delta is also a popular destination for transformers, electronic components, visual displays, industrial automation, and networking equipment. Customers include HP , Sony , Fujitsu , Dell , Siemens , and AUDI . Established in Taiwan in 1971, Delta distributes its wares throughout Asia, Europe, and the US. The company gets more than 90% of sales from the Asia/Pacific region.
