Family-owned Seavin operates two wineries, San Sebastian Winery and Lakeridge Winery and Vineyards. Seavin wineries produce mid-market varietal, blended, and sparkling wines made primarily from muscadines and other grape varieties that are bred to be adaptable to the warm semi-tropical climate. Seavin, whose total vineyard acreage makes it one of the one of the largest winemakers in the state of Florida, sells its wines at select retailers throughout the US and online through its Web site.
