AT&S Austria Technologie and Systemtechnik develops and makes complex printed circuit boards (PCBs). Its fabrication plants in Austria, China, India, and South Korea supply PCBs to original equipment manufacturers and contract manufacturers who produce everything from cellular phones and handheld electronics to autos, aircraft, and industrial and medical equipment. It offers services including industrial/mechanical/electronics design and component assembly. AT&S has a list of clients that speaks for itself, including big names like General Electric , Honeywell , Nokia , Philips , and Siemens , all of which sell products internationally. In a joint venture with Solland Solar, AT&S is producing photovoltaic modules.
