A killer-looking pair of heels can be murder on a gal's feet, but Foot Petals is there to soften the blow. Foot Petals makes insoles and shoe cushions designed for stilettos, strappy sandals, pointy-toed shoes, and other fashionable footwear. Its shoe cushions are made from a foam urethane called PORON (made by Rogers Corporation ) and are named Killer Kushionz, Sole Stopperz, Tip Toes, Haute Heelz, Strappy Slips, and Amazing Arches. The company also offers slippers, legwear, gift sets, and accessories. Foot Petals peddles its products at retailers such as Nordstrom and Target and online through Zappos . Founded in 2001, the company was acquired by slipper giant R.G. Barry in 2011.
