GN Store Nord's products make sure you hear loud and clear. Founded as a telegraph company, GN today consists of subsidiaries that develop, make, and sell hearing-related devices. GN Netcom produces Jabra brand headsets for call center and office settings, cell phone users, and other equipment makers. GN ReSound specializes in hearing aids marketed under the Beltone, Interton, and ReSound brands. Most of GN Store Nord's products are made in China, a lower-cost production country, and are sold primarily to markets in Europe and North America.
