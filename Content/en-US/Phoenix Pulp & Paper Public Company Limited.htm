Phoenix Pulp & Paper Public Company Limited is a pioneer in pulp products. Founded in 1975 the company is an affiliate to Siam Cement Group Paper , a leading integrated producer of paper products in Thailand. It is one of the only businesses in the world to market pulp made from bamboo and kenaf (a non-wood plant). Its mill has the capacity to produce about 230,000 tons of pulp a year. Phoenix Pulp & Paper sells its products to customers in Thailand and abroad. Over 90% of its products are made from eucalyptus and bamboo, which are harvested from local farmers' plantations. The company has developed partnerships with farmers by offering training, as well as seedlings, fertilizer, and guaranteed prices for trees.
