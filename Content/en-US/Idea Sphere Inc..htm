Some people will tell you it's a good idea to take your vitamins, but Idea Sphere (operating as IdeaSphere or ISI Brands) thinks its an even better idea to sell them. The company makes more than 1,000 natural and organic vitamins and nutritional supplements. It produces tablets, capsules, drink mixes, and snacks under the Twinlab, Metabolife, Nature's Herbs, Alvita, Cole Water, Dr. Green, and TeaZen brands. IdeaSphere sells its products through distribution partnerships in the Americas, Europe, Africa, and the Asia/Pacific region, as well as through its website.
