I am a rock. I am an island. And I subscribe to Islands. Islands Media publishes lifestyle and tourism magazine Islands , billed as "the global authority on island travel". The magazine is published eight times a year and has a readership of about 520,000. In addition, the company offers vacation packages, travel news, and contests through its Inslands.com Web site and free eNewsletter. Islands Media also provides customized marketing programs, commercial photography, promotional videos, and corporate Web sites for hotels and resorts. Islands Media is a subdisiary of consumer publishing firm Bonnier Corporation .
