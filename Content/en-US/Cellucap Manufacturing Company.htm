Cellucap Manufacturing supplies disposable apparel for workers in foodservice, health care, industrial manufacturing, and other fields where sanitary clothing is a must. The company makes aprons, caps, coats and jackets, coveralls, footwear covers, gloves, gowns, and visors, including those paper hats seen on employees behind the counters of fast-food places. It even provides "beard restraints," to hold back the facial hair of bearded employees. Cellucap Manufacturing has expanded through acquisitions. In 2011 it purchased DISCO, a maker and distributor of fryer filters and cleaning tools for restaurant/food service operators, from Katy . Established in 1954, the company is owned by co-founder/CEO Jane Harris.
