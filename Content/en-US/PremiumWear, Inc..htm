PremiumWear pairs penguins and apparel. The company, previously named Munsingwear, designs and markets sportswear and accessories, primarily golf shirts, which are distributed to golf pro shops and resorts in Canada and the US. The company serves the US promotional products/advertising specialty industry. Its Munsingwear-licensed brand (the penguin label) includes men's and women's knit and woven shirts, sweaters, and outerwear. It also licenses the Jockey brand. PremiumWear sells its own Page & Tuttle-brand knit golf shirts, backpacks, and other bags, as well as items under the Forsyth brand. PremiumWear was owned by Toronto's Forsyth Holdings until 2009, when it was sold to River's End Trading Company.
