UK-company A.G. Barr manufactures, markets, and sells an array of carbonates, still drinks, and water. Brand name offerings include: IRN-BRU, based on a complex, well-guarded, century-old recipe, it leads all soft drink brands in Scotland; D'N'B, a dandelion and burdock blend, as well as its namesake Barr, produced in a range of flavors; and Rubicon, the UK's #1 exotic juice drink brand. The company also holds the UK distribution license to brands Orangina owned by Suntory , and Rockstar energy drinks, by Dr Pepper Snapple . In 2012 A.G. Barr merged with rival soft drinks maker Britvic plc .
