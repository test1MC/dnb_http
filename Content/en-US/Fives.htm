Fives can equip virtually any business. The company, which specializes in thermal and mechanical equipment, makes industrial machinery for the aluminum, automotive, cement, energy, glass, steel, and sugar industries. Offerings range from furnaces and cryogenic pumps to the construction of large-scale manufacturing plants. Customers have included such big names as Baosteel , LafargeHolcim , Air Liquide , ThyssenKrupp Stainless , and Qatar National Cement Company. Subsidiary Fives Cinetic makes sorting and materials handling equipment for logistics, postal, and freight forwarding applications. Fives operates in more than 30 countries.

Though Europe accounts for more than a third of sales, Fives is expanding internationally in regions such as China, Brazil, and India. The company is also growing through acquisitions that compliment existing operations and offer synergies with other group businesses.

Fives has grown through the use of acquisitions. In 2015 it picked up Lund Engineering, an engineering firm known for designing and building electro-mechanical equipment for the manufacture of composite aerospace structures and components. In the same year, Fives obtained Bryant Grinder's technology and intellectual property, expanding its expertise to the design and manufacture of high-precision/high-production inner-outer diameter grinders.

Fives was founded in 1812 to make equipment for the sugar industry. Financiere Alexandre III, part of the Industri Kapital private equity firm, took Fives private in 2001. Industri Kapital sold the Fives Group in 2004 to Barclays Private Equity and the group's management. FL Investco, a privately held investment firm in France, owns most of the company.
