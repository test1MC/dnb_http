Sumitomo Pipe & Tube, a part of the Sumitomo Metal Industries group, manufactures seamless, wide-bore welded, and electric-resistance welded pipes. The company's customers primarily are in the oil and gas business, where Sumitomo's tubes and pipes are used in pipelines and oil wells. Its products are also used as boiler tubes for industrial projects and in power generation plants. Sumitomo Pipe & Tube accounts for about a quarter of its parent's business; Sumitomo Metal Industries owns 57% of the company.
