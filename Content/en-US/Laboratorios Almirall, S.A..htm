Laboratorios Almirall can help patients with a lot of ailments. The pharmaceutical company develops, manufactures, and markets drugs for a wide range of conditions affecting the digestive, central nervous, respiratory, and cardiovascular systems. Products include ebastine (antihistamine), aceclofenac (anti-inflammatory), and almotriptan (anti-migraine drug). The company, which is the leading drugmaker in Spain, operates from offices in Europe and Mexico and markets its drugs in more than 70 countries. Laboratorios Almirall is currently focusing its research efforts on treating asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, rheumatoid arthritis, and dermatological problems, among others.

The company manufactures its drugs at plants in France, Germany, and Spain. It also manufactures active pharmaceutical ingredients at two facilities in Spain. Its products are sold in more than 70 countries.

Laboratorios Almirall's international operations now account for almost 60% of the company's total business (Spain accounted for 41.5% of revenue in fiscal 2012). The firm expects international trade will account for more 70% of its business during 2014.

In addition to marketing its own products, Laboratorios Almirall has licensing agreements with other drugmakers to market their products in different markets.

The revenue increased by 3% in fiscal 2012 compared to fiscal 2011 due to an increase in other revenue partially offset by a slight decline in net sales. The decline in net sales was due to the measures implemented in Spain over the past three years.

The company's net income decreased by 9% in fiscal 2012 compared to the previous period due to increased spending on R&D and additional operating expenses.

Laboratorios Almirall remains focused on expanding its direct presence internationally. The company uses external partners, licensing, acquisitions, and partnerships to expand its product portfolio and geographic reach.
