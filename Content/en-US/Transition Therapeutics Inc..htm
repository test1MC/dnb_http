Transition Therapeutics would like to help people suffering from disease to make the transition to good health. The biotechnology firm is working on therapies to treat metabolic and neurological ailments such as diabetes, Alzheimer's disease, multiple sclerosis, and brain injury. It works on research and development projects both independently and through partnerships with other drug companies. For instance, Transition Therapeutics is developing a potential treatment for Alzheimer's disease in partnership with Elan . The company also works with Eli Lilly on a series of compounds it hopes to develop into treatments for diabetes. Opko Health acquired Transition Therapeutics for $60 million in mid-2016.
