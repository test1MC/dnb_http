Whether you have a hankering to read about sushi or prefer to delve into the exploits of the former bass player of the Rolling Stones, Dorling Kindersley is sure to offer something that satisfies your literary appetite. The German subsidiary of UK-based Dorling Kindersley Ltd., the publishing house releases hundreds of titles on a plethora of subjects, including architecture, astrology, food, gardening, economics, travel, hobbies, nature, health, sex and relationships, fitness, and children's reading. The company was started in 1999 in a converted Bavarian barn.
