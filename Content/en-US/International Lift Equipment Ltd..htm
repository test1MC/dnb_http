Need a lift? International Lift Equipment (ILE) manufactures electrical and mechanical lift products and components such as small assemblies, brackets, limit ramps, push boxes, and safety gears. It also produces a complete line of lift packages for new and refurbishment projects. ILE's GAL Door Systems unit makes overspeed governors, electronic door protectors, door packages, and gear units. It serves local authorities, elevator companies, and clients in the housing market in the UK and Europe. Founded in 1976, ILE has a manufacturing facility and office in Leicester, with other offices in Bradford and London.
