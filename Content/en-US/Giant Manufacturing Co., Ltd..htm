Giant Manufacturing has peddled its way to the top of the bicycle industry. In 1980 it became the largest bicycle maker in Taiwan; today Giant Manufacturing is the largest in the world. The company manufactures bikes for competition, exercise, recreation, and transportation, and its two-wheelers come in both traditional pedal-driven styles or equipped with battery-powered engines. Giant Manufacturing also produces cycling apparel, helmets, saddles, grips, and other related gear. Its products are sold by more than 10,000 retailers in about 50 countries. The company sponsors more than 30 professional cyclists.
