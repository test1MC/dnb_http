Life at Organic Milling is a grind -- and that's a good thing. The company manufactures a plethora of private-label and and licensed high-fiber grain-based foods, including cereals, granola, cookies, sweet and savory crackers, toaster pastries, ready-made cracker pie crusts, microwave popcorn, and other salty snack foods. Its products are sold in retail food stores across the US. Kraft Foods purchased the company's biggest brand, Back to Nature, in 2003; however, it continues to manufacture Back to Nature products for Kraft. Organic Milling's other brands include Cinnabon , Nutritious Living, and Vita-Crunch.
