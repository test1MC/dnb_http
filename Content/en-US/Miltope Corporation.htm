Miltope makes rugged computers and peripherals for trying situations. Also known as VT Miltope, the company manufactures servers, mass storage devices, and portable computers for combat and other environments that prove hostile to sensitive electronics. The equipment is used by the US military, NATO forces, and foreign militaries in severe land, sea, and airborne circumstances. Miltope also serves the commercial aviation industry with airborne networking equipment and other peripherals. Commercial customers have included Lufthansa and United Airlines . Miltope is a subsidiary of VT Systems, a unit of the US branch of Singapore Technologies Engineering.

Miltope produces a wide range of computers, computer workstations, servers, disk cartridges, mass storage systems and other related products.

The company serves defense, government, industrial, and commercial customers.

The company clarified its product line in 2014 when it sold the ruggedized aerospace printer business to Astro-Med Inc. for $6.7 million. .

Miltope was founded in 1972. Singapore Technologies Engineering acquired the company in 2003.
