Overture Networks has a proposition for telecommunications carriers: Offer more services. The company makes networking equipment (sold under the ISG, UTX, and UTS brands) that allows carriers to deliver Ethernet data, as well as traditional voice services, over legacy network equipment. Its multi-service gateways are designed primarily for service providers transitioning from traditional SONET-based hardware. It also provides equipment to private network operators such as large businesses. Overture's customers have included Verizon Business and COLT . In January 2016, Overture was acquired by ADVA Optical Networking . ADVA will deploy Overture's products to extend its line of cloud access tools.
