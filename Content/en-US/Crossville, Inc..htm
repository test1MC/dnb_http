Work at Crossville is all set in stone. The company, also known as Crossville Ceramics and Crossville Porcelain Stone, is a part of diversified industrial firm Curran Group . It manufactures a broad range of porcelain floor tiles for residential and commercial use. It offers products with finishes from rugged to high-gloss textures and many color and pattern options. Crossville distributes its product mainly through design professionals and floor covering stores throughout the US; it also operates two outlet stores in Tennessee. The Curran family owns the company. Crossville started its business by selling imported porcelain tiles from Switzerland. It began manufacturing its own porcelain stone in 1986.
