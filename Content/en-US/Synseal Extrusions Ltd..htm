Synseal knows that its window of opportunity is a plastic one. The company makes PVC profiles, gaskets, and associated components for windows, doors, and roofs. Synseal's products are used by window, door, and conservatory and sun-room manufacturers throughout the UK. The company also makes roofing products such as cresting, eaves, glazing bars, gutters, and ridges. Its conservatories and sun-rooms come in a ready-to-assemble kits. Products are sold under the Shield, Global, Legend, and SynerJy brand names. Customers have included more than 650 fabricator and over 3,600 installers in the UK, including West Yorkshire Windows.

Synseal enhances its product portfolio through the help of acquisitions. In 2014 the company acquired Prospect, a glass supplier providing insulated glass units for window frame and roof applications as well as quantities of single sheet toughened glass. Also that year, Synseal acquired the trade and operating assets of the Litchfield Group's UK window and trade extrusion and door businesses.
