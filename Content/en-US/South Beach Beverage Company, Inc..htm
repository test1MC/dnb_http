It was meant to be -- SoBe, or South Beach Beverage Company as it is officially known, makes exotic teas, fruit juices and blends, elixirs, vitamin- and antioxidant-enhanced water (Lifewater), and sports drinks. The company also licenses its name for gum and chocolate products. South Beach's beverages, named for the trendy area of Miami, are commonly referred to using the SoBeacronym. It has a partnership with 7-Eleven convenience stores and makes a SoBe-branded energy Slurpee sold at 7-Eleven stores. The company's products are available throughout the US and Canada. South Beach Beverage is a subsidiary of PepsiCo .
