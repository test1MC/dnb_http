Armanino Foods would tell us: "You're too skinny! Eat!" Armanino Foods of Distinction makes upscale frozen and refrigerated Italian-style food. Its flagship product is pesto and it makes half a dozen varieties. The company also manufactures frozen filled pastas and frozen meatballs. It counts other US food manufacturers, restaurants, and foodservice vendors among its customers, as well as club stores and major retail food chains. Near its headquarters in Hayward, California, Armanino Foods operates pesto and pasta production facilities. The firm processes its meat in nearby Stockton, California. It has branch offices in San Francisco, Sacramento, Los Angeles, Las Vegas, Boston, and Sioux City, Iowa.
