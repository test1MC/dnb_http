IZZE makes fruit fizzy. The company mixes carbonated spring water with 100% fruit juices. The soda-like product contains no refined sugar, caffeine, or preservatives, as IZZE pasteurizes its juices right in the bottle. The beverage company offers more than half dozen fizzy fruit flavors, including apple, blackberry, pear, and pomegranate. The drinks come in three iterations -- IZZE, the original fruit drink that contains 70% juice; IZZE Fortified, with vitamins C and B; and IZZE esque, a low-calorie version that contains 25% juice. The company's product lineup is available in the US, as well as Canada, the Caribbean, and the South Pacific. IZZE Beverage is a unit of PepsiCo , which purchased the company in 2006.
