While you and I are getting another cup of coffee in the break room, folks at ArrayIt (formerly TeleChem International) are helping decipher the human genome. The company provides life sciences and research technologies used by researchers in genetic, clinical, pharmaceutical, government, and academic laboratories worldwide. Its genetic analysis products, including microarray slides and reagents, are used to diagnose diseases, monitor patient drug regimes, and develop potential new treatment therapies. ArrayIt's products also have applications in agricultural scientific research. The company was founded in 1993 and serves customers in about 50 countries worldwide.
