Tropical Cheese Industries manufactures specialty dairy products under the Paisano, El Molino Rojo and Tropical brand names to satisfy the specialty fresh cheese needs of Caribbean and Hispanic consumers living throughout the US. The company also offers sour cream, yogurt shakes, flan, rice pudding, and chorizo and farmer-style sausage, as well as a line of tortillas. It distributes its products primarily along the East Coast. Tropical Cheese Industries was founded in the early 1980s by Rafael Mendez, a Cuban immigrant who started a milk delivery service in New Jersey in the 1950s, driving a single used truck.
