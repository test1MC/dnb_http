SCA Hygiene, a division of Sweden's Svenska Cellulosa Aktiebolaget (SCA), makes and sells a comprehensive line of tissue and personal care products worldwide. It makes absorbent tissue products, such as baby diapers and incontinence items, and feminine hygiene care, to name a few. In addition to selling its products to home consumers, it sells to hotels, health care facilities, and industrial and service institutions. Hygiene products account for about 50% of the parent company's total sales. Its consumer brands include Edet, Tork, Zewa, Favorita, and Velvet. The hygiene products operations are conducted through four business groups: SCA Tissue Europe, SCA Personal Care, SCA Americas, and SCA Asia Pacific.

Germany is SCA's largest market in terms of sales. SCA has four German tissue mills; its Mannheim mill is Europe's largest.

The company develops and produces hygiene, tissue, and forest products for consumers and companies in the four market segments of industrial, commercial, HoReCa (hotels, bars, restaurants, canteens), and health care.
