For a speedier Internet and the ability to watch video on the Web, you might need Source Photonics. The company makes two primary optical components, passive optical network (PON) subsystems and transceivers, and sells them both to telecommunications equipment vendors, including Alcatel-Lucent , Tellabs , UTStarcom , and ZTE Corporation . These customers supply such telecom service providers as Verizon with the fiber-optic technology needed to increase computer network bandwidth and speed to homes and businesses. Source Photonics makes most of its sales in China and the US. In 2010 MRV Communications sold the company to private equity firm Francisco Partners for $146 million.
