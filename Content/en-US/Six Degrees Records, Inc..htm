Getting connected to music that isn't mainstream or the typical radio fare is a little easier thanks to Six Degrees. Six Degrees Records is an independent music label that releases a mix of world music, ambient, folk, classical, and pop. The label's roster is comprised of more than forty artists, including dj Cheb i Sabbah, Another Fine Day, and DJ Spooky. Six Degrees Records brings Brazilian music to North American audiences through its partnership with Ziriguiboom Discos. The label hosts a weekly Internet radio show and distributes its music through its online shop and through a number of national distributors in the US and around the world.
