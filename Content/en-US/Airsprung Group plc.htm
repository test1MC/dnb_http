Airsprung Group wants its customers to leap out of bed in the morning because they're so refreshed. The firm is a leading manufacturer and distributor of beds and upholstered furniture in the UK. Mattresses, divans, and bedsteads are made through its Airsprung Beds, Gainsborough Beds, and Hush divisions; Airofreem produces foam components for packaging and furniture; and the Cavendish, Collins & Hayes, and Gainsborough divisions roll out upholstered furniture. Airsprung produces over 600,000 beds each year and traces its historical roots all the way back to 1871.
