ModeLabs Group is more than happy to go mobile. The company designs and distributes cell phones and accessories. Its distribution business accounts for more than 90% of sales. The company also designs phones for both luxury brands, such as TAG Heuer and Dior , as well as others, such as Lacoste. Outside of France, ModeLabs has operations in China, Hong Kong, Italy, the UK, and the US. France-based partner Bigben Interactive acquired the company in 2011.
