Through its subsidiary, China Yuchai International , Hong Leong Asia is a leader in manufacturing and distribution, especially diesel engines and construction equipment. It is also one of the largest suppliers of building materials in Singapore and surrounding regions. Hong Leong Asia makes steel products, cement, and ready-mix concrete, and it quarries rock and supplies granite for construction. Other subsidiaries include Rex Industrial Packaging Group (containers), Air Well Air-Conditioning Technology (China) Co., Ltd., and FEDDERS (lifestyle appliance arm). Most of its sales come from China. Hong Leong Asia was founded in 1941 and is the trade and industry arm of Singapore conglomerate Hong Leong Group.
