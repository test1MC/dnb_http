Maisto International builds cars -- not the kind you drive, but the kind you collect or play with. The company makes authentic-looking scale models of cars, ranging from the 1965 Pontiac GTO to the latest Lamborghini Gallaro, as well as motorcycles and trucks. Models vary from 1:64 to 1:10 scale. Maisto (pronounced "mace-toe") works with carmakers and with toy retailers, selling to national and regional chains and independent toy and hobby stores. It also makes special-edition pieces for premium and promotional customers. Maisto, which sells its products throughout the world, is part of the May Cheong Group, a Hong Kong-based vertically integrated manufacturer.

The company has its customers North America, Central America, South America, Africa, Europe, Asia, and Australasia, primarily in the US, Germany, Greece, the Netherlands, Switzerland, and Turkey

Maisto designs, creates, and produces die-cast toys, radio-control vehicles, and replicas. It offers cars, motorcycles, boats, hobby collections, assembly lines, airport and downtown play sets, shuttle launches, race launchers, fresh tracks, extreme and highway haulers, power racers, ATVs, earth shockers, dirt demons, finger wheels, and tailwinds. The company also provides custom and cycle shop, road race, off-road, truck stop, emergency city, triple play, and floor display toys.

Maisto sells its products through online retailers and retail stores and via distributors.

The company grows by expanding its partnerships. Its strategic partners include BBurago, DiecastLive, Ducati , Lamborghini , and MySpace .

Maisto was founded in 1967 by the Ngan family.
