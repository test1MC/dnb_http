Pactiv is active in containing both food and the people who eat it. The company makes packaging for the foodservice market, as well as building products. Pactiv's food packaging products, which include plastic cups, takeout, deli, catering containers, and tableware, are used by the supermarket, restaurant, packer/processor, and institutional industries, primarily in North America, where most of Pactiv's manufacturing facilities are based. The company's GreenGuard products include insulation board, drainage mats, and sheathings for residential and commercial buildings. Pactiv is a subsidiary of Reynolds Group Holdings .

Headquartered in Lake Forest, Illinois, the company operates through 50 manufacturing plants, mixing centers, and distribution centers in North America, Europe, Asia.

The company's major markets include foodservice and food packaging, food processors, restaurants, supermarkets, convenience stores, institutions, and foodservice outlets across North America. Major customers include McDonald's, Wendy's, Wal-Mart, and Kroger, as well as regional customers and smaller local businesses.

The company generally sells its products on either a purchase order basis or under formal supply agreements with durations ranging from one to three years. It primarily uses a direct sales force and also utilizes third-party brokers for selected products and accounts.

Pactiv Foodservice's top ten customers accounted for 53% of the segment's revenue in 2015, out of which Reynolds Consumer Products accounted for 11% of its revenue.

The company operates under the Pactiv Foodservice segment of its parent company. This segment accounted for 33% of the parent's revenue in 2015. Pactiv Foodservice's net sales decreased by 2% for the year due the sale of its building products business and the unfavorable impact of foreign currency.
