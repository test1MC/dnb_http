Iriver keeps digital media flowing. The company manufactures digital music and CD players, portable gaming devices, portable media players, electronic dictionaries, and other digital entertainment devices. Its line of digital music players includes both hard-disk drive and flash memory-based devices. The company also offers digital music and photo management software. Iriver markets digital music and video players in Asia, Europe, and North America. Formerly called Reigncom, the company was founded in 1999. Best known by its Iriver brand, the company adopted the Iriver name as its corporate identity in 2009.
