Don't take it the wrong way when Wenzel Downhole Tools says "pipe down." The company makes drilling tools (drilling motors, shock tools, and drilling jars) used by the oil and gas drilling industry. Among the company's products are coatings (installation of protective coatings), downhole motors (straight hole, short radius, coiled tubing, and multi-point drilling motors), fabricated equipment, solids-control equipment (screens), and underground tooling. It also provides oil field equipment rental and repair. Wenzel Downhole Tools operates from Canada and the US and offers its products to customers worldwide. The company was taken private in mid-2013.
