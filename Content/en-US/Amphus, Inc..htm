Amphus primarily distributes and supports computer semiconductors and related components which are no longer available from or supported by their original manufacturers. The company deals in PC card host adapters, serial communication controllers, and system-on-a-chip devices made by such manufacturers as Vadem, Intel , and Cirrus Logic . These components are used for such applications as network data routers and switches, retail point-of-sale equipment, and cell phone displays.
