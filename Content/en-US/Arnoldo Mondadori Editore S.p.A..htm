On your next Roman holiday, take a turn through the pages produced by one of Italy's top publishing groups, Arnoldo Mondadori Editore. Its magazine division publishes women's magazines ( Donna Moderna ), TV guides ( Guida TV ), newsmagazines ( Panorama ), and publications on topics such as home, travel, and entertainment. Through joint venture operations, the company also publishes titles from Hearst ( Cosmopolitan ), Rodale ( Men's Health ), and Gruner+Jahr . In addition, Mondadori has book publishing, direct marketing, and printing divisions, as well as online versions of its magazines. Italian media mogul, and former Prime Minister, Silvio Berlusconi controls Mondadori through his holding company Finnivest.
