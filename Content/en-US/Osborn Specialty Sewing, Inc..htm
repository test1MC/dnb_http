Osborn Specialty Sewing makes athletic apparel under the Cobblestones Activewear and Game Gear brand names. Its products include baseball, volleyball, lacrosse and other sport practice and game clothing for schools and amateur sports teams as well as active wear for men and women. Osborn sells its wares through specialty retailers and sports shops throughout the US, Puerto Rico, and the US Virgin Islands. The family-owned and operated company was founded by chairman David Osborn in 1974. The Osborn family also runs sister company contract sewer Osborn Apparel, which was founded by Osborn patriarchs in 1946.
