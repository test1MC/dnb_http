Hi-Grade Computers studies to be the head of the class as a manufacturer and seller of made-to-order desktop, laptop, netbook, and home media center PCs, as well as servers. As part of its custom-built approach, its laptops, for example, sport changeable covers with available designs such as pink leopard, zebra, and a red, England-themed cover. In addition to standard technical support and financing services, the company provides Internet-based telephony (VoIP) and related services through its Hi-Grade Solutions division. Hi-Grade serves the home, business, education, and government markets. The company was founded by its managing directors Ori Yiassoumis and James Siabi.
