International Automotive Components Group (doing business as IAC Group) isn't too concerned with what's under the hood. The company is a leading supplier of automotive interior parts such as door and trim systems, instrument panels, consoles and cockpits, flooring and acoustic systems, and headliner and overhead systems. Its plastic, fabric, and leather parts are supplied to every major automotive company around the world, from Ford and GM in the US to Daimler and Volkswagen in Germany and Honda and Toyota in Japan. IAC Group operates through subsidiaries IAC Asia, IAC Europe, and IAC North America ; altogether, it has almost 75 manufacturing facilities in more than 15 countries.
