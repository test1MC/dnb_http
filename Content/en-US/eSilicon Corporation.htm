eSilicon tries to make it easy for electronics makers to come up with custom silicon. The company offers design, engineering, and testing services, helping customers produce semiconductors for applications in communications, consumer electronics, networking, security, storage, and wireless devices. eSilicon formed alliances with such companies as Agilent , Amkor , ARM , Rambus , Synopsys , and TSMC . The company helps small chip startups get better prices from chip foundries by representing dozens of companies at a time. The fabless application-specific integrated circuit (ASIC) design firm was established in 2000. eSilicon is owned by its executives and investors.
