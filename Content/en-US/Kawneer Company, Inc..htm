Although not really a headbanger, Kawneer is definitely into metal. Operating as Kawneer North America, the company manufactures architectural aluminum building products and systems for use in commercial and industrial construction. The Arconic subsidiary's products include sliding and swing entrances, framing systems for shopping malls and storefronts, hung/sliding and ribbon/fixed windows, building panels, sun control products, and curtain wall systems for stadiums, sports facilities, schools, low-rise offices, and high-rise buildings. Its Traco division makes window sliders, window wall, and other window accessories.

Kawneer operates about 10 manufacturing plants and fabricating service centers throughout the US and Canada. The company also sells products in 35 countries spanning Europe, Asia, and the Middle East.

Kawneer is a part of Arconic's global Building and Construction Systems (BCS) business unit, which operates under the Global Rolled Products segment. This segment contributed about 26% of the parent company's total revenue in 2013.

The company has turned more of its attention toward the green building market with thermally efficient and sustainable products and solutions. Eco-friendly products include ventilators, sunshades, and photovoltaic systems that capture sunlight and convert it into electricity.

Kawneer received its first patent for metal store-front molding in 1906; it has since been awarded more than 300 patents. The company is part of Arconic's global building and construction systems business.
