Forget about bad hair days; Farouk Systems can iron things out with its own version of CHI energy. The company makes and markets personal care products, including BioSilk hair care and spa items, in addition to CHI styling products and implements (hair dryers, straightening and curling irons, clippers, and hairstyling irons) for men and women. It also makes CHI men's and women's fragrances, as well as CHI nail polish. The company caters to both consumers and professionals and operates the Farouk Systems CHI Academy in Houston for training hair professionals. The company, founded by Palestinian immigrant Farouk Shami (chairman) in 1986, is owned and operated by professional hairdressers.

Houston-headquartered Farouk Systems makes hair care implements, shampoos, conditioners, hair color, and other liquid products in Houston. In 2013 the firm expanded into Turkey, where it established CHI Cosmetic Import Export Industry and Trade Ltd. to make and distribute its products there.

The company continues to grow nationwide and overseas. It distributes its products in more than 100 countries worldwide. Besides its production facilities in the US, Farouk Systems is opening more warehouses in Europe and recently established a partner company in Turkey. The company bought European distribution company, TrendyHair Company, to broaden its distribution lines overseas.

It also regularly rolls out new products. More recent launches include its BioSilk Color Therapy line, paraben-free BioSilk Volumizing line, and automatic rotating curling iron.

Farouk Systems boasts a history of firsts in the hair care industry. In 1986 it launched the industry's first ammonia-free hair lightener and color system, SunGlitz, which today is sold under the CHI name. With its CHI iron, the company became the first company to manufacture a hair tool that incorporates ceramic, ionic, and far infrared technologies.
