Otsuka Pharmaceutical Co. (OPC) is a manufacturer and distributor of pharmaceuticals, clinical testing and medical equipment, and consumer products. The company operates business segments specializing in branded prescriptions, over-the-counter health products, diagnostic substances and devices, nutraceuticals and nutritional foods, and skin care products ("cosmedics"). OPC's medicinal products include gastric ulcer agent Mucosta and Pletaal anti-platelet treatment. The company's line of nutraceutical consumer health products include FIBE-MINI dietary fiber supplement, Calorie Mate foods, and Pocari Sweat sports drink. OPC is part of the very diverse Otsuka Group. OPC bought US-based AVANIR Pharmaceuticals in 2015.
