You won't find most of True World Foods International's seafood products crawling around on the ocean floor. The company, which does business as Shining Ocean, sells imitation crab and lobster products, also known as surimi. Its brand names include Kanimi, Emerald Sea, Crab Smart, Crab and Lobster Elite, Sushi Supreme, and Seafarer; its Kanimi Deluxe product has real crab and lobster meat added. It also makes pre-made seafood salads and offers private-label services. True World Foods, founded in 1986, distributes its products to retail food outlets including supermarkets and delicatessens, as well as to restaurant and other foodservice operations (including sushi bars) throughout the US.
