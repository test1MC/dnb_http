ATI Airtest Technologies advances its cause with various technologies for testing air. The company manufactures sensors and monitoring instruments used to detect gases, including carbon dioxide and propane. Some of its systems are handheld, while others are installed in buildings. Its offerings include not only gas-detection equipment, but also humidity and dew point sensors designed to improve energy efficiency and inhabitant comfort in buildings. Customers include Honeywell International and Invensys Building Systems . ATI Airtest Technologies has offices in Canada and the US.
