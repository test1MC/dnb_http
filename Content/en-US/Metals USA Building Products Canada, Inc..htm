Fear not if business at Metals USA Building Products Canada is over your head! Doing business as Allmet Roofing Products, the company produces stone-coated metal roofing materials. Allmet manufactures its high-end lineup from steel, coated with ceramic grains to achieve a variety of traditional building material finishes. Allmet's products are marketed in North America under the Continental, Shadowline, and Woodshake brands. The company serves architects, commercial contractors, and residential customers through independent distributors, with installation frequently through home improvement contractors. Formerly Dura-Loc Roofing Systems, the company was purchased by Metals USA in 2006, and renamed in 2007.
