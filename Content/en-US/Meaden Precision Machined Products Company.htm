Meaden Precision Machined Products makes and sells precision turned parts and screw machine products from brass, steel, aluminum, stainless steel, plastic, and other materials. The company offers engineering assistance and has the capability of turning bar stock with diameters as small as 1/16-inch to stock with diameters as large as 3-1/4-inch. Other services include grinding and finishing operations (broaching, drilling, tapping, threading, and tumbling).
