Resolute FP US is the US subsidiary of Canadian paper and pulp giant Resolute Forest Products . The parent company, which operates more than 40 pulp and paper mills, produces newsprint, specialty papers, market pulp, and wood products. Newsprint and specialty papers together account for about 60% of sales,while market pulp is responsible for almost 25%. The US is Resolute Forest Product's largest market, and Resolute FP US accounted for 66% of the parent company's total revenues in 2015. Resolute Forest Product has about 11% of total North American capacity, making it the third largest pulp producer in North America.

Parent Resolute Forest Products operates seven major pulp mills, five in the US and two in Canada, with a total capacity of 1.8 million metric tons.

Resolute FP US has locations in Augusta, Georgia; Calhoun, Tennessee; Catawba, South Carolina; Coosa Pines, Alabama; Fairmont, West Virginia; Grenada, Mississippi; Menominee, Michigan; and Usk, Washington.
