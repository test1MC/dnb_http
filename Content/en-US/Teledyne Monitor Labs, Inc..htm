When Mother Nature needs monitoring, Teledyne Monitor Labs is ready. The company supplies continuous emissions monitoring (CEM) products used by industries worldwide. Its instrumentation, systems, and software detect, measure, and report air quality and pollution from industrial stack emissions. Monitor and software brands, including LightHawk, RegPerfect, and sensor-e, use source and ambient measurement processes to analyze such gases as SO2, NOX, CO, CO2, and O2. The company operates facilities in Colorado and Pennsylvania. In 2002 Teledyne Technologies acquired Monitor Labs from Spirent (now Spirent Communications ). The company is part of Teledyne's largest business segment, Electronics and Communications.
