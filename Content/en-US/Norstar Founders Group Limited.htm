BeijingWest Industries International (BWI International) manufactures and sells auto brake parts (brake plates and brake shoes) and construction decorative hardware products (door hinges and door tracks). It also produces other automobile spare parts, such as oil cylinder covers and hose brackets. The company produces more than 35 million brake shoes and 50 million brake plates per year. The majority of BWI International's products go to customers in North America. Key clients include Honeywell , UTIL Automotive, and NUCAP Industries. Its manufacturing facility is located in Anhui Province, with another under development in the Czech Republic. The company was founded in 1998.
