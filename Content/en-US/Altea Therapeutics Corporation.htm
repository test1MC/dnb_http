Altea Therapeutics' painless patches bypass the need for needles. The company makes transdermal drug delivery patches for medicines traditionally administered by injection. Its PassPort patch could be used to administer insulin, pain relief medications, flu and other vaccines, and medications used by Parkinson's disease patients. The patch uses bursts of thermal energy to create channels in the surface of the skin, allowing medicines to pass through the top layer of skin.
