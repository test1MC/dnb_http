Johnson Foods processes fruits and vegetables, puts them in glass jars, and sells them under the Princess label to you via the Web. The company specializes in pickled vegetables (asparagus, green beans, snap peas, and carrots). Its products are available as individual jars, twelve-packs, variety packs, and in gift baskets. Johnson Foods also offers maraschino cherries -- the bright red kind ( á la banana splits) and natural (no coloring added). The family-owned company started out in business in 1949 as Johnson Fruit, later became an asparagus packer, and now offers its jarred goods as Johnson Foods.
