From the smallest mosaic tile to yard-long porcelain slabs, Marazzi has your bathroom covered. One of Europe's leading ceramic tile producers, Marazzi designs, makes, and distributes tile and other flooring and ceramic bathroom products; it also provides raw materials for other manufacturers. In addition to the Marazzi brand name, the company owns such brands as Ragno, Kerama Marazzi, and Cerabati. Marazzi also owns retail outlets in the US and Russia. The company is owned by Mohawk Industries , one of the largest rug and tile makers in the world.

In April 2013 Marazzi was acquired by global rug and tile making giant Mohawk Industries in a deal valued at $1.5 billion.

The group operates about 20 manufacturing facilities in Italy, Spain, France, the US, Russia, and China; it exports its products throughout the world.

Marazzi Group is the largest tile producer in Italy, where it carries a market share of more than 10%. It is also a top seller in France, Russia, and the US, each among its largest markets (it makes roughly three-quarters of its revenues outside of Italy). The company plans to continue its international growth as well as further diversifying its product lines. Being owned by its new parent company Mohawk will allow Marazzi to extend its reach exponentially in the US.

The company was founded in 1935 by Filippo Marazzi, grandfather of Marazzi's current chairman.
