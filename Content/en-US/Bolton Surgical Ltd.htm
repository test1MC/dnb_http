Bolton Surgical manufactures more than 3,500 different types of surgical instruments at its plant in northern England. Supplying NHS and private hospitals, clinics, and other medical centers worldwide, the specialty medical manufacturer's extensive range of products includes such standard items as clamps, gouges, nippers, osteotomes, retractors, and scissors, as well as bespoke products made to customer specifications. Bolton Surgical also offers repair services and instrument disposal for customers. The founding Bolton family still owns and operates the company.

Bolton Surgical distributes its products in Europe, South America, North America, the Middle East,North Africa, and Africa, Asia, and Australasia.

The company's production facility is in the UK city of Sheffield (famous for its long history of quality steel production).

Bolton Surgical promotes its products and services internationally, buoyed by Britain's reputation for high-quality manufacturing standards and stainless steel expertise.

In 2011 the company began distributing its products in Egypt, Kuwait, and Saudi Arabia, expanding its presence in the Middle East.

William Henry Bolton started the business that would become Bolton Surgical in the early 1900s, initially making scissors and forceps in his workshop and delivering them by bicycle to local hospitals.
