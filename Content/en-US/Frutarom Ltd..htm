Roam around wherever you want and your food is very likely going to contain flavors made by Frutarom. The flavors and fragrance company maintains 40 production and R&D facilities on three continents and sells to some 120 countries. Founded in 1933, Frutarom divides its business into two units. The Flavors Division (about two-thirds of company sales) makes flavor and seasoning products, and the Fine Ingredients Division makes natural flavor extracts, citrus oils, aroma chemicals, and nutraceutical extracts. US chemical company ICC Industries , headed by Frutarom's chairman, John Farber, owns about 37% of Frutarom. In 2016 Frutarom acquired Wiberg GmbH for $130.4 million.
