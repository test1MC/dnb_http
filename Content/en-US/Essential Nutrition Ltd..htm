Essentially, Essential Nutrition offers research, testing, and manufacturing services to drug companies. It is an Medicines and Healthcare Products Regulatory Agency licensed manufacturer of solid oral dosage form products such as tablets, hard shell capsules, and powders. The family owned and operated company produces its own brand of health food supplements which Alternative Medicines Limited markets and sells online. Essential Nutrition also partners with various agronomy firms to determine optimum location and growth cycles for its herbal medicine crops. It has collaborated on nutritional research with universities throughout the UK and in the US.

Essential Nutrition holds a UK product license for Diethylpropion 25mg, an active pharmaceutical product used as an anorectic for short term use as an adjunct to the dietary treatment of some patients with moderate to severe obesity and a body mass index of 30 kg/mÂ² or higher, who have not responded to an appropriate weight-reducing regimen alone and for whom close support and supervision are also provided.

Essential Nutrition was founded as a research and development company in 1989.
