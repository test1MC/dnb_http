BWXT Nuclear Operations Group (BWXT NOG) is concerned with a different kind of nuclear power -- national security. The company designs and manufactures components and materials for the US government's nuclear reactors and nuclear-powered submarines. Core activities include fabricating nuclear power generation components using applied mechanics, robotics engineering, and integrated CAD/CAM computer systems. Along with subsidiary Nuclear Fuel Services , BWXT NOG maintains the only two commercial plants licensed to possess and store highly enriched uranium (HEU) in the US. BWXT NOG is a subsidiary of BWX Technologies , which was spun off from McDermott International in 2010.
