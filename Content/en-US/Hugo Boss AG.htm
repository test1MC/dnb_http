Watch out: your boss might be feeling extra-boss in his new Boss suit. Hugo Boss outfits inhabitants of (and aspirants to) the corner office with brands including BOSS, a line targeted at executives; trendy, casual HUGO; and the more uptrend BOSS Selection. Hugo Boss designs and licenses clothes, accessories, cosmetics, and fragrances sold through its more than 1,000 shops and other retailers in some 80 countries. Known for its menswear, Hugo Boss has also entered womenswear, footwear, and leather goods. The firm was founded by its namesake in 1923 to make work clothes. It moved into suits in the 1950s and expanded beyond business wear in the 1990s. Hugo Boss is part of the Valentino Fashion Group .

The company has strong global market position. Hugo Boss has enjoyed above-average growth in Europe. Sales in the Americas were also up during the most recent fiscal period.

Hugo Boss is looking to extend its brand control in European markets. The company has also been expanding its foothold in China and the Middle East. Hugo Boss has given its Boss Woman business a shot in the arm in recent years (and brought it to the forefront), as well, by relaunching its women's accessories line.
