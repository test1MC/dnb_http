From analog to digital, telecommunications pioneer Iwatsu Electric has moved its technology with the times. The company sells VoIP telephones for small offices and headsets for switchboard operators and TV/radio studios; test and measurement equipment, such as oscilloscopes and Blu-ray disc inspection machines; and hardware for the printing industry, such as ink jet platesetters. Major customers include Japan's Ministry of Defense, the Japan Post Office , J-Power , and broadcasting corporation NHK . Iwatsu Electric was founded in 1938.
