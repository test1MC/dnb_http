Crystal Group clearly serves its customers. A server manufacturer, a systems integrator, and a custom design and manufacturing services provider, the company supplies servers for telecommunications service providers and original equipment manufacturers. Crystal Group also serves the government and military markets. In addition to servers, Crystal Group sells single-board computers, server rack systems, storage arrays, and peripheral devices. Its service offerings range from systems integration to contract manufacturing. Crystal Group's customers include Boeing , Lockheed Martin , and Raytheon . The company was founded in 1991 and is owned by Dexter Apache Holdings.
