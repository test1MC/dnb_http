This company's belts don't keep pants up; they keep vehicles running instead. One of the largest manufacturers of automotive and industrial belts and hoses, The Gates Corporation (also known as Gates Global) caters to the oil and gas, construction, transportation, automotive, and manufacturing sectors. Its line of industrial belts help power everything from rock crushers to snowmobiles. Gates' products keep cars and heavy equipment engines cool through its automotive belts that are used in air conditioners and alternators; in addition, its hydraulic hoses channel fluid in mining and construction equipment. In 2014 Blackstone Group acquired Gates Global for $5.4 billion.

In 2014 private equity juggernaut Blackstone Group purchased Gates Global for $5.4 billion. The major deal was Blackstone's largest since it purchased Hilton Worldwide in 2007.

The Gates Corporation operates through more than 100 locations spanning 30 countries.

The company has sales and marketing operations in major industrial and automotive markets in Asia, Australia, Europe, the Middle East, and North and South America. Customers reside in the energy, oil and gas, agriculture, construction, transportation, and automotive sectors.
