Next time you fill in the signature blank on the back of your credit card, think about ABnote USA. Once known as Arthur Blank & Company, the company is one of North America's primary suppliers of secure printed products, documents, such as checks, and other services. It can print more than 1.4 billion plastic cards (including credit cards, access cards, I.D. cards, drivers' licenses, and smart cards) a year. Founded in 1934, ABnote USA serves the financial, insurance, security, entertainment, and leisure industries, including AAA , American Express , AT&T , Barnes & Noble , British Airways , Blue Cross Blue Shield , and Costco . The company is part of ABnote North America, a division of American Banknote Corporation .
