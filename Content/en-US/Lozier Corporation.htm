In the market for a pallet rack or an end cap? Lozier works to satisfy its customers' wildest shelving desires. The company makes retail store fixtures, including display shelving and back-room storage systems, for supermarkets, discount stores, mass merchandisers, and other retail formats. It has manufacturing operations in Alabama, Missouri, Nebraska, and Pennsylvania. Lozier distributes fixtures across the US, as well as internationally. The company maintains a sales and service network for its international retailers. In addition to selling fixtures, Lozier also offers installation services.

Based in Nebraska, Lozier operates manufacturing and distribution facilities in Pennsylvania, Alabama, Missouri, and Nebraska. It sells its products globally through a sales and service network.

Lozier specializes in making and marketing display shelving, multi-function, widespan, CubeSaver, storage shelving, wood products, and showcase.

As part of its business, Lozier serves discounters, catalog showrooms, supercenters, chain drug stores, and superstores for multiple markets, such as automotive, office, computer, pet, and sporting goods.

The company leverages its showroom in Omaha for its sales and marketing efforts. The facility features display shelving configurations, accessories, colors, and finishes to provide retail customers with a variety of ideas and solutions.

Lozier serves its customers through 48 account territory representatives, 9 sales managers, and 66 customer service team members.

Lozier's business is supported by marketing and engineering resources, design concepts professionals, and an installation department, which is strategically located at its corporate office.

In November 2014, Lozier acquired Leggett & Platt' s store fixtures business for $62 million.

The company was founded in 1956.
