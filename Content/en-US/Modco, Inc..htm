Modco makes voltage-controlled oscillators (VCOs) for use in various applications, including medical, industrial, and wireless communications. The company offers custom and standard, general-purpose VCOs, along with drop-in replacements for oscillators made by other manufacturers. Modco also provides specialty VCO packaging. CEO Robert Wehe, who started the company in 1992, owns Modco.
