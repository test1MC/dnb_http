Bust out the fried catfish and sweet tea -- The folks at Toyota Motor are soaking up some good ol' Southern hospitality with a little help from Toyota Motor Manufacturing Alabama (TMMAL). TMMAL builds V-6 and V-8 engines for Toyota's Tacoma and Tundra pickup trucks, and for the Sequoia SUV. The manufacture of four-cylinder engines is being added to the facility's repertoire in 2011. Toyota Motor has increased TMMAL's engine manufacturing capacity in order to meet increased North American demand in the wake of higher consumer buying and lower oil prices.
