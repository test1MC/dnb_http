Most shrimp aficionados would say that they "sure am glad" Suram Trading supplies the growing American appetite for shrimp. The company imports shrimp and scallops from Asia, Central America, and South America (including from Thailand, Vietnam, India, Indonesia, Malaysia, China, Ecuador, and Argentina) and sells it to food retailers and restaurants in the US. Along with the raw, cooked, and peeled shrimp, the company markets marinated and breaded seafood products. Suram Trading is the parent company of Royal Tiger and Royal White frozen shrimp brands. It also supplies private label items. The company was founded in 1984.
