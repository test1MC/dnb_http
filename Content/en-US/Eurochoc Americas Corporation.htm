Chocolates and chocolate-lovers are international. Eurochoc Americas knows and bases it business on this. The company distributes gourmet chocolate and non-chocolate confectionary products made in Europe. It imports and markets high-end Swiss and French candy, including the Grether's Pastilles, Halter BonBons, Revillon Finesses, Swiss Prestige, and Favarger Fondue brands. Eurochoc's goodies are shipped to retailers and wholesalers throughout North America and can also be ordered through the company's Web site. Gift baskets and special-occasion selections are available.
