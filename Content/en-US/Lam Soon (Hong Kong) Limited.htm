Lam Soon has flour power. A member of Malaysia's Hong Leong Group , the company manufactures flour and edible oil products that are marketed throughout China, Macao, Taiwan, and Hong Kong. Established in Singapore in the 1930s as a trader of oils, rice, and copra, the company has evolved into a leading Southeast Asian food distributor with dozens of branches located throughout the region. Its more popular regional brand names include American Roses (flour), Golden Statue (flour), and Knife (edible oil). Lam Soon also manufactures dishwashing liquid and laundry detergent, and produces metal cans.
