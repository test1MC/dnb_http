While elbow grease will get the job done, Campbell Hausfeld can pour on the pressure! Power expert Campbell Hausfeld manufactures and markets air compressors, pneumatic tools (hammers, drills and screwdrivers, and sanders), nailers and staplers, paint sprayers, pressure washers (detergents to garden hose attachments), and welders. Its tools and kits are used by both the professional and do-it-yourself home improvement and automotive markets worldwide. The company sells its lineup directly through its Ohio headquarters, and through such retailers and e-tailers as Ace Hardware , Amazon , CARQUEST , Lowe's , and Wal-Mart . Campbell Hausfeld is a subsidiary of Scott Fetzer Co.

Campbell Hausfeld sells it products in the US and exports to some 100 countries, including in Latin America, Europe, and the Asia/Pacific region.

The company manufactures products for the home improvement and automotive industries.

In the US the diversified manufacturer markets its products through home centers, mass merchandisers, catalogs, Internet retailers, hardware stores, and online.

For more than a decade, Campbell Hausfeld has bolstered sales by routing its products, at competitive prices, to developing regions. Its investment strategy also includes buying strong companies with a history of profitability, a competitive advantage, and a stable management team.

The company is targeting acquisitions in select industries, including makers of advanced compressor, tank, and vacuum technologies, and manufacturers of high-grade air and power equipment, tools, and accessories, as well as other niche market manufacturers.

Campbell Hausfeld was founded in 1836 as a manufacturer of horse-drawn wagons and agricultural equipment.
