Materia manufactures catalysts and provides process development services and technology licenses to some of the world's top chemical and pharmaceutical companies. It markets them directly and through research chemical distributor Sigma-Aldrich . Materia's metathesis catalyst technology, known as Grubbs Catalysts, enables more efficient, cleaner production of chemicals and materials. The catalysts have been used by the likes of German industrial chemical manufacturer Lanxess , Swiss agrochem and seed maker Syngenta , and pharma giant Merck . Originally known as Advanced Sports Materials, Materia was founded in 1998 to commercialize the technology, which was created at the California Institute of Technology .
