Bond, WestBond, a company with a license to make sanitary paper. WestBond Enterprises makes disposable paper goods for the medical industry (dental bibs, examination table paper, and gowns) as well as personal hygiene products (bathroom tissues and hand towels). Popular with healthcare professionals, the company manufactures a waterproof mitten, Bio-Mitt Plus, that helps to prevent contamination. WestBond targets janitorial contractors that provide public restroom maintenance services and a swath of distributors that market to these businesses. The company also sells its lineup directly to nursing homes, airlines, and hotel and restaurant chains.  Customers are primarily located in Canada and the western US.
