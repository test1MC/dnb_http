The eyes have it at Elbit Vision Systems (EVS). The company designs automatic inspection systems that detect flaws in fabrics and industrial webs during processing (spinning, weaving, dyeing, finishing, and coating). EVS makes I-TEX systems that can inspect 330-cm-wide pieces of fabric at up to 300 meters per minute, PRIN-TEX systems for printed fabric, and Broken Filaments Analyzers for inspecting glass fibers. Elbit Vision Systems has used acquisitions and capital investments to broaden its product line and increase its reach into new geographic markets. Third party firms manufacture its products, which EVS tests and inspects.
