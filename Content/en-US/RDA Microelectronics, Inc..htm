RDA Microelectronics has a little something to offer manufacturers of communications electronics. Through its subsidiaries, the company designs and develops mixed-signal and radio-frequency semiconductors for broadcast, cellular, and other communication uses. Its semiconductors are integrated into a variety of consumer electronics, including 3G mobile phones, satellite TV set-top boxes, MP3 players, Bluetooth devices, and walkie-talkies. RDA markets primarily to communications electronics manufacturers in China; it has counted ZTE , Huawei Technologies , and TCL Corporation as customers. Tracing its roots back to 2004, the company completed an IPO in late 2010.
