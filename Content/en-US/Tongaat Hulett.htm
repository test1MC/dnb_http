Tongaat Hulett is a South African agri-processing business that also does business in land management and property development. Through its sugar and starch operations, the company produces refined carbohydrate products from sugar cane and corn. Its wet-milling operation is a major African producer of starch and glucose for food manufacturing companies. Tongaat owns sugar assets in Africa, including in Mozambique, Swaziland, and Zimbabwe. It is also the maker of the Voermol brand of animal feed. Tongaat Hulett's land and property operations develop sites for residential, commercial, resort, and industrial use.
