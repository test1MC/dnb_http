Scottish distiller Burn Stewart's products are meant to be smooth on the palette, not to burn. Though the company is relatively young, after less than 20 years it is responsible for a popular array of distilled Scotches and liquors, specializing in single malt Scotch, which it produces through its Deanston, Tobermory, and Bunnahabhain distilleries. Burn Stewart's products include blended and single malt Scotches, single malt Scotch whisky liqueur, Scottish cream liqueur, gin, and vodka. The company distributes its Scotches and liquors to over 60 markets worldwide. Burn Stewart is owned by The Distell Group.

In 2013 South African alcoholic beverage maker The Distell Group purchased Burn Stewart from CL World Brands for $244 million.

Burn Stewart has its headquarters and a bottling plant in East Kilbride and three distilleries in Perthshire, the Isle of Islay, and the Isle of Mull. The company also operates a sales and marketing branch in Taiwan.
