Pumping out products in the Poconos, J.A. Reinhardt manufactures precision aluminum dip-brazed housings, which are built especially for the aerospace and telecommunications industries. The company also makes aluminum beryllium fabricated products. Other products include air and liquid cooling avionics housings, dip-brazed electronic chassis, and microwave antennas and transmitters. The company's processes include aluminum dip brazing, aluminum beryllium and beryllium machining and fabrication, and electromechanical assembly. A family-owned company for more than 60 years, J.A. Reinhardt was acquired in 2009 by Arlington Capital Partners, which combined the company with thermal management specialist TSI Group.
