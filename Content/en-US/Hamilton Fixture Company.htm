Hamilton Fixture is in business to see and be seen. One of the nation's largest store fixture manufacturers, the company designs and makes custom display cases and other fixtures for retailers. It uses such materials as wood, metal, glass, wire, and acrylic. Customers have included Barnes & Noble , Starbucks , T.J. Maxx , and Wal-Mart . Hamilton Fixture manufactures, warehouses, and distributes its products through 750,000 sq. ft. of facilities in Ontario, California, and Hamilton, Ohio. The company was founded in 1959, providing counters to banks, post offices, and other retailers.
