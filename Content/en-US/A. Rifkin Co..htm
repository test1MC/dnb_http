"It's in the bag" is a reassuring phrase for A. Rifkin, a leading manufacturer of fabric security bags and other cash handling products used by the banking industry. It makes bags for coins, currency, and sensitive documents, along with products for bank lobbies and tellers. The family-owned company also makes cash handling bags designed for grocery stores, restaurants, and other retailers, as well as specialty products designed to handle such items as works of art and medical records. A. Rifkin pioneered the development of a high-security courier bag.

The company operates a manufacturing and headquarters facility in Wilkes-Barre, Pennsylvania, along with a European office in England.

A. Rifkin's products are marketed through sales representatives, and via trade journal advertising and direct mail. The company serves a range of specialty markets, including elections, libraries, medical records security, personal/home, and government.

Russian immigrant Abraham Rifkin started the company in 1892.
