Solocal Group (formerly PagesJaunes Groupe) is the leading French publisher of printed telephone directories (PagesJaunes and PagesBlanches). It also operates directory enquiry services by telephone and SMS (118008), and the Internet (pagesjaunes.fr, mobile.pagesjaunes.fr, mappy.com, and annoncesjaunes.fr). In addition, it offers online and direct marketing services. The company has some 700,000 local and national advertising customers. Medianuaire, a company controlled by affiliates of Kohlberg Kravis Roberts and Goldman Sachs Capital Partners, has a controlling interest in Solocal.

Outside of France, the Group's subsidiaries also publish directories in Spain, Belgium, Luxembourg, and Morocco.

The company reports revenue in three segments: Internet, printed directories, and "other businesses" revenue. Its Internet segment accounted for almost 60% of total annual revenue during fiscal 2012 while the printed directories segment brought in the bulk of the remaining 40% of revenue during the year.

In addition to the company's directory business, PagesJaunes provides direct marketing services including online classifieds through its annoncesjaunes.fr; and offers online directions and mapping services through its Mappy subsidiary.

The consolidated annual revenues of Solocal were down 3.2% in fiscal 2012 compared to the previous year, while its net profit decreased by 17% in fiscal 2012 compared to 2011. The decline was attributed to decreases in revenue from the company's printed directories and other businesses segment.

The company's printed directories segment revenues were down by 15% in fiscal 2012 while the other businesses revenues decreased by 25% in during the year due to the sharp decrease in revenues from telephone directory enquiry services.

The Internet segment's revenues spiked by 8% during fiscal 2012 driven by the search activities on the company's websites and mobile sites.

In 2012 the company acquired Chronoresto, a key player in ordering meals online and it has the number-two Internet portal in France in its field, with solutions for fixed Internet, mobile telephones, tablets, and TV, allowing a meal to be ordered from locally-listed restaurants, from among the 800 partnerships in France. With this acquisition the company accelerated its digital development strategy in the restaurant industry.
