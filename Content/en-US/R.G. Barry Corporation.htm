R.G. Barry would be perfectly happy if women and men would scuff around everywhere in their slippers. As the world's leading maker and marketer of comfort footwear and slippers, the company specializes in manufacturing soft slippers and casual footwear for men, women, and children. They're sold under such brands as Angel Treads, Dearfoams, EZfeet, My College Footwear, Terrasoles, Soluna, and Snug Treds, as well as licensed names, which account for most of its revenue. R.G. Barry sells its footwear through department stores nationwide. The company was acquired in mid-2015 by private-equity firms Mill Road Capital and Blackstone.

R.G. Barry is based in the Columbus, Ohio suburb of Pickerington. It has sales or administrative offices in New York; Dongguan City, China; Glen Rock, New Jersey; and Bentonville, Arizona. Its products are manufactured by a large network of contract manufacturers, primarily based in China, but also located in the US and Vietnam.

Its products are sold through national chains, mass merchants, warehouse clubs, mid-tier department stores, non-promotional independent and specialty retailers, boutiques, catalogs, home shopping networks, online retailers and its own websites at www.dearfoams.com, www.baggallini.com and www.footpetals.com. Major customers include Wal-Mart Stores , Kohl's, Costco , and J.C. Penney .
