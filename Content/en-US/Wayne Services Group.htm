Wayne is not a body builder, but it is pumped up about its products. A division of GE , Wayne (formerly Dresser Wayne) makes retail and commercial petroleum fuel dispensers (aka gas pumps), as well as vapor recovery systems that reduce emissions during fueling. In addition, it manufactures point of sale systems for gas station retailers. Its products are sold under the brand names Ovation, Global Century, Vista, and Global Star; Exxon Mobil and Chevron have been major customers. Wayne maintains significant operations in the US, Brazil, China, and Sweden and operates sales and service offices around the world. Founded in 1891, the company became a part of GE in 2011 after GE bought its former parent, Dresser .
