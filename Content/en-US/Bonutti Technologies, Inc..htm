Bonutti Technologies is on the lookout for the next big thing in medical devices. The company discovers, develops, and delivers innovative surgical and rehabilitation products. Its research and development programs focus on instruments, implants, and procedure technologies with applications in orthopedic surgery and rehabilitation therapy; successfully developed products are often spun off into independent companies. Other programs are conducted through licensing and partnership agreements with medical equipment firms such as Stryker . Bonutti Technologies markets a limited-incision total knee replacement called the L-I Approach. The company was founded by CEO Peter Bonutti.
