DeMet's Candy Company finds treasures in turtles and flips for sticks. The company manufactures four candy brands -- Flipz, Turtles, Treasures, and STIXX -- that it bought from Nestlé USA . Flipz are chocolate-covered pretzels; Turtles are chocolate-covered caramel and pecan candies; Treasures are filled chocolate squares; and STIXX are chocolate-covered cookie wafers. (Each candy is made with purchased Nestlé brand chocolate.) DeMet's is owned by Yildiz Holding, which is based in Istanbul, Turkey. It also owns the Godiva chocolate company.

DeMet's, which produces 81 million units a year, is expanding its manufacturing facility in Big Flats, New York. The first phase, which began work in 2015, is to add 20,000 square feet. That would increase production capacity 50%. The expansion also includes a research and development lab and processing lines. Also in 201t, the company introduced Turtles Dark Almond.
