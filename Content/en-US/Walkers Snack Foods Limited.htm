Walkers Snack Foods is always ready for a crisp walk down the snack aisle of the local food emporium. The company makes the UK's leading brand of snack food -- its namesake Walkers crisps (or potato chips as they are known in the US). Wotsits cheese snacks and Monster Munch (featuring Muppet-like creatures on the packaging) are some of the other brands in the company's portfolio. Walkers is owned by PepsiCo and overseen by its PepsiCo International division. The company also distributes the popular US snack, Doritos, owned by PepsiCo subsidiary Frito-Lay . The food company's Walkers brand is the UK market leader in crisps, followed by Pringles.
