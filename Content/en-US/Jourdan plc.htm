Jourdan believes in the importance of life's tiny details, so its group exists to explore a variety of small interests. Jourdan plc is a holding company with interests in both the consumer products and health care markets. Its core group of businesses includes Westfield Medical (single-use sterilization packaging material), and Clinipak (single-use sterilization products), which together account for the majority of its sales. More than 60% of Jourdan's sales originate in its home country of the UK. The company traces its historical roots to 1926.
