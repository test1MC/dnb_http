Bats in yer belfry? Nixalite of America can lend a hand. The company makes and supplies architectural bird, animal, and pest control products to customers throughout the world through its 24,000 sq. ft. manufacturing facility in East Moline, Illinois. Its humane products include wire netting, wire barriers, foggers, repellants, spikes, and noisemakers. Nixalite of America, which was established in 1950, is a third generation family-owned enterprise. Founder Charles Kaufmann started the business by patenting and manufacturing the first Nixalite bird control models during the1940s.
