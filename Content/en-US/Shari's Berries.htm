This company is for the berries, in a good way. Shari's Berries International makes and sells chocolate-dipped strawberries. The company's product line includes yogurt-dipped strawberries, and chocolate-dipped champagne bottles and drinking goblets. Its signature item is strawberry roses -- chocolate-dipped strawberries mounted on stems and sold by the dozen and half dozen. Shari's Berries confections are available in retail stores located in and around Sacramento, California, as well as through TV and catalog sales and on the company's Web site. Shari Fitzpatrick founded the company in 1989. In 2006 the company was sold to Provide Commerce .
