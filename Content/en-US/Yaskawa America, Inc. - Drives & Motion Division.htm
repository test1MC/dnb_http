Yaskawa America - Drives & Motion, a division of Yaskawa Electric , manufactures industrial automation equipment. Its products include industrial AC drives, commercial HVAC drives, spindle drives and motors, servo amplifier systems and motion controls, low-voltage industrial control switches, and medium-voltage switches, breakers, and contactors. The unit's products are used in automotive, building automation, chemical and petrochemical, food and beverage, machine tool, material handling, metal forming, packaging, pharmaceutical, plastics and rubber, and textile applications. Its Motoman subsidiary makes industrial robots that can weld, assemble, cut, and handle goods for manufacturers.

Yaskawa Electric America (YEA) serves customers throughout the Americas. Its divisions operate from facilities in Oak Creek, Wisconsin; Waukegan and Buffalo Grove, Illinois; Plain City, Ohio; and Cypress, California. It also has operations in Brazil and Canada.

YEA offers import and generational product support (system engineering and integration, upgrades, replacement parts, installation and startup, technical training and support, factory repair, and round-the-clock field service).

Yaskawa America markets its products through its own direct sales force, as well as via partners, representatives, dealers, and distributors.

In 2014 it introduced iQpump Micro intelligent pump drive to its portfolio, and has partnered with Valley Water Management (VWM) to provide iQpump1000 variable frequency drives for VWM pump stations.

Further boosting its portfolio of products, in 2014 Yaskawa America bought Massachusetts-based Solectria Renewables (a manufacturer of high performance photovoltaic inverters for the residential, commercial, and utility-scale power systems).

YEA was established in 1967. Parent Yaskawa Electric was formed in 1915.
