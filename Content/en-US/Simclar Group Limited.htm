Simclar Group was once one of Europe's leading contract manufacturers. Through facilities in the UK, China, Mexico, and the US, the company manufactured a variety of electronic products, including cables and harnesses, printed circuit boards, backplanes, and electromechanical assemblies. It also made injection molded plastics, office seating mechanisms, and sheet metal products. Simclar Group served blue-chip customers in the automotive, computer, communications, medical, defense, transit, and security industries. Its US-based Simclar, Inc. subsidiary primarily serves customers in the US and Mexico. Chairman Sam Russell started the company in 1975. Simclar Group went into administration in June 2011.
