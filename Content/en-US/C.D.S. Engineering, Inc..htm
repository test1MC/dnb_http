The CDS Group is a contract manufacturer of electromechanical assemblies, modules, systems, and components. The company also makes vacuum chambers, precision frames and cabinets, and semiconductor production equipment, as well as offers design, engineering, test, and supply chain management services. Its products are used in market applications including, aviation, biotech, threat and explosives detection, medical, pharmaceutical, and solar, among others. CDS Group was built up through a series of acquisitions, including those of CDS Engineering (1977), CMS Welding & Machining (1998), Rave Precision (1999), and ENCO Engineering (2001). CDS Group has manufacturing facilities in the US.
