Tamedia is a leading Swiss media company with operations in publishing, broadcasting, and digital media. It operates a portfolio of more than 20 daily and weekly newspapers, including Der Bund, Tages-Anzeiger , and popular commuter paper 20 Minuten . Its magazine holdings include special interest and niche titles such as Annabelle (women's magazine), Automobil Revue , and TVäglich . Tamedia also owns Edipresse , a leading European newspaper publisher serving French-speaking Switzerland with such titles as 24 heures, Le Temps , and Tribune de Genève . Members of the founding Coninx family control Tamedia.
