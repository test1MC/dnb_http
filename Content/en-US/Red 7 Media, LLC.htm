Consider Red 7 Media a magazine publisher for publishers and a trade show producer for trade show producers. Red 7 Media publishes magazines and newsletters and produces conferences and trade shows serving the event marketing, media, and publishing industries. The company's FOLIO provides targeted content, features, and services focused on the publishing industry;  other publications include Circulation Management , Event Marketer , Event Design , EXPO , and Agenda . The company also produces high level executive conferences Experiential Marketing Summit, The FOLIO Show, Agenda Xpo, and Circulation Management Expo. Media firm Access Intelligence owns Red 7 Media.
