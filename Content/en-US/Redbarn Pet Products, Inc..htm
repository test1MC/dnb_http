Whether it's an attempt to avoid a repeat of George Orwell's Animal Farm or not, it's clear that pet owners worldwide will go to great lengths to keep their beloved canines content. Redbarn Pet Products aims to help in that quest. The company manufactures and markets dog foods, chews, and treats in a variety of flavors. Products include natural bones and chews, chicken jerky, filled hooves and rawhide, Bully Sticks, pig ears and pork slices, and deli items. Redbarn has manufacturing centers in the US and China. Consumers can place their orders over the phone or through the company's website.
