General Dynamics UK sees to C4I for the British military and other clients. Operating primarily in the UK but also internationally, General Dynamics UK focuses on specialties that include C4I (command, control, communications, computers, and intelligence) technologies and Armored Fighting Vehicles (AFVs). The company's C4I projects include the Bowman system, which integrates digital voice and data technology for secure radio, telephone, intercom, and Internet communications. General Dynamics UK also provides systems integration for critical infrastructure facilities, such as WiFi networks.

General Dynamics UK is part of the Europe operations of its parent company. It delivers avionics equipment used in rotary and fixed wing platforms, highly integrated mission and video management systems, flexible stores management systems, data link processing and video, and data recorders for UK and international customers.

The company operates through two facilities in the UK.

General Dynamics UK's customer list includes government, military, and private companies around the world. The company targets the air, maritime, and land markets.
