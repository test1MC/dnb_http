What's going on in this burg? We're chillin'. It beats gellin'. BERG Chilling Systems designs, manufactures, installs, and services industrial refrigeration systems. The company's products include blast freezers, fluid pumping systems, ice makers, and cooling towers. It also makes filtration and chemical treatment systems. BERG's equipment is used for such applications as chemical treatment, food processing, and marine cooling.
