Swiss-based Nestlé headed west to seek a fortune in 1868. Its UK subsidiary generates billions in sales and rivals the best within the British food industry. Nestlé UK's confectionary business produces and sells the Kit Kat chocolate brand, one of the country's most popular sweets. The company operates nearly 20 sites that also manufacture beverages (including NESCAFÉ brand coffee), breakfast cereals, dairy products (canned milk, yogurt), processed meats, pasta, cheese, pet food, bottled water, and Jenny Craig weight management supplements. Nestlé UK estimates that 95% of UK households use its brands; all told, two billion products are purchased each year. The company also exports to more than 70 countries.
