Aspira Networks wants more people to skip the mall and shop online. The company's e-commerce platform is used by businesses that offer Wi-Fi (hotels, restaurants, coffee shops) to turn Web surfers into shoppers. Its networkÂ routes Internet users through Aspira's e-commerce environment and grants businesses a referral commission every time a shopper makes an online purchase. Online retailers generally provide incentives to drive traffic to their sites, and Aspira Networks cuts the commission with Wi-Fi network operators. Shoppers get a deal, too; Aspira offers a couple of sites, such as lilideals.com and rewardfinds.com, that offer cash back from more than 1,200 e-tailers. Aspira Networks was founded in 2009.
