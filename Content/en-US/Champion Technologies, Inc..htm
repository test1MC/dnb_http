Champion Technologies produces specialty chemicals and provides services that help oil and gas companies with productivity. Among its products are biocides to control bacteria, corrosion inhibitors, paraffin inhibitors, water clarifiers, and surfactants for cleaning. Its applications include keeping pipes clean, oil flowing, and liquids separated in support of production. It also provides engineering support for drillers in US shale plays. Champion was a subsidiary of Permian Mud Service. Seeking a deeper cash resource, in 2013 Permian Mud Service sold Champion Technologies and its related company Corsicana Technologies (collectively referred to as Champion Technologies) to Ecolab for $2.3 billion.

The company operates in more than 50 countries and its manufacturing and technology centers in North America (United States and Canada), South America (Argentina and Brazil), Australia, Netherlands, Nigeria, Russia, Singapore, and the UK.

Champion Technologies offers innovative and engineering, sustainable chemistry, technology, and other services for upstream and midstream oil and gas markets. Applications include flow assurance, integrity management, phase separation, scale control, shale oil and gas, and water products and services. It also makes and distributes frequency control devices for wireless and wireline communications networks (voltage-controlled crystal-oscillators).

The company serve companies in a number of oil and gas segments, including Arctic, Conventional, Deepwater/Offshore, Enhanced Oil Recovery, Heavy Oil, Industrial Water Treatment, Midstream, Oil Sands, Refinery, and Shale.

Growing its portfolio, in 2012 Champion Technologies teamed up with the Chemical EOR Alliance ( Rhodia /Beicip-Franlab/IFPEN) to market and deliver integrated chemical products and services for chemical enhanced oil recovery (reservoir engineering, chemical formulation, laboratory experiments, pilot design, and implementation) in Canada and the US.

In 2011 Champion Technologies broadened its offerings after acquiring Louisiana-based Chemtech Chemical Services, a water-treatment and engineering company that serves refineries.
