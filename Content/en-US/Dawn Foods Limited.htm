Just add egg and liquid, Dawn Foods does the rest. The UK-based manufacturer makes and distributes dry blends to prepare biscuits and scones, cakes, and muffins, as well as frozen dough for American-style cookies. Its other core products include flour, icings and glazes, and fruit fillings, and a hefty lineup of prepared frozen goodies, from cheesecake to brownies. Its consumer sweet baked goods are marketed under the name brand Weight Watchers . Dawn Foods caters primarily to the foodservice and retail markets: travel (air, rail, sea concessions), fast-food restaurants, coffee shops, and supermarket in-store bakeries. The company is part of the international business of American family-owned Dawn Food Products .

The company has its plants and sales offices in Belgium, France, Germany, Hungary, Netherlands, Portugal, Romania, and the UK.

Through a network of distributors, Dawn Foods serves retail bakers, food service companies, manufacturers, and in-store bakeries.

Under its US parent, Dawn Foods and its sister manufacturing site in The Netherlands have grown through new product development coupled with business acquisitions.

It also launched three versatile ready to use cream fillings ideal for a range of applications including layering, filling or topping patisserie, desserts, donuts, cakes, and muffins.

In late 2011 Dawn Foods launched a new luxury American-style ready-bake and frozen-puck cookie line filled with Belgian chocolate.

The cookie introduction followed Dawn Food Products' buy out of the Unifine Food & Bake Ingredients business from Dutch food ingredient giant Royal Cosun . Unifine produces and supplies pastry ingredients under brand names Sucrea, Fruibel, and Caullet. Its operations include production plants and sales offices in about a dozen European countries.
