Only the best oil piping will do for Premier Pipe, a distributor and manufacturer of oil country tubular goods. The company represents such clients as Sumitomo Metals, Tubular Corp of America, and Tenaris . It offers threading services through clients such as Grant Prideco and Tuboscope. Premier Pipe also offers transportation services through a recommended network of trucking companies. Through an arrangement with Tokyo-based parent Sumitomo Corporation , Premier Pipe distributes products in Europe, Asia, and the Middle East as well as in North and South America. The company has offices in Casper (Wyoming), Dallas, Denver, and Houston.
