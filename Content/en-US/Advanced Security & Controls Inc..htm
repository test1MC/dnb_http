You'll find no remedial level of security systems here. Advanced Security & Controls designs and installs a range of security systems and products, including burglar alarm, ID card and telephone entry, camera surveillance, closed circuit television (CCTV), fire alarm, gate entry, and monitoring systems. In addition to design and installation, it offers repairs and maintenance, as well as security assessment and consulting services. The company serves commercial and residential customers in the southern US.  President Al Aitken owns Advanced Security & Controls. He founded the company in 1984.
