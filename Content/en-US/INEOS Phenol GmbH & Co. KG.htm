INEOS Phenol has had phenomenal success producing phenol and acetone, two basic chemicals used in the manufacture of products ranging from aspirin to home appliances. A member of the INEOS Group of companies, INEOS Phenol is the world's largest phenol producer, ahead of the likes of Shell and Dow . Acetone is a by-product of the process that manufactures phenol, the oxidation of cumene. Other products include cumene and alphamethylstyrene, used as an additive in the manufacture of advanced plastic resins. INEOS Phenol maintains three manufacturing facilities in Europe (two in Germany, one in Belgium), two more in the US (Mobile and Port Arthur), and is jointly developing a plant in China with Sinopec .
