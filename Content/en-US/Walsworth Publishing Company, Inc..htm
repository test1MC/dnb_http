What's it worth to get your yearbook published? Walsworth Publishing knows. Best known for publishing high school yearbooks, Walsworth also provides commercial printing for specialty publications including textbooks, cookbooks, encyclopedias, and travel guides. The company has printing facilities in Missouri and Michigan. Walsworth's additional offerings include Online Design, a comprehensive Web-based yearbook creation tool, and ClassScene, a media-sharing website devoted specifically to school communities. Owned and led by the Walsworth family, the company was established in 1937 by three Walsworth brothers (Don, Ed, and Bill) to print playbills.
