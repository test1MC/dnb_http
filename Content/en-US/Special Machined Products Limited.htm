Special Machined Products Limited has a sharp eye on engineering matters. The company sub-contracts its engineering services manufacturing components and assemblies and provides machining, CAD/CAM, and dimensional reporting. Special Machined Products' steel group also sells nickel-based super alloys and other special steels, as well as providing specialized services such as annealing, hardening, stress relieving, and tempering. The company serves customers in the aerospace, automotive, marine, mining, and power generation industries, among others.
