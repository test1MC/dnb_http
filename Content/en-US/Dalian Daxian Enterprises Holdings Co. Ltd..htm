If not the mother of modern electronic processing, Dalian Daxian is its enterprising son. Operating through multiple subsidiaries and joint ventures, "Daxian Group" makes a slew of electronics, including digital video and audio equipment, parts and components, and a communications network lineup. The short list offers cell and video phones, plasma and digital HGTV sets, satellite set-top boxes, circuit boards, and car audio components. Its electrical components line comprises electron guns, insert bearings, breadboards, and metal parts. Nearly all products are exported to the US and Europe. Daxian Group also dabbles in real estate and various financial investments. It is majority owned by the Chinese state.
