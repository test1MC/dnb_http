Novelion Therapeutics (formerly QLT) is a biopharmaceutical firm with a focus on rare diseases. The company's commercial portfolio includes orphan drug Myalept, a treatment for people with generalized lipodystrophy (which causes a lack in the hormone leptin) and cholesterol-lowering medication Juxtapid. Both drugs were developed by subsidiary Aegerion Pharmaceuticals , which the company acquired in November 2016. Pipeline products under development include metreleptin, a synthetic analog of leptin for the treatment of diabetes and dyslipidemia, and zuretinol for the treatment of inherited retinal diseases. Upon the acquisition of Aegerion, QLT changed its name to Novelion Therapeutics.
