An expert in the tricks of the trade, Mercantile succeeds by importing and wholesaling an array of safety products. Operating through its MercaTurvanasi business segment, the company offers fire and occupational safety equipment, such as fire extinguishers and personal protective gear, including ear plugs, goggles, and ergonomic safety shoes. The lineup is available across Finland to industrial and commercial customers, and to consumers through its Turvanasi-retail chain. In 2008 the company's parent, Mercantile Group , sold Mercantile's MercaPartner business unit to BUFAB Group. Renamed Bufab Mercapartner Oy, the unit offered ordering and shelving services for fastening equipment and small parts.
