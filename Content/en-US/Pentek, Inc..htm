Pentek Pentek provides electronic assemblies and single-board computers for cellular phones and other wireless devices, control and imaging equipment, radar and sonar systems, satellite communications, and telecommunications, among other applications. The company's digital signal processing boards use processors manufactured by Freescale Semiconductor and Texas Instruments . Its software radio products include transceivers and digital receivers. The company was established in 1986. Pentek's ownership is evenly split between co-founders Daniel Shamah (president) and Rodger Hosking (VP).
