IDT International keeps it light. The company is a global leader in the manufacturing of consumer electronics featuring LCDs (liquid crystal displays). These include projection clocks, digital thermometers and other measuring devices, multifunction watches, heart rate monitors, and pedometers. It also makes electronic learning products (such as laptops and talking books) and telecommunications gadgets (cordless phones and two-way radios). Many of IDT's LCD and electronic learning products are produced by its Oregon Scientific subsidiary. Products are marketed throughout Europe, the US, and the Asia/Pacific region. Founder, chairman, and CEO Raymond Chan and his wife, Shirley, own a controlling interest in IDT.
