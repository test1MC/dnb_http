You may have never heard of viscose, but chances are you're sitting on it, looking at it, wearing it, or all of the above. And chances are Thai Rayon manufactured and distributed it. Thai Rayon is the sole producer of Viscose Rayon Staple Fiber (VSF) in Thailand, and the world's largest exporter. VSF is used to make apparel, such as shirts and intimates; home textiles, like bed linens and upholstery; and personal care products, including facial wipes, sanitary napkins, and diapers. It's also found in medical care products, like swabs and surgical gowns; home care products, such as kitchen wipes; and industrial products, including filters and nets. Thai Rayon is a member of the Aditya Birla Management Corp.
