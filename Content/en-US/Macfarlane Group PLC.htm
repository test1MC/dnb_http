Macfarlane Group gets all wrapped up in itself. The British package maker's largest business, packaging distribution, operates via more than a dozen regional centers, distributing a slew of packaging for consumer goods manufacturers in the food, pharmaceutical, and retail industries. (Allpoint Packaging, bolted on in 2008, has boosted Macfarlane's presence and efficiency.) The company's manufacturing operations produce protective packaging used by aerospace, automotive, and electronic OEMs. Manufacturing activities include producing self-adhesive and the Reseal-it brand of re-sealable labels for such image-conscious industries as food, toiletries, and household products in Europe and the US (through Printpack ).
