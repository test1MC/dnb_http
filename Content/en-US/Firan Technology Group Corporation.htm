Firan Technology Group (FTG) is all fired up about making electronic components for the aviation, defense, and high technology industries. The company's FTG Circuits unit makes complex printed circuit boards; the division also offers related manufacturing services. Meanwhile, the company makes luminescent cockpit display panels and keyboards through its FTG Aerospace division. About three-quarters of FTG's sales are made in the US. The company's customers include Boeing , Honeywell , and Rockwell Collins , as well as the US Army . It also serves big companies in the electronics manufacturing services industry, such as Celestica and Jabil Circuit . FTG has manufacturing operations in Canada and the US (California).
