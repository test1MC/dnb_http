If it doesn't clean or taste good, it doesn't cut the mustard with Reckitt Benckiser. A subsidiary of UK-based Reckitt Benckiser plc , the company makes and markets household, cleaning, and food products for sale in North America. Some of Reckitt Benckiser's household and cleaning products include Air Wick air fresheners, Electrasol dish detergent, Jet-Dry rinse agent, Lysol cleaners and disinfectants, Resolve cleaners, and Spray 'n Wash laundry stain removers. The US-based subsidiary serves North America through offices, manufacturing facilities, and logistics centers throughout the US and in Toronto, Canada.

Reckitt Benckiser's regional center is located in Parsippany, New Jersey, while its technical center is based in Montvale, New Jersey. The company, which serves North America boasts offices, manufacturing facilities, and logistics centers throughout the US and in Toronto, Canada.

During the past couple of years, the US subsidiary has seen gains in its household segment through increased sales in surface care (Lysol), automatic dishwashing (Electrasol 3-in-1 tablets), and home care (Air Wick Freshmatic and Air Wick Electrical Oils) products. Lysol disinfectant products got a sales boost from concern over the H1N1 virus.
