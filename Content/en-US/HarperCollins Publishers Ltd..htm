HarperCollins Publishers Ltd., doing business as HarperCollins UK, is one of the country's top publishers. The company publishes fiction, non-fiction, academic, and reference titles, as well as children's works (such as timeless classics like The Chronicles of Narnia and The Hobbit , and the popular Paddington Bear series). Its The Times Comprehensive Atlas of the World is an authoritative reference atlas. Popular authors include Bill Bryson ( Shakespeare ) and Frank McCourt ( 'Tis ). HarperCollins UK publishes more than 1,200 books a year and employs about 1,000 people in London, Glasgow, and Cheltenham. The company is a unit of News Corp. 's HarperCollins .
