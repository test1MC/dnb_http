Draka Holding made low-voltage and special-purpose cable and telecommunication cable and systems. Its Energy & Infrastructure division produced standard low and medium-voltage cable products for the construction, industrial, and infrastructure markets. Draka's Industry & Specialty unit specialized in a wide range of cable used in such applications as elevators, oil platforms, wind turbines, cruise ships, autos, and airplanes. Its Communications segment made copper and optical fiber telecommunication cable for broadband, mobile network, multimedia, and other telecom products. Prysmian , the world's #2 cable maker, acquired Draka in spring 2011.
