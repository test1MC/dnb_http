Hindustan Motors Limited (HML) pioneered India's automobile manufacturing. HML is the flagship company of the C.K. Birla Group -- it was established in 1942, five years before India's independence. In addition to cars (Ambassador), HML has manufactured trucks (Winner), multi-utility vehicles (Trekker, Porter, and Pushpak) and the RTV (a versatile minibus-ambulance-pickup model). HML partners with Mitsubishi Motors to manufacture the Pajero SUV. The company operates manufacturing facilities in Madhya Pradesh and West Bengal.
