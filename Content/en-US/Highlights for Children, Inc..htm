Need help finding the hidden picture? Highlights for Children publishes Highlights , a children's magazine with a circulation of more than two million. Highlights targets children ages six to 12. It is delivered to homes, schools, and libraries, and remains a staple of doctors' and dentists' office waiting rooms throughout the country. Highlights for Children also operates six book clubs, including Hidden Pictures Playground , Puzzlemania , and Mathmania , which send kids a new kit or pair of books in the mail every four to six weeks. The company also produces a line of games and puzzles. Highlights for Children was founded in 1946 by husband-and-wife team Dr. Garry Cleveland Myers and Caroline Clark Myers.
