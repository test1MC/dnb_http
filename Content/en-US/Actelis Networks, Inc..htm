Actelis Networks prefers copper over glass. The company's transmission equipment enables carriers and telecom providers to deliver Ethernet services over copper wires. Its products are intended to increase the data-carrying capacity of the copper lines and eliminate the need for installing fiber optics in the "last mile" between service providers' networks and their subscribers. Actelis targets regional telephone companies, independent and competitive local exchange carriers, and alternative carriers worldwide. Customers include more than 200 service providers around the world, such as Easynet and Frontier . Founded in 1998 by Tuvia Barlev, Actelis operates from its headquarters in California and a R&D center in Israel.
