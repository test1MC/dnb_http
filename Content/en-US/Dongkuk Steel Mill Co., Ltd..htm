Temper may spoil children and ruin adults, but it strengthens Dongkuk Steel Mill. The company is South Korea's third largest steel producer. As the country's first producer of heavy steel plates, Dongkuk operates multiple plants, churning out an annual production capacity of 3 million tons of crude steel. Its products include steel plates, H-sections, general sections, and steel bars, of which annually more than 4 million tons are produced for ship building, boilers and pressure vessels, welded structures, and concrete reinforcement. Since its founding in 1954, the company has grown into a formidable player in the international steel market, with subsidiaries and affiliates in the US, Japan, Brazil, and China.
