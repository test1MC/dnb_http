Lion Raisins isn't just lyin' around. The company keeps busy as one of California's largest raisin processors. It grows its own grapes and purchases even more from more than 600 other grape growers. Lion's products include seedless and organic raisins, sugar-coated raisins, raisin paste, and raisin juice concentrate. Lion Raisins sun-dries its grapes and then processes them at its facility in Selma, California. Raisins are packaged under the Lion brand or for private-label customers and are shipped to locations throughout the US, as well as to worldwide destinations. The company was founded in 1903 by Alex Lion and is still owned by the Lion family.

Lion Raisins maintains a 130,000-sq.-ft. facility in the city of Selma, California -- the raisin capital of the world. The company's namesake raisin products are shipped nationwide as well as internationally.

As part of its business, Lion Raisins has expanded its products portfolio to include a variety of marketable items. The company's products include Flame Raisins, sugar-coated raisins, Zante currants, Monukkas raisins, organically-grown natural seedless raisins, raisin juice concentrate, raisin paste, midget and mini-midget raisins, and select natural seedless raisins, among others.

The company processes, packages, and ships its products globally. Lion Raisins specializes in the dried-on-the-vine (DOV) production and processing method whereby black sun-dried Thompson Seedless raisins are laid on the ground to oxidize and bake in the direct sunlight. Lion Raisins is known for its lighter-colored DOV raisins, which incidentally grade higher by the US Department of Agriculture .
