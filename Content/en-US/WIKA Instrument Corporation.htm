The company makes electronic and mechanical pressure gauges, thermometers, sensors, transmitters, and other instrumentation, as well as diaphragm seals for HVAC and other systems. Its products are used in oil and gas, biodiesel, ethanol, water treatment, hydraulic and fluid power, and manufacturing applications. WIKA sells directly, through distributors, or via the company's website to more than 100 countries. The company is a fully owned subsidiary of Germany-based components manufacturer WIKA Alexander Wiegand , which has been in business for more than 60 years.
