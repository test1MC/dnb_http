Don't tell Vallourec & Mannesmann Tubes (V&M Tubes) to pipe down. The subsidiary of Vallourec S.A. , one of the world's top producers of steel tubing, manufactures seamless hot-rolled steel tubes and pipes used in the automotive, construction and civil engineering, mechanical engineering, and oil and gas industries. The company manufactures its seamless tube and pipe in Brazil, France, Germany, the US, and China. Established in 1997 as a joint venture of Vallourec and Mannesman , Vallourec took full ownership in 2005. V&M Tubes owns another Vallourec subsidiary, V & M Star , located in Pennsylvania.

The company is based in Houston, and has other offices in Plano, Texas; Cranberry, Pennsylvania; Mandeville, Louisiana; Youngstown, Ohio; and Muskogee, Oklahoma
