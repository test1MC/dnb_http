Weber Metals is forging its way through a variety of industries. The company, a unit of Germany-based metals fabricating company Otto Fuchs, manufactures and distributes aluminum and titanium forgings that are used by customers in the commercial aerospace, electronics, military aerospace, jet engine components. and semiconductor industries. The company offers processing services such as cutting, cleaning, and surface preparation; heat treating; penetrant inspection; and sonic testing. Edmond Weber founded Weber Metals in 1945; Otto Fuchs acquired the company in 1979.
