Surgical Innovations Group is savvy on minimally invasive surgeries. The company makes equipment including the YelloPort laparoscopic access system, the LogiRange and QuickRange laparoscopic instruments, and FastClamp, an instrument used in endoscopic procedures. The company sells its products through distributors, as well as via partnerships with original equipment manufacturers (OEMs), primarily in Europe. Though its minimally invasive surgical products represent its core business, Surgical Innovations also makes autologous blood transfusion equipment, which collects and re-infuses a patient's own blood during surgery, through its Haemocell subsidiary.
