Octane Fitness plans to run circles around the competition using an ellipse. The company makes elliptical trainers for use in homes and health clubs. Octane Fitness's elliptical machines exercise the upper and lower body in both forward and backward motions and come equipped with trademarked programming for timing and setting up workout routines. The company's machines are available from retailers and are sold to health clubs, including 24 Hour Fitness , Equinox, Gold's Gym , and the YMCA . They are also used by the Arizona Diamondbacks , the Cleveland Browns , the San Francisco Forty Niners , and a handful of college athletic programs. Executives Dennis Lee and Tim Porth founded Octane Fitness in 2001.
