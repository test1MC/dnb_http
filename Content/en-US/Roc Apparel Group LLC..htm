Roc Apparel Group spins some savvy sportswear. The apparel firm was founded by hip-hop artists and operates as Rocawear. It makes and licenses apparel, footwear, and accessories under several labels, including Signature Apparel, Gina Group, M. London, Endurance, Age Group, Topline, Two Feet Productions, and Colors in Optics. Products are sold online and in Macy's , as well as other US retailers and specialty chains. Rocawear, which caters to urban crowds, is steered by music mogul and creative director Shawn "Jay-Z" Carter. It has diversified, adding bling to its portfolio when it teamed with Tiffany . Rocawear now peddles Tired-brand luxury watches, jewelry, and accessories. Brand firm Iconix owns the company.
