At Kepak, the daily grind is the meat of its business. The Irish company processes beef, lamb, and convenience foods for the retail grocery, food manufacturing, and foodservice industries. It was the first to prepare ground beef patties for the UK fast-food industry. Handling 1.5 million ovines a year, it is now one of Europe's largest lamb providers. Kepak also has significant beef operations, processing some 300,000 head of cattle a year. Its meat brands include KK Beef Club, Donald Russell, and Kepak Gold; its convenience meal and snack brands include the Global Cuisine, Big Al's, Rustlers, Ugo's, and Speedy Snacks brands. Kepak's Agra Trading division exports meat products around the globe.
