BioSeek has the human body all mapped out. The company's BioMAP technology to help in the drug discovery process. It maintains collaborative agreements to help identify and test potential drug compounds for pharmaceutical manufacturers, including Roche , Merck Serono , and Takeda . Clients can either use its standard compound profiling services or use the BioMAP system to develop a custom assay for testing clinical stage compounds. It also collaborates with the EPA to test environmental compounds. The company was acquired by DiscoveRx, which develops, manufactures, and commercializes reagents and cell-based assay kits, in 2012.
