It's hardly outlandish for E3P Technologies (formerly Outland Technologies) to tout its pump designs. The company licenses its patented CvR technology to makers of industrial pumps and motors. The technology aims to improve the performance of such devices by allowing them to operate at higher speeds and higher power output levels. Applications include pumps used in oil and gas exploration; high-pressure and hydraulic pumps used in aerospace applications; watercraft propulsion systems; and industrial production systems for refrigeration, food processing, car manufacturing, and semiconductor manufacturing.
