Aeroflex Control Components (formerly Advanced Control Components) makes radio-frequency and microwave control components, including switches, digital attenuators, switch-based assemblies, and attenuators, for customers in the aerospace, commercial, instrumentation, and military markets. The company was founded in 1982. In 2008 EMRISE acquired Advanced Control Components for $18 million in cash. Two years later, debt-plagued EMRISE sold it to Aeroflex for $18.6 million and its name was changed to Aeroflex Control Components. The company now operates as part of its parent's Aeroflex Microelectronic Solutions segment.
