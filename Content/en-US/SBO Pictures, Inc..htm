Some would say SBO is in a wicked business. Doing business as Wicked Pictures, SBO Pictures is a producer of adult films and videos designed to appeal specifically to women and couples. The company sells its videos and DVDs direct to consumers through its Web site, as well as through other retail outlets. It also distributes content through cable and satellite pay-per-view channels. The company licenses the Wicked name for a product line, which includes erotic toys, cell phones, erotic action figures, and adult trading cards. The company's partnership with porn star Jenna Jameson in the early 1990s put both parties on the adult entertainment map. Wicked president Steve Orenstein founded SBO Pictures in 1993.
