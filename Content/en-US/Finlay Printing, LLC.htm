Finlay Printing has burned through a lot of ink in the past 130 years. One of the oldest commercial printers in Connecticut, the company serves clients primarily in the distribution, financial services, food and beverage, and insurance industries in its home state of Connecticut, as well as New England, New Jersey, and New York. The company offers prepress, sheet-fed printing, finishing, and fulfillment services.
