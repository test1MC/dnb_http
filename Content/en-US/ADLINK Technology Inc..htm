ADLINK Technology provides the nuts and bolts of industrial computing. The company makes embedded computing products used to build test and measurement, industrial automation, and process control applications. Its industrial computing products include CompactPCI systems and single-board computers. The company's test and measurement division provides data acquisition boards. Its industrial automation offerings include communications, motion control, and video compression cards. ADLINK sells its products worldwide and has operations in Asia and North America. With the 2008 acquisition of Ampro Computers , ADLINK began offering rugged and single-board rugged computers.
