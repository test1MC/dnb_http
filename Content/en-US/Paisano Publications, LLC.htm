If motorcycles and tattoos are your thing, Paisano Publications can hook you up. The company publishes about a dozen magazines, including motorcycle bible Easyriders , and biker magazines V-Twin and Biker . It also publishes custom truck magazine Tailgate and several tattoo magazines. Its In the Wind publishes photographs reflecting the biker lifestyle, sent in from readers. Paisano Publications also produces events such as Easyriders Bike Shows, Motorcycle Rodeos, and the V-Twin Expo Industry Trade Show, and sells casual clothing, accessories, and novelty items (lighters, ash trays, decals, and shot glasses). The company was originally founded in 1970.
