Enova Systems makes commercial digital power management systems for controlling and monitoring electric power in automobiles and stationary power generators. Products include hybrid-electric drive systems, electric drive motors, electric motor controllers, hybrid drive systems, battery care units, safety disconnect units, generator units, fuel cell management units, and fuel cell power conditioning units. The company counts EDO , First Auto Works of China, Ford Motor , Hyundai Motor , Navistar International , and Volvo/Mack among its customers. Enova gets more than half of its sales outside the US, primarily in China.
