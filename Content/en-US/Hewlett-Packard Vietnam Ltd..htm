Hewlett-Packard Vietnam is a sales, marketing, and customer service subsidiary of Palo Alto, California-based Hewlett-Packard (HP). The company sells products including personal computers, network storage devices, printing and imaging machinery, servers, and enterprise software. HP Vietnam also offers a full range of IT services such as consulting, maintenance, systems integration, and technical support. The company serves consumers, small businesses, and corporate customers from two offices in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City. Goods are distributed through four distributors and 300 retailers; services are administered by 20 affiliated service providers.
