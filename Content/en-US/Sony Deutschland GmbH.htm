Sony Deutschland (also known as Sony Germany) is the German arm of consumer electronics giant Sony Corporation and a primary subsidiary of Sony Europe. The company, which was founded in 1970, sells professional and consumer audio, video, communications, and computer products, along with accessories such as batteries, displays, and digital camera sensors and modules. Sony Deutschland has struggled with sluggish sales and growing losses in recent years. With Germany considered to be one of Sony's most important European markets, Sony has significantly expanded its advertising budget for Sony Deutschland.
