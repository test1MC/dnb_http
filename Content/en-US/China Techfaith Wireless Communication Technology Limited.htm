China Techfaith Wireless Communication Technology has faith in the future of  mobile phones and online games. The company specializes in the development and production of mobile handsets, including multimedia phones, smartphones, pocket PC phones, and gaming terminals that also function as phones. China Techfaith creates both CDMA- and GSM-based mobile handsets. In addition to its own designs, the company sells completed handsets manufactured by third-party contractors. China Techfaith also develops mobile and online gaming content through its 798 Entertainment subsidiary.
