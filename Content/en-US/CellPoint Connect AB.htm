If you can walk and listen at the same time, CellPoint Connect probably has a device for you. The company manufactures and sells mobile headsets for use with cell phones, computers, and music players. Its branded Flamingo product line includes stereo headsets for use with MP3 players, iPods, iPhones, game devices, and cell phones; it also makes wireless Bluetooth-enabled devices. In addition, the company manufactures MySound, a hearing amplifier marketed to hearing impaired consumers. CellPoint sells its products in Europe, Asia, North America, and Latin America.
