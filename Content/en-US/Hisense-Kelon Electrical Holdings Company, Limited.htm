Hisense-Kelon is giving refrigerator giants like GE , Haier and LG a run for their money. Established as Kelon in 1984 and acquired by Hisense in 2005, Hisense-Kelon is China's largest manufacturer of refrigerators. The company also builds residential air conditioners, freezers, and a variety of small home appliances. Kelon markets its products under the brand names Kelon and Ronshen (refrigerators), Kelon and Hisense (air conditioners), and Kelon freezers. About 70% of its total sales are generated from China; the rest are from other countries.
