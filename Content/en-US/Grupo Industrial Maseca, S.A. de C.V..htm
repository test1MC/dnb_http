Grupo Industrial Maseca (GIMSA) has its job cut out for it -- providing the main ingredient for food in the round. The company is the largest producer of corn flour in Mexico (18 plants producing a total of 2.3 million tons per year). It supplies homemakers and industrial tortilla manufacturers in that country with some 40 different varieties of MASECA-branded corn flour. (Tortillas on the menu? Add water to MASECA, mix, shape, and cook.) Offering corn flour is in contrast to some other competitors that make and sell wet corn dough, which has a more limited shelf-life than packaged flour. GIMSA is owned by GRUMA , the world's #1 corn flour producer and tortilla manufacturer.
