Aqua Lung breathes life into water trekking. It makes scuba diving and snorkeling gear and related accessories for recreational, technical, and military customers. The company's name was established more than 60 years ago when Jacques-Yves Cousteau and Emille Gagnan created the first aqua-lung. Aqua Lung sells its products -- such as regulators, masks, fins, snorkels, and wetsuits -- through selected dive shops and the firm's catalog. It sells and distributes a variety of brands, including Aqua Flex, Aqua Lung, Apeks, Impulse, SeaQuest, and Tyke. The company and its sister company Aqua Lung International sell products in more than 30 countries. In 2016 Air Liquide sold the business to Montagu Private Equity .

Based in California, Aqua Lung operates across America in Hawaii, California, Florida, and Illinois, as well as in the Cayman Islands and in the Asia Pacific region.

Aqua Lung operates as a division of Aqua Lung International, which has operations in Europe, the Middle East, and Africa and distributes its products in nearly 95 countries.

Subsidiary company Aerial is a US-based contract manufacturer of equipment for life support, military, SAR, and other applications.

Aqua Lung maintains a bulging portfolio of brands, such as SeaQuest, Apeks, Aqua Lung, Aqua Sphere, and Stohlquist, among others. It's a multichannel marketer, selling its products to municipal and state governments, international military commands, the US Department of Defense , and others.

To give it an extended reach, Aqua Lung sells its products worldwide through distributors, such as Pino Sub S.A., Aqua Windies, Amerimex Intl. Co. Inc., Technic Plongee Pro, and La Casa Del Buceador.

Aqua Lung is known for its diving instruments, diving boots and gloves, sport diving equipment, and other gear. The company specializes in providing life support products and services for the US Department of Defense , state and municipal governments, and many international military commands in South America and the Asia Pacific region. It also maintains partnership with the likes of Worldwide Dive and Sail, Stuart Cove's, Aggressor Fleet, and Dancer Fleet.

To broaden its customer base, Aqua Lung in 2013 launched a line for women designed by women that works to meet the needs of female divers.

It boasts a state-of-the art clean room, non-magnetic testing equipment, and computerized breathing simulator machine that are deemed tops in the diving industry.
