Everel Group (formerly Vemer Siber) keeps current with its products. Through its two divisions -- automotive and large and small appliances -- Everel makes digital thermometers, energy meters, gas detectors, electromechanical time switches, modular transformers, signal lights, and thermostats, among other products. Everel sells its products worldwide under brand names including Dreefs, Giem, Molveno, and Signal Lux. Italian holding company Hopa S.p.A. (headed by businessman Emilio Gnutti) owns over 50% of Everel Group, directly and through GPP International. The company was formed from the 2000 merger of Vemer Elettronica and Siber (an Italian maker of appliance parts).
