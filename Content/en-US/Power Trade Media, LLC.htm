For the people who manage public transportation systems and administer churches, there's the trade magazine publisher Power Trade Media. The company's Bus Ride targets the bus, transit, and motor coach industry with profiles of bus companies, transit systems and manufacturers; reports on equipment; technical information; and coverage of industry people, meetings, forums, and trade shows. Power Trade's Church Executive is circulated to 25,000 administrators and executive pastors at churches with 1,000 or more members.
