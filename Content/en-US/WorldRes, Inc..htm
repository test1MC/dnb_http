WorldRes wants to connect travelers with a warm place to lay their weary heads. As the Internet platform of the American Hotel & Lodging Association, WorldRes allows travel Web sites to offer reservations to their users without actually putting together the information or the technology themselves. Travelers can book reservations at more than 40,000 hotel and resort properties including Marriott, Accor, and Hilton. Hotels can update their reservation information and provide content such as photographs and descriptions of their properties. WorldRes has offices in Dublin and Düsseldorf, Germany and in the US in San Mateo, California. The company is owned by online travel-planning company, TravelHero.
