If you have king-size cargo to haul, Load King (formerly Manitex Load King) has the right equipment for you. The company produces custom material handling and construction trailers, including bottom dumpers (touting the largest lower hopper opening in the industry) for transporting paving materials, and a host of goosenecks (folding tail, rail, walking beam suspension, and tag) for moving utility, heavy construction and maintenance equipment, as well as for use by the US military. Its trailers are distributed through regional dealers. The company began in 1956 and merged with Terex subsidiary Genie Industries in 2001; it was acquired by Manitex in 2010 and then by Utility One Source in late 2015.
