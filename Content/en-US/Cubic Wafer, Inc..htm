Wafers, especially most silicon wafers, are generally round. Cubic Wafer has some new angles. The company, founded in 2000 and formerly known as Xanoptix and Xan3D Technologies, previously made the XTM line of optical transmission components and is now focusing on 3-D packaging technology. Its products combine lasers, detectors, and integrated circuits into a single device to speed fiber-optic transmission in telecom, data communications, corporate networking, and data storage applications. Cubic Wafer's components include transmitters, receivers, and transceivers designed to be integrated into devices, such as network interface cards, made by other equipment makers.
