Paris is for (paper) lovers. The US paper maker once cranked out continuous forms for dot matrix printers. Paris, a holding company with subsidiaries, now produces jumbo paper rolls for commercial printers, custom business forms, and checks, distributed nationally through paper merchants and wholesale suppliers. It also offers services, such as product fulfillment, warehousing, and logistical support. The company's consumer products, found in some 50,000 retail locations, include professional paper, expanding files, labels and envelopes, and office cord and cable products, made under licensed brands Docit, Printworks, Dotz, and HP . Started in 1964, Paris is owned and led by its co-founder, the Toscani family.
