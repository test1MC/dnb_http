When it comes to factory automation, sensors provide the sweet cadence for Keyence. Established in 1974, Keyence manufactures a slew of sensors, machine vision and measurement systems, barcode readers, microscopes, and laser markers. Its short list includes photoelectric sensors, pressure sensors, and ultrasonic sensors. A line for vision measurement offers optical micrometers, digital microscopes, and other products. Keyence also provides technical support and training to customers. Along with an online retail site, the company's global offices serve more than 200,000 customers worldwide; it generates most of its sales domestically.

The company serves clients in 100 countries. It operates warehouses in Asia (Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, India, Thailand, China, Taiwan, South Korea), Europe (the UK, Germany, France, Belgium, Italy), and North and South America (the US, Canada, Mexico, Brazil). Japan accounts for 49% of its sales, while the US generates 10%.
