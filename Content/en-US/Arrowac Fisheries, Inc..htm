Arrowac Fisheries uses more advanced techniques than spear-fishing to bring in its catch. The company harvests, buys, processes, and markets fresh and frozen seafood products under the Arrow, Merco, and Ocean Dawn brands. Products include herring, salmon, sardines, sea bass, flounder, crab, shrimp, and shark meat. Its Merco Intertrade division provides international marketing services. Arrowac's fishing and processing operations are located along the Pacific coasts of Alaska, Canada, and the Pacific Northwest. The company is owned by president Frank Mercker, who founded Arrowac in the 1970s.
