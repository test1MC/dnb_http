An integrated aluminum producer, Aluminium of Greece mines bauxite, produces alumina, and makes aluminum created through electrolysis. The company's finished aluminum is marketed to automobile manufacturers and manufacturers of building materials and packaging. Aluminium of Greece's Delphes & Distomon subsidiary operates underground mines to extract bauxite used by its parent for aluminum production.The company's production capacity is about 800,000 tons of alumina and 150,000 tons of aluminum products annually. Greek industrial firm the Mytilineos Group owns Aluminium of Greece.
