La Tempesta Bakery Confections works in a storm of flour and sugar. The confections bakery manufactures cookies and candy, specializing in the Italian treat we've all come to love with our coffee -- biscotti. La Tempesta also makes cookies and chocolate-covered caramel clusters. Its products are distributed nationally through specialty-food and gift shops, coffee houses, cafés, and upscale grocery stores, including Whole Foods , Balducci's , and Dean and DeLuca . La Tempesta was founded by Bonnie Tempesta and Aurora Marcheschi in 1983. Now a subsidiary of Horizon Food Group , its sister subsidiaries include Ne-Mo's Bakery and Horizon Snack Foods.
