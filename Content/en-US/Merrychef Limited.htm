A happy chef is one with a Merrychef doing the cooking for her. Since 1950 Merrychef has been making what it calls "accelerated cooking systems," by which it means convection, microwave, and combination ovens. Its products are intended for the professional kitchens, be they for restaurants, hospitals, schools, hotels, or catering. Merrychef was a subsidiary of Enodis , which was acquired in 2008 by Manitowoc . Merrychef maintains operations in the UK and the US. The company also services its equipment worldwide, offering its own technicians and, in some regions, those of other affiliated subsidiaries, like Garland.
