Cascades Boxboard (formerly Paperboard Industries) puts its paper where its name is. Touted as Canada's #1 coated and recycled boxboard producer, the company makes boxboard, folding cartons, and other paper packaging used in manufacturing and retailing various goods. Three divisions drive Cascades Boxboard's manufacturing clout. A folding cartons segment pushes a diversity of options for restaurant take-out, cold and dry foods, household goods, as well as specialty packaging. Mills, three in Canada, one in the US, churn out coated, uncoated, and recycled fiber varieties. Catering and array of foodservice packaging, the Dopaco business rounds out the list. Cascades Boxboard operates as a subsidiary of Cascades .
