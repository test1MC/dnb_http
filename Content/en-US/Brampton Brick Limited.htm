Brampton Brick has built an empire out of clay. One of Canada's largest clay brick manufacturers, the company's masonry products are used in residential construction and commercial building projects. Its Oaks Concrete Products segment makes landscape materials, such as concrete paving stones and retaining walls, and includes Da Vinci Stone Craft, which specializes in limestone cast fireplace surrounds. Products are sold to landscape contractors, a network of dealers and distributors, and to do-it-yourself customers. Other business operations consist of a 50% joint venture interest in Universal Resource Recovery, a waste composting and material recycling facility, and property held for sale in Quebec.
