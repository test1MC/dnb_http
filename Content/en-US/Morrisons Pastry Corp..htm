Was your grandpa a pastry king, a real original? At Morrisons Pastry, grandpa is both. The New York company manufactures yogurt muffins, mini-bundt cakes, and loaf cakes under the Grandpa's Original brand name. Its flagship muffins come in more than 20 different flavors, including Blueberry Crumb, Chocolate Chocolate Chip, and Sin-A-Muffin. Morrisons' baked goods are sold throughout the US through distributors hired by the company. Morrisons Pastry was founded in 1953 by Philip and Morris Wattenberg and is owned and operated by their descendants.
