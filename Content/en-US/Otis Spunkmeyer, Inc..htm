Otis Spunkmeyer puts the spunk back in languishing cookie-lovers. As the nation's #1 manufacturer of frozen cookie dough, it makes prebaked and packaged cookies, as well as sweet breads, coffee cakes, muffins, bagels, pastries, and brownies. Otis Spunkmeyer serves nearly 60 countries, delivering its products to convenience stores, supermarkets, restaurants, and foodservice distributors throughout the US, Canada, Mexico, and Europe. It sells its cookie products to colleges and universities, drug stores, fundraising programs, and healthcare facilities, as well as mass merchandisers, the military, school cafeterias, and vending-machine operators. Otis Spunkmeyer is owned by European specialty bakery firm ARYZTA .

Otis Spunkmeyer sells its products worldwide, providing its sweet baked goods to consumers in North America and Europe.

Otis Spunkmeyer serves several sectors, such as convenience stores, the military, colleges, supermarkets, restaurants, and foodservice distributors. It also markets its products through fundraising.

It has a mixture of business-to-business and consumer brands, including Otis Spunkmeyer, La Brea Bakery, Fresh Start Bakeries, Maidstone Bakeries, and Great Kitchens. It boasts a diversified customer base within the foodservice and retail sales channels.

The food company has achieved the designation as the nation's top frozen cookie dough maker, thanks to its focused foodservice operation and its fundraising efforts. Named Otis Easy Direct-to-You, the company's fundraising program offers schools, youth and religious organizations, and athletic groups the opportunity to sell frozen cookie dough and keep the change to fund their group's efforts.

Otis Spunkmeyer boasts a handful of manufacturing sites in California, Pennsylvania, South Carolina, and Texas, and operates some 50 sales centers throughout the country.

Otis Spunkmeyer's growth strategy includes expanding and securing sales in the lucrative foodservice sector, extending its reach in the chain restaurant business, increasing its presence in the retail sector and international markets, and developing new products.

Parent company ARYZTA has been making aggressive acquisitions to expand its holdings in the Americas. To this end, Otis Spunkmeyer's business has blossomed. ARYZTA added Fresh Start Bakeries (FSB), a specialty bakery products provider, for $900 million and Great Kitchens (pizza and appetizers) for more than $1 billion. Expanding the Otis Spunkmeyer operation, ARYZTA in late 2010 rolled FSB's Pennant Foods and Sweet Life units into Otis Spunkmeyer to consolidate these complementary businesses. The two FSB operations produce frozen bread dough and specialty bakery products sold to restaurant chains, retail, and foodservice.
