Pure Technologies helps ensure that bridges, pipelines, parking structures, and nuclear structures remain sound. The company's SoundPrint acoustic monitoring system bounces sound patterns through a structure and collects them for analysis to determine possible weaknesses. The system's sensors can detect defects and damage in concrete, pipe, and other materials. Its P-Wave system monitors water and wastewater lines using electromagnetic waves. Pure also provides engineering and remote monitoring services. The company sells in North America, Europe, Mexico, and the Middle East, through branch offices and regional distributors. Key investors include Canaccord Genuity , Cormark Securities, and Fraser Mackenzie.
