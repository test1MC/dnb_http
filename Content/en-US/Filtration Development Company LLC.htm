Helicopters are notorious for kicking up dust and debris. That dust and debris, in turn, is notorious for fouling helicopter engines. Filtration Development Company, which does business as FDC/aerofilter, can't do much about the kicking-up-dust part, but the company does make filters that keep helicopter turbine engines from getting clogged and damaged, thus lowering maintenance and repair costs and decreasing downtime. The company's systems are used on Bell , Eurocopter , and Sikorsky models.
