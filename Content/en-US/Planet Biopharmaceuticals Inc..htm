Planet Biopharmaceuticals (formerly Planet Technologies) wants to make it easier to avoid the irritating things in life. The company markets and sells a wide range of products for allergy sufferers, including its own Allergy Control branded bedding and Allergy Free branded air filters. It also markets third-party allergen-reducing products, such as air cleaners and dehumidifiers, vacuums, and carpet cleaning products made by the likes of Miele and Whirlpool . The company sells its products directly to consumers through its allergycontrol.com Web site. To complement its avoidance products, the company also owns the Antigen Laboratories line of allergenic extracts used by physicians for allergy shots.
