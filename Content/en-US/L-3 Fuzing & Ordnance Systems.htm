L-3 Fuzing & Ordnance Systems (formerly BT Fuze Products) ensures that shells, bombs, and missiles blow up. . .but only if and when they are supposed to. The days of lighting fuzes by hand and watching them burn down with growing drama are long gone; today's electronic, mechanical, and electro-mechanical fuzes are highly engineered products used to detonate tank, artillery, bombs, and mortar shells with precision timing. L-3 Fuzing & Ordnance Systems (FOS) also makes arming and safe devices for artillery; canister munitions; rockets; and air, ground, and anti-ship missiles. FOS, which traces its roots to the Bulova Watch Company, is a subsidiary of L-3 Communications .
