Rover loves Exopack. His dog food often shows up in one of its bags. Exopack manufactures a slew of flexible packaging, film, and paper products for over 1,300 customers that market pet food, as well as consumer food and beverages, and lawn and garden, cement, agricultural staples, and more. Its core products are plastic films, such as laminated rollstock, converted plastics (standup pouches and edge seam bags), consumer paper for popcorn, coffee, and pet food bags, and coated products for printing, electronic, medical, or security uses. The company's facilities dot North America and the UK. Exopack and sister subsidiaries, Cello-Foil and The Packaging Group, are owned by an affiliate of Sun Capital .
