Running Press Book Publishers publishes books covering a variety of categories, including general non-fiction, science, history, children's fiction and non-fiction, food and wine, pop culture, and illustrated gift books. The company publishes some 200 new titles a year through four imprints: Running Press, Running Press Miniature Editions, Running Press Kids, and Courage Books. Featured series include the Skinny Bitch diet books ( Skinny Bitch in the Kitch ) and Doodles! (such as Oodles of Doodles ). The company also has a licensed publishing business that sells classic Peanuts books ( A Charlie Brown Christmas , It's the Great Pumpkin, Charlie Brown! ). Running Press is a unit of the Perseus Books Group .
