Craig & Derricott is all about control. The specialist manufacturing company makes products such as isolators, rotary switches, push-buttons, control and push-button assemblies, foot switches, and safety and limit switches. It also provides manufacturing, design, and reengineering services, primarily for the rail, marine, transportation, and utility markets. Customers have included such noteworth names as Metronet Rail , the BBC , British Gas , Cadbury Schweppes , Rolls Royce , and Caterpillar . Craig & Derricott traces its historical roots back to 1944.

Craig & Derricott has a range of products designed specifically for control panel and switchboard construction.

The company has operations in the UK, Republic of Ireland, France, Netherlands, Belgium, Norway, Poland, Hong Kong, China, UAE, Qatar, Oman, Pakistan, ZA (Republic of South), Malta, and Jordan.

The company markets its products in markets such as panel builder, electrical contractor, distributor, original equipment manufacturer, consultant, hazardous areas (atex), rail infrastructure and rolling stock, and the Ministry of Defence and military organizations. Customers have included such big names as Metronet Rail , the BBC , British Gas, Cadbury Schweppes , Rolls Royce , and Caterpillar .

In 2015 Craig & Derricott further extended its range of London Underground Limited / Transport for London -approved switchgear.

Founded in 1944 to make contactor starter assemblies, Craig & Derricott made its name in the 1950s with the Diacam 30-amp rotary switch.
