Plasti-Fab isn't a character from an Austin Powers flick, but rather a brand of foam building material made by PFB Corporation. Through its Plasti-Fab Ltd. subsidiary, PFB makes expanded polystyrene resin (EPR) products for use as insulation in ceilings, walls, and floors; as flotation components in docks; as packaging material; and as road fill. Plasti-Fab operates eight manufacturing plants, including a polymer plant in Alberta that producesÂ EPR for internal production. The company distributes its products to clients that include contractors, manufacturers, building product distributors, retail chains, and government agencies throughout Canada and the US. PFB bought US-based Precision Craft Homes in 2011.
