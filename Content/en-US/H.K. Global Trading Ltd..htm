It might not be the Inspector's Go Go Gadget , but H.K. Global Trading's Gotech and GPro brands are on the up and up. The company distributes audio and video electronics, including TVs, DVD and CD players, karaoke systems, and clock radios, under the Gotech and GPro labels. Established in 1977 as a foreign exchange and electronic sales firm, H.K. Global Trading distributed big brands, such as Sharp , Panasonic , and Toshiba , for several years until it developed and fine-tuned its own brand. The founding Khaledi family owns the company.
