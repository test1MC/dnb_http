Pland makes and distributes stainless-steel equipment and other items, primarily for construction contractors. Headquartered in the UK, the company makes its products at two manufacturing sites, one in Leeds and one in Glasgow, but distributes its products worldwide. In addition to washbasins, toilets, and taps, the company also makes stainless-steel equipment for caterers, laboratories, veterinarians, and janitors. Specializing in durable products, it also makes stainless-steel washroom equipment and sanitary ware for use in prisons. Pland was established in 1919 as Taylor Rustless Fittings; over the years it has been known as The Stainless Steel Sink Company and TRF Pland.

Headquartered in the UK, the company distributes its products worldwide. It has its manufacturing sites in Leeds and Glasgow.

In addition to its standard product lines of sinks and fixtures the company offers custom manufacturing services for clients in a variety of industries. With expertise crafting stainless steel, the company has made decorative signage along with crafting furniture, baths, and basins for designers and contractors.
