Mobile Climate Control Industries (MCC) makes heating, ventilating, and air conditioning systems (HVAC) and other equipment for on-road and off-road vehicles, such as buses and shuttles, ambulances and fire trucks, and military tanks. The company has manufacturing operations in Canada, Germany, Poland, Sweden, and the US. Its products are distributed worldwide, with approximately 60% of sales in North America. Major customers include Bobcat , John Deere , Hitachi , Komatsu , Renault , and Volvo . Expanding its North American footprint in 2011, MCC acquired Carrier 's bus air conditioning business for $32 million. MCC is owned by Ratos AB, a Swedish private equity.
