MetoKote wants its many customers to say "protective coatings -- me too." The manufacturer provides protective coating applications such as electrocoating, powder coating, and liquid painting. Its equipment division manufactures process lines and support equipment like building racks, carriers, shipping pallets, and part containers. MetoKote services customers primarily in the automotive, agricultural and construction equipment, and appliance and furniture manufacturing industries. It produces and sells more than 70 coating systems. Other services include labeling, specialty packaging, and sub-assembly. In 2016 Platinum Equity signed a definitive agreement to sell MetoKote to PPG .

The company has 30 manufacturing locations mainly in the US, but also in Brazil, Canada, Germany, Mexico, and the UK.

The company's wide range of high technology custom coating services includes electrocoating, powder coating, liquid paint, and other custom coatings. It also has metal finishing and value-added services (labels and packaging) divisions, and an in-house equipment unit which offers custom services to its clients. The MetoKote Equipment Division designs, builds and installs the majority of MetoKote's coating production and support equipment including Poka-Yoke (mistake proof) devices, equipment retrofitting, line conversions, racks, shipping pallets, and containers.

MetoKote serves more than 800 customers worldwide in a range of markets, including agriculture, appliance, automotive, computer, construction equipment, electrical, furniture, industrial equipment, recreational, and truck and bus.

Expanding its product offerings, in 2014 the company opened its newest facility in Mebane, North Carolina.

MetoKote has also upgraded its wide area network connections at its 30 production facilities that year, and (for security purposes) installed a fully functional remote site that duplicates all its critical business systems.

In 2015 the company bought Malcolm Enamellers ACP Limited, a leading coating company in the Greater Birmingham (UK) area.

Boosting its industrial services portfolio, in 2013 Platinum Equity acquired MetoKote from CCMP Capital . MetoKote was looking to take advantage of Platinum Equity's international reach and experience working with global companies.

In 2013 the company added a new powder coat system at its Peru, Illinois facility.

Growing its position in the US automotive market, in 2011 the company secured major electrocoating services contracts with Toyota and Volkswagen .

In 2010 MetoKote expanded into Central Europe with a regional coating center near Mannheim, Germany. The facility is designed to supply electro-deposition coating on tractor components for Germany's leading manufacturer of agricultural equipment, John Deere . The company's 124,000 sq. ft. site features an advanced e-coat operation, capable of coating multiple substrates including steel, aluminum, and iron castings. Though the facility began operations primarily in support of John Deere, MetoKote plans to supply electrocoating services to other customers in the region.

That same year, MetoKote moved to expand its Asia operations through a consulting agreement with Foxmark International of Tokyo. The venture is working to expand MetoKote's coating services with Asia/Pacific businesses. Foxmark supports foreign market entry and expansion in the region.
