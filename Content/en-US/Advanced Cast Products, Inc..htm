Advanced Cast Products is not a challenging series of performances from a drama class, but it is a class act when it comes to iron products.Â The subsidiary of Neenah Foundry makes molds and assembles ductile iron products at its single foundry and production facility. The company makes ductile iron, austempered ductile iron, finished, machined, painted, and assembled cast products. Its 15,000-sq.-ft. machining facility houses state-of-the-art CNC machining centers, including double spindled lathes, horizontal machining centers, vertical machining centers, and custom-built lathes. The company filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection in February 2010 and emerged later that year along with parent Neenah Foundry.
