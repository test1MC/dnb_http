LISI Group helps all kinds of things keep it together. LISI (meaning "Link Solutions for Industry"), formerly known as GFI Industries, makes fasteners used in a wide range of industries. Its automotive, aerospace, medical, and cosmetics segments make products that include fastening systems for airframes and aircraft engines, solid rivets, wheel screws and nuts, engine fasteners, and ball joints and clamp washers, as well as plastic packaging and aluminum enclosures for the cosmetics industry, and implants for the medical industry. Among its clients are Boeing , Renault , and Proctor & Gamble . LISI's product reach is global, although Europe accounts for more than 70% of its sales.
