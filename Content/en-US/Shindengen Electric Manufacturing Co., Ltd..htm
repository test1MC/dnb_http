Though you may not recognize it, Shindengen Electric Manufacturing has a powerful presence. The company makes a variety of power management products -- including DC/DC converter modules, diodes, rectifiers, solenoids, and varistors -- used in automobiles, construction equipment, consumer electronics, industrial machinery, networking equipment, and terrestrial broadcasting systems. Shindengen sells primarily to OEM customers, including BMW , Fujitsu , Hitachi , IBM Japan , Kawasaki Heavy Industries , Nokia , NTT , Panasonic , SANYO Electric , Sony , Tokyo Electric Power , and Toshiba . Most of Shindengen's sales are made in Japan.
