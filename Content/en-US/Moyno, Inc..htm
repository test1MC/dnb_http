Messing with slush and slurry is a dirty business, but Moyno says, "Yes!" to the challenge. The company manufactures cavity pumps, grinders, sanitary pumps, sludge pumps, metering pumps, and fluid transfer systems used in shear-sensitive fluids, solid-laden slurring and sludge, and water-like liquids. Under the Moyno Ultra® Technologies brand, products are marketed to the environmental remediation, specialty chemical, building materials, food and beverage, petroleum, and mining industries. The company, a subsidiary of Robbins & Myers , maintains the largest in-field inventory of progressing cavity pumps in North America. A network of distributors also offers a slate of maintenance and repair services.
