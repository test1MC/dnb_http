In the world of semiconductors, ASM Pacific Technology (ASMPT) rules; it is the #1 assembly and packaging equipment supplier. The company makes and markets equipment and lead frames used in microelectronics, semiconductor, photonics, and optoelectronics industries. Its equipment division offers an array of die-handling equipment and die-bonders for applications such as discrete, power, system in package, and fine pitch bonding. Back-end tools range from encapsulation systems to test handlers. Lead frame services ply micro etching treatment to selective plating. ASMPT operates in China, Hong Kong, Malaysia, and Singapore, and serves a mainly Asian market. ASM International formed ASMPT, and owns more than 50%.
