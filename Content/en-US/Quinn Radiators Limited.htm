If you can stand the heat, go see Quinn Radiators. The company manufactures compact, panel, and towel radiators, as well as low surface temperature radiator ranges (commonly used in nurseries, hospitals, and schools) for residential and commercial use. It markets its products under the Quinn Round Top, Slieve, and Adagio brands, among others. The firm has production facilities in the UK and distribution centers in the UK, Ireland, and Belgium. Quinn Radiators is a subsidiary of industrial products and equipment maker Quinn Group .
