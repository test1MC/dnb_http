If you need pipe, Hirsch has the supply. From around 20 store locations in Southern California, Hirsch Pipe & Supply distributes more than 25,000 different commercial and residential plumbing, heating, and industrial products to plumbers, HVAC professionals, and building contractors working on repair or remodel projects. It sells brands such as Moen , Elkay , Grohe , and Delta . Beyond California, Hirsch uses its US and international sales teams to supply customers in the US as well as China and other countries along the Pacific Rim. Founded in 1933 by the Mariscal family, father Dan (chairman) and son Greg (director of export) run the company.

The Van Nuys, California-based supplier has more than 20 store locations in southern California, in the greater Los Angeles, Orange, and San Diego county areas. It also sells to customers across the US and the Pacific Rim through its national and international sales teams.

Some of the company's manufacturers include, A.Y. McDonald, Access Able, Acorn Engineering, Advanced Drainage Systems, Alhambra Foundry, Alno, Brizo Faucets, California Faucets, Cuno, Dahl Brothers, Danze, Febco, Fernco, Fiat, General Wire, Gerber, Ginger, Keeney, Kindred, PHD Manufacturing, Phylrich, Pioneer Industries, Plumberex, T&S Brass, Takagi, Tapco, Wilkins, and Zoeller.

Hirsch Pipe & Supply reiterated in 2016 that it works with manufacturers to keep costs low for customers. It also invests in computer systems to improve its service, and is "always looking for ways to improve its ability to service the industry."
