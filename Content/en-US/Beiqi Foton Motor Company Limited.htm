Beiqi Foton Motor Company is not lacking in drive. The state-owned Chinese auto maker supplies domestic and select international markets with cars, light and heavy-duty trucks, vans, pickups, buses, and farm machinery under the brand names Auman, Aumark, and View. Foton builds its cars, trucks, and machinery from plants in Beijing, Guangdong, Hebei, Hunan, Liaoning, Shandong, and Tianjin. Although the company still makes most of its products for sale in China, it has expanded export operations to 90 countries including Algeria, Angola, Pakistan, Iran, India, Russia, and Ukraine.

Foton operates through nearly 20 vehicle plants and engine plants around China, with the production capacity of vehicles and assorted engines of more than 1 million vehicles per year. In addition, Foton has established Foton Daimler Automotive Co., Ltd. and Foton Cummins Engine Co., Ltd. -- jointly with Daimler and Cummins , respectively.

Daimler, along with other international auto makers, is taking advantage of China's demand for automobiles, as well as the country's economic stimulus packages that are being offered to industries willing to manufacture in China. Any actions require the approval of the Chinese government.
