Most companies want to stay out of hot water; this company banks on being in it alongside its customers. American Water Heater manufactures and distributes residential and commercial water heaters throughout the US. It also exports products to about 30 other countries. The appliance company markets its products under the American, Craftmaster, and Flame Guard brand names through professional plumbers, wholesalers, and hardware retailers. The company is part of the water products division of Milwaukee-based A. O. Smith .
