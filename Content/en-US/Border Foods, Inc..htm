Instead of hitching a ride down south and over the border for some spicy grub, pick up a can of Border Foods' peppers and make some belly-blasting food yourself. The company is a major global processer of private-label green chile peppers and a leading US supplier of jalapeño peppers, tomatillos, and banana pepper products. Border Foods supplies the foodservice, food manufacturing, and retail food industries throughout the US with canned, fresh, frozen and dried peppers, salsas, dips, enchilada sauce, as well as other Mexican condiment products. The company was acquired by condiment manufacturer Mizkan Americas in mid-2011.
