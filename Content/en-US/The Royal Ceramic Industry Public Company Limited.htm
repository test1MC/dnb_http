What happens when you combine Thai workmanship with Italian machinery and raw materials? Royal Ceramic Industry (RCI) ... that's what. One of Thailand's top manufacturers and distributors of ceramic tiles, RCI produces floor, wall, clay, porcelain, and glazed porcelain tiles using machines and raw materials imported from Italy. Its wares include tiles, treads, listellos, and borders under the RCI  brand name sold by some 330 dealers in Thailand and exported to 50 countries (the majority of sales are domestic). The company operates about 20 RCI Solutions stores offering tiles, design services, and classes. RCI was founded in 1969.
