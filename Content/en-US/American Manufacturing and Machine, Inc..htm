Thank goodness, this company's products suck! American Manufacturing and Machine's vacuum products clean up wet and dry (non-hazardous) messes in manholes to storm drains and retention ponds. Through its VAC-TRON Equipment division, the company manufactures a line of StreetSmart branded industrial strength vacuums, including a low profile series of vacuum excavation systems, high-pressure water pumps, and trailer mounted vacuum units. American Manufacturing sells through US dealers as well as distributors in Australia, South America, and the UK, to serve boring contractors, various utilities, wastewater and storm water departments, and military installations. It is led by owner and president, Don Buckner.
