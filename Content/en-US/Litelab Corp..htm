It's not that Litelab has shunned the spotlight, it is just that it has seen that there is more money to be made in busway and track lighting products for architectural, suspended ceiling, and big-box grid commercial applications. The company, which formerly focused on serving the entertainment industry, produces track and track mounted lighting fixtures, down lights, spot lights, and wall washers for a range of uses. Litelab has received numerous awards and recognition from the American Institute of Architects and the International Association of Lighting Designers for its lighting products and fixtures.
