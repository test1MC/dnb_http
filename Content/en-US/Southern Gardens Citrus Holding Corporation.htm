Big name, little name, big glass, little glass. It all squeezes out the same. Southern Gardens Citrus Holding, a subsidiary of U. S. Sugar Corporation , processes and packages not-from-concentrate orange juice for both private-label and name-brand customers. It grows some 1.8 million trees on 12,500 company-owned acres of orange groves in Southern Florida; it also uses contract growers. The company is capable of processing 15 million boxes of Valencia and Hamlin oranges and can produce 90 million gallons of orange juice a year. Among the company's customers are Tropicana and Simply Orange.
