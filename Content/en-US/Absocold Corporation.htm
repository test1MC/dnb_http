Absocold wants the opportunity to keep your perishables abso lutely chilled and well-preserved. The company makes compact refrigerators, freezers, and microwaves under the Absocold brand and distributes major appliances (dishwashers, free-standing electric ranges, and clothes washers and dryers) under the Frigidaire , GE , and Whirlpool brands. Absocold's refrigerators and other appliances are typically used in hotels, dormitories, military housing units, and senior living centers. As a general services administration contract supplier, Absocold has been supplying appliances to the federal government since 1974. Several Absocold products have earned the EnergyStar designation for energy efficiency. The company was founded in 1969.

Absocold also makes the Cook 'N Cool, a combination microwave oven and refrigerator with optional freezer. The combo unit has varying storage capacities and is marketed to university dorms, hotels, and motels.

Richmond, Indiana-based Absocold serves customers across the US.

The company serves federal government agencies as well as the assisted-living, hospitality, and university/collegiate markets.
