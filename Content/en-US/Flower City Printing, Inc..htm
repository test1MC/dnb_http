Flower City Printing is a commercial printing firm that specializes in large-format printing and other services primarily aimed at packaging for consumer products manufactures. The company provides printing for packaging and labels, as well as point-of-purchase marketing materials. It also offers fulfillment and kit-packing services. It has 350,000 sq. ft. of production space. In addition to its production and distribution facilities, Flower City Printing has sales offices in Massachusetts, New Jersey, North Carolina, Texas. Wayne Scheible co-founded the business in 1970.
