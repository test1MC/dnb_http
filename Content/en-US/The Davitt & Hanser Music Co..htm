Hanser Music Group hopes to get its hold on musicians and music lovers worldwide by offering a variety of guitars, musical instruments, and amplifiers, among other items. The company's portfolio of brand names includes B.C. Rich Guitars, Michael Kelly Guitars, Rockfield Pickups, Traben Bass Company, and Kustom Amplification. The company's Nuvo Technologies unit manufactures and markets home audio systems. Hanser Music Group was established in 1924 as a family-operated music-distribution business. In 2014 the company sold its Davitt & Hanser distribution business to JAM Industries .
