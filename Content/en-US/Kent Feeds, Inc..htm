No, Superman didn't found the place, but sans cape as the Clark Kent, he may have purchased cat food from this Iowa farm. Kent Feeds produces animal-care and animal-feed products (including feed for cattle, dairy cows, horses, goats, poultry, sheep and swine, as well as for household pets). The company's goods are available though dealers in more than a dozen US states , as well as on its Web site. Kent also makes pet food products under such brand names as Active Dog, Blue Seal, Kit & Cat, and Tasty Nuggets. Kent Feeds is a subsidiary of Kent Corporation . It operates 10 plants located in the Midwest. The company dates back to 1927, when Gage Kent started making livestock feed on his Iowa farm.
