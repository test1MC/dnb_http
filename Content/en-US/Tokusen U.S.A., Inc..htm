Employees at Tokusen USA cross the wire every day. Tokusen is a manufacturer of steel tire cord, tire fabric, and shaped wire. Its products, made by drawing down the wire in steel coils and then weaving the hair-thin strands together, are used primarily in manufacturing steel-belted radial tires. Tokusen USA also produces a range of wire products, including saw wire, die polishing wire, precision pins, and probe card pins, for a variety of technological end uses. Among many, the company serves the automotive, medical, and semiconductor industries. Clients include Goodyear , Bridgestone , and Michelin . Tokusen USA is a subsidiary of Tokusen Kogyo Co., an affiliate of Japan-based Kanai Juyo Kogyo .
