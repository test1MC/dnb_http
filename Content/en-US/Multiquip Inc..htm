Multiquip manufactures and distributes light and mid-sized construction equipment used in concrete placement and finishing, soil compaction, and masonry cutting. The short list includes rollers and rammers, plate compactors, pumps, and welders, as well as mixers, trowels, saws, and concrete-cutting saws and blades. Multiquip also supplies portable power generators, along with light towers and balloon lighting systems for a range of applications. Multiquip is a subsidiary of New York-based diversified manufacturer ITOCHU International .

Stationed in Carson, California, Multiquip has offices and manufacturing facilities throughout North America, China, and the UK. Domestic locations reside in Boise, Idaho; Lewisville, Texas; Honey Brook, Pennsylvania; and Danville, Kentucky. International facilities are located in Montreal; Shanghai; Puebla, Mexico; and Manchester, UK.

Multiquip's business lines include construction equipment, power generation, lighting equipment, and related parts. It also makes a line of diesel, gasoline, and natural gas powered generators ranging from 2.3 kW to 2,000 kW. Major brands include Mikasa, Denyo, and Rammax. The company manufactures and distributes other brands of equipment such as MQ Power, Whiteman, Sanders, Mayco, and Essick.

Products are sold in more than 70 countries worldwide, largely to the construction industry followed by government, telecom, entertainment, and other industries.

Multiquip operates as part of its parent's Machinery segment, which contributed nearly 18% of its parent's total sales in 2015.
