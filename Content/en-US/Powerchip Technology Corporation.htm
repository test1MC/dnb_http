Powerchip Technology (formerly Powerchip Semiconductor Corp.) focuses on producing a variety of DRAM products for its customers. The company, using 300mm wafer production technology, provides memory for consumer electronics (CRAM), mobile devices (MRAM), CMOS image sensors, flash, and logic integrated circuits. Powerchip developed a synchronous DRAM (SDRAM) line, intended for pipelining instructions in a computer design. It also offers foundry services, including customized devices and processes for clients. Powerchip has partnerships with Advanced Semiconductor Engineering , Elpida Memory , Mitsubishi Electric , and Renesas Electronics .
