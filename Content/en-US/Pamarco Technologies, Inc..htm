You'd be hard-pressed to top Pamarco Technologies' contribution to the print industry. Founded in 1994, the company makes its imprint through five printing-related divisions. Its Accel Graphic Systems unit makes ink management systems for the graphic arts industry. Pamarco's Diamond Holding Corporation business makes press rollers, and envelope and specialty presses. Pamarco Global Graphics' products include laser engraved rolls and embossing machinery, while Chicago Manifold Products makes rubber rolls used in offset presses and laminators. The company's ArmoTek group provides laser and electronic engraving and embossing equipment. In 2006 J. P. Kotts & Co., a private equity firm, acquired Pamarco.
