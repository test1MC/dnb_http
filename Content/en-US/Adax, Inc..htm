Adax makes signaling transmission equipment used in telecommunications networks. Its products include protocol controllers, signaling blades and gateways, and related software. The company counts Alcatel-Lucent , Ericsson , Fujitsu , and NEC among its customers. Adax has offices in China, the UK, and the US; it partners with other companies around the world. The company was founded in 1982 by Barry Zuckerman, Adax's president and CEO. Adax broadened its product portfolio by acquiring the Signaling Gateway division of RadiSys .
