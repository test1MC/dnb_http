Reiman Media Group publishes about a dozen magazines in the US and Canada, primarily about country living, cooking, crafts, and nostalgia. Titles include the flagship Taste of Home magazine, as well as Country Woman , Farm & Ranch , and Birds & Blooms . Revenues are generated by subscription and newsstand sales. Its publications are free of advertisements, though it does offer promotional products such as coupons and special offers. Reiman Media also publishes a mail-order catalog featuring country-style products. The company was founded in 1965 by Roy Reiman in the basement of his home in Wisconsin. Reiman Media Group is owned by The Reader's Digest Association .
