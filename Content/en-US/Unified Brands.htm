When you've got to feed an army (or a navy, a body of students, a hotel full of guests, or a wedding with 400 attendees), Unified Brands is the force behind you. The unit of Dover Corp. 's Food Equipment segment integrates four formerly and separately held subsidiaries: Avtec, Capkold, Groen, and Randell. Groen brand makes convection and combo ovens, steamers, mixers, braising pans, and water filtration systems. Randell's line of products includes food prep and hot food tables, display cases, blast chillers, and mobile serving units. Avtec makes Taku, Chinook, and ErcoArch ventilation systems, dish conveyors, and electrical distribution systems. Capkold makes cooking and chilling equipment, and water jets.
