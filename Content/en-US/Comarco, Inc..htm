Comarco develops universal power supplies that charge various portable devices. Operating solely through wholly owned subsidiary Comarco Wireless Technologies, the company's flagship product is its ChargeSource line of adapters that recharge consumer electronic devices, such as notebooks, mobile phones, and music players. Comarco sells directly to consumers through its chargesource.com retail website and to notebook OEMs such as Lenovo, who brand the accessories and sell them in conjunction with their notebooks. Comarco was spun off from Genge Industries in 1971. Elkhorn Partners Limited Partnership holds a 49% stake in Comarco.
