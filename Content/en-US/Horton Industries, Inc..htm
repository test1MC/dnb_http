If home is where the heart is, Horton Industries has given refuge to many hearts. The company's Horton Homes and Dynasty Homes subsidiaries make manufactured houses primarily for sale in the southeastern US, but also as far away as Mexico, China, and Russia. Buyers can choose from more than 50 floor plans and select such amenities as bay windows, vaulted ceilings, walk-in closets, and skylights. Horton is a large producer of HUD code homes. The company's Horton Components unit produces decorative molding and Horton Iron Works, LLC makes I-beam frames for the company's homes. Another subsidiary makes trailers and haulers. Founder and CEO Nevils Horton Jr. owns the company.
