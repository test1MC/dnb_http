Sunrise Telecom thinks every day should start with a test. The company makes equipment-testing products for broadband network access equipment. Communications service providers use products such as its RxT network installation and operation optimization product, introduced in 2011. Sunrise Telecom also makes products for testing cable, fiber-optic, and telephone signaling connections. Its customers span 65 countries and have included AT&T , BT Group , NTT , and Time Warner Cable . Sunrise Telecom earns less than a third of its revenue from the US.

The company's product focus is concentrated on the continuing need for greater broadband and wireless capacity and the attendant need of ensuring deployed infrastructure performs at its best.

Sunrise restructured to focus on its core strengths: the RxT platform and its cable, Ethernet, and transport business.

Sunrise Telecom was founded in 1991 by Robert Pfeiffer, Paul Ker-Chin Chang, and Paul Marshall. It grew through acquisitions to expand its offerings of cable testing, radio-frequency (RF), and wireless measurement products to reduce its reliance on sales of wireline products. Sales more than doubled from 1998 to 1999, and it listed on the NASDAQ in 2000.
