KeyMed (Medical & Industrial Equipment) is a key player when it comes to endoscopic equipment. As part of Olympus Keymed Group , a unit of Japan's Olympus Corporation , KeyMed Medical makes endoscopic instruments and related supplies to explore either the human body or the innards of industrial/electronic equipment. In addition to serving as one of Olympus' primary endoscopic manufacturing divisions, the firm is the exclusive UK distributor of other Olympus products, including flexible and rigid endoscopes. Keymed Medical's customers include hospitals, physicians, surgery centers, and other health care providers.

The company's main manufacturing location in Southend-on-Sea produces a variety of ancillary endoscopic supplies, including suction units, irrigation pumps, workstations, video monitors, and image recording equipment. It also provides repair and maintenance services and conducts research and development efforts on new products and equipment enhancements. The firm has branch distribution and sales offices in Essex, Devon, and Ireland.

A sister company, Algram (located in Devon), makes molded plastic products that are used as components in other Olympus equipment. Another division of the Olympus Keymed Group, the Industrial Division, makes remote visual inspection and high speed video components for industrial inspection equipment.

As a representative distributor of other Olympus medical products in the UK, KeyMed Medical handles endoscopic equipment (videoscopes, fiberscopes, light sources, lenses, and processors) made at the conglomerate's Japanese and German facilities. Keymed Medical and its affiliates also sell diagnostic ultrasound equipment made by Olympus' Aloka division in the UK and have distribution responsibilities for some medical and industrial equipment in other parts of Europe, Russia, the Middle East, and Africa as well.

KeyMed Medical has locations in the UK and Ireland.

The Medical division provides 69% of the parent company's total revenue. European business accounts for about a quarter of the Medical division's earnings.

Keymed Medical was founded in 1970. It became a subsidiary of Olympus in 1978, and later became part of the Olympus Keymed Group when it merged with the Olympus UK distribution entity.
