In the workaday world, Howle Holdings is kind of a halfway house; it serves as both a holding company of Total Carbide and a subsidiary of electronic components manufacturer Versarien PLC. Total Carbide, a powder metallurgy company, produces Tungsten Carbide wear parts and inserts. Its custom-made, "bespoke" engineered lineup is used by a diverse group of industries requiring corrosion-resistant, hard metal components that roll, cut, drill, pump, shape, spray, extrude, or mold. Its customers are primarily engaged in oil and gas exploration, and, less so, the semiconductor, food manufacture, and industrial measurement sectors.
