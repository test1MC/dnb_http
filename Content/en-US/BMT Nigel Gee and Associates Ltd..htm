BMT Nigel Gee can get you out sailing the open seas in no time. The company's naval architects and marine engineers provide a variety of shipbuilding and design services, encompassing designs for ferries, windfarm support vessels, yachts, coast guard, patrol, and defense vessels, and cargo and work boats. BMT Nigel Gee serves the commercial sector, military agencies, and private clients worldwide. The company's services include concept feasibility assessment, production design, and construction support and inspection. Nigel Gee and John Bonafoux founded the company in 1986; it was acquired by the BMT Group in 2001.

BMT Nigel Gee operates worldwide through its headquarters in Southampton, UK. It also has fabrication yards located in Singapore and Vietnam.

The company serves across a range of industries including shipyards, consultants, management companies, brokers, naval architects, legal practices, insurance companies, and equipment and materials suppliers.
