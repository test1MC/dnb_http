Lotto Sport has a lotto clout in the worlds of soccer and tennis. The company was established in 1973 to produce tennis shoes and other sporty footwear for the Italian market. Still known best for its sneakers, the company has expanded into athletic wear and leisure wear for men, women, and children, as well as licensed lines that include eyewear, stationery, and underwear. Lotto Sports's products are available in about 80 countries. Lotto sponsors a number of professional sports players and teams, and has partnerships with the ATP, the WTA Tour , and Wimbledon. As part of a joint venture with US-based consulting and investment firm DCS Group , Lotto established Lotto North America, an outpost in the US.
