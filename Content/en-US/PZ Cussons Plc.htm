PZ Cussons is no washout at washing up. Its focus is making and distributing body and hand soaps. It also produces detergents, surface cleaners, over-the-counter medicines, skin and hair care products, men's toiletries, and other consumer products, including food and beverages. While the company sells some internationally recognized brands, the majority of its products are branded and marketed for local markets in Africa, Asia, and Europe. In addition to its personal care and soap and detergent products, PZ Cussons sells home appliances and food and beverages in Africa. Founded in 1879 by George Paterson and George Zochonis as a trading post in Sierra Leone, the Zochonis family owns about 30% of the company.
