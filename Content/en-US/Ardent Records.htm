It may not be the biggest record label around, but Ardent Records knows a good thing when they see it. Christian rock label Ardent Records is home to artists such as Big Tent Revival, Brother's Keeper, Skillet, and Todd Agnew. Many of the label's bands seek to achieve secular crossover success after starting out in the Christian market. The company is also equipped with three recording/mix studios that have been used by high profile clients such as ZZ Top, Led Zeppelin, and Travis Tritt. Ardent Records was founded in 1966 by owner and CEO John Fry.
