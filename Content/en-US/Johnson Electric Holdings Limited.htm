Johnson Electric Holdings puts a lot of power in small places. Founded in 1959, the company designs and manufactures a slew of motion products, flexible interconnects, switches, and control systems. Customers are concentrated in the automotive industry, and, to a lesser extent, home appliance, power tool, business equipment, and personal care markets. Johnson Electric's micro-motors end up in everything from automotive door locks and mirror adjusters to can openers, vending machines, and electric toothbrushes. The company straddles three operating divisions: Automotive Products, the largest, Components & Services, and Industry Products. Johnson Electric sells its products to clients in more than 30 countries.
