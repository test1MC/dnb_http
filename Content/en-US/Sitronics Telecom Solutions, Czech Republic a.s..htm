SITRONICS Telecom Solutions (formerly STROM telecom) is starting to branch out, not just in its product offering, but also in its reach across Europe. The company is a manufacturer of communications and data transmission equipment used primarily by telephone service carriers. Products include equipment for digital exchanges, billing systems, and call centers; wireless network routers; and interfaces to bridge old networks with new systems. The company's European operations include offices throughout the Czech Republic and in Bosnia, France, Russia, Slovakia, and the Ukraine; it also has facilities in Malaysia and the United Arab Emirates. SITRONICS, founded in 1993, is a subsidiary of Russian telecom giant Sistema .
