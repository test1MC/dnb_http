Scentsy throws a party to make dollars from scents. Its more than 181,000 independent consultants showcase the company's scent system to customers at home parties in the US, Puerto Rico, Canada, Guam, Germany, the UK, and Ireland. The system, marketed as a safer alternative to candles, includes a lightbulb-powered warmer and scented bars available in more than 80 fragrances. The company also sells scented travel tins, hanging air fresheners, room spray, and foaming hand sanitizer. To spread its scented message to the whole family, Scentsy offers Buddies, stuffed animals with pockets for scent packs. Husband and wife Orville and Heidi Thompson formed the company in 2004.

Through the company's independent sales consultants, Scentsy sells fragrance products globally. Based in Meridian, Idaho, the company extends its reach from its primary operations in the US to Canada, Puerto Rico, Guam, Germany, the UK, and Ireland.

Scentsy operates distribution centers in Meridian, Idaho; Lexington, Kentucky; Coppell, Texas; and Warsaw, Poland. Its wax is manufactured in Meridian, Idaho. Glazed warmers are produced by third-party vendors.

As an international leader in home and personal care fragrance items, Scentsy markets multiple products that extend beyond the typical candle alternative. The Scentsy Buddy is a plush animal -- either large or small -- that boasts a built-in fragrant Scent Pak.

Direct marketer Scentsy leverages a sales force of Independent Scentsy Consultants that sells products directly to consumers through home parties, open houses, fairs, trade shows, and fundraisers.

Scentsy celebrated its fifth anniversary by making its first foray outside the US. Initially, it allowed US-based consultants to sell and recruit in Canada; the Canadian business was profitable within three months and had more than 1,000 consultants the first quarter.
