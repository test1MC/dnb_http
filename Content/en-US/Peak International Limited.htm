Peak performance and strong global sales are the twin goals of Peak International, which makes plastic packaging products used to ship, store, and handle semiconductor devices and related components, as well as precision medical equipment. Peak's products include matrix trays, reels, and carrier tape; it provides design, prototyping, and tooling services. Its SemiCycle division collects Peak's plastic products, recycles, and sells them. Peak designs products to be used with automated production and testing equipment. It primarily operates in Asia, but it also serves customers in Europe and North America. Plastic extrusion firm S&G Company acquired Peak International in mid-2008 for about $25 million.
