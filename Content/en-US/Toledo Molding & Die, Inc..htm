Holy Toledo! Toledo Molding & Die (TMD) provides automotive subsystems for OEMs and other auto component manufacturers. It designs parts -- such as air induction systems, fans, and front-end modules -- based on customer specifications, then builds prototypes and the tools to make the parts, performs necessary tests on them, and assembles the finished products. TMD manufactures and assembles injection-molded parts and blow-molded parts. It is privately owned and operates several plastic molding manufacturing facilities in the Midwest, primarily in Ohio.

TMD is headquartered in Toledo, Ohio. It operates four manufacturing plants, a research and development center, test lab, and tooling facility. The company targets customers in the US, Mexico, Canada, Europe, China, and South America.

TMD operates through several divisions. Its services segment is comprised of Contract Design Services, Engineering, Testing Lab, Tooling, and Prototyping. The production segment consists of Injection Molding, Blow Molding, and Assembly.

TMD's sales agents manages all aspects of engineering, purchasing, and manufacturing support for a specific commodity (ductwork, HVAC molding, assembly, etc). They are assigned based upon their overall technical capabilities and product expertise.

The company was founded in 1955 as a model and pattern shop.
