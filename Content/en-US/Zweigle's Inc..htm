Zweigle's is a regional food manufacturer best known for its hot dogs and other sausage products. The company makes skinless and natural casing frankfurters, along with Italian sausages, chicken sausages, knockwurst, and Polish sausages. Zweigle's also markets a variety of deli meats and condiments. It also makes private label products for some of the largest retailers in the Northeast. Zweigle's products are sold through supermarkets and retailers mostly in New York and other East coast states. It also sells products through its website and it distributes to food service suppliers. Zweigle's is a fifth-generation, family owned business controlled by Julie Camardo-Steron.

The company makes all its products at its manufacturing facility in Rochester, New York.

Sering the Northeastern US, Zweigle's sells in Pennsylvania, the Southern Tier, Syracuse, Western Massachusetts, Albany, Central New York, the Greater Rochester Area, Western New York, Northern New York, and Oswego.

The company sells through supermarkets, retailers, distributors, and online. Its selected distributors include US Foods Buffalo, Sysco Syracuse, JH Wattles, Renzi Food Service, A&G Meats, and Rochester Meat.

Zweigle's is focusing on expanding its product portfolio to include a variety of high quality, convenient meal products with the introduction of Savory Sensations and Mama Camardo brands.

The family-owned company was started in 1880 by Wilhelm and Josephine Zweigle.
