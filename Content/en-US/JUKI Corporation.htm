JUKI is making the lives of seamstresses just a little bit easier with its array of industrial, semi-professional, and home sewing machines. The company serves the global apparel industry, in which speed and accuracy are critical for making jeans wear, men's shirts, and intimate apparel, as well as shoes, seat belts, and golf bags. Its sewing machines are capable of producing different stitches, finishing seams, sewing pockets and buttonholes, and reinforcing leather or heavyweight materials. JUKI also makes surface mounting equipment, which are systems that place electronic components onto printed circuit boards. About half its sales come from selling its industrial sewing machines within Japan.
