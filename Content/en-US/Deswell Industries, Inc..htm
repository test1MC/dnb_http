At Deswell Industries they don't break the mold, they make the mold. Deswell makes injection-molded plastic parts and components, electronic products, and metallic molds for electronics manufacturers through its Jetcrown Plastics and JIMCO subsidiaries. Deswell's plastic parts are used to make cases for devices such as calculators, flashlights, telephones, remote controls, and photocopy machines. Its electronic products include printed circuit board assemblies, telephones, computer peripherals, and professional audio equipment. Top customers include Digidesign , Epson Precision (H.K.), and Line 6 Manufacturing . Deswell Industries gets more than half of its sales outside the US.
