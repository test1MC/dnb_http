HandEra provides contract engineering and design services to companies that make electronics for the consumer, medical, automotive, and industrial markets. It develops electronics hardware and technology for devices running Windows, Linux, and Android operating systems. The company got its start in mobile device by making handheld devices running the the Palm operating system. It offers turnkey design and manufacturing services. HandEra also developed embedded software products ranging from WiFi modules to AC power supplies. The company operates its design and manufacturing facility in Des Moines, Iowa. Customers have included Samsung , Epson , AT&T , Honeywell , and IBM . The company was established in 1992 and is owned by its officers and outside investors.
