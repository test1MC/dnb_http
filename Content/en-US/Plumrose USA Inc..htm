Plumrose USA sells neither plums nor roses, but it does offer a comprehensive range of packaged meat products. The company provides sliced deli meats, bacon, barbecued ribs, and shelf-stable and canned meats for distribution under the Plumrose, Danola, and DAK brands to grocery stores, delicatessens, and foodservice operators across the US. The company owns and operates production plants in Indiana, Iowa, Mississippi, and Vermont. Owned by Denmark-based packaged meats company Danish Crown , Plumrose began business as a sliced-ham company in 1932.

Headquartered in Downers Grove, Illinois, Plumrose operates production facilities in Booneville, Mississippi; Elkhart, Illinois; Swanton, Vermont; and Council Bluffs, Iowa. It has other offices in Mississippi, Iowa, New Jersey, Indiana, Arkansas, Vermont, and California.

Plumrose produces and sells meat products to food distributors, retailers, and wholesalers in the US.

The company is part of the foods business area of Danish Crown , which contributed 41% of the total net sales of the parent company in fiscal 2015.
