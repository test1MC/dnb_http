Alco Holdings is a consumer electronics contract manufacturer specializing in audio, video, telephone, and computer products. Products include portable DVD players, DVD home theater systems, LCD TVs, CD changers, multidisc (MD) recorders, CD clock radios, car stereos, cordless phones, and karaoke machines. The company, which also sells products under its own Venturer brand, provides additional manufacturing services, such as design and engineering, prototyping, product packaging, and supply chain management. Alco Holdings was founded in 1968.
