Senvion (formerly REpower Systems) is a big fan of renewable energy; it makes wind turbines for on- and offshore wind farms. The company specializes in system development, licensing, production, and sale of its high-power turbines, which range from 2.05 MW to 6.15 MW. Senvion production plants in Germany and Portugal support sales, largely to export wind markets. Through its PowerBlades joint venture, it also produces rotor blades. Services include installation, monitoring, and maintenance. The company's operations and partnerships dot Europe, the US, China, and Australia. Altogether, it has installed more than 3,000 turbines around the world. India's Suzlon Energy , a wind turbine maker, owns more than 95% of Senvion's stock.
