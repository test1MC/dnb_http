If raindrops keep falling on your head, NCI Group (which does business as MBCI) can put a roof over it. The company, a subsidiary of NCI Building Systems , manufactures metal roofing and wall systems for the commercial, industrial, architectural, residential and agricultural markets. Products include steel and aluminum roofing, siding, and light gauge framing materials. MBCI also makes trims, flashings, and accessories. All products are available in a variety of colors and textures. Founded in 1976, MBCI operates about 15 manufacturing and sales facilities throughout the US. The company sells to regional manufacturers, contractors, distributors, lumber yards, and other groups.

NCI Group belongs to its parent company's metal components segment, which reported $663.1 million in sales in fiscal 2013 (ended October), a 24% increase versus the prior year.

MBCI employs a hub-and-spoke distribution system to ship orders from the closest manufacturing location to the construction site. Shorter distances between manufacturing facilities and project sites result in decreased delivery times, lower transportation costs, and less environmental impact.

In June 2012 parent NCI Building Systems acquired Metl-Span LLC for about $145.7 million. At closing, Texas-based Metl-Span became a wholly-owned subsidiary of NCI Group. Metl-Span opened its seventh manufacturing facility in early 2014. The Virginia plant makes architectural insulated metal panels and custom fabrication.
