GigPeak (formerly GigOptix) hopes its light shines bright in an optical universe. The company develops polymer materials and products for use in wireless and optical communications networks. Products include optical and radio-frequency (RF) amplifiers, compact panel wireless antennas, and electro-optic modulators and optical interconnects for use in telecommunications. Its products are used in fiber-optic communications networks and for connecting to other types of electronic equipment. GigPeak also develops custom electro-optic devices for the US government, particularly the Department of Defense , which accounts most of sales. In early 2016 it acquired Magnum Semiconductor and took its present name.
