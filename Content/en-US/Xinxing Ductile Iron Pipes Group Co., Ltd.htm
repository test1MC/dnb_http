Xinxing Ductile Iron Pipes Group Co. makes it clear what it does for customers. The state-owned company focuses on producing ductile cast-iron pipes and other related products, as well as iron smelting through its Xinxing Ductile Iron Pipes Co. subsidiary. Its other subsidiaries also provide a variety of other products such as clothing, leather goods, machinery, and rubber. In addition, the company offers warehousing, logistics support, and research and development services for its activities. Xinxing Ductile Iron Pipes Group was established in 1997. It set up an international arm, Xinxing Pipes International Development Company, in 2012.

Xinxing Ductile Iron Pipes Group is China's largest producer of ductile iron pipes and steel gratings. About 30% of the company's pipes are exported to 100 countries and regions in the world; its market share in China is more than 45%. Xinxing Ductile Iron Pipes Group has seven major product lines: Xinxing Ductile Iron Pipes; Xinxing Fittings; Xinxing Steels; Xinxing Special Steel Pipes; Xinxing Forgings; Xinxing Gratings; Xinxing PSP Pipes;

The company has an annual output of 8 million tons of metal products, including 1.8 million tons of ductile iron pipes, 6.3 million tons of steel, 180,000 tons of special steel pipes, 80,000 tons of steel gratings with annual output, and 40,000 tons of fittings.

Xinxing Ductile Iron Pipes Group has production bases in Handan City, Hebei Province; Wuhu City, Anhui Province; Bazhou City, Xinjiang Province; Huangshi City, Hubei Province; Taojiang City, Hunan Province; and Chongzhou City, Sichuan Province.

Selected projects include the Yellow River diversion project; water projects in Changsha City and in Jiangsu Province; a power plant project in Henan Province; and the TAJURA water project in Libya.

The company sells its products via 17 sales subsidiaries.

Xinxing Ductile Iron Pipes Group is expanding and improving its industrial network and value chain through strategic acquisitions, recombinations, holdings, and equity participation. It has three production bases covering East China and South-central China (Wuhu Xinxing Pipes Co., Ltd, Taojiang Xinxing Fittings Co., Ltd. and Huangshi Xinxing Pipes Co., Ltd). Xinxing Ductile Iron Pipes Group also has four production bases covering Southwest China and Northwest China (Chuanjiang Pipes Co., Ltd in Sichuan Province, Xinjiang Jinte Steel Co., Ltd, Xinxing Pipes(Xinjiang) Resources Development Co., Ltd, and Xinxing Pipes Xinjiang Co., Ltd).

In 2016, the company acquired 5% of Emerging Jihua Group Limited for about $243.3 million. The China-based firm is engaged in metallurgy and the processing of ferrous metal, textiles and garment production, special equipment manufacturing, and real estate development.
