Conergy makes and distributes solar water pumps, installs small wind turbines, and distributes solar electric power components, including solar panels, batteries, and related electronics. The company also helps arrange financing for residential installations, since solar panels can be an expensive proposition to purchase. Conergy uses photovoltaic modules made by its German parent company and by Canadian Solar , SANYO Electric , and Suntech Power . Customers include Exelon , the South San Joaquin Irrigation District in California, and the US Army 's Fort Carson in Colorado. The company is a subsidiary of Conergy AG .
