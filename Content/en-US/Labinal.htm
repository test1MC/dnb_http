Labinal is all about the wiring. The company makes wiring systems and provides engineering services for the aerospace, defense, automotive, and transportation industries. The company, which has operations in France, Germany, Mexico, Morocco, the UK, and the US, operates through four divisions: EuropeWiring, North America Wiring, and Engineering & Technology, and Labinal Services. Labinal's home country accounts for nealy 40% of sales. The company serves primarily civil aircraft customers. Labinal is a subsidiary of Snecma , which was acquired by and absorbed into SAFRAN in 2005.
