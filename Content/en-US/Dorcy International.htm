Dorcy International makes flashlights (including LED flashlights), lanterns, and batteries in sizes that range from key chains and penlights to 2-million candle-power handheld spotlights. Its products are sold to major retail chain customers, including Target , Sears , and Wal-Mart . (The three chains account for about 90% of the firm's US sales.)
