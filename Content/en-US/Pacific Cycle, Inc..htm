Don't tread on Pacific Cycle. Founded in 1983, it designs and markets bicycles and scooters under several brands, including Schwinn, Mongoose, DYNO, InSTEP, Roadmaster, Pacific Outdoors, and others. It's also a top maker of bicycle helmets in the US. The company's bikes are sold by mass merchandisers ( Target , Wal-Mart ), sporting goods chains ( Dick's , The Sports Authority ), and independent suppliers. Pacific Cycle, which outsources its manufacturing to China and Taiwan, got its operations in gear by buying Brunswick Bicycles, Schwinn/GT Corp. , InSTEP, and PTI Sports, all within a few years. Dorel Industries owns Pacific Cycle alongside its popular Cannondale brand.
