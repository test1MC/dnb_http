Spa Settings wants to transport your hot tub into a mini oasis, or maybe a mirage. The company makes redwood hot tub gazebos with four basic models -- the Mini Oasis, Mirage, Sedona Octagon, and Skylighter. It also makes accessories such as canopies, privacy screens, steps, and towel trees. Many of the gazebo models come with optional bar kits, including stools and wine racks. Spa Settings' manufacturing facilities are located in Mexico. The company was founded in 1989.
