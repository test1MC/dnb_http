Terra-cotta isn't just the latest color on some trendy fashion designer's palette, it's the medium with which DEROMA excels. The company has shaped itself into a top terra-cotta pot maker worldwide. With manufacturing and marketing companies, DEROMA offers both decorated and undecorated terra-cotta pots and vases, as well as candles in terra-cotta holders, clay decorations, and gift products (such as clocks and holders for wine). It sells its products worldwide and has operations in France, Hong Kong, Italy, the UK, and the US. DEROMA was founded in 1955.
