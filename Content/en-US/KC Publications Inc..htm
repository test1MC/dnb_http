KC Publications likes to tell the story behind the scenery. The company publishes and sells products about US national parks, early westward exploration, and Native American culture. Its offerings include books, calendars, bookmarks, flash cards, and matted photographic prints. The company also offers free online access to Western Gateways magazine, a vintage magazine published in the 1960s. KC Publications was founded in 1963 by photographer KC DenDooven.
