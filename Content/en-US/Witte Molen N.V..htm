Alanheri focuses on providing food for humans and their pets. Alanheri, a holding company, has subsidiaries in the Netherlands, South Africa, and Switzerland. Its subsidiary in the Netherlands makes WITTE MOLEN petfood and birdfeed, as well as participates in the trade of cereals, coffee, spices, herbs, bakery ingredients, and nuts. The company also processes and distributes agricultural products including rice, beans, dried fruits, and peanuts through subsidiary Umgeni Products in South Africa. Alanheri has additional offices in China and Ethiopia.
