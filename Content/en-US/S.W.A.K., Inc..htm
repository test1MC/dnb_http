S.W.A.K. (also known as The Great American Sweater Company) manufactures women's and children's cotton and acrylic knitted sweaters. The company, which was founded by former president and CEO Howard Gursky's father in the 1920s, sells its apparel lines to major department store chains nationwide. Formerly known as Melrose Knitting Mills, the company took the name S.W.A.K. in 1980.
