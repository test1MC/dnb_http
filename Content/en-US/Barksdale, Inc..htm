In control while going with the flow, Barksdale is a manufacturer of switches, valves, and other industrial controls, with a focus on making components for the control and measurement of fluids. The company's components are used in auto plants, in chemical processing plants and distribution facilities, on machine tools, on offshore drilling rigs, in power generation, in steel mills, and in a host of other industries. In agriculture, for instance, Barksdale pressure switches are used to monitor center pivot irrigation systems, while its temperature switches help maintain optimum growing climates in greenhouses, and its valves control the water supply in cattle feedlots. Barksdale is a subsidiary of Crane Co .
