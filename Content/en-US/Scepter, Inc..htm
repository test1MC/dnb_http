Scepter's businesses include companies engaged in aluminum production and recycling. Its plants process aluminum dross and scrap into de-ox cones, specification casting ingot, rod and rolling sheet ingots, molten aluminum, and other products. The company also trades aluminum and recycles used beverage cans (UBCs). Scepter companies operate from facilities in the US (Indiana, New York, and Tennessee), Canada (Quebec), and Hungary. Those facilities generally rank among the leading aluminum recyclers in their regions. Company president Garney Scott founded Scepter in 1986.
