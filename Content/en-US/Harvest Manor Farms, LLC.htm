Snack-food cravers and nut munchers from all around will be beating down the door to get in this nut house. Harvest Manor Farms buys, processes, and sells almonds, cashews, peanuts, pecans, sunflower seeds, snack mixes, and other nut-related products to food retailers throughout the US and direct to the consumer through its Web site. The company's national brand names include The Original Nut House, All American, Hoodys, and Manor Creek Naturals. Harvest Manor Farms traces its beginnings back more than a century when Dutch immigrant A.C. Hoodenpyle began selling peanuts in Oregon. In 2009, the company was acquired by Ralcorp , which itself was purchased by ConAgra in 2013.
