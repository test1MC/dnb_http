When Cobra Products strikes, porcelain and pipes are pristine. Cobra Products, a unit of Masco Corporation , makes and markets tools and machinery for cleaning and unclogging sinks, toilets, and drains. Its products include hand tools and power-operated drain-cleaning machines. Products are sold through more than 25,000 home-improvement retail outlets and hardware stores. Created specifically for professional plumbers, the company introduced its Speedway Tools line of heavy-duty, motorized machines for drain cleaning. This line is sold through wholesalers, catalogs, and rental firms.
