A family that welds and slices together, bends together. TRUMPF Inc. is the North American member of Germany-based TRUMPF GmbH , a family-owned company comprising more than 55 subsidiaries that manufacturer laser machine tools. TRUMPF, the largest subsidiary within the group, divides its power-packed products across several categories: machine tools (for flexible sheet metal and material processing), laser technology (cutting, welding, or marking applications), power tools (slicing, fastening and shaping), electronics (laser, induction, and vacuum technology applications), and medical technology (surgery tables and systems for physical therapy and surgery). TRUMPF GmbH established its US headquarters in 1969.

TRUMPF Inc. operates facilities in Connecticut, Michigan, New Jersey, Mexico, and Canada. The company generates about 30% of its business outside of the US.

The company sells its products through distributors and an independent sales representative company.

The US subsidiary is focused on expanding its product portfolio in order to attract additional customers. TRUMPF hopes to capitalize on the production needs of manufacturers of auto and aircraft components to agricultural machinery, household appliances, furniture and decorative fixtures, as well as medical and electronic technologies.
