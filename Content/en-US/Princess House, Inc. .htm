Princess House caters to princesses with its vast housewares portfolio. The company is a direct seller of household goods, including cookware, food storage items, lighting products, bakeware, textiles, gifts, home d√©cor items, flatware, and seasonal and hostess-exclusives. Collections are sold under the names Barrington, Fantasia, Princess Heritage, and others. Princess House designs its own products and markets them through more than 25,000 Lifestyle Consultants, many of whom are independent business owners. Consultants market items through in-home parties held in the US, Puerto Rico, Australia, Canada, and Mexico. Princess House celebrated its 50th anniversary in 2013. Ray Chambers owns Princess House.

Direct selling company Princess House maintains a corporate headquarters in Taunton, Massachusetts, and a 210,000-sq.-ft. distribution center in Rural Hall, North Carolina. Altogether, its pair of facilities house more than 200 people. Outside North America, Princess House sells its products in Puerto Rico and Australia.

Princess House is a direct seller. More than 25,000 Lifestyle Consultants market Princess House products in all 50 US states, Puerto Rico, the Virgin Islands, Guam, Australia, and Canada. About two-thirds of its consultant network is of Hispanic descent.

Princess House has expanded outside the US in recent years into Puerto Rico and as far away as Australia. The company, which was named by The Boston Globe as one of the Top 100 Places to Work in Massachusetts in 2011, also boasts a namesake bridal gift registry, giving engaged couples access to more options than the ones provided by rivals Target and Bed, Bath & Beyond and giving Princess House yet another sales channel.

The company's Spanish-speaking Lifestyle Consultants, which comprise about two-thirds of its sales force, represents the fastest-growing segment of its business. To aid in training, the company offers all of its business tools, programs, and training materials available in both English and Spanish.

Princess House generated $148 million in revenue in 2012, up from $102 million in 2011, and $94 million in 2010.
