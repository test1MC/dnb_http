Don Pancho Authentic Mexican Foods sets about satisfying the growing craving for Mexican and Tex-Mex foods in the US. Manufacturing some 2.5 million tortilla shells every day, the company provides regular, organic, low-fat, and trans-fat-free corn, flour, and multi-grain tortillas and wraps, as well as tortilla chips, tosdatas, and Mexican spices under the Don Pancho brand name. Don Pancho's foodservice operation also offers Mexican cheeses and beans and rice. It has operations Salem, Oregon; Yakima and Seattle, Washington; and Nashville, North Carolina. The company was founded as Puentes Brothers in 1979 and changed its name to Don Pancho Authentic Mexican Foods in 2006. It is owned by Reser's Fine Foods .
