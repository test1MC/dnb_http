King Nut processes, packages, and distributes nuts, dried fruits, snack mixes, pretzels, popcorn, trail mixes, chocolate and non-chocolate confectionary, soy nuts, peanut butter, and salad toppings to supermarkets, airlines, drugstores, foodservice companies, convenience stores, gift companies, and mass merchandisers, and foodservice operators. Its products are sold under the King's, Peterson's, and Summer Harvest brands. The company and its sister company, Peterson Nut, also produce private-label nuts and snacks and offer gift baskets and fundraising opportunities. King Nut Companies is owned by CEO Martin Kanan and his family.
