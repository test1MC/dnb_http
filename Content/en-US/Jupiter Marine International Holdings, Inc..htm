Women are from Venus, men are from Mars, and fishing boats are from Jupiter, as in Jupiter Marine International Holdings (JMIH). The company designs and manufactures offshore sport fishing boats under the Jupiter brand name; the nine model lineup ranges in size from a 29 foot forward seating outboard to a 39 foot cruiser. JMIH offers open center console and forward cabin models, as well as yacht-like amenities. A build-a-boat option allows color selection. The company builds all of its boats at its plant in Palmetto, Florida. Products are sold through US dealers, primarily on the East and Gulf Coast. President and CEO Carl Herndon, his family, and CFO Lawrence Tierney collectively own more than half of JMIH.
