You might say this company helps elevate the moment with a special card. SPS Studios creates and sells greeting cards under the brand Blue Mountain Arts. Its selection of cards includes anniversary, birthday, and sympathy cards sold under such lines as Children of the Inner Light, Occasion Gallerie, and Thoughts of Life. It also creates Spanish-language greeting cards under the Sentimientos banner. SPS Studios also markets bookmarks, calendars, and mugs, as well as blank books and illustrated poetry books. Self-described hippies Stephen Schutz, a physicist, and Susan Polis Schutz, a poet, founded SPS Studios in 1970 while traveling across the US selling their silk-screened posters and greeting cards.
