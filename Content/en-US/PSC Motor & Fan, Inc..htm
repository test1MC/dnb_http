To paraphrase a verse from the movie Grease , PSC Motor & Fan has got chills, they're multiplyin', cause the power you're supplyin', it's electrifyin'. If you want thermal components, PSC is the supplier. Components from air moving devices to heat sinks, heat pipes, and units composed of all three, put the chill on integrated circuits, printed circuit boards, chassis, on up to rack-mounted computer servers. The company supplies backward curve impellers, AC and DC, in various millimeters, too. PSC's sales and marketing efforts are supported by a China-based factory, begun as a joint venture with Revcor and Bao An Shajing Dong Chang Electric Motor Company. Electronics OEMs SGI and Allen-Vanguard are key customers.
