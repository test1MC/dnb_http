The gleam of chrome is irresistible to A.G. Simpson. The company captures about 35% of the chrome-plated bumper business in North America. Doing business as AGS Automotive Systems, the auto parts maker has six facilities in Canada that produce mainly front and rear impact system assemblies/modules. It also produces some general stampings, such as cross members, oil pans, and frame assemblies, and it offers assembly and sequencing services. Customers have included such noteworthy names as Chrysler , GM , Karmax, and Johnson Controls .
