TFH Publications has something for your best friend to chew on. The company is a leading worldwide publisher of pet and animal books, covering dogs, cats, birds, reptiles, and farm animals. Book titles include the World Atlas of Dog Breeds and The Encyclopedia of Dog Sports and Activities . TFH also publishes the monthly magazine Tropical Fish Hobbyist . The company additionally makes premium dog chews, edible bones, carriers, dog houses, and other pet products through its Nylabone Products division. TFH Publications is a subsidiary of Central Garden & Pet Company .
