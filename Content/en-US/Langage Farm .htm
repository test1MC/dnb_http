The cows at Langage Farms are big business for the company. The farm's herd of more than 250 Jersey and Guernsey cows produces the raw material for Devon clotted cream, fresh yogurt, ice cream, frozen yogurt and sorbet, and cheese. The company's distribution service delivers its products to grocery retailers, hotels, and restaurants in Cornwall, Devon, and southern England, as well as retail and foodservice outlets in Bangkok, Hong Kong, and Japan. Langage Farm has been run for many years by farmers in the Harvey Family; today it is owned by senior partner L. Harvey.

Langage Farm sells its products through major retailers such as Morrison , Sainsbury , Tesco , and Waitrose .

The farm's management has made major steps into becoming environmentally friendly by building an anaerobic digester plant, which extracts methane from the plant and in turn converts it into electricity and powers the whole plant, and more importantly, the farm. It also provides the farm with organic fertilizers, thus cutting the use of any inorganic fertilizers in agricultural production.
