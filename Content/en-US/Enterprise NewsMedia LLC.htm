Enterprise NewsMedia is a local newspaper publisher with two titles serving markets near Boston. Its papers include The Patriot Ledger (Quincy, Massachusetts), which boasts a circulation of more than 47,000, and and The Enterprise (Brockton, Massachusetts), with a circulation of more than 28,000. The papers have been in operations since 1837 and 1880, respectively. Founded in 1996, Enterprise NewsMedia was acquired by GateHouse Media in 2006. In September 2013, the company (along with parent GateHouse) filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection. GateHouse emerged from bankruptcy later in 2013.
