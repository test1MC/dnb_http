AuGRID is in the cross-hairs of change. A holding company which acquires ownership interest in a diverse range of businesses, AuGrid owns Avatar Design and Manufacturing (F-5 citrus and veggie juicer) and has intellectual property licenses with CF Ballistics and Carbon Composite Technologies. It has re-engaged its planned acquisition of a Halal meat processing plant, as well as launched its Optipure Plasma HDTV line. In early 2010 it agreed to acquire J&K Futures, but plans to merge with Silver Star Capital Holdings, are off. Additionally, the company is targeting automotive, consumer electronics, medical, minerals and mining, and military applications. CEO Muhammad Shaheed owns about 40% of the company.
