Carclo manufactures technical plastic products, organized among three divisions. Its technical plastics arm, which generates about three-fifths of sales, makes injection-molded plastic parts for electronic, medical, and optical applications, such as touch screens, blood testing devices, and LED products. A precision products unit supplies aircraft and supercars, including Lamborghini and Bugatti, high-power lighting and control systems. Carclo operates across six different countries and regions: Canada, the US, the UK, the Czech Republic, China, and India.

The company serves various sectors such as the medical, industrial, aerospace, luxury, and supercar lighting markets. Its top five customers collectively accounted for 49% of its net sales in 2014.

Carclo's revenues increased 11% from 2014 to 2015. The growth for 2015 was fueled by a 21% uptick in LED technologies sales and a 11% spike in technical plastics sales.
