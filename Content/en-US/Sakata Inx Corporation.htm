Do you need more toner? Sakata INX Corporation might be able to help you out. The company manufactures copy machine toner and also printing inks for newspapers, packaging, and industrial applications. Other products include graphic art materials including film, chemicals, and printing equipment as well as packaging materials and photo processing chemicals. About two-thirds of the company's revenues come from ink sales. Sakata INX has six Japanese subsidiaries and more than 20 international ones in Asia, Europe, and North America. Sakata INX operates in the US through subsidiary INX International .
