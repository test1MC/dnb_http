Circa Enterprises manufactures and sells electrical equipment, outdoor enclosures, and other goods to customers in the telecommunications, electrical, and construction industries. The company's products include electrical surge protection terminals mainly for the US market and modules and weather-resistant equipment cabinets for voice and data communications lines. Circa Enterprises was founded in 1985 and has operations in the US and Canada.

Circa's Telecom segment, which accounts for 52% of revenue, manufactures, markets, and sells surge protection and related equipment to the telecommunications industry in the United States and Canada. Its products provide primary protection against voltage surges to telephone systems and other data transmission systems.

The Circa Metals segment, 48% of revenue, provides custom metal fabrication services to the company's Telecom segment and to third-party customers. Through its Hydel product line, Circa Metals also designs, manufactures, markets and sells fabricated enclosures, pole line hardware and other products to the Canadian electrical industry. The Circa Metals business operates in Vaughan, Ontario.

Circa is headquartered in Calgary, Alberta with operations in Ontario and Florida. Customers in Canada account for 55% of sales with US customers providing the other 45%.

Circa works with a number of distributors including Accu-Tech , Anixter , Graybar Electric , KGP Logistics and Mayer Electric . The company's customers are in the aerospace, energy, telecommunications, and pharmaceutical industries. Its top five customers accounted for 52% of sales for 2014.

Circa posted revenue of about (Canadian) $28 million in 2014, a 6% increase from 2013's $22.6 million. Telecom sales jumped 26% in 2014 on the strength of sales to an OEM customer.

Net income reached $989,000 in 2014, a 30% increase from $764,000 in 2013.

The company generated (Canadian)$1.35 million in cash from operations in 2014, up from $900,000 in 2013.

In 2015, Circa acquired Alberta Computer Cable Inc., whcih makes and distributes digital connectivity products and specializes in custom cables and wiring harnesses for OEMs. The acquisition expands Circa's product line, extends its market reach, and bolsters its manufacturing capabilities.
