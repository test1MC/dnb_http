With a single-minded focus, Univenture ventures out to make eco-packaging products for all of life's recorded sights and sounds. Its lineup includes disc binders and wallets, multidisc products, sleeves, and DJ cases. The company makes automated disc packaging equipment, too. Univenture products are engineered to not only protect the media item but to use sustainable processes and biodegradable plastics in their manufacture. Among its firsts, the company introduced the safety-sleeve design. Univenture caters to corporations around the globe in entertainment, publishing, and computer software industries.

Univenture has sales and manufacturing operations in Marysville, Ohio; and Dongguan in the Guandong province of China. The company also operates across Europe, Middle East, and Africa through distributors.

The company's products are sold through its internal sales force and distributors and at Micro Electronics . Major customers include Corporate Disk, Disney Consumer Products, Eastman Kodak Co. , Eddie Bauer , Electronic Arts , Energizer , General Electric , Girl Scouts of America , Google , and Kohler Company .
