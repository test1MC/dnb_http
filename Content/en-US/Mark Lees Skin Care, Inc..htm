Dr. Lees can spot great skin from a mile away. His company, Mark Lees Skin Care, is a personal care products firm that specializes in skin care and operates a day spa in Florida. It makes cleansers, masks, moisturizers, sunscreens, and toners under brand names Concept Clean, Moisture Support System, Sensitive Science, Alpha Advance, Pept-Eyes, Mark Lees signature line, and others. The company also manufactures Hydramatte non-clogging makeup and produces instructional books and videos for its products. Customers can order products by calling, emailing, mailing, shopping through the company's Web site, or through local sales reps. President and CEO Dr. Mark Lees founded the company in 1983.
