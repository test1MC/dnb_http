If art indeed decks the walls at Masters Gallery Foods, it must all revolve around cheese. The company cuts, shreds, and packages private-label cheese products for sale to grocery retailers and deli operators; it also sells bulk products to food processing companies. Masters Gallery conducts its production, packaging, shipping, and warehousing services from its distribution plant in Plymouth, Wisconsin. VP Jeffrey Gentine is the son of company founder Leonard Gentine.
