Intermatic is into automatic equipment. The family-owned integrated manufacturer makes energy-control products for both industrial and residential use. The company manufactures home timers that include pool and spa controls, portable burglar alarms, and appliance timers. It makes night-lights and portable alarms, as well as outdoor lighting systems, such as its Malibu brand of low-voltage systems. Other products include occupancy/vacancy sensors, photo controls, LED and HID lighting, industrial surge protectors, weatherproof covers for electrical outlets, and solar-powered lights.

Intermatic sells its products directly through an online store, and through retailers and wholesalers in Canada, Europe, and the US, and Asia-Pacific, the Middle-East, and Africa.

Intermatic's product range includes a wide variety of energy management solutions and timed-based controls used in hundreds of applications, as well as pool and spa controls, professional lighting, sensors and photo controls, surge protection devices, and weatherproofing.

The company's industry affiliations include Air Conditioning Contractors of America, IMARK Group, Nalmco, Nemra, and Northeast Spa and Pool Association.

The company's diverse markets include a range of markets include Residential; Industrial (Agriculture, Aquaculture, Brewery, Factory/Manufacturing, and Food Processing); Commercial (Airport, Apartment Complex, Bank, Gym/Health Club/Spa, and Gas Station), and Government/Institutional (Airport, Capital/Embassy, Correctional Facility, Fire Station, Hospital, Library, and Military Complex).

To grow its business Intermatic's strategy is to keep investing in technology to improve the value of its products and its services.

In 2012 the company launched a number of new products, including the EJ600 Series In-Wall Timers and the AG3000 power surge preventer.

Chairman emeritus Douglas Kinney Sr. and his family own the company.

Intermatic was formed in 1891 as a maker of streetcar fare registers.
