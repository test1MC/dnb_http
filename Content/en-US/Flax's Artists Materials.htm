Flax's Artists Materials pushes the edge of the envelope. The company operates through its FLAX art & design supply store, stocking more than 40,000 products to satisfy the hobbyist and professional alike. The store carries the proper utensils for painting, drawing, silk screening, calligraphy, sculpting, carving, and more. It even offers arts and crafts supplies specially designed for children. FLAX also sells fine pens, stationery and specialty papers, journals, frames, and gifts. The family-owned art supply firm was founded in 1938 by Herman Flax. Unable to turn a profit on its direct business, Flax folded its catalog and e-commerce operations in 2007.
