What else can be built factory tough? Your new Colonial-style home! Apex Homes makes custom modular homes, condominiums, and light commercial structures for the wholesale market across the East Coast. It offers dozens of Apex-brand floor plans with styles ranging from chalet and ranch to split-level and cape designs; options include breakfast rooms, window dormers, and mud rooms. The company also sells plans for two- to four-bedroom homes bearing the Alpine brand. Apex Homes partners with M&T Bank to provide financing services. The late Robert Nipple founded the company in 1989.
