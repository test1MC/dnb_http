Most consumers weren't born yesterday, but Mayborn Group's may be the exception. The group makes and distributes baby products under brand names Tommee Tippee (infant and toddler feeding accessories) and Sangenic (diaper disposal systems), and under some private labels. It also owns sub-brands Close to Nature, Soother, and Explora. Reaching more than 70 countries, the group still produces dyes in addition to household cleaners under various labels. Mayborn Group began as a maker of fabric dyes in 1946 and, years later, extended its offerings to baby and other goods.
