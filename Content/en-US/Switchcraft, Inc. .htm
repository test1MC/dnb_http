Switchcraft products take the magic out of making connections. The company makes jacks, plugs, switches, power cords, connectors, and molded cable assemblies. Its more than 5,000 standard products are sold to customers in the pro audio, video, computer, communications, appliance, military, medical, instrumentation, and transportation industries. Switchcraft has manufacturing facilities in the US and South Korea. The company sells products globally through distributors and sales reps. In 2005 California-based private equity firm ClearLight Partners invested in Switchcraft, which was founded in Chicago in 1946. Switchcraft was acquired by HEICO in late 2011.
