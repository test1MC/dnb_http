Macrolink designs and builds computer hardware components, including ruggedized enclosures, chassis, portable workstations, communications controllers, and storage systems. The company specializes in expansion products for computers from Concurrent Computer ; Macrolink has supplied products for the VMEbus market since 1987. The company markets its products worldwide, through independent sales representatives in North America and distributors elsewhere. It has counted Boeing , General Dynamics , Lockheed Martin , NASA , Raytheon , and Rockwell Collins among its customers. Macrolink was established in 1975 by David Vednor. It was acquired by B/E Aerospace in 2011.
