Need advice on how to fix your home? HomeAdvisor (formerly ServiceMagic) helps you hire residential contractors with the click of a mouse. The company operates an online marketplace where homeowners can find information about qualified contractors and service professionals in their area and post projects and service requests for contractors to bid on. HomeAdvisor fields requests for services in more than 500 categories, including bathroom remodeling, locksmiths, and handymen. It has more than 80,000 pre-screened businesses that pay fees for leads received as a result of the service. Michael Beaudoin and Rodney Rice started the firm as ServiceMagic in 1998. Today it is a part of IAC/InterActiveCorp (IAC).

The company has international operations in Canada, France, the Netherlands, and the UK.

In addition to its online marketplace, HomeAdvisor also provides Internet marketing services for small and medium-sized businesses through its Market Hardware subsidiary. Specifically, Market Hardware offers website design and hosting services, as well as search engine optimization and "online reputation management" for professionals. Market Hardware serves more than 5,000 small business clients across the US and in Canada.

HomeAdvisor also offers several mobile applications, including the HomeAdvisor.com, Home911, and HomeSavvy iOS and Android applications.

In 2013 HomeAdvisor introduced three new subscription offerings for home services professionals. The basic membership package includes membership in the HomeAdvisor network, as well the inclusion of the home services professional's contact information in HomeAdvisor's online directory.

The company has used partnerships to expand its business. It has also consistently rolled out new apps. In 2014 HomeAdvisor released its DesignMine app, an inspiration and collaboration design tool for homeowners to create and share personalized design boards. The DesignMine app is part of the company's suite of products available for iPad users.
