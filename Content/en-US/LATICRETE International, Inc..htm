LATICRETE gets tile to latch on to walls and floors without slipping an inch. The company manufactures and sells cement and latex adhesives, epoxy grouts, and waterproof membrane products for ceramic tile and stone installation systems. LATICRETE operates around the globe; its customers include architectural firms, general contractors, and distribution companies. LATICRETE's products have been used in the Petronas Towers, the St. Louis Gateway Arch, and on Disney 's world headquarters. The firm was founded by chemical engineer Henry Rothberg in 1956 and still is owned by the Rothberg family.

LATICRETE makes and markets green flooring and facade materials, used in residential, commercial, and industrial applications.

The company operates in North America (including Mexico), Europe, Africa, the Middle East, Central America and the Caribbean, South America, Southeast Asia (including the Philippines), India, China, and Australia.

Sold via local distributors, dealers, or online, LATICRETE's products are used in residential, commercial, and industrial applications.

The company is focused on providing the construction industry with innovative adhesives, mortars, materials and technology for the installation and repair of floors and facades.
