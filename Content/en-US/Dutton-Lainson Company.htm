Dutton-Lainson has something for everyone who labors. The company manufactures and sells equipment for marine, industrial, auto, and agriculture markets around the world. Its manufacturing arm turns out anchors, winches, couplers, and fence stretchers, fuel filers, jacks, and trailer accessories, as well as wheel bearings and protectors. Custom manufacturing services handle metal stamping, machining, and finishing, tooling and dying, welding and brazing. Printing services, offered by its Cornhusker Press, include electronic prepress, design and layout, brochures, and business forms. Founded in 1886, Dutton-Lainson grew from a harness and horse collar shop. It is owned and led by members of the Lainson family.
