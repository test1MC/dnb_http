Omega Diagnostics can help determine if you have an an infectious disease, an autoimmune disorder, or simply cannot tolerate gluten. The company provides in vitro diagnostic tests for a range of ailments including anemia, allergies, food intolerance, and infectious disease. It specializes in diagnostics for such diseases as dengue fever, syphilis, and tuberculosis. Subsidiary Genesis Diagnostics Ltd. focuses on autoimmune and allergy diagnostic products, while Cambridge Nutritional Sciences provides tests and laboratory services for food allergies and intolerances. Omega Diagnostic products are distributed to clinics, hospitals, blood banks, and laboratories in more than 100 countries.
