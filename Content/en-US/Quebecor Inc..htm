This company is the king of all Canadian media, or at least the operations of Quebecor Media . Quebecor is a holding company that owns Quebecor Media, a publicly traded conglomerate with telecommunications, broadcasting, publishing, and various retail assets. Though Quebecor itself has no real operations, it has given the Péladeau family, led by Pierre Karl Péladeau, a vehicle to build its business empire mostly through acquisitions. The Péladeau family owns about 85% of Quebecor and Pierre Karl Péladeau controls some 70% of its votes.
