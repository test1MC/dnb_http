Clean Seal seals the deal when its customers need to get a grip. The company makes extruded rubber products such as grip seals, trim moldings, and hose protection systems. Clean Seal's supporting operations include die cutting, cutting and notching, cut to length, splicing (hot and cold), mounting clamps, and other manufacturing services. End products include hoses, seals, and trims for the automotive, marine, military, recreational vehicle, and transportation industries. The company also extrudes plastic parts. Clean Seal keeps more than 5 million ft. of extruded rubber products in inventory.
