A hose by any other name might not smell as sweet to Manuli Rubber Industries. The company is a leading manufacturer of rubber components and systems for transmitting fluids. Manuli's hydraulics division supplies high-pressure hoses, couplings, adaptors, and other fittings for construction, mining, off-highway vehicles, and machine tool applications. Its Fluiconnecto unit distributes hydraulic components to construction, mining, agriculture, marine, oil and gas, and other industrial customers. The company's small oil and marine group makes floating and subsea large-bore reinforced rubber hoses and elastomeric pipelines. Manuli has operations in more than 20 countries worldwide.
