Stremicks Heritage Foods is the dairy company behind the big brands. Focused on the production of specialized dairy products, the company offers organic milk and associated dairy foods under the Heritage, 8th Continent, and other labels. Additionally, the family-owned firm provides packaging and distribution services for food giant Nestlé , churning out NesQuik flavored milks and Coffee-mate creamers. Stremicks subsidiary Kern's Beverages makes fruit nectars. The company's foodservice operations supply ice cream, novelties, ice cream and soft-serve mixes, coffee creamer, and fluid milk to a variety of customers, such as Arby's , Del Taco , Jack in the Box , and Disneyland .

Based in California, Stremicks sells its products nationwide and serves as a contract manufacturer for global food businesses.

To support its business, Stremicks operates production facilities in Riverside, California; Cedar City, Utah; Joplin, Missouri; and Mexicali, Mexico.

Subsidiary Kern's Beverages makes and markets 18 different fruit nectars, as well as Latin American milk and rice drink horchata. Its majority-owned Jasper Products LLC, based in Missouri, makes refrigerated and shelf-stable milks, coffee creamers, juices, teas, soups, and other items.

The company's products are available in the US through major grocery chains and independent retailers. Its organic milk is sold in Arizona, California, Nevada, Texas, and in the Midwest through major supermarkets.

Stremicks sells its specialized dairy products, such as ice cream novelties, fluid milks, and other items, to foodservice operators the likes of fast food restaurants and entertainment venues. Under license with Hain Celestial , Stremicks makes Rice Dream and Soy Dream alternative milks. The company makes products for food behemoth Nestlé under license.

The dairy company boosted its slim portfolio of owned brands when it bought soy milk manufacturer 8th Continent from its 50-50 joint venture owners, General Mills and DuPont . The deal allowed Stremicks to expand its substitute dairy beverage brands -- Soy Dream and Rice Dream -- with the 8th Continent Soymilk name.

Stremicks was founded by CEO Lou Stremick in 1988.
