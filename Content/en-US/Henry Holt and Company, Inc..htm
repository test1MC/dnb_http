Henry Holt and Company is an old hand at the books business. Founded in 1866, the company publishes books of American and international fiction, as well as biographies, children's books, and volumes on history, politics, science, psychology, and health. The company also publishes books under other imprints, including Metropolitan Books (provocative fiction and non-fiction authors), Times Books (science, business, and current events, co-published with the New York Times ), Holt Paperbacks (paperback fiction) and Henry Holt Books for Young Readers (children's and young adult books). Henry Holt operates as part of Holtzbrinck Publishers .
