New Wave Group has a lot of style -- it isn't stuck in the 1980s with A Flock of Seagulls haircut. The retail company boasts a portfolio of about 40 brands in Europe of promotional apparel and gifts, work clothes, athletic clothing, men's and women's casual wear, shoes, glassware, and home décor. Its brands include Cutter & Buck (golf apparel), Sköna Marie (shoes), Orrefors Kosta Boda (glassware), and ProJob (work clothes). The company also is licensed to sell the Easton , Speedo , and Umbro brands in Northern Europe. New Wave Group was founded in 1990 by Torsten Jansson, the group's largest shareholder as well as its chairman and CEO. In 2011 the company bought Ahead (hats) and Paris Glove of Canada (gloves).
