United Spirits stocks a full bar on the Indian continent. The country's #1 liquor company (and the world's #3 company behind Diageo and Pernod Ricard ) markets more than 140 brands of whisky, vodka, rum, brandy, scotch, and imported wine. United Spirits sells more than 120 million cases per year, most of which is produced at its 88 distilleries and bottling factories. The company enjoys a 60% market share in India and exports to more than 35 countries in Asia, the Middle East, and Africa. United Spirits is part of Indian billionaire Vijay Mallya's UB Group, which also owns United Breweries and Kingfisher Airlines . London-based Diageo controls the company.
