Keeping entertained or catching up on business on long airplane trips may be easier, thanks to ASI Entertainment. The company develops communications systems for the airline industry. Subsidiary ASiQ holds the rights to a wireless device and related software designed to enable the use of cellular phones during airline flights. The technology, known as SafeCell, is intended to minimize interference with flight communications and avionics systems, while enabling passengers to send e-mail and text messages through a wireless Internet connection. Though legally based in the US, the company operates from Victoria, Australia.
