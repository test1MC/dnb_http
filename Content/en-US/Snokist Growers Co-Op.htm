Picky, picky, picky. And that's just the beginning of the story at Snokist Growers. The agricultural cooperative's  member/farmers grow their fruit on their California, Oregon, and Washinton State orchards. After harvesting, the Snokist cooperative processes, cans, and sells the fruit. The co-op's canned Snokist-branded fruits include pears, apples, cherries, and plums. Also offering private-label services, the Snokist cannery is located just east of Yakima, and the cooperatives products are sold in the US and overseas. The company ceased its fresh fruit operations in 2008.
