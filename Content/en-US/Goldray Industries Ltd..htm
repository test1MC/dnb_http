Goldray helps to shed light on the situation. It designs and manufactures architectural and decorative glass. The company customizes its products using such techniques as ceramic frit application, graphic lamination, heat treating, slip resistant texturing, and LCBA (low cure baked acrylic) coloring. Goldray's products are used for applications including entryways, elevator cabs, office partitions, skylights, wall paneling, window treatments, and counter tops.
