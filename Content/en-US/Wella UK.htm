Wella UK serves the professional and consumer hair care markets. Through its professional division, salons can order Wella hair care products (Wellastrate, High Hair, Blondor, Lifetex, Koleston Perfect, Color Touch, and Magma), as well as salon equipment, furnishings, and training through the Wella UK website. The company's consumer unit is managed by ultimate owner Procter & Gamble , which sells the products nationwide through supermarkets. Wella UK traces its historical roots back to Germany in the 19th century, when it was founded by hairdresser Franz Strohe.
