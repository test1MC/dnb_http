Kathabar Dehumidification Systems, a subsidiary of Niagara Blower Company, manufactures dehumidification and heat recovery systems for controlling temperature and humidity conditions in industrial, commercial, and institutional settings. Its systems, based on the use of lithium chloride to scrub and dehumidify air, were developed in the 1930s. The company serves the food and beverage, plastics and glass processing, and health care industries, among others. It sells its products in North and South America and Europe. Industrial heat transfer and refrigeration equipment manufacturer Niagara Blower Company acquired Kathabar in 2008 from Ross Air Systems.
