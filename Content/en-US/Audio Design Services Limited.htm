Audio Design Services (ADS) wants the world to hear whatever it is you have to say over the din of everyday life. ADS makes amplifiers, home theater systems, microphones, loudspeakers, remotes, rach systems, and other audio equipment under the ADS Worldwide brand. Many of its products are used for public address systems in places such as airports, railways, public buildings, and stadiums. The company works with customers in designing its products to blend in with whatever environment they are to be used. Products are sold through specialty retailers in more than 40 countries worldwide, as well as via the company's website. The company was founded in 1993.

ADS specializes in loudspeakers for use in what the company terms the "hostile" noise environment, such as Marks and Spencer , Matalan Stores , London Stock Exchange , and the London Underground . Its public address systems have been installed in airports, education establishments, government buildings, historic buildings, holiday resorts, hotels, indoor arenas, courts, leisure and fitness centers, oil rigs, places of worship, railway stations, ships, shopping malls, sports stadiums, and theaters.
