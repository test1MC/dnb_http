Air Products Healthcare supplies the air you breathe when you're in need of home health care services. Part of the Gases and Equipment segment of the giant industrial gas company Air Products and Chemicals , the division is comprised of a number of different regional businesses that supply medical gases for the home health care market. Formed in 1999 Air Products Healthcare operates along the East Coast of the US. Subsidiary companies include American Homecare Supply, Nightingale Medical, Rx Pharmacy Services, and Vanguard Home Medical Equipment.
