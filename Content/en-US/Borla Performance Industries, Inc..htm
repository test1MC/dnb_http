Borla Performance Industries leads the way when it comes to designing and manufacturing automotive stainless steel performance exhaust systems. The company began operating in the early 1980s as a manufacturer of exhaust systems for Rolls-Royce and Ferrari cars. Since that time, Borla Performance Industries has grown to be a premier manufacturer of aircraft-quality stainless steel performance systems for street, off-road, and racing vehicles. Borla even backs its street exhaust products with million-mile warranties. Its products, which also include mufflers, polished tips, and its XR-1 Raceline products, can be found on the streets of Australia and New Zealand, Europe, Asia, the Middle East, and the Americas.
