China Motor Corporation (CMC) has been on a roll since 1969. CMC makes a variety of vehicles and auto parts for customers throughout Asia and beyond. The company manufactures Mitsubishi sedans (Grunder, Lancer, Colt), through a technical collaboration agreement, and CMC minivans (Savrin, Zinger), light commercial vehicles (Delica, Veryca), as well as auto parts for Mitsubishi and non-Mitsubishi brands. CMC operates three manufacturing plants in Taiwan. CMC's joint ventures on the Mainland, including China's South East (Fujian) Motor (SEM), with Fujian Motor Industrial Group (FJMG) and Mitsubishi, and Fujian Daimler Automotive, with Daimler and FJMG, accelerate its market presence and manufacturing capacity.
