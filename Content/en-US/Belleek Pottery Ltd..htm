Belleek Pottery heavily discourages the act of re-gifting. Known for its traditional parian china pottery designs, Belleek Pottery is a leading UK giftware manufacturer. Its products include bowls, candlesticks, clocks, lamps, picture frames, platters, and vases. The company operates close to 20 stores throughout Ireland and England. Belleek Pottery became what it is today by an aggressive acquisition strategy, which included the purchases of Galway Crystal, Aynsley China, Donegal Parian China. The company is owned by Dr. George Moore (chairman).

Belleek Pottery has three centers - Belleek Tea Room, Belleek Pottery Museum, and Belleek Pottery Tour.

The company sells its products via stores and online.

Belleek Pottery targeted the US retail market in 2011 and started a new retail operation, which sells, markets, and distributes the Belleek, Galway Irish Crystal, and Aynsley China brands.

The pottery maker was established in 1857.
