Too hot at your office? ICS Cool Energy provides products that ensure the air gets a bit more frosty. Serving the UK and Europe, the company designs and manufactures cooling and refrigeration equipment such as air and water-cooled chillers, cooling towers, air blast coolers, and regular air conditioning units. Its products are available for rental and are geared toward the food and beverage, manufacturing, pharmaceutical, petrochemical, and disaster support industries; hospitals and ice skating rinks also use the company's equipment. In addition, ICS Cool Energy provides parts supply and repair and maintenance services.

The company is headquartered in Southampton, UK. It has offices in Birmingham, Bradford, London, Scotland, and Ireland. ICS Cool Energy operates European offices out of Germany, Switzerland, Austria, Poland, Italy, France, and the Netherlands. In addition, it has a network of re-sellers in Asia, South America, Australia, and Russia.

Experiencing a rise in its customer base within the Northern borders of the UK, ICS Cool Energy in 2015 expanded its position in Scotland by moving to a larger office while also opening a Scottish depot. With the move, the company is aiming to improve lead times for those customers situated in and around Scotland.

ICS Cool Energy expands its core offerings through the use of acquisitions. In 2014 the company acquired MTA UK, a specialist in compressed air and gas treatment products. The acquisition gave ICS Cool Energy a broader focus on the OEM market and additional expertise in the compressed air treatment field.
