At Renault Trucks UK, the accent may be French, but the emphasis is on British sales. From its base in Dunstable, Renault Trucks UK provides support services for the UK's 70 or so Renault truck dealers and customers. It has a dealership network comprising over 1,600 service outlets selling new and used trucks used for long haul transportation, construction and heavy construction, and distribution services. Renault Trucks UK customers have ranged from Iceland Foods Group to Transwaste Recycling and Aggregates. Renault Trucks is a subsidiary of the Volvo Group .
