It's clear what Deft makes: clear wood finishes and more. Founded in 1953, the company manufactures coatings for both the residential (interior wood stains and finishes) and industrial (aerospace and transportation coatings) markets. Its home products include clear wood finishes, interior and exterior clear polyurethanes, and wood stains. Its aerospace products include polyurethane and epoxy primers and coatings. Deft manufactures its coatings from two plants in California and Ohio. Its products can be bought from hardware suppliers and paint-supply stores such as Do It Best , Lowe's , Home Depot , and Wal-Mart . The company is owned and run by members of the founding Desmond family.
