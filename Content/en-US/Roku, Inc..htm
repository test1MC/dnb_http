If you ever lamented not being able to acquire masterpieces by Michelangelo or da Vinci, Roku might have a product to ease your sorrows. The company designs digital media players -- including the Roku HD1000 -- that allow users to display digital photos on HDTV and offers digital image galleries that include famous works of art. Its SoundBridge player, which lets digital music files stored on PCs to be played on home stereos, can be connected either wirelessly or with Ethernet. Roku also offers the entire Netflix streaming library for Netflix members. Roku's products are sold at electronic retail stores such as Best Buy and Fry's Electronics . The company is said to be eyeing an IPO.
