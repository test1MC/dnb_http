Fine Foods International (FFI) aims to serve a fine cup of coffee. Along with its sister company, Fine Foods International (Manufacturing), it supplies UK food retailers and the the country's food service industry with 75 different coffee blends, 50 ground and roast products, and 40 other hot-beverage drinks. Many of its coffees are repackaged under other brand names. FFI's product range includes a variety of organic and fair-trade offerings that are sourced by sister company Wertform GmbH. The company's customers have included ASDA , Safeway , Somerfield , and Tesco , among others. FFI is part of Hamburg, Germany's CAFEA Group, which itself operates about a dozen divisions.

FFI specializes in making and marketing soluble coffee in granulated form, a Brit favorite. To this end, its plant located just north of London in the town of Dunstable is where it focuses its production efforts. FFI has access to more than half a dozen plants companywide through its parent. In turn, FFI offers its parent distribution across the UK and the US.
