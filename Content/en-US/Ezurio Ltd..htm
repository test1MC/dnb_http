Ezurio makes wireless networking components based on the Bluetooth and 802.11 (Wi-Fi) transmission standards. Its embedded devices are sold primarily to computer and wireless device manufacturers for incorporation into their products. They are also used in automotive, medical, and retail applications. Ezurio also makes PC cards for consumers. The company was spun off by TDK Systems Europe in 2004. The company is part of Laird Technologies .

Ezurio's products include short range wireless machine-to-machine products, which offer data capture and secure voice, as well as data transmission through Bluetooth and 802.11.

Ezurio is based in London.
