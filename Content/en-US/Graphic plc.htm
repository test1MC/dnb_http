Graphic plc paints its picture with PCBs. The company manufactures printed circuit boards (PCBs) -- rigid and flexible multilayer, advanced substrates, and custom products -- used in automotive, broadcasting, commercial, defense, and industrial applications. It also offers prototyping and engineering services. Through Graphic's manufacturing facilities in the UK, South China, and Denmark, the company provides small, medium, and large volume production services to customers throughout Europe, Asia, and the Americas. Its Dongguan, China, manufacturing facility is a joint venture (since 2007) with Somacis, an Italian firm. Graphic was founded in 1968.
