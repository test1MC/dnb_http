Some companies in Eastern Europe turn to Eurocontrol Technics for technology to measure and control electrical signals. The company's operating affiliate, Ganz Instruments, makes a variety of electronic instruments; its wares find their way to energy utilities, industrial firms, schools, and laboratories in Hungary. Eurocontrol Technics distributes equipment made by Ganz through its 50%-owned subsidiary, C & D Automatika.
