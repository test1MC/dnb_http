Penobscot McCrum, which does business as Penobscot Frozen Foods, is a leading producer of frozen potato products. It makes such items as potato rounds, potato skins, and potato wedges, as well as frozen baked and mashed potatoes. It supplies the frozen potato products to foodservice operators and other customers. Potato supplier County Super Spuds acquired Penobscot Frozen Foods (founded in 1952) out of bankruptcy in 2004 to form Penobscot McCrum.
