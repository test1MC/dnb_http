Route1 doesn't want remote workers to feel out of touch or insecure about their work. The company develops remote access systems for employees on the go. Available on a subscription basis, its MobiNET platform manages identity validation and access authorization. MobiNET works in conjunction with MobiKey, a portable USB device that allows workers to access their PC desktop and network resources from any Internet-enabled, Windows-based PC. Route 1 serves customers in the financial services, government, health care, and telecommunications sectors. The company was formed through a reverse merger in 2004.
