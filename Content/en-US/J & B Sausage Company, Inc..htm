The J & B Sausage Company knows how to meat hungry Texans' need for good food. The company (which does business as J Bar B Foods) offers value-added meat products, including a number of different brands of sausages and barbeque. Its best-known brand is Texas Smokehouse Sausage, a line which includes barbecue, briskets, hickory-smoked sausage and marinated items. It also produces private-label sausages, such as Earl Campbell Foods. J Bar B uses QX sausage-making process at its plant in Weimar, Texas, which is intended to reduce pathogens via a process that cooks products inside their packages. The family-owned and -operated company was founded by Felix and Billie Mae Janecka in 1959.
