Established in 1905, Kokuyo sells business stationery and office furniture worldwide. Stationery products include notebooks, binders, albums, tape, and pencils. It also sells office furniture including desks, tables, chairs, and cabinets. Kokuyo sells store fixtures such as display shelves, counters, showcases, and shopping carts. Key subsidiaries include Kokuyo S&T (paper products and stationery goods), Kokuyo Furniture (office furniture), Kokuyo Store Creation (store fixtures), Kokuyo International (overseas marketing operations), and Kokuyo Commerce (mail order office supplies in China). Kokuyo owns and operates facilities in China, India, Japan, Malaysia, Thailand, the UK, the US, and Vietnam.
