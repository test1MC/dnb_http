Though named to the contrary, Topline Products Company is just as dependent on its bottom line as the next guy. The firm, a manufacturer of cosmetics and fragrance packaging, was founded in 1975. Its product offerings include dial-up lipstick holders, lip gloss containers, compacts, makeup jars and pots, and eyeliner bottles. In addition to packaging components, Topline also creates finished (or "filled") products, and an array of personal care formulas for the cosmetic and fragrance industries. The company's products are made at its five facilities in China, and sold worldwide through sales offices in Los Angeles and Paris. Customers include L'Oréal USA.
