Spang & Company operates three manufacturing divisions. Its magnetics segment makes passive electronic components for consumer, industrial, and military electronics applications. Spang Power Electronics makes dry transformers and power control systems. Spang Engineered Solutions makes custom inductive components.The company is certified on several international quality standards and is compliant on the European Commission's restriction of certain hazardous substances directive. Spang has manufacturing facilities in Arkansas, Ohio, and Pennsylvania, and in China. The company was established in 1894 by George Spang and has been controlled by the Spang family since 1908.

Spang is headquartered in Pittburgh and has operations, including production and distribution centers, elsewhere in the US and in several countries.

Customers incorporate Spang's products in a number of industrial applications including glass, polysilicon, coatings, fibers, rectifiers, and heating.
