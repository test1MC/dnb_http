Bright Food has a hearty appetite for other companies. The government-owned conglomerate has interests in food companies in its home country and overseas. Bright Food has stakes in three publicly traded Chinese companies -- dairy products manufacturer Bright Dairy, logistics firm Shanghai HaiBo, and Shanghai Maling Aquarius, a diverse maker and canner of meat, seafood, fruit, vegetables, bottled water, and other food. It also owns a candy company, Big White Rabbit, makes rice wine, and has a retail network of 3,300 supermarkets and convenience stores. Outside of China, Bright Food owns majority stakes in Australia's Manassen Foods and UK cereal company Weetabix .
