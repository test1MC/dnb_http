When most folks see a junkyard as an eyesore, Kirk's Automotive sees profits. The company rebuilds automotive parts such as brake systems, electrical components, engines, and hydraulic systems. The company also distributes a full line of OEM parts for cars, trucks, buses, forklifts, and industrial vehicles, including NAPA-branded parts. Products include everything from alternators to wiper systems. Kirk's Automotive was founded in 1946.
