Video may have killed the radio star, but it's been pretty good to Video Display. The company makes and distributes flat-panel, projection, and cathode-ray tube (CRT) display systems. Its products -- both new and reconditioned -- are often customized for specific needs such as space limitations or being ruggedized for adverse environments. They are targeted at niche settings such as military training and simulation displays among other applications. Its data display business (about 10% of sales) focuses on CRTs for uses such as medical monitoring equipment and computer terminals. Its largest customer is the US government, primarily the Department of Defense (more than 40% of sales).
