Shinsho manufactures and sells a diversified line of metal products. Steel products and materials (iron ore) account for most of Shinsho's sales. The company makes auto parts, metallurgy manufacturing equipment, and welding materials. In addition to steel-based products, Shinsho manufactures nonferrous metal products, such as aluminum sheet, leadframes, aluminum photoconductor drums, and copper sheet and strip. The company, founded in 1946, sells primarily to customers in Japan. Kobe Steel , one of Japan's largest steel producers, is a top customer.

Shinsho has its head office in Osaka and Tokyo. Branch offices reside in Nagoya, Kobe, and Kyushu, while representative offices are in Beijing and Dubai. The company has launched a number of projects in the US, China, Southeast Asia, and other regions throughout the world.

From 2013 to 2014, Shinsho's revenues climbed by 68% due to increased sales, additional dividend income, gains on revaluation of derivatives, and the absence of a loss on impairment of fixed assets and taxes.

Shinsho's international strategy includes advancing its business development in China and the ASEAN countries based on its operations in Thailand.
