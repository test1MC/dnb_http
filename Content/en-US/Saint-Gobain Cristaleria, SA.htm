Saint-Gobain Cristalería has looked into its crystal ball and seen a shiny future. The company -- formerly Cristalería Española -- is Spain's leading manufacturer of glass products for the automobile and building industries. The company and Saint-Gobain subsidiaries serve the Iberian peninsula and is part of the Spain, Portugal, and Morocco General Delegation. The company's Saint-Gobain Vicasa affiliate manufactures glass containers. Saint-Gobain Cristalería also makes insulation materials (rock wool and glass wool). The company is part of French industrial giant Compagnie de Saint-Gobain , which has operations around the globe, including Saint-Gobain de Colombia and Saint-Gobain Glass Mexico.
