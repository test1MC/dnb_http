Blue Tower Communications manufactures automated meter-reading systems for the utility industry. The company's products include wireless transponders which connect to electric, gas, and water meters and electronically transmit information to hand-held or vehicle-mounted receivers used by meter reading personnel. This energy usage data is fed into the billing system of the utility provider. Products are sold under the Blu Tower brand. The company sells its 900 Series to utilities companies in Australia, New Zealand, and the US; its 400 Series is designed for Europe and other regions.

Based in the UK, the company maintains an office for the US market in Florida.

The company was formed in 2005 when contract electronics manufacturer Kenure Developments acquired the assets of bankrupt meter-reading systems maker Advanced Technology. New management was installed and the company name was changed to the enigmatic Blue Tower Communications.
