This company's jive talk was keeping music fans happy. Zomba Label Group operated top R&B and hip-hop imprints Jive Records and LaFace Records, boasting a roster full of popular artists such as R. Kelly, Outkast, Britney Spears, Justin Timberlake, and Usher. Zomba also operated gospel labels GospoCentric Records and Verity Records, which produced albums from such artists as Kurt Carr, Dorinda Clark Cole, Deitrick Haddon, Donnie McClurkin, and Joann Rosario. Zomba Records was founded in 1975 by partners Clive Calder and Ralph Simon. Zomba was absorbed by Sony Music Entertainment .
