Camozzi S.p.A., a member of the Camozzi Group, has got a pneumatic lease on life. Camozzi S.p.A. focuses on the group's core business, pneumatics; it manufactures components used for industrial automation. Other subsidiary companies within the group make machine tools (Innse-Berardi, Retco, Ingersoll Machine Tools , and others), textile machines (Marzoli, Vouk), and nuclear energy products (Ansaldo-Camozzi). Group products are sold in more than 70 countries around the world. Founded in 1964 by the Camozzi family, the Camozzi Group is owned and operated by chairman Attilio Camozzi.
