Crackle (formerly Grouper Networks), the online video network of Sony Pictures Entertainment , hopes to bring a bit of snap and pop to your laptop. The company provides original content developed specifically for the Web, as well as online programming from Sony's library of television series and feature films. Crackle's content can be found on Crackle.com, as well as on third party outlets such as YouTube , Myspace , and Hulu , and via mobile carriers such as Sprint , Verizon , and AT&T . Crackle's website consists of about 10 categories of programming, including comedy, music, and animation; its target audience is young males.
