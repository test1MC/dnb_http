Taking orders from the boss is no problem for Quality Shipyards. A subsidiary of fleet operator Tidewater , Quality Shipyards builds, repairs, and modifies vessels primarily for its parent company, which serves customers in the offshore energy industry worldwide. Quality Shipyards operates two shipyards in Houma, Louisiana, on the Gulf Coast. Its projects have included deepwater platform supply vessels as well as towboats. Other customers have included the US Army and Kirby Corporation . For Kirby the company designed a special double hulled vessel, a unique feature in towboats.
