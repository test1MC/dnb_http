imagelinx knows that appearances count. The company provides packaging graphics services to consumer goods makers in more than 100 markets throughout the globe. imagelinx works with designers, artists, and printers to produce film, metal, and paper packaging. Services include pre-press, proofing, consulting, and digital asset management. Among its portfolio of clients are big-name brands such as Carlsberg , Groupe Danone , Novartis , and P&G . imagelinx was founded in 1983 and has operations in France, Germany, the UK, and the US.  LTG Technologies, a family-owned packaging printing press manufacturer, owns more than 35% of imagelinx.
