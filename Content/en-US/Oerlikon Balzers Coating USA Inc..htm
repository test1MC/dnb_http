Oerlikon Balzers Coating USA Inc., a subsidiary of Switzerland-based OC Oerlikon Corporation AG , offers coating services for metalworking tools and precision components. Its PVD (physical vapor deposition) coatings are marketed under the BALINIT brand name. The company's coatings, which are extremely thin but hard, are designed to impart wear resistance and increase the reliability of tools, medical instruments, and plastic bottle preforms. Oerlikon Balzers Coating USA also also offers contract coating services through its network of 12 North American coating centers. The first coating center in North America opened in 1984.
