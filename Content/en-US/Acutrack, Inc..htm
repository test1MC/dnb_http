If you need to lay down some tracks, look no further than Acutrack. The company is a full service CD and DVD production firm for large-scale and small-scale distribution of audio, video, software, and document files. Its services include CD and DVD authoring, replication, and duplication, as well as packaging, printing, graphic design, copy protection, and shipping and fulfillment. Acutrack also provides Blu-ray disc authoring, production, and duplication services. Acutrack's customers come from the telecom, health care, financial services, manufacturing, and retail industries. The company was founded in 1992 by CEO Raj Barman.
