Virgin Drinks wants to take some of the fizz out of soft-drink venerables Coca-Cola and Pepsi . Having first launched operations as Virgin Cola in 1994, the company has reached beyond colas to make and distribute energy drinks, tea, flavored colas, vodka, and other alcoholic beverages. Altogether, the company boasts more than 30 products in its beverage lineup. Selling some 1 million products globally each day, Virgin Drinks peddles its products in the UK, US, Canada, France, China, Italy, Switzerland, Japan, Sweden, Iraq, and other countries. Virgin Drinks runs its business through franchise and licensing agreements and offers marketing and technical support.

The company is based in London, and sells its drinks throughout the world.

Virgin Drinks makes and distributes energy drinks, tea, and flavored colas, as well as vodka and other alcoholic beverages.

Although the company's plan to dethrone the reigning cola kings hasn't quite worked out, Virgin Drinks has not given up. The company has regrouped, focusing its soft drink business on markets where it has been more successful and adding product lines that include alcoholic drinks and soft drink alternatives such as fast-growing energy drinks. The company also has made inroads to China, where rivals Coca-Cola and Pepsi are not yet synonymous with soft drinks.
