Hewlett-Packard Australia provides enterprise and consumer customers with a wide range of information technology products and services. The company's computing and electronics products include personal computers, servers, storage devices, printers, cameras, displays, and networking equipment. Its software portfolio includes operating systems and print management tools, as well as business software that encompasses application, network infrastructure, and product life cycle management. Hewlett-Packard Australia's IT service organization offers consulting, systems integration, support, training, outsourcing, and financing. The company is a subsidiary of US-based Hewlett-Packard .
