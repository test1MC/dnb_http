Milk gives Dairygold Co-operative its muscle, but retail helps its members build their muscle. The Irish farmer-owned cooperative processes dairy products, including ingredients, which it sells worldwide. Dairygold's retail arm includes 30 co-op Superstores, selling farm products, along with home-improvement and building materials. In 2007 Dairygold, along with two other Irish dairy companies, the Kerry Group and Shinagh Estates formed the Munster AI Group to provide agricultural services to Ireland's Munster region. Dairygold owns 66% of the operation.
