Large global companies stay agile by responding to market conditions throughout the world. Agilent Technologies UK has been doing this for parent Agilent Technologies (and its predecessor, Hewlett-Packard ) for more than 40 years. The US-based parent company is a top global maker of instrumentation equipment, scientific laboratory instruments, and semiconductors. Agilent serves customers in the chemical, communications, and life sciences industries. Agilent Technologies UK works with the National Laboratory Service, a unit of the UK Environment Agency, to train operators on Agilent instruments.

Besides sales and marketing operations, the UK subsidiary based in Edinburgh, carries out manufacturing, as well. Its factory in the Queensferry area of Edinburgh, which makes test equipment, has been in operation for four decades.
