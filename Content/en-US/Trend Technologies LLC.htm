Responding to the trend of increased global demand for components, Trend Technologies manufactures metal and plastic enclosures and casings for the automotive, computer, medical, networking, and communications industries. It offers mold and die fabrication, soft tool metal fabrication, plastic injection molding, metal stamping, finishing, and assembly. The company has expertise in every part of the engineering process, including design, tooling, production, assembly and testing, quality control, and logistics. Trend Technologies has plants in North America (US and Mexico), Ireland, China, Malaysia, and Singapore, as well as a design office in India.
