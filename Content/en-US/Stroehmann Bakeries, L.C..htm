Dagwood Bumstead would appreciate Stroehmann Bakeries. Mr. B. (as the neighbor kid, Elmo, always calls him) could certainly make one of his formidable sandwiches using the company's products, which include some 10 different varieties of prepackaged, sliced, white, wheat, rye, and Italian-style breads. Stroehmann also makes rolls, buns, and boxed and bagged stuffing mixes. Its brands include D'Italiano, Dutch Country, Maier's, and Stroehmann. Formerly part of Canadian food and grocery company, George Weston Limited , it was sold to Mexico's Grupo Bimbo in 2009 and integrated it into Bimbo's US operations .
