Maison Burtin may not have been there on the "the night they invented champagne," but the company has been making champagne since 1760. The French champagne vintner (one of the largest in the world) produces its tantalizing and tingly beverages from grapes grown in the Champagne region. The company's bubbly is available under such brands names as Champagne Lanson, Noble Cuvée Brut Rosé, Black Label Brut, Ivory Label Demi-Sec, and Gold Label Brut Millésimé. The vintner is part of France's Boizel Chanoine Champagne group.

The company distributes its products through traditional networks such as restaurants and wine stores.
