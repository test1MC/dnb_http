Ervin Industries, founded in 1920, is a pioneering developer of cast steel abrasives. It makes steel grit and steel shot -- sold under the AMACAST and AMASTEEL names -- for use in blast cleaning, cutting, and etching applications. Customers include manufacturers in the automotive, aerospace, construction, mining, railroad, and stone-cutting industries. Its Ervin Technologies unit develops super alloys used in aerospace and commercial applications. Ervin Industries also makes steel shot for shotgun shells. It has locations in the US, as well as in Germany and the UK.

Ervin Industries operations include AMASTEEL (cast steel shot and grit abrasives); AMACAST (stainless steel shot abrasive); and Ervin Technologies (advanced metal particles providing solutions for particle shape, chemistry, microstructure and performance).

The company's products are used in applications in diverse markets such as automotive, aerospace, agriculture, construction, foundry, mining, railroad, shipyard, stone cutting, petrochemical, and other industries.
