Dominion Enterprises can help you sell what you've got, no matter what it is. The company publishes classified advertising and provides information resources in the real estate, apartment, specialty vehicle, employment, and travel industries. Its paid and free magazine titles include Boat Trader , The Employment Guide , and For Rent . Dominion also operates dozens of websites, such as Homes.com, ForRent.com, and TraderOnline.com. In addition, the company owns businesses that offer Internet marketing, website design and hosting, lead generation, customer relationship management software, and data capture and distribution services.

The company has more than 145 offices in 33 states and 5 countries. Its websites reach more than 20 million unique monthly visitors.

In addition to its classified advertising and information holdings, Dominion hosts an online job board and a variety of annual job fairs through its employment operations, which include EmploymentGuide.com, CareersinGear.com, and Jobalot.com. Such holdings are experiencing increased traffic during the challenging employment market.

Dominion is focused on growth across all areas of the business through organic growth and strategic acquisitions. In 2013, Boat Trader, a Dominion Marine Media business advertising the widest selection of boats for sale in the US, launched an innovative iPad application for boat buyers and sellers. The Boat Trader iPad app is designed to make searching and finding a boat easier.
