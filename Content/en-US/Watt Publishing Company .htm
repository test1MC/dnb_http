If it's information on cabinet making or poultry raising you need, look no further than Watt Publishing Company. The firm offers agribusiness information through its WATTAgNet.com portal, and also publishes niche magazine titles such as WATT PoultryUSA, Feed International , and Pig International . Woodworking information is offered through its CabinetMaker + FDM Magazine (a result of the merger of two previously separate titles), as well as related Web site CabinetMakerFDM.com. The company also offers online social networking services and free Web seminars such as Sustainability in Packaging for Petfood and New Products and New Opportunities in the Meat and Poultry Industry . Watt Publishing was founded in 1917.
