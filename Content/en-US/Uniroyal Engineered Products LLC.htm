Uniroyal Engineered Products boasts a plethora of "pleather" (plastic/leather) products. The company, which also goes by Naugahyde Company, is the sole manufacturer of Naugahyde -- knit fabric backed by polyvinyl chloride (PVC) plastic coating. The company makes Naugahyde products for the automotive industry primarily, but also for hospitality, health care, marine, office, recreational vehicle, residential facility, and transportation industries. The Nauga name comes from Naugatuck, Connecticut, where the product was first made and trademarked in 1936.

Headquartered in Sarasota, Florida, the company has manufacturing facilities, including research and development (R&D) and testing laboratories at its Stoughton, Wisconsin, facility.

Uniroyal provides a selection of patterns and colors. It also offers customized and specialty products using proprietary back coat systems in addition to micro-perforation and flame blocker technology.

The company has more than 500 standard products. Its key brands include NAUGAHYDE, NAUGAFORM, and DURAN. Cleaning agent-resistant coated fabrics are branded under the name BEAUTY GARD, and its flame and smoke retardant coated fabrics are branded under the FLAME BLOCKER.
