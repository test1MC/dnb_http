U. S. Steel Košice (USSK, also known as the East Slovakian Steelworks), a subsidiary of United States Steel , is among Europe's larger steel producers along with Tata Steel Europe and ArcelorMittal . The company's customers include automotive manufacturers, appliance makers, the construction industry, radiator panel producers, and tube and section makers. USSK also provides shipping, stocking, transportation, and financing of its mills products. It sells its steel products in sheet, strip, tubular, precision tube, and specialty steel forms. The company primarily does business in Central and Western Europe.
