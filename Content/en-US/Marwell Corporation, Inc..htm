Marwell manufactures electric meter socket adaptors. It sells its products directly to OEMs and power utility companies, and through distributors in Canada and the US. The company was established in 1979. Marwell is owned by its officers; co-founder and CEO Larry Blackwell owns about half of the company, while GM Kathleen Powell holds around one-quarter of the equity.
