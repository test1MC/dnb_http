Prince Lionheart wants to keep all little princes and princesses across the land safe and happy. The company makes products for infants -- including safety guards for furniture edges, stoves, stairs, and fireplaces; diaper holders and other storage containers and organizers for baby products; and bottles and dishwasher baskets for infant feeding implements. Prince Lionheart's Original Slumber Bear, a revamp of a toy first marketed in the 70's, uses recorded womb sounds to lull babies to sleep. Its products are sold online through the company's Web site and at Toys R' Us and Babies R' Us stores. Thomas McConnell owns and runs the company, which he founded in 1973.
