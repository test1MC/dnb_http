King Par can spice up your golf game faster than an order of Kung Pao. The company manufactures and distributes golf clubs and accessories under such brands as Affinity, Fortune, Intech, and Knight. It also develops private-label programs for retailers the likes of Wal-Mart and JJB Sports . In addition, King Par operates a 25,000-sq.-ft. superstore in Flushing, Michigan, offering up its own brands as well as pro-line products (such as TaylorMade ). Amenities at the store include a driving range with more than 30 covered-and-heated tees and a Par 3 executive course. The company was formed by management in 2009, however, the roots of its manufacturing and retailing businesses reach back to 1966.
