Vale Fertilizantes is invested in growth. Known simply as Fertilizantes, it supplies the raw materials, phosphate, and nitrogen products for Brazilian fertilizer manufacturers. The company supplements its fertilizer business -- about three-quarters of sales -- by supplying the chemical industry with ammonium nitrate, acids, and other raw materials, and with logistics including rail, highway terminals, and pipelines from its maritime terminal in Santos. Fertilizantes boasts about a dozen processing plants. While Brazil imports more than half of the fertilizer it consumes, Fertilizantes is the country's largest producer of those raw materials. Vale SA acquired the fertilizer business in 2011.
