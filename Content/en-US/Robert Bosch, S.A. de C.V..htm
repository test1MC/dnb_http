Robert Bosch, S.A. de C.V. keeps your car running and your house humming. The company is a regional branch of Robert Bosch GmbH , one of the world's largest private industrial corporations. In Mexico, the company sells Bosch's automotive spare parts, including alternators; brake pads; engine starters; fuel pumps; oil, air, gasoline, and diesel fuel filters; oxygen sensors; spark plugs; and wiper blades. It also sells Bosch home appliances from food processors to washing machines; power tools for construction industry professionals to do-it-yourselfers; heating systems ( Buderus brand); automation systems ( Bosch Rexworth ); and security systems for such things as fire and intrusion detection, and access control.
