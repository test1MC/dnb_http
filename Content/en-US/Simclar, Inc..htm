Simclar could be the name of a new superhero. The company, together with its subsidiaries, provides contract electronics manufacturing and other services for equipment makers that cater to the data processing, food preparation, instrumentation, military, and telecommunications markets. Products include printed circuit boards, custom electro-mechanical assemblies, cables, and wire harnesses. The company also provides product repair and refurbishment services. Simclar, which was majority-owned by the UK-based Simclar Group , primarily operates in the US and Mexico.
