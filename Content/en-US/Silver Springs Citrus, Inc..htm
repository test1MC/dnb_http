Silver Springs Citrus can slake the thirst of every OJ drinker in the land. The company processes and packs single-strength (not concentrate or made from concentrate) canned, chilled, and bulk orange and grapefruit juices for retail customers in the US and internationally. Silver Springs' business comes from producing private-label brands but it also offers co-packing services for other firms. In the heart of the US's southern citrus-growing region, the firm, headquartered in the colorfully named Florida burg of Howey-in-the-Hills, traces its roots back to a small neighborhood operation that had its beginnings in the mid-1920s. The company is 51% owned by Sapporo International and 49% by Toyota Tsusho America .
