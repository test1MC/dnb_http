With a name that sheds light on what it does, Environmental Lighting for Architecture (ELA) lights up commercial and residential buildings. The company designs and manufactures outdoor lighting fixtures, including bollards, crossarms, pier mounts, pole bases, and accessories, for commercial, hospitality, theme park, and high-end residential customers. For building interiors, its short list runs from chandeliers to wall sconces, torchieres, and floor lamps, with a variety of finishes. While some products are distributed through lighting showrooms, ELA touts custom services for landmark buildings to casinos, as well as restoration of original fixtures. It operates a plant in California, and a foundry in Tijuana.
