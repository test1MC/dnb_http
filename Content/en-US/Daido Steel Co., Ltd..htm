Daido Steel has built a big business making and marketing specialty steel products and components for automobiles. Founded in 1916, Daido also specializes in making industrial and electrical machinery. It boasts a vast portfolio of specialty steel parts that it supplies to the likes of Nissan Motor , Honda Motor , and Mitsubishi Heavy Industries . Products include tool steel, stainless steel, die forgings, welding wire, rare earth magnets, high alloys, titanium products, mechanical construction steel, and nickel alloys. Daido has a dozen plants in Japan and operates an international network with locations in Asia and North America. Several entities, such as Nippon Steel , Toyota , and Honda, hold stakes in Daido.
