ClearCapital.com helps provide a clear picture of a property's value. The company's network of 29,000 real estate professionals provides loan valuation services on properties throughout the US and Puerto Rico. Clients include financial institutions such as banks, loan servicers, and others in the mortgage industry. ClearCapital.com's technology helps customers order, manage, and track broker price opinions (BPOs) and property inspections. The company also provides access to a screened list of local real estate owned (REO) brokers and vendors.
