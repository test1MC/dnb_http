This publisher covers all the news that a soldier can use. Sightline Media Group (formerly Gannett Government Media Corporation) is a leading producer of newspapers that target US military personnel and government employees throughout the world. Its weekly papers include Air Force Times, Defense News, Federal Times, Marine Corps Times , and Navy Times , as well as its namesake Army Times . The company's portfolio also includes in-depth news journals such as Armed Forces Journal, C4ISR Journal , and Training & Simulation Journal . In addition to print publications, it distributes news through a number of websites. Melvin Ryder started publishing Army Times in 1940. The company is part of media giant TEGNA .
