With Draka Elevator Products around, there's was need for draconian measures to find parts for your elevator system. Draka manufactured elevator cables and wire rope and sold a variety of hoistway hardware, kits, and elevator electromechanical parts. It also made components for escalator systems and offered customized products such as power-conducting cables, coaxial cable units, and other instrumentation wires and cables. Draka was acquired by and integrated into Milan-based Prysmian in 2011.
