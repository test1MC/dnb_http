It's a fact: Nicholas Brealey Publishing is devoted to non-fiction. The company specializes in books on such topics as business, management, popular psychology, self-help, humor, leadership development, and Asian philosophies. In recent years it has expanded to include digital publishing operations and works of narrative non-fiction, such as adventure and travel writing. Nicholas Brealey Publishing also owns cross-cultural relations imprint Intercultural Press , which focuses on titles for readers who live or do business in cultures other than their native ones. The firm has offices in London and Boston. Nicholas Brealey Publishing was founded in 1992 and is headed by, you guessed it, Nick Brealey.
