Peter Pepper wants its customers to pick a peck of its workspace furnishings and products. The company designs and manufactures office furniture and accessories, including clocks, coat racks, magazine holders, tables, benches, waste baskets -- even planters. Peter Pepper also imports items from Canada, Europe, and Asia. Products are sold through corporate and government contract sales, as well as through retailers (which have included Boise Office Solutions , Ivan Allen Workspace , and Herman Miller ). The company operates two facilities in Southern California, runs a showroom in Chicago, and has factory representatives across the US. The office products maker was founded in 1952 by its namesake, Peter Pepper.
