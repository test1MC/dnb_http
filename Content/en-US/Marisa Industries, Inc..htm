Marisa Industries provides contract manufacturing for metal stamping and welding and mechanism assembly services primarily through its BAE Industries subsidiary. Products include cam and bolt assemblies, door check assemblies, seat frame assemblies, and hose attachment brackets. The company's metal stamping and welding segment manufactures small soap dish, cam and bolt, door check, and heavy-gage mount assemblies. The mechanism assembly unit specializes in back plate, door check, and reinforcement assemblies. Marisa Industries' major clients are in the automotive industry.
