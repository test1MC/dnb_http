AsteelFlash Group (formerly Flash Electronics) doesn't make flashy products -- but its customers do. The company provides contract electronics manufacturing services, such as printed circuit board assembly, prototyping, custom packaging, systems integration, logistics management, and testing. AsteelFlash's target industries include telecommunications, Internet, computers, automation, and instrumentation. The company partners with distributors such as Arrow Electronics and Future Electronics . Nokia , Occam , and Pinnacle Systems are among its roster of OEMs. AsteelFlash Group was created in 2008 when ASTEEL, a contract electronics manufacturer based in France, acquired Flash Electronics.

Asteelflash operates about 20 manufacturing sites around the world. The company's subsidiary, Asteelflash California Inc., provides electronics manufacturing and integrated supply chain services. It offers manufacturing, testing, and fulfillment solutions.

Based in France, Asteelflash has operations elsewhere in Europe and in the US and Asia.

Asteelflash's are in businesses ranging from telecom, Internet, computers, and automation and instrumentation to industrial control, audio/video, and storage markets.
