Products made by Global-Tech Advanced Innovations (formerly Global-Tech Appliances) whir, brew, and polish their way through homes in Asia, Australia, Europe, and the US. The company makes kitchen appliances (blenders, coffee machines, food processors), garment care (irons), and floor care (vacuums) products. It also makes electronic components such as cell phone screens, home security systems, and car audio systems. Most of its revenue comes from sales to other manufacturers, Royal Appliance and Electrolux Home Care Products North America , which sell its products under their own brands, represent 70% of sales. President and CEO John C.K. Sham owns about 62% of the firm through Wing Shing Holdings.
