They may not be straight from the booth at the farmers' market, but the products of Small Planet Foods are back-to-the-earth-for-you good. A subsidiary of General Mills , the company offers more than 100 products that are USDA -certified organic. Small Planet Foods markets its products under the Cascadian Farm, Muir Glen, Lärabar, and Small Planet brand names. Cascadian products include breakfast cereals, frozen fruits and vegetables fruit spreads, granola and granola bars. Muir Glen makes premium tomato products such as pasta sauces, soups, ketchup, salsas, and canned tomatoes. As part of its business, Small Planet Foods also makes and markets frozen smoothie mix kits.
