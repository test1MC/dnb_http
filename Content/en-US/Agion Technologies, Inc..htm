Agion Technologies (under the Agion Antimicrobial brand) is fighting the good fight in the battle against dirt and diseases. The company provides engineered antimicrobials that inhibit the growth of bacteria, mold, and fungus. Its antimicrobials are used in a variety of industrial, consumer, and medical products, including appliances, building products, food packaging, medical devices, molded plastic, fibers, and coatings, in a variety of products including cell phones, shoes, keyboards, and ice machines. Agion Technologies has operations in North America and Europe. Its customers include leading brands such as PPG , Carrier , DuPont and Adidas . Agion Technologies was acquired by Sciessent LLC in 2011.
