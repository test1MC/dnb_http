China Green Agriculture's business is rich in soil. The Nevada-incorporated parent owns a New Jersey holding company, Green Agriculture Holding, that operates a fertilizer business in China through main subsidiary Shaanxi Tech Team Jinong Humic Acid Product Co. (or simply Jinong). Jinong and its China-based subsidiaries operate greenhouse and production facilities from which they develop and produce various types of fertilizer and agricultural products, such as fruits, vegetables, flowers, and seedlings. Its production capacity totals more than 350,000 metric tons of fertilizer per year. Products are sold through a network of regional distributors covering more than 20 provinces in China.
