PIKA Technologies is a manufacturer of computer networking subsystems designed to add telephony capabilities to computers. The company's networking card products include digital voice, analog circuit, fax interface, as well as specialized DSP and recording models. PIKA's customers have included such service and hardware providers as BT , CTD Systems, Red Fig, and Sytech . The company's products are used for applications including VOIP, call center quality monitoring, and PBX implementation.
