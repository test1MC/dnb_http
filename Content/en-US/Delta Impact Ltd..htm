Delta Impact can help make a small company seem big. The company offers a range of services meant to increase the operating leverage of smaller electronics manufacturers. It offers contract electronics manufacturing, through which it produces printed circuit boards (PCBs) for makers of electronic gear. Delta Impact provides offers related services in product design, packaging, global component sourcing, and supply chain management. The company primarily serves customers in the automotive, security, lighting, health care, and broadcasting industries. Clients have included automation and control systems maker Honeywell and breath alcohol testing equipment maker Lion Laboratories. Delta Impact was founded in 1988.
