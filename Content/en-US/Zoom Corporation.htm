Zoom Corporation manufactures professional sound and recording equipment used by amateur and professional musicians alike. The company's products include amplifiers, effects pedals and consoles (for guitar, bass, acoustic, and studio use), digital and analog recording systems, and related digital signal processor (DSP) systems. Its H4 and H2 digital recorders have become more affordable alternatives to CAT and MiniDisc recorders. Zoom, which launched its multi-effect Processor 9002 in 1990, sells its products worldwide through distributors and retail partners including Samson Technologies in the US. Zoom rolled out its RhythmTrak Series (a drum and bass machine) in 1998 and has expanded that category since.
