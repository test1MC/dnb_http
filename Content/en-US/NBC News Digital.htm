Forget about 24-hour news, this company files reports on Internet time. NBC News Digital operates NBCNews.com, a leading online news site. Formerly MSNBC Interactive News, the business is the online channel for the NBCUniversal 's various news holdings, including the Today show, NBC Nightly News , Dateline NBC , and Meet the Press . MSNBC Interactive News was formed in 1996 as a 50-50 partnership, jointly owned by software giant Microsoft (through its MSN division) and NBC Universal, which is 51%-owned by cable giant Comcast . However, in 2012 Microsoft pulled out of the venture to form its own online news service. MSNBC Interactive subsequently changed its name and moved from Redmond, Washington to New York City.
