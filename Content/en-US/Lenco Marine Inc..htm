Lenco Marine makes electric trim tabs, trolling motors, hatch lifts, tactile switches, and actuators for the boating industry. Trim tabs are stainless steel plates attached to the transom of a boat -- the tabs keep the vessel from rocking violently while it is stopped in the water, and also smooth the feel of the boat's ride while moving in choppy waters. Trim tabs are raised and lowered using hydraulic actuators; they can also be fitted with small trolling motors. The company has distributors throughout the US and around the world. The company, which was established in 1998,  is owned by its officers.
