EISENMANN AG (formerly Eisenmann Maschinenbau) keeps things movin' along. The company designs, builds, delivers, and installs body shop conveyor systems, surface finishing systems, and final assembly lines, primarily for the automotive industry. Its automation systems, including its branded VarioRobot, provides mechanized processes for pretreating, painting, and sealing metal, plastics, and wood, via atomizer, powder coating, spray painting, and dip coating systems. It builds firing lines for ceramics, thermal treatment of aluminum, as well as sintering plants for powder metal. It also develops environmental processing technology for air purification, waste water treatment, waste management, and recycling.
