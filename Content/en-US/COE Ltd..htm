COE Ltd. offers a range of equipment used in closed-circuit television (CCTV) networks. COE designs and manufactures video surveillance equipment for surveillance, transmission, and management of video over IP, fiber, and hybrid video networks worldwide. Products include single- and multi-channel transmitters, digital video transmission equipment (video over IP; VoIP), and command and control systems. COE markets the gear for applications focused on traffic and transport, and heavy industrial and urban surveillance. It has some 10,000 installations in Europe and Asia. The company is owned by technology consulting firm Digital Barriers .

COE offers X-stream IP encoders; transmission products, such as X net expansion cards and rail approved video transmission products; and I-command video management products, such as I-command pro video management systems, SmartDetect analytics, MultiView video wall managers, and SecureStream remote viewers.

The company was established in 1989 by six founding members, including CTO Mark Marriage and chief engineer Mark Waddington.
