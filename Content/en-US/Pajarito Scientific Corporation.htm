The business of Pajarito Scientific is to keep its eyes and ears on the nuclear sector. The company offers detection, monitoring, and measurement products and services primarily to nuclear power plants, fuel manufacturing facilities, military entities, and waste storage centers. Products include uranium enrichment monitors, spent fuel monitors, and radioactive waste analysis equipment. Formed as a spinoff from Los Alamos National Laboratory , Pajarito was bought by British Nuclear Fuels (BNFL) in 1995, renamed BNFL Instruments, and then BIL Solutions Inc. President David Heath acquired the company in 2007 and rechristened it Pajarito Scientific. It has offices in China, the UK, and the US.
