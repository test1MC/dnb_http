edel is trying to tune in music lovers in Europe. The independent music company has operations in nine countries across Europe (including Germany, France, and Italy). edel has signed recording deals with artists such as Lisa Stansfield, Chris Rea, Chris de Burgh, Deep Purple, and Toni Braxton. In addition, the company has CD manufacturing operations, a music publishing business, and marketing and promotional subsidiaries. CEO Michael Haentjes founded edel in 1986.
