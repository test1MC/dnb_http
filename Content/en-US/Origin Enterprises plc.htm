Origin is committed to the basics -- food production. The agri-nutrition firm has operations devoted to feed ingredients, fertilizer blending and distribution, integrated on-farm agronomy, and marine proteins and oils, such as fishmeal. Origin has operations in Ireland, the UK, Poland, the Ukraine, and Romania. Its subsidiaries include Goulding Chemicals and R&H Hall . The company spun off its food unit (comprising the Odlums and Shamrock baking brands) in 2010. Origin is 14% owned by Canada's Mawer Investment Management Ltd. The company was created in 2008 when former owner IAWS Group sold shares in the company.
