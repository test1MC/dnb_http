Barlow Sheet Metal has the low-down on all the ways to process sheet metal for just about any application. Established in 1974, the company provides precision sheet metal fabrication, largely for the electronics industry. Its operations are driven by a computer numerical controlled (CNC) punching/programming tool that creates the complex series of steps for making the end sheet metal product. Barlow's production capacity stretches from cutting to punching, bending, de-burring and surface finishing, welding, as well as insert assembly. Products include air powered sheet metal shears, assembled components, brass sheet metal panel chips, clip-jointed metal ducting, and electronic equipment metal casings.
