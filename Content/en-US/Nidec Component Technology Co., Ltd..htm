If its disk drive parts are not brilliant, then this company wouldn't be living up to its name. Nidec Brilliant is a contract manufacturer of parts used in the production of computer disk drives. Core products include base plates, brackets, top covers, and metal stampings. The company also offers coating and materials research services. Customers include Seagate Technology . Increased demand has enabled the company to expand into new countries throughout Southeast Asia. Former CEO Harry Koh founded Brilliant Manufacturing in 1984. Nidec , a manufacturer of spindle motors for hard-disk drives and motors for other applications, in 2007 acquired Brilliant for about S$170 million ($111 million) in cash.
