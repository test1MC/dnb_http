If you find mainstream journalism a dull, cold world, you might try this online publication. Slate Magazine operates a popular website offering news and commentary slightly skewed for hip and erudite readers. The Internet magazine covers the arts, business, politics, sports, travel, and technology and attracts an audience of about 5 million people. The website is supported through advertising and sponsorships. Started by Michael Kinsley in 1996, Slate Magazine is owned by Graham Holdings (formerly The Washington Post Company). In 2008 the magazine became part of Washington Post's newly created Slate Group.
