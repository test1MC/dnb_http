You might say Beacon Journal Publishing shines a light on the news in northeastern Ohio. The company publishes The Akron Beacon Journal which serves the Akron, Ohio, area. In addition to its print edition, Beacon Journal Publishing operates Ohio.com, offering news and sports information online. The Akron Beacon Journal was started in 1897 when the Summit Beacon (founded in 1839) merged with the Akron Evening Journal (founded in 1896). Beacon Journal Publishing is owned by Canadian newspaper company Black Press Group through subsidiary Sound Publishing.
