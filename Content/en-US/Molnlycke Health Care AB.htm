Mölnlycke Health Care products help doctors to clean you up and pull you together. Hospitals and health care professionals turn to the company's single-use surgical and wound care products to treat infected wounds, scars, and skin tears. Its surgical division makes antiseptics, face masks, scrub suits, and surgical gloves and gowns, while its wound care division makes dressings and swabs. Mölnlycke Health Care operates about 25 offices in Africa, Asia, Europe, the Middle East, and North America, with manufacturing plants in Asia and Europe. Established in 1997, Mölnlycke is controlled by Swedish investment firm Investor AB .
