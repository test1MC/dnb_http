Seicon takes the stress out of bad vibrations, dude. Seicon Limited develops, applies, and licenses technology that helps reduce seismic and mechanicaly induced vibration for bridge design or other applications. The company's products include tunable noise and vibration isolators, pipe supports, and motor mounts. In addition to being tunable, the Seicon Systems also provide redundant load paths, effective functioning at high and low frequencies, and effectiveness at heavy loads. Chairman, sculptor, and engineer John Cunningham founded Seicon Limited and holds patents on engineering structures for bridges and pipelines and motor mounts and isolators.
