On a damp, chilly winter's night, there's nothing quite like the comfort of a soft and refined Dawson-inspired cashmere apparel piece. Dawson International has built a business making and distributing cashmere apparel. Its UK operations comprises the Barrie knitwear business, based in Hawick in the Scottish Borders, while its US unit comprises its Dawson Forte cashmere business based in Boston. Dawson sells its products to couture houses, department stores, and private label retail outlets. Barrie's knitwear products are sold in stores around the world from Japan and Korea to the US and particularly in Continental Europe.
