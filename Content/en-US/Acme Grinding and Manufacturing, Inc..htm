Acme Grinding and Manufacturing provides precision metal grinding and machining services, was founded in 1948 as a bowling pin manufacturer. Acme Grinding and Manufacturing's machines are equipped for throughfeed, infeed, bar, and crush processes, a machining diversity that allows for faster turnaround rates and lower costs. The company has more than 40 machine tools on the premises. In CNC-based machining, Acme Grinding and Manufacturing offers milling and turning services. Former owner Judy Pike sold Acme Grinding in 2003 to Charles Zaluckyi, owner of Rockford-based Reliable & Accurate Manufacturing Co.
