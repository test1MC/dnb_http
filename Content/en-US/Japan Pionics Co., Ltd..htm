Japan Pionics manufactures industrial equipment used in the production of semiconductors. The company's products include gas purification and liquid delivery systems, line filters, and waste gas cleaners. Japan Pionics also makes emergency oxygen masks, disposable body warmers, and a variety of heaters, including fog-free mirror heaters. Mitsubishi Gas Chemical Company owns 75% of the company; Ryoyo Trading owns the remaining 25%. The company was founded in 1962.
