Carolina Beverage makes and distributes cherry-flavored Cheerwine and Diet Cheerwine soft drinks, Blue Mist bottled water, and Savage Energy Drink. Despite its name, Cheerwine does not contain any alcohol, but is named for its red color. Carolina Beverage's products are available in the south and southeastern US -- primarily in North and South Carolina, Georgia, Tennessee, Virginia, and West Virginia. Due to expanded distribution, Cheerwine is now available in some 20 states, including California. Retailers that carry Cheerwine include Wal-Mart , Kroger , Publix , Food Lion , and Ingles .
