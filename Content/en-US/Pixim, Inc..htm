Pixim develops imaging technology for the security camera industry. Its chipsets are designed with the company's branded Digital Pixel System technology, which filters out glare, allowing for a clearer picture. Unlike analog CCD cameras where pixels work as one unit, Pixim's all-digital technology allows each pixel to receive and adjust images separately for a higher-resolution outcome. The Digital Pixel System has applications in industrial automation, medical imaging, motion picture production, and surveillance. Customers include Cisco , GE Security , Honeywell , Pelco , and Siemens . The Digital Pixel technology was originally developed at Stanford University and licensed to Pixim. Pixim was founded in 1999.
