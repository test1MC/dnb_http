At the junction of high fashion and high technology stands Auri Inc. (dba Auri Footwear), a maker of technology-infused footwear for men and women. The company uses high-end materials, including Italian leathers and NASA grade materials, and hand stitching to distinguish its shoes and make them comfortable to wear. Auri makes and sells casual and dress shoes, as well as sandals. Its products are sold online and in upscale retail stores, including Nordstrom (beginning in spring 2011), throughout North America. Formerly Auri Design Group, the company changed its name to Auri, Inc. when it completed a reverse merger with Wellstone Filter Sciences in early 2011 to become a public company.
