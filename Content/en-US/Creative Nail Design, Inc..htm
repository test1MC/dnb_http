Back in dentistry school, founder Dr. Stuart Nordstrom, even in his most creative dreams, likely didn't glimpse what the future held for him. Creative Nail Design (CND), a family-run company, makes nail polish, skin care, and nail care products. In the 1970s one of Nordstrom's dental patients (also a manicurist) recognized the scent of sculpted porcelain nails in a mix Nordstrom used to create a temporary cap and challenged him to improve upon existing nail enhancement products. That he did, and today CND's products are sold nationwide through retailers such as Sally Beauty Supply and Ulta . Its brand names include Cuticle Eraser, Scentsations, SolarBalm, and SolarOil. CND is owned by Revlon .

The company is based in San Diego, California and has a presence in 65 countries worldwide.

CND is a leading professional nail, hand, and foot beauty product provider. Products include CND, SHELLAC, brand 14+ day nail color, and VINYLUX Weekly Polish.

The company's Creative Academy educational division offers nail, foot, and hand care courses in conjunction with about 70 cosmetology training centers nationwide including Paul Mitchell The School and Federico Career College locations.

The company operates through salons, retail stores (such as ULTA and Beauty Brands), distributors, trade shows, and retailers.

In 2016 CND's newest addition to its product portfolio was the CREATIVE PLAY Nail Lacquer, with 80 colors across 10 different finishes.
