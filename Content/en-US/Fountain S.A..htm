When you want something to drink, go to The Fountain. The company manufactures and markets a myriad of beverages and soups in single-serve pouches. It sells an equally dizzying array of dispensers that turn the little packet into steaming cups of coffee, tea, and hot chocolate. Some beverage, including chocolate, are also available iced. Most of the company's revenues come from small and medium-sized businesses in Europe where its brands include Memento, Bahia, Silverline, and Ottimo. Fountain sells its products through a network of independent licensees. The company was founded in 1972 and went public in 1999.
