Who wouldn't love a perfect candy? As a subsidiary of Italy-based Perfetti Van Melle S.p.A. , Perfetti Van Melle USA manufactures candy and gum. Its most well known brands include Airheads and Mentos. Its Brooklyn gum and Fruittella and Chupa Chups candy brands, although available in the US, are more popular with Europeans and Canadians than with all the confirmed chocolate lovers in the US. Like most modern-day confectioners who expand on their well-known brands rather than spend the time and money on a new product, the company's leading brands include Air Action, Airheads Xtremes, and Mento Rolls.

The Kentucky-based company sells its candy and gum products across North America.

The company aims to woo Millennials by leveraging social media, including Twitter, YouTube, and Facebook to connect with young candy lovers.
