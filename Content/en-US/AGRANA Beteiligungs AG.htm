This Austrian company's central European sugar factories produce beet sugar, which is then sold to consumers and food manufacturers worldwide. AGRANA also operates starch factories which refine potato starch and cornstarch for the food, paper, textile, alternative energy, and chemical manufacturing industries through about 55 production facilities in more than 25 countries. The company also does fruit processing and starting in 2007 fruit became (and remains) AGRANA's largest revenue sector. Its fruit plants make fruit juice concentrates, frozen fruits, and other fruit preparations for use by food and beverage manufacturers.
