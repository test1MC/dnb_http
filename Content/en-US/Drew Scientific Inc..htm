Drew Scientific specializes in the design, manufacture, and distribution of analytical systems for laboratory testing. It provides cell counters, hemoglobin analyzers, and other instruments and testing consumables (reagents) for use in the diagnosis and monitoring of diabetes, cardiovascular diseases, and hematology. The company also sells its products to the animal health and veterinary research markets. Drew Scientific has operations in the US and in the UK, and its products are sold in countries around the globe. Former parent Escalon Medical sold Drew Scientific and its JAS Diagnostics subsidiary to ERBA Diagnostics in 2012.
