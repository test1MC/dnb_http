If you want to fix it, make it, or learn how to do it, Creative Publishing international might have the book for you. Creative Publishing international (or CPi) specializes in DIY and instructional books covering topics such as photography, home and garden, and hunting and fishing. Titles include The Complete Photo Guide to Home Repair , The Quilting Bible , and Backyard Grilling .  Through licensing deals, CPi publishes step-by-step photography books with brands such as Stanley Black & Decker . Founded in 1969, CPi offers more than 300 titles. The company operates along Rockport Publishers and other imprints as part of Quayside Publishing Group , which is itself owned by Quarto Group .
