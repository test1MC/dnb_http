Boulevard Brewing Co. does not recommend that one hit the streets after enjoying its beers. One of the top 10 craft brewers in the US, Boulevard Brewing Co. offers seven beers year-round -- Bully! Porter, Dry Stout, Lunar Ale, Pale Ale, Pilsner, Single-Wide IPA, and Unfiltered Wheat Beer. Seasonal brews include Dark Truth Stout, Irish Ale, Maibock, Nutcracker Ale, and an Oktoberfest. It also offers a line of limited-release artisanal beers under the Smokestack Series label. The company brews about 140,000 barrels a year, which are sold in bottles across 13 states, mostly throughout the Midwest. Kegs are distributed to bars and restaurants in Kansas City and St. Louis. Boulevard Brewing was founded in 1989.
