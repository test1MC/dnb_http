The Walt Disney Company Limited operates all of the UK businesses for US-based The Walt Disney Company , the world's largest media conglomerate. The company distributes and promotes movies from all of Disney's film imprints, and it distributes such TV channels as The Disney Channel, Disney XD, and Playhouse Disney to UK television audiences through satellite and cable providers including Sky plc and Virgin Media . The subsidiary also operates a localized version of Disney's flagship website, which serves as a destination for kids to play games, watch videos, and to interact with one another.
