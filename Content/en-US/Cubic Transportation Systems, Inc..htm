While the rest of Cubic Corp. is going to war, Cubic Transportation Systems (CTS) is going to work. The Cubic subsidiary aids commuters in their inexorable progress toward their daily jobs by supplying fare-collection systems for mass transit systems in Asia, Australia, Europe, and North America. CTS also provides payment systems for parking facilities. Among its customers are BART , the Chicago Transit Authority , MARTA in Atlanta, and the Metro in Washington, DC. Cubic Transportation's fare-collection systems for bus and rail transit agencies often incorporate smart cards, credit card-like devices that have an embedded semiconductor.

Cubic Transportation Systems has offices and manufacturing facilities in Australia, Denmark, Germany, the UK, and the US.

Transportation Systems accounts for about one-quarter of Cubic Corp.'s sales.
