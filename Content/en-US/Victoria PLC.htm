Though founded in the late Victorian era, Victoria rolls with the 21st-century. The carpet maker designs, manufactures, and distributes mid- to high-end residential and commercial carpets, and carpet yarns. Victoria's family of businesses includes Victoria Carpets, operating in the UK and Australia; brands Munster Carpets and Navan Carpets, run in Ireland; Colin Campbell, a Canadian affiliate distributor and design showroom; and yarn maker Westwood Yarns. Victoria promotes an eco-friendly line, too, Nature's Carpet. The company sells carpet to retailers and contract markets, such as hotels, and corporate and government offices. It also holds distribution deals with various concerns, including Mannington Mills .
