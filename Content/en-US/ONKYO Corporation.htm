Onkyo Corporation's products live up to their name. The consumer products manufacturer (its name literally means "sound") makes consumer audio equipment for home and car use. Its products include car stereo speakers, CD players, DVD players, home stereo speakers, home theater systems, and home network systems. Onkyo also sells speakers manufactured by others. The firm sells its products worldwide through traditional and Internet retailers including Fry's Electronics . Onkyo, which was founded in 1946, operates more than 20 facilities (production plants, development centers, and sales units) worldwide.
