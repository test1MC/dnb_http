Salmolux wants consumers to eat its salmon while dining in the lap of luxury. The company processes, packages, and distributes gourmet seafood products, including Alder smoked salmon, poached salmon, lox, and smoked rainbow trout. It also sells deli seafood spreads. Located in suburban Seattle, Salmolux purchases fresh salmon caught in the Pacific Northwest and Alaska, and it packages its products for use by foodservice providers and retail food outlets. The company was founded in 1988 by president, George Kuetgens, and was controlled by the Kuetgens family until it was acquired by Pacific Seafood Group in 2009.
