Medical Nutrition USA develops and supplies liquid protein, fiber, urinary tract infection (UTI), and other health supplement products for the elderly, diabetics, and patients who may be nutritionally deficient. Its Pro-Stat line was developed to replenish protein lost from bed sores and malnutrition. The company's main market is long-term care facilities and assisted-living patients; other institutional customers include hospitals, dialysis clinics, and bariatric surgery clinics. Medical Nutrition USA sells through such wholesale distributors as McKesson and Medline Industries , as well as through online retail distributors. The company is a wholly owned subsidiary of Danone North America.
