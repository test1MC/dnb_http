Audi of America is the US sales and marketing arm of Volkswagen 's AUDI brand. AUDI models in the US include the A3, A4, A5, A6, and A8, as well as the R8 high-performance sports car, S Series and the TT Coupe and TTS Roadster. AUDI makes the Q7 SUV and is debuting its Q5 crossover vehicle in the US in 2011. It established an e-tron division to produce upscale performance cars with electric drivetrains; these e-tron models and TDI clean diesel products debuted in the US in 2011. Audi of America, which has around 275 dealers, offers a range of financial services for leasing and purchasing options through Audi Financial Services and Audi Bank USA. North America accounts for about 12% of AUDI's worldwide sales.
