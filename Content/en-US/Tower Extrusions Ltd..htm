Tower Extrusions is heavily into aluminum. The company, founded in 1977, produces extruded aluminum products. Its facilities, all based in the state of Texas, include five extrusion presses (ranging in size from 6 inches to 12 inches), a cast house (with an annual capacity of 50 million pounds), and an anodizing plant that can handle material as long as 38 feet. Tower Extrusions was established with the purchase of the former Republic Aluminum facility in Olney, Texas.
