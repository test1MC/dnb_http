Unlike Victoria, Van de Velde doesn't keep any secrets; its wares are right out in the open (under the dresses, skirts, pants, and blouses of women across Europe). The company makes and sells high-end women's undergarments, including bras, panties, and corsets. Its brands include Marie Jo (luxury), Marie Jo l'Aventure (classic, no frills), and PrimaDonna (plus sizes). Marie Jo and PrimaDonna account for most sales. Van de Velde sells its products through third parties ( Sara Lee , VF Corporation ), department stores, and its own stores in Benelux, Germany, France, and North America. It has production facilities in Belgium, Spain, Tunisia. and China. The founding Van de Velde family controls the company.
