And Klöckner Pentaplast (KP) created plastic films, and this was good because Man could then save that which was good to eat in the refrigerator! The global manufacturer produces and markets plastic sheets and rigid films for packaging food, as well as pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and electronics. Its lineup includes a breadth of printing and specialty applications, such as adhesive packaging tapes, technical films for cladding insulated pipes, and decorative surface films for furniture. KP operates more than 20 production sites in Asia, Europe, Canada, South America, and the US, ( Klöckner Pentaplast of America ). KP was acquired from private equity Cinven by an affiliate of The Blackstone Group in 2007.
