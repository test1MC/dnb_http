R.P.M. Tech has a different take on the old proverb "make hay while the sun shines." It makes dough as the snow blows. The company manufacturers snow blowers and snow removal equipment such as airport runway sweepers. R.P.M Tech's snow blowers are typically used as attachments to or in conjunction with front-end loaders, trucks, and other heavy equipment. In addition, the company makes sweepers and cleaning equipment for railroads. R.P.M Tech also distributes parts for other manufacturers and makes equipment used in agriculture, forestry, and mining.
