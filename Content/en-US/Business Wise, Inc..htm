Business Wise provides detailed corporate information about companies, including contact information and names of executives, located in the metropolitan areas of Atlanta, Georgia and Charlotte, North Carolina. The company's database of information is designed for target marketing and research use and is updated quarterly. Its Business Wise Georgia includes some 154,000 firms and more than 290,000 contacts; Business Wise Charlotte has about 50,000 firms and 96,000 contacts.
