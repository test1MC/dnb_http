Forget bangers and eggs, bacon and fried bread, kippers and baked beans, The Jordans & Ryvita Company, formerly W. Jordan (Cereals), is king of the UK breakfast table. As one of the largest breakfast cereal makers in the UK, the company manufactures some 30 organic, wholegrain breakfast cereals and health bars under the Jordans label. Its cereals include just-add-milk flaked products and muesli, along with hot cereals such as oat porridge. Jordans & Ryvita's products are available for purchase through leading UK grocers including Sainsbury , Tesco ,and Waitrose . Jordans & Ryvita also exports its cereals. The company is a subsidiary of Associated British Foods .

While competition heats up in the UK market for cereal and cereal bars, the company's business conducted internationally continues to flex its muscle by performing well.

To ensure it keeps its operations cost-effective, Jordans & Ryvita transferred production activities from its Stockport facility to Poole.

In 2015 the company introduced a lighter granola product.

Jordans & Ryvita sells its products through major supermarkets such as Tesco , Morrisons , Sainsburys , Waitrose , Ocado , and Asda .
