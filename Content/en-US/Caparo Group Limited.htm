Caparo Group is so diverse it could supply the steel needed to construct a hotel and then run the hotel, too. The holding company has operations in specialty steels, materials engineering, mill products, services and distribution, leisure, and innovation and investment. Its steel products include precision tubes, spiral weld tubes, structural steels, cold rolled steel strip, and spring steel strip. Its materials engineering division was formed by merging its engineering and vehicle products units. The group also has two regional divisions, North America and India. The group is controlled by the founding Paul family, including Lord (Swraj) Paul of Marylebone.

Caparo has more than 80 locations in Australia, Europe, Asia, and North America. It has 43 sites in the UK alone.

A major steel and engineering enterprise, the company's operating segments includes Materials Engineering, Mill Products, Services and Distribution, Specialty Steels, Leisure, and Innovation and Investment. Caparo's wider activities include new product development, materials testing services, hotels, media, furniture and interior design, financial services, energy, medical products, and private equity investment.

Caparo supplies a range of products to many industries, such as automotive, construction, off-highway, aerospace, motorsport, marine, defense, oil, gas and energy.

Caparo's business strategy includes acquisitions, organic growth of established businesses, and the development of greenfield projects.

While its focus is the UK, the group continues to be active in India through businesses such as Caparo Maruti (large sheet metal; set up in 1994 as a joint venture with carmaker Maruti Suzuki to make automotive body panels), Caparo Forging India, Caparo Stampings, and Caparo Steel Tubes. Through its Indian businesses, the group manufactures automotive systems, assemblies, advanced composites, modules, and auto components for Indian automotive OEMs and other engineering markets.
