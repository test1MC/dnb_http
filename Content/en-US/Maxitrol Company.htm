Giving customers maximum control over the flow of natural gas is a maxim at Maxitrol. The company is a manufacturer of gas regulators, filters, controls, and accessories under brand names including EXA Star and Selectra. Other products include combination gas valves, ball valves, gas filters, and shut-off valves. Maxitrol's products are used to regulate the flow of natural and manufactured gas, as well as liquefied petroleum gas. The company sells its products through an independent network of North American wholesalers and distributors. Maxitrol was founded by Frank Kern Jr. in 1946, when he acquired Detroit Regulator Co., a small firm making gas pressure regulators.

Maxitrol's headquarters is located in Southfield, Michigan, and the company has production facilities in Blissfield and Colon, Michigan.

Mertik attributes more than 95% of its sales to products developed since 1993 and holds 51 patents in 15 countries.

The company offers its products for a range of industries including central heating ventilation and air-conditioning (HVAC), heat processing, commercial cooking, and engine power generation.
