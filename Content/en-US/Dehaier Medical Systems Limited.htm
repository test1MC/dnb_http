Dehaier offers products that can breathe relief into patients. A developer and manufacturer of respiratory and oxygen home care equipment, the company offers more than 30 products, including ventilators, sleep breathing therapy and screening systems (to ease or monitor snoring and other sleep disorders), and oxygen concentrators. Its non-core offerings include vitals monitors, anesthesia machines, and X-ray machines. Dehaier sells products in China through a network about 2,000 distributors; it also sells directly to hospitals, clinics, and consumers. Founded in 1991, Dehaier announced in 2016 that it would change its name (to Lianluo Smart) and focus (to smart wearable devices).
