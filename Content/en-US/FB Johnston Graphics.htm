FB Johnston Graphics sees big ads as a sign of its future success. One of the largest industrial printers in the US, the company also offers graphic design, marketing, fulfillment, and inventory management services. It specializes in point-of-purchase promotional materials, such as window signage, in-store graphics, menu boards and slats, and other large-scale pieces. Clients have included Rockwell Automation , BP , KFC , and Burger King . Founded in 1928, FB Johnston operates facilities for both digital and screen printing in South Carolina and North Carolina. It is a subsidiary of industrial products manufacturer Illinois Tool Works .
