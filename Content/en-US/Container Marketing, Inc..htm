Container Marketing makes containers for its customers' posteriors. The company, which does business as CMI, manufactures and wholesales a range of contemporary and traditional home furnishings, including occasional and Parsons chairs, dining room tables, bar stools, and bed frames. Metal, wood, fabric, and leather are the primary materials used in its designs. CMI's product lines are on display online and at its showroom in High Point, North Carolina, but they are only sold in the US through authorized dealers.
