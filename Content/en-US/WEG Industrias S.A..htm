WEG Industrias is the industrious arm of Brazilian holding company WEG SA. WEG Industrias represents domestic operations of the Brazilian multinational, and along with WEG Exportadora (which is responsible for the holding company's international operations) makes up the WEG SA global empire. From multiple Brazilian manufacturing plants, the company produces industrial process automation systems, low voltage switch and control gear, motors, electric panels, industrial paints and varnishes, and equipment used in electricity generation and distribution. The company's largest unit, WEG Motores, develops electric motors for use in everything from heavy industry to consumer appliances.
