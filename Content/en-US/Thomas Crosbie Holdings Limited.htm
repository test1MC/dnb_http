Thomas Crosbie Holdings holds plenty of media power in Ireland. Thomas Crosbie Holdings (TCH) publishes nearly 20 newspapers in Ireland, including the daily Irish Examiner and The Sunday Business Post , as well as The Irish Post , a British-based publication. Subsidiary Thomas Crosbie Media operates a network of Web sites featuring recruiting, sports, and news content. Broadcasting holdings include stakes in Red FM and Midwest Radio. Additionally, it has recently acquired stakes in Waterford's WLR FM and southeast Ireland's youth-oriented station Beat 102-103 FM. TCH traces its history back to the 1841 launch of The Cork Examiner ; it has been owned and controlled by the Crosbie family since the early 1870s.
