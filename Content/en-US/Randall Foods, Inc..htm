And this little chickie is now on the menu, thanks to Randall Foods. The company processes poultry, pork, and beef, and distributes the fresh packaged and bulk meats and chicken to retail food outlets and foodservice operators in Southern California and Arizona. It makes and markets more than 500 different beef, chicken, and pork products under the Randall Farms brand name. The company's value-added (prepared dinner) fresh meats are sold under the Easy Does It Meals brand and feature everything to make a complete home-cooked dish. Randall also offers a line of natural meats raised free of antibiotics or growth hormones, with no added artificial ingredients. Founded in 1953, the company is owned by Stan Bloom.
