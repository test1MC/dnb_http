Concentric Storage Systems' creative solution is to use gravity, not fight gravity, in the moving and storing of materials. The company (formerly Creative Storage Systems) designs, manufactures, distributes, and installs first-in-first-out (FIFO) and last-in-first-out (LIFO) gravity flow storage systems, primarily for warehousing and distribution companies, which assist with material handling, storage, and inventory management. Its branded products and systems, such as Dura-Flo Carton Flow System, Dura-Load FreeWheeler, and Dura-SAM (stand-alone system), save storage space by eliminating excess aisles.

Concentric Storage Systems' roller-based systems allow stored products to be conveyed from the input end to the dispatch end of a material handling process without additional mechanical equipment or manpower. Its Dura-Load systems can handle loads weighing from 50 lbs. to 2,000 lbs.

The former Creative Storage Systems was acquired by Unarco Material Handling in 2015 and changed its name shortly after.
