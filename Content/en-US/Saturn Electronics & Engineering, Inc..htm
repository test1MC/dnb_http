While Saturn Electronics & Engineering isn't affiliated with General Motors ' defunct Saturn division, it is in the same orbit. The Flextronics subsidiary provides contract electronics manufacturing services (EMS) to automotive (most of its revenues), communications, and appliance customers (original equipment manufacturers). Saturn manufactures electronics products such as printed circuit board assemblies, solenoids, and electrical wiring cables, harnesses, straps, and blocks. and customers have included Ford , Hewlett-Packard , and Volkswagen .

To grow its asset portfolio and strengthen its presence in the automotive and consumer electronics businesses, global electronic manufacturer Flextronics acquired Saturn in 2012.

Saturn operates sales, manufacturing, and engineering locales in the US, China, Mexico,and the Philippines.

The company is a leading producer of high-value electronics, solenoids, and wiring harnesses.

The company provides of electronics manufacturing services, solenoids, and wiring for the automotive, appliance, consumer, energy and industrial markets.

Saturn was founded in 1985.
