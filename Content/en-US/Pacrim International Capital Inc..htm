Pacrim International Capital (PICI) is looking to the Pacific Rim for its future. The company left its Canadian real estate investments to pursue its Far East ambitions -- corrugated paper products and the packaging industry -- particularly in Hong Kong and Shenzhen, China (also known as the "World's Factory"). PICI holds 35% stake in Wah Sang Paper Products; it holds an indirect interest in excess of 75% in the corrugated packaging company. Customers include Foxconn Technology , International Paper , and Leijin Electronics. PICI keeps its head office in Hong Kong, but is still listed on the Toronto stock exchange.
