To answer the curious: The land of the Couris is covered with carpets. The company, Couristan, imports, manufactures, and distributes power-loomed and handmade area rugs, as well as residential broadloom carpets. Its North American business comprises five divisions, including handmade and power-loomed area rugs, licensed area rugs, residential broadloom, and contract custom carpeting. The company touts more than 35 area rug handmade collections and 35-plus power-loomed. The line is plied across retail outlets and design studios in the US, and a global service network. Two custom-carpet factories are run, one in the UK, and one, through a joint venture, in China. The company is led by the founding Couri family.
