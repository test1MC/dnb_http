Portable electronics manufacturing is no small affair at Yinlips Technology. Operating through its Podium Technology and Shenzhen Yinlips subsidiaries, the Chinese-based company designs and manufactures gaming, video, and music devices like MP3, MP4, and MP5 players,Â as well as micro PCs and digital photo frames. The company also makes CRT (cathode ray tube) and LCD screens. Yinlips markets its products to wholesalers and consumer resellers in China and abroad, and sells to companies like IBM and Kingston , who often resell Yinlips's products under their own brand names. Formerly known as SRKP 17, Yinlips adopted its current name in 2008 after acquiring Podium Technology and its subsidiary, Shenzhen Yinlips.
