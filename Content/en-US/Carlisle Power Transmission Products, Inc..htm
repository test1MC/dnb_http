Carlisle Power Transmission Products doesn't know how to belt out a tune, but it sure can put out belts, industrial ones, that is. The company manufactures power transmission belts, bushings, pulleys, and related components. Carlisle Power Transmission sells its belt lineup through a direct sales force to OEMs -- primarily lawn and garden and construction equipment makers, and to some extent home appliance and power sports/recreational vehicle manufacturers. The company also caters to mass merchandisers and wholesale industrial distributors. Founded in 1905 as the Dayton Rubber Manufacturing Company, and subsequently renamed Dayco Products, the company became a division of the Carlisle Companies in 2001.
