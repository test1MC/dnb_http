Navigating in the PDF

To download the free, latest version of Adobe Reader, go to this web site: http://get.adobe.com/reader/.

  1. To return to the page you were on after you click a cross reference link, press Alt + Left Arrow.
  2. To return to the first page of the document, press the Shift + Home.
  3. To navigate in the PDF file or change the Adobe Reader settings, on the Adobe Reader menu,
    select View > Page Navigation, Page Display, and Zoom.
