Regional Host Availability

Data requests for the following 2.0 products are serviced by regional D&B hosts, which have regularly scheduled local maintenance windows:

  • Alternative Detailed Company Profile [DCP_ALT_*]
  • Predictive Bankruptcy & Payment Risk [PBPR_*]
  • Financial Statements [FIN_ST_PLUS]
  • Predictive Global Payment Risk - D&B Emerging Market Mediation Alert Score (EMMA) [PGPR_EMMA]
  • Payment Info and PAYDEX Rating - Detail [PIAP_*]
  • U.S. Patriot Act Plus [PAC_PLUS]

During these maintenance windows, requests may receive PD005 "product not available" response codes depending on the components under maintenance at the time.
